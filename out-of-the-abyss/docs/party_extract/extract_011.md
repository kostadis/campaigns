## Relationships & Decisions

- **Daz** made the pivotal argument that convinced the group to accept Ploopploopeen's offer, framing it as a psychological necessity after days of aimless fleeing and the trauma of a party member killing another party member.
- **Grygum** engaged diplomatically with Ploopploopeen, probing the nature of the sacrifice ritual and whether willingness mattered, helping the party understand the plan.
- **Thorin** assessed the village's defenses (noting the netting walls were ineffective) and pushed for retreat when the party was spotted near the Deep Father altar.
- **Zalthir** repeatedly clarified the party's exact obligations — confirming they would not be required to throw the first punch and that the kuo-toa civil conflict remained the kuo-toa's responsibility. He secured verbal confirmation from Glooglugogg.
- The party collectively formed an alliance with **Ploopploopeen** and his faction (Sea Mother followers), agreeing to pose as sacrificial offerings to lure out **Bloppblippodd** and her Deep Father cultists.
- The party met **Glooglugogg (Gloog)**, Ploopploopeen's son, who despite initial disapproval of outsider involvement, warmed to them after they showed respect to the Sea Mother. He clarified that the party *would* be expected to help fight, correcting Zalthir's assumption of total non-involvement.
- **Gloog** noted that the party chose not to hand over the captive derro as the sacrifice, calling it "both honorable and odd" — suggesting the party's moral choice was noticed.
