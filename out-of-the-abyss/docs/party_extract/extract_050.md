## Relationships & Decisions

**Daz** — Formed a provisional alliance with Asha Vandree. Terms: the party kills Ilvara; in exchange they receive Ilvara's magical items (possibly contaminated) and Asha's gratitude/Lolth's favor. Asha will hold the perimeter during the fight but will not initiate. Daz recognizes Asha operates on her own schedule and self-interest, but considers the deal workable. He also pushed back on Asha's offered payment of "the gratitude of Lolth," negotiating for material compensation.

**Daz** — Revealed the full party to Asha, presenting his "followers" as evidence of legitimacy. This exposed the group to Asha's knowledge but strengthened the alliance's perceived value.

**Thorin** — Assessed Asha as a self-interested drow ally who will fight on her own terms. Filed this as acceptable but noted it. Assessed Jorlan as a predictable threat — useful because predictable.

**Zalthir** — Made the decision to open the fight by teleporting directly to Ilvara alone, accepting maximum personal risk to seize initiative. The gambit dealt significant damage but left him poisoned and exhausted of actions.

**Grygum** — Made the decision to expend the Dust of Suleiman (a limited resource) on the Big Heart Fungus rather than saving it, prioritizing neutralizing the environmental hazard.

**The Party** — Collectively decided on the battle plan: Glyph of Warding on the bridge as a trap, Zalthir opens with a teleport strike on Ilvara, Daz uses Sculpted Fireball on Ilvara and the icon, Grygum handles the Big Heart Fungus with the Dust, Asha covers the perimeter. The party is now committed to killing Ilvara as an obligation to Asha Vandree.
