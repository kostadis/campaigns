## Relationships & Decisions

- **Party collectively** decided to return the corrupted dragon egg to Gartokkar (Keeper of the Flame), framing the theft as the work of a "crazed group" rather than specifically the Derro — a deliberate deception to avoid triggering a genocidal war between Duergar and Derro.
- **Grygum** devised the diplomatic strategy: leverage Gartokkar for escape from Gracklstugh, with the implicit threat that if Errde Blackskull imprisoned them, they might reveal the truth under torture. This positions Gartokkar as their patron for leaving the city.
- **Zalthir** consciously chose restraint — he recognized the journal and letters could condemn the Derro and spark genocide, but questioned whether that was the right thing to do.
- **Thorin and Zalthir** aligned on a "least chaos possible" exit strategy, a notable shift from earlier inclinations.
- **Thorin** named the party **"The Ember Vanguard"** — all members accepted the name.
- **Thorin–Zalthir relationship** strained by Thorin's thoughtless dragon comments, though Thorin attempted an apology.
- **Daz** chose to use intimidation (Dancing Lights deception) rather than violence against the Derro guards, supported by the party's decision not to attack.
