## Personality
**Character Backstory:**
Born in a bustling coastal city known for its vibrant docks and seafaring trade, Zalthir "Zal" Thunderscale grew up amidst the salty air and the roar of the ocean. As a Brass Dragonborn, he bore the distinctive golden-hued scales of his kind, glimmering like the sun on the water's surface. His early life was marked by the stories of his ancestors—noble protectors of the seas who championed freedom and adventure.

Zalthir's parents were skilled sailors, known for their expertise in navigating treacherous waters and trading with distant lands. They instilled in him a love for the sea, teaching him the ways of sailing, survival, and respect for the natural world. From a young age, Zal felt a calling toward the ocean, spending endless hours by the docks, dreaming of the adventures that awaited him beyond the horizon.

However, it was not just the thrill of sailing that captivated him. Zalthir's upbringing was also steeped in the teachings of a local monastery, where a sect of monks revered the elements, including the mighty ocean. Drawn to their philosophy of harmony and discipline, he began training under their guidance, learning martial arts and meditation to unify his body, mind, and spirit. His instructors recognized his innate connection to the elemental forces, particularly the gentle but powerful currents of the sea.

As he matured, Zalthir embraced his dual passions: the life of a sailor and the path of a monk. He joined a merchant crew, where he quickly became known for his agility and fierce loyalty to his homeland and the crew, his family. They sailed to distant ports, trading goods and sharing tales of adventure. But the life of a sailor was not without its dangers. Zal faced storms, sea monsters, and rival pirates, all while honing his skills in unarmed combat and meditation during the quiet nights at sea.
