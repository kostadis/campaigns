# Out of the Abyss — module proper-noun inventory

Complete inventory of NPCs, deities, locations, planes, items, factions, and
concepts extracted from the module text in
`docs/background/Out of the Abyss.md` (D&D 5e, Wizards of the Coast).

This is the **source-material list**, not the campaign's homebrew. For the
modified-OOTA adaptation's adjusted roster and current NPC states see
`docs/campaign_state.md`, `docs/world_state.md`, and the dossiers in `docs/npcs/`.
