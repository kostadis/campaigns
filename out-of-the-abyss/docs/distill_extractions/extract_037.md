## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Ooze Infestation**: Blingdenstone is suffering from a significant ooze infestation. The party has been conscripted by Quartermaster Senni Diggermattock to resolve it in exchange for help reaching the Overbright. The source, extent, and nature of the infestation are not yet revealed.
- **Glabbagool and the Oozes**: Glabbagool has experience with oozes and describes them as "very not talkative," suggesting he has interacted with them. His role in the ooze quest is unresolved — he may serve as a resource or guide.
- **The Emerald Vanguard**: The party considered using this name and rejected it because the group is blamed for destroying a city. The identity of this group, their relationship to the party, and the destroyed city are unresolved.
- **Jimjar's Bet**: Jimjar wagered five empty spell gems that meeting with the gnomes was the right thing to do. The outcome of this bet — whether the party considers it won or lost — is unresolved. The nature and value of empty spell gems is not clarified.
- **Drow Intelligence**: Clan Diggermattock intercepted the party specifically to gather intelligence about the Drow from Drow escapees. Whether this intelligence exchange has occurred or will occur is unresolved.
- **Reaching the Overbright**: The party's stated goal of reaching the Overbright remains unaccomplished; they must resolve the ooze infestation before receiving gnome assistance.
