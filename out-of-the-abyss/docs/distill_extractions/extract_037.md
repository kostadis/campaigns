**Jimjar**
- Current location: Traveling with the party in Blingdenstone

**Glabbagool**
- Current location: Traveling with the party in Blingdenstone
- Current state: Active, sentient, friendly gelatinous cube; companion of the party
- Recent actions: Repeatedly clarified he is a gelatinous cube, not an ooze (citing higher viscosity and ability to maintain a square shape); expressed confusion about why oozes were causing gnomes pain; demonstrated his pseudo-pod abilities (can absorb things and punch); became excited about shambling mounds and asked if they contained treasure when they exploded; noted oozes are "very not talkative"
- Faction: Party companion
- Key traits: Can extend pseudo-pods, absorb things, and punch; retains treasure inside his body (unlike shambling mounds); has experience with oozes

## World Events

- A sergeant among the deep gnome guards failed to thoroughly read a memo requiring all Drow escapees to be escorted to Chief Dorbo Diggermattock, allowing the party to initially pass unchecked.
- Deep gnome guards chased down and caught up with the party at a gate deep within Blingdenstone's caverns.
- The party was escorted through Blingdenstone to Diggermattock Hall and presented to Chief Dorbo and Quartermaster Senni.
- The party was tasked with resolving the ooze infestation in Blingdenstone as a condition for receiving help reaching the Overbright.

## Locations

**Blingdenstone**
- Deep gnome (Svirfneblin) community in the Underdark caverns
- Currently plagued by an ooze infestation
- Territory was taken from the Drow by Clan Diggermattock
- Contains multiple named areas including a reception hall, Trader's Grotto, and Diggermattock Hall

**Diggermattock Hall**
- A well-lit cavern within Blingdenstone
- Occupied by approximately two dozen Svirfneblin doing administrative work (carrying messages, huddled around stone tables with maps and papers)
- Features a raised dais at the back with stone desks where Dorbo and Senni sit
- Functions as the administrative/leadership center of Clan Diggermattock

**Trader's Grotto**
- A bustling area within Blingdenstone the party passed through en route to Diggermattock Hall

**Velkynvelve**
- Drow location from which the party previously escaped

**The Overbright**
- The party's desired destination; appears to refer to the surface world
- Reaching it requires assistance from the Blingdenstone gnomes

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Ooze infestation of Blingdenstone**: The party must resolve this before receiving help to reach the Overbright. The source and nature of the infestation is unexplained.
- **Why are oozes plaguing Blingdenstone?**: Glabbagool expressed confusion about why his "fat friends" were causing the gnomes pain, suggesting the ooze behavior may be unusual or driven by an external cause.
- **Jimjar's bet**: Five empty spell gems wagered that the party would have to deal with the oozes — bet accepted but not yet resolved.
- **Drow threat to Blingdenstone**: Clan Diggermattock is actively preparing defenses against their long-time Drow enemies and seeking intelligence; the Drow's current plans are unknown.
- **The Emerald Vanguard**: The party is associated with (or is) a group blamed for destroying a city — details of this event remain unclear.
- **The party's route to the Overbright**: Dependent on completing the ooze quest and receiving gnomish assistance.
