## Threads & Mysteries

- **Ooze infestation origin**: Oozes are normally solitary; the mass infestation is highly unusual. Jadgar suspects a demon has empowered an intermediate agent to direct the oozes. The identity of this agent and the demon involved (possibly Juiblex, as raised by Zalthir) is unresolved.
- **Juiblex connection**: Zalthir raised the possibility of Juiblex being involved; Jadgar did not confirm or deny but offered a related clarification. Whether Juiblex is the source of the infestation remains open.
- **Intermediate agent**: Jadgar advised the party to find whoever or whatever is motivating the oozes. This agent has not been identified.
- **Jadgar's deal**: The party has used one question (about the ooze problem). Further questions are available for each additional ghost they put to rest. The number of remaining ghosts in Blingdenstone is unknown.
- **Jadgar's young mentee**: Jadgar is secretly training a young gnome to lead the new Borough Wardens and recommended the party speak with this individual. The gnome has not been named or introduced.
- **Borough Wardens reconstitution**: Jadgar is secretly rebuilding this organisation. Its relationship to current Blingdenstone leadership and whether the leadership is aware remains unclear.
- **Gold Whisker clan**: Chief Dorbo suspects them of being wererats and refuses to cooperate. Senni is more open to reconciliation. Whether the clan are wererats and what role they might play in Blingdenstone's future is unresolved.
- **Chief Dorbo's assistance**: Dorbo agreed to help the party with their own concerns after the ooze threat is resolved. The nature of the party's concerns and what Dorbo can offer has not yet been addressed.
- **Source of the gelatinous cube attack in the Trader's Grotto**: Multiple non-sentient cubes emerged from the rocks simultaneously; whether this attack is connected to the broader ooze infestation and the suspected demonic agent is unresolved.
- **Buppido**: Named as Pelek's killer; current whereabouts and status unknown.
