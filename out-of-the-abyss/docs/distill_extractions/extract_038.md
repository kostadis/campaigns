## NPCs

- **Chief Dorbo Diggermattock**: Located in Diggermattock Hall, Blingdenstone. Leader of the mining coalition. Concerned about ooze infestations hindering mining operations and housing construction. Suspects the Gold Whisker clan of being wererats and refuses to cooperate with them. Agreed to help the party with their concerns only after they deal with the ooze threat.
- **Senni Diggermattock**: Located in Diggermattock Hall, Blingdenstone. Represents the non-mining population. Confronted Chief Dorbo about his failure to address the ooze problem. More open than Dorbo to reconciliation with the Gold Whisker clan.
- **Pelek**: Ghost who had been traveling with the party. Was killed by Buppido. His bones were carried by Grygum. Interred in the catacombs below the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough. Expressed profound relief and gratitude upon burial, feared he would be lost forever tormenting people for eternity. His spirit faded away and passed on after the burial.
- **Buppido**: Killed Pelek (referenced as past event).
- **Grygum**: Party member. Carried Pelek's bones. Performed a dragon-themed burial ritual, planting a gold coin as a symbolic foundation. Channeled divine energy through a holy symbol during combat, using necrotic force against gelatinous cubes.
- **Glyphic Shroomlight**: Priest at the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough in Blingdenstone. Initially hesitant to inter unknown bones but agreed when Pelek's ghost materialized. Asked that Glabbagool remain upstairs to avoid accidents.
- **Borough Warden Jadgar**: Ghost in the catacombs below the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough. Three-foot-tall spirit (svirfneblin). Once defended Blingdenstone against the Drow and other threats. Working to re-establish the Borough Wardens. Secretly training young gnomes for this purpose. Offered the party a deal: one question answered about the Underdark per ghost they help put to rest. Explained the difference between devils and demons. Advised the party that a demon likely granted power to an intermediate agent controlling the oozes. Mentoring a young gnome who would soon lead the new Borough Wardens.
- **Glabbagool**: Intelligent gelatinous cube companion of the party. Viewed with suspicion by Blingdenstone gnomes. Protested that non-sentient cubes give intelligent gelatinous cubes a bad name. Advised the party on how to fight gelatinous cubes (avoid engulfment, use acid protection, pull out trapped allies). Asked Zalthir if he was an ooze upon seeing his eldritch claw tattoo tentacles.
- **Zalthir**: Party member. Monk. Demonstrated a martial technique for Jadgar. Mentioned a possible connection to Juiblex regarding the oozes. Has an eldritch claw tattoo that grows tentacles from his hands. Delivered the killing blow on the last gelatinous cube. Told Glabbagool he was "an aspiring ooze."
- **Daz**: Party member. Unleashed massive fire explosions during combat, dissolving one cube entirely and damaging others while avoiding merchant stalls.
- **Thorin**: Party member. Used darts in combat against the cubes. Complained that he wanted to deliver the killing blow.

## Factions

- **Blingdenstone Mining Coalition**: Led by Chief Dorbo. Operations hindered by ooze infestations. Responsible for mining and housing construction.
- **Blingdenstone Non-Mining Population**: Represented by Senni. In tension with the mining coalition over the handling of the ooze problem and the Gold Whisker clan.
- **Gold Whisker Clan**: A group in Blingdenstone suspected by Chief Dorbo of being wererats. Dorbo refuses to cooperate with them. Senni is more open to reconciliation with them.
- **Borough Wardens**: Historical defenders of Blingdenstone, now defunct. Borough Warden Jadgar (ghost) is secretly working to re-establish them by training young gnomes. A young gnome mentee will soon lead the new organization.

## World Events

- The party arrived at Diggermattock Hall and witnessed a heated debate between Chief Dorbo and Senni about Blingdenstone's ooze infestation and the Gold Whisker clan.
- Chief Dorbo tasked the party with dealing with the ooze threat before he would assist them with their own concerns.
- The party traveled to the cemetery adjacent to the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough to inter Pelek's bones.
- Glyphic Shroomlight agreed to allow the burial after Pelek's ghost materialized before him.
- Grygum interred Pelek's bones in the catacombs with a dragon-themed ritual and a gold coin, and Pelek's spirit passed on peacefully.
- Borough Warden Jadgar's ghost appeared in the catacombs and offered the party a deal: answers about the Underdark in exchange for putting ghosts to rest.
- Zalthir demonstrated a monk technique for Jadgar in exchange for information about the ooze problem.
- Jadgar explained that demons empower proxies to cause widespread destruction (unlike devils who corrupt through deals), and that an intermediate agent likely controls the oozes, empowered by a demon.
- The party visited the Trader's Grotto, Blingdenstone's central market.
- Several non-sentient gelatinous cubes emerged from the rocks in the Trader's Grotto, causing panic among merchants.
- The party defeated the gelatinous cubes in combat; several gnomes were engulfed during the fight but the party intervened.

## Locations

- **Diggermattock Hall**: In Blingdenstone. Where Chief Dorbo and Senni hold discussions about community governance. Site of the debate about oozes and the Gold Whisker clan.
- **Blingdenstone**: Svirfneblin (deep gnome) settlement in the Underdark. Suffering from ooze infestations that hinder mining and housing construction. Contains Diggermattock Hall, the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough, and the Trader's Grotto.
- **Temple of the Ruby in the Rough**: In Blingdenstone. Dedicated to Segojan Earthcaller, the Svirfneblin god of deep earthen nature. Has an adjacent cemetery. Contains catacombs below where the dead are interred. Staffed by priest Glyphic Shroomlight.
- **Catacombs (below the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough)**: Burial site beneath the temple. Where Pelek's bones were interred. Where Borough Warden Jadgar's ghost resides.
- **Trader's Grotto**: Blingdenstone's bustling central market. A cavern where fungi once grew but were turned to stone centuries ago by a medusa's spell. Merchants operate makeshift stalls among the petrified fungi. Caravans deliver goods to warehouses carved beneath the grotto. Site of the gelatinous cube attack.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **What or who is the intermediate agent controlling the oozes?** Jadgar advised that a demon likely empowered a proxy to motivate the ooze infestation. The party needs to find this agent.
- **Possible connection to Juiblex**: Zalthir mentioned a possible connection between the ooze infestation and the demon lord Juiblex. Jadgar grew thoughtful at this suggestion.
- **The Gold Whisker Clan**: Suspected wererats by Chief Dorbo. Senni is open to reconciliation. Their true nature and role in Blingdenstone remain unresolved.
- **Jadgar's ghost deal**: The party can earn more answers about the Underdark by helping additional ghosts find rest. It is unclear how many ghosts remain in the catacombs or elsewhere.
- **Jadgar's young gnome mentee**: Jadgar recommended the party speak with a young gnome he is mentoring to lead the new Borough Wardens once they gather more information. This person has not yet been met.
- **The party's own concerns**: Chief Dorbo agreed to help the party with unspecified concerns after they resolve the ooze threat. The nature of these concerns is not detailed in this session.
- **Blingdenstone's broader recovery**: Tensions between mining and non-mining populations, ooze infestations, and the Gold Whisker situation all threaten the community's stability.
