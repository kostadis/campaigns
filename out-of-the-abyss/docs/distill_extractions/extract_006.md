## Locations

**The Underdark**
- The broader subterranean region the caravan is traveling through en route to Sloopbludop.

**Narrow Passageway (with Ormu moss)**
- A narrow passage lined with bioluminescent green Ormu moss; warm and damp.
- The group passed through it; Jimjar disguised/blocked the entrance behind them.

**The Silken Paths**
- A network of giant spider webs crisscrossing a chasm: 500 feet deep, 2,000 feet wide, stretching nearly 5 miles.
- Major strands are traversable on foot.
- Ever-changing: old webs disintegrate; giant spiders constantly spin new ones.
- Inhabited by giant spiders.
- Fire can burn the webs but anyone within 30 feet of a burning point risks falling to death; spiders rebuild faster than webs can be burned.
- A drow wizard once tried and failed to burn them down; the alternative without the webs requires climbing dangerous cliffs.
- Current state: The caravan is approximately one hour into traversing them.

**Sloopbludop**
- The caravan's destination; located beyond the Silken Paths.
- Accessible via passageways on the other side of the Silken Paths.

**The Darklake**
- A large body of water in the Underdark; Shuushar says it will provide abundant water.
- Not yet reached.

**Lost Tomb (unnamed)**
- A tomb that Fargas Rumblefoot's adventuring band was searching for.
- Fargas claims to know its location and the location of treasure within it.
- Location unknown to the party.
