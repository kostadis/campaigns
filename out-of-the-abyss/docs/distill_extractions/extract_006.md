## World Events

- The escapee caravan faced a water storage problem after abandoning their gear during their escape.
- Daz devised a solution: using shape water to freeze water into portable ice blocks, refreshed hourly.
- The caravan found Barrelstalk mushrooms, obtaining 1–4 gallons of water and 5–10 pounds of food.
- Eldeth covered the rear, obscuring the caravan's tracks; Daz, Grygum, Thorin, and Zalthir assisted.
- The caravan passed through a narrow passageway lined with Ormu bioluminescent fungus; Zalthir harvested some.
- Zalthir negotiated with Jimjar to block the passageway; Daz defused the confrontation by claiming Zalthir had a vow against gambling; Jimjar accepted this as true.
- Grygum made the bet on Jimjar's behalf and deliberately bet on the winning side without Jimjar realising which side he had taken.
- Jimjar successfully blocked the passageway in a way that did not appear to be caused by the caravan.
- Daz observed Topsy and Turvy conversing privately but could not hear what they said.
- Sarith roused the group on the 8th day; the march toward Sloobludop continued.
- Thorin found a pool of water; Daz converted it entirely into ice blocks.
- Grygum found Trillimac mushrooms; the group harvested enough for approximately 20 pounds of food.
- Shuushar reassured the group that the Darklake would provide ample water.
- The caravan arrived at the Silken Paths; Sarith confirmed they are heading in the right direction toward Sloobludop.
- Yukyuk and Spiderbait, goblin web-surfing guides, approached the caravan offering guidance for 2 gold per day.
- The caravan admitted to being destitute; Jimjar proposed a bet to get a free demonstration of web navigation.
- Jimjar bet Yukyuk and Spiderbait 20 gold they could not navigate the webs; the goblins accepted and won.
- Jimjar admitted he lacked the 20 gold to pay; Spiderbait proposed accompanying the group until the debt is repaid; Jimjar accepted.
- Zalthir assessed the goblins and detected no subterfuge.
- Spiderbait recounted a story of a Drow wizard who tried to burn the Silken Paths but failed because spiders rebuilt webs faster than he could burn them.
- Eldeth devised a rope safety system using silk rope to tie party members together to arrest falls; the goblins took mental notes on this innovation.
- After approximately one hour of walking on the Silken Paths, the group discovered a humanoid-sized, moving cocoon.
- Stool used spores to establish telepathic contact with the cocoon's occupant, identified as Fargas Rumblefoot.
- Fargas Rumblefoot identified himself, described being the survivor of a gnoll attack on his adventuring band, and explained he was cocooned by spiders as stored food.
- Thorin urged caution before freeing Fargas, raising the possibility of gnoll infection; Fargas denied being infected.
- Fargas revealed he knows the location of a lost tomb and its treasure, offering this as leverage for rescue.
- Fargas reported feeling spiders approaching; the group assessed the web strands around them and determined that cutting the right strands with fire or blades could stop the spiders, while cutting wrong strands could trap them.
