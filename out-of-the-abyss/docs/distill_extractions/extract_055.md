## Threads & Mysteries

- **Who paid for Daz's protection?** House T'sarran's matron mother is using Daz as bait specifically to identify an unknown party who arranged for his protection. This party has not been identified. House T'sarran will continue sending agents regardless of what happens to the captured spy.
- **What is the nature of Daz's protection arrangement?** Who made it, when, and why? What resources were involved?
- **Eldeth's oath and Thorin's clan membership**: Eldeth has sworn to advocate for Thorin's embrace by her clan after briefing the lords of Gauntlgrym and Mithril Hall. This is unresolved pending her mission's success.
- **The briefing at Mithril Hall and Gauntlgrym**: Eldeth must inform the dwarven lords about the Underdark dangers (demon lords, etc.). The outcome of this briefing is unresolved and may trigger further events.
- **Jimjar's celestial nature**: The party believes Jimjar was "apparently a celestial the whole time." Zalthir has assigned his Candlekeep question to this thread; Khell-Vire's interest connects to it. The full nature and purpose of Jimjar's involvement remains unexplained.
- **Thorin and Dawnbringer's relationship**: Philemon has been assigned to work through their "interpersonal conflict issues." The nature and depth of their conflict is unresolved; both are described as needing "couples counseling."
- **Glabbagool's question**: Glabbagool has been given one question to ask at Candlekeep. What he will ask is unknown and the subject of active curiosity among the party.
- **House T'sarran's continued surveillance**: The spy warned that killing her would not stop House T'sarran from sending more agents. Further agents are implied to be possible or likely.
- **The drow spy's fate**: She is currently miniaturised and in the bag of holding. Her longer-term fate — release, continued captivity, death, or some deal — is unresolved. She expressed confidence she could escape if handed to Candlekeep guards.
- **What does the spy's matron want beyond identifying Daz's protector?** The matron's two-hundred-year rule and active surface surveillance suggest resources and patience, but her deeper agenda is unknown.
- **Spiderbait's integration into Candlekeep**: He proposed seeking employment there doing something innocuous. Whether this succeeds is unresolved.
- **Grygum's specific divine assignment from Bahamut**: He arrived at Candlekeep explicitly wanting to understand Bahamut's personal assignment for him, beyond general doctrine. Vareth's interview may begin to address this.
- **"Stroud's Gambit" and the half-orc Bahamut conversion phenomenon**: Vareth's excited reception of the term and his theories suggest this is an active academic controversy with broader theological implications not yet explored in this chunk.
- **The books donated to Candlekeep**: Ilvara's prayer book with Zuggtmoy corruption marginalia, Asha Vandree's Lolth/Zuggtmoy theological argument, Pliinki's demonic birthing documentation, and Princess Ebonmire's spellbook — what the avowed scholars discover in them may generate new information or plot threads.
- **Princess Ebonmire's spellbook**: Origin and significance of this spellbook is not explained in this chunk; it is donated without elaboration.
- **Who is the unknown protector and what is their relationship to the broader threat landscape?** The House T'sarran situation implies a third faction operating in the background with resources sufficient to arrange Daz's protection and attract a matron mother's sustained attention.
