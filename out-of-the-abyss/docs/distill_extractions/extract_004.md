## Locations

**Velkynvelve**
- Drow outpost/prison in the Underdark
- Features: Guard tower, rope bridge leading to Ilvara's residence, waterfall, spider webs below (with very large spiders), cavern floor below
- Current state: Under demon attack; prisoners have escaped; drow guards dead; ongoing battle between drow and demons
- Directional notes: North leads to Menzoberranzan; south leads toward Blingdenstone (through the battle zone); a tunnel leads to the Darklake

**The Darklake**
- A vast network of underground rivers, natural tunnels, and canals connecting innumerable water-filled caverns and chambers
- Stretches over a hundred miles across; ceilings miles high in some places; unfathomable depths
- Passages extended and widened by duergar engineers; duergar-constructed locks allow watercraft to travel between levels
- Navigated by many Underdark creatures including kuo-toa
- Connects to Gracklstugh, Blingdenstone, and Sloobludop

**Sloobludop (Sloopdopblop as spoken)**
- A kuo-toa town accessible via the Darklake
- Approximately eight days' travel from the group's current position
- Where the group hopes to find a navigator and a boat
- Associated with Shuushar

**Gracklstugh**
- A destination city in the Underdark (duergar city implied)
- Buppido's stated destination; "the divine plan" says he will reach it
- Reachable via the Darklake

**Blingdenstone**
- A location in the Underdark, south of Velkynvelve
- Reachable via the Darklake; direct southern route from Velkynvelve goes through the demon/drow battle

**Menzoberranzan**
- Drow city, north of Velkynvelve

**Large Cavern (unnamed)**
