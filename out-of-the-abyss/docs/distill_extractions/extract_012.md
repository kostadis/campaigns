## Factions

**Cult of the Sea Mother (Kuo-toa faction)**
- Current goals: Eliminate the Deep Father faction
- Recent actions: Ploop brought the escapees as offerings to lure Blopp into a trap; Ploop's faction attacked the Deep Father faction during the sacrifice ceremony
- Key members: Ploopploopeen (Archpriest)
- Relationships: At genocidal war with the Cult of the Deep Father

**Cult of the Deep Father (Kuo-toa faction)**
- Current goals: Summon the Deep Father ("Leemooggoogoon") through blood sacrifice
- Recent actions: Bloppblippodd accepted sacrificial offerings; attempted to perform the ritual; Blopp was killed but the ritual completed, summoning Demogorgon
- Key members: Bloppblippodd (Archpriest, now dead); unnamed second-in-command who tried to block the escapees
- Relationships: At genocidal war with the Cult of the Sea Mother

**Followers of Bahamut**
- Current goals: Stand against evil, protect the innocent
- Key members: Grygum (cleric), Gorg'Bahamut/Grog'Bahamut (Grygum's mentor)
- Details: Bahamut represents justice, order, and compassion; antithesis of Demogorgon

**Duergar (Deep Dwarves)**
- Current goals: Acquisition, trade, hoarding wealth
- Key members: Hemath (arms dealer from Gracklstugh)
- Details: Masters of craft focused on utilitarian perfection; driven by greed, envy, and resentment; culturally cynical; subterranean empire organized by clan with rigid conformity; individuality is crushed; they scorn kindness and mercy

## World Events

- Ploop, Archpriest of the Sea Mother, presents the escapees as offerings to Bloppblippodd, Archpriest of the Deep Father, at the altar in Sloobludop
- Bloppblippodd accepts the offerings and orders the sacrifices brought to a blood-stained depression with a grate, 20 feet from the altar
- Bloppblippodd calls for the sacrificial knife
- Ploop betrays his daughter (not the escapees) and attacks her along with his allies, triggering a civil war among the kuo-toa
- A full-scale genocidal battle erupts between the Sea Mother and Deep Father factions; the escapees are ignored
- Grygum loots Ploop's thatched hut, taking gold and magic items
- The escapees regroup and begin moving toward boats at the eastern docks
- Kuo-toa fishing in the Darklake are attacked and killed by an unknown underwater enemy; multiple patches of blood appear across the lake
- Ploop strikes a final fatal blow, killing Bloppblippodd at the altar
- With her dying breath, Bloppblippodd calls out "Leemooggoogoon!" — completing the sacrificial ritual
- The Darklake begins bubbling and foaming; thick oily tentacles emerge, followed by a thirty-foot-tall two-headed creature identified by Grygum as Demogorgon, Prince of Demons
- Demogorgon fully emerges from the Darklake, throws back both heads, and roars
