## NPCs

- **Zalthir**: Monk. Used fire breath against the giant centipede creature (failed on second attempt). Remembered the story of monk Korrh Erann, who survived Drow pursuit by running rather than fighting. Scouted ahead using monk speed, found hiding spot behind stalagmites, fell from hiding place (alerting Drow scouts). Used Stool's mind link to communicate hiding spot to group.
- **Daz**: Injured and unconscious after rockfall; revived by Zalthir. Noted they were being followed. Recognized the sound of a drow scout. Expressed hope that their actions helping companions would earn reciprocal loyalty.
- **Thorin**: Dwarf, armored. Struck the centipede creature with a short sword. Ordered the twins (Topsy and Turvy) to save Eldeth and Jimjar. Carried Stool on his back during the flight.
- **Grygum (Gyrgum)**: Healer. Grappled by the centipede creature and knocked unconscious. Revived by Zalthir. Used Thaumaturgy to create a tremor that fooled the Drow scouts into thinking the cave might collapse. Spotted the tunnel entrance that Sarith was looking for.
- **Sarith (also referred to as Sathir)**: Drow, guide. Survived the rockfall (was not under the rocks). Killed the centipede creature with crossbow bolts (along with Buppido). Shouted "Rations!" after the creature died. Explained the Underdark tunnel system—twisting, constantly changing, enemies can appear from unexpected directions. Directed the group's travel. Ordered Eldeth to cover their trail the next day. Annoyed when Grygum found the tunnel before him but acknowledged the help. Warned that Ilvara has found and caught up with them.
- **Stool**: Myconid child. Moves very slowly. Rode on Thorin's back. Released rapport spores creating a mind link among the group. Enthusiastic and childlike personality.
- **Buppido**: Prisoner. Shouted that "the divine plan" foretold Sarith would rescue him. Killed the centipede creature with crossbow bolts alongside Sarith.
- **Nym**: Drow soldier/scout from Velkynvelve. Has a distinctive gait recognized by Zalthir. Led the pursuing Drow scouts. Drew weapon when Zalthir fell from hiding. Retreated after feeling the tremor (Grygum's Thaumaturgy). Muttered "I told you it was the other tunnel" — believed the prisoners went a different way.
- **Topsy and Turvy**: Twin prisoners. Fired crossbows at the centipede creature. Ordered by Thorin to save Eldeth and Jimjar.
- **Eldeth**: Dwarf prisoner. Was taught to cover tracks in the Underdark. Tasked by Sarith to hide the group's trail the following day.
- **Jimjar (Jim Jar)**: Prisoner. Complained about not being able to find food due to running. Offered a bet. Claimed Faerzress has never affected a living thing and its only magical power is to prevent teleportation spells. Stated Kuo-Toa think they aren't mad but are insane.
- **Shuushar (Suushar)**: Kuo-Toa. Stool was sent to help him. Felt uncomfortable and strange running through the Faerzress-filled cave; said it felt like poison rather than just light as it used to be.
- **Ilvara**: Drow. Has found and caught up with the escaped prisoners. Based at or associated with Velkynvelve.
- **Korrh Erann**: Historical figure. Monk of Zalthir's order. Was trapped in the Underdark and pursued by Drow. Part of a group; some wanted to kill the scouts, but Korrh insisted on running. He was the only survivor.

## Factions

- **Drow of Velkynvelve**: Pursuing the escaped prisoners. Scouts deployed ahead of main force. Ilvara has caught up with the group. Nym is one of their scouts. Pursuing through the Underdark tunnel system.
- **Zalthir's Monastic Order**: Historical records include the account of Korrh Erann's survival in the Underdark by fleeing rather than fighting Drow pursuers.

## World Events

- The group survived a rockfall; Daz was buried and knocked unconscious, Sarith was not under the rocks.
- A massive centipede-like creature attacked the group in the tunnels; it grappled and knocked out Grygum. The group fought it with crossbows and a short sword; Buppido and Sarith killed it with bolts. Sarith claimed it as rations.
- Zalthir and Daz detected Drow scouts following them; Zalthir identified the gait as Nym from Velkynvelve.
- The group fled through the tunnels; Zalthir scouted ahead and found a hiding spot behind stalagmites in a left-branching tunnel.
- Zalthir fell from his hiding place, alerting the Drow scouts. Grygum used Thaumaturgy to create a tremor, causing the Drow to retreat, believing the cave unstable.
- The Drow scouts left, believing the prisoners took a different tunnel.
- The group continued running; passed through a cave filled with Faerzress that affected Shuushar negatively.
- Jimjar claimed Faerzress only prevents teleportation spells and has never affected living things before — contradicting Shuushar's experience.
- Grygum spotted a tunnel that Sarith was searching for, allowing the group to continue.
- By end of day, the group has put distance between themselves and the Drow but has no water.

## Locations

- **Velkynvelve**: Drow outpost/prison from which the group escaped. Origin point of the Drow pursuit.
- **Underdark tunnel system**: Twisting, non-linear, constantly changing tunnels. An enemy far away can appear in front of you via shortcuts. Described as maze-like by Sarith.
- **Stalagmite hiding spot**: Space behind stalagmites in a left-branching tunnel. Used by the group to hide from Drow scouts. Located after a fork where Zalthir chose the left tunnel.
- **Faerzress cave**: A cave filled with Faerzress (glowing magical substance). Previously only known to prevent teleportation spells; now appears to have a poisonous or disturbing effect on at least Shuushar (Kuo-Toa). Passed through on the 6th day of the 2nd Tenday of Taraskh 1493.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Ilvara's pursuit**: Ilvara and the Drow of Velkynvelve have caught up. The group must maintain distance or be recaptured. The tunnels may give the Drow shortcuts.
- **Changed Faerzress**: Shuushar experienced Faerzress as poisonous/disturbing rather than merely luminous. Jimjar insists it has never affected living things. Why has Faerzress changed? Is this connected to broader Underdark disturbances?
- **Buppido's "divine plan"**: Buppido referenced a divine plan that foretold his rescue by Sarith. What is this divine plan? What deity or force does Buppido serve?
- **Kuo-Toa madness**: Jimjar claims Kuo-Toa are inherently insane but their madness seems reasonable to them. Is Shuushar's perception of the Faerzress real or a product of Kuo-Toa instability?
- **Water shortage**: The group has no water and must find some soon. Travel will slow the next day.
- **Covering tracks**: Eldeth tasked with hiding the group's trail. Whether this will be sufficient to evade Drow scouts is uncertain.
- **Group cohesion**: Daz hopes their actions helping companions will earn loyalty from the other prisoners. Outcome uncertain.
- **Sarith's reliability/attitude**: Sarith is knowledgeable but prickly; annoyed when others help. His loyalty and true motivations remain unclear.
