## Locations

**Neverlight Grove**
- A Myconid settlement in the Underdark, now heavily corrupted by Zuggtmoy's influence.
- Contains the Inner Circle (Phyllo's domain), the Circle of Explorers (Rusharoo/Basidia's domain), and the Garden of Welcome (Yestabrod's area).
- Current state: Under Zuggtmoy's control via Phyllo; loyalist Myconids have fled.

**Yestabrod's Garden of Welcome**
- A garden area on a crescent-shaped ledge in the Neverlight Grove.
- Described as a "pale reflection" of the horrors surrounding Yggmorgus.
- Features: 20-foot-high crescent ledge hugging cavern wall, carpet of moss and fungi, suppurating pools of fluid, lumps and pockmarks.

**Yggmorgus**
- A towering mushroom of immense size at the center of a large cavern in Neverlight Grove.
- The central stalk splits into several lesser stalks, each with caps large enough to be tower-tops.
- Slitted windows carved into the trunk emit eerie luminescence; atonal music echoes within.
- Surrounded by a carpet of fungi covering the cavern floor.
- Proximity causes madness and visions; stench of rot and decay.
- Associated with Zuggtmoy's presence (party felt similar sensation as in their earlier dream of Zuggtmoy).

**The Great Tower (Grygum's Vision)**
- Interior of a tower with spiraling stairs and balconies carved into walls.
- Lit by phosphorescent mold growing in whorls.
- Zuggtmoy floats at center in humanoid fungal form, attended by fungal bridesmaids.
- May be the interior of Yggmorgus or a connected structure.

**Inner Circle (Neverlight Grove)**
- Phyllo's domain within Neverlight Grove.
- Scene of the confrontation between Basidia and Phyllo.
- Contains sentient Zurkhwood trees that began moving during the confrontation.

**Circle of Explorers (Neverlight Grove)**
- Rusharoo/Basidia's allied area within Neverlight Grove.
- Where the loyalist Myconids gathered near exit holes before fleeing.
- Rusharoo had pre-identified escape routes here.

**Blingdenstone**
- Deep gnome (svirfneblin) settlement in the Underdark.
- The party's current destination.
- Jimjar claims it offers a way out of the Underdark to the surface.
- Jimjar is serving as guide.

**Menzoberranzan**
- Drow city; Ilvara's intended destination for the captured party.

**Velkynvelve**
- Drow outpost; previously destroyed. Its destruction increases Ilvara's desperation.

**Explosive Gas Cavern (unnamed)**
- A cavern with ledges on either side and a large basin below filled with explosive gases.
- Party encountered and killed an eight-legged octopus creature here.
- Encountered on 2nd day of the first tenday of Kythorn 1495.

**Collapsed Tunnel (unnamed)**
- A tunnel where a tremor caused collapses ahead and behind the party, trapping them.
- Jimjar found an alternative passageway leading into more caves.
- Occurred on 4th day of the first tenday of Kythorn 1495.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Zuggtmoy's Wedding to Araumycos**: Zuggtmoy plans to unite with Araumycos (a massive sentient fungal growth in the Underdark), which would give her untold power over the world. The wedding rehearsal has been observed; the actual wedding has not yet occurred. Can it be stopped?
- **Demon Lords in the Underdark**: Both Demogorgon and Zuggtmoy have been sighted. The denizens of the Underdark are being driven mad and cannot defend themselves. What is causing multiple demon lords to appear?
- **Reaching the Surface**: The party's primary goal is to escape the Underdark via Blingdenstone and seek help from the "overbrite" (surface world). Will they succeed?
- **Ilvara's Pursuit**: Ilvara continues hunting the party. She faces becoming a Drider if she fails. She will not stop until they are found or she is dead. When and where will she catch up?
- **Daz's Madness Vision**: Daz saw a city of drow without spiders, elves and drow living together, and drow with dogs as pets. Is this purely madness, or a prophetic/significant vision?
- **Araumycos**: A massive sentient fungal growth described as the natural habitat of the Myconids. What is its current state? Is it aware of or resisting Zuggtmoy's plans?
- **The Underdark Madness**: Widespread insanity is affecting denizens across the Underdark. What is the root cause, and is it connected to the demon lords' presence?
- **Neverlight Grove's Fate**: Phyllo remains in control with Zuggtmoy's corrupted forces. What will happen to the Grove and remaining Myconids?
- **Basidia and the Loyalist Myconids**: Where will Basidia, Rusharoo, Loobamub, and the loyalist Myconids go? Will they survive?
- **Stool and Rumpadump**: Now with the loyalist Myconid group. Will they be safe?
- **Jimjar's Alternative Passageway**: After the tunnel collapse, Jimjar found a new passage into unknown caves. Where does it lead?
- **Xinaya's Scrolls**: Zalthir took scrolls from Xinaya's corpse. Their contents and utility are unknown.
- **The Shared Dream of Zuggtmoy**: The party previously had a dream involving Zuggtmoy. The details and significance of this earlier dream remain relevant.
