## Threads & Mysteries

- **Jimjar's true position**: The deference shown to Jimjar by other gnomes and his unusually specific knowledge about the ooze crisis suggest he holds a significant but unstated role in Blingdenstone. This has not been confirmed or explained.
- **Jimjar's reaction to Juiblex**: Jimjar's demeanor changed noticeably and painfully when Juiblex was mentioned. The reason for this personal reaction is unresolved.
- **Jimjar's specific bets**: Jimjar bet that the party would need to arm an army with acid-resistant gear before the month ended, and made a side bet involving retrieving ingredients from a fungal wedding celebration. The basis for this foreknowledge is unexplained.
- **The ooze problem in Blingdenstone**: The city is experiencing a serious ooze problem. Its cause, extent, and relationship to Juiblex are unresolved.
- **The "oozapalooza"**: Glabagool has observed an unusual increase in agitated oozes in the region. Whether this is connected to Juiblex or another cause is unknown.
- **Glabagool's origin of sentience**: Glabagool gained the ability to think and speak after an "unknown event." The nature and cause of this event have not been revealed.
- **Permissions for Glabagool**: The guards specifically noted that Glabagool's presence requires discussion with Chief Dorbo Diggermattock and Quartermaster Senni. This conversation has not yet occurred.
- **Acid-resistant gear and the fungal wedding**: Jimjar's bet implies a future need to gather ingredients from a fungal wedding celebration. Neither the wedding nor the ingredient-gathering has occurred yet.
- **The history of Blingdenstone's changing hands**: The city has changed hands between gnomes and drow multiple times. The implications of this history for current tensions have not been fully explored.
- **The relationship between drow and Blingdenstone**: The guards' deep distrust of Daz raises questions about how drow will be received within the city and whether Daz's presence will cause further complications.
