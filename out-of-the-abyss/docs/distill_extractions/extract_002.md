- On the 3rd day of the 2nd tenday of Taraskh, 1492, prisoners were divided into four labor groups: water-carrying (Ront, Prince Derendil, Thorin under Beremil), winch/cargo (Zalthir, Buppido, Jimjar, Eldeth under Balok), chamber pot disposal (Daz, Sarith, Stool under Guldor), and cooking (Gyrgum, Suushar, Topsy, Turvy under Imbros).
- Balok deliberately dropped the basket during winch duty, frightening the prisoners, and laughed at their reaction.
- Zalthir discovered a piece of paper listing people who owe favors and pocketed it; Eldeth found and pocketed a comb from the basket.
- Gyrgum performed an elaborate cooking show as a distraction; Topsy and Turvy successfully stole food while the Drow were entertained.
- Gyrgum attempted to steal a knife; Guldor caught him and confiscated it with threats.
- Thorin, Ront, and Prince Derendil delivered water to Ilvara's quarters and observed her shrine to Lolth, her valuables, and her erratic behavior; Ilvara threatened Prince Derendil with a knife.
- Daz, Stool, and Sarith disposed of waste at the lake and noticed the lake was unusually clean despite the volume of waste deposited.
- That evening, Stool released spores connecting all prisoners in a group mind-link; the group shared intelligence and discussed escape.
- Buppido declared a "divine plan" requiring the group's cooperation; initially proposed only his own escape; accepted group escape after prompting from Thorin.
- On the 4th day of the 2nd tenday of Taraskh, six Drow soldiers attended Gyrgum's cooking performance; Topsy and Turvy stole several knives during the performance.
- Jorlan approached Thorin and proposed an escape deal: he would open the prison door and create a distraction, leaving the armory path unguarded.
- Thorin, communicating via spore-link, consulted the group; Prince Derendil assessed Jorlan's motives as self-interested rather than a trap.
- Thorin accepted Jorlan's deal; Jorlan said the escape attempt would happen after the next long rest, before morning chore assignments.
- The group debated escape destinations: Gracklstugh (Buppido), the myconid great circle (Stool), Blingdenstone via Darklake (Topsy/Suushar), Darklake and then Blingdenstone avoiding Menzoberranzan (Suushar).
- Thorin identified Ilvara's locked zurkhwood chest as the likely location of the prisoners' confiscated equipment.
- All prisoners experienced disturbing nightmares involving dark shadows, tentacles, suppurating wounds, spores, and maggots.
- Daz woke from nightmares and recalled Sethir's information: a grey ooze in the lake processes the waste, explaining the lake's unusual cleanliness.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **The escape plan**: Jorlan has agreed to open the prison door and create a distraction after the next long rest; the armory path will be unguarded. The prisoners have knives (stolen by Topsy and Turvy) but their main equipment is believed to be in Ilvara's locked chest. Escape route options include the waterfall/pool below and the spider webs. The plan has not been fully detailed by Jorlan.
- **Jorlan's true reliability**: Jorlan stated he does not care whether the escape succeeds, only that it is attempted to embarrass Shoor and Ilvara. The prisoners noted this but accepted the deal. His trustworthiness remains unverified.
- **Prisoners' confiscated equipment**: Thorin believes all confiscated gear is in Ilvara's zurkhwood chest. This has not been confirmed and the chest has not been accessed.
- **The piece of paper with favor-debts**: Zalthir pocketed a paper listing names of people who owe favors to its original owner. The identities, locations, nature of the favors, and the original owner are all unknown.
- **The grey ooze in the lake**: A grey ooze inhabits the lake and processes waste. Its size, nature, and potential danger to escaping prisoners (who may use the waterfall/pool route) is unestablished.
- **Buppido's "divine plan"**: Buppido believes he is a god or divinely appointed figure. He has referenced a divine plan but refused to reveal its details ("In due time"). His actual intentions beyond self-serving escape are unknown.
- **The nightmares**: All prisoners experienced identical disturbing nightmares involving dark shadows, tentacles, spores, maggots, and a sense of something vast and terrible in the Underdark depths. The source or meaning of these shared nightmares is unexplained.
- **Ilvara's manic behavior**: Thorin recognized Ilvara as behaving atypically for a Drow — erratic and manic rather than cold and calculating. The cause is unknown.
- **Escape destination**: The group has not agreed on where to go after escaping. Competing destinations are Gracklstugh (Buppido), the myconid great circle (Stool), and Blingdenstone via Darklake (Suushar/Topsy). No consensus reached.
- **Zalthir's winch sabotage plan**: Zalthir is investigating whether fire breath can cut the spider silk rope or whether ice can break the winch gears. A rope sample was taken for testing. This plan has not been executed.
- **Sethir's identity and role**: Sethir provided key intelligence (Jorlan's backstory, the grey ooze) but their precise status — prisoner, informant, or other — is not fully established in this chunk.
- **The Drow with scarring**: Prince Derendil described one of Ilvara's two male Drow escorts on arrival as having heavy scarring along one side of his face and neck. This individual has not been identified or encountered directly in this chunk.
