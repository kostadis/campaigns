## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Pudding King:** Major unresolved threat to Blingdenstone; party has agreed to deal with him but has not yet confronted him. Spoiled booze barrels acquired as anti-ooze weapons suggest an ooze-related threat.
- **EntemochBoon Quest:** Party tasked with finding EntemochBoon to allow clerics to safely summon Earth Elementals; reward is a Stone of Elemental Control. Not yet pursued.
- **Crazed Earth Elementals:** Earth elementals in the area have been driven mad. One just erupted from the ground mid-battle. The cause of their madness is unresolved and connects to the EntemochBoon quest.
- **Ghost Rescue Mission:** Burrow Warden Jadger asked for specific ghosts to be rescued. Udhask's bones recovered, but presumably more ghosts remain. Glabbagool, Eldeth, Jimjar, and Spiderbait are handling this separately — their fate is unknown.
- **Udhask's Last Words:** "They're coming! Everyone must hide!" — who or what was Udhask warning about? His death showed no signs of violence despite his terrified ghost state.
- **Dancing Myconid Wedding:** A deep gnome scout encountered dancing myconids attending a "wedding celebration." Myconids don't typically marry or celebrate — what is actually happening? Potentially connected to Zuggtmoy or other Underdark demonic influence.
- **Portly Red Dragon in Gracklstugh:** A peculiarly portly red dragon with a double chin and two bellies is being used as a matchlighter in Gracklstugh. Its nature and significance are unclear.
- **Wanted Posters / Ember Vanguard:** The party is wanted in Gracklstugh with somewhat inaccurate descriptions. The Ember Vanguard is a source of paranoia there. The party's connection to the Ember Vanguard and the political fallout remain unresolved.
- **Protective Crystals and Gaze Attacks:** The party collected crystals that protect against harmful gaze attacks. This foreshadows an encounter with a creature possessing such an attack (e.g., Medusa or basilisk).
- **Glabbagool as a Ghost:** Glabbagool's sincere question about whether he'd be the first gelatinous cube ghost remains unanswered — possibly foreshadowing his potential death.
- **Current Battle:** Party is mid-combat with a crazed earth elemental that erupted from the ground after the gargoyle fight; outcome unresolved.
