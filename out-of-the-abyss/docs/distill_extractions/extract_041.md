## World Events

- The party met with the Blingdenstone community in Diggermattock Hall and agreed to deal with the Pudding King and clear the rock blight caverns in exchange for settlement support.
- The party was offered a quest to find Entemoch's Boon, with a Stone of Elemental Control promised as reward.
- Glabbagool, Eldeth, Jimjar, and Spiderbait volunteered to tackle the ghost problem; Burrow Warden Jadger encouraged them and noted they could still fight as ghosts if killed.
- The party rested at the Foaming Mug Tavern and gathered intelligence from patrons.
- A merchant from Gracklstugh reported strange peace in the city, paranoia about the Ember Vanguard, a wanted poster matching the party, mass hangings and executions, and a portly red dragon used as a matchlighter.
- The party confirmed that their exit from Gracklstugh had not provoked all-out war or destruction of the city.
- A deep gnome scout reported encountering dancing myconids speaking of attending a wedding celebration.
- Glabbagool became intoxicated from consuming spilled alcohol and sang sea shanties.
- The party negotiated with Tappy Foamstrap to purchase barrels of spoiled booze for use as weapons against oozes, under condition of protecting the tavern's reputation.
- The party visited the cultivation cave; a Stone Heart Enclave member provided fungi supplies.
- Glabbagool cleared a path through the cave by consuming dirt and gravel, and the party reached the entrance to the rock blight caverns after several hours.
- The party entered the rock blight caverns and discovered a stone statue of a cowering gnome holding a broken crystal, apparently petrified by a gaze attack.
- Daz determined that looking through the crystals provides protection against harmful gaze attacks; the party collected several crystals.
- The ghost of Udhask appeared and attacked the party; Zalthir struck it, causing it to shout "They're coming! Everyone must hide!" and flee in fear.
- The party pursued the ghost into a burrow, where it disappeared; they found Udhask's skeletal remains showing no signs of violent death.
- The party discovered a hidden compartment under a stone bed containing six gems worth 600 gp each and a potion of invisibility.
- The party collected Udhask's bones to fulfill Jadger's quest.
- The party reached a shallow stream with crystal formations and a stone ledge; they sent Glabbagool to investigate two winged statues.
- The statues animated as gargoyles and attacked; Zalthir, Daz, and Thorin fought one gargoyle, with Thorin finishing it off.
- The second gargoyle attacked Grygum, wounding him significantly.
- A crazed earth elemental erupted from the ground mid-battle; combat was ongoing at end of session.
