## NPCs

**Janussi (Keeper of the Tome)** — Deceased; corpse laid out and examined in his tower chamber. Final state: heart torn out (to prevent magical questioning of the dead), ribcage smashed by an axe-like instrument with precise, close blows; killed before chest was opened (no blood spray). Broken nose with settled bruising (occurred while alive, before death). Chipped front tooth, jagged and recent (from falling face-first). Blackened left forefinger, center of lower lip, and tip of tongue (poison ingestion via licking fingers while turning pages). Blue ink on right sleeve cuff (working hand). Locket torn from his neck (two broken lead links, worn chain impression). Body fell forward, then was rolled onto its back by the killer to take the heart. Was a wizard cataloguing unfamiliar spells. Loved tobacco; medicated failing lungs with cherry tobacco smoke. Was writing in his last unguarded minute — jokes from *The Golden Ass*, then notes on obscure spells in *The Discoveries*. Held one of two keys to the Hightower Library. Described as "a nice man, a little stiff." Had an on-and-off relationship with Sylvira Savikas. Years-long rivalry with Daral Yashenti. Spent his last morning in one-on-one meetings with Great Readers before Deadwinter Day.

**Daz** — Investigator using a Stonespeaker crystal to speak with beasts. Led examination of the body and interrogated the tower cat. Recently educated by "Milo's murder mystery."

**Grygum** — Cleric of Bahamut with a healer's eye for forensics. Determined the bruising occurred pre-death. Catalogued the chamber, the missing locket, the safe contents, and the chip. Took notes. Interviewed Hollypocket.

**Zalthir** — Monk; auditor of others' competence. Worked the ink-and-parchment evidence; deduced tobacco smoke could mask poison smell. Suggested Glabbagool taste-test the black substance.

**Thorin** — Of giant background; investigator with a "hammer" approach. Theorized about ciphers and poison; spooked Tadric about animate statue-candles. Identified Daral as prime suspect.

**Glabbagool** — An ooze; given the black substance to taste/identify (from Underdark experience). Failed to name it even with help, but confirmed something dangerous about it. Did not directly appear.

**Tadric** — Hovered guarding the murder scene. Spooked by Thorin's claims about magical statue-candles.

**A'lai Aivenmore (the Great Reader)** — Found Janussi's body at dawn; knocked over the marble statue in shock.

**Fembris** — Janussi's assistant; eager to help. Identified Daral Yashenti as Janussi's rival. Revealed Janussi held one of two Hightower Library keys; the other is held by gate warden Kalan.

**Daral Yashenti** — Great Reader; Janussi's years-long rival; called the party "sellswords" / "glorified book peddler." Drinks heavily; had a hangover. Argued with Janussi twice in one day (eleventh bell, and again at half past ten at night, drunk, pounding on the door). Fled down the stairs after the night argument. Left the chamber carrying *The Golden Ass* ("hypnotic block" per the cat). Janussi was still alive when Daral left. Prime suspect.

**Sylvira Savikas (Curator of the Infernal Fortress)** — Tiefling; studies devils ("devil woman"). Had an on-and-off relationship with Janussi. Argued violently with him that morning, screaming he was a traitor, nearly coming to blows. Opened that morning's quarrel by firing a lance of force at the armchair (three force darts, two punched through chair back). Hollypocket saw her (or her likeness) descending the tower around 2–3 a.m. carrying a lantern — though as a tiefling she sees in the dark without light. The cat saw "the torn demon lady" who "didn't smell like the demon lady" — suggesting a shapeshifter impersonating her.

**Kalan Strongbranch (gate warden)** — Holds the second of two keys to the Hightower Library. Passed Hollypocket on the steps that morning with "a face like thunder," nearly knocking her tray. Argued with Janussi.

**Teles Ahvoste** — Met Janussi around the ninth bell; meeting became a shouting match. Shouted "hypocrite!" at Janussi before storming out.

**Hollypocket** — Elderly gnome housekeeper; lived in an apartment below the Keeper's tower. Tended to Janussi's needs. Provided testimony on the day's arguments and the midnight thud. Never sleeps before eleven. Heard a thud upstairs after the bell tower rang at midnight. Saw Sylvira's glowing red face by candlelight around 2–3 a.m. Confirmed Janussi always wore the locket.

**Fheminor / Fheminor ("the short-legged one")** — Evening guest who arrived first after supper (per the cat). Listed by Hollypocket implicitly among visitors.

**Queenie (the tower cat)** — Five-year-old sleet-grey housecat. Witness. Napped in Janussi's armchair; awakened by Sylvira's force-light blast that morning. Saw evening guests arrive (Fheminor first, Daral later). Saw Daral run downstairs with the "hypnotic block" (*The Golden Ass*). Saw a Sylvira-shaped figure descend at night that "didn't smell like the demon lady." Did not know what the locket was.

**Brother Tharusk** — Referenced; a tall monk who "always smelled of incense" at the monastery. Did not appear.

**Milo** — Referenced; associated with a murder mystery that educated Daz. Did not appear.

## Factions

Janussi (Keeper of the Tome, deceased), A'lai Aivenmore, Daral Yashenti, Teles Ahvoste, Sylvira Savikas (Curator of the Infernal Fortress), Kalan Strongbranch (gate warden). Two keys to the Hightower Library held separately (Janussi + Kalan). Deadwinter Day approaching; Janussi held pre-holiday meetings, half ending in shouting.

**The party / "sellswords"** — Daz, Grygum, Zalthir, Thorin (and Tadric, Glabbagool associated). Called sellswords by Daral; now investigating Janussi's murder.

**Garden Shadow** — Referenced as a source of a saying Zalthir half-recalled. Not detailed.

## World Events

- Janussi spent his last morning in one-on-one meetings with Great Readers ahead of Deadwinter Day; half ended in shouting matches.
- Morning: Kalan Strongbranch passed Hollypocket on the steps with a furious face after meeting Janussi.
- Around ninth bell: Teles Ahvoste argued with Janussi, shouted "hypocrite," and stormed out.
- That morning: Sylvira Savikas raged at Janussi, called him a traitor, fired a lance/three darts of force at his armchair (waking Queenie), nearly came to blows, stormed out.
- Around eleventh bell: Daral Yashenti (hungover) argued with Janussi, called him unfit and a "glorified book peddler."
- Evening, after supper (~8 p.m.): guests arrived — Fheminor first, then Daral.
- Half past ten at night: Daral returned drunk, pounded on the door, argued, and fled downstairs — leaving with *The Golden Ass*. Janussi was still alive at this point.
- Janussi, struck in the face earlier (broken nose), returned to his desk and wrote notes — jokes from *The Golden Ass*, then spell research in *The Discoveries*.
- Janussi read a poisoned page, ingesting the black substance; tobacco smoke masked the poison's smell.
- Midnight: bell tower rang; Hollypocket heard a thud (Janussi's death/fall).
- ~2–3 a.m.: A figure resembling Sylvira (but lacking her scent, carrying an unneeded lantern) descended the tower; Hollypocket glimpsed her glowing red face.
- The killer rolled the corpse onto its back, smashed open the ribcage, removed the heart, and tore the locket from Janussi's neck. One sapphire and one spell-parchment taken from the safe; a fortune left behind.
- Dawn: A'lai Aivenmore found the body and knocked over the marble statue in shock.

## Locations

Contains: desk with spellbook *The Discoveries* (newest blue ink, spell notes), *The Golden Ass* (annotated, now missing), an inkwell. A silver pedestal, ebony carved pipe, cherry tobacco. A wall safe (metal, arm-wide, door open, undamaged lock — no forced entry) holding four sealed sapphire pouches, a raven figurine, potion of supreme healing, potion of water breathing, five fortune-grade diamonds, platinum and gold — one sapphire and one parchment removed. Armchair with three circular force-dart impacts, scattered horsehair, two holes punched through the back. A toppled three-foot marble statue (hooded bearded old man holding a candle), knocked over by A'lai Aivenmore. A wastebasket containing a gift tag. A tea table.

**Hightower Library** — Secured by two keys (Janussi's, now missing; Kalan's). A book/material there was allegedly "deleted" or tampered with by someone who "snuck in" — possibly enabled by the stolen key.

**The Underdark** — Referenced; source of Glabbagool's poison/material experience and the party's tunnel travels.

**The monastery** — Referenced; Zalthir's background, where Brother Tharusk taught.
