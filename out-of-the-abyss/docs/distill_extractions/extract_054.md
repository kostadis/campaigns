## Threads & Mysteries

- **Demonic corruption of the Underdark**: Multiple demon lords (Demogorgon, Jubilex) are actively affecting the Underdark — rampaging chaotic sentience (Demogorgon) and ooze proliferation (Jubilex). The full scope and interconnection remains unclear.
- **Glabbagool's sentience**: His consciousness is a symptom of Demogorgon's chaotic influence. What this means for him long-term, and whether other creatures are similarly affected, is unresolved.
- **Jimjar's prophetic knowledge**: His uncannily specific foreknowledge (acid-resistant gear, fungal wedding) has no explained source. How does he know these things?
- **The fungal wedding celebration**: Jimjar wagered the party would need to attend a fungal wedding to acquire ingredients for acid-resistant gear. This quest has not yet begun.
- **Acid-resistant gear**: Thorin specifically wants magical armor for corrosion defense; the party needs acid-resistant equipment for dealing with Blingdenstone's ooze problem.
- **Princess Ebonmire's spellbook**: Daz has not fully explored or copied all spells from this book. Its full contents remain unknown.
- **Path to the surface**: The party's overarching goal is to escape the Underdark. Blingdenstone is described as holding "the promise of a path back to the surface world."
- **Meeting Blingdenstone leadership**: The party must consult Chief Dorbo Diggermattock and Quartermaster Sennai for formal authorization and presumably to learn about available quests/resources.
- **Blingdenstone's ooze crisis**: The city is besieged by proliferating oozes linked to Jubilex. The party's role in addressing this is not yet defined.
- **Thorin's moral trajectory**: His consideration of Zhentarim Tactics represents an unresolved character arc — how far has the Underdark changed him?
- **No ranged capabilities**: The entire party lacks ranged weapons/attacks. This is explicitly flagged as a vulnerability that will be exploited ("the universe is that kind of petty").
- **Daz and Zalthir's Darkness conflict**: Zalthir's Darkness spell and Daz's fire-based targeting continue to interfere with each other. The Ember Grapple/Cloud of Daggers combo partially addresses this but the fundamental tension remains.
