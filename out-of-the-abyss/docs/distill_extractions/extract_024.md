## World Events

- The Gray Ghosts, directed by the Demogorgon cult, previously stole a red dragon egg from the Keepers of the Flame and Clan Thrazgad acid-resistant ore.
- Pliinki conducted mutagenic experiments on the dragon egg over ~54 days, mutating it toward a two-headed wyrmling intended as a mount/gift for Demogorgon.
- Pliinki attempted multiple times to use Thrazgad ore to mend the Obelisk's fractures; all attempts failed. The ore sits discarded near her workbenches.
- Pliinki channeled energy into the Obelisk and it responded with increasing intensity (flashed, then the air flared).
- The Demogorgon cult indoctrinated almost all members of the Council of Savants using 'Y' carvings.
- The party (now the Ember Vanguard) killed Pliinki and her derro on the mesa in the Obelisk chamber.
- Daz secured the mutated dragon egg.
- Pelek (a ghost) possessed a dead derro's body.
- Zalthir recovered a statue of Demogorgon from the northeast alcove and Pliinki's journal and letters from the northwest desk.
- Thorin found a dragon-sized baby rattle made by Clan Thrazgad.
- Grygum declared the egg should be returned to Gartokkar, reasoning it is no longer a true dragon but a demonic manifestation.
- The party adopted the name "The Ember Vanguard."
- The party bluffed past a derro guard post by posing as a powerful Drow raiding force (Daz used dancing lights to simulate a meteor swarm).
- The derro guards (Diirdeklin, Diinarnnmyr) now believe a Drow incursion from Menzoberranzan has occurred and that the Drow may be working with the Duergar.
- A Trapper (manta ray-like creature) ambushed the party in a cavern, enveloping Grygum; the party killed it.

## Locations

**Mesa / Obelisk Chamber (Whorlstone Tunnels)**
- Upper levels covered in blood and broken derro bodies after the battle.
- Contains the Obelisk ("Stone of Diirinka") — a black metal monolith with fractures that leaks quasi-magical energy; believed hidden by Diirinka. Resists repair with Thrazgad ore.
- Northeast alcove: contained a statue of Demogorgon (recovered by Zalthir).
- Northwest corner: contained a desk with Pliinki's journal and correspondence.
- Tunnel entrance: where Eldeth and the Spectator were located.
- Workbenches nearby with discarded Thrazgad ore.

**Whorlstone Tunnels**
- Network of caves beneath/near Gracklstugh.
- Contains fungi and other tunnel organisms.
- Derro guard post with double doors south of the Obelisk chamber (manned by Diirdeklin and squad).
- A large 20-foot-wide cavern heading west from the double doors, where the Trapper ambush occurred.
- Southern tunnels lead toward the Empty-Scabbard Killers' territory (party avoided).

**Gracklstugh**
- Duergar city in the Underdark; currently destabilized by the Demogorgon cult's machinations.
- Factions in conflict: Keepers of the Flame, Council of Savants, Stone Guard, Empty-Scabbard Killers, Gray Ghosts, the Deepking's authority.
- Madness and corruption spreading throughout the city.
- The Deepking issues erratic, paranoid edicts weakening his authority.

**Temple of Bahamut (unspecified location)**
- Where Grygum learned of Bahamut's power and received scriptural teachings about dragon eggs and freedom of choice.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Obelisk**: What is its true nature and purpose? It resists repair with Thrazgad ore but responds to channeled energy with increasing intensity. How can it be fully activated? What would happen if it were?
- **The mutated dragon egg**: The egg has been mutagenically altered toward a two-headed wyrmling. What will actually hatch from it? Will Gartokkar detect the mutations? How will Themberchaud react?
- **Return the egg to Gartokkar**: Will the plan to trade the egg for safe passage out of Gracklstugh work? Will Gartokkar believe their story that "some other crazed group" (not the derro) stole it?
- **The cult's surviving members**: Narrak, Uskvil, and Aliinka are still alive and active. The cult's destabilization plan continues. Almost all Council of Savants members are indoctrinated.
- **The Deepking's hit list**: Still in the hands of the Empty-Scabbard Killers; being used to assassinate opponents and destabilize the city.
- **Errde Blackskull's investigation**: She is hunting for corruption; the party has a deal with the Stone Guard. If she catches them, they fear imprisonment and torture. What is the nature of their deal?
- **The Keepers' secret**: The Keepers are hiding the egg's theft from the Deepking. What happens when this is revealed?
- **Derro belief in Drow incursion**: Diirdeklin and Diinarnnmyr now believe a Drow force from Menzoberranzan attacked. This misinformation could trigger further conflict or panic.
- **The statue of Demogorgon**: Zalthir recovered it. What significance does it have? What will happen to it?
- **The dragon baby rattle (Clan Thrazgad)**: Why was a dragon-sized baby rattle made by Clan Thrazgad found in Pliinki's chamber? What is the relationship between Clan Thrazgad and the dragon/Keepers?
- **The 'Y' carvings**: Used to indoctrinate Council of Savants members. What are they exactly, and do they have magical properties?
- **Buppido's loss**: What happened to Buppido? His absence weighs on the party.
- **Pelek's ghost**: Pelek now inhabits a derro body. What are his goals? How long can he maintain possession?
- **Escape from Gracklstugh**: The party's primary goal. Route and method still uncertain.
- **The Spectator**: A beholder-kin creature near Eldeth at the tunnel entrance. Its allegiance and role are unclear.
- **Themberchaud's awareness**: Does the dragon know about the egg theft or the cult's plans? What will he do?
- **"Leemoogoogon, Demorgogon, two-headed baby dragons"**: Zalthir's musing connecting Demogorgon's two-headed nature to the two-headed wyrmling mutation.
