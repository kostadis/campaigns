## NPCs

- **Daz**: Drow. Took decisive control of the political situation in Blingdenstone out of exasperation with bureaucratic debates. Proposed and led the plan to deal with the Pudding King. Insisted on personal accountability for any casualties. Led the grand moot. Impressed Chief Chipgrin with his blunt negotiation style. Chipgrin remarked he'd been told drow were difficult to negotiate with, but Daz took it to another level.
- **Chief Dorbo (Diggermattock)**: Co-leader of Blingdenstone. Annoyed when the party intruded into his private kitchen. Displays an obsession with money that seems unhinged given the circumstances—possibly influenced by demonic corruption from Juiblex. Agreed to support Daz's plan. Suggested attacking from the Temple of the Steadfast Stone as a staging area. Offered a Stone of Controlling Earth Elementals as reward for cleansing the temple and dealing with Ogremoch's Bane.
- **Senni (Diggermattock)**: Co-leader of Blingdenstone. Agreed to support Daz's plan. Asked Daz to lead and call the grand moot to order.
- **Chief Chipgrin**: Leader of the Gold Whisker clan (wererat gnomes). Sits on a stone chair draped with a cave badger pelt, flanked by two giant rats and surrounded by guards, on a plateau of soft green moss. Initially assumed the party was sent by the Diggermattocks to remove his clan. Agreed to fight against the ooze but refused to be led by Chief Dorbo or sent to slaughter under his command. Accepted Daz's terms of personal accountability. Knew the Pudding King before his transformation, when the Pudding King worked in the tunnels. Shared that the Pudding King is driven by vengeance, pride, madness, and demonic influence. Agreed to attend the grand moot. Informed the party that Juiblex's influence was bleeding through the Pudding King and corrupting the entire gnome community.
- **The Pudding King**: A transformed gnome, formerly worked in Blingdenstone's tunnels. Driven by vengeance, pride, madness, and demonic influence (Juiblex). His presence serves as a conduit for Juiblex's corrupting influence over Blingdenstone's population. Currently commands an army of oozes centered at an ooze amphitheater.
- **Nomi Pathshutter**: Leader/representative in Blingdenstone. Attended the grand moot. Participated in what appeared to be a rehearsed presentation with Gurnik Tapfinger advocating for cleansing the temple.
- **Gurnik Tapfinger**: Cleric in Blingdenstone. Attended the grand moot. Explained the mechanics of cleansing the Temple of the Steadfast Stone: a red spell gem containing a sanctification spell must be placed into a menhir, which provokes Ogremoch's Bane to send servants to attack; the party must defend until three temple guardians awaken. Advocated strongly for cleansing the temple to gain Earth Elementals.
- **Kazook Pickshine**: Representative of the Miners Guild in Blingdenstone. Attended the grand moot. Concerned about acidic damage to gnome weapons and armor. Proposed two solutions: collecting ingredients from Neverlight Grove for acid-protection potions, or trading salt for high-quality weapons in Gracklstugh.
- **Barrow Warden Uth-Jadgar**: Ghost. Attended the grand moot. Praised Daz's plan as technically brilliant, comparing it to strategies the Barrow Wardens would have used in their prime. Revealed that the Temple of the Steadfast Stone is in the Rock Blight and must be cleansed before use. Offered help from other Barrow Warden ghosts if the party undertook a quest for them.
- **Glabbagool**: Gelatinous cube companion of the party. Enthusiastically cleaned Chief Dorbo's kitchen floor, earning the nickname "Roomba." Assigned to assist the Barrow Warden ghosts alongside Eldev and Jimjar.
- **Thorin**: Party member. Explored Chief Dorbo's kitchen out of curiosity. Rolled a die to decide between caution and directness; result favored the safer route.
- **Grygum**: Party member. Expressed genuine, non-judgmental interest in meeting wererats, which pleasantly surprised the Gold Whisker guards.
- **Eldev**: Party member/companion. Assigned to assist the Barrow Warden ghosts alongside Glabbagool and Jimjar.
- **Jimjar**: Party member/companion. Assigned to assist the Barrow Warden ghosts alongside Glabbagool and Eldev.
- **Gnome Chef**: Works in Chief Dorbo's private kitchen. Mistook the party for new hires and offered snacks. Accepted Glabbagool's offer to clean the kitchen floor.

## Factions

- **Blingdenstone Leadership (Diggermattocks)**: Led by Chief Dorbo and Senni. Currently coordinating defense against the ooze threat. Agreed to support Daz's plan. Attempted to leverage the party to complete multiple side tasks beneficial to the community. Relationship with Gold Whisker clan is tense—Chipgrin assumed they sent the party to remove his clan.
- **Gold Whisker Clan**: Wererat gnome clan residing in the Gold Whisker Warrens behind massive double doors. Led by Chief Chipgrin. Guards have rat-like features (stringy mustaches, pronounced noses). Keep giant rats. Typically face hostility from other gnomes. Agreed to fight the ooze but refused to serve under Chief Dorbo's command. Accepted Daz's personal accountability framework. Philosophical about the ecological niche of rats and wererats.
- **Miners Guild**: Represented by Kazook Pickshine. Concerned about practical logistics of fighting oozes, specifically acid damage to equipment.
- **Barrow Wardens**: Organization of ghosts, led by Barrow Warden Uth-Jadgar. Formerly a military/guardian order in Blingdenstone. Offered ghost allies in exchange for the party completing a quest for them. Glabbagool, Eldev, and Jimjar assigned to assist them.
- **Forces of Juiblex**: Demon lord of oozes. Influencing the Pudding King, who serves as a conduit. Corruption bleeding through to affect all of Blingdenstone's population—amplifying greed, aggression, and worst attributes in gnome leaders and citizens. Controls/commands oozes centered at an ooze amphitheater.

## World Events

- Daz previously took decisive control of a political meeting, cutting through bureaucratic debate and insisting on a direct plan against the Pudding King. Chief Dorbo and Senni agreed to support the plan.
- The party intruded into Chief Dorbo's private kitchen; Glabbagool cleaned the floor.
- The party visited the Gold Whisker Warrens and successfully negotiated an alliance with Chief Chipgrin and the wererat clan against the ooze threat.
- Chief Chipgrin revealed that Juiblex's demonic influence, centered on the Pudding King, is corrupting the entire gnome population of Blingdenstone, amplifying negative traits like greed and aggression.
- A grand moot was convened with all major Blingdenstone leaders: Chief Dorbo, Senni, Nomi Pathshutter, Gurnik Tapfinger, Kazook Pickshine, and Barrow Warden Uth-Jadgar.
- Daz presented the battle plan at the grand moot: gnome army creates a full-scale distraction attacking the ooze amphitheater while the party uses magic to covertly snatch the Pudding King.
- Gnome leaders proposed multiple side quests to strengthen the assault: cleansing the Temple of the Steadfast Stone, obtaining acid-resistance ingredients from Neverlight Grove, trading for weapons in Gracklstugh, and assisting the Barrow Warden ghosts.
- The party decided to pursue cleansing the Rock Blight/Temple of the Steadfast Stone and traveling to Neverlight Grove for acid-resistance ingredients.
- The party split forces: Glabbagool, Eldev, and Jimjar sent to assist the Barrow Warden ghosts; the main party handles the temple and Neverlight Grove tasks.
- The party explicitly declined to send anyone to Gracklstugh due to the uncertain state of the city and their problematic departure from it.

## Locations

- **Blingdenstone**: Deep gnome city in the Underdark. Currently plagued by an ooze/slime threat originating from the Pudding King. Population is being subtly corrupted by Juiblex's influence. Contains multiple faction territories and districts.
- **Chief Dorbo's Kitchen/Private Quarters**: Located in the bureaucratic hub of Blingdenstone. Contains a gnome chef. Dorbo was annoyed at the intrusion.
- **Foaming Bug Inn**: An inn in Blingdenstone where Dorbo directed the party to eat.
- **Gold Whisker Warrens**: Home of the Gold Whisker wererat gnome clan. Accessed through massive double doors. Contains a plateau covered in soft, well-manicured green moss where Chief Chipgrin holds court.
- **Ooze Amphitheater**: Location where the Pudding King's ooze forces are concentrated. Target of the planned gnome army distraction attack.
- **Temple of the Steadfast Stone**: Located in the Rock Blight area of Blingdenstone. Needs to be cleansed before it can be used as a staging area for the attack. Contains a menhir where a red spell gem must be placed. Cleansing activates three temple guardians but provokes attack from Ogremoch's Bane's servants. Once cleansed, allows Earth Elementals to be summoned and protected from being driven insane by Ogremoch's Bane.
- **The Rock Blight**: An area of Blingdenstone containing the Temple of the Steadfast Stone. Currently corrupted/dangerous, requiring cleansing.
- **Neverlight Grove**: A location in the Underdark. Contains ingredients needed for acid-protection potions. The party plans to travel there.
- **Gracklstugh**: A city in the Underdark. The party had a problematic departure from it previously. Its current state is uncertain—the party suggested it might no longer exist due to their previous actions there. The party decided against sending anyone there.
