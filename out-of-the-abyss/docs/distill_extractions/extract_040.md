## World Events

- Daz took decisive control of political discussions in the previous session, cutting through bureaucratic debate and forcing agreement on a direct plan to deal with the Pudding King using the Gold Whisker clan as a large-scale distraction.
- Thorin wandered into Chief Dorbo's private kitchen; the party was given snacks before Dorbo appeared and redirected them to the Foaming Bug inn.
- Glabbagool offered to clean Chief Dorbo's kitchen floor and was accepted by the gnome chef, earning the nickname "Roomba."
- The party approached the Gold Whisker Warrens and knocked, announcing their purpose; a guard fetched Chief Chipgrin.
- Grygum expressed genuine non-judgmental interest in wererats; guards responded positively and engaged in philosophical debate about rats' ecological niche.
- The party was escorted through the warrens and met Chief Chipgrin on a moss-covered plateau; he was seated on a stone chair draped with a cave badger pelt, flanked by two giant rats.
- Chipgrin initially assumed the party was sent to remove his clan; Daz clarified their actual purpose.
- Daz negotiated terms with Chipgrin: the Gold Whisker clan would help fight the ooze, would not be subordinated to Dorbo, and Daz personally accepted accountability for any casualties.
- Chipgrin agreed to join the fight and attend the grand moot.
- Chipgrin revealed that the Pudding King was a gnome he had known, whose transformation was driven by vengeance, pride, madness, and demonic amplification.
- Chipgrin prompted the party to reflect on unusually pronounced greed and aggression among the Blingdenstone population; the party concluded this was Juiblex's influence bleeding from the Pudding King into the community.
- A grand moot was convened with Chief Dorbo, Senni, Nomi Pathshutter, Gurnik Tapfinger, Kazook Pickshine, and ghost Barrow Warden Uth-Jadgar attending.
- Senni asked Daz to lead and call the grand moot to order.
- Daz presented the plan: gnome army creates a distraction at the ooze amphitheater while the party uses magic to covertly capture the Pudding King; he also disclosed the Juiblex demonic influence affecting the leadership.
- Uth-Jadgar praised the plan as technically brilliant, comparing it to historical Barrow Warden strategies.
- Chief Dorbo proposed using the Temple of the Steadfast Stone as a staging area to keep oozes away from population centers.
- Uth-Jadgar revealed the temple is in the Rock Blight and must be cleansed before use.
- Gurnik explained the cleansing ritual: a red spell gem containing a sanctification spell must be placed in a menhir at the temple, which will provoke Ogremoch's Bane to send servants; the party must defend the gem until three temple guardians are awakened.
- Gurnik and Nomi presented a rehearsed proposal that cleansing the temple would allow safe summoning and use of Earth Elementals.
- Daz pointed out that attacking from the north would avoid all these complications, exposing that the gnome leaders were trying to leverage the party for community benefit tasks.
- The leaders nominally agreed with Daz's simpler suggestion but Gurnik continued advocating for the temple cleansing due to the value of Earth Elementals.
- Kazook Pickshine raised the problem of acid damage to gnome weapons and armor and proposed two solutions: Neverlight Grove ingredients for an acid-protection potion, or trading salt for better weapons in Gracklstugh.
- The party noted Gracklstugh may no longer exist due to their prior actions there.
- Uth-Jadgar offered the help of other Barrow Warden ghosts contingent on the party completing a quest for them.
- Chief Dorbo offered a Stone of Controlling Earth Elementals as a reward for dealing with Ogremoch's Bane and cleansing the temple.
- Thorin rolled a die to decide between a cautious and direct approach; the result favored the safer route.
- The party decided to cleanse the Rock Blight (hoping to befriend rather than control the awakened Earth Elemental) and to travel to Neverlight Grove for acid-resistance potion ingredients.
- The party decided against sending anyone to Gracklstugh.
- The party divided their forces: Glabbagool, Eldev, and Jimjar assigned to assist the Barrow Warden ghosts with their quest; the main party to handle the Rock Blight and Neverlight Grove tasks.
