## Locations

**Silken Paths**
- A network of giant spider webs spanning caverns in the Underdark, used as a traversal route
- Hundreds of possible routes through it
- Contains territorial Giant Spiders that spin new web strands tactically
- The group has now exited the Silken Paths
- A mimic disguised as a chest sits at the cavern wall edge where the Silken Paths end
- The Spectator encounter occurred here on a strand far removed from other strands

**Velkynvelve**
- Starting point of the escapees' journey; where they were imprisoned by Drow
- Shown on Sethir's map

**Sloobludop**
- Destination the group is heading toward; three days away from current position
- Shown on Sethir's map as a stop on the route to Gracklstugh

**The Darklake**
- A large body of water in the Underdark
- Does not have a conventional surface; described as "a large water balloon in the Underdark" where intersecting caverns, caves, and tunnels allow navigation
- The Tomb of Brysis of Khaem is located on its eastern edge

**Gracklstugh**
- A large Derro city in the Underdark
- Destination after Sloobludop; recommended by both Buppido and Sethir for acquiring weapons and gear
- The Tomb of Brysis of Khaem is on the route between Sloobludop and Gracklstugh

**Tomb of Brysis of Khaem**
- Floating tomb of the half-elf sorcerer Brysis of Khaem, created during the height of Faerûn's great empires of magic
- After the fall of the empire of Netheril and its flying cities, the tomb plummeted into a crevasse and ended up in the Underdark, where it has remained for centuries
- Located on the eastern edge of the Darklake
- Contains a false burial chamber with a sarcophagus (decoy for treasure hunters)
- The real treasure is hidden in another chamber accessible via a secret passageway from the servants' tomb
- Exact location unknown to Fargas, but Sethir claims to know where it must be

**Cormyr**
- A region of Faerûn mentioned in the context of the game "cut-the-graph"; known for more sophisticated militaries; has a variation of the game allowing magical bridge-building units
