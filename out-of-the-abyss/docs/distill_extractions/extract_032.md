## Locations

**Brysis's Tomb (Real Tomb)**
- Underground tomb with a sarcophagus containing a mummified corpse holding Dawnbringer. Also contained an invisible chest with treasure. Connected to a passageway leading to a room full of sarcophagi.
- Current state: Looted; wraith destroyed.

**Brysis's False Tomb**
- Separate room with an easily opened sarcophagus. Had a magical trap tied to Brysis that dissipated upon her death. The place Fargas Rumblefoot warned about.
- Current state: Trap neutralized.

**Neverlight Grove**
- A vast Underdark cavern; a myconid community. Accessed via a natural tunnel formed by a drying underground stream, dimly lit by lichen. The tunnel opens above the grove's main floor, offering a panoramic view. Contains a mushroom forest covering every surface (including ceiling) with brilliantly colored bioluminescent fungi. The cavern narrows into a ravine beyond the forest, with a majestic mushroom visible in the distance beyond the ravine. A central basin contains a large clear pool with a central dry mound. A small cliff rises above the cavern floor on the far side.
- Current state: Under the divided rule of Phylo and Basidia; under growing Zugtmoy influence; the party is present.

**Northern Terraces (Neverlight Grove)**
- Terraces lining the northern walls, suffused with faerzress. Water trickles from walls into a crude terraced irrigation system. Thick sweet smell from thousands of colorful, glowing fungi.
- Circle of Builders operates here; Gasbide is present.

**Garden of Welcome (Eastern Plateau, Neverlight Grove)**
- Located on the eastern plateau of Neverlight Grove. The Circle of Masters is preparing something here described as a "wondrous and glorious surprise." A previous group of visitors was taken here and never seen again. Phylo forbids casual visits but promised to show the party the next day.
- Current state: Dangerous; under Circle of Masters' control; unexplored by the party.

**Beyond the Garden of Welcome**
- Where the "Great Seeder" (Zugtmoy) allegedly resides, according to Phylo.

**Yggmorgus**
- Referenced as a great fungal tower; Gasbide dreams of building something even greater. Exact location not specified.

**The Underdark**
- The party has been traveling through it extensively. Three days of uneventful travel between Brysis's tomb and Neverlight Grove.

**Menzoberranzan**
- Referenced as Daz's place of origin. Tales of "two in a box" co-rulers (a metaphor for a tomb, as co-rulers typically end up dead). K'tharr-li is a childhood strategy game from there.

**Whorlstone Caves**
- Referenced by Daz; he saw reanimated creatures there previously.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **What is happening in the Garden of Welcome?** The Circle of Masters is preparing something there; a previous group of visitors disappeared after going; Basidia has asked the party to investigate. Phylo promised to take the party there the next day.
- **What is the "great celebration" / "day of joy"?** Phylo promises something marvelous is imminent; Gasbide links it to Araumycos. Likely connected to Zugtmoy's influence.
- **Who/what is the "Great Seeder"?** Phylo describes a female entity beyond the Garden of Welcome. Daz suspects it is Zugtmoy. Myconids normally have no gender, so Phylo's consistent use of female pronouns is anomalous.
- **What is Araumycos?** Gasbide says it is "part of a great celebration to come" promised by Phylo; he hopes to use it to build a tower to the surface. Its true nature is unclear to the myconids.
- **Can Phylo be saved?** Basidia wants Phylo restored; says Phylo would "enter the great circle and all would be well" if the demon is destroyed. The party worries destroying the demon might kill Phylo.
- **What is the nature of Sarith's infection?** Basidia cannot recognize his spores (unprecedented for a sovereign). It is unclear if his infection is the same as Phylo's or different. Daz identifies it as Zugtmoy's influence.
- **What happened to the previous group of "softers"?** They accepted Phylo's offer to visit the Garden of Welcome and were never seen again; Phylo made no further mention of them.
- **What is the Circle of Masters doing with the carcasses?** They are taking most carcasses from hunters and reanimating them; they are also sending myconid groups outside the grove secretly.
- **What is Jimjar's bet with Grygum?** Jimjar reminded Grygum of a bet as they entered Neverlight Grove; details unspecified.
- **The reanimated Grick**: Basidia is animating the Grick corpse (minus its head) as an ally for the party; outcome pending.
- **Scroll of protection against fiends**: Given by Basidia; suggests fiends are expected to be encountered (likely in the Garden of Welcome).
- **Who were the Drow pursuers and why did they stop?** Eldeth confirmed they lost the trail; Grygum speculated they "didn't like the tomb."
- **Phylo's restructuring of myconid society**: Circles now meld exclusively within themselves; only circle leaders meld with other leaders and sovereigns; the Inner Circle and Circle of Masters hold special status. Basidia considers this contrary to myconid harmony and unity.
