## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Garden of Welcome**: What happened to the prior group of softers who accepted Phylo's invitation? What is the Circle of Masters preparing there? Phylo has promised to show the party tomorrow.
- **Zuggtmoy's influence**: Daz recognises Phylo's gendered references to the "Great Seeder" as indicators of Zuggtmoy's involvement. The full extent of Zuggtmoy's presence in Neverlight Grove is unknown. Whether the spores infecting Phylo and those infecting Sarith are the same or different is unconfirmed.
- **Araumycos**: Gasbide references Araumycos as the means by which he hopes to build his tower to the surface, describing it as connected to a "great celebration" promised by Phylo. Its nature and role in the coming events are unexplained.
- **Yggmorgus**: Gasbide dreams of building a fungal tower greater than Yggmorgus. The nature and location of Yggmorgus are not explained.
- **The Great Seeder**: Phylo directs questions to this entity, described as living "beyond the Garden" and as female. Suspected to be Zuggtmoy, but not confirmed in-text.
- **Phylo's recovery**: Basidia believes destroying the demon could free Phylo, but Zalthir notes this may cause Phylo to explode; Basidia says Phylo would "enter the great circle" and be well. The actual outcome is unresolved.
- **Basidia's promised additional treasure and potions**: Contingent on the party bringing back information about what is happening in the grove and restoring Phylo.
- **Sarith's spores**: Basidia cannot identify them as belonging to any known sovereign, suggesting an entirely novel or demonic source. The implications for Sarith's condition and future behavior are open.
- **Myconid groups leaving the grove**: The Circle of Masters is sending groups of myconids outside the grove covertly. Their destination and purpose are unknown.
- **The Grick's head**: The party harvested it; its intended use is not yet stated.
- **Jimjar's bet**: Jimjar reminded Grygum of a bet as they entered Neverlight Grove. The nature of the bet is not stated.
- **Grygum's unnamed worry**: Grygum persisted in asking for Stool and Rump-a-dump to accompany the group; Daz noted Grygum seemed worried, but the reason is not stated.
- **The Drow pursuers**: Lost in or near the tomb area; their identity, affiliation, and whether they will resume pursuit are unresolved.
