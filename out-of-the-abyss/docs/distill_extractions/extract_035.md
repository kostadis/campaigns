## World Events

- The party emerged from an unexpected detour through the Underdark and located a passage they believed could serve as a shortcut to Blingdenstone.
- Thorin and Jimjar made a wager over whether tears—literal or metaphorical—would mark the journey before reaching safety.
- Daz confirmed that an earthquake had cracked an underground reservoir, actively flooding the cave complex with rising water.
- The party encountered and passed through a square-shaped chamber where a gray ooze emerged from the rising waters; Zalthir dispatched it quickly.
- At an intersection, gray oozes lurked in pools; Thorin attempted ranged attacks with darts while Glabbagool inadvertently blocked the passage; Daz destroyed one ooze with a spell; Glabbagool moved to the rear.
- The party discovered Glabbagool in a chamber containing the hovering remains of a drow, a dark metal mace, and scattered coins; they learned he had recently gained sentience.
- Thorin invited Glabbagool to join the party and gifted him a lucky foot; Glabbagool accepted.
- Thorin fell into a hidden pit trap; a black pudding emerged from the pit; the party retreated to a larger area and defeated the black pudding at range.
- The party reached a large chamber containing a stone fountain, a broken statue on a pedestal, and seven niches with formless sculptures; water seeped through a crack in one niche.
- Glabbagool touched the formless sculptures one by one; each materialized into a gray ooze that Zalthir immediately dispatched.
- Glabbagool investigated the fountain and discovered a hidden cache: 112 silver pieces, 3 gold pieces, a drow dagger, and a vial of oil of slipperiness; he consumed them and will need several days to digest them.
- The party proceeded down an eastern passageway and found water entering through ceiling cracks caused by the earthquake; Eldeth identified this as an underground river breaking through.
- The party debated options and decided to use a spell to create air pockets, advancing five feet at a time through the flooded passages.
- Thorin broke through a wall; the ceiling partially collapsed, doubling the water flow, but the breach also revealed a diverted underground river passage leading to safety.
- As water rose to the ceiling, the party swam approximately thirty feet upward through the new passage and emerged into a large cavern.
- Jimjar declared the bet a draw owing to Glabbagool's addition to the group; Thorin agreed.
