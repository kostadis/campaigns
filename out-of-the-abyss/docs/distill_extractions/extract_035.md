## World Events

- An earthquake cracked an underground reservoir, causing water to pour into the cave system the party was traveling through.
- The party encountered and befriended Glabbagool, a sentient gelatinous cube, in a chamber containing the hovering remains of a drow along with a dark metal mace and scattered coins.
- The party fought and killed multiple gray oozes and a black pudding throughout the flooded complex.
- Formless sculptures in a fountain chamber materialized into gray oozes when touched.
- A hidden treasure cache was discovered inside a stone fountain: 112 silver pieces, 3 gold pieces, a drow dagger, and a vial of oil of slipperiness.
- Thorin broke through a wall, collapsing more of the ceiling and doubling the water flow, but revealing a diverted underground river passage.
- The party swam approximately 30 feet upward through the revealed passage and escaped the flooding complex into a large cavern.
- Jimjar and Thorin agreed to call their wager a draw.

Contains: a square-shaped chamber; an intersection with pools; a chamber with a stone fountain, broken statue on a pedestal, and seven wall niches (some empty, some holding formless sculptures that transform into gray oozes); an eastern passageway with water entering through ceiling cracks; a hidden pit trap containing a black pudding. Current state: fully flooded and impassable.

**Large Cavern (Exit Point)**
