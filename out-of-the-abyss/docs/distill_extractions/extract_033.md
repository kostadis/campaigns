## World Events

- The party arrived at Neverlight Grove and received a guided tour from Basidia.
- Basidia explained that Phylo has restructured Myconid society, replacing the single communal meld with a two-tiered hierarchy: a Circle of Leaders and a Circle of Masters.
- The party met Hepbobe of the Circle of Growers, who defended Phylo's reorganisation as pragmatically neutral.
- Zalthir confirmed with Basidia that Hepbobe's food supply is not infected with the harmful spores.
- The party encountered Yrberop, a corrupted Myconid circle leader, dancing and singing Zuggtmoy's melody over fields of unhatched Myconid sprouts while speaking ecstatically of the Great Seeder's wedding.
- Thorin identified Yrberop's song as the same melody heard during his dream encounter with Zuggtmoy.
- The party met Rusharoo of the Circle of Explorers, who confirmed the Grove is deteriorating; Rusharoo revealed knowledge of escape routes, nutrient caches, and a secret passage into the Circle of Welcome.
- The party discovered the Garden of Welcome: a plateau garden where the heads of dozens of Underdark creatures protrude from the ground, infected with mold and fungi.
- Xinaya, a Drow sent by Ilvara to capture the party, was found partially corrupted in the Garden of Welcome; she begged to be killed.
- Xinaya revealed that Ilvara predicted the party would come to the Myconids, and that her entire squad was corrupted after accepting Phylo's invitation to the Circle of Welcome.
- Xinaya warned Daz to inform other Drow that the Demon Queen of Fungi (Zuggtmoy) and Demogorgon are present in the Prime Material Plane and the Underdark.
- A fungal abomination (possibly a former Myconid) rose from the Garden and initiated combat, accompanied by two Drow spore servants and two Myconids.
- During combat, Jimjar was covered in spores and temporarily stopped moving.
- During combat, exploding corpses in the garden healed the fungal abomination; one corpse detonated in acid, drenching the party.
- During combat, Sarith's head exploded mid-fight and transformed him into a Drow spore servant; Jimjar subsequently killed the Sarith-spore-servant.
- Zalthir killed the fungal abomination, ending the combat.
- Zalthir killed the last remaining Myconid creatures.
- Thorin, after consulting Dawnbringer, killed Xinaya as a mercy.
- The party collected treasure from the Garden of Welcome.
- The party advanced toward the giant mushroom cavern and encountered a parade of corrupted humanoid creatures singing Zuggtmoy's wedding hymn, confirming the wedding of Zuggtmoy and Araumycos is actively underway.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Zuggtmoy's Wedding**: The wedding of Zuggtmoy (the Great Seeder) to Araumycos (the Great Body) is actively underway in the cavern beyond the Garden of Welcome. The party is about to enter. It is unknown what completing this wedding would mean cosmically or for the Underdark.
- **Araumycos**: The nature of Araumycos ("the Great Body") is unexplained. It is unclear whether it is a sentient entity, a vast fungal mass, or something else, and whether it is a willing participant.
- **Phylo's True Nature**: Phylo has not been directly encountered. Whether Phylo is fully corrupted, voluntarily serving Zuggtmoy, or being controlled is unestablished.
- **Gasbide's Plan**: Gasbide intends to send Myconids to the surface. The relationship between this plan and Zuggtmoy's agenda is unclear.
- **Jimjar's Prior Knowledge of the Wedding**: Jimjar appeared to already know about the wedding and was visibly worried, which is unexplained. His sudden cessation of betting is noted as anomalous.
- **Demogorgon's Presence**: Xinaya confirmed both the Demon Queen of Fungi (Zuggtmoy) and Demogorgon are present in the Prime Material Plane and the Underdark simultaneously. The implications of two Demon Lords being loose are unaddressed.
- **Ilvara's Continued Pursuit**: Ilvara remains active, is aware the party seeks Myconid refuge, and wants the party captured alive. With Xinaya's squad eliminated, her next move is unknown.
- **Warning Menzoberranzan**: Xinaya charged Daz with warning other Drow about Zuggtmoy's presence. Whether the party will act on this is unresolved.
- **Rusharoo's Escape Routes**: Rusharoo has prepared nutrient caches and escape routes for potential evacuation of the Grove. These have not been used yet and may become critical.
- **The Secret Passage into the Circle of Welcome**: Rusharoo offered to route the party in via a secret passage. Whether they used it or approached directly is unclear at chapter's end.
- **Thorin and Z**: Thorin cryptically remarks "I think I dodged a bullet there with Z" after Sarith's transformation. The nature of this reference is unexplained.
- **Severin**: Referenced by Grygum as a comparison to Phylo/cult leaders. Severin's identity and history within this campaign are unelaborated in this chunk.
- **The Corrupted Sprouts**: Yrberop was singing Zuggtmoy's melody over fields of unhatched Myconid sprouts. What this means for the next generation of the colony is unresolved.
- **Are the Party Guests or the Feast?**: Thorin's question at chapter's end — whether the party is invited as guests or intended as the meal/sacrifice at the wedding — is unresolved.
