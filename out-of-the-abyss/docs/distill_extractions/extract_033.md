## World Events

- The party arrived at Neverlight Grove and was greeted by Basidia, who explained Phylo's reorganization of Myconid society under the influence of the "Great Seeder."
- Hepbobe gave the party a tour of the Circle of Growers; confirmed the food supply is not infected with evil spores.
- The party encountered Yrberop, an infected Myconid circle leader, dancing and singing Zuggtmoy's wedding song to new sprouts.

- Rusharoo revealed escape routes and nutrient caches prepared for potential evacuation; offered the party a secret passageway into the Circle of Welcome.
- The party entered the Circle of Welcome via the secret passageway and discovered a garden of dozens of Underdark creatures partially buried and infested with fungi.
- Xinaya, an infected Drow, revealed she was sent by Ilvara to capture the party at Neverlight Grove; her squad was infected after accepting Phylo's invitation to the Circle of Welcome.
- Xinaya warned Daz to alert the Drow of Menzoberranzan about Zuggtmoy's presence in the Underdark.
- A fungal abomination (possibly a former Myconid, larval with vestigial fungal growths) appeared and invited the party to the "wedding rehearsal."
- Combat ensued: corpses exploded (one healing the abomination, another dealing acid damage); Drow spore servants attacked; Sarith's head exploded and he became a spore servant.
- Jimjar was covered in spores and temporarily immobilized during combat; later killed spore-servant Sarith.
- Zalthir killed the fungal abomination; Daz killed remaining Myconid creatures with fire magic.
- Thorin mercy-killed Xinaya with Dawnbringer at her request.
- The party collected treasure from the battlefield.
- The party proceeded toward the giant mushroom and encountered a parade of decaying humanoid creatures singing Zuggtmoy's wedding hymn about the union of Araumycos and Zuggtmoy.

## Locations

**Neverlight Grove**
- Underground cavern; bright, cheery light with vibrant colors; open air
- Thorin considers it poorly excavated, lacking structural and defensive elements
- Home of the Myconid colony; currently deteriorating according to Rusharoo
- Contains multiple circles/areas: Circle of Growers, Circle of Explorers, Circle of Welcome, and others
- A plateau with a path leading to a giant mushroom
- Currently under Zuggtmoy's spreading influence

**Circle of Growers**
- Area within Neverlight Grove managed by Hepbobe
- Contains irrigation tunnels and various kinds of cultivated fungi
- Food supply confirmed not infected with evil spores

**Circle of Explorers**
- Area within Neverlight Grove led by Rusharoo
- Connected to escape routes and nutrient caches
- Has a secret passageway to the Circle of Welcome

**Circle of Welcome**
- Area within Neverlight Grove; located on/near a plateau
- Contains a "garden" where dozens of Underdark creatures are partially buried in the ground, infested with mold and fungi
- Illuminated only by a few glowing mushrooms
- Where Xinaya's squad was infected
- Site of combat with fungal abomination, spore servants, and exploding corpses

**The Giant Mushroom / Misty Cavern Beyond**
- Located past the Circle of Welcome along a path from the plateau
- Filled with mist; a parade of infected humanoid creatures sing Zuggtmoy's wedding hymn
- Current location of the "wedding rehearsal"

**Menzoberranzan**
- Drow city; Daz's origin; Xinaya urged Daz to warn the Drow there about Zuggtmoy

**The Overbright (Surface)**
- Gasbide has plans to send Myconids there to "share stories"
- Rusharoo considers this a terrible idea since Myconids thrive in darkness

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Zuggtmoy's Wedding to Araumycos**: The "Great Seeder" (Zuggtmoy) plans to wed/merge with "the Great Body" (Araumycos). The wedding rehearsal is underway. What will happen if the wedding is completed?
- **Araumycos**: Identified as Zuggtmoy's intended; Rusharoo speculated that the caves and tunnels themselves are "another Myconid that connects everything." Is Araumycos this vast fungal entity?
- **Demon Lords in the Prime Material Plane**: Both Zuggtmoy and Demogorgon are confirmed present in the Underdark. How and why are they here?
- **Phylo's Full Corruption**: How deeply is Phylo under Zuggtmoy's control? Can Phylo be saved or must they be destroyed?
- **Jimjar's Behavioral Change**: Jimjar has stopped making bets and seemed to know about the wedding. He looks worried. Basidia confirmed he is not infected. What is wrong with him?
- **Ilvara's Pursuit**: Ilvara is still hunting the party. Xinaya's squad was lost, but Ilvara likely doesn't know this yet. Will she send more forces?
- **Warning Menzoberranzan**: Xinaya urged Daz to warn the Drow about Zuggtmoy. Will Daz act on this?
- **Escape from Neverlight Grove**: Rusharoo has prepared escape routes and nutrient caches. Will the party and Basidia's allies need to flee?
- **Gasbide's Plan to Send Myconids to the Surface**: Yrberop mentioned this plan. Is this part of Zuggtmoy's scheme to spread infection to the surface world?
- **The Wedding Hymn / Melody**: The same chaotic melody appeared in Thorin's dream of Zuggtmoy, in Yrberop's singing, and in the wedding procession. What power does this music hold?
- **Sarith's Fate Foreshadowed**: Thorin noted he "dodged a bullet" with Zalthir regarding Sarith's transformation. Was Sarith already infected before arriving at the grove?
- **The Party's Status at the Wedding**: Thorin asked whether they are "guests or the feast" — their role and danger at the wedding event remains unclear.
