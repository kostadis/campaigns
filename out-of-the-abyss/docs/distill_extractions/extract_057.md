- **What killed Janussi before the chest was opened?** The blood pattern is inconsistent with the wound; the actual cause of death has not been established
- **What was taken from the safe?** Blue gems and empty pouches left behind; the thief was looking for something specific that was not jewelry; identity of the item unknown

- **What did Kalan do with whatever he found?** He was relieved before he could report to the council; his findings are unknown and may now be suppressed or acted upon independently
- **Why did Bookwyrm summon the party specifically, before the Avowed awoke?** She is working a timeline and controlling information flow; her reasons for choosing the party as the instrument of containment are unstated

- **Who is the hooded Avowed figure seen running past the Refectory?** Moving at a half-run with something concealed under their robe; identity and errand unknown
- **What was Kazryn Nyantani's relationship with Janussi?** She arrived weeping and distraught; the nature of her connection to the Keeper is unestablished
