**Jimjar**
- Location: Vanished entirely; last seen heading back toward Blingdenstone before disappearing.
- Current state: Vanished from existence; implied to be a deity or celestial being.

## Locations

**Throne Room (Blingdenstone)**
- Site of the Pudding King's defeat. Earth Elemental held the passage open here before departing. Fungi growing at accelerated rate due to Zuggtmoy's influence. Contains burial grounds considered sacred by the Burrow Warden ghosts. Subject to a sustainable mining compromise.

**Hall of Melting Mirrors (Blingdenstone)**
- Chamber filled with lingering psychic static from the mirrors. Chipgrin and wererats maintained a chanting vigil here. Thorin identified the harmonic center to neutralize the noise. Current state: psychic static neutralized.

**Support Beams area (Blingdenstone)**
- Area with over-engineered temporary bridges and structural beams. Gnomish miners worked here and argued over construction methods (zurkhwood vs. steel). Some miners suffered burns. Thorin helped winch a critical support beam into place. Current state: repaired.

**Farming Area (Blingdenstone outskirts)**
- Area beyond the tunnels near Trader's Grotto. A panicked gnome farmer warned the party of the ooze attack here.

**Trader's Grotto (Blingdenstone)**
- Gnomish defensive position with a breached wall. A large gnomish army gathered here to face the ooze swarm. Site of the ooze crisis and its resolution via Glabbagool. Current state: threat dissolved; oozes dispersed peacefully.

**Blingdenstone**
- Deep gnome city in the Underdark. Recently liberated from the Pudding King's threat. Council brokered a peace between all factions. Reopening for trade with the surface. Zuggtmoy's influence is creeping in (accelerated fungal growth, pale puffball mushrooms). Legends of the Ember Vanguard spreading through its tunnels.

**Upper Boroughs (Blingdenstone)**
- Granted to the Gold Whisker wererats as their territory, to serve as a self-funded defense force.

**Northern Exit Tunnels**
- Tunnels leading from Blingdenstone to the surface. Party was escorted here by a gnomish honor guard. Site of the party's departure and Jimjar's final warning/disappearance.

**Gracklstugh**
- Duergar city in the Underdark. Referenced as the location where Jimjar won the bone die from a blind Duergar.

**Menzoberranzan**
- Drow city. Referenced as the extent of the Voucher of the Varmint's recognition among wererats and outcasts.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Zuggtmoy's encroachment**: Fungi growing at accelerated rates throughout Blingdenstone; a pale white puffball mushroom growing at impossible speed from a wall crack with a sweet rotting scent. Jimjar warned that Zuggtmoy "prefers to move in while you're sleeping." Her influence is spreading even after Juiblex's expulsion.
- **Jimjar's true identity**: Confirmed to possess divine-level immunities and resistances (immune to poison, psychic, charm, fear; resistant to radiant and non-magical physical attacks). Vanished from existence entirely. Stat block resembles an angel or deity. His true nature and purpose remain unknown. Implied the party will meet him again.
- **The bone die's nature**: Carved from a saint's knucklebone; had "a memory for the truth." Jimjar said "a little uncertainty could go a long way" in a place where the blink had "turned the world's math into a bad joke." Now spent and inert, but its full origin and mechanics are unexplained. What is "the blink" that affected the world's math?
- **The Demon Lords in the Underdark**: Juiblex has been driven out; Zuggtmoy is actively spreading. The broader demon lord threat persists. Daz urged factions to build an alliance "strong enough to outlast the demons."
- **Fragile peace of Blingdenstone**: Multiple factions agreed to compromise, but the peace is described as "fragile." Whether it holds remains to be seen, especially with Zuggtmoy's influence growing.
- **The party's journey to the surface**: The Ember Vanguard is preparing to exit the Underdark for the first time. What awaits them on the surface is unknown.
- **Glabbagool's expanded abilities**: Glabbagool briefly connected telepathically with every ooze in the cavern. Whether this experience has lasting effects on him is unresolved.
- **Entemoch's favor**: The Prince of Good Elementals now feels favorably toward the party. Potential future implications are unresolved.
- **The Voucher of the Varmint**: A token recognized by wererats and outcasts from Blingdenstone to Menzoberranzan — potential future use in the Underdark or beyond.
