## World Events

- **Historical — Well of the Dragons:** Events occurred at the Well of the Dragons involving opposition to Tiamat. The chess game between Stroud and Protanther took place in this context.
- **Historical — The Chess Game:** Dwarf Stroud defeated Gold Dragon Protanther in a chess match Protanther had been studying for 400 years. This led to Protanther siding with those who opposed Tiamat, interpreting it as a divine sign. Three competing interpretations emerged: (1) Protanther chose to lose to create an opening to oppose Tiamat, (2) Stroud was the right person at the right time proving individual agency, (3) Bahamut guided Stroud's hand and hid the move from Protanther.
- **10th day, 3rd Tenday of Taraskh 1493:** Boat ran aground on a sandbar in the Darklake. Demonic Manta Rays observed in the water nearby. Daz freed the boat using Shape Water.
