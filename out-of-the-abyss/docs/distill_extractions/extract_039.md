## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Pudding King's identity and history**: The Pudding King is a gnome, but his identity, origins, how he came to serve Jubilex, and how he gained control over hundreds of oozes are unexplained.
- **Jubilex / the Faceless Lord**: The demon lord is being heralded by the Pudding King as coming to Blingdenstone. Whether Jubilex is already present, on the way, or merely being invoked is unclear. A ghost previously mentioned Jubilex in an earlier session; the full scope of this threat is unresolved.
- **Glabbagool's mysterious voices**: Glabbagool heard voices calling him south toward the ooze cavern. Whether these voices originate from Jubilex, the Pudding King, or another source, and whether Glabbagool is susceptible to demonic corruption, remains unresolved.
- **The spherical structure in the ooze cavern**: A large spherical structure at the center of the ooze cavern, held up by stone pillars, was observed but not investigated or explained.
- **Recruiting the Gold Whisker clan**: The party has been tasked by Senni with bringing the Gold Whisker clan (wererat gnomes) into the alliance against the oozes. Chief Dorbo's opposition to their inclusion remains a political obstacle. The outcome of this recruitment is unresolved.
- **Chief Dorbo's character and leadership**: A citizen accused him of caring only for wealth and neglecting the common people. This accusation is unresolved and may have broader implications for Blingdenstone's political situation.
- **The plan's feasibility**: The party's plan requires Zalthir to extract the Pudding King while the gnome army creates a distraction, but the green slime on the ceiling poses a significant danger during escape. The logistics of the plan are not yet fully worked out.
- **Glabbagool's future employment**: A gnome from the mining operations expressed interest in hiring Glabbagool for future work. This potential arrangement is unresolved.
- **The two digested corpses**: The identities of the two deep gnome victims found in the refuse cave are unknown.
- **Jimjar's bet**: Jimjar placed a bet with Chief Dorbo on the adventurers' success. The terms and stakes of this bet are unstated.
