## Locations

**Blingdenstone**
- Deep gnome city; under threat from the ooze incursion; governed by the Diggermattocks; contains multiple factions including the Gold Whisker Clan, Stoneheart Enclave, and Miners Guild

**Rubble Passageway**
- Path leading from Blingdenstone toward the ooze source; initially blocked; cleared by Glabbagool

- Filled with refuse and foul stench; contained two digested deep gnome corpses; ooze trail leading deeper

**The Ooze Cavern**
- Vast cavern filled with countless oozes covering walls and floor; contains a large spherical structure at its center held up by stone pillars; the disembodied voice of the Pudding King emanates here

**The Pudding King's Throne Room**
- Cave illuminated by phosphorescent lichen; pools of green slime; green slime covering the ceiling; a slime-covered throne where the Pudding King holds court; south of the main ooze cavern

**Gold Whisker Clan Territory**
- Located in Blingdenstone area; home of the wererat gnomes; accessible via an alternative path

- **The Pudding King's plan to "cleanse" Blingdenstone**: What exactly does this entail? How imminent is the attack?

- **Political unrest in Blingdenstone**: At least one citizen is discontent with Chief Dorbo's leadership; the party's actions may have political ramifications
- **The ghost who previously mentioned Juiblex**: Referenced but not elaborated upon in this session
