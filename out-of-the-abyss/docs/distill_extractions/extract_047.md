- Chief Dorbo Diggermattock formally declared the assault on the Pudding King and designated Daz as the leader, with Grygum and Zalthir as lieutenants.

- Deep gnome city under threat from the Pudding King's ooze forces
- Civilian areas are at risk if the assault fails
- Currently under active military operations

**Area Twenty-Two**
- Staging/reserve area where non-bridge allied forces waited before the signal

**The Black Pudding Pit (20-foot passageway)**
- A tunnel hollowed out into a pulsating pit of black pudding with acid-dripping ceiling
- Now spanned by a mithril bridge with salt neutralizers, maintained by Pickshine Miners
- The scout noted that if rubble could be cleared, oozes could get through the other way

**The Psychic Slime Corridor**
- Tunnel where walls have transformed into sheets of gelatinous slime acting as dark, weeping mirrors with psychic effects
- A "brain slime" in the ceiling emits rhythmic wet clicking and a psychic song that "turns minds into puddles"
- Currently being drowned out by wererat howling

**The Ochre Jelly Barricade (Grand Vaulted Corridor)**
- A massive wall of translucent ochre jelly filling the hallway floor to ceiling before the throne room
- Pulsed with a rhythmic thrum matching the Pudding King's heartbeat
- Had thick pseudopods that lashed out at intruders
- Heavy stone doors to the throne room visible through its semi-clear body
- Smashed through by the Earth Elemental

**The Pudding King's Throne Room**
- Located behind heavy stone doors past the ochre jelly barricade
- Green slime patches on the ceiling that can drop on anyone standing beneath them
- Currently the site of combat between Zalthir and the Pudding King

**Pickshine Mines**
- Mining operation owned by Dasco Pickshine
- Referenced as one of the allied groups and a brand

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Glabbagool's loyalty**: Glabbagool heard a voice calling him from a large pool of slimes, suggesting the Pudding King may be able to influence or control him. His reliability in proximity to the Pudding King is uncertain.
- **No plan for the Pudding King after capture**: The party has no clear plan for what to do with the Pudding King once captured beyond the initial snatch-and-grab.
- **Zuggtmoy vs. Juiblex rivalry**: The Pudding King ranted that Zuggtmoy thinks she can take his kingdom, while Juiblex promised it to him. The implications of this demon lord conflict for Blingdenstone are unresolved.
- **Wererat survival**: Chipgrin's wererats are drawing all the oozes to themselves by howling. Their fate is uncertain.
- **The ongoing battle**: The main distraction force has begun its attack. The outcome and casualties are unknown.
- **Green slime ceiling hazard**: Green slime patches on the throne room ceiling remain a threat during the ongoing combat.
- **Princess Ebonheir and Prince Livid**: The two ooze henchmen are still in the throne room and not yet engaged; they are separated from the Pudding King but still a threat.
- **Pudding King's ooze form**: The Pudding King has transformed, granting him new combat capabilities; the full extent of his powers in this form is unknown.
- **Bridge vulnerability**: The Pickshine Miners must continuously maintain the mithril bridge against acid. If they fail or are overwhelmed, the party's escape route is compromised.
- **Ooze passage through rubble**: The wererat scout noted that if oozes could get through the rubble, they could attack Blingdenstone from this direction—the cleared wall may be a two-way vulnerability.
