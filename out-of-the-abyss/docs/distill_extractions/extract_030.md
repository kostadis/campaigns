## NPCs

**Grygum**
- Half-orc cleric of Bahamut
- Current location: departing Gracklstugh via boat on the Darklake, heading toward the Lost Tomb of Khaem and then Neverlight Grove
- Repeatedly escapes from prisons (overbrite, Drow, Duergar)
- Flattered Themberchaud, calling him "Great Themberchaud"
- Nearly fell to his death from Themberchaud's back; saved by Jimjar throwing a rope
- Convinced a Stone Guard they were impersonators trying to flush out the Ember Vanguard; gave the guard a caltrop as a fake "symbol of their mission"
- Can cast Mending (significant for naval contexts)
- Took Jimjar's bet about the Neverlight Grove

**Themberchaud**
- Young red dragon, but very large (not old—overfed)
- Title: Wyrmsmith of Gracklestugh
- Current location: Gracklestugh
- Appearance: obese, polished scales, painted claws, double chin; lacks muscle definition of a hunting predator
- Believes he rules Gracklestugh ("my town"), thinks he's in charge
- Actually serves as a "glorified lighter" for the Duergar forges
- Maturity adversely affected by his pampered role
- Declared the party his "agents, bound by my protection"
- Offered the party an "express service" flight; flew them to the docks
- Nearly lost passengers during a clumsy turn mid-flight
- Stopped to breathe fire on a forge mid-flight, shouting "I am Themberchaud the Wyrmsmith, bringer of flame!"
- Lacks grace in flight and landing

**Daz**
- Current location: departing Gracklstugh via boat
- Can cast Misty Step (used it when falling from the dragon)
- The Duergar recognized Daz specifically and panicked, associating the group with the Ember Vanguard
- Noted the party betrayed many people in Gracklstugh

**Thorin**
- Current location: departing Gracklstugh via boat
- Previously avoided a "gift from a demon" but seems inclined to take risks again
- Quoted an old giant saying: "To save the village, you must smash the village"
- Suggested "deputizing" the Stone Guard
- Likes giants but not giant mosquitoes

**Zalthir**
- Current location: departing Gracklstugh via boat
- Can sprout wings (used this ability when falling from the dragon; caught Thorin mid-fall)
- Has fangs
- Recognized the Duergar's panic as them mistaking the party for the Ember Vanguard; suggested exploiting the mistaken identity
- Proposed going to the Lost Tomb of Khaem first before Neverlight Grove to secure a weapon
- Knows a very dangerous and mighty demon is at Neverlight Grove
- Compared Themberchaud to abbots he has known who just want face-saving gestures

**Jimjar**
- A ghoul
- Current location: departing Gracklstugh via boat
- Threw a rope to save Grygum from falling off the dragon
- Made a bet: "an empty shell gem that this circle of joy will reveal itself to be a circle of unwilling vows before you leave it" (referring to Neverlight Grove)
- Grygum accepted the bet

**Serith**
- Current location: departing Gracklstugh via boat
- Insists on going to Neverlight Grove; feels a strange compulsion drawing him there
- The same compulsion drew him to dancing Myconids in the Whorlestone Caverns
- Announced on the 7th day that the party must leave the boat and travel on foot; the path to Neverlight Grove passes the Lost Tomb of Khaem

**Eldeth**
- Current location: with the party (on the boat)
- Would have demanded to see what the party found and inspecting would trigger violence
- Knowledgeable about naval warfare: explained that Mending casters are rare because navies target them first in combat, so spellcasters avoid learning the spell

**Rump-a-dump (Rumpadump)**
- Wants to go to Neverlight Grove
- Believes "the circle cannot be corrupted" and that "our sovereign would never let that happen"
- Described as a child by Zalthir

**Stool**
- Wants to go to Neverlight Grove
- Current location: with the party on the boat

**Gartokkar**
- Previously warned the party that everyone in Gracklstugh is on edge
- Warned that the Stone Guard, the Grey Ghosts, and the Deep King are all looking for/worried about the Ember Vanguard

**Captain Errde**
- Duergar captain in Gracklstugh
- The Stone Guard wanted to warn her about the Ember Vanguard sighting
- Party expects she will have told the Drow that they passed through her city, meaning the Drow will pursue them again

**The Deep King**
- Ruler of Gracklstugh
- Worried about the Ember Vanguard
- Has plots (secrets the party carries)

**Unnamed Duergar Stone Guard**
- Location: the docks of Gracklstugh's Darklake District
- Stood his ground when everyone else fled upon Themberchaud's landing
- Initially challenged the party in the name of the Deep King
- Recognized the party from an Ember Vanguard poster
- Was tricked by Grygum into believing they were impersonators; given a caltrop and ran off

## Factions

**Keepers of the Flame**
- Faction in Gracklstugh associated with Themberchaud
- Themberchaud considers them short-sighted; complained they would have the party "waste precious time navigating checkpoints and patrols"

**Ember Vanguard**
- A powerful mercenary group
- The Stone Guard, Grey Ghosts, and the Deep King are all searching for them
- Wanted posters exist with their likenesses on the docks
- The party is being mistaken for them; the Duergar specifically panicked upon seeing Daz, associating the group with the Ember Vanguard

**Grey Ghosts**
- Faction in Gracklstugh
- Members found dead littering the street after recent events
- Were searching for the Ember Vanguard

**Stone Guard**
- Gracklstugh's military/guard force
- Searching for the Ember Vanguard
- At least one guard posted at the Darklake District docks

**The Drow**
- Previously imprisoned the party
- Expected to resume pursuit once Captain Errde informs them the party passed through Gracklstugh

**Duergar of Gracklstugh**
- Use Themberchaud as a forge-lighter; don't take him seriously (roll their eyes at him)
- Panicked when they saw the party on Themberchaud, believing them to be the Ember Vanguard
- Were seen rushing to send warnings

## Locations

**Gracklstugh**
- Duergar city in the Underdark; described as making "an orc camp look pretty"
- Contains forges producing thick clouds/smoke
- Currently on high alert; multiple factions searching for the Ember Vanguard
- The party has departed, carrying dangerous secrets that could have provoked mass death if revealed

**Laduguer's Furrow**
- Has barred gates; any escape from Gracklstugh requires getting through them
- A "clever and insane scheme" would be needed to pass

**Darklake District**
- District in Gracklstugh containing the docks
- The party needed passes to enter; Eldeth would have demanded to inspect their findings
- There was a previous attack on the docks (used as justification to Themberchaud)
- Everyone at the docks fled when Themberchaud landed except one Stone Guard

**The Darklake**
- Underground lake; the party is traveling on it by boat
- Route from Gracklstugh toward Neverlight Grove and the Lost Tomb of Khaem

**Neverlight Grove**
- Myconid settlement; destination desired by Rumpadump, Stool, and Serith
- A very dangerous and mighty demon is located there
- Rumpadump believes the sovereign there would never let the circle be corrupted
- Jimjar predicts it involves a "circle of unwilling vows" (implied demonic wedding)
- Serith feels a compulsion drawing him there (same compulsion that drew him to dancing Myconids in the Whorlestone Caverns)

**Lost Tomb of Khaem**
- Located along the path between the Darklake shore and Neverlight Grove
- Contains a weapon the party wants to secure before going to Neverlight Grove
- The party plans to visit it first

**Whorlestone Caverns**
- Previously visited location where Serith encountered dancing Myconids that compelled him

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Corrupted dragon egg**: The party carries knowledge of a corrupted dragon egg in Gracklstugh; its nature, origin, and implications remain unresolved
- **Deep King's plots**: The party knows secrets about the Deep King's schemes; these have not been revealed or acted upon
- **Demonic plots in Gracklstugh**: Lurking demonic influence in the city; the party chose to take this knowledge with them rather than expose it
- **Why a red dragon as a forge-lighter?**: Grygum wonders why the Duergar chose a red dragon when magic would be cheaper and less dangerous
- **Themberchaud's manipulation**: The dragon is being manipulated by the Duergar and doesn't realize it; what happens when/if he discovers the truth?
- **Ember Vanguard identity**: The party is being mistaken for this powerful mercenary group; who are the real Ember Vanguard and what are they doing?
- **Captain Errde informing the Drow**: The party expects Errde will tell the Drow they passed through Gracklstugh, triggering renewed Drow pursuit
- **The Stone Guard with the caltrop**: Grygum's deception may hold temporarily but could unravel; the guard may report to Captain Errde
- **Demon at Neverlight Grove**: A powerful demon lurks there; Rumpadump naively believes the sovereign would prevent corruption
- **Serith's compulsion**: An unexplained force draws Serith toward the Myconids and Neverlight Grove; its source and nature are unknown
- **Jimjar's bet about Neverlight Grove**: Predicts a "circle of unwilling vows"—a wedding involving demons and mushrooms; Grygum accepted the bet
- **What weapon is in the Lost Tomb of Khaem?**: The party plans to retrieve it but its nature is unknown
- **Thorin's temptation**: Previously refused a demon's gift but seems inclined to take risks again; Zalthir is concerned
- **The scuttled/hidden boat**: The party debated scuttling the boat (with Grygum able to Mend it later); leaving it is a sign for pursuers; its fate is unresolved
- **The "overbrite"**: Referenced as a previous location with prisons; details unclear
