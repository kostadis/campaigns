- Recent actions: Leadership debated the use of Earth Elementals for labor; religious leaders agreed that enslaving summoned creatures violated their deepest beliefs; ultimately decided Earth Elementals may be summoned for defense and warfare but NOT for building or mining labor

**Menzoberranzan / Drow**

## World Events

- The party completed an ancient ritual involving a steel dart and two gems placed in designated positions, releasing Entemoch's boon
- The ritual caused the ground to rumble with a deep, soothing vibration and corrected "an ancient wrongness in the stone"
- The party gained the ability to summon Earth Elementals and received a protective blessing (turn skin to stone once per day when struck in battle)
- Two basilisks attacked during the ritual; one was mentally ensnared by magical patterns, the other was killed by Zalthir in melee combat
- The party successfully convinced Blingdenstone's leadership to ban the use of Earth Elementals for mining and building labor, restricting their summoning to defense and warfare only
- Blingdenstone's economic plans were fundamentally disrupted by this decision
- Working gnomes expressed gratitude and admiration toward the party
- Traveling merchant Starlace arrived in Blingdenstone with magical wares
- The party purchased and upgraded equipment in preparation for the battle against the Pudding King
- A locking scabbard was commissioned and obtained for Dawnbringer from Valimor Brightgem
