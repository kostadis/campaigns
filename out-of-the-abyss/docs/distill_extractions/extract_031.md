## Threads & Mysteries

- **Identity of the trapped feminine voice:** Who or what is telepathically pleading for help from inside the gilded sarcophagus? Is it Brysis of Khaem herself? Is it the Wraith deceiving them? Is it a separate entity imprisoned with or by the Wraith?
- **The Wraith's claim:** It stated the party's deaths would "free me from this prison" — what is imprisoning the Wraith, and what happens if it is freed?
- **Spectres will reform:** Daz's knowledge indicates the destroyed Spectres will return as long as the central evil force (likely the Wraith) persists. The party must deal with the Wraith or face them again.
- **The invisible stone chest:** Grygum spotted it near the gilded sarcophagus but it has not yet been opened or examined.
- **Fargus Rumblefoot's warning:** He mentioned a "secret room" or "false tomb" in the Lost Tomb of Khaem — has the party found the true tomb, or is there still a false/secret element?
- **The sarcophagus passage south:** The party chose east at the shrine; the southern passage remains unexplored.
- **Fuurm Coppernose's lost sister:** He came to the Underdark looking for her; her fate is unknown.
- **Drow pursuit:** Significantly reduced but not eliminated; Eldeth worries that environmental disturbances (crystal collection, cave smashing) may help trackers.
- **Usefulness of Faerzress quartz crystals:** The party has collected many; Grygum and Jimjar have a bet on whether they'll prove useful. No magical effect observed when thrown at a Shrieker.
- **Grygum's secret Mending ability:** He is deliberately keeping it hidden to avoid being assigned to ship duty.
- **The Netherese Calendar Stone:** Found on a staircase landing; its purpose is unclear.
- **Netherese treasure:** Valuable items found in the four upper sarcophagi; potentially worth more to the right collector.
- **Demon attacks on the Drow:** Demons attacked the Drow (enabling Fuurm and Baedora's escape) — part of the broader demon incursion in the Underdark.
