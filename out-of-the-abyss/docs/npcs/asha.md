# Asha

## Identity
- Drow cleric stationed at Velkynvelve
- Appears to hold a subordinate position beneath Ilvara, the outpost's commander

## Personality & Motivations
- Ambitious and politically calculating. She is actively positioning herself to challenge or replace Ilvara as commander of Velkynvelve. Her core drive appears to be the pursuit of power and authority within the outpost's hierarchy.

## History with the Party
- The party learned of Asha's ambitions through Jimjar, who described her as "angling for the top job" at Velkynvelve.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Velkynvelve
- **Active plans:** Maneuvering to supplant Ilvara as commander
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows of her ambitions via Jimjar's tip but has had no direct interactions with her noted in the records

## Relationships
- **Ilvara:** Superior and rival. Asha seeks to replace her as commander of Velkynvelve.
- **Jimjar:** Jimjar is aware of Asha's political maneuvering and shared this intelligence with the party.
