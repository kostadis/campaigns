# Uskvil

## Identity
- Leader of the Gray Ghosts, a thieves' organization
- Referenced in Pliinki's letters as "Master Thief"
- Affiliated with the Demogorgon cult (operational asset)
- Has not appeared in person; known only through intercepted correspondence

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goals and drives are unclear beyond serving the Demogorgon cult's interests, possibly for profit or mutual benefit. Operates as a high-level coordinator of theft and subterfuge. His willingness to orchestrate assassinations and large-scale diversions suggests ruthless pragmatism.

## History with the Party
- The party has never met Uskvil directly. They learned of him through Pliinki's intercepted letters, which revealed:
  - His operatives successfully stole both a **red dragon egg** and **Clan Thrazgad ore** on behalf of the Demogorgon cult.
  - Pliinki corresponded with him about the **Obelisk's resistance to repair** using the Thrazgad ore.
  - Pliinki instructed him to **continue diversions** and ensure the **Deepking's hit list** remained in the hands of assassins to sow chaos.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Unknown; has not appeared in person.
- **Active plans:** Maintaining diversions and ensuring the Deepking's hit list circulates among assassins, per Pliinki's instructions.
- **What the party knows:** His name, his title as leader of the Gray Ghosts, his role in the thefts, and his correspondence with Pliinki.
- **What remains hidden:** His location, appearance, full capabilities, the extent of the Gray Ghosts' network, and whether he has independent motivations beyond serving the cult.

## Relationships
- **Pliinki:** Handler or cult contact; issues him instructions and receives operational reports. Pliinki holds authority in the relationship.
- **The Gray Ghosts:** Leader and commander of this thieves' organization.
- **Demogorgon Cult:** Serves as an operative, carrying out thefts and diversions to further the cult's agenda.
- **The Deepking:** Indirectly undermining the Deepking's authority by weaponizing the hit list through assassins.
- **The Party:** No direct relationship; they are aware of him only through intercepted letters.
