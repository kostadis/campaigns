# Uskvil

## Identity
- Leader of the Gray Ghosts, a thieves' organization
- Referenced in Pliinki's letters as "Master Thief"
- Affiliated with the Demogorgon cult (at least operationally)
- Has not appeared in person; known only through correspondence and the results of his operations

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goals and drives are unclear beyond serving the cult's interests and executing high-profile thefts. Appears to be a capable and reliable operative willing to carry out complex, multi-layered schemes involving theft, diversion, and political destabilization.

## History with the Party
- The party has not met Uskvil directly. They learned of him through Pliinki's intercepted correspondence, which revealed:
  - His operatives successfully stole both a **red dragon egg** and **Clan Thrazgad ore** on behalf of the Demogorgon cult.
  - Pliinki informed him that the Obelisk was resisting repair using the Thrazgad ore.
  - Pliinki instructed him to continue running diversions and to ensure the **Deepking's hit list** remained in the hands of assassins, deliberately stoking chaos.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Unknown
- **Active plans:** Maintaining diversionary operations and ensuring the Deepking's hit list circulates among assassins, per Pliinki's instructions
- **What the party knows:** His name, his role as Gray Ghosts leader, his involvement in the dragon egg and ore thefts, and his correspondence with Pliinki
- **What remains hidden:** His location, appearance, full scope of operations, and the depth of his personal commitment to the Demogorgon cult

## Relationships
- **Pliinki:** Handler or cult superior who issues instructions; Pliinki directs Uskvil's operations and shares intelligence about the Obelisk
- **Gray Ghosts:** Leads this organization; his operatives carried out the key thefts
- **Demogorgon Cult:** Works on the cult's behalf, at minimum as a contracted operative
- **The Deepking:** Indirectly undermining the Deepking's authority by weaponizing the hit list to spread chaos
- **The Party:** No direct contact; known to them only by reputation and through intercepted letters
