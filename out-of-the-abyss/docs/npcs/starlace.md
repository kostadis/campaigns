# Starlace

## Identity
- Traveling merchant specializing in magical wares
- First appeared in Blingdenstone during the city's preparations for battle against the Pudding King and his oozes

## Personality & Motivations
- A pragmatic businessperson willing to trade in dangerous locations, arriving in a city on the brink of war. Deals fairly — both sides felt satisfied with negotiations. Carries a diverse inventory of enchanted goods and offers enchanting services.

## History with the Party
- **Blingdenstone (pre-battle):** Sold the party Zalthir spectacles — glowing crystal lenses that allow the wearer to see invisible creatures and peer through magical darkness. Enchanted Grygum's mace and shield with protective magic. Purchased excess armor from the party. All transactions were mutually agreeable.

## Current Status
- Last seen in Blingdenstone during battle preparations
- No known ongoing plans or operations
- Nothing hidden — interactions have been purely commercial

## Relationships
- **Grygum:** Customer; enchanted his mace and shield with protective magic
- **The Party (general):** Positive commercial relationship; fair dealings on both sides
