# Ploopploopeen (Ploop)

## Identity
- Archpriest of the Sea Mother (kuo-toa deity)
- Father of Bloppblippodd
- Faction: Sea Mother loyalists among the kuo-toa, opposed to the Deep Father cult
- First encountered when the party became entangled in the kuo-toa religious civil conflict

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Destroy the Deep Father cult and eliminate his own daughter Bloppblippodd as its leader
- A cunning manipulator willing to sacrifice family for religious conviction. Orchestrated an elaborate ruse, pretending to offer the escapees as sacrifices to gain proximity to Blopp before turning on her. Ruthless enough to personally deliver the killing blow against his own daughter.

## History with the Party
1. **The Ruse at the Altar:** Ploop presented the escapees as offerings to Bloppblippodd at the altar, appearing to cooperate with the Deep Father cult. This was a deception — his true intent was to betray his daughter, not the party.
2. **The Civil War:** Ploop attacked Bloppblippodd alongside his allies, triggering an all-out civil war among the kuo-toa. He struck the final, fatal blow against his daughter before the altar.
3. **Thorin's Realization:** Thorin noted that Ploop could have paralyzed the entire party with hold person spells if he had truly been working against them — evidence that Ploop had been playing a deeper game all along.
4. **Post-Battle Looting:** Grygum looted Ploop's thatched hut, justifying it by noting Ploop had promised to pay them.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The kuo-toa settlement, near the altar where the battle took place
- **Status after the battle and Demogorgon's emergence is unknown**
- The party looted his home; any remaining relationship is uncertain

## Relationships
- **Bloppblippodd:** His daughter and archpriest of the Deep Father cult. He killed her personally at the altar.
- **The Party:** Used them as pawns in his scheme. Had promised them payment. Whether he views them as allies or expendable tools remains ambiguous.
- **Thorin:** Thorin recognized Ploop's cunning, acknowledging that Ploop had been manipulating the situation.
- **Grygum:** Looted Ploop's hut, citing the promised but unpaid reward.
