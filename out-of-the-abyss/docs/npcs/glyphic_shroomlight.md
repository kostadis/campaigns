# Glyphic Shroomlight

## Identity
- Priest at the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough, dedicated to Segojan Earthcaller (Svirfneblin god of deep earthen nature)
- Met when the party brought Pelek's bones to the temple seeking proper interment

## Personality & Motivations
- Cautious and duty-bound; initially reluctant to inter the remains of a stranger without proper justification. Pragmatic enough to reverse course immediately when Pelek's ghost materialized before him—motivated more by self-preservation than piety in that moment. Showed practical concern by asking that Glabbagool, the party's gelatinous cube, stay upstairs to prevent accidents in the temple.

## History with the Party
- The party arrived at the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough seeking to lay Pelek's bones to rest.
- Glyphic was hesitant to perform rites for someone unknown to him.
- Pelek's ghost appeared before Glyphic, which immediately changed his mind—he did not wish to be haunted.
- He directed the party to the catacombs below the temple for the interment.
- He requested that Glabbagool remain upstairs during the process.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The Temple of the Ruby in the Rough
- **Activity:** Presumably continuing his priestly duties at the temple
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows him as the priest who facilitated Pelek's burial; no hidden information noted

## Relationships
- **Pelek (ghost):** The spirit whose appearance compelled Glyphic to act; Glyphic had no prior connection to Pelek
- **Segojan Earthcaller:** Glyphic's deity; serves as priest in his temple
- **The Party:** Cooperative but initially guarded; helped them once sufficiently motivated
- **Glabbagool:** Wary; asked the gelatinous cube to stay away from the catacombs
