# Glyphic Shroomlight

## Identity
- Priest at the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough, dedicated to Segojan Earthcaller (Svirfneblin god of deep earthen nature)
- Met when the party sought to inter the bones of Pelek at his temple

## Personality & Motivations
- Cautious and dutiful; initially reluctant to inter the remains of a stranger, suggesting he takes his responsibilities seriously. Quickly changed his mind when Pelek's ghost materialized before him, revealing a pragmatic streak—he'd rather perform the rites than risk being haunted. Politely but firmly asked that Glabbagool the gelatinous cube stay upstairs, showing practical concern for safety in his temple.

## History with the Party
- The party brought Pelek's bones to the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough for proper interment.
- Glyphic initially refused, not knowing the deceased.
- Pelek's ghost appeared, and Glyphic immediately agreed to perform the burial rites.
- He directed the party to the catacombs below the temple to complete the interment.
- He requested that Glabbagool remain on the upper level to prevent accidents.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Temple of the Ruby in the Rough
- **Activity:** Continuing his priestly duties
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows him as a cooperative, if cautious, priest willing to help once properly motivated

## Relationships
- **Pelek (ghost):** Agreed to inter his remains after the ghost's appearance; motivated by not wanting to be haunted
- **Glabbagool:** Wary of the gelatinous cube; insisted it stay out of the catacombs
- **The Party:** Cordial relationship; assisted them with the burial task
