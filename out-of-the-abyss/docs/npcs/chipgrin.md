# Chief Chipgrin

## Identity
- Leader of the Gold Whisker clan, a faction of wererat gnomes in Blingdenstone
- First encountered seated on a stone chair draped with cave badger pelt, flanked by giant rats and guards, on a plateau of manicured green moss
- The party met him when they approached the Gold Whiskers to recruit allies against the ooze threat; he initially assumed they had been sent by the Diggermattocks to eliminate his clan

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: secure autonomy, territory, and respect for the Gold Whisker clan — refuses to be subordinate to Chief Dorbo Diggermattock or treated as expendable. Fiercely protective of his people.
- Chipgrin is proud, shrewd, and blunt. He respects directness and personal accountability (was impressed by Daz's approach). He has a dry, self-deprecating wit — after Thorin outperformed spellcasters at a magical task, Chipgrin muttered he should have asked the dwarf first, then hastily reassured Zalthir he meant no offense. He is quick to take offense at any slight to his clan's competence, particularly their stealth capabilities.

## History with the Party
1. **Recruitment:** Daz approached Chipgrin to join the fight against the ooze. After Daz took personal accountability for outcomes, Chipgrin agreed to fight but refused to serve under Dorbo. He shared critical intelligence about the Pudding King — a gnome he had known before the transformation — explaining his motivations (vengeance, pride, madness, demonic influence) and revealing that Juiblex's corruption, centered on the Pudding King, was bleeding through and warping the entire Blingdenstone community with greed and aggression. Attended the grand moot.
2. **Earth Elemental Debate:** Present at the leadership meeting; agreed (under pressure from the party's moral/religious arguments) that elementals should only be summoned for defense and warfare, not labor or mining.
3. **Scouting the Ooze Tunnels:** Led wererat scouts ahead of the main force. When a scout was caught by psychic effects from gelatinous slime walls and a "brain slime," Chipgrin personally investigated and reported threats in detail. He offered two options; the party chose the loud path (wererats howling to suppress psychic song), which drew all oozes to the wererats and sacrificed Chipgrin's continued scouting/backup support.
4. **Hall of Melting Mirrors:** Found maintaining a chanting vigil with his wererats to suppress lingering psychic static. Asked the party's spellcasters to locate and silence the harmonic center permanently. Thorin succeeded where casters couldn't, prompting Chipgrin's wry reaction.
5. **Territorial Negotiations:** Argued with Chief Diggermattock over newly opened territory. Demanded the upper boroughs for the Gold Whiskers. The party brokered a compromise: wererats take the upper burrows as a self-funded defense force, removing Diggermattock's need to pay for military protection. Accepted.
6. **Farewell:** Pressed the **Voucher of the Varmint** (a braided rat's foot) into Daz's hand upon the party's departure — a token recognized by wererats and outcasts from Blingdenstone to Menzoberranzan as a sign of friendship.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Upper burrows of Blingdenstone, now recognized Gold Whisker territory
- **Activity:** Establishing the wererats as Blingdenstone's self-funded upper defense force
- **Known to Party:** His intelligence about the Pudding King, Juiblex's influence, and the territorial compromise are all fully known. The Voucher of the Varmint is in Daz's possession and may prove useful with wererat/outcast contacts along the route to Menzoberranzan.

## Relationships
- **Daz:** Strongest bond with the party. Respects Daz's directness and willingness to take personal accountability. Gave Daz the Voucher of the Varmint as a personal mark of friendship.
- **Thorin:** Amused respect after the dwarf outperformed spellcasters in the Hall of Melting Mirrors.
- **Zalthir:** Cautious politeness; went out of his way not to offend him during the Thorin incident.
- **Chief Dorbo Diggermattock:** Rival and political adversary. Refuses to serve under Dorbo. Clashed over territory at the Trader's Grotto. The party-brokered compromise eased but likely did not eliminate tensions.
- **The Pudding King:** Knew him personally before his transformation. Understands his psychology and the demonic influence driving him.
