# Uth-Jadgar

## Identity
- Barrow Warden ghost; title of Barrow Warden
- First appeared at the grand moot, where he addressed the party during strategic planning

## Personality & Motivations
- Takes pride in the legacy and tactical prowess of the Barrow Wardens. Respects competence and strategic thinking, as shown by his praise of the party's plan. Willing to offer aid in exchange for service — transactional but not adversarial.

## History with the Party
- **Grand Moot:** Attended the gathering and praised the party's plan as "technically brilliant," likening it to strategies the Barrow Wardens would have used in their prime. Revealed that the Temple of the Steadfast Stone is located in the Rock Blight and must be cleansed before it can serve as a staging area. Offered the assistance of other Barrow Warden ghosts in exchange for the party undertaking a quest on their behalf. The party delegated Glabbagool, Eldev, and Jimjar to assist with this quest.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Grand moot area (presumably still tied to the Barrow Warden haunt or the Rock Blight vicinity)
- **Active plans:** Awaiting completion of the quest he proposed; Glabbagool, Eldev, and Jimjar have been assigned to help
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows the Temple of the Steadfast Stone is in the Rock Blight and needs cleansing. They know Uth-Jadgar can rally other Barrow Warden ghosts if his quest is fulfilled. Details of the quest itself and the full nature of the Barrow Wardens' unfinished business may remain unclear.

## Relationships
- **Barrow Warden ghosts:** Leader or representative; can mobilize them if his conditions are met
- **The Party:** Respectful ally-of-convenience; offered a quid pro quo arrangement
- **Glabbagool, Eldev, Jimjar:** Assigned by the party to work with the Barrow Warden ghosts on their quest
