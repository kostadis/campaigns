# Rust

## Identity
- Duergar assassin, member of the Empty Scabbard guild (referred to as "empty scabbard killers")
- First appeared at the intersection near the assassin's lair alongside Grimgrim; introduced himself to the party there

## Personality & Motivations
- Pragmatic and open to alliance — was pleased to learn the party were enemies of Demogorgon, suggesting shared opposition to the Demogorgon cult. Accommodating enough to offer a simpler name when his true Derro name proved unpronounceable. Serves as a go-between for the guild's leadership, indicating a mid-rank operative role.

## History with the Party
- Materialized at the intersection with Grimgrim and introduced himself, giving the name "Rust" in lieu of his unpronounceable Derro name.
- Expressed approval upon learning the party opposed Demogorgon.
- Explained the guild's identity as the "empty scabbard killers," then left to confer with his leadership about the party's proposal to cooperate against the Demogorgon cult.
- Returned and escorted four party members (Daz, Thorin, Zalthir, Grygum) to Eldgrim, ordering the remaining party members to wait outside.
- Was distracted by Thorin (who asked about the empty scabbard moniker) along with Grimgrim, allowing Daz to steal the contract unnoticed.

## Current Status
- Last known location: with or near Eldgrim at the Empty Scabbard guild's meeting area.
- Unaware that Daz stole the contract during the distraction.
- The party has successfully used him as a point of contact with the guild's leadership.

## Relationships
- **Grimgrim**: Fellow guild member; the two operate together as a pair and were distracted simultaneously by Thorin.
- **Eldgrim**: Rust's superior in the Empty Scabbard guild; Rust deferred to leadership before acting on the party's proposal and ultimately led the party to Eldgrim.
- **The Party**: Tentative allies against the Demogorgon cult; Rust has been cooperative, though the party has already acted against guild interests by stealing the contract without his knowledge.
