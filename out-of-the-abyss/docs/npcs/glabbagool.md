# Glabbagool

## Identity
- Sentient gelatinous cube (later transformed into sentient ooze form) and beloved party companion
- Unofficially nicknamed "Roomba" by the party
- First encountered in a chamber containing a hovering drow corpse, a dark metal mace, and scattered coins in the Underdark

## Personality & Motivations
- Core drives: friendship, belonging, and having a sense of purpose — when asked about payment, he revealed what he truly wanted was friends and to feel needed
- Endlessly cheerful, curious, and earnest to the point of endearing incompetence; he narrates his own attempts at stealth, plays games with animated statues, and gets drunk cleaning tavern floors
- Fiercely proud of his identity as a *cube* (not an ooze), insisting on the distinction of higher viscosity and square shape — though he later ended up in ooze form anyway
- Remarkably guileless and sincere; tries hard to be helpful even when his ten-foot-by-ten-foot body makes that difficult

## History with the Party
1. **First encounter**: Found in an underground chamber; Thorin invited him to join the party and gave him a lucky foot as a welcome gift. He promised to eat anything they dropped on the floor.
2. **Early exploration**: Blocked a narrow passage with his body, agreed to stay at the rear. Touched mysterious sculptures that materialized into gray oozes. Discovered a hidden treasure cache in a fountain (112 sp, 3 gp, a drow dagger, oil of slipperiness — all requiring digestion time). Confirmed he didn't need to breathe during a flooding event. Agreed to allow resurrection attempts on fallen members before consuming them.
3. **Blingdenstone**: Caused nervousness among deep gnomes. Corrected anyone calling him an ooze. Expressed confusion about why oozes were hurting gnomes, noting from experience they were "very not talkative." Asked if shambling mounds contained treasure when they exploded.
4. **Temple of the Ruby in the Rough**: Asked to wait upstairs to avoid catacombs accidents.
5. **Trader's Grotto attack**: When mindless gelatinous cubes attacked the market, protested they were giving intelligent cubes a bad name. Gave tactical advice on avoiding engulfment. Was fascinated by Zalthir's eldritch claw tentacles, asking if he was an ooze; seemed pleased when Zalthir called himself "an aspiring ooze."
6. **Hired for work**: Expressed desire for friends and purpose over payment. Eagerly cleared a rubble passageway over an hour, impressing a gnome miner who wanted to hire him.
7. **Ooze trail investigation**: Began hearing voices calling him south. Zalthir persuaded him to stay with the group by appealing to friendship. Led the party toward the Pudding King's cavern, expressing discomfort at the speaking oozes.
8. **Blingdenstone tasks**: Volunteered to clean Chief Dorbo's kitchen floor (earning "Roomba" nickname). Assigned to help Barrow Warden ghosts with Eldeth, Jimjar, and Spiderbait. Asked sincerely if he'd be the first gelatinous cube ghost. Got drunk on spilled alcohol at the Foaming Mug, sang sea shanties "in a voice that made everyone question their life choices." Plowed through mushroom cultivation caves like "a gigantic transparent snowblower." Sent to "clean" suspicious statues that turned out to be gargoyles — his googly-eyed appearance confused their ambush.
9. **Animated statues chamber**: Played a game of entering and exiting a room to watch drow statues animate and freeze.
10. **Medusa's lair**: Expressed delight at tasting the Medusa's remains, cheerfully cleaning the aftermath.
11. **Ooze barricade**: Suggested the party could pass through him to bypass an ochre jelly barricade (with acid burn and oxygen risks). Promised he would never hurt his friends but admitted the Pudding King's voice had called to him before.
12. **Pudding King throne room battle**: Zalthir dragged the Pudding King through Glabbagool ("this tickles"), causing Zalthir acid damage. During the fight, Glabbagool and the Pudding King swapped bodies — Glabbagool ended up in ooze form. Thorin confirmed his identity by asking where they first met ("we met in a cave"). After the Pudding King's defeat, Glabbagool was cheerful about his new smaller form, noting he'd always been inconveniently slow as a cube. Enthusiastically suggested the party carry him in a jar or Bag of Holding as "the ultimate garbage disposal."
13. **Telepathic ooze bridge**: When Jimjar tossed his mysterious bone die onto Glabbagool, he briefly became a telepathic bridge connecting every ooze in the cavern. He gently told them all that the world was fine and there was no need to kill anyone. The oozes went peaceful and dispersed. He later described briefly being part of one vast mind.
14. **Asha Vandree negotiation**: Instructed by Daz to remain motionless as a rock; failed immediately, narrating his own invisibility and tracking the conversation with wandering eyes. Asha accepted him as Daz's "bound familiar," which actually bolstered Daz's cover as a powerful mage. Didn't seem to mind being called a familiar, though Grygum felt he deserved something for the indignity.
15. **Ilvara battle**: Caught in Ilvara's Insect Plague, taking 26 points of damage; described as "churning and trembling." Grygum's first concern during and after the battle was checking on Glabbagool. Daz specifically sculpted his Fireball to exclude Glabbagool from the blast.

## Current Status
- **Last known location**: With the party, in ooze form (no longer a cube) following the body swap with the Pudding King
- **Condition**: Survived Ilvara's Insect Plague (took 26 damage); otherwise intact
- **Active plans**: Party has discussed beginning monk training for him to control his form and acidity with precision; he is enthusiastic about traveling in a jar or Bag of Holding
- **Hidden concerns**: The Pudding King previously had some influence over him (heard voices calling him toward the slime pool); unclear if this vulnerability persists now that the Pudding King is defeated

## Relationships
- **Thorin**: The one who originally invited him to join the party and gave him a welcome gift; later confirmed his identity during the body swap by asking where they met
- **Zalthir**: Close bond; Zalthir persuaded him to resist the Pudding King's call by appealing to friendship; Glabbagool views Zalthir as "an aspiring ooze" and a protector
- **Daz**: Acts as Daz's "familiar" for cover purposes; Daz protects him in combat (sculpted Fireball around him) and directs him during social encounters
- **Grygum**: Deeply protective of Glabbagool; first to check on him after battles; advocated he deserved recognition after being called a familiar; suggested using him for rubble clearing
- **Jimjar**: Partnered for the ghost quest; Jimjar's bone die triggered Glabbagool's telepathic ooze-bridge moment
- **Eldeth & Spiderbait**: Volunteered alongside them for the Barrow Warden ghost mission
- **Asha Vandree**: Accepted by Asha as Daz's bound familiar, reinforcing her perception of Daz's magical power
- **Deep gnomes of Blingdenstone**: Viewed with suspicion and nervousness; earned some goodwill through work (kitchen cleaning, rubble clearing, mushroom plowing)
- **Oozes generally**: Considers them "fat friends" but notes they are "very not talkative"; briefly connected to all of them telepathically and calmed them

## Arc Score Events
- **Increase**: Joined the party willingly; discovered treasure in the fountain; cleared rubble passageway; resisted the Pudding King's call when Zalthir appealed to friendship; served as telepathic bridge to pacify all oozes; survived the Pudding King battle and cheerfully adapted to new form
- **Decrease**: Heard the Pudding King's voices and was drawn toward them (vulnerability to influence); body swap during throne room battle (involuntary, potentially destabilizing)
