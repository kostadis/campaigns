# Grimholl Forgebrand

## Identity
- Subordinate of Erdde Blackskull
- Goes by "Holl" to friends
- First appeared when brought into a room to brief Grygum on a series of mysterious killings

## Personality & Motivations
- Blunt and guarded — immediately establishes distance with strangers ("Holl to my friends, and you are not my friends"). His core drive in the interaction was delivering intelligence on the killings accurately; Zalthir later corroborated that Holl was likely telling the truth.

## History with the Party
- **Briefing on the Killings:** Grimholl was brought in by Erdde Blackskull to brief Grygum on mysterious deaths. He reported that the bodies showed no entrance wounds or exit wounds. The party was present for this briefing.
- **Corroboration:** Zalthir later examined a Duergar merchant who had been attacked by assassins wielding glowing weapons. The merchant bore no visible wounds, leading Zalthir to conclude that Holl's account was likely truthful.

## Current Status
- Last seen during or after the briefing with Grygum
- No further known activities or plans
- The party knows he is a reliable source on the killings and operates under Erdde Blackskull's authority

## Relationships
- **Erdde Blackskull:** Direct superior; Grimholl serves under them
- **Grygum:** Recipient of Grimholl's briefing; professional interaction
- **Zalthir:** No direct interaction noted, but Zalthir independently validated Grimholl's claims
- **The Party:** Adversarial or at minimum cold — made clear they are not considered friends
