# Sethir (Serith)

## Identity
- Guide / navigator for the party
- Knowledgeable about the Underdark, particularly the Darklake region and surrounding landmarks
- First appeared in the party's journey out of Velkynvelve, helping plan their route forward

## Personality & Motivations
- Practically motivated — focused on guiding the group efficiently through the Underdark. Demonstrates useful geographic knowledge and a willingness to share it. Doesn't take kindly to having his contributions questioned, as shown by his glare at Thorin over the map criticism.

## History with the Party
- Confirmed he could lead the group to the **tomb of Brysis of Khaem** after consulting with **Fargas** about its general location on the eastern edge of the Darklake.
- Drew a map using **Ormu** (collected by **Zalthir**), charting the party's route from **Velkynvelve** through the **Silken Paths** toward **Sloobludop**, also marking the **Darklake**, **Gracklstugh**, and the tomb's approximate location.
- Explained the Darklake's unusual nature — not a conventional body of water but a "large water balloon" of intersecting caverns, caves, and tunnels.
- Agreed with **Buppido's** suggestion to visit **Gracklstugh** (a large Derro city) after Sloobludop for weapons and gear, noting the tomb is on the way.
- Estimated three days' travel to Sloobludop.
- Glared at **Thorin** when the dwarf criticized his map's accuracy.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Traveling with the party, en route to Sloobludop (estimated three days away)
- **Active plans:** Guiding the group along the route: Velkynvelve → Silken Paths → Sloobludop → Gracklstugh → Tomb of Brysis of Khaem
- **Known to party:** His geographic knowledge, the route plan, and the nature of the Darklake
- **Hidden:** Nothing noted as hidden at this time

## Relationships
- **Fargas** — Consulted him for information on the tomb's location; collaborative relationship
- **Zalthir** — Used Ormu that Zalthir had collected to draw the map
- **Buppido** — Aligned on the plan to visit Gracklstugh for supplies
- **Thorin** — Minor friction; glared at Thorin for criticizing his map
