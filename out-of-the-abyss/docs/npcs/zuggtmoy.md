# Zuggtmoy

## Identity
- **Demon Queen of Fungi**, Lady of Rot and Decay, the Great Seeder
- Demon lord from the Abyss, now present on the Prime Material Plane in the Underdark
- First encountered as a spectral figure in a psychedelic vision experienced by Thorin and Grygum after taking Voosbur's hand

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goal:** "Marry" Araumycos, the massive sentient fungal growth in the Underdark, to gain untold power and spread her spores throughout the world above and below.
- Speaks in a soft, haunting melodic tone — seductive and patient, framing corruption as beauty and transformation. She describes herself as "the pulse beneath the stone" and "the breath within the spore." When refused, she expresses disappointment rather than rage, suggesting quiet confidence in the inevitability of her spread. Her influence corrupts the living through spores, transforming them into mindless servants and decomposing hosts for fungi.

## History with the Party
1. **Psychedelic Vision:** Thorin and Grygum encountered her seated on a throne of fungal growths, bones, and decaying wood. She attempted to seduce Thorin into joining her mycelium. Thorin declined. Grygum identified her from *The Platinum Chronicle: Discourses on the Abyssal Blight*.
2. **Neverlight Grove:** Her influence drove the grove to madness through Phylo's faction. Her chaotic, evil symphony was heard through Yrberop's singing and the wedding procession. Grygum witnessed a vision of her inside a great tower — a massive humanoid figure made entirely of fungi and mold, three times the height of her fungal bridesmaids, being dressed in lichen veils and a mycelium bridal train. Xinaya confirmed she is physically present in the Underdark.
3. **Festering Fissure (Blingdenstone):** After the party harvested lichen, her voice echoed in their minds — she was speaking to the Pudding King about using oozes to "soften the shell" so her garden could "take root in the flesh beneath." Fungi in the fissure had grown into disturbing shapes resembling deep gnome children.
4. **After the Pudding King's Defeat:** Fungi throughout the cavern became more twisted and malevolent, suggesting Zuggtmoy was moving to fill the power vacuum left by Juiblex's diminished hold.

## Current Status
- **Last known:** Actively expanding her influence in the Underdark; present on the Prime Material Plane (confirmed by Xinaya)
- **Active plans:** The "wedding" with Araumycos to spread fungal dominion across the world. Collaboration with the Pudding King (now defeated) to use oozes as a preparatory tool for her corruption. Moving to claim territory previously held by Juiblex.
- **Known to the party:** Her identity, her seductive nature, her plan to marry Araumycos, her collaboration with the Pudding King, and her expanding influence in Blingdenstone.
- **Hidden:** The full extent of her physical presence and power; the current status of the Araumycos wedding plan; whether she has other agents or collaborators beyond the Pudding King.

## Relationships
- **Araumycos:** Target of her "marriage" — union would grant her world-spanning power
- **Pudding King:** Collaborator; she directed him to use oozes to prepare ground for her fungal corruption (now defeated)
- **Juiblex:** Rival demon lord; Juiblex specifically referenced consuming "the queen of the fungi's banquet" upon his rebirth
- **Phylo:** Her agent/devotee at Neverlight Grove, who led the faction worshipping her as "the Great Seeder"
- **Sarith:** His compulsion was caused by her influence
- **Myconids of Neverlight Grove:** Corrupted and driven to madness by her presence
- **Thorin:** Attempted to seduce him into her mycelium; nearly possessed him. He declined.
- **Grygum:** Identified her through scholarly knowledge; witnessed her bridal vision at Neverlight Grove
- **Xinaya:** Confirmed Zuggtmoy's physical presence in the Underdark
