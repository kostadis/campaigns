# Baedora

## Identity
- Female human, former captive of the Drow
- First encountered in a Faerzress crystal cave alongside Fuurm Coppernose; found by the party in a starving, ragged state

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: survival — she seized the opportunity to flee when demons attacked her Drow captors, prioritizing escape over any other consideration. She is pragmatic and resourceful enough to run when the odds shifted. Nervous but capable of lifting her spirits once basic needs are met.

## History with the Party
1. **Discovery in the Faerzress cave:** The party found Baedora and Fuurm Coppernose together, both dressed in rags and starving. She explained their situation — demons had attacked the Drow holding them captive, the Drow fought back, and the two escapees fled during the chaos.
2. **Invited to join the party:** Daz extended an invitation for her to travel with the group, which she accepted.
3. **Post-rescue:** After eating and resting, she was seen chatting nervously with Fuurm, in noticeably better spirits.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Traveling with the party
- **Condition:** Fed and recovering; still shaken but improving
- **Known to the party:** Former Drow captive who escaped during a demon attack; no further backstory has been revealed
- **Hidden:** Her life before captivity, how long she was held, and any skills or background she may possess remain unknown

## Relationships
- **Fuurm Coppernose:** Fellow escapee and apparent companion; the two share a bond forged through shared captivity and flight. They stick close together and converse regularly.
- **Daz:** Invited her into the party; likely feels some gratitude toward him.
- **The Party (general):** A recent addition; relationship is nascent.
