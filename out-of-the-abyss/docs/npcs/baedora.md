# Baedora

## Identity
- Female human, former captive of the Drow
- First encountered in a Faerzress crystal cave alongside Fuurm Coppernose; found by the party while both were starving and dressed in rags

## Personality & Motivations
- A survivor driven by self-preservation — she and Fuurm fled when demons attacked their Drow captors, reasoning they were safer away from both sides. She appears cautious and nervous, but capable of making quick, pragmatic decisions under pressure.

## History with the Party
- **Faerzress Crystal Cave:** Discovered alongside Fuurm Coppernose, both in terrible condition — starving and in rags. She explained the circumstances of their escape from Drow captivity during a demon attack. Daz invited her to join the party. After being fed, her spirits lifted somewhat, and she was seen chatting nervously with Fuurm.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Traveling with the party
- **Condition:** Recently fed and in improved but still fragile spirits; recovering from captivity and starvation
- **Hidden information:** None indicated beyond what she has shared

## Relationships
- **Fuurm Coppernose:** Fellow escapee and apparent companion; the two share a bond forged through their shared captivity and flight. They were seen talking together after joining the party.
- **Daz:** Extended the invitation for Baedora to join the group.
- **The Drow (former captors):** Escaped during a demon attack on the Drow; no loyalty or connection remains.
