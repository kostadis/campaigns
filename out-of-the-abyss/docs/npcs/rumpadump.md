# Rumpadump

## Identity
- Myconid sprout, member of Stool's circle
- First spotted by Zalthir looking upset and not dancing among other myconids; Stool identified him by description and became frantic to rescue him

## Personality & Motivations
- A young myconid whose primary desire is to return home to Neverlight Grove. He is sociable and bonds easily with fellow myconids, particularly Stool. His joyful reaction upon arriving at Neverlight Grove — running in and shouting "We're home!" — suggests a deep attachment to his community.

## History with the Party
1. **Initial Discovery:** Zalthir spotted Rumpadump among a group of myconids, notably upset and not dancing like the others. Stool recognized him from the description and urged the party to rescue him, believing Rumpadump could explain the strange dancing behavior.
2. **Traveling Companion:** After being rescued, Rumpadump traveled with the party. He was observed chatting with Stool as the group left the Whorlestone Caves and passed through the Derro slums.
3. **Arrival at Neverlight Grove:** Rumpadump ran into Neverlight Grove alongside Stool with visible delight, shouting "We're home!" Basidia assured the party that Rumpadump is happy among his kind.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Neverlight Grove, reunited with his myconid community
- Basidia confirmed he is happy there
- Grygum expressed concern about the safety of both sprouts (Rumpadump and Stool) and suggested they continue traveling with the party, but it appears Rumpadump remained at the Grove

## Relationships
- **Stool:** Close bond; fellow sprout from the same circle. They traveled together and were frequently seen chatting. Arrived home together with shared joy.
- **Basidia:** Elder/authority figure at Neverlight Grove who vouched for Rumpadump's happiness.
- **Grygum:** Showed protective concern for Rumpadump's safety, wanting him to stay with the party.
- **The Party:** Rescued him at Stool's urging; he traveled with them as a companion until reaching Neverlight Grove.
