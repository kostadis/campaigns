# Chief Dorbo

## Identity
- Leader of the mining coalition in Blingdenstone
- Based in Diggermattock Hall
- First encountered during a heated discussion with Senni about the settlement's future

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: restart and protect Blingdenstone's mining operations and housing construction
- Pragmatic and territorial — was annoyed when Thorin intruded into his private kitchen quarters, directing the party to eat at the Foaming Bug inn instead. Shows an obsession with money that the party found "unhinged" given the existential threats facing the settlement. Chipgrin suggested this greed may be the result of demonic corruption bleeding through the Pudding King and affecting the community's leadership.

## History with the Party
1. **Diggermattock Hall (first meeting):** Argued with Senni about settlement priorities. Explained the ooze infestations hindering mining and construction. Insisted the party deal with the ooze threat before he could help them with their own concerns. Revealed suspicions that the Gold Whisker clan are wererats and refused to cooperate with them.
2. **Follow-up meeting:** Agreed to support Daz's plan. Snapped at Thorin for entering his private kitchen. At the grand moot, proposed using the Temple of the Steadfast Stone as a staging area to keep oozes away from population centers. Offered a **Stone of Controlling Earth Elementals** as reward for dealing with Ogremoch's Bane and cleansing the temple.
3. **Elemental slavery debate:** Present when the party argued against enslaving Earth Elementals for labor, comparing it to Drow slavery. Witnessed religious leaders side with the party's position.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Diggermattock Hall, Blingdenstone
- **Active plans:** Coordinating the assault on the ooze threat using the Temple of the Steadfast Stone as a staging area; reward offer (Stone of Controlling Earth Elementals) still in play
- **Hidden from party:** The extent to which demonic corruption may be influencing his judgment and amplifying his greed/aggression

## Relationships
- **Senni:** Contentious dynamic — she confronted him about his failure to address the ooze problem; they disagree on cooperating with the Gold Whisker clan
- **Gold Whisker clan:** Openly hostile; suspects them of being wererats and refuses to work with them
- **Chipgrin:** Chipgrin views Dorbo's behavior as evidence of the Pudding King's corrupting influence on Blingdenstone's leadership
- **Thorin:** Minor friction after Thorin intruded into his private quarters
- **The Party (general):** Transactional relationship — willing to reward them for solving Blingdenstone's problems

## Arc Score Events
- Dorbo's obsession with money flagged as potential sign of **demonic corruption** (Pudding King's influence amplifying greed and aggression in community leaders) — direction: negative/escalating threat.
