# Fuurm Coppernose ("Copper")

## Identity
- **Role:** Civilian / rescued captive — no adventuring background; self-described farmer
- **Faction:** None
- **First Appearance:** Day 9, 1st Tenday of Myrkhul — discovered by the party in a Faerzress crystal cave in the Underdark

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Drive:** Finding his missing sister, who was the reason he entered the Underdark in the first place
- Defensive and frightened on first contact, but stabilizes quickly once shown kindness. Nervous in social situations but willing to engage. His survival against all odds suggests more resilience than his "just a farmer" self-assessment implies.

## History with the Party
- **Day 9, 1st Tenday of Myrkhul:** Found starving and in rags inside a Faerzress crystal cave, wielding a crystal shard as a improvised weapon. Challenged the party aggressively before standing down. Explained he had been captured by Drow while searching for his sister. Daz, impressed by his sheer survival, invited him to join the group. After being fed, his spirits lifted and he was seen chatting nervously with Baedora.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Traveling with the party following rescue from the crystal cave
- **Active Situation:** Recovering from captivity; no stated plan beyond finding his sister
- **What the Party Knows:** Lost his sister, went into the Underdark to find her, was captured by Drow, is a farmer with no combat skills
- **What Remains Hidden:** Fate and whereabouts of his sister; how he was originally captured; how long he survived alone

## Relationships
- **Daz:** Daz vouched for him and invited him into the group — Copper owes his rescue largely to Daz's initiative
- **Baedora:** Engaged in nervous conversation with her after being fed; earliest sign of him warming to the group
- **The Party (general):** Newly joined, still finding his footing; grateful but clearly out of his element
