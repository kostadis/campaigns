# Fuurm Coppernose (Copper)

## Identity
- Male human farmer with no adventuring skills
- First encountered by the party on the 9th day of the 1st tenday of Myrkhul, hiding in a Faerzress crystal cave in the Underdark

## Personality & Motivations
- Core drive: Finding his lost sister, which led him into the Underdark in the first place
- Frightened and defensive when first encountered — shouted at the party to back off while brandishing a crystal as a makeshift weapon. Nervous but capable of warming up to people once shown kindness; his spirits lifted after being fed and he began chatting with Baedora.

## History with the Party
1. **Discovery in the Crystal Cave:** The party found Copper in a Faerzress crystal cave, dressed in rags, starving, and armed only with a crystal. He was initially hostile out of fear.
2. **Shared His Story:** Explained he had gone looking for his sister in the Underdark and was captured by the Drow. Daz expressed astonishment that a farmer with no adventuring skills had survived and invited him to join the group.
3. **Joined the Party:** After being fed, he was seen chatting nervously with Baedora, his morale somewhat improved.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Traveling with the party in the Underdark
- **Condition:** Recently rescued from starvation; still shaken but in better spirits
- **What the party knows:** He is a farmer searching for his lost sister; he was captured by the Drow and escaped or was separated from them
- **What remains hidden:** The fate and whereabouts of his sister; details of his capture and time with the Drow

## Relationships
- **Daz:** Impressed by Copper's survival; personally invited him to join the group
- **Baedora:** Copper was seen chatting nervously with her; appears to be a social anchor for him
- **The Drow:** Former captors — circumstances of his capture and escape are not fully detailed
