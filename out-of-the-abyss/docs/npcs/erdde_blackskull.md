# Erdde Blackskull

## Identity
- **Captain of the Stone Guards** (Duergar city guard/military)
- Met through Grygum, who she summoned for an investigative assignment

## Personality & Motivations
- Projects an aura of **paranoia** — guarded even in dealings with her own operatives
- Tries to appear nonchalant and dismissive about threats, but clearly more affected than she lets on — two victims were her own clan members, including her uncle
- Pragmatic and skeptical; refuses to believe in supernatural explanations, preferring political ones (government agency using legends as cover)
- Willing to leverage threats (setting the Drow on the party) to maintain control

## History with the Party
- **Provided passes** to the party granting them access through gates that normally restrict non-Duergar movement in the city
- **Threatened** the party that she could set the Drow on them if needed
- **Assigned Grygum** to investigate the "empty scabbard killers" — mysterious assassins whose victims bear puzzling wounds; her subordinate **Grimholl Forgebrand** was brought in to brief him further
- Revealed that **two of her own clan members** (including her uncle) were among the victims, though she insists the killings are the work of a government agency hiding behind an old legend

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Her post within the Duergar city
- **Active investigations:** Hunting the "empty scabbard killers"; also **searching for Droki**, the same individual the **Keepers of the Flame** have identified as a **Gray Ghosts agent**
- **What the party knows:** She's given them city access, wants the empty scabbard killers found, and is looking for Droki
- **Hidden:** The depth of her personal stake (clan losses) may run deeper than she's shown; her true beliefs about the killers' nature may differ from what she states publicly

## Relationships
- **Grygum** — treats him as an operative/investigator she can task; seemed disappointed during their meeting
- **Grimholl Forgebrand** — her subordinate, brought in to share intelligence on the empty scabbard killers
- **The Party** — transactional relationship; provided access passes but holds leverage (Drow threat)
- **Keepers of the Flame** — parallel interest in Droki, though the nature of any direct relationship is unclear
- **Gray Ghosts** — adversarial, at least regarding Droki's activities
