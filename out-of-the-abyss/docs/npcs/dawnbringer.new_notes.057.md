# New notes for Dawnbringer (from dossier_extract_057.md)

Dawnbringer was present in the Refectory during dinner, housed in her scabbard against the wall within Thorin's reach — described as close but not drawn, which had become its own ongoing negotiation. During the Chapter House meeting, when Thorin and others were making the case for why the party should be trusted with the investigation, Dawnbringer interjected from her scabbard to note that the party must surely be the greatest discoverers of all, having found her tomb after two thousand years. Thorin clarified that he had not personally been present for that discovery.
