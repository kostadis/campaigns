# Topsy and Turvy

## Identity
- Deep gnome twins; fellow escapees from captivity
- Part of the group that fled to Ghohlbrorn's Lair

## Personality & Motivations
- Little is known about their personality or drives from observed interactions. They have kept a low profile among the escapees.

## History with the Party
- Escaped alongside the party and other NPCs.
- Remained at Ghohlbrorn's Lair while other escapees began disappearing one by one.
- Daz suspects they are wererats, which would explain why they are the only other escapees who have not gone missing — they may be the cause of the disappearances rather than potential victims.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Ghohlbrorn's Lair
- **Activity:** Still present and accounted for, unlike the other missing escapees
- **Hidden information:** Daz's wererat theory has not been confirmed or disproven. The true reason for the other escapees' disappearances remains unresolved.

## Relationships
- **Daz:** Views them with suspicion; believes they are wererats responsible for the disappearances.
- **Other escapees:** The missing NPCs may have been their victims, if Daz's theory is correct.

None recorded.
