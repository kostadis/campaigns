# Philemon

## Identity
- **Role:** Master Archivist, Candlekeep; assigned handler to Thorin
- **Faction:** Candlekeep
- **First Appearance:** Party's arrival at Candlekeep; met when Thorin and Dawnbringer were brought to his study

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Scholarly documentation of rare artifacts and sentient items; genuine care for the psychological wellbeing of sentient items and their wielders
- A careful academic who defaults to clinical detachment but is capable of genuine recalibration when corrected. He adjusts his entire approach mid-interaction when Thorin frames Dawnbringer as a friend rather than a specimen — setting down his tablet and engaging with her as a person. He is honest about his own missteps, catching himself mid-prescription when he forgets her physical limitations.

## History with the Party
- **First meeting:** Received Thorin and Dawnbringer in his study (smells of old ink and something faintly mineral). Immediately identified Dawnbringer as a late Netherese-era artifact, sun-worship origin, with approximately 3,000–4,000 years of sustained darkness exposure. Reached for a clay tablet to take notes.
- Corrected by Thorin that Dawnbringer is a friend, not a specimen. Adjusted approach, set the tablet down, expressed evident relief that the bond between Thorin and Dawnbringer was "better than he had feared" — implying prior concerns before the meeting.
- Explained the psychological dimension of Dawnbringer's condition: the deep unease caused by long darkness, disuse, or possession by someone who doesn't recognize a sentient item as a being.
- Addressed Dawnbringer directly throughout. Asked about her previous wielder and the circumstances of her recovery. When she went quiet, he let the silence hold.
- Began to prescribe polishing, caught himself when he registered she cannot be polished, and reset to an honest assessment.
- Issued a two-stage prescription: **Stage 1** — she needs to remember daylight without needing to fight; **Stage 2** — when ready, she needs to grieve. Confirmed the party is enrolled in both phases.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** His study at Candlekeep
- **Active Plans:** Overseeing the two-stage recovery process for Dawnbringer; acting as handler for Thorin during their stay
- **What the party knows:** The full prescription and Philemon's assessment of Dawnbringer's condition; that he had concerns before their meeting
- **What remains hidden:** The nature of those prior concerns; what specifically worried him about the Thorin/Dawnbringer bond before he saw it firsthand

## Relationships
- **Thorin:** Assigned handler; earned Thorin's respect by adjusting his approach when corrected
- **Dawnbringer:** Treats her as a patient and a person; the first scholar she has encountered who addresses her directly
- **Other NPCs/Factions:** No additional relationships noted
