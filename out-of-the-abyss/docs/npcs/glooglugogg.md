# Glooglugogg ("Gloog")

## Identity
- Son of Ploopploopeen, brother of Bloppblippodd
- Kuo-toa aligned with the Sea Mother faction
- First appeared in Ploopploopeen's quarters while the party was resting, startling the escapees who couldn't tell if he was awake or asleep

## Personality & Motivations
- Deeply loyal to the Sea Mother and his father's faction, but critical of his father's leadership decisions — particularly what he sees as excessive deference toward Bloppblippodd. Harbors intense resentment toward his sister, viewing her as a failure as a sibling, daughter, and former cleric who has succumbed to madness and fractured kuo-toa society. Pragmatic and somewhat suspicious of outsiders; he disapproves of his father's choice to involve the party but respects that they showed proper deference to the Sea Mother. Blunt and direct in conversation.

## History with the Party
- Appeared unexpectedly in Ploopploopeen's quarters during the party's rest period.
- Expressed disapproval of outsiders being involved but acknowledged the party's respect for the Sea Mother.
- Clarified expectations to Zalthir: the party is expected to fight, but must **not** strike first — doing so would insult the honor of Ploopploopeen and his people.
- Briefed the party on the political situation: the Deep Father's hard-core supporters are few, but broader village allegiances are uncertain, making swift action necessary while the Sea Mother faction retains majority support.
- Remarked that it was "honorable and odd" that the party didn't simply offer a tied-up derro as a sacrifice.

## Current Status
- Last seen in Ploopploopeen's quarters before sleeping
- Preparing for an imminent confrontation with the Deep Father faction
- **Party knows:** The fight is coming soon; they must not throw the first punch; village loyalty is uncertain
- **Hidden:** The full extent of the political fractures and how volatile the situation truly is remain unclear

## Relationships
- **Ploopploopeen** — Father. Loyal to him but critical of his leadership, particularly his past leniency toward Bloppblippodd.
- **Bloppblippodd** — Sister. Deep animosity; considers her a madness-touched traitor who destroyed their society.
- **Zalthir** — Direct point of contact in the party; delivered key tactical instructions to them.
- **The Party** — Grudging acceptance. Disapproves of their involvement on principle but respects their conduct toward the Sea Mother.
