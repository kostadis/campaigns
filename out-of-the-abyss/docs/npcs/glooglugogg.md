# Glooglugogg ("Gloog")

## Identity
- Kuo-toa; son of Ploopploopeen, brother of Bloppblippodd
- Appears to hold status within the Sea Mother faction of the kuo-toa village
- First appeared in Ploopploopeen's quarters while the party was resting, startling the escapees

## Personality & Motivations
- Deeply resentful of his sister Bloppblippodd, viewing her as a failed cleric, daughter, and sister who has succumbed to madness and fractured their society. Blames his father for showing her too much deference.
- Skeptical of outsiders but pragmatic — he disapproves of his father's choice to involve the party but acknowledges they showed respect to the Sea Mother.
- Direct and tactical in conversation; concerned with honor, propriety, and the political survival of the Sea Mother faction.

## History with the Party
- Appeared while the escapees rested in Ploopploopeen's quarters. Initially startled the group (they couldn't tell if he was awake or asleep).
- Voiced disapproval of involving outsiders but conceded the party had earned some measure of respect.
- Enumerated his sister's failings at length, making clear the depth of the ideological rift in the village.
- Clarified tactical expectations to Zalthir: the party is expected to help fight, but must **not** throw the first punch — doing so would insult the honor of his father and people.
- Explained the political calculus: the Deep Father's hard-core supporters are few, but broader village loyalties are uncertain, necessitating action while the Sea Mother faction still holds majority support.
- Remarked before sleeping that it was "both honorable and odd" that the party didn't simply offer up their tied-up derro captive as a sacrifice.

## Current Status
- Last seen settling down to sleep in Ploopploopeen's quarters
- Preparing alongside his father's faction for an imminent confrontation with the Deep Father cultists
- The party knows the tactical ground rules (don't strike first) and the political stakes

## Relationships
- **Ploopploopeen** — Father. Gloog is loyal but critical, believing his father has been too lenient with Bloppblippodd.
- **Bloppblippodd** — Sister. Deep personal and ideological resentment; considers her fallen to madness and responsible for tearing society apart.
- **Zalthir** — Addressed directly when clarifying combat expectations; appears to view Zalthir as the party's point of contact.
- **The Party** — Grudging respect. Disapproves of their involvement on principle but accepts they honored the Sea Mother. Found their choice not to sacrifice the derro captive puzzling but honorable.
