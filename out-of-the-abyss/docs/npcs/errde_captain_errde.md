# Captain Errde

## Identity
- Captain in the Stone Guard of Gracklstugh
- Appears to hold an intelligence-coordinating role within the city's military/guard structure
- Referenced by name but has not appeared directly to the party

## Personality & Motivations
- Operates as an authority figure who receives reports from subordinate guards and acts on intelligence. Her willingness to share information about the party's movements with the Drow suggests she prioritizes political alliances or self-interest over neutrality toward surface-world fugitives.

## History with the Party
- A lone Stone Guard stationed at the Gracklstugh docks recognized the party from an Ember Vanguard poster and declared he needed to warn "Captain Errde" immediately.
- The party did not encounter Errde directly, but her actions had downstream consequences: she likely informed the Drow that the party had passed through Gracklstugh, reigniting Drow pursuit.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Gracklstugh (presumed)
- **Active operations:** Has reportedly tipped off the Drow about the party's passage through Gracklstugh, meaning Drow pursuit is once again active
- **Known vs. hidden:** The party knows her name and that she commands the Stone Guard; they likely suspect (or can infer) she is responsible for the Drow learning their route. Her full motivations and the nature of her relationship with the Drow remain unknown.

## Relationships
- **Stone Guard:** Commands at least the dock garrison; guards report to her by name
- **Ember Vanguard:** The wanted poster that flagged the party originated from this faction, suggesting Errde acts on their intelligence
- **The Drow:** Has a working information-sharing arrangement with Drow forces — the nature of this relationship (alliance, obligation, transaction) is unclear
- **The Party:** Adversarial by default; she is actively enabling their pursuit

## Arc Score Events
- No direct arc score changes noted, but her intelligence leak to the Drow re-escalated the Drow pursuit threat for the party.
