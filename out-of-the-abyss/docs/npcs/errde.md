# Errde

## Identity
- Head of the Stone Guard in Gracklstugh
- Based at Overlake Hold (also referenced at Underlake Hold for arming the party)
- First encountered as a potential employer/ally; the party visited her at Overlake Hold to report on their investigation into the Grey Ghosts and the Council of Savants

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goal:** Prove that the Grey Ghosts and the Council of Savants are collaborating to corrupt the Deepking and destabilize the city. She also wants evidence about the Empty Scabbard Killers and proof that assassins targeted her clan/family.
- Deeply paranoid and conviction-driven; she immediately seized on the Flumph's psychic testimony as proof of Grey Ghost–Council collaboration and had to be talked down by Daz. She is pretending not to believe assassins targeted her clan, but privately craves proof. Daz privately assessed her as blinded by her fixation on the Council, noting she and the Keepers of the Flame both want the destruction of the Derro — scapegoating a helpless group for everything broken in the city.

## History with the Party
1. **Initial contact:** Established as a potential employer willing to provide weapons and assign investigative missions.
2. **Overlake Hold briefing:** Party reported findings from their Grey Ghost/Council investigation. She demanded they return to gather concrete evidence (papers they failed to retrieve). Refused to send Duergar troops into the Derro slums but offered to arm the party's crew instead. Pressured them on a timeline.
3. **Cover plan:** Approved Daz's plan to have the party visibly interact with Clan Ironhead and Clan Xardelva so their entry into the Whorlestone Caves would be attributed to those clans rather than to her.
4. **Intel shared:** Provided intelligence on inter-clan politics — Clan Ironhead cannot take weapons orders directly and must accept Clan Steelshadow surplus; Clan Xardelva fears absorption by Clan Steelshadow; both clans are seen as opposing the Deepking.
5. **Armed the crew:** Provided weapons to the party's companions (Jimjar, Spiderbait, Sarith, Eldeth, and others including Flumph and Stool).
6. **Name used as leverage:** Grygum invoked Errde's name to intimidate Grogluk at a checkpoint — the mention visibly made Grogluk sweat.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Overlake Hold
- **Active operations:** Awaiting concrete evidence (papers) from the party proving Council of Savants and Grey Ghost collaboration; also seeking proof regarding the Empty Scabbard Killers and assassination of her family
- **Key constraint:** Cannot send Stone Guard forces into the Whorlestone Caves without triggering a clan war that would expose her incompetence as city guardian
- **Hidden from party:** Daz suspects her anti-Council crusade is partly scapegoating; she is concealing the depth of her personal vendetta (assassins targeting her family)

## Relationships
- **The Party (esp. Daz):** Employer and uneasy ally. Daz serves as the primary negotiator and privately views her as a paranoid leader. He planned further negotiations with her.
- **Grygum:** Asked her for reinforcements (denied); uses her name as authority.
- **Zalthir:** Aware of Errde's personal desire for proof about her family's assassins.
- **Council of Savants:** Primary antagonists in her worldview; she believes they are corrupting the Deepking.
- **Grey Ghosts:** Believes they are collaborating with the Council.
- **Keepers of the Flame:** Aligned interest — both want the Derro destroyed.
- **Clan Steelshadow (Deepking's clan):** Ambiguous; she serves the city but her paranoia about the Deepking's corruption complicates loyalty.
- **Clan Ironhead & Clan Xardelva:** Used as cover for the party's operations; she shared intel about their political vulnerabilities.
- **Empty Scabbard Killers:** Wants evidence about them; possibly connected to the assassination of her family.
- **Droki:** Believes he is helping the Council of Savants; wants proof of his involvement.

## Arc Score Events
- Daz talking her down from premature conclusions about Grey Ghost–Council collaboration (potential trust/influence shift).
- Party accepting her mission and arming up at her expense (alliance deepening).
- Party's plan to provide cover via other clans (strategic trust increase).
- No concrete evidence delivered yet — timeline pressure suggests potential decrease if the party delays.
