# Shuushar the Awakened

## Identity
- Kuo-Toa, fellow prisoner at Velkynvelve
- Claims to have "found enlightenment" and discovered a way to resist the madness common among his people
- First encountered as one of the captives in the drow prison at Velkynvelve

## Personality & Motivations
- Speaks in a soothing manner; presents himself as calm and spiritually aware. His core claim is that he has transcended the inherent madness of the Kuo-Toa. He seeks to return to his home settlement of Sloobludop near the Darklake.

## History with the Party
- **Velkynvelve:** Met the party as a fellow prisoner. Offered to guide the group to Sloobludop, citing extensive knowledge of the Underdark's winding roads. His offer was received with cautious interest, particularly from Daz, though trust has not been fully established.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Prisoner at Velkynvelve
- **Active plans:** Wants to travel to Sloobludop on the Darklake; has offered his services as a guide through the Underdark
- **Known vs. hidden:** The party knows his stated goal and his claim of enlightenment. Whether his enlightenment is genuine, and whether he can truly be relied upon, remains uncertain.

## Relationships
- **Daz:** Cautiously interested in Shuushar's offer to guide the group, but uncertain about trusting a Kuo-Toa.
- **Sloobludop:** His home settlement; destination he wishes to reach.
