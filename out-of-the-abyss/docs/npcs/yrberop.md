# Yrberop

## Identity
- Myconid circle leader
- Follower of Phylo and devotee of the Great Seeder (Zuggtmoy)
- Encountered by the party in the Myconid settlement

## Personality & Motivations
- Fanatically devoted to the Great Seeder and Phylo's plan. Speaks in a sing-songy voice and dances in ecstasy to a chaotic, evil symphony. Enthusiastically proselytizes to newly sprouted Myconids about the Great Seeder's "wedding" to the Great Body, promising joy to all. Blissfully unaware — or unconcerned — that the movement is effectively a death cult.

## History with the Party
- The party encountered Yrberop dancing ecstatically and singing about Phylo's great plan and the coming of the Great Seeder.
- Yrberop asked the party if they had heard of Gasbide's plan to send Myconids to the "Overbright" (the surface) to share their stories.
- Yrberop was observed singing to newly sprouted Myconids about the Great Seeder's "wedding" to the Great Body, telling them every Myconid was invited.
- Thorin recognized the melody Yrberop was dancing to as the same chaotic, evil symphony from his dream of Zuggtmoy — a piece with no rhythm or beauty.
- Thorin concluded that Yrberop and this movement constitute a death cult.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The Myconid settlement, actively preaching and dancing among newly sprouted Myconids.
- **Active plans:** Spreading devotion to the Great Seeder's "wedding" and supporting Phylo's plan.
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows Yrberop is connected to Zuggtmoy through the music and believes this is a death cult. The full scope of the "wedding" and what it entails may not yet be fully understood.

## Relationships
- **Phylo:** Follows Phylo's great plan with zealous enthusiasm.
- **Gasbide:** Referenced Gasbide's plan to send Myconids to the Overbright; relationship unclear but aware of Gasbide's initiatives.
- **The Great Seeder (Zuggtmoy):** Object of worship and devotion.
- **Thorin:** Thorin recognized the demonic music and identified the cult for what it is; views Yrberop with suspicion and alarm.
