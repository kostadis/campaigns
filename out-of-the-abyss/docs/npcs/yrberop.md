# Yrberop

## Identity
- Myconid circle leader
- Encountered by the party while dancing in ecstasy
- Follower of Phylo's "great plan" and devotee of the Great Seeder (Zuggtmoy)

## Personality & Motivations
- Fanatically devoted to the Great Seeder and the coming "wedding" to the Great Body. Speaks in a sing-songy voice, perpetually caught up in ecstatic revelry. Evangelizes to newly sprouted Myconids, promising joy and universal invitation to the wedding. Entirely consumed by what Thorin has identified as a death cult.

## History with the Party
- The party encountered Yrberop dancing to music that Thorin recognized as the same chaotic, evil symphony from his dream of Zuggtmoy — a melody with no rhythm or beauty.
- Yrberop enthusiastically spoke about Phylo's great plan and the coming of the Great Seeder.
- Asked the party whether they had heard of Gasbide's plan to send Myconids to the "Overbright" (surface) to share their stories.
- Was observed singing to newly sprouted Myconids about the Great Seeder's "wedding" to the Great Body, telling them every Myconid was invited and promising joy to all.
- Thorin concluded that this is a death cult.

## Current Status
- Last seen among the Myconid community, dancing and proselytizing to new sprouts
- Actively spreading the Great Seeder's influence and promoting the "wedding"
- **Party knows:** Yrberop follows Phylo's plan, worships the Great Seeder, and the music is connected to Zuggtmoy. Thorin believes this is a death cult.
- **Potentially hidden:** The true nature and timeline of the "wedding," and Yrberop's awareness (or lack thereof) of the destructive consequences

## Relationships
- **Phylo:** Follows Phylo's "great plan" with fervent loyalty
- **Zuggtmoy (the Great Seeder):** Object of worship and devotion
- **Gasbide:** Referenced Gasbide's plan to send Myconids to the Overbright; relationship unclear but aware of Gasbide's activities
- **Thorin:** Thorin recognized the evil symphony and identified the movement as a death cult
- **Newly sprouted Myconids:** Actively indoctrinating them into the cult of the Great Seeder
