# Werz Saltbaron

## Identity
- Duergar merchant operating near the piers of Ghohlbrorn's Lair
- First encountered when the party found him unconscious after being attacked by two assassins on the docks

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goals are unclear beyond commercial activity and self-preservation.
- Comes across as grateful but guarded — he expressed genuine surprise and thanks when healed, but quickly turned invisible and departed, citing unspecified business. His evasiveness ("no idea why he was attacked") suggests he knows more than he lets on, especially given the Deepking's personal vendetta against him.

## History with the Party
1. **Pier Rescue:** The party found Werz unconscious on the piers after two assassins attacked him with glowing weapons (possibly psychic — no physical wounds on his body). The assassins carried a charcoal drawing of him, confirming he was a deliberate target. Grygum healed him.
2. **Grateful Departure:** Upon waking, Werz claimed ignorance about why he was targeted. When shown the drawing, he acknowledged he was specifically marked. He invited the party to the Shattered Spire the next day to reward them, then turned invisible and left.
3. **Contract Cancellation:** The party later sought out the assassin Eldgrim to get the hit on Werz cancelled. Eldgrim called Werz "small potatoes" and offered to cancel the contract and permanently brightlist him for 20 gold — or for free if the party eliminates the Demogorgon cult.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Departed the piers invisibly; invited the party to meet him at the Shattered Spire the next day.
- **Active threat:** A stolen contract from Eldgrim's desk reveals the hit was ordered under the **personal seal of the Deepking**, with a furious, ranting note: *"WERZ SALTBARON THAT SNIVELING SQUEALING WRITHING LITTLE RAT! BIND HIS FEET AND THROW HIM OFF HIS BELOVED DOCKS!"*
- **What the party knows:** Werz was specifically targeted; Eldgrim had the contract; the Deepking personally ordered it.
- **What remains hidden:** Why the Deepking wants Werz dead (the "sniveling squealing" language suggests Werz may have informed on or betrayed the Deepking in some way). Werz himself has not acknowledged any connection to the Deepking.

## Relationships
- **The Deepking:** Intensely hostile — the Deepking personally ordered Werz's assassination with extreme vitriol. The nature of their conflict is unknown.
- **Eldgrim:** Held the assassination contract on Werz; considered him insignificant ("small potatoes"). Contract cancellation is contingent on the party dealing with the Demogorgon cult.
- **The Party:** Grateful for the rescue; owes them a reward at the Shattered Spire. The party has gone beyond the initial save by actively working to cancel the hit on his life — though Werz may not yet know this.

## Arc Score Events
- No explicit arc score changes noted, but the party's rescue of Werz and subsequent effort to cancel his assassination contract represent significant positive investment in his survival.
