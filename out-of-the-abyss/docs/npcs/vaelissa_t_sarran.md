# Vaelissa T'sarran

## Identity
- Matron Mother of House T'sarran, a drow noble house of Menzoberranzan
- Iron traditionalist; runs her house on ceremonial protocol and house-honor doctrine
- Has not appeared directly; referenced only by her subordinates during combat at Velkynvelve and by the surviving warrior's report back to her afterward

## Personality & Motivations
- **Archetype**: the traditionalist matron. Protocol is doctrine; deviation from house-colors etiquette, ritual greetings, seating order, or matron-deference forms is evidence of either disrespect or non-drow origin. She trusts ceremony over testimony — what a drow *performs* in her presence tells her more than what they say.
- **Temperament**: composed, unhurried. Her surviving warrior delivered her promise of retribution without heat because that is the house's register. She does not threaten; she states outcomes.
- **Twin motives toward the party** (both active, reinforcing):
  1. **Revenge for Velkynvelve.** House T'sarran sent a kill team on contract, expected an easy kill, lost a mage and most of a warrior squad to Daz's Fireball and Zalthir's shadow-step. The loss is a house-honor wound that protocol requires her to answer.
  2. **The extraction question.** Her surviving warrior witnessed Nym Duskryn hit Jorlan with a lightning bolt mid-battle — i.e., a rival house's mercenaries were already present on a separate contract to *extract Daz alive*. Vaelissa does not know who paid the Duskryns, and that gap is intolerable. She wants Daz in her presence to answer both questions: *who paid for you?* and *why are you worth extracting?*
- She treats both motives as one problem: the drow the Duskryns rescued is the drow whose fireball killed her house's force. Identifying him resolves the revenge and the contract mystery simultaneously.

## Method: the Invitation Gauntlet
Vaelissa's scrutiny vector is ceremonial, not investigative. When Daz surfaces in Menzoberranzan, House T'sarran issues formal invitations to "the new Master" for House functions:
- ritual greeting ceremonies (which words, which order, which posture)
- seated dinners with house-colors protocol (which chair, which side, which goblet)
- memory-of-alliance conversations ("your grandmother's cousin served mine during the Third Silence — you recall the accord, of course?")
- liturgy-adjacent pleasantries that only a Master-caste drow raised in the rites would have by muscle memory
Each invitation is a test. Each failure is logged. Declining is also a signal — a Master does not decline a matron's invitation without a ritually acceptable cause, and the cause is itself inspected. The probe is repeatable, granular, and compounds.

## History with the Party
- **Velkynvelve encounter (Fungal Cavern / Ilvara death scene):** House T'sarran sent soldiers and a mage under contract, expecting an easy kill. The party destroyed most of the force. The mage's dying line: "The matron mother of House T'sarran will hear about this." The last surviving warrior retreated without urgency: "We will meet again. Enjoy your victory for the moment."
- **Grygum's reaction:** noted that a threat delivered without heat is always more credible than one made in anger, and resolved to identify the matron mother before she identifies the party.
- **Witness to the Duskryn extraction:** the surviving warrior also observed Nym Duskryn's intervention in the cavern. That observation is the seed of Vaelissa's second motive.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** House T'sarran's estate in Menzoberranzan.
- **Active plans:** briefing from the surviving warrior has been delivered. Vaelissa is constructing the invitation gauntlet for the moment Daz arrives and surfaces in noble society. She does not yet have Daz's current location — she is waiting for him to come to her.
- **Party knowledge vs. hidden:** the party knows House T'sarran exists and has a matron mother, and knows one witness reported back. They do not know her name, the shape of her probe, or that she already has a personal read on Daz (via the Duskryn-extraction observation). She does not yet know Daz's name, his DeVir connection, or who contracted the Duskryns — these are all things she is *probing for*.

## Relationships
- **House T'sarran forces:** commands the remaining soldiers and the rebuilt mage line. The Velkynvelve dead require replacement, which itself is a house-colors ceremony she will conduct publicly.
- **Unknown Velkynvelve contractor:** hired T'sarran for the original kill. Their identity is unknown to Vaelissa; she treats them as a second-tier problem to be resolved after the party is identified.
- **House Duskryn (Nym and Kaelira's house):** rival. The Duskryns were the extraction force her warrior witnessed. Vaelissa has open protocol grievance against House Duskryn for mercenary interference in a T'sarran contracted action, and will pursue that grievance through formal channels — which is itself a Masquerade pressure point on the party (House Duskryn's questioning of the extraction will cross-reference Vaelissa's probes).
- **Grygum:** views her as a priority intelligence target; wants to identify her before she identifies the party.
- **The party broadly:** no direct contact yet. The implicit enmity from Velkynvelve is compounded by the extraction-contract question.

## Role in Menzoberranzan
- **Risk lever holder:** Social Masquerade Collapse (see `docs/risk_levers.md`). The invitation gauntlet is the mechanical vector by which the Masquerade degrades.
- **Does not directly hold Heat.** She operates through her own house's channels, not Orthodoxy enforcement. A Vaelissa probe does not raise Heat on its own — but a *failed* probe produces a report she can hand to the Orthodoxy if she chooses, which can accelerate the Heat tier transition.
- **Neutralization is a political earthquake.** Killing or dishonoring a sitting matron mother is not a Masquerade mitigation — it is a city-shaking event that cascades into other levers. See mitigation path in the Masquerade lever for the cheaper options (cover identity swap, noble-district retreat).
