# Shuushar the Awakened

## Identity
- Kuo-toan pacifist and self-styled philosopher
- Fellow prisoner / traveling companion of the party
- First appeared as part of the group traveling toward Sloobludop

## Personality & Motivations
- Deeply committed to non-violence and diplomacy; visibly distressed by unnecessary conflict. Advocates for hearing all perspectives before making judgments. Has a tendency to lecture and drone on about his philosophical views, sometimes to the point where others tune him out. Eager and excitable when reconnecting with his own people.

## History with the Party
- **Ambush battle near Sloobludop:** After the party fought kuo-toan ambushers, Shuushar provided tactical intelligence about kuo-toan monitors (special leaders who handle insanity breaks; defeating them demoralizes other kuo-toans). Lamented the fight as unnecessary.
- **Approach to Sloobludop:** Affirmed the group should continue when Sethir asked. Became very excited upon encountering a kuo-toan party about an hour from the settlement and introduced the group to **Ploopploopeen**, archpriest of the Sea Mother.
- **Meeting with Ploopploopeen:** Followed her in the surreal school-of-fish formation. Ploopploopeen cast a pointed glance at him when mentioning "visionaries" who caused trouble, suggesting Shuushar has a reputation as a troublemaker among his people.
- **Debate over killing Ploopploopeen's party:** Argued passionately against violence, insisting the Sea Mother is an old god and that the **Deep Father** is the one who demands humanoid sacrifices. Urged the party to speak with Ploopploopeen's daughter **Bloppblippodd** before acting. Droned on philosophically and eventually wandered off.

## Current Status
- **Location:** With the party, in or near Sloobludop
- **Activity:** Serving as cultural guide and intermediary with kuo-toan factions
- **Hidden information:** His exact history as a "visionary" troublemaker among the kuo-toans has not been fully explained; the party only knows Ploopploopeen views him with some suspicion

## Relationships
- **Ploopploopeen (Archpriest of the Sea Mother):** Known to each other; she regards him warily as a past troublemaker
- **Bloppblippodd (Ploopploopeen's daughter):** Shuushar recommended the party speak with her; implies some trust or familiarity
- **Sethir (Party member):** Responds to Sethir's questions; defers to the group's travel decisions
- **The Party (general):** Valued for his knowledge of kuo-toan culture and politics; tolerated despite his long-winded pacifist philosophizing
