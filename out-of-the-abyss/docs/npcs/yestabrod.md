# Yestabrod

## Identity
- Deceased NPC; previously encountered and killed by the party
- His corpse was repurposed by fungal forces as a puppet in a ritualistic ceremony

## Personality & Motivations
- No longer applicable — Yestabrod is dead. Any "actions" attributed to him post-mortem are the result of external manipulation of his corpse.

## History with the Party
1. **Killed by the party** at some prior point (details of the original encounter not provided in these notes).
2. **Post-death:** His corpse was carried by three spore servants that rose from a garden during a mock wedding rehearsal procession. The spore servants manipulated his body to play the role of a **priest presiding over wedding vows** between stand-ins for **Araumycos** and **Zuggtmoy**.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** His corpse was last seen being used as a puppet-priest in the mock wedding ceremony.
- His body appears to be under the control of fungal/spore forces aligned with Zuggtmoy's plans.

## Relationships
- **Zuggtmoy:** His corpse is being used in service of her ritual — a mock wedding between herself and Araumycos.
- **Araumycos:** The other party in the wedding ceremony Yestabrod's corpse was forced to "officiate."
- **Spore Servants:** Three rose from the garden specifically to carry and puppeteer his body.
- **The Party:** Killed him previously; witnessed the desecration of his corpse during the procession.
