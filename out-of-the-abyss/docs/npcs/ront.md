# Ront

## Identity
- Orc prisoner held in the Drow slave pen
- First encountered on water-carrying duty at the waterfall, assigned under Drow guard Beremil alongside Prince Derendil and Thorin

## Personality & Motivations
- A proud, aggressive melee combatant who preferred close-quarters fighting over ranged weapons. Deeply driven by a need to prove his courage and overcome feelings of cowardice. His refusal to flee from overwhelming danger ultimately defined him—and killed him.

## History with the Party
1. **Slave Pen** – Held as a prisoner alongside the party. Assigned to water-carrying duty at the waterfall with Prince Derendil and Thorin, under the Drow guard Beremil.
2. **Escape / Guard Chamber Battle** – During the breakout, Ront squeezed into the fight in the guard chamber and struck down the last remaining Drow guard, preferring melee combat over crossbows.
3. **Death by Vrock** – When an injured vrock landed on the cavern floor, Ront refused to flee with the other prisoners. Still angry at his own cowardice and desperate to prove himself, he attacked the demon. His first blow cut through its hide and drew blood, but the vrock killed him with terrifying speed—clawing him, snapping off his head with its beak, and swallowing it before flying off and dropping his headless corpse.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed by a vrock during the escape from the Drow prison. His headless body was dropped to the cavern floor.

## Relationships
- **Prince Derendil & Thorin** – Fellow prisoners on the same water-carrying work detail.
- **Beremil** – Drow guard who oversaw Ront's work duty.
- **The Party** – Fellow prisoners; Ront fought alongside them during the escape before his death.
