# Ront

## Identity
- Orc prisoner held in the Drow slave pen
- First encountered on water-carrying duty at the waterfall, working under Drow guard Beremil alongside Prince Derendil and Thorin

## Personality & Motivations
- A warrior at heart who preferred melee combat over ranged weapons. Driven by a need to prove his courage and overcome what he perceived as his own cowardice. This compulsion ultimately proved fatal—he refused to flee from a fight he could not win.

## History with the Party
1. **Slave Pen** — Encountered as a fellow prisoner, assigned to water-carrying duty at the waterfall alongside Prince Derendil and Thorin under the Drow guard Beremil.
2. **Guard Chamber Battle** — During the escape, Ront squeezed into the fight in the guard chamber and struck down the remaining Drow guard, killing him. Demonstrated his preference for direct melee combat.
3. **Death by Vrock** — When an injured vrock landed on the cavern floor, the other prisoners fled. Ront refused to run, still angry at his own cowardice and determined to prove himself. His first blow cut through the demon's hide, but the vrock killed him with devastating speed—one taloned hand killed him, the other grabbed his body, and its beak snapped off his head and swallowed it. The vrock then flew off and dropped Ront's headless body to the ground.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed by a vrock during the escape. His headless body was dropped to the cavern floor.

## Relationships
- **Prince Derendil & Thorin** — Fellow prisoners on the same water-carrying work detail.
- **Beremil** — Drow guard who oversaw his labor.
- **The Party** — Fellow prisoners; fought alongside them during the escape before his death.
