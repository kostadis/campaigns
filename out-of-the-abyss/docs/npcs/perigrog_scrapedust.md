# Perigrog Scrapedust

## Identity
- Gnome worker/demolitions specialist at the White Shell Mine, the largest salt mine in the Underdark
- First encountered by the party at the White Shell Mine when they investigated an impassable tunnel

## Personality & Motivations
- Practical and resourceful; focused on solving the immediate problem threatening the mine's operations. Eager and enthusiastic about his craft—he and his fellow gnomes were excited to deploy their specialized explosives once a solution was identified. Celebratory and good-natured when the problem was resolved.

## History with the Party
- **White Shell Mine encounter:** Perigrog described a tunnel that had become impassable—impossibly dry, with blinding crystallite walls and absolute, suffocating silence. Shadows appeared painted on the walls rather than flickering, as if time itself had stopped. He and the other gnomes presented their specialized explosives to help breach the obstruction. After Zalthir identified the solution and successfully redirected a magical blast through shadow space to shatter the wall without collapsing the surrounding tunnels, Perigrog and the gnomes celebrated the success.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** White Shell Mine
- **Status:** The tunnel obstruction has been resolved; presumably back to normal mining operations
- **Unknown:** The source or nature of the time-stopped phenomenon in the tunnel remains unexplained—whether Perigrog knows more about its origin is unclear

## Relationships
- **The gnome miners:** Works alongside them; they share expertise in specialized explosives
- **Zalthir:** Relied on Zalthir's magical knowledge to solve the tunnel problem; collaborative and respectful relationship
- **The Party:** Grateful for their assistance in clearing the impassable tunnel
