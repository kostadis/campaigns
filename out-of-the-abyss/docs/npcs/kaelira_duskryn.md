# Kaelira (Duskryn)

## Identity
Member of House Duskryn; sister of Nym Duskryn and one of the Duskryn sisters. Sent specifically to extract Daz from a dangerous situation as part of a contract. *Also known as: Kaelira.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Ensure Daz's survival, seemingly under orders from House Duskryn as part of an extraction contract
- Direct, physical, and no-nonsense in approach - grabbed Daz bodily and hauled him to safety without waiting for his agreement
- Blunt and impatient personality with little tolerance for hesitation, evidenced by her quip "Come with me if you want to live, you idiot"
- Action-oriented and willing to risk poisonous gas and insect-choked caverns to fulfill her objective

## History with the Party
- **Extraction Battle:** Appeared mid-combat alongside her sister Nym during a battle in an insect-choked cavern with poisonous gas. Physically seized Daz's arm and dragged him northeast through the danger to where Nym was waiting at a safer position. Daz did not consent to the extraction; she enforced his survival regardless
- **Accusation by Asha Vandree:** Asha Vandree challenged her presence and identified Kaelira as a potential traitor to Lolth. Daz intervened and defused the situation by claiming Kaelira was a "friend of Lolth" and vouching for her loyalty

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Northeast of the battlefield at the safer position with Nym, after successfully extracting Daz
- **Active plans:** Fulfilling the extraction contract - delivering Daz alive
- **Party knowledge vs. hidden:** The party knows she is a Duskryn operative sent for Daz as part of a contract. The details of the extraction contract (who hired them, the terms, and the ultimate destination for Daz) remain unknown. Asha Vandree's accusation of treachery against Lolth remains unresolved - whether the accusation has merit or was baseless is unclear

## Relationships
- **Nym (Duskryn):** Sister and mission partner working together on the extraction contract. Nym waited at the extraction point while Kaelira did the physical retrieval
- **Daz (Party):** Extraction target who recognized her and vouched for her, suggesting prior acquaintance or awareness of her allegiance. She treated him with rough familiarity and he protected her reputation when confronted by Asha
- **Asha Vandree:** Adversarial relationship - Asha publicly challenged her presence and accused Kaelira of being a traitor to Lolth, creating tension that Daz only superficially resolved
- **House Duskryn:** Operates on behalf of the house; extraction mission implies she answers to Duskryn leadership
- **Lolth:** Affiliated in some capacity according to Daz's identification, though the nature of this connection remains unclear
