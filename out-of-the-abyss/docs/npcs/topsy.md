# Topsy

## Identity
- Female deep gnome (svirfneblin) and lycanthrope (wererat)
- Twin sister of Turvy
- Originally from Blingdenstone
- First encountered as a fellow prisoner in the slave pen at Velkynvelve

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: escape the Underdark and return to Blingdenstone, which she claims has a route to the surface
- Resourceful and opportunistic — quick to spot chances to steal food, knives, or loot during chaos
- Somewhat prickly and dismissive; looks down on Jimjar despite sharing a hometown, and reacts to strangers (like Yukyuk) with cold confusion
- Closely bonded with her twin Turvy; the two frequently whisper together and act in concert

## History with the Party
1. **Velkynvelve (slave pen):** Introduced herself and Turvy to the party. Advocated for Blingdenstone as an escape destination. Disparaged Jimjar as "always pretending he's better than us."
2. **Kitchen assignments:** Worked with Turvy and Suushar to propose a distraction scheme; Gyrgum's theatrical cooking allowed the twins to steal food (Day 1) and several knives (Day 2).
3. **The escape:** Armed herself with a hand crossbow from the armory and helped kill a Drow guard alongside Eldeth and Ront.
4. **Post-escape cavern:** Offered (with Turvy) to retrieve the party's gear for a price. When declined, both twins transformed into rats — revealing their lycanthropic nature. Later transformed back into gnomes. Trapped under rocks after a cave-in.
5. **Giant centipede encounter:** Participated in a crossbow volley with Sarith, Buppido, and Turvy. Thorin ordered the twins to save Eldeth and Jimjar.
6. **Corpse disposal debate:** Sided with Jimjar, arguing dead bodies left behind would attract monsters and slow the Drow pursuit.
7. **Silken Paths:** Stared blankly at Yukyuk when he tried to chat about web-surfing.
8. **Kuo-toa settlement:** Recognized the kuo-toan factional battle as a prime opportunity to steal. Fled with the group toward the docks during the chaos.
9. **Demogorgon's appearance:** Screamed and wailed in terror; had to be physically carried to the boat by Grygum.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** On the boat fleeing the Darklake after Demogorgon's emergence
- **Condition:** Alive but badly shaken by Demogorgon's appearance
- **Hidden information:** The party may or may not be fully aware that she and Turvy are wererats. Daz noted it in his summary, and the party witnessed the rat transformation, but the implications (infection risk, full moon behavior) may not be understood. The twins have been observed whispering privately at night — their private conversations remain unknown.

## Relationships
- **Turvy (twin brother):** Inseparable; they whisper together, scheme together, and act as a unit in nearly every situation
- **Jimjar:** Fellow Blingdenstone native; Topsy is dismissive of him, though they aligned on the corpse disposal debate
- **Grygum:** Cooperative working relationship — he provided distractions for their thefts; he carried her to the boat during the Demogorgon panic
- **Yukyuk:** Cold and uninterested in his attempts at socializing
- **Daz:** Daz has observed the twins' private behavior and noted their lycanthropy
- **Eldeth & Ront:** Fought alongside them during the escape
