# Topsy

## Identity
- Female deep gnome (svirfneblin) and lycanthrope (wererat)
- Twin sister of Turvy
- Originally from Blingdenstone
- First appeared as a fellow prisoner in the slave pen at Velkynvelve

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: escape the Underdark and return to Blingdenstone, which she claims has a route to the surface
- Cunning, opportunistic, and practical — always looking for an angle to steal or gain advantage. Works seamlessly with her twin brother in coordinated schemes. Dismissive of Jimjar despite sharing a hometown, accusing him of thinking he's better than them. Socially cold toward strangers (stared blankly at Yukyuk's attempts at conversation).

## History with the Party
1. **Velkynvelve (Slave Pen):** Introduced herself and Turvy to the party. Advocated escaping to Blingdenstone. Made dismissive remarks about Jimjar.
2. **Kitchen Assignments:** Day 1 — the twins proposed (via Suushar) that Gyrgum create a distraction; successfully stole food. Day 2 — winked at Gyrgum and stole several knives during his theatrical cooking performance.
3. **The Escape:** Armed herself with a hand crossbow from the armory and helped kill a Drow guard alongside Eldeth and Ront.
4. **Descent from Velkynvelve:** Offered to retrieve the party's gear for a price. When declined, she and Turvy transformed into rats (revealing their lycanthropy). Later transformed back into gnomes on the cavern floor. Was trapped under rocks during a cave-in.
5. **Travel Encounters:** Participated in a crossbow volley against a giant centipede creature. Sided with Jimjar in the corpse disposal debate, arguing dead bodies would slow Drow pursuers.
6. **Silken Paths:** Ignored Yukyuk's attempts at conversation.
7. **Kuo-toa Settlement:** Recognized the factional battle as a prime opportunity to steal. Fled with the group toward the docks during the chaos.
8. **Demogorgon's Appearance:** Screamed and wailed in terror; had to be physically carried to the boat by Gyrgum.
9. **Ghohlbrorn's Lair:** Escaped captivity alongside the party and other NPCs. She and Turvy are the only other escapees besides the party who have not gone missing from Ghohlbrorn's Lair.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Ghohlbrorn's Lair
- **Active plans:** Still wants to reach Blingdenstone
- **Active concerns:** Daz suspects the reason Topsy and Turvy haven't disappeared like the other escapees is that they are **wererats**. This suspicion has not been confirmed by the broader party.
- **Known vs. hidden:** The party knows she and Turvy are lycanthropes (wererats). Daz has noted the twins whispering privately at night — their private conversations remain unknown to the group.

## Relationships
- **Turvy** — Twin brother; inseparable. They coordinate schemes together, whisper privately, and act as a unit in nearly all situations.
- **Gyrgum** — Cooperative working relationship; he provides distractions while she and Turvy steal. He carried her to safety during the Demogorgon encounter.
- **Jimjar** — Fellow Blingdenstone native. Topsy is dismissive of him, though they aligned on the corpse disposal debate.
- **Eldeth** — Fought alongside her during the escape from Velkynvelve.
- **Yukyuk** — No rapport; she showed zero interest in his attempts to socialize.
- **Daz** — He has been observing the twins' private behavior with suspicion and suspects them of being wererats.
- **Other escapees** — The rest of the escapee group has gone missing from Ghohlbrorn's Lair; Topsy and Turvy are the sole exceptions.
