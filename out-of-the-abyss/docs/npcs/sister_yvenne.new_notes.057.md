# New notes for Sister Yvenne (from dossier_extract_057.md)

Sister Yvenne does not appear in this chunk but is referenced by Vareth in his account of the theological controversy at Candlekeep. According to Vareth, she has threatened to confiscate everyone's bookplates in response to the escalating conflict between the Stroudite, Bahamutian, and Draconic schools — including three duels fought in the library over which polemic shelf gets the larger window. As of Vareth's account, she had not yet followed through on the threat, but he expected she would.
