# Grygum

## Identity
- **Role:** Cleric (cult-affiliated, later identified as cleric of Bahamut)
- **Faction:** Unclear cult affiliation (references "the lessons of his cult"); recognized and welcomed by Galeb Duhr as aligned with draconic/stone realms
- **First Appearance:** Joined the party as a fellow prisoner, arming himself from the prison armory before following the group

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Survival and pragmatic service to his companions; advancement of his cult's teachings
- **Traits:** Tactically minded and resourceful — he takes notes on NPC statements, bets on party members' antics, and engineers traps with satisfaction. Has a mischievous streak and a dry wit ("Come get it, idiot"). Calm under pressure; willing to punch an ally in the face if it gets the job done.

## History with the Party
1. **Prison Escape:** Armed himself at the armory. Healed Thorin from critical wounds with a magical touch. Attempted *Toll the Dead* on Imbros (resisted). Telepathically calmed the grief-maddened Serith after Prince Derendil's death, crediting "the lessons of his cult."
2. **Fargas Bargain:** Cross-referenced his own written notes to pressure Fargas into honoring his deal. Assessed Fargas as truthful. Bet Jimjar that the gnome couldn't fool the Spectator.
3. **Spectator Fight:** Complained about lacking better ranged spells; resorted to his hand crossbow.
4. **Blingdenstone & Galeb Duhr:** Welcomed by a Galeb Duhr as entering "the Realm of the Dragon" — he grinned and played along before the Galeb corrected itself. Immediately identified the "Lady of Rot" reference as Zuggtmoy, alarming city leaders. Lent his light crossbow to Thorin.
5. **Festering Fissure:** Summoned a bat familiar to scout fungal cave. Led protective warding chant during lichen harvest. Punched (then stabbed) Zalthir to break a hypnotic trance — first punch was too weak to land.
6. **White Shell Mine:** Sensed the Galeb Duhr required divine authority. Provided a blessing supporting Zalthir's meditation before the explosive tunnel breach.
7. **Big Heart Fungus Battle:** Inscribed an invisible *Glyph of Warding* on Jorlan's side of the bridge. Committed the Dust of Suleiman directly onto the fungus. Baited Jorlan across the bridge to trigger the glyph (21 damage, destroyed one spore servant). Assessed the combined Dust + Fireball result with dry approval.
8. **Summoning Circle:** Present when Thorin deciphered giant runes requiring three sacrifices.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Active with the party in the Blingdenstone region
- **Active Operations:** Monitoring the summoning circle situation; spell resources partially depleted (1 known 4th-level slot treated as non-renewable)
- **Party Knows:** Cleric of Bahamut, cult background, capable healer and battlefield controller
- **Hidden:** True nature and identity of his "cult"; depth of his telepathic/mind-affecting abilities

## Relationships
- **Thorin:** Healed him from near death; lent him his light crossbow — a practical, protective bond
- **Serith:** Telepathically redirected his grief-rage; significant intimate intervention
- **Zalthir:** Punched and stabbed him to break a spell; provided a blessing before the tunnel breach — complicated but functional
- **Fargas:** Distrustful but analytical; documented Fargas's words and used them as leverage
- **Jimjar:** Playful enough to bet against him
- **Galeb Duhr:** Recognized and welcomed; appears to carry some divine authority they respond to
- **Zuggtmoy:** Awareness of prior encounter (Neverlight Grove); treats her as a serious known threat
