# Grygum

## Identity
- Cleric of Bahamut, though he references "the lessons of his cult," suggesting an unorthodox or fringe sect rather than a mainstream faith
- Fellow prisoner alongside the party; first appeared during a prison breakout sequence where he chose to equip himself at the armory before joining the fight

## Personality & Motivations
- Pragmatic and resourceful—treats spell slots as precious non-renewable resources and arms himself before heading into danger. Possesses a mischievous streak, making jokes and placing bets (e.g., betting Jimjar couldn't fool a Spectator). Intellectually sharp: takes detailed notes, holds people to their word, and assesses others' truthfulness with confidence. Not above stabbing a companion in the face to break a magical trance when a punch doesn't cut it.

## History with the Party
1. **Prison Breakout:** Equipped himself at the armory, then healed a critically wounded Thorin with magical touch. Attempted Toll the Dead against the Drow Imbros (resisted). Telepathically calmed Serith's madness after Prince Derendil's death, redirecting his rage toward spirituality.
2. **Fargas's Bargain / Spectator Encounter:** Pressured Fargas by reading back the NPC's own earlier statements. Assessed Fargas as truthful. Bet Jimjar on the Spectator bluff. Fought the Spectator with a handheld crossbow, lamenting his lack of ranged spells.
3. **Blingdenstone / Galeb Duhr Realm:** Welcomed to "the Realm of the Dragon/Rock" by Galeb Duhr; joked about being in a cavern. Identified the "Lady of Rot" as Zuggtmoy, alarming Blingdenstone's leaders. Summoned a bat familiar to scout the Festering Fissure. Punched then stabbed Zalthir to break a hypnotic pattern. Sensed the Galeb Duhr awaited divine authority and provided a blessing to aid Zalthir's meditation before the tunnel breach. Present at the summoning circle with three-sacrifice runes.
4. **Bridge Battle (Big Heart Fungus):** Lent his light crossbow to Thorin. Inscribed an invisible Glyph of Warding on the bridge. Applied Dust of Suleiman to the Big Heart Fungus. Baited Jorlan across the bridge ("Come get it, idiot"), detonating the glyph for 21 damage and destroying a spore servant. Assessed the combined Dust/Fireball effect favorably.

## Current Status
- **Last known:** Present at the bridge battle site after the successful destruction/assault on the Big Heart Fungus
- **Resources:** Down to one 4th-level spell slot (treated as non-renewable); light crossbow lent to Thorin
- **Hidden:** The exact nature of his "cult" and its teachings remain unexplained to the party

## Relationships
- **Thorin:** Healed him from critical wounds; lent him his crossbow — a trusted ally
- **Zalthir:** Blessed his meditation; stabbed him awake from hypnosis — practical camaraderie
- **Serith:** Telepathically calmed his madness — has demonstrated psychic/pastoral influence over him
- **Jimjar:** Casual betting relationship
- **Fargas:** Held him accountable to his bargain with meticulous note-keeping
- **Jorlan:** Hostile; baited him into a trap
- **Galeb Duhr:** Recognized as a figure of divine authority; welcomed warmly
- **Zuggtmoy:** Identified her as a known threat from the Neverlight Grove incident
