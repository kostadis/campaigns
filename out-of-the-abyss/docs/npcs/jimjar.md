# JimJar

## Identity
- Deep gnome (svirfneblin) NPC companion
- Traveling companion of the party; no stated faction affiliation
- Met as a fellow traveler/prisoner (exact first meeting not detailed in these notes)

## Personality & Motivations
- Compulsive gambler who constantly proposes bets to party members on mundane and consequential matters alike. Despite frequent losses, he remains undeterred and good-natured about paying up. Shows moments of genuine tactical insight beneath the gambling facade, offering shrewd observations about the party's strategic position.

## History with the Party
1. **Knot-Tying Bet:** Wagered with Grygum that the knots used to restrain Pelek/Derro wouldn't hold through the night. Lost the bet — the knots held — and paid Grygum the next morning.
2. **Post-Narrak Battle:** After the party defeated Narrak, JimJar noted the significant evidence they had collected from various factions. He recommended they keep it for future use and proposed a bet that the evidence would prove helpful. Daz considered his counsel wise.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Traveling with the party (post-Narrak battle)
- **Activity:** Continuing as a companion; offering bets and occasional strategic advice
- **Known vs. Hidden:** The party sees him as a quirky but occasionally insightful companion. No hidden agendas indicated in available notes.

## Relationships
- **Grygum:** Frequent betting partner; friendly dynamic. Grygum collected winnings from the knot bet.
- **Daz:** Respects JimJar's judgment — considered his advice about keeping evidence to be wise.
- **General Party:** Accepted member of the traveling group; tolerated and likely enjoyed for his betting antics.
