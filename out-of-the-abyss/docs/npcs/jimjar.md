# JimJar

## Identity
- Deep gnome (svirfneblin) NPC companion
- Traveling companion of the party; no stated faction affiliation
- Met as a fellow traveler/prisoner (exact first meeting not detailed in these notes)

## Personality & Motivations
- Compulsive gambler who constantly proposes bets to party members on mundane and consequential matters alike. Despite frequent losses, he remains undeterred and good-natured about paying up. Shows moments of genuine tactical insight beneath the gambling facade, offering shrewd observations about the party's strategic position.

## History with the Party
1. **Sacrificial Depression:** Was among the prisoners herded toward the sacrificial depression. As the group faced potential death, he proposed a bet on how many would survive. Grygum specifically questioned Jimjar's trustworthiness, wondering whether Jimjar would let someone die just to avoid losing a bet — this remains unresolved.
2. **Escape from the Drow:** Fled alongside the party during the escape. Complained that Zalthir's insistence on running disrupted his planned foraging bet. When Shuushar voiced concern about the Faerzress, Jimjar dismissed the worry outright, claiming Faerzress has never affected a living thing and that its only magical property is blocking teleportation spells.
3. **Knot-Tying Bet:** Wagered with Grygum that the knots used to restrain Pelek/Derro wouldn't hold through the night. Lost the bet — the knots held — and paid Grygum the next morning.
4. **Post-Narrak Battle:** After the party defeated Narrak, JimJar noted the significant evidence they had collected from various factions. He recommended they keep it for future use and proposed a bet that the evidence would prove helpful. Daz considered his counsel wise.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Traveling with the party (post-Narrak battle)
- **Activity:** Continuing as a companion; offering bets and occasional strategic advice
- **Known vs. Hidden:** The party sees him as a quirky but occasionally insightful companion. No hidden agendas indicated in available notes.

## Relationships
- **Grygum:** Frequent betting partner; friendly dynamic. Grygum collected winnings from the knot bet.
- **Daz:** Respects JimJar's judgment — considered his advice about keeping evidence to be wise.
- **Zalthir:** Mild friction — Jimjar blamed Zalthir's urgency during the escape for ruining his betting opportunity.
- **Shuushar:** Dismissive; mocked kuo-toan nature and waved off Shuushar's Faerzress concerns.
- **General Party:** Accepted member of the traveling group; tolerated and likely enjoyed for his betting antics.
