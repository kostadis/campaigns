# Eldgrim

## Identity
- Leader of the Empty Scabbard assassin's guild
- Duergar with an eye patch
- Operates from a 70-foot cave containing a large garden and a desk with papers
- First encountered when the party sought him out regarding a contract on Werz Saltbaron

## Personality & Motivations
- Arrogant and confident in the secrecy of his guild — dismisses any attempt to expose them, noting anyone who knows they exist will deny it. Susceptible to flattery; was visibly pleased when Daz claimed to be writing a book and wanted to record the guild's name. Quick to anger (furious when Zalthir burned his desk papers) but pragmatic enough to calm down when he realized the burned contracts were ones he'd memorized. Transactional in all dealings, but backs his deals with lethal threats.

## History with the Party
- The party approached Eldgrim regarding a contract on **Werz Saltbaron**.
- Eldgrim confirmed the contract, calling it "small potatoes."
- He offered to cancel the contract and add Werz to the **"brightlist"** (a permanent protection list) for 20 gold, contingent on the party eliminating the **Demogorgon cult**.
- After negotiation, he agreed to brightlist Werz **for free** if they destroy the cult.
- **Daz** tried to get proof of the guild's existence; Eldgrim arrogantly refused but was flattered by the book angle and wrote something down on a piece of paper for Daz.
- **Zalthir** accidentally burned Eldgrim's desk papers, provoking fury — though he calmed upon realizing the destroyed contracts were memorized.
- Eldgrim threatened to kill the entire party as a **"free contract"** if they fail to eliminate the Demogorgon worshippers.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** His cave headquarters
- **Active operations:** Waiting on the party to destroy the Demogorgon cult; the contract on Werz Saltbaron is suspended pending completion of this task
- **Hidden from party:** Full scope of the Empty Scabbard's operations, membership, and other active contracts remain unknown. The party knows of at least three visible soulblade guards and at least two invisible sentries posted 50 feet north of the cave entrance.

## Relationships
- **Werz Saltbaron:** Target of a contract Eldgrim considers trivial; willing to permanently protect him as payment for the party's services
- **Demogorgon Cult:** Wants them destroyed — motivation unclear but important enough to trade a contract and brightlist entry
- **Daz:** Mildly amused by; gave him a written note after being flattered
- **Zalthir:** Nearly killed over the desk fire; a source of irritation
- **The Party (general):** Views them as useful tools with a death sentence hanging over them if they fail

## Arc Score Events
- **Deal struck:** Party agreed to destroy the Demogorgon cult in exchange for Werz's brightlist protection — creates a ticking obligation with lethal consequences for failure.
