# Eldgrim

## Identity
- Leader of the Empty Scabbard assassin's guild
- Duergar with an eye patch
- Operates from a 70-foot cave containing a large garden and a desk with papers
- First encountered when the party sought him out regarding a contract on Werz Saltbaron

## Personality & Motivations
- Arrogant and self-assured; dismisses attempts to extract proof of the guild's existence, confident that "anyone who knows they exist will deny it." Susceptible to flattery — was visibly pleased when Daz claimed to be writing a book and wanted to record the guild's name. Quick to anger (furious when Zalthir burned his desk papers) but pragmatic enough to calm down when he realized the burned contracts were ones he'd memorized. Ruthless in his dealings, readily threatening death as consequence for failure.

## History with the Party
- The party approached Eldgrim regarding an active contract on **Werz Saltbaron**, which he confirmed, calling it "small potatoes."
- He offered to cancel the contract and add Werz to the **"brightlist"** (a permanent protection list) for 20 gold, contingent on the party eliminating the **Demogorgon cult**.
- After negotiation, he agreed to brightlist Werz **for free** if they destroy the cult.
- Daz attempted to get proof of the guild's existence; Eldgrim dismissed this but was flattered by the book angle and wrote something down on a piece of paper.
- **Zalthir accidentally burned Eldgrim's desk papers**, causing a tense moment — Eldgrim was furious but calmed upon realizing the destroyed contracts were memorized.
- He threatened to kill the entire party as a **"free contract"** if they fail to eliminate the Demogorgon worshippers.

## Current Status
- **Location:** His cave headquarters
- **Active deal:** Awaiting the party's destruction of the Demogorgon cult; will brightlist Werz Saltbaron upon completion
- **Threat:** Will treat the party as a free kill contract if they fail
- **Hidden info:** The party has no proof the Empty Scabbard exists; the guild's full reach and membership remain unknown

## Relationships
- **Werz Saltbaron:** Target of a current contract; Eldgrim considers it a minor job
- **Demogorgon cult:** Wants them eliminated — motive unclear but important enough to trade a contract and brightlist spot
- **Daz:** Amused by Daz's flattery and book premise; gave him a written note
- **Zalthir:** Source of tension after the desk fire incident
- **The Party (general):** Transactional relationship under threat of death if they don't deliver
