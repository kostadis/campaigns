# Werz

## Identity
- Duergar merchant and deal-broker operating in Gracklstugh
- First met at the Shattered Spire tavern, built on a broken stalagmite jutting from the Darklake, where he arranged to meet the party

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: survive his assassin problem and continue brokering trade deals undisturbed. He insists he cares only about trade, not politics.
- Pragmatic and generous to those who help him — gifted each party member (including the Flumph) a 10gp gemstone in gratitude for saving his life. Dismissive of conspiracy theories, calling the idea of an organized assassin's guild "a children's fable." Shrewd but forthcoming with useful intelligence when he trusts his audience, though he warns gravely about the consequences of mishandling what he shares.

## History with the Party
1. **Pier Rescue:** Found unconscious on the piers after two assassins attacked him with glowing weapons (possibly psychic — no physical wounds). Assassins carried a charcoal drawing of him confirming he was a deliberate target. Upon waking, claimed ignorance about why he was targeted; Grygum healed him. The party later discovered the Deepking's personal seal on the assassination contract, with the verbatim rant: *"WERZ SALTBARON THAT SNIVELING SQUEALING WRITHING LITTLE RAT! BIND HIS FEET AND THROW HIM OFF HIS BELOVED DOCKS!"* Eldgrim held the contract and offered to permanently brightlist Werz for 20gp, or free if the party eliminates the Demogorgon cult.
2. **Referenced by the Flumph** — The flumph read the party's thoughts and mentioned Werz by name as someone assassins with "a magical light blade" had tried to kill.
3. **Meeting at the Shattered Spire** — Werz met the party, thanked them with gemstones, and revealed his situation: assassins are targeting him because he brokered forbidden weapons deals between clans in violation of the Deepking's decree (only Clan Steelshadow may handle weapons orders). The Stone Guards won't believe him.
4. **Business deals** — He offered to facilitate a weapons deal between the Deep Gnomes and Duergar clans to help the party reach Blingdenstone. Daz negotiated an NDA (starting at 1,250gp, settled at 1,100gp) and separately charged him for the assassin removal job. Werz paid in gems — later identified by Jimjar as empty spell gems valuable to Kazook Pickshine, an alchemist in Blingdenstone.
5. **Political intelligence shared** — Provided a detailed briefing on clan politics and the city's instability (see Current Status).

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Gracklstugh, presumably continuing his trade operations
- **Active operations:** Wants the party to eliminate his assassin threat (preferably without his death). Facilitating the Deep Gnome–Duergar weapons deal.
- **Key intelligence he shared (party knows):**
  - Clan Ironshadow's leader is willing to wait out the Deepking's madness, but other members are unhappy
  - Clan Tharzgad complains about Grey Ghost raids
  - Clan Xundom will support the Deepking in a war
  - Stone Guards have recently started taking bribes — a new development since the Deepking's madness began a few months ago
  - The city is ready to explode; giving the wrong faction the wrong information could trigger all-out war

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Grateful employer; trusts them enough to share sensitive political intelligence and hire them for the assassin job
- **Daz:** Direct business relationship — negotiated the NDA and assassin removal fee
- **Jimjar:** Identified Werz's payment gems as empty spell gems tied to Kazook Pickshine
- **Kazook Pickshine (Blingdenstone alchemist):** Connected via the spell gems — potential future contact
- **Duergar Clans:** Works as a deal-broker between clans, violating the Deepking's weapons decree — this is why he's being targeted
- **Clan Steelshadow:** His dealings undercut their monopoly on weapons orders
- **The Grey Ghosts:** Likely connected to his assassin problem (assassins with "a magical light blade")
- **Stone Guards:** Considers them unreliable; knows they've been taking bribes
