# Bloppblippodd

## Identity
- Archpriest of the Deep Father (Leemoogoogon) faction in Sloobludop
- Daughter of Ploopploopeen; sister of Glooglugogg
- Kuo-toan religious leader and rival to her father's Sea Mother faction
- Has not yet appeared in person

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven by a powerful vision of "Leemooggoogoon the Deep Father," which she proclaimed as the new god of the kuo-toan people. She backs her claims with a tremendous increase in magical power, drawing new followers. She has rejected diplomatic overtures from her father, refusing even a white flag meeting. Her brother considers her mad and disrespectful of kuo-toan traditions.

## History with the Party
- The party has not yet met or seen Bloppblippodd directly. They know of her entirely through Ploopploopeen's account and Glooglugogg's contemptuous remarks. She is the central target of Ploopploopeen's plan: the party will be presented to her as sacrificial offerings, and when she comes to inspect them, the Sea Mother faction will ambush her followers.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Sloobludop, leading the Deep Father faction
- **Active operations:** Conducting escalating blood sacrifices—killing humanoid offerings and casting bloody chum into the Darklake, where something unknown consumes it
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows she is Ploopploopeen's daughter, that she experienced a transformative vision, that she gained significant magical power, and that she leads a rival cult making blood sacrifices. They know the ambush plan: she will demand to see the prisoners in the morning.
- **Hidden:** The true nature of "Leemoogoogon the Deep Father"; what is actually consuming the sacrificial chum in the Darklake; the full extent of her magical power; her actual mental state and whether her vision was genuine

## Relationships
- **Ploopploopeen (father):** Rival faction leader. He plans to use the party as bait to lure her into an ambush. She refused his diplomatic overture.
- **Glooglugogg (brother):** Holds her in deep contempt; considers her mad and disrespectful of kuo-toan tradition. Also blames their father for being too soft on her.
- **The Party:** Unknown to her; she will encounter them as supposed sacrificial offerings.
- **Deep Father followers:** Her hardcore supporters are few in number, but the broader village population's loyalty is uncertain and in flux.
