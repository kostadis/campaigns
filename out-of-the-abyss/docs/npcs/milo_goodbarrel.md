# Milo Goodbarrel

## Identity
- Author and memoirist; wrote a book detailing events surrounding "the war of the dragon"
- Head of a publishing concern
- Member of the Order of the Gauntlet
- Has never appeared in person; known entirely through his written works and as signatory on historical monuments

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations
- A prolific writer whose daily journaling habit was publicly praised by at least one commentator. His works blend historical accounts with vivid character portraits, covering topics as varied as psychic blade attacks, metallic dragon diplomacy, and shrewd negotiators. His writing is practical and narrative-driven enough to serve as both entertainment and tactical reference.
- Acts as an official witness and recorder for the Order of the Gauntlet, personally signing off on monuments of historical significance.

## History with the Party
- **Pre-campaign / Ongoing:** Daz obtained a copy of Milo's book at some point before or during the adventure. The book has become a key personal resource for Daz.
- Daz adopted Milo's daily writing practice after reading a commentator's praise of it, mentally composing journal entries since his escape from Velkynvelve.
- Daz referenced Milo's book during a negotiation with **Werz**, citing a passage about a character named **Ezra** who always claimed he couldn't pay but always could — using this as leverage to extract additional payment.
- Daz has also drawn on the book's content regarding **psychic blade attacks** that leave no trace (including a scene where a Kenku named **Whistler** faked an attack using psychic blades) and passages about **chess playing a significant role in negotiating with metallic dragons**.
- **Daggerford market square:** The party discovered the Tarvis Ulain monument bearing a secondary inscription on the back reading "Witnessed and recorded for the Gauntlet," signed by Milo Goodbarrel in his capacity as head of a publishing concern and member of the Order of the Gauntlet. Thorin noted that Milo personally signed off on this monument.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Unknown; has not appeared in person.
- **Relevance:** Exists as an intellectual influence on Daz and as an official recorder for the Order of the Gauntlet. His book continues to serve as a source of tactical insight, negotiation strategies, and lore that Daz applies in real situations. His publishing concern appears to handle official historical documentation.
- **What the party knows:** Only what Daz has shared or read aloud from the book, plus the discovery of his signature on the Tarvis Ulain monument in Daggerford. No one has met Milo or knows his current whereabouts.

## Relationships
- **Daz:** Primary connection. Daz is a devoted reader of Milo's work, treats the book as a practical reference guide, and credits Milo (via a commentator) as the inspiration for his journaling habit.
- **Order of the Gauntlet:** Member of the organization; serves in an official capacity as witness and recorder for historically significant events.
- **Tarvis Ulain:** Witnessed and recorded monument dedication in Daggerford (posthumous or historical connection).
- No known relationships with other NPCs or factions.
