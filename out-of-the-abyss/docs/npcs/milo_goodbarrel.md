# Milo Goodbarrel

## Identity
- Author of a book (memoirs) detailing events surrounding "the war of the dragon"
- Never appeared in person; known only through his written work
- No faction affiliation noted

## Personality & Motivations
- A chronicler and memoirist whose daily writing practice was notable enough to be praised by commentators. His work covers war, espionage, and diplomacy, suggesting broad interests in history and intrigue.

## History with the Party
- **Ongoing reference:** Daz carries a copy of Milo's book and references it repeatedly:
  - Cited passages about psychic blade attacks that leave no trace, including a scene where a Kenku named **Whistler** used psychic blades to fake an attack.
  - Referenced Milo's account of chess playing a significant role in negotiating with metallic dragons.
  - Read aloud a passage about a character named **Ezra** — who always claimed he couldn't pay but always could — using it as leverage during a negotiation with **Werz** to extract additional payment.
- **Influence on Daz:** A commentator on Milo's work praised his daily writing habit, which directly inspired Daz to begin composing journal entries (mentally, since his escape from Velkynvelve).

## Current Status
- No in-world location; exists only as a referenced author
- His book serves as a recurring narrative and mechanical tool for Daz — providing both roleplay flavor and in-character justification for knowledge

## Relationships
- **Daz:** Admirer and reader; carries Milo's book, draws practical and personal inspiration from it
- **Whistler (Kenku):** Character featured in Milo's book; used psychic blades to fake an attack
- **Ezra:** Character featured in Milo's book; a shrewd negotiator who feigned poverty
