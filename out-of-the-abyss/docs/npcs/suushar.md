# Suushar the Awakened

## Identity
- Kuo-toa, referred to as "the Awakened"
- Fellow NPC traveler with the party; likely a former prisoner or companion from early captivity
- Follower (or at least admirer) of the Sea Mother faith and its archpriest Ploopploopeen in Sloopbludop

## Personality & Motivations
- Spiritually oriented and calm; sees spiritual awakening as a path to ending madness among his people. Genuinely earnest and somewhat naïve — was sincerely confused when Grygum called "Ploopploopeen" a tongue twister, demonstrating by repeating the name multiple times without issue. Practical in his own way, arguing logically during the corpse disposal debate that natural scavenging would obscure tracker evidence. His goal is to return to Sloopbludop and does not wish to travel beyond it.

## History with the Party
1. **Thorin's Madness:** Witnessed Thorin suffer a bout of madness and recommended the party visit Sloopbludop, where the archpriest Ploopploopeen could potentially help. Praised the archpriest as a follower of the Sea Mother.
2. **Corpse Disposal Debate:** Argued that leaving corpses for scavengers would effectively mask evidence from trackers.
3. **Travel Plans:** Stated he had no interest in traveling past Sloopbludop but offered he might be willing to help with travel on the Darklake.
4. **Influence on Buppido:** Later referenced by Buppido during Buppido's desperate plea for his life — Buppido claimed he "learned from Suushar that I was mad," implying Suushar had a stabilizing or truth-telling influence on the derro.

## Current Status
- Last known destination: **Sloopbludop** — he intended to part ways with the group there.
- No indication in the notes of whether the party has reached Sloopbludop yet or whether Suushar is still traveling with them.
- Buppido's reference to him in past tense during a desperate moment may suggest Suushar is no longer present with the group, but this is ambiguous.

## Relationships
- **Ploopploopeen (Archpriest):** Speaks highly of him; sees him as a spiritual authority and healer of madness.
- **Thorin:** Showed concern for Thorin's mental state; recommended help.
- **Buppido:** Had a meaningful impact on the derro — Buppido credits Suushar with helping him recognize his own madness.
- **Grygum:** Brief lighthearted exchange over the pronunciation of Ploopploopeen's name.
- **The Party (general):** Cooperative and helpful; willing to assist with Darklake travel but firm about not going beyond Sloopbludop.
