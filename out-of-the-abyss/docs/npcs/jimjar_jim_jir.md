# Jimjar (Jim Jir)

## Identity
- Fellow prisoner and escapee
- First appeared during the escape sequence, among those herded toward the sacrificial depression

## Personality & Motivations
- Compulsive gambler — defaults to betting even in life-or-death situations. His first instinct when facing possible sacrifice was to propose a wager on survival odds. Displays a darkly flippant attitude toward danger.

## History with the Party
- **Escape:** Was among the prisoners herded toward the sacrificial depression. As the group faced potential death, he proposed a bet on how many would survive. After the battle broke out and all escapees made it through alive, he noted, "We all made it alive." Fled with the group toward the docks.
- Grygum specifically questioned Jimjar's trustworthiness, wondering whether Jimjar would let someone die just to avoid losing a bet — suggesting early suspicion within the party.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Fleeing with the group toward the docks.
- **Active plans:** None known beyond immediate escape.
- **Hidden information:** His true character and loyalties remain untested; Grygum's suspicion about his willingness to sacrifice others for a bet is unresolved.

## Relationships
- **Grygum:** Source of early distrust. Grygum suspects Jimjar's gambling compulsion could make him unreliable or even dangerous to the group.
- **Other escapees:** Traveling companion; no deeper bonds established yet.
