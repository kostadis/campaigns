# Narrak

## Identity
- Derro cult leader and head priest of the Demogorgon worshippers in the Whorlstone Tunnels beneath Gracklstugh
- Encountered by the party in a deeper cavern reeking of brimstone and chemicals, standing on a natural 5-foot-high platform surrounded by a glowing ritual circle

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Elevate the Derro race, destroy the Duergar, conquer Gracklstugh, and deliver it as a sacrificial gift to Demogorgon. Sought to accomplish this through ritual creation of two-headed Stone Giant monsters and the eventual repair of the Obelisk as "the instrument of their ascension."
- Imperious, short-tempered, and consumed by chaotic evil madness. His pale Derro features were perpetually contorted into a grotesque mask of spiraling madness, with unnervingly bright eyes darting with frenetic energy. He berated his followers like an exasperated choir director ("Silence, you blithering half-wits! More vigor!") and radiated fanatical conviction—though it visibly waned as his allies fell in battle.

## History with the Party
1. **Discovery:** The party found Narrak in his ritual chamber in the Whorlstone Tunnels, leading Derro apprentices in chanting invocations to Demogorgon. He was reading from Trillimac-bound books on madness and ritual spawn creation.
2. **Confrontation:** Narrak announced he had already converted one of Hgraam's Stone Giants into a two-headed monster and planned to convert another. He cast *Fear* (using a white feather), terrifying Thorin, Grygum, and Zalthir.
3. **Counter & Defeat:** Zalthir broke the Fear by moving magical darkness over Narrak. Daz trapped him with *Maximilian's Earthen Grasp*. Narrak failed to break free, attacked Grygum and Thorin with a short spear while restrained, but **Thorin delivered the killing blow**. His last words: *"No, the ritual cannot be undone!"*
4. **Loot:** The party recovered gold, Keoghtom's Ointment, and texts including *"The Rituals of the Two-Headed Beast."*

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed by Thorin during the battle in the Whorlstone Tunnels.
- His ritual to create two-headed Stone Giant monsters has been disrupted, though at least one of Hgraam's giants was already successfully converted.
- The party holds evidence from his body and chest proving Derro perfidy and Demogorgon worship—usable as leverage.
- **Hidden from the party:** Narrak's broader conspiracy involved an agent named **Pliinki** working on repairing the Obelisk using a stolen egg. The letter describing this plan and the full scope of the conspiracy (Deepking's paranoia, Errde Blackskull chasing phantoms, Keepers of the Flame distracted) may or may not have been found among his effects.

## Relationships
- **Pliinki:** Key operative; Narrak directed Pliinki's work on the Obelisk and the egg, calling it "paramount" to their plans.
- **Demogorgon:** Patron deity; Narrak served as high priest channeling the Prince of Demons' madness.
- **Derro apprentices/followers:** Led them in ritual work; berated and dominated them.
- **Hgraam / Stone Giants:** Target victims—Narrak's ritual turned at least one of Hgraam's giants mad and two-headed.
- **Gartokkar:** Referenced in Narrak's letter; Narrak viewed Gartokkar's desired "genocidal war" against the Derro as merely "the first cleansing," suggesting he planned to co-opt or outlast it.
- **Errde Blackskull:** Narrak was aware she was searching for evidence of Demogorgon's influence; he considered her to be "chasing phantoms."
- **The Party:** Enemies. Thorin killed him; Daz restrained him; Zalthir neutralized his Fear spell.
