# Narrak

## Identity
- Derro cult leader and head priest of Demogorgon worshippers in the Whorlstone Tunnels beneath Gracklstugh
- First encountered by the party in a deeper cavern reeking of brimstone and chemicals, standing on a natural 5-foot-high platform surrounded by a glowing ritual circle

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Elevate the Derro, destroy the Duergar, conquer Gracklstugh, and deliver it as a sacrificial gift to Demogorgon. Sought to graft second heads onto Stone Giants to create Ettin-like monsters as weapons of war.
- Imperious, short-tempered, and consumed by chaotic evil madness. His pale Derro features were permanently twisted into a grotesque mask of spiraling insanity, with unnervingly bright, darting eyes and wild gesticulations. He berated his followers like an exasperated choir director demanding more vigor in their chanting, and his warped intellect radiated the insidious influence of Demogorgon. His conviction visibly waned only as his allies fell around him.

## History with the Party
1. **Discovery:** The party found Narrak in his ritual chamber in the Whorlstone Tunnels, leading chants and reading from Trillimac-bound books. He screamed at an apprentice that they were "conducting experiments" for "the elevation of the Derro, the Triumph of Demogorgon, and the destruction of the Duergar!"
2. **Confrontation:** Narrak announced he had already successfully converted one of Hgraam's Stone Giants into a two-headed monster and planned to convert another. He was performing a ritual involving a two-foot-tall two-headed stone giant statue and reading from *The Rituals of the Two-Headed Beast*.
3. **Battle:** Narrak cast Fear using a white feather, terrifying Thorin, Grygum, and Zalthir. He shouted, "Demogorgon's madness flows through me! You will embrace it or *break* trying!" Zalthir countered by moving magical darkness over Narrak, breaking line of sight. Daz trapped him with Maximilian's Earthen Grasp; Narrak failed to break free.
4. **Death:** While restrained, Narrak struck at Grygum and Thorin with a short spear. Thorin delivered the killing blow. Narrak's last words: *"No, the ritual cannot be undone!"*
5. **Loot:** The party recovered gold, Keoghtom's Ointment, and his texts including *The Rituals of the Two-Headed Beast*, *The Spiraling Descent: A Treatise on Duality and Madness*, and *The Prince's Gift: On the Creation of Favored Spawn*.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed by Thorin in the Whorlstone Tunnels.
- His ritual was responsible for turning at least one of Hgraam's Stone Giants mad/two-headed.
- The party holds evidence from his body proving Derro perfidy and Demogorgon worship — usable as leverage.
- **Hidden from the party:** Narrak's letter to Pliinki revealed a broader conspiracy — the Obelisk and the egg are paramount to the cult's plans. Once the Obelisk is repaired, it would be "the instrument of their ascension." He viewed the spreading madness in Gracklstugh (the Deepking's paranoia, Errde Blackskull chasing phantoms, the Keepers of the Flame losing the egg) as all going according to plan. It is unclear how much of this letter's contents the party has seen.

## Relationships
- **Pliinki:** Fellow conspirator; Narrak directed Pliinki's work with the Obelisk and the egg via letter, calling it paramount to the plan.
- **Demogorgon:** Patron deity; Narrak served as his primary instrument in Gracklstugh.
- **Hgraam / Stone Giants:** Victims of Narrak's rituals — at least one giant was transformed into a two-headed monster.
- **Errde Blackskull:** Narrak viewed her investigations as ineffectual ("chasing phantoms"); she was seeking evidence of exactly the kind of Demogorgon influence Narrak was spreading.
- **Gartokkar / Keepers of the Flame:** Narrak saw Gartokkar's desire for a "genocidal war" against the Derro as merely "the first cleansing" — useful chaos, not a threat.
- **The Party (Thorin, Daz, Zalthir, Grygum):** Direct combatants. Thorin killed him; Daz restrained him; Zalthir neutralized his Fear spell.

## Arc Score Events
- Party obtained evidence of Derro/Demogorgon conspiracy from Narrak's body — likely relevant to Errde Blackskull and Gracklstugh political arcs (direction: advances party leverage in Gracklstugh).
