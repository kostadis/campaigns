# Daz

## Identity
- Drow (implied by Chipgrin's remark about negotiating with drow)
- Magic user (employs magic missiles)
- No stated faction affiliation; party member
- First appearance context not specified in notes; earliest recorded activity is during the Silken Paths and spider retreat

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: escape the Underdark; willing to cut through any obstacle—social or physical—to achieve it
- Pragmatic and blunt to the point of abrasiveness; takes charge not out of ambition but out of frustration with inefficiency. Views allies and bystanders in coldly utilitarian terms (called goblin guides "fodder"). Holds himself to a strict personal accountability, willing to accept full blame and consequences for plans he sets in motion.

## History with the Party

1. **Silken Paths & Spider Retreat** — Used magic missiles during the party's retreat from spiders. Privately muttered that the goblin guides could serve as battle fodder; Buppido overheard this remark.
2. **Spectator Fight** — Killed the Spectator with magic missiles ("glowing teardrops of blue-white force"), a decisive finishing blow.
3. **Blingdenstone Political Negotiations** — Seized control of the svirfneblin political debate out of exasperation. Insisted on a direct plan to deal with the Pudding King and the slime threat. Secured support from Chief Dorbo and Senni for a two-pronged strategy: the Gold Whisker clan as a distraction, the party handling the Pudding King directly.
4. **Meeting with Chief Chipgrin** — Clarified the party's true motivations (clearing a path out of the Underdark, not politics). Refused to facilitate political maneuvering. Accepted full personal accountability for battle outcomes, telling Chipgrin he could seek vengeance against Daz personally if the plan caused casualties. Chipgrin noted Daz exceeded even the drow reputation for being difficult.
5. **Grand Moot** — Asked by Senni to lead the meeting. Presented the battle plan: gnome army distraction at the ooze amphitheater while the party covertly snatches the Pudding King via magic. Exposed gnome leaders' attempts to exploit the party through side quests by questioning why a simple northern attack wasn't viable. Leaders quickly conceded, though the party chose to pursue some side quests voluntarily.

## Current Status
- **Last known:** In Blingdenstone, preparing for the assault on the Pudding King
- **Active plans:** Lead the covert strike against the Pudding King while the combined gnome forces create a distraction at the ooze amphitheater
- **Hidden from party:** Buppido overheard Daz's "fodder" comment about the goblin guides — potential leverage or friction point

## Relationships
- **Buppido** — Overheard Daz's callous remark about goblin guides; relationship implications unclear but a potential source of distrust or blackmail
- **Chief Dorbo & Senni** — Allied; they deferred to Daz's leadership and plan. Senni specifically asked Daz to lead the grand moot
- **Chief Chipgrin (Gold Whisker clan)** — Grudging respect; Chipgrin found Daz exceptionally difficult but agreed to cooperate. Daz refused to be his political tool and bound himself personally to the plan's consequences
- **Gnome leaders (general)** — Daz called out their attempts to exploit the party; they fell in line but the dynamic is transactional
