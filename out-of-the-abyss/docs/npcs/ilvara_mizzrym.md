# Ilvara Mizzrym

## Identity
- High Priestess of House Mizzrym, corrupted instrument of Zuggtmoy
- Stationed in the cavern above the heart fungus at the center of Velkynvelve's fungal infestation
- By the time of her death, she was no longer truly herself — Zuggtmoy's work inhabiting a drow priestess's body

## Personality & Motivations
- Completely devoted to Zuggtmoy, whom she referred to as "the bride," muttering prophecy about "the chaos and mayhem to come" with sincere belief rather than theatrical menace. She had fully accepted the future Zuggtmoy promised. She never begged, never offered surrender, and fought with focused fury and tactical intelligence even while gravely wounded.

## History with the Party
- **The Velkynvelve Confrontation:** Ilvara served as the primary antagonist at the heart of the fungal infestation. Zalthir opened aggressively — grappling her with his Eldritch Claw Tattoo, dragging her forty feet up, landing two critical hits for sixteen damage, and dropping her to the stone floor. Wounded and bleeding, she retaliated with a legendary reaction fear effect (DC-14 Wisdom save), successfully frightening Zalthir and neutralizing his close-range combat for a full round. She cast Insect Plague at the cavern entrance, killing her own fungal minions (thirteen HP each) and dealing twenty-six damage to Glabbagool. She called divine fire down on Daz and Nym Duskryn, used fungal growth to reshape the battlefield, and survived Thorin destroying the bridge beneath her by leaping clear. Jorlan Duskryn charged her through the insect swarm in a personal vendetta — she had discarded him after his scarring per drow custom. They traded blows on the cavern floor. Grygum's Guiding Bolt ultimately killed her. Her body erupted in a massive cloud of poisonous spores, catching Zalthir and Thorin in the toxic bloom and confirming the totality of Zuggtmoy's corruption.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed by Grygum's Guiding Bolt. Her death released a cloud of demonic spores, confirming she had been fully consumed by Zuggtmoy's influence — corruption expressed as biology rather than identity.

## Relationships
- **Zuggtmoy:** Fully claimed by the demon lord; by the end she was less an ally and more a vessel.
- **Jorlan Duskryn:** Former lover/consort, discarded after his scarring per drow societal values. He charged her screaming her name — their history made the fight deeply personal for him.
- **Nym Duskryn:** Target of her divine fire during the battle (relationship to Jorlan implied by shared surname).
- **Zalthir:** Mutual combatant; he opened the fight with devastating aggression, she shut him down with fear.
- **Grygum:** Delivered the killing blow; noted afterward he was glad to have been standing back from the spore eruption.
- **Thorin:** Destroyed her bridge during the fight; caught in her death spores.
- **Daz:** Targeted by her divine fire.
- **Glabbagool:** Took heavy damage (twenty-six points) from her Insect Plague.
