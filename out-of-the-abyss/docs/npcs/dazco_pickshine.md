# Dazco Pickshine

## Identity
- Representative of the Miners Guild
- First encountered at a council meeting where the party was brokering a compromise regarding mining operations

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: secure funding for city repairs through mining revenue
- Pragmatic and economically driven; initially pushed for aggressive strip mining as the fastest path to needed funds. Willing to compromise when presented with a viable alternative that still met economic needs.

## History with the Party
- **Council Meeting:** Advocated strongly for immediate strip mining to generate revenue for city repairs. The party brokered a sustainable mining compromise that balanced the Miners Guild's economic demands with the need to respect ghosts' sacred grounds. Dazco ultimately agreed to the compromise.

## Current Status
- Last known status: operating under the terms of the sustainable mining compromise
- Presumably overseeing Miners Guild operations in accordance with the agreed-upon plan
- No hidden agendas noted in available information

## Relationships
- **Miners Guild:** Serves as their representative and advocate
- **The Party:** Initially at odds over mining approach; relationship shifted to cooperative after the party successfully brokered a deal that addressed his economic concerns
- **Ghosts / Sacred Grounds stakeholders:** In tension with whoever represents the interests of the sacred grounds; now bound by the compromise to respect those boundaries

## Arc Score Events
- **Compromise accepted:** Dazco's agreement to sustainable mining over strip mining likely represents a positive shift in relations between the Miners Guild and the party / sacred grounds faction.
