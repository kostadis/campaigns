# Entemoch

## Identity
- Prince of Good Elementals (Earth)
- Historical ally of the deep gnomes of Blingdenstone, who visited his throne regularly in ages past
- Has not appeared directly to the party; known only through lore, intermediaries, and the summoning ritual

## Personality & Motivations
- A benevolent elemental prince who once aided the deep gnomes and granted his boon to allies. His disposition toward the party is reportedly favorable after they assisted and healed an Earth Elemental in his throne room. His core drive appears to be opposing Ogremoch and supporting those who prove worthy through sacrifice.

## History with the Party
1. **Galeb Duhr at the Hallowed Temple** informed the party they still needed to obtain Entemoch's boon. The Galeb Duhr argued amongst themselves about his location, concluding the gnomes would know.
2. **Naomi Pathshutter** reported that the tunnels to Entemoch's throne had shifted and sealed after Ogremoch's altar was hallowed.
3. **Earth Elemental encounter** — the party assisted and healed an Earth Elemental in Entemoch's throne room, earning his favorable disposition.
4. **Summoning circle discovered** beyond a breached wall in the White Shell Mine — a cavern guarded by two basilisks moving with mechanical precision. Giant runes inscribed in the circle outlined the ritual requirements.
5. **Thorin deciphered the runes** and determined three sacrifices are needed: wealth, a weapon, and blood.
6. **Weapon sacrifice completed** — Thorin placed a dart on the runes; it was instantly swallowed and rusted to dust. The runes transformed from yellow to blinding orange.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Imprisoned or sealed away; accessible only through the summoning ritual in the White Shell Mine cavern.
- **Active ritual:** Two of three sacrifices remain — **wealth** and **blood**. The runes are glowing blinding orange.
- **What the party knows:** The ritual requirements, that Entemoch views them favorably, and that the tunnels to his throne sealed after Ogremoch's altar was hallowed.
- **What remains hidden:** Entemoch himself has not appeared or spoken; the exact nature of his boon is unknown.

## Relationships
- **Deep Gnomes of Blingdenstone** — long-standing alliance; they visited him regularly in ages past.
- **Ogremoch** — implied opposition (hallowing Ogremoch's altar sealed Entemoch's tunnels).
- **Galeb Duhr** — serve as his intermediaries/guardians at the hallowed temple.
- **Naomi Pathshutter** — gnome contact who provided intelligence on the sealed tunnels.
- **Thorin** — party member who deciphered the runes and completed the first sacrifice.
- **The Party** — favorably disposed toward them following the Earth Elemental healing.
