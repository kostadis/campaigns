# Entemoch

## Identity
- Prince of Good Elementals (Earth)
- Historical ally of the deep gnomes of Blingdenstone, who visited his throne regularly in ages past
- Has not appeared directly to the party; known only through lore, intermediaries, and his summoning ritual

## Personality & Motivations
- A benevolent elemental entity who once aided the deep gnomes and whose boon carries significant power. His disposition toward the party is reportedly favorable after they assisted and healed an Earth Elemental in his throne room. His current state suggests imprisonment or displacement requiring a ritual to free him.

## History with the Party
1. **Hallowed Temple:** The Galeb Duhr at the temple informed the party they still needed Entemoch's boon. The Galeb Duhr argued amongst themselves about his location, ultimately agreeing the gnomes of Blingdenstone would know since they had visited him regularly.
2. **Tunnels Sealed:** Naomi Pathshutter reported that the tunnels leading to Entemoch's throne had shifted and sealed themselves after Ogremoch's altar was hallowed.
3. **Earth Elemental Encounter:** The party assisted and healed an Earth Elemental in Entemoch's throne room, which reportedly improved Entemoch's disposition toward them.
4. **Summoning Circle Discovered:** Beyond a breached wall in the White Shell Mine, the party found a summoning circle inscribed with giant runes, guarded by two basilisks moving with mechanical precision. Thorin deciphered the runes, revealing three sacrifices are required to free Entemoch: **wealth**, **a weapon**, and **blood**.
5. **First Sacrifice Completed:** Thorin placed a dart on the runes as the weapon sacrifice. It was instantly swallowed and rusted to dust. The runes transformed from yellow to blinding orange. Two sacrifices remain.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Trapped or displaced; accessible only through the summoning circle in the White Shell Mine cavern
- **Active Plans:** The ritual to free him is in progress — weapon sacrifice complete, wealth and blood sacrifices still required
- **Known vs. Hidden:** The party knows the three sacrifice requirements and has completed one. Entemoch's exact nature, the specifics of his imprisonment, and what his boon actually grants remain unknown.

## Relationships
- **Deep Gnomes of Blingdenstone:** Long-standing alliance; the gnomes visited his throne regularly in ages past
- **Ogremoch:** Implied opposition (Entemoch is the *good* elemental prince; Ogremoch's altar was hallowed as part of the larger quest)
- **The Galeb Duhr:** Serve as his intermediaries/guardians at the hallowed temple
- **The Party:** Favorably disposed toward them after they healed his Earth Elemental; the party is actively working to free him
- **Thorin:** Deciphered the giant runes and completed the first sacrifice

## Arc Score Events
- **Increase:** Party assisted and healed the Earth Elemental in Entemoch's throne room (improved his disposition toward the party).
- **Increase (implied):** Completion of the weapon sacrifice advances the ritual to free him.
