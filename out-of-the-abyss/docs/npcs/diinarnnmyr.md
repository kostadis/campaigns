# Diinarnnmyr

## Identity
- Derro guard, stationed at a guard post
- First encountered when the party passed through the guard post where Diinarnnmyr was stationed alongside fellow guard Diirdeklin

## Personality & Motivations
- Hot-headed and confrontational — Diirdeklin feared Diinarnnmyr would foolishly attempt to fight the party rather than let them pass. Suspicious of outside factions and politically aware enough to recognize the Ember Vanguard, interpreting their presence as evidence of a Drow-Duergar alliance aimed at subduing the Derro.

## History with the Party
- The party passed through Diinarnnmyr's guard post. Though Diirdeklin managed to prevent a violent confrontation, Diinarnnmyr muttered afterward that "The Ember Vanguard — the Drow are now working with the Duergar to subdue us!" — suggesting the party's passage has fueled Derro resentment and conspiracy fears.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The Derro guard post, alongside Diirdeklin
- **Active concerns:** Believes the Drow and Duergar are collaborating against the Derro, using the Ember Vanguard as evidence
- **What remains hidden:** Unknown whether Diinarnnmyr will act on this suspicion or spread it further among the Derro

## Relationships
- **Diirdeklin:** Fellow guard at the same post; Diirdeklin views Diinarnnmyr as reckless and a liability in tense situations
- **The Party (Ember Vanguard):** Hostile and suspicious; sees them as agents of a Drow-Duergar conspiracy against the Derro
- **Drow & Duergar:** Views both as enemies conspiring to subjugate the Derro people
