# Diinarnnmyr

## Identity
- Derro guard stationed at a guard post
- First encountered by the party when they passed through the guard post where Diinarnnmyr was on duty

## Personality & Motivations
- Aggressive and impulsive — his fellow guard Diirdeklin worried he might foolishly try to fight the party. Appears to harbor deep resentment toward the Drow and Duergar, viewing them as threats to Derro autonomy.

## History with the Party
- The party passed through the guard post where Diinarnnmyr and Diirdeklin were stationed. Though Diirdeklin feared Diinarnnmyr might start a fight, the party passed without incident. After they moved on, Diinarnnmyr muttered that "The Ember Vanguard — the Drow are now working with the Duergar to subdue us!"

## Current Status
- Last known location: the Derro guard post
- No active plans noted, though his comment suggests simmering resentment and a belief that the Ember Vanguard represents a Drow-Duergar alliance aimed at subjugating the Derro
- **What the party may not know:** Diinarnnmyr's muttered comment about the Ember Vanguard — it was said after they passed, so they may or may not have overheard it

## Relationships
- **Diirdeklin:** Fellow Derro guard at the same post. Diirdeklin views Diinarnnmyr as reckless and potentially dangerous in tense situations.
- **The Party (Ember Vanguard):** Hostile suspicion. Believes they represent a Drow-Duergar collaboration to oppress the Derro.
