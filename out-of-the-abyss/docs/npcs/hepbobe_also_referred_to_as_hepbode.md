# Hepbobe

## Identity
- Leader of the Circle of Growers
- Ally of Phylo; not aligned with Basidia
- Responsible for growing food for the Myconid colony

## Personality & Motivations
- Supports Phylo's reorganization of Myconid society into specialized circles, believing it is more efficient than the old communal meld. Dismisses Basidia's concerns as "exaggerated doom and gloom."
- Patient and good-natured — calmly explained Myconid biology and diet when Grygum awkwardly asked about mushrooms eating mushrooms.
- Genuinely enthusiastic about Phylo's vision, seeing it as a path toward "a better day for Myconid kind."

## History with the Party
- The party visited Hepbobe's domain and received a tour of the irrigation tunnels and various fungi grown to feed the community.
- Grygum asked an awkward question about mushrooms eating mushrooms; Hepbobe patiently explained that Myconids are sentient fungus, herbivores with individual preferences and cooking styles.
- When prodded about Phylo, Hepbobe defended the new circle-based organizational structure, criticizing the old communal meld as inefficient — people would ramble endlessly about niche topics no one else cared about.
- Hepbobe dismissed Basidia's warnings and spoke positively about Phylo's leadership.
- Zalthir later confirmed with Basidia that Hepbobe's food supply was **not contaminated** with evil spores.

## Current Status
- Last known location: the Circle of Growers within the Myconid colony.
- Actively managing food production for the community.
- **Party knows:** Hepbobe is a Phylo loyalist, the food supply is clean, and Hepbobe genuinely believes in the new system.

## Relationships
- **Phylo:** Friend and political ally; supports Phylo's reorganization.
- **Basidia:** Dismissive of Basidia's concerns; views them as overly negative.
- **The Party:** Hospitable and forthcoming; gave them a tour and answered questions openly.
- **Grygum:** Interacted directly; patient with his awkward line of questioning.
- **Zalthir:** Zalthir investigated Hepbobe's food supply (via Basidia) and found it clean.
