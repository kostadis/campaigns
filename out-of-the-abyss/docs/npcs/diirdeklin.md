# Diirdeklin

## Identity
- Derro guard, stationed at a guard post before double doors south of the obelisk chamber
- Loyal to Pliinki's cause — believed her plan would free the Derro from Duergar bondage
- First encountered when the party approached his guard post from the north

## Personality & Motivations
- Motivated by liberation from Duergar oppression; followed Pliinki out of hope for Derro freedom. Pragmatic and survival-oriented — when faced with overwhelming force, he immediately capitulated rather than fight. Showed protective instincts toward his companion Diinarnnmyr, shouting him down to prevent a suicidal confrontation.

## History with the Party
- **Guard Post Encounter:** The party approached from the north. Diirdeklin reached for an alarm bell but was stopped by a combined intimidation effort — Daz used dancing lights to impersonate a Drow Archmage summoning meteor swarm, while Zalthir declared "The people you are trying to warn are dead." Terrified, Diirdeklin dropped the alarm, shouted "Yes, pass!" and waved the party through, preventing his companion from engaging.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The guard post south of the obelisk chamber, still alive alongside Diinarnnmyr.
- **Current belief:** He concluded that a Drow incursion has occurred and that Pliinki is dead, meaning larger plans are afoot. He was not corrected on this assumption.
- **What the party knows:** He is a Derro guard who was easily intimidated and let them pass.
- **What remains hidden:** His deeper loyalties and what actions he may take now that he believes Pliinki is dead and Drow are involved.

## Relationships
- **Pliinki:** Followed her leadership; believed in her plan to liberate the Derro from Duergar control. Now believes she is dead.
- **Diinarnnmyr:** Fellow guard and companion at the post. Diirdeklin exercised authority over him in the moment, ordering him to stand down.
- **Daz:** Believes Daz to be a powerful Drow Archmage — deeply afraid of him.
- **Zalthir:** Heard Zalthir's declaration that Pliinki's allies were dead; views him as part of the Drow incursion force.
- **Duergar:** Oppressors of the Derro; Diirdeklin's primary enemy, and the reason he followed Pliinki.
