# Diirdeklin

## Identity
- Derro guard stationed at a guard post in front of double doors south of the obelisk chamber
- Loyal to Pliinki's cause — believed her plan would free the Derro from Duergar bondage
- Paired with fellow guard Diinarnnmyr

## Personality & Motivations
- Motivated by Derro liberation from the Duergar. He had endured days of tedious guard duty on the strength of Pliinki's promise alone. When confronted with overwhelming force, he chose pragmatic survival over futile resistance, actively restraining his companion from fighting. He is shrewd enough to read a situation and draw broader strategic conclusions from limited information.

## History with the Party
- **Guard Post Encounter:** The party approached from the north. Diirdeklin reached for an alarm bell to warn those beyond the double doors. Daz used dancing lights to impersonate a Drow Archmage apparently summoning meteor swarm, and Zalthir declared in a booming voice that "The people you are trying to warn are dead." Diirdeklin was sufficiently terrified to drop the alarm, shout "Yes, pass!" and prevent Diinarnnmyr from engaging in a hopeless fight. The party passed through without combat.
- **Aftermath:** After the party departed, Diirdeklin concluded that a Drow incursion had taken place and that Pliinki's death signaled larger plans were in motion.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The guard post south of the obelisk chamber, presumably still with Diinarnnmyr.
- **Active Plans:** None known beyond processing the apparent Drow incursion. He believes Pliinki is dead and that the Derro liberation effort has been disrupted.
- **Party Knowledge vs. Hidden:** The party knows him only as a guard they intimidated into letting them pass. They likely do not know his name, his loyalty to Pliinki, or that he now believes a Drow incursion is underway — a misunderstanding that could spread and have wider consequences among the Derro.

## Relationships
- **Diinarnnmyr:** Fellow guard and companion; Diirdeklin exercises authority or at least influence over him, restraining him from reckless action.
- **Pliinki:** Believed in her cause and her plan to free the Derro from Duergar control. Now believes she is dead.
- **Daz:** Believes Daz to be a powerful Drow Archmage — deeply fearful.
- **Zalthir:** Heard Zalthir's declaration that the people beyond the doors were dead; regards him as part of a dangerous Drow force.
