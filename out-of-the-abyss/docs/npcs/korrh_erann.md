# Korrh Erann

## Identity
- Monk of Zalthir's monastic order (historical figure)
- Referenced in Zalthir's retelling of monastic teachings; never encountered directly by the party

## Personality & Motivations
- Pragmatist who valued survival over confrontation. When trapped in the Underdark and pursued by drow, he refused to gamble lives on an ambush and chose to flee instead. His instinct proved correct — he was the sole survivor of his group.

## History with the Party
- **No direct interaction.** Korrh Erann's story was invoked by Zalthir as a parable to justify his own decision to flee from pursuing drow scouts rather than stand and fight. The other members of Korrh's original group wanted to kill the drow scouts to slow pursuit; Korrh argued it was too dangerous and ran. Everyone who stayed behind died.

## Current Status
- Historical figure; presumably deceased (no indication he is alive in the present day)
- Exists in the campaign as a cautionary tale from Zalthir's monastic tradition

## Relationships
- **Zalthir:** Member of the same monastic order. Zalthir knows Korrh's story well enough to cite it in moments of crisis, suggesting it is a significant teaching within the order.
