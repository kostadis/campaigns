# Valimor Brightgem

## Identity
- Proprietor of The Gilded Sheath in Blingdenstone
- Scabbard craftsman and merchant
- First encountered when the party sought him out to commission a scabbard for Dawnbringer

## Personality & Motivations
- A skilled artisan with a sharp entrepreneurial streak. Became visibly excited when Thorin pitched marketing concepts like tiered product lines, platform-based business models, and upgrade paths. Willing to offer discounts when genuinely inspired — suggesting he values clever ideas as much as coin.

## History with the Party
- The party visited The Gilded Sheath in Blingdenstone to commission a scabbard for Dawnbringer, their sentient glowing sword.
- Valimor presented two options: an expensive gem-encrusted showpiece and a more practical locking scabbard designed to prevent theft.
- Thorin pitched product naming ideas ("Vaultmaster 9000") and business concepts involving tiered models with replaceable components.
- Valimor was so energized by these marketing ideas that he offered the practical locking scabbard at a reduced price.

## Current Status
- **Location:** The Gilded Sheath, Blingdenstone
- **Activity:** Presumably crafting or delivering the commissioned locking scabbard for Dawnbringer; likely mulling over the new business concepts Thorin introduced.
- **Known to party:** A fair-dealing craftsman open to creative negotiation.
- **Hidden:** Unknown whether he acts on Thorin's business ideas or if this leads to any broader enterprise.

## Relationships
- **Thorin:** Impressed and inspired by Thorin's marketing instincts; gave a discount as a direct result of the conversation.
- **The Party (general):** Positive — treated them as valued customers and engaged enthusiastically with their input.
