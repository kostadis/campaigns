# Valimor Brightgem

## Identity
- Proprietor of **The Gilded Sheath** in **Blingdenstone**
- Deep gnome craftsman specializing in scabbards and sheaths
- First encountered when the party sought him out to commission a scabbard for **Dawnbringer**

## Personality & Motivations
- A skilled artisan with a keen eye for business opportunity. Became visibly excited when Thorin pitched modern marketing concepts like tiered product lines, platform-based models, and catchy branding (e.g., "Vaultmaster 9000"). His enthusiasm for entrepreneurial innovation matched his craftsmanship. Generous when inspired — he offered the party a reduced price after their brainstorming session sparked new business ideas.

## History with the Party
- **Commission visit:** The party came to The Gilded Sheath seeking a scabbard for Dawnbringer, the sentient glowing sword. Valimor presented two options: a gem-encrusted showpiece (expensive) and a practical locking scabbard designed to prevent theft. Thorin suggested product naming schemes and tiered models with replaceable components, which thrilled Valimor. In gratitude for the marketing inspiration, he offered the practical locking scabbard at a **reduced price**.

## Current Status
- **Location:** The Gilded Sheath, Blingdenstone
- **Activity:** Presumably crafting or delivering the commissioned locking scabbard for Dawnbringer; likely developing new product lines inspired by Thorin's suggestions
- **Known to party:** A friendly, fair-dealing craftsman open to collaboration
- **Hidden:** Nothing noted

## Relationships
- **Thorin (party member):** Strong rapport — Thorin's marketing ideas genuinely excited Valimor and earned the party a discount
- **The Party (general):** Positive; viewed as valued customers and idea partners
- **Dawnbringer connection:** Aware the scabbard is for a sentient, glowing sword (and thus aware the party possesses a notable magical weapon)
