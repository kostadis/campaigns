# Grimgrim

## Identity
- Duergar assassin; guild member serving under Eldgrim
- First appeared at the intersection near the assassin's lair, materializing alongside Rust

## Personality & Motivations
- Operates as a loyal enforcer/guard within the assassin's guild. No strong individual personality traits have been demonstrated yet beyond dutiful obedience to Eldgrim.

## History with the Party
- Materialized with Rust at the intersection near the assassin's lair, serving as an initial point of contact/escort.
- Stood guard behind the party during their meeting with Eldgrim.
- Was successfully distracted by Thorin, who engaged him and Rust with questions about the guild's name, providing cover for Daz to steal the contract from Eldgrim's desk.

## Current Status
- Last known location: the assassin's lair/guild headquarters
- Likely unaware that the contract was stolen during the meeting
- No individual plans or operations known

## Relationships
- **Eldgrim**: Serves as one of his guards; appears to follow his authority without question
- **Rust**: Fellow guild member and partner; the two operate together and were paired during the party's visit
- **Thorin**: Engaged with him directly (was distracted by Thorin's questions about the guild name)
- **Daz**: Unaware of Daz's theft carried out while he was distracted
