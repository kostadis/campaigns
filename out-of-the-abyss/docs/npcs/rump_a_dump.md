# Rump-a-dump

## Identity
- Myconid sprout, friend and circle-mate of Stool
- Originally from Neverlight Grove
- First mentioned by Stool as an unhappy friend who needed to be found; later encountered in person among dancing Myconids in a cave

## Personality & Motivations
- Terse, precise, and restrained — a sharp contrast to Stool's enthusiastic chattiness. Visibly uncomfortable and worried about the state of his fellow Myconids.
- Core goal: understand what has gone wrong with the Myconids since Voosbur's spores, and return to Neverlight Grove to warn his Sovereign and find a way to fix things.
- Demonstrates dry wit and intellectual pushback (e.g., countering Thorin's cannibalism remark by asking if humanoids who eat other creatures are cannibals).

## History with the Party
1. **First mention (earlier session):** Stool insists to Zalthir that Rump-a-dump is unhappy and must be found. Stool vouches for Rump-a-dump's friendliness and assures the party he would never let them be turned into zombies (referencing the Myconid community's practice of converting dead enemies into zombies). Zalthir defers, prioritizing survival.
2. **Found in the cave:** The party discovers Rump-a-dump among the dancing Myconids. He explains that Voosbur released strange spores that changed everyone — the Myconids began dancing unnaturally and speaking of "the Lady's dream," which he insists is not the Myconid way. Voosbur's spores somehow transported the Myconids from Neverlight Grove to this location, after which they ate strange mushrooms (shrinking/growing kind). Rump-a-dump did **not** eat the mushrooms, which is why he remains unaffected.

## Current Status
- **Location:** In the cave with the dancing Myconids
- **Active concern:** Deeply worried that Voosbur's strange spores may have infected the rest of the Myconid community back at Neverlight Grove; hopes his Sovereign there might know a way out
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows about Voosbur's spores, the unnatural dancing, "the Lady's dream," and the strange mushrooms. The full nature of what Voosbur is doing and the identity/nature of "the Lady" remain unclear.

## Relationships
- **Stool:** Close friend and circle-mate. Stool is deeply concerned for Rump-a-dump's wellbeing and vouched for him to the party.
- **Voosbur:** Source of the strange spores responsible for the corruption of the Myconids. Rump-a-dump is clearly opposed to what Voosbur has done.
- **Zalthir:** Stool directed the initial plea to find Rump-a-dump at Zalthir specifically.
- **Thorin:** Brief verbal exchange regarding mushroom-eating and cannibalism.
- **Neverlight Grove Sovereign:** Rump-a-dump looks to the Sovereign as a potential source of answers and salvation.
