# Captain Blackskull

## Identity
- Referenced by title "Captain"; no faction affiliation specified beyond a connection to the Deepking's court
- Has not appeared directly; known only through a stolen contract found on Eldgrim's desk

## Personality & Motivations
- Suspected by the Deepking of harboring ambitions for his throne since she was young. Her core drive, at least as the Deepking perceives it, is seizing power from him.

## History with the Party
- The party discovered a contract on Eldgrim's desk that references Captain Blackskull. The contract, apparently authored or ordered by the Deepking, states she has "always had an eye on my throne" and directs that she receive "what's coming to her" — implying an assassination or punishment order.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Unknown
- **Active plans:** The Deepking has issued orders (via the stolen contract) for action to be taken against her
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows the Deepking considers her treasonous and has ordered retribution; they do not know her current whereabouts, her side of the story, or whether the contract has already been acted upon

## Relationships
- **The Deepking:** Views her as a long-standing threat to his rule; has ordered consequences against her
- **Eldgrim:** Possessed the contract regarding Blackskull on his desk, suggesting he is involved in carrying out or facilitating the Deepking's orders
- **The Party:** No direct interaction; they know of her only through the stolen document
