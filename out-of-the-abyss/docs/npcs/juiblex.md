# Juiblex

## Identity
- Demon lord of oozes
- Operated in the region through his proxy, the Pudding King
- No direct appearance; presence felt as a demonic force permeating the environment

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven by an insatiable hunger to consume and grow. Views other demon lords' power as fuel for his own return.
- His core stated goal is to consume Zuggtmoy ("the queen of the fungi") and her power in order to be reborn and grow stronger.

## History with the Party
- The party defeated the Pudding King, who had been channeling Juiblex's power.
- Upon the Pudding King's defeat, Juiblex's toxic, oppressive demonic presence visibly receded from the cavern—described as seeping away like water draining from a basin.
- A demonic voice (possibly Juiblex himself) echoed through the chamber, declaring: *"You haven't won. I will be reborn. I will consume the banquet of the queen of the fungi, and I will grow."*

## Current Status
- **Last known status:** His presence has withdrawn from the cavern following the Pudding King's defeat. Current location unknown.
- **Active plans:** Intends to return by consuming Zuggtmoy's power.
- **What the party knows:** They heard his threat directly and know he plans to target Zuggtmoy. They understand he is diminished but not destroyed.
- **What remains hidden:** His current whereabouts, timeline for return, and how he intends to reach or consume Zuggtmoy.

## Relationships
- **The Pudding King:** Former proxy and channel for Juiblex's power in the material world. Now defeated.
- **Zuggtmoy ("Queen of the Fungi"):** Rival demon lord and Juiblex's declared target. He intends to consume her power.
- **The Party:** Views them as obstacles but not as a true threat to his long-term plans, based on the dismissive tone of his parting words.
