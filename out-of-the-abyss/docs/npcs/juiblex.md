# Juiblex

## Identity
- Demon lord of oozes
- Operated in the region through his proxy, the Pudding King
- No direct appearance; presence felt as a demonic force permeating the environment

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Return to power by consuming Zuggtmoy ("the queen of the fungi") and her domain. Driven by an insatiable hunger to grow and consume.
- Radiates a toxic, oppressive presence. Communicates through disembodied proclamations—threatening, certain, and patient. Does not consider defeat permanent.

## History with the Party
- The party encountered Juiblex's influence indirectly through the Pudding King, who channeled the demon lord's power.
- Upon the Pudding King's defeat, the party felt Juiblex's demonic presence recede from the cavern—described as seeping away like water draining from a basin.
- A demonic voice, possibly Juiblex himself, echoed through the chamber afterward, declaring: *"You haven't won. I will be reborn. I will consume the banquet of the queen of the fungi, and I will grow."*

## Current Status
- **Last known status:** His immediate influence over the cavern has receded following the Pudding King's defeat. No known physical manifestation.
- **Active plans:** Intends to return by consuming Zuggtmoy's power. Timeline and method unknown.
- **What the party knows:** They heard the threat directly. They know Juiblex considers the Pudding King's defeat a setback, not an ending, and that he has designs on Zuggtmoy.
- **What remains hidden:** How and when Juiblex plans to act on this threat; his current location or state; the full scope of his remaining influence.

## Relationships
- **The Pudding King** — Former conduit/proxy for Juiblex's power (now defeated).
- **Zuggtmoy** — Rival demon lord; Juiblex explicitly intends to consume her power ("the banquet of the queen of the fungi").
- **The Party** — Enemies. They disrupted his proxy's operations; he has promised his return directly to them.
