# Basidia

## Identity
- Co-Sovereign of Neverlight Grove, sharing power with Phylo
- Faction: Loyalist myconids opposed to Phylo's corruption — allied with the Circle of Hunters and Circle of Explorers
- First met the party when they arrived at Neverlight Grove; released spores and offered to guide them through the grove as an alternative to Phylo

## Personality & Motivations
Basidia's core drive is preserving traditional myconid society — the single group meld — against Phylo's hierarchical restructuring under the influence of the "Great Seeder." They are cautious, politically aware, and deeply loyal to their people, but also pragmatic enough to seek outside help rather than risk personal corruption. Basidia is emotionally expressive for a myconid, showing visible grief when forced to abandon Phylo and the grove. They demonstrate quiet courage, ultimately attempting to confront Phylo directly despite their own fears.

## History with the Party
1. **Initial Meeting:** Welcomed the party and offered a tour of the gardens to speak privately, away from Phylo. Confided concerns about missing "softers" taken to the Garden of Welcome, the Circle of Masters hoarding carcasses and sending secret expeditions, and Phylo's potential infection by a diseased spore. Could not recognize Sarith's spores, which disturbed them.
2. **Tour of the Grove:** Guided the party through Neverlight Grove, introducing circle leaders and identifying which were allies and which were Phylo-aligned. Confirmed Hepbobe's food was uncontaminated. Introduced Rusharoo (Circle of Explorers) as a friend and privately arranged a secret passageway into the Circle of Welcome for the party. Told Jimjar he had not been infected. Assured Grygum that Rumpadump and Stool were happy among their kind.
3. **Offered Rewards:** Gave the party a scroll of protection against fiends upfront. Offered to animate a Grick corpse the party brought (despite the stripped head). Promised additional treasures and potions if they investigated and brought back Phylo.
4. **Confrontation with Phylo:** After learning what the party found in the Inner Circle, Basidia insisted on confronting Phylo directly, accusing them of betrayal and alliance with a monster. Refused to flee despite the party's urging. Zalthir physically grappled and dragged Basidia away. Basidia eventually relented upon witnessing the madness of the awakening trees, overcome with grief as Phylo pleaded for them to stay.
5. **Departure:** In the Circle of Explorers, Basidia gave the party potions of greater healing and accumulated myconid treasures. Departed Neverlight Grove with Rusharoo, Loobamub, and the circles of hunters and explorers.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Departed Neverlight Grove with Rusharoo, Loobamub, and loyal myconid circles (hunters and explorers). Destination unknown.
- **Active Plans:** Presumably leading a myconid refugee group; no stated next steps.
- **Known vs. Hidden:** The party knows Basidia's full perspective on the corruption. It remains unclear whether Basidia has a plan to reclaim the grove or where they are headed.

## Relationships
- **Phylo:** Former co-sovereign and close partner ("We used to work so well together"). Now estranged — Basidia views Phylo as corrupted and a betrayer of the Circle, though clearly grieves the loss.
- **Rusharoo:** Trusted ally, leader of the Circle of Explorers. Basidia confided privately in Rusharoo and they departed together.
- **Loobamub:** Allied circle leader who departed with Basidia.
- **The Party:** Primary ally within Neverlight Grove. Entrusted them with investigating the corruption and rewarded them generously. Zalthir physically saved Basidia from a suicidal confrontation.
- **Sarith:** Could not identify his spores, found this deeply strange; could not confirm if the same disease affecting Phylo was also affecting Sarith.
