# Gurnik Tapfinger

## Identity
- Cleric, affiliated with the Steadfast Stone faction/temple
- First appeared at the grand moot, where he presented alongside Nomi Pathshutter

## Personality & Motivations
- Deeply invested in cleansing the Temple of the Steadfast Stone and awakening its guardians. Advocates strongly for this course of action even when simpler alternatives are proposed. His presentation at the moot was rehearsed and coordinated with Nomi Pathshutter, suggesting he is politically organized and prepared — not just a spiritual figure but someone who lobbies for his priorities.

## History with the Party
- **Grand Moot:** Explained the mechanics of the temple cleansing ritual to the assembled group — a red spell gem must be placed into a menhir, which will provoke Ogremoch's Bane into sending attackers. The party would need to defend the menhir until three temple guardians awaken. He gave a rehearsed joint presentation with Nomi Pathshutter extolling the benefits of Earth Elementals. When Daz pointed out a simpler northern attack route, Gurnik doubled down on the temple plan, arguing the Earth Elementals would be a significant asset in battle.

## Additional History
- **Temple Mission:** Sent the party on the mission to hallow Ogremoch's temple.
- **Reward:** Upon the party's successful completion, rewarded them with an empty fourth-level spell gem. The party loaded it with a healing spell.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Blingdenstone, post-temple cleansing
- **Active plans:** Temple mission completed successfully.
- **Known vs. hidden:** The party knows the full ritual mechanics he described.

## Relationships
- **Nomi Pathshutter:** Collaborative partner; they coordinated a rehearsed presentation together at the moot, indicating shared goals around the temple cleansing and Earth Elemental alliance.
- **Daz:** Mild tension — Daz proposed an alternative (northern attack route) that Gurnik dismissed in favor of his temple plan.
- **Ogremoch's Bane:** Antagonistic; the cleansing ritual Gurnik advocates would directly provoke this entity.
