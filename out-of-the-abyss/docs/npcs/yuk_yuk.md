# Yuk-Yuk

## Identity
- Goblin hireling / companion
- First joined the party after being convinced by Jimjar to follow the group into dangerous tunnels, along with fellow goblin Spiderbait, in exchange for 20 gold

## Personality & Motivations
- Motivated primarily by coin — agreed to venture into dangerous territory for 20 gold. Little else is known of his personality; he did not survive long enough to reveal much beyond a willingness to take risks for pay.

## History with the Party
- **Hired:** Jimjar persuaded Yuk-Yuk and Spiderbait to accompany the party into the Silken Tunnels and onward into the Underdark for 20 gold.
- **Traveled** with the group through the Silken Tunnels and into the Underdark.
- **Murdered (1st day, 3rd Tenday of Taraskh, 1493):** Found dead in his sleep with his throat cut by a shortsword. Spiderbait discovered the body and was screaming. Grygum observed that any member of the group could have been the killer.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Murdered in his sleep; throat cut with a shortsword.
- The killer has not been identified. Grygum noted that anyone in the group could be responsible.
- It is unknown whether the party investigated further or if the murder remains unsolved.

## Relationships
- **Spiderbait:** Fellow goblin companion; discovered Yuk-Yuk's body and reacted with visible distress.
- **Jimjar:** The one who recruited Yuk-Yuk and Spiderbait into the group.
- **Grygum:** Commented on the murder, noting any group member could be the culprit.
- **The Party:** Hired help; no deep bonds indicated before his death.

None noted.
