# Dorhun

## Identity
- Stone giant; former apprentice to Stonespeaker Hgraam
- Faction: Stone giants of Cairngorm Cavern; commands authority over Duergar Stone Guards
- First appeared after the party's battle with a crazed two-headed stone giant near the Blade Bazaar

## Personality & Motivations
- Somber and taciturn, consistent with stone giant temperament. Showed grief over the death of the crazed giant but accepted the truth of events without hostility. Appears to value honesty and measured judgment — he verified Thorin's account before responding and did not assign blame unfairly.

## History with the Party
- **Blade Bazaar encounter:** Arrived flanked by Duergar armed with stone weapons immediately after the party fought a crazed two-headed stone giant. He stared at Thorin, whose body and blade bore evidence of the fight. Thorin explained he had tried to subdue the giant rather than kill it, and that the Duergar had delivered the killing blow. Dorhun confirmed the story, sighed, and introduced himself and his companion Rihaud as apprentices to Stonespeaker Hgraam. He requested that Thorin visit him at Cairngorm Cavern before leaving the city and instructed the Stone Guards to grant the party passage.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Presumably at or near Cairngorm Cavern
- **Active plans:** Awaiting Thorin's visit at Cairngorm Cavern; reason for the invitation not yet revealed
- **Hidden information:** His specific agenda for the meeting is unknown; the party does not yet know why the two-headed giant was crazed or what broader situation Dorhun may be dealing with

## Relationships
- **Stonespeaker Hgraam:** Dorhun's master; Dorhun serves (or served) as his apprentice
- **Rihaud:** Fellow apprentice to Hgraam; accompanied Dorhun during the Blade Bazaar encounter
- **Duergar Stone Guards:** Dorhun commands or has authority over them
- **Thorin (party member):** Primary point of contact; Dorhun addressed Thorin directly and invited him specifically to Cairngorm Cavern
