# Dorhun

## Identity
- Stone giant; apprentice to Stonespeaker Hgraam
- First appeared after the party's battle with a crazed two-headed stone giant outside the Blade Bazaar, arriving flanked by Duergar armed with stone weapons

## Personality & Motivations
- Somber and taciturn, consistent with stone giant nature. Showed visible resignation (sighed) upon confirming the death of the crazed giant. Appears to serve as a representative or enforcer for Stonespeaker Hgraam's interests. Values truth — he confirmed Thorin's account of events rather than assuming the worst.

## History with the Party
- Arrived on the scene after the party fought a crazed two-headed stone giant. Stared at Thorin, whose body and blade showed evidence of the fight. After Thorin explained he had tried to subdue rather than kill the giant and that the Duergar delivered the killing blow, Dorhun confirmed the account and sighed. He introduced himself and his companion Rihaud as apprentices to Stonespeaker Hgraam. He asked Thorin to visit him at **Cairngorm Cavern** before leaving the city and instructed the Stone Guards to grant the party passage.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Vicinity of the Blade Bazaar, presumably returning to Cairngorm Cavern
- **Active plans:** Awaiting Thorin's visit at Cairngorm Cavern; the purpose of the meeting is unknown
- **Hidden information:** The reason Dorhun wants Thorin to visit Cairngorm Cavern has not been revealed; the party does not yet know what Stonespeaker Hgraam wants or what is happening with the stone giants (e.g., the crazed two-headed giant)

## Relationships
- **Stonespeaker Hgraam** — Master; Dorhun serves as his apprentice and apparent emissary
- **Rihaud** — Fellow apprentice to Hgraam; accompanied Dorhun during the encounter
- **Thorin** — Directly addressed Thorin and invited him specifically to Cairngorm Cavern; seemed to accept Thorin's honesty about the giant fight
- **Stone Guards / Duergar** — Commands or coordinates with Duergar armed with stone weapons; has authority to instruct Stone Guards to grant passage
