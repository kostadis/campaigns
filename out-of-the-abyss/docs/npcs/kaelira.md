# Kaelira

## Identity
- Sister of Nym Duskryn
- Affiliated with Lolth (identified by Daz as a "friend of Lolth")
- First appeared during the battle to extract Daz alive, as part of a contract

## Personality & Motivations
- Direct, physical, and fearless in combat situations — willing to drag someone bodily through poisonous gas and an insect-choked cavern. Blunt and irreverent in speech ("Come with me if you want to live, you idiot."). Appears motivated by the contract to extract Daz alive rather than any personal attachment.

## History with the Party
- **Extraction Battle:** Participated in a contracted mission to extract Daz alive. During the fight, she physically grabbed Daz's arm and hauled him northeast through an insect-choked cavern filled with poisonous gas, bringing him to a safer position where her brother Nym was waiting. When Asha challenged her presence, Daz vouched for her, identifying her as a "friend of Lolth."

## Current Status
- Last seen at the extraction point where Nym was waiting, having successfully delivered Daz
- No further plans or operations noted
- The party knows she is Nym's sister and connected to Lolth; deeper motivations and the details of the extraction contract remain unclear

## Relationships
- **Nym Duskryn:** Brother; they coordinated on the extraction contract, with Kaelira delivering Daz to Nym's position
- **Daz:** Extraction target; he recognized and vouched for her, suggesting prior acquaintance or at least awareness of her allegiance
- **Asha:** Challenged Kaelira's presence during the battle; relationship is tense or at minimum distrustful
