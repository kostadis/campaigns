# Nomi Pathshutter

## Identity
- Major leader of Blingdenstone
- First appeared at the grand moot alongside other Blingdenstone leaders

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Cleanse the Temple of the Steadfast Stone to enable the summoning of Earth Elementals, protected from the corrupting influence of Ogremoch's Bane.
- Appears politically coordinated and strategic — delivered what seemed to be a rehearsed joint presentation with Gurnik Tapfinger at the grand moot, suggesting careful preparation and alliance-building.

## History with the Party
- **Grand Moot:** Presented alongside Gurnik Tapfinger, proposing a plan to cleanse the Temple of the Steadfast Stone. The proposal argued that doing so would allow Earth Elementals to be summoned safely, shielded from being driven insane by Ogremoch's Bane. The presentation appeared rehearsed and coordinated between the two leaders.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Blingdenstone (at the grand moot)
- **Active plans:** Seeking support for the cleansing of the Temple of the Steadfast Stone
- **Party knowledge:** The party is aware of her proposal and its connection to the Ogremoch's Bane threat. It is unclear what further details about the temple, the summoning process, or the nature of Ogremoch's Bane she may be withholding.

## Relationships
- **Gurnik Tapfinger:** Close political ally; the two coordinated a joint presentation at the grand moot, suggesting an established working relationship.
- **Blingdenstone:** One of the settlement's major leaders.
- **The Party:** No direct personal interaction noted beyond the moot presentation.
