# Prince Derendil

## Identity
- Quaggoth prisoner at Velkynvelve who claims to be a cursed elven prince
- Claims to be prince of the "Kingdom of Nelrindenvane" in the High Forest, allegedly transformed by an evil wizard named Terrestor
- First appeared as a fellow prisoner in the Velkynvelve slave pen

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: escape captivity and presumably return to his "true" elven form
- Speaks with a rarefied upper-kingdom elven accent and demands respect befitting his claimed royal station. Refers to his Quaggoth body as "hideous," fully committed to his identity as a transformed prince. Shows courage and willingness to fight, declaring himself "already armed in this hideous form." Provides thoughtful tactical and political observations, such as analyzing Jorlan's motives and noting Drow guard positions.

## History with the Party
- **Velkynvelve (slave pen):** Met the party as a fellow prisoner. Provided tactical intelligence about the three Drow guards stationed across from the cell door. Daz identified him as a liar — no Kingdom of Nelrindenvane exists in the High Forest — but chose not to confront him, seeing the "Fake Prince" as a useful escape ally.
- **Work detail:** Assigned to water-carrying duty under Beremil alongside Ront and Thorin. Recalled Ilvara's arrival greeting in vivid detail. Was threatened by Ilvara with a knife after objecting to his treatment.
- **Escape planning:** Contributed political insight, noting there is "no abolitionist movement amongst those who worship Llolth" and that Jorlan's offer to help must be self-serving.
- **Escape battle:** Charged at elite Drow warrior Imbros, declaring it would bring him great joy to kill him. Was struck twice with Drow Poison and ultimately stabbed to death by Imbros.
- **Posthumous:** Remembered by Zalthir upon encountering quaggoth spore servants, who recalled him as "the mad quagoth, who was a polymorphed elf."

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed by Imbros during the Velkynvelve escape.
- His death deeply affected Serith and was noted as a significant loss to the escape party.

## Relationships
- **Daz:** Saw through his claims but kept silent, viewing him as a useful ally.
- **Serith:** Deeply affected by Derendil's death.
- **Thorin / Ront:** Fellow water-carrying work detail under Beremil.
- **Ilvara:** Subjected to her threats and domination; she threatened him with a knife for insubordination.
- **Imbros:** His killer.
- **Zalthir:** Remembered him fondly/notably after his death.

## Arc Score Events
- Derendil's death during the escape — likely a negative arc event for party morale, particularly impacting Serith.
