# Fargas Rumblefoot

## Identity
- Halfling adventurer, member of a lost adventuring band
- First encountered alive inside a moving spider cocoon in the Silken Paths; communicated telepathically via Stool's rapport spores

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: survival — he was desperate to be freed and willing to trade valuable information to incentivize rescue.
- Shrewd but not deceitful; he tried to walk back his promise of treasure when pressed but quickly relented under social pressure. Grygum assessed he was telling the truth. He has a sense of dignity — was very miffed when Zalthir touched his belly to check for gnoll spawn.

## History with the Party
1. **Silken Paths (Discovery):** Found cocooned by spiders as a "snack for later." Explained his adventuring band had been searching for a lost tomb when they were attacked by gnolls. Claimed he escaped without being infected. Offered knowledge of a hidden treasure to motivate the party to free him. Thorin was preparing to cut him free as spiders closed in.
2. **Post-Rescue Interrogation:** Zalthir pressed him to reveal the treasure. He reluctantly shared detailed knowledge of the **floating tomb of Brysis of Khaem** — a half-elf sorcerer from the era of Faerûn's great empires of magic. Explained the tomb fell into the Underdark when Netheril collapsed. Revealed the critical secret: most treasure hunters find a **false tomb** with a fake sarcophagus, but the real treasure lies in a hidden chamber accessible via a secret passageway from the servants' tomb. Said the tomb is on the **eastern edge of the Darklake** but doesn't know the precise location. Submitted to Zalthir's gnoll-spawn check with indignation.
3. **Tomb Exploration (Validated):** His warning about the false tomb proved accurate — the party encountered a suspiciously easy-to-open sarcophagus with a magical trap, exactly as he described.

## Current Status
- Last known status: freed from the cocoon and traveling with or near the party (exact current location unclear from notes).
- No active plans mentioned beyond survival.
- The party knows everything he shared; no indication of hidden information remaining, though he admitted he doesn't know the tomb's precise location.

## Relationships
- **Zalthir:** Tense dynamic — Zalthir pressured him for information and physically checked him for gnoll spawn, which Fargas resented.
- **Grygum:** Took notes on Fargas's promises and served as a truth-checker; assessed Fargas as honest.
- **Thorin:** Acted to free him from the cocoon.
- **Stool:** Served as communication bridge via rapport spores.
- **His adventuring band:** Lost after the gnoll attack; fate unknown.
