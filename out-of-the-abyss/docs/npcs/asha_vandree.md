# Asha Vandree

## Identity
Drow priestess, devout follower of Lolth, former subordinate of Ilvara at the drow outpost. *Also known as: Asha, Asha Vandry.*

## Personality & Motivations
- True believer — sharp, doctrinally rigid, and deeply committed to Lolth
- Core goal: the death of Ilvara and destruction of her mushroom artifact; cannot accomplish this alone
- Ambitious — actively positioning herself to challenge or replace Ilvara as commander
- Paranoid and contemptuous of those she considers beneath her
- Pragmatic enough to use others for violence she cannot do herself, but everything filters through her faith
- Refuses physical contact out of visceral fear of fungal contamination
- Shows flashes of relatable exasperation, particularly regarding Jorlan and Ilvara's unresolved romantic entanglement

## History with the Party
1. **Intelligence from Jimjar:** Party learned through Jimjar that Asha was "angling for the top job" at Velkynvelve, seeking to supplant Ilvara as commander
2. **Fungal Cavern Encounter:** Found as one of only three individuals not yet enslaved by Ilvara's spores
3. **Daz's Deception Attempt:** Daz tried to pass himself off as Lolth-touched using Dancing Lights absorbed into his spider familiar. Asha recognized the con immediately but reframed it as a divine test of her own discernment, believing she had passed Lolth's test by identifying him as a charlatan. She rationalized keeping him as "Perhaps my prayers have been answered through this… confused vessel"
4. **Intelligence Briefing:** Provided critical tactical intelligence before the fight — revealed Ilvara had been seduced by Zuggtmoy's influence, that her allies had turned into mindless fungal creatures, and that only she and Jorlan remained uninfected. Identified the mushroom artifact and Big Heart Fungus as key threats. Warned that Thorin and Grygum specifically must not approach the artifact due to a marker from Whorlstone Caverns. Advised targeting the Heart Fungus with their most powerful spell rather than Ilvara directly
5. **Alliance Negotiation:** Concluded tentative alliance — party kills Ilvara and destroys the mushroom artifact in exchange for everything Ilvara has accumulated and "the gratitude of Lolth"
6. **Tactical Role:** Held the perimeter during battle rather than engaging directly, handling external threats while the party did close-range killing
7. **Confrontation with Duskryn Sisters:** When Kaelira and Nym appeared, immediately moved for her weapon, denouncing them as traitors to Lolth. Daz defused this by introducing them as Lolth-aligned allies
8. **Post-Battle Prayer:** After Ilvara's death, dropped to her knees murmuring prayers to Lolth, already reshaping events into a narrative her goddess could use

## Current Status
**Last known location:** The cavern where Ilvara was killed, praying to Lolth over the aftermath
**Active plans:** Building a theological narrative around the events — the destruction of a Zuggtmoy-corrupted priestess, the party's role, and her own survival through faith
**Known vs. hidden:** Party knows she is a genuine Lolth zealot who manipulated them into serving her ends. Hidden: what theology she is constructing from this moment and whether Lolth will actually answer her prayers

## Relationships
- **Daz:** Views him as "an insane mage who had converted to Lolthism" — sees through his deceptions but keeps him as a useful vessel she can frame within her worldview. Treats him as the group's leader and only worthy interlocutor
- **Ilvara:** Deep personal hatred, not merely political. Former superior corrupted by Zuggtmoy. Wanted her butchered and sent to "an unearthly grave"
- **Jorlan:** Fellow uninfected drow at the outpost who survived through hatred rather than faith. Asha exhausted by mediating his unresolved romantic drama with Ilvara
- **Kaelira & Nym Duskryn:** Initially hostile, suspected them as traitors to Lolth. Accepted Daz's lie that they were Lolth-aligned allies
- **Thorin:** Warned him specifically about the Whorlstone marker. Thorin acknowledged with concern that her prayers to Lolth might actually be answered
- **Zalthir & Grygum:** Dismissed with disdain or outright ignored. Openly mocked Grygum's tactical suggestions. Grygum watches her warily
- **Glabbagool:** Initially alarmed, then accepted as Daz's bound familiar within her theological framework
- **Jimjar:** Aware of her ambitions and willing to share that information with the party
