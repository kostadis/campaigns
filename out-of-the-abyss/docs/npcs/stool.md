# Stool

## Identity
- Myconid sprout (child), member of the Neverlight Grove circle
- Fellow prisoner at Velkynvelve; met the party in the slave pen
- Communicates via rapport spores that create a telepathic group mind

## Personality & Motivations
Stool's core drive is returning home to the Neverlight Grove and reuniting with his people. He is childlike, emotionally needy, and desperate for friendship and validation — but also surprisingly perceptive and socially strategic (e.g., turning to Thorin when Grygum wouldn't budge). He craves being useful, frequently celebrating his own helpfulness: "I'm helpful, aren't I? I love being helpful." He is easily frightened and prone to crying when scared or denied, but fiercely loyal to his friends and circle-mates.

## History with the Party
- **Velkynvelve:** Imprisoned with the party; assigned to chamber pot duty with Daz and Sarith. His rapport spores became the group's secret communication method, enabling escape planning without alerting the Drow.
- **Post-Escape:** Curled up against Grygum during a long rest, cried when pushed away, and emotionally bonded with Grygum over the orc's inspirational speech. They became "best friends" and marched together.
- **Rockfall Incident:** Relieved to find Grygum alive. Too slow to flee on his own, so Thorin carried him on his back — a recurring arrangement Stool loved.
- **Silken Paths:** Used rapport spores at Zalthir's request to communicate with a trapped halfling (Fargas Rumblefoot) inside a cocoon. Partnered with Grygum to approach it.
- **Mushroom Identification:** Spotted and identified Nilhogg's Nose near a bottomless gorge, explaining its properties (enhances smell dramatically).
- **Duergar Keelboat Ambush:** Captured by duergar; Thorin noticed and attacked, freeing Stool.
- **Gracklstugh — Dancing Myconids:** Recognized the distressed sprout Rumpadump as his friend from his own circle. Insisted myconids don't dance and something was wrong. Sobbed when Zalthir hesitated to prioritize a rescue, successfully guilt-tripping Thorin into adding it to the task list.
- **Gracklstugh — Assassin Mission:** Served as telepathic relay for Daz's plan. Waited outside the assassin's lair, looking worried.
- **Rescuing Rumpadump:** Persistently demanded the party save "Rump-a-dump." Strategically appealed to Thorin when Grygum raised concerns about myconid mind-control. Excitedly jumped up and down upon reuniting with Rumpadump.
- **Whorlestone Caves Departure:** Chatted happily with Rumpadump as the group left.
- **Neverlight Grove Arrival:** Ran joyfully into the grove shouting "We're home!" Told stories about the party to Sovereign Phylo — praising Thorin for carrying him and calling Zalthir grouchy but secretly nice. Phylo silenced him. Basidia assured the party Stool was happy among his kind.
- **Neverlight Grove Departure:** Grygum convinced Stool and Rumpadump the grove was no longer safe. Said goodbye to Daz with sadness before departing with other myconids.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Departed the Neverlight Grove with Rumpadump and other myconids.
- **Situation:** Homeless — the grove is compromised. Traveling with the myconid group, no longer with the main party.
- **What the party knows:** Stool confirmed myconids don't normally dance or speak of "the Lady's dream," validating that something is deeply wrong at Neverlight Grove.

## Relationships
- **Grygum:** "Best friends." Deep emotional bond formed in Velkynvelve. Grygum is protective but also pragmatic about Stool's naivety regarding myconid dangers. Stool idolizes him.
- **Thorin:** Stool's preferred mount and backup emotional advocate. Thorin is soft-hearted toward Stool and can be persuaded by his tears.
- **Zalthir:** Frequently asks Stool to use his spores for tactical purposes. Stool calls him grouchy but secretly a "softer." Zalthir is more utilitarian in the relationship.
- **Daz:** Said goodbye with visible sadness upon parting; shared chamber pot duty in Velkynvelve.
- **Rumpadump:** Childhood friend from the same circle. Stool was the driving force behind his rescue and the two are now traveling together.
- **Phylo & Basidia (Neverlight Grove Sovereigns):** Stool's leaders. Basidia vouched for his happiness; Phylo silenced his storytelling.
