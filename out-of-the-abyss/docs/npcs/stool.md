# Stool

## Identity
- Myconid sprout (child), member of the Neverlight Grove circle
- Fellow prisoner at Velkynvelve; met the party in the slave pen
- Communicates via rapport spores that create a telepathic group mind

## Personality & Motivations
Stool's core drive is to return home to the Neverlight Grove and reunite with his people. He is childlike, emotionally expressive, and desperate for connection — clinging to Grygum after his inspirational speech and declaring him his best friend. He is eager to prove his usefulness, often exclaiming how helpful he is, and is surprisingly savvy at social manipulation (e.g., turning to Thorin when Grygum resists his pleas). He views the world with innocent certainty — myconids are harmless, quaggoths are bad — and reacts to setbacks with immediate, loud sobbing.

## History with the Party
- **Velkynvelve:** Imprisoned alongside the party. Assigned to chamber pot duty with Daz and Sarith. His rapport spores became the group's primary method of covert coordination, enabling escape planning without alerting the drow.
- **Escape & Underdark Travel:** Curled up against Grygum during rest; cried when pushed away but won Grygum over with earnest affection. Rode on Thorin's back when the group fled drow scouts due to his slow movement speed. His rapport spores proved tactically crucial — Zalthir used the mind link to communicate a hiding spot.
- **Silken Paths:** Used rapport spores to communicate with a trapped halfling (Fargas Rumblefoot) inside a cocoon. Identified Nilhogg's Nose mushroom near a gorge and explained its properties.
- **Duergar Ambush:** Captured during the keelboat ambush; Thorin noticed and attacked the duergar, who released Stool.
- **Gracklstugh:** Observed dancing myconids and became distressed — insisted myconids don't dance and something was wrong. Recognized one non-dancing sprout as his friend Rumpadump from his own circle. Sobbed when Zalthir initially refused to prioritize a rescue, moving Thorin to add it to the task list. Served as telepathic relay for Daz's intelligence-sharing plan. Waited outside the assassin's lair and the final cavern.
- **Rumpadump Rescue:** Persistently demanded the party save Rumpadump. Strategically appealed to Thorin when Grygum pushed back on myconid safety. Jumped excitedly upon reuniting with Rumpadump.
- **Neverlight Grove:** Ran joyfully into the grove shouting "We're home!" Told stories about the party to Sovereign Phylo — praising Thorin for carrying him and calling Zalthir grouchy but secretly nice. Was silenced by Phylo. Basidia assured the party Stool was happy among his kind.
- **Departure from Neverlight Grove:** Grygum convinced Stool that the grove was no longer safe. Said a sad goodbye to Daz before departing with the other myconids.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Departed the Neverlight Grove with the myconid group after being convinced it was unsafe.
- **Current situation:** Traveling with the myconids, no longer at the grove. Exact whereabouts unknown.
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows the Neverlight Grove is compromised (referenced "the Lady's dream" and frantic dancing as signs of corruption). Stool does not understand these phenomena either.

## Relationships
- **Grygum:** Best friend. Bonded over Grygum's inspirational speech in Velkynvelve. Grygum recognizes Stool's tactical value (telepathy) and emotional manipulation skills. Grygum ultimately convinced Stool to leave the grove.
- **Thorin:** Carried Stool on his back during travel. Stool adores Thorin and told stories about him at the grove. Stool knows Thorin is a soft touch and appeals to him when others refuse.
- **Zalthir:** Called him grouchy but "really a nice softer" who saved Rumpadump. Zalthir frequently tasked Stool with using rapport spores for reconnaissance and communication.
- **Daz:** Shared chamber pot duty at Velkynvelve. Stool relayed Daz's plans via telepathy in Gracklstugh. Said a sad goodbye when departing.
- **Rumpadump:** Childhood friend from the same myconid circle. Stool was emotionally desperate to rescue him and the two were inseparable afterward.
- **Phylo:** Sovereign of the Neverlight Grove; silenced Stool's storytelling with a wave of the hand.
- **Basidia:** Another grove authority who assured the party of Stool's wellbeing.
