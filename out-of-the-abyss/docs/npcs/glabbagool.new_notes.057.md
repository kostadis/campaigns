# New notes for Glabbagool (from dossier_extract_057.md)

Glabbagool — the grey ooze — had been confined to the bag of holding for most of the day at Candlekeep. By the evening at the House of Rest, he was agitated and wanting out, which Thorin compared to keeping a puppy in a closet during a thunderstorm. The want was present even if the destination was unclear.

When the party was summoned before dawn to the crime scene, Glabbagool assessed the situation from his corner — door open, party dressed, urgency established, no visible threat — and made the decision to flow into the bag of holding himself, arrange himself inside, and peer out from the opening with two eyeballs tracking the room. Daz observed this and noted: "at least we have that."

Zalthir had been patiently teaching Glabbagool monastic discipline, which Glabbagool had apparently been attempting to formalize into practice during his time in the bag of holding.
