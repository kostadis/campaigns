# Sarith

## Identity
- Drow NPC, fellow escapee/prisoner from Velkynvelve
- Serves as the group's primary Underdark navigator and guide
- First appearance context not detailed in these notes, but he is established as part of the escaped prisoner group

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Survive the Underdark and stay ahead of the drow pursuit from Velkynvelve
- Pragmatic and survival-focused — immediately shouted "Rations!" after the group killed a giant centipede creature, urging them to harvest it for food. Prideful and somewhat prickly; visibly annoyed when Grygum spotted a tunnel route before he did, insisting "I saw that" and "I didn't need your help." Takes a natural leadership role in navigation and tactical decisions, issuing orders to others like Eldeth without hesitation. Possesses deep knowledge of the Underdark and shares it freely when survival demands it.

## History with the Party
- **Rockfall Incident:** Survived the rockfall unscathed (Zalthir confirmed he was not under the rocks). Buppido shouted that Sarith was supposed to rescue him per "the divine plan."
- **Centipede Fight:** Fought alongside the group using hand-held crossbow bolts, contributing to the kill alongside Buppido, Topsy, and Turvy.
- **Underdark Navigation Briefing:** Provided critical intelligence about Underdark tunnel systems — how they twist and change, how enemies can appear suddenly, and how non-flying surface dwellers ("Overbriters") are the easiest prey. Urged the group to keep moving.
- **Faerzess Cave:** Navigated and searched for the next tunnel; was beaten to the discovery by Grygum, which irritated him, though he grudgingly acknowledged Grygum's usefulness.
- **End-of-Day Assessment:** Gave a status report — they had gained distance from the drow but had no water, which would become critical. Ordered Eldeth to cover their trail the next day and insisted they slow their pace. Revealed that **Ilvara has found and caught up with them**.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Traveling through Underdark tunnels with the escaped group
- **Active concerns:** No water supply; Ilvara and drow scouts are in active pursuit and have caught up
- **Operational plan:** Ordered trail-covering (assigned to Eldeth) and a slower pace for the next day
- **Party knows:** His Underdark expertise, his warning about Ilvara's proximity, the water shortage
- **Hidden:** No hidden information noted in these sessions

## Relationships
- **Buppido:** Buppido believes Sarith has a role in his "divine plan" and expected Sarith to rescue him from the rockfall
- **Grygum:** Mild friction — Sarith was annoyed when Grygum found the tunnel route first, though he acknowledged Grygum's contribution
- **Eldeth:** Sarith issued her a direct order to cover the group's trail, suggesting he sees himself in a command role over her (or at least over trail logistics)
- **Topsy & Turvy:** Fought alongside them against the centipede; no further dynamic noted
- **Ilvara / Velkynvelve Drow:** Former captors now in pursuit; Sarith is keenly aware of their tactics and proximity
- **The Party (general):** Positions himself as the Underdark authority figure; shares knowledge but maintains pride about his expertise
