# Suushar the Awakened

## Identity
- Kuo-Toan prisoner held in the slave pen at the Drow outpost
- Serves as translator and cultural guide for party members who don't speak Undercommon
- Self-styled spiritual figure among his race

## Personality & Motivations
- Claims to pursue a spiritual practice of balance to cope with "the madness of my race." In practice, this philosophy is inconsistent — he claims to omit offensive words but then translates every insult anyway.
- Eager to return to his people and share what he calls "the enlightenment." Advocates strongly for traveling to Darklake to reach them.
- Enthusiastic and excitable, particularly about Gyrgum's cooking — flatters freely, telling Gyrgum his people "might imagine a god like you."

## History with the Party
- **Reassuring Gyrgum:** When Gyrgum worried about Ront and Prince Derendil being taken away, Suushar calmed him by explaining the Drow chore rotation and identifying each taskmaster by name (Beremil — water, Balok — winch, Guldor — chamber pots, Imbros — cooking).
- **Kitchen Duty:** Relayed Topsy and Turvy's request for Gyrgum to create a distraction so they could steal food. Provided "translation" for the Drow, humorously claiming to filter out offensive language while doing no such thing.
- **Escape Planning:** Advocated traveling to Darklake first, arguing the direct route to Blingdenstone passes through Menzoberranzan. Pushed for the group to visit his Kuo-Toan people.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Slave pen at the Drow outpost
- **Active agenda:** Wants the group's escape route to go through Darklake so he can return to his people
- **Hidden vs. known:** The party has seen his spiritual claims don't fully hold up (the translation incident). His true intentions regarding his people and "the enlightenment" remain unclear.

## Relationships
- **Gyrgum:** Primary point of contact; acts as Gyrgum's translator and advisor. Flatters him openly.
- **Topsy & Turvy:** Cooperative enough to relay their food-theft scheme to Gyrgum.
- **Ront & Prince Derendil:** Aware of their situation; reassured Gyrgum about their safety.
- **Drow Taskmasters (Beremil, Balok, Guldor, Imbros):** Knows them by name and assignment, suggesting some degree of familiarity or observation.
