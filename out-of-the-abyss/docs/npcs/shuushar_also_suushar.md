## Personality & Motivations
- Appears sensitive to magical or environmental phenomena, particularly Faerzess. His reaction to the Faerzess suggests he has prior familiarity with it — he previously regarded it as merely a light, but now perceives it as something toxic and changed. This unsettles him.

## History with the Party
1. **Rockfall incident:** Was injured or incapacitated during a rockfall. Grygum directed Stool to go help him.
2. **Faerzess cave passage:** While the group moved through a cave suffused with Faerzess, Shuushar was visibly uncomfortable. Afterward, he remarked: *"That was odd. It was the first time I had felt so strange. The Faerzess was just a light, but now it seems to be a poison."* — indicating something fundamental has changed about the Faerzess.

## Current Status

- Physically recovering (was aided by Stool after the rockfall)
- **Hidden information:** His observation about Faerzess changing from harmless light to "poison" may signal a larger corruption spreading through the Underdark — significance not yet fully understood by the party
