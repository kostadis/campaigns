# Jadgar

## Identity
Ghost of a Svirfneblin (deep gnome), three feet tall, former Borough/Burrow Warden of Blingdenstone who defended the city against Drow and other threats. *Also known as: Burrow Warden Jadger, Jadger, Uth Jadgar, Uth-Jadgar.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Re-establish the Borough Wardens by secretly training young gnomes to serve in the reformed order
- Pragmatic and transactional — willing to share knowledge but demands something in return. Values fairness in exchanges and rewards effort
- Deeply protective of sacred ground and burial sites of the Burrow Wardens. Willing to engage in political negotiation rather than resort to obstruction alone
- Commands respect and ceremony — demonstrated by formal banner salutes
- Cheerful and pragmatic to the point of dark humor — casually noted that if the party died on their mission, they could still fight as ghosts via resurrection magic
- Takes pride in the legacy and tactical prowess of the Barrow Wardens. Appreciates well-crafted strategy
- Grateful and fair, rewarding service with meaningful boons
- Carries the weight of a guardian who never stopped protecting his city, even in death

## History with the Party
1. **Ghost Mission Assignment:** Initially assigned a group including Glabbagool, Eldeth, Jimjar, and Spiderbait to handle the ghost problem in Blingdenstone's catacombs, specifically requesting rescue of certain ghosts including Udhask
2. **Council Meeting:** Jadgar's ghost faction objected to the Miners Guild's plans to strip-mine the sanctum, considering it sacred burial grounds. A sustainable mining compromise was brokered that respected the burial sites
3. **Post-Mission Thanks:** After the party put two tormented spirits to rest, Jadgar materialized as a friendly ghost to personally thank them, offering two boons—any two questions he would answer truthfully
4. **Catacombs Encounter:** Appeared to the party in the catacombs after Pelek's ghost moved on. Proposed a standing deal: for every ghost the party helps put to rest, he answers one question about the Underdark
5. **Ooze Investigation:** When asked about the ooze problem, explained that oozes are normally solitary — a mass infestation is highly unusual. Zalthir raised a possible connection to Juiblex
6. **Demonic Intelligence Exchange:** After Zalthir performed an impressive martial display as payment, Jadgar explained the distinction between devils (corrupt individuals through personal deals) and demons (empower proxies to cause widespread carnage). Advised searching for an intermediate agent — a proxy empowered by a demon to motivate the oozes
7. **Grand Moot:** Attended and praised the party's plan as "technically brilliant." Revealed that the Temple of the Steadfast Stone is located in the Rock Blight and must be cleansed before use as a staging area. Offered assistance of other Barrow Warden ghosts contingent on the party undertaking a quest. The party accepted and delegated Glabbagool, Eldev, and Jimjar to assist
8. **Departure from Blingdenstone:** Jadgar and the Burrow Warden ghosts dipped their spectral banners in salute as the Ember Vanguard left the city

## Current Status
- **Location:** The Ruby in the Rough temple in Blingdenstone / Catacombs beneath the temple
- **Active operations:** Secretly training young gnomes to re-establish the Borough Wardens; training the next generation of Burrow Wardens
- **Standing deals with party:** One answered question per ghost laid to rest (still active); two truthful questions available as reward from earlier service
- **Awaiting:** Completion of the quest proposed at the grand moot; Glabbagool, Eldev, and Jimjar assigned to assist
- **Hidden:** The identity of the young gnome he is mentoring has not yet been revealed; he withheld it until the party gathers more information

## Relationships

- **Young gnome protégé:** Unnamed mentee being groomed to lead the new Borough Wardens
- **Zalthir (party member):** Positive rapport — impressed by Zalthir's martial demonstration
- **Udhask:** A ghost Jadgar specifically wanted rescued from the catacombs
- **Miners Guild (Blingdenstone):** Tension over use of the sanctum resolved through sustainable mining compromise
- **Barrow Warden ghosts:** Leader or spokesperson; can mobilize them as allies if quest is fulfilled
- **Glabbagool, Eldev, Jimjar:** Assigned by the party to work with the Barrow Wardens on their quest
- **The Ember Vanguard/Party:** Respectful ally and cooperative relationship built on mutual exchange; grateful for their service to Blingdenstone
