# Borough Warden Jadgar

## Identity
- Ghost of a Svirfneblin (deep gnome), approximately three feet tall
- Former Borough Warden of Blingdenstone; defender against Drow and other Underdark threats
- Currently working to secretly re-establish the Borough Wardens
- First appeared in the catacombs beneath the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough, manifesting immediately after Pelek's spirit departed

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: rebuild the Borough Wardens by secretly training young gnomes to defend Blingdenstone once more
- Pragmatic and transactional — willing to share valuable knowledge but expects something in return. Trades answers about the Underdark for ghosts the party puts to rest. Showed flexibility by also accepting a martial demonstration from Zalthir as payment for additional information. Clearly invested in the practical training of his recruits.

## History with the Party
1. **First Contact (Catacombs beneath the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough):** Appeared after Pelek's ghost was put to rest. Revealed his mission to rebuild the Borough Wardens and proposed a standing deal — one answered question per ghost the party helps move on.
2. **Ooze Intelligence:** When asked about the ooze infestation, explained that oozes are normally solitary creatures, making a mass gathering highly unusual.
3. **Juiblex Connection:** After Zalthir raised a possible link to Juiblex, Jadgar offered deeper insight in exchange for Zalthir demonstrating a monk technique useful for his trainees. Zalthir performed an impressive martial display, satisfying the bargain.
4. **Devils vs. Demons Briefing:** Explained that devils corrupt individuals through personal deals, while demons empower proxies to cause widespread carnage. Advised the party to search for an intermediate agent — someone or something a demon had empowered to motivate the oozes.
5. **Future Lead:** Mentioned a young gnome he is mentoring to lead the new Borough Wardens; suggested the party speak with this individual once they've gathered more information.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Catacombs beneath the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough
- **Active operations:** Secretly training young gnomes to form a new generation of Borough Wardens
- **Known to the party:** His deal (ghosts for answers), his analysis of the ooze threat, his advice to find a demonic proxy, and the existence of his young protégé
- **Hidden:** The identity of the young gnome he is mentoring; full extent of his knowledge about the Underdark and current threats

## Relationships
- **Pelek:** Fellow ghost; Jadgar appeared only after Pelek's spirit was put to rest, suggesting awareness of or connection to other Blingdenstone spirits
- **Young gnome protégé (unnamed):** Mentoring this individual to lead the reconstituted Borough Wardens
- **Zalthir (party member):** Positive rapport; Jadgar was impressed enough by Zalthir's monk technique to share additional intelligence and sees potential training value for his recruits
- **The Party (general):** Transactional but cooperative relationship; mutual benefit through the ghost-for-information exchange

## Additional History
6. **Catacomb Mission:** Jadger tasked a group including Glabbagool, Eldeth, Jimjar, and Spiderbait with resolving a ghost problem in Blingdenstone's catacombs. He specifically requested the rescue of certain ghosts, including Udhask. After the party put two tormented spirits to rest, Jadger materialized as a friendly ghost to personally thank them. He offered two boons — any two questions he would answer truthfully — and told them to find him at the Ruby and the Rough temple when ready to claim the reward. Casually noted that even if the party died on the mission, they could still be useful as ghosts through resurrection magic. **Outstanding: The party has two truthful answers they can claim from Jadger at any time.**
7. **Mining Dispute:** Jadger's ghost faction objected to the Miners Guild's proposal to strip-mine the sanctum, which the Burrow Wardens considered sacred burial grounds. A sustainable mining compromise was ultimately brokered.
8. **Farewell:** Jadger and the Burrow Warden ghosts were present to see the Ember Vanguard off, dipping their spectral banners in salute as the party left the city.
