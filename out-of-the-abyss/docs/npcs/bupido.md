# Bupido

## Identity
- Derro prisoner, fellow captive during the party's imprisonment
- First appeared during the prison escape, where he helped lead the other prisoners

## Personality & Motivations
- Initially appeared cooperative and helpful during the escape. His true nature was far darker — he maintained a hidden lair containing an altar to some demonic power and a collection of bones from his victims. He ultimately betrayed the party, killing one of their group members.

## History with the Party
1. **Prison Escape:** Led the other prisoners in rushing the guard chamber and armory during the distraction created by Thorin, Zalthir, and Daz's confrontation with the guards. Fired a crossbow at Imbros during the escape but missed.
2. **Betrayal (details not fully documented):** At some point after the escape, Bupido betrayed the party and killed one of their group members. The specifics of this event are not captured in these notes.
3. **Death (details not fully documented):** Bupido was killed at some point, as he is later referenced posthumously.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** His former lair — a grim space filled with dead bones and a demonic altar — was later used by the party as a resting place before their assault on a cultist cavern.
- His betrayal left a lasting impact on the party's trust; Daz specifically cites Bupido as the reason they now restrain uncertain allies at night.

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Initially an ally of convenience during the escape; became a traitor who murdered one of their companions. His name is now invoked as a cautionary tale.
- **Demonic Power (unknown):** Maintained an altar to some demonic entity in his lair, suggesting a dark religious devotion.
