# Princess Ebonmire (The House T'sarran Spy)

## Identity
- **Role:** Spy for House T'sarran
- **Faction:** House T'sarran
- **First Appearance:** Encountered and captured by the party, subsequently transported inside a bag alongside the gelatinous cube Glabbagool
- **Not to be confused with** the deceased ooze "Princess Ebonmire" killed by Thorin in the Pudding King's throne room (see `princess_ebonmire.md`) — distinct NPC who shares the name.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Serving House T'sarran's interests; escaping party custody
- Imperious and self-possessed — she attempts to commandeer her own imprisonment at Candlekeep by addressing the Gate Warden directly, as though the matter is already settled.
- Resourceful in seeking any avenue of escape or leverage, including appealing to institutional authority.
- Openly hostile toward Glabbagool, actively attempting to kill him despite being confined in a bag with him.

## History with the Party
1. **Captured** by the party and placed in their bag alongside Glabbagool.
2. **At Candlekeep's gate**, she was revealed when the bag was opened and drew the attention of the First Reader. Gate Warden Kalan Strongbranch was summoned.
3. Learning that Candlekeep releases prisoners rather than holding them long-term, she immediately pivoted — formally declaring herself a prisoner requesting Candlekeep's custody, framing it as non-negotiable.
4. The First Reader ruled that as a guest of the party, her disposition was the party's decision. She expressed considerable displeasure.
5. The party retained custody. She was returned to the bag.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Inside the party's bag, with Glabbagool
- **Active:** Ongoing attempts to kill Glabbagool; vocal objections to his meditation instruction
- **What the party knows:** She is a House T'sarran spy, she will use any available avenue to escape custody, and she is committed to killing Glabbagool
- **Hidden:** Her specific mission, what intelligence she has gathered, and what House T'sarran knows about the party

## Relationships
- **House T'sarran:** Agent and loyalist; her escape attempts appear motivated by returning to her handlers
- **Glabbagool:** Actively hostile — ongoing physical attempts to kill him; objects to his meditation practice
- **The Party:** Captive/custodian relationship; views them as a problem to be circumvented rather than negotiated with
- **Gate Warden Kalan Strongbranch:** Attempted to use him as an escape vector at Candlekeep's gate
- **Candlekeep First Reader:** Ruled against her bid for institutional custody
