# Eldeth

## Identity
- Female shield dwarf, former prisoner at Velkynvelve
- NPC companion traveling with the party since their escape from drow captivity
- Skilled tracker, forager, and spelunker; serves as the group's rear guard and trail-concealer
- First met as a fellow prisoner in the Velkynvelve slave pen

## Personality & Motivations
- Pragmatic survivalist with deep knowledge of Underdark flora, geology, and travel. Her core goal is reaching the surface—specifically Gauntlygrym, which she claims the Shield Dwarves now hold—and warning the surface world about the demonic incursion.
- Carries strong racial prejudices: contempt for duergar ("demonic spawn of dwarven corruption"), derro ("dwarven perversion"), and orcs; deeply skeptical of giants due to the War of the Giants. Blunt, matter-of-fact, and sometimes irritable ("Another day in the dark"), but fundamentally dedicated to keeping the group alive.
- Resourceful and self-reliant—pockets useful items, arms herself at every opportunity, and volunteers for dangerous missions without fanfare.

## History with the Party
1. **Velkynvelve (Captivity):** Met Thorin, bonded briefly over shared dwarven heritage but was put off by his Giant accent and pro-giant views. Warned the group about the anti-magic cell, assessed the drow garrison's strength, and suggested Gauntlygrym as a destination. Assigned to winch duty with Zalthir, Buppido, and Jimjar.
2. **Escape:** Armed herself with a hand crossbow from the armory; shot and helped bring down a drow guard during the breakout.
3. **Cave-in:** Trapped under rocks in the large cavern; rescued after Thorin urged the twins to save her and Jimjar.
4. **Underdark Travel:** Became the group's indispensable rear guard—hiding tracks, foraging waterorbs and Trillimac mushrooms, identifying dangerous Timmask mushrooms, and devising the rope-tether system at the Silken Paths. Zalthir credited her shrewdness with reducing drow pursuit to "a mere whisper." By the 8th day she confirmed they had lost their pursuers entirely.
5. **Darklake / Sloobludop:** Fled the melee during the chaos and had to be carried by Thorin to the boat after Demogorgon appeared—screamed and wailed but recovered to identify the demon prince and urge the group to warn the surface.
6. **Duergar Ambush (Water):** Survived the attack; cursed the duergar with open hatred. Buppido (under Tongue of Madness influence) blurted out that Eldeth is "not interested" in Thorin romantically.
7. **Gracklstugh:** Armed by Errde at Underlake Hold. Hit by poisoned projectiles during a battle; suffered a near-death experience in the mesa's upper chambers, which weighed on the party's conscience. Waited outside the final cavern with other NPCs. The party discovered evidence that the king has been working with assassins to kill Eldeth's clan—evidence they now possess but have not yet shared with her (sharing it would trigger violence).
8. **Continued Travel:** Hid the party's tracks through Faerzress crystal caves over multiple days, worried about environmental disturbances compromising concealment.
9. **Neverlight Grove:** Descended with Thorin; present during the Circle of Welcome battle but no specific actions noted.
10. **Blingdenstone:** Volunteered to tackle the ghost problem; helped destroy Vazuk, the tormented deep gnome ghost, arriving at Diggermattock Hall with Jimjar afterward. Identified the geological danger in the Oozing Temple as an underground river breaking through stone.

## Current Status

- **Hidden from the party:** Nothing noted
- **Hidden from Eldeth:** The party holds evidence that Gracklstugh's king conspired with assassins to kill her clan—they have not told her, and revealing it would provoke a violent reaction

- **Buppido:** He showed interest in her tracking techniques; she does not appear to reciprocate warmth

- **Zalthir:** Fellow winch-duty prisoner; he publicly praised her trail-hiding skill
- **Grygum:** Somewhat exasperated by his help but directed him to useful foraging
- **Daz:** Aware of his magical limitations in the cell; no strong personal bond noted
- **Spiderbait / Glabbagool:** Co-volunteers on the Blingdenstone ghost mission
- **Sarith:** Assigned her to trail-hiding duty; functional working relationship
- **Duergar / Derro / Orcs:** Open hostility and contempt
- **Shield Dwarves / Gauntlygrym:** Strong loyalty; considers it home and safe haven
