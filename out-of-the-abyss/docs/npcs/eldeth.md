# Eldeth

## Identity
- Female shield dwarf, former prisoner at Velkynvelve
- Party companion and NPC follower since the escape from drow captivity
- Affiliated with a shield dwarf clan; advocates for Gauntlygrym as a destination
- First met by the party in the slave pens of Velkynvelve, where she was pleased to encounter fellow dwarf Thorin (though put off by his Giant accent and pro-giant views)

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: survive the Underdark, get a warning about Demogorgon to the surface worlds, and protect her people (dwarven miners especially)
- Pragmatic, blunt, and competent — a natural survivalist who takes pride in her tracking and foraging skills. Carries strong racial prejudices: contempt for duergar ("demonic spawn of dwarven corruption"), orcs, and derro ("dwarven perversion"). She is skeptical and guarded in personal relationships but deeply invested in the group's collective survival. Her dry fatalism surfaces under pressure ("Then I suppose we all die").

## History with the Party
- **Velkynvelve:** Met the party in the slave pen. Warned them about anti-magic in the cell, assessed the overwhelming drow opposition, and suggested Gauntlygrym as an escape destination. Assigned to winch duty. Pocketed a comb from a cargo basket.
- **Escape:** Armed herself with a hand crossbow from the armory and helped kill a drow guard during the breakout.
- **Cave-in:** Trapped under rocks; Thorin ordered the twins to rescue her and Jimjar.
- **Underdark Travel:** Volunteered her tracking skills to cover the group's trail — a role she performed consistently and effectively across many days. Zalthir credited her shrewdness with reducing drow pursuit to "a mere whisper." She found and identified waterorbs, Timmask mushrooms, and Trillimac mushrooms, proving indispensable as a forager. At the Silken Paths, devised a rope-tying system to prevent fatal falls from the webs. Grew mildly annoyed when too many party members tried to help her cover tracks; barked at Grygum to forage instead.
- **Darklake/Sloobludop:** Fled during the melee confusion. When Demogorgon appeared, she screamed and had to be carried by Thorin to the boat. Once recovered, she identified the demon lord and urged the group to warn the surface.
- **Duergar Ambush:** Cursed the duergar attackers with venom; survived without being captured.
- **Gracklstugh:** Traveled with the group through the Derro slums. Was ordered by Rust to remain outside the assassin's lair; she complied. Armed by Errde at Underlake Hold. Hit by poisoned projectiles during a battle. Had a near-death experience in the mesa chambers, which weighed heavily on the party's conscience. Was told to wait outside the final cavern. The party discovered evidence that the king has been working with assassins targeting Eldeth's clan — evidence she has not yet seen. The party noted that showing her this information would trigger violence.
- **Continued Travel:** Successfully concealed the party's trail through a Faerzress crystal cave. By the 8th day announced she believed they had lost their pursuers entirely.
- **Neverlight Grove:** Descended into the grove with Thorin; present during the battle at the Circle of Welcome.
- **Underground River Flood:** Recognized the flooding as a river breaking through stone; shared stories of both successful and catastrophic escapes from similar events.
- **Blingdenstone:** Volunteered to deal with the ghost problem. Helped destroy Vazuk, the tormented deep gnome ghost, and arrived at Diggermattock Hall afterward.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Blingdenstone, at Diggermattock Hall, having just helped destroy the ghost Vazuk
- **Active concerns:** Getting a warning about Demogorgon to the surface; reaching Gauntlygrym
- **Hidden from Eldeth:** The party possesses evidence that Gracklstugh's king has been working with assassins to kill her clan. She does not know this. Sharing it would trigger violence/outrage.

## Relationships
- **Thorin:** Fellow dwarf, but their relationship is strained by Thorin's positive attitude toward giants. Eldeth cites the War of the Giants as reason for distrust. Buppido (under magical influence) blurted out that Eldeth is "not interested" in Thorin romantically. Despite this tension, Thorin has repeatedly looked out for her (carrying her to the boat, ordering her rescue from the cave-in).
- **Buppido:** He showed notable interest in her trail-hiding techniques. Otherwise no warm relationship indicated.
- **Jimjar:** Frequent companion — trapped together in the cave-in, armed together at Underlake Hold, and partnered to fight the ghost in Blingdenstone.
- **Zalthir:** Worked together on winch duty; he acknowledged her critical role in evading pursuit.
- **Grygum:** She barks orders at him but their dynamic is functional.
- **Runc:** Sneered at him; mocked orcs generally.
- **Duergar / Derro:** Deep racial hatred and contempt.
