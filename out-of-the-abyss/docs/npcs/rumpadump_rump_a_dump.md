# Rumpadump (Rump-a-dump)

## Identity
- Myconid child; associated with the Neverlight Grove
- Likely encountered as a fellow captive or companion during Underdark travel (context suggests early party acquisition alongside Stool)

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Return to the Neverlight Grove
- Naïvely devoted to the myconid sovereign and the sanctity of the myconid circle. Displays a child's unwavering faith in authority figures, insisting that corruption of the Grove is impossible. Dismissive of warnings about danger, not out of bravery but out of genuine inability to conceive that his homeland could be threatened.

## History with the Party
- Expressed a strong desire, alongside Stool, to travel to the Neverlight Grove.
- When Zalthir warned the group that a very dangerous and mighty demon was present at the Grove, Rumpadump firmly rejected the idea, declaring, "The circle cannot be corrupted. Our sovereign would never let that happen."
- Zalthir considered correcting him but chose not to, recognizing that Rumpadump is a child.

## Current Status
- Last known status: Traveling with or near the party, intent on reaching Neverlight Grove
- Unaware of (or in denial about) the demonic threat at the Grove
- **What the party knows:** Zalthir has warned them about a powerful demon at the Grove; Rumpadump refuses to believe it
- **What remains hidden:** The true state of the Neverlight Grove and what has become of the myconid sovereign

## Relationships
- **Stool:** Fellow myconid child and travel companion; shares the same destination and presumably a close bond
- **Zalthir:** Received Zalthir's warning about the Grove but rejected it outright; Zalthir regards him with a degree of pity or gentleness due to his youth
- **The Party:** A dependent NPC traveling under the party's protection

None recorded.
