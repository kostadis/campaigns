# Bimble

## Identity
- Scout in service to Kazook Pickshine
- Encountered by the party strapped to a table in Kazook's chambers, after returning from a mission gone wrong

## Personality & Motivations
- Original personality is difficult to assess due to his infected, delirious state. Prior to infection, he was evidently a capable and trusted scout, sent on a high-value reconnaissance mission. In his current state, he is raving and incoherent, fixated on visions of "the Lady of Rot" and a singing garden.

## History with the Party
- **Discovery in Kazook's chambers:** The party found Bimble strapped to a table, clearly infected by something from the rock blight. He was coughing up spores that formed tiny laughing faces before dissolving. He raved about a garden of stone that sang to him and "the Lady of Rot feeding the soil."
- **Investigation:** The party carefully studied his ramblings, the smells clinging to him, and the textures of rock under his fingers. Through this investigation, they successfully pinpointed the exact location of the cave containing the Ghost Hold Lichen.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Strapped to a table in Kazook Pickshine's chambers, severely infected.
- **Condition:** Delirious, coughing spores, raving. No indication of recovery or treatment in progress.
- **What the party knows:** He found the Ghost Hold Lichen in a garden of stone deep in the rock blight. Something there infected him—possibly connected to "the Lady of Rot." The party has used his condition to locate the cave.
- **What remains hidden:** The true nature of his infection, whether he can be cured, the identity of "the Lady of Rot," and what exactly happened to him in the cave.

## Relationships
- **Kazook Pickshine:** Bimble's employer/superior. Kazook sent him on the scouting mission and is keeping him restrained post-infection.
- **The Party:** No prior relationship. He served as an unwitting source of intelligence, his infected state providing the clues needed to find the cave.
- **"The Lady of Rot":** An entity Bimble references in his ravings—unclear whether real, metaphorical, or a hallucination born of infection.
