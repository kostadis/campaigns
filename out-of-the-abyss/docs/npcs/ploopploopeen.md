# Ploopploopeen

## Identity
- Archpriest of the Sea Mother, kuo-toa religious leader of Sloobludop
- First encountered by the party about an hour outside Sloobludop, where he approached them with his followers moving in a choreographed school-of-fish formation

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Destroy the rival Deep Father faction led by his daughter Bloppblippodd and restore the Sea Mother as the sole faith of Sloobludop.
- Ploopploopeen is a pragmatic and somewhat manipulative leader, willing to use the party as bait to achieve his ends. He walks constantly while talking, stares unblinkingly, and is fiercely defensive of his traditional faith. He is delighted by genuine (if crude) gestures of respect toward the Sea Mother, such as when Grygum and Thorin spit on her altar as an offering. He sleeps standing up outside, as is kuo-toan custom.

## History with the Party
1. **First mentioned** by Suushar, who suggested Ploopploopeen could help Thorin with his madness and would welcome Suushar's spiritual awakening.
2. **Met the party** an hour from Sloobludop. Declared "The Seamother has answered my prayers!" and proposed using them as false sacrifices to infiltrate the Deep Father faction.
3. **Negotiated terms**: Initially offered 500 gold and two potions of healing. Sweetened the deal with two boats and Darklake guides when the party seemed unimpressed.
4. **Was offended** when Grygum called his faith a "weird religion," insisting the Sea Mother is the traditional kuo-toan faith.
5. **Hosted the party** in Sloobludop—fed them, assured Thorin that kuo-toa eat cooked fish. Was delighted by their altar offerings.
6. **Explained backstory**: Previously attempted a "white flag" approach with Bloppblippodd, but she refused reconciliation.
7. **On the morning of the 2nd day**, admitted a kuo-toa monitor and announced he, his whip, and the prisoners would accompany the monitor's group to see the archpriest of the Deep Father—putting the plan into motion.

8. **Altar Ruse Execution:** The ruse was executed at Bloppblippodd's altar. Ploopploopeen personally delivered the fatal blow against his own daughter, completing her summoning ritual and triggering Demogorgon's emergence.
9. **Thorin's Realization:** Thorin realized that Ploopploopeen could have paralyzed the whole party with Hold Person if he'd truly been working against them — cited as evidence Ploop was playing a deeper game all along.
10. **Post-Battle:** Grygum looted Ploopploopeen's thatched hut, justifying it because Ploop hadn't paid them.

## Current Status
- **Status unknown** after Demogorgon's emergence from the Darklake.
- The party never received payment from him.

## Relationships
- **Bloppblippodd** (daughter): Deeply strained. She leads the rival Deep Father faction after experiencing a powerful vision of "Leemooggoogoon." Ploopploopeen views her as a dangerous heretic to be stopped.
- **Glooglugogg** (son): Believes Ploopploopeen showed Bloppblippodd too much deference growing up, contributing to the current crisis.
- **Suushar**: Fellow Sea Mother follower; Suushar speaks highly of Ploopploopeen and expected a warm reception.
- **The Party**: Transactional alliance. He needs them as bait and infiltrators; they need boats, guides, and gold.
