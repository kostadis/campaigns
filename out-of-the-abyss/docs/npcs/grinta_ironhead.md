# Grinta Ironhead

## Identity
- Female Duergar of Clan Ironhead
- Apparent political operative or intermediary within the clan
- First appeared approaching the party just after they left the Clan Ironhead stronghold

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: See the Deepking deposed so Clan Ironhead can trade freely with the Deep Gnomes of Blingdenstone at discounted rates.
- Calculated and deliberate — she approached the party with specific intelligence about their plans and a clear proposition, then left immediately without allowing follow-up questions. She is not interested in dialogue, only in planting the seed.

## History with the Party
- Intercepted the party after they departed the Clan Ironhead stronghold.
- Demonstrated she had intelligence on the party's intentions (traveling to the surface via Blingdenstone).
- Laid out the political situation: the Deepking has imposed exorbitant prices on the Deep Gnomes, and his removal would allow Clan Ironhead to offer discounted trade.
- Departed abruptly, giving the party no chance for questions or negotiation.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Vicinity of the Clan Ironhead stronghold; whereabouts after departure unknown.
- **Active plans:** Seeking the Deepking's removal to open favorable trade with Blingdenstone. Appears to be using the party as a potential instrument toward this end.
- **What the party knows:** Her name, clan affiliation, and her proposal regarding the Deepking.
- **What remains hidden:** Her exact rank or authority within Clan Ironhead; whether she acts on her own initiative or on behalf of clan leadership; how she learned of the party's plans; what support (if any) she or Clan Ironhead could offer.

## Relationships
- **Clan Ironhead:** Member; unclear whether she speaks for the clan's leadership or a faction within it.
- **The Deepking:** Opposed; views his trade policies as harmful to Clan Ironhead's interests.
- **Deep Gnomes of Blingdenstone:** Desires trade relations with them; positioned as a potential ally to the gnomes if the Deepking falls.
- **The Party:** Treats them as useful agents; demonstrated awareness of their movements and goals.
