# Grinta Ironhead

## Identity
- Female Duergar of Clan Ironhead
- Apparent political operative or intermediary acting on behalf of clan trade interests
- First appeared approaching the party outside the Clan Ironhead stronghold

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Deposing the Deepking to free Clan Ironhead from his price controls and enable direct, discounted trade with the Deep Gnomes of Blingdenstone.
- Calculated and deliberate — she intercepted the party at a precise moment, delivered her pitch, and left before they could ask questions. She demonstrated good intelligence-gathering (knew the party's travel plans) and a preference for controlling the conversation on her terms.

## History with the Party
- **Single encounter:** Approached the party after they departed the Clan Ironhead stronghold. Revealed she knew of their intent to travel to the surface via Blingdenstone. Pitched a transactional proposal — if they help depose the Deepking, Clan Ironhead would offer the Deep Gnomes discounted trade rates. Departed immediately, allowing no follow-up discussion.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Unknown; she left quickly after the encounter near the Clan Ironhead stronghold.
- **Active plans:** Seeking the Deepking's removal to benefit Clan Ironhead's trade position. Unclear whether she is acting alone, on behalf of Clan Ironhead leadership, or as part of a broader conspiracy.
- **Known vs. hidden:** The party knows her name, clan, and her stated proposal. They do not know her rank within the clan, whether Clan Ironhead leadership sanctioned her approach, who else may be involved, or what she expects in return beyond the Deepking's removal.

## Relationships
- **Clan Ironhead:** Member; acting in the clan's trade interests (or at least framing it that way).
- **The Deepking:** Opposes his rule, or at minimum his economic policies regarding Deep Gnome trade.
- **Deep Gnomes of Blingdenstone:** Positioned as a potential trade beneficiary if her plan succeeds.
- **The Party:** Sees them as useful agents — leveraging their surface-bound travel plans to align their interests with hers.
