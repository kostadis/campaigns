# Pliinki

## Identity
- Derro devotee of Demogorgon; cult authority figure and experimenter
- Faction ties: Cult of Demogorgon, coordinated with the Gray Ghosts and Council of Savants
- Never encountered alive by the party; found dead (killed by the party off-screen or prior to discovery), with her journal and letters recovered in the obelisk chamber

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Conquer Gracklstugh and offer the city as a sacrificial gift to Demogorgon. She also sought to mutate a stolen red dragon egg into a two-headed wyrmling intended as a mount for Demogorgon.
- A charismatic ideologue who inspired loyalty among lesser Derro by promising liberation from Duergar bondage. She combined religious fanaticism with hands-on experimentation, working directly with the Obelisk ("The Stone of Diirinka") and Thrazgad ore. Both Skiit and Ulnara sought her approval, suggesting she commanded genuine devotion.

## History with the Party
- **Obelisk Chamber:** The party killed Pliinki (exact circumstances not detailed). Zalthir found her journal and letters on a desk in the northwest corner of the chamber, revealing the full scope of her plans.
- **Guard Post:** Derro guards at the double doors confirmed her death as the party passed through. Pliinki had promised these guards that her plan would free the Derro from Duergar bondage.
- **Indirect References:** Prior to discovering the chamber, the party heard about Pliinki from Skiit (who said Pliinki promised him a better job and proclaimed Demogorgon's followers would make the Derro proud and powerful) and Ulnara (who hoped the party would tell Pliinki she had been helpful).

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed by the party.
- Her journal revealed key intelligence: the dragon egg mutation experiment, the failed attempt to mend the Obelisk's fractures with Thrazgad ore, and her broader conspiracy to destabilize Gracklstugh.
- **Known to the party:** Her plans, her co-conspirators (Uskvil, Aliinka, Narrak), the stolen egg scheme, assassination lists, and her intent to use the Obelisk as a weapon of conquest.

## Relationships
- **Uskvil** — Leader of the Gray Ghosts; co-conspirator in destabilizing Gracklstugh (stolen eggs, assassination lists, sowing discord)
- **Aliinka** — Member of the Council of Savants and Gray Ghosts; co-conspirator
- **Narrak** — Cult leader; co-conspirator
- **Skiit** — Derro follower; Pliinki promised him a better position and inspired his loyalty
- **Ulnara** — Derro follower; sought Pliinki's approval and wanted to be seen as helpful
- **Derro guards (double doors)** — Promised freedom from Duergar bondage in exchange for their service
- **Zalthir** (party member) — Recovered her journal and letters, uncovering the conspiracy
