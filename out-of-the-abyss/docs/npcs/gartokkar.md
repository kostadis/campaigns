# Gartokkar

## Identity
- Duergar member of the Keepers of the Flame
- First encountered through a prior arrangement to retrieve a dragon egg; the party visited his house to complete the deal

## Personality & Motivations
- Described in Narrak's letter as paranoid and desiring a "genocidal war" against the Derro. In person, he came across as cautious and genuinely fearful about the volatile state of Gracklstugh. He honored his deals and showed pragmatic reliability, but his warnings carried the weight of someone who understands how close the city is to civil war.

## History with the Party
1. **Pre-session arrangement:** Gartokkar had a deal with the party to retrieve a dragon egg.
2. **Narrak's letter:** The party learned of Gartokkar's paranoia and his desire for conflict with the Derro through correspondence found on Narrak.
3. **Egg delivery:** The party returned the mutated dragon egg to Gartokkar at his house. He noticed the strange occult markings and was worried, but asked Grygum (cleric of Bahamut) to evaluate it. He was pleased by Grygum's assessment that the chromatic egg would produce an evil creature. He paid the party a bag of coins and upheld their non-disclosure agreement.
4. **Warning issued:** Before the party left, Gartokkar warned them about the Deepking's agents searching for the **Ember Vanguard** — shadowy assassins from Menzoberanzan with powerful magic and soul-rending blades. He stressed that the Derro, Stone Guard, and Grey Ghosts are all on edge and some factions actively want conflict.
5. **Escape plan:** The party used their completed deal with Gartokkar as a stepping stone to flee Gracklstugh via the ports before Errde Blackskull could imprison them. Grygum's cover story was that the egg had been stolen by "some other crazed group," not the Derro.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** His house in Gracklstugh
- **Active concerns:** The Ember Vanguard threat; rising tensions between the Derro, Stone Guard, and Grey Ghosts
- **Party knowledge vs. hidden:** The party knows about his deal-making reliability and his warnings. They also know from Narrak's letter that he harbors genocidal intentions toward the Derro — something Gartokkar himself has not disclosed to them directly.

## Relationships
- **Keepers of the Flame:** Member and loyalist
- **Grygum:** Trusted his clerical assessment of the dragon egg; dealt with him directly
- **Daz:** Interpreted Gartokkar's warning as genuine fear about civil war
- **Narrak:** Narrak's letter characterized Gartokkar as paranoid and warmongering
- **Errde Blackskull:** No direct relationship noted, but the party used Gartokkar as leverage to escape before Blackskull could act
- **The Derro:** Gartokkar wants conflict with them (per Narrak's letter), though he framed his warnings more diplomatically in person
