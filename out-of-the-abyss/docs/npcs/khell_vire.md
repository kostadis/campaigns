# Khell-Vire

## Identity
- **Role:** Archivist and handler within Candlekeep's Shadow Discipline Lineage Records (lower-gallery office)
- **Faction:** Former Garden of Shadow monk; currently operates within Candlekeep's scholarly structure
- **First Appearance:** Candlekeep, assigned as Zalthir's handler upon his arrival for formal assessment

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Unclear in full scope, but professionally focused on evaluating and guiding Shadow Discipline practitioners through structured assessment rather than direct instruction
- A precise, philosophically oriented man — his opening question ("what were you sent *toward*") signals he cares more about transformation than biography. He is not unkind, but his framing irritates those who prefer directness. He maintains deliberate distance, positioning himself as a facilitator rather than a participant in others' development.

## History with the Party
- **Session [Zalthir arrival]:** Khell-Vire received Zalthir at Candlekeep in his lower-gallery office. A note on his desk — *Zalthir, the Underdark, until the Bone King sings* — had no identifiable origin; neither man could explain it. He opened the consultation by asking what Zalthir had been sent *toward*, framing the assessment as being about who Zalthir has become, not what he knows. He arranged for a blind monk to conduct the formal trial in his stead, explicitly declining to walk through the door himself. After the trial — which included poor skill rolls, strong combat performance, and a self-developed shadow-strike technique — he delivered his assessment: unfocused and undertrained, but the shadow step/strike combination redeems all deficiencies. He noted Zalthir is beginning to remember the shadow monks who shaped him by opposition. He awarded Zalthir a specialized shadow technique (one of three options offered). He raised an eyebrow at Zalthir's final demonstration and appeared genuinely surprised.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Candlekeep, lower-gallery office
- **Active operations:** Continuing role as archivist and handler for the Shadow Discipline Lineage Records
- **What the party knows:** He is a former Garden of Shadow monk who left eighty years ago after refusing an unspecified order; he still wears a faded shadow monk under-robe; he assessed Zalthir and found him worth investing in
- **Hidden:** The nature of the order he refused; the full scope of his interest in Zalthir; the origin of the desk note, which he also could not explain

## Relationships
- **Zalthir (party):** Assigned handler; relationship is formal but not cold — he delivered a notably positive final assessment and appeared genuinely moved or surprised by the closing demonstration
- **Blind monk (unnamed):** Trusted colleague within Candlekeep; called upon to conduct the practical trial in Khell-Vire's place
- **Garden of Shadow:** Estranged; departed eighty years ago under unspecified circumstances involving a refused order
