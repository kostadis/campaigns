# Rump-a-dump (Rumpadump)

## Identity
- Myconid companion of the party
- Traveled with the party alongside fellow Myconid, Stool

## Personality & Motivations
- Surprisingly opinionated and observant, particularly regarding others' shortcomings. Despite being a Myconid, Rump-a-dump demonstrated a deeply perceptive (if blunt) nature, compiling an extensive list of approximately fifty specific ways Zalthir could improve himself. Showed genuine emotional attachment to party members, particularly Daz, expressing sadness upon departure.

## History with the Party
- Traveled with the party as a companion alongside Stool.
- Grygum convinced Rump-a-dump and Stool that Neverlight Grove was no longer safe.
- Before departing, Rump-a-dump delivered a lengthy and specific list of self-improvement advice to Zalthir, including gems such as "Listen to Stool, he knows what he is doing," "Don't assume you know everything; other people's points of view are important," and "Eat more Zurkhwood, it's good for your digestion."
- Said a sad goodbye to Daz before leaving with the other Myconids.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Departed with the Myconids, away from Neverlight Grove (deemed unsafe).
- **What the party knows:** Neverlight Grove is no longer safe; Rump-a-dump left with their people.

## Relationships
- **Stool:** Fellow Myconid companion; Rump-a-dump holds Stool in high regard ("Listen to Stool, he knows what he is doing").
- **Grygum:** Trusted Grygum's warning about Neverlight Grove.
- **Zalthir:** Had strong (unsolicited) opinions about Zalthir's need for self-improvement; delivered ~50 specific critiques before departing.
- **Daz:** Showed genuine emotional attachment; parted with visible sadness.
