# Terrestor

## Identity
- Allegedly an evil wizard from the Kingdom of Nelrindenvane
- Referenced only through Prince Derendil's personal narrative; never encountered directly

## Personality & Motivations
- Described by Derendil as a malevolent wizard who cursed him into Quaggoth form and exiled him from his kingdom. No independently verified information about Terrestor's personality or motives exists.

## History with the Party
- Mentioned by Prince Derendil as the source of his curse and exile. The party has never met or interacted with Terrestor directly.

## Current Status
- **Likely fictional.** Daz knows that Derendil's entire backstory — including the Kingdom of Nelrindenvane and his identity as an elven prince — is fabricated. Terrestor almost certainly does not exist.
- The party has no leads, location, or independent confirmation of this figure.

## Relationships
- **Prince Derendil:** Cast as Derendil's nemesis in his self-constructed narrative — the wizard who cursed him and stole his kingdom.
- **Daz:** Knows Derendil's story is false, making Terrestor's existence highly suspect.
