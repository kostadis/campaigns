# Nym

## Identity
- Drow soldier from Velkynvelve, part of the drow scout force pursuing the party
- First identified during a pursuit encounter when Zalthir recognized his distinctive gait among the drow scouts

## Personality & Motivations
- Motivated by duty as part of the recapture force hunting the escaped prisoners. Shows a cautious, self-preserving streak — when he felt a tremor in the cave, his first instinct was concern about a collapse rather than pressing the search. Willing to second-guess the group's decisions ("I told you it was the other tunnel"), suggesting a somewhat grumbling, practical disposition.

## History with the Party
- **Pursuit encounter:** Nym was scouting tunnels with other drow, tracking the escaped prisoners. Zalthir recognized him by his gait. When Zalthir fell from a hiding spot and made noise, Nym drew his weapon and searched the area. Grygum used Thaumaturgy to create a tremor, which Nym mistook for a natural cave instability. Believing the cave might collapse and finding no sign of the prisoners, he abandoned the search and headed back, convinced they had taken the wrong tunnel.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Underground tunnels, departing the area where the party was hiding
- **Active operations:** Still part of the drow scout force actively pursuing the party; currently heading down what he believes is the correct alternate tunnel
- **Party knowledge vs. hidden info:** The party knows Nym is among their pursuers and that he was fooled by the Thaumaturgy trick. Nym does not know how close he came to finding them, nor that the tremor was magical.

## Relationships
- **Velkynvelve drow:** Rank-and-file soldier in the pursuing scout force; appears to have peers he bickers with about navigation decisions
- **Zalthir (PC):** Zalthir recognizes Nym specifically from their time in Velkynvelve — familiar enough to identify him by gait alone
- **The Party:** Nym views them as escaped prisoners to be recaptured; has no personal knowledge of their current capabilities or location
