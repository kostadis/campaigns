# Ogremoch

## Identity
- Evil elemental earth god / force from the elemental plane of earth
- Referenced through the concept of "Ogremoch's Bane" and his corrupted altar within the ancient temple
- Never encountered directly; known only through his corrupting influence and information provided by the Galeb Duhr guardians

## Personality & Motivations
- Seeks to extend his corrupting influence from the elemental plane of earth into the material world. His power operates through domination and corruption of earth-aligned creatures and sacred sites. He had infested the rocks of the ancient temple for centuries, subverting its guardians and elementals to serve his will.

## History with the Party
- The party learned of Ogremoch's influence from the Galeb Duhr stone guardians at the ancient temple, who explained that his corruption had taken control of the Earth Elementals and prevented the Galeb Duhr from fulfilling their sacred protective duty.
- The party undertook a mission to hallow the temple and cleanse Ogremoch's presence. Each Earth Elemental they destroyed freed a Galeb Duhr from his control, requiring multiple such victories to fully liberate the site.
- The party successfully hallowed the temple, purging Ogremoch's corrupting power. However, when Naomi Pathshutter expected that hallowing his altar would open the path to Entemoch's boon, the tunnels instead shifted and sealed, and the earth seemed frozen — an unexpected consequence.

## Current Status
- His corrupting influence has been purged from the ancient temple following the party's hallowing ritual.
- The unexpected reaction of the temple (tunnels shifting, sealing, earth freezing) after his altar was hallowed suggests unresolved consequences or complications.
- **What the party knows:** Ogremoch is an evil elemental earth god whose centuries-long corruption of the temple has been cleansed.
- **What remains hidden:** The full extent of Ogremoch's power, whether his influence persists elsewhere, and why the temple reacted unexpectedly after the hallowing.

## Relationships
- **Galeb Duhr Guardians:** His victims — he overrode their sacred duty and held them in thrall through corrupted Earth Elementals.
- **Earth Elementals (temple):** Controlled and weaponized by his influence against the temple's true guardians.
- **Entemoch:** Implied opposition — Ogremoch's corruption blocked access to Entemoch's boon, suggesting they are rival or opposed elemental earth entities.
- **Naomi Pathshutter:** She understood enough about Ogremoch's altar to expect that hallowing it would unlock Entemoch's boon, indicating prior knowledge of the dynamic between these forces.
- **The Party:** Adversarial — they actively dismantled his hold on the temple.
