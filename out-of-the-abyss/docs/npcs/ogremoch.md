# Ogremoch

## Identity
- Evil elemental earth god / force from the elemental plane of earth
- Referenced primarily through the title "Ogremoch's Bane"
- Never encountered directly; known through his corrupting influence on the ancient temple

## Personality & Motivations
- Seeks to extend his dominion from the elemental plane of earth into the material world through corruption of sacred sites. His influence operates insidiously, subverting existing guardians and elementals rather than through direct confrontation.

## History with the Party
- **Temple Corruption (pre-campaign):** Ogremoch's influence had infested the rocks of the ancient temple for centuries, taking control of the Earth Elementals within and preventing the Galeb Duhr stone guardians from performing their sacred protective duty.
- **Temple Cleansing:** The party undertook a mission to hallow the temple and cleanse Ogremoch's presence. Each Earth Elemental they destroyed freed a Galeb Duhr from his control, though multiple elementals needed to be defeated to fully liberate the site.
- **Hallowing Complete:** The Galeb Duhr confirmed the party successfully hallowed the temple from Ogremoch's corrupting power. However, when Naomi Pathshutter expected hallowing Ogremoch's altar would open the path to Entemoch's boon, the tunnels instead shifted and sealed, and the earth seemed frozen — an unexpected consequence.

## Current Status
- **Last known status:** His influence has been cleansed from the temple following the party's hallowing.
- **Remaining unknowns:** It is unclear whether Ogremoch is aware of or will respond to the loss of his foothold. The frozen, sealed tunnels after the hallowing suggest his influence may have had deeper structural ties to the temple than expected, or that his removal triggered unforeseen consequences.

## Relationships
- **Entemoch:** Implied opposition — Ogremoch's corruption blocked access to Entemoch's boon. The two appear to be rival elemental earth powers (one benevolent, one malevolent).
- **Galeb Duhr Guardians:** Former victims of his control; now freed by the party's actions.
- **Earth Elementals:** Served as corrupted agents under his influence within the temple.
- **Naomi Pathshutter:** Knowledgeable about Ogremoch's altar and its connection to Entemoch's boon; her expectations about the hallowing's outcome were not met.
