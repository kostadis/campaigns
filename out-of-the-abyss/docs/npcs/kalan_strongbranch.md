# Kalan Strongbranch

## Identity
- **Role:** Gate Warden of Candlekeep
- **Faction:** Candlekeep
- **First Appearance:** Summoned to address the party after a House T'sarran spy was discovered hidden in their bag.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Maintain Candlekeep's institutional order and neutrality; resolve security matters cleanly and within established protocol.
- Efficient and transactional — presents Candlekeep's position plainly and without sentiment. Does not moralize, editorialize, or advocate for a particular choice. He states facts, outlines options, and leaves decisions to others.

## History with the Party
- **First and only recorded interaction:** Kalan was called in to handle the spy situation. He laid out Candlekeep's terms clearly: the institution would accept custody of the spy, hold her in the bell tower's anti-magic cells, and eventually release her at the gate with a warning. He was explicit that Candlekeep would not hand her to House T'sarran and would not execute her. He was equally explicit that this arrangement is a delay, not a resolution, and that any longer-term solution was the party's problem to solve.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Candlekeep
- **Active Role:** Gate Warden; presumably resumed normal duties after the spy matter was resolved or handed off.
- **What the party knows:** His offer, his limits, and his institutional neutrality.
- **What remains hidden:** Whether he has any personal opinions about House T'sarran, the spy, or the party's situation.

## Relationships
- **Candlekeep:** Institutional representative; speaks for the keep's policy without apparent personal stake.
- **The Party:** Professional and neutral. No warmth or hostility recorded.
- **The Spy:** Treats her as a security matter to be managed per protocol, not a person to be judged.
