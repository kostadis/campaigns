# Voosbur

## Identity
- Taller Myconid, distinguished among a group of dancing Myconids
- Devotee of Zuggtmoy ("the Dark Lady")
- Identified by Rump-a-dump as the one who released the strange spores that caused the other Myconids to change and begin their unnatural dancing
- First encountered by the party amid the group of dancing, infected Myconids

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: spread Zuggtmoy's influence and recruit others into her "dream" / psychic communion
- Speaks telepathically in an ecstatic, countercultural tone — treats Zuggtmoy's domain like a spiritual awakening. Describes it as "the ultimate underground rave" and a "mind-expanding journey" through her "cosmic garden." Dismisses concerns about infection, calling Zuggtmoy "the mama of the mushrooms, the queen of the dreamscape." Not overtly hostile, but clearly fully enthralled.

## History with the Party
- The party approached the group of dancing Myconids; Voosbur paused his frantic dance momentarily before continuing.
- He pitched Zuggtmoy's domain to the party and extended his hand, offering them a chance to enter the Dark Lady's dream.
- Thorin and Grygum took his hand and were transported into a psychedelic vision of Zuggtmoy's domain.
- After they woke and declined to join, Voosbur expressed mild disappointment: *"I guess you guys didn't want to join us. You still can if you want. But hurry."*
- He then released glowing spores, and the dancing Myconids walked towards the moss at the cave's edge and disappeared.

## Current Status
- **Last known:** Vanished with the other infected Myconids into the moss at the cave's edge. Current whereabouts unknown.
- Presumably still serving Zuggtmoy and spreading her influence somewhere in the Underdark.
- The party knows he is an agent of Zuggtmoy's corruption among the Myconids but did not stop him or the infected group.

## Relationships
- **Zuggtmoy:** Fully devoted servant; regards her with reverence and acts as a recruiter/evangelist for her cause.
- **Rump-a-dump:** Rump-a-dump identified Voosbur as the source of the corrupting spores — implying Voosbur is known (and likely feared/resented) among uncorrupted Myconids.
- **Thorin & Grygum:** Briefly shared the psychedelic vision with them; parting was non-hostile but disappointed.
- **The Party (general):** No animosity, but the party rejected his offer. He left the door open for them to follow.
