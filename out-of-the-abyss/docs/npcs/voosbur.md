# Voosbur

## Identity
- Taller Myconid, notable figure among the dancing Myconid group
- Infected agent of Zuggtmoy ("the Dark Lady"), responsible for releasing the strange spores that caused other Myconids to change and begin their unnatural dancing
- Identified to the party by Rump-a-dump

## Personality & Motivations
- Devoted evangelist for Zuggtmoy, whom he reveres as "the mama of the mushrooms, the queen of the dreamscape." Speaks telepathically in an ecstatic, countercultural tone — framing Zuggtmoy's influence as a liberating, mind-expanding experience rather than an infection. Eager to recruit others into the Dark Lady's dream, but doesn't force the issue when refused — he simply moves on with a sigh of disappointment.

## History with the Party
- The party encountered Voosbur among a group of dancing Myconids behaving unnaturally. Rump-a-dump identified Voosbur as the one who released the spores causing the strange behavior.
- When approached, Voosbur paused his frantic dance briefly before continuing. He telepathically pitched Zuggtmoy's domain as "the ultimate underground rave" and invited the party to join a "mind-expanding journey" through her "cosmic garden."
- He denied Zuggtmoy was an infection and extended his hand, offering entry into the Dark Lady's dream.
- **Thorin** and **Grygum** took his hand and were transported into a psychedelic vision of Zuggtmoy's domain. Both woke and declined to join.
- Voosbur expressed mild disappointment, offered a final chance to join, then released glowing spores. The dancing Myconids walked toward the moss at the cave's edge and vanished.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Vanished into the moss at the cave's edge along with the other infected Myconids. Current whereabouts unknown.
- Presumably still serving Zuggtmoy and potentially spreading her influence elsewhere in the Underdark.
- The party knows he is an agent of Zuggtmoy's corruption but does not know where he went or what he is doing now.

## Relationships
- **Zuggtmoy:** Fanatical devotee; serves as a recruiter and spore-spreader for the Dark Lady.
- **Rump-a-dump:** Known to Rump-a-dump, who identified him as the source of the corrupting spores (implicitly adversarial relationship).
- **Thorin & Grygum:** Both accepted his offer and experienced Zuggtmoy's psychedelic vision firsthand, but ultimately rejected the invitation.
- **The Party (general):** Parted on neutral-but-unresolved terms; Voosbur left the door open for them to join before disappearing.
