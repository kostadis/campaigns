# Prince Livid

## Identity
- Ooze henchman of the Pudding King
- Present in the Pudding King's throne room alongside Princess Ebonmire

## Personality & Motivations
- Loyal servant of the Pudding King, fighting alongside him in defense of the throne room. No independent personality or goals noted beyond serving as the Pudding King's enforcer.

## History with the Party
1. **Throne Room Encounter:** Present during the Pudding King's monologue. When Zalthir grappled and dragged the Pudding King away, Prince Livid was separated from his master.
2. **Throne Room Battle:** Attacked Zalthir with a psychic blast dealing 14 damage (reduced to 7 via a successful Intelligence saving throw). Later hit Daz with a psychic attack for 5 damage. Survived Daz's Fireball due to fire resistance — took 14 of 29 points dealt, leaving him at 8 HP. Was killed by Daz's Magic Missile after Princess Ebonmire had already fallen.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed by Daz's Magic Missile during the throne room battle.

## Relationships
- **Pudding King:** Loyal ooze henchman; served alongside him in the throne room.
- **Princess Ebonmire:** Fellow ooze henchman of the Pudding King; both fought in the same battle. Ebonmire fell first.
- **Daz:** Dealt the killing blow via Magic Missile; also targeted Daz with a psychic attack during the fight.
- **Zalthir:** Targeted with a psychic blast; Zalthir's grappling of the Pudding King separated Livid from his master.
