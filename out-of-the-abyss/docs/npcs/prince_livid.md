# Prince Livid

## Identity
- Ooze henchman of the Pudding King
- Present in the Pudding King's throne room alongside Princess Ebonmire

## Personality & Motivations
- Loyal servant of the Pudding King, fighting alongside him in defense of the throne room. No independent motivations demonstrated beyond serving his master in combat.

## History with the Party
- **Throne Room Encounter:** Present during the Pudding King's monologue. When Zalthir grappled and dragged the Pudding King away, Prince Livid was separated from his master and remained in the throne room to fight.
- **Combat:** Opened with a psychic blast against Zalthir dealing 14 damage (reduced to 7 via successful Intelligence saving throw). Later hit Daz with a psychic attack for 5 damage. Survived Daz's Fireball due to fire resistance, taking only 14 of 29 points dealt and remaining at 8 HP. Ultimately killed by Daz's Magic Missile after Princess Ebonmire had already fallen.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed by Daz's Magic Missile during the throne room battle.

## Relationships
- **Pudding King:** Loyal ooze servant; fought in his defense.
- **Princess Ebonmire:** Fellow ooze henchman; both served the Pudding King and fought together in the throne room.
- **Daz:** Killed by Daz after exchanging blows in combat.
- **Zalthir:** Targeted Zalthir with a psychic blast; was separated from the Pudding King when Zalthir grappled their master.
