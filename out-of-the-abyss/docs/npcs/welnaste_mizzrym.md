# Welnaste Mizzrym

## Identity
- Drow caravan leader; "house sister" to Ilvara, affiliated with a Drow noble house (likely House Mizzrym)
- First appeared as the leader of the caravan that captured and transported the party through the Underdark to Velkynvelve

## Personality & Motivations
- Businesslike and transactional; treats prisoners as cargo and commerce. Her primary concern upon delivery was ensuring accountability for the "shipment" — making clear that money had changed hands and losses would not be tolerated. She carries authority and speaks to Ilvara as an equal or near-equal, not a subordinate.

## History with the Party
- **Capture & Transport:** Led the Drow caravan that captured the party and marched them through the Underdark.
- **Delivery to Velkynvelve:** Handed the prisoners over to Ilvara, referring to them as "the shipment." Explicitly warned Ilvara that payment had been made for the prisoners and that Ilvara would be "held to account" if any were lost or died before the next caravan arrived to move them on to Menzoberranzan.
- **Departure:** Left Velkynvelve after completing the handoff.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Departed Velkynvelve, presumably traveling the Underdark with her caravan.
- **Active plans:** A future caravan is expected to arrive at Velkynvelve to transport the prisoners to Menzoberranzan — Welnaste may or may not lead it.
- **Known vs. hidden:** The party knows she captured them and delivered them, and that she has leverage over Ilvara regarding their safety. The nature of who paid for the prisoners, why they were purchased, and what awaits them in Menzoberranzan remains unknown.

## Relationships
- **Ilvara:** House sister. Their dynamic is peer-level; Welnaste issued a direct warning about accountability, suggesting she holds influence or backing that Ilvara must respect.
- **The Party:** Captor. No personal relationship — the party is merchandise to her.
- **Unknown Buyer(s):** Someone paid money for the prisoners. Welnaste's connection to this patron or faction is unclear.
