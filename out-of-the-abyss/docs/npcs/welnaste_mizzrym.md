# Welnaste Mizzrym

## Identity
- Leader of the Drow caravan that captured and transported the party through the Underdark
- "House sister" to Ilvara, connected through Drow noble House Mizzrym
- First appeared during the prisoner handoff at Velkynvelve

## Personality & Motivations
- Businesslike and transactional; treats the prisoners as a paid shipment, not people. She made clear that money changed hands for them and expects the investment to be protected. Her warning to Ilvara suggests she is accountable to higher authorities and takes that chain of obligation seriously.

## History with the Party
- **Capture & Transport:** Led the Drow caravan that captured the party and marched them through the Underdark to Velkynvelve.
- **Handoff at Velkynvelve:** Delivered the prisoners to Ilvara, referring to them as "the shipment." Explicitly warned Ilvara that she would be "held to account" if any prisoners were lost or died before the next caravan arrived to transport them onward to Menzoberranzan.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Departed Velkynvelve after completing the prisoner handoff; current whereabouts unknown (presumably traveling the Underdark with her caravan).
- **Active plans:** A future caravan is expected to arrive at Velkynvelve to move the prisoners to Menzoberranzan — Welnaste may or may not lead it.
- **Hidden information:** The party does not know who paid for them, why they were captured, or what awaits them in Menzoberranzan. Welnaste likely knows the answers.

## Relationships
- **Ilvara Mizzrym:** House sister. Their interaction was professional but carried an implicit threat — Welnaste holds leverage over Ilvara regarding the prisoners' safety and the financial obligations attached to them.
- **The Party:** Captor. No personal relationship; she views them as cargo.
- **Unknown Patron(s):** Someone paid for the prisoners. Welnaste acts as an intermediary in this transaction.
