# Kazook Pickshine

## Identity
- Alchemist and Miners Guild representative in Blingdenstone
- Deep gnome (svirfneblin)
- First referenced by Jimjar as a potential buyer for empty spell gems; later met in person at the grand moot and in his chambers

## Personality & Motivations
- Dedicated alchemist driven by practical problem-solving for Blingdenstone's survival. Deeply compassionate toward those close to him — he reacted with visible upset when the party made light of Bimble's condition, insisting Bimble was a dear friend. Politically engaged, representing the Miners Guild and proposing concrete solutions at the grand moot.

## History with the Party
1. **Spell Gems (early reference):** Jimjar identified Kazook as someone who could transform empty spell gems (received from Werz) into gems of immense magical power and would pay well for them. No direct meeting occurred at this point.
2. **Grand Moot:** Kazook represented the Miners Guild and raised concerns about acidic damage to gnomish weapons and armor in the fight against the oozes. He proposed two solutions: gathering ingredients from Neverlight Grove for an acid-protection potion, or trading salt for high-quality weapons in Gracklstugh.
3. **Bimble's Infection:** Kazook approached the party with troubling news. He had recalled a legend about a garden of stone in the rock blight and sent his scout Bimble to investigate. Bimble found rare Ghost Hold Lichen needed for alchemical preparations but returned infected and raving. The party visited Kazook's chambers, where Bimble was strapped to a table amid bubbling beakers and potions. Kazook was upset when the party joked about the scene and asked for compassion.

## Current Status
- **Location:** His alchemical chambers in Blingdenstone
- **Active situation:** Dealing with Bimble's infection; likely still needs Ghost Hold Lichen from the rock blight
- **Spell gems:** It is unclear whether the party has yet brought him the empty spell gems from Werz
- **Ooze preparations:** His two proposed solutions (Neverlight Grove ingredients or Gracklstugh weapon trade) may still be in play

## Relationships
- **Bimble:** Scout and dear friend, currently infected and in Kazook's care
- **Jimjar:** Knows of Kazook's capabilities with spell gems; served as the party's connection to him
- **Werz:** Indirect link — Werz provided the empty spell gems Jimjar suggested bringing to Kazook
- **Miners Guild:** Official representative at the grand moot
- **The Party:** Functional relationship, though mildly strained by their insensitivity regarding Bimble
