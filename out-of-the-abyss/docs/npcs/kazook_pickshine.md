# Kazook Pickshine

## Identity
- Alchemist and Miners Guild representative in Blingdenstone
- Deep gnome (svirfneblin)
- First mentioned by Jimjar as a contact who could work with spell gems; later appeared in person at the grand moot and during the Bimble incident

## Personality & Motivations
- Practically minded problem-solver who thinks in terms of resources and trade solutions. Deeply loyal to his friends and assistants — he reacted with genuine upset when the party made light of Bimble's condition, insisting on compassion. He balances his role as a civic leader (Miners Guild) with his personal alchemical research and exploration interests.

## History with the Party
1. **Spell Gems (early mention):** Jimjar identified Kazook as someone who could transform empty spell gems (received from Werz) into gems of immense magical power and would pay well for them. The party has not yet pursued this transaction.
2. **Grand Moot:** Kazook represented the Miners Guild at the grand moot discussing the ooze threat. He raised concerns about acidic damage to gnomish weapons and armor, and proposed two solutions: gathering ingredients from Neverlight Grove for an acid-protection potion, or trading salt for high-quality weapons in Gracklstugh.
3. **Bimble Incident:** Kazook approached the party with troubling news. He had recalled a legend about a garden of stone in the rock blight and sent his scout Bimble to investigate. Bimble found rare Ghost Hold Lichen needed for alchemical preparations but returned infected and raving. Kazook had Bimble strapped to a table in his chambers (surrounded by bubbling beakers and potions). When the party joked about the scene, Kazook was upset and asked for compassion, calling Bimble a dear friend.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Blingdenstone, in his alchemical chambers
- **Active concerns:** Bimble's infection and the Ghost Hold Lichen from the rock blight; the ooze threat and acid-protection solutions
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows about his alchemical expertise, his role in the Miners Guild, his ability to work with spell gems, and Bimble's condition. No hidden information is indicated in the notes.

## Relationships
- **Bimble:** Scout and dear friend — currently infected and in Kazook's care
- **Jimjar:** Knows of Kazook and his capabilities; referred the party to him regarding spell gems
- **Werz:** Indirect connection — Werz provided the spell gems that Jimjar says Kazook could enhance
- **Miners Guild / Blingdenstone leadership:** Serves as the guild's representative at civic gatherings
