# Jimjar

## Identity
- Deep gnome (svirfneblin) NPC, fellow escapee/companion
- Part of the group fleeing from drow captivity

## Personality & Motivations
- Compulsive gambler — constantly looking for opportunities to make bets, even in dire survival situations. Attempted to wager on foraging outcomes mid-escape.
- Dismissive and blunt, particularly toward Shuushar and Kuo-Toan beliefs. Called Kuo-Toans insane but acknowledged their madness "seems reasonable."
- Presents himself as knowledgeable about the Underdark, though the accuracy of his claims is uncertain.

## History with the Party
- Fled alongside the party during the escape from the drow. Complained that Zalthir's insistence on running disrupted his planned foraging bet.
- When Shuushar voiced concern about the Faerzress, Jimjar dismissed the worry outright, claiming Faerzress has never affected a living thing and that its only magical property is blocking teleportation spells.

## Current Status
- Traveling with the party as they flee from drow pursuit
- No known plans beyond immediate survival (and gambling)
- **What the party knows:** His gambling habit, his opinions on Kuo-Toans, and his claims about the Faerzress
- **What remains hidden:** Whether his Faerzress claims are accurate or dangerously wrong

## Relationships
- **Zalthir:** Mild friction — Jimjar blamed Zalthir's urgency for ruining his betting opportunity
- **Shuushar:** Dismissive; mocked Kuo-Toan nature and waved off Shuushar's Faerzress concerns
- **The Party (general):** Sociable in a self-serving way; treats companions as potential betting partners

## Arc Score Events
None recorded.
