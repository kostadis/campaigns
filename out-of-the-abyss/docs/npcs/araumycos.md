# Araumycos

## Identity
- Known as "the Great Body"
- A vast entity intended to be the spouse of Zuggtmoy, the Demon Queen of Fungi
- Has not appeared directly; known only through references in Zuggtmoy's wedding ceremony

## Personality & Motivations
- No personality traits have been directly observed, as Araumycos has not appeared or acted on screen. Its own desires and agency remain entirely unknown to the party.

## History with the Party
- The party stumbled upon a wedding ceremony orchestrated by Zuggtmoy. During the ceremony, a wedding song identified Araumycos as Zuggtmoy's intended spouse, describing a union meant to join the two "heart to heart, becoming one 'til death do part."

## Current Status
- **Last known status:** Has not appeared directly; location and condition unknown.
- **Active plans:** Central to Zuggtmoy's scheme — she seeks to merge with Araumycos through a ceremonial wedding, which would unite the two entities into one being.
- **Known vs. hidden:** The party knows Araumycos is called "the Great Body" and is the target of Zuggtmoy's wedding ritual. The nature, scope, and sentience of Araumycos remain unknown to them.

## Relationships
- **Zuggtmoy:** Intended spouse. Zuggtmoy is actively pursuing a union/merger with Araumycos through a wedding ceremony. The nature of Araumycos's consent or awareness is unclear.
