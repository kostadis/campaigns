# Araumycos

## Identity
- Known as "the Great Body"
- A vast entity intended to be the spouse of Zuggtmoy, the Demon Queen of Fungi
- Has not appeared directly; known only through references in the wedding ceremony the party stumbled upon

## Personality & Motivations
- No personality traits have been directly observed. Araumycos has not appeared, spoken, or acted in any scene the party has witnessed.
- Its own desires or willingness regarding the union with Zuggtmoy are unknown.

## History with the Party
- The party encountered a wedding ceremony intended to unite Zuggtmoy and Araumycos. A wedding song referenced Araumycos as "the Great Body," describing a joining "heart to heart, becoming one 'til death do part." The party has had no direct interaction with Araumycos itself.

## Current Status
- **Last known status:** Has not appeared in person; location and condition unknown beyond its role as the intended subject of Zuggtmoy's wedding ritual.
- **Active plans:** Zuggtmoy is actively pursuing a ceremonial union with Araumycos. Whether Araumycos is a willing participant, a victim, or even aware of the ceremony is unclear.
- **What the party knows:** Araumycos is called "the Great Body" and is the target of Zuggtmoy's wedding. That is the extent of their knowledge.
- **What remains hidden:** Araumycos's nature, location, sentience, and stance on the union are all unknown to the party.

## Relationships
- **Zuggtmoy:** Intended spouse. Zuggtmoy's wedding ceremony is built around merging with Araumycos. The nature of this relationship — consensual, parasitic, or otherwise — has not been revealed.
