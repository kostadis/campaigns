# Prince Derendil

## Identity
- Self-styled prince; fellow prisoner of the drow
- Described as "deluded," suggesting his claim to royalty was questionable
- Met by the party as a fellow captive during their imprisonment

## Personality & Motivations
- Claimed royal status, though the validity of this claim is dubious. Little else is known of his personality beyond this delusion.

## History with the Party
- Held captive alongside the party by the drow.
- Died during the fight against the drow guards as the prisoners attempted their escape. His death is noted as part of the cost of that victory.
- His large corpse was left behind as the surviving prisoners prepared to flee.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed during the escape from drow captivity.
- Body was left behind at the site of the battle.

## Relationships
- Fellow prisoner of the party and other captives.
- No further relationships noted.

## Arc Score Events
None recorded.
