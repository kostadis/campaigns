# Vizeran Blink Clues (Beat-Linked)

Use these clues to reveal that Faerzress "blink" radicalized Vizeran's doctrine without replacing his agency.

## Running Rule

- Early clues imply corruption.
- Mid clues show pattern and timeline.
- Late clues prove escalation from preexisting beliefs to absolutist execution.

## Beat C-D (Market + Records Conflict)

1) Apothecary Ledger Fragment
- Content: "Patient V.D. exhibits recurrent certainty loops post-faerzress exposure. No memory loss. No identity break."
- Meaning: not possession; cognitive hardening.

2) Contraband Margin Note (infernal tell)
- Content: "He rewrote the pact clauses to force any council into binary outcomes."
- Meaning: legal-architect mindset intensifies.

3) Street Witness Rumor (abyssal tell)
- Content: "He speaks of districts as fuel-load now."
- Meaning: collateral abstraction is increasing.

## Beat E-F (Intelligence Unlock + Tripod Setup)

4) Archive Time-Lock Record
- Content: pre-blink memos propose targeted reform and controlled pressure.
- Post-blink memos replace reform with irreversible collapse language.
- Meaning: same thinker, more absolute doctrine.

5) Failed Counterproposal Packet
- Content: advisers offered lower-casualty alternatives; rejected as "recurrence risk."
- Meaning: tradeoff collapse, not ignorance.

6) Ritual Lab Annotation
- Content: "Do not optimize for victory. Optimize for non-restoration."
- Meaning: anti-recurrence absolutism is now primary objective.

## Beat G-H (High-Heat Detonation Window)

7) Sealed Safe-Room Transcript
- Content: "If one district survives intact, memory of order survives with it."
- Meaning: ideological purge framing.

8) Survivor Testimony
- Content: "He once argued for pruning the system. After the blink, he argued for fire."
- Meaning: confirms escalation trajectory.

## Beat I-J (Apocalypse + Aftermath)

9) Final Planning Slate
- Content: fallback plans all preserve collapse trajectory, even if mission success probability drops.
- Meaning: destruction criterion now outranks conventional success.

10) Post-Event Scholium
- Content: "He feared recurrence more than defeat."
- Meaning: defines villainy in strategic-moral terms.

## GM Note: Patron Ambiguity

If you have not locked a patron:
- Keep clues compatible with mixed infernal/abyssal imprint.
- Do not over-attribute to one demon/devil until desired reveal chapter.

If you do lock a patron later:
- Retcon by emphasis, not contradiction:
  - infernal-heavy clues become deliberate methods,
  - abyssal-heavy clues become appetite and escalation pressure.
