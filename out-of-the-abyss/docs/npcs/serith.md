# Serith

## Identity
- Drow defector; former member of Drow society who suffered a fall from grace
- First encountered as a prisoner who had turned against his former Drow comrades prior to the group's escape attempt

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven by a mysterious compulsion toward the Neverlight Grove — the same force that drew him to the dancing Myconids in the Whorlestone Caverns. He is insistent and single-minded about reaching this destination.
- Emotionally volatile; deeply affected by the death of Prince Derendil, nearly breaking into a scream before being telepathically calmed. Capable of channeling rage into decisive violence, as when he delivered the killing blow to Imbros.
- Carries himself with the knowledge and competence of a Drow warrior, but has clearly broken from his people. Curses his former kin in uniquely Drow fashion.

## History with the Party
1. **Prison Escape**: Identified Imbros as an elite Drow fighter. Armed himself from the armory and joined the fight, attacking a Drow guard. After Prince Derendil's death at Imbros's hands, he nearly lost control — Grygum telepathically calmed him by redirecting his rage toward spirituality. Serith was confused but steadied, then killed Imbros with his sword.
2. **Kuo-toa Civil War**: Fled during the chaos alongside the other escapees, running toward Grygum with the group.
3. **Travel to Neverlight Grove**: Insisted the party must go to Neverlight Grove. On the fourth day of travel, announced they must disembark and proceed on foot, noting the path passes the Lost Tomb of Khaem.
4. **Lost Tomb Approach**: Confirmed with a nod that the direction indicated by a mysterious feminine voice aligned with the route he was already leading them on.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location**: Traveling on foot through the Underdark with the party, approaching the Lost Tomb of Khaem en route to Neverlight Grove.
- **Active Plans**: Determined to reach Neverlight Grove; the compulsion driving him remains unexplained.
- **Hidden Information**: The nature of his compulsion toward the Myconids/Neverlight Grove is unclear. His specific "fall from grace" — referenced by Imbros's fear of suffering the same fate at Ilvara's hands — has not been fully revealed.

## Relationships
- **Grygum**: Responsive to Grygum's telepathic influence; Grygum successfully calmed him during a mental break. Some level of trust or susceptibility.
- **Prince Derendil (deceased)**: Deeply affected by Derendil's death — the emotional reaction was severe enough to nearly compromise the escape.
- **Imbros (deceased)**: Killed him. Imbros feared suffering Serith's fate, suggesting Serith's punishment was known and dreaded among the Drow.
- **Ilvara**: Implied to have been the one who punished or disgraced Serith. A figure of authority and fear in his past.
- **Zalthir**: Fought the same Drow guard Zalthir had engaged; interacts cooperatively with him during travel.

## Arc Score Events
- **Grygum's telepathic intervention** during the escape: Calmed Serith and redirected his rage toward spirituality — potential arc shift toward spiritual awakening or dependency on Grygum's influence.
- **Killing Imbros**: A decisive act of vengeance/closure against his former people.
- **Compulsion toward Neverlight Grove**: Ongoing; origin and implications unresolved.
