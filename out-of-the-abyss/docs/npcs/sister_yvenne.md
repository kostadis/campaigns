# Sister Yvenne

## Identity
- **Role:** Candlekeep Avowed, assigned handler in the Hall of Divination
- **Faction:** Candlekeep
- **First Appearance:** Met Daz during his consultation at Candlekeep; she was managing his divination session as his assigned liaison

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Uphold Candlekeep's information exchange protocols; provide meaningful guidance without directly violating sealed records or institutional constraints
- Practiced, patient, and methodical — someone who has spent decades managing scholars and difficult questions without losing composure
- Communicates through careful redirection, structured hints, and deliberate omission rather than direct answers; she leads patrons to conclusions rather than stating them
- Her body language occasionally betrays her words, suggesting she operates under real constraints she does not fully explain

## History with the Party
1. **First meeting (Daz's consultation):** Yvenne received Daz in the Hall of Divination and applied Candlekeep's standard reciprocal information policy — information for information
2. **Daz's disclosure:** Daz gave a full honest account (no memories, jail cell, Underdark escape, headache). She responded with genuine surprise and proceeded to provide substantive help
3. **House warfare briefing:** Explained Menzoberranzan noble house structure, the doctrine of complete elimination in house warfare, and the consequence of failing to kill a single surviving child
4. **The struck fourth seat:** Walked Daz through the significance of the word *struck* on the fourth seat — confirmed it requires specific infrastructure and is a deliberate, expensive act
5. **Sealed record deflection:** Stated there is one house she cannot speak of in the room because the public record is sealed; redirected Daz to "other doors she cannot point to directly"
6. **The heraldry book:** Provided *Geography and Cities, Menzoberranzan, Pillars of Woe* (~200 years old), which contains a footnote referencing *Daz'issin* and an appendix removed by razor at least 50 years prior
7. **The name:** Explained *Daz'issin* as a softening name mothers use for boys they want to live; noted few uses in the past three centuries and that most houses that used it did not last. When asked directly if she knows his name, she confirmed she does
8. **Final exchange:** When Daz asked what would happen if the relevant houses were *not* all successfully eliminated, her body language contradicted her spoken answer — Daz noticed and correctly read it as meaningful silence

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Hall of Divination, Candlekeep
- **Active operations:** Continuing her role as Avowed handler; likely aware that Daz's inquiry has touched something the institution has formally sealed
- **What the party knows:** She knows Daz's full name; she is constrained by sealed records from speaking about at least one relevant house directly; she communicated through redirection and implication rather than open disclosure
- **What remains hidden:** What specifically is in the sealed record and why it was sealed; what she knows about Daz's patron; the full significance of her final non-answer regarding incomplete elimination

## Relationships
- **Daz:** His assigned handler; has shown him genuine, if carefully bounded, assistance; clearly knows more than she has said and made that legible to him without saying so explicitly
- **Candlekeep (institution):** Loyal to its protocols; operates within institutional constraints she treats as real and binding, not merely bureaucratic
