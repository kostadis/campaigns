# New notes for The Matron Mother of House T'sarran (from dossier_extract_055.md)

Does not appear in person in this chunk. She is discussed at length during the interrogation of the drow spy. The spy identifies herself as of House T'sarran and states her matron has ruled House T'sarran for two hundred years. The matron sent multiple agents to the surface — described as "a couple of us" sent to the overbright — to find where the party was surfacing. Her objective is not the party itself but to identify the person who paid to have Daz protected. She is treating Daz as bait: the plan is to observe what happens to him once he reaches the surface, so that whoever paid for his protection will reveal themselves, and that person can then be identified and reported. The spy states that the matron mother cares about finding this person, and therefore the spy cares. No additional motivation is given; the matron's interest is offered as complete and sufficient explanation. The spy warns the party that killing her will not stop House T'sarran from sending more agents. Last known status: in Menzoberranzan or House T'sarran's base of operations, waiting for intelligence from surface agents.
