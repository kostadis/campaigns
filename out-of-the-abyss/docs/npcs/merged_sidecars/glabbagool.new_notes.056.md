# New notes for Glabbagool (from dossier_extract_056.md)

Glabbagool is being transported in the party's bag in miniaturized form alongside the captured House T'sarran spy. When the party arrives at Candlekeep's gate and opens the bag for the guards, both Glabbagool and the spy are visible, which draws the attention of the First Reader. During the subsequent discussion about what to do with the spy, Thorin asks for Glabbagool's perspective on the matter. Glabbagool reports that he has been occupied with prison-watching, which he finds somewhat boring, but that the spy provides entertainment value by repeatedly attempting to kill him. He describes their dynamic: she tries to kill him, he slaps her around, she tries to run, he expands, she screams, he attempts to teach her shadow monk meditation, she screams about the meditation, and then they talk. He characterizes them as having become great friends. When Zalthir confirms that watching and containing the spy constitutes legitimate pre-monk training in patience under provocation, Glabbagool takes this with complete seriousness and declares he will keep an eye on her, fixing six pseudo-eyes on the spy simultaneously. Later, a Candlekeep sage of xenobiology approaches after hearing that a gelatinous cube is being taught the ways of the shadow. When the sage questions whether a mindless digestive system can achieve monastic discipline, Zalthir vigorously defends Glabbagool as a student who is already walking, already learning, and already performing the difficult work of containing an actively hostile prisoner. The sage is satisfied by this defense and produces a vial of Resonant Slime as a gift, which Glabbagool receives with evident pleasure. Glabbagool remains in the bag with the spy at the end of these events.
