# New notes for Dawnbringer (from dossier_extract_056.md)

Dawnbringer is present throughout these events as Thorin's sentient sword. At the River Shining Tavern in Daggerford, she encounters Elin, the silent seven-year-old child. When Thorin mentions Dawnbringer could help, she performs a lesser restoration on the child without waiting for further invitation, announcing "Let me bring the light to you." The restoration is sufficient to allow Elin to speak one sentence — "The wells are not wells anymore" — before the child falls silent again. The restoration does not fully resolve whatever is affecting Elin; the party concludes the problem is something more profound than lesser restoration can fix. At the River Shining that evening, Thorin takes her outside for what is described as his first real encounter with the sea. She lights up everything around them, which defeats the stated purpose of going outside to look at the sea, but Thorin finds the experience peaceful regardless. At Candlekeep, Thorin brings her to Master Archivist Philemon's study for what amounts to a therapeutic consultation. Philemon initially assesses her as a specimen — late Netherese-era recovery, sun-worship origin, sustained darkness exposure of approximately three to four thousand years — before Thorin corrects him and introduces her as a friend. Philemon revises his assessment upward, noting a better bond between Thorin and Dawnbringer than he had feared. When Philemon asks directly about her previous wielder — the corpse she was found in — Dawnbringer goes quiet with the particular silence of someone confronting a long-avoided subject. Philemon's prescription is a two-stage process: first, she needs to remember what daylight feels like without needing to fight; second, when ready, she needs to grieve. He notes she cannot be polished, correcting his initial clinical instinct mid-sentence. Thorin accepts both stages of the process on her behalf and expresses hope she will grow in the course of it. The pronoun question regarding Dawnbringer's gender remains unresolved at the end of the session, with Thorin alternating between she/her and they/them without settling on either.
