# New notes for Dawnbringer (from dossier_extract_055.md)

Dawnbringer is present throughout the session, carried by Thorin. At the tunnel mouth, she says nothing when the group sees the sun for the first time, though she is noted to have been waiting longer than any of them. The hilt warms slightly when Thorin says they have each other. She is described as having an observable mood — radiating warmth like a hearth, not directed at anything in particular but present, increasing over the days since reaching the surface. On the evening of the second day, she watches the light die on the horizon. When Thorin expresses frustration about Tremor Sense not working on dirt, Dawnbringer tells him he has anger issues. Thorin responds with something about driving issues. During the interrogation of the drow spy, Dawnbringer's light is brought close as a form of pressure — sunlight proximity used as motivation for a drow captive. Thorin notes he wants Candlekeep to help with Dawnbringer and describes it as needing a therapist rather than a one-question situation. He is directed to an avowed named Philemon, described as specializing in psychological preservation and historical cataloging of ancient sentient artifacts, with a track record of resolving interpersonal conflict issues between sentient artifacts and their bearers. Thorin receives this with enthusiasm and describes it as couples counseling with Dawnbringer. Last known status: with Thorin at Candlekeep, scheduled for consultation with Philemon.
