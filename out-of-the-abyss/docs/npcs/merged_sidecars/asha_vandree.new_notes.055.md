# New notes for Asha Vandree (from dossier_extract_055.md)

Does not appear in person in this chunk. She is referenced in connection with a theological document she pressed into Grygum's hands — described as a theological argument about Lolth and Zuggtmoy as equivalents. The document is now in the party's possession and is among the five books donated to Candlekeep. Zalthir briefly reflects on Asha Vandree: she had looked through him the entire time they were in the same room, treating him as furniture in the structural way of the Underdark rather than from personal hostility. Zalthir notes she would have hated that her document now unlocks questions in one of the greatest libraries in the world.
