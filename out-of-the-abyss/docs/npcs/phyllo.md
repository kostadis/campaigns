# Phyllo

## Identity
- Myconid Sovereign of Neverlight Grove
- Allied with Zuggtmoy (referred to by Phyllo as "the Great Seeder")
- Encountered by the party during their visit to the Inner Circle of Neverlight Grove

## Personality & Motivations
- Devoted to Zuggtmoy and genuinely believes her influence will improve Neverlight Grove and bring happiness to all myconids. Shows no malice or aggression even when confronted—instead responds with pleading and emotional appeals. Phyllo's demeanor suggests a true believer rather than a calculating villain; there is something plaintive and almost pitiable in the conviction.

## History with the Party
- Had secretly planned to have the party killed the following day after their initial visit to the Inner Circle.
- When the party returned to the Inner Circle acting nonchalant, Phyllo greeted Basidia and asked if the visitors had enjoyed the circle and what they thought of "the changes."
- When Basidia accused Phyllo of betrayal, Phyllo did **not** attack the fleeing party. Instead, Phyllo pleaded with Basidia not to leave, calling out: *"The Neverlight Grove will be better than ever! And the Great Seeder will make us all happy!"* and *"Basidia don't go."*

## Current Status
- **Location:** Neverlight Grove (Inner Circle)
- **Allegiance:** Remains allied with Zuggtmoy
- **Active plans:** Presumably continuing to serve Zuggtmoy's interests and spread her influence through the Grove
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows Phyllo is allied with Zuggtmoy and had planned to kill them; they escaped before the plan could be executed

## Relationships
- **Basidia:** Fellow Myconid Sovereign; Phyllo clearly values this relationship and was distressed by Basidia's departure and accusation of betrayal
- **Zuggtmoy ("the Great Seeder"):** Phyllo's patron and the object of devoted loyalty
- **The Party:** Phyllo intended to have them killed but did not pursue when they fled; current disposition unknown but likely still hostile to those who reject Zuggtmoy
