# Runc

## Identity
- Orc prisoner held at Velkynvelve
- Met by the party as a fellow captive in the drow prison

## Personality & Motivations
- Devout follower of Gruumsh; his faith is central to his identity. He fought poorly in battle and is actively seeking absolution from his god for this perceived failure. His shame over his combat performance appears genuine and weighs heavily on him.

## History with the Party
- **Velkynvelve (imprisonment):** Runc learned that fellow prisoner Grygum is a cleric of Bahamut and was visibly disappointed, having hoped Grygum served Gruumsh and could grant him absolution. Eldeth mocked him over the orcish defeat at Gauntlgrym, adding to his humiliation. Buppido proposed using Runc as a sacrificial distraction during an escape attempt, treating him as expendable.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Imprisoned at Velkynvelve
- **Active plans:** None known; he is focused on his spiritual shame rather than escape planning
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows he is an orc warrior seeking religious absolution. They are aware Buppido views him as disposable.

## Relationships
- **Grygum:** Runc approached him hoping for spiritual guidance from a cleric of Gruumsh; disappointed to learn Grygum serves Bahamut instead
- **Eldeth:** Hostile/antagonistic — she mocked him for the orcish defeat at Gauntlgrym
- **Buppido:** Views Runc as expendable; proposed using him as a sacrificial distraction during escape
- **Party:** No strong bond established; regarded by at least some as a potential tool rather than an ally
