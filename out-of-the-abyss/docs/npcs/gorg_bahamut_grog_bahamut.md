# Gorg'Bahamut

## Identity
- Mentor to Grygum; likely a follower or devotee of Bahamut
- Has not appeared in person; known only through Grygum's memories

## Personality & Motivations
- A teacher figure who imparted religious and philosophical knowledge to Grygum. Values the tenets of Bahamut and views Demogorgon as the ultimate adversary. Emphasizes the importance of hoards as sacred blessings from Bahamut, suggesting a draconic or dragon-adjacent cultural perspective.

## History with the Party
- **Referenced only.** Gorg'Bahamut appears in Grygum's recollections as the source of key teachings:
  - Detailed description of Demogorgon — the Prince of Demons — including his two heads (one whispering lies, the other roaring with fury), tentacles dripping with chaos venom, and his nature as the antithesis of Bahamut.
  - The belief that "a hoard is always a valuable thing and a blessing from Bahamut."

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Unknown. Has not appeared in the campaign.
- **Active plans:** None known.
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows of him only through Grygum's references. His current whereabouts, status (alive or dead), and any ongoing activities remain entirely unknown.

## Relationships
- **Grygum:** Student/mentee. Gorg'Bahamut shaped Grygum's understanding of Bahamut, Demogorgon, and draconic values.
- **Bahamut (deity):** Devoted follower; frames his teachings around Bahamut's doctrine.
