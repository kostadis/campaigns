# Gorg'Bahamut

## Identity
- Role: Mentor and teacher; likely a religious or philosophical guide within a Bahamut-worshipping tradition
- Faction: Implied affiliation with followers of Bahamut
- Does not appear in person — known only through Grygum's memories

## Personality & Motivations
- Dedicated to teaching the ways of Bahamut, including knowledge of opposing demonic forces
- Presented as a sage figure who imparts both theological doctrine and practical wisdom
- No direct interactions observed; personality is filtered entirely through Grygum's recollections

## History with the Party
- Referenced during exploration of Grygum's memories
- Taught Grygum detailed lore about Demogorgon: his two heads (one whispering lies, the other roaring with fury), his chaos-venom tentacles, and his nature as the antithesis of Bahamut
- Also instilled the belief that "a hoard is always a valuable thing and a blessing from Bahamut"

## Current Status
- Last known status: Unknown — exists only as a figure from Grygum's past
- No active plans or operations known
- Party knows him only as Grygum's mentor; nothing further has been revealed

## Relationships
- **Grygum:** Mentor. Shaped Grygum's religious worldview and his understanding of demonic forces
