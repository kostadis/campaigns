# Aliinka

## Identity
- Member of the Council of Savants in Gracklstugh
- Connected to the Gray Ghosts
- Secretly aligned with a cult operating within Gracklstugh's power structures

## Personality & Motivations
- Operates as a covert agent embedded within Gracklstugh's intellectual elite. Her letter to Pliinki demonstrates calculated, methodical thinking — she tracks multiple operations simultaneously and reports on how external actors unknowingly serve the cult's agenda. She appears confident in the cult's progress and views the city's internal tensions as tools to be exploited.

## History with the Party
- The party has not interacted with Aliinka directly. Her existence and activities were revealed through a letter addressed to Pliinki, which the party presumably intercepted or discovered.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Gracklstugh, embedded within the Council of Savants.
- **Active plans/operations:**
  - Gray Ghost operatives are spreading whispers of dissent throughout Gracklstugh.
  - Exploiting the Deepking's reliance on the Empty-Scabbard Killers for assassinations.
  - Benefiting from Captain Errde Blackskull's paranoid search for corruption, which unwittingly aids their efforts.
  - Near-complete indoctrination of the Council of Savants, marked by placement of 'Y' carvings on members.
  - Confirmed that Uskvil successfully retrieved both a dragon egg and Clan Thrazgad ore.
  - Narrak is pleased with the Council's progress.
- **What the party knows:** Contents of the letter — Aliinka's name, her Council membership, Gray Ghost connection, and the scope of cult infiltration.
- **What remains hidden:** Aliinka's specific identity within the Council, her personal capabilities, and the full extent of the cult's plans for the dragon egg and ore.

## Relationships
- **Pliinki:** Recipient of Aliinka's intelligence report; appears to be a superior or key coordinator.
- **Narrak:** Cult figure who is pleased with Council indoctrination; likely a leader or authority Aliinka reports to (through Pliinki).
- **Uskvil:** Operative who retrieved the dragon egg and Clan Thrazgad ore; Aliinka tracked and confirmed the success of this mission.
- **Gray Ghosts:** Faction whose operatives Aliinka coordinates with to spread dissent.
- **Council of Savants:** Aliinka's official body; nearly all members have been indoctrinated into the cult.
- **Captain Errde Blackskull:** Unwitting asset; her investigations into corruption inadvertently benefit the cult's operations.
- **The Deepking:** His reliance on the Empty-Scabbard Killers serves the cult's purposes.
