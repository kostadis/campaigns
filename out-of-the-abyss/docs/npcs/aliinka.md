# Aliinka

## Identity
- Member of the Council of Savants in Gracklstugh
- Connected to the Gray Ghosts
- Covert cult operative working to subvert duergar power structures

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Advance the cult's infiltration of Gracklstugh's institutions, particularly the Council of Savants.
- Methodical and strategic; her letter to Pliinki demonstrates careful tracking of multiple operations and political dynamics simultaneously. She appears satisfied with the progress of their subversion and frames even enemies' actions (like Errde Blackskull's investigations) as advantageous.

## History with the Party
- The party has not interacted with Aliinka directly. Her existence and activities are known through a **letter addressed to Pliinki** that the party obtained. The letter detailed the status of several Gray Ghost and cult operations within Gracklstugh.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Gracklstugh, operating within the Council of Savants.
- **Active plans/operations:**
  - Gray Ghost operatives spreading whispers of dissent throughout Gracklstugh.
  - Uskvil successfully retrieved a **dragon egg** and **Clan Thrazgad ore** (confirmed in her letter).
  - Near-total indoctrination of the Council of Savants, marked by placement of **'Y' carvings** among members.
  - Exploiting the Deepking's reliance on the Empty-Scabbard Killers and Captain Errde Blackskull's paranoid corruption investigations — both unwittingly serving the cult's purposes.
- **What the party knows:** Contents of the letter — the scope of cult infiltration, Uskvil's retrieval missions, and that Narrak is pleased with the Council's indoctrination.
- **What remains hidden:** Aliinka's precise identity within the Council, her location, and any operations not mentioned in the letter.

## Relationships
- **Pliinki:** Recipient of Aliinka's letter; fellow operative. Aliinka reports to or coordinates with Pliinki.
- **Narrak:** Higher authority in the cult. Aliinka reports that Narrak is pleased with the Council's progress.
- **Uskvil:** Gray Ghost operative who carried out retrieval of the dragon egg and Clan Thrazgad ore.
- **Council of Savants:** Aliinka's cover and target of infiltration; nearly all members now indoctrinated.
- **Captain Errde Blackskull:** Unwitting asset; her paranoid search for corruption inadvertently aids the cult.
- **The Deepking:** His reliance on the Empty-Scabbard Killers serves the cult's purposes.
- **Gray Ghosts:** Faction she coordinates with to spread dissent in Gracklstugh.
