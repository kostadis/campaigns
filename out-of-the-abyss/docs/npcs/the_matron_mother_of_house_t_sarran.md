# The Matron Mother of House T'sarran

## Identity
- Matron Mother of House T'sarran, a drow noble house
- Has not appeared directly; referenced only by her subordinates during combat at Velkynvelve

## Personality & Motivations
- Unknown firsthand. Her house operates through contracted military operations, suggesting political ambition or mercenary pragmatism.
- The calm confidence of her surviving warrior ("We will meet again. Enjoy your victory for the moment.") implies a leader whose retribution is considered inevitable by her own people.

## History with the Party
- **Velkynvelve encounter:** House T'sarran sent soldiers and a mage to Velkynvelve under contract, expecting an easy kill. The party destroyed most of the force — Daz's Fireball was pivotal. The T'sarran mage warned of the matron mother before dying. The last surviving warrior retreated without urgency, promising a future reckoning.
- Grygum noted that a threat delivered without heat is always more credible than one made in anger, and resolved to learn who the matron mother is before she learns who the party is.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Unknown; presumably wherever House T'sarran is based in the Underdark.
- **Active plans:** Will likely receive a report from the surviving warrior about the destruction of her forces. Retaliation or investigation is expected.
- **Party knowledge vs. hidden:** The party knows House T'sarran exists and has a matron mother. They do not know her name, location, resources, or who contracted the hit at Velkynvelve. The matron mother may not yet know who the party is — this is the current window of advantage Grygum identified.

## Relationships
- **House T'sarran forces:** Commands soldiers and at least one mage; they operated on contract at Velkynvelve.
- **Unknown contractor:** Someone hired House T'sarran for the Velkynvelve job. Identity unknown.
- **Grygum:** Views her as a priority intelligence target; wants to identify her before she identifies the party.
- **The party broadly:** No direct relationship yet, but the destruction of her forces creates an implicit enmity.

## Arc Score Events
- **Potential trigger (pending):** The surviving warrior's report to the matron mother may escalate hostilities. No arc score change noted yet, but the seeds of an antagonist arc are clearly planted.
