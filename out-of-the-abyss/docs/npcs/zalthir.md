# Zalthir

## Identity
- **Role:** Drow monk; party scout and combatant
- **Faction:** Traveling companion to the party; no stated faction affiliation
- **First Appearance:** Encountered in the prison area of a drow holding facility; escaped alongside the party

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Survival and escape from the Underdark; acquiring magical equipment; protecting a friend named Rump-a-dump
- **Personality Traits:** Pragmatic and unsentimental — he loots bodies without hesitation and evaluates threats in terms of cost-benefit. He is willing to act decisively under pressure (casting darkness, grappling enemies, intervening to stop Sarith) but openly voices reluctance when the mission scope expands beyond his personal interests. He has a dry, sardonic edge, as shown by his sarcastic "The knowledge of the Drow…" remark.

## History with the Party

**Prison Escape**
Devised the initial breakout plan but stumbled immediately — tripping on a goblet and botching an unarmed shove against guard Imbros (accidentally caressing him instead). Survived the fight regardless and wasted no time stripping the dead for weapons and rope.

**Silken Paths**
Cut a web strand, triggering a Giant Spider encounter. Performed an impressive acrobatic strike on a Spectator from below — missed, then was paralyzed by its green ray. His flashy attempt nonetheless impressed goblin guides Yukyuk and Spiderbait enough to keep them from deserting the group. Ate the Tongue of Madness mushroom and took a vow of silence to resist its truth-compelling effect.

**Demogorgon Appearance / Darklake**
Muttered "We need to get out of here" when Demogorgon appeared, but stopped to help carry incapacitated companions. Was uncomfortable in low-ceilinged cave sections due to his height. Annoyed at having been swept into the ritual that may have summoned Demogorgon. Failed to object to Shuushar's departure in time — Thorin spoke first.

**Underdark / Myconid Scouting**
Scouted southwest cave and found myconids dancing erratically (unusual per Stool's descriptions) and quagoth spore servants. Recommended leaving them alone. Noted Sarith's compulsion intensifying near myconids and physically pulled him back, then expressed pointed skepticism toward Daz's earlier safety assurances about spore range.

**Flumph Contact / Blingdenstone Region**
Served as primary contact for a flumph, receiving telepathic visions of a derro (Plinki) conducting evil rituals at a pyramid/obelisk, and a separate chamber linked to the Gray Ghosts and Council of Savants. Pressed for actionable intelligence but received little. Openly stated his reluctance to expand the mission: *"I was just trying to kill some time before I got some magical equipment."* Participated in negotiations with Gartokkar Xundorn and pushed for compensation.

**Mesa Confrontation with Plinki**
When Plinki's derro surrounded Grygum for sacrifice, Zalthir intervened immediately — cast magical darkness over the entire upper tier and grappled Plinki within it, restraining her. Grygum had earlier used him as leverage, warning Plinki that "the Drow hates dragons and will destroy the egg."

**Egg Dispute / Rump-a-dump**
Became increasingly vocal about rescuing his friend Rump-a-dump, claiming the friend would never threaten him and referencing zombie creatures harvested from the dead. Ultimately agreed to hand the egg to Gartokkar and escape via the ports.

**Derro Patrol / Gray Ooze Chamber**
Acted as forward scout, reported contacts ahead. Summoned darkness during the derro patrol fight and landed a critical strike. Dispatched gray oozes efficiently with unarmed flurries during the fountain chamber encounter.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Traveling with the party; most recent reference places him on a boat on the Darklake (10th day, 3rd Tenday of Taraskh 1493), with subsequent activity in Blingdenstone-adjacent tunnels
- **Active Plans:** Retrieve magical equipment; rescue Rump-a-dump; agreed to deliver the dragon egg to Gartokkar and exit via the ports
- **What the Party Knows:** He is a drow monk, capable scout, and reliable (if reluctant) combatant
- **Hidden/Uncertain:** The nature of his connection to Rump-a-dump and the zombie creatures; his full reaction to Buppido's comment about him being "part of my divine plan" (he noted it but did not pursue it)

## Relationships
- **Daz:** Primary tactical partner; trusts him as backup but openly criticizes his misinformation about spore range
- **Thorin:** Mild friction — Thorin preempted Zalthir's objection to Shuushar's departure
- **Shuushar:** Recognized his navigational value; wanted to keep him in the group
- **Fargas Rumblefoot:** Skeptical; pressed Fargas to honor his bargain about treasure; assessed his claims as desperate or boastful
- **Sarith:** Watchful; physically intervened when Sarith began walking toward the myconids
- **Grygum:** Cordial enough that Grygum used Zalthir's reputation as a threat to leverage Plinki
- **Buppido:** Noted Buppido's comment about a "divine plan" involving him; did not act on the suspicion
- **Yukyuk & Spiderbait (goblin guides):** Inadvertently secured their loyalty through his acrobatic display against the Spectator
- **Rump-a-dump:** An off-screen friend Zalthir is increasingly insistent about rescuing
