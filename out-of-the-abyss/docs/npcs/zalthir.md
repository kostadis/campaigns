# Zalthir

## Identity
- Drow monk, companion and fellow prisoner of the party
- First encountered as a prisoner in a drow prison; helped devise and execute the initial escape plan alongside Daz and the other captives

## Personality & Motivations
- Pragmatic and self-interested, openly reluctant to take on missions beyond his immediate needs ("Saving the Duergar from a mad king and defeating another cult of Demogorgon is not what I am here for"). Pushes for compensation when drawn into others' problems. Quick-thinking and resourceful in dangerous situations — he immediately looted drow bodies after the prison fight and proposed burning the place down before fleeing. He carries a dry, sarcastic wit ("The knowledge of the Drow…") and shows little sentimentality, though he is fiercely loyal to his friend Rump-a-dump and will act decisively to protect companions when it matters.

## History with the Party
- **Prison Escape:** Devised the initial escape plan (sneak into tower, arm up, return weapons to prisoners). The plan fell apart when he tripped over a goblet. Fought guards including Imbros with ineffective unarmed strikes while weakened from captivity. Survived the battle, immediately looted bodies and silk ropes, and escaped with the group down the waterfall and through tunnels.
- **Tongue of Madness:** Ate the mushroom and took a vow of silence to avoid being compelled to speak truth.
- **Cave-in:** Was among those not trapped. Examined unconscious Daz and concluded he would die if not freed in time.
- **Silken Paths:** Cut a web strand, initiating the Giant Spider encounter. Participated in the fighting retreat. Confronted Fargas Rumblefoot about his promised treasure, pressing the halfling on inconsistencies. Touched Fargas's belly and announced "No gnoll spawn." During the Spectator encounter, attempted an acrobatic strike from beneath the web but missed and was paralyzed by a green ray. His acrobatics deeply impressed goblin guides Yukyuk and Spiderbait, preventing them from abandoning the group. Privately considered convincing the guides to retrieve gold from a mimic.
- **Demogorgon's Appearance:** Muttered "We need to get out of here" but stayed to help carry incapacitated companions. Was annoyed at being caught up in the religious ritual that may have summoned Demogorgon. Wanted to object to Shuushar's departure (recognizing his navigational value) but Thorin preempted him.
- **Darklake Journey:** Uncomfortable in low-ceilinged caves due to his height. Prepared for combat when a Kuo-Toan boat was spotted. Shouted "Anyone got oars?" when the boat hit a sandbar.
- **Myconid Scouting:** Scouted ahead and discovered dancing, madness-afflicted myconids and quagoth spore servants. Reported findings; group agreed to leave them alone.
- **Flumph Contact:** Served as initial contact with a flumph, receiving telepathic images of Plinki's evil rituals at a pyramid/obelisk chamber and intelligence about a derro connected to the Council of Savants and Gray Ghosts. Questioned the flumph for actionable intelligence. Expressed reluctance about the mission's expanding scope. Participated in negotiation with Gartokkar Xundorn, pushing for compensation.
- **Mesa Confrontation:** When Plinki's derro attempted to sacrifice Grygum, Zalthir cast magical darkness over the mesa top, attacked derro within it, and grappled Plinki. Grygum had used Zalthir as a threat, claiming the drow hated dragons and would destroy the egg.
- **Derro Patrol & Scouting:** Served as point scout through tunnels. Cast darkness on enemies and landed a critical strike during a derro fight. Pragmatically questioned whether to engage mad derro near a tent.
- **Sarith Incident:** Physically stopped Sarith when the drow began walking compulsively toward myconids. Noted the effect's range exceeded Daz's earlier assurances, sarcastically remarking on drow knowledge. Positioned himself far from myconids — noted by Grygum as "particularly clever."
- **Rump-a-dump:** Became increasingly insistent the party save his friend Rump-a-dump, claiming the friend would never threaten him. Mentioned zombie creatures harvested from the dead.
- **Egg Decision:** Agreed with the plan to hand the egg to Gartokkar and escape via the ports.
- **Fountain Chamber:** Dispatched a gray ooze with powerful bare-fisted strikes, then systematically destroyed oozes materializing from formless sculptures with precise flurries.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Fountain chamber area (most recent session); previously seen grappling Plinki atop the mesa in magical darkness
- **Active concerns:** Wants to save his friend Rump-a-dump; wants magical equipment; wants to escape the Underdark
- **Hidden/uncertain:** Privately considered manipulating goblin guides to retrieve mimic gold; briefly wondered if the Spectator identified Buppido as a demon but dismissed the thought

- **Daz:** Backup partner from the prison escape; Zalthir relies on him but also challenges his knowledge (spore range disagreement)
- **Rump-a-dump:** Close friend; Zalthir is increasingly insistent on rescuing him
- **Fargas Rumblefoot:** Distrusts him; pressed him on broken promises about treasure
- **Sethir:** Questioned him about knowledge of a tomb's location
- **Buppido:** Buppido expressed interest in Zalthir being "part of my divine plan" — Zalthir briefly suspected Buppido might be connected to demonic activity
- **Yukyuk & Spiderbait (goblin guides):** Impressed them with his acrobatics; privately considers manipulating them
- **Sarith:** Physically intervened to protect him from myconid compulsion
- **Grygum:** Grygum used Zalthir's reputation as a threat against Plinki
- **Thorin:** Some tension — Thorin preempted Zalthir's objection to Shuushar leaving
- **Gartokkar Xundorn:** Negotiated with him for compensation; agreed to hand over the egg
- **Stool:** Referenced Stool's knowledge of myconids when scouting
