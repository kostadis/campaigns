# Balok

## Identity
- Drow overseer of the winch-and-basket apparatus
- Stationed at the winch platform
- Commands a work detail of prisoners for cargo loading and unloading

## Personality & Motivations
- A petty tyrant who relishes the small authority he holds over prisoners. Takes visible pleasure in cruelty, directing Quaggoth attendants to drop the cargo basket suddenly and laughing as it bounces with prisoners inside. Runs his operation with blunt threats: "You will clean and unload. If you steal anything, then there will be consequences."

## History with the Party
- Encountered as the overseer of the cargo loading/unloading chore. Issued a direct warning to the prisoners about theft before putting them to work.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Stationed at the winch platform.
- **Activity:** Overseeing cargo operations with Quaggoth attendants assisting him.
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows his role, his threat regarding theft, and his habit of tormenting workers via the basket drops.

## Relationships
- **Zalthir, Buppido, Jimjar, Eldeth:** Prisoners assigned to his work detail; he commands them directly.
- **Quaggoth attendants:** Uses them to operate the winch and to torment prisoners on his orders.
