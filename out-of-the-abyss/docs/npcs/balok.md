# Balok

## Identity
- Drow overseer of the winch-and-basket apparatus
- Stationed at the winch platform
- Commands a work detail of prisoners for cargo loading and unloading

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: maintain order and productivity at the winch platform
- Cruel and petty; takes visible pleasure in tormenting prisoners. He has Quaggoth attendants deliberately drop the cargo basket to frighten and jostle those inside, laughing as it bounces. Enforces discipline through threats: *"You will clean and unload. If you steal anything, then there will be consequences."*

## History with the Party
- Encountered as a captor/overseer. The party (or fellow prisoners) were assigned to the cargo loading and unloading chore under his supervision.
- Delivered his warning about theft and consequences directly to the prisoners.
- Demonstrated his sadistic streak by ordering the Quaggoths to drop the basket while prisoners were in it.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Stationed at the winch platform.
- **Activity:** Actively overseeing cargo operations with Quaggoth attendants.
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows he is cruel, commands the winch detail, and has Quaggoth muscle at his disposal. No hidden information noted.

## Relationships
- **Zalthir, Buppido, Jimjar, Eldeth** — prisoners assigned to his work detail.
- **Quaggoth attendants** — serve as his enforcers and operate the winch under his orders; he uses them to intimidate prisoners.
