# Burrow Warden Jadger

## Identity
- Spectral leader of the Burrow Warden ghosts of Blingdenstone
- Represents the ghost faction in Blingdenstone's council deliberations

## Personality & Motivations
- Deeply protective of sacred ground, particularly the burial sites and sanctum tied to the Burrow Wardens. Willing to engage in political negotiation but firm in opposing desecration of these places. Demonstrated a sense of honor and respect toward the party through ceremonial gestures.

## History with the Party
- **Council Meeting in Blingdenstone:** Jadger's ghost faction objected to the Miners Guild's proposal to strip-mine the sanctum, which the Burrow Wardens considered sacred burial grounds. A sustainable mining compromise was ultimately brokered that respected their burial sites.
- **Party's Departure from Blingdenstone:** Jadger and the Burrow Warden ghosts were present to see the Ember Vanguard off, dipping their spectral banners in salute as the party left the city.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Blingdenstone
- **Active Concerns:** Overseeing adherence to the mining compromise that protects the sanctum and burial grounds
- **Party Knowledge:** The party is aware of Jadger, his faction's role in the council, and the compromise reached regarding the sanctum

## Relationships
- **Miners Guild (Blingdenstone):** Adversarial on the sanctum mining issue, though a compromise was reached
- **The Ember Vanguard (the party):** Respectful; honored them with a spectral banner salute upon departure
