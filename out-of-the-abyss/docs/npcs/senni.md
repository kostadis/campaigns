# Senni

## Identity
- Quartermaster of Blingdenstone; represents the non-mining population
- Faction: Blingdenstone civic leadership (non-mining populace)
- First mentioned by city guards as a key authority the party should speak with regarding permissions (specifically about Glabagool's presence in the city)

## Personality & Motivations
- Advocates for the safety and interests of Blingdenstone's non-mining citizens. Frustrated with Chief Dorbo Diggermattock's failure to adequately address the ooze infestation threatening the settlement. More pragmatic and open-minded than Dorbo—willing to consider reconciliation with the Gold Whisker clan despite suspicions of lycanthropy. Demonstrated willingness to collaborate with outsiders and delegate leadership when she sees competence.

## History with the Party
1. **Initial mention:** City guards directed the party to speak with Senni (alongside Chief Dorbo) about obtaining full permissions in Blingdenstone, particularly regarding Glabagool.
2. **Diggermattock Hall confrontation:** The party witnessed Senni publicly confronting Dorbo about his inaction on the ooze infestation. She also showed openness to reconciling with the Gold Whisker clan, contrasting with Dorbo's suspicion.
3. **Grand Moot:** Senni had previously agreed to support Daz's plan. At the grand moot, she asked Daz to lead the meeting and call it to order, signaling trust and endorsement. She was present among Blingdenstone's major leaders during the planning session.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Diggermattock Hall / Grand Moot assembly
- **Active concerns:** Pushing for action against the ooze infestation; supportive of Daz's plan (details of plan not specified in notes)
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows she is a key political figure, that she opposes Dorbo on the ooze issue, is open to the Gold Whisker clan, and has aligned with Daz's plan

## Relationships
- **Chief Dorbo Diggermattock:** Political tension; she publicly clashed with him over the ooze infestation and disagrees on the Gold Whisker question
- **Daz (party member):** Allied; she agreed to support Daz's plan and entrusted them with leading the grand moot
- **Gold Whisker Clan:** More open to reconciliation than Dorbo, though no direct interaction noted
- **City Guards:** Recognized as a co-authority alongside Dorbo for granting permissions

## Arc Score Events
- Senni's agreement to support Daz's plan and her public endorsement at the grand moot suggest a positive arc score shift (increase in trust/alliance with the party).
