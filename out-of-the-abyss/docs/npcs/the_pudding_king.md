# The Pudding King

## Identity
- Small deep gnome, now corrupted and transformed; self-styled ruler of an ooze kingdom
- Allied with **Juiblex** (the Faceless Lord, demon lord of oozes); also in communication with **Zuggtmoy** (Queen of Rot), though hostile toward her ambitions
- Central source of the ooze plague threatening **Blingdenstone**
- First encountered via disembodied voice in the vast ooze-filled cavern south of Blingdenstone; later observed directly by Daz's bat familiar on his slime-covered throne

## Personality & Motivations
Driven by vengeance, pride, and madness, all amplified by Juiblex's demonic influence. He holds court like a deranged monarch, monologuing to his ooze "henchmen" and gloating over Blingdenstone's suffering. His goal is to convert all of Blingdenstone's biomass into ooze biomass—consuming everyone—as promised to him by Juiblex. He is territorial and resentful of Zuggtmoy's attempts to co-opt his domain for her fungal designs.

## History with the Party
1. **Disembodied voice**: Welcomed intruders to his domain, proclaiming the coming of the Faceless Lord to "enhance" Blingdenstone.
2. **Scouting via bat familiar**: Daz's familiar located him on his slime-covered throne in a phosphorescent lichen-lit cave, surrounded by pools of green slime and hundreds of oozes.
3. **Intel from Chief Chipgrin**: The party learned the Pudding King was once an ordinary gnome tunnel worker before his transformation. Chipgrin knew him personally.
4. **Psychic intercept**: The party overheard Zuggtmoy's message to the Pudding King about using oozes to "soften the shell" so her garden could "take root in the flesh beneath."
5. **Final confrontation**: Observed through the ochre jelly barricade monologuing to Princess Ebonheir and Prince Livid, gloating about Blingdenstone's army being consumed. When **Zalthir** reached him and grappled him, the Pudding King transformed into an amorphous ooze form. Zalthir maintained his grip and dragged him fifteen feet south, isolating him from his henchmen.

## Current Status
- **Last known**: Grappled by Zalthir in ooze form, dragged away from his throne and separated from his two ooze henchmen. Battle is ongoing.
- **Active threat**: His ooze army is engaged with the Blingdenstone army at the amphitheater as a distraction while the party executes the snatch plan.
- **Key risk**: If incapacitated without a coordinated effort, the uncontrolled ooze army could overrun Blingdenstone.
- **Party knows**: His origin, his alliance with Juiblex, his communication with Zuggtmoy, his ooze transformation ability, the green slime ceiling defenses, and his two named henchmen.
- **Hidden**: The full extent of his ooze-form capabilities; whether killing him will truly end the ooze threat or if Juiblex's influence persists independently.

## Relationships
- **Juiblex**: Patron and source of his power. Juiblex promised him Blingdenstone as his kingdom. The demon's influence bleeds through the Pudding King and corrupts the broader gnome community (amplifying greed and aggression).
- **Zuggtmoy**: In communication with her but hostile—she wants to use his oozes as a tool for her own fungal colonization. He resents her encroachment.
- **Princess Ebonheir & Prince Livid**: His two ooze henchmen; serve as his court and throne room guards.
- **Chief Chipgrin**: Knew him before his transformation; a personal connection from their days in the tunnels.
- **Blingdenstone**: His target for total destruction/conversion. His demonic corruption is passively destabilizing the city's leadership and populace.
- **Zalthir**: Currently grappling him in combat.
