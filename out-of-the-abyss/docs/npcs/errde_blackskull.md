# Errde Blackskull

## Identity
Captain of the Stone Guards, Gracklstugh, based at Overlake Hold. Connected to the Ember Vanguard faction. *Also known as: Errde, Errde Blackstaff, Erdde Blackskull, Captain Errde.*

*Also known as: Errde, Errde Blackstaff, Erdde Blackskull, Captain Errde, Errde (Captain Errde), Errde Captain Errde, Captain Blackskull.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Core obsession: expose what she believes is deep corruption in the Deepking's court, linked to the Council of Savants and possibly elements of the Clan Lairds. She sees this as her path to the Deepking's favor.
- Deeply paranoid and consumed by "conspiracy theories" — believes the Grey Ghosts and Council of Savants are working together to corrupt the Deepking and destabilize Gracklstugh.
- Personally affected by the "empty scabbard killers" — two victims were her own clan members, including her uncle. Pretends not to believe assassins targeted her family but privately craves proof.
- Wants the destruction of the Derro, whom she scapegoats for the city's problems.
- Deeply rationalist, refusing to accept supernatural explanations and insisting killings must be the work of a government agency using legend as cover.
- Pragmatic and resourceful — willing to use outsiders as expendable assets for tasks she can't handle with her own forces due to political constraints.
- Projects authority but personal stakes betray her composure. Combines hospitality with veiled threats. Willing to fabricate evidence if real evidence isn't provided.

## History with the Party
1. **Darklake Brewery:** Approached the party with armed Stone Guards. When Daz challenged whether this was a threat, she defused the tension and assured them it was friendly.
2. **Overlake Hold Meeting:** Served refreshments, then requested they track down Droki in the West Cleft District (Derro territory). Offered 175gp in equipment upfront, 175gp more on delivery of Droki and proof of conspiracy. Leveraged powerful incentive: she can tell any Drow searching for escaped slaves that those slaves are not in the city.
3. **Gate passes:** Provided the party with passes allowing them through gates that restrict non-Duergar access.
4. **Investigation briefing:** Agreed to employ the party to investigate the Grey Ghosts, Council of Savants, and Droki's involvement. When they reported back, immediately overinterpreted Flumph testimony as proof of conspiracy until Daz talked her down. Demanded they return for concrete evidence — specifically papers they failed to retrieve.
5. **Cover plan approval:** Approved Daz's plan to have the party publicly engage with Clan Ironhead and Clan Xardelva for plausible deniability. Provided intelligence on inter-clan politics.
6. **Armed the crew:** Equipped the party's companions (Jimjar, Spiderbait, Sarith, Eldeth, and others) with weapons.
7. **Party's avoidance:** The party grew wary and deliberately chose to avoid her, fearing imprisonment and torture. They planned to use Gartokkar as their escape route instead.
8. **Docks incident:** A Stone Guard recognized the party from an Ember Vanguard poster and warned Errde, who subsequently alerted the Drow to the party's passage through Gracklstugh.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Overlake Hold, Gracklstugh
- **Active operations:** Hunting Droki and the conspiracy; investigating the "empty scabbard killers"; coordinating intelligence
- **Awaiting:** Concrete evidence from the party about Council of Savants involvement and proof about the Empty Scabbard Killers
- **What remains hidden from her:** According to intercepted letters, she is unwittingly aiding a cult's efforts to sow discord in the city through her paranoia. She does not know she is being manipulated.

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Transactional alliance turned adversarial. Initially cooperative but the party now fears and avoids her. Her actions put the Drow back on their trail.
- **Daz:** Primary negotiator. Daz sees through her paranoia and views her scapegoating critically, but works with her pragmatically. Daz talked her down from overinterpreting evidence.
- **The Deepking / Clan Steelshadow:** Seeks his favor through exposing corruption but believes the Council corrupts him.
- **Council of Savants / Clan Lairds:** Primary adversary; suspects them of conspiracy.
- **Droki:** Her primary investigative target.
- **The Cult (Aliinka, Narrak):** They view her as a useful fool whose paranoia serves their agenda — she is unaware of this.
- **Grygum:** Tasked him with investigating the "empty scabbard killers." She can invoke intimidation through his name.
- **Grimholl Forgebrand:** Her subordinate who briefs on the killings.
- **Clan Ironhead & Clan Xardelva:** Views them as useful cover; shared intelligence that both oppose the Deepking.
- **Grey Ghosts:** Believes they collaborate with the Council.
- **Derro population:** Wants their destruction; uses them as scapegoats.
- **Empty Scabbard Killers:** Suspects they assassinated her clan members; craves proof.
- **The Drow:** Maintains communication channel; passed intelligence about the party's movements.
- **Stone Guard:** Commands their loyalty; guards report directly to her.
- **Ember Vanguard:** Connected to this faction.


## Notes from the Eldking Contract
- A contract found on Eldgrim's desk, written by the Deepking, names Captain Blackskull as a suspected traitor and orders that she "receive what's coming to her." The Deepking states she has been suspected of treason since she was young and has "always had an eye on my throne."
