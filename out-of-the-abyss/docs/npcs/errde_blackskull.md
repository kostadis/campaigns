# Errde Blackskull

## Identity
- Captain of the Stone Guards, based at Overlake Hold in Gracklstugh
- First appeared at the Darklake Brewery, where she approached the party with armed Duergar and invited them to her office

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Expose corruption she believes is linked to the Council of Savants and possibly the Clan Lairds, thereby earning favor with the Deepking
- Politically ambitious and genuinely convinced something is deeply wrong in the Deepking's court
- Presents as professional and hospitable (pastries, warm drink) but backs her charm with veiled threats — she controls whether Drow slavers learn the party's whereabouts
- Responded to Daz's confrontational tone with composure and a smile, suggesting confidence and restraint

## History with the Party
1. **Darklake Brewery meeting:** Approached the party with Stone Guards and invited them to Overlake Hold, assuring them it was friendly despite Daz's challenge.
2. **Overlake Hold briefing:** Offered the party a job — track down a Duergar named Droki in the West Cleft District (Derro territory), where she cannot send guards without provoking violence. Offered 175gp in equipment upfront, 175gp more upon delivery of Droki and proof of conspiracy. Also leveraged her ability to misdirect any Drow searching for escaped slaves.
3. **Party later chose to avoid her:** Fearing imprisonment and torture, the party opted to use Gartokkar as their escape route from the city rather than risk further dealings with the Stone Guard.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Overlake Hold, Gracklstugh
- **Active operations:** Still hunting Droki and pursuing her corruption investigation
- **Hidden from the party:** Intercepted letters from Aliinka and Narrak describe her as "consumed by conspiracy theories" and "hunting phantoms" — her investigation, while genuine in intent, may be unwittingly aiding a cult's efforts to sow discord. The party has read these letters and is aware of this framing.

## Relationships
- **The Deepking:** Seeks to earn his favor by exposing court corruption
- **Council of Savants / Clan Lairds:** Suspects them of corruption; actively investigating
- **Droki:** Her primary investigative target, believed to be a key link in the conspiracy
- **Aliinka & Narrak (cult-affiliated):** View her as a useful pawn whose paranoia serves their goals
- **Gartokkar:** Indirect rival for the party's trust — the party chose him over her as their ally for leaving the city
- **The Party:** Transactional relationship; deal was struck but the party fears her power and chose to distance themselves. Daz was initially confrontational.

## Arc Score Events
- No explicit arc score changes noted, but the party's deliberate decision to avoid her and fear of Stone Guard imprisonment suggests a deterioration in trust/alignment with her faction.
