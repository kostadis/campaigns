# Naomi Pathshutter

## Identity
- Major leader of Blingdenstone, with expertise in earth magic and elemental summoning
- Political ally of Gurnik Tapfinger
- Also referred to as "Nomi Pathshutter" in some records
- First appeared at the grand moot alongside Gurnik Tapfinger

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven to restore the natural order of the earth and reopen the path to Entemoch's boon. Frustrated that hallowing Ogremoch's altar did not produce the expected results.
- Hardworking and dedicated to her craft — she had been awake for forty-eight hours attempting to summon earth elementals. Can be blunt and socially clumsy (mistook Daz for an ogre, causing a tense moment), but is capable of genuine respect when she sees competence demonstrated. Relies on community support and takes her responsibilities seriously.

## History with the Party
- **Grand Moot:** Presented alongside Gurnik Tapfinger, proposing a plan to cleanse the Temple of the Steadfast Stone. The proposal argued that doing so would allow Earth Elementals to be summoned safely, shielded from being driven insane by Ogremoch's Bane. The presentation appeared rehearsed and coordinated between the two leaders.
- **Meeting Hall Briefing:** Naomi briefed the party on the current crisis — the tunnels to Entemoch's throne had shifted and sealed after Ogremoch's altar was hallowed, the earth seemed frozen and unable to heal, and miners were reporting strange vibrations ("as if the rock was trying to speak but couldn't find the words").
- **Social Friction with Daz:** She mistakenly identified Daz as an ogre, leading to a tense exchange about respect. The community stepped in to defend their allies and smooth things over.
- **Shift to Respect:** When the party revealed they had defeated four insane earth elementals and a Medusa, Naomi's attitude changed to genuine admiration.
- **Quest Given:** She tasked the party with investigating the strange vibrations and finding the lost path to Entemoch's boon.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The community meeting hall
- **Active Plans:** Attempting (so far unsuccessfully) to summon earth elementals; seeking answers about why the earth remains frozen and the tunnels have sealed
- **Party's Active Quest from Her:** Investigate the strange vibrations and locate the lost path to Entemoch's boon
- **Hidden Information:** None indicated beyond the party's current knowledge

## Relationships
- **Gurnik Tapfinger:** Close political ally; the two coordinated a joint presentation at the grand moot, suggesting an established working relationship.
- **The Party (general):** Initially dismissive or unaware of their capabilities; now genuinely respectful after learning of their accomplishments
- **Daz:** Rocky start due to her mistaking him for an ogre; relationship likely still somewhat strained
- **The Mining Community:** Clearly a respected figure; the community rallied to mediate the conflict with Daz, suggesting mutual loyalty
- **Entemoch / Ogremoch:** Knowledgeable about the earth princes and their domains; invested in reaching Entemoch's boon

## Arc Score Events
- **Increase:** Party's revelation that they defeated four insane earth elementals and a Medusa shifted her attitude from frustration/dismissiveness to genuine respect and admiration.
