# Imbros

## Identity
- Elite Drow male fighter, trained in Menzoberranzan from a very young age
- Guard stationed at Velkynvelve (the Drow outpost/prison)
- First identified by name through Suushar; later identified by Serith as one of the more elite Drow male fighters

## Personality & Motivations
- Confident and dismissive — viewed the initial prisoner revolt as routine, finding Thorin's attempt to overpower him amusing rather than alarming. Pragmatic enough to avoid killing prisoners when possible, knowing their deaths are costly. Deeply feared punishment from Ilvara, refusing to call for help during the revolt rather than risk being disciplined the way Serith had been. Fought to the bitter end out of pride and self-preservation.

## History with the Party
1. **Pre-Revolt:** Assigned cooking chores to prisoners, selecting Suushar, Topsy, Turvy, and Gyrgum for kitchen duty.
2. **The Revolt:** Was playing the Drow game Sava with two other guards in the guard chamber when the prisoner revolt began. Thorin attempted to overpower him; Imbros was amused and tried to subdue rather than kill. Deftly dodged Zalthir's shove attempt. As the revolt escalated and prisoners armed themselves, he struck Thorin unconscious. Resisted Daz's Mind Sliver and Grygum's Toll the Dead, but was hit by crossbow bolts from multiple prisoners and two volleys of Daz's Magic Missiles. Despite being severely wounded, he killed **Prince Derendil** with poisoned strikes (Drow Poison). Ultimately slain by **Serith's** sword.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed by Serith during the prisoner revolt at Velkynvelve.

## Relationships
- **Ilvara:** His superior; Imbros feared her punishment deeply — enough that he refused to call for reinforcements during the revolt, preferring to handle it alone.
- **Serith:** Fellow Drow who identified Imbros as elite; ultimately Serith killed him. Imbros feared suffering whatever punishment Serith had previously received from Ilvara.
- **Thorin:** Attempted to overpower Imbros; Imbros subdued him, knocking him unconscious.
- **Prince Derendil:** Killed by Imbros with Drow Poison during the revolt.
- **Daz:** Hit Imbros with two volleys of Magic Missiles; Imbros resisted Daz's Mind Sliver.
