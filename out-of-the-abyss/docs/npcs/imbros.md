# Imbros

## Identity
- Elite Drow male fighter, stationed as a guard at the Drow outpost
- Trained in Menzoberranzan from a very young age
- First appeared assigning cooking chores to prisoners; identified by name through Suushar

## Personality & Motivations
- Pragmatic and disciplined — initially chose to subdue rather than kill Thorin, knowing that killing prisoners is costly. Viewed the early prisoner revolt with amusement rather than alarm, treating it as routine. Deeply feared Ilvara's punishment (having seen what happened to Serith), which drove him to refuse to call for help during the revolt, preferring to handle it alone rather than admit failure.

## History with the Party
1. **Kitchen Duty Assignment:** Selected Suushar, Topsy, Turvy, and Gyrgum for cooking chores. A mundane interaction; the party learned his name from Suushar.
2. **The Revolt:** Was playing the Drow game Sava with two other guards when the prisoner revolt began. Amused by Thorin's initial attempt to overpower him, he subdued Thorin rather than killing him. Dodged Zalthir's shove. As the revolt escalated and prisoners armed themselves, he struck Thorin unconscious. Resisted Daz's Mind Sliver and Gyrgum's Toll the Dead, but was hit by crossbow bolts from multiple prisoners and two volleys of Daz's Magic Missiles. Despite grievous wounds, he killed **Prince Derendil** with poisoned strikes (Drow Poison). Ultimately slain by **Serith's** sword.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed by Serith during the prisoner revolt.

## Relationships
- **Ilvara:** Feared her authority and punishment deeply — this fear drove his refusal to call for reinforcements during the revolt.
- **Serith:** Identified by Serith as one of the more elite Drow fighters. Ironically killed by Serith during the escape.
- **Prince Derendil:** Killed Derendil with Drow Poison during the revolt.
- **Thorin:** Subdued and knocked unconscious during the fight; viewed him with condescension.
- **Daz:** Targeted by multiple spells (Mind Sliver, Magic Missiles); resisted some but was worn down.
