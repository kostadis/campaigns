# Bookwyrm (First Reader)

## Identity
- **Role:** First Reader of Candlekeep — the institution's senior authority figure on-site
- **Faction:** Candlekeep
- **First Appearance:** Received the party at Candlekeep's gate upon their arrival; first point of institutional contact

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Maintaining Candlekeep's order, neutrality, and protocols
- Efficient and unsentimental — assigns handlers without ceremony and moves quickly to address complications
- Politically precise: when the House T'sarran spy attempted to invoke formal prisoner status, she deflected institutional responsibility back onto the party through careful legal framing rather than direct confrontation
- Prefers to communicate uncomfortable institutional positions through intermediaries (used Kalan Strongbranch rather than speaking directly about Candlekeep's prisoner policy)

## History with the Party
- **Gate arrival:** Received the party and assigned each member a personal handler — Daz to Sister Yvenne, Zalthir to Khell-Vire, Thorin to Master Archivist Philemon, Grygum to Vareth
- **Spy incident:** When the party opened their bag at the gate and revealed the House T'sarran spy, Bookwyrm immediately identified the situation as unacceptable. She informed the spy that because they technically traveled as a guest of the party — not a formal prisoner — custody decisions rested with the party, not Candlekeep. Candlekeep's prisoner policy (hold but do not keep; eventually release at the gate with a warning) was communicated via Kalan Strongbranch rather than stated by her directly

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Candlekeep
- **Active Role:** Overseeing the party's access and conduct within the institution
- **What the Party Knows:** She is the senior authority at Candlekeep, she noticed the spy immediately, and she will not absorb institutional liability for the party's complications
- **Hidden:** Her broader motivations, her read on the party's true purpose, and whether she has independent interest in the spy or the party's business

## Relationships
- **Kalan Strongbranch:** Used as a proxy to deliver sensitive institutional policy — suggests a trusted, functional working relationship
- **Sister Yvenne, Khell-Vire, Master Archivist Philemon, Vareth:** Candlekeep handlers assigned to party members — she controls access to these figures
- **The Party:** Formal and watchful; cooperative but not warm
- **House T'sarran Spy:** Identified immediately; legally maneuvered to keep the situation the party's problem
