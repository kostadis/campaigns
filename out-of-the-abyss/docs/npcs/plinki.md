# Plinki

## Identity
- Female derro, recent convert to the Cult of Demogorgon
- Affiliated with the Council of Savants and the Grey Ghosts
- First encountered indirectly through a flumph's psychic trauma dump; later met in person atop a mesa near a black obelisk in a large Underdark cavern

## Personality & Motivations
- Fanatically devoted to Demogorgon (whom she mentally referred to as the "Big Baboon Demon"). Her core goal was to corrupt a stolen red dragon egg by infusing it with a demon's spirit, creating a dual-headed dragon to offer Demogorgon as a mount.
- Erratic and gleeful, she displayed classic derro mania — squealing with delight at the obelisk's magical pulses, reverently stroking its surface, and scribbling feverishly in a notebook. She was quick to pivot from excitement to murderous intent, declaring that Grygum's heart would seal her ritual. She refused all attempts at reasoning.

## History with the Party
1. **Flumph encounter:** The party first learned of Plinki through a flumph's psychic impressions — a derro conducting evil activities around a "mighty and tasty obelisk" in a chamber with a circular pathway and pyramid, working with the Council of Savants.
2. **Obelisk confrontation:** The party found Plinki atop the highest tier of a mesa, tending to a fifty-foot-tall black obelisk. She was accompanied by six other derro. When Grygum introduced himself as a cleric of Bahamut, she became ecstatic, interpreting his arrival as proof the ritual was working.
3. **Revelation and betrayal:** She explained that the Grey Ghosts stole the red dragon egg from the Keepers of the Flame, and she brought it here to corrupt it using the obelisk's magic. She then declared Grygum would be sacrificed to complete the ritual.
4. **Combat and death:** Grygum threatened the egg's destruction; Plinki hesitated. Zalthir enveloped her in magical darkness, grappled her, and Grygum struck her with a necromantic claw. Zalthir identified her as the enemy lynchpin and killed her with a fire breath attack. Her death shattered her followers' morale.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed by Zalthir's fire breath attack atop the mesa.
- Her death caused her derro followers to panic and break.
- The fate of the corrupted dragon egg and the black obelisk following her death is unresolved.

## Relationships
- **Demogorgon:** Object of her fanatical worship; she sought to create a mount for the demon lord.
- **Council of Savants:** Collaborators in her obelisk work.
- **Grey Ghosts:** Stole the red dragon egg from the Keepers of the Flame; Plinki obtained it from them.
- **Keepers of the Flame:** Original custodians of the egg Plinki corrupted.
- **Grygum (party):** Viewed him as a divinely sent sacrifice — "the beating heart of a cleric of Bahamut" to seal the ritual. Refused his attempts at persuasion.
- **Zalthir (party):** Identified Plinki as the priority target; grappled and ultimately killed her.
- **Derro followers (6):** Served as her retinue at the obelisk; collapsed into panic after her death.
