# Tappy Foamstrap

## Identity
- Owner of the Foaming Mug Tavern, a massive cave converted into an inn with human-sized rooms
- Deep gnome
- First encountered when the party stayed at her tavern overnight

## Personality & Motivations
- Deeply bored, having heard every local patron's stories dozens of times over. Desperate for fresh tales and new faces. Protective of her tavern's reputation — she agreed to sell the party spoiled booze but only on the condition it wouldn't reflect poorly on the Foaming Mug.

## History with the Party
- **Evening stay:** Hosted the party at the Foaming Mug Tavern, engaging with them as a welcome source of new stories.
- **Next morning:** Negotiated a deal to sell the party barrels of spoiled booze, which they intended to use as improvised weapons against oozes. She agreed on the condition that her tavern's reputation would be kept intact.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The Foaming Mug Tavern
- **Active plans:** Running her establishment as usual
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows her as a friendly, story-hungry innkeeper willing to make unconventional deals. No hidden information noted.

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Friendly and transactional. She valued them primarily as a source of fresh entertainment and stories, and they leveraged that rapport to acquire supplies.
