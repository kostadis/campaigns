# Thorin

## Identity
- Orc Cleric of Bahamut
- First appeared vouching for the party at the gates of Blingdenstone, where his unusual faith as an orc devoted to Bahamut surprised the gnome guards

## Personality & Motivations
- Holds strong opinions about combat and tactics — notably frustrated when enemies don't fight "as they're meant to." Pragmatic and observant, critiquing Sethir's map for inaccurate relative distances. Stubborn in a fight, pressing his attack even while confused. Devotion to Bahamut appears genuine and defining, enough to mark him as unusual among his kind.

## History with the Party
- Vouched for the party's honesty at the gates of Blingdenstone, securing their entry.
- During a web-cutting retreat from Giant Spiders, expressed irritation at the spiders' unpredictable behavior.
- In the Spectator fight, was struck by a confusion ray but continued his charge and attacked regardless. Was hit by a blue ray from a second eyestalk with no effect. Attempted a "hill strike" — a technique learned from a hill giant battle master — to knock the Spectator prone; the creature proved too resilient.

## Current Status
- Last known location: Blingdenstone / the Spectator encounter site
- No confirmed active plans beyond traveling with or alongside the party
- **Party knows:** His faith, his combat style, his hill giant training, and that he vouched for them at the gate
- **Hidden:** Deeper backstory, motivations for following Bahamut, and reasons for association with the party are not yet established in notes

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Ally; willing to put his credibility on the line for them at Blingdenstone
- **Sethir:** Mildly critical of his cartography — noted the map's distances were inaccurate
- **Gnome Guards of Blingdenstone:** Surprised by him; his endorsement carried weight despite (or because of) their shock
