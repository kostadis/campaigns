# Thorin

## Identity
- Orc cleric of Bahamut
- Party member (PC)
- Notable for being an orc devoted to Bahamut, which surprises NPCs who encounter him

## Personality & Motivations
- Holds strong opinions about how combat "should" be fought — was annoyed at Giant Spiders for not "playing the game as it was meant to be played" during a web-cutting retreat. Favors direct, honorable engagement.
- Observant and practical — noticed that Sethir's map didn't reflect relative distances accurately.
- Brave and tenacious in combat, pressing attacks even when afflicted by magical effects.

## History with the Party
- **Giant Spider encounter:** Expressed frustration at the spiders' retreat tactics during a web-cutting sequence.
- **Spectator fight:** Was hit by a confusion ray from the Spectator but charged and attacked regardless. Struck by a second (blue) ray from another eyestalk but was unaffected. Attempted a "hill strike" — a technique learned from a hill giant battle master — to knock the Spectator prone, but the creature proved too strong.
- **Arrival at Blingdenstone:** Vouched for the party's truthfulness at the gates, surprising the gnome guards with his unusual faith as an orc cleric of Bahamut.

## Current Status
- Last seen at or near Blingdenstone after vouching for the party at the gates.
- No specific active plans noted beyond the party's ongoing journey.

## Relationships
- **Sethir:** Traveling companion; Thorin critiqued the accuracy of Sethir's map.
- **Blingdenstone gnome guards:** Made an impression as an orc devoted to Bahamut; his vouching for the party carried enough weight to gain entry.
- **Hill giant battle master (unnamed):** Past encounter or training — source of the "hill strike" technique Thorin uses in combat.
