# Pelek

## Identity
- Ghost of a Svirfneblin (deep gnome) merchant; former business partner of Buppido
- First appeared in Buppido's shrine cave after the party killed Buppido, emerging from the ground and exclaiming "Wonderful!"

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: have his bones transported to Blingdenstone so he could be released from his ghostly existence.
- Verbose, philosophical, and theatrical — fitting for someone who always aspired to be an actor. Prone to dry humor ("I am a ghost, not a fungi expert") and existential musings about the nature of death and sleep. Practically minded despite his eccentricities, offering useful advice and scouting assistance. Deeply grateful and emotionally vulnerable beneath the wit.

## History with the Party
1. **Buppido's Shrine Cave:** Appeared after Buppido's death. Explained his backstory — murdered and dismembered by Buppido, trapped within 30 feet of his bones. Provided critical lore about the Faerzress "blinking" event and Buppido's subsequent descent into believing himself divine. Advised the party on how to behave in Blingdenstone (no Lolth references, no spiders, no eights). Requested his bones be taken to Blingdenstone.
2. **Whorlstone Caverns:** Scouted through a crack in the wall, identifying Pygmywort and Bigwig mushrooms on the other side. Traveled with the party, carried via one of his bones by Grygum.
3. **Battle with the Thief-Derro:** Attempted to possess a Derro during combat but was psychically repelled — the Derro's mind resisted "like a fortress."
4. **Mesa Battle:** Successfully possessed a different Derro body after the fight. Asked to be tied up before the party slept, worried he might lose control of the body.
5. **Traveling Companion:** Remained with the party through the Whorlstone Caverns. When Zalthir exhibited compulsive hoarding, Pelek confirmed it wasn't ghost-related and prompted Grygum to use clerical magic, leading to Dawnbringer casting lesser restoration.
6. **Blingdenstone — Laid to Rest:** At the catacombs beneath the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough, Grygum interred his bones with a dragon-themed ritual and a gold coin. Pelek first convinced the reluctant priest Glyphic Shroomlight to allow the interment. Expressed profound relief and gratitude, then faded away peacefully, leaving a warm, comforting feeling.

## Current Status
- **Laid to rest.** Pelek's spirit departed peacefully after his bones were interred in Blingdenstone's catacombs. His arc is complete.

## Relationships
- **Grygum:** Primary caretaker — carried his bones, performed his burial ritual. Deep mutual respect.
- **Daz:** Friendly; exchanged pleasantries and engaged in lore discussions (Pelek dismissed Daz's planar theory about the Faerzress as a "ghost story").
- **Zalthir:** Pelek was referenced as a point of comparison when Zalthir tried to punch Spectres; Pelek also helped diagnose Zalthir's madness.
- **Buppido (deceased):** Former business partner and murderer. Buppido lured him to the caves, killed him, and used his bones for a shrine.
- **Glyphic Shroomlight:** Pelek materialized before the priest to convince him to allow the bone interment at the catacombs.
- **Council of Savants (Gracklstugh):** Buppido had been working with them; Pelek was tangentially connected through their business partnership.
