# Hemath

## Identity
- Duergar arms dealer
- First encountered as a captive at the kuo-toa sacrificial altar, bloodied and bound among other prisoners

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Survival above all else
- A pragmatic opportunist — he had been dealing weapons to the kuo-toa Archpriest of the Deep Father (Bloppblippodd) before she turned on him. Despite innate duergar cultural cynicism, his will to live allowed him to quickly accept help from strangers. He is transactional by nature: if you help him, he'll return the favor.

## History with the Party
- **Kuo-toa Altar Encounter:** Found among sacrificial captives, desperate and pleading with Grygum for help. Explained that Bloppblippodd had betrayed him — he had promised her weapons, but she declared his sacrifice would be "weapon enough." Grygum told him cryptically, "there's more than meets the eye," which surprised and comforted him. He agreed to act on the party's signal and offered to meet them in Gracklstugh if they all survived. A battle then broke out at the altar.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Among the captives at the kuo-toa altar when fighting erupted
- **Fate unknown** — it is unclear whether he escaped, was killed, or was recaptured during the battle
- **Standing offer:** Told the party to find him in Gracklstugh if they survive, implying he has resources and connections there

## Relationships
- **Grygum (party member):** Direct rapport; Grygum was the one who spoke with him and offered cryptic reassurance
- **Bloppblippodd (Archpriest of the Deep Father):** Former business partner turned betrayer; she reneged on their arms deal and marked him for sacrifice
- **Gracklstugh connections:** Implied network as an arms dealer operating out of the duergar city
