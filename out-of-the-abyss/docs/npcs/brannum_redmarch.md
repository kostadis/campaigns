# Brannum Redmarch

## Identity
- Castaway and traveling merchant/trader
- Has been traveling with the party; encountered prior to their approach to Gracklstugh

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: self-preservation and maintaining his freedom above all else
- A merchant by trade with connections in the underground economy — lights up at the mention of procuring magical items. Nervous and quick to negotiate when cornered, but eager to prove his usefulness. Will make deals to save his own skin.

## History with the Party
- **Approach to Gracklstugh:** Thorin raised the question of what to do with Brannum. Brannum panicked and pleaded not to be handed over to the guard, fearing loss of his freedom.
- **Trade Deal:** When Zalthir asked about purchasing uncommon magical items, Brannum seized the opportunity. He claimed he could procure an **Eldritch Claw Tattoo**, stating "I know a guy. But it will take a few days." He negotiated a deal — he would deliver the items in exchange for a clean slate with the party. Thorin threatened consequences if Brannum betrayed them or failed to deliver. Brannum agreed nervously.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** With the party, near or arriving at Gracklstugh
- **Active plans:** Procuring an Eldritch Claw Tattoo (and possibly other uncommon magical items) through a contact — expected to take a few days
- **Party knows:** He's a trader with connections who can source magical items; he's desperate to stay free
- **Hidden:** The identity of his contact ("I know a guy"); the full extent of his trade network; why exactly he fears being turned over to the guard

## Relationships
- **Thorin:** Tense dynamic. Thorin holds leverage over Brannum and has explicitly threatened consequences for betrayal or failure.
- **Zalthir:** Transactional relationship. Zalthir is the buyer interested in the Eldritch Claw Tattoo.
- **The Party (general):** Brannum is tolerated for his usefulness but not trusted. His continued presence is contingent on delivering on his promises.
