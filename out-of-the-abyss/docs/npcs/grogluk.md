# Grogluk

## Identity
- Duergar guard stationed at the guardhouse at the entrance to the West Cleft District
- Serves under the Deepking's authority (wears a clenched-fist pendant symbolizing loyalty to the Deepking)
- Part of a newly manned checkpoint; the guardhouse had previously been abandoned
- First encountered when the party attempted to pass from the Derro slums into the city proper

## Personality & Motivations
- Enforcing security at the checkpoint and screening for the wanted Ember Vanguard. Also clearly motivated by personal profit — he extracted a bribe before letting the party through.
- Projects a tough, solitary front but is actually well-supported (at least five invisible guards hidden in the passageway). His sneering, confrontational posture is a calculated act. He was visibly intimidated when Grygum invoked Errde's name, suggesting he respects — or fears — that authority.

## History with the Party
- **West Cleft Checkpoint:** Confronted the party and demanded they prove they were not the Ember Vanguard. Referenced a wanted poster with inaccurate depictions of the party members (created by the Derro). He offered two options: surrender weapons and magic items for inspection, or perform the secret Stone Guard salute. Grygum performed the salute perfectly and name-dropped Errde, which visibly shook Grogluk. He confirmed the party was not the Ember Vanguard but then demanded 20 gold as a bribe to "pay off his guards." Grygum paid, and the party was allowed through.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The West Cleft District guardhouse checkpoint
- **Activity:** Actively screening travelers entering the city proper from the Derro slums, looking for the Ember Vanguard
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows about his invisible guards (at least five), his loyalty to the Deepking, his corruptibility, and that he can be leveraged by invoking Errde or Stone Guard credentials. The party may not know why this previously abandoned guardhouse was recently manned.

## Relationships
- **The Deepking:** Serves his authority directly; wears his symbol openly
- **Errde:** Visibly intimidated at the mention of Errde, suggesting deference or fear toward that figure
- **The Derro:** Dismissive of them — noted they "get details wrong" regarding the wanted posters
- **Grygum:** The primary party member he interacted with; responded to Grygum's Stone Guard salute and authority, though still extracted a bribe
- **The Ember Vanguard:** Actively hunting them on behalf of the Deepking's forces
