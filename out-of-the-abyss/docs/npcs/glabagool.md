# Glabagool

## Identity
- Sentient gelatinous cube; companion to the party
- First encountered prior to arriving at Blingdenstone (details of initial meeting not in provided notes); appeared with the party at the gates of Blingdenstone

## Personality & Motivations
- Uniquely sentient and capable of speech — an anomaly among oozes. Glabagool is curious about its own nature and aware of broader ooze activity in the Underdark. It is friendly and cooperative, willingly traveling with the party and allowing them to vouch for it. It shows no hostility and seems genuinely invested in understanding what is happening to oozes in the region.

## History with the Party
- **Arrival at Blingdenstone:** Glabagool accompanied the party to the gates, where its appearance caused the deep gnome guards to panic. The party intervened, assuring the guards Glabagool was friendly and had aided them on their journey. Glabagool spoke aloud, shocking the guards, and described a surge of agitated oozes it called an "oozapalooza." The guards, already dealing with a serious ooze problem in Blingdenstone, directed the party to speak with Chief Dorbo Diggermattock and Quartermaster Senni to obtain full permissions — especially regarding Glabagool's presence.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The gates of Blingdenstone; entry permissions pending approval from Chief Dorbo Diggermattock and Quartermaster Senni.
- **Active concerns:** Glabagool has observed an unusual increase in agitated oozes across the area, the cause of which remains unknown.
- **What the party knows:** Glabagool gained sentience and speech after some unknown event; ooze activity in the region is spiking. **What remains hidden:** The cause of Glabagool's sentience and the source of the broader ooze agitation are both unexplained.

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Loyal companion; the party trusts Glabagool enough to advocate for it before armed guards.
- **Blingdenstone Guards:** Viewed with suspicion and fear; its presence is a source of tension given the settlement's existing ooze crisis.
- **Chief Dorbo Diggermattock / Quartermaster Senni:** Not yet met; their approval is required for Glabagool to be permitted in Blingdenstone.
