# Themberchaude

## Identity
- Red dragon residing in Gracklstugh
- Associated with the Keepers of the Flame
- Has not appeared in person; referenced only through NPC dialogue (Daz's political analysis and Thorin's planning)

## Personality & Motivations
- Paranoid about his position and security within Gracklstugh's power structure. Suspects the Keepers of the Flame have their own plans that may not align with his interests.
- Desires information about the political machinations happening around him. Uncertain whether he truly holds power or is being managed by others.

## History with the Party
- Mentioned by Daz during his breakdown of Gracklstugh's political landscape. Daz conveyed that Themberchaude feels insecure about his standing and wants intelligence on current events.
- Thorin floated a plan to escape Gracklstugh by flying out on Themberchaude's back.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Gracklstugh (exact lair location unspecified)
- **Active concerns:** Distrustful of the Keepers of the Flame; seeking information about what is happening in the city
- **Party knowledge:** The party knows he exists, that he is politically uneasy, and that he could theoretically serve as an escape route. They have not met him directly.

## Relationships
- **Keepers of the Flame:** Tense, suspicious relationship. Themberchaude believes they have agendas they are not sharing with him.
- **Daz:** Source of intelligence about Themberchaude's mindset; unclear if Daz has direct contact with the dragon.
- **Thorin:** Views Themberchaude as a potential means of escape from Gracklstugh.
