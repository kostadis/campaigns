# Gasbide

## Identity
- Myconid Builder and supporter of Sovereign Phylo
- Affiliated with Phylo's faction and the Great Seeder movement at Neverlight Grove
- First encountered by the party at the Northern Terraces of Neverlight Grove

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Build a fungal tower greater than Yggmorgus that breaks through the Underdark ceiling to reach the surface world. Dreams of myconids reaching the "Overbright."
- Intensely curious and obsessive about surface architecture — fixates on precise details (materials, shapes, windows, doors). His curiosity is undisguised and childlike, bordering on manic. He has no concept of what a window is but desperately wants to know. He is a dreamer with grand ambitions but limited understanding of the surface world's dangers to myconids.

## History with the Party
- **Northern Terraces encounter:** Approached the party with open curiosity upon learning they were from "the Great Bright." Bombarded them with architectural questions. Was captivated when Zalthir used Minor Illusion to conjure a small-scale building and asked if Zalthir was an architect. Daz engaged him in extended technical discussions about construction and design.
- Revealed his dream of building a tower to the surface, possibly aided by Araumycos. Was distraught when told the sun would harm myconids and immediately pivoted to asking how to build protections against it.
- Admitted he doesn't understand what Araumycos truly is — only that it is "part of a great celebration to come" as promised by Sovereign Phylo.
- **Referenced later by Yrberop:** Described as having a plan to send myconids to the surface to share their stories. Rusharoo found this idea repulsive, arguing myconids thrive in darkness.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Northern Terraces, Neverlight Grove
- **Active plans:** Aspires to build a fungal mega-tower to the surface; aligned with Phylo's upcoming "great celebration"
- **Party knowledge vs. hidden:** The party knows his architectural obsession and tower ambitions. They know he's connected to Phylo's celebration and has heard of Araumycos but doesn't understand it. The true nature of the celebration and its connection to the Great Seeder remains unclear to both Gasbide and the party.

## Relationships
- **Sovereign Phylo:** Loyal supporter; takes Phylo's promises about the celebration and Araumycos at face value
- **Yrberop:** Fellow Phylo-aligned myconid who references Gasbide's surface plans
- **Rusharoo:** Opposes Gasbide's surface ambitions; finds the idea of myconids in sunlight abhorrent
- **Zalthir:** Particularly fascinated by Zalthir's illusion magic; mistook him for an architect
- **Daz:** Engaged in extended construction discussions; potential rapport built through shared technical interest
- **The Great Seeder / Araumycos:** Connected to these through Phylo's promises, but lacks genuine understanding of either
