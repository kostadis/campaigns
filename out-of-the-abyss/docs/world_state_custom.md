# World State Document

## Current Party Location
**Silken Paths, Underdark** - Network of spider webs spanning a 500-foot-deep, 2,000-foot-wide chasm stretching five miles. Party is beginning traverse with goblin guides Yukyuk and Spiderbait. Travelling toward Darklake.

## Active Party Members

**Gyrgum (Grygum)** - Dragonborn follower of Bahamut. Armed, bonded with Stool. Can use Thaumaturgy. Protective instincts, survival-focused.

**Daz** - Human wizard. Armed, scholarly. Cast Mind Sliver and Magic Missiles in combat. Identified Prince Derendil as liar, demons as unusual occurrence. Mediates disputes.

**Thorin Giantfriend** - Dwarf raised among Giants. Armed with Drow weapons. Carries Stool when moving fast. Has Giant accent. Under Tongue of Madness compulsion, revealed truths about Giants.

**Zalthir** - Dragonborn monk. Armed, tactical. Vow of silence (speaks only when necessary). Can breathe fire. Interested in exploiting Jimjar's gambling.

## Allied NPCs Travelling With Party

**Sarith** - Former Drow warrior. Armed with studded leather, shield, hand crossbow. Suffers bouts of madness. Convicted of murdering fellow Drow, claims he was framed but also believes he may have done it. Knows Underdark tunnels, identified Silken Paths.

**Shuushar the Awakened** - Kuo-Toa from Sloobludop. Claims to have overcome racial madness through enlightenment. Wants to return to Sloobludop to spread enlightenment. Familiar with Darklake navigation. Disturbed by Faerzress.

**Stool** - Myconid child from Neverlight Grove. Rides on Thorin's back. Can use rapport spores for group telepathy. Bonded with Gyrgum. Wants to return to sovereign at Neverlight Grove.

**Buppido** - Derro with hand crossbow. Believes himself divinely appointed or a god. Wants to reach Gracklstugh. Avoids direct action, "big-picture thinker." Has wagered with Jimjar about reaching Gracklstugh.

**Jimjar** - Deep gnome gambler with perfect recall of wagers. Makes constant bets. Currently owes Spiderbait and Yukyuk 20 gold. From Blingdenstone area with Topsy and Turvy.

**Topsy** - Deep gnome twin who can transform into rat. Advocates for Blingdenstone (claims surface exit exists there). Communicates for Turvy.

**Turvy** - Deep gnome twin who can transform into rat. Mostly communicates through Topsy.

**Eldeth** - Shield Dwarf with hand crossbow. Skilled tracker, covers group's tracks. Wants to return to surface. Hostile toward orcs, doesn't believe Giants and "little folk" can make peace.

**Yukyuk** - Goblin web-surfer guide. Orange colour motif, Draconic tattoos. Enthusiastic about web-surfing. Guiding party until Jimjar's 20-gold debt is paid.

**Spiderbait** - Goblin web-surfer guide. Pink colour motif, Draconic tattoos, rolled R's and clipped vowels. Pragmatic. Co-creditor with Yukyuk for Jimjar's debt.

## Recently Deceased

**Prince Derendil** - Quaggoth killed by Imbros at Velkynvelve. Claimed to be from non-existent Kingdom of Nelrindenvane. True identity unknown.

**Ront** - Orc killed by Vrock during escape. Sought redemption through combat. Head consumed by demon.

**Imbros** - Drow male fighter, killed by Sarith during escape from Velkynvelve.

## Active Antagonists

**Ilvara** - Drow high priestess commanding Velkynvelve outpost. Currently pursuing escaped prisoners, honour-bound to recapture them. Described as unusually manic for Drow. Member of Cult of Lolth.

**Jorlan** - Demoted Drow fighter at Velkynvelve. Face scarred, missing two fingers. Secretly enabled prisoner escape for revenge against Ilvara and Shoor. Former primary consort, previously carried wand of globs.

**Nym** - Drow scout leading pursuit of escapees. Was misled by Gyrgum's false tremor, turned back taking wrong tunnel.

## Other Significant NPCs

**Shoor** - Current primary consort to Ilvara, replaced Jorlan after his demotion.

**Welnaste Mizzrym** - Drow caravan operator who delivered prisoners to Velkynvelve. Warned Ilvara of financial accountability.

## Key Locations

**Velkynvelve** - Drow outpost 100 feet above cavern floor. Four hanging stalactite towers connected by walkways. Features giant spiders, winch-basket lift, waterfall with grey ooze pool. Under demon attack during party's escape.

**Ilvara's Quarters** - Two-level chamber with personal quarters and Lolth shrine. Contains party's confiscated gear in locked zurkhwood chest (not recovered).

**The Darklake** - Vast underground water network over 100 miles across. Party's near-term destination. Duergar-engineered passages and locks enable watercraft travel.

**Silken Paths** - Current location. Ever-changing spider web network inhabited by giant spiders.

**Sloobludop** - Kuo-Toan town near Darklake, eight days from Velkynvelve. Shuushar's destination.

**Gracklstugh** - Derro homeland reachable via Darklake. Buppido's destination.

**Blingdenstone** - Deep gnome settlement. Topsy and Turvy's destination, claimed to have surface route.

**Neverlight Grove** - Myconid settlement with powerful sovereign. Stool's destination.

## Active Factions

**Cult of Lolth** - Drow faction operating Velkynvelve. Goals: recapture prisoners, transport to Menzoberranzan for slavery/sacrifice. Led by Ilvara.

**House Mizzrym** - Drow house profiting from slave trade. Business relationship with Velkynvelve.

**Goblin Web-Surfers** - Independent guides operating in Silken Paths. Currently allied with party.

## Unresolved Mysteries

**Demon Incursion** - Four Chasme and two Vrock attacked Velkynvelve simultaneously with escape. Daz noted this concentration is highly unusual. Cause unknown.

**Nightmares** - All prisoners experienced disturbing dreams of shadows, tentacles, wounds bursting into spores/insects, and something terrible in deep Underdark. Source unidentified.

**Faerzress Anomaly** - Shuushar felt disturbed/poisoned by Faerzress despite Jimjar claiming it never affects living creatures.

**Sarith's Crime** - Alternates between claiming innocence and guilt for murdering fellow Drow. True circumstances unknown.

**Buppido's Delusion** - Believes he's divinely appointed/a god. Origin of belief unexplained.

**Zalthir's Debt-Favour Note** - Found note listing people who owe favours. Location and nature of debts unknown.

**Party's Gear** - Original equipment locked in Ilvara's chest, not recovered during escape.

## Current Objectives

- Navigate Silken Paths safely
- Reach Darklake 
- Evade Drow pursuit
- Decide on destination (Sloobludop, Gracklstugh, Blingdenstone, or Neverlight Grove)
- Resolve Jimjar's 20-gold debt to goblin guides
