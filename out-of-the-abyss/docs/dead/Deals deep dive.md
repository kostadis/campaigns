Deals deep dive
The party has engaged in several significant deals and received various offers from Themberchaud, the Keepers of the Flame, and the Stone Guard in Gracklstugh, often navigating the hidden agendas and political landscape of the city.
Here are the details of the deals:
**1. With Themberchaud (The Wyrmsmith of Gracklstugh):**
- **Offer:** Themberchaud "enlisted" the party as his agents, not asking but commanding them to accept this role. He promised his protection for them as his agents.
- **Terms:** As his agents, their first mission was to do whatever the Keepers of the Flame asked them to, but to report back to Themberchaud first. He instructed Gartokkar to provide them with gold badges.
- **Party's Action/Understanding:** Thorin noted that Themberchaud was not asking but telling them they were his agents, recognizing the dragon's fragile ego. Thorin and Grygum later discussed an idea to "ride this Dragon out of here, laying waste to everything behind us," indicating a potential betrayal of Themberchaud's trust.
**2. With Gartokkar Xundorn (Custodian of the Flame, Chief of the Keepers of the Flame):**
- **Initial Offer (Safe Passage & Protection):** Gartokkar initially offered safe passage through Gracklstugh and protection from prisons if the party performed a service for him. This offer was set to expire the next day. He also hinted at the value of their "skill set" to the city.
- **Mission 1 (Retrieve Red Dragon Egg):** Gartokkar explicitly tasked the party with retrieving a valuable red dragon egg that was stolen by the Gray Ghosts and taken into the Whorlstone Tunnels. He explained this egg was intended as Themberchaud's replacement, and its loss was a secret he wished to keep from the Deepking.
  - **Terms:** He provided a holy symbol of Laduguer and gold pins featuring Themberchaud’s profile, allowing limited travel within Gracklstugh.
  - **Coercion/Threat:** Gartokkar threatened to turn the party over to the Stone Guards with a fabricated list of severe charges against Thorin if they refused the mission. These charges included public disturbance, interference with the City Watch, questioning authority, unauthorized access, association with undesirables, possession of unauthorized items, and suspicion of espionage. He implied that revealing the egg's theft to the Stone Guards would be a "disaster".
- **Mission 2 (Proof of Demonic Worship):** After the Flumph provided information about demonic worship in the Whorlstone Tunnels, Gartokkar demanded physical proof, specifically a "statue of Demogorgon," to justify a genocidal war against the derro.
  - **Terms:** He initially offered 2,000 gold pieces, which Daz and Thorin negotiated up to a total of 3,000 gold. This included an upfront payment of 30% (600 gold) and an additional 1,000 gold for a confidentiality rider.
  - **Threat:** He warned of severe consequences if the party betrayed him, stating, "If you betray me, there is no cave, no pool of still water, no river, no place in the dark that I will not find you and hunt you down".
- **Party's Understanding:** The party recognized that Gartokkar intended to use this proof to convince the Deepking to eliminate the derro, essentially planning a "genocidal war". They also understood that the Keepers of the Flame were engaged in a secret war with the Council of Savants.
**3. With Errde Blackskull (Captain of the Stone Guards):**
- **Request for Meeting:** Errde initially asked the party to come to her office for a conversation, which she insisted was "friendly" despite Daz's challenge and Thorin's readiness to flee.
- **Offer (Freedom for Services):** Errde offered freedom from imprisonment and safe passage out of Gracklstugh if the party performed two services: finding a duergar named Droki in the West Cleft District and gathering evidence of corruption within the Deepking's court.
  - **Terms:** She offered an upfront payment of 175 gold pieces worth of equipment from the Stone Guard armory, and another 175 gold pieces upon successful completion. She also promised to tell any pursuing Drow that the party was not in Gracklstugh, guaranteeing their freedom from that specific threat.
  - **Party's Action:** Daz negotiated for remuneration, stating it would be "helping us help you". Daz provided initial proof of corruption by recounting strange merchant behaviors in the Blade Bazaar. Thorin accepted the terms.
- **Implicit Task (Investigate "Empty-Scabbard Killers"):** Errde implicitly tasked the party with investigating rumors of the "Empty-Scabbard Killers," despite publicly dismissing them as an "old wives' tale". Her subordinate, Grimholl Forgebrand, detailed mysterious psychic blade attacks, including on Errde's own clan members.
- **Request for Further Proof & Arming Companions:** After the party provided Flumph-derived information about demonic worship, Errde demanded more concrete physical evidence, specifically "the papers," to justify sending her duergar troops into the derro slums.
  - **Terms:** She offered to arm the party's companions (Jimjar, Spiderbait, Sarith, Stool, Eldeth, and the Flumph) to collect this evidence.
  - **Party's Action:** Daz suggested they be seen talking to opposing clans (Clan Ironhead or Clan Xardelva) to maintain secrecy and disguise their true mission for the Stone Guard, a tactic Errde approved.
- **Party's Understanding:** Daz recognized Errde's desire to eliminate the derro, believing them to be the source of corruption, though he doubted the derro's actual influence over the Deepking. He also noted that Errde and the Keepers of the Flame essentially desired the same outcome: the destruction of the derro.
