Deals made in Gracklstugh

In Gracklstugh, the party has received numerous offers and entered into various deals with different individuals and factions, each with their own motivations and agendas. These interactions often involve navigating complex political dynamics and hidden objectives within the city.
Here are the offers that have been made to the party:
- **From Brannam Redmarch (a captured duergar):**
  - **Offer:** Brannam pleaded not to be turned over to the guard and offered to acquire an "Eldritch Claw Tattoo" and other uncommon magical items for the party.
  - **Terms:** He promised to acquire the items in a few days, after which "the slate is wiped clean".
  - **Party's Action:** Zalthir asked if Brannam could obtain the specific tattoo, and Brannam affirmed he knew someone. Thorin added a threat of consequences if Brannam betrayed them. The party later decided to let him loose in the city as they leave.
- **From Gartokkar Xundorn (Custodian of the Flame, chief of the Keepers of the Flame):**
  - **Initial Offer:** Gartokkar offered safe passage through Gracklstugh and protection from prisons if the party performed a service for him. This offer was set to expire the next day.
  - **Mission to Retrieve the Red Dragon Egg:** Gartokkar later explicitly tasked the party with retrieving a valuable red dragon egg that was stolen by the Gray Ghosts and taken into the Whorlstone Tunnels. He explained this egg was intended as Themberchaud's replacement and its loss was a secret he wished to keep from the Deepking.
    - **Terms:** He provided a holy symbol of Laduguer and gold pins featuring Themberchaud’s profile, allowing limited travel within Gracklstugh.
    - **Coercion/Threat:** Gartokkar threatened to turn the party over to the Stone Guards with a fabricated list of severe charges against Thorin if they refused the mission.
  - **Mission for Proof of Demonic Worship:** After a flumph "trauma dumped" information about Demogorgon worship in the Whorlstone Tunnels, Gartokkar demanded physical proof, specifically a "statue of Demogorgon," to justify a genocidal war against the derro.
    - **Terms:** He initially offered 2,000 gold pieces, which was negotiated up to a total of 3,000 gold, including an upfront payment of 30% (600 gold) and an additional 1,000 gold for a confidentiality rider.
    - **Threat:** He warned of severe consequences if the party betrayed him.
- **From Errde Blackskull (Captain of the Stone Guards):**
  - **Request for Meeting:** Errde initially asked the party to come to her office for a conversation.
    - **Party's Action:** The party attended the meeting.
  - **Freedom for Services (Droki & Corruption Proof):** Errde offered freedom from imprisonment and safe passage out of Gracklstugh if the party performed two services: finding a duergar named Droki in the West Cleft District and gathering evidence of corruption within the Deepking's court.
    - **Terms:** An upfront payment of 175 gold pieces worth of equipment from the Stone Guard armory, and another 175 gold pieces upon successful completion. She also promised to tell any pursuing Drow that the party was not in Gracklstugh, guaranteeing their freedom from that specific threat.
    - **Party's Action:** Daz negotiated for remuneration and provided initial proof of corruption by recounting strange merchant behaviors in the Blade Bazaar. Thorin accepted.
  - **Request to Investigate "Empty-Scabbard Killers":** Errde implicitly tasked the party with investigating rumors of the "Empty-Scabbard Killers," despite publicly dismissing them as an "old wives' tale." Her subordinate, Grimholl Forgebrand, detailed mysterious psychic blade attacks, including on Errde's own clan members.
  - **Arming the Crew for Further Proof:** After the party provided flumph-derived information about demonic worship, Errde demanded more concrete physical evidence, specifically "the papers," to justify sending her duergar troops into the derro slums. She offered to arm the party's companions (Jimjar, Spiderbait, Sarith, Stool, Eldeth, and the Flumph) to collect this evidence.
    - **Party's Action:** Daz agreed, suggesting they be seen talking to opposing clans (Clan Ironhead or Clan Xardelva) to maintain secrecy and disguise their true mission for the Stone Guard.
- **From Themberchaud (the Wyrmsmith of Gracklstugh):**
  - **Enlistment as Agents:** Themberchaud "enlisted" the party as his agents, not asking but commanding them to do whatever the Keepers of the Flame ask, but to report back to him first.
  - **Terms:** He promised his protection for them in this role. Gartokkar was instructed to provide them with gold badges.
  - **Party's Action:** Thorin noted Themberchaud wasn't asking but telling. Thorin and Grygum later discussed an idea to "ride this Dragon out of here, laying waste to everything behind us".
- **From Stonespeaker Hgraam (Stone Giant):**
  - **Request for Visit:** Dorhun, an apprentice to Hgraam, asked Thorin to visit Stonespeaker Hgraam in Cairngorm Cavern after Thorin's role in subduing the mad stone giant.
  - **Hgraam's Gift/Information:** Hgraam provided cryptic warnings about evil stirring in the Underdark. He also gifted Thorin a "stonespeaker crystal," which aids in casting *Speak with Animals*, *Speak with the Dead*, and *Speak with Plants*, and assists with divinations.
- **From Werz Saltbaron (a duergar merchant):**
  - **Reward for Rescue:** After the party saved him from assassins, Werz gave Grygum and each companion a gemstone worth 10 gold pieces and promised to take care of their tab at the Ghohlbrorn's Inn.
  - **Protection from Assassins & Weapon Deals:** Werz wanted the party to eliminate his "assassins' problem". In return, he offered to facilitate a deal for weapons with gnomes in Blingdenstone, especially if the Deepking were unseated.
    - **Party's Action:** Daz negotiated an additional fee for getting rid of the assassins, despite Werz's claims of being "broke," and Werz provided "empty spell gems".
  - **Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA):** The party also negotiated an NDA for their discussions with Werz.
    - **Werz's Payment:** He paid 1,100 gold pieces for the NDA after negotiation.
- **From Eldgrim (leader of the Empty-Scabbard Killers/Duergar assassins):**
  - **Deal to Eliminate Demogorgon Cult:** Eldgrim offered to cancel the contract on Werz Saltbaron and "brightlist" him (protect him) if the party "got rid of his Demogorgon problem" (meaning the Demogorgon cultists in the Whorlstone Tunnels).
    - **Terms:** He initially suggested a 20-gold fee on top of eliminating the cult, which the party balked at. He dismissed the idea of a formal contract as proof, stating no one would believe it anyway.
    - **Party's Action:** Daz cleverly obtained a piece of paper (the Deepking's hit list) from Eldgrim's desk, disguised as an author's request for a signature, which could serve as proof of their encounter.
    - **Threat:** He explicitly stated that if the party failed to eliminate his problem, they would die, and he would enjoy fulfilling that "free contract".
- **From Grinta Ironhead (Clan Ironhead):**
  - **Contingent Offer:** Grinta Ironhead, a duergar female seemingly opposed to the Deepking, approached the party with an offer: if the Deepking were unseated, Clan Ironhead would be free to do business with the gnomes (Blingdenstone) and would offer discounted rates for weapons.
  - **Party's Action:** Zalthir feigned confusion, and Grinta left before a direct response was given.
