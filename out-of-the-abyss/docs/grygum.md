**Preserve Life** (PHB-2024 73)
As a Magic Action, you can expend a use of your Channel Divinity to restore 40 HP to Bloodied creatures within 30 ft. of yourself (which can include you), and divide the HP among them. This feature can only restore a creature up to half its maximum HP.
