## Completed Encounters & Quests

**Stealing Weapons from the Guard Chamber (Velkynvelve)**
- Session: 4th day of the 2nd Tenday of Taraskh 1493
- Outcome: Success, with casualties
- Description: The prisoners launched a revolt to seize weapons from the drow guard post/armory in Velkynvelve. Zalthir and Daz attempted a stealth approach but failed, alerting guards. This escalated into a full prisoner revolt. The party, aided by fellow prisoners, fought two drow guards and the elite drow warrior Imbros. All three drow were killed. The prisoners successfully raided the armory and armed themselves.
- Consequences: Prince Derendil was killed by Imbros. Sarith Kzekarit had a sudden madness episode (triggered by the death) but was calmed by Gyrgum. The prisoners are now armed with weapons and at least some armor from the Velkynvelve armory.
