## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Werz Saltbaron assassination attempt witnessed
- **Session date:** 9th-10th day of 1st Tenday of Myrtul 1493
- **Outcome:** Success — the party intervened and saved Werz Saltbaron from two assassins wielding psychic blades at the piers near the Darklake. - **Consequences:** Werz Saltbaron is grateful and invited the party to the Shattered Spire for a reward. Party looted coins, bloodstained gloves, and scented candles from the assassins.

### Errde Blackskull — employment offer accepted
- **Session date:** 9th day of 1st Tenday of Myrtul 1493
- **Outcome:** Completed — Errde Blackskull previously recruited the party as agents for the Stone Guards. She provided passes allowing non-Duergar access through city gates. She tasked them with investigating "empty scabbard killers" (psychic blade assassins) who killed two of her clan members with no visible wounds.
- **Consequences:** Party has Stone Guard passes and is operating as Errde's agents. She also holds leverage — threatening to set the Drow on them if they cross her.

- **Outcome:** Completed — Themberchaud "recruited" the party as his agents without waiting for consent. He issued gold badges and instructed them to do whatever the Keepers of the Flame ask but report to him first.
- **Consequences:** Party received gold badges featuring Themberchaud's profile. They are now nominally triple agents: working for the Stone Guards (Errde Blackskull), the Keepers of the Flame (Gartokkar), and Themberchaud — while loyal to none.

### Gartokkar Xundorn — quest giver (Keepers of the Flame mission accepted)
- **Session date:** 10th day of 1st Tenday of Myrtul 1493
- **Outcome:** Completed (quest given) — Gartokkar revealed that the Gray Ghosts thieves' guild stole a red dragon egg meant to be Themberchaud's eventual replacement. He tasked the party with finding the egg, recovering proof of the Gray Ghosts' involvement, and tracking an identified Gray Ghosts agent named **Droki**. The party was given a holy symbol of Laduguer and gold pins for travel within Gracklstugh.
- **Consequences:** Party learned Themberchaud doesn't know about the egg or that the Keepers plan to eventually replace him ("move him on"). Gartokkar threatened the party with an extensive list of fabricated legal charges if they refused cooperation. The party now has Keepers of the Flame credentials in addition to Stone Guard passes.
