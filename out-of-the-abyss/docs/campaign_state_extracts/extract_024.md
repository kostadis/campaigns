## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Pliinki's Chamber / Obelisk Chamber — Whorlstone Tunnels
- **Session:** Chapter 19
- **Outcome:** Success. The party defeated Pliinki and her derro forces (referenced as having happened just prior to this chapter's opening). The upper levels of the mesa are covered in blood and broken derro bodies. Pliinki is confirmed dead.
- **Consequences:** The party recovered the mutated red dragon egg, Pliinki's journal, and correspondence revealing the full scope of the derro cult's conspiracy in Gracklstugh. They also recovered a statue of Demogorgon and a baby dragon rattle made by Clan Thrazgad.

### Black Obelisk — Whorlstone Tunnels Discovery
- **Session:** Chapter 19 (confirmed via Pliinki's journal)
- **Outcome:** The obelisk was discovered but remains fractured. Pliinki's journal confirms the Thrazgad ore failed to mend it. The obelisk was not activated or repaired — it "refuses to accept the material."
- **Status:** Discovered but unresolved. The party did not interact with it further.

### Gray Ghosts Dragon Egg — Recovered
- **Session:** Chapter 19
- **Outcome:** The red dragon egg stolen by the Gray Ghosts (on behalf of the derro cult) is now in the party's possession. Daz is carrying it. The egg has been mutated by Pliinki's experiments into a two-headed demonic wyrmling — it is no longer a true dragon.
- **Consequence:** The party decides to return the egg to Gartokkar Xundorn (Keeper of the Flame) as leverage to secure safe passage out of Gracklstugh, while concealing the derro cult's full involvement to avoid triggering a genocidal war.

### Buppido — True Nature Revealed, Death Confirmed
- **Session:** Prior to Chapter 19 (referenced as past event)
- **Outcome:** Buppido is confirmed dead. His loss is referenced alongside other traumatic events.

### Pliinki's Conspiracy — Intelligence Gathered
- **Session:** Chapter 19
- **Outcome:** Zalthir recovered Pliinki's journal and four letters revealing:
  - The derro Council of Savants (led by Narrak) orchestrated the theft of the dragon egg and the Thrazgad ore via the Gray Ghosts (led by Uskvil).
  - Aliinka is a Council of Savants member coordinating with the Gray Ghosts.
  - The cult's goal: destabilize Gracklstugh, conquer it, and offer it as a sacrificial gift to Demogorgon.
  - The egg was being mutated into a two-headed wyrmling intended as Demogorgon's mount.
  - The obelisk was intended as the instrument of ascension but could not be repaired.
  - Errde Blackskull's paranoia and the Deepking's reliance on the Empty-Scabbard Killers are being deliberately exploited by the cult.
  - The Keepers of the Flame are hiding the egg's loss from the Deepking.
  - The cult has placed 'Y' carvings as part of indoctrination; almost all Council of Savants members are now corrupted.
- **Consequence:** The party possesses enough evidence to condemn the derro but chooses not to use it to provoke a genocidal war.

### Narrak — Confirmed as Cult Leader
- **Session:** Chapter 19 (via letters)
- **Outcome:** Narrak is confirmed as the leader of the Demogorgon cult among the derro, coordinating with Pliinki, Aliinka, and Uskvil. His letter explicitly calls for conquering Gracklstugh and offering it to Demogorgon.

### Derro Guard Post — Whorlstone Tunnels Exit
- **Session:** Chapter 19
- **Outcome:** Success (non-combat). The party intimidated a small squad of derro guards (led by Diirdeklin) into letting them pass by using dancing lights and bluffing that they were a powerful Drow incursion called "The Ember Vanguard." No combat occurred.
- **Consequence:** The derro now believe a Drow incursion from Menzoberranzan killed Pliinki and is operating in the Whorlstone Tunnels. This misinformation may spread.

### Trapper Encounter — Whorlstone Tunnels Cavern
- **Session:** Chapter 19
- **Outcome:** Success. A trapper (manta ray-like creature) ambushed Grygum. The party killed it with combined attacks from Zalthir (unarmed strikes), Thorin, and Daz (silvery barbs, toll the dead). Grygum was injured and covered in acid slime but survived.

### Pelek the Deep Gnome Ghost — Laid to Rest (Partial)
