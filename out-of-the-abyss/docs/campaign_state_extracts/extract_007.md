## Completed Encounters & Quests

**Silken Paths traversal — spider web chasm**
- Session: 8th day of the 2nd Tenday of Taraskh 1493
- Outcome: Success. The party escaped the Giant Spiders by running, cutting strands, and using magic missiles (Daz) and swords to slow the spiders. The spiders' territorial nature meant they stopped pursuit once the party left their domain. The party successfully traversed the Silken Paths and reached solid stone on the far side.

**Yuk Yuk and Spiderbait — goblin guides encounter**
- Session: Same session, ongoing from prior
- Outcome: The two goblin guides successfully guided the party through the Silken Paths. Despite not being paid yet (Jimjar promised payment "the minute I get it"), they chose to stay with the escapees out of admiration for Zalthir's acrobatic fighting style. They remain with the party.

**Fargas Rumblefoot — halfling rescue in Silken Paths**
- Session: Same session
- Outcome: Fargas was rescued and kept his bargain to share treasure information. He revealed the existence of the Lost Tomb of Khaem (half-elf sorcerer Brysis of Khaem), including knowledge of a secret passage from a servants' tomb to the real burial chamber (bypassing a false sarcophagus). He could not provide precise location but indicated it is on the eastern edge of the Darklake, on the route between Sloobludop and Gracklstugh. Sethir confirmed he could identify the location.

**Spectator (mad beholder-kin) encounter — Silken Paths**
- Session: Same session
- Outcome: Success. A Spectator that had escaped imprisonment from a dead drow wizard confronted the party, claiming to bring warnings of demons. It became paranoid, accused the party of being demons/drow agents, and attacked. It used a confusion ray on Thorin (who still charged and attacked), a paralysis ray (green) on Zalthir (paralyzed him), and a blue ray on Thorin (no effect). Thorin used a "hill strike" technique but failed to knock it prone. Daz killed it with magic missiles. Zalthir attempted an acrobatic under-the-web attack but missed.

**Mimic avoidance — Silken Paths exit**
- Session: Same session
- Outcome: The party spotted a large chest at the edge of the cavern wall along their strand. All agreed it was a mimic (including Buppido). They bypassed it without engagement.
