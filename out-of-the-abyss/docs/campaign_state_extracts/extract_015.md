## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Duergar Keelboat Ambush (Darklake)
- **Session/Time:** During travel to Gracklstugh, around Greengrass
- **Description:** The party discovered an abandoned Duergar keelboat with invisible Duergar slavers hiding on and around it. Daz used Shape Water to reveal an invisible Duergar. Zalthir used Darkness to negate their invisibility advantage while striking with his monk abilities. The party killed multiple Duergar slavers and captured one prisoner alive.
- **Outcome:** Success. All Duergar slavers defeated, one captured for interrogation. Stool and Spiderbait were briefly grappled but rescued. Party recovered gold, and an Alchemy Jug (can generate any liquid) from the keelboat's cargo.

### Reach Gracklstugh — duergar city
- **Session/Time:** 9th day of the 1st Tenday of Myrtul
- **Description:** The party arrived at Gracklstugh via the Darklake. Hemeth had briefed them that non-Duergar are restricted to the Darklake District, that the city is xenophobic and regimented, and that Duergar can turn invisible at will.
- **Outcome:** Successfully reached. Party entered the Darklake District and visited the Blade Bazaar.

### Buppido — interrogation via Tongue of Madness, released
- **Session/Time:** Upon arrival at Gracklstugh
- **Description:** The party debated what to do with Buppido. Spiderbait wanted to kill him. Zalthir fed him a Tongue of Madness mushroom, which compelled him to speak truthfully. He revealed he wanted to return to his bolt hole near the city to worship his "Divine Plan," admitted his killings were madness, and insulted each party member candidly. The party decided to release him into the city rather than kill him.
- **Outcome:** Buppido released. His true nature as a killer was already known; he was judged not to be an ongoing threat worth killing. Zalthir confirmed Buppido cannot summon demon lords.

### Brannum Redmarch — deal struck
- **Session/Time:** Upon arrival at Gracklstugh
- **Description:** Brannum Redmarch, a captured castaway/merchant, pleaded not to be turned over to the guard. Zalthir negotiated: Brannum would procure an Eldritch Claw Tattoo (uncommon magic item) for Zalthir in exchange for freedom ("wiping the slate clean"). Thorin reinforced the deal with a threat of consequences for betrayal.
- **Outcome:** Deal agreed. Brannum stated it would take a few days. Item not yet delivered (in progress).

## Resolved Plot Threads

### Buppido's true nature and disposition
- Buppido's madness and killings were previously revealed. Under the Tongue of Madness, he confirmed his motivations (the "Divine Plan") and that he recognized his actions as madness. He was released in Gracklstugh, ending his active threat to the party.

### Jimjar explains demon lord summoning rules
- Jimjar explained that since the Second Sundering, bringing an avatar of an outer planar power to the Prime Material Plane requires powerful magic and artifacts — Buppido has no such ability. This raises the unresolved question of who summoned the demon lords (foreshadowing Gromph Baenre's ritual).
