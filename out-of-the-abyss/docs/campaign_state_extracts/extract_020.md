## Completed Encounters & Quests

**Werz Saltbaron gem delivery/NDA deal — Gracklstugh**
- **Session/Time:** 10th day of 1st Tenday of Myrtul 1493
- **Outcome:** Success. The party met Werz Saltbaron at the Shattered Spire tavern. He paid each party member a 10gp gemstone for saving his life previously. The party then negotiated a Non-Disclosure Agreement with Werz for 1,100 gold pieces (starting at 1,000, raised by Daz due to Werz's haggling). Werz also paid additional spell gems as a fee for the party to deal with his assassin problem. Werz revealed political intelligence about Gracklstugh clans and confirmed the city is on the brink of civil war.
- **Consequences:** Party gained 1,100gp NDA fee, individual 10gp gemstones, and empty spell gems (valuable to Kazook Pickshine in Blingdenstone). Werz committed to facilitating a weapons deal between Gracklstugh clans and Blingdenstone gnomes to help the party reach Blingdenstone. Party now has obligation to deal with Werz's assassin problem (the Empty Scabbard Killers).

**Errde Blackskull debriefing — Overlake Hold**
- **Session/Time:** 10th day of 1st Tenday of Myrtul 1493
- **Outcome:** Partial success. Party reported findings to Errde Blackskull at Overlake Hold. Daz successfully argued that proximity of Grey Ghosts to the Council of Savants does not prove alliance, preventing Errde from launching an immediate purge of the Derro. Errde demanded the party return to gather more evidence. She agreed to arm the party's NPC companions (Jimjar, Spiderbait, Sarith, Eldeth) and approved the party talking to other clans as cover for their true mission.
- **Consequences:** Errde armed the party's NPC crew. Party tasked with returning to the Whorlstone Tunnels to gather definitive evidence. A Derro massacre was averted for now.

**Clan Ironhead audience — Gracklstugh**
- **Session/Time:** 1st day of 2nd Tenday of Myrtul 1493
- **Outcome:** Completed. The party met with Thangus Ironhead, who gave a public statement of loyalty to the Deepking (which Daz and Zalthir recognized as dishonest self-preservation). Afterward, Grinta Ironhead privately approached the party, proposing that if the Deepking were deposed, Clan Ironhead would trade weapons to the Deep Gnomes at a discount, facilitating the party's route to the surface via Blingdenstone.
- **Consequences:** Party now has a potential future trade alliance with Clan Ironhead contingent on the Deepking's removal. Their visit to Clan Ironhead serves as cover for their true mission into the Whorlstone Tunnels per Errde's plan.
