## Resolved Plot Threads

### Deep gnome escort to leadership
- The deep gnome guards caught up with the party because a sergeant had failed to read a memo requiring all Drow escapees to be escorted to Chief Dorbo Diggermattock. The party's insight confirmed the request was genuine — the gnomes wanted intelligence about their Drow enemies to strengthen defenses. This was resolved when the party agreed to follow the guards.
