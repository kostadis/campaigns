## Completed Encounters & Quests

- **Oozing Temple — flooded dungeon exploration**: The party explored a flooding underground complex caused by an earthquake cracking an underground reservoir. They navigated rising waters, fought multiple gray oozes, a black pudding, and activated formless sculptures that turned into gray oozes, defeating them all. They discovered a hidden treasure cache in a stone fountain (112 silver pieces, 3 gold pieces, a drow dagger, and a vial of oil of slipperiness). They escaped by breaking through a wall to reveal a diverted underground river, swimming upward ~30 feet to reach a large cavern. Outcome: Success — party escaped the flooding complex alive and with treasure.

- **Gray ooze encounters (multiple)**: Zalthir dispatched several gray oozes throughout the flooded complex with unarmed strikes. Daz killed one with a spell. Additional oozes were spawned from formless sculptures in a fountain chamber and dispatched one by one. Outcome: All defeated.

- **Black pudding pit trap encounter**: Thorin fell into a hidden pit trap where a black pudding emerged. The party retreated to a larger area and kited it with ranged attacks until destroyed. Outcome: Defeated.

## NPC State Changes

- **Glabbagool — sentient gelatinous cube, first contact**: Encountered in a chamber containing the remains of a drow, a dark metal mace, and scattered coins. Glabbagool explained he had recently gained sentience and the ability to think and speak after previously being a mindless ooze. He was confused by his newfound awareness but eager to learn. Thorin invited him to join the party, and he accepted enthusiastically. He now travels with the group, positioned at the rear of the formation due to his 10×10-foot body blocking passages. He confirmed he does not need to breathe. He consumed ooze remains and recovered treasure from the fountain for the party (items to be passed after digestion).

- **Jimjar — deep gnome prisoner**: Served as navigator/guide through the flooded complex. Made a bet with Thorin about whether the journey would "end in tears." After the escape, he declared the bet a draw due to Glabbagool's recruitment. Continues with the party.

- **Eldeth Feldrun — shield dwarf prisoner**: Recognized the flooding as an underground river breaking through stone. Shared knowledge about escaping similar situations by breaking through rock, while warning of risks. Still traveling with the party.
