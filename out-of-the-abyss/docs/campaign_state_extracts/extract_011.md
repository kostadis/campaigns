## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Reach Sloobludop — kuo-toa settlement
- **Session/Time:** 1st day of the 3rd Tenday of Taraskh 1493
- **Outcome:** Success. The party arrived at Sloobludop, a village of approximately 500 kuo-toa on the western shore of the Darklake.
- **Consequences:** The party was brought into the village under the protection of Ploopploopeen and housed in his quarters.

## Resolved Plot Threads

### Ploopploopeen's alliance offer — accepted
- The party agreed to serve as a false offering to lure Bloppblippodd into a trap. Ploopploopeen's plan: present the escapees as sacrifices to the Deep Father, insist on accompanying them, then have his followers attack Bloppblippodd's faction.
- The party's role is to serve as bait; they are not expected to initiate combat.

### Buppido's status acknowledged
- Glooglugogg's comment about "the derro whom you tied up" confirms the party has Buppido restrained and chose not to hand him over as a sacrifice. His true nature as a killer has been established.

### Shuushar the Awakened
- **Status:** Previously provided cultural context about kuo-toa society (referenced as background knowledge the party used). Present with the group at Sloobludop.
