## Resolved Plot Threads

**Cause and tempo of Janussi's death established** — Janussi was poisoned via a treated page of a book, fell, and was later desecrated (heart removed) by a patient killer. Confirmed he ate dinner intact; death occurred between dinner and the midnight thud.

**The missing book identified** — *The Golden Ass*, a comedy book Janussi had been annotating, was the ONLY item taken besides heart/sapphire/locket. Confirmed carried out by Daral Yashenti ("hypnotic block" per the cat) while Janussi was still alive. The black poison was on its pages.

**Tobacco smoke explained** — Janussi's habitual ebony pipe and cherry tobacco haze would mask the smell of poison.

**Robbery motive eliminated** — Safe was opened with the correct combination (no forced entry); killer took exactly ONE sapphire and ONE spell parchment, leaving a fortune (four sealed sapphire pouches, raven figurine, potion of supreme healing, potion of water breathing, five diamonds, platinum and gold). Killer needed one specific thing — building a spell requiring a sapphire. This eliminates greedy former adventurers.

**The fallen statue explained** — Three-foot marble statue (hooded man with candle) was knocked over accidentally by Great Reader A'lai Aivenmore when he discovered the body at dawn. The one honest accident in the room.

**The force darts explained** — Three darts of pure force punched through the armchair (per Queenie the cat) during Sylvira Savikas's morning lover's quarrel with Janussi; not aimed at him.
