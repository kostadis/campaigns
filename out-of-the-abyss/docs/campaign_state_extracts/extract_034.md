## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Reach Neverlight Grove — myconid colony
- **Outcome:** Completed (visited and fled). The party explored the Garden of Welcome, witnessed the mock wedding rehearsal for Zuggtmoy's union with Araumycos, observed Yggmorgus (Zuggtmoy's mushroom tower), and determined the colony was beyond saving. They fled with allied myconids.

### Zuggtmoy — Demon Queen of Fungi, presence at Neverlight Grove
- **Outcome:** Confirmed. The party witnessed Zuggtmoy's wedding rehearsal and had a vision of her in humanoid fungal form inside Yggmorgus preparing a bridal gown. They recognized her presence from a previous dream encounter. They determined she intends to marry Araumycos to gain untold power.

### Sovereign Phylo — myconid sovereign, corrupted by Zuggtmoy
- **Outcome:** Confirmed corrupted. Phylo pleaded with Basidia not to leave, referencing "the Great Seeder." Did not attack the party as they fled. The party confirmed Phylo had betrayed the Circle and allied with Zuggtmoy.

### Sovereign Basidia — myconid sovereign, resistance leader
- **Outcome:** Rescued. Basidia confronted Phylo but was physically grappled and dragged to safety by Zalthir. Basidia gave the party potions of greater healing and accumulated treasure before parting ways.

### Yestabrod — Circle of Masters leader, confrontation
- **Outcome:** Previously killed (confirmed dead). His corpse was puppeteered by three spore servants during the mock wedding rehearsal to play the role of priest.

### Xinaya — drow scout, fate in Garden of Welcome
- **Outcome:** Dead (confirmed). The party looted her corpse on the way out of the Garden area. Zalthir took scrolls and other items.

### Stool — return to Neverlight Grove
- **Outcome:** Completed. Stool was returned to Neverlight Grove but then convinced to leave with the party due to the danger. Stool departed with the myconid explorers/hunters (Rusharoo's group) after bidding the party farewell.

### Rumpadump — return to Neverlight Grove
- **Outcome:** Completed. Rumpadump was returned to Neverlight Grove but then convinced to leave with the party. Before departing with the myconid group, Rumpadump gave Zalthir a list of approximately fifty self-improvement suggestions. Rumpadump left with the myconid explorers.

### Escape from Neverlight Grove
- **Outcome:** Success. The party fled the Inner Circle when conflict with Phylo seemed imminent. Rusharoo led them through a pre-identified escape route. Basidia, Rusharoo, Loobamub, and the Circle of Explorers/Hunters all evacuated. Rusharoo provided a guide for half the distance to Blingdenstone before departing.
