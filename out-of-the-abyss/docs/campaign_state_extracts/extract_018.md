## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Stonespeaker Hgraam Audience — Stone Giant Leader
- **Session**: ~Session 18-19, 10th day of 1st Tenday of Myrtul 1493
- **Outcome**: Success. The party visited Cairngorm Cavern and had an audience with Stonespeaker Hgraam.
- **Consequences**: Hgraam shared cryptic prophecy about evil stirring in the Underdark ("a cave with two faces, rock devoured, land overgrown, pebble believes itself flesh, earth rejects its wards, tunnels shake in fury"). He confirmed the best route to the surface ("Dreamlands") is through Blingdenstone, validating Jimjar's earlier claims. He revealed that the Deep King has a mysterious advisor named "Shal" who has made the Deep King paranoid. Hgraam gifted the party the **Stonespeaker Crystal** (19-inch quartz shard; casts Speak with Animals, Speak with Dead, Speak with Plants; aids divination).

### Buppido's Shrine and Killings — Revealed and Confronted in Whorlstone Tunnels
- **Session**: ~Session 18-19
- **Outcome**: Success. Buppido's true nature as a serial killer was fully revealed. He had constructed a shrine of humanoid remains arranged in a spiral pattern in the Whorlstone Tunnels.
- **Consequences**: Buppido attacked the party claiming divine status. The party killed Buppido in combat (Daz delivered the killing blow with Magic Missiles after Grygum turned undead to neutralize skeleton minions). Buppido is dead. His madness was connected to the Faerzress fluctuation.

### Pelek the Deep Gnome Ghost — Encountered, Quest Accepted
- **Session**: ~Session 19
- **Outcome**: Pelek's ghost was discovered at Buppido's shrine. He was one of Buppido's victims — a svirfneblin merchant and former business partner of Buppido who was murdered and dismembered.
- **Consequences**: Pelek asked the party to collect his bones and bring them to Blingdenstone so he can be laid to rest. The party agreed. Pelek mentioned a family obsidian ring on his severed hand, which wandered off — party can keep the ring if found. Pelek provided intel: confirmed Faerzress "blinked" a few months ago (went dark then came back brighter), after which Buppido's madness intensified. He also advised the party that in Blingdenstone they must deny any association with Lolth or Menzoberranzan and avoid references to the number eight or spiders.

### Droki Delivery Interception — In Progress (Trail Found)
- **Session**: ~Session 18-19
- **Outcome**: Partial/in progress. The party successfully located and followed Droki through the Derro slums of West Cleft into the Whorlstone Tunnels entrance (a narrow fissure hidden behind cloth and canvas). Droki entered a crack too small for the party to follow. They discovered Pygmywort (shrinking) and Bigwig (enlarging) mushrooms that Droki uses to traverse narrow passages. Grygum tried Pygmywort but still couldn't fit through the crack. Daz froze a thermal lake to cross to the other side, finding Droki's trail continuing east through a fungal thicket.
- **Status**: Still pursuing Droki through the Whorlstone Tunnels.
