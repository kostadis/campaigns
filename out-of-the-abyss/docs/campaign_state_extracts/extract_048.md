## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Encounter with the Pudding King — Blingdenstone
- **Session:** Chapter 43
- **Outcome:** Success. The Pudding King was defeated and dissolved.
- **Details:** The Pudding King had transformed into a sentient telepathic ooze through his connection to Juiblex. Zalthir grappled him, dragged him out of his throne room (neutralizing his lair actions), and the party coordinated to destroy him. His allies Prince Livid and Princess Ebonmire were also killed.
- **Consequences:** Juiblex's demonic influence began receding from the area. However, Zuggtmoy's fungal corruption began filling the power vacuum, with fungi in the cavern becoming more twisted. A demonic voice (possibly Juiblex) declared he would be reborn and consume "the banquet of the queen of fungi." The party recovered a spellbook from Princess Ebonmire's stomach. The party leveled up. The victory secured the party's path to escape the Underdark.

### Battle for Blingdenstone — Ooze Incursion
- **Session:** Chapter 43 (culmination)
- **Outcome:** Success. With the Pudding King destroyed and his ooze lieutenants killed, the ooze threat to Blingdenstone was ended.
- **Consequences:** Juiblex's power receded from the area; Zuggtmoy positioned to fill the void.

## Resolved Plot Threads

### The Pudding King's Reign
- The Pudding King, an insane deep gnome who had embraced Juiblex and transformed into a sentient ooze, was destroyed. His telepathic last words: "Behold the true feast, the soul's final blend!" His ooze army leadership was decapitated with the deaths of Prince Livid and Princess Ebonmire.

## NPC State Changes

### Pudding King — Dead
- Dissolved completely after being dragged from his throne room and assaulted by the full party. His consciousness dissipated as his physical form melted into inert puddles.

### Prince Livid — Dead
- Killed by Daz's Magic Missile after surviving a Fireball due to fire resistance.

### Princess Ebonmire — Dead
- Killed by combined attacks from Grygum, Zalthir, and Thorin. A spellbook was found in her stomach.

### Glabbagool — Alive, Form Changed
- During the battle, Glabbagool and the Pudding King swapped bodies/forms. After the Pudding King's defeat, Glabbagool is now in an ooze form (no longer a gelatinous cube). He is enthusiastic about this change, viewing the smaller, more mobile form as an improvement. He proposed being carried in a jar or the Bag of Holding, serving as a "garbage disposal," and expressed interest in monk training.

### Juiblex — Influence Receding, Threatened Return
- His power began receding from Blingdenstone after the Pudding King's death. A demonic voice (possibly Juiblex himself) declared: "You haven't won. I will be reborn. I will consume the banquet of the queen of the fungi, and I will grow."

### Zuggtmoy — Expanding Influence
- With Juiblex's power receding, fungi in the area became more twisted and malevolent, indicating Zuggtmoy is moving to fill the power vacuum.
