## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Escape from Velkynvelve — drow outpost
- **Session/Time:** 4th day of the 2nd Tenday of Taraskh 1493
- **Outcome:** Success. The prisoners defeated the drow guards (bodies littering the floor), scavenged weapons and armor from the dead drow, and escaped during a demon attack (four chasme and two vrock fighting the drow). They descended via the waterfall, briefly encountered a gray ooze, and fled through the tunnel to the Darklake.
