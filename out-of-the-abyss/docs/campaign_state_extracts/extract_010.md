## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Kuo-toa Ambush on the Road to Sloobludop
- **Session/Chapter:** Chapter 10
- **Outcome:** Partial success. The party was ambushed by eight kuo-toans (including a Kuo-Toan monitor) in two groups of four. The party discovered that kuo-toan bodies release a sticky liquid that traps melee weapons, and learned that hand crossbow volleys combined with teamwork were effective. Three kuo-toans fled (including the monitor); the rest were defeated.
- **Consequences:** The party learned tactical lessons about fighting kuo-toans (avoid melee, use ranged attacks).

### Encounter with Ploopploopeen — Archpriest of the Sea Mother
- **Session/Chapter:** Chapter 10
- **Outcome:** Successful negotiation. Ploopploopeen proposed using the party as false sacrificial bait to infiltrate the Deep Father cult led by her daughter Bloppblippodd. The party agreed.
- **Consequences:** Alliance formed with Ploopploopeen. Promised reward: 500 gold, two potions of healing, two boats, and guides to navigate the Darklake.
