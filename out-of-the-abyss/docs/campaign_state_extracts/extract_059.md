## Resolved Plot Threads

- **Janussi murder — poison identified:** Through library research (Grygum), the murder weapon was established. Janussi was killed by *midnight tears* — a highly toxic pale ochre dust made from Feywild plant pollen. The poison is delivered via food or liquid, causes no effect until the stroke of midnight (matching Janussi's death time), and turns black on contact with saliva. The poison was applied to a book; Janussi licked his fingers turning pages and poisoned himself reading. His black fingers and lips confirm this.

- **Sapphire spell identified:** Grygum determined the spell requiring an expensive sapphire is a summoning working that calls a single hidden object back into the caster's hand. Established it does NOT work on worn/carried items (so not directly usable on the lanyard-worn High Tower keys), but works on objects hidden elsewhere — a method to hide a key "nowhere at all" and summon it when needed.
