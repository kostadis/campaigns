- Session: Chapter 39
- Outcome: Success. The party located the Festering Fissure, a Zuggtmoy-corrupted cave with mushroom figures, violet fungi, and a shambling mound. - Session: Chapter 39
- Outcome: Success. Four Galeb Duhr blocked the path in Pickshine Mine, vibrating in a pattern waiting for a response. Daz identified the vibrations as a linguistic pattern, found resonant geodes, arranged them, and hummed to complete the ancient handshake protocol. The Galeb Duhr opened the way. Dasco Pickshine rewarded the party with two ruby gems worth 500 gp.

**White Shell Mine time-snagged tunnel**
- Session: Chapter 39
- Outcome: Success. A tunnel had become impassable due to magical pressure causing time to snag. Zalthir identified that a Shadow Monk technique was needed to redirect a controlled explosion through shadow space. With Grygum's blessing and gnome explosives, Zalthir executed the maneuver perfectly, shattering the wall without collapsing the tunnels.
