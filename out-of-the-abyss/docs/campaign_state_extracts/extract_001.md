### Velkynvelve — drow outpost, slave pen
- **Session/Time:** 2nd day of the 2nd tenday of Taraskh, 1492
- **Outcome:** In progress — party has arrived and been imprisoned, not yet escaped
- **Details:** The party (Gyrgum, Daz, Thorin, Zalthir) was captured by a drow caravan led by Welnaste Mizzrym and delivered to the Velkynvelve outpost. - **Status:** Introduced in the slave pen. Presents as personable, gregarious, and talkative. Claims to follow a "divine plan" and advocates escape. Suggests sacrificing Ront as a distraction. Has not yet revealed his true nature as a killer.

### Prince Derendil — quaggoth prisoner, first contact
- **Status:** Introduced. Claims to be Prince Derendil of the Kingdom of Nelrindenvane in the High Forest, transformed into a quaggoth by the wizard Terrestor. Daz has already internally identified him as a liar, knowing no such kingdom exists, but has not confronted him.

### Eldeth Feldrun — shield dwarf prisoner, first contact
- **Status:** Introduced. Befriended Thorin. Advocates escaping to Gauntlgrym. Displays hostility toward orcs and derro. Provides tactical information about the prison's anti-magic properties and the outpost's defenses.

### Jimjar — deep gnome prisoner, first contact
- **Status:** Introduced. Compulsive gambler. Provides intelligence about the drow power dynamics (Jorlan/Shoor/Ilvara/Asha). From the same town as Topsy and Turvy.

### Ront — orc prisoner, first contact
- **Status:** Introduced. Fought poorly and seeks absolution from Gruumsh. Disappointed that Gyrgum is not a follower of Gruumsh.

### Sarith Kzekarit — drow prisoner, first contact
- **Status:** Introduced. Convicted of murdering a fellow drow but claims it was a setup, then becomes uncertain. Shows signs of instability/insanity. Mentioned to have fungal rot. Provides information about a gray ooze in the pools at the bottom of the cave.

### Shuushar the Awakened — kuo-toa prisoner, first contact
- **Status:** Introduced. Claims to have found enlightenment. Offers to guide the group to Sloobludop near the Darklake, saying he knows the roads well.

### Stool — myconid sprout prisoner, first contact
- **Status:** Introduced. A child myconid from Neverlight Grove. Communicates via spores. Offers to guide the group to Neverlight Grove and claims it will be safe.

### Topsy and Turvy — svirfneblin twins, first contact
- **Status:** Introduced. Twins. From the same town as Jimjar. Advocate going to Blingdenstone. Turvy mumbles; Topsy speaks for both. Lycanthropy secret not yet revealed.
