# Party Overview

**Party Name:** The Ember Vanguard

**Current Location:** Exiting the Underdark via Blingdenstone's northern tunnels, approaching the surface for the first time. Most recent combat occurred at Velkynvelve, where the party killed their original captor Ilvara Mizzrym.

**Date:** Approximately early Kythorn 1493 DR

**Active Quests:**
- **Reach the Surface** — Primary objective. Blingdenstone's northern tunnels lead to the Overbright.
- **Warn the Surface World** — Multiple demon lords (Demogorgon, Zuggtmoy, Juiblex) have manifested in the Underdark. Someone or something summoned them. The surface must be warned.
- **Zuggtmoy's Wedding to Araumycos** — If completed, grants the Demon Queen of Fungi untold power. Pale puffball mushrooms are spreading even into Blingdenstone.
- **House T'sarran Retaliation** — A surviving T'sarran warrior escaped Velkynvelve to report to the Matron Mother. Future retaliation expected.
- **Daz's Extraction Contract** — Nym and Kaelira Duskryn are under paid contract to extract Daz alive. The contractor is unknown.
- **Burrow Warden Jadger's Boons** — Two truthful-answer questions remain unclaimed at the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough.

**Collective Resources:**
- Gold: Substantial (1,022 gp on Daz's sheet alone; party earned ~3,000+ gp across Gracklstugh dealings, minus expenditures on equipment and bribes)
- Key magic items: Dawnbringer (sentient sunblade), Stonespeaker Crystal, Necklace of Fireballs, Alchemy Jug, Scroll of Protection vs. Fiends, Spellbook (from Princess Ebonmire), empty spell gems, Voucher of the Varmint, Dustsight Spectacles (Daz), Zalthir's crystal spectacles
- Blessings: Entemoch's Boon (Earth Elemental summoning; stone-skin reaction, 1/day each member)
- Companion: Glabbagool (sentient ooze, carried in Bag of Holding)
- Traveling NPCs: Eldeth, Spiderbait

**Group Reputation:**
- **Blingdenstone:** Celebrated heroes. Brokered peace between factions. Legends spreading (increasingly exaggerated).
- **Gracklstugh:** Wanted criminals under the name "Ember Vanguard." Inaccurate wanted posters provide some cover.
- **Drow Houses:** Ilvara dead (thread resolved). House T'sarran aware and hostile. House Duskryn extracting Daz (contract active). Asha Vandree believes Daz serves Lolth.
- **Underdark at large:** The Ember Vanguard's name is spreading through tunnel networks.

## Characters

### Daz — Wizard (Evoker) 7
**Player:** Mike | **Species:** Elf (Drow lineage) | **Background:** Sage

**Personality & Motivations:** A scholarly Drow apostate who has undergone a profound ideological transformation during the Underdark journey. Once focused purely on escape, he now embraces discovery and exploration, rejecting Menzoberranzan's values while still leveraging its social instincts for manipulation and negotiation. He is the party's political strategist and primary arcane threat, comfortable operating in webs of deception — including a "load-bearing" false identity as a Lolth convert maintained with Asha Vandree.

**Key Stats:** AC 11 | HP 44 | Spell Save DC 15 | Spell Attack +7 | INT 19 (+4) | Passive Investigation 22

**Notable Abilities:** Sculpt Spells (Evoker), Potent Cantrip, Fireball, Magic Missile, Scorching Ray, Maximilian's Earthen Grasp, Shape Water (creative utility), Misty Step, Hypnotic Pattern, Shield, Mind Sliver, Faerie Fire/Darkness (Drow lineage), spider familiar, bat familiar

**Current Arc Score Tracks:**
- *Leadership & Identity* — Has evolved from frightened prisoner to "the feared mage of the Ember Vanguard." Served as general of the Blingdenstone coalition against the Pudding King. Brokered peace between factions, negotiated with wererat chiefs and duergar merchants. Growing into a role he initially found ironic.
- *Moral Complexity* — Holds genuine convictions about freedom and slavery ("Free people are the basis of any society") while comfortably using Mind Sliver as an interrogation tool and suggesting killing NPCs out of impatience. Pragmatic rather than cruel, but the line blurs.
- *Deception Web* — Maintains a false Lolth-convert identity with Asha Vandree, a fabricated gambling-oath story covering for Zalthir, and the Ember Vanguard reputation among the Derro. Each lie is load-bearing.
- *Drow Heritage Tension* — Recognized as an apostate and male Drow, making him the most endangered member in Drow society. Charged by the dying Xinaya to warn Menzoberranzan about Zuggtmoy. Infected by Zuggtmoy's spores (visibly). Nym and Kaelira Duskryn have a paid contract to extract him alive — contractor unknown. Jorlan Duskryn may be his brother.

**Notable Relationships:**
- **Asha Vandree** — Believes Daz serves Lolth. A deception that could collapse catastrophically.
- **Nym & Kaelira Duskryn** — Mercenaries contracted to extract Daz. Relationship strained after Daz refused to hide during the Ilvara battle.
- **Jorlan Duskryn** — Possibly Daz's brother (shared surname). Status: alive but staggered at Velkynvelve.
- **Zalthir** — Close ally. Daz has covered for Zalthir socially (fabricated gambling oath) and Zalthir has rescued Daz physically.
- **Chief Chipgrin (Gold Whisker wererats)** — Formal alliance; Daz holds the Voucher of the Varmint.
- **Eldeth** — Eldeth publicly declared Daz changed her prejudice against Drow.

**Items of Significance:**
- Stonespeaker Crystal (attuned) — Speak with Animals/Dead/Plants, divination aid
- Dustsight Spectacles (attuned)
- Shortsword, +1
- Voucher of the Varmint — Recognized by wererats from Blingdenstone to Menzoberranzan
- Empty spell gems (to be charged by Kazook Pickshine)

### Zalthir — Monk (Way of Shadow)
**Player:** (Not specified) | **Species:** Dragonborn (bronze) | **Background:** (Not specified on sheet)

Emotionally guarded, tactically brilliant, and capable of glacier-like violence when protecting allies. Carries unresolved feelings connected to someone named Valen.

### Thorin Giantfriend — Fighter (Battle Master)
**Player:** (Not specified) | **Species:** Dwarf | **Background:** Raised by hill giants

Impulsive and direct, he has nevertheless shown growing tactical awareness and a genuine capacity for compassion — befriending Glabbagool, carrying Stool when the sprout was tired, and mourning fallen companions.

**Current Arc Score Tracks:**

## Party Dynamics

**Core Bond:** The Ember Vanguard's identity was forged through shared captivity, escape, and survival across the entire Underdark. They have fought demon cults, brokered peace between warring factions, and killed their original captor together. This is not a party of convenience — it is a unit tested under extraordinary pressure.

**Leadership Structure:** Daz serves as political strategist and negotiator (claimed the general's role at Blingdenstone). Grygum is the moral anchor and crisis manager (heals, rallies, keeps records). Zalthir is the tactical combat leader (scouts, strikes first, makes the decisive kill). Thorin is the emotional catalyst and naming force (named the party, befriends unusual allies, pushes for direct action).

**Active Tensions:**
- **Daz ↔ Zalthir (tactical friction):** Zalthir's Darkness spells block Daz's targeting; Daz's premature reveals spoil Zalthir's ambushes. They have worked around it but never resolved it.
- **Thorin ↔ Zalthir (heritage friction):** Thorin's giant-culture anti-dragon prejudice has produced careless comments ("pure dragon") that visibly affected Zalthir. Thorin attempted apologies; Zalthir chose restraint but noted the pattern.
- **Thorin ↔ Eldeth (unresolved):** Thorin's romantic interest is not reciprocated. Eldeth rejected his giant ties but evolved her views on Drow (credited to Daz). The dynamic is strained but functional.
- **Grygum's faith wavering:** Privately finds Bahamut "boring" and his own theatrics partly self-deceptive. Has used necromantic magic and looted allies. No one has called him on it, but the internal tension is building.

**Shared Goals:**
- Reach the surface and warn the world about the demon lord incursion.
- Survive the consequences of their Gracklstugh dealings (wanted status, faction debts, House T'sarran retaliation).
- Protect their companions — Glabbagool, Eldeth, Spiderbait.
- Understand who summoned the demon lords and why the Faerzress blinked.

**Unspoken Bonds:**
- Zalthir rescued Daz from a rockfall early in the campaign; Daz fabricated a story to protect Zalthir's dignity. Neither has acknowledged this aloud, but it anchors their trust.
- Grygum carried Stool's hand from Velkynvelve through the entire Underdark. Though Stool has departed, the parental instinct remains — now directed at Glabbagool.
- Thorin gave Glabbagool a lucky foot and verified the ooze's identity when no one else thought to ask. The party's willingness to defend a gelatinous cube at every gate and checkpoint says more about who they are than any title.
