## Party Overview

Zuggtmoy's influence is visibly growing (accelerated fungal growth, pale puffball mushrooms). A confirmed route to the surface exists through Blingdenstone.

**Primary Goal:** Escape the Underdark to the surface ("the Overbright").

**Active Quests:**
- Escape through Blingdenstone (Diggermattocks have agreed to assist)

- Process empty spell gems with Kazook Pickshine

## Characters

### Thorin Giantfriend
**Class/Level:** Fighter 8 | **Player:** *(not specified)*
**Species:** Dwarf (raised by hill giants)

**Personality & Motivation:** Direct, blunt, increasingly hardened by months in the Underdark. Raised by hill giants, speaks Dwarvish with a Giant accent, and holds a deep belief that giants and smaller folk can coexist—a conviction that puts him at odds with shield dwarves like Eldeth. He abhors the secrecy and political maneuvering of the Underdark and advocates for radical transparency. Privately considering darker fighting methods (Zhentarim Tactics), signaling moral erosion.

**Arc Score Tracks:**
- *Giant Heritage / Identity:* Tested repeatedly. Eldeth rejected his giant affiliation (War of the Giants damage). His "Giantfriend" identity is a source of pride but also ongoing conflict. Compelled by Tongue of Madness to reveal an embarrassing giant-related truth. His antipathy toward dragonkind (absorbed from giants) flared violently at Themberchaud. No formal score value assigned in source material.
- *Leadership:* Consistently positive. Made the decisive call to flee Velkynvelve during the demon distraction; ordered Topsy/Turvy to save Eldeth and Jimjar during centipede combat; negotiated the Jorlan escape deal; managed Buppido's megalomania.
- *Moral Trajectory:* Gradual darkening. Currently considering Zhentarim Tactics fighting style. Bears a Whorlstone Tunnels contamination marker (Zuggtmoy artifact danger).

**Notable Relationships:**
- *Eldeth:* Positive rapport, damaged by her rejection of his giant ties. Unresolved tension.
- *Jorlan Duskryn:* Pragmatic alliance. Negotiated the Velkynvelve escape deal; both acknowledged mutual distrust.
- *Buppido (deceased):* Successfully manipulated into cooperation; never trusted.
- *Giants:* Adoptive family; source of martial training (hill strike from a hill giant battle master).
- *Themberchaud:* Deep animosity (dragon-giant grudge); was forced into the dragon's service in Gracklstugh.

**Items of Significance:**
- **Dawnbringer** — Sentient sunblade; radiant damage; 20-foot light radius; casts Lesser Restoration; refuses to extinguish its light; housed in a locking scabbard crafted by Valimor Brightgem
- Stone Skin (Entemoch's boon; once/day)
- Stonespeaker Crystal *(noted on Daz's sheet as attuned — possible shared or transferred item; source documents credit it to Thorin as Hgraam's gift)*
- Looted Drow weapons (abandoned original gear at Velkynvelve)

### Daz
**Class/Level:** Wizard (Evoker) 8 | **Player:** kostadis1
**Species:** Elf (Drow Lineage)

**Personality & Motivation:** Quiet and observational, Daz processes experience analytically before acting. Trained in Menzoberranzan but privately holds beliefs about freedom and society that diverge sharply from Drow norms. He is the self-described "feared mage of the Ember Vanguard" and has been mentally composing journal entries since the escape from Velkynvelve, inspired by Milo Goodbarrel's writing practice. Pragmatic to the point of callousness (suggested keeping goblin guides as "battle fodder"), but capable of genuine moral nuance—gave Buppido a fair investigation before condemning him.

**Arc Score Tracks:**
- *Identity / Apostasy:* High-stakes. Daz is a male Drow apostate—the most endangered prisoner at Velkynvelve (faced Drider transformation or execution). Maintains a false Lolth-follower identity with Asha Vandree, described as "load-bearing." Privately transformed by experiences in the Underdark.
- *Leadership / Arcane Value:* Positive trend. Took charge of group discussions at Sloobludop; led the Buppido murder investigation; delivered the pivotal speech convincing the party to help the kuo-toa; landed the killing blow on both Imbros (Magic Missile) and the Spectator.
- *Moral Complexity:* Mixed. Used Mind Sliver as an interrogation tool ("motivation") but drew on personal experience of Drow prejudice to give Buppido a fair hearing. Suggested killing the kuo-toan archpriest's group out of impatience—then shifted to tactical analysis. No formal score values assigned in source material.

**Notable Relationships:**
- *Zalthir:* Close bond. Zalthir rescued him from the rockfall; Daz covered for Zalthir with the fabricated gambling oath story.
- *Asha Vandree:* Drow cleric of Lolth; believes Daz is a genuine convert. "Load-bearing" deception—its collapse would be catastrophic.
- *Nym & Kaelira Duskryn:* Hired through cutouts to extract Daz alive; contracting party unknown to them; purely transactional. They do not know who Daz is and do not care.
- *Jorlan Duskryn:* Brother of Nym and Kaelira; estranged from them. At Velkynvelve as Ilvara's lover before she turned on him — no DeVir connection, no relation to the extraction contract. Nym's "Can I kill your brother?" line was addressed to Kaelira about Jorlan, not to Daz about a sibling.
- *Buppido (deceased):* Investigated and condemned him; restrained rather than killed; later destroyed him with Magic Missiles in the Whorlstone Caverns.

**Items of Significance:**
- **Princess Ebonmire's spellbook** — Numerous spells; not fully catalogued
- **Stonespeaker Crystal** (attuned per character sheet) — Speak with Animals (2 charges), Speak with Dead (4 charges), Speak with Plants (3 charges); 10-charge pool

## Party Dynamics

**Cohesion:** High. The group has survived Velkynvelve, the Silken Paths, the Darklake, Gracklstugh, Neverlight Grove, and Blingdenstone together. Shared trauma and repeated mutual life-saving have built genuine trust.

**Internal Structure:**
- **Daz** has emerged as the de facto strategic voice—calls huddles, drives negotiations, frames group purpose. His speech convincing the party to help the kuo-toa ("we need something to focus on beyond just running") was the clearest example.
- **Thorin** is the tactical/physical lead in combat and direct action. His instinct is to charge; he has learned (partially) to check this against group counsel.
- **Grygum** is the moral and emotional anchor—the one who heals, protects NPCs, calms crises, and builds relationships with dependents. Also the most diplomatically creative (Thaumaturgy ruse).
- **Zalthir** is the scout, the specialist, and the silent intelligence gatherer. Speaks when it matters; acts when others hesitate.

**Active Tensions:**
- **Thorin ↔ Eldeth:** Unresolved. Her rejection of his giant identity is the sharpest ongoing interpersonal wound.
- **Daz's false identity with Asha Vandree:** The party is aware this deception is "load-bearing." Its collapse would affect everyone.
- **Daz and Jorlan — misread brother claim:** The party may believe Jorlan is Daz's brother based on Nym's "Can I kill your brother?" line. Canonically she addressed Kaelira about Jorlan (the Duskryns' own estranged sibling). The party's belief is theirs to discover and unwind.
- **Zalthir's moral drift:** Small but noted—his reconsideration of the torture prohibition, his frustration-fueled aggression toward Jimjar. No confrontation has occurred yet.
- **Thorin's moral darkening:** Considering Zhentarim Tactics. The party has not discussed this; it may not be visible to the others yet.

**Shared Goals:**
1. Reach the surface
2. Survive the demon lord crisis (increasingly understood as a world-scale threat, not just a personal problem)
3. Honor obligations to Glabbagool, Eldeth, Spiderbait, and other surviving companions
4. Understand what is happening to the Underdark—and whether they are the people who have to stop it

**The Underdark Has Changed Them:** Every member of the party entered Velkynvelve as a prisoner with nothing. They have since killed a High Priestess of Lolth, destroyed a Pudding King, witnessed Demogorgon's physical manifestation, navigated three major city-states simultaneously, and accumulated enemies across multiple powerful factions. The party that reaches the surface will not be the party that was captured.
