# Zalthir Power Prompt System

Purpose:
- Support a player who enjoys mechanical mastery and rules optimization.
- Turn power-gaming energy into plot-driving choices.
- Keep growth fun without making consequences optional.

## Core Principle

Optimization is canon behavior, not out-of-character "meta."
Zalthir is deliberately experimenting with combat doctrine under extreme pressure.

Each optimization success grants advantage and visibility:
- More power now,
- more counters later.

## Session Start Prompt (Pick 1)

Ask at the beginning of each session:

1) "What combo are you trying to unlock tonight?"
2) "What rule interaction are you stress-testing?"
3) "What weakness in your build are you trying to patch?"
4) "What risky optimization are you willing to attempt for a spike turn?"
5) "What tactical identity are you pushing this session: control, burst, mobility, or survival?"

Write the answer as the session's "Power Goal."

## Session End Prompt (Pick 1)

Ask after a major fight:

1) "Who saw this technique and can now adapt to it?"
2) "What did this power cost your team or your reputation?"
3) "What enemy behavior changed because of your optimization?"
4) "What new weakness did this expose?"

Write the answer as the session's "Counter Hook."

## Reward/Cost Loop

When Zalthir executes a clever, rules-legit optimization:
- Gain 1 Edge Token.

Edge Token can be spent to:
- Pre-wire a tactic ("I set this up during downtime.")
- Reroll one setup check for a combo enablement.
- Declare a plausible positioning preparation.
- Ask for one enemy pattern tell before initiative.

Each Edge Token spent also adds 1 Signature Point.

## Signature Effects (Escalation)

At Signature 1:
- Minor enemy adaptation appears (positioning awareness, bait tactics).

At Signature 2:
- Named opponents prepare explicit counters to Zalthir's preferred line.

At Signature 3:
- Faction-level response: bounty notes, tactical briefs, anti-Zalthir doctrine.

At Signature 4+:
- Boss encounters include dedicated anti-mobility/anti-control layers.

Reset Option:
- Signature can be reduced by 1 if Zalthir intentionally abandons his strongest pattern for one major encounter.

## Four Optimization Tracks (Rotate Weekly)

1) Efficiency Track
- Prompt: "How do you end this fight one round earlier?"

2) Control Track
- Prompt: "How do you reduce enemy actions this encounter?"

3) Mobility Track
- Prompt: "How do you exploit geometry to break enemy plans?"

4) Resilience Track
- Prompt: "How do you survive focused pressure without external rescue?"

## Quick DM Procedure (90 Seconds)

Before session:
1) Pick one track.
2) Ask one start prompt.
3) Define one likely counter from prior signature.

During session:
4) Award Edge for successful optimization moments.
5) Track Signature when Edge is spent.

After session:
6) Ask one end prompt.
7) Convert answer into next session's counter scene.

## Guardrails

Do:
- Reward creativity and rules fluency.
- Make counters visible and fair.
- Keep tradeoffs explicit.

Don't:
- Punish optimization by fiat.
- nullify class fantasy on every encounter.
- let "best combo" become consequence-free autopilot.

## Integration Note (Campaign Canon)

This system should coexist with:
- Vizeran safe-room mission briefings,
- Heat escalation,
- Daz/DeVir reveal pacing.

Zalthir's optimization choices can influence:
- collateral risk,
- mission speed,
- and faction response intensity.
