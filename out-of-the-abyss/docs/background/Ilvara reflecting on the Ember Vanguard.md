*(Ilvara, her dark eyes narrowing with cold fury, paces her command post, the soft scuff of her boots the only sound in the oppressive quiet. She runs a gloved hand over the hilt of her dagger, a gesture of simmering impatience. The very thought of those insolent surface-dwellers and that apostate Drow still free sends a prickle of venomous irritation up her spine. They were merely a ****shipment****, a simple transfer to be handled, and yet…)*
The situation is becoming an **intolerable insult** to my house and to Lolth herself. They were nothing more than **slaves**, a collection of disarmed rabble brought to Velkynvelve by Welnaste Mizzrym for temporary holding before their final transfer to Menzoberranzan. Welnaste was *quite clear* on my responsibility: I was to ensure they were **not lost or allowed to die** before the next caravan arrived. A simple task for a priestess of my standing.
Yet, they **escaped**. A profound **vulnerability** was exposed, and by my hand. That disfigured fool, Jorlan, was given direct orders to secure them. Their lives, I made clear, **belonged to me** while they were under my command, and I ensured they understood this with the constant reminder of my **scourge**. To see them vanish into the tunnels is a direct challenge to my authority, and to the will of the Matron Mother.
My scouts have been **pursuing them for some time** now, confirming their presence in the Underdark. They were identified as the same wretched group from Velkynvelve, a persistent annoyance. They have been resourceful, I will grant them that, navigating the treacherous Silken Paths and, it seems, even the Darklake. But that merely makes their capture more gratifying.
Now, however, the situation has become... *messy*. There are these preposterous rumors circulating in Gracklstugh, fostered by the incompetent Duergar, of this "Ember Vanguard". They have even produced **wanted posters**. The depictions are utterly inaccurate – a crude, flattering caricature of the Drow wizard, a "red" dragonborn who is clearly bronze, a too-fierce orc, and a dwarf whose likeness is, insultingly, *flattering*. These **wanted posters** claim this "Ember Vanguard" has **killed several members of the Derro High Council** and **stolen a dragon egg**. A Duergar guard even remarked that this supposed "Ember Vanguard" has been **causing so much havoc**.
This is, of course, a ridiculous fabrication. My escaped slaves are hardly a "feared mercenary group," much less capable of such widespread chaos. Yet, this new, exaggerated reputation now precedes them, making their recovery a matter of *even greater* urgency and necessity. The Stone Guards, particularly Captain Errde Blackskull, were *warned* that my Drow would be pursuing them through her city. This perceived "Ember Vanguard" now creates a false narrative, but one I must exploit.
These are no longer mere runaways; they are a **blight upon my honor** and a stain upon my reputation. Their continued existence, particularly under this absurd "Ember Vanguard" moniker, makes them an increasingly dangerous and insolent problem. They will be found, they will be brought back to Menzoberranzan, and they will face the judgment of Lolth. This pursuit is no longer just about retrieving escaped property; it is about **eradicating a nuisance** and reasserting the absolute authority of the Drow. They will learn that defying Ilvara of House Mizzrym is a **fatal mistake**.
