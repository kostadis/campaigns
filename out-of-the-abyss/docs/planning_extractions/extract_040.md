- **Juiblex's influence is not contained to the Pudding King.** It is radiating outward through him and warping the behavior of Blingdenstone's entire population, including its leadership. Chief Dorbo's erratic financial obsession is one confirmed symptom.

- **Temple cleansing mechanics:** A red spell gem with a sanctification spell must be inserted into a menhir at the Temple of the Steadfast Stone. This triggers an attack by servants of Ogremoch's Bane. Surviving the attack and defending the gem awakens three temple guardians.
- **Gracklstugh's status is uncertain** following the party's prior actions there; the party ruled out sending anyone to trade there.
- **The gnome leadership was using the planning session opportunistically** — attempting to task the party with outstanding community problems framed as tactical necessities.
