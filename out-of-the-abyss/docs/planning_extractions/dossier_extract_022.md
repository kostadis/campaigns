## Plinki

Plinki is a female derro found standing near the base of a fifty-foot-tall black obelisk atop one of the mesa's highest tiers in a large Underdark cavern. She was observed gently patting and stroking the obelisk's surface with reverent touch. When the obelisk emitted a flash of light—causing the faerzress in the cavern to momentarily flare—she squealed with glee, then bent down to snatch an object from the ground near the obelisk and began writing diligently in a small notebook.

When Grygum introduced himself as a cleric of Bahamut, Plinki was initially confused but then became excited, exclaiming, "A cleric of Bahamut to see my creation! Excellent! Then it has worked!" She motioned for several other derro (six total) to come to the egg.

Plinki revealed key information about the red dragon egg: the Grey Ghosts stole it from the Keepers of the Flame, and she brought it to this location. She has been using the black obelisk—described as a magical artifact—to infuse the egg with the spirit of a demon in honor of Demogorgon. She is deeply devoted to Demogorgon and views this corrupted egg as her creation.

After explaining her work, Plinki declared that "the beating heart of a cleric of Bahamut will seal the ritual," revealing that she and the surrounding derro intended to kill Grygum as a sacrifice. She hesitated when Grygum threatened that the drow (Zalthir) would destroy the egg if she didn't back down. She was then caught in Zalthir's magical darkness, grappled by Zalthir, and struck by Grygum's necromantic claw attack. Her last known status is grappled and under attack atop the mesa in magical darkness, shouting "Let me go!"

## Zalthir

Zalthir is a drow companion traveling with the party. When Plinki and the derro surrounded Grygum and declared their intent to sacrifice him, Zalthir intervened decisively by casting magical darkness over the entire top tier of the mesa. Within the darkness, he physically attacked the derro and successfully grappled Plinki, restraining her. His last known status is atop the mesa in his own magical darkness, holding Plinki in a grapple. Grygum had earlier used Zalthir as a threat, warning Plinki that "the Drow hates dragons and will destroy the egg" if she didn't back down.

## Demogorgon

Demogorgon is referenced as the demon lord in whose honor Plinki is conducting her ritual. Plinki has been using the black obelisk to infuse a stolen red dragon egg with the spirit of a demon, dedicating this act to Demogorgon.
