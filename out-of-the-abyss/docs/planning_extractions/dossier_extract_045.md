## Zuggtmoy

Zuggtmoy, the Lady of Rot, made her presence known through a chilling voice that echoed in the party's minds after they finished harvesting rare lichen in the Festering Fissure. She was speaking to the Pudding King about her sinister plans, revealing her intention to use oozes to "soften the shell" so her garden could "take root in the flesh beneath." This suggests an active collaboration between Zuggtmoy and the Pudding King, with oozes serving as a preparatory tool for her fungal corruption to spread.

## Dasco Pickshine

Dasco Pickshine is the owner of Pickshine Mine, where four massive Galeb Duhr were blocking the path. He had tried everything to move them, including striking them with his pickaxe, but the ancient sentinels refused to budge. After Daz solved the ancient handshake protocol by arranging resonant geodes and humming to complete the Galeb Duhr's call, the path was opened. Dasco grudgingly offered the party two ruby gems worth five hundred gold pieces as thanks for resolving the blockage.

## Perigrog Scrapedust

Perigrog Scrapedust was encountered at the White Shell Mine, the largest salt mine in the Underdark. He explained to the party that a tunnel had become impassable—impossibly dry, with blinding crystallite walls and absolute, suffocating silence, where shadows appeared painted on walls as if time itself had stopped. Perigrog and the other gnomes eagerly presented their specialized explosives to help breach the wall once Zalthir identified the solution. After Zalthir successfully redirected the magical blast through shadow space and shattered the wall without collapsing the tunnels, the gnomes celebrated their success.

## Entemoch

Entemoch did not appear directly but is the entity whose boon the party seeks. Beyond the breached wall in the White Shell Mine, the party discovered a summoning circle inscribed with runes in giant script, guarded by two basilisks moving with mechanical precision. According to the giant runes Thorin deciphered, freeing Entemoch and claiming his boon requires three sacrifices: wealth, a weapon, and blood. The weapon sacrifice was completed when Thorin placed a dart on the runes, which was instantly swallowed and rusted to dust. The runes transformed from yellow to blinding orange as the ritual began, with two sacrifices still remaining.

## Pudding King

The Pudding King was referenced in Zuggtmoy's psychic message overheard by the party. Zuggtmoy was speaking directly to him about her plans to use oozes to "soften the shell" so her garden could "take root in the flesh beneath," indicating that the Pudding King is actively collaborating with Zuggtmoy and likely commanding oozes in service of her fungal corruption schemes.
