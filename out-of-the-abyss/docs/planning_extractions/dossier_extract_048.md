## Pudding King

The Pudding King was confronted in his throne room after the party breached the walls using an Earth Elemental. When Zalthir attempted to grapple him, the Pudding King transformed before the party's eyes, shedding his humanoid form and becoming a sentient, telepathic ooze—a manifestation of his complete embrace of Juiblex, the demon lord of oozes. He telepathically screamed, "I have shed my humanoid form for the supreme power! I shall reduce all of deep known kind into nothing but ooze!" though the party asked him to lower his mental volume.

In combat, the Pudding King utilized his lair's green slime ceiling hazards and powerful lair actions. He unleashed devastating slime wave attacks that knocked multiple party members prone and covered them in corrosive slime. Despite his power, Zalthir maintained a grapple on him and made the crucial tactical decision to drag the Pudding King out of his throne room entirely, stripping him of his lair actions. During this maneuver, Zalthir dragged the Pudding King directly through Glabbagool's body.

At some point during the battle, the Pudding King and Glabbagool swapped bodies or positions, causing significant confusion—the party accidentally attacked Glabbagool before realizing what had happened. The Pudding King attempted to use the green slime against the party even after being dragged from his lair.

Under a coordinated assault—including Daz's third-level Magic Missile (20 damage), Grygum's Inflict Wounds (23 damage), Thorin's Dawnbringer strikes (12 radiant damage), and Zalthir's devastating flurry of strikes enhanced by Eldritch Claw tentacles (37 damage)—the Pudding King was unable to maintain his form. He dissolved completely with a final telepathic scream: "Behold the true feast, the soul's final blend!" His consciousness dissipated and his physical form melted into inert puddles.

## Glabbagool

The party's gelatinous cube companion was present during the throne room battle. When Zalthir dragged the Pudding King through Glabbagool's body, Glabbagool remarked that "this tickles." Zalthir took acid damage from passing through Glabbagool and mentally noted that Glabbagool should have the discipline to control his body's acidity, resolving to suggest monk training for the cube.

During the battle, Glabbagool and the Pudding King somehow swapped bodies or positions. When Thorin struck what he thought was the Pudding King with darts, a familiar voice cried out asking "Why are you hurting me?" Thorin tested the ooze's identity by asking where they first met, and it responded "we met in a cave," confirming it was Glabbagool. The real Pudding King had become a Gelatinous Cube and Glabbagool had become an ooze.

After the Pudding King's defeat, Glabbagool was remarkably cheerful. Now in ooze form rather than his original gelatinous cube body, he expressed excitement about the change—being a sentient gelatinous cube had always been inconvenient because he moved slowly and people had to wait for him. He enthusiastically suggested the party could carry him in a jar or even in the Bag of Holding, allowing him to travel with them and see the world while serving as "the ultimate garbage disposal." The party discussed beginning his monk training to teach him to control his form with precision.

## Prince Livid

One of the Pudding King's ooze allies who fought in the throne room battle. Prince Livid attacked Zalthir with a psychic blast dealing fourteen points of damage (reduced to seven by a successful intelligence saving throw). Later, he used a psychic attack on Daz dealing five points of damage. He was resistant to fire damage, which allowed him to survive Daz's Fireball with only eight hit points remaining (taking fourteen of the twenty-nine points dealt). He was ultimately killed by Daz's Magic Missile after Princess Ebonmire had already fallen.

## Princess Ebonmire

One of the Pudding King's ooze allies who fought in the throne room battle. She attacked by hurling blobs of acidic ooze at the party, initially targeting Thorin but missing. She later charged at Thorin during the mopping-up phase. She took the full twenty-nine points of damage from Daz's Fireball and was further struck by Grygum's Inflict Wounds (eighteen damage) and Zalthir's grapple attacks before Thorin delivered the killing blow. After her death, the party discovered a spellbook containing numerous magical formulas inside her stomach.

## Juiblex

The demon lord of oozes, whose power was channeled through the Pudding King. After the Pudding King's defeat, the party felt Juiblex's toxic, oppressive demonic presence recede from the stones and air of the cavern, seeping away like water draining from a basin. However, a demonic voice—possibly Juiblex himself—echoed through the chamber declaring: "You haven't won. I will be reborn. I will consume the banquet of the queen of the fungi, and I will grow." This suggests Juiblex intends to return by consuming Zuggtmoy's power.

## Zuggtmoy

The demon queen of fungi. Though she did not appear directly, her influence was immediately felt after the Pudding King's defeat. The fungi growing throughout the cavern became more twisted and malevolent in appearance, suggesting Zuggtmoy was already moving to fill the power vacuum left by Juiblex's diminished hold on the area. Juiblex's voice specifically referenced her as "the queen of the fungi" whose banquet he intended to consume upon his rebirth.
