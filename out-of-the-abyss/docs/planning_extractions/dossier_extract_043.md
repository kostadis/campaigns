## Glabbagool

Glabbagool, the intelligent gelatinous cube traveling with the party, expressed unusual delight at tasting the Medusa's remains after the battle. He cheerfully noted that the party provided an excellent supply of dietary flavoring as he slowly cleaned up the aftermath of the fight in the Medusa's lair.

## Gerdig Katfinger

Gerdig Katfinger had previously provided the party with a Ruby spell gem, which was used in this session to begin the hallowing ritual. Grygum placed the gem into the great menhir at the center of the ancient temple as part of the effort to cleanse it of Ogremoch's Bane.

## Ogremoch

Ogremoch (referenced as "Ogremoch's Bane") is an evil force from the elemental plane of earth that had infected the ancient temple. According to the Galeb Duhr guardians, Ogremoch's influence had taken control of the Earth Elementals within the temple and prevented the Galeb Duhr stone guardians from performing their sacred duty to protect the site. The party's mission to hallow the temple is directly aimed at cleansing his corrupting presence. As each Earth Elemental was destroyed, a Galeb Duhr was freed from Ogremoch's control, though the process appeared to require defeating multiple elementals to fully liberate the temple.
