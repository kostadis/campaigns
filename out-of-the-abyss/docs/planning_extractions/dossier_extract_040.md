## Daz

Daz took decisive control of Blingdenstone's political proceedings out of sheer exasperation with the bureaucratic debates. He cut through the noise and insisted on a direct plan to deal with the Pudding King and the slime threat. His assertiveness surprised everyone but came from frustration rather than a desire for power. Chief Dorbo and Senni agreed to support his plan, which involved recruiting the Gold Whisker clan as a distraction while the party dealt with the Pudding King directly.

When meeting Chief Chipgrin, Daz quickly clarified the party's true purpose: they needed help resolving the slime problem to secure a path out of the Underdark. He offered to arrange a meeting between Chipgrin and Chief Dorbo for cooperation only, explicitly refusing to facilitate any political maneuvering. Chipgrin remarked that he had been told drow were difficult to negotiate with, but Daz took it to another level. Daz declared the party would make all battle plans and take full accountability for outcomes—if mistakes led to casualties, the blame would fall on him personally, and Chipgrin could seek vengeance against him.

At the grand moot, Senni asked Daz to lead the meeting. He presented the plan: a full-scale gnome army distraction attacking the ooze amphitheater while the party covertly used magic to snatch the Pudding King. He explained the demon of oozes' influence on the Pudding King and the gnome leadership. When gnome leaders began proposing various side quests, Daz questioned why they couldn't simply attack from the north, exposing that the leaders were trying to exploit the party's capabilities for community benefit. The leaders quickly agreed with his suggestion, though the party ultimately chose to pursue some of the side quests voluntarily.

## Chief Dorbo

Chief Dorbo eventually agreed to support Daz's plan in the previous meeting. He appeared annoyed when Thorin intruded into his private kitchen quarters, directing the party to eat at the Foaming Bug inn instead. At the grand moot, Dorbo suggested attacking from the Temple of the Steadfast Stone as a staging area to keep oozes away from main population centers. He sweetened the deal for the party by offering a Stone of Controlling Earth Elementals as a reward for dealing with Ogremoch's Bane and cleansing the temple. The party noted that Dorbo's obsession with money seemed unhinged given the circumstances, which Chipgrin suggested was due to the demon's corrupting influence bleeding through the Pudding King and affecting the entire community's leadership—amplifying greed and aggression.

## Senni

Senni had agreed to support Daz's plan in the previous meeting. At the grand moot, she asked Daz to lead the meeting and call it to order. She was present among the major leaders of Blingdenstone during the planning session.

## Chipgrin

Chief Chipgrin, leader of the Gold Whisker clan of wererat gnomes, sat on a stone chair draped with a cave badger pelt, flanked by two giant rats and surrounded by guards on a plateau of soft, well-manicured green moss. He initially assumed the party had been sent by the Diggermattocks to remove his clan. After Daz clarified their purpose, Chipgrin was impressed by Daz's blunt approach.

Chipgrin agreed to fight the ooze but insisted the Gold Whisker clan would not be led by Chief Dorbo or sent to slaughter under his command. He was satisfied when Daz took personal accountability for any outcomes. Chipgrin shared valuable intelligence about the Pudding King: he had known the gnome when he worked in the tunnels and had seen him after his transformation. He explained the Pudding King's actions were driven by vengeance, pride, madness, and the influence of a demon that amplified his worst attributes. Chipgrin prompted the party to consider whether they had noticed anything odd about the other gnomes, leading them to realize that greed and aggression were unusually pronounced among the population. He agreed with the party's theories about Juiblex and confirmed the demon's influence, centered on the Pudding King, was bleeding through and corrupting the entire community. He attended the grand moot to finalize plans.

## Glabbagool

Glabbagool, the party's gelatinous cube companion, enthusiastically offered to clean Chief Dorbo's kitchen floor, which the chef accepted with relief. This earned Glabbagool the affectionate nickname "Roomba." In the final plan, Glabbagool was assigned to assist the Barrow Warden ghosts with their quest alongside Eldev and Jimjar, while the main party handled other tasks.

## Nomi Pathshutter

Nomi Pathshutter attended the grand moot as one of Blingdenstone's major leaders. She participated in what appeared to be a rehearsed presentation with Gurnik Tapfinger, proposing that cleansing the Temple of the Steadfast Stone would allow Earth Elementals to be summoned and protected from being driven insane by Ogremoch's Bane.

## Gurnik Tapfinger

Gurnik Tapfinger, the cleric, attended the grand moot. He explained the mechanics of cleansing the Temple of the Steadfast Stone: placing a red spell gem containing a powerful sanctification spell into a menhir at the temple would provoke Ogremoch's Bane to send servants to attack, and the party would need to defend it until three temple guardians awakened. He participated in a rehearsed presentation with Nomi Pathshutter about the benefits of Earth Elementals. Even after Daz pointed out the simpler northern attack route, Gurnik continued to advocate for cleansing the temple, emphasizing the value of having Earth Elementals in battle.

## Kazook Pickshine

Kazook Pickshine represented the Miners Guild at the grand moot. He expressed concern about acidic damage to the gnomes' weapons and armor during the battle against the oozes. He proposed two solutions: collecting ingredients from Neverlight Grove for an acid-protection potion, or trading salt for high-quality weapons in Gracklstugh.

## Uth-Jadgar

Barrow Warden Uth-Jadgar, a ghost, attended the grand moot. He praised the party's plan as technically brilliant, comparing it to strategies the Barrow Wardens would have employed in their prime. He revealed that the Temple of the Steadfast Stone was located in an area called the Rock Blight and needed to be cleansed before it could serve as a staging area. Uth-Jadgar offered the help of other Barrow Warden ghosts if the party undertook a quest for them. The party assigned Glabbagool, Eldev, and Jimjar to assist with the Barrow Warden ghosts' quest.

## The Pudding King

The Pudding King was discussed extensively but did not appear in person. Chief Chipgrin revealed he had known the Pudding King when he was a gnome working in the tunnels and had seen him after his transformation. His actions are driven by vengeance, pride, madness, and the influence of a demon (likely Juiblex, the demon lord of oozes) that amplified his worst attributes. The demon's influence, centered on the Pudding King, is bleeding through and corrupting the entire gnome community, amplifying greed and aggression among the population and leadership. The party's plan is to use a full-scale army distraction at the ooze amphitheater while covertly using magic to snatch the Pudding King.
