## Jimjar

Jimjar excitedly announced the party was only a day's journey from Blingdenstone upon reaching the Dark Lake shores. He immediately made a bet that someone would demand a rest and celebrated winning it when the party agreed. During the ascent to Blingdenstone, he explained the city's history—how it had changed hands between gnomes and drow multiple times and was currently experiencing difficulties under gnome control. At the gates, he attempted to smooth over the guards' suspicion of Daz by downplaying his drow heritage and emphasizing his interest in exploration rather than domination.

When Juiblex, the demon lord of oozes, was mentioned in connection with Blingdenstone's ooze problem, Jimjar's demeanor changed noticeably, as if recalling something painful. He explained that Juiblex represented the corrupted nature of the Abyss, a being of unrivaled obscenity that corrupted everything into a shapeless mess. He then made a remarkably specific bet that the party would need to arm an army with acid-resistant gear before the month ended, with a side bet about retrieving ingredients from a fungal wedding celebration. His unusual knowledge and the deference shown to him by the other gnomes suggested he held some significant but unstated position in Blingdenstone.

## Glabagool

Glabagool, the party's sentient gelatinous cube companion, appeared at the gate of Blingdenstone, causing the guards to panic and prepare to attack. The party reassured them that Glabagool was friendly and had helped them on their journey. Glabagool spoke aloud, demonstrating its unique sentience, which astonished the guards. It explained that it had gained the ability to think and speak after an unknown event and had noticed an unusual increase in agitated oozes in the area, describing the phenomenon as an "oozapalooza." Its presence was particularly concerning to the guards given Blingdenstone's serious ooze problem. The guards recommended the party speak with Chief Dorbo Diggermattock and Quartermaster Senni about full permissions, especially regarding Glabagool.

## Dorbo Diggermattock

Mentioned by the guards as the Chief of Blingdenstone. The guards recommended the party speak with him to obtain full permissions to enter and move freely within the city.

## Senni

Mentioned by the guards as the Quartermaster of Blingdenstone. The guards recommended speaking with her alongside Chief Dorbo Diggermattock about full permissions, particularly regarding Glabagool's presence in the city.

## Thorin

Thorin, the orc cleric of Bahamut, vouched for the party's truthfulness at the gates of Blingdenstone, surprising the gnome guards with his unusual faith as an orc devoted to Bahamut.
