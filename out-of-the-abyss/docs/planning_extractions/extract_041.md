Noted that casualties could still contribute to the coming battle as ghosts via resurrection magic. Previously tasked the party with rescuing named ghosts, including Udhask. Was sent by the party to "clean" the gargoyle statues, triggering their animation and attack.

**Unnamed Merchant from Gracklstugh** — Tavern patron. Provided intelligence about Gracklstugh: described a strange peace in the city, mentioned mass hangings and executions, referenced paranoia surrounding the Ember Vanguard, and possessed a wanted poster matching several party members' descriptions. Noted the party resembled the wanted individuals but with notable differences (red dragonborn vs. the poster's description; drow mage, orc cleric, and dwarven fighter appeared more fearsome than depicted). Also described a portly red dragon with a double chin and two bellies being used as a matchlighter.

**Unnamed Deep Gnome Scout** — Tavern patron. Shared an account of encountering dancing myconids who spoke of attending a wedding celebration, behavior described as highly unusual given that myconids do not typically marry or celebrate.

**Unnamed Stone Heart Enclave Member** — Greeted the party warmly at the cultivation cave. Provided fresh fungi supplies for the party's quest into the rock blight caverns.

**Ghost of an Unnamed Deep Gnome** — Appeared in the rock blight caverns and attacked the party in an agitated state. Under combat pressure from Zalthir, shouted "They're coming! Everyone must hide!" and became visibly frightened. Turned ethereal and fled into a burrow. Disappeared inside, leaving behind skeletal remains.

**Udhask (deceased)** — One of the ghosts Burrow Warden Jadger tasked the party with rescuing. Skeletal remains identified inside the burrow the ghost fled to. Bones showed no signs of violent death, suggesting a peaceful or natural end. Remains collected by the party.

**Gargoyles (two, destroyed/engaged)** — Animated stone winged statues perched on a ledge above a stream. Attacked when Glabbagool approached. One destroyed by Thorin via darts; the second engaged Grygum with bites and claws before the encounter was interrupted by a crazed earth elemental.

**Crazed Earth Elemental** — Erupted from the ground during the gargoyle battle. Confirmed prior warnings that earth elementals in the rock blight cavern area have been driven mad. Combat ongoing at session end.
