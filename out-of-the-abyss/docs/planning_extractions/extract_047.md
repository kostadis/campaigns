## Revealed Information

- **Juiblex Alliance:** The Pudding King has explicitly allied with the demon lord Juiblex, who has promised him the kingdom of Blingdenstone. The Pudding King views Zuggtmoy (Queen of Rot) as a rival threat to his claim.

- **Zuggtmoy as Rival:** The Pudding King believes Zuggtmoy is attempting to take his kingdom, suggesting inter-demonic competition over the region.
- **Brain Slime:** A creature referred to as a "brain slime" occupies the tunnel ceiling near the throne room approach and emits a psychic song capable of incapacitating minds; tunnel walls in that section have transformed into gelatinous psychic mirrors with similar effects.
- **Pudding King Ooze Form:** The Pudding King can transform into an amorphous ooze form when threatened; he retains combat capability and the ability to strike with precision in this state.
- **Glabbagool Vulnerability:** Glabbagool previously heard the Pudding King's voice calling to him, suggesting partial or potential psychic influence remains possible.
- **Ochre Jelly Barricade:** The hallway leading to the throne room was blocked by a full-corridor ochre jelly whose heartbeat matched the Pudding King's own, suggesting a connection between the creature and the villain.
- **Green Slime Ceiling Hazards:** The throne room ceiling is covered in green slime patches that can drop on characters standing beneath them.
