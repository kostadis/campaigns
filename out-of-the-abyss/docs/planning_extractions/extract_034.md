## NPC Activity

**Zuggtmoy (Queen of Fungi)**
- Absent physically but her influence pervades the Neverlight Grove. Her thralls are rehearsing a wedding procession for her planned union with Araumycos.
- Grygum receives a vision of Zuggtmoy: a humanoid figure three times the height of her fungal bridesmaids, composed entirely of fungi and mold, floating inside a great tower (assumed to be Yggmorgus), being dressed in lichen veils and a mycelium bridal gown by attending Myconids.
- Her presence near Yggmorgus inflicts madness visions on Daz (disturbing images of drow living peacefully alongside elves and keeping dogs as pets).

**Yestabrod**
- Deceased. His corpse is being puppeted by three spore servants during the mock wedding rehearsal, playing the role of the officiating priest.

**Phylo**
- Presiding over the Inner Circle when the party returns. Greets Basidia with feigned normalcy, asking what they thought of "the changes."
- Does not attack the fleeing party. Instead pleads with Basidia not to leave, invoking "the Great Seeder" and promising the Neverlight Grove will be "better than ever." Last heard calling out plaintively as the party escapes.
- Confirmed as having allied with Zuggtmoy, betraying the Myconid circle according to Basidia's accusation.

**Basidia**
- Confronted Phylo directly upon learning the full situation, accusing them of betrayal and alliance with "that monster."
- Refused all attempts at persuasion to flee, requiring Zalthir to physically grapple and drag them away from the Inner Circle.
- Visibly devastated leaving Phylo and the grove. Upon reaching safety with the Circle of Explorers, handed the party potions of greater healing and accumulated treasure from years of collecting items from travelers.

**Rusharoo**
- Urged caution against Basidia's impulse to confront Phylo immediately.
- Followed Basidia into danger when they moved to confront Phylo.
- Warned the party telepathically to flee with them via a pre-identified escape hatch.
- Had gathered the Circle of Hunters and Explorers (including Loobamub) near an exit tunnel and led a group mind meld before departure.
- Escorted the party partway toward Blingdenstone, providing a guide for half the distance before bidding farewell.
- Last seen departing with Basidia, Loobamub, and remaining loyal Myconids into the Underdark.

**Loobamub**
- Present with Rusharoo and the Circle of Explorers at the escape tunnel. No independent action recorded.

**Stool**
- Convinced by Grygum to leave the Neverlight Grove given their shared history and what Stool had witnessed.
- Said a farewell to Daz before departing with the Myconids, with apparent sadness.

**Rump-a-dump (Rumpadump)**
- Convinced alongside Stool to flee.
- Before departing, delivered an unprompted enumeration of approximately fifty specific ways Zalthir could improve himself (e.g., "Listen to Stool," "Don't assume you know everything," "Eat more Zurkhwood").
- Said goodbye to Daz and departed with the Myconids.

**Xinaya**
- Deceased. Her corpse was looted on the party's way back through the grove. Zalthir seized all her belongings including scrolls before others could act (later attributed to a temporary madness effect, subsequently cured).

**Jimjar**
- Suggested Blingdenstone as a destination and source of aid, and that a way out of the Underdark could be found there.
- Took over as guide after Rusharoo's escort ended. Led the party into a cavern with explosive gases where they were attacked by a large eight-legged creature (defeated).
- Later, a tremor collapsed passages ahead and behind the party — Jimjar's navigational error. Attempted to distract the group with an incoherent bet ("The next emotion Zalthir shows will be as if made of chipped black obsidian"), admitted he was just trying to distract them, then identified a previously unnoticed side passage as an alternate route.
- Last seen leading the party into newly discovered tunnels.

**Ilvara**
- Absent but discussed. Daz explains she is still pursuing the party because she personally promised their delivery to Menzoberranzan, the outpost of Velkynvelve was destroyed on her watch, and failure would result in her transformation into a Drider. Assessed as relentless until either the party is captured or she is dead.

**Pelek (ghost)**
- Consulted when Zalthir behaved erratically after looting Xinaya's corpse. Confirmed Zalthir was not possessed by a ghost. Prompted Grygum to consider using a cleric's lesser restoration to cure the madness.

## Revealed Information

- **Araumycos** is a massive sentient fungal growth native to the Underdark and the natural habitat of Myconids. Zuggtmoy intends to marry it, which the party infers would grant her vast power over decay and death across the world.
- **Yggmorgus** is the towering central mushroom of the Neverlight Grove. Its interior is lit by phosphorescent mold and emits atonal music; it is where Zuggtmoy is being prepared for the wedding.
- **Phylo** has fully allied with Zuggtmoy and is aware that the party has seen too much — Zalthir notes Phylo believes they will be killed the following day.
- **Blingdenstone** is identified as a potential refuge and possible exit point from the Underdark.
- **Ilvara's motivation** fully explained: personal oath to deliver the party to Menzoberranzan, destruction of Velkynvelve requiring her to recapture them or face transformation into a Drider.
