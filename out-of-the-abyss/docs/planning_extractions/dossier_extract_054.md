## Jimjar

Jimjar served as the party's navigator prior to reaching the Oozing Temple, where his "disastrous navigational blunder" led the Ember Vanguard into the flooding complex of tunnels. Despite the near-catastrophe, Jimjar claimed the disaster was intentional and won a bet he had placed — that the situation would not "end in tears" — when the party discovered both a shortcut to Blingdenstone and gained Glabbagool as an ally.

Upon the party's arrival at the gates of Blingdenstone, Jimjar stepped in during the tense interrogation by the deep gnome guards. He began pressing wagers on the party's future and displayed an unusually profound and specific knowledge of the greater demonic plague afflicting the Underdark. In a moment of high-stakes foreshadowing, Jimjar offered a side bet detailing the heroes' next likely quest: retrieving ingredients for "acid-resistant gear" from a "fungal wedding celebration," wagering 50 gold on it. The party accepted the wager and the intelligence it contained. His eerily precise foreknowledge and gambling compulsion continue to mark him as an enigmatic figure whose true nature and motivations remain unclear.

## Glabbagool

Glabbagool is a newly sentient gelatinous cube the party encountered in the Oozing Temple. His consciousness is described as a curious sign of the rampaging chaotic sentience phenomenon haunting the Underdark alongside the plots of Demogorgon. He joined the Ember Vanguard as an ally and offered to hold onto the party's loot temporarily.

Upon arrival at Blingdenstone's gates, Glabbagool's appearance caused the deep gnome guards to panic, confirming the city's crippling "ooze problem." When Grygum tried to defend the creature, one guard demanded to know why they were friends with an ooze, insisting that "gelatinous cubes aren't sentient." Glabbagool confirmed that the "ooza-palooza" of increasingly hostile oozes was occurring, reinforcing the connection to Jubilex's influence. His presence remains a source of tension with Blingdenstone's inhabitants.

## Eldeth

Eldeth identified the geological danger in the Oozing Temple as an underground river breaking through the stone, providing the party with crucial information about the cause of the flooding.

## Dorbo Diggermattock

Chief Dorbo Diggermattock is one of the leaders of Blingdenstone. The party was directed by the gate guards to consult with him for formal entry permissions into the city. He has not yet been met directly.

## Sennai

Quartermaster Sennai is a leader in Blingdenstone whom the party was directed to consult alongside Chief Dorbo Diggermattock for full authorization to enter the city. She has not yet been met directly.

## Princess Ebonmire

Princess Ebonmire is referenced only through a spellbook the party previously took from her. Daz has been studying the spellbook and considering it as part of his level-up decisions, noting it contains additional spells he could prepare, particularly in conjunction with the Fey Touched feat. The spellbook is described as sitting in Daz's pack "like a promissory note I hadn't finished reading."
