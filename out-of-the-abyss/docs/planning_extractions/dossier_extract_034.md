## Zuggtmoy

Referred to as the Queen of Fungi and the Queen of decay and death. She intends to "marry" Araumycos, the massive sentient fungal growth in the Underdark. Her union with Araumycos would give her untold power over the world. A vision of her was witnessed by Grygum during the mock wedding procession: inside a great tower, a humanoid figure, womanlike in form but made entirely of fungi and mold, floated in the center of an open space. She was easily three times the height of the fungal bridesmaids who tended to her, weaving her substance into delicate lichen veils and a long mycelium train like a bridal gown. The party had previously felt her presence in a dream. Her influence has driven the Neverlight Grove to madness. She is also referred to by Phyllo as "the Great Seeder."

## Yestabrod

Previously killed by the party, his corpse was used as a puppet during the mock wedding rehearsal procession. Three spore servants rose from the garden, carried his corpse, and moved it to play out the part of a priest presiding over the wedding vows between stand-ins for Araumycos and Zuggtmoy.

## Xinaya

Her corpse was looted by the party on their way back from witnessing the mock wedding. Zalthir ran up to the corpse and stuffed everything into his bag before anyone else could act, including scrolls that Grygum wanted. Xinaya's identity and how she died are not elaborated on in this session.

## Basidia

A Myconid Sovereign of the Neverlight Grove. Upon learning from the party what they witnessed in the Inner Circle, Basidia insisted on going to confront Phyllo directly. When they arrived, Basidia openly accused Phyllo: "Phylo, you have betrayed the Circle. You've allied yourself with that monster!" The party tried to persuade Basidia to flee rather than fight, but the Sovereign wouldn't listen. Zalthir physically grappled and dragged Basidia away from the Inner Circle. Basidia eventually gave up resisting when they saw the madness of the awakening trees. If a Myconid could sob, Basidia would have been crying as they heard Phyllo's plaintive pleas to stay. Once safe in the Circle of Explorers, Basidia handed the party potions of greater healing and whatever treasure the Myconids had accumulated from passersby over the years. Basidia departed with Rusharoo, Loobamub, and the circle of hunters and explorers.

## Phyllo

A Myconid Sovereign who has allied with Zuggtmoy. Phyllo had planned for the party to be killed the following day. When the party returned to the Inner Circle acting nonchalant, Phyllo greeted Basidia and asked if the visitors had enjoyed seeing the circle and what they thought of "the changes." When Basidia accused Phyllo of betrayal, Phyllo did not attack the fleeing party. Instead, Phyllo pleaded with Basidia not to leave, saying "The Neverlight Grove will be better than ever! And the Great Seeder will make us all happy!" and plaintively called out "Basidia don't go." Phyllo remains in the Neverlight Grove, allied with Zuggtmoy.

## Rusharoo

A Myconid who urged caution when Basidia wanted to confront Phyllo, but followed their Sovereign into danger regardless. When the situation in the Inner Circle deteriorated, Rusharoo shouted telepathically "If you want to live, come with me," leading the party to a pre-prepared escape route. Rusharoo had previously identified exit holes from the grove. After escaping, Rusharoo provided a guide for half the distance to Blingdenstone and then bid the party farewell on the 4th day of the third tenday of Myrkhul 1495. Rusharoo departed with Basidia, Loobamub, and the circle of hunters and explorers.

## Loobamub

A Myconid who gathered with Rusharoo and the circle of hunters and explorers near the exit hole to escape the Neverlight Grove. Participated in a deep mind meld before departing. Left with Basidia and Rusharoo.

## Pelek

A ghost encountered by the party. When Grygum asked if Pelek knew anything about Zalthir's sudden compulsive hoarding behavior, Pelek replied, "No. I am a ghost, not a fungi expert." He confirmed Zalthir was not possessed by a ghost. He then prompted Grygum by asking "Aren't you a cleric?" which led to Dawnbringer casting lesser restoration on Zalthir to cure his madness.

## Dawnbringer

A sentient magical weapon (sword) carried by the party. Dawnbringer shouted "I can cast lesser restoration!" and cast the spell on Zalthir to cure his madness before Grygum could even act. During the tense confrontation in the Inner Circle, Dawnbringer's bright light was counterproductive, causing the Myconids to retreat from the party and undermining their attempts at stealth.

## Jimjar

A deep gnome NPC traveling with the party. Irritatingly perky even in dire circumstances, he suggested the party could get help from the "overbrite" (surface) and find a way out of the Underdark through Blingdenstone. After Rusharoo's guide left, Jimjar took over as guide, declaring "I'll bet you I can get you there!" He made a nonsensical bet with Grygum about the journey. His guidance started poorly: he led the party into a cavern where they were attacked by a large eight-legged octopus creature, and later a tremor caused passageways to collapse around them, trapping the group. He attempted to distract the party with an inscrutable bet about Zalthir's emotions and "chipped black obsidian," then admitted he was just trying to distract them. He then spotted a hidden passageway leading into more caves and declared they should go in. Last seen leading the party into the newly discovered tunnels.

## Rump-a-dump (Rumpadump)

A Myconid companion of the party. Grygum convinced Rump-a-dump (along with Stool) that the Neverlight Grove was no longer safe. Before departing with the other Myconids, Rump-a-dump approached Zalthir and enumerated approximately fifty specific ways Zalthir could improve himself, including "Listen to Stool, he knows what he is doing," "Don't assume you know everything; other people's points of view are important," and "Eat more Zurkhwood, it's good for your digestion." Said goodbye to Daz with a hint of sadness before departing with their people.

## Stool

A Myconid companion of the party. Grygum convinced Stool (along with Rump-a-dump) that the Neverlight Grove was no longer safe. Said goodbye to Daz with a hint of sadness before departing with the other Myconids.

## Ilvara

A Drow priestess who continues to pursue the party through the Underdark. Daz explained to his companions why she won't give up: she had promised the party would arrive in Menzoberranzan, likely with money exchanged for that promise, and the outpost of Velkynvelve was destroyed on top of that. She needs to find the party or risk becoming a Drider. Daz stated she won't rest until the party is found or she is dead. She does not appear directly but her pursuit continues to influence the party's behavior.
