## NPC Activity

**Ploopploopeen** (Archpriest of the Sea Mother)
- Clarified his plan to the escapees: they are to act as bait/false offering, not combatants. He will present them to his daughter Bloppblippodd, insist on accompanying them, and then his followers will attack.
- Noted that a previous attempt at peaceful negotiation ("white flag") failed — Bloppblippodd requires sacrifices.
- Confirmed that Leemoogoogon's rituals prefer unwilling sacrifices, as it is an evil god.
- Led the party through Sloobludop's western gate; guards snapped to attention on his arrival.
- Stopped at the Sea Mother statue and was pleased when party members spat on the altar as offering.
- Announced a meal and reassured the party that kuo-toans eat cooked fish.
- Fell asleep standing and walked outside (normal kuo-toan sleep behavior).
- In the morning, admitted a kuo-toa monitor and twelve kuo-toa to his quarters, and declared that he, his whip, and the prisoners would accompany them to see Bloppblippodd.

**Bloppblippodd** (High Priestess of the Deep Father, Ploopploopeen's daughter)
- Absent but central to the plan. She is expected to demand to see the offering in the morning, accept Ploopploopeen's insistence on accompanying the party, and then be ambushed by Sea Mother loyalists.
- Has broken from the Sea Mother faith and converted to worship of Leemoogoogon/the Deep Father.
- Described by Glooglugogg as having been shown too much deference by their father, lacking respect for kuo-toan ways, and falling prey to "the madness."
- Described as having few hardcore supporters at the Deep Father altar, but her broader village support is unknown.

**Glooglugogg ("Gloog")** (Son of Ploopploopeen, brother of Bloppblippodd)
- Was present in Ploopploopeen's quarters, initially motionless enough that the party was uncertain if he was awake.
- Expressed disapproval of his father's choice to involve outsiders, but acknowledged the party showed respect to the Sea Mother.
- Provided intel on the village's political situation: Bloppblippodd has few hardcore supporters at the altar, but broader village allegiances are unknown — this is why action must be taken now, while Sea Mother loyalists still hold the majority.
- Clarified the party's expected role: they are to visibly support the Sea Mother side during the confrontation, though they should not throw the first punch, as that honor belongs to the kuo-toans.
- Noted with mild surprise that the party did not simply hand over their derro prisoner as the offering.
- Fell asleep after the conversation.

**Shuushar** (Referenced, not present in scene)

**Kuo-Toa Monitor** (Unnamed)

## Revealed Information

- **Ploopploopeen's plan in full**: The party serves as a false sacrificial offering. Ploopploopeen will accompany them to Bloppblippodd's altar. Once assembled, Sea Mother loyalists will attack. The party is not expected to initiate violence.
- **Leemoogoogon's ritual preferences**: Unwilling sacrifices are preferred; provenance (willing vs. unwilling) matters less than the act of sacrifice itself.
- **Bloppblippodd's position**: She requires sacrifices to validate her god. Previous attempts at peaceful resolution by Ploopploopeen have failed.
- **Village political balance**: Sea Mother loyalists currently hold the majority, but the situation is unstable. Gloog believes action must be taken now before that changes.
- **Kuo-toan sleep behavior**: Kuo-toans sleep standing with eyes open and walk around while asleep; some move in school-like group patterns. This is normal behavior and not a threat indicator.
- **Sea Mother worship**: Spitting on or regurgitating at the Sea Mother's altar is considered a respectful offering — confirmed by Ploopploopeen's positive reaction.
