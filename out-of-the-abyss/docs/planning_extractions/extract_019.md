## Revealed Information

- **Plinki's plan:** Recently converted to the Cult of Demogorgon and is attempting to use an obelisk in the cave complex to transform the dragon egg into a two-headed dragon mount for Demogorgon.
- **Two entrances to the obelisk chamber:** A main entrance and a side passageway. The side passageway leads through a chamber occupied by a dual-affiliated Council of Savants / Gray Ghosts Derro with four guards.
- **Faerzress disruption as a causal event:** The Whorlestone Caves were formerly a neutral social space for Derro and Duergar. The Faerzress anomaly (full darkness, followed by abnormal blinking, followed by altered return to twilight) is directly identified as the trigger for the breakdown of relations and the onset of violence and conspiracy.
- **Gartokkar's true objective:** He intends to use the Demogorgon statue and evidence of cult involvement to petition the Deepking to eliminate the Derro from Gracklstugh — a genocidal political move, not merely law enforcement.
- **The Keepers of the Flame's constraint:** They cannot enter the Whorlestone Caves without revealing the loss of the egg (a key power asset) and triggering open war, explaining why they need outside agents.
- **Errde's constraint:** She cannot enter the Whorlestone Caves without exposing her failure as city guardian.
- **Mind flayer confirmed present** in the Whorlestone Caves complex, down a specific hallway, in proximity to the Empty Scabbard Killers.
- **Buppido connection:** The Flumph identifies Buppido as a former friend who went mad — Grygum's silence implies the party has prior knowledge of or involvement in Buppido's fate.
- **Flumph intelligence method:** The Flumph does not read minds; it absorbs ambient psychic residue. It cannot report on written documents or specific thoughts, only on emotional and psychic impressions and memories it has absorbed.
