## Gru Manga

Gru Manga is a hulking Ettin who served as a major combatant during the battle against Narrak and the Derro. When Grygum's acid spell caught several Derro, Gru Manga roared in rage directly at Grygum, convinced Grygum was the source of his pain. Zalthir moved a sphere of darkness to envelop Gru Manga, blinding him, then used his Eldritch Maul tentacles to strike and grapple the Ettin. Despite being grappled and blinded, Gru Manga stubbornly clung to life until Zalthir delivered the final decisive blow, killing him. Narrak screeched about sacrifice and glory as Gru Manga fell. Status: **dead**.

## Narrak

Narrak is a Derro cult leader who was orchestrating a dark ritual involving a two-foot-tall two-headed stone giant statue and reading from a text called "The Rituals of the Two-Headed Beast." Throughout the battle, Narrak's voice boomed out phrases like "Fools, your sacrifice is but a step toward glory!" though his conviction visibly waned as his allies fell. He was restrained by Daz's Maximilian's Earthen Grasp and failed to break free. While restrained, he attacked Grygum and struck at Thorin with a short spear. Thorin ultimately delivered the killing blow, and Narrak's last words were a desperate plea: "No, the ritual cannot be undone!" His ritual was connected to Demogorgon and was responsible for turning one of Hargaam's stone giants mad. The party looted his body and chest, finding gold, Keoghtom's Ointment, and strange texts including "The Rituals of the Two-Headed Beast." The evidence found on him proves Derro perfidy and support for Demogorgon, which the party now holds as leverage. Status: **dead**.

## JimJar

After the battle with Narrak, JimJar observed that the party now possesses significant evidence from the various factions and recommended they hold onto it for later usefulness. He wanted to make a bet that the evidence would be helpful to them in the future. Daz considered his advice wise. JimJar appears to still be traveling with the party.

## Stool

After the battle concluded, Stool eagerly addressed Zalthir and Grygum, pleading with them to go save "Rump-a-dump" (Rumpadump), showing clear emotional urgency about rescuing their companion.

## Eldeth

Eldeth is not directly present in this session but is referenced in relation to the evidence the party discovered: proof that the king has been working with assassins to kill Eldeth's clan. This evidence is now in the party's possession.

## Hargaam

Hargaam is referenced indirectly. One of Hargaam's stone giants was driven mad by Narrak's ritual involving the two-headed stone giant statue and "The Rituals of the Two-Headed Beast."
