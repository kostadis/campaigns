**Shal**

Achieved a post-Pudding King peace settlement: Earth Elementals restricted to defense and warfare roles; Gold Whisker Wererats assigned upper borough defense; Burrow Wardens reconstituting under Jadger. Dorbo announced reopening for surface trade. However, Zuggtmoy's fungi are already accelerating growth within the cavern — a pale white puffball mushroom was observed growing at impossible speed from a tunnel wall as the party departed, indicating Zuggtmoy is moving to fill the power vacuum left by Juiblex's expulsion.

**Neverlight Grove — Zuggtmoy's Faction**
Basidia's loyalist faction has fled, leaving Zuggtmoy's agents in full control of the grove. Phylo and the Circle of Masters continue reanimating carcasses and preparing for the wedding to Araumycos. Yrberop continues conducting wedding hymn rehearsals. Zuggtmoy remains physically present in Yggmorgus. The wedding has not yet been completed.

**Neverlight Grove — Basidia's Loyalist Faction**
Evacuated the grove with Circles of Explorers and Hunters. Provided the party with potions of greater healing and treasure upon separation. Current location unknown; long-term goal remains saving Phylo.

**Ember Vanguard (Player Party)**
Departed Blingdenstone. Legends of the group spreading through Underdark tunnels with significantly exaggerated accounts of their feats. Formally recognized in Blingdenstone as an allied force (Daz holds general's recognition). Currently traveling toward the surface via an unestablished post-Blingdenstone route.

**Gray Ghosts**
Severely disrupted: Plinki dead, Narrak dead, egg delivered to Gartokkar, five Gray Ghost Derro killed in an alley ambush. Leader Uskvil remains at large. The Gray Ghosts believe the party stole the dragon egg and are likely motivated to pursue retaliation.

**Cult of Demogorgon (Whorlstone Tunnels)**
Destroyed as an operational force: Plinki, Narrak, Gru Manga the Ettin, and most Derro cultists killed by the party. Ritual site abandoned. Residual effects persist — two Stone Giants permanently converted to two-headed form; the ritual cannot be undone per Narrak's dying declaration.

**Keepers of the Flame**
Now hold the corrupted dragon egg as leverage over Themberchaud. Narrak's cult was destroyed (by the party, effectively serving Gartokkar's interests). Gray Ghosts partially disrupted. Gartokkar faces the ongoing risk of the assassination list the party holds.

**Juiblex**
Expelled from Blingdenstone following the Pudding King's defeat. Declared intent to be reborn by consuming Zuggtmoy's domain: *"You haven't won. I will be reborn. I will consume the banquet of the queen of the fungi, and I will grow."*

## Revealed Information

- **Jimjar's true nature:** Possesses immunities and resistances inconsistent with any mortal goblin — immune to poison, psychic damage, charm, and fear; resistant to radiant and non-magical weapon damage. He is aware of Zalthir's scrutiny, chose to reveal himself before vanishing, and explicitly signaled he will return. His identity and allegiance remain unknown but he is clearly not what he appeared.

- **Deepking's assassination list:** The document held by the party (recovered from Eldgrim's desk) is a personal assassination contract bearing the Deepking's royal seal. Named targets include Thangus Ironhead, Werz Saltbaron, Errde Blackskull, Gartokkar Xundorn ("the Wyrmsmith"), and Lingrick Xardelvar (annotated "MY MOST GASEOUS ALLY!!!!!!"), plus dozens of others. This document constitutes evidence of the Deepking's paranoid purge of allies and rivals alike.

- **Juiblex's declared strategy:** Juiblex intends to be reborn specifically by consuming Zuggtmoy's domain, not simply by regenerating elsewhere. This suggests the two demon lords' power bases are on a collision course regardless of party action.

- **Shal's influence:** The Deepking's behavioral transformation is directly linked to Shal's arrival as advisor. Hgraam refused a meeting with Shal and attributes the Deepking's deterioration to Shal specifically. Shal's identity, origin, and purpose remain entirely unknown to any faction or the party.

- **Corrupted dragon egg:** Demonically infused by Narrak's ritual; delivered to Gartokkar. Gartokkar is concealing this from Themberchaud and using it as leverage. The egg's demonic nature may have consequences beyond its use as political insurance.

- **Zuggtmoy's expansion vector:** Jimjar warned that unlike Juiblex, Zuggtmoy moves quietly — *"she prefers to move in while you're sleeping."* The accelerating fungal growth in Blingdenstone confirms this assessment is already operationally accurate.
