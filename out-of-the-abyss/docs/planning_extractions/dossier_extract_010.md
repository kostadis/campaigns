## Shuushar (Shuushar the Awakened)

Shuushar explains to the group what a Kuo-Toan monitor is—a special leader whose primary role is to deal with sudden breaks of insanity, and that defeating the monitor makes the other kuo-toans less likely to keep fighting. After the ambush battle, he sighs, "Such an unnecessary battle. Why would this be happening?"

When prompted by Sethir whether they should continue, Shuushar affirms they should. An hour from Sloobludop, he becomes very excited upon encountering another kuo-toan party and begins speaking to one of them. He introduces the party to Ploopploopeen, archpriest of the Sea Mother.

Shuushar follows Ploopploopeen as she walks around in the surreal school-of-fish formation, not always staying next to her even as she speaks to him. Ploopploopeen casts a "walleyed glance" at Shuushar when mentioning occasional "visionaries" who stirred up trouble, implying he has been considered a troublemaker among his people.

When the group considers killing Ploopploopeen's party, Shuushar is aghast and argues against it, insisting the Sea Mother is an old god and that it is the Deep Father who demands humanoid sacrifices. He suggests the group should talk to Ploopploopeen's daughter Bloppblippodd before taking action, saying, "I have learned that one must open themselves to all points of view and then only then make decisions and judgments." He then drones on and thankfully walks away.

## Ploopploopeen

Ploopploopeen is the archpriest of the Sea Mother and a kuo-toan leader encountered by the party an hour from Sloobludop. One of her companions casts a spell that makes not only the kuo-toan language clear to the party but also the nuances of their communication and body language.

Upon meeting the party, Ploopploopeen declares, "The Seamother has answered my prayers!" She walks around constantly as she talks, with her kuo-toan followers moving around her in a choreographed school-of-fish formation.

She explains that Sloobludop has been split into two factions. Her daughter, Bloppblippodd, experienced a powerful vision of "Leemooggoogoon the Deep Father" a few weeks ago, proclaiming him the new god of her people. Bloppblippodd has backed her claims with a tremendous increase in magical power, attracting many new followers. The followers of the Deep Father have been making increasing blood sacrifices on his altar, casting bloody chum into the Darklake where it is consumed by something unknown.

Ploopploopeen proposes using the party as bait—offering them as a "token of peace and reconciliation" to the Deep Father faction so they can get close to Bloppblippodd. The party would act as infiltrators to disrupt the upstart faction's rituals while Ploopploopeen's Sea Mother faithful strike.

She is offended when Grygum calls her faith a "weird religion," insisting, "The Seamother is not a weird religion. It is the traditional faith of the Kuo-toans! It is Leemooggoogoon the Deep Father that is the real evil!"

She offers 500 gold and two potions of healing as a reward, and when the party seems unimpressed, she sweetens the deal with two boats and guides to navigate the Darklake. When asked about the opposing faction's numbers, she dismisses the concern: "Do not worry yourself about that. All you have to do is distract Bloppblippodd enough. I will take care of her, and my followers will take care of the followers of this false god."

## Bloppblippodd

Bloppblippodd is Ploopploopeen's daughter who, a few weeks ago, experienced a powerful vision of "Leemooggoogoon the Deep Father" and proclaimed him the new god of the kuo-toan people. She has backed up her claims with a tremendous increase in magical power, and new followers have flocked to her cause, splitting Sloobludop into two factions. She serves as the archpriest of the Deep Father faction, whose followers have been making increasing numbers of blood sacrifices—killing humanoid offerings and casting bloody chum into the Darklake, where something unknown consumes it. She does not appear in person but is the target of Ploopploopeen's plan: the party is to be offered to her as sacrifices and then disrupt her rituals so that the Sea Mother faction can strike.

## Sethir

Sethir appears briefly after the kuo-toan ambush, muttering, "Should we continue?" before the group presses on toward Sloobludop.
