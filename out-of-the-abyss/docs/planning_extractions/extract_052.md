## NPC Activity

**Ilvara Mizzrym** — Corrupted high priestess of House Mizzrym and vessel of Zuggtmoy. Grappled and dropped forty feet by Zalthir at the fight's opening, sustaining significant damage. Used a legendary reaction to inflict the Frightened condition on Zalthir, tactically neutralizing him for one turn. Conjured Insect Plague at the cavern entrance, catching multiple combatants and Glabbagool in the swarm. Called down divine fire on Daz and Nym Duskryn. Leapt clear when Thorin collapsed the bridge. Muttered prophecy about Zuggtmoy, "the bride," and coming chaos throughout her final stand. Killed by Grygum's Guiding Bolt; her body detonated into a cloud of poisonous spores rather than leaving a conventional corpse — the demonic corruption venting outward in a toxic bloom that damaged nearby party members.

**Jorlan Duskryn** — Scarred drow warrior, former lover of Ilvara. Had been fighting alongside or near the party when he broke formation upon seeing Ilvara wounded, charging through the active Insect Plague while shouting her name. Described as motivated by an old wound — Ilvara discarded him after he received his scarring, consistent with drow social values punishing physical imperfection. Was struck by a lightning bolt from Nym Duskryn during the chaos and went staggering. Status at end of session: injured but not confirmed dead.

**Nym Duskryn** — Drow operative, sister of Kaelira. Present under contract to extract Daz alive. Physically hauled Daz northeast through the Insect Plague swarm alongside Kaelira. Expressed transactional frustration at Daz's refusal to stand down from combat. Redirected frustration into a request to kill Jorlan Duskryn ("Can I kill your brother?"). Launched a lightning bolt that struck Jorlan. Status: active in the cavern, contract unresolved.

**Kaelira Duskryn** — Drow operative, sister of Nym. Physically seized Daz's arm and hauled him to a protected position alongside Nym, delivering the line "Come with me if you want to live, you idiot." Cooperated with Nym in the extraction attempt. Status: active in the cavern.

**Asha Vandree** — Drow follower of Lolth traveling with the party. Reacted with hostility toward Nym and Kaelira upon their appearance, identifying them as potential traitors to Lolth. Pacified by Daz's cover story that they were Lolth-aligned allies. After Ilvara's death, dropped to her knees and began praying aloud to Lolth, apparently processing the events through a theological framework. Status: alive, praying in the cavern.

**Glabbagool** — Sentient gelatinous cube companion. Caught in Ilvara's Insect Plague and took 26 points of damage. Survived. Was actively attempting to participate in the battle prior to being struck. Grygum's first post-combat concern was checking on him. Status: alive but damaged, present in the cavern.

**House T'sarran Mage** — Unnamed female drow mage, member of the T'sarran reinforcement group. Arrived at the cavern mouth with five drow warriors. Debated charging through the Insect Plague with fellow soldiers; argued she lacked Dispel Magic. Launched a lightning bolt into the cavern that struck nothing. Was targeted by Zalthir via shadow-step and killed in one strike. Before dying, or as she retreated, delivered the threat: "The matron mother of House T'sarran will hear about this." Status: dead.

**House T'sarran Warriors** — Five unnamed drow soldiers accompanying the mage. Entered the fight late. At least some were caught in Daz's Fireball and killed. One surviving warrior delivered the parting line "We will meet again. Enjoy your victory for the moment" before retreating into the tunnels. Status: one confirmed escaped into the tunnels; others destroyed.

**House T'sarran Mage (unnamed)** — See above. Deceased.
