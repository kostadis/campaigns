## Glabbagool

Glabbagool, the curious gelatinous cube, traveled with the party through winding passageways and expressed excitement about the strange sights they encountered. When the party discovered the chamber of animated Drow statues, Glabbagool found the mechanics of the animation particularly entertaining, playing a game of entering and exiting the room to watch the statues animate and return to stillness. He remained a companion of the party throughout their exploration.

## Dawnbringer

Dawnbringer, Thorin's intelligent sword, was involved in a negotiation with Thorin about entering the darkness of the chamber where they fought the Earth Elemental. Thorin promised the weapon a finer scabbard in exchange for its cooperation, and Dawnbringer apparently agreed to the arrangement.
