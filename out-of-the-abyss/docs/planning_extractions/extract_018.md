## NPC Activity

**Hgraam (Stone Giant, Cairngorm Cavern)**
Appeared seated at the entrance to the Cairngorm Cavern tunnel. Offered condolences regarding the slain mad stone giant. Delivered a cryptic prophecy about evil stirring in the Underdark: "A cave with two faces. Rock devoured, and the land overgrown. The pebble believes itself flesh. The earth rejects its wards, and the tunnels shake in fury." Confirmed that the best route to the Dreamlands runs through the deep gnome city. Commented on the Deep King's increasing paranoia and deteriorating relationship with old allies, including Hgraam himself. Identified a figure named Shal as a new advisor to the Deep King whose influence has been corrosive. Noted that Shal has refused to meet with Hgraam. Gifted the party the Stonespeaker Crystal (allows Speak with Animals, Speak with Dead, Speak with Plants, and aids divination) before departing into his cave.

**Droki (Derro Courier)**
Observed in the West Cleft district moving at an unnaturally brisk pace while muttering repetitively about being late. Navigated through Derro streets without apparent concern for being followed. Located a hidden entrance behind canvas and cloth scraps against a chasm wall, revealing a narrow fissure leading into the Whorlstone Tunnels. Entered the tunnels and was tracked by the party. His trail continued northeast through the tunnels, marked by habitual movement patterns and the presence of Pygmywort and Bigwig mushrooms near a crack in the wall. Last seen following his trail east through a fungal thicket.

**Buppido (Derro, deceased)**
Released from captivity at Ghohlbrorn's Lair by Grygum and led the party to the western entrance of Laduguer's Furrow. Revealed knowledge of Droki, confirming Droki's role as a trusted courier for factions operating in the Whorlstone Tunnels. Guided the party to the hidden entrance into the Whorlstone Tunnels. Revealed as the murderer of Pelek upon arrival in his shrine cave. Found to have constructed a shrine of humanoid remains arranged in a spiral pattern. Attacked the party while claiming divine status and demanding worship. Killed by Daz's Magic Missile. Corpse remains in the shrine cave.

**Pelek (Ghost, Svirfneblin merchant)**
Emerged from the ground after Buppido's death. Confirmed Buppido killed him and dismembered his body, incorporating his bones into the shrine. Recounted that he and Buppido were business partners: Pelek supplied goods and Buppido promised to move product out of Gracklstugh without Duergar taxes via the Council of Savants. Reported witnessing the Faerzress behave anomalously months ago—it appeared to be "sucked out," went dark, then returned brighter—immediately after which Buppido's temperament changed and he became convinced of his divinity. Confirmed Droki works for the Council of Savants. Provided the party with practical intelligence about Blingdenstone: advised them to deny any association with Lolth or Menzoberanzan, avoid references to the number eight or spiders, and not carry anything that counts to eight. Requested the party collect his bones (and optionally his severed hand bearing an obsidian family ring) and transport them to Blingdenstone in exchange for his continued assistance. Scouted through the crack in the northern cave wall, identifying the passage as tiny throughout, and spotted Pygmywort and Bigwig mushrooms growing inside. Remains bound within 30 feet of his bones in the shrine cave.

## Faction Movements

**Council of Savants (Derro)**
Confirmed as operators within the Whorlstone Tunnels. Employ Droki as a courier who can navigate safely through Derro territory and the tunnels without detection. Buppido previously worked in association with this faction. Buppido's death removes one operative but does not appear to disrupt Droki's ongoing courier runs. The Council has not delivered on promises of freedom to the broader Derro population, generating resentment among some Derro (per Buppido's outburst).

**Keepers of the Flame / Stone Guard (Duergar factions)**
Badges from either faction are sufficient to suppress scrutiny within Gracklstugh. Both factions previously tasked the party with finding Droki and accessing the Whorlstone Tunnels. Their inability to enter the Derro slums themselves is confirmed as the reason they required outside agents.

**Deep King's Court (Gracklstugh)**
Hgraam confirmed the Deep King has become paranoid and has been pulling away from traditional advisors and allies. The Deep King's reliance on invisible enforcers has pushed opposition underground. The underground opposition has settled in tunnels accessible only through Derro-controlled space, making direct Duergar intervention tantamount to a bloodbath.

## Revealed Information

- The best overland route to the surface from the Underdark runs through Blingdenstone (corroborated by both Hgraam and Pelek, aligning with Jimjar's earlier claims).
- Droki operates as a courier for the Council of Savants and uses the Whorlstone Tunnels as his route, entered via a hidden fissure in the West Cleft chasm wall.
- The Council of Savants has factions operating inside the Whorlstone Tunnels.
- Shal is a named advisor who arrived at the Deep King's court and whose influence correlates directly with the Deep King's paranoia and political deterioration. Hgraam has been denied a meeting with Shal.
- The Faerzress experienced an anomalous fluctuation (drain and resurgence) approximately a few months ago, witnessed by Pelek in the Whorlstone cave area. This event preceded Buppido's violent divine delusion.
- Blingdenstone has cultural sensitivities around Lolth, Menzoberanzan, the number eight, and spiders—all associations the party must conceal.
- Pygmywort mushrooms (reduce effect) and Bigwig mushrooms (enlarge effect) grow in the Whorlstone Tunnels near the crack passage and on the far side of the thermal lake.
- The thermal lake in the Whorlstone Tunnels is approximately 100 feet across with abnormally black, opaque water heated by a thermal spring.
