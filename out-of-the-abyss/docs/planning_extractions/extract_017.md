## Revealed Information

- **The Keepers of the Flame's control structure is inverted:** Themberchaud believes he is in command; in fact, the Keepers manage him. The dragon egg represents their succession plan, which Themberchaud knows nothing about.
- **The dragon egg was stolen by the Gray Ghosts.** This is the source of the Keepers' institutional humiliation and their primary current objective.
- **Droki is a Gray Ghost agent** inside Gracklstugh, sought by both the Stone Guards and the Keepers of the Flame.
- **Themberchaud is physically deteriorated** — overweight, pampered, and likely incapable of surviving independently in the wild. His dependence on the Keepers is total.
- **Themberchaud's first instruction to the party** is to spy on the Keepers from within, meaning the party now holds genuine leverage between two factions that each believe the party serves them.
- **Psychic assassins are confirmed real**, not a government cover story. They use weapons consistent with psychic blades (as described in Milo Goodbarrel's account of the Kenku Whistler). Two victims were Erdde Blackskull's clan members. Werz Saltbaron was a confirmed premeditated target.
- **Milo Goodbarrel's book** describes psychic blade attacks — specifically the Kenku Whistler using them to fake attacks — providing the party's only current reference for identifying this type of weapon.
