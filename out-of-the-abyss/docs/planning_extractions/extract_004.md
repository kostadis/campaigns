## NPC Activity

**Sarith (Drow warrior)**
- Armed himself with studded leather armor, shield, and a hand crossbow from fallen Drow bodies
- Appeared to experience bouts of madness
- Warned that Ilvara would be honor-bound to pursue the escaped prisoners
- Revealed knowledge of the surrounding Underdark geography, identifying Menzoberranzan to the north and Blingdenstone to the south
- Proposed traveling via the Darklake to reach Gracklstugh, citing prior experience as a member of the warrior caste

**Topsy and Turvy (deep gnome twins)**
- Offered to retrieve the party's confiscated gear from Ilvara's residence in exchange for a price
- When the offer was declined, transformed into rats (revealing shapeshifting ability)
- Transformed back into gnomes and headed toward the Darklake tunnel independently, effectively leading the group's direction of escape

**Buppido (derro)**
- Warned the group about large spiders in the webbing below the tower
- Declared that his "divine plan" guarantees he will reach Gracklstugh
- Made a formal wager with Jimjar on that outcome
- Laughed at and questioned Thorin while under the effect of the Tongue of Madness mushroom
- Trapped under rocks in the cavern collapse

**Jimjar (deep gnome)**
- Disrupted the group's sense of safety by proposing a morbid bet on how many escapees would survive
- Accepted Buppido's counter-wager regarding reaching Gracklstugh
- Was the one to spot the Tongue of Madness fungus
- Trapped under rocks in the cavern collapse

**Shuushar (kuo-toa)**
- Confirmed that the kuo-toa settlement of Sloobludop ("Sloopdopblop") is approximately eight days' travel away via the Darklake
- Described the Tongue of Madness mushroom as allowing the eater to "connect with the gods"
- Later clarified its actual effect: it compels the eater to speak truth
- Trapped under rocks in the cavern collapse

**Sarith** *(additional Darklake exposition)*
- Provided detailed description of the Darklake: a network stretching over a hundred miles, with duergar-engineered locks and passages, connecting to Gracklstugh, Blingdenstone, and other destinations

**Stool (myconid sprout)**
- Woke curled against Grygum, expressing fear, loneliness, and attachment
- Cited Grygum's previous speech as the reason for his trust
- Extended his hand to Grygum and was accepted as a companion
- Marched hand-in-hand with Grygum through the Underdark tunnels

**Ront (orc)**
- Remained behind to fight an injured vrock, motivated by a desire to redeem himself for prior cowardice
- Landed one blow that drew blood from the vrock
- Was killed by the vrock in a single strike; his body was seized, his head bitten off and swallowed, and his headless corpse was dropped on the cavern floor
- **Status: Deceased**

**Prince Derendil**
- Corpse present at the scene; described as a large body serving as a "silent monument"
- His body was among those looted for weapons and equipment
- **Status: Deceased, corpse looted**

**Ilvara (Drow Mistress of Velkenyvelve)**
- Not present but discussed; Sarith warned she would be honor-bound to pursue the escaped prisoners
- Her residence (past the rope bridge) was identified as the location of the party's confiscated gear but was not raided
