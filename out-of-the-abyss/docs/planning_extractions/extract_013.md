## NPC Activity

**Demogorgon**
Roared upon manifesting, inspiring ecstasy in Leemoogoogoon's followers and terror/madness in followers of the Deep Sea Mother. Advanced toward Sloopbludop and destroyed the town entirely, with the screams of Kuotoans echoing across the Darklake as the party escaped by boat.

**Shuushar the Awakened**
Collapsed wailing at Demogorgon's appearance and had to be carried to the boat by Spiderbait. Recovered during the escape and reflected that Kuo-Toan collective imagination had brought Leemoogoogoon/Demogorgon into existence, arguing his people must change. Acted as navigator and guide for several days, identifying food and water sources. Upon encountering a boatload of Kuo-Toan survivors from Sloopbludop who were receptive to his message of reform, declared he had found his calling and departed the party, leaving his navigational instructions with Sarith. Gave a tearful farewell to the group.

**Buppido**
No longer gagged. Advocated for traveling to Gracklstugh, claiming to know people there who could help the party acquire better equipment and assistance. Accepted Jimjar's skeptical challenge with a sigh.

**Hemeth**
Present on the boat. Suggested taking the Kuo-Toan boat by force, referencing a prior encounter with Kuo-Toans in which he nearly became a sacrifice while attempting to sell them weapons. Was overruled by Shuushar and Grygum before he could act.

**Eldeth**
Collapsed wailing at Demogorgon's appearance and had to be carried by Thorin. Recovered and identified the creature as Demogorgon, expressing that his presence on the Prime Material Plane should be impossible. Asserted that the danger extends to all of Faerûn and specifically to dwarven miners on the surface, urging the party to send a warning upward.

**Jimjar**
Carried Daz during the escape from Sloopbludop. Responded to Buppido's offer with a sardonic "Wanna bet?"

**Topsy and Turvy**
Collapsed wailing at Demogorgon's appearance and were carried by Grygum during the escape.

**Sarith**
Present on the boat throughout the journey. After Shuushar's departure, admitted uncertainty about his ability to correctly follow Shuushar's navigational instructions, now holding them on behalf of the group.

**Kuo-Toan Monitors (Sloopbludop dock)**
Were standing guard at the dock when the party arrived. Accepted Grygum's claim that the Archpriest of the Sea Mother commanded them to help the community, appeared relieved to have a purpose, and rushed off — allowing the party to take the boat uncontested.

**Kuo-Toan Survivors (Darklake boat)**
Encountered on the 8th day of the 3rd Tenday. Survivors from Sloopbludop who had fled the destruction. Receptive to Shuushar's reformist message about ending the Kuo-Toan compulsion to create gods. Departed with Shuushar to begin building what he called "a better Kuo-Toan world."

## Faction Movements

**Followers of Leemoogoogoon (Demogorgon cultists among Kuo-Toans)**
Cheered in ecstasy at Demogorgon's manifestation. Their ritual appears to have achieved its purpose: Demogorgon is now physically present on the Prime Material Plane and destroyed Sloopbludop.

**Followers of the Deep Sea Mother (Sloopbludop Kuo-Toans)**
Devastated. The faction was either driven mad, killed, or scattered by Demogorgon's arrival. Sloopbludop was entirely destroyed. A small group of survivors escaped by boat and encountered the party on the Darklake.

**Shuushar's Reform Movement (nascent)**
Founded on the 8th day of the 3rd Tenday when Shuushar departed with sympathetic Kuo-Toan survivors from Sloopbludop. Mission: reform Kuo-Toan society away from the compulsive god-creation cycle. Currently operating on the Darklake with an unknown number of followers.

## Threat Arc Events

**Demonic Incursion / Demon Lords on the Prime Material Plane**
- *Triggering event:* Demogorgon physically destroyed Sloopbludop on the Darklake, confirming active presence of a demon lord on the Prime Material Plane. The party witnessed this directly.
- *Triggering event:* Demonic manta ray-like creatures were encountered in the Darklake waters (6th day, 3rd Tenday), suggesting demonic corruption of local fauna is spreading outward from the incursion point.
- *Party inference:* Grygum connected the spectator's earlier warning about demons, the demons at Velkynvelve, and Demogorgon's appearance into a unified conclusion — demons have reached the Prime Material Plane in large numbers and someone deliberately summoned them.

## Revealed Information

- **Demogorgon is physically present on the Prime Material Plane** in the Underdark, confirmed by direct observation. Eldeth states this should be impossible by known rules of planar travel.
- **Demons do not appear spontaneously in the Underdark** — they must be summoned. Daz assessed that summoning a demon lord would require extraordinarily powerful magic, implying a deliberate and capable actor is responsible.
- **The demonic incursion is not contained to the Underdark.** Eldeth warns the demons will not remain below ground, posing a threat to all of Faerûn including surface settlements.
- **Kuo-Toan collective belief may function as a literal summoning mechanism.** Shuushar stated that Kuo-Toan imagination brought Leemoogoogoon into existence, suggesting the faction's god-creation compulsion was the vector for Demogorgon's manifestation.
- **Hemeth was nearly sacrificed by Kuo-Toans** in a prior encounter while attempting to sell them weapons, establishing his personal hostility toward the race.
- **Buppido has contacts in Gracklstugh** who he claims can provide equipment and assistance to the party.
