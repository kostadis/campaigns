## Ploopploopeen

Ploopploopeen is the archpriest of the Sea Mother in the kuo-toa village of Sloobludop. He stares unblinkingly at the escapees when presenting his plan, then clarifies his intentions when the Tongues spell fails to convey his meaning properly. His plan is to use the escapees as a false offering to lure his daughter, Bloppblippodd, into a trap: the escapees will be presented to her as sacrifices for Leemoogoogon, Ploopploopeen will insist on accompanying them, and then his followers will attack. He explains that an unwilling sacrifice is better for the evil god Leemoogoogon, and that the escapees' role will essentially be over once the ambush begins. He mentions he previously tried a "white flag" approach with his daughter, but she refused.

He is delighted when Grygum and Thorin spit on the altar of the Sea Mother as an offering. He assures Thorin that kuo-toa eat cooked fish, not raw rotting food. After eating, he falls asleep and walks outside standing up, as is kuo-toan custom.

His relationship with his daughter Bloppblippodd is deeply strained — she leads a rival faction worshipping the Deep Father. His son Glooglugogg believes Ploopploopeen showed Bloppblippodd too much deference and let her get away with too much, contributing to the current crisis.

On the morning of the 2nd day, Ploopploopeen admits a kuo-toa monitor and informs him that he, his whip, and the prisoners will accompany the monitor's group to see the archpriest of the Deep Father.

## Bloppblippodd

Bloppblippodd is the daughter of Ploopploopeen and the high priestess (archpriest) of the Deep Father, Leemoogoogon. She leads a rival faction in Sloobludop that worships Leemoogoogon, a false and evil god. According to her father's plan, she will demand to see the offering of prisoners in the morning, at which point Ploopploopeen's followers will ambush her faction. Her brother Glooglugogg holds her in deep contempt, describing her as having no respect for kuo-toan ways and as having fallen prey to madness. Glooglugogg also blames their father for being too deferential toward her. She previously refused a diplomatic overture (a "white flag") from Ploopploopeen. Her hardcore supporters are few, but the broader allegiance of the village population is uncertain. She has not yet appeared in person.

## Glooglugogg

Glooglugogg (who goes by "Gloog" among friends) is the son of Ploopploopeen and brother of Bloppblippodd. He appears in Ploopploopeen's quarters while the escapees are resting, initially startling them as they are unsure if he is awake or asleep. He states that he does not approve of his father's choice to involve outsiders but acknowledges that the party showed respect to the Sea Mother. He harbors deep resentment toward his sister Bloppblippodd, enumerating her failings as a sister, daughter, and former cleric of the Sea Mother, and declaring that she has fallen prey to madness and torn their society apart. He blames his father for showing her too much deference.

He clarifies to Zalthir that the party will be expected to help in the fight, though they will not throw the first punch — that would insult the honor of his father and people. He notes that the hard-core supporters of the Deep Father are few, but the broader village allegiances are unknown, which is why the situation must be dealt with now while the Sea Mother faction still has majority support. Before sleeping, he remarks that it was both honorable and odd that the party didn't simply offer up the tied-up derro as a sacrifice instead.

## Shuushar

Shuushar is mentioned only in passing as having previously explained the role of kuo-toan leader types who walk along the docks, responsible for ensuring that the madness of the kuo-toa doesn't tear their society apart. His current whereabouts and status are not specified in this passage.
