## Demogorgon

Demogorgon roared upon appearing at the village of Sloopbludop, causing terror and madness among the followers of the Deep Sea Mother while the followers of Leemoogoogoon cheered. The demon lord advanced toward the village and ultimately destroyed the town of Sloopbludop entirely—the screams of the Kuo-Toans echoed in the darkness as the party fled by boat. His presence in the prime material plane was noted as seemingly impossible by Eldeth. The party concluded that someone must have summoned him (and other demon lords) using extraordinarily powerful magic. His appearance served as the catalyst for the party's urgent flight across the Darklake and their growing realization that demons have invaded the Underdark in large numbers.

## Shuushar

Shuushar screamed and wailed at the appearance of Demogorgon but eventually recovered while the party was on the boat. He reflected on the event philosophically, stating, "Enlightenment is necessary! We must change as a people. Our imagination brought into existence, Leemoogoogoon." During the journey across the Darklake, he served as the group's guide and navigator, pointing out where they could find water and food. On the 8th day of the 3rd Tenday of Taraskh, the party encountered another boat of Kuo-Toans who had fled Sloopbludop. Shuushar spoke with them and discovered they were receptive to his message of building a better Kuo-Toan world free from the madness of creating new gods. He announced he had found his calling and chose to leave the party, departing with the other Kuo-Toans. His departure left the group without their primary navigator; Sarith admitted he had Shuushar's instructions but was unsure he could follow them correctly.

## Eldeth

Eldeth screamed and wailed at Demogorgon's appearance and had to be carried by Thorin to the boat. Once recovered, she identified the creature as Demogorgon and expressed disbelief that he could be on the prime material plane. She agreed that the danger to all of Faerun was undeniable, warning that the demons wouldn't stay in the Underdark and that dwarven miners were at risk. She urged the group to get a warning to the surface worlds.

## Zalthir

Zalthir muttered "We need to get out of here" when Demogorgon appeared and was about to flee before noticing that several companions were incapacitated and needed to be carried. During the Darklake journey, he was uncomfortable in the low-ceilinged cave sections due to his height. On the 8th day, when the party spotted a Kuo-Toan boat ahead, Zalthir prepared to attack in case negotiations failed. He was annoyed at having been caught up in the religious ritual that may have summoned Demogorgon. He wanted to object to Shuushar's departure, recognizing that the Kuo-Toan's navigational knowledge was critical to their survival, but Thorin encouraged Shuushar to leave before Zalthir could speak.

## Buppido

Buppido, no longer gagged, advocated for the group to travel to Gracklstugh, claiming he knew people there who could help. When the group discussed needing better equipment, Buppido reiterated that he knew people who could assist them in Gracklstugh.

## Jimjar

Jimjar picked up and carried Daz when the latter collapsed at the sight of Demogorgon. When Buppido claimed to know people in Gracklstugh, Jimjar quipped skeptically, "Wanna bet?"

## Hemeth

Hemeth suggested the party take the Kuo-Toan boat they spotted, recounting a past bad experience: "Like the last time I talked to Kuo-Toans, I almost got sacrificed. I was just trying to sell them weapons. I didn't think they would think sacrificing me was a weapon. Crazy fish people."
