## NPC Activity

**Pudding King**
- Transformed from humanoid form into a sentient, telepathic ooze upon the party's entry to the throne room, declaring his complete embrace of Juiblex and his intent to "reduce all of the deep known kind into nothing but ooze."
- Leveraged throne room lair actions, including dropping green slime from the ceiling and unleashing slime wave attacks that forced dexterity saves and dealt acid damage.
- Swapped positions with Glabbagool during the battle (mechanism unclear), briefly causing party confusion about target identity.
- Was grappled by Zalthir and forcibly dragged out of the throne room, neutralizing his lair actions.
- Unleashed a second slime wave during the coordinated assault phase, affecting Thorin, Daz, and Grygum.
- Dissolved and died under Zalthir's final assault, delivering a final telepathic declaration: *"Behold the true feast, the soul's final blend!"*

**Prince Livid**
- Retaliated against Zalthir with a psychic blast early in combat, dealing 14 points (reduced to 7 on successful save).
- Charged toward the party during the coordinated assault phase.
- Demonstrated fire resistance, surviving Daz's Fireball with only 8 hit points remaining.
- Used a psychic attack on Daz during the final phase, dealing 5 points of damage.
- Killed by Daz's Magic Missile volley.

**Princess Ebonmire**
- Threw a blob at Thorin during initial combat exchanges (missed).
- Charged at Thorin during the final phase.
- Was killed by the party's combined efforts (Grygum's Inflict Wounds, Zalthir's grapple and strikes, Thorin's killing blow).
- Found to have a spellbook in her stomach, recovered post-battle.

**Glabbagool**
- Was present as a gelatinous cube companion during the battle.
- Had both Zalthir and the Pudding King pass through his body during Zalthir's tactical drag maneuver; remarked "this tickles."
- Somehow swapped positions or apparent identity with the Pudding King mid-battle, causing the party to briefly attack him before a verbal confirmation revealed the error.
- After the Pudding King's defeat, returned to his gelatinous cube form and expressed confusion, then enthusiasm about his situation.
- Proposed being carried in a jar or Bag of Holding to travel with the party as a more mobile companion and "garbage disposal."
- Expressed interest in monk training to gain control over his form and acidity.

**Juiblex**
- Channeled through the Pudding King throughout the battle; his infernal influence receded from the area following the Pudding King's death.
- A demonic voice (attributed possibly to Juiblex) echoed through the chamber after the battle: *"You haven't won. I will be reborn. I will consume the banquet of the queen of the fungi, and I will grow."*

**Zuggtmoy**
- Did not appear directly but her influence was observed: fungi throughout the cavern became more twisted and malevolent immediately after Juiblex's power receded, indicating she was actively moving to fill the power vacuum left by the Pudding King's defeat.

## Faction Movements

**Juiblex's Forces (Ooze Cult / Pudding King's Court)**
- Suffered total collapse: the Pudding King (leader), Prince Livid, and Princess Ebonmire all destroyed in a single engagement.
- Juiblex's demonic influence over the throne room area dissipated following the Pudding King's death.
- Juiblex declared intent to reconstitute by consuming Zuggtmoy's domain ("the banquet of the queen of the fungi"), suggesting a future cross-faction demonic conflict.

**Zuggtmoy's Forces (Fungi / Rot)**
- Immediately began opportunistic expansion into territory vacated by Juiblex's defeat; fungi in the cavern visibly twisted and grew more malevolent in the battle's aftermath.
- No direct operatives present, but territorial gain was passive and swift.

## Revealed Information

- The Pudding King had fully shed his humanoid identity and consciousness, becoming a pure manifestation of Juiblex; his transformation was irreversible and complete.
- The Pudding King possessed lair actions tied specifically to his throne room, which were neutralized when Zalthir dragged him outside — confirming that his power was location-dependent.
- Princess Ebonmire had consumed a spellbook at some point prior to the battle; it survived intact within her body.
- Juiblex, even after the destruction of his primary mortal vessel, retains enough presence or will to issue threats and declare a plan: consuming Zuggtmoy's "banquet" to reconstitute himself.
- Zuggtmoy has been passively monitoring or contesting this territory and moved immediately upon Juiblex's defeat, suggesting she has been waiting for this opportunity.
- Glabbagool's swap with the Pudding King occurred when Zalthir dragged the Pudding King through Glabbagool's body — the exact mechanism was left ambiguous, but proximity and passage through the gelatinous cube's form appears to have been the trigger.
