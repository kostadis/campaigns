- Deepking's agents are actively deployed throughout the city searching for the Ember Vanguard.
- The faction is on high alert; the Stone Guard are described as afraid to face the Ember Vanguard head-on, with street rumor elevating the group's reputation significantly.

**The Grey Ghosts (Derro faction)**
- Lost several members of the Derro High Council (killed by the party, attributed to the Ember Vanguard).
- Issued a written vendetta order to remaining operatives to stalk and destroy the Ember Vanguard and recover the dragon egg, which they claim belongs to the Grey Ghosts.
- Dispatched an ambush cell of at least five Derro; all five were killed.
- Faction is actively pursuing the party with orders emphasizing psychological warfare (paranoia, invisibility, illusion), shadow strikes, and egg recovery.

**The Derro (general population)**
- Described as "on edge" with a sense of pre-revolutionary violence building in the slums.
- The death of High Council members has increased the likelihood of civil war between the Derro and the Duergar.

**Keepers of the Flame**
