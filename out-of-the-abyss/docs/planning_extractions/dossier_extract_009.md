## Jimjar

Jimjar quieted down the upset Spiderbait and reassured him that he was more likely to survive if he stayed with the group. Daz noted that Jimjar "makes bets all the time" when summarizing what the party knew about each NPC.

## Shuushar

Shuushar whispered to Zalthir that Yukyuk had been murdered and Spiderbait was very upset. Spiderbait accused Shuushar of being the killer, claiming that Kuo-toans go mad and then snap and kill people. Daz noted that Shuushar "claims to have figured out the cure to Kuo-toan insanity." Shuushar proposed the group head to Sloopbludop to meet the arch-cleric of the Seamother, who could cast Zone of Truth to determine who killed Yukyuk. When Daz suggested cutting Buppido loose after determining he was mad, Shuushar insisted they tie him up instead, offering to try his "techniques of mental sanity" on the derro. He then walked alongside the gagged Buppido, talking endlessly about a technique he called "Kuo-toan-fulness" — creating a space between an impulse and an action to question whether the impulse is a reaction to reality or just a response in one's mind.

## Spiderbait

Spiderbait screamed at Jimjar demanding money and declaring he was leaving. After being calmed down by Jimjar, he was questioned by Daz about Yukyuk's murder. He described Yukyuk as "the kindest, gentlest goblin you could imagine" and said they were pals, and that Yukyuk had nothing of value. He accused Shuushar of being the murderer, claiming Kuo-toans go mad and kill people.

## Buppido

Buppido was identified as Yukyuk's killer. When questioned by Daz, he gave evasive answers, saying Yukyuk's death was "all part of the plan" and "necessary for the divine plan," and that he "cannot be held accountable for the divine plan." Daz found blood on Buppido's shortsword; Buppido claimed it was there because he wasn't a maid. After Daz cast Mind Sliver on him twice for information, Buppido continued to deflect, claiming "the agent of the divine plan" was responsible. Daz concluded that Buppido was genuinely mad rather than simply having a different perspective. The party gagged and tied him up. He was forced to walk alongside Shuushar, who subjected him to endless lectures on "Kuo-toan-fulness." Buppido pleaded for a mercy killing, though only muffled moans could be heard through the gag.

## Sarith

Sarith approached Daz during Buppido's interrogation and complimented the Mind Sliver technique as "excellent torturing." He offered to employ more effective methods he had learned as a member of Lolth's guard, including removing eyes, nails, and other body parts.
