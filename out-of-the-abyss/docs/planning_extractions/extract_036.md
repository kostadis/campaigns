**Jimjar**
- Announced the party was one day from Blingdenstone upon reaching the Dark Lake shore; immediately made and won a bet about the party taking a rest.
- Explained Blingdenstone's history of changing hands between gnomes and drow multiple times, noting the city is currently under gnome control but experiencing difficulties.

- Reported observing an unusual increase in agitated oozes in the surrounding area, which it described as an "oozapalooza."
