## Chief Dorbo

Chief Dorbo is the leader of the mining coalition in Blingdenstone. He was present in Diggermattock Hall where he engaged in a heated discussion with Senni about the future of the settlement. He explained that ooze infestations had been hindering both mining operations and housing construction. After Senni confronted him about his failure to adequately address the ooze problem, he agreed that the party would need to deal with the ooze threat before he could help them with their own concerns. He also revealed his suspicions about the Gold Whisker clan, believing them to be wererats, and refused to cooperate with them—a stance that put him at odds with Senni. Last seen in Diggermattock Hall.

## Senni

Senni represents the non-mining population of Blingdenstone. She confronted Chief Dorbo in Diggermattock Hall about his failure to adequately address the ooze infestation problem. While she clashed with Dorbo on the ooze issue, she also appeared more open than him to reconciliation with the Gold Whisker clan, whom Dorbo suspects of being wererats. Last seen in Diggermattock Hall.

## Pelek

Pelek was a ghost who had been traveling with the party after being killed by Buppido. His bones had been carried by Grygum in hopes of finding a proper resting place. At the catacombs beneath the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough in Blingdenstone, Grygum interred his bones, performing a dragon-themed ritual and planting a gold coin as a symbolic foundation. Pelek expressed profound relief and gratitude, having feared he would be lost forever tormenting people for eternity. After saying goodbye to the party, his spirit faded away peacefully, leaving a warm, comforting feeling. He materialized earlier before Glyphic Shroomlight to convince the reluctant priest to allow the interment. Pelek has now been laid to rest.

## Glyphic Shroomlight

Glyphic Shroomlight is a priest at the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough, dedicated to Segojan Earthcaller, the Svirfneblin god of deep earthen nature. He was initially hesitant to inter the bones of someone he didn't know, but when Pelek's ghost materialized before him, he quickly reconsidered—not wishing to be haunted for the rest of his days. He directed the party to the catacombs below and asked that Glabbagool, the party's intelligent gelatinous cube, remain upstairs to avoid accidents. Last seen at the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough.

## Buppido

Buppido is identified as the one who killed Pelek. He does not appear directly in this session but is referenced as the reason Pelek became a ghost whose bones needed to be interred.

## Jadgar

Borough Warden Jadgar is the ghost of a three-foot-tall Svirfneblin who once defended Blingdenstone against the Drow and countless other threats. He appeared in the catacombs beneath the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough immediately after Pelek's spirit departed. He explained that he is working to re-establish the Borough Wardens and is secretly training young gnomes for this purpose. He offered the party a deal: for every ghost they helped put to rest, he would answer one question about the Underdark and its dangers. When asked about the ooze problem, he explained that oozes are typically solitary creatures, making a mass infestation highly unusual. When Zalthir mentioned a possible connection to Juiblex, Jadgar offered a further clarification in exchange for Zalthir demonstrating a monk technique he could teach his trainees. After Zalthir performed an impressive martial display, Jadgar explained the difference between devils and demons—devils corrupt individuals through personal deals while demons empower proxies to cause widespread carnage. He advised the party to search for an intermediate agent that a demon had likely empowered to motivate the oozes. He also recommended they speak with a young gnome he is mentoring who will lead the new Borough Wardens, once the party has gathered more information. Last seen in the catacombs beneath the temple.

## Glabbagool

Glabbagool is the party's intelligent gelatinous cube companion. He was asked to remain upstairs at the Temple of the Ruby in the Rough to avoid accidents in the catacombs. At the Trader's Grotto, gnomes eyed him with suspicion, though he tried to explain he was not a threat. When non-sentient gelatinous cubes attacked the market, Glabbagool protested that these mindless creatures were giving intelligent gelatinous cubes a bad name. He advised the party to avoid being engulfed, to use acid protection if available, and to pull out anyone trapped as quickly as possible. During the fight, he asked Zalthir in wonder if he was an ooze after seeing his eldritch claw tattoo sprout tentacles, and seemed satisfied when Zalthir called himself "an aspiring ooze." Last seen at the Trader's Grotto.
