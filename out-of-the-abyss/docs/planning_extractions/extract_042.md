## NPC Activity

**Glabbagool (Gelatinous Cube)**
- Accompanied the party through winding passageways as a companion.
- Expressed excitement and curiosity at strange sights encountered during exploration.
- Played a game of repeatedly entering and exiting the animated statue chamber, finding the statues' responses entertaining.
- Demonstrated ongoing awareness and personality consistent with prior sessions.

**The Medusa (unnamed, Drow)**
- Located in a chamber decorated in Drow city style, featuring spider-themed tapestries and fine furniture; seated on a throne at the chamber's center.
- Described as female Drow with white hair writhing with pale serpents.
- Has been imprisoned in this location for an extended period, during which she has gone mad.
- Upon sensing the party's presence, immediately opened her eyes and charged forward.
- Attempted to use her petrifying gaze on Zalthir; this was countered by the ice mirror.
- Upon seeing her own reflection, screamed in horror and lamented her disheveled appearance.
- Resisted being petrified by her own reflected gaze.
- Retaliated with claw attacks and venomous serpent-hair bites, successfully poisoning Zalthir.
- Knocked prone by Thorin's dart throw at end of session; combat ongoing.

**The Malevolent Entity (unnamed)**
- Responsible for the curse corrupting the entire area, causing any elemental summoned within it to go mad and attack indiscriminately.
- Animating the Drow warrior statues in the chamber; can control a maximum of six animated statues simultaneously.
- Demonstrated awareness of intruders: animated statues gave chase to Daz's bat familiar when it entered the chamber.
- Assessed by the party as likely impervious to normal weapons and requiring specific weapons or magic to defeat.
- Physical location and nature not yet determined; party is gathering information before direct confrontation.

**Dawnbringer (Intelligent Sword)**
- Thorin's intelligent weapon; expressed reluctance to enter the darkened chamber.
- Negotiated with Thorin; agreed to cooperate in exchange for a promise of a finer scabbard.
