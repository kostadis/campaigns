## Sarith (also referred to as "Sathir" in the text)

Sarith survived the rockfall at the beginning of the session — Zalthir noted he was not under the rocks. Buppido shouted that Sarith was supposed to rescue him according to "the divine plan." During the fight with the giant centipede-like creature, Sarith fired hand-held crossbow bolts at it alongside Buppido, Topsy, and Turvy, and ultimately helped kill the creature with his bolts. After the beast was slain, Sarith shouted "Rations!" suggesting they harvest the creature for food.

Later, when the group realized they were being pursued by drow scouts from Velkynvelve, Sarith provided critical guidance. He explained the nature of Underdark tunnel systems — that they twist, turn, and constantly change, meaning an enemy can appear far away and then suddenly be in front of you. He compared it to a maze. He warned the group that "Overbriters that cannot fly are the easiest prey in the Underdark" and emphasized the importance of keeping moving and hoping no tunnel gives the drow a shortcut.

At the cave with Faerzess, Sarith was navigating and searching for a path to the next tunnel. When Grygum spotted the route first, Sarith was visibly annoyed, saying "I saw that" and "I didn't need your help. But it's good that you can help," before rushing down the tunnel Grygum found.

At the end of the day, Sarith assessed their progress positively — they had put distance between themselves and the drow — but warned they had no water and that things would get unpleasant if they didn't find some. He ordered Eldeth to cover their trail the next day and said they must go slowly.

He also revealed that Ilvara has found them and caught up with them, urging the group to either put distance between themselves and the drow or have someone cover their trail.

## Buppido

Buppido shouted to Sarith during the rockfall aftermath, declaring "The divine plan said you would rescue me, Sarith!" During the fight with the giant centipede creature, Buppido fired crossbow bolts alongside Sarith and the twins, and was one of the two (with Sarith) who ultimately killed the creature with their bolts.

## Stool

Stool was relieved to see that Grygum was alive after the rockfall. Grygum told the frightened Myconid child to go help Shuushar. Because Stool moves very slowly, Thorin told him to get on his back during the flight from the drow scouts. After jumping on Thorin's back, Stool released his rapport spores (creating a mind link) and then asked everyone, "Was that a good idea? I think it was a good idea. I'm really glad you liked my good idea!" — speaking before anyone could respond. The rapport spores proved crucial, as Zalthir used the mind link to communicate the hiding spot he found behind stalagmites.

## Nym

Nym is identified as one of the drow soldiers from Velkynvelve. Zalthir recognized his particular gait among the pursuing drow scouts. When Zalthir fell from his hiding place and made noise, Nym drew his weapon and looked around. However, when he felt a tremor (actually Grygum's Thaumaturgy), Nym became worried the cave would collapse. Seeing no evidence of the prisoners, he turned to leave, muttering "I told you it was the other tunnel" — unaware that the tremor was magical in origin. He is currently still pursuing the party as part of the drow scout force.

## Ilvara

Ilvara was mentioned by Sarith as having found the escaped prisoners and caught up with them. She is commanding the drow pursuit from Velkynvelve. She does not appear directly but her proximity is the driving threat of the session.

## Shuushar (also "Suushar")

Grygum told Stool to go help Shuushar after the rockfall. Later, while running through a cave with Faerzess, Shuushar seemed uncomfortable. After passing through, he muttered, "That was odd. It was the first time I had felt so strange. The Faerzess was just a light, but now it seems to be a poison." This suggests a change in the nature of Faerzess that is affecting him in new, unsettling ways.

## Jimjar (also "Jim Jar")

After the group began running from the drow, Jimjar complained that he had been planning to bet that he would find food and the others wouldn't, but Zalthir's insistence on running meant nobody would find food. He asked, "Wanna bet?" Later, when Shuushar expressed discomfort about the Faerzess, Jimjar dismissed his concerns, saying that's what happens with Kuo-Toans "who think they aren't mad" — calling them insane but noting their madness seems reasonable. He stated that Faerzess has never affected a living thing and that its only magical power is to prevent teleportation spells.

## Eldeth

Eldeth was trapped during the rockfall — Thorin shouted to the twins to save her and Jimjar. She later volunteered her skills, whispering that she "was taught to cover our tracks in the Underdark." At the end of the day, Sarith assigned her the task of hiding the group's trail the following day.

## Topsy

Topsy participated in the crossbow volley against the giant centipede creature alongside Sarith, Buppido, and Turvy. Thorin shouted to the twins to save Eldeth and Jimjar during the encounter.

## Turvy

Turvy participated in the crossbow volley against the giant centipede creature alongside Sarith, Buppido, and Topsy. Thorin shouted to the twins to save Eldeth and Jimjar during the encounter.

## Korrh Erann

Korrh Erann is a historical figure — a monk of Zalthir's monastic order who was once trapped in the Underdark and pursued by drow. He had been part of a group where some members wanted to kill the drow scouts to slow them down. Korrh insisted this was a dangerous gamble and chose to run instead. He was the only survivor of the group. Zalthir remembered this story and used it to justify his decision to flee rather than fight the pursuing drow scouts.
