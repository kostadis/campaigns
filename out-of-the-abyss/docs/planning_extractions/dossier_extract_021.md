## Flumph

Flumph shared an image of the egg with the party earlier. When invited to join the group entering the assassin's redoubt, Flumph declined, saying "I want to observe a tragedy, not be in one. I will stay here." Flumph later accompanied the group (along with Jimjar, Sarith, Stool, Elbeth, and Spiderbait) as they sneaked past the Derro caves under Zalthir's Darkness spell.

## Jimjar

Jimjar attempted to follow Rust into the assassin's chambers but was stopped by Rust, who only permitted Daz, Thorin, Zalthir, and Grygum to enter. He waited outside with the other companions and later moved with the group past the Derro caves.

## Sarith

Sarith was among the armed companions (equipped with Errde's weapons) following the party through Gracklstugh. He was ordered to wait outside the assassin's lair by Rust and later accompanied the group as they sneaked past the Derro caves.

## Stool

Stool served as the telepathic communication link for the group, allowing Daz to whisper his plan about "distributing information to all the power brokers of Gracklstugh simultaneously" to the others. Stool was ordered to wait outside the assassin's lair and later traveled with the group.

## Elbeth

Elbeth was among the armed companions traveling through the Derro slums. She was ordered to stay outside the assassin's lair by Rust and continued with the group afterward.

## Spiderbait

Spiderbait was among the armed companions following the party. Ordered to wait outside the assassin's chambers by Rust, Spiderbait later traveled with the group past the Derro caves.

## Errde

Errde provided weapons to the party's companions (Flumph, Jimjar, Sarith, Stool, Elbeth, and Spiderbait). Zalthir recalled that Errde craved proof that the assassins killed her family, which factored into the party's negotiations with the Empty Scabbard guild.

## Werz Saltbaron

Werz is the reason the party engaged with the assassins — they needed to get a contract on him cancelled. Eldgrim confirmed there was a hit on Werz, calling him "small potatoes," and offered to cancel the contract and permanently brightlist him for 20 gold, later agreeing to do it for free if the party eliminates the Demogorgon cult. The stolen contract from Eldgrim's desk also references Werz directly: "WERZ SALTBARON THAT SNIVELING SQUEALING WRITHING LITTLE RAT! BIND HIS FEET AND THROW HIM OFF HIS BELOVED DOCKS!" — written under the personal seal of the Deepking.

## Rust

Rust is a Duergar assassin and member of the Empty Scabbard guild who materialized at the intersection near the assassin's lair along with Grimgrim. He introduced himself with an unpronounceable Derro name and offered "Rust" as an alternative. He was pleased to learn the party were enemies of Demogorgon. He explained they were the "empty scabbard killers" and then went to confer with his leadership about the party's proposal to cooperate against the Demogorgon cult. He returned and led the four party members (Daz, Thorin, Zalthir, Grygum) to Eldgrim, while ordering the rest to wait outside. Thorin distracted Rust and Grimgrim by asking about the empty scabbard moniker while Daz stole the contract.

## Grimgrim

Grimgrim is a Duergar assassin who materialized alongside Rust at the intersection near the assassin's lair. He served as one of the guards standing behind the party during their meeting with Eldgrim. Thorin distracted him and Rust with questions about the guild's name while Daz stole the contract from the desk.

## Eldgrim

Eldgrim is the leader of the Empty Scabbard assassin's guild, a Duergar with an eye patch. He operates from a 70-foot cave containing a large garden and a desk with papers. He confirmed a contract exists on Werz Saltbaron, calling it "small potatoes," and offered to cancel it and add Werz to the "brightlist" (a permanent protection list) for 20 gold on top of the party eliminating the Demogorgon cult. After negotiation, he agreed to brightlist Werz for free if they destroy the cult. He arrogantly dismissed Daz's attempts to get proof of the guild's existence, saying anyone who knows they exist will deny it. He was flattered when Daz claimed to be writing a book and wanted to record the guild's name, and wrote something down on a piece of paper. When Zalthir accidentally burned his desk papers, Eldgrim was furious but calmed when he realized the burned contracts were ones he'd memorized. He threatened to kill the party as a "free contract" if they fail to eliminate the Demogorgon worshippers. He has three soulblades as visible guards plus at least two invisible ones posted 50 feet north of the entrance.

## Thangus Ironhead

Thangus Ironhead is named on the stolen contract from Eldgrim's desk, written under the Deepking's seal. The contract orders him boiled in oil and fed to roaches for trying to "encroach on my family's business."

## Captain Blackskull

Captain Blackskull is referenced in the stolen contract from Eldgrim's desk as someone the Deepking has suspected of treason since she was young. The contract states "She's always had an eye on my throne" and orders that she receive "what's coming to her."

## Lingrick Xardelvar

Lingrick Xardelvar is named in the stolen contract from Eldgrim's desk, described as the Deepking's "most gaseous ally." The entry is enthusiastic, with multiple exclamation marks.
