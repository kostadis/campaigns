## Fuurm Coppernose (Copper)

A male human found in a Faerzress crystal cave on the 9th day of the 1st tenday of Myrkhul. He was dressed in rags, starving, and holding a crystal as a makeshift weapon. He shouted "Who are you? And back off!" when the party first encountered him. He explained that he had lost his sister and went looking for her in the Underdark, which is how he ended up captured by the Drow. He has no adventuring skills — he is just a farmer. Despite this, Daz was astonished he survived and invited him to join the group. He was later seen chatting nervously with Baedora, his spirits somewhat lifted after being fed by the party.

## Baedora

A female human found alongside Fuurm Coppernose in the Faerzress crystal cave. Also dressed in rags and starving. She explained that demons had attacked their Drow captors, the Drow fought back, and the two of them ran, figuring they were more likely to stay alive away from both Drow and demons. She was invited to join the party by Daz. Later seen chatting nervously with Fuurm, her belly full and spirits somewhat lifted.

## Eldeth

Actively hiding the party's tracks for at least two days as they traveled through dark passages. She successfully concealed their trail in the Faerzress crystal cave. She was worried that every time the party disturbed the environment (such as collecting crystals), it made them easier to track, though Grygum told her not to worry. By the 5th day of the 2nd tenday of Myrkhul, Zalthir credits her shrewdness with reducing the Drow pursuit to "a mere whisper behind us."

## Serith

Insisted he knew where the party was going as they traveled through the dark passages. When the mysterious feminine voice led them toward the Lost Tomb of Khaem and Zalthir asked whether that was the direction they were already heading, Serith confirmed with a nod that it was indeed the way.

## Stool

Grygum's myconid companion, described as curled up contentedly against Thorin's back, "probably still reliving his 'good idea' to ride on the dwarf" as they traveled through the fungal forest.

## Jimjar

Shouted "Want to make a bet?" when the party found another cave full of crystals. Grygum turned the tables on him by proposing the bet himself — that the crystals would be useful — which left Jimjar confused, as he wasn't used to other people initiating the wager. He smiled and accepted anyway. Later, Zalthir noted that Jimjar had already started a new round of bets, "probably on how many steps Thorin takes before tripping."

## Brysis of Khaem

A Netherese sorceress whose Lost Tomb the party entered. She is depicted in a stone diorama near the tomb's entrance, shown as a Netherese noble surrounded by her retinue, flaunting wealth and power. Murals on the walls depict her alongside fantastic floating cities of the Netheril Empire. Her gilded sarcophagus sits in the deepest chamber of the tomb. A Wraith rose from the sarcophagus and declared, "Your deaths will free me from this prison, and how my victims will serve me even in death!" It is unclear whether the Wraith is Brysis herself or a separate entity, as a soft feminine voice — the same one that telepathically called out to the party upon entering the tomb pleading for help — spoke again saying "In the sarcophagus! I can help you!" when Zalthir moved the sarcophagus lid aside. Her true nature and current status remain unresolved as the session ended with Zalthir looking inside.

## Pelek

Mentioned only in passing — when Zalthir declared "I will punch the ghost!" while fighting the Spectres, Grygum noted he could "almost hear Pelek, our ghost, correcting him." Pelek is the party's ghost companion, not physically present in this scene but referenced by association.

## Fargus Rumblefoot

Not present during these events, but previously warned the party about a "secret room" or "false tomb" within the Lost Tomb of Khaem. This warning was recalled by the party as they explored the tomb and discovered the hidden passage beneath the northeast sarcophagus.
