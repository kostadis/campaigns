## NPC Activity

**Glabbagool** (intelligent gelatinous cube): Helped clear rubble passageway for approximately one hour, enabling the party's advance. Began hearing mysterious voices calling him southward deeper into the caves, raising concern about demonic influence (later identified as Jubilex-related). Expressed discomfort at the gathered oozes, noting that oozes do not normally speak. Confirmed the voices originated from the south. Revealed his core motivation: a desire for friends and a sense of purpose rather than material payment. Agreed to stay with the party after Zalthir appealed to their friendship.

**The Sergeant** (Blingdenstone guard): Managed the crowd in the Trader's Grotto aftermath, deflecting public discontent by crediting Chief Dorbo Diggermattock for hiring the adventurers. Directed the party toward the rubble path leading to the ooze source. Appeared nervous about the party's continued presence stirring unrest.

**The Pudding King** (gnome, identity not yet named beyond title): Located in a cave illuminated by phosphorescent lichen, seated on a slime-covered throne, holding mad court and speaking of "cleansing Blingdenstone." Confirmed as the source of the disembodied voice filling the ooze cavern, which proclaimed the coming of the Faceless Lord to "enhance" Blingdenstone. Controls hundreds of oozes. Surrounded by green slime covering the ceiling of his chamber, posing a hazard to any extraction attempt.

**Chief Dorbo Diggermattock**: Questioned the party upon their return, expressed alarm upon learning the ooze source was a gnome controlling hundreds of oozes. Dismissed Grygum's battle plan as "the dumbest he had ever heard." Refused to include the Gold Whisker clan in any coordinated response. After Daz seized control of the discussion, agreed to the party's terms and cooperation.

**Senni Diggermattock**: Silenced Chief Dorbo when he criticized the party's plan and asked whether he had a better one. Advocated for including the Gold Whisker clan in the operation, citing their abilities as an asset against oozes. Tasked the party with bringing the Gold Whisker clan on board.

**Jimjar** (deep gnome): Present during the meeting with Chief Dorbo. Placed a bet with Chief Dorbo, expressing confidence in the adventurers' success and subtly backing their mission.

**A merchant from the Chamber of Commerce**: Rewarded the party with a ten percent discount coupon following their defeat of the gelatinous cubes in the Trader's Grotto.

**An unnamed gnome from the mining operations**: Witnessed Glabbagool clearing the rubble passageway and expressed interest in hiring him for future mining work.

**A citizen (unnamed)**: Publicly voiced discontent with Chief Dorbo Diggermattock during the Trader's Grotto crowd scene, accusing him of caring only for wealth and neglecting common people.
