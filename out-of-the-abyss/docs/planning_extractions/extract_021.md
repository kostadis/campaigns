- **The Deepking of Gracklstugh** issued kill orders against multiple named targets. The destroyed contract (partially recovered) bore his personal seal and royal seal and contained orders to kill: **Thangus Ironhead**, **Werz Saltbaron**, **Captain Blackskull** (described as someone the Deepking suspects of coveting his throne), **the Wyrmsmith** (described as "His Arrogance, His Rotundity… the Pampered, the Portly, the Scheming Red" — to be frozen until his heart turns crystalline then smashed), and **Lingrick Xardelvar** (described as "my most gaseous ally"). Dozens of other targets were listed with petty descriptors.

- The Werz Saltbaron contract originated with someone powerful enough to use the Deepking's seal — suggesting the contract was placed by or on behalf of the Deepking himself.
- The "brightlist" is a formal protection mechanism used by the guild, described as permanent and visible to all guild members.
- The dragon egg contains a demon, not a dragon. The possession is already complete.
- The black obelisk at the Raucus Mesa interacts with the faerzress of the cavern and produces or dislodges physical objects when it activates.
