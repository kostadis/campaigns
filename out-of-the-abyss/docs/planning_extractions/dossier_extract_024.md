## Eldeth

Eldeth shouted "Is everyone okay?" from near the tunnel entrance after the battle in the upper levels of the mesa. She was positioned near a spectator (a beholder-like creature) at the tunnel entrance. She had a near-death experience during the events in the chamber, which weighed on the party's conscience and made the idea of leaving Gracklstugh as a smouldering inferno less appealing.

## Pelek

Pelek is a ghost who occupied the body of a Derro after the battle on the mesa. An unfamiliar Derro voice announced, "My goodness, this body is uncomfortable," before clarifying, "Oh, yes, I am Pelek. I seem to have occupied this body." He exchanged pleasantries with Daz after the battle.

## Pliinki

Pliinki was a Derro devotee of Demogorgon, now dead (killed by the party). Her journal and letters were found by Zalthir on a desk in the northwest corner of the obelisk chamber. The journal detailed her experiments to mutate a stolen red dragon egg into a two-headed wyrmling intended as a mount for Demogorgon. She had been working with the Obelisk (which she called "The Stone of Diirinka"), attempting to mend its fractures using Thrazgad ore, though the ore proved useless for that purpose. She planned to use the Obelisk as an instrument to conquer Gracklstugh and offer the city as a sacrificial gift to Demogorgon. She coordinated with Uskvil (Gray Ghosts leader), Aliinka (Council of Savants/Gray Ghosts), and Narrak (cult leader) to destabilize Gracklstugh through stolen eggs, assassination lists, and sowing discord. She had promised the Derro guards at the double doors that her plan would free the Derro from Duergar bondage. Her death was confirmed when the party passed through the guard post.

## Uskvil

Uskvil is the leader of the Gray Ghosts. Referenced in Pliinki's letters as "Master Thief." His operatives successfully stole both the red dragon egg and Clan Thrazgad ore on behalf of the Demogorgon cult. Pliinki corresponded with him about the Obelisk's resistance to repair using the Thrazgad ore, and instructed him to continue diversions and ensure the Deepking's hit list remained in the assassins' hands to create chaos. He does not appear in person.

## Aliinka

Aliinka is a member of the Council of Savants and connected to the Gray Ghosts. In a letter to Pliinki, she confirmed that Uskvil had successfully retrieved both the dragon egg and the Clan Thrazgad ore. She reported that Gray Ghost operatives were spreading whispers of dissent throughout Gracklstugh, that the Deepking's reliance on the Empty-Scabbard Killers for assassinations served their purposes, and that Captain Errde Blackskull's paranoid search for corruption was unwittingly aiding their efforts. She noted that Narrak was pleased with the Council's indoctrination and that almost all Council members had embraced the cult's path through placement of 'Y' carvings.

## Narrak

Narrak is the cult leader of the Demogorgon worshippers. In a letter to Pliinki, he described the spreading madness and corruption throughout Gracklstugh as going according to plan. He noted the Deepking's paranoid edicts weakening his own authority, Errde Blackskull chasing phantoms, and the Keepers of the Flame being distracted by the egg's loss. He emphasized that Pliinki's work with the Obelisk and the egg was paramount—once repaired, the Obelisk would be the instrument of their ascension, allowing them to conquer Gracklstugh and deliver it as a sacrificial gift to Demogorgon. He suggested Gartokkar's desired "genocidal war" against the Derro would merely be the first cleansing.

## Gartokkar

Gartokkar is referenced as a figure associated with the Keepers of the Flame. Narrak's letter mentions Gartokkar's paranoia and his desire for a "genocidal war" against the Derro. The party decided to return the mutated dragon egg to Gartokkar and use him as their means of escape from Gracklstugh. Grygum's plan involves telling Gartokkar that the egg was stolen not by the Derro but by "some other crazed group," and leveraging his help to leave the city before Errde Blackskull could imprison them.

## Errde Blackskull

Errde Blackskull is the Captain of the Stone Guard. Aliinka's letter describes her as consumed by her own "conspiracy theories," hunting for corruption but unwittingly aiding the cult's efforts to sow discord. Narrak's letter similarly describes her as "hunting phantoms." The party has a deal with her Stone Guards and fears that if she imprisoned them, they might be tortured into revealing information. The party specifically chose to avoid her by planning to use Gartokkar as their escape route instead.

## Themberchaud

Themberchaud (also spelled "Thermbechaude") is the red dragon of the Keepers of the Flame. The Keepers are guarding the secret of his egg's theft. Pliinki's journal describes the egg as originally intended as a "replacement" for Themberchaud, but the cult repurposed it for Demogorgon. Grygum declared the egg should be returned to Gartokkar, reasoning that when the mutated abomination hatches, Themberchaud will have his revenge on those who sought to control him. Thorin expressed interest in riding Themberchaud out of Gracklstugh, but the party ultimately abandoned that plan.

## Buppido

Buppido is referenced only as having been lost. His loss, alongside other recent traumas, contributed to the party's decision to leave Gracklstugh as quietly as possible rather than causing further destruction.

## Diirdeklin

Diirdeklin is a Derro guard stationed at a guard post in front of double doors south of the obelisk chamber. He had been posted there for days on Pliinki's promise that her plan would free the Derro from Duergar bondage. When the party approached from the north, he reached for an alarm bell but was intimidated by Daz's display of dancing lights (which made him appear to be a Drow Archmage summoning meteor swarm) and Zalthir's booming declaration that "The people you are trying to warn are dead." Terrified, Diirdeklin dropped the alarm and shouted "Yes, pass!" to prevent his companion Diinarnnmyr from foolishly fighting. After the party left, he concluded that a Drow incursion had occurred and that Pliinki's death meant larger plans were afoot.

## Diinarnnmyr

Diinarnnmyr is a Derro guard at the same post as Diirdeklin. Diirdeklin worried Diinarnnmyr might foolishly try to fight the party. After the party passed, Diinarnnmyr muttered that "The Ember Vanguard — the Drow are now working with the Duergar to subdue us!"

## Demogorgon

Demogorgon is the Prince of Demons, referenced extensively in Pliinki's journal and the cult's correspondence. The mutated two-headed dragon egg was being created as a gift and mount for Demogorgon. The cult's ultimate plan was to conquer Gracklstugh and offer the entire city as a sacrificial gift to Demogorgon. Zalthir found a statue of Demogorgon in an alcove in the northeast corner of the chamber and picked it up. The cult members refer to him as "the Prince" and sign their letters with phrases like "The Prince Awaits" and "For the Coming."
