## Plinki

Plinki was the derro leader who confronted the party. Her mind was consumed with thoughts of "Sacrifice! Power! For the Big Baboon Demon!" — indicating her devotion to a demon lord (likely Demogorgon, referred to as the "Big Baboon Demon"). She refused Grygum's attempts to reason with her about choice and dragon gods. Zalthir identified her as the lynchpin of the enemy force and targeted her specifically, killing her with a fire breath attack. Her death was described as sudden — "a bright, nasty, red shriek and then... nothing" — and it caused her followers' morale to shatter into panic. She is dead.

## Eldeth

Eldeth, described as a "nice lady-dwarf," was struck by projectiles fired from the darkness at the start of the battle. She was hit by what seemed to be poisoned darts or crossbow bolts — her thoughts went to pain, described as feeling like "getting stabbed with a rusty-tasting needle." Her status after the initial hit is not further detailed in this account.

## Pelek

Pelek is a ghost who attempted to possess or mentally infiltrate the thief-derro during the battle. His attempt at psychic intrusion failed — described as "trying to pour hot tea and cold water into the same cup." The thief-derro's mind resisted like a fortress and pushed Pelek out. His current status remains as a ghost allied with the party.
