## NPC Activity

**Errde Blackskull** (Commander of the Stone Guard)
- Met with the party in her chambers at Overlake Hold
- Received a Flumph-assisted trauma dump and initially claimed it proved the Grey Ghosts and Council of Savants were working together
- Was talked down from that conclusion by Daz, who argued proximity doesn't equal conspiracy
- Demanded the party return to retrieve documentary evidence (papers) from the Derro areas
- Ordered the party to arm Jimjar, Spiderbait, Sarith, and Eldeth as a crew for the mission
- Tasked the party with visibly consulting Clan Ironhead and Clan Xardelva as cover to disguise their true allegiance to her
- Provided context on Clan Ironhead's grievances (forbidden from direct weapons orders, forced to take only surplus from Clan Steelshadow) and Clan Xardelva's fears of being absorbed by Clan Steelshadow
- Remains convinced the Council of Savants is corrupting the Deepking; described as showing signs of paranoia and obsession

**Werz** (Duergar merchant)
- Met the party at the Shattered Spire tavern
- Paid each party member a 10gp gemstone as reward for saving his life; also gave the Flumph a gem
- Revealed he has been targeted by assassins, which he attempted to frame as surprising, though Daz suspected Werz himself had previously commissioned an assassination
- Admitted to illegally brokering weapons deals between clans, circumventing the Deepking's monopoly on weapons orders in favor of Clan Steelshadow
- Offered to facilitate a weapons deal between the Deep Gnomes of Blingdenstone and the Duergar clans in exchange for help eliminating his assassin problem
- Paid 1,100gp NDA fee after Daz negotiated him up from a counter-offer
- Paid an additional fee in gems (value unknown; Jimjar identified them as empty spell gems) for the party to handle the assassins
- Warned the party that the city is on the brink of war and that providing the wrong faction with wrong information could trigger it
- Disclosed that Stone Guards have been taking bribes for the past few months, correlating with the onset of the Deepking's madness
- Disclosed faction intelligence: Clan Ironshadow's leader is willing to wait out the Deepking but internal members are restless; Clan Tharzgad has suffered raids by the Grey Ghosts; Clan Xundom will support the Deepking if war breaks out

**Jimjar** (Deep Gnome NPC traveling with the party)
- Identified the gems given by Werz as empty spell gems
- Revealed that the alchemist Kazook Pickshine in Blingdenstone can convert them into gems of immense magical power and would pay well for them
- Accompanied the group to Overlake Hold the following day to be armed by Errde

**Thangus Ironhead** (Clan Ironhead leader)
- Granted the party an audience after their badges were presented
- Stated publicly that Clan Ironhead approves of the Deepking's policies and supports Clan Steelshadow's pre-eminence
- Assessed by Daz as lying and trying to survive; party treats his stated position as a facade

**Grinta Ironhead** (female Duergar, Clan Ironhead)
- Approached the party outside Clan Ironhead's stronghold after the official meeting
- Revealed her clan's true position: the Deepking's pricing has harmed trade with the Deep Gnomes
- Proposed that if the Deepking were deposed, Clan Ironhead would freely trade with the gnomes at discounted rates
- Departed quickly after delivering her message; clearly aware the party is heading into the Whorlstone Tunnels

**Buppido / The Flumph** (Derro-turned-Flumph, traveling with the party)
