Killed in combat by the combined efforts of Thorin, Daz (magic missiles), and Grygum (crossbow).

**The Mimic** (creature, not engaged): Observed stationary on a strand near the cavern wall, disguised as a large chest. Identified and avoided by all escapees including Buppido. Jimjar considered wagering on its nature but found no takers.

## Revealed Information

- **Tomb of Brysis of Khaem**: A half-elf sorcerer from a Netherese-era empire, interred in a floating tomb that crashed into the Underdark after Netheril's fall. The tomb contains a **false burial chamber** (sarcophagus and main chamber) and a **real treasure chamber** accessible via a secret passageway from the servants' tomb. Located on the **eastern edge of the Darklake**, roughly on the route between Sloobludop and Gracklstugh. Sethir believes he can identify the precise location.
- **Fargas's knowledge was partially bravado**: Zalthir assessed that Fargas's original claim to know the location may have been made out of desperation; Grygum's read confirms Fargas is not lying about the limits of his knowledge.
- **The Spectator was imprisoned by a long-dead drow wizard**: It escaped its imprisonment and was operating independently in the Silken Paths. Its claim about demon warnings was not fully explored before it turned hostile.
- **Drow pursuit may not be hindered**: Spiderbait indicated skilled Drow guides would not be significantly delayed by the spider encounter; the hundreds of available paths through the Silken Paths make interception unpredictable.
- **Buppido has a "divine plan"**: He referenced it in response to overhearing Zalthir and Daz discuss using the goblin guides as fodder, implying he sees Zalthir as a potential instrument of that plan.
