## NPC Activity

**Senni Diggermattock**
Attended a leadership meeting with the party regarding elemental labor. Initially part of a broader leadership group, she seized control of the discussion after the party's arguments proved effective. As a leader of the labor groups (not the business sector), she declared "Gnome Labor, not Summoned Labor!" and aligned herself with the party's position, effectively driving the policy shift against elemental servitude.

**Chief Dorbo**
Attended the leadership meeting regarding elemental labor plans. Was present when Senni took control of the discussion; no independent stance recorded beyond attendance.

**Chipgrin**
Attended the leadership meeting regarding elemental labor plans. Present during the debate; no independent stance recorded beyond attendance.

**Religious Leaders of Blingdenstone** (unnamed)
Were party to the leadership meeting. Agreed with the party's arguments, concluding that enslaving summoned creatures violated their deepest religious beliefs. Supported the final policy decision.

**Starlace**
A traveling merchant who arrived in Blingdenstone during the city's preparations for battle against the Pudding King. Operated a shop of magical wares. Sold Zalthir crystal spectacles (see-invisibility/magical darkness). Enchanted Grygum's mace and shield with protective magic. Purchased excess armor from the party. Both parties were satisfied with the transactions.

**Valimor Brightgem**
Operates The Gilded Sheath in Blingdenstone. Met with the party to commission a scabbard for Dawnbringer. Offered two options: a gem-encrusted showpiece or a practical locking scabbard. Became excited by Thorin's casual marketing concepts (tiered models, replaceable components, "Vaultmaster 9000" branding). Offered the locking scabbard at a reduced price in response.

**Dawnbringer** (sentient sword)
Referenced throughout. Had been attracting attention from local children due to its flaming appearance. Received a practical locking scabbard from Valimor Brightgem. Expressed satisfaction with having a proper, secure home.

**The Pudding King**
Referenced as the imminent threat; Blingdenstone is actively preparing for battle against him and his oozes. Not present.

## Faction Movements

**Blingdenstone Leadership / City of Blingdenstone**
Completed the release of Entemoch's Boon via party-assisted ritual. Had planned to use newly available Earth Elementals as mining and construction labor, representing a significant economic development strategy. This plan was fully disrupted by the party's intervention. Final policy outcome: Earth Elementals may be summoned for defense and warfare only, not for labor. The working gnome class responded with gratitude and admiration toward the party. The city is now actively preparing for imminent conflict with the Pudding King.
