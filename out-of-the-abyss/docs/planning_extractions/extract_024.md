## NPC Activity

**Pliinki (Derro Cultist — deceased, body/notes in play)**
Zalthir recovers Pliinki's journal and associated letters from her desk. Her writings reveal she was conducting mutagenic experiments on a stolen red dragon egg to create a two-headed wyrmling intended as a mount/gift for Demogorgon. She was also attempting to repair a fractured Obelisk using Thrazgad ore to use it as a weapon to conquer Gracklstugh and offer the city as a sacrificial gift to Demogorgon. Her journal entries confirm she was in contact with Uskvil, Narrak, and Aliinka, and was aware of Gartokkar's paranoia and Errde Blackskull's investigative activities.

**Pelek (Ghost — now inhabiting a Derro body)**
Pelek announces his presence by speaking through the body of a derro, identifying himself as the ghost. He is now occupying a derro body and is present at the scene after the battle. He exchanges pleasantries with Daz.

**Uskvil (Gray Ghosts Leader — referenced, not present)**
Named in two letters from Pliinki as the operative who organized the theft of the red dragon egg from the Keepers of the Flame and the acquisition of Thrazgad ore. Served as Pliinki's logistical assistant. Aware of the Obelisk repair project.

**Aliinka (Council of Savants / Gray Ghosts operative — referenced, not present)**
Named in a letter to Pliinki. Confirmed the Gray Ghosts stole both the dragon egg and the Thrazgad ore. Aware that the Keepers of the Flame do not know the egg's true purpose. Reports that nearly all Council of Savants members have been indoctrinated into Demogorgon worship via placement of 'Y' carvings, orchestrated by Narrak.

**Narrak (Cult Leader — referenced, not present)**
Named in a letter to Pliinki as the broader orchestrator of destabilization efforts in Gracklstugh. Confirmed pleased with Council indoctrination progress. Identified the Obelisk as central to the plan to conquer and offer Gracklstugh to Demogorgon. Originally suggested use of Thrazgad ore on the Obelisk.

**Gartokkar (Keeper of the Flame — referenced, not present)**
Referenced in Pliinki's journal and Narrak's letter as paranoid and unaware the egg was stolen and mutated. His desire for a "genocidal war" against the derro is noted by Narrak as a useful first step in the coming cleansing. The party plans to return the egg to him and use him as leverage for escape.

**Themberchaude (Dragon — referenced, not present)**
Referenced by Grygum as the dragon who will seek revenge when the mutated egg hatches and is returned to the Keepers of the Flame.

**Errde Blackskull / Captain Blackskull (Stone Guard Captain — referenced, not present)**
Referenced in Aliinka's letter as "consumed by conspiracy theories" and actively hunting for corruption, which the cult regards as inadvertently aiding their efforts. Also referenced as a threat to the party, who fear imprisonment and torture at her hands.

**Eldeth (referenced — present at scene)**
Called out to check on the party after the battle. Located near the tunnel entrance with a spectator creature.

**Diirdeklin (Derro Guard — present)**
On guard duty at the double doors leading out of the cave complex. Stood down when Daz used dancing lights to simulate a Drow archmage's meteor swarm and the party announced themselves as the Ember Vanguard. Chose to let the party pass to survive. Afterward, interprets the event as a Drow incursion working with the Duergar.

**Diinarnnmyr (Derro Guard — present)**
Present at the same guard post as Diirdeklin. Muttered after the party passed that "The Ember Vanguard — the Drow are now working with the Duergar to subdue us." Appears to have nearly attempted resistance.

Successfully stole the red dragon egg from the Keepers of the Flame and delivered it to Pliinki. Also acquired Thrazgad ore. Leadership (Uskvil) remains at large and aware of the full conspiracy.

Remain unaware that the stolen egg has been mutated and is no longer a viable dragon egg. Party plans to return it to Gartokkar.

## Revealed Information

- **The stolen red dragon egg** was taken by the Gray Ghosts on orders from Pliinki/Aliinka/Narrak, not by ordinary thieves. Its intended purpose was to become a two-headed demonic wyrmling mount for Demogorgon.
- **The Obelisk** in the Whorlstone Tunnels was hidden by Diirinka (derro deity) and is a quasi-magical artifact that the cult intended to repair and weaponize using Thrazgad ore. The ore has so far failed to mend its fractures.
- **The Obelisk responds to demonic will and city madness**, not conventional repair methods.
- **Narrak is the overall cult leader** coordinating Pliinki, Aliinka, and Uskvil. He is pleased with the Council of Savants indoctrination.
- **Aliinka is a double agent** within the Council of Savants, actively placing 'Y' carvings to induct members.
- **The Deepking's hit list** is being kept active by the cult deliberately, to erode his authority.
- **Gartokkar does not know** the egg is mutated or that it was part of a demonic conspiracy.
- **Errde Blackskull** is investigating corruption but is unknowingly chasing phantoms the cult has planted.
- The letters constitute evidence sufficient to trigger a genocidal war between derro and duergar if revealed.
