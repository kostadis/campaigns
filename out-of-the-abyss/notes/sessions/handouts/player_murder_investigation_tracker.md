## Clues — the chamber

### The body
- Body slumped near the desk.
- Rib cage **caved in** by something like **an axe** — multiple deliberate blows, close together. (Zalthir's read.)
- **Heart missing.** No other organs taken.
- Blood pattern **wrong for the wound** — far less than a violent kill should produce, and localised. He was almost certainly **dead before his chest was opened.**
- Grygum: removing the heart prevents resurrection by ritual. Not a souvenir. A **precaution.**

### The room (3rd-floor apartment)
- **Tobacco smoke** still hanging in the closed room hours after sunrise. No one had been smoking recently. The smoke says the room had been sealed for hours.
- **Candelabra** — seven-arm bronze — burned **entirely** to wax puddles. Tadric: Miss Hollypocket would **never** have let the Keeper's candles burn that low.
  → Together: **Janussi was killed well before he was found at 7:30am.**
- **Safe open** on the far wall. Blue gemstones and empty pouches scattered on the floor. The thief looked at the jewels and **walked past them.** Whatever they came for was worth more.
- **Chair near the desk toppled.** Marble statue and its pedestal on the floor.

### The desk
- Spellbook open: **"The Discoveries."**
- **Notes in Celestial** on one side — generic academic musings on unfamiliar workings. Janussi's own hand.
- A larger pile of notes pinned under a quartz weight:
  - **Recent annotations in midnight-blue ink** on top.
  - **Older notes in purple ink** below.
- **Quill on the floor**, nib still midnight-blue.
- **Inkpot spilled.** Linen ink-carrier in a wicker basket, slightly damp.
- **The book the blue ink was annotating — *The Golden Ass* — is missing.** Whatever Janussi was working on **on the last night of his life** is what was taken.

### The third floor landing
- The **large apartment door** on the third-floor landing was left **conspicuously ajar.** Not yet entered.

## Clues — the people

### Kalan Strongbranch
- Janussi had asked him to resign **the morning before he died.** Cited mental faculties and negligence.
- Performed (or attempted) **Speak with Dead** on the body during his 11am inspection. **The dead did not speak.**
- A speak-with-dead ritual cannot be repeated on the same corpse for a window of time — so either Kalan used it and got nothing, or **someone else used it first** and Kalan didn't know it was already spent.
- Walked out of his inspection **"more determined."**

### Bookwyrm
- Volunteered Kalan's resignation order **before being asked.**
- Was at the baths at nine.
- Did not answer when Thorin asked where she was **earlier.**
- "Everyone should be examined. But I can assure you I had nothing to do with it." *(Daz, not aloud: everyone who did it says that.)*

### The Endless Chant — last night at dinner
A prophecy of **Alaundo** was recited from the gallery during the Refectory dinner. It landed:

> *On the longest night of winter's deepest cold,*
> *the keeper falls, the heart is sold.*

You are on **Day 2 of Deadwinter** — winter's deepest cold. The Keeper has fallen. The heart was sold (or taken).
