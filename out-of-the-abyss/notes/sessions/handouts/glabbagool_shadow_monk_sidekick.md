# Glabbagool — Way of Shadow Monk Sidekick (Level 8)

**Type:** Sidekick (Tasha's Cauldron of Everything, Warrior
chassis) with a *light* Way of Shadow re-skin. **Apprentice
tier — deliberately less capable than Zalthir on every axis.**

**Belongs to:** Zalthir. Gabe controls in combat. Joe / Mike /
Ben can interact with Glabbagool narratively but do not roll
his actions.

**Acquired at:** Whispering Dome, Candlekeep Day One. Zalthir
presented the question on Glabbagool's behalf. The dome's
inspirational words confirmed what was already true: Glabbagool
has been training in monk discipline since the Oozing Temple,
and the Way of Shadow is the only monk path whose mechanics
*describe what an ooze already is.*

> ⭐ **Design intent — setup-and-follow-through tool, not a
> frontline carry.** Glabbagool is mobile, quirky, and
> mechanically dependent on Zalthir's grapple to deliver his
> best round. He is *capable* — blindsight, telepathy,
> Extra Attack, Flurry of Blows — but his signature move
> (**Corrosive Embrace**) only fires when Zalthir is already
> holding the target, and the auto-no-damage-to-master
> phrasing is the design knob that keeps the synergy
> deliberate. **Zalthir's grapples remain the load-bearing
> combat contribution.** Glabbagool is the second beat in
> the combo, never the first.
>
> Trade-offs that keep him in his lane: **blind beyond 60 ft**
> (blindsight only — he cannot perceive at range), **CHA 10
> with no speech** (telepathic only), **Ki pool capped at 4
> at L8** (rises slowly per the table below). When in doubt
> at the table, *spend his ki on Corrosive Embrace, not
> Flurry* — the synergy ability is the point.

## Stat block

**Glabbagool, Shadow Apprentice**
*Medium Ooze (8th-Level Warrior Sidekick), Neutral Good*

- **Armor Class** 14 (Shadow Chakra Unarmored Defense — 10 +
  Dex + Wis)
- **Hit Points** 44 (8d8 + 8)
- **Speed** 25 ft, climb 25 ft

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 14 (+2) | 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) | 10 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 10 (+0) |

- **Saving Throws** Str +5, Dex +5
- **Skills** Acrobatics +5, Stealth +8
- **Damage Resistances** Acid, Cold, Fire
- **Condition Immunities** Blinded, Charmed, Deafened,
  Exhaustion, Frightened, Prone
- **Senses** Blindsight 60 ft. (blind beyond this radius),
  passive Perception 12
- **Languages** Telepathy 60 ft.; understands Common and
  Undercommon, but cannot speak
- **Proficiency Bonus** +3

### Traits

**Amorphous.** Glabbagool can move through a space as narrow
as 1 inch wide without squeezing.

**Artificial Chakra (Ki Focus).** Glabbagool's embedded shadow
conduit grants him **4 Ki points.** He regains all spent Ki
points after a short or long rest. His Unarmored Defense is
equal to 10 + Dex modifier + Wis modifier.

**Shadow Arts.** Glabbagool can spend **2 Ki points** to cast
*Darkness, Darkvision, Pass without Trace,* or *Silence*
without providing material components.

### Actions

**Extra Attack.** Glabbagool makes two attacks with his
Pseudopod.

**Pseudopod (Monk Weapon).** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +5 to
hit, reach 5 ft, one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) bludgeoning
damage plus 2 (1d4) acid damage.

⭐ **Corrosive Embrace (Costs 2 Ki).** *Prerequisite: the
target is currently grappled or restrained by Glabbagool's
master.* Glabbagool bypasses a normal attack roll, using his
master's hold to surge over the restrained target. The target
must make a **DC 13 Dexterity saving throw** (made with
disadvantage if the target is restrained).

- **Failure:** the target takes **14 (4d6) acid damage.** If
  the target is wearing nonmagical metal armor, its armor
  takes a **permanent and cumulative −1 penalty** to the AC
  it offers. The armor is destroyed if the penalty reduces
  its AC to 10.
- **Success:** the target takes half as much damage and
  suffers no armor penalty.

**Note: thanks to shared ki training, Glabbagool contours
around his master perfectly; the master takes no acid
damage.**

### Bonus Actions

**Shadow Step.** When Glabbagool is in dim light or darkness,
he can teleport up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space he can
see that is also in dim light or darkness. He then has
advantage on the first melee attack he makes before the end
of the turn.

**Flurry of Blows (Costs 1 Ki).** Immediately after Glabbagool
takes the Attack action on his turn, he can make two
additional Pseudopod attacks as a bonus action.

**Corrosive Surge (Costs 1 Ki).** When Glabbagool hits a
creature with a Pseudopod attack, he can force his acidic
shadow-ki into the wound. The attack deals an extra **7 (2d6)
acid damage.** If the target is wearing nonmagical metal
armor, it must succeed on a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw or
its armor takes a permanent and cumulative −1 penalty to the
AC it offers (destroyed if reduced to AC 10). **Once per
turn.**

## Level 9–20 progression

Blends Tasha's Warrior Sidekick scaling with custom Monk/Ooze
upgrades so Glabbagool grows alongside the party without
overshadowing the PCs.

### Core scaling rules

- **Hit Points:** +1d8 + Con modifier per level.
- **Ki Save DC:** 8 + Proficiency Bonus + Wis modifier.
- **Attack Bonus:** Proficiency Bonus + Dex modifier.

### Notes on high-level play

By Level 20, Glabbagool achieves AC 19, attack bonus +11, and
10 Ki points, maintaining his role as a highly mobile
skirmisher. Damage output scales nicely (1d10 + 5 bludgeoning
+ 1d4 acid baseline; Corrosive Embrace 6d6 acid; Corrosive
Surge +2d6 once/turn) but **remains tightly controlled so he
operates as the perfect setup-and-follow-through tool for his
Shadow Monk master, rather than a frontline carry.**

### Advancement style

The table will need one in-fiction event to justify each
advance — *a moment Zalthir witnesses and approves of* is the
right shape. Don't roll for it; lean on character beats.

## Table rules

1. **Gabe controls Glabbagool in combat.** Initiative is
   Glabbagool's own (+2 Dex). Gabe rolls everything.
2. **Glabbagool still travels in the bag of holding** when the
   party enters cities or settled areas. The bag is no longer
   a constraint at Candlekeep specifically — the avowed have
   accepted him as a noted curiosity — but Waterdeep's Watch
   ruling stands elsewhere.
3. **Shadow Step requires dim light or darkness.** Don't let
   the table forget this in bright sunlight. *Glabbagool is a
   shadow monk, not a sun monk.* This is Joe-bait if anyone
   asks Dawnbringer to brighten a room mid-fight.
4. **Ki refreshes on short or long rest.** 4 points at L8; see
   the progression table for scaling.
5. **Spend ki on Corrosive Embrace, not Flurry, when the
   choice is live.** The Embrace synergy is the design point;
   Flurry is the fallback when Zalthir hasn't grappled.
6. **Blind beyond 60 ft.** Mike or Joe will at some point ask
   Glabbagool to spot something at distance. He cannot. *He
   has blindsight, not vision.* This is a real constraint.
7. **No speech.** Glabbagool communicates by 60-ft telepathy.
   He understands Common and Undercommon. The Stonespeaker
   Crystal is no longer required for speech (the chakra fixed
   that) but the Crystal is still useful for Speak with
   Animals/Plants/Dead via Daz.
8. **Cheese.** Glabbagool can still eat cheese. As a bonus
   action. This is not a ki feature. It is just a thing he
   does.

## Narrative integration

- **Zalthir's role:** master to Glabbagool's apprentice. After
  the Whispering Dome, this is no longer informal. Zalthir
  may give Glabbagool single-word instructions in combat
  (*"stillness," "shadow," "step"*) which Gabe reads as
  permissions, not commands.
- **The First Faction echo.** The inspirational words
  Glabbagool hears in the Whispering Dome can be the kenku-
  monk sentence — *"the body that mimics nothing is already
  shaped by what it watches."* Gabe will catch the Whistler
  resonance. Don't gloss it. Honor and move on.
- **Juiblex resistance:** the open question of whether
  Glabbagool's brief telepathic merger with the ooze horde
  left lingering Pudding-King residue is *partially* answered
  by the Way-of-Shadow training. He is now a creature with an
  *explicit non-ooze identity* — apprentice, sidekick, monk —
  that gives him traction against Juiblex's reach. **Not
  immune, but resisting.** Useful in the OOTA back half if
  Juiblex's "I will be reborn" thread surfaces.
- **The googly eyes.** Still on. Forever. The monk discipline
  did not address the googly eyes. The googly eyes are a
  fashion choice and Glabbagool stands by them.

## What the Whispering Dome scene looks like at the table

~5–8 minutes. Gabe is the spotlight; Joe / Mike / Ben watch.

1. Zalthir presents the question: *"He wants to know how he
   becomes what I am."* The Avowed at the dome (a senior monk
   acolyte; not a named officer) accepts the formulation.
2. Glabbagool enters alone. Zalthir watches from the threshold
   under the one-supplicant rule. The dome closes.
3. **Inside:** Glabbagool sits — flattens — *settles* — for
   sixty seconds of silence. The dome's voices begin. They
   speak in turn. The last voice is the kenku-monk sentence.
4. Glabbagool emerges. He does not say anything. He does not
   need to. **Hand Gabe the stat block.** *That is the
   answer.*
5. Zalthir, in the doorway, says one word — *"begin"* — and
   they leave.

End scene.
