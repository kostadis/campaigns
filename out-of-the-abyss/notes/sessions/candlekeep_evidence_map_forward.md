## TIME SYSTEM

### What one block buys
One **block = one scene** (travel + engage + a clue). Test: a spotlight
and either a roll or a real decision.
- **1 block:** a Reader interview · a physical-evidence room · a full
  scholar sitting · a library lookup.
- **Free:** questions to Fembris/Tadric in passing · Kalan's key handoff ·
  a scholar one-liner · re-reading evidence they hold · planning.
- **Share a block:** adjacent quick stops — **Kitchens + Southern Dining
  Hall** are neighbors; "you sweep both" = one block.
- **Split party:** both halves act in the **same** block (parallel). That's
  how 4 blocks reaches 6–8 nodes.

### Day-2 block sheet  (pick up: late morning, post-Queenie)
```
[~] LATE MORNING   finishing chamber / witnesses (in progress)
[ ] MIDDAY
[ ] AFTERNOON         <- Casketball Tournament runs now (optional, skippable)
[ ] EVENING
[ ] NIGHT          long rest = advance to Day 3
  MIDNIGHT: Daral dies (if unsaved) · Bookwyrm cleans up · chant fragments 2am
```
~4 usable blocks before midnight. Make the budget semi-visible — *"time
for maybe two more stops before the keep settles"* — and let them triage.
