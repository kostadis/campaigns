## What carries in (read your High Tower carry-forward first)

The single thing that colors this whole session is the **master fork** from
Monday's race:

- **LOST** (Manshoon holds both keys): the party is **chasing.** Manshoon
  reaches the Vault **ahead of them** (the canonical setup — Iron Owlbear killed
  by him, doors forced from inside). Standard rails.
- **WON** (party holds the real key #2): Manshoon is **back-doored** — denied the
  High Tower, he runs the *same* lava-chamber route the party does, so they're
  **racing level.** If the party still holds key #2, they can also use it as
  **leverage / bait** at the threshold. The front door of the Vault stays the
  party's to deny.

Either way the **lava-chamber Vault** (B2/B3) is reached through the cryptogram →
House of Alaundo → 97 steps. The keys gate the **High Tower wards**, not this
door — so the race runs regardless.

# PART 2 — THE VAULT + FINALE (was Session 8)

## Beat 1 — Threshold (~10 min)  ♻️ KEY RE-KEY
Plant the Iron Owlbear beak trophy. Then the Sylvira fork bites hardest here:

- **v1 (Sylvira Path B):** she stayed outside with `counterspell` ready — *"I am
  one archmage to his one archmage. I will hold the door."*
- **v2 default (absent):** **there is no archmage at the door.** Manshoon (even
  as a CR-6 simulacrum) can open with impunity unless **Daz anchors the
  counterspell himself.** Make the party feel the missing pillar — this is the

## Beat 2 — B2, the Vault proper (~30 min)
~100 glyph-warded tomes (`glyph of warding` + `suggestion`; 4d8 force + Wis DC
15; Zalthir's Watcher's Stillness = advantage). **Plant the Echoes cabinet
clearly** (far wall, ten prismatic gems, *"The Final Voices of the Seer"*).

(3) ⭐ **go for the Echoes** — *Manshoon does not care* — cabinet DC 18 thieves'
tools. **This is the correct play.**

## Beat 3 — Manshoon fight (~30 min, only if engaged)
**Manshoon simulacrum, scaled CR 6.** Tactics (unchanged): R1 `mirror image` +
`counterspell` ready; R2 `fireball` / `wall of force`; R3+ tries to
grapple-capture **Daz** (visible drow — he wants him). **HP ≤ 30 or
counterspelled twice → 7th-level `teleport`, escapes.**

♻️ **Without Sylvira at the door,** the counterspell economy is the party's
alone — if Daz is the one dueling counterspells *and* the target Manshoon wants,
that squeeze is sharper than v1. Price it honestly; it's earned by the Day-One
skip.

**Outcomes by ledger:** best = escapes empty-handed (takes a lesser tome, Book
stays); mid (canon) = escapes with the Book + a wound; worst = escapes with the
Book, unscathed. **Trophy:** ⭐ **metal-fingered glove** (*"his metal hand"*) if
