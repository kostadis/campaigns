# Candlekeep — Day 3 (Sessions 4–6)

GM-facing tactical script for **in-fiction Day 3** — *the day
after Deadwinter Day. The day everything accelerates.* Three
real-world sessions cover this day:

- **Session 4** — morning + afternoon. Reader interviews #2,
  physical evidence, scholar peak-interleave, Fembris breaks.
  **Milestone level-up to 9.**
- **Session 5** — evening. Daral status, Kalan missing,
  three converging paths, Bookwyrm dies, wards begin to flex.
- **Session 6** — night. High Tower fight, A'lai escapes,
  Manshoon arrives.

## Day 3 at a glance

**Weather/light.** Clear and cold. Snow from the night before
last has crusted. The sea is glittering and rough.

**The Endless Chant** is **visibly broken** by mid-morning —
multiple stutters, missed verses, occasional silence. Avowed in
the courts try not to look at the gallery rail.

interrogated her last night and may have chosen to treat or not
treat her plague. By afternoon she will be visibly worse. By
evening her role pivots once more — depending on path choice
she becomes either prime suspect (Paths A/C) or recruited
battlefield ally (Path B).

# SESSION 4 — morning + afternoon

## Atmosphere opening (~5 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 4 atmosphere.]

Morning. Two Avowed sweeping broken glass from the Western
Gallery floor of a window facing the Cursed Tower. (Moziqodo
passed close last night.) **Three madness incidents this
winter.**

The chant overhead **stops mid-line and re-starts a verse
earlier.** Bookwyrm is at the rail this morning, watching. She
does not look down at the courtyard.

## Kalan check-in (covert, ~10 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 4 Kalan check-in.]

If the party seeks Kalan at the Sea Warden's Tower, he is
**alone and worse.** Sitting at the window. Hands shake.

> *"Tomorrow I will not be at my post. I have given the second
> key to the right hand. I have made my arrangements. The being
> I have been speaking to in the Lost Lore is not what it was.
> It has begun to wear another voice. I do not know whose. I am
> sorry."*

He refuses healing. *"It is not a sickness in me. It is
something I have been making room for, for years, that I should
have shut out. I am not the right vessel anymore. Tadric is."*

⭐ **He is saying goodbye.** The party may not realize.

## Surface-madness escalator — Watcher Brevin (~10 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 4 surface-madness escalator.]

Late afternoon. **Tadric** finds the party with bad news.
**Watcher Brevin** — missing two days — has been found at the
foot of the western cliffs by a coastal gull-keeper. Alive.
Soaked. Reciting the same word: ⭐ ***Sloobludop. Sloobludop.
Sloobludop.***

**The party knows what Sloobludop means.** No one in Candlekeep
should know that name.

**Optional:** Daz speaks with Brevin in the Oratory. Confirms
the field-effect signature. **Yvenne is there.** *"Now you know
what I have been afraid of. Tell me what we do."*

## Cliffhanger — Fembris breaks (~15 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 4 cliffhanger.]

Investigators' Office, end of day. Fembris waiting, white-faced.
He confesses: broke curfew the night of the murder; climbed the
Bell Tower; saw **Sylvira** walking on the rooftops near the
Cursed Tower **with the Beast.** She was unafraid.

The party now has the **two-Sylvira contradiction.**
**Bookwyrm is the cover-up.** Alkrist (bronze-scaled, her
nephew) is the killer.

**🟢 Milestone level-up to 9.** Earned. Take it.

End Session 4.

# SESSION 5 — evening

## Atmosphere opening (~5 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 5 atmosphere.]

Daz wakes feeling worse. Outside the inn, **the Endless Chant
has stopped completely** for the first time. Resumes a minute
later — but the verse is from earlier in the cycle. **Brevin**
in his sanitarium dormitory is silently arranging bedclothes
in the same six-pointed star Marin made with quills. **Two
minds, the same shape.**

## Beat 1: The Bookwyrm summons (~25 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 5 Beat 1.]

Investigators' Office. Tea poured. Curtains closed. **She has
prepared.**

Choice point. If the party confronts: Bookwyrm pivots, doesn't
break. *"I have been investigating my own blood."* She offers
**arrest authority on Alkrist on her behalf.** **She is
sacrificing Alkrist to save herself.**

## Beat 2: Daral situation (~15 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 5 Beat 2.]

**If saved Session 3:** in the Apothecary, lucid. *"I had that
book in my hands before he died. I saw the lizardskin at the
Evergreen Tree at four. I told no one because Alkrist is
Bookwyrm's nephew and I am a drunk."*

**If dead:** found face-down in the bath; midnight tears in the
stomach lining; ink-stained fingertips.

## Beat 3: Kalan missing — Pont de Paramours (~25 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 5 Beat 3.]

The covered bridge. **Broken railings. Smear of blood. Claw
prints toward the Cursed Tower.** A dropped note in the snow
addressed to Kalan: *"Cursed Tower, midnight. Bring no one. — S."*
**DC 16:** the handwriting is forged. Trophy plant: **Kalan's
Gatewarden pin** on the bridge floor.

## Beat 4: Three converging paths (~varies)

[Full prep: arc file Session 5 Beat 4.]

🟣 Player choice. **All three paths funnel into the same
Bookwyrm-dead cliffhanger** — variable is who's with the party
when they arrive.

- **Path A:** arrest Alkrist at Drakonoikos.
- **Path B:** confront Sylvira about the forged note → **she joins as battlefield ally.**
- **Path C:** Cursed Tower direct → find ritual evidence; arrive alone.

## Beat 5: Glabbagool's bad night (~10 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 5 Beat 5.]

Through the crystal: ⭐ *"The mother voice came back. Pudding
King. It said my name."* Pudding King is dead. **Juiblex is
reaching.** Zalthir's chapter-50 oath load-bearing.

## Beat 6: Casketball Tournament (~20 min — optional)

[Full prep: arc file Session 5 Beat 6.]

Module canon. Whole keep gathers at the Oval Theatre. **A'lai
absent. Bookwyrm conspicuously present.** Glabbagool wants to
play. **Last fun moment of the arc.**

## Beat 7: OOTA scholar threads close (~30 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 5 Beat 7.]

These complete or transition this session. Sessions 6–8 are
pure crisis.

- **Daz/Yvenne** — final sitting. Drow Material Culture
  handout. Polly Pocket disposition resolves. ⭐ **Third piece
  of the Fourth-Seat erasure trail lands** (fresh-ink survivor
  redaction; locked behind 4+ ticks). **Synthesis Investigation
  DC 20** (18 with framing) available.
- **Zalthir/Khell-Vire** — closing letter sealed in front of
  Zalthir.
- **Grygum/Vareth** — Stations 9–10 + the unsigned sting.
- **Thorin/Philemon** — prescription delivered; sealed letter
  banked if Path C.

**Closure is earned, but not celebrated.**

## Beat 8: Cliffhanger — Bookwyrm dead (~15 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 5 Beat 8.]

Bell tower bells toll out of cycle. Three short, three long.
Tadric, ashen: *"Reader's Tower. The Beast was on the roof."*

**Bookwyrm dead on the floor of her study. Heart not removed —
throat torn.** **The High Tower key is gone.** On her desk,
fresh ink, unfinished:

> *"He is using the Beast to —"*

**Trophy plant:** Bookwyrm's First Reader silver pendant.

🟡 **The keep's wards have begun to drop.** From the High Tower
comes a wrong grinding hum.

**Tadric grabs the party.** *"I cannot fly with all of you. I
will fly two, then come back. Whichever of you has the second
key — you are the one who can open the door from outside.
Move."*

End Session 5.

# SESSION 6 — night

## Beat 1: Convergence (~15 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 6 Beat 1.]

Open with whichever Beat-4 path the party took. All three
converge at Bookwyrm's body; texture differs:

- **Path A:** Alkrist in shackles witnesses his aunt's death; **breaks** with a screaming confession.
- **Path B:** Sylvira kneels at Bookwyrm. *"I will help you stop him."* **Battlefield ally locked in.**
- **Path C:** alone. *"Then it is the four of you. As it has been."*

**Tadric flies two PCs at a time** to the Sea Warden's Tower
roof. PC with the second key goes wave one.

## Beat 2: The wards drop — the field flexes (~5 min, narrative only)

[Full prep: arc file Session 6 Beat 2.]

Mid-flight, A'lai pulls the lever on the lightning orb. **Shared
one-round vision per PC + Sylvira.** Narrative only. No
mechanical effect.

- **Daz:** Vaelissa T'sarran's face for half a second.
- **Zalthir:** Jimjar's voice — *"I am sorry I had to leave you."*
- **Thorin:** Brysis's hand on the back of his neck.
- **Grygum:** ⭐ **Bahamut absent.** Negative space. Returns when the wave passes.
- **Glabbagool:** *"Mother. Mother. Mother."*
- **Sylvira (Path B):** *"It will get worse before it gets better. We are running."*

**Tadric** almost loses concentration on `fly`; lands wave one
hard on the Sea Warden's Tower roof.

## Beat 3: The High Tower Library — A'lai and the Beast (~45 min combat)

[Full prep + scaled stat blocks: arc file Session 6 Beat 3.]

**A'lai (scaled CR 9), Moziqodo (scaled CR 5).**

PC with second key opens the door. Inside: A'lai at the dormant
lightning orb, holding a sapphire. He smashes it.

> *"That was the signal. He is on his way."*

**Moziqodo enters through the high windows.** Iron Bands hex-
marks on his neck.

**Path B variant:** Sylvira dispels the binding on Moziqodo
(Arcana DC 19, succeeds on 10). **Moziqodo turns on A'lai.**

**Trophy plants:** Iron Bands fragment (Path B); A'lai's
vestment if A'lai takes a heavy hit before escaping; Moziqodo's
hex-rune scar if examined post-fight.

**A'lai escapes via `dimension door` scroll at HP ≤ 50%.**
Takes the High Tower key. Direction: **lava chamber.**

## Beat 4: Cryptogram + manual restart (~10 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 6 Beat 4.]

**Cryptogram book** in indestructible glass. Daz transcribes
(DC 10 Arcana). **Six clues** [verbatim from module — see arc
file].

**Tadric reroutes ward power manually** (10 min, DC 18 Arcana)
to **30% strength.** Chant has stopped.

## Beat 5: Manshoon arrives below (~10 min, no combat this session)

[Full prep: arc file Session 6 Beat 5.]

Teleportation pulse. Manshoon's `magic mouth` voice projection
through the keep.

> *"Citizens of Candlekeep. My name is Manshoon. I am here for
> one book."*

## Beat 6: Cliffhanger (~5 min)

🟡 Cryptogram in their hands. Wards 30%. Manshoon in the keep.
A'lai gone. **Sylvira (Path B):** departs to find Moziqodo.

**Bookwyrm dead. Janussi dead. Daral dead or recovering. Kalan
dead. Alkrist in the Bell Tower cells.**

End Session 6.
