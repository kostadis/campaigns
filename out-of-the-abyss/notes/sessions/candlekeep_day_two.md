Day, the day after Janussi's death.* Two real-world sessions

interviews + scholar interleaves).

## Day 2 at a glance

**Weather/light.** Cold. **First snow of Deadwinter overnight** —
roofs, courts, and the cliffs above the Sea of Swords are white
by dawn. Sea wind. Dawn breaks late this time of year.

**The Endless Chant** resumes at 6 am but is **visibly more
fragmented than yesterday.** A chanter at the gallery rail above
the courtyard is replaced mid-shift around mid-morning. Acolyte
Marin (six-pointed quill star, Western Gallery classroom) is in
silent crisis by the afternoon; Lorekeeper Kei Tigersteel has
moved her to a sanitarium dormitory.

**Sylvira's role today:** *visibly ill.* Plague onset from
Moziqodo's bite the night before last. **The party doesn't know
yet.** She is the prime suspect off-screen and the demon-lord
ally on-screen — *the two roles will collide in the afternoon.*

# SESSION 2 — pre-dawn through noon

## Pre-dawn — Fembris at the inn door (~5 min)

[Session 1 cliffhanger fires.] Hammering on the door of the
House of Rest and the Hearth. Fembris, white-faced, snow on his
shoulders. *"The First Reader sent me. The Keeper of Tomes is
dead. She wants you. Now. Through the Emerald Door, before the
Avowed wake."*

The party throws on cloaks; Fembris hurries them out into the
not-yet-dawn. **Snow.** First snow of Deadwinter — they didn't
know it was coming.

## Early morning — Walk through snow + Chant snippet (~5 min)

Crossing the inner courtyard from the Outer Ward, the **Endless
Chant** overhead picks up its next verse: *"...the many-faced
man · his metal hand pulling strings..."* Fembris doesn't notice;
he's focused on running. Anyone listening hears it. **Plant.**

## Mid-morning — The Chapter House (~30 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 2 Beat 1.]

**Setting:** Chapter House inside Exaltation; dodecagonal table;
rose window throwing pale snow-light across the floor.

Council convenes. **Bookwyrm publicly:**
- Removes **Kalan** from the investigation (cites Lost Lore consultations / loss of focus).
- Conscripts the party. *"You owe this institution nothing. Investigate. Report to me."*

**One line of texture per Reader. In particular:**
- **Daral storms out** muttering about Janussi.
- **Fheminor watches Daz with wary curiosity.**
- **Sylvira stays seated longer than necessary, leaning on the table.** *(Plague.)*
- **A'lai is composed.** *(Too composed.)*
- **Alkrist is at his aunt Bookwyrm's right hand.** *(Don't underline.)*

**Kalan catches the party in the corridor on the way out:**
> *"Whatever Bookwyrm is telling you, listen for what she isn't.
> I am not the Gatewarden today. But the keep still has corners.
> Find me at the Sea Warden's Tower if you need a key turned."*

## Late morning — The Crime Scene (~45 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 2 Beat 2.]

**Setting:** Janussi's chamber, Keeper's Tower. Daylight now;
snow visible through the window. Hollypocket has been making
tea in the next room and crying.

**Findable clues, in order (DCs in `candlekeep_murders_arc.md`):**

1. **Body** — heart hacked post-mortem. No defensive wounds.
2. **The Golden Ass** open on the desk — DC 15: midnight tears poison residue.
3. **`Magic missile`–scorched armchair** — someone fought *here* with magic; Janussi himself wasn't burned.
4. **Lead chain links** on the floor — **trophy plant: empty lead locket** (key was inside it, gone).
5. **Safe blasted by `knock`** — a sapphire missing.
6. **Appointment book** — every Great Reader visited Janussi yesterday.

**Witnesses:**

- **Miss Hollypocket** — Sylvira at 1 am; **Queenie hissed**
  for the first time in ten years; A'lai at dawn before the body was found.
- **Tadric** (Watcher; Kalan's lieutenant) — **morning timeline
  only** (8 am crowd control; no overnight sighting).
- **Deidran** (Watcher; dawn door shift) — greeted **Bookwyrm** at
  first light heading to the baths; she detoured behind the Erudite
  Outfitters first → the bloodstained cloak in the bins.
  *(Module-accurate source for "Bookwyrm out at night," not Tadric.)*

## Noon — Investigators' Office + Session 2 cliffhanger (~15 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 2 Beat 3.]

Bookwyrm assigns the party an office in Exaltation. **Walking-
permit medallions issued.** Fembris attaches as adjutant.

🟡 **Cliffhanger:** Kalan finds them in the corridor and presses
**a small brass key** into one PC's hand.

> *"The High Tower has two keys. Janussi wore one. I held the
> other. I have given it now to someone who is not me. If I do
> not see you tomorrow, do not look for me at my post."*

**End Session 2.** Note which PC holds the key.

# SESSION 3 — afternoon through late evening

## Early afternoon — Atmosphere opening (~5 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 3 Beat-0 atmosphere.]

Two Avowed sweeping broken glass from the Western Gallery floor
(window faces the Cursed Tower; Moziqodo passed close last
night). **Daz feels the pressure on his sinuses for the first
time** since arriving — first somatic field-perception tell.

The chant overhead **drops three verses in a row**; an Avowed at
the gallery rail is replaced mid-shift. **Acolyte Marin** is in
the Western Gallery classroom arranging quills in a six-pointed
star. *"It's the only shape that holds. Everything else slips."*
Other Avowed lift her gently and lead her away with the patience
of people who have done this several times this winter.

**Three this winter.** Plant only.

## Mid-afternoon — Search the Southern Dining Hall (~20 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 3 Beat 1.]

The party can run this if they pieced together "the 'Sylvira'
people saw smelled wrong + she carried a light a tiefling
wouldn't need + the killer knew Candlekeep's own spell library."

- DC 15 Investigation if systematic; DC 12 if specifically looking at altars/statues.
- **The lead chalice on the statue of Milil** holds Janussi's
  heart and a bloodstained meat cleaver.
- **`Speak with Dead`** on the heart **fails.** *(Bookwyrm cast
  it first to lock the corpse.)* **The failure is the clue.**
- **Lead chalice = anti-divination.** *Someone planning this knew Candlekeep's spell library.*

## Late afternoon — Reader interviews #1 (~75 min, party may split)

[Full prep: arc file Session 3 Beats 2–4.]

Three interviews available; party can rotate or split.

**Sylvira (~25 min)** — Infernal Fortress. **Visibly worse than
yesterday.** Sores on her forearm she keeps covered. Story: she
was alone last night. Truth (if pressed honestly): the bite is
from her **son Moziqodo;** Janussi knew, was her lover, gave her
ten days to remove him. **Abyssal Plague onset.** She did not
kill him.

**This interview is also Sylvira's scholar-handoff continuation**
— she is the demon-lord-evidence ally from yesterday. *The party
is interrogating their ally.* Land it.

Optional Grygum beat: DC 18 Medicine or `lesser restoration` to
treat the plague.

**Daral (~20 min)** — Bath House. Soaking, drinking, hung over
and hostile. Bloody sleeve from punching Janussi. Bragged loudly
to two waiters at supper that he was *"going to give that man a
real Deadwinter present."*

**Daral is dying as the party speaks.** Re-reading *The Golden
Ass* in the bath all morning. DC 14 Medicine: *that man should
sit down.* `Lesser restoration` saves him *if caught in time.*

🟣 **Player choice — save Daral or let him die?** Saved → key
witness in Session 5. Let die → module-canon path.

**Fheminor (~20 min)** — Founder's Court cloisters. Old,
careful halfling. Watches Daz the whole conversation. Story:
nothing to hide. Truth (only if earned): **Janussi was about to
name her his successor.** And: ⭐ ***"Bookwyrm was not
surprised."*** **The campaign hinge.**

## Evening — Scholar interleaves (~30 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 3 Beat 5.]

- **Thorin/Philemon Phase 2** (the pivot — defended scabbard /
  good-parent / equal-partner). Reading Salon's permanent
  silence. Philemon doubled-down clinical today (Janussi was
  his hierarchical superior; he is rattled). Right time for
  Thorin to push for **Path C** if the table wants C.
- **Daz/Yvenne — the Vaelissa name lands.** ⭐ *Matron Mother is
  Vaelissa T'sarran.* Mirror-investigation framing. Connects
  forward to the Polly Pocket disposition — ⭐ **Bell Tower
  deadline: Kalan is alive today; tomorrow he won't be.**

**The other two scholar pairings (Khell-Vire + Vareth) work
offstage today.** If Gabe or Ben wants to pop in for a one-line
update, give them the next-tier reveal as narration without a
full scene. *Yvenne is in her office. So are they.*

## Late evening — Optional cliffhanger (~5 min)

As the party returns to the inn through the snow, they pass
through Founder's Court and **see something on the rooftop above
the Cursed Tower** — a long, hunched silhouette dragging
something. Gone in a blink. **Moziqodo.** Don't explain.

End Session 3.
