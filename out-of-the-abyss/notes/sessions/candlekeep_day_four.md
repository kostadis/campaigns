## Beat 6: Lava chamber + Session 7 cliffhanger (~15 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 7 Beat 6.]

> *"The owlbear's iron beak is intact. It's a foot long, the

> *"The pages turn slowly down here. I am not in a hurry. I

# SESSION 8 — afternoon → evening

## Beat 1: Threshold + entering (~10 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 8 Beat 1.]

Plant the Iron Owlbear beak trophy. Whoever wants it gets it.

**Sylvira (Path B):** stays just outside with `counterspell`
ready. *"I am one archmage to his one archmage. I cannot be
inside that room. I will hold the door."* **Don't argue her in.**

**Daz** (with field-perception) senses the simulacrum's
*not-quite-there* nature. Tell him.

## Beat 2: B2 — the Vault proper (~30 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 8 Beat 2.]

~100 dangerous tomes. Each: `glyph of warding` + `suggestion`
(*"Put it back. Leave. Forget."*) — 4d8 force + Wis DC 15.
Zalthir's Watcher's Stillness gives advantage.

**Plant the Echoes cabinet** clearly. Far wall. Locked velvet-
tray cabinet. Ten prismatic gemstones humming faintly. Brass
plaque: *"The Final Voices of the Seer."*

**Manshoon in B2:**

> *"By all means, browse. The trick is leaving with what you
> came for. Did you come for the book, or did you come for me?"*

🟣 **Three player choices:**
1. Engage Manshoon → Beat 3.
2. Race him to B3 → worse position.
3. **Go for the Echoes.** *Manshoon does not care.* Cabinet DC 18 thieves' tools. ⭐ **This is the correct play.**

## Beat 3: Manshoon fight (~30 min, only if engaged)

[Full prep + scaled stat block: arc file Session 8 Beat 3.]

**Manshoon (scaled simulacrum, CR 6).**

Tactics:
- R1: `mirror image`, `counterspell` ready
- R2: `fireball` or `wall of force`
- R3+: tries to grapple-position one PC for capture (Daz, by visible-drow inference)
- **HP ≤ 30 or counterspelled twice:** burns 7th-level `teleport`. **Escapes.**

**Outcomes by ledger:**
- **Best:** Manshoon escapes empty-handed; takes a single lesser tome. Book of Vile Darkness stays.
- **Mid (canon):** Manshoon escapes with the Book and a wound.
- **Worst:** Manshoon escapes with the Book and unscathed.

**Trophy plant:** if Manshoon loses an item — focus, **metal-
fingered glove** (the prophecy's *"his metal hand"*), signet —
it falls. Whoever wants it grabs it.

## Beat 4: Echoes of Alaundo payoff (~25 min — load-bearing)

[Full prep: arc file Session 8 Beat 4.]

Cabinet opens. Ten prismatic gemstones. Activation: *"Speak,
seer."* **Each Echo plays in full once.** Subsequent activations
play fragments. **Glabbagool's Whispering Dome boon** (if
banked) re-coaxes a partial Echo.

**Six are GM-picked atmospheric.** **Four are OOTA load-
bearing — run them one at a time.**

### Echo 1 — The Surface Contamination Prophecy

> *"When three queens move beneath the world, the world above
> will sicken. The chant will stutter. The keep will crack. The
> mad will speak the names of drowned cities. This is not the
> wedding. This is the symptom. The wedding is what comes
> next."*

### Echo 2 — The Wedding Prophecy

> *"In a grove that does not breathe, a queen of rot will marry
> a king of growth. When the union is consummated, the fungal
> lattice of the world will become her wedding band. Above and
> below will be spores. The bride is not yet veiled. There is
> still time. There is not much time."*

### Echo 3 — The Keeper Prophecy (Eldeth's call)

> *"A dwarf will return to a hall of stone with the names of
> demons in her mouth. The lords of the deep dwarves will
> believe her, because she will not flinch. They will call to a
> giant-raised dwarf and his shadow-walker, his crow, and his
> bright-blade. They will summon them to a forge that has not
> lit in an age."*

### Echo 4 — The Witness Prophecy (Jimjar / Callarduran)

> *"A god will walk as a gambler. He will bet on lives. He will
> pay his debts and he will not be paid. When his work is done
> he will leave the marker of a knucklebone, and the holder of
> the marker will be heard once."*

**Trophy plant:** the gemstones themselves are takeable —
ten-stone tray.

## Beat 5: B3 — Book of Vile Darkness (~10 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 8 Beat 5.]

Riddle door (answer: *"candle"*). Inside, the Book on a cracked
stone pedestal. Touching as non-evil = 8d6 necrotic.

🟣 **If Manshoon didn't take it:**
1. Take it (Manshoon-pursuit thread).
2. Leave it (Avowed reseal).
3. Destroy it (Grygum arc — `dispel evil and good` → `holy aura` → holy fire).

**Don't push.** Player choice.

## Beat 6: Attic — rocket option (~5 min, optional)

[Full prep: arc file Session 8 Beat 6.]

Sign in archaic Common: *"Last Resort. Pull only when nothing
remains worth keeping."* 60 crystal lights. **The lever
launches the Vault tower into the Sea of Swords.** Don't tell
players what it does until they pull it. **Manshoon will be
furious if pulled.**

## Beat 7: Return to surface (~15 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 8 Beat 7.]

Climb. Inda at the brass statue. *"You are alive. The Seer
foresaw this also."* Seals the staircase.

Keep stabilizes. Tadric's reroute holds. **Chant resumes —
fragmentary.** Daz feels the pressure ebb.

**Institutional cleanup:**
- **Alkrist:** Bell Tower cells; tried by surviving council; exile likely.
- **A'lai:** dead in Vault / dead in lava chamber / escaped.
- **Bookwyrm, Janussi, Kalan:** dead. Daral: dead or convalescing.
- **Sylvira:** alive. Senior surviving Great Reader.
- **Fheminor: becomes Keeper of Tomes.** Thanks the party formally.
- **Tadric:** acting Gatewarden.

Small ceremony in the **Oratory** with Lorekeeper Kei
Tigersteel. Party named *guest seekers of the Avowed* with
permanent Inner Ward access.

## Beat 8: Eldeth's letter — the Gauntlgrym call (~10 min)

[Full prep: arc file Session 8 Beat 8.]

Next morning. House of Rest and the Hearth. **Travel-stained
dwarf courier — not Eldeth herself.**

> *"I have spoken to the Lords of Mithril Hall and the Lords of
> Gauntlgrym. They believe me. They have been believing me for
> two months — they were waiting for someone whose word weighed
> more than a soldier's. Yours does. Come to Gauntlgrym. The
> Council has questions only you can answer, and the forge has
> been cold long enough that someone is going to need to relight
> it.*
>
> *I told them you would come. Do not make me a liar.*
>
> *— Eldeth Feldrun, Mithril Hall sworn, Ember Vanguard
> giant-friend."*

**Echo 3 clicks.** End campaign arc.
