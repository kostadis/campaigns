# Gauntlgrym Chapter 8 — Canonical Module Reps with Custom Asset Rewards

Reconciles OotA Chapter 8 "Forging an Alliance" canonical NPCs with the campaign's custom asset framework (Ready / Strained / Burned; Entry + Pivot slots; Objective Locks).

Personalities and canonical hooks come from the module. Asset rewards replace the module's default resources.

---

## 1. Harpers — Lord Zelraun Roaringhorn

*Lawful neutral human wizard. Waterdhavian noble. Proud, self-assured, fickle, spendthrift. Dines, drinks, gambles.*

**Asset: Whisper Chain**
- **Offer:** before any district operation, query the Harper network — 2 questions, 1 confirmed answer + 1 probable. No source names.
- **Cost:** 7 descriptions of unaccounted Harper informants in Menzoberranzan. If the party encounters any, get word back. Extract if possible, regardless of what they've become.

**Canonical gift preserved (separate from asset):** chess match after dinner. Best-of-3 INT contest, Zelraun has +9. First PC to beat him borrows the **Shield Guardian** (currently holds a stored *lightning bolt*) + control amulet. Unbeatable → loaned on vow of rematch post-Underdark.

**If attacked:** archmage stats.

---

## 2. Order of the Gauntlet — Sir Lanniver Strayl

*Lawful good human fighter, devout Tyrran. Patient, determined, witty, unshakable faith. Most respected figure among the Gauntlgrym factions.*

Supportive of good-aligned parties; neutral/evil parties must earn him. Travels with squire **Rhiele Vannis** (guard).

**Asset: Breach Team** (reframed as Gauntlet veterans)
- **Offer:** 4-person Gauntlet veteran unit attached to one op per mission cycle. Doors, chokepoints, extraction windows. Pick 4 from the roster: Thora Nabal, Sylrien Havennor, Olaf Renghyi, Elias Drako, Tamryn Tharke.
- **Cost:** veterans take exposure when the op goes loud. Bad calls made knowingly → asset pulled and Bruenor hears why.

**Canonical gift preserved (separate from asset):** **+1 warhammer** from the order's treasury, preferred for a good/neutral cleric or paladin. Swap item if no one fits.

**If attacked:** knight stats.

---

## 3. Emerald Enclave — Morista Malkin

*Neutral good shield dwarf scout. Stern, stubborn, insightful. Originally from the Silver Marches; followed Bruenor to reclaim Delzoun's glory. The only faction rep who lives in Gauntlgrym. Serves as Bruenor's advisor.*

**Asset: Oathsafe Sanctuary** (reframed as a warded natural chamber inside Gauntlgrym)
- **Offer:** one sealed long-rest location inside the city, warded against casual surveillance.
- **Cost:** spare or evacuate noncombatants when the choice is available (not at mission cost). Framed through her lens — civilians are part of the natural balance.

**Canonical hook preserved:** her missing scout. She'll also offer **giant lizard mounts** for one overland Underdark leg as a secondary favor if the party commits to searching for her scout.

---

## 4. Lords' Alliance — Lord Eravien Haund

*Lawful neutral half-elf noble. Charming, sly, worldly. Frames intel as diplomacy, not espionage.*

**Asset: Counter-Doctrine Brief**
- **Offer:** before a social/negotiation scene, ask 2 questions about a target faction. DM answers 1 true and 1 partially true (and says which is which — but the partial truth can still mislead).
- **Cost:** carry an **Archive Witness** — a non-combatant Lords' Alliance scholar/spy — into one op zone (not the finale, not direct combat). Keep her alive.
- **First-payment gate:** before the brief activates, the party reports three observations about how the drow Orthodoxy *actually* communicates internally (not the public version).

---

## 5. Zhentarim — Davra Jassur ⚠️ CRITICAL PATH

*Lawful evil human assassin. Driven, focused, ruthless. Clipped sentences, watches the door. Without her cooperation, the path to Mantol-Derith → Gravenhollow (Chapter 11) gets much longer.*

**Asset: Deep Route Ledger**
- **Offer:** Zhent smuggler waypoints through three tunnel systems. Skip one dangerous travel segment **or** force pursuers into a longer route. **Includes the route to Mantol-Derith** — meet agent **Ghazrim DuLoc**, who holds the Gravenhollow map.
- **Cost:** **Objective Lock** — before the finale, the party completes one Zhent-priority strike target. Target named when Davra decides. Will accept a time limit (lock expires if she hasn't named it by finale). Will not accept veto rights on the objective itself.
- **Bonus:** Zhentarim thugs available as backup muscle on one op.

## Optional Local Bonuses (Gauntlgrym-internal, not faction commitments)

### Sister Caldra Scale-Vigil — Bahamut-shrine local
**Asset: Seventh Move Counsel** — keep as a Bahamut-shrine scene, Grygum-focused. Includes the Stroud/Grygum beat ("What do you think moved your hand?") and the optional obsidian tile retrieval from an unstable shrine below forge level. Does not consume a faction slot.

### Guildmaster Torvin Ashpick — Forgewright Syndicate local
**Asset: Black-Forge Kit** — custom ordnance (explosives, ward-breakers, chemical effects). Materials upfront, no credit. Refund on unused kits minus labor. Available because Bruenor signs his operating charter. Does not consume a faction slot.

## Recommended Pitch Order for Session

1. **Morista Malkin** — she's local, easy to bump into first; sets Underdark-corruption tone.
2. **Sir Lanniver Strayl** — moral spine; good-aligned party gets quick buy-in.
3. **Davra Jassur** — the critical one; plot gate to Mantol-Derith.
4. **Lord Zelraun Roaringhorn** — chess match as the memorable set-piece scene.
5. **Lord Eravien Haund** — political wrap-up; can slide to next session if needed.
6. (Optional) **Sister Caldra** Grygum scene if there's time.

## DM Note on the Zhent

Davra's cooperation unlocks Ghazrim DuLoc at Mantol-Derith, who gives the Gravenhollow map. If the party walks away without her, the path to Chapter 11 gets much longer. Do not let this pitch whiff.
