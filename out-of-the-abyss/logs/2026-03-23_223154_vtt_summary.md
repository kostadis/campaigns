## Session Summary

# Session — 2026-03-23

## Overview

Departing Blingdenstone through the northern tunnels toward the surface, the party quickly discovered they were being followed — a young, fungally-infected drow scout from Ilvara's diminished pursuit group. After restraining and questioning him (with Daz's decisive use of Maximilian's Earthen Grasp and a staged spider-familiar reconnaissance), they learned Ilvara's force has collapsed from forty to eight, with multiple members transformed into Zuggtmoy's spore servants. More critically, they learned Ilvara herself has apparently abandoned Lolth, entering communion with a fungal mass she calls "the bride" — almost certainly Zuggtmoy — and that she has renewed her pursuit of the party following the Pudding King's defeat. Rather than flee, the party chose to go on the offensive: they traveled with the now-defected scout (identified as Valen) to the vicinity of Ilvara's camp and began a covert operation to sow internal discord, staging a false divine visitation from Lolth to manipulate Ilvara's dissident priestess Asha Vandree into defecting or acting against Ilvara. The session ended on a cliffhanger as Daz's Dancing Lights illusion descended toward Asha.

The entity informed Ilvara (roughly coinciding with the Pudding King's defeat) that the party had been located, prompting renewed pursuit.

- Valen provides a rough map of Ilvara's camp: a cavern with elevated platforms and bridges, a 15-foot-deep mud pit below, cycling poisonous vents (15-foot radius), corrupted fungal pools beneath the bridges, and a large heart-fungus creature under Ilvara's command.

Grygum identifies (Medicine 13) the sealed food as the likely reason she has avoided infection.
