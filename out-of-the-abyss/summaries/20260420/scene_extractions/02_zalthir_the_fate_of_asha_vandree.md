[Scene 2] The Fate of Asha Vandree
Narrator: Zalthir
Focus: Zalthir is the one who ends Asha when no one else will act decisively, stepping in with the same quiet finality that defines him — "I'm making a call" — and bearing the weight of that choice alone.

- Asha Vandree, believing herself divinely blessed by Lolth after surviving the chaos, attempts to take command of the party and treats non-Drow members with open condescension.



<!-- Declaring herself in charge after surviving the chaos -->
Asha: "Excellent! Now that I am in charge!"

<!-- After Grygum's Guiding Bolt misses her -->
Asha: "I am blessed by Lolth!"

- Thorin moves to intercept Asha, successfully grappling her and covering her mouth to silence her commands.




- Nym critiques the party's methods, suggesting that a blade is more efficient than a grapple when dealing with a cleric of Lolth.

<!-- After Thorin grapples Asha Vandree instead of killing her -->
Nym: "When attacking a cleric of Lolth, Thorin, one does not just simply grapple them. One takes your blade and cuts their heads off. Are you well?"

<!-- Explaining his grapple decision -->
Thorin: "I also put my hand across her mouth so she can't talk, so..."

<!-- Reacting with incredulity after Thorin's grapple -->
Nym: "It's amazing that you people have made it this far."


<!-- After Zalthir kills Asha -->
Thorin: "I could have taken her out if I wanted to."

<!-- Responding dryly -->
Drow: "Of course. And that is precisely the problem."

<!-- Defending himself -->

- Grygum attempts to strike Asha with a guiding bolt but misses as she proclaims divine protection.
<!-- Reacting to Asha's condescension -->
Grygum: "I certainly don't like condescension, regardless of—oh."

<!-- Quietly, after Asha declares herself in charge -->
Grygum: "Let's put a period after that 'now that I'm in charge' thing."

- Zalthir decides to end the threat permanently, unleashing a flurry of strikes and tentacle attacks that kill Asha Vandree.


<!-- Deciding to end the standoff with Asha -->
Zalthir: "I'm making a call."

<!-- After Zalthir kills Asha; sardonic applause -->
Kaelira: "Well done, well done, well done! ...See, he wasn't completely defenseless."
- The Drow mercenaries express a mix of amusement and begrudging respect for the party's sudden decisiveness.
