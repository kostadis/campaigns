**Investigating the Heart Fungus**
Thorin moved to the corrupted fungal mass at the center of the chamber and investigated its heart. At its core he found a fist-sized, dense, pearl-like node — smooth and heavy, radiating a faint but unmistakable necromantic energy.
The node was worth something to the right buyer — approximately 300 gp — and Thorin pocketed it.

---

**Grygum Burns the Corruption**
Grygum turned his fire breath on the remaining spore corruption atop Ilvara's elevated stone platform, incinerating fungal corruption that bore Zuggtmoy's influence — not the demon lord herself, who remains active at Neverlight Grove, but a localized expression of her corrupting reach — and reducing it all to ash.
The chamber smelled of scorched rot as the last of the fungal corruption collapsed.

---

**The Leather-Wrapped Bundle**
Beneath the smoldering remains of the burned fungus, the party uncovered a leather-wrapped bundle. Inside: incense cones, a small silver bowl, and a cloth stitched with the fused sigils of both Lolth and Zuggtmoy — one woven inside the other.
It was the physical embodiment of Ilvara's madness, proof that something deeply wrong had been spreading through the Underdark.

---

**Looting the Fallen Guards**
The party moved through the bodies of the fallen Drow guards, stripping them of gold, short swords, a long sword, and a steel whistle.
The dead soldiers would never collect their pay.

---

**The Drow Mage's Body**
On the body of the Drow mage, the party found a signet ring bearing the seal of House T'sarran and an obsidian wand tipped with a captive spider set in amber.
Daz immediately identified the ring as both proof of House T'sarran's involvement and a tool that could be used to forge orders in their name.

---

**The Burnt Extraction Contract**
Also on the mage: partially burned documents. When examined, they confirmed what the party had begun to suspect — there had been a contract to extract Daz alive and unharmed, and House T'sarran had been the ones behind it.
The orders were only partially legible, but what remained was damning enough.

---

**Nym and Kaelira Examine the Contract**
Nym and Kaelira Duskryn were shown the burnt extraction contract. Kaelira examined it with cool professionalism before speaking.
Nym: "That is an excellent question. You don't belong to any noble house, your background is a complete mystery, you suddenly show up, and all of a sudden, we're contracted to protect a nobody. Do you know how much money that required us to get, for us to do? A lot. Like, they interrupt you. A lot."
Daz: "So then maybe there's more to me than meets the eye?"
Nym: "Yes, yes, yes, that's what every orphan thinks. Do you have daddy issues? Do you have mommy issues? Every drow thinks there's some noble things. For all we know, this was a game being played by the houses."
Kaelira: "Perhaps, but we're not paid to think that hard."
The mercenaries found the whole situation baffling — a nobody from Menzoberranzan commanding a price that suggested something far more than wishful thinking.
