## Scenes
### The Level Eight Milestone
#### The party reaches a significant milestone in their journey through the Underdark, gaining new powers and specialized abilities.
- The party is informed they have reached level eight, triggering a period of reflection and growth.
- Thorin considers whether to address his lack of ranged capabilities or double down on his current specializations.
- The characters begin discussing the importance of specialization at higher levels, noting that damage output must scale significantly to keep pace with increasingly powerful foes.
- The group discusses the lack of ranged weapons in the party and how it might be a disadvantage in future encounters.

### Choosing Feats and Abilities
#### The party members deliberate over their feat selections, weighing specialized combat options against general ability score improvements.
- Daz considers the Warcaster feat to improve concentration on spells, and explores the Fey Touched feat to gain additional prepared spells and free castings.
- Daz notes that Dawnbringer deals radiant damage rather than slashing damage, which influences his tactical thinking.
- Daz selects the Fey Touched feat, which allows him to cast certain spells without expending spell slots and opens up additional spell slots for new spells from the found spellbook.
- Thorin considers the Speedy feat to increase his movement speed and mobility across difficult terrain, noting that closing distance quickly is critical given his limited ranged options.
- Zalthir discovers the Street Justice feat, which allows him to use rope to restrain enemies he has grappled, and becomes excited about its synergy with his grappling style.
- The group discusses the 'Gabe theory of micro-optimization,' debating whether to increase base ability scores or rely on magical items like potions of giant strength.
- Thorin, guided by an AI suggestion, settles on the Sharpshooter feat to improve his effectiveness with javelins at long range, with the DM allowing a house rule to apply the stat bonus to strength instead of dexterity.

### Tactical Synergies and Final Decisions
#### The party finalizes their level-up choices and discusses new combat synergies, particularly around grappling and area-of-effect spells.
- Daz and Zalthir coordinate a strategy to use the Cloud of Daggers spell on enemies held in place by a grapple, ensuring continuous damage.
- The group clarifies that a grapple does not place the grappler in the same five-foot space as the target, meaning area-of-effect spells can be placed on the grappled enemy without harming the grappler.
- The party discusses the limits of Cloud of Daggers, confirming that a creature can only take the damage once per turn.
- Zalthir works through the action economy of using rope in combat — using his bonus action to grapple and his action to bind the target with rope, consuming his full turn.
- The group jokingly considers renaming themselves the 'Ember Grapple' due to their newfound focus on restraining tactics.
- Thorin plans to acquire javelins at the next opportunity to take advantage of his new Sharpshooter training.
- The DM confirms the party will not receive a long rest at this time, as no combats are imminent.
