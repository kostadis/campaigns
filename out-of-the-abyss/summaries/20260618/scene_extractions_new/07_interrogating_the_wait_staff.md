# Interrogating the Wait Staff

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, presence-corrected)

#### Thorin interviews the remaining junior avowed who served the Deadwinter's Eve supper to uncover suspicious behavior.
- Sprig "gushes in great detail about the 12 delicious dishes," insisting she "enthusiastically volunteered to be a taster of each of the dishes" despite the chefs telling her it was unnecessary — earning Daral's commendation as a "best practice going forward."
- Irony's rolled-eyes remark that "these avowed, sometimes they're a little bit too interested in the junior avowed" points Thorin toward a romantic subplot.
- Sprig reveals that Great Reader Teles Ahvoste has been making unwanted advances toward Irony, and that Orrin "notices everything, even if he hears nothing."
- Thorin questions Orrin Glass, a waiter deaf in one ear and "a bit of an oddball."
- Orrin reveals that Alkrist "seemed to drop a small scrap of paper that he had been holding towards the candle upon seeing two junior members of the [avowed] arrive[]."
- Orrin notes that the string on Janussi's gift "had clearly been cut and retried," confirming the package was tampered with while under the tree — landing Thorin on the conclusion that "whatever happened, happened under the tree."
