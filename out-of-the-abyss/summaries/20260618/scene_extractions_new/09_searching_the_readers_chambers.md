# Searching the Readers' Chambers

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, presence-corrected)

#### Zalthir and Glabbagool conduct a stealthy search of several high-ranking officials' rooms to find evidence of the murder.
- The search runs as a series of escalating skill checks (DCs roughly 12, 16, 20, 24), with Glabbagool aiding Zalthir — "we're going on a secret mission!" — and the two functioning as "Voltron."
- Zalthir searches A'lai Aivenmore's chambers — "sparsely but tastefully decorated, including several fine pieces of glassware and ceramics. A large mirror and a portrait of himself" — and finds a cage sized for five messenger doves with only two remaining; the feeding and droppings confirm "there were more than 2."
- In Alkrist's lavishly decorated quarters, Zalthir finds his spellbook, "several tightly sealed glass jars containing dead rats, their skin blistered and [pustulated]," and a locked safe (left unopened — Glabbagool could not fit through any opening less than an inch).
- Zalthir finds "1001 Tashalian Nights," bound in black leather with stars and gold filigree, bookmarked to the tale of the commander poisoned by a gift coated in Midnight Tears. (Insight 18 with Glabbagool's aid revealed the find; a sleight-of-hand check let it be set down without Alkrist noticing.)
- Zalthir and Glabbagool investigate Bookwyrm's chambers — "spacious... occupying half of the top floor of the Readers' Tower... comfortable and neatly arranged without being extravagant" — but a failed final check (a DC 20) turns up nothing of significant interest, and Glabbagool detects no poison.
