# The Rivalry Revealed

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party questions their assistant Fembris Lancer about Janussi's professional enemies and learns of a long-standing feud.
- Fembris Lancer identifies Daral Yashenti as Janussi's primary rival, noting the two have been at each other's throats for years.
- The party learns that Daral was the same person who dismissively referred to them as "sell swords" earlier.
- Fembris confirms that Janussi held one of the two keys to the Hightower Library.
- The party decides to seek out the housekeeper, Hollypocket, to learn more about the victim's daily routine and the missing locket.
