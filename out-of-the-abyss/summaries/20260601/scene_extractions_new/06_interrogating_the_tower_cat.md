# Interrogating the Tower Cat

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### Daz uses the Stonespeaker Crystal to communicate with Queenie, with Zalthir and the party speaking through him, the tower's resident cat, to gather clues about the night of the murder.
- Daz uses the power of the Stonespeaker Crystal to speak with Queenie, a five-year-old female cat residing in the tower, while Zalthir and the others speak through him.
- Queenie reveals that Sylvira Savikas blasted an armchair with magic missiles earlier in the morning during what appears to have been a lover's quarrel with Janussi.
- The cat describes seeing Fheminor Scrivenbark, the "short-legged one," arrive at the chamber in the evening, followed later by Daral, the "stinky breath one."
- Queenie recalls seeing Daral flee the room carrying a "hypnotic block," which the party identifies as the missing book — *The Golden Ass*.
- The cat mentions seeing a figure that looked like Sylvira leaving the tower going downstairs, but notes the person did not smell like her, suggesting a magical disguise or shapeshifter.
- Daz rolls an Insight check (18) and presses Queenie on the "hypnotic block" — she clarifies: "Every time they open that block, they just sit there and don't tickle!" — confirming it’s a book, not a device.
- The party learns from Hollypocket that Janussi always wore a locket on his person.
- Fembris informs the party that Kalan has the other key to the Hightower Library.
- Queenie concludes the interview by saying: "You may now leave, and please give yourself a proper licking before returning to my tower," before walking off.
