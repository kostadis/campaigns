# Session — 2026-04-05

## Overview
The Ember Vanguard concluded their assault on Velkynvelve in a climactic battle against Ilvara, the Zuggtmoy-corrupted drow priestess. After securing an alliance with Asha Vandree through an elaborate (if imperfect) deception, the party laid traps and launched a coordinated assault. Zalthir opened with a devastating aerial grapple that sent Ilvara plummeting forty feet, Thorin annihilated the Big Heart Fungus with Dawnbringer's radiant energy in a single round, and Daz incinerated an unexpected House T'sarran strike team that emerged mid-battle. The fight turned chaotic as Ilvara summoned spore servants, conjured an insect plague, and unleashed divine fire — while Jorlan Duskryn broke ranks to pursue his personal vendetta and a drow elite named Kaelira pulled Daz from the fray on behalf of House T'sarran agents working with Nym Duskryn. In the end, Grygum's Guiding Bolt struck Ilvara with radiant energy she proved catastrophically vulnerable to, causing her body to detonate in a massive cloud of poisonous spores. Asha Vandree dropped to her knees in prayer to Lolth as the cavern fell silent.

## Session Events
- **Pre-combat alliance:** Daz used Dancing Lights, his bat familiar (disguised as a spider), and a staged Lolth visitation to approach Asha Vandree. The full deception faltered (multiple low rolls including two natural 5s), but Daz pivoted based on an Insight check (18) and Religion check (18) — he repositioned himself as a delusional magic user who believes he communes with Lolth, and deferred to Asha's local knowledge. Asha accepted the party as useful allies.
- **Asha's intelligence briefing:** Asha revealed Ilvara carries a mushroom artifact from Neverlight Grove that is the mechanism of Zuggtmoy's corruption. She and Jorlan are the only uninfected members of Ilvara's retinue. The Big Heart Fungus does not transmit Zuggtmoy's spore infection — only Ilvara's artifact does. Asha wants Ilvara dead and the mushroom artifact destroyed.
- **Glabbagool** was introduced to Asha as Daz's "ooze familiar"; she was impressed.
- **Trap placement:** Grygum inscribed a Glyph of Warding on a bridge on Jorlan's side of the cavern.
- **Combat opens:** Zalthir teleported adjacent to Ilvara, struck for 16 damage with a grapple, then 15 more damage. He attempted to fly-carry her into Grygum's Dust of Suleiman zone, but Ilvara used a legendary action (fungal meld/teleport) to escape, reappearing elsewhere.
- **Big Heart Fungus** attacked Zalthir with a poison spore cloud (DC 15 CON); Zalthir failed and was Poisoned. He flew 10 feet up to exit the cloud.
- **Ilvara summoned four drow spore servants** (former Velkynvelve guards converted to fungal creatures).
- **Glyph of Warding detonated** when Thorin lured Jorlan into it — 21 damage destroyed one spore servant outright and wounded Jorlan and two others.
- **Thorin engaged Jorlan** with Dawnbringer: hit for 14 damage (natural 20), missed second attack (rolled 2). Applied Sap maneuver giving Jorlan disadvantage on his next attack.
- **Grygum cast Dust of Suleiman** (4th-level) centered on the Big Heart Fungus for 20 force damage.
- **Daz cast Fireball** (4th-level, Sculpt Spells) between Ilvara and the Zuggtmoy icon — Ilvara saved (rolled 19 vs DC 15) for 12 damage; the icon also took 12 damage.
- **Zalthir grappled Ilvara mid-air again**, delivering two critical strikes, then dropped her forty feet. Ilvara burned a legendary action to instill magical fear in Zalthir (Frightened condition, unable to approach her).
- **Big Heart Fungus struck Zalthir** with a massive bludgeoning/poison attack; Zalthir deflected much of the physical damage.
- **Ilvara cast Insect Plague** (20-foot radius sphere) at the cave entrance, hitting Grygum, Daz, and Glabbagool, and creating a visibility barrier.
- **Jorlan Duskryn** broke away from the party to charge at Ilvara independently, driven by personal hatred.
- **Thorin descended into the cavern** and struck the Big Heart Fungus with Dawnbringer — the radiant energy destroyed it in a single round, cutting short what appeared to be an elaborate encounter.
- **Five House T'sarran drow warriors emerged** from the shadows. Their crossbow bolts at Daz missed due to the insect swarm. A drow elite named **Kaelira** grabbed Daz and pulled him through the swarm to safety, stating her mission was to keep him alive.
- **A T'sarran drow mage** fired a Lightning Bolt blindly into the cavern and missed everyone.
- **Daz retaliated** with an empowered Fireball into the darkness, incinerating the T'sarran drow guards and leaving the mage barely standing. Daz then convinced Asha the T'sarran drow were "allies of Lolth."
- **Zalthir**, unable to approach Ilvara due to the Frightened condition, teleported to the T'sarran side and finished off the wounded mage.
- **Ilvara conjured fungal bloom** (difficult terrain) to slow Jorlan, dropped concentration on earlier spells, and cast **Flamestrike** on Daz and Nym Duskryn, scorching both.
- **Nym Duskryn** struck Jorlan with a Lightning Bolt (internal drow politics playing out mid-battle). She asked Kaelira "Can I kill your brother?" after Daz refused to disengage.
- **Thorin destroyed a bridge** beneath Ilvara and Jorlan, collapsing it. Both leapt clear and landed on the cavern floor.
- **Grygum cast Guiding Bolt**, striking Ilvara directly with radiant energy. Her vulnerability to radiant damage caused her to **explode in a massive cloud of poisonous spores**. Zalthir and Thorin were caught in the toxic bloom but endured.
- **The remaining T'sarran elite warrior** retreated, vowing they would meet the party again.
- **Asha Vandree** knelt and began praying to Lolth amid the settling spores.
