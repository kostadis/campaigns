## Succession Contradiction (Major)

**Location**: Summary / Scenes / NPCs (Bookwyrm, A'lai, Alkrist)
**Issue**: The recap contradicts itself on who Janussi named as successor. The Summary and Alkrist's confession say Janussi intended to name **A'lai** as successor (devastating Bookwyrm). But the NPC entry for **A'lai** says he "was rumored to be Janussi's chosen successor but had actually learned he was more likely to be exiled than elevated," and Bookwyrm's entry says she "was distraught at being passed over for Keeper of Tomes in favor of A'lai." Meanwhile the "full picture" summary says Janussi "threatened A'lai with exile rather than elevation." These cannot all be simultaneously coherent as stated.
**Evidence**: Recap internal: Bookwyrm passed over "in favor of A'lai" vs. A'lai "more likely to be exiled than named Keeper."
**Suggested fix**: Reconcile — likely Bookwyrm *believed* A'lai was favored, while A'lai himself feared exile. State this perception gap explicitly rather than as fact.
