# Voice Critique — Zalthir, scene 06: Glabbagool's Trial at the Whispering Dome

**Narration:** `summaries/20260608/narration/session_doc_scene_06_glabbagool_s_trial_at_the_whispering_dome.scrubbed.md`
**Voice spec:** `voice/zalthir_voice.md`
**Per-char examples:** `examples/zalthir.md`

## Flags

### [1] Over-philosophized double-simile

> "Not paused. Still — the way the surface of deep water is still, the way a thing is still when nothing inside it is wasted."

**Why:** Zalthir observes, categorizes, moves. The distinction "Not paused. Still." is already doing the work — it's a classic Zalthir declarative split. The two-part simile that follows it breaks the rhythm he just established and philosophizes in a register ("nothing inside it is wasted") that reads more like a monastic koan than Zalthir's terse notation. He'd mark the distinction and let it stand.
**Suggested rewrite:** "Not paused. Still. The difference matters. Paused costs something; still costs nothing."

### [2] Vocabulary bleed from Daz

> "What I felt, watching him arrange himself over my scales, was something I did not have a clean column for."

**Why:** "Column" is Daz's bookkeeping vocabulary — used three times across sc01 and sc05 ("Both columns stayed open," "I sorted him into the column marked…"). Zalthir buries feelings in tactical observations and categories, but he does not use financial-ledger terminology. This is direct convergence with a specific word from a different narrator's register.
**Suggested rewrite:** "What I felt, watching him arrange himself over my scales, was something I did not have a name for." or "…something I had not built a category for."

### [3] Spec-echo language

> "He had survived Blingdenstone and the ooze crisis and however long in the dark, and his instinct, at the moment of his own transformation, was housekeeping. I found that admirable in a way I was not going to say out loud."

**Why:** The closing clause — "I found that admirable in a way I was not going to say out loud" — is reproduced almost verbatim from the voice spec's illustrative example of Zalthir watching Glabbagool look at the sky for the first time. Same sentence structure, same exact phrasing. Both moments are Glabbagool-and-Zalthir beats, so the voice is right; the problem is that the two scenes will read as identical if the narration repeats the spec's example phrase. The surface moment should feel distinct from the sky moment.
**Suggested rewrite:** "His first concern, at the moment of his own transformation, was the drow in the bag. I noted that. Filed it under: *knows what matters.*"

## Verdict

This is the strongest Zalthir scene of the two — the opening (quiet room, occupied by people who won't stop making noise), "I want to be precise about that," the monastery callback (Brother Heslin), and "There are, by now, a great many days I was sleeping" are all excellent and exactly in spec. The "column" bleed [2] is the most important fix because it imports a specific vocabulary item from a different narrator. [1] is the second-strongest flag — the double-simile interrupts the declarative rhythm that makes the scene's best lines land.
