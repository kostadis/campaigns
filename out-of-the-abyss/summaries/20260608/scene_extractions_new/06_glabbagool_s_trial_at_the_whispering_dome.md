# Glabbagool's Trial at the Whispering Dome

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### Glabbagool visits the Whispering Dome of Introspection and undergoes his transformation, becoming a shadow monk sidekick bonded to Zalthir.
- Glabbagool makes his way to the Whispering Dome of Introspection, a sacred space where supplicants seek self-knowledge; Zalthir follows.
- A monk poses a philosophical question about the art of movement. Glabbagool goes completely still for roughly sixty seconds.
- After the silence, Glabbagool answers: *"The body that mimics nothing is already shaped by what it watches."* The monk smiles and nods, accepting the answer as wisdom.
- Glabbagool announces: *"I've been waiting to do this for a very long time"* — and flows up onto Zalthir's arm, declaring a *"Glabbagool Zalthir combo action."*
- Glabbagool becomes an eighth-level sidekick bonded to Zalthir, gaining the following abilities: teleport up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space while in darkness; perform a flurry of blows; force his acidic shadow key into a wound; and a corrosive embrace (costs 2 Ki, requires the target to be grappled — Glabbagool bypasses the normal attack sequence to surge over the restrained target, dealing significant acid damage).
- Glabbagool manifests several eyes on Zalthir's arm; Zalthir suggests he might want to blend into his scales rather than appear as a separate creature. Glabbagool notes he thought people expected eyes on him.
- Before settling in, Glabbagool reminds Zalthir that the drow captive in the bag of holding still needs checking on.
- A former shadow monk observing the exchange remarks dryly that Zalthir would have understood exactly what Glabbagool's answer meant had he paid more attention in the relevant training sessions — noting that this had apparently been the day Zalthir was sleeping.
