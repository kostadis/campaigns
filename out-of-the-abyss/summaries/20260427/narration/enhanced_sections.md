**Issue 3 — Glabbagool's creature type (Medium priority):** The recap describes Glabbagool as a "gelatinous ooze." The party document specifies he is a "sentient grey ooze, formerly gelatinous cube." These are distinct creature types. The GM should confirm which description is currently canonical; the party document's wording is preferred for internal consistency.

The Summary separately describes the spy's perception as "sharp enough to notice him."
