# A Grim Awakening

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party is woken early by an acolyte with urgent news of a murder within the library.
- Fembris bangs on the party's door at the House of Rest and the Hearth before dawn, shouting: "Quick! The first reader sent me! The Keeper of Tomes is dead! She wants you! Now, through the emerald door, before the avowed awake!"
- Fembris is described as a commoner — enthusiastic and boyish — visibly out of breath.
- Grygum opens the door, privately relieved that he is not being asked to become the new Keeper of Tomes himself.
- The party had a long rest and is fully refreshed. Grygum notes he has not quite thought through what orcs wear to bed.
- Glabbagool takes stock of the situation and tucks himself into the bag of holding, eyeballs peering out, as the party sets off.

## Verbatim moments

**[The Knock at the Door]**
- The GM describes the party asleep at the House of Rest and the Hearth when someone begins banging on the door.

**GM** — *announcing the disturbance*
> "you wake up in the morning, and somebody's banging at the door."

**Grygum** — *reacting*
> "Banging at the door."

**GM** — *confirming*
> "Yes."

**[Fembris Delivers the News]**
- Grygum opens the door to find Fembris, breathless and urgent.

**GM** — *describing the messenger*
> "he, he, he, he says, he says... And he says... That you must head… we must head to the Emerald… we must head to meet the… with the bookworm. Something terrible has happened."

**GM** — *voicing Fembris's opening cry*
> "Quick! He's a, he's a, he's, a commoner, he's enthusiastic… no, yeah, commoner, enthusiastic, boyish, He looks very, you know, excited. And he says... The first reader sent me! The Keeper of Tomes is dead! She wants you! Now, through the emerald door, before the avowed awake!"

**Grygum** — *reacting to the news*
> "The Keeper of Tomes?"

**GM** — *clarifying*
> "Tomes, tomes. It's one of the chief readers."

**Grygum** — *processing*
> "Oh."

**[Grygum's Private Relief]**

**Grygum** — *aside, OOC*
> "Gergamum has this lurking suspicion that he's going to be asked to become the new Keeper of Tomes, and he's preparing to decline politely."

**[The Party Stirs]**

**Grygum** — *confirming they follow*
> "Let's… let's go. Alright. I, I… I don't know. put on my clothes, or whatever it is, I have not… Thought about what to… orcs were to bed, so…"

**Grygum** — *checking rest status*
> "I'm going to guess that we had a long rest."

**GM** — *confirming*
> "Yes, everybody had a long rest."

**[Glabbagool Joins the Party]**
- As the group sets off, Glabbagool reacts to the commotion.

**GM** — *narrating Glabbagool's action*
> "Glabbagool sort of looks and kind of says, jumps into your bag of holding, eyeballs out."
