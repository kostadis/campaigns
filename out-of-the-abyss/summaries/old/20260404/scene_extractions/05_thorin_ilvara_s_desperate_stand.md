**[Thorin Watches the Chaos Unfold]**
Thorin: "And I'm just sitting here watching trade blows like a tennis match."
Thorin thinks outloud:  "This is different, usually I'm the one giving the blows, but everybody else here seems to be doing it."

Zalthir seized Ilvara in midair and rained blows upon her before releasing his grip entirely, sending her plummeting forty feet to the stone below. Ilvara struck the ground hard and immediately flooded Zalthir's mind with supernatural terror, leaving him frozen in place. The heart fungus lashed out at the ceiling with thunderous limbs. Jorlan Duskryn broke from the party and charged toward Ilvara with fury in his eyes. Drow warriors emerged from the shadows at the cavern's edge, blocked by the churning insect swarm Ilvara had conjured at the entrance.

The cavern floor was a sprawl of difficult terrain, elevated walkways, and enemies who never stayed still — and Thorin was at the center of all of it with nowhere to swing.

---

**[Thorin Descends and Dawnbringer Ends It]**

Thorin descended from the elevated walkways and plunged into the difficult terrain below, driving straight for the heart fungus. Dawnbringer's radiant strikes crackled through the massive creature, which recoiled under the holy light before wilting entirely and collapsing in on itself. An elaborate encounter the GM had prepared died in a single round.

The vast fungal shape that had dominated the cavern floor simply folded and fell silent, leaving the chamber suddenly, starkly emptier.

---

**[The Bridge Question]**
Thorin: "Is the bridge undead?"

Thorin turned his attention to the bridge beneath Ilvara's feet and struck it with enough force to send it crashing down. Ilvara and Jorlan Duskryn both leapt clear of the collapse and landed on the cavern floor below — shaken but unbroken.

Destroying the terrain itself was the only way left to close the distance, and it cost Ilvara the high ground she had held since the fight began.
