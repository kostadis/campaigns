[Scene 1] The Mercenary Contract
Narrator: Daz
Focus: Daz is the subject of the mercenary contract's mystery, and the revelation that an unknown benefactor paid a fortune to keep him alive strikes at the core of his uncertain identity and apostate's existence.

- The party discovers that two Drow mercenaries, Nym and Kaelira Duskryn, were hired in Menzoberranzan to ensure Daz's survival.

- Nym and Kaelira Duskryn reveal their contract was specifically to protect Daz from Ilvara of House Mizzrym and the forces of House T'sarran.

<!-- Pausing, then addressing Daz directly -->
Nym: "Daz, I understand why your concern for your safety existed."

<!-- On whether the mercenaries need to protect Daz from Asha -->
Nym: "If Daz can't protect himself from a low-level cult initiate of Lolth, then quite frankly, whoever paid for his survival deserves to lose his money."

<!-- When asked who contracted them -->
Drow: "I don't know. The money was provided to us in Menzoberranzan. We took the money, and that was it."


- Daz attempts to negotiate for continued services, but the mercenaries explain their current contract is fulfilled and further service is beyond the party's means.


<!-- Pressing the mercenaries on the terms of their contract -->
Daz: "What is the terms of your contract, just to make sure that I... am alive, or that I won this particular battle?"

<!-- When Mike/Daz asks what the terms of their contract were -->
Nym: "Our terms of our contract were to make sure that the other drow factions didn't get you killed."

<!-- When Daz pushes about whether all threats have been eliminated -->
Nym: "You really want us to hold your hand until the exit tunnel a few miles down the road?"

<!-- When Joe/Thorin asks what it would cost to retain them -->
Nym: "That is a fee... that is a price you cannot afford."

<!-- Clarifying the two specific threats named in their contract -->
Kaelira: "There is a psychotic drow of House Mizzrym, and there is House T'sarran... We were told to protect you from those two groups. We hoped that you'd be smart enough to avoid Ilvara... well, we could have handled House T'sarran and whatever goons they threw at you. But you were able to do it quite on your own. Bravo, bravo, bravo."


<!-- Making the case for why the mercenaries shouldn't leave yet -->
Daz: "If you haven't eliminated all the other factions, and I go and get myself killed in the next 15 minutes, and you guys go and collect on the bond, and then your employer finds out — it's not gonna be a good day for you."

- The mercenaries express disdain for Asha Vandree, claiming she is a minor threat that Daz should be able to handle himself.
<!-- On why they're unconcerned about Asha Vandree -->
Nym: "You could probably kill her in a few seconds."
<!-- Diplomatically thanking the mercenaries -->
Daz: "Hey guys, thanks for the help."

- The party discusses the implications of noble house intrigue and why Daz would be worth such a high price to an unknown benefactor.


<!-- After the party shows the mercenaries the burnt extraction contract, Kaelira questions why Daz would command such a high protection fee -->
Kaelira: "That is an excellent question. You don't belong to any noble house, your background is a complete mystery, you suddenly show up, and all of a sudden, we're contracted to protect a nobody. Do you know how much money that required us to get, for us to do? A lot. Like, they interrupt you. A lot."

<!-- Responding to Kaelira's dismissal -->
Daz: "So then maybe there's more to me than meets the eye?"

<!-- Sardonically deflecting Daz's suggestion of hidden importance -->
Kaelira: "Yes, yes, yes, that's what every orphan thinks. Do you have daddy issues? Do you have mommy issues? Every drow thinks there's some noble things. For all we know, this was a game being played by the houses."

<!-- When pressed further on the implications of the contract -->
Kaelira: "Perhaps, but we're not paid to think that hard."

<!-- Proposing the party investigate the mystery of his background together -->
Daz: "I literally have no idea why there's all this focus on me."

<!-- After the party agrees to investigate, making a characteristically dry joke -->
Daz: "You know what would help? To audit their books."

<!-- On the party recognizing their own capture may have been about Daz all along -->
Daz thinks: "For all you know, the reason this all got started was because they all decided to be friends with the wrong drow."
