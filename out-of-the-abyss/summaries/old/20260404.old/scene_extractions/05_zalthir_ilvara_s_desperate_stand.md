**Ilvara Conjures Fungal Bloom to Slow Jorlan**
Zalthir, still locked in the grip of supernatural fear and unable to close the distance to Ilvara, watched from across the battlefield as the priestess reshaped the terrain around her. The scarred drow warrior, burning with personal vendetta, found his advance mired in the slick, writhing bloom.
