# Farewell to Eldeth

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party enjoys the sunlight of the surface world as Eldeth prepares to depart for Mithril Hall and Gauntlgrym.
- The party celebrates their escape from the Underdark and enjoys being on the surface, with Glabbagool staring up at the sun with visible emotion.
- Zalthir reflects on what it meant to help Glabbagool reach the surface, contemplating the peculiar relationship with the ooze.
- The DM notes that Zalthir had "offered" (in some sense) to teach Glabbagool the ways of a monk — Glabbagool is apparently very much looking forward to the "run, run, slap, slap, slap," unaware that taking a single step would be a good start given how slowly he moves.
- Thorin observes that Glabbagool does have some advantages as a prospective monk: he is unpredictable, has no fixed appendages, and could theoretically land 12 hits in a round.
- Zalthir briefly entertains the idea of becoming an ooze himself, which the DM firmly shuts down.
- Zalthir also asks if Glabbagool could be "equipped" as a weapon, which is also firmly denied.
- Eldeth informs the party that she must travel to Mithril Hall first to explain to the king what is going on in the Underdark, and asks Thorin not to follow until she has spoken.
- Thorin asks if there is any way to petition to join Eldeth's clan, or whether a hazing ritual might be involved.
- Eldeth swears sincerely: "Let me go first to Mithril Hall. After I have spoken and explained to the lords of Gauntlgrym and Mithril Hall the dangers of the Underdark, I am certain they will want to speak to you, and I will make it a statement of purpose that you shall be embraced by our clan."
- Thorin responds: "I'm taking this seriously. I don't think you're just being socially nice here. I think you really do want me to." Eldeth confirms with a nod and a handshake.
- Dawnbringer throws a hissy fit at being described as "a really cool sword who's kind of annoying sometimes"; Thorin acknowledges her, saying "I'm annoying also, Dawnbringer, look, you know, we got each other."
- The DM notes that Eldeth also wanted to go first so that Thorin does not become the topic of conversation rather than the danger in the Underdark — she gives Zalthir a knowing look at this, aware that a drow traveling with a dwarf raised by giants could easily derail the briefing.
- Spiderbait cheerfully announces that he hasn't been on the surface in his whole life and intends to explore, confident goblins are "totally integrated into society" and that nobody would attack a goblin for being a goblin.
- The party gently corrects him; Grygum notes "the state of goblin equality and inclusion is rough at the moment."
- Spiderbait asks if he can just stick with the party and find work at Candlekeep doing something innocuous, like making food. Grygum endorses this plan. The DM notes that Spiderbait is, in fact, terrible at making food.
- Glabbagool is happy to stay with the party and continue west.
