# Voice Critique — Daz, scene 03: A Grim Awakening

**Narration:** `summaries/20260518/gm-assist-doc.md` (section 3)
**Voice spec:** `voice/daz_voice.md`
**Per-char examples:** `examples/daz.md`

## Flags

### [1] Purple-prose flourish

> ...the kind that had felt almost suspicious after the Underdark, where a full night's sleep was a negotiation with the geological hostility of your surroundings.

**Why:** Daz's spec is "measured, categorical, half-step removed" — the voice that says "I will need area-of-effect spells" and "I have an alternative proposal." "Negotiation with the geological hostility of your surroundings" is the *opposite* register: long, abstract, ornamental. The sentence is also reaching for an aphorism Daz wouldn't bother with — he'd note the surprise of being rested and move to the next column.
**Suggested rewrite:** "...the kind that had felt almost suspicious after the Underdark, where a full night's sleep had been a luxury good and rarely available. Candlekeep's beds had done their work."

### [2] Stock closer

> He flowed toward the bag, arranged himself inside it, and peered out from the opening with two eyeballs tracking the room. Ready. Committed. Thoroughly himself about the whole business.

**Why:** "Thoroughly himself about the whole business" is a workshop sign-off — vague approval that doesn't *categorize* anything. Daz's voice prefers a tactical note over a warm one. The verbatim Daz tic from the chapter-1 example — *"At least we have that"* — does land in the very next sentence, which is correct; the flourish just before it weakens the contrast.
**Suggested rewrite:** "He flowed toward the bag, arranged himself inside it, and peered out from the opening with two eyeballs tracking the room. Equipped. The spy somewhere inside him, presumably also equipped."

### [3] Mild convergence pattern — see summary

> I heard his voice land somewhere between inquiry and bracing for bad news.

**Why:** Cute but reaches for the same "X land[s] somewhere between Y and Z" construction that turns up across scenes. Daz's voice would either name the tone or note the specific thing about Grygum's voice that gave it away. Flagged as light because it's the only instance in this scene.
**Suggested rewrite:** "I heard Grygum at the door. The voice he uses when he's already deciding what to bring."

## Verdict

The strongest-voiced scene in the doc — "I was already cataloguing before the sentence finished," "That clause was doing a great deal of work," "the running account in the back of my head was already sorting columns," and the *at least we have that* callback are all unimprovable. Only one real purple-prose lapse; spot-edit and ship.
