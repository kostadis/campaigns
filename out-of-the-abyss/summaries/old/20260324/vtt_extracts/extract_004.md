## Events

- The party finalized their tactical plan for assaulting Ilvara's fungal cavern, with Grygum lending Thorin a light crossbow to compensate for his low DEX and lack of ranged options.
- Zalthir placed a zone of darkness behind the party's position (not near the cavern) to avoid alerting Ilvara during preparations.
- Combat began with initiative rolls; Ilvara rolled 16, Daz rolled 4.
- Zalthir teleported directly to Ilvara, struck her with advantage for 16 damage, grappled her, hit again for 15 more damage, then attempted to fly her over the Heart Fungus into the area of Grygum's planned Dust of Suleiman.
- Ilvara used a legendary action to melt into a fungal patch and reappear elsewhere in the cavern, screaming in fury, escaping Zalthir's grapple.
- The Heart Fungus lashed out at Zalthir, forcing a DC 15 Constitution save, which he failed; he became poisoned while within the spore cloud area.
- Zalthir flew 10 feet up to escape the poisonous spore cloud, ending the poison effect.
- Ilvara summoned spore walkers — reanimated drow guards the party recognized from their former prison — from the ground near the party's position.
- Thorin taunted Jorlan ("come get it, idiot"), luring him directly into Grygum's pre-set Glyph of Warding, which detonated with a 20-foot radius.
- The Glyph of Warding explosion dealt 21 damage to Jorlan (failed DEX save), destroyed one spore walker outright, left another at 3 HP, and dealt 21 damage to the Heart Fungus (also failed DEX save). One spore walker succeeded its save and took 10 damage.
- Thorin moved to engage Jorlan in melee combat with Dawnbringer.

## NPC Interactions

- **Ilvara**: Howled in frustration after escaping Zalthir's grapple, then commanded the Heart Fungus to attack Zalthir and summoned spore walkers from the ground. She had been preoccupied with her Zuggtmoy mushroom token, ignoring the party during the dialogue phase.
- **Asha Vandry**: Moved into a support position intending to cast spells in support of the party's attacks. Previously confirmed the Heart Fungus does not have the power to convert/take over people (that's the small mushroom artifact).
- **Jorlan**: Was throwing a fit due to anger at Ilvara. Was lured by Thorin's taunt directly into Grygum's Glyph of Warding trap, taking 21 damage and appearing very confused.
- **Spore Walkers**: Recognized by the party as former drow prison guards who followed Ilvara, were converted into drowspores, and are now controlled by her. Described as fungal creatures (not undead), moving with a mix of zombie-like slowness and sudden drow dexterity. Four were summoned; one was immediately destroyed by the glyph explosion.
- **Glabbagool**: Not directly interacted with during this portion.

## PC Actions & Decisions

- **Thorin (Joe Beda)**: Borrowed Grygum's light crossbow for ranged capability. Discussed whether to attack the Heart Fungus directly, noting his blindsight within 10 feet would negate any blindness effects from spore clouds. Taunted Jorlan into Grygum's trap. Chose to engage Jorlan in melee rather than the spore walkers or the Heart Fungus.
- **Zalthir (Gabe)**: Teleported to Ilvara, struck her twice (16 + 15 = 31 total damage), grappled her, attempted to fly her over the Heart Fungus for area-of-effect damage. Failed DC 15 CON save against poison spores. Flew up 10 feet to escape the spore cloud. Placed darkness zone behind the party during prep (not near the cavern, per DM ruling).
- **Grygum (Ben Pfaff)**: Confirmed Dust of Suleiman spell details. Lent Thorin the light crossbow and offered poisoned crossbow bolts (declined). His pre-set Glyph of Warding triggered successfully on Jorlan and nearby spore walkers, dealing 21 or 10 damage (DEX save for half).
- **Daz (Mike Hall)**: Rolled a 4 on initiative. Noted he couldn't fireball if Thorin was standing near the Heart Fungus. Otherwise held action for later in the round.

## Out-of-Character Notes

- DM (Kostadis) confirmed the spore walkers are fungal creatures, NOT undead — relevant for Thorin's Dawnbringer and other undead-specific abilities.
- DM clarified Heart Fungus poison spore cloud mechanics: persists until the start of the Heart Fungus's next turn; requires DC 15 CON save; causes poisoned condition while in the area; can be escaped by leaving the area (e.g., flying 10 feet up); Heart Fungus can regenerate the cloud on its turn.
- DM clarified smaller spore clouds around spore walkers: triggered on a D6 roll of 5 or 6, cause blindness for one round. Thorin's blindsight negates this within 10 feet.
- DM confirmed Dust of Suleiman targets a chosen creature type and won't harm the party.
- DM ruled that placing darkness near the cavern during prep would have alerted Ilvara; Zalthir agreed to place it behind the party instead.
- Multiple layer/map management issues on the virtual tabletop caused brief confusion about token positions and the poison cloud display.
