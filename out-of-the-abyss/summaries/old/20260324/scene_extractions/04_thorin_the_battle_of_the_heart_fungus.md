**[Zalthir Teleports to Ilvara — The Assault Begins]**
Zalthir teleported directly to Ilvara's position, striking her with a furious flurry of blows—landing the first hit for 16 damage with advantage, then grappling her and striking again for 15 more. He attempted to fly her bodily toward the Heart Fungus, intending to drop her into the area where Grygum's magic could ravage her.
The opening salvo was devastating—31 damage in moments—and Thorin watched from across the cavern as their plan roared to life.

---

**[Ilvara's Fungal Escape]**
Just as Zalthir hauled Ilvara over the Heart Fungus, she dissolved. Her body melted into a fungal patch beneath them and she reappeared elsewhere in the cavern, screaming in fury. The grapple was broken, the plan foiled by a legendary ability no one had anticipated.
The ease with which she slipped away was a cold reminder that this was no ordinary priestess.

---

**[The Heart Fungus Strikes Back]**
The massive Heart Fungus lashed out at Zalthir, releasing a choking cloud of poisonous spores. Zalthir failed the DC 15 Constitution save and was poisoned, gasping in the haze before managing to fly ten feet upward and escape the cloud.
The fungus was not just terrain—it was a weapon, and it had drawn first blood against the party's strongest fighter.

---

**[Ilvara Summons the Spore Walkers]**
Ilvara summoned four drow spore servants from the ground—former Velkynvelve prison guards, now shambling fungal creatures rising from the muck and earth of the cavern floor. They emerged around the battlefield, closing in on the party's positions.
The cavern, already hostile, was suddenly teeming with enemies.

---

**[Thorin Taunts Jorlan into the Trap]**
Thorin: "Come get it, idiot!"
Jorlan, the bitter, lovelorn drow warrior, charged forward—directly onto the bridge where Grygum had inscribed the Glyph of Warding. The explosion detonated perfectly, dealing 21 damage to Jorlan (who failed his save), destroying one spore servant outright, leaving another at 3 HP, and dealing 21 damage to the Heart Fungus itself.
Nobody expected the taunt to work so cleanly; the trap turned the opening round of combat decisively in the party's favor.

---

**[Thorin Engages Jorlan with Dawnbringer]**
Thorin waded into melee with the wounded Jorlan, swinging the blazing sun-blade Dawnbringer. The first strike hit true for 14 damage, and Thorin executed a Sap maneuver, giving Jorlan disadvantage on his next attack. The second swing went wide—Thorin rolled a 2, and even his Precision Attack couldn't overcome Jorlan's AC 18.
Dawnbringer's radiance lit the fungal gloom, and Jorlan staggered under the weight of the first blow, but the miss left the drow still standing—battered but not broken.

---

**[A Spore Walker Climbs Up to Attack Thorin]**
Ilvara used a legendary action to command one of her spore servants to attack Thorin. The shambling fungal creature climbed up and swung—its first attack was a natural 1, a wild miss—but the second connected, dealing 5 damage to Thorin.
A glancing hit, but the spore walkers were closing in, and Thorin was now caught between Jorlan and the risen dead.

---

**[Grygum Drops the Dust of Suleiman on the Heart Fungus]**
Grygum cast Dust of Suleiman centered on the massive Heart Fungus, and 20 points of force damage hammered into the creature with no save. The fungal monstrosity shuddered to its roots, and the sustained spell promised 20 more damage every turn it held.
The Heart Fungus—Ilvara's weapon and anchor of control—was being ground down.

---

**[Daz Shapes a Fireball onto Ilvara and the Mushroom]**
Daz unleashed a 4th-level Fireball, carefully sculpting the flames with Sculpt Spells to engulf both Ilvara and her precious Zuggtmoy mushroom icon while sparing his allies. The blast dealt 24 total damage; Ilvara partially shielded herself (saving for 12 damage), and the mushroom smoldered under 12 points of fire.
The mushroom artifact—the conduit for Zuggtmoy's corruption—was burning, and Ilvara, though she'd saved, was visibly wounded. The tide had unmistakably turned against the corrupted priestess.
