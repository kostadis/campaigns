# Session — The Ilvara Ambush

## Overview

The party engaged in a climactic confrontation with Ilvara at her drow outpost, allying with her rival Asha Vandree through Daz's clever deception. After convincing Asha he was a delusional magic user who believed himself to commune with Lolth, the party learned that Ilvara had been corrupted by Zuggtmoy's influence and was spreading fungal infection through a mushroom artifact. Combat erupted when Zalthir teleported to grapple Ilvara while the party coordinated area-effect spells, but Ilvara proved more dangerous than expected, using legendary actions to escape and summoning drow spore servants to complicate the battlefield.

## Session Events

- Party resumed mid-encounter at the drow outpost with Ilvara on an elevated platform near a Zuggtmoy icon
- Daz executed elaborate deception using bat familiar and Dancing Lights to simulate Lolth visitation for Asha Vandree
- When direct divine claims failed, Daz pivoted to presenting himself as a delusional magic user who genuinely believes he communes with Lolth
- Asha accepted the party as allies and revealed Ilvara's corruption by Zuggtmoy, requesting her death and the destruction of the mushroom artifact
- Party placed Glyph of Warding trap on bridge as defensive measure
- Combat began with Zalthir teleporting to attack and grapple Ilvara (31 total damage)
- Ilvara used legendary action to meld through fungal patches and escape the grapple
- Big Heart Fungus attacked Zalthir with poison spore cloud, inflicting poisoned condition
- Ilvara summoned four drow spore servants from the environment
- Glyph of Warding detonated when Jorlan was lured into it, destroying one spore servant and damaging others
- Thorin engaged Jorlan in melee while Grygum cast Dust of Suleiman on the Big Heart Fungus
- Daz hit both Ilvara and the Zuggtmoy icon with a shaped Fireball
- Session ended mid-combat with multiple combatants active

## NPC Interactions

**Asha Vandree**: Revealed as secret ally who has been avoiding Ilvara's infection by maintaining distance and eating separate food. Explained that only she and Jorlan remain uninfected—she through devotion to Lolth, Jorlan through personal hatred. Impressed by Daz's tactical thinking and accepted Glabbagool as his "ooze familiar." Will enter combat after it begins and defend against outside threats.

**Ilvara**: Primary antagonist in active combat. Demonstrated legendary actions including fungal teleportation to escape grapples. Summoned spore servants and directed their attacks. Has taken approximately 53 damage total.

**Jorlan**: Confirmed as ally of convenience driven by personal animosity toward Ilvara. Caught in Glyph of Warding trap and under disadvantage from Thorin's Sap maneuver.

**Big Heart Fungus**: Supernatural creature using poison spore attacks. Clarified as not transmitting Zuggtmoy's infection—only Ilvara's mushroom artifact does that.
