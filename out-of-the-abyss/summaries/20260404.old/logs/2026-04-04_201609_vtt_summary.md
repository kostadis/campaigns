# Session — 2026-04-04

## Overview
The Ember Vanguard concluded their confrontation with Ilvara at Velkynvelve in a sprawling, multi-phase battle. After Daz successfully brokered a tactical alliance with Asha Vandree through an elaborate (if imperfect) Lolth-deception, the party prepared and launched their assault. Zalthir opened with a devastating aerial grapple on Ilvara, dropping her forty feet to the cavern floor. Thorin carved through the Big Heart Fungus with Dawnbringer's radiant energy, destroying it in a single round. Mid-battle, a squad of House T'sarran drow emerged — including Nym Duskryn and an elite warrior named Kaelira — who pulled Daz out of Ilvara's Insect Plague and positioned themselves as his protectors. Daz smoothly convinced Asha these newcomers were Lolth's servants. Ilvara fought desperately, deploying legendary actions, summoning spore servants, casting Insect Plague and Flamestrike, and magically frightening Zalthir, but the party ground her down. Grygum's Guiding Bolt delivered the killing blow — Ilvara, deeply vulnerable to radiant damage, detonated in a massive cloud of poisonous spores that caught Zalthir and Thorin but did not fell them. The remaining House T'sarran elite retreated with a promise of future confrontation, and Asha Vandree dropped to her knees in prayer to Lolth amid the settling silence.

## Session Events
- **Pre-combat: Daz's deception of Asha Vandree.** Daz used his bat familiar (disguised as a spider), Dancing Lights, and familiar dismissal to simulate a Lolth visitation. After multiple mediocre deception rolls and an Insight check (18), he pivoted from claiming to be Lolth's vessel to presenting himself as a delusional but powerful magic user — which Asha accepted. Alliance secured.
- **Asha briefed the party** on Ilvara's corruption, the Big Heart Fungus's role, the Zuggtmoy mushroom artifact, and which of Ilvara's retinue remain uninfected (only Asha and Jorlan). She clarified the Big Heart Fungus does not transmit Zuggtmoy's spore infection — only Ilvara's mushroom artifact does.

- **Combat began:** Zalthir teleported to Ilvara, struck for 16 damage, grappled her, struck again for 15 damage, then flew her forty feet up and dropped her. Ilvara used a legendary action to break the grapple via fungal meld/teleport, but Zalthir adapted and dropped her anyway (she fell and took additional damage).
- **Ilvara retaliated** with a fear effect on Zalthir (legendary action), leaving him frightened and unable to approach her for much of the fight.
- **Big Heart Fungus** hit Zalthir with a poison spore cloud (DC 15 CON — failed, Poisoned condition). Zalthir flew up to escape.
- **Ilvara summoned four drow spore servants** (former Velkynvelve guards, now fungal creatures).

- **Grygum cast Dust of Suleiman** (4th-level) on the Big Heart Fungus for 20 force damage.
- **Daz cast Fireball** (4th-level, Sculpt Spells) hitting Ilvara (saved, 12 damage) and the Zuggtmoy icon (12 damage).
- **Ilvara cast Insect Plague** at the cave entrance, catching Grygum, Daz, and Glabbagool in a swarm that also blocked line of sight.
- **Jorlan Duskryn** broke away from the party to charge Ilvara independently, driven by personal hatred.
- **Thorin destroyed the Big Heart Fungus** in a single round with Dawnbringer's radiant strikes, cutting short the GM's elaborate encounter design.
- **Five House T'sarran drow emerged** from the shadows. A drow elite warrior (Kaelira) grabbed Daz and pulled him through the Insect Plague to safety, stating they only get paid if he lives. A T'sarran mage (Zalith T'sarran) fired a Lightning Bolt blindly into the cavern and missed.
