## Overview
The Ember Vanguard concluded their confrontation with Ilvara Mizzrym in the fungal cavern of Velkynvelve. After Daz successfully deceived Asha Vandree into accepting the party as allies against Ilvara, the group prepared an ambush using Glyph of Warding and coordinated spell placement. Jorlan was struck by Nym Duskryn's lightning bolt during the chaos. With Ilvara destroyed, the surviving T'sarran elite retreated with a promise of future reckoning, and Asha Vandree dropped to her knees in prayer to Lolth.
