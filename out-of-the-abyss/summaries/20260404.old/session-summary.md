# Session — 2026-04-04

## Overview
The Ember Vanguard concluded their confrontation with Ilvara Mizzrym in the fungal cavern of Velkynvelve. After Daz successfully deceived Asha Vandree into accepting the party as allies against Ilvara, the group prepared an ambush using Glyph of Warding and coordinated spell placement. Zalthir opened with a devastating aerial grapple on Ilvara, dropping her forty feet to the cavern floor and burning a legendary action. Thorin destroyed the Big Heart Fungus in a single round with Dawnbringer's radiant energy, collapsing a major encounter element before it could fully activate. Mid-battle, a squad of House T'sarran drow emerged — including an elite warrior named Kaelen T'sarran and a drow named Nym Duskryn — who pulled Daz from danger, claiming they only get paid if he survives. Daz convinced Asha these newcomers were Lolth loyalists, then turned a fireball on the T'sarran mage and guards when they proved hostile, with Zalthir finishing the wounded mage. Ilvara fought desperately with Insect Plague, Flamestrike, fungal blooms, and fear effects that locked Zalthir out of melee range, but Grygum's Guiding Bolt struck true — exploiting her vulnerability to radiant damage and causing her to detonate in a massive cloud of poisonous spores. Jorlan was struck by Nym Duskryn's lightning bolt during the chaos. With Ilvara destroyed, the surviving T'sarran elite retreated with a promise of future reckoning, and Asha Vandree dropped to her knees in prayer to Lolth.
