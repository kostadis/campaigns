# Chapter 46 — The Ambush at the Fungal Heart

Date: Mar 24th, 2026

## Summary

The party pressed their advantage from the previous session, completing Daz's elaborate deception of Asha Vandree before launching their assault on Ilvara.

Daz's initial gambit — presenting himself as a divine vessel of Lolth — began to unravel under Asha's sharp theological scrutiny. A drow cleric of Lolth, she knew that the Spider Queen does not speak directly through male magic users. Daz pivoted instantly, recasting himself as a delusional wizard who merely believes he communes with Lolth, and presenting his companions as followers who indulge his madness. The adjustment worked. Asha, more interested in useful allies than theological purity, accepted the party and laid out what she knew: Ilvara had been fully corrupted by Zuggtmoy's influence through a mushroom artifact brought back from Neverlight Grove, and the Big Heart Fungus at the center of the cavern was her primary weapon. Asha had survived only by maintaining twenty feet of distance from Ilvara and eating her own food. She wanted Ilvara dead and the mushroom destroyed, and offered the matron's magical items as payment. Glabbagool, introduced as Daz's unusual ooze familiar, drew a moment of surprised admiration from the priestess before the party moved on to planning.

The party used the hour before combat to lay a Glyph of Warding on the bridge on Jorlan's side of the cavern. When combat began, Zalthir teleported directly to Ilvara, struck her twice for thirty-one damage, and locked her in a grapple — intending to carry her into Grygum's Dust of Suleiman. Ilvara used a legendary action to melt into the fungal matter beneath her feet and reappear out of reach, screaming in fury. The Big Heart Fungus responded by erupting a cloud of poison spores that caught Zalthir in its radius; he failed the Constitution save and flew upward ten feet to clear the cloud, poisoned and hovering.

Ilvara summoned four drow spore servants from the cavern floor — former Velkynvelve guards, now mindless fungal automatons. Thorin called out to Jorlan, luring the bitter warrior across the bridge; the Glyph detonated for twenty-one damage, destroying one servant outright and badly wounding Jorlan and two others. Thorin pressed forward and struck Jorlan with Dawnbringer, landing a critical hit, before applying his Sap maneuver to leave Jorlan at disadvantage. Grygum centered Dust of Suleiman on the Big Heart Fungus for twenty force damage. Daz shaped a fourth-level Fireball to land between Ilvara and the Zuggtmoy icon — Ilvara saved but still took twelve damage, and the icon took the same. The session ended mid-combat, the battlefield reshaped but the fight far from over.

## Scenes

### The Deception Completed
#### Daz convinces Asha Vandree to ally against Ilvara by abandoning his claim to divine status.
- Daz recalls the bat familiar to his hand, uses Dancing Lights to simulate a Lolth-light absorbing into his body, then dismisses the familiar — intended as proof of divine communion.
- Asha is skeptical: a drow cleric knows Lolth does not speak through male magic users without priestly intermediaries.
- Daz pivots: he presents himself as a delusional wizard who genuinely believes he communes with Lolth, and his companions as loyal followers.
- Asha rolls Insight, sees through the divine claim but accepts the reframing — a useful madman is still useful.
- Asha reveals Ilvara's corruption: the mushroom artifact from Neverlight Grove is the mechanism; the Big Heart Fungus is Ilvara's primary weapon and must be killed with sustained damage.
- Asha has survived by staying twenty feet from Ilvara and eating separate food; she and Jorlan are the only two of Ilvara's party not yet converted to spore servants.
- She offers Ilvara's magical items as payment, noting they may be contaminated.
- Glabbagool appears. Daz introduces him as his ooze familiar. Asha is impressed.

### Tactical Preparations
#### The party plans the assault and places a Glyph of Warding on the bridge.
- Party discusses options: Glyph of Warding, Dust of Suleiman, Phantasmal Killer, Fireball — all 4th-level slots, only one available for Grygum and Daz each.
- Zalthir notes he can teleport directly to Ilvara or grab her and drop her into a hazard.
- Grygum and Thorin are warned by the GM not to touch the mushroom artifact — both previously encountered Zuggtmoy.
- Thorin borrows Grygum's light crossbow as a ranged backup.
- Grygum inscribes a Glyph of Warding on the bridge on Jorlan's side, triggered to detonate on a hostile creature crossing it.
- Asha confirms she will wait to enter combat until after the party initiates, then defend against outside threats.

### Zalthir Opens the Fight
#### Zalthir teleports to Ilvara, lands two strikes and a grapple, and is foiled by her legendary action.
- Zalthir teleports adjacent to Ilvara on her elevated platform.
- First strike: 16 damage, grapple established.
- Second strike with advantage from the grapple: 15 damage.
- Zalthir attempts to fly-carry Ilvara into the Dust of Suleiman area.
- Ilvara uses a legendary action: she melts into the fungal matter beneath her, moves through it, and reappears elsewhere. The grapple breaks. She screams.

### The Heart Fungus Retaliates
#### The Big Heart Fungus erupts a spore cloud; Zalthir is poisoned and retreats upward.
- The Big Heart Fungus erupts a 20-foot-radius poison spore cloud.
- Zalthir fails the DC 15 Constitution save and is Poisoned (disadvantage on attacks) while in the cloud.
- He flies 10 feet straight up to exit the area.
- Ilvara summons four drow spore servants from the cavern floor — former Velkynvelve guards, now fungal automatons.

### The Glyph Detonates
#### Thorin lures Jorlan across the warded bridge; the trap fires, destroying a spore servant and wounding Jorlan.
- Thorin calls out to Jorlan, drawing his attention.
- Jorlan charges across the bridge on his side of the cavern.
- The Glyph of Warding detonates: 21 damage in a sphere. One spore servant is destroyed outright. Jorlan and two others take 21 damage (Jorlan fails his save; two servants pass and take ~10 damage). One servant survives at 3 HP.
- Thorin engages Jorlan directly: Dawnbringer crits for 14 damage on the first swing; second attack misses.
- Thorin uses the Sap maneuver — Jorlan has disadvantage on his next attack.
- A spore servant uses a legendary action to attack Thorin: natural 1 on the first swing, hits for 5 on the second.

### Concentrated Fire
#### Grygum hits the Big Heart Fungus; Daz shapes a Fireball onto Ilvara and the Zuggtmoy icon.
- Grygum casts Dust of Suleiman centered on the Big Heart Fungus: 20 force damage, no save.
- Daz casts Fireball at 4th level (8d6), using Sculpt Spells to exclude Zalthir and Thorin.
- Centered between Ilvara and the Zuggtmoy icon on her platform.
- Ilvara saves (rolled 19 vs DC 15) and takes 12 damage. The Zuggtmoy icon takes 12 damage.
- Session ends mid-combat.

## NPCs

**Asha Vandree** — Junior drow priestess of Lolth; Ilvara's subordinate and rival. Has survived the fungal infection by isolation and dietary discipline. Motivated by theological disgust at Ilvara's "bride heresy." Agreed to ally with the party and defend against outside threats once combat begins. Will expect Ilvara's magical items to be destroyed or surrendered.

**Ilvara** — Corrupted drow matron, now devoted to Zuggtmoy. Demonstrated legendary actions: fungal meld/teleport to escape grapples, and directing spore servants to attack. Has taken approximately 53 damage this session. Still fighting.

**Jorlan Duskryn** — Ilvara's discarded former lover, driven by personal bitterness. Was lured into the Glyph trap (21 damage, save failed). Currently at disadvantage on his next attack from Thorin's Sap. Technically on the same side as the party against Ilvara, though no formal alliance has been struck.

**Big Heart Fungus** — Supernatural fungal creature central to Ilvara's control of the area. Spore Burst: 20-foot-radius poison cloud, DC 15 CON save, Poisoned condition while within the area; regenerates on a 5–6 on a d6 at start of its turn. Has taken 20 force damage from Dust of Suleiman. Destroying it may have implications beyond removing Ilvara's weapon.

**Drow Spore Servants (x4)** — Former Velkynvelve guards, now fungal automatons. Not undead — fungal creatures. One destroyed by the Glyph. One at 3 HP. Two at approximately half health. One directed to attack Thorin.

**Glabbagool** — Sentient gelatinous cube, traveling with the party. Introduced to Asha as Daz's ooze familiar. Told to stay quiet and pose as a rock. Struggled with this.

## Locations

**Ilvara's Fungal Outpost** — A cavern with elevated stone platforms connected by narrow rope bridges over pools of difficult terrain (3–4 feet deep). Poisonous vents cycle open and shut beneath the bridges. The Big Heart Fungus occupies the center. Ilvara's platform holds the Zuggtmoy mushroom icon. Asha's platform is separated from Ilvara's. Jorlan's approach is via the warded bridge.

## Items

**Zuggtmoy Mushroom Icon** — Small green mushroom token carried by Ilvara, source of the fungal corruption spreading through her followers. Took 12 fire damage from Daz's Fireball. Must be destroyed to prevent another from picking it up.

**Glyph of Warding** — Inscribed by Grygum on the bridge leading from Jorlan's side; detonated for 21 damage in a 20-foot sphere.

**Dawnbringer** — Thorin's radiant longsword; used to strike Jorlan (critical hit, 14 damage).
