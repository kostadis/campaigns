# Session — 2026-03-24

## Overview
The party returned to Velkynvelve in a bold offensive move, making contact with Asha Vandree — Ilvara's subordinate and secret rival — and forging a temporary alliance against their long-standing drow pursuer. Daz executed a two-phase deception using his bat familiar and Dancing Lights to simulate a Lolth visitation, pivoting smoothly when Asha showed skepticism, and ultimately winning her cooperation by presenting himself as a useful delusional magic user with tactical judgment. Asha revealed that Ilvara has been corrupted by a Zuggtmoy mushroom artifact from Neverlight Grove and is actively spreading fungal infection through the outpost; she and Jorlan are the only two uninfected members of Ilvara's retinue. With a Glyph of Warding laid and tactical options debated, the party engaged Ilvara in full combat — Zalthir teleporting in to grapple her, Thorin luring Jorlan into the glyph trap, Grygum hammering the Big Heart Fungus with Dust of Suleiman, and Daz unleashing a sculpted Fireball — before the session ended mid-combat with Ilvara having taken significant damage but still standing.

---

## Session Events

- Daz approached Asha Vandree using his bat familiar (posed as a spider) and Dancing Lights to simulate a Lolth communion; Asha was skeptical, suspecting either a test of her faith or a charlatan.
- Daz rolled Religion 18, recalled that drow clerics distrust magic users claiming direct Lolth communion without priestly intermediaries, and pivoted: presented himself as a delusional magic user who *believes* he communes with Lolth, with the party as his followers.
- Daz rolled Insight 18; understood Asha wanted an ally, not proof of divinity. The adjusted approach succeeded and Asha accepted the alliance.
- Asha briefed the party: Ilvara is carrying a mushroom artifact from Neverlight Grove that is the mechanism of her corruption and is spreading Zuggtmoy's spore infection to her allies. The Big Heart Fungus in the cavern is Ilvara's primary area-control weapon. Asha has maintained 20-foot distance from Ilvara and eaten her own food to avoid infection.
- Asha identified herself and Jorlan as the only two uninfected members of Ilvara's party — Asha through faithful devotion to Lolth, Jorlan through personal hatred of Ilvara.
- Asha offered Ilvara's magical items as reward, noting they may be contaminated; agreed to rush in once combat begins and defend the party against additional attackers.
- Party debated tactics: Glyph of Warding (placed on the bridge on Jorlan's side of the cavern), Dust of Suleiman on the Heart Fungus, Phantasmal Killer, and Fireball discussed; all major options identified as 4th-level with only one slot available per caster.
- Glabbagool introduced to Asha as Daz's "ooze familiar"; she accepted this and was impressed.
- Combat initiated. Zalthir placed Darkness off to the side, teleported adjacent to Ilvara, struck for 16 damage, established a grapple, struck again for 15 damage, and attempted to fly-carry Ilvara into Dust of Suleiman range.
- Ilvara used a legendary action to meld into a fungal patch and reappear elsewhere, breaking the grapple. She screamed in fury.
- The Big Heart Fungus released a poison spore cloud (DC 15 CON save, 20-foot radius); Zalthir failed, was Poisoned, and flew 10 feet up to exit the area.
- Ilvara summoned four drow spore servants (former Velkynvelve prison guards, converted to fungal creatures).
- Thorin called out to Jorlan, luring him into the Glyph of Warding on the bridge; the glyph detonated for 21 damage. One spore servant was destroyed outright; Jorlan and two others were caught in the blast (one failed the save, two passed for half). Jorlan survived.
- Thorin attacked Jorlan with Dawnbringer: natural 20 on the first attack for 14 damage; second attack missed. Used the Sap maneuver — Jorlan has disadvantage on his next attack.
- A spore servant used a legendary-action-directed attack against Thorin, rolling a natural 1 on its first attack and hitting on the second for 5 damage.
- Grygum cast Dust of Suleiman (4th level) centered on the Big Heart Fungus for 20 force damage (no saving throw).
- Daz cast Fireball at 4th level (Sculpt Spells, up to 3 targets excluded) centered between Ilvara and the Zuggtmoy icon. Ilvara saved (rolled 19 vs. DC 15) and took 12 damage. The Zuggtmoy icon also took 12 damage.
- Session ended mid-combat. Ilvara has taken approximately 53 total damage (16 + 15 from Zalthir, 12 from Fireball). Jorlan has taken 21+ damage. The Big Heart Fungus has taken 20 damage.

- **Daz holds the con together through three consecutive crumbles.** The possession angle fails. The Lolth angle fails. Asha invents her own explanation — "confused vessel" — and Daz, without missing a beat, adopts it and uses it to introduce the rest of the party as proof of his power. - **Grygum baits Jorlan into a glyph trap with four words and no plan.** "Come get it, idiot." worked. The surprise in his own voice after the explosion was more entertaining than any rehearsed taunt would have been.

- **The Fireball-and-Dust-of-Suleiman combination exceeds all reasonable expectations.** Grygum's post-combat assessment — *"That worked better than I had any right to expect"* — doubles as an accurate description of the session's tactical throughline.

- **Glabbagool attempts to masquerade as a rock.** He talks. His eyes move. He needs to be corrected on both. Daz manages him with the practiced calm of someone who has had this exact problem before.
