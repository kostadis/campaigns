# Session 4 Improv Log — 2026-06-17

Running notes from mid-session improv. Not canon until confirmed.

---

## The Stonehill Inn — Morning

Party met Lanar, Narth, and Freda as prepped. A fourth NPC appeared — invented on the spot.

**Marto** — broad jaw, two-day stubble, dressed as a laborer. Eating breakfast, not drinking. Has been nursing one meal for two hours, watching the room. He's a Redbrand out of uniform doing threat assessment: the party came back from the goblin cave alive, and Glasstaff wants to know whether to act now.

**Toblen recognizes him.** Knows every face in town. When Marto walks in, Toblen's smile goes 10% tighter and he finds a reason to be busy behind the bar. Doesn't look at Marto. Doesn't look at the party. DC 10 Insight to notice something shifted.

---

## The Tail

Party decided to follow Marto when he left.

**Established:** Glasstaff's read on the situation is *kill them early, not late.* The party didn't die to the goblins. A 2nd-level adventurer is a problem; a 3rd-level adventurer is a bigger one. Marto's job was assessment; the answer he's bringing back is "yes, deal with it now."

---

## The Ambush

**Shape:** 4 Redbrand ruffians total (Marto + 3 sent by Glasstaff).

**Redbrand Ruffian stat block:**
- AC 14 (studded leather) | HP 16 | Speed 30 ft
- Shortsword +4 to hit, 1d6+2 piercing
- Light crossbow +4 to hit, 1d8+2 piercing

**Deployment:**
- 2 step out front, blocking the street
- 2 come from behind (rear alley / around a building)
- Marto is one of the rear two — he knows which direction the party was heading

**Surprise check:** DC 12 Perception. Party watching Marto = watching the wrong direction.

**Tactical priority:** Go for Veyra first. Marto clocked her at the inn. Two crossbow shots at the caster in round one.

**Pip's role:** Protective Instinct eats one attack per round. Maela's bless turns this from a coin-flip to a winnable fight.

**Morale:** If 2 Redbrands drop, remaining two try to disengage and run. Not dying for Glasstaff.

---

## What Actually Happened

Zenvon snuck around, spotted the ambush, tried to talk to Marto. Marto attacked. Zenvon landed two cuts (Nick ability), then Disengaged and fled. Veyra killed Marto with Magic Missile. Three Redbrands remain, wounded.

---

## Options for What Comes Next (pick one or steal from two)

**1. They run.** Three wounded Redbrands scatter — one toward Tresendar Manor to report, two blend into town. Party can chase one, not all three. The one who reaches Glasstaff says Marto is dead and the caster is dangerous. Glasstaff starts moving his correspondence tonight.

**2. They surrender.** Wounded and leaderless, the three drop weapons. Now the party has three Redbrands kneeling in the street with Phandalin waking up around them, and the only jail is Harbin Wester's two-cell townmaster hall. Harbin opens his door and says: *"I did not ask for this."* The prisoners know the hideout layout and the skeleton password.

**3. Toblen saw everything.** He followed at a distance when he noticed Marto leave. He's standing twenty feet away holding a bread loaf, having just watched a tiefling kill a man with magic in his town's main street. Not afraid of the party — afraid of what comes next. *"How many more of them are there?"*

## Sildar / Iarno Background (came up mid-session)

Sildar refers to Iarno as an "old contact" through the Lords' Alliance — professional colleagues, probably years in the same organization, not close friends. **Sildar is the one who sent Iarno to Phandalin** on the constabulary assignment. When Glasstaff turns out to be Iarno, some of the weight falls on Sildar — he didn't just lose a colleague, he vouched for him.
