## Party Dynamics

The group runs on a clear division of labor: Veyra and Maela are the eyes in the dark, Pip and Zenvon depend on them to operate underground. In combat the seams interlock well — Pip's shoves feed Maela's attacks, Maela's healing keeps Zenvon upright, and Zenvon's skirmishing buys the casters room to work. Zenvon also acts as the party's social engine, having taken the lead on recruiting Sildar.

Shared goals: finish clearing the goblin cave and survive the flood the goblins triggered. The most pressing tension is immediate and physical rather than interpersonal — Veyra is caught in the flood with her status unconfirmed, and both Pip and Maela are carrying significant unhealed damage. Veyra's tie to Professor Orryn Voss (and the dangling thread about Elsa's sister) is the one open background hook surfaced in these notes.
