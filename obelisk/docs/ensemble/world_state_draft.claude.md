The party has cleared the **Cragmaw Hideout** goblin cave and killed the bugbear leader **Klarg**. During the fight, goblins released a dam, triggering a **flood** that caught **Veyra** (she failed her save; her immediate post-flood condition is unconfirmed — treat as an open beat). - The Triboar Trail ambush was **not random — an insider tipped off the attackers**.
- A **strange goblin with an elongated head** was present and whispered the garbled phrase *"You're not what Ruxithid wants."*

- **Gundren was dragged away separately** from the ambush site.
- Tasked the party with **capturing or killing Klarg** to stop word being sent ahead (now resolved — Klarg is dead).

## PHANDALIN NPCs

**Daran Edermath** — Alive. Retired **drow** adventurer (500+ years old), former marshal/herald of the Dragon Coast, now an orchard keeper. Local informant/quest-giver. Urges rescuing Gundren first. Wants **Glasstaff** (Red Brand leader) confronted; estimates **3–10 Red Brands** at Tresendar Manor. Tasked the party with the **Old Owl Well** undead investigation; offers **Boots of Striding and Springing** as reward. Praised Pip.

**Freda** — Alive, Phandalin (near the Miners Exchange). Slight gnomish woman, silver-streaked hair, spectacles. Anti–Red Brand; wants them gone without her neighbors getting hurt. Believes the Red Brands are behind Gundren's disappearance; suspects townsfolk fear or owe **Halia Thornton**. Pointed investigators to the Miners Exchange.

**Halia (Thornton)** — Alive, works at the **Phandalin Miners Exchange**. Directed the party to investigate the Red Brands. **Suspected connection to the Red Brands** (the only resident they reportedly don't bother) — nature of the tie unconfirmed.

**Toblen Stonehill** — Alive. Owner of the **Stonehill Tavern**. Broad-shouldered young human. Fears the Red Brands (lowers his voice about them); valued Gundren's business and is worried about his disappearance. Father of Tuck.

**Elsa** — Alive. Sharp-eyed bartender at the Stonehill Tavern. Has a **scholar sister in Neverwinter** (family pride; possibly known to Orryn Voss — see Mysteries). Directed the party to Daran Edermath.

**Tuck** — Alive. Nine-year-old son of Toblen. Offered to guide the party to a **tunnel** (the one Carp discovered). Worried about people who are "taken and don't return."

**Carp Alderleaf** — Alive (last seen near the woods). Friend of Tuck. **Discovered a tunnel into the woods**; his mother forbade him from going near it. Recently nearly caught by the Red Brands.

**Harbin Wester** — Alive. Town Master of Phandalin. Widely regarded as **useless**; hides behind official notices and wax seals. Criticized by Lenar.

**Lenar** — Alive. Lean human miner, dusty gray beard. Outspoken about lawlessness (goblins, bandits, orcs, Red Brands); calls Harbin Wester useless; confirmed the party's road attack; referenced Gundren's capture.

**Sister Garaele** — Alive but **wounded** (limping, pale); returned to town. **Refuses to speak about how she was hurt**, likely to protect the townspeople. (See Mysteries.)

**Narth** — Alive. Older, weathered human. Believes Garaele is withholding information to protect the town; suggested temple folk like **Sister Maela** might get her to talk.

## OPEN THREADS & MYSTERIES

**Gundren Rockseeker (high priority, time-sensitive).** Missing — dragged away separately from the ambush and **not** in the cleared cave. Captors were extracting information and threatened to kill him once done. The party still does not know where he was taken.

**The Black Spider.** Name overheard among the goblins; identity, location, and motives unknown. Likely the ambush's mastermind / the insider tip's beneficiary.

**Who tipped off the ambush?** Sildar confirms an insider betrayed the Triboar Trail caravan. The insider is unidentified.

**The obelisk / mind-and-memory thread.** Professor **Orryn Voss** (Veyra's mentor) **vanished in Phandalin**. Before disappearing he reported **black stone fragments from a large obelisk** in Phandalin, feared the obelisk was tied to **minds, memory, and transformation**, and **warned against assembling the fragments**. His current fate (alive/dead/transformed) and whether he held any fragments are unknown. Possibly linked phenomena:
- The **elongated-head goblin's** garbled whisper at the ambush: *"You're not what Ruxithid wants."*
- Pip's Ch1 whisper: *"returned."*
- Maela's holy symbol going **cold** at that same moment.
*(These are clustered as a working hypothesis, not confirmed connections.)*

**Sister Garaele's injuries.** She returned wounded and silent about the cause. Unresolved; Narth suggests Maela could get her to talk.

**Halia's true allegiance.** Suspected Red Brand ties (they alone leave her alone); the relationship is unconfirmed.

**Glasstaff.** Red Brand leader at Tresendar Manor; not yet seen or confronted.

**Veyra's condition post-flood.** She was caught in the Ch3 dam flood; survival/condition needs confirmation at next play.

## CANON EVENTS TIMELINE (chronological reference)

- **Pre-campaign:** Carp Alderleaf discovers a tunnel into the woods; his mother forbids him from approaching it. Professor Orryn Voss reports obelisk fragments in Phandalin, warns against assembling them, then disappears.
- **Ch1 — Ambush & Phandalin arrival groundwork:** The party is ambushed on the Triboar Trail (later revealed insider-tipped). Gundren is captured and dragged away separately; goblins begin interrogating him. An elongated-head goblin whispers *"You're not what Ruxithid wants."* Pip hears a whisper, *"returned"*; Maela's holy symbol turns cold. In Phandalin the party meets Toblen, Elsa, Lenar, Freda, Halia, Narth, Daran Edermath, and Tuck; learns of the Red Brands, Glasstaff, Tresendar Manor, and Old Owl Well. Sister Garaele returns to town wounded and silent. Veyra kills a goblin and the goblin boss in early combat.
- **Ch2 — To the cave:** The party learns more of the Red Brands; arrives in/around Phandalin; advances on the Cragmaw cave. Klarg is flagged as the bugbear threat deeper in the cave.
- **Ch3 — Cave cleared:** The party fights through wolves and goblins. Maela subdues wolves and heals Zenvon from ~1 HP to full. Goblins release a dam, flooding the cave; Veyra is caught in the flood (Zenvon saves her with a Sleight of Hand check). The party kills Klarg (Zenvon's killing blow; Veyra's Magic Missile for 15). They rescue Sildar (1 HP) and settle him at a camp; he relays intel on the Black Spider, the insider tip, and Gundren's separate abduction. The cave (Cragmaw Hideout) is cleared. The party sets out to return to Phandalin for a long rest, Gundren still missing.
