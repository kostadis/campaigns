- **Convergence — the abduction triad:** Black Spider, the "Ruxithid" whisper, and Gundren's separate disappearance are one knot. Sildar is the hinge NPC tying all three together; let his recovery (and the 50-gp escort to Phandalin) be the scene where the party connects them.
- **Veyra as the target:** *"You're not what Ruxithid wants"* + Voss's obelisk warnings (minds/memory/transformation) + Voss being Veyra's mentor strongly imply Veyra **is** what something wants. Foreshadow with Pip's overheard whisper *"returned."* Hold this slow.
- **Crossing paths in Phandalin:** Returning to town for the long rest puts the party simultaneously in reach of Daran (Old Owl Well boots), Freda/Halia (Exchange), Sister Garaele (via Narth → Sister Maela), and the Red Brand pressure (Toblen's fear, the Sleeping Giant, Tell Dendar's murder). Stack two or three of these in the same town visit.
- **The forbidden tunnel as a back door:** Carp's tunnel + Red Brands nearly catching him there hints the manor safehouse connects to the woods. Tuck's offered guidance is a low-stakes way to open the Tresendar approach off-script.
- **Loose threads to seed:** the jade frog statuette (buyer/origin?), Tell Dendar's vanished family, and Orryn Voss's fate are all dangling hooks ready to reconnect to the obelisk or Black Spider lines.
