## Active Plots
*Ordered by urgency.*

- **What:** Gundren is held captive, interrogated for information; captors threaten to kill him once they have what they need.
- **Trigger/Timeline:** Time-sensitive — multiple NPCs warned the window closes when interrogation ends.
- **Party intersection:** Their patron; their primary mandate (Daran urges this above all else). They do not yet know where he was taken.

### 2. The Black Spider
- **What:** Unknown figure whose name was overheard among the goblins; the ambush was an inside job, tipped off.
- **Trigger/Timeline:** Open thread; identity and motives unknown.
- **Party intersection:** Connected to the ambush that targeted them and took Gundren. The elongated-headed goblin's phrase (*"You're not what Ruxithid wants."*) is an unresolved clue.

### 3. The Red Brands / Tresendar Manor
- **What:** Gang controls Phandalin; suspected behind Gundren's disappearance; Glasstaff to be confronted.
- **Trigger/Timeline:** Standing threat; party pointed here by Freda, Halia, and Daran.

### 4. The tunnel in the woods

- **Trigger/Timeline:** Available now; the Red Brands nearly caught Carp near it.
- **Party intersection:** Immediate, low-friction lead with a child guide on offer.

### 5. Sister Garaele's injury
- **What:** Garaele returned wounded and silent, likely shielding the townspeople.
- **Trigger/Timeline:** Unresolved; no deadline stated.
- **Party intersection:** Narth offers a path in via Sister Maela / temple folk.

### 6. Old Owl Well
- **What:** Undead and dangerous magic have driven off prospectors NW of Phandalin.
- **Trigger/Timeline:** Open; reward (Boots of Striding and Springing) offered by Daran.
- **Party intersection:** Optional side quest with a fixed magic-item reward.

### 7. The obelisk fragments (slow burn)

- **Trigger/Timeline:** Dormant; no party awareness yet.
- **Party intersection:** Not yet engaged; ties to Voss's disappearance and his student Veyra.

## DM Notes
- **Convergence — the ambush:** Black Spider, the inside tip, and Gundren's separate abduction all stem from the same setup. Sildar's intel is the connective tissue; let the goblin trail, the "Black Spider" name, and the elongated-headed goblin's phrase resolve toward one source.
- **Halia is the pivot:** She points the party at the Red Brands *and* is the one resident the Red Brands leave alone. Freda's and Daran's suspicions can foreshadow her double game before it's confirmed.
- **Paths about to cross:** Tuck (guide) → Carp's tunnel → Red Brands (who nearly caught Carp). The tunnel is a natural bridge from town intrigue into the manor/Red Brands plot.
- **Clergy thread:** Narth → Sister Maela → Sister Garaele gives a non-combat route to her unresolved injury; consider tying her wounds to a regional threat (Red Brands or beyond).
- **Long-game seed:** Voss's obelisk warning ("minds, memory, transformation") and Veyra are planted but unengaged. Drop a black-stone fragment or a Voss reference early so the payoff lands later.
- **Reward leverage:** Daran's Boots (Old Owl Well) and Sildar's 50 gp + Klarg objective are concrete pulls if the party stalls on the main rescue.
