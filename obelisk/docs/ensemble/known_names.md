# Known names — obelisk (Phandelver & Below)

Roster of named individuals, factions, and places that should each collapse to a
single global dossier in `facts_to_state.py`. Anything not listed here (goblins,
wolves, anonymous guards) stays location-scoped. Bold proper nouns are what the
known-names extractor reads; the first word of a multi-word name (≥4 chars) is
auto-added as a short-form match.
