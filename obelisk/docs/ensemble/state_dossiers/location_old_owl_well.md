### Old Owl Well
**Type:** Location (Ancient Ruins)
**Current Status:** Active threat zone; prospectors have been driven away.
**Description:** An ancient watchtower ruin originally constructed by the magical empire of Netheril.
**Current Control/Occupancy:** Occupied by undead creatures that have successfully chased prospectors away from the site.
**Location:** Hills northwest of Phandalin.
**Current Situation:** The site is the focus of an active investigation regarding the undead threat. A reward of *boots of striding and springing* is associated with resolving this threat.

None.
