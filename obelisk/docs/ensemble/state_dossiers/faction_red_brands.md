### Red Brands

**Current Status & Location**
*   **Status:** Active gang operating freely in Phandalin.
*   **Base of Operations:** Tresendar Manor (surface) and a safehouse located directly beneath the manor.
*   **Strength:** Estimated 3–10 members present at Tresendar Manor (based on Chapter 1 intel; no newer count provided).
*   **Public Hangout:** The Sleeping Giant tap house.

**Allegiance & Relationships**
*   **Local Relations:** Harass the general population of Phandalin; notably exempt from bothering Halia (Halia Thornton).
*   **Threat Level:** Identified as a major threat to the town.
*   **Recent Conflicts:** Nearly captured Carp Alderleaf.

**Current Goals & Role**
*   **Primary Role:** Criminal gang operating out of the ruins of Tresendar Manor.
*   **Recent Actions:** Continued free operation in Phandalin; conducting patrols or searches that nearly apprehended Carp Alderleaf.
