### Daran Edermath

**Current Status:** Alive; retired adventurer and current orchard keeper in Phandalin.
**Identity:** A drow over 500 years old; formerly served as a marshal and herald on the Dragon Coast.
**Location:** Phandalin (specifically tending his orchard).
**Current Assignment / Role:** Acting as an information broker and quest-giver for the party. He has urged the party to prioritize rescuing Gundren Rockseeker over other tasks.
**Key Relationships:**
- **The Party:** Met with enthusiasm; specifically praised Pip for their dedication to doing good.
- **Gundren Rockseeker:** Urges the party to rescue him.
- **Elsa:** Recommended the party to seek out Daran.
- **Red Brands/Glasstaff:** Concerned about their activities; believes someone must confront their leader, Glasstaff.
- **Prospectors:** Aware that prospectors northwest of Phandalin have been driven from Old Owl Well by undead.
**Current Concerns:**
- The Red Brands and their leader, Glasstaff.
- Dangerous magic and undead creatures at Old Owl Well.
- Estimated 3 to 10 Red Brands currently occupy Tresendar Manor.
**Possessions / Rewards:**
- **Boots of Striding and Springing:** Currently in his possession; offered as a reward to the party for investigating the undead threat at Old Owl Well.
**Defining Recent Actions:**
- Provided intelligence on the Red Brands, Tresendar Manor, and Old Owl Well.
- Formally offered the boots of striding and springing as a quest reward.
