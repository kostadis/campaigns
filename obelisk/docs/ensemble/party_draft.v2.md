## Party Dynamics

The party runs on a clear division of labor: **Maela** and **Veyra** carry the light and the firepower, **Zenvon** scouts and finishes, and **Pip** sets up the heavy hitters. Because Zenvon and Pip both lack darkvision, the group is operationally dependent on Maela and Veyra in any dark space — a standing vulnerability if those two are separated or downed.

The tightest bond is **Zenvon–Veyra**, who coordinate in darkness and have already saved each other (Zenvon pulling Veyra from the flood). **Maela** functions as the party's safety net, having healed Zenvon back from the brink. Shared near-term goals: stabilize after the flood, recruit **Sildar**, and follow the **Orryn Voss / Elsa's sister** lead. Two unresolved supernatural threads — Maela's chilling holy symbol and the whisper Pip heard ("returned") — are open hooks the GM may want to track.
