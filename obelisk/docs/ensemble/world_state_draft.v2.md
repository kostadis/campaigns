# WORLD STATE — Phandalin Campaign (Lost Mine / Cragmaw arc)

**Canon current as of Chapter 3.** Later chapters override earlier facts. This is a current-state reference, not a history; a chronological timeline appears at the end.

The party is escorting Sildar back to Phandalin to take a long rest.

## KEY NPCs

### Allies & quest-givers
- **Gundren Rockseeker** — Party patron (dwarf merchant). **Missing/captive.** Separated from the party during the Triboar Trail ambush and dragged away by a separate group; his current location and survival are unconfirmed. Captors are interrogating an unnamed prisoner for information; multiple NPCs warn he may be killed once the captors get what they want. *(Note: the party assumed he was in the Cragmaw cave — he was not.)*
- **Sildar Hallwinter** — Lord's Alliance agent. Rescued from the Cragmaw cave; recovered enough to walk and negotiate but cites lingering wounds. Offers **50 gp** to be escorted to Neverwinter or Phandalin (party agreed: Phandalin). Source of the campaign's key intel (see Mysteries).
- **Daran Edermath** — Retired adventurer / orchard keeper in Phandalin; secretly a drow 500+ years old, former Dragon Coast marshal-herald. Info broker. Urges prioritizing Gundren's rescue. Offers **Boots of Striding and Springing** for clearing the undead at Old Owl Well.
- **Freda** — Gnomish woman near the Miners Exchange. Opposes the Red Brands; names them as responsible for Gundren's disappearance. Wants them gone but no townsfolk harmed. Points the party at the Miners Exchange / Halia.
- **Sister Garaele** — In Phandalin; **wounded** (limps, pale) and refusing to explain why, apparently to protect townspeople. Unresolved.
- **Toblen Stonehill** — Owner, Stonehill Tavern. Fearful of the Red Brands, won't speak of them openly. Liked Gundren's business.
- **Elsa** — Bartender, Stonehill Tavern; sharp-eyed. Has a scholar sister in Neverwinter. Recommended Daran Edermath as a local source.
- **Lenar** — Human miner; outspoken critic of Town Master Harbin Wester. Informant on road threats (goblins, bandits, orcs, Red Brands).
- **Narth** — Older human local; suggested Sister Maela ("temple folk") approach Garaele. Believes Garaele is hiding things to protect the town.
- **Tuck** — 9-year-old son of Toblen; offered to guide the party to a tunnel into the woods.
- **Carp Alderleaf** — Child; friend of Tuck. Found a tunnel into the woods (forbidden by his mother). Was nearly caught by the Red Brands.
- **Orryn Voss** — Scholar; Veyra's mentor. **Missing in Phandalin.** Was investigating black stone fragments of a large obelisk tied to *minds, memory, and transformation*; warned Veyra to never let anyone assemble the fragments. May have known Elsa's sister. (See Mysteries.)

### Neutral / unclear

- **Harbin Wester** — Town Master of Phandalin. Widely seen as useless; hides behind official seals and notices instead of acting.

### Antagonists
- **Glasstaff** — Leader of the Red Brands; operates from Tresendar Manor. **Not yet confronted.**
- **Klarg** — Bugbear, former Cragmaw goblin leader. **DEAD** — killed by Zenvon (Nick scimitar+dagger) in the main cavern, Ch. 3.

## OPEN THREADS & MYSTERIES

**Gundren's whereabouts (urgent).** He was dragged off separately at the ambush and was not in the Cragmaw cave. Location and survival unknown. Captors are interrogating a prisoner and may execute him once they have what they want — time-sensitive.

**The Black Spider.** Name overheard among the Cragmaw goblins. Identity, location, and motives unknown. Likely tied to the targeted ambush.

**"Ruxithid."** A goblin with an elongated head whispered *"You're not what Ruxithid wants"* at the ambush. Meaning unknown to both Sildar and the party.

**The ambush was a setup.** Sildar confirms the Triboar Trail hit was targeted, not a random robbery — someone tipped off the goblins.

**The Red Brands / Glasstaff.** Gang still rules Phandalin unopposed; Glasstaff not yet confronted; the manor safehouse not yet breached. Connected: who killed Tell Dendar and what happened to his vanished family.

**Halia's allegiance.** Why the Red Brands leave her alone — ally, leader, or simply ignored — is unconfirmed. Investigation deferred.

**Sister Garaele's injuries.** She is wounded and refuses to say how or why. Unresolved.

**Orryn Voss & the obelisk.** Voss (Veyra's mentor) vanished in Phandalin while investigating black-stone obelisk fragments linked to minds, memory, and transformation, warning they must never be assembled. His fate is unknown. Possible link to Elsa's scholar sister.

**Pip's whisper.** Pip reported hearing a disembodied whisper saying *"returned."* Unexplained — possibly tied to the same supernatural source whose whispers made Maela's holy symbol go cold.

## CANON EVENTS TIMELINE (chronological reference)

**Chapter 1 — Phandalin & the trail**
- Party arrives in Phandalin; the Shrine of Luck acolyte blesses them.
- They learn of Gundren's disappearance and the Red Brands' grip on town (Freda, Lenar, Toblen, Daran Edermath, Narth all weigh in).
- Daran offers the Old Owl Well quest (undead; Boots of Striding and Springing reward) and urges prioritizing Gundren.
- Sister Garaele turns up wounded and silent; Carp Alderleaf is nearly caught by the Red Brands near a newly found woods tunnel; Tuck offers to guide the party.
- The party follows the goblin trail toward the Cragmaw cave, surviving a pit and a snare. Zenvon scouts past chained wolves, meets a giant coiled poisonous snake, and silently retreats — session ends on the cliffhanger.

**Chapter 2 — Into the Cragmaw cave**
- Party ambushes and defeats the goblin lookouts at the cave entrance (surprise round); Pip takes heavy damage and is healed by Maela.
- The giant poisonous snake is killed; the wolves are bypassed/subdued.
- The goblin boss **Yimek** is killed (Zenvon + Maela). Goblin chamber cleared.
- **Sildar Hallwinter** is found and freed.
- Zenvon reaches **Level 2** (Cunning Action; Nick/Vex weapon masteries — Scimitar + Short Sword). Party exits to rest; warned that the bugbear Klarg still lurks.

**Chapter 3 — Clearing the hideout**
- (Recap establishes the Triboar Trail ambush was a targeted hit; Sildar relays the "Black Spider" name, the "Ruxithid" whisper, and the elongated-headed goblin.)
- Party pushes deeper; goblins release a dam, flooding the cave (Veyra fails her save and is swept up). Veyra kills a fleeing goblin with a critical Firebolt to stop an alarm.
- A wolf bites Zenvon; Maela subdues the remaining wolves non-lethally.
- **Klarg** engages, badly hurting Pip and Maela, and is **killed by Zenvon**. Loot recovered: 1,700 cp, two potions of healing, a jade golden-eyed frog statuette.
- **Cragmaw Hideout cleared.** Party agrees to escort Sildar (50 gp) and heads back to **Phandalin for a long rest** — Gundren still unaccounted for.
