# Sister Maela Dawnforge

**Sidekick** — dwarf cleric, lawful good. Healer, protector, practical advisor for Zenvon Foreput.
*Extracted from `notes/open.md` (2026-06-12). Campaign-original character, not from the module. Lineage confirmed dwarf by Kostadis, 2026-06-12 (open.md's block omitted it; Dwarven Resilience, darkvision, and Dwarvish already implied it).*

## Why She's Going to Phandalin

Maela is traveling to Phandalin after receiving a troubling letter from an old dwarven contact who works claims near the Triboar Trail. The letter mentioned miners finding "black stone not born from any mountain" and hearing voices underground.

Maela believes something ancient is stirring beneath the frontier town. She also knows that if Gundren Rockseeker is involved in any rediscovery of old dwarven sites, trouble is probably close behind.

## First Impressions

- **Protector's Enclave glimpse:** speaking with a priest or city official about strange reports from miners near Phandalin. "If miners are hearing voices underground, you don't file that under superstition. You send someone with a lantern and sense."
- **Anchorhook Yard:** has paid for passage as far as Phandalin.
- **On the road:** sits beside the driver, mending a torn cloak and quietly judging everyone's posture.

## Bonds to Zenvon

Maela respects the rogue if the rogue:
- Protects civilians
- Takes practical precautions
- Shows courage without being reckless
- Treats wounds and burials seriously

## Obelisk Hook

- **First Road Omen:** when the black stone cracked under the wagon wheel, Maela felt a brief chill in her holy symbol.
- **After the ambush:** of the goblin's black stone charm, she says the stone feels "wrong in the hand, like it remembers being part of something larger."
- **Obelisk Unease** (trait, below) is the mechanical face of this connection.

## Combat Role

Casts bless if the fight looks dangerous, keeps cure wounds for emergencies, and tries to keep the rogue standing. Opens with bless if the fight looks serious; uses sacred flame or warhammer; saves cure wounds for when someone drops low. She is brave, but not reckless.

## Stat Block (2nd-level sidekick)

*Medium humanoid (dwarf), lawful good*

**Armor Class** 16 (scale mail, shield)
**Hit Points** 16 (3d8 + 3)
**Speed** 25 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 14 (+2) | 10 (+0) | 13 (+1) | 11 (+0) | 15 (+2) | 12 (+1) |

**Saving Throws** Wis +4
**Skills** Insight +4, Medicine +4, Religion +2
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 12
**Languages** Common, Dwarvish
**Proficiency Bonus** +2

***Dwarven Resilience.*** Maela has advantage on saving throws against poison, and she has resistance to poison damage.

***Sacred Medic.*** When Maela restores hit points to a creature with a spell, that creature also gains 1 temporary hit point.

***Obelisk Unease.*** Maela can feel when stonework or relics have been corrupted by aberrant or psionic influence. This does not identify the magic, but it tells her something is spiritually wrong.

***Spellcasting.*** Maela's spellcasting ability is Wisdom. Spell save DC 12, +4 to hit with spell attacks.

- Cantrips: guidance, sacred flame, spare the dying
- 1st level (3 slots): bless, cure wounds, detect evil and good, sanctuary

### Actions

***Warhammer.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 6 (1d8 + 2) bludgeoning damage, or 7 (1d10 + 2) bludgeoning damage if used with two hands.

***Sacred Flame.*** One creature Maela can see within 60 feet must succeed on a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw or take 4 (1d8) radiant damage.
