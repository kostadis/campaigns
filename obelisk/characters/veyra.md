# Veyra of the Blue Candle

**Sidekick** — tiefling mage, neutral good. Arcane support, obelisk researcher, puzzle-solver for Zenvon Foreput.
*Extracted from `notes/open.md` (2026-06-12). Campaign-original character, not from the module. Her missing mentor is Professor Orryn Voss (full dossier in `notes/open.md`).*

> ⚠ **Editor note:** open.md's level-1 block listed 8 HP, but 2d6 + 2 averages 9. The level-2 HP below is computed from the dice formula (3d6 + 3 = 13), which supersedes the discrepancy going forward.

## Why She's Going to Phandalin

Veyra is following the trail of her missing mentor, Professor Orryn Voss, who vanished while investigating fragments of an ancient obelisk. His last known destination was Phandalin.

She has a charcoal map covered in nervous annotations, a blue candle wrapped in cloth, and a half-burned note that reads: "The town is not the prize. It is the threshold."

**Emotional hook:** Veyra does not simply want to know what happened to Orryn. She wants proof that his work mattered and that he was not losing his mind. The more the party uncovers, the more frightened she becomes that he was right about everything.

## First Impressions

- **Protector's Enclave glimpse:** at another table surrounded by notes, pretending not to listen when Gundren mentions Phandalin. Her wrapped candle gives off one faint blue pulse when Gundren says "Something old."
- **Anchorhook Yard:** asks, awkwardly but urgently, whether the wagon has room for one more traveler and several extremely important books.
- **On the road:** rides in the back of the wagon, trying to read while the road makes her handwriting worse by the mile.

## Table Voice

- "Has anyone else noticed the birds stopped singing about ten minutes ago, or is that only unsettling to me?"
- "That charm is connected to something larger. But alive first, mystery second. Probably."
- (Stonehill Inn prep) "If anyone says 'strange stone,' 'blue light,' or 'voices underground,' please make them repeat it slowly."
- "Professor Voss used to say a ruin is only dead if nothing remembers it. I thought that was beautiful. Lately, I have started to think it was a warning."

## Bonds to Zenvon

Veyra trusts the rogue if the rogue:
- Listens to warnings
- Asks about strange magic
- Protects her while she studies clues
- Does not mock her theories too much

## Obelisk Hook

- **The Blue Candle:** enchanted candle (trait, below); Orryn did not invent it — he found it in a sealed Netherese reliquary and modified it. It pulsed when the black stone cracked on the road, and glows near the goblin's stone charm.
- **What Veyra knows:** Orryn came to Phandalin shortly before vanishing; he believed the black stone fragments were parts of a larger obelisk; he feared it was connected to minds, memory, and transformation; he warned her never to let anyone assemble the fragments.
- **What she doesn't know yet:** his research overlaps with the Far Realm / mind flayer plot; someone may still have his field journal.

## Combat Role

Stays behind cover, opens with mage armor if danger is likely, and uses magic missile when the party needs reliable damage. She should spend most turns using fire bolt or helping interpret strange clues.

## Stat Block (2nd-level sidekick)

*Medium humanoid, neutral good*

**Armor Class** 12, or 15 with mage armor
**Hit Points** 13 (3d6 + 3)
**Speed** 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 8 (−1) | 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) | 16 (+3) | 13 (+1) | 12 (+1) |

**Saving Throws** Int +5
**Skills** Arcana +5, History +5, Investigation +5, Perception +3
**Senses** darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 13
**Languages** Common, Infernal, Undercommon
**Proficiency Bonus** +2

***Blue Candle.*** Veyra carries an enchanted blue candle. It gives off dim blue light in a 10-foot radius when within 60 feet of obelisk fragments, aberrations, psionic magic, or strange Underdark portals.

***Nervous Genius.*** Veyra has advantage on Intelligence checks related to aberrations, ancient magic, magical symbols, obelisk fragments, and psionic phenomena.

***Spellcasting.*** Veyra's spellcasting ability is Intelligence. Spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks.

- Cantrips: fire bolt, mage hand, minor illusion
- 1st level (3 slots): detect magic, mage armor, magic missile, shield

### Actions

***Dagger.*** *Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) piercing damage.

***Fire Bolt.*** *Ranged Spell Attack:* +5 to hit, range 120 ft., one target. *Hit:* 5 (1d10) fire damage.

### Reaction

***Shield.*** When Veyra is hit by an attack or targeted by magic missile, she casts shield if she has a 1st-level spell slot available.
