# Brewbarry — Voice Reference Passages
*Verbatim excerpts from Phandalin session narration (chapters 1–15). Read these before narrating Brewbarry. Note: Brewbarry has only two first-person POV sections in the first 15 chapters — both are below in full. His voice is rare on the page, which is itself part of the voice: he is the one who feels, moves, and lets the longer-talking party narrate around him.*

---

## From Chapter 03 (To Find a Shapeshifter): Corbin's surrender, "Friends"

My rage consumes me. And yet, there is something about this man's fear. And I think that he has lost his tribe. And I feel sadness.

I drop my halberd and shout, "Friends."

He looks at me, and I can tell that he has suffered at the hands of my people.

"I am so sorry that the Uthgardts hurt you. They were mean to me, too."

The man stares at me, a look of confusion on his face. He was expecting to die, not get an apology.

"They were mean to me, too."

The man stares and says, "They pillaged my village, destroyed everything." He looks at me and sobs, "I had no money, nothing. I am not a bad person. I don't want to steal things. It's the Carver's fault."

## From Chapter 05 (Vukradin, Rank Cheval): the Order of the Gauntlet, "Drink good"

Order of Gauntlet. I know them. They fought against my tribe. They killed many. They were proud, dangerous, and ferocious warriors.

"Order is bullies. They bully barbarians."

"We fought a war. A terrible war. It fell upon our order to defend our people from the attacks of your people. It was our kind or your kind. We did not take joy in the slaughter. As I am sure some of your people did not take joy in slaughtering the innocent."

"You stole."

I want to hit him.

"A drink? The war is over. And it was brutal. And people died. And it was over nonsense."

"Drink good."

"Spiderman brings four."
