# Epic Tier — Open Design Questions

Staging notes pulled from the four existing epic-tier design docs (`iymrith_finale_design.md`, `the_anchor_and_the_kill.md`, `glazhael_and_the_return.md`, `zariel_and_the_breaking.md`). These are the unresolved design choices that shape how levels 14–20 actually run at the table.

## 1. The Third Option — what are its mechanics?

`zariel_and_the_breaking.md` flags a "Third Option" where the party saves Iymrith's identity instead of killing the Doom of the Desert. Currently TBD.

- What does "saving her identity" look like in play — a skill challenge, a contested ritual, a planar intervention, something Vukradin-specific?
- What does the party have to *give up* for it? Zariel doesn't release a weapon cheaply.
- Does the Third Option require Dr. Iymrith to have retained some self-awareness through the breaking, and if so, what earlier table beat establishes that?
- Is this a genuine third path, or a hard-mode variant of the "kill the Doom" route?

## 2. How does the Mind Flayer schism interact with KP's conduit?

`iymrith_finale_design.md` establishes both the Mind Flayer schism and KP's Planar Energy Conduit work, but the interaction between them is implicit.

- Is the schism *caused* by the conduit (KP's collateral damage), or merely *exploitable* via it?
- Which faction of the schism is aligned with Iymrith, and which (if any) is recruitable by the party?
- Does the conduit need to be shut down, inherited, or weaponized by the party at the finale?
- Where does Aletra Sotorra (KP's field agent, Valphine's sister) sit in this — does she broker the Mind Flayer alignment, or is she a parallel track?

## 3. Do the three artifacts gate specific beats, or combine at the finale?

`iymrith_finale_design.md` names **Ruinstone**, **Ubbalux**, and **Planar Energy Conduit**. Unclear whether each artifact unlocks a specific story beat (one per tier milestone) or whether they combine at the endgame.

- One-to-one mapping (e.g. Ruinstone → Reverse Heist, Ubbalux → Myrkul-ritual disruption, Conduit → Doom of the Desert)?
- Or a three-key finale where all three converge on the final confrontation?
- Which artifact(s) does the party already have a line on, and which are still blind?
- Does any artifact change hands during Glazhael's subversion of the Myrkul ritual?

## 4. Sequencing around the Myrkul ritual

`glazhael_and_the_return.md` has Glazhael subverting Ularan Mortus's Myrkul ritual in the Divine Contention frame to pull Iymrith's soul into Claugiyliamatar's body.

- Does the party know the ritual is happening before it fires, or do they learn about it post-hoc?
- Is there a table beat where the party *could* prevent the subversion (and thus keep Iymrith in Avernus permanently) — and if so, do they know to?
- What does Ularan Mortus think happened? Does he become an ally-of-convenience against Glazhael afterward?
- Claugiyliamatar's body is the vessel — is Claugiyliamatar herself dead/displaced/merged, and does that matter for the Doom of the Desert's final form?

## 5. The Reverse Heist — scope and pacing

`the_anchor_and_the_kill.md` frames the restitution quest as the Gold Anchor's severance. The mechanical shape of the Reverse Heist isn't fully specified.

- How many heirs / rightful claimants does the party have to find? Is this one climactic scene or a multi-session arc?
- What counts as "return" — literal transfer of gold, or a ritualized legal acknowledgment of provenance?
- What's the resistance? Who benefits from the anchor staying intact (Zhentarim? other dragons? the heirs themselves)?
- Does Vukradin's Silver Tongue bard politics mechanically drive the Heist, or is it a party-wide skill challenge that Vukradin's worldview narratively frames?

## 6. The kill/return/break cycle — is it canonical or contingent?

The design assumes: first kill → Myrkul-ritual return → Zariel's breaking → final confrontation with the Doom. That's four distinct phases across levels 14–20.

- Is that the *default* path, or the *only* path? Does the party have agency to short-circuit it (e.g. prevent the first kill from triggering the soul-to-Avernus transit)?
- What's the tempo — roughly how many sessions per phase?
- Are there off-ramps where the party can end the arc earlier at a cost (e.g. Iymrith escapes intact but the Gold Anchor is gone)?

## 7. Party-specific hooks

The design leans heavily on Vukradin's "conflict-free gold" worldview as the anchor-solvent. What are the analogous hooks for the other PCs?

- **Soma (druid):** does the desert's biological-research angle put Adabra Gwynn / the Fury of the Wild track in play at epic tier?
- **Valphine (Lathander cleric / secret Drow):** Aletra is her sister and KP's agent — is the Planar Distortion track the family-reckoning spine of the epic tier for Valphine?
- **Brewbarry (goliath barbarian, Dragon Slayer Sword):** the sword is named for this moment. Does it do something special against the Doom specifically, or against Iymrith specifically, and does the Gold Anchor gate its lethality?
