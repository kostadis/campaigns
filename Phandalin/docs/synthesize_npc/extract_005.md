## Revealed Information

- **Falcon the Hunter is not a dragonslayer.** He was the sole survivor of his party's catastrophic encounter with Iymrith and found her already-plundered hoard, taking only gold scraps and a brass ring as seed capital for the lodge. He maintains the lie for business reasons.
- **The brass ring at Falcon's Lodge is from Iymrith's hoard**, connected to lightning and the god Talos. The Cult of Talos was attempting to steal it as an offering.
- **The Kraken Society used psionic (non-Mind Flayer) mind control** to capture and reprogram the Emerald Enclave patrol, compelling them to steal an inscription about a drowned gate beneath Yllyn in the Underdark.
- **Jenna Roscoe knows more about the Kraken Society than she admitted** at the council meeting; her dismissal of them as a harmless social club is likely a cover.
- **House Maragaster has a demonic alliance** and agents are actively trying to intercept the Elara Seasong Meliame statue en route to Neverwinter.
- **The Neverwinter mining consortium owning Mountain's Toe Gold Mine includes at least one Kraken Society associate.**
- **The white dragon is actively ranging into the valley**, killing orcs; its presence is causing orc displacement and factional chaos throughout the region.
- **Zeleen Varnaster (Whiskered Gang) is likely lying** about her intentions to vacate the mine; she probably intends to keep it regardless of the deal struck.
- **The displaced orc factions stem from a white dragon seizing their home in the Ice Spire of the Sword Mountains**, causing a cascade of banditry, cult recruitment, and independent raiding.
- **The drowned gate beneath Yllyn** is a piece of planar infrastructure that the Kraken Society is actively seeking information about.
