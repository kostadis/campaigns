## NPC Activity

### The Carver
Passed through the mountain pass en route to Icespire Hold, leaving a trail of psychically distorted terrain — footprints pressed with inhuman precision, rocks phased out of reality along the path. Traveled alone. Carried an obsidian sword with level-draining properties. Described by the stone giants as "the little one with the obsidian sword." Crossed the Glacier of Whispers in a straight, mechanical line. Confirmed by Sister Kaella as no longer fully human — described as a hollow shell, soulless, talking to his sword in a tongue that makes ears bleed. His possession appears total: the original self is absent, not suppressed.

### Sister Kaella
Stumbled into the party's clearing wounded, warning that the Carver was marching on Icespire Hold to bind the white dragon and burn Phandalin. Was attacked and knocked unconscious by Vukradin and the party before completing her warning. Later revived and interrogated by Valphine. Confirmed the Carver's complete transformation and his destination. Revealed she had been feeding intelligence to Valphine covertly, having established a private arrangement after assessing Vukradin as unable to operate in morally gray situations. Stated that after the party destroyed the Carver's muscle, the Carver accused her of intelligence failures, making her survival contingent on his death. Requested imprisonment in Axholm. Left in the custody of Corbin with explicit instructions to be kept alive at one hit point.

### Jax
Last of the Carver's named field agents. Ambushed the party with thugs and a Rot-Tusk Ogre immediately after Sister Kaella was subdued. Was paralyzed by Hold Person and defeated by combined party attacks. Status: deceased.

### Rot-Tusk Ogre
Fought alongside Jax. Paralyzed by Hold Person. Defeated by Brewbarry. Status: deceased.

### Thugs
Multiple henchmen accompanying Jax. Several defeated by Vukradin's Array of Cards. One surviving thug fled and was felled by Brewbarry in pursuit. All accounted for.

### Xanth
Wounded centaur, healed by Soma after the battle. Identified Meril's Staff as a powerful artifact formerly belonging to Meril, who was an old friend of his. Explained the displacement chain: white dragon moved into Icespire Mountains, displaced orcs moved into human-occupied valleys. Confirmed the Carver's three lieutenants as Borg, Sister Kaella, and Jax. Agreed to carry Vukradin and Valphine to the foothills near the gold mine. Last known location: traveling with the party toward Icespire Hold.

### Lady Alagondar's Ghost
Appeared after the Dragon Slayer Sword was secured. Stared silently and sadly in the direction of the Ice Spire Mountains. Did not speak. Status: spectral, location of apparition unknown after appearance.

### Stone Giants (unnamed)
A gathering of stone giants in the mountain pass. Observed the Carver's passage but did not intervene, classifying him as "a little one" beneath their concern. Confirmed he traveled alone and carried an obsidian sword. Acknowledged the psychic damage to the rocks when Soma used Meril's Staff as evidence, but reaffirmed Hekaton's doctrine of non-interference with non-giant affairs. Gave two potions (greater healing, hill giant strength) to the party before they departed. Last known location: mountain pass defile.

### Corbin
Former member of the Carver's organization. Assigned to guard Sister Kaella. Expressed visible eagerness at the assignment. History with Kaella implied but not stated. Last known location: camp with Sister Kaella as prisoner.

### Boney
Sentient bone horse, bonded with Valphine. Participated in transport logistics for the mountain crossing. Noted to no longer have digestive concerns. Status: active, traveling with party.

## Faction Movements

### The Carver's Organization
Effectively destroyed at field level. Jax and the Rot-Tusk Ogre killed. Remaining thugs killed or fled. Sister Kaella captured and defected. The Carver himself is advancing on Icespire Hold alone, no longer coordinating with agents. The organization has no known remaining operational capacity.

### Stone Giant Conclave (Hekaton Doctrine)
Maintaining non-interference policy following the failed sundering war. Acknowledged planar distortion evidence but declined to act. Provided minor material support (potions) without committing to involvement. Policy stance unchanged.

### Emerald Enclave
Referenced by Xanth as factionalized and paralyzed over whether the orc displacement is a naturalist concern or warrants intervention. No field actions taken. Internal conflict ongoing.

## Threat Arc Events

### Brundar's Echo / The Carver Threat Arc
**Score increase triggered.** The Carver has completed his march through the mountain pass and is confirmed to be approaching Icespire Hold alone. His footprints demonstrate complete loss of human movement patterns. Rocks phased along his trail show persistent psychic contamination that Soma's staff confirmed is not spell residue but identity-erasure at a fundamental level. The Glacier of Whispers whispered a direct confirmation: *"He walks without rest, without breath, without soul."* The obsidian sword's level-draining property is now known to the party.

### Planar Distortion
**Score increase triggered.** Psychic damage to rocks along the Carver's trail is persistent — not a standard magical signature but a wound in the landscape. Rocks phased in and out of reality at the contact points. The Glacier of Whispers is showing increased psychic resonance, with trapped souls visibly pressing against the violet ice. Soma's use of Meril's Staff confirmed active psychic energy warping physical matter along the Carver's entire route.

## Revealed Information

- The Carver is completely soulless — not possessed in a recoverable sense, but emptied. The original self is absent.
- The Carver's obsidian sword is level-draining.
- The Carver is traveling alone to Icespire Hold with no remaining support structure.
- Sister Kaella was secretly acting as an informant for Valphine independently of the party, having assessed Vukradin as unable to handle morally gray intelligence arrangements.
- Sister Kaella's survival was made contingent on the Carver's death after he blamed her for the intelligence failures that led to the party destroying his assets.
- Meril's Staff is a powerful artifact. Meril and Xanth were old friends, and Meril's decision to leave the staff with Soma was intentional. The staff once had significantly more leaves.
- The orc presence in the valleys is a downstream consequence of white dragon territorial displacement, not independent aggression.
- The Carver's three lieutenants were Borg, Sister Kaella, and Jax — all now neutralized.
- Lathander's light, specifically invoked as a ward, can suppress the Glacier of Whispers' psychic resonance for approximately ten minutes. Valphine discovered this as a previously unknown application of her priestly training.
- The stone giants' non-interference doctrine stems from the failed sundering war, in which giants who allied with Tiamat's plans were destroyed. Hekaton codified this as a binding policy.
