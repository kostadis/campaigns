# Soma - Tortle Druid
By tortle standards, Soma is old.  She doesn’t track the exact number, though it must be over 40, and she can feel the age throughout her body and mind.  Time has given her wisdom and patience, causing her to present as brooding and slow from the outside.  Still, when the moment calls for it, she can come out of her shell and stand up for herself.

Most of her life had been spent in the comfort of nature, living along the coast doing what might be called the bare minimum to survive.  Foraging, fishing, keeping a crude cabin in good repair, and otherwise taking each day as they came.  Years ago, she had the fortune to start a family, and so somewhere out there, she hopes her eggs have hatched and survived their own perilous start to life.  In service to her possible progeny, she carries on tending to the local wilds to keep them safe and inviting on the off chance her kids stumble upon her abode.

Living beyond the outskirts of any town gave her a generally undisturbed existence.  A few times a year, some lost hunter or adventurer would stumble upon her cabin.  Soma regularly took them in, offering shelter, tea, and an ear for stories or songs.  This was her primary way of keeping tabs on the world and all the craziness she found better to enjoy from a distance.

One recent autumn, an elven druid named Meril happened upon the tortle’s sanctuary, and immediately recognized Soma’s connection with the land.  Unlike other visitors, he lingered for over a year before moving on.  Soma quickly picked up the basics of druid craft.  A lifetime of connection to the land had made her a natural.  Before taking his leave, Meril lamented that he had not found her when she was young, no amount of potential was going to make up for her limited time remaining.

Soma wished him well when he departed, and promised to keep up with her training no matter how little time she’d have to grow her skills.  Meril paid for her hospitality with the lessons he’d given her as well as an intricate ash staff he hoped she’d put to good use some day.  In Soma’s eyes, it was nothing more than a memento of a friend, and she kept it safely in her cabin.

After Meril’s departure, Soma started striking out more with the help of her druid magics.  Using them helped stave off the feelings of age and brought back some vigor.  Her newfound energy turned the tables and had her coming across other adventurers who weren’t lost.  She’d offer her services as a guide for little more than the experience.  It was a simple offer to guide a passing bard that set her on her path.

## Traits
- **Patient and unhurried**, even when danger looms—she believes most problems can be solved with a calm mind and a warm drink
- **Soft-spoken and dry-witted**, with a tendency to let silence do the talking
- **Instinctively maternal**, she looks after those around her as if they were her own hatchlings—whether they like it or not
## Loose Ends
- **Her children, **something must have become of them
- **Meril**, his path may cross Soma’s again some day
- **Meril’s Staff**, left behind in her cabin.  Did he leave it for a reason, or just a token
- **Druid Studies**, learning too late in life that she had a knack for it.  In her own methodical way, she’s got to make up for lost time
