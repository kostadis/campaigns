### **The Echoes Score: Telemetry of the Deep**

**Definition:** A hidden, DM-tracked metric (scaled 0–10) that dictates the "Influence Level" and narrative impact of the Kraken Society's actions. It ensures a slow-burn reveal that shifts the campaign from mundane faction politics into creeping cosmic horror.

#### **I. The Escalation Tiers (Influence Levels)**

| Score    | Influence Level         | Narrative Impact & Key Manifestations                        |
| -------- | ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **0–2**  | **Subtle Whispers**     | Odd dreams, feelings of "missing time", and vague symbols. The party encounters "too helpful" strangers like Serel Mardun (a disguised Society agent) who subtly plant seeds of doubt and false information. |
| **3–5**  | **Threads Appear**      | Names repeat, allies begin acting strangely, and people vanish. The party encounters Brother Halgar deciphering "dragon dreaming" (planar memory manipulation), or clashes with "rival adventuring groups" wielding salvaged illithid-tech weaponry. |

Trusted figures, such as Leilon guard commander Captain Tharros, become mentally compromised by illithid dream entities, issuing erratic and illogical commands. | **9–10** | **The Depth Revealed**  | The mind flayers' presence becomes undeniable. The Society acts as "false mediators" to intentionally escalate conflicts between local cults. The party is confronted with the horrifying truth of Ubbalux's legacy and the full scope of the psionic conspiracy. |

#### **II. Catalysts for Increase (Triggers)**

The cosmos responds to the party's investigations. Add **+1** to the Echoes Score whenever the party inadvertently interacts with the Society's web:

- Recovering an item tied to the sea or dreams with an unclear origin (e.g., black coral trinkets).
- Interacting with an NPC who has been mentally compromised or subjected to memory tampering.
- Sleeping within 1 mile of a submerged Kraken Society ritual site.
- Casting *Detect Magic* or *Divination* near a Kraken Society relic.
- Reading, intercepting, or stealing correspondence from a Society agent.
- Engaging in combat with overt Kraken Society agents (+2 to +3 for significant encounters).

#### **III. Symptoms of Resonance (Thematic Clues)**

As the score rises, inject these recurring motifs into the Agentic Flow to build paranoia:

- **Memory Tampering:** Townsfolk completely deny conversations that happened the day before or experience sudden "blank spots" in their memory.
- **Aberrant Symbology:** The party spots the "Inverted Eye", stylized spiral tentacles, or "Echo Marks" (tiny hash-mark circles visible only via *Detect Magic* or *See Invisibility*).
- **Intrusive Dream Sequences:** At score thresholds (3, 6, 9), target a specific player with a dream of submerged cities, silver chains, or a voice whispering backwards, indicating that a mind flayer is observing them.
