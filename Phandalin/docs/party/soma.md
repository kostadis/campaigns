As a Bonus Action, you shape-shift into a Beast form that you have learned for this feature. You stay in that form for 3 hours or until you use Wild Shape again, have the Incapacitated condition, or die. You can also leave the form early as a Bonus Action.

You are able to learn a number of Beast forms as outlined below, and whenever you finish a Long Rest, you can replace one of your known forms with another eligible form.

- Level 8: You know 8 four Beast Forms with a max CR of 1 and can adopt a form that has a Fly Speed.
