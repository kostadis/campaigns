### Overview
The Carver is a man who a life of violence and hardship has shaped. At 40, he is not a hot-headed youth but a seasoned predator who rules his bandits through raw intimidation and brutal, swift punishment. His authority is absolute, forged in the fear he instills and the grim examples he has made of those who crossed him. He is not a strategist or a politician; he is a force of nature, driven by a simple and insatiable greed.
### Appearance
The Carver is a thick, powerful man built like a backcountry butcher. His shoulders are broad, his neck is a trunk, and his hands are calloused and heavy. He stands with a slight stoop, not from weakness, but like a wolf about to spring.
His face is a roadmap of past brawls and close calls. A jagged scar runs from his left temple down across his cheek, pulling one corner of his mouth into a permanent, humorless sneer. His nose was broken long ago and set badly, and his dark, close-set eyes are flat and merciless, missing nothing. His hair is a wiry, dark thatch peppered with grey, cropped short and practical. He is clean-shaven, but a permanent shadow of stubble clings to his jawline, giving him a perpetually unkempt look.
### Demeanor and Habits
He is a man of few words and even fewer wasted movements. When others are celebrating or boasting, the Carver is often found sitting silently in the corner of the room, observing, his gaze unsettling. He has a habit of cleaning his fingernails with the tip of a long, wicked-looking skinning knife—a slow, deliberate action that draws the eye and serves as an unspoken threat. He rarely raises his voice, speaking in a low, gravelly tone that forces others to lean in and listen, ensuring he always has their complete attention.
### Gear and Weapons
The Carver disdains ornate or flashy equipment. He wears a suit of practical, well-worn studded leather armor over simple woolen clothes. A heavy, dark cloak, stained and frayed at the hem, is often thrown over his shoulders. His primary weapon, worn on his hip, is a heavy, cleaver-like handaxe with a notched blade that has clearly seen extensive use. In addition to the skinning knife he is so fond of, several more throwing knives are strapped to his chest and boot, all within easy reach.
