# Main Quests
- Gulch Quest — obtain Gnomengarde grenade
- Icingdeath and Twinkle Quest — petition Lord Neverember for troops
- Thalivar's Beacon Quest — tame extraplanar monsters for town defense
- Leilon Besieged Quest — defend Leilon from cult invasion
- Storm Lord's Hideout Quest — locate and sink the death knight dreadnaught
- Ebondeath's Mausoleum Quest — destroy Ebondeath's spirit in the Mere of Dead Men
