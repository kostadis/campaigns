## Threads & Mysteries

- **Who owns the druidic shield?** The shield has a half-orc tally (five notches) on the inside rim and predates its Talosian iron bolt overlay by at least a generation. The owner is unknown — possibly dead. Valphine is holding it until the owner appears.
- **What is Brewbarry's history with Talos and the Uthgardt?** He references being found guilty while sober, "a long time ago." The nature of that judgment and its connection to his current Talos relationship is unresolved.
- **Soma's staff is sprouting.** A new leaf pushed out during the unnatural storm. The staff is visibly responding to events; the significance is unclear.
- **What is "something worse than the dragon" at Icespire Hold?** Valphine stated something worse than Cryovain now occupies the area. No identification given.
- **Gorthok arrived before the ritual was complete.** Soma did not expect him until the Woodland Manse summoning finished. His early presence implies either the ritual is further along than anticipated, a backup was triggered, or something else accelerated his arrival.
- **The Woodland Manse ritual is ongoing.** The party chose the lodge over the manse. The summoning there has not been interrupted. The documents confirm a backup ritual and personal summoning fallback exist — the redundancy suggests the Talosians anticipated interference.
- **Prutha and the "good recruit" note.** A recovered note described a boy from the East recovered at the Circle of Thunder as a good recruit. Soma looked at Prutha when this surfaced. Whether Prutha is or was a Talosian/Anchorite recruitment target is unresolved.
- **What is the Anchorites' relationship to the lodge?** They previously moved their own people through Falcon's Hunting Lodge. Whether this implies prior alliance with lodge staff, infiltration, or simple use of an empty building is unclear.
- **Falcon's dead partner.** Falcon lost a partner in an attack the party failed to prevent. The circumstances, the partner's identity, and whether this creates ongoing obligation or tension are unresolved.
- **Valphine's background work with Falcon.** She had been working something with him before the Sending. The nature and extent of that arrangement is not detailed.
- **The three Talosian factions at the lodge.** Prutha identified three groups: former tribe, Talosian-aligned, and independent. The relationship between them and whether they are all present in the current attacking force is unresolved.
- **Drubbak's missing chant.** He said the storm came wrong — no chant. Whether this means the summoning is improperly performed, performed by someone new, or performed through a different method entirely is unresolved.
- **What will the orc clan do after the siege?** They intend to return to Icespire Hold. The "something worse than the dragon" makes that a potential future complication.
