## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Druid Conclave**: Adabra is actively mobilising druids to condemn the party's actions at the Whispering Grove. The conclave's outcome is unresolved. Some druids may attempt to strip Soma of Meril's staff.
- **Meril's Staff**: **The staff gains leaves over time as Soma accepts her role (it does not lose them).** Its full nature, significance, and why Meril gave it to Soma after only one year of training remain unexplained. The staff holds symbolic standing among druids that even Ser Kaelen does not fully understand.
- **The Whispering Grove Incident**: Referenced as involving a treant and some form of concealment of what "really happened." The full details of the incident and what Adabra has specifically discovered are not disclosed in these notes.
- **The Earthstone Gem**: Required by Dwolin the Grout before the gold vein at Mountain's Toe Mine will produce. Nature of the gem (whether genuinely magical or merely superstition) is unresolved. Were-rats have been sent into orc-occupied forest to retrieve it; their success is uncertain.
- **Plan B — The Fake Gem**: Horia Stonetallow has 50 gold authorised to purchase a fake gem if the were-rats fail. This agreement is being concealed from Vukradin. Whether the fake gem would satisfy Dwolin is unknown.
- **Planar Disturbances**: Referenced by both Soma (considering abjuration against extra-planar creatures) and Ser Kaelen (Adabra has not experienced the planar incursions directly). The nature, source, and extent of the planar disturbances are not elaborated on in these notes.
- **Dragon Materials**: The breath pouch and scales from Cryovain are in the party's possession. Discussion of dragon scale mail and a possible item made from the breath pouch was had, but no crafting decisions were finalised.
- **Brewbarry's Exile**: His exile from his Uthgardt tribe is referenced as formative but the circumstances are not elaborated upon. He mentions "something he drank his way into and is still drinking his way back out of."
- **The Studio**: Referenced by Brewbarry as a partial stake in something "not yet real." Nature and status of the studio are unresolved.
- **Historical Footnotes — War of the Giants**: The footnotes reference a War of the Giants and a War of the Dragons that have depleted the Order of the Gauntlet and left their mark on the world. The full significance of Orsic, Vardis, Unla Kee, KP, and Hekaton to the current campaign is unresolved.
- **Fairies added to the Goodly Races of Silverymoon**: Referenced in a historical footnote as potentially connected to the events of the War of the Giants. Significance to the current campaign unresolved.
