## World Events

- The interdimensional device collapsed into the void and Aletra escaped.
- The party searched the device room and found Riftweaver interdimensional spider silk.
- The party discovered Aletra's makeshift quarters in a former barracks at Axeholm.
- Soma uncovered Aletra's forgeries in the ledger (Midnight Tears substituted for iron shipment).
- Vukradin left the room; Valphine discovered a hidden bag containing a platinum coin, a malachite gem, fine wine, and four vials of Midnight Tears poison.
- The party found and took the Operator's Guide to the Model 1489 Spatial Rectification Calliope, including Aletra's personal notes.
- The party returned to the Chief Accountant and informed her Aletra fled and the machine imploded.
- The party presented the Chief Accountant with an interplanetary bill for 42,000 gold pieces; she screamed for approximately ten minutes.
- The Chief Accountant declared "The book is closed," disappeared, and was finally at peace.
- The party explored the accountant's old bedroom and Valphine found a secret door hidden in the fireplace.
- Four steampunk audit drones emerged from behind the secret door and attacked the party.
- One drone inserted a needle into Valphine and drew half a vial of her blood.
- Vukradin killed two drones; another attacked him; Valphine captured the fourth drone.
- The party found a ten-by-ten room containing an 80-pound iron chest with clawed feet and a circular lock.
- After multiple failed lock-picking attempts, Brewbarry smashed the chest open.
- The party recovered a dread helm and gauntlets of ogre power from the chest (former property of the Chief Accountant).
- Brewbarry immediately donned the dread helm; his eyes began glowing red.
- The party took a long rest and returned to Phandalin after several days of travel.
- Harbin Wester paid the party 250 gold pieces for securing Axeholm.
- Harbin Wester clarified Axeholm was intended as a last-resort backup, not a new settlement.
- Brewbarry approached Harbin Wester while wearing the dread helm; Harbin nervously asked if he was demon-possessed.
- Vukradin intimidated Harbin Wester by invoking his role as head of the Privy Council.
- Harbin Wester tasked the party with delivering cheese to the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine on behalf of Qelline Alderleaf.
- Sir Kaelen reminded the party about returning a statue; Brewbarry persuaded him the gold mine was higher priority.
- Jenna Roscoe suggested Brewbarry sell out to fund his music studio; he declined.
- The party traveled fifteen miles to the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine.
- At the west entrance, the party encountered two ogres and eight orcs confronting a wererat leader, demanding the mine.
- The ogres recognized the party as those who killed their tribesmen and became enraged.
- Combat began: orcs threw javelins; one struck Brewbarry; Valphine attempted a magical deflection.
- An ogre charged Brewbarry; Brewbarry struck it as it approached.
- Vukradin cast slow on both ogres and two orcs.
- Soma summoned a sphere of flame, killing two orcs instantly, then transformed into a bear.
- Valphine summoned spectral guardians, killing three orcs and severely wounding an ogre.
- Brewbarry killed the wounded ogre with a devastating blow.
- The remaining ogre fired a ranged weapon at Valphine and backed away; another orc threw a missed javelin.
- Combat continued, unresolved at session end.
