## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Carver's nature**: The Carver is confirmed to be walking "without rest, without breath, without soul" and leaves psychic residue in stone. Whether this is full possession (original self gone) or some form of corruption is not definitively resolved. Soma suspects possession; Valphine believes the original person is absent entirely. The mechanism and the possessing entity are unknown.
- **The obsidian sword**: Confirmed as a level-draining weapon. The Carver carries it. No further information on its origin, who made it, or whether it is connected to his current state.
- **The Carver's plan**: Intends to possess the white dragon at Icespire Hold and use it for vengeance. Who specifically he intends to target is not specified beyond "everyone and everything in reach."
- **The white dragon**: Described by Vukradin as "chained." Whether the dragon is literally restrained or the term is metaphorical is unclear. The dragon may be immune to fire, which would render the necklace of fireballs less useful.
- **Jax / Zax**: One of the Carver's three lieutenants, unaccounted for. Location and current activities unknown.
- **Meril's Staff**: Once had more leaves, now has one. Xanth identified it as powerful and believes Meril deliberately left it with Soma. The staff's full capabilities are unknown. Its relationship to Soma's growing connection to nature magic is unresolved.
- **Meril's whereabouts and intentions**: Unknown. Xanth was his old friend. Why Meril left the staff with Soma has not been fully explained.
- **Corbin and Kaella's history**: There is a clearly unresolved and charged prior relationship between Corbin and Kaella. The nature of it — whether professional, personal, adversarial, or something else — is not stated. Kaella began screaming immediately upon being left with him.
- **Valphine's arrangement with Kaella**: Described as predating the interrogation. How and when the arrangement was made, what its full terms are, and what Valphine was promised or expected in return are not detailed.
- **Valphine's sacraments**: Valphine told the party she has been teaching Corbin "some of our sacraments." The nature of these sacraments is unspecified and unexplained.
- **The Glacier of Whispers — the trapped faces**: Dozens of entities are trapped within the violet ice, silently screaming. Their identities, how they came to be trapped, and whether they can be freed are entirely unresolved.
- **Valphine's new/confirmed ability**: She discovered she possesses (or confirmed she possesses) a Lathander priestly ability to create a ten-minute psychic ward. Whether this was truly unknown to her or simply unexamined is an open question she herself raised.
- **Hekaton's doctrine and the giants**: The stone giants follow Hekaton's post-sundering non-intervention doctrine. Whether this will hold if the Carver successfully possesses the dragon and begins affecting giant-scale things is foreshadowed but unresolved.
- **Am and the breaking of the sundering**: Referenced as historical context — Am was tempted by Tiamat's power during the sundering and failed. The full history and its implications for current events are not explored in this session.
- **The Emerald Enclave's paralysis**: The Enclave is internally divided and cannot agree on terms regarding the orc crisis. This represents an ongoing institutional failure with no resolution in sight.
- **Vukradin's Privy Council opponents**: Named as a background threat. Who they are, what they want, and what specific actions they may take against Vukradin are unknown.
- **The boulder wall**: At the session's close, Soma was in the process of using Mold Earth to clear a boulder wall blocking the path. Whether this succeeds is unresolved.
- **The necklace of fireballs**: Vukradin is holding it but refuses to claim ownership. Its utility against a potentially fire-immune white dragon is doubtful. What to do with it remains unresolved.
