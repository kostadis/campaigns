## Factions

**Order of the Gauntlet**
- Current goals: Combat evil (dragons, demons); clear land for civilization; find and eliminate the Carver
- Recent actions: Operating in the Phandalin region through Ser Kaelen Thorne; seeking agents to locate the Carver
- Relationships: Allied with Emerald Enclave during War of the Dragons; distrusted by Adabra; Vukradin is a member (pin lost)
- Key members: Ser Kaelen Thorne (knight, Phandalin)
- Alleged secrets: Anonymous letter claims connections to House Margaster (associated with evil cults in Waterdeep); alleged forcible appropriation of artifacts from neutral parties
- Philosophy per Ser Kaelen: Believes heroism is a necessity to prevent evil from crushing everything beautiful; if good is not actively striven for, evil will win

**Emerald Enclave**
- Current goals: Maintaining natural balance; addressing the blight in the Whispering Wood
- Recent actions: Adabra lobbying Harbin and the party to investigate the Whispering Wood; previously sent Treants and forces to assist in the War of the Dragons
- Relationships: Historical alliance with Order of the Gauntlet (War of the Dragons); Adabra formerly cooperative with Vukradin until he joined the Order of the Gauntlet; some members now declare demons part of the natural world, causing concern among others
- Key members: Adabra (midwife, Phandalin); Delaan Winterhound (referenced); Malfire Morazana (referenced, convinced Enclave to join war)
- Philosophy: The multiverse is one unified thing; there is no inherent good or evil; striving to permanently alter the world's nature forces evil to respond and harms everyone; balance must be preserved

**Harpers**

## Locations

**Phandalin**
- What it is: A town; the party's current base of operations
- Current state: Ongoing threats from the Carver's bandits, orcs on the move, and a dragon in the region; lighthouse recently cleansed; general population aware of the party's comings and goings
- Key sites: Town Master's house (Harbin Wester); Stonehill Tavern (Stonehill, innkeeper); Lionshield Coster (Linene Graywind)

**The Whispering Wood (also called the Whispering Grove)**
- What it is: A grove approximately one hour's walk from Phandalin
- Current state: Afflicted by a magical blight; trees twist unnaturally; leaves are too bright or too sickly pale, colors described as wrong; Adabra reports a strange magical blight spreading through it; Ser Kaelen notes strange stories over the last few months but no confirmed casualties
- Notes: Adabra believes the blight is the result of interventionist meddling, not directly caused by the Order of the Gauntlet; Ser Kaelen speculated it may involve an interplanar event but dismissed the idea; the party is now heading there

**The Lighthouse (Tower of the Storms)**
- What it is: A lighthouse on the coast
- Current state: Cleansed; Talos cult removed; altar sanctified; false light destroyed; needs a new keeper
- Recent events: Party defeated Talos shaman with a green light in his chest; salvaged treasure from five wrecked ships nearby

**Neverwinter**
- What it is: A major city; seat of Lord Neverwinter/Neverember
- Current state: Referenced as origin of the anonymous letter; Harbin plans to send a note there regarding the lighthouse
- Notes: Rumors of House Margaster's association with evil cults circulated there

**Waterdeep**
- What it is: A major city
- Notes: House Margaster associated with unsavory businesses there

## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Whispering Wood blight**: Nature and cause of the magical blight are unknown; Adabra attributes it to interventionist meddling; Ser Kaelen speculated about an interplanar event; the party is now en route to investigate
- **Soma's staff awakening**: The staff given to Soma by Merill activated with new magic as the party headed toward the Whispering Wood; the nature and significance of this awakening are unknown
- **The anonymous letter**: Origin and identity of the author ("A Friend to True Justice") are unverified; the letter alleges Order of the Gauntlet connections to House Margaster and dubious artifact acquisition; whether these allegations are true is unresolved
- **House Margaster**: Their alleged connection to the Order of the Gauntlet and to evil cults requires investigation
- **The Carver**: Identity, location, and full operations of this bandit leader remain unknown; he is unusually skilled at hiding from divination and his own henchmen
- **Salvaged treasure provenance**: Rightful ownership of the five salvaged items (magical halberd, blue mermaid statue, cloak of many fashions, six pearls, spellbook and wand) is unresolved; Ser Kaelen is researching this
- **Secret code in the chest**: A coded message found in the locked chest from the Golden Gull has not yet been deciphered or traced; Valphine asked Ser Kaelen to inquire about it
- **Talos shaman's green light**: Ser Kaelen indicated a typical Talos shaman could not generate the green light phenomenon; he suggested a hidden greater force may be behind it; this force has not been identified
- **Lord Neverember/Neverwinter**: The party's relationship with Lord Neverwinter is developing; Harbin warned against visiting while the mission is incomplete; the consequences of Harbin's note are unknown
- **Ser Kaelen's trustworthiness**: Both Valphine and Soma distrust him; Valphine deliberately withheld information about the coded chest from him; no confirmed evidence of betrayal
- **Orcs on the move**: Referenced by Ser Kaelen as an ongoing threat in the region; not further detailed
- **Dragon in the region**: Referenced as an ongoing threat; not further detailed
- **New lighthouse keeper**: The lighthouse requires a new keeper; no candidate has been identified
