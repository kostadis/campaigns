## World Events

- Three harpies attacked the party as they approached the Tower of Storms; Valphine and Brewbarry were charmed by a male harpy in grey rags.
- Vukradin cast Silence in a 20-foot radius sphere, nullifying the harpy songs and breaking the charm on Valphine and Brewbarry.
- A female harpy later charmed Vukradin; he used Bardic Inspiration to convert the failure and then struck the female harpy with a spell, breaking her concentration.
- Vukradin attacked the male harpy in grey rags with daggers, landing one hit and missing a second.
- Soma transformed into a wolf spider and scaled the tower walls to attack the female harpy.
- A female harpy in blue rags attacked Soma.
- Vukradin delivered the killing blow to the male harpy in grey rags using Vicious Mockery.
- The party took a short rest following the harpy combat; Brewbarry retrieved his handaxes.
- The party discovered a 15-foot-high empty room with a dirty window overlooking shipwrecks below.
- The party found a room with frescoes depicting stormy seas and a dark and terrible god, and a stone altar with lightning bolts carved into it bearing a metal lightning rod.
- The party discovered a harpy nest on a nearby ledge containing a potion of water breathing.
- The party ascended to find Moesko, the Orc Anchorite, seated in a barnacle-encrusted chair holding the opalescent conch.
- Soma opened the fight against Moesko with a minor poison spray attack, then transformed into a brown bear and used multi-attack, dealing significant damage.
- Vukradin cast Cloud of Daggers, trapping Moesko in a 5-foot cube and dealing 9 initial damage.
- Moesko cast Lightning Bolt, striking most of the party (all except Brewbarry); damage was halved for most due to saving throws.
- Moesko cast Thunderwave, dealing additional damage and pushing Soma back.
- Brewbarry charged Moesko and hit him, reducing the anchorite to 1 hit point.
- Soma reverted from bear form and delivered the killing blow to Moesko.
- The party recovered the opalescent conch from Moesko.
