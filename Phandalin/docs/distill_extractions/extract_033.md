- Vukradin cast Silence in the corridor as a protective measure against the Will-o'-wisps.
- Valphine healed Brewbarry for 25 hit points.
- Valphine used a readied Turn Undead action on a Will-o'-wisp in the room with Boney, causing it to flee in terror.
- Vukradin ran toward the sword but turned west at the intersection instead of north, splitting the party (Soma followed Vukradin; Brewbarry and Valphine continued north).
- Vukradin fell into a pit trap in the western corridor, taking 19 points of damage and losing concentration on Silence.
- A Will-o'-wisp appeared and attacked Vukradin, dealing 9 points of damage; Vukradin used a reaction attack hitting for 6 damage.
- Soma, hearing danger, raced after Vukradin.
- The Will-o'-wisp disappeared into a sarcophagus; Vukradin opened it and discovered the creature inside.
- Vukradin cast Cloud of Daggers on the Will-o'-wisp, rolling four ones for a total of 4 damage.
- Vukradin retrieved a Necklace of Fireballs (seven beads) from a southwest sarcophagus.
- Vukradin retrieved a Flute of Illusions from a northwest sarcophagus containing a dead bard.
- Soma cast Moonbeam on the Will-o'-wisp caught in Cloud of Daggers, dealing 12 radiant damage and destroying it.
- Brewbarry moved north past the intersection, arrived at an archway, and identified an odd light source using Thaumaturgy as a magical spell rather than a Will-o'-wisp.
- Brewbarry deliberately triggered a pressure plate trap using his halberd.
- Brewbarry entered a battle rage, approached a pit, and attacked a Will-o'-wisp, hitting with a 23 for 10 damage and destroying it with a second attack.
- Valphine moved north, jumped over a pit trap, and entered a room containing dragon bones.
- Valphine discovered the Dragon Slayer Sword resting atop the dragon's skull in the northern chamber, across from where Lady Alagondar was laid to rest.

## Locations

### Dragonbarrow
- **What it is:** A dungeon containing narrow tunnels, hidden traps (pressure plates and pit traps), sarcophagi, Will-o'-wisp inhabitants, and the remains of a dragon
- **What happened here:** The party navigated traps, defeated multiple Will-o'-wisps, recovered the Dragon Slayer Sword and additional magical items
- **Current State:** Will-o'-wisps defeated; Dragon Slayer Sword retrieved; party present

### Secret Passageway (Western Tunnel)
- **What it is:** A narrow two-foot-wide tunnel to the west within the Dragonbarrow
- **What happened here:** Vukradin triggered a five-foot pressure plate causing walls to collapse inward, burying the party; Soma excavated the entire tunnel using Mould Earth
- **Current State:** Cleared and passable

### Southern Niche
- **What it is:** An alcove within or adjacent to the tunnel containing horse bones and a rotting saddle
- **What happened here:** Soma discovered the bones; the skeletal horse Boney reanimated; party spoke with and recruited Boney
- **Current State:** Boney present and active

### Sepulcher Chamber
- **What it is:** A chamber containing multiple sarcophagi; site of a dead wizard (southwest sarcophagus) and a dead bard (northwest sarcophagus)
- **What happened here:** Party encountered Will-o'-wisps; Vukradin fell into pit trap; Cloud of Daggers and Moonbeam destroyed a Will-o'-wisp; Necklace of Fireballs and Flute of Illusions recovered
- **Current State:** Will-o'-wisps cleared; magical items removed from sarcophagi

### Archway / Room with Odd Light (Near Northern Path)
- **What it is:** An archway leading to a tomb near the northern path; contained a magical light source (not a Will-o'-wisp); associated with the earlier death of Soma's rat
- **What happened here:** Brewbarry used Thaumaturgy to identify the light as magical; deliberately triggered a pressure plate; killed a Will-o'-wisp
- **Current State:** Will-o'-wisp destroyed; trap triggered

### Northern Area (Dragon Bone Chamber)
- **What it is:** The northernmost chamber of the Dragonbarrow; contains the bones and skull of a dragon; location of Lady Alagondar's burial site or the area opposite it; contains a pair of sarcophagi
- **What happened here:** Valphine jumped the pit trap and entered; discovered the Dragon Slayer Sword resting atop the dragon's skull
- **Current State:** Dragon Slayer Sword retrieved; dragon bones present

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Lady Alagondar's identity:** Who was Lady Alagondar? What was the quest she brought Boney back from the dead to complete? Her history and significance are entirely unexplored.
- **Lady Alagondar's resting place:** Her remains are somewhere in the Dragonbarrow ("across the way" from the sword); the party has not examined or acknowledged her burial site.
- **Boney's future role:** The party discussed Boney as a potential sidekick. His loyalty is based on the deception that Soma is Lady Alagondar reincarnated. What happens if this deception is discovered or tested?
- **The Dragon Slayer Sword as trade:** The party retrieved the sword for use in a fair trade. The identity of the trading party, the terms of the trade, and whether the sword will satisfy requirements remain unresolved.
- **The dragon's bones:** A dragon's skeleton is present in the northern chamber. No information is provided about the dragon's identity, how it died, or its connection to Lady Alagondar and the sword.
- **The turned Will-o'-wisp:** One Will-o'-wisp was turned by Valphine and fled; it was not confirmed destroyed. Its current whereabouts within the dungeon are unknown.
- **Previous death of Soma's rat:** A room near the archway is referenced as the location where Soma's rat was previously killed. The circumstances and cause of that death are unaddressed this session.
- **Necklace of Fireballs provenance:** Found with the remains of a dead wizard in a sealed sarcophagus. The wizard's identity and how they came to be interred in the Dragonbarrow are unknown.
- **Flute of Illusions provenance:** Found with the remains of a dead bard in a sarcophagus. The bard's identity and connection to the dungeon are unknown.
