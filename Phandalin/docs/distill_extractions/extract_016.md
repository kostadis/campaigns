## World Events

- The party spotted four of the Carver's disposable brigands and attempted a parley via Corbin, which failed when the bandits drew weapons.
- Soma transformed into a Brown Bear and killed one bandit instantly.
- Brewbarry grew to large size and killed a second bandit instantly.
- Vukradin used magic to put a third bandit to sleep; the fourth bandit fled and was not pursued.
- The sleeping bandit (Skippy) was captured, bound, and interrogated by Vukradin.
- Skippy revealed that Butterskull Ranch is an ambush site deliberately engineered by the Carver using Harbin's quest board.
- Skippy revealed that the ambush is led by Borg the Hammer, a half-orc enforcer.
- Skippy revealed that Sister Kayla, embedded in Phandalin, serves as the Carver's Spymaster and runs his spy network there.
- Corbin was left behind at the remote camp to guard Skippy; Vukradin warned Corbin that Skippy must be alive on his return.
- The party approached Butterskull Ranch from the north via a stealthy route, bypassing the main trail.
- The party discovered dead bodies of both orcs and humans near the outbuildings, already fly-swarmed; Vukradin chose not to loot them.
- Vukradin descended into the ranch cellar (noting butter skulls on shelves) and found Big Al Kalazorn bound to a chair with a burlap sack over his head.
- Vukradin freed Big Al and learned that orcs first raided the ranch, then Borg the Hammer arrived with Rot-Tusk Ogres, killed the orcs, and seized the property, keeping Big Al alive as bait.
- Big Al revealed he had kept Petunia off-site; she was not at the ranch.
- Big Al offered his mithril chainmail (confirmed legally purchased) as reward for recovering Petunia.
- Soma used Speak with Animals to question the pigs, leading the party to locate Petunia.
- Petunia was recovered and escorted to safety.
- The party retreated to a hill approximately 250 feet from the farmhouse to observe.
- Borg the Hammer emerged and confronted Bandit Number 4, the brigand who had fled the earlier skirmish.
- Borg executed Bandit Number 4.
- Borg commanded three Rot-Tusk Ogres, identified by the insignia of the tribe whose members the party had previously killed.
- Borg set fire to the farmhouse, cornfields, and gardens, burning Butterskull Ranch in its entirety.
- Borg split his forces: he sent the three Rot-Tusk Ogres in one direction and departed himself in a different, opposite direction.
- The party decided to follow Borg rather than the ogres.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Carver's identity and location**: The Carver remains unseen and his base of operations is unknown. The party is following Borg to find him.
- **Borg the Hammer's destination**: Borg departed Butterskull Ranch in an unknown direction after splitting from the ogres. The party is in active pursuit.
- **The Rot-Tusk Ogres' destination**: The three ogres were sent in a direction opposite to Borg's. Their objective and destination are unknown. They bear a grudge against the party for previously killed tribe members.
- **Sister Kayla's activities in Phandalin**: The Carver's Spymaster is embedded in Phandalin and running an active spy network. Her specific intelligence gathering, contacts, and cover identity are unknown.
- **Skippy's fate**: Corbin was left alone guarding Skippy with apparent intent to harm him. Vukradin warned that Skippy must be alive on his return, but the outcome is unresolved.
- **The mithril chainmail reward**: Big Al promised the chainmail for Petunia's recovery. Petunia has been recovered; whether the exchange has been formally completed is not stated.
- **The Carver's broader plan**: The ambush at Butterskull Ranch was one operation. The Carver's larger goals, resources, and network extent beyond the known members remain unknown.
- **The source of the Carver's intelligence**: Sister Kayla is identified as Spymaster, but the full extent of the spy network in Phandalin and elsewhere is unknown.
- **Prior ogre deaths**: The Rot-Tusk Ogres are explicitly seeking revenge for ogres previously killed by the party. The circumstances of those prior deaths are not detailed in this session but represent an ongoing threat.
- **The original orc raid on Butterskull Ranch**: A separate band of orcs raided the ranch before Borg arrived. Their origin, affiliation, and fate (other than being killed by Borg's forces) are unexplained.
