- **Dragon hoard origins**: Vukradin has privately resolved to investigate the dragon hoard from which Falcon built his lodge, intending to find its rightful owners and confront Falcon.

Falcon has offered a quest to deal with them; the party was advised they may need more experience first.
- **Dragon in the area**: A dragon has appeared near the mountains, displacing the orc population. Its identity, lair, and intentions are unknown.
- **Supply delivery to the Logger's Camp**: The party has accepted this quest but has not yet completed it.
- **Harbin Wester's private dealings**: The townmaster conducted a private financial transaction bypassing the Privy Council and described this as standard practice in the Overbright. The full extent of such corruption is unaddressed.
- **Sridar's artifact collection**: She collects items related to planar events; the scope of her collection and her ultimate goals are unexplored.
