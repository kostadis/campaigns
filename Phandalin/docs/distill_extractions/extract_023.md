## World Events

- The party arrived at Falcon's Hunting Lodge with prisoners (including Shrimpy), rescued companions (Teega the Smith, two Emerald Enclave guards), and Vukradin.
- Falcon expressed pleasure at Vukradin's safe return and was impressed by the party's success.
- Teega the Smith explained her last memory was working at the Wayside Inn and that she had been called by "the Kraken," which promised her peace.
- The party determined Teega's mind had been psionically manipulated; Brewbarry confirmed it was not the work of Mind Flayers.
- Vukradin offered Teega a blacksmithing position with the Church of Lathander; she declined but expressed possible future interest.
- Falcon disclosed that Mountain Toe's Goldmine had been poisoned, threatening to shut down operations, and proposed giving the party a share of the mine as a reward for investigating.
- The party chose to pursue the Woodland Manse quest over the gold mine, in order to obtain the promised magical boots.
- Falcon agreed to provide free accommodation for the entire party at Vukradin's persuasion.
- The two Emerald Enclave guards agreed to remain at the lodge two nights before reporting to their order.
- The Drow woman who had been interested in artifacts departed the lodge unannounced during the night.
- The party set out at 5:00 a.m. and traveled ten miles through the forest to the Woodland Manse without incident, following Falcon's directions.
- The party arrived at a foggy clearing containing a crumbling two-story manor overgrown with thorny ivy; wild pumpkins and boars were present.
- Soma communicated with a boar, who described "new boars" that did not speak proper boar language or respect pumpkin patch etiquette; one boar to the south was identified as particularly rude and recently arrived.
- The party suspected the southern boar was an Anchorite of Talos in disguise.
- Brewbarry summoned a moonbeam on the southern boar, forcing it to drop its disguise and reveal itself as an Anchorite of Talos.
- Combat began: Valphine struck the Anchorite with a radiant bolt marking it; Brewbarry charged but missed; Soma wounded the Anchorite further.
- Vukradin attempted to silence the Anchorite; the cultist resisted.
- The Anchorite stepped out of the moonbeam, shouted to alert manor inhabitants, and unleashed a lightning bolt striking Valphine, Soma, and Brewbarry.
- Brewbarry used stone-like endurance but still took the full brunt of the lightning and was left badly wounded; Valphine partially dodged; Soma was struck.
- The session ended mid-combat with the Anchorite's shout likely drawing reinforcements from the manor.
