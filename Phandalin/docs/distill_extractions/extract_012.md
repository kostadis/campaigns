## World Events

- The party entered the Whispering Wood and detected no signs of rotting trees or obvious blight visually, but sensed an unnatural quality to the place.
- A shimmering, teleporting stag materialized before the party; Soma cast Speak with Animals and communicated with it.
- The stag confirmed the forest has been abnormal "for some time," stating the disturbances began at the center of the forest.
- The stag revealed that other individuals previously visited, communicated with it, and promised to fix the problem but have not.
- The party offered mushrooms from Gnomengarde to the stag.
- The party located a dense thicket of brambles with pulsing, discolored, glowing leaves; Soma identified the phenomenon as planar leakage or bleeding.
- Soma approached within ten feet of the brambles; thorny tendrils struck her, dealing piercing and necrotic damage.
- Valphine cast Sacred Flame on the brambles; it failed to damage them.
- Brewbarry moved within ten feet of the brambles; tendrils missed him; he entered a rage and attacked with his halberd.
- Soma transformed into bear form and struck the brambles.
- The bramble thicket was rendered inert by Brewbarry and Soma before it could regenerate; the party passed through it.
- The party encountered wolves with glowing eyes and an unnatural howl that magically rooted Vukradin in place.
- The wolves were defeated; Vukradin and others skinned them and took their pelts.
- The party discovered a pool of water in a clearing whose surface reflected distorted versions of the party members.
- Vukradin looked into the pool and saw a vision of himself obsessively counting coins.
- Vukradin entered the pool; he experienced an overwhelming barrage of visions, whispers, and emotions; he heard a distorted creature say "He saw beyond, but only what he was meant to see. The numbers. Always the numbers."
- Vukradin exited the pool, collapsed from psychic/energy drain, and fell unconscious; he was revived with a slap.
- Brewbarry looked into the pool despite Soma's warning; he cited Uthgardt legends about such pools being shadows to other places offering knowledge.
- Brewbarry asked the pool "What side of the bread should I butter?"; he saw a vision of himself saying "butter both sides simultaneously."
- Brewbarry was seized by terror and fled screaming about "both sides"; Soma in bear form subdued him.
- Brewbarry recovered but continued intermittently muttering "Both sides!"
- The party continued moving toward the center of the Whispering Wood.

## Threads & Mysteries

- The source of the planar disturbance in the Whispering Wood lies at the center of the forest; the party is heading there but has not yet arrived.
- "Other nice people" previously promised the stag they would fix the forest's disturbances and have not done so; their identity, current location, and fate are unknown.
- The voice in the pool told Vukradin: "He saw beyond, but only what he was meant to see. The numbers. Always the numbers." The identity of the "he" referenced, and the significance of "the numbers," is unresolved.
- The nature and origin of the planar leakage/bleeding affecting the Whispering Wood is unexplained.
- The distorted pool remains only partially investigated; Valphine expressed desire to ask it questions and has not done so.
- The wolves encountered had glowing eyes and a magically paralyzing howl; the source or cause of their magical enhancement is unexplained.
- The stag's involuntary teleportation and constantly shifting antlers are symptoms of the planar disturbance but their specific cause is unresolved.
- Vukradin's vision of himself counting coins, and the pool's reference to "the numbers," may connect to broader thematic or plot significance not yet clarified.
