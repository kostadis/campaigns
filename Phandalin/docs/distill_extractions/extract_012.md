## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Treant's death is concealed from Adabra.** Adabra believes the Treant is alive ("in stable condition"). The consequences of her learning the truth are unresolved.
- **The planar rift at the Whispering Grove remains unsealed.** No faction has a clear plan to address it. Adabra opposes sealing it; the Interventionists failed; no other solution has been proposed.
- **Growing planar effects.** Thomas confirmed that planar effects have been increasing. The source and trajectory of this escalation are unknown.
- **The "planar magpie" creature.** Adabra identified it as a symptom drawn to planar energy rather than a cause. Its current whereabouts and ultimate purpose remain unresolved.
- **Lyra's career and the Enclave's internal conflict.** Lyra acted without authorisation and failed. The consequences from her superiors have not yet played out. Vukradin offered to speak on her behalf; Thomas said this would be a bad idea.
- **The Lionshield Coster's interest in planar phenomena.** Linene's bosses have specific interest in planar effects and the pelts. An interplanar shipping device associated with this merchant network stopped working at some prior point. The connection between these facts is unresolved.
- **The ship and its treasures.** Vukradin asked Ser Kaelen about the rightful owner of treasures recovered from a ship. Kaelen was evasive. Ownership and legal/moral status of these goods is unresolved.
- **Lyra's agent agreed to return to the tavern if drinks are provided.** Whether this meeting occurs and what information or alliance might result is open.
- **Adabra's marginalisation within the Enclave.** She claims to have been forced to seek outside allies. The extent of her resources, allies, and plans beyond the party is unknown.
- **Valphine's demon incursion in Menzoberanzan.** She fled a demon incursion threatening to level the city. Its outcome and whether it has any bearing on current events is unresolved.
- **Valphine's self-harm practices.** Marian's questioning suggests these practices may have significance beyond personal worship, or may attract attention from others. No resolution offered.
- **Vukradin's music for the Order of the Gauntlet.** Ser Kaelen is waiting on a composition. Vukradin has not yet produced it and is resistant to being rushed.
- **"Spiderman."** Referenced as someone who will pay for drinks and presumably has a financial arrangement with the party. Identity and nature of this arrangement not explained in this session.
