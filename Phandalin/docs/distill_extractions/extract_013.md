## World Events

- The party entered the Whispering Grove, a sixty-foot clearing exhibiting severe planar corruption: obsidian-gleaming trees, iridescent shifting sap pools, blackened thorny underbrush, flickering planar tears, and a corrupted Treant
- Lyra and two Emerald Enclave warriors were found trapped by blighted vines; they had been attempting a cleansing ritual when the Treant turned on them
- Vukradin attempted to Command a blighted vine and failed
- The Treant warned the party to leave its grove, claiming ownership and declaring itself happy to be powerful and dangerous
- Soma (in bear form) attacked the blighted vines, freeing Lyra and one other Enclave member; she also struck herself in the process
- Valphine cast Guiding Bolt on the blighted vines (hit) and later cast Fairy Fire on the Treant and fired a crossbow bolt at a vine
- Brewbarry enlarged, attacked a vine (missed), entered the Treant's corruption aura, resisted its madness effect, and struck both a vine and the Treant
- Lyra cast Moonbeam at the Treant and was subsequently re-ensnared by its vines
- Vukradin cast Starry Whip against the Treant (hit); failed the first ritual attempt; suffered psychic pain from the corruption aura
- Soma dropped bear form and succeeded on the first ritual attempt; cast Moonbeam at the Treant; suffered psychic pain from the aura
- Vukradin healed a near-dead Enclave warrior; succeeded on a second ritual attempt (with Soma's assistance); tally stood at two successes, one failure
- Brewbarry succeeded on a third ritual attempt; tally stood at three successes, one failure
- Soma failed a subsequent ritual attempt; tally stood at three successes, two failures
- Soma and the first Enclave warrior killed the Treant
- Soma used her mentor's staff on the grove; the staff confirmed the Treant, the pools, and the bushes were all unnatural
- Lyra explained the Naturalist/Interventionist schism within the Emerald Enclave to Soma
- Lyra explained that Tiamat's defeat in the War of the Dragons was blamed by Naturalists for inspiring the Giant gods to shatter the Ordning, causing the War of the Giants
- Soma made two further ritual attempts; one succeeded (fourth success), one failed (third failure); the ritual failed with three failures total
- A searing psychic pain struck the entire party simultaneously upon the ritual's final failure
- The planar pools and wells began growing and pulsing with more violent light; the Whispering Grove became possessed by a full planar rift
- A phased creature of distorted appearance emerged from the rift, collected materials from the Treant's corpse in a purposeful manner, and retreated back through the planar well
- The party departed the Whispering Grove

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Identity and master of the phased creature**: The puppet-creature that emerged from the rift, harvested materials from the Treant's corpse, and retreated into the planar well is under the control of an unidentified entity. Its purpose and its controller are unknown.
- **Purpose of the harvested materials**: The phased creature deliberately collected specific ingredients from the corrupted Treant. What these materials are for, and for whom, is unresolved.
- **The growing planar rift in the Whispering Grove**: The ritual failed; the rift is now expanding. What consequences this will have for the surrounding forest and region is unknown.
- **Adabra's true knowledge and position**: Soma intends to confront Adabra. It is unresolved whether Adabra is simply a committed Naturalist philosopher, whether she is aware of motivated or deliberate corruption, or whether she has some other connection to the planar intrusions.
- **The Naturalist/Interventionist schism**: The debate over where the boundary of the natural world lies remains unresolved within the Emerald Enclave and within Soma personally. Soma's philosophical position is now actively in flux.
- **Responsibility for the War of the Giants**: Naturalists claim that Tiamat's defeat inspired the Giant gods to shatter the Ordning, triggering the War of the Giants. Whether this causal link is accurate is unconfirmed.
- **Delaan Winterhound's current status and position**: He is described as the wisest of the Enclave; his current whereabouts and stance are unknown.
- **Malfire's current status**: Referenced as a hero of the War of the Dragons who persuaded the Enclave to intervene; current whereabouts and role unknown.
- **Who or what created the planar scar in the Whispering Grove**: The origin of the initial planar corruption is unaddressed. Whether it was accidental, natural, or deliberately caused is unknown.
- **Soma's mentor's staff**: The staff detects unnatural elements and glowed in the grove. Its full capabilities and provenance have not been fully explored.
- **Vukradin's emotional state**: He is noted to be upset; the specific cause beyond general frustration is not fully established.
