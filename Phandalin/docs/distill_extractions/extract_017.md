## World Events

- Ser Kaelen publicly declared Vukradin's name cleared and condemned Harbin's accusations as scandalous.

- Sister Kayla paid Valphine with the coin that had been intended for Locutus and then disappeared.
- Brewbarry identified Jenna as a Lord's Alliance operative in the Phandalin tavern, based on information from Spiderman.
- Brewbarry approached Jenna and disarmed her before introducing her to Vukradin.
- Jenna revealed she sent the mysterious letter to Vukradin about House Margaster funding the Order of the Gauntlet.
- Jenna offered to be Vukradin's agent, promising access to Waterdeep, Neverwinter, and Lord Nevermember's court.

- No deal was struck between Jenna and Vukradin.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Carver**: Identity unknown; remains in hiding; possesses unexplained telepathic communication ability; one named lieutenant still alive and targeted by Sister Kayla. The Carver's reaction to Borg's death and Locutus's death is unknown.
- **Sister Kayla's arrangement with Valphine**: No explicit agreement was made; Sister Kayla departed assuming a partnership exists; Valphine holds a down payment. Whether Valphine will pursue this arrangement, and whether the party knows about it, is unresolved.
- **The Carver's telepathy**: The mechanism by which the Carver communicates telepathically with his people is unknown; Sister Kayla does not know it either. Valphine noted it was reminiscent of mind flayers, aboleths, or eldritch monsters.
- **The identity of the Carver's last lieutenant**: Unnamed; Sister Kayla intends to have Valphine kill them to draw the Carver out.
- **House Margaster**: Alleged to be funding the Order of the Gauntlet with blood money; the extent and nature of this relationship is unconfirmed.
- **Jenna's letter**: Jenna confirmed she sent Vukradin the letter about House Margaster; her deeper motives beyond recruiting Vukradin are unresolved.
- **Jenna and the Lord's Alliance**: Jenna failed to secure Vukradin; whether she will make further approaches or take other action is unresolved. Her contempt for Uthgartians (Brewbarry's people) and its implications remain unaddressed.
- **The white dragon**: Repelled this session by illusion; still at large in the region; has not been dealt with permanently.
- **Sister Kayla's true allegiances and agenda**: Her stated goal is to destroy the Carver, but her broader motivations and who (if anyone) she ultimately serves are unknown.
- **Valphine's concealed identity**: Sister Kayla believes Valphine is a cleric of Lolth; the rest of the party does not appear to know about Valphine's covert meeting or the arrangement.
- **Big Al**: Survived Borg's assassination attempt; current location and state unknown; potential witness to the failed plot.
- **Privy council governance**: Newly formed; how effective it will be, and whether it represents genuine power-sharing or merely cosmetic change, remains to be seen.
