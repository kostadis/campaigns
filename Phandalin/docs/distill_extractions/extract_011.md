## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Whispering Wood blight**: Nature, origin, and source of the interplanar event (if any) are unknown. Adabra attributes it to interventionist faction activity around Phandalin and Leilon. Ser Kaelen doubts this. Party is now traveling to investigate.
- **The Carver**: Identity, location, and base of operations unknown. Unusually adept at evading magical detection and hiding from own henchmen. No leads established.
- **The anonymous letter**: Authorship unknown. Claims Order of the Gauntlet has funding ties to House Margaster and has acquired artifacts through dubious means. Unverified. It is unclear who sent it from Neverwinter or why, and what their true motive is.
- **House Margaster**: Alleged ties to both the Order of the Gauntlet and evil cults; no confirmation or investigation yet undertaken.
- **Rightful owners of shipwreck treasures**: Ser Kaelen agreed to research ownership of the magical halberd, blue mermaid statue, cloak of many fashions, six pearls, spellbook, and magical wand; no results yet.
- **Secret code in the shipwreck chest**: Valphine noted this to Ser Kaelen; its content and meaning remain unresolved. Valphine and Soma appear to distrust Ser Kaelen with this information.
- **Soma's awakened staff**: The staff given by Merill activated with new magic as the party departed for the Whispering Wood; the nature and trigger of this awakening are unknown.
- **Lighthouse keeper vacancy**: The lighthouse requires a new keeper and the light needs repair; no candidate identified and no action taken.
- **Vukradin's Order of the Gauntlet membership**: His pin is described as lost; his allegiance is ambiguous. The anonymous letter may be intended to drive him away from the Order.
- **The ambush en route to the lighthouse**: Who ordered it and why remains unresolved; Vukradin's suspicion of Ser Kaelen appears unfounded given the public knowledge of their plans, but the true orchestrator is unknown.
- **The Talos cultist with the green-light heart**: Ser Kaelen stated a typical Talos shaman could not produce such phenomena and suggested a hidden force behind the cult; this greater power has not been identified.
- **Dragon threat near Phandalin**: Referenced by Harbin and Ser Kaelen as an active threat; no further details given this session.
- **Orc movements**: Referenced by Ser Kaelen as an active concern; no further details given this session.
- **Lord Neverember and Phandalin's mission**: The party's overarching mission for Lord Neverwinter remains incomplete; Harbin warned against approaching him prematurely.
- **Malfire Morazana**: Hero of the War of the Dragons; current whereabouts and role unknown.
