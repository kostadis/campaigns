## Threads & Mysteries

- **The unnamed summoning target**: The ritual at the Circle of Thunder was interrupted before completion. What entity was being called, and has the interruption permanently prevented its arrival or only delayed it?
- **Gorthok the Thunder Boar**: Narux's sketches show a creature of enormous scale with lightning-trailing tusks; annotations read "feed before unleashing" and "the leash holds only if the stones sing." The leather cord found in the cave may be related to the leash. Gorthok's current location and state are unknown.
- **The leather cord**: Found beneath Narux's sketches; not touched by the party; its function (possibly the leash referenced in annotations and letters) is unconfirmed.
- **The black glassy heart-cavity**: The material that replaced the deer's heart at the henge center was cool, faintly humming, and of unknown substance. Its nature and purpose have not been identified.
- **Grannoc at the Woodland Manse**: Described in letters as reckless and likely to summon before the Lodge is ready. His ritual status is unknown. The manse is the designated fallback summoning site if the henge fails — which it now has.
- **The cult's fallback chain**: Third letter states: if the henge falls, the manse summons; if the manse falls, the letter-writer summons personally. The henge has now fallen. Escalation is anticipated.
- **"K" and the unsigned letters**: The partial burned letter is signed only "K." Valphine references prior intelligence that never assembled into a face. K's identity, rank, and location are unknown.
- **"The Chosen"**: Referenced as pleased with the cultivation of "the boy from the eastern clan." Identity unknown. Appears to be the highest authority in the cult structure referenced in these documents.
- **The cultivated boy from the eastern clan**: Being fed a designed narrative; "the leash" has not yet been revealed to him. His identity is unknown. He may not know he is being manipulated.
- **Falcon's Hunting Lodge as convergence target**: Both lines of the cult map point to the lodge. The cult's plan appears to culminate there. The party has not yet gone.
- **Soma's staff**: Grew a fourth bud and stopped trembling after the battle. The significance of the bud count and the conditions that triggered the new growth are not yet explained.
- **The henge's original druidic purpose**: The site was identified as originally druidic, with its purpose gradually overwritten by Talosian ritual. The original purpose and who built or used it remain unknown.
- **Vukradin's hidden vial**: Valphine secretly placed a vial of powerful defensive magic in Vukradin's pack. He does not know he carries it. Its effects have not been tested and he has no knowledge of its existence.
- **The apology and Falcon's relationship with the party**: Falcon was rude at the druid council; the party failed to act on the Woodland Manse before his lodge was attacked and a person close to him died; Valphine has made diplomatic overtures and he has accepted a gesture. Whether a functional working relationship can be established, and whether the promised light-footed boots reward remains available, is unresolved.
- **Corbin**: Referenced briefly by Vukradin in the pre-battle observation, with the thought quickly suppressed ("I pushed the thought somewhere useful"). The nature of Vukradin's connection to Corbin and why the thought arose at that moment is not explained in this chunk.
