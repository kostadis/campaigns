## World Events

- The party encountered Aletra in a room covered in interplanar spider webs, where she was extracting a package from a broken planar machine guarded by six Rift Weavers.
- Aletra warned the party that the malfunctioning machine was causing dangerous spatial anomalies and leveraged its catastrophic failure as a threat to ensure non-interference.
- Valphine successfully slowed three Rift Weavers with magic at the start of combat.
- Soma summoned a flaming sphere that scorched multiple Rift Weavers and began burning through webs.
- Aletra used her wand to mark Brewbarry with a red light, causing the planar machine to register him as a spatial anomaly and redirecting all six Rift Weavers to attack him exclusively.
- One Rift Weaver ensnared Brewbarry in sticky webs, restraining him; Valphine cast protective magic on him.
- Soma's flaming sphere burned away the webs restraining Brewbarry and prevented further ensnaring.
- Valphine struck a Rift Weaver with radiant energy, revealing a second vulnerability (in addition to magical fire).
- The Rift Weavers freed themselves from Valphine's slowing effect using cosmic abilities.
- Brewbarry and Vukradin systematically destroyed the Rift Weavers; each creature dissolved into crackling sparkles upon death.
- The remaining Rift Weavers became confused and divided between attacking Brewbarry and the malfunctioning machine as it began emitting catastrophic failure warnings.
- Aletra successfully extracted a box containing vials of interdimensional poison from the machine.
- Aletra opened a shimmering portal using a small device in preparation for escape.
- Valphine struck Aletra with a blinding curse as she attempted to flee.
- Aletra, blinded, stumbled through the portal but dropped several vials of interdimensional poison on the ground before escaping.
- Aletra disappeared through the portal; her final words referenced her boss's displeasure and a prior painful/humiliating encounter with Valphine.
- Valphine entered the shutdown code into the machine's keypad; the machine ceased its countdown and collapsed, folding in on itself and disappearing entirely.
- The last surviving Rift Weaver winked out of existence once the planar device was gone.
- A bill from Interplanar Trade Consultants Incorporated was found on a slip of paper left behind after the machine dematerialised.
- The spatial anomalies plaguing the area were resolved following the machine's disappearance.
