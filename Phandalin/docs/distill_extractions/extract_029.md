## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Carver's ritual at Icespire Hold**: The Carver is marching on Icespire Hold to perform a dark chant to bind the white dragon. If completed, he will be able to control the dragon and intends to burn Phandalin. The party must stop him before the ritual is complete, but the urgency is unresolved.
- **Sister Kaella's knowledge of Icespire Hold**: Sister Kaella is the only known character who knows the location of Icespire Hold. She is currently unconscious. The party cannot reach Icespire Hold without her or another source of this information.
- **Sister Kaella's true allegiance**: Vukradin has always viewed her as an agent of the Carver, yet she arrived wounded and appeared to be warning the party in good faith. Jax's gratitude to Vukradin for dealing with her suggests she had turned against the Carver, or at minimum that Jax viewed her as a liability. Her true loyalty remains unresolved.
- **Lady Alagondar's ghost and her connection to the Ice Spire Mountains**: Lady Alagondar's ghost appeared after the Dragon Slayer Sword was claimed and stared sorrowfully toward the Ice Spire Mountains without speaking. The significance of this gesture and what she may have known about the white dragon or the Carver is unexplained.
- **Boney's original purpose**: Boney was brought back from the dead specifically to aid Lady Alagondar on a quest but was killed by Will-o'-wisps before fulfilling that purpose. The nature of the original quest remains unknown, as does who resurrected Boney and how.
- **The Dragon Slayer Sword's suitability for trade**: The party retrieved the sword as part of a quest, but questions remain about whether it will satisfy the requirements of the intended fair trade.
- **Xanth's condition and the half-orc spellcasters**: Xanth was found wounded and bloodied outside the Dragonbarrow. The cause of his injuries is unexplained. His earlier offer to lead the party to evil half-orc spellcasters who worship Thalos (and their treasure) remains an open thread.
- **The white dragon ("the white worm")**: The dragon resides at or near Icespire Hold. Lady Alagondar's ghost gestured toward it. The Carver seeks to bind it. The Dragon Slayer Sword was presumably made to fight it. The dragon's current state and actions are unknown.
- **The Carver's transformation**: Sister Kaella described the Carver as no longer fully human — a hollow shell who speaks to his blade in a tongue that makes ears bleed. The nature and cause of this transformation are unexplained.
- **Neverwinter and the stolen goods**: The party still intends to travel to Neverwinter to return stolen goods and find an art collector. This errand has not yet been addressed and may conflict with the urgency of the Carver's march on Icespire Hold.
- **The Rot-Tusk Ogre clan blood feud**: The party's ongoing blood feud with the Rot-Tusk Ogre clan, established in earlier forest encounters, remains unresolved. A Rot-Tusk Ogre fought alongside Jax this session.
- **The fleeing thug**: One thug from Jax's ambush force fled into the darkness. His fate is noted only as Brewbarry "felled him," but no confirmation of death is explicit — he may carry information about the Carver's plans.
- **Who summoned the Invisible Stalker**: The stalker was guarding the Dragon Slayer Sword and is the type of creature wizards employ. Who placed it there and when is not addressed.
