## World Events

- Ser Kaelen publicly declared Vukradin's name cleared and insisted the Order of the Gauntlet's integrity had been questioned by Harbin's accusations
- Harbin Wester asserted that only Lord Nevermember has authority to remove him as Townmaster
- Vukradin threatened Harbin with a public "revolution" and called for an immediate town meeting
- Ser Kaelen privately warned Vukradin that removing Harbin would provoke Lord Nevermember to send troops and urged a puppet arrangement instead
- Vukradin convened an unsanctioned town meeting on the town green by playing music publicly
- Local notables and residents of Phandalin gathered on the town green, including Elmer, Ander, Thistle, Halia Thornton, Linene, Spiderman, Adabra, farmers, and miners
- A white dragon appeared and dive-attacked toward the town meeting, causing the crowd to flee
- Vukradin cast Phantasmal Force on the dragon, creating an illusion of a peasant in its claws; the dragon flew away believing it had caught prey
- Following the dragon's retreat, Harbin announced the formation of a new privy council including himself, Tolbin, Elmer, Halia, Linene, Adabra, and Vukradin
- Ser Kaelen advised Vukradin to accept the privy council compromise rather than seize the Townmaster position outright
- Vukradin accepted the compromise and joined the privy council
- Valphine disguised herself as a drow of the Underdark, removing all signs of Lathander, and escorted Locutus to a meeting with Sister Kayla in an abandoned building at the edge of Phandalin
- Valphine presented Borg's severed head as proof of the kill
- Sister Kayla killed Locutus with a rapier and offhand dagger (first two rapier strikes missed; dagger strike killed him)
- Sister Kayla revealed she had orchestrated the plot using Borg to kill Big Al and frame Valphine's party for theft from a merchant; the plot failed because Borg did not eliminate all witnesses
- Sister Kayla identified Valphine by name despite her disguise and assumed she was a cleric of Lolth
- Sister Kayla offered Valphine a partnership to kill the Carver's remaining lieutenants, drawing the Carver out of hiding
- Sister Kayla disclosed that the Carver communicates telepathically with his people and must be weakened before he can be confronted
- Sister Kayla paid Valphine with Locutus's intended payment as a down payment, then disappeared
- Brewbarry identified Jenna as a Lord's Alliance operative based on Spiderman's information
- Brewbarry approached Jenna, who confirmed she sent the mysterious letter Vukradin had received about House Margaster
- Brewbarry forced Jenna to disarm (rapier, dagger, second dagger, hand crossbow) before allowing her to speak with Vukradin
- Jenna attempted to negotiate an exclusive or semi-exclusive agent relationship with Vukradin, offering access to Waterdeep and Neverwinter nobility and a potential performance for Lord Nevermember
- Jenna proposed a revised moral framework for looting, arguing that redistributing dungeon treasure stimulates community economies and thus returns gold to "the people"
- Vukradin rejected Jenna's moral argument and declined her offer, insisting on returning items to rightful owners
- Jenna failed to secure any agreement with Vukradin

## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Carver's identity and location**: The Carver remains in hiding. His ability to communicate telepathically with his people is unexplained; Sister Kayla does not know how it works. He must be drawn out by eliminating his remaining lieutenants.
- **Sister Kayla's true allegiances and goals**: She has defected from the Carver but her ultimate aims beyond survival and taking over the gang's "new management" are unclear. She identified Valphine by name despite a disguise — how she knew is unexplained.
- **Valphine's arrangement with Sister Kayla**: Valphine accepted payment but did not explicitly agree to the partnership. Whether she will act on Sister Kayla's intelligence or pursue another course is unresolved.
- **The Carver's remaining lieutenants**: Sister Kayla stated she would provide information in a few days to identify and kill the last lieutenant. This information has not yet been delivered.
- **House Margaster and the Order of the Gauntlet**: Jenna alleges House Margaster funds the Order with blood money. This has not been confirmed or investigated. Ser Kaelen's awareness of this is unknown.
- **Jenna's letter to Vukradin**: Confirmed sent by Jenna. Its full contents and intended effect on Vukradin's relationship with the Order are not fully explored.
- **Jenna's ongoing interest in Vukradin**: She failed to secure a deal but showed genuine admiration. Whether she will return with a revised offer or pursue another approach is open.
- **Lord Nevermember's response to the privy council**: The new council was formed without Nevermember's authorisation. Whether Nevermember will accept, ignore, or respond to this development is unresolved.
- **Brewbarry's people and the Lord's Alliance**: Brewbarry stated the Lord's Alliance destroyed his people. The history and consequences of this conflict have not been explored.
- **The white dragon**: Its origin, territory, and motivations are unknown. It was repelled by illusion and may return. No character has investigated where it came from or where it went.
- **Big Al**: Survived Borg's assassination attempt. His identity, role, and current whereabouts are unknown.
- **Jenna's identity beyond her name**: Her full name, specific role within the Lord's Alliance, and how long she has been watching Vukradin are not established.
