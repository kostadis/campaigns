## Threads & Mysteries

- **The White Dragon**: A white dragon has moved into the area around Phandalin and the Sword Mountains. Its lair is unknown. Soma was aware of rumors before the party arrived but withheld this information. The dragon is mentioned in all three active quests as a threat. It has reportedly been discouraging bandits from attacking in open areas. No direct encounter has occurred yet.
- **Gnomengarde Quest**: The party is en route to Gnomengarde to retrieve a magical invention potentially useful against the white dragon, or to sell one to Townmaster Harbin Wester for 50 gp. Outcome unknown.
- **Umbrage Hill Quest**: Adabra Gwynn, acolyte of Chauntea and local midwife, is living alone in a stone windmill south of Phandalin. The party has not yet attempted this quest (25 gp reward).
- **Oozes in the Abbathor Temple**: Dazlyn warned of oozes deeper in the temple. The party did not explore beyond the orc-occupied sections. The temple's inner areas remain uncleared.
- **Abbathor Temple Secrets**: The temple was built for blood and gemstone sacrifice during solar eclipses. The reason for Abbathor's destruction of his own temple is stated to be insufficient sacrifice, but the full history and any remaining valuables or dangers are unknown.
- **Sending Stones**: The party received sending stones from Dazlyn and Norbus as payment. Their full capabilities, who they are paired with, and any prior owners are unknown.
- **Lionshield Coster Supply Chain Disruption**: Linene confirmed that Lionshield's preferred transportation network has been disrupted. The cause is unspecified ("tariffs" suggested by Valphine but not confirmed). The full scope of this disruption to trade across the Realms is unknown.
- **Strange Magic in Neverwinter Wood**: Linene shared a rumor that strange magic pervades Neverwinter Wood, confounding navigators and obscuring ancient ruins. Origin and nature of this magic unknown. Possibly related to Lionshield's transport disruption.
- **Lolth's Contingency Plans**: Lolth is stated to be trapped in a demi-plane but to have surviving contingency plans. The nature of these plans and whether they are still in motion is unresolved.
- **Iymrith's Escape**: The Great Ancient Blue Dragon Iymrith escaped after the giant crisis. Her current location and intentions are unknown.
- **The Kraken Society**: Revealed during the giant crisis and survived. Current goals and activities unknown.
- **Sage KP's Artifact**: The sage KP found a piece of an artifact during the giant crisis. The nature of the artifact, its purpose, and whether KP continues to seek remaining pieces are unknown.
- **The Faerzess and Planar Bonds**: Gromph Baenre's spell weakened the bonds between the Abyss and Toril. Whether these bonds have been fully restored or remain weakened is not stated.
- **The Item from the First Ogre's Corpse**: Soma retrieved an unspecified item from the first ogre's corpse. Its nature is unknown. A magical bullet found on this ogre was later used by Valphine against the second ogre, suggesting the ogre carried unusual equipment.
- **Second Ogre Encounter (unresolved)**: The session notes end mid-combat with the second ogre knocked prone by Valphine using the magical bullet. Outcome of this combat is not yet recorded.
- **Vukardin's Ethics vs. Adventuring Economy**: Vukardin's repeated moral objections to the adventuring-as-plunder system create ongoing tension with the party's practical need for income and with official Neverwinter policy. Whether this leads to conflict with authorities or other adventurers is unresolved.
