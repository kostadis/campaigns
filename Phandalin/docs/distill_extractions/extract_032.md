## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Dragonblade**: The party entered Dragonbarrow seeking a dragon slayer sword. Its exact location within the tomb has not been found. The northwest sarcophagus contains a musical instrument rather than a weapon; the sealed southeast sarcophagus has not been opened. The sword's whereabouts remain unknown.
- **Will-o'-wisps in Dragonbarrow**: The GM noted that will-o'-wisps are intelligent creatures. Their purpose, motivations, and whether they can be negotiated with or bypassed is unresolved. They killed Soma's rat familiar and remain active in the western passage.
- **Empty Sarcophagi**: Both eastern sarcophagi were trapped but completely empty—no corpses, no bones. The reason for their emptiness is unexplained.
- **Narrow Two-Foot-Wide Passageway**: A small passageway noticed in the eastern area of Dragonbarrow has not been explored. Its destination and contents are unknown.
- **The Dragon**: A dragon is actively raiding the region, causing farmers to flee and eliminating beer production for the year. Its identity, location, and motivations are unknown. The Shrine of Savras is suggested as a source of intelligence about it.
- **Anchorites of Talos**: Half-orc spellcasters performing dark rites at a standing stone circle atop a cave-ridden hill in Neverwinter Wood, allied with regional orcs and ogres. Xanth has offered to guide the party there. Their ritual's purpose and the full extent of their operations are unknown.
- **The Carver**: An unresolved antagonist previously allied with orcs and ogres. Sister Kella was supposed to contact the party about the Carver but never has. The Carver's identity, goals, and current activities remain open.
- **Sister Kella's Silence**: Sister Kella promised to contact the party about the Carver's situation and has not done so. Reason unknown.
- **Falcon's Hunting Lodge**: Overrun by Anchorites of Talos and their orc and ogre allies. The Falcon has offered a reward (special wood, music venue partnership) if the party reclaims it. Status: uncompleted quest.
- **Woodland Manse**: Previously driven the party away with fireballs and lightning. Still uncleared. Its contents and current occupants remain unknown.
- **Statue from the Lighthouse**: Needs to be returned to an art collector in Neverwinter before House Markaster attacks. The Order of the Gauntlet must be credited. Not yet delivered.
- **Ser Kaelen's Magical Object**: Ser Kaelen has information about one of the party's magical objects. The object's identity and Kaelen's information remain unreveal.
- **Elara 'Seasong' Meliamne**: The party plans to visit her in Neverwinter to unlock some of their loot. The nature of the locked loot and what she can do with it are unspecified.
- **Lyra and the Emerald Enclave**: An outstanding quest to help Lyra with the Emerald Enclave remains unaddressed.
- **Mine Weekly Earnings and Investment**: Horia suggested ~100 gp/week from the mine, possibly more with investment. Overhead and details have not been finalised.
- **Xanth's Offer to Guide the Party**: Xanth offered to take the party to the Anchorites of Talos after their Dragonbarrow visit. Whether the party will take him up on this is unresolved.
- **Petunia's Cheese and the Wererats**: Petunia produces a uniquely superior cheese described as better than Gouda. The nature of Petunia and the source of the cheese's quality have not been elaborated upon.
- **Valphine's Fame**: Xanth recognised Valphine as a famous cleric of Lathander and praised her courage. The extent of her reputation and its implications have not been explored.
