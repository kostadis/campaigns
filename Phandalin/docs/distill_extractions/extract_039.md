## Threads & Mysteries

- **Adabra's next move**: She left the council without accepting the verdict, described as "seething" and choosing "a different battlefield." Soma fears she may return "in greater numbers." What will she do next?
- **Sister Kaella at large**: Killed Corbin and escaped. Her current location and intentions are unknown. She is described as a deadly assassin in priest's cloth.
- **Meril's Staff — diminishing leaves, growing power**: The staff is losing leaves but gaining power. Soma finds this correlation uncomfortable. What does it signify? What is the staff's true nature and full potential?
- **Meril's intentions**: Why did Meril choose Soma specifically? Was it deliberate strategic placement of the staff outside Enclave orthodoxy? Xanthopoulos confirmed it was intentional. Where is Meril now?
- **Reidoth's absence and response**: Reidoth was away when the council was called. Vukradin sent him a Sending about the schism. How will Reidoth respond? Will he return?
- **Growing resentment toward Vukradin's persuasive abilities**: Soma privately observes that people around Phandalin who made choices "with him in the air" are starting to count backward and wonder how much of their choices were their own. "Resentment grows slow. Like rot under a floorboard."
- **The orc threat**: Orcs are an active problem requiring attention; discussed as a Privy Council obligation. Falcon acknowledged "we need to go deal with those orcs."
- **Talosian blight on the land**: Talosians are actively corrupting the land (grey soil, dead grass, wrongness). They overtook Falcon's Lodge. The extent and progression of this blight is unresolved.
- **Planar anomalies / infestations**: Referenced as ongoing problems — planar rifts, planar creatures, metastasized treant. The root cause of planar displacement is unresolved.
- **Falcon's apology to Vukradin**: Falcon asked Valphine to mediate; plans a substantive gift (instrument or studio space). This has not yet occurred.
- **Boney's inaction**: The skeletal horse did nothing while Corbin was killed. Why? What is Boney's nature and allegiance?
- **The party's journey to Neverwinter**: Chosen as the next destination, potentially addressing orcs along the way.
- **Valphine's growing temple and its reception**: The Temple of Lathander's Searing Pain of Justice is growing in Phandalin, but its practices (self-flagellation parades) unsettle townspeople. How will Harbin Wester and the Privy Council respond?
- **The Emerald Enclave schism's long-term consequences**: The interventionist position won this vote, but the philosophical divide remains. Adabra retains followers and conviction.
