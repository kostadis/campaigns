## World Events

- The party arrived at Axelholm, an ancient dwarven fortress carved into a mountain south of Phandalin.
- Soma summoned a rat familiar, which scouted the fortress interior and discovered a winch mechanism controlling the portcullis gate.
- The rat perceived spatial distortions inside the fortress where distances appeared out of phase with reality.
- The party attached a rope to the winch and raised the main portcullis gate.
- Brewbarry smashed through an inner portcullis door.
- The rat familiar scouted the main hall and observed cold, unnatural light that refused to flicker, and a forty-foot-high vaulted chamber covered in dust.
- Several doors in the main hall opened and out-of-phase dwarves emerged.
- Soma used her staff to detect a planar anomaly that had warped reality within Axelholm and driven its inhabitants mad.
- Combat erupted between the party and the out-of-phase dwarves.
- Valphine invoked Lathander's power, causing several out-of-phase dwarves to flee in terror.
- Soma struck multiple enemies with radiant moonlight beams and thunderous force waves.
- Vukradin struck a dwarf with his rapier, causing it to blink out of existence as space folded in on itself.
- Brewbarry transformed into Big Brewbarry and cleaved through multiple out-of-phase dwarves.
- Additional out-of-phase dwarves materialized from the unnatural light and joined the battle.
- An out-of-phase dwarf struck Soma, paralyzing her with the sensation of being packaged, sealed, and delivered like an object.
- The out-of-phase dwarves swarmed Soma's paralyzed form, landing critical blows that nearly killed her.
- Valphine used divine healing to restore Soma and break her paralysis.
- Soma transformed into bear form and attacked her assailants.
- An out-of-phase dwarf struck Brewbarry with a paralysis attempt; Brewbarry heard a voice say "incorrect packaging" and was momentarily displaced to a cold storage space before breaking free.
- Soma cast a tremor spell, knocking several dwarves prone and making the terrain difficult.
- An out-of-phase dwarf successfully paralyzed Soma in her bear form, leaving her vulnerable as the session ended.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Nature of the planar anomaly:** Soma detected a planar anomaly that has warped reality inside Axelholm; its origin, source, and extent are unexplained.
- **The ticking and quill-scratching sounds:** A constant low-frequency ticking (like an accelerating and decelerating clock) and the sound of quills scratching parchment emanated from within the walls of Axelholm; their source and meaning are unknown.
- **The out-of-phase dwarves' obsession with efficiency:** The transformed dwarves constantly mutter about efficiency; the significance of this fixation and what it implies about the nature of the anomaly is unresolved.
- **"Incorrect packaging" and the cold storage:** Brewbarry briefly experienced displacement to a cold storage space and heard a voice declare "incorrect packaging"; the nature of this space and the voice are unexplained.
- **The paralysis ability's mechanism:** The paralysis inflicted by the dwarves causes victims to experience being packaged and delivered as an object; the full nature and origin of this ability is unexplained.
- **Soma paralyzed in bear form:** The session ended with Soma paralyzed and vulnerable; her fate in the next session is unresolved.
- **Full extent of Axelholm's corruption:** Only the main hall has been explored; the rest of the fortress, its inhabitants, and the depth of the planar corruption remain unknown.
- **Dragon threat to Phandalin:** Harbin Wester indicated dragon attacks could become imminent, necessitating evacuation; this threat has not yet been directly confronted.
- **Spatial distortions in the fortress:** The rat familiar perceived distances as out of phase (moving twenty feet covering only one foot of actual distance); the extent and implications of this distortion are unexplored.
