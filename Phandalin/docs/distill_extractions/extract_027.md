## Threads & Mysteries

- **Spatial distortion in Axeholm**: The fortress exhibits unnatural dimensional properties (objects appearing closer than they should, dust moving in geometric patterns, tooth-rattling hum). The source and nature of this phenomenon remain unexplained.
- **The out-of-phase dwarves**: The nature of the dwarves' corruption and what caused it is unexplained.
- **The Dwarven King's transformation**: What transformed the King into a three-headed monstrosity is unknown.
- **The turned corrupted dwarf**: One corrupted dwarf fled up a fireplace after being turned by Valphine; its fate and whereabouts are unresolved.
- **The locked empty room**: Why the room was cleaned out and then locked by someone is unexplained.
- **The signet ring as a key**: Valphine identified the Axeholm crest signet ring as a key of some kind; what it unlocks is unknown.
- **The mechanical mosquito construct**: Who created it, who deployed it in Axeholm's smithy, and for what purpose blood samples are being collected are all unresolved.
- **Markings on the mechanical construct**: Vukradin noted markings on the construct; their meaning and origin have not been determined.
- **Treasure disposition**: The party has not resolved whether the platinum Moradin amulet, potion of superior healing, and signet ring should go to Townmaster Wester or the Privy Council.
- **Deeper secrets of Axeholm**: The party has not fully explored the fortress; further discoveries are anticipated.
- **The tooth-rattling hum**: A low hum described as like a massive heart beating deep within the stone was present in the pillared hall; its source was not identified.
