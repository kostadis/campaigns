## NPC Activity

**Teega the Smith**
- Arrived at Falcon's Lodge in a disoriented state with memory gaps
- Last recalled working at the Wayside Inn (corner of the High Road and Tribor Trail)
- Revealed she had been experiencing blackouts for some time prior to capture
- Revealed she had been called by "the Kraken," which promised peace from the endless wars and chaos of the Sword Coast
- Declined Vukradin's offer of employment with the Church of Lathander, expressing intent to return to the Wayside Inn first
- Determined by the party to have undergone psionic mind manipulation, not traditional magical compulsion

**Falcon**
- Greeted the returning party at his lodge, expressing particular pleasure at Vukradin's safe return
- Disclosed a troubled financial investment: a share in Mountain Toe's Goldmine, 15 miles northeast of Phandalin, acquired through a Neverwinter mining consortium in exchange for scouting consulting work
- Reported the mine's waters have been poisoned, threatening to shut down operations
- Cited strained finances as reason he cannot offer a cash reward; proposed a share of the gold mine instead
- Confirmed details of the Woodland Manse quest (occupied by orcs and half-orcs), offering magical boots as reward
- Provided directions to the Woodland Manse; preferred the party address the mine first but was overruled

**Anchorite of Talos (unnamed)**
- Had infiltrated the Woodland Manse perimeter in boar shapeshifted form, positioned to the south of the property
- Identified as suspicious by both the party and a native wild boar, who noted the disguised creature had appeared only a few days ago and did not respect established boar territorial etiquette
- Forced out of boar form by Brewbarry's moonbeam, revealing its true nature
- Resisted Vukradin's Silence attempt
- Stepped out of the moonbeam radius and shouted loudly to alert reinforcements inside the manor
- Unleashed a lightning bolt striking Valphine, Soma, and Brewbarry before the session ended
- Status: badly wounded but still active; reinforcements likely incoming

**Emerald Enclave Guards (unnamed, two individuals)**
- Recovered from captivity at Falcon's Lodge
- Expressed urgency about reporting to their order regarding planar disruptions, mind-manipulating societies, and the Talos cult
- Agreed to remain at Falcon's Lodge for two nights to rest before departing

**Unnamed Drow Woman**
- Departed Falcon's Lodge during the night, prior to the party's early morning departure
- Had previously shown interest in artifacts
- Current whereabouts: unknown

## Revealed Information

- **Kraken Society recruitment method**: Psionic in nature, not magical; rewires the brain, creates memory gaps, and makes targets susceptible to cult influence. Determined by Brewbarry to be unrelated to Mind Flayer methods.
- **Kraken Society messaging**: Frames joining the cult as a path to peace from the wars and chaos of the Sword Coast—ideologically targeted recruitment.
- **Anchorites of Talos**: Confirmed capability to shapeshift into animals (boar form) and use that form for infiltration or concealment near occupied locations.
- **Mountain Toe's Goldmine**: Located 15 miles northeast of Phandalin; owned by a Neverwinter mining consortium including Falcon; currently suffering from water poisoning of unknown origin.
- **Woodland Manse**: Occupied by orcs and half-orcs; now confirmed to also have at least one Anchorite of Talos operative present in disguise.
