## Revealed Information

- **Talosian recruitment method**: The Talosians deliberately killed clan shamans first when absorbing orc clans, to eliminate spiritual leadership and make orcs easier to coerce. Prutha's great-uncle was killed this way.
- **Prutha's great-uncle's teaching**: A pre-Talosian orc shamanic tradition of greeting the sunrise with a bone whistle. The saying passed down: "Dawn comes for everyone, even those born in shadow." This tradition predates the clan's forced conversion to Talos.
- **Xanth's knowledge of the staff**: Xanth recognized Soma's staff as having been blessed by Meril specifically, and stated that Meril chose Soma intentionally. His final look at the staff suggested he held personal history with it or with Meril.
- **Circle of Thunder layout**: A ring of standing stones at the summit of a ninety-foot hill. Two ritual figures inside the ring. Twelve twig blights capering around them. Storm actively building above — described as intentional. The half-orc spellcasters identified as the primary targets.
- **Twig blights**: Confirmed non-convertible, intelligence of four, acting on instinct and malice only.
- **Vukradin's slip**: During the conversion attempt, Vukradin was speaking to Lathander before correcting himself. Valphine noted this and filed it without comment.
