## NPC Activity

**Harbin Wester (Townmaster)**
- Accused Vukradin publicly, triggering confrontation on the town green
- Initially resisted calls for a town meeting, citing procedural delays and Lord Nevermember's authority over his appointment
- Under pressure from the party and the white dragon attack, proposed a compromise: a privy council including himself, Tolbin, Elmer, Halia, Linene, Adabra, and Vukradin
- Attributed the dragon attack to adventurer inaction as a deflection
- Appears shaken by accusations of using "blood money" but did not deny them

**Ser Kaelen**
- Publicly defended the Order of the Gauntlet's reputation, framing the trial as vindicating the Order rather than Vukradin personally
- Privately counseled Vukradin against removing Harbin, warning that Lord Nevermember would send troops if his appointed Townmaster were deposed
- Proposed a privy council as a compromise solution
- Has been telling Jenna that Vukradin is a "full-fledged member" of the Order of the Gauntlet — either misinformed or misrepresenting the relationship
- Identified by Jenna as connected to House Margaster funding

**Sister Kayla**
- Met covertly in an abandoned building at the edge of Phandalin at night
- Killed Locutus immediately upon receiving confirmation that Borg was dead
- Revealed she orchestrated the plot to frame the party for theft from a merchant, using Borg as the instrument; the plan failed because Borg did not eliminate all witnesses
- Revealed the Carver has a unique telepathic communication ability that makes direct assassination impossible without first drawing him out
- Stated she needs to disappear — the Carver does not tolerate failure, and she fears she will be killed like Borg
- Proposed a partnership with Valphine: kill the Carver's remaining lieutenant, force the Carver into the open, then kill him
- Paid Valphine Locutus's fee as a down payment
- Assumed Valphine is an agent of Lolth and that her Lathander affiliation is a cover identity
- Assessed and dismissed the other party members as unsuitable for her purposes
- Vanished at the end of the meeting

**Jenna (Lord's Alliance operative)**
- Present at the town meeting on the green; observed Vukradin and the dragon incident without intervening
- Approached Brewbarry in the tavern, identified herself as having sent the anonymous letter about House Margaster's funding of the Order of the Gauntlet
- Revealed the Lord's Alliance's interest in Vukradin — specifically his public reputation and potential leverage over or separation from the Order of the Gauntlet
- Offered to serve as Vukradin's agent, promising performances before Neverwinter and Waterdeep nobility, including Lord Nevermember himself
- Attempted to reframe looting dungeon treasure as ethically acceptable via an economic stimulus argument — rejected by Vukradin
- Expressed contempt for Uthgartians, briefly unmasked before recovering
- Did not reach a deal; disarmed by Brewbarry before the conversation; departed without agreement but with apparent lingering admiration for Vukradin

**Spiderman (tavern)**
- Identified Jenna as a Lord's Alliance operative by her insignia and "stench"
- Provided Brewbarry with this intelligence unprompted

- The Carver retains at least one remaining lieutenant (unnamed)
