## Revealed Information

- **Talos's nature:** Xanth confirmed Talos does not reside in a fixed location but lives in the impulse toward destruction in his followers. The cult is historically small, self-destructive, and structurally unstable — but the emergence of a new Chosen signals a period of unusual cohesion.
- **The new Chosen:** A Talosian cleric has recently risen to Chosen status and is likely the source of the cult's current expansion and stability. This individual is not in the forest; Xanth does not know their identity or location.
- **Adabra's position on Talos:** She holds that Talos represents "the necessary element of destruction in the cosmic balance." Xanth explicitly rejected this as wrong.
- **Tibor Wester:** Harbin's brother is running an illegal logging operation in the protected forest, hiring crews ignorant of permit requirements. Logging crews agreed to expose him to city authorities.
- **The cursed clearing:** Ten fetish dolls with pig's hearts at the base of a forty-foot burned pine constituted an active Talosian ritual working. Uthgardt folk knowledge (Brewbarry) identifies this as "black magic, voodoo, no touch." Destruction of the dolls and hearts cleared the wrongness; Soma confirmed the change through staff and elk-form perception.
- **The Enclave outcome:** Vukradin's deliberate withdrawal from the Druid Enclave proceeding rendered Adabra's prepared arguments moot and allowed Soma to win the hearing. Adabra lost.
