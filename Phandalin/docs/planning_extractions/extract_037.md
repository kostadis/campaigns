## NPC Activity

**Cryovain (White Dragon)**
Appeared on the rooftop battlements of Icespire Hold, held within a burning summoning circle of non-Euclidean geometry. Was in a mentally compromised state — eyes glazed, partially controlled by the Carver's domination ritual. Fought the party from within the circle, ascending and using breath weapons. Was killed when Valphine's sacred flame delivered the final blow. The Carver's essence dissipated simultaneously with the dragon's death.

**The Carver**
Operated through an obsidian weapon (mental amplifier) to conduct a domination ritual on Cryovain within the summoning circle. Its essence was present as long as the circle held and the dragon lived. When Cryovain died and the circle broke, the Carver's essence dissipated entirely — described as unmade rather than fled or retreated. Status: **destroyed**.

**The Carver's Veteran (unnamed)**
A paralyzed soldier found on the battlements, held by Vukradin's Hold Person. Soma argued for his life. Was killed by Brewbarry while paralyzed. Status: **dead**.

**Sylvine Wintermoon**
Arrived at Icespire Hold with her sister Runa and a group of veterans immediately after the battle. Approached Vukradin by name, presenting herself as a rightful claimant to the dragon's hoarded treasure using religious framing ("saint Vukradin," invoking the gods). When Vukradin rejected her claim, she dropped the performance instantly and ordered her companions to kill the party. Was defeated in the ensuing fight. Current status: **unclear** (not confirmed dead — one veteran fled, but Sylvine's own fate is not stated).

**Runa**
Sylvine's sister. Present as backup during the con attempt. Delivered performative religious affirmations ("Blessed be!"). No independent action described beyond her supporting role. Current status: **unclear**.

**Ser Kaelen**
Not physically present. Received Vukradin's Sending message and replied. Praised the victory, used Vukradin's own vocabulary ("conflict-free triumph"), and redirected the party toward Phandalin rather than Neverwinter. Mentioned unspecified "issues with the Emerald Enclave" as a secondary prompt. Current location: **Phandalin**.
