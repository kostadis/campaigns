## Revealed Information

- **Adabra's isolation**: She has been politically marginalized within the Emerald Enclave for her views opposing the Interventionist sub-faction, forcing her to recruit outside allies—including the party—under false or incomplete pretenses.
- **Lyra's motivation**: The ritual at the Whispering Grove was personal as well as professional. Lyra had a specific affection for the Grove and saw the ritual as both a solution to the planar crisis and a path to advancement within the Enclave. She acted without authorization from Enclave leadership.
- **Enclave internal politics**: The label "Interventionist" is the most damning accusation within the Enclave. All members consider themselves preservers, not interveners. The faction is effectively paralyzed by this internal terminology dispute.
- **The creature**: Adabra believes it was a planar scavenger attracted to the rift's energy—not an agent or cause of the distortion.
- **Lionshield Coster's planar interests**: The organization has prior knowledge of interplanar shipping technology (now non-functional) and is actively collecting intelligence on planar effects through commercial channels.
