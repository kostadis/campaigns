## NPC Activity

**Zeleen Varnaster** — Appeared at the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine entrance with the Whiskered Gang, doing no work. Attempted to deflect responsibility for the mine's inactivity. When pressured by Valphine and Brewbarry, capitulated and dispatched his were-rat crew into orc-controlled forest to retrieve the Earthstone Gem. Was not informed of the fake gem contingency plan (Plan B).

**Dwolin the Grout** — Mine foreman. Revealed the reason for work stoppage: he believes the gold vein is "shy" and requires a specific ritual object, the Earthstone Gem, before mining can proceed. Described as the best gold-seeker Horia has ever known, but bound to idiosyncratic rituals.

**Horia Stonetallow** — Mine administrator. Vouched for Dwolin's competence while acknowledging his eccentricity. Proposed acquiring a fake Earthstone Gem as a contingency (Plan B) and requested 50 gold pieces from mine accounts to fund it. Negotiated an informal addendum with Brewbarry authorizing the expenditure, deliberately keeping Vukradin out of the arrangement. Warned the party that Vukradin would not approve of the scheme.

**Harbin Wester** — Appeared at Phandalin celebration. Publicly credited Vukradin's leadership and "management" for the dragon's defeat, positioning himself adjacent to the victory without having participated.

**Ser Kaelen** — Appeared at the Phandalin festival and sought out Soma urgently. Delivered intelligence about Adabra's activities. Expressed that neither the Lords' Alliance nor the Order of the Gauntlet can intervene in the druid conclave situation due to depleted resources following the War of the Dragons and War of the Giants. Showed personal affinity for Brewbarry's halberd and attempted to arrange a sparring session.

**Adabra** — Not present but referenced extensively. Has discovered the truth of what happened at the Whispering Grove (the treant, the concealment). Has mobilized her allies and is traveling to convene a druid gathering, intending to argue that the party's "reckless interventionism" caused harm to the forests. Ser Kaelen characterized her as misidentifying the defenders as the problem rather than the dragons or planar incursions, because she witnessed only the consequences of the conflict, not its causes.

## Revealed Information

- The Earthstone Gem is required by foreman Dwolin the Grout before he will resume mining operations at Mountain's Toe; without it, the mine produces nothing regardless of how many guards are present.
- The Earthstone Gem's last known location is in orc-controlled forest territory adjacent to the mine.
- A fake gem is considered a viable substitute by Horia Stonetallow, suggesting Dwolin's requirement is ritual/psychological rather than magical in nature.
- Adabra's intelligence about the Whispering Grove is accurate — she knows about the treant and the concealment. Her interpretation, however, frames the party as aggressors rather than the planar/draconic threats as the root cause.
- Some factions within the druid conclave are advocating for stripping Soma of the staff, not merely censuring the party's actions.
- Ser Kaelen believes the staff carries significant symbolic standing among druids and could influence the outcome of the conclave if Soma brings it.
- The Order of the Gauntlet and Lords' Alliance are both effectively unable to provide support in the conclave dispute, leaving the party without institutional backing.
