## Elara

Elara is a middle-aged human merchant encountered with her groom Jarek on the road to the Tower of Storms. The party found her and Jarek huddled behind an overturned trade cart, visibly shaken after an orc attack that scattered their pack horses. She was initially terrified upon seeing the party's unusual composition, but was calmed when Valphine invoked Lathander — though she was confused by Valphine's description of the god as "painful," noting she had never heard that teaching.

Elara described her attackers as moving with unnerving discipline unusual for orcs, and mentioned they bore strange markings — possibly a swirl or an eye shape — though she seemed unable to hold the memory clearly. She accepted Soma's offer to mend the cart and asked the party to retrieve one of her horses from a nearby field. Upon Brewbarry's return with the horse, she offered a reward, which she gave directly to Valphine in the name of the Morninglord rather than to Vukradin, who had expected payment.

The party, at Vukradin's insistence, escorted Elara and Jarek safely back to Phandalin. Last known status: safely returned to Phandalin.

## Jarek

Jarek is Elara's groom, encountered alongside her after the orc ambush. He was largely quiet during the encounter but contributed a notable detail: before the orcs struck, he heard what he described as a hum — not an external sound, but a sensation inside his head — which then vanished. This detail, combined with Elara's description of the orcs' unusual discipline and strange markings, suggests a possible controlling or coordinating intelligence behind the attack. He also remarked pointedly that he had warned Elara they should travel with protection. Last known status: safely returned to Phandalin with Elara.

## Ser Kaelen Thorne

Ser Kaelen Thorne was still present in Phandalin when the party returned escorting Elara and Jarek. Vukradin reported the incident to him, and Thorne responded positively, expressing that Vukradin's attention to good deeds on an individual level aligned with the broader values of the Order of the Gauntlet. He told Vukradin, "Bringing you into the fold was the right thing to do." When Vukradin pressed him for a cheer or battle chant of the Order, Thorne stammered with embarrassment before reluctantly producing: "Steal and shield, Heart and band. We stand as one. A steadfast band." He acknowledged the chant was embarrassing and that Vukradin's talents would improve anything the Order had. Last known status: in Phandalin.

## Adabra

Adabra was encountered near Barthen's Provisions on the road to the Triboar Trail as the party departed Phandalin. She approached the group, smiled at Vukradin, but appeared put off when he drew near and instead pulled Soma aside privately. She relayed a concern about an old grove past Whisper Woods — once a vibrant blessing of Chauntea — that was now withering unnaturally. She described trees twisted into impossible shapes, colors bleeding wrong, and creatures with too many eyes moving in jerky, broken ways. She made a passing remark about interventionists and the Emerald Enclave. Soma agreed to look into the matter. Last known status: near Barthen's Provisions outside Phandalin.

## Martisha Vinetalker

Martisha Vinetalker is the current owner of the Wayside Inn, encountered when the party stopped there overnight during their journey toward the Tower of Storms. She is described as a striking half-elf with pleasing features offset by a stern, cold, and calculating demeanor, though also charismatic. She directed the party to the coastal trail leading toward the Tower of Storms. Last known status: at the Wayside Inn.

## Backes Dunfield

Backes Dunfield is the bartender at the Wayside Inn, a human notable for a scarred face and a crooked smile. He was present during the party's overnight stay. No further interaction recorded. Last known status: at the Wayside Inn.

## Cooragh Struckt

Cooragh Struckt is the cook at the Wayside Inn, noted for producing incredibly tasty and alarmingly well-spiced stews and meat pies. Her dishes made a positive impression on at least Valphine. No further interaction recorded. Last known status: at the Wayside Inn.
