## Skippy

Skippy was one of four brigands from Carver's disposable crew encountered on the road near Butterskull Ranch. When Corbin's attempt at smooth-talking failed, combat broke out, and Skippy was the bandit put to sleep by Vukradin's magic rather than killed outright. Upon waking, he found himself surrounded by Soma in bear form, the imposing bulk of Brewbarry, and Vukradin, and cracked instantly under the pressure.

Skippy revealed that Carver had prepared an ambush at Butterskull Ranch, and that the entire setup was deliberate: Carver knew that raiding the ranch would generate a quest on Harbin's board, drawing adventurers predictably into a trap. He identified the ambush leader as Borg the Hammer, a half-orc enforcer. He also revealed that Sister Kayla, one of Carver's henchmen, serves as Spymaster and runs Carver's spy network in Phandalin.

After the interrogation, Vukradin insisted on returning Skippy to Phandalin rather than leaving him defenseless. Corbin volunteered to guard him at the remote camp, though Vukradin noted that Corbin appeared to want revenge and explicitly warned him that Skippy had better be alive upon the party's return. Skippy's last known location is the remote camp with Corbin.

## Borg the Hammer

Borg the Hammer is a half-orc enforcer working for the Carver, identified by Skippy as the leader of the planned ambush at Butterskull Ranch. He commanded a force of Rot-Tusk Ogres and had seized the property after his group killed the orc raiders who had attacked first. He kept Big Al Kalazorn alive deliberately as bait to lure adventurers into the trap.

When the party bypassed the ambush entirely and rescued Big Al without confrontation, Borg emerged from the farmhouse to interrogate the fleeing bandit who had escaped the earlier skirmish. Unable to extract useful information, he executed the bandit on the spot. His rage at being denied his planned bloodshed manifested in wanton destruction: he ordered and participated in burning the farmhouse, cornfields, and gardens of Butterskull Ranch.

After completing the destruction, Borg demonstrated a degree of tactical awareness by splitting his forces, sending the three Rot-Tusk Ogres in one direction while he departed separately in another. The party chose to follow Borg rather than the ogres, reasoning he would lead them to the true center of the Carver's operation. His last known status is moving away from the burning Butterskull Ranch, being tracked by the party.

## Alphonse "Big Al" Kalazorn

Big Al Kalazorn is the owner of Butterskull Ranch and the primary rescue target of the party's contract. He was found by Vukradin in the ranch's cellar, bound to a chair with a burlap sack over his head, surrounded by shelves of butter skulls. He confirmed he had been kept alive deliberately as bait for adventurers responding to the quest.

He explained the sequence of events at the ranch: orcs had raided it first, but Borg the Hammer and the Rot-Tusk Ogres subsequently arrived, killed the orcs, and seized the property. Big Al is a retired veteran and former Sheriff of Triboar, the predecessor to the current sheriff Darathra. Upon rescue, his immediate and urgent concern was not his own safety but the fate of his prized cow, Petunia. He offered a suit of mithril chainmail as reward for recovering her. Vukradin pressed him on the chainmail's origin; Big Al maintained he purchased it legitimately, which was accepted as credible given his background.

Petunia was located shortly after through Soma's Speak with Animals magic, identified by a stamp reading "Big Al's Cow," and secured safely. Big Al was escorted back to Corbin's camp with the cow. He and Petunia were last known at the remote camp away from the ranch.

## Sister Kayla

Sister Kayla was identified by Skippy as one of the Carver's henchmen and the Spymaster responsible for running the Carver's spy network in Phandalin. She was not encountered directly during these events but was named as the intelligence source behind the Carver's detailed knowledge of how Harbin uses the quest board to hire adventurers. She does not appear to have been present at Butterskull Ranch. Her current whereabouts are presumed to be Phandalin, where she operates the spy network.
