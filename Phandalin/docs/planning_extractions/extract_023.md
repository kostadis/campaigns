## NPC Activity

**Shrimpy** – Attempted to convince Corbin to release him while the party was fighting at the Woodland Manse, arguing they must be dead. Subsequently delivered to Town Master Harbin Wester by the party in Phandalin.

**Corbin** – Present at the clearing with Shrimpy during the retreat; resisted Shrimpy's arguments for release. Awaited the party's return.

**Town Master Harbin Wester** – Received Shrimpy into custody during the council meeting in Phandalin.

**Ser Kaelen (Order of the Gauntlet)** – Attended the council meeting. Expressed concern about the Cult of the Kraken and voiced suspicions that powerful psionic creatures are involved with the organization. Revealed that Candlekeep research had identified the original owners of a magical item the party recovered from ships, noting this cost the Order considerable time and money. Promised rewards for returning the items but cautioned the process would not be simple.

**Jenna Roscoe (Lords' Alliance)** – Attended the council meeting. Dismissed the Kraken Society as a harmless social club for wealthy elites. Valphine sensed she was concealing greater knowledge of the organization.

**Adabra** – Attended the council meeting. Upon learning of the party's interference with the Kraken cult, became furiously enraged, characterized their actions as a temporal violation and planar distortion, and argued that interventions trigger cascading catastrophic consequences across the multiverse. Cited the Emerald Enclave members' cult involvement as evidence against interventionism. Stormed out of the meeting. Last seen departing the council in Phandalin.

**Valphine's Congregation (Brin, Giles, and worshippers)** – Brin and Giles, the church reconstruction workers, responded with ecstasy to Valphine's sermon. The congregation misinterpreted her message and began voluntarily blinding themselves by staring at the sun, establishing an unintended new sacrament of self-blinding and divine healing.

**Teega the Smith (Kraken Society member)** – Observed Valphine's church sermon and the blinding/healing sacrament from the outside. Appeared simultaneously disturbed and strangely conflicted by the ritual's theme of pain and redemption. Last seen near the church in Phandalin.

**Don-Jon Raskin** – Encountered in the Phandalin tavern. Self-described gold prospector, miner, fur trader, privateer, and whaler. Revealed he is the newly hired overseer for the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine, engaged by a Neverwinter consortium. Admitted he knows nothing about the mine itself. Hired the party as escort. His friendly demeanor helped defuse initial tensions with the wererats at the mine. Currently at or near the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine.

**Zeleen Varnaster (Whiskered Gang)** – Leader of the wererats occupying the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine. Explained her gang was displaced from the Shrine of Savras by approximately sixteen orcs and several ogres, some from a tribe the party previously antagonized. Proposed a deal: party clears the shrine, wererats vacate the mine. Refused to participate in reclaiming the shrine herself, citing personal value, and offered only two warriors if pressed. Found Valphine's cheese negotiation stereotypical but eventually admitted interest in cheese from Butterskull Ranch. Agreed to the final arrangement of working the mine in exchange for regular Butterskull Ranch cheese shipments. Currently occupying the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine.

## Faction Movements

**Cult of Talos (Anchorites)** – Successfully repelled the party from the Woodland Manse using shapeshifting and lightning magic. Remain in control of the Manse. At least two Anchorites were active: one in humanoid cultist form, one disguised as a wild bull. Multiple additional cultists emerged from the house and surrounding area. Cult presence at the Manse remains undisturbed.

**Kraken Society** – Identified as having at least one associate within the Neverwinter consortium that hired Don-Jon Raskin to oversee the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine, suggesting a financial or strategic interest in the mine's operation. Teega the Smith, a known member in Phandalin, remains in place and is showing signs of ideological wavering after witnessing Valphine's church activities.

**Order of the Gauntlet** – Invested significant resources in Candlekeep research to identify the original owners of a party-held magical item. Ser Kaelen is actively working to build a closer relationship with Vukradin by leveraging this investment. Operating from Phandalin.

**Lords' Alliance** – Represented by Jenna Roscoe, who is actively downplaying the Kraken Society threat. Her concealment of knowledge about the organization suggests either complicity or covert involvement.

**Whiskered Gang (Wererats)** – Displaced from the Shrine of Savras; currently occupying the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine. Struck a deal to work the mine and reside in a portion of it in exchange for cheese from Butterskull Ranch. Valphine suspects they intend to retain the mine regardless of any agreement. Two gang members offered as potential fighters if negotiations at the shrine ever proceed.

**Emerald Enclave** – Multiple fighters were confirmed to have been involved with the Kraken cult, cited by Adabra as evidence at the council meeting. The party retrieved these fighters from the Falcon Hunter and returned with them. Their current status and any disciplinary or organizational consequences are unresolved.
