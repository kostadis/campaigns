## NPC Activity

**Twisted Dwarves (Out-of-Phase)**
- Appeared in the mustering hall and grand pillared hall of Axeholm.
- Attacked the party; one successfully paralyzed Soma with an unnatural touch (effect shaken off).
- One dwarf, affected by Valphine's Turn Undead, fled up a fireplace chimney in terror rather than continuing combat.
- All encountered specimens were killed by the party.

**The Dwarven King (Three-Headed Monstrosity)**
- Emerged from behind the limestone throne in the grand pillared hall.
- Demonstrated heightened speed and combat capability compared to lesser twisted dwarves.
- Successfully paralyzed and knocked Soma unconscious with claw and bite attacks.
- Resisted Vukradin's first illusion attempt; succumbed to a second illusion of a giant bear, suffering psychic damage.
- Killed by the party. Found carrying a signet ring bearing the Axeholm crest (a hand clutched in a dwarven gauntlet), identified as both a valuable artifact and a functional key.

**Mechanical Mosquito Construct**
- Discovered in a fireplace in the Axeholm smithy.
- Described as a tiny steampunk-aesthetic construct resembling a mosquito, carrying a small glass jar containing blood.
- Subdued alive and intact by Valphine.
- Examined by Vukradin, who identified it as a sampling device based on its markings.
