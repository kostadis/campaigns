## NPC Activity

**Cryovain (White Dragon)**
Appeared at Icespire Hold's rooftop battlements, held within a burning summoning circle of non-standard geometry. Was under partial mental domination by the Carver via the obsidian weapon. Fought the party in a confused, enraged state — breath-weaponed Brewbarry twice, took significant damage from Brewbarry's halberd strikes and Soma's moonbeam. Killed by Valphine's sacred flame (3 damage) when the Carver's essence simultaneously dissipated. Confirmed deceased; body remains on the battlement.

**The Carver**
Present as an animating essence within the obsidian weapon/mental amplifier, actively conducting a domination ritual on Cryovain via a summoning circle. Required three additional rounds to complete the ritual at the point of engagement. Essence dissipated entirely upon Cryovain's death — described as unmade, not fled. Confirmed destroyed.

**The Paralyzed Veteran (unnamed)**
A soldier in the Carver's service, incapacitated by Vukradin's Hold Person. Soma applied bear claw attacks and the moonbeam to weaken him further. Killed by Brewbarry despite Soma's stated objection. Last known status: deceased, Icespire Hold rooftop.

**Sylvine Wintermoon**
Arrived at Icespire Hold with her sister Runa and a group of armed veterans shortly after the dragon's death. Presented herself as a rightful claimant to the hoarded treasure, invoking Vukradin's reputation for returning stolen goods. Performed theatrical piety ("Blessed be!") before dropping the pretense entirely when Vukradin refused. Immediately ordered her companions to kill the party ("Second plan — we kill them all!"). Fate unrecorded; presumed fled or deceased following the skirmish.

**Runa**
Accompanied Sylvine as apparent backup, repeated scripted responses ("Blessed be!"). Minimal independent action observed. Fate unrecorded.

**Ser Kaelen**
Not physically present. Received Vukradin's Sending ("The dragon is dead, so is the Carver. We're heading to Neverwinter.") and replied via the same spell. Redirected the party toward Phandalin rather than Neverwinter. Mirrored Vukradin's own vocabulary ("conflict-free triumph") in his response. Last known location: Phandalin.

Ser Kaelen issued a recall directive to the party, redirecting them to Phandalin rather than allowing travel to Neverwinter. Cited unspecified "issues with the Emerald Enclave" as a secondary reason. No resources lost or gained recorded this session.

Referenced by Ser Kaelen as a source of current problems in Phandalin. No further detail provided. Vukradin noted that Adabra (implied Enclave-adjacent contact) has been "cooling" toward him for weeks.
