## Harbin Wester

Harbin Wester posted a quest on his notice board in Phandalin offering a reward of 100 gp for traveling to Butterskull Ranch, five miles east of Conyberry along the Triboar Trail, to assess orc attack damage and help any way possible. The quest specified that if ranch owner Alfonse "Big Al" Kalazorn was found dead, proof of his demise returned to Harbin would still earn the full reward.

## Alfonse Kalazorn

Also known as "Big Al" Kalazorn, he is the owner of Butterskull Ranch and a retired sheriff. He is named in Harbin Wester's quest posting as the contact who can reward the party for their efforts if found alive. His current status is unknown — the quest implies he may be dead following an orc attack on the ranch.

## Corbin

Corbin is traveling with the party as their aide and has a past membership in Carver's gang. When the party encountered four brigands in the ruined town of Conyberry whom Corbin identified as members of Carver's gang, Vukradin pressured him to approach and speak with them. Corbin resisted, explaining that leaving a gang carries serious consequences, but Vukradin overruled his objections and insisted he go speak to his former associates.
