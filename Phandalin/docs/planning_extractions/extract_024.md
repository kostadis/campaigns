## NPC Activity

**Harbin Wester**
- Greeted the party warmly upon their return to Phandalin following the gold mine negotiations.
- Turned serious when questioned about ale supply; warned that a dragon occupying nearby mountains and resulting orc incursions are preventing farmers from working, threatening seasonal ale production.
- Presented the party with three new funded quests: investigating Dragon Barrow, securing Axeholm fortress, and exploring the Woodland Manse.
- Revealed that Neverwinter has suffered decades of catastrophes, corruption, and crime, and that Lord Neverember is attempting to remedy this through progressive reforms.

**Petunia (Big Al's cow)**
- Communicated via magical means; expressed that she misses the alfalfa fields of Butterskull Ranch, her former home.

**Big Al**
- Mentioned that Qelline Alderleaf had offered Petunia grazing rights on her land for one gold piece per week, but that he lacked the funds.
- Under party questioning, broke down and confessed that he and Qelline had been engaged to be married, but that he abandoned her the night before their wedding; stated he had always loved her but was not ready to settle down.
- Accepted the condition that he apologize to Qelline personally; departed with Petunia toward her farm to make amends.

**Qelline Alderleaf**
- Recognized that Big Al had sent the party rather than coming himself; expressed deep resentment toward him.
- Revealed that Big Al's competitive pricing during his prosperous years at Butterskull Ranch had significantly damaged her farm's income.
- Admitted the one-gold-piece fee was a deliberate "hurt price" intended to cause Big Al financial pain.
- Offered to reduce the fee to five silver pieces per week on the condition that Big Al come to apologize to her personally.

**Lyra**
- Appeared in Phandalin, reunited with the two Emerald Enclave members the party had previously rescued.
- Expressed deep discouragement about her leadership, citing the treant incident and her men's capture as evidence of her failures.
- Learned from one of the rescued men that the party had credited her with the plan that led to their rescue.
- Agreed to allow Vukradin to help advocate for her competence before the Emerald Enclave, pending a meeting the following day.

**Ser Kaelen**
- Approached the party with intelligence about the statue recovered from the lighthouse.
- Identified the statue as belonging to **Elara Seasong Meliame**, a wealthy art collector.
- Warned that agents of **House Maragaster** intended to intercept the statue to embarrass the Meliame family.
- Requested the party escort the statue to Neverwinter and present it to Elara personally, citing the Order of the Gauntlet's involvement in hopes of securing future donations.

**Spider-Man (tavern owner)**
- Rewarded the party with free drinks following their tavern performance.
