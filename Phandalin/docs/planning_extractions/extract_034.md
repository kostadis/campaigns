## NPC Activity

**Xanth (Xanthopoulos)** — Centaur encountered in the field after being healed by Soma. Revealed that the white dragon's arrival in the Icespire Mountains displaced the orcs into the valleys, causing the current crisis. Identified Soma's staff as Meril's, noted it once had more leaves, and confirmed he and Meril were old friends. Volunteered to carry the party to the foothills near the gold mine after Vukradin's persuasion. Named the Carver's three lieutenants: Borg, Sister Kaella, and Zax. Provided intelligence on the Carver's destination (Icespire Hold) and intent (to possess the dragon). Expressed bewilderment at Vukradin's refusal of the dragon's hoard. Departed after transporting the party to the foothills.

**Sister Kaella** — Captured lieutenant of the Carver. Revived and interrogated by Valphine. Revealed the Carver has gone mad and is heading to Icespire Hold to possess the white dragon for vengeance. Confirmed she had been working covertly with Valphine after the Carver accused her of intelligence failures following the party's elimination of his muscle, making her survival contingent on the Carver's death. Requested transfer to Axholm prison. Left in the custody of Corbin, a former colleague, after which screaming was reported. Last known status: alive, in Corbin's custody, location implied to be the field camp.

**Corbin** — Former member of the Carver's organization. Assigned custody of Sister Kaella. Explicitly requested permission to beat her and accepted the instruction "until she has one hit point." Described by Vukradin as a friend. Valphine noted she had been teaching him "sacraments." Last known location: field camp with Kaella.

**The Carver** — Not directly encountered. Trail of supernaturally straight footprints confirmed his passage through the mountain pass and across the Glacier of Whispers. Stone giants confirmed he traveled alone carrying an obsidian sword. Psychic residue in displaced rocks indicated he walks without rest, breath, or soul. Killed a young drake in its lair without deviation from his path. Destination: Icespire Hold.

## Faction Movements

**The Carver's Organization** — Effectively defunct as an operational force. Two of three lieutenants (Borg and Sister Kaella) are accounted for; Zax remains unaccounted for. Kaella has defected in all but name. The Carver is operating alone, no longer commanding subordinates.

**Emerald Enclave** — Referenced by Xanth as fractured and paralyzed. Internal factions are split over whether the orc displacement constitutes a natural event or one requiring intervention, and members are "accusing each other of everything." No operational capacity in the current crisis area.

**Stone Giants (unnamed faction in the pass)** — Gathered in the mountain defile, troubled by the Carver's passage and the resulting psychic damage to the rocks. Declined to intervene based on Hekaton's post-sundering doctrine: giants concern themselves only with giant matters. Acknowledged the evidence Soma presented via Meril's Staff but did not alter their position. Provided two potions (greater healing, hill giant strength) to the party before departure. No change in stance toward the dragon or the Carver.

## Revealed Information

- **The Carver's intent**: To reach Icespire Hold and possess the white dragon, using it as an instrument of vengeance.
- **The Carver's condition**: Psychically confirmed as no longer fully inhabited — walking without rest, breath, or soul. Possession of a nature that may have eliminated rather than merely suppressed the original self.
- **The obsidian sword**: Identified by both Soma and Brewbarry as level-draining. Party consensus is to avoid contact.
- **Meril's Staff**: Confirmed by Xanth to be powerful and deliberate — formerly bore more leaves, currently down to one. Meril left it with Soma knowingly. Xanth's friendship with Meril is the basis for his willingness to assist the party.
- **Kaella's arrangement with Valphine**: Pre-existing covert contact. Kaella approached Valphine specifically because she assessed Vukradin as unable to operate in morally gray arrangements.
- **Orc displacement cause**: White dragon's arrival in the Icespire Mountains drove orcs into the valleys, creating the cascade of pressure on surrounding communities.
- **Stone giant doctrine post-sundering**: Following Hekaton's guidance and the failure of the giants who allied with Tiamat, stone giants now hold a strict non-interventionist position regarding both dragons and "little ones."
- **Emerald Enclave**: Operationally split and unable to coordinate a response to the current crisis.
