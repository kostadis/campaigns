## NPC Activity

**Boney (Sentient Bone Horse)**
Observed quietly from the northern chamber as Valphine claimed the Dragon Slayer Sword. Made no aggressive action; remarked humorously on his partial undeath state. Appears fully bonded to Valphine and present as a companion.

**Invisible Stalker**
Emerged from concealment in the northern chamber immediately after the Dragon Slayer Sword was taken. Attacked Valphine repeatedly, then repositioned over the dropped sword in a defensive guarding posture. Ceased attacking when the sword was abandoned. Resumed aggression when the party re-engaged it. Destroyed in combat by the party.

**Lady Alagondar's Ghost**
Materialized in the northern chamber after the invisible stalker's defeat. Did not speak. Stared with visible sorrow in the direction of the Ice Spire Mountains, then faded. No direct interaction with the party.

**Xanth (Centaur)**
Found outside the chamber bloodied and wounded at dusk. No dialogue recorded this session; condition suggests he encountered significant opposition prior to the party's emergence.

**Sister Kaella**
Stumbled into the clearing injured, cloak gone, armor slashed. Delivered a partial warning: the Carver is no longer fully human, speaks to his blade in a harmful tongue, and is actively marching on Icespire Hold to bind the white dragon. Before she could complete her warning or reveal the hold's location, she was paralyzed by Vukradin and knocked unconscious by the party. Currently incapacitated. She is the only known party-accessible source for Icespire Hold's location.

**The Carver**
Did not appear directly. Described by Sister Kaella as transformed — a hollow shell, no longer fully human, speaking to his blade in a tongue that causes physical pain to listeners. Confirmed to be actively marching on Icespire Hold with the intent to bind the white dragon and use it as a weapon. Plans to burn Phandalin as a demonstration of power once the binding is complete.

**Jax**
Emerged from the tree line with thugs and a Rot-Tusk Ogre immediately after Sister Kaella was subdued. Identified himself as the last of the Carver's agents. Thanked Vukradin for neutralizing Sister Kaella before initiating the ambush. Paralyzed by Vukradin's Hold Person; killed by concentrated party assault.

**Rot-Tusk Ogre**
Fought alongside Jax. Paralyzed by Vukradin's Hold Person. Killed by Brewbarry.

**Thugs (Carver's Agents)**
Several accompanied Jax. One successfully netted Vukradin. Multiple killed by Vukradin's area spell while he was restrained. One survivor fled; run down and killed by Brewbarry.
