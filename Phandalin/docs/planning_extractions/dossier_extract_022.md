## Falcon
Falcon greeted the returning party with surprise and pleasure, particularly relieved that his friend Vukradin had returned safely. He was impressed by the party's success in capturing prisoners and rescuing companions. Unable to offer a cash reward due to financial strain from his investment in Mountain Toe's Goldmine, he instead proposed giving the party a share of the mine if they investigated and resolved the poisoning of its waters. He revealed he is part of a Neverwinter mining consortium that acquired a discounted share in the mine in exchange for his consulting and scouting work. Falcon also mentioned an outstanding quest to clear orcs and half-orcs from the Woodland Manse, offering a pair of magical boots as reward. He provided the party with clear directions to the Woodland Manse and expressed a preference that they address the gold mine first, though the party ultimately chose the Manse. He agreed, after Vukradin's persuasion, to provide free accommodation for the entire group. Last known location: Falcon Hunting Lodge.

## Teega the Smith
Teega arrived at the lodge disoriented and suffering from significant memory gaps. She remembered working at the Wayside Inn, a tavern with an attached forge on the corner of the High Road and Tribor Trail, as her last coherent memory before her captivity. She disclosed that she had been experiencing blackouts for some time and had been drawn to the Kraken cult by promises of peace following the endless wars and chaos of the Sword Coast. The party determined through examination that her mind had been manipulated through psionic means, leaving her brain rewired and her memory compromised, though Brewbarry concluded this was not the work of Mind Flayers. Valphine attempted to intimidate her about her choices, while Vukradin offered her a position as a blacksmith for the Church of Lathander. She declined the immediate offer but expressed interest in potentially visiting the church after first returning to the Wayside Inn. Last known location: Falcon Hunting Lodge.

## Vukradin
Vukradin reunited with Falcon, who was particularly pleased to see him safe. He recounted Teega's rescue story with moderate success. He took a gentle approach with Teega, offering her a position with the Church of Lathander. He successfully persuaded Falcon to provide free accommodation for the entire party by invoking their future business partnership. Last known location: Woodland Manse clearing, mid-combat.
