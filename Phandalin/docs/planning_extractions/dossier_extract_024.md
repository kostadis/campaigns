## Harbin Wester

Harbin Wester, the Lord Mayor of Phandalin, greeted the party warmly upon their return from the gold mine peace negotiations, expressing satisfaction with recent changes in the community. His mood turned serious when Brewbarry raised the topic of the town's ale supply; Harbin warned that a dragon occupying the mountains and the resulting orc incursions were preventing farmers from tending their crops, threatening the season's ale production. He presented the party with several new quests—investigating Dragon Barrow, securing Axeholm fortress, and exploring the Woodland Manse—explaining these were funded by Neverwinter's tax system and that the rewards could help Vukradin establish his music studio. When Vukradin questioned the safety of gold transport from Neverwinter, Harbin revealed the city's troubled history of decades of catastrophes, corruption, and crime, noting that Lord Neverember was attempting to remedy this through progressive reforms. Last seen in Phandalin in his role as Lord Mayor.

## Qelline Alderleaf

Qelline Alderleaf is a local Phandalin farmer with deep-seated resentment toward Big Al. She had offered to let Big Al's prized cow Petunia graze on her land for one gold piece per week—a deliberately inflated "hurt price" meant to make Big Al suffer financially, stemming from her history with him. When the party approached to negotiate on Big Al's behalf, she immediately recognized that he had sent intermediaries rather than coming himself, which sharpened her hostility. She revealed that during Big Al's prosperous days at Butterskull Ranch, his competitive pricing had severely damaged her farm's income. She agreed to reduce the fee to five silver pieces per week, but only on the condition that Big Al come to apologize to her in person. Her motivation is personal injury compounded by financial grievance. Last seen at her farm, where Big Al arrived carrying Petunia to make amends; the party chose not to interfere in their private reconciliation.

## Big Al

Big Al is the owner of Butterskull Ranch and Petunia, a prized cow the party needed to secure for the wererat cheese supply. He mentioned that Qelline Alderleaf had offered to let Petunia graze on her land but that he lacked the one gold piece per week required. When the party returned with Qelline's condition that he apologize in person, they pressed him about his relationship with her, at which point he broke down in tears and confessed that he and Qelline had once been engaged. He had abandoned her the night before their wedding, admitting he had always loved her but hadn't been ready to settle down. At the party's insistence, he agreed to tell Qelline the full truth. He was last seen picking up Petunia and heading to Qelline's farm to make amends.

## Lyra

Lyra appeared in Phandalin reunited with the two Emerald Enclave members the party had previously rescued. Despite this success, she was deeply discouraged about her leadership abilities, citing the treant incident and her men's capture as proof of her failures. Vukradin offered her encouragement, and one of the rescued men bolstered her further by revealing that the party had told him it was Lyra's plan that led to his rescue. Heartened by this support, she agreed to let Vukradin help convince the Emerald Enclave of her competence. They arranged to do so the following day, when the organization was scheduled to meet. Last seen in Phandalin, preparing for the Emerald Enclave meeting.

## Ser Kaelen

Ser Kaelen approached the party with intelligence about the statue recovered from the lighthouse. His contacts had identified it as belonging to Elara Seasong Meliame, a wealthy art collector currently attending a gala in Neverwinter hosted by her eccentric cousin, Lord Cassian Meliame. He warned the party that agents of House Maragaster—a family notorious for demonic alliances—intended to intercept the statue to embarrass the Meliame family. Ser Kaelen requested that the party personally escort the statue to Neverwinter and present it to Elara, with the specific ask that they mention the Order of the Gauntlet's assistance in order to secure future donations for the organization. His motivations center on building financial and political goodwill for the Order of the Gauntlet. Last seen in Phandalin delivering this mission briefing.

## Elara Seasong Meliame

Elara Seasong Meliame is a wealthy art collector identified by Ser Kaelen's contacts as the rightful owner of the statue recovered from the lighthouse. She is currently attending an extravagant gala in Neverwinter hosted by her eccentric cousin, Lord Cassian Meliame. She has not yet appeared directly but is the target of House Maragaster agents who seek to intercept the statue to embarrass her family. She is the intended recipient of the statue delivery mission Ser Kaelen assigned to the party.

## Lord Cassian Meliame

Lord Cassian Meliame is described as the eccentric cousin of Elara Seasong Meliame and the host of an extravagant gala currently taking place in Neverwinter. He has not appeared directly but is relevant as the location anchor for the statue delivery mission. No further details about his motivations or character were revealed beyond his eccentricity and his hosting of the event.

## Spider-Man

Spider-Man is the owner of the local tavern in Phandalin. After the party's evening performance—during which Vukradin energized the crowd, Brewbarry created a spectacle with physical displays that the audience began imitating, and Valphine struggled—Spider-Man rewarded the party with free drinks in recognition of their successful entertainment. Last seen at his tavern in Phandalin.
