## Xanth

Xanth is a centaur who appeared in the wilderness after being healed by Soma following a battle. Standing enormous once restored, he immediately identified and named Vukradin by his Centaurian equivalent, Vucravinios, and greeted the party with the enthusiasm of someone whose near-death had been a brief administrative inconvenience. He was familiar enough with the region and its creatures to explain, unprompted, the causal chain behind the orc displacement: a white dragon had come down into the Icespire Mountains, pushed the orcs out, and the orcs in turn had moved into inhabited valleys, creating a cascade of emergencies. He found Vukradin's reluctance to claim a dragon's hoard genuinely baffling and looked to Soma for interpretation.

Xanth recognized Meril's Staff immediately upon seeing it in Soma's possession, identifying it as a powerful object that once had many more leaves than its current single surviving one. He stated that Meryl and he were old friends, and that Meryl must know Soma to have left the staff with her. He asked Soma directly what she wanted, framing his willingness to help as an extension of that old friendship. When Soma answered that she had no grand ambitions — only to keep the world safer and ensure everyone had a place to live — Xanth accepted this as a correct answer.

He was deeply frustrated with the Emerald Enclave's internal paralysis over the orc situation, describing factions accusing each other of everything and failing to agree on whether the orcs represented a naturalist phenomenon or an intervention requiring action. He found immediate common ground with Soma on this point, framing city dwellers' failure to understand animal communication networks as a related form of willful blindness. He confirmed knowledge of Borg's death through these same networks and named the Carver's lieutenants: Borg, Sister Kaella, and Zax.

Xanth agreed to carry Vukradin and Valphine on his back to the foothills near the gold mine, on the condition that he not be asked to enter mountain terrain. He was not entirely reconciled to being asked to serve as transport and noted that Vukradin was asking him to be a horse. He accepted the arrangement, apparently on Soma's account rather than Vukradin's. Last seen departing with the party toward the Icespire foothills.

## Sister Kaella

Sister Kaella was captured following the battle and interrogated by Valphine, who revived her using physical pressure techniques rather than magical healing. Her first words upon regaining consciousness were relief at being alive, framed as gratitude to God — she had calculated that if Jax had won, she would have been tortured to death over a protracted period as punishment for perceived intelligence failures after the party killed the Carver's muscle. She was entirely composed about this assessment.

She confirmed the Carver's current status: completely mad, heading for Icespire Hold with the intention of possessing the white dragon and using it to visit vengeance on everyone in reach. She disclosed that her own loyalty to the Carver had not been absolute throughout, and that after the Carver accused her of intelligence failures, her survival had become contingent on his death. She described this as a reason she had made private arrangements with Valphine rather than approaching the party openly, stating that Vukradin had a difficulty dealing with morally gray environments and was therefore not a viable contact for that kind of arrangement. Vukradin responded by telling her she would do well in prison.

She acknowledged her prior arrangement with Valphine when challenged, and Valphine confirmed it was still in force. She expressed the philosophy that as long as she was alive, there was always hope, and showed no indication of viewing her captivity as permanent. She provided directions to Icespire Hold without further persuasion.

Her preference for imprisonment was Axholm. She was left in Corbin's custody when the party departed for Icespire Hold. She began screaming immediately upon being left alone with him, calling him a lunatic, the screaming continuing as the party walked away. Vukradin had authorized Corbin to beat her to one hit point and no further. Valphine noted she had been teaching Corbin certain sacraments. Last known location: in Corbin's custody at the camp.

## Corbin

Corbin was assigned to guard Sister Kaella while the party traveled to Icespire Hold. When told the assignment, he responded that it would be his pleasure, and immediately asked whether he was permitted to beat her. Vukradin authorized this to the limit of one hit point. Valphine noted she had been teaching him sacraments of her practice. Both Vukradin and Valphine observed something unresolved between Corbin and Kaella — a history characterized by a specific hunger belonging to people who have been made small and are now presented with the chance to be otherwise. Kaella began screaming the moment the party cleared the tent. Last known location: guarding Sister Kaella at camp.
