**Moesko, the Orc Anchorite**
- Found seated in a barnacle-encrusted chair in the upper level of the Tower of Storms
- Wearing armor crafted from giant octopus hide
- Holding an opalescent conch identified as the artifact belonging to Miral the Self (a sea elf, described elsewhere as undead)
- Engaged the party in combat, retaliating with Lightning Bolt (striking most of the party) and Thunderwave (pushing Soma back)
- Reduced to 1 hit point by Brewbarry, then killed by Soma
- The opalescent conch was recovered from his body
