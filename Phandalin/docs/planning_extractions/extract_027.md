## NPC Activity

**Chief Accountant**
- Appeared on the upper floor of the dwarven fortress after the party defeated six out-of-phase dwarven spirits in her chamber.
- Physical appearance: luminous dwarven figure, flickering and glitching, face composed of shifting geometric shapes, ink-like shadows leaking from eyes.
- Spoke in a voice blending grandmother's warmth with screeching static; referenced ledgers that wouldn't close and regulators dividing by zero.
- Addressed Valphine as "sister of the sun" and accused her of carrying "the scent of bleach and betrayal."
- Unleashed a wail that knocked both Valphine and Vukradin unconscious.
- Immediately panicked upon seeing Valphine unconscious—attempted to stabilize her with blue-white static energy, expressing fear that "the auditor" would liquidate her for closing Valphine's account.
- Fled incorporeally through a closed door after this outburst.
- Later emerged and revealed the following: Valphine's sister Aletra (fourth daughter of the Satora family) made a deal with the dwarven king to improve mine efficiency; the king was jealous of the Lionshield Coster's multi-dimensional merchant revenue; Aletra promised a special machine would solve the problem; the machine broke and unleashed chaos throughout the keep.
- Confirmed she complained to Aletra about unbalancing books, was laughed at, and believes Aletra then killed her.
- Offered a deal: provide the override code to shut down the machine in exchange for being released and not being reported to Aletra.
- Directed the party to the machine room on the lower level.
- Last known status: negotiated with the party; whereabouts after directing them downward unconfirmed.

**Aletra (Satora)**
- Drow woman, fourth daughter of the Satora family; Valphine's sister.
- Physical description: sharp cheekbones, cold violet eyes, high-collared uniform.
- Found standing calmly beside the malfunctioning machine in the lower-level machine room, typing on a glowing keypad with one hand and holding a rapier in the other.
- Recognized by Valphine through scent: lavender perfume and chemical bleach characteristic of Satora family poison labs.
- Controls or is accompanied by four spectral static spiders clinging to the ceiling.
- Claims to be in a "delicate equilibrium" with the machine and its guardian constructs.
- Spoke to Valphine in Undercommon with condescension, dismissing her and the party as surface dwellers.
- Proposed a deal: party kills the spectral spiders, she extracts her cargo and allows them to use the override code to shut down the machine. Threatened that a forced extraction without their cooperation could cause the fortress to explode.
- Last known location: machine room, lower level of the dwarven fortress.
