## NPC Activity

**Aletra**
- Had already fled the scene before the session began; her makeshift quarters were discovered in a former barracks at Axeholm.
- Her desk contained a forged ledger where she had crossed out "iron for 500 pounds" and substituted "Midnight Tears - crate 4 - do not drop," evidencing her tampering with House Xorlarrin's poison operation records.
- Her personal notes in the Operator's Guide to the Model 1489 Spatial Rectification Calliope included the annotation "use only if KP isn't watching," suggesting she operated under oversight from an individual referred to as "KP."
- A hidden bag in her quarters contained four vials of Midnight Tears poison, a platinum coin, a malachite gem, and a bottle of fine wine.

**The Chief Accountant (deceased/departed)**
- Became calm and relaxed once the spatial distortions resolved.
- Reacted with extreme distress upon receiving the interplanetary bill for 42,000 gold pieces, screaming for approximately ten minutes.
- Declared "The book is closed" and disappeared, apparently achieving final rest once she considered all accounts settled.
- Her former quarters concealed a secret door behind the fireplace containing four audit drones, an iron chest, a dread helm, and gauntlets of ogre power.

**Audit Drones (four, mechanical)**
- Emerged from behind a secret door in the chief accountant's fireplace room and attacked the party.
- One drone extracted a half-vial of blood from Valphine via needle before being captured.
- Two were destroyed by Vukradin; one was captured by Valphine for investigation.

**Harbin Wester (Townmaster of Phandalin)**
- Paid the party 250 gold pieces for securing Axeholm.
- Clarified Axeholm was intended as a last-resort refuge, not a permanent relocation site.
- Visibly unnerved by Brewbarry's dread helm and glowing red eyes, fearing demonic possession.
- Commissioned the party to deliver cheese to the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine, citing dragon and orc threats as the reason no other couriers were available.
- Noted that the dragon threat is directly sustaining orc pressure on surrounding farming communities.

**Sir Kaelen**
- Briefly reminded the party about returning an unnamed statue before they departed Phandalin.
- Was persuaded by Brewbarry to accept the gold mine delivery as a higher priority.

**Jenna Roscoe**
- Interacted with Brewbarry briefly, suggesting he could fund his music studio by abandoning his principles and "selling out."

**Wererat Leader (unnamed)**
- Present at the west entrance of the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine.
- Was being confronted by two ogres demanding the mine be surrendered in exchange for the wererats' lives at the time the party arrived.
- Status and outcome not yet resolved; combat interrupted the standoff.

**Ogres (two) and Orcs (eight) — Mountain's Toe Gold Mine**
- The ogres were attempting to extort the mine from the wererat leader.
- Recognized the party as responsible for killing members of their tribe and immediately shifted to attacking them.
- One ogre was killed by Brewbarry after being severely wounded by Valphine's spectral guardians.
- The second ogre fell back and fired a ranged weapon at Valphine; its current status is unresolved.
- Two orcs were killed instantly by Soma's flame sphere; three more were killed by Valphine's spectral guardians. Three orcs remain as of the end of the session.

- The Chief Accountant's dread helm and gauntlets of ogre power had been secured behind a secret fireplace door, guarded by automated audit drones programmed to collect blood samples from intruders.

- Axeholm was always intended by Harbin Wester as a contingency refuge, not a planned resettlement site.
