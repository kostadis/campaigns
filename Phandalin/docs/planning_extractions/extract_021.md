## NPC Activity

**Teega the Smith**
- Found standing rigidly at the riverbank east of the river crossing, in a mind-controlled state with glassy, unfocused eyes.
- Spoke in monotone about metal bending, minds being cleansed, and waves seeking uniformity.
- Referenced the missing Emerald Enclave patrol cryptically, describing them as having "brittle thoughts" reformed by "the current."
- Drew a spiral symbol in the mud unprompted.
- Briefly broke conditioning when Vukradin persuaded her, then glazed over again and wandered into the glade.
- Was tracked through wilderness by Soma (in bear form) after the combat and subdued, brought back to the group.
- Status: Recovered after the Cult Fanatic was killed; mind still reeling.

**The Cult Fanatic (unnamed)**
- Encountered in the glade wielding a black coral dagger, commanding three mind-controlled Emerald Enclave members.
- Declared the party was interfering with "necessary adjustments" and offered them a chance to submit.
- Attempted and succeeded in magically compelling Brewbarry to flee during combat.
- Could maintain control over minions even while unconscious.
- During brief interrogation, spoke only in vague references to "tides rising" and "waves coming," refusing to divulge information.
- Handed over to Talosian orc raiding party; executed by Valphine. Head taken by the orcs.
- Status: **Deceased.**

**The Anchorite (half-orc, Cleric of Talos)**
- Present among the Talosian orc raiding party that ambushed the group on day two.
- Identified the Cult Fanatic as an enemy of Talos and declared his death "one more obstacle crushed by Talos."
- Warned the party to leave the forest after the exchange.
- Status: Last seen departing with the orc raiding party into the forest.

**Tibor (Loggers Chief)**
- Refused to transport prisoner Shrimpy to Neverwinter.
- Status: Remains at the Loggers Camp.

**Lyra (Emerald Enclave, low-ranking member)**
- Not present this session, but the party is actively working to credit her with the patrol rescue to boost her standing within the faction.
- One of the rescued patrol members confirmed Lyra had sent the party to find them.

## Faction Movements

**Kraken Society**
- Actively operating in the wilderness near Phandalin/Neverwinter region.
- Has infiltrated and mind-controlled at least four individuals (three Emerald Enclave patrol members and Teega the Smith).
- Their operative (the Cult Fanatic) was using mind-controlled agents to steal an inscription describing a **drowned gate beneath Yllyn**, located deep in the Underdark.
- Lost their field operative this session (Cult Fanatic executed).
- Spiral symbol confirmed as their emblem, worn on gold badges by their operatives.

**Emerald Enclave**
- Missing patrol (three members) was located, confirmed mind-controlled by Kraken Society operative.
- Patrol was coerced into stealing the inscription of the drowned gate.
- All three members recovered after the Cult Fanatic's death, though mentally shaken.
- Party plans to present the rescue as Lyra's success to build her as a faction ally.

**Talosian Orcs (Talos-aligned raiding party)**
- Ambushed the party on day two of the return journey, specifically targeting the Cult Fanatic.
- Identified him as an enemy and demanded his death as proof of victory.
- Accepted the Cult Fanatic's head in exchange for granting the party safe passage.
- Warned the party to leave their forest.
- Represent an active armed presence in the region hostile to Kraken Society operatives.
