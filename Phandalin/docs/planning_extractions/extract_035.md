## NPC Activity

**Cryovain**
Appeared chained within a summoning circle atop Icespire Hold's roof. Observed by Soma in spider form to be severely diminished — eyes hollowed, physical features distorted ("where's his nose"), radiating suppressed madness rather than conscious rage. Was not sleeping but held, with a seething, non-deliberate fury beneath the restraints. When Vukradin's Silence spell disrupted the Carver's ritual, the chains dissolved and Cryovain was seized through direct domination by the Carver-sword rather than the slower ritual process. Became an active combatant: breathed a frost cone at Brewbarry, ascended thirty feet to escape melee range, and was struck critically by Vukradin's thorn whip. Estimated three rounds before domination becomes total and irreversible.

**The Carver (the obsidian sword)**
Revealed to be the true entity — a black obsidian sword, not the veteran humanoid body wielding it. Was mid-ritual at the summoning circle when the party arrived, performing a working to consume or fully dominate Cryovain through a slow process. Ritual was interrupted by Vukradin's Silence spell. The vessel-body moved with legendary precision out of the silence zone without drawing attacks. When Vukradin paralyzed the vessel, the sword spoke directly into Vukradin's mind, calling his magic cowardice and announcing it would take what it always wanted. Abandoned the ritual in favor of immediate domination, dissolving Cryovain's chains and seizing control of the dragon directly. The sword itself went dark and inert after expending its accumulated power to complete the domination.

**The Carver's vessel (veteran humanoid)**
Body was paralyzed by Vukradin's Hold Person spell during the confrontation. Moved with exceptional tactical competence before paralysis — escaped the Silence zone without provoking opportunity attacks. Serves purely as a container for the sword-entity.
