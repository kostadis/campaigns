## Shrimpy

Shrimpy is the mocking name Brewbarry gave to an orc raider captured at the end of the ambush. He had been put into a magical slumber after attempting to flee, while his fellow raiders were killed or routed and the original scout escaped into the woods. Bound and interrogated by the party, Shrimpy revealed that his group was an independent raiding faction, one of several splinter groups that formed after their orc clan was driven from their home in the Ice Spire of the Sword Mountains by a white dragon. Following their exile into the valley below, the clan fractured: their chief went mad, their shaman was killed, some orcs joined the Cult of Talos, others became brigands, and Shrimpy's group attempted to remain independent. When Shrimpy asked whether he would be paid for the information he provided, Brewbarry coldly told him his life was payment enough. He was subsequently bound to the party's cart and forced to pull it, arriving with the party at the deserted Logger's Camp along the river's south shore.

## Tibor Wester

Tibor Wester was mentioned as the individual the party needed to find at the Logger's Camp in order to have their delivery notice signed and claim their payment. He was not present when the party arrived; the camp was completely deserted despite the early morning hour, with tents, a cabin, stacked logs, and old stone foundations showing signs of habitation but no people. His current whereabouts and the fate of the camp's inhabitants are unknown, prompting the party to investigate before they can complete their delivery contract.
