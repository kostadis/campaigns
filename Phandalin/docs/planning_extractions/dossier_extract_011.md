## The Stag

The stag appeared suddenly in the Whispering Wood, materializing before the party with shimmering, flickering fur and constantly shifting antlers. The creature exists in a state of planar instability, teleporting short distances and leaving afterimages. It demonstrated immediate territorial aggression upon the party's arrival, repeatedly demanding they leave its territory before calming once most of the group retreated to its boundary.

When Soma cast Speak with Animals and engaged it directly, the stag confirmed that its condition — the blinking, the changing antlers, the difficulty navigating brambles — began "some time ago" and that "the whole forest has gotten weird." It identified the center of the forest as the origin point of the disturbances. It also mentioned that "other nice people" had previously spoken with it and promised to fix the problem, though they have not done so. The stag accepted mushrooms from Gnomengarde as payment for the information. Its last known location is within its territory at the edge of the Whispering Wood.

## Adabra

Adabra does not appear in this session but is referenced by Vukradin during his wolf-prompted reflection. He recalls her suggesting that he "only did it for the music," a comment that prompted him to reconsider whether his motivation was truly the music alone or whether it also included the audience. Her earlier words continue to shape his internal conflict over artistic identity versus crowd-pleasing performance.
