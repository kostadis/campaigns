## NPC Activity

**Zeleen Varnaster**
Appeared at the mine to negotiate with the party. Initially demanded 1,000 gold upfront for six months of protection. Was persuaded by Brewbarry's intimidation and argument to protect the mine for free, reframing it as defending their home and family territory. Offered Vukradin a piece of Petunia's cheese as a goodwill gesture. Warned the party that a dragon's raids are devastating local farming and will prevent beer production this year. Directed the party to the Shrine of Savras for information on the dragon. Successfully detected Vukradin's deception attempt about dragon polymorphing (natural 20), turning it into a bonding moment. Shook hands with Horia Stonetallow to formalize the mine agreement.

**Horia Stonetallow**
Present at the mine during the wererat negotiation. Provided etymological explanation of the Stonetallow clan name ("stone to allow the arch to pass"). Endorsed the division-of-labor agreement with the wererats. Finalized the mine contract with Zeleen, though the dwarves viewed the wererats' modifications skeptically. Estimated mine output at approximately 100 gold pieces per week pending overhead calculations.

**The Falcon**
Encountered on the Triboar Trail traveling toward Phandalin. Revealed his Falcon's Hunting Lodge has been overrun by orcs and ogres (confirmed later as Anchorites of Talos). Expressed distress and refused to return to the lodge. Offered Vukradin a special wood for his music studio in exchange for clearing the lodge. Proposed converting the lodge into a music venue featuring Vukradin and Valfine with ale service. Departed toward Phandalin to settle debts and regroup.

**Xanth**
A centaur encountered near Dragonbarrow. Approached the party peacefully and warned them that Dragonbarrow is haunted by will-o'-wisps at night, describing them as restless spirits he avoids after dark. Informed the party that Neverwinter Wood has been overrun by orcs allied with half-orc spellcasters performing dark rites at a circle of standing stones atop a cave-ridden hill. Identified these spellcasters as the Anchorites of Talos. Recognized Valphine as a cleric of Lathander. Offered to guide the party to the Anchorites' ritual site after they finish at Dragonbarrow.

## Faction Movements

**Stonetallow Clan (Shield Dwarves)**
Formalized agreement to mine the operation in exchange for wererat protection. Now actively working the mine under contract. View wererats with skepticism but accept the arrangement. The clan traces founding to a disgraced Gauntlgrym engineer with exclusive knowledge of proper archway construction.

**Wererats (led by Zeleen Varnaster)**
Transitioned from displaced squatters to contracted mine protectors. Surrendered demand for 1,000 gold upfront payment. Now occupy the mine as territory, receiving shelter and Petunia's cheese in exchange for acting as muscle. View the dwarves as suckers. Have accepted the arrangement enthusiastically despite their modifications to the contract.

**Anchorites of Talos**
Confirmed to have occupied the Falcon's Hunting Lodge, driving out its owner. Operating in Neverwinter Wood conducting dark rites at a circle of standing stones atop a cave-ridden hill. Allied with local orc and ogre forces. Their activities are causing farmers to flee the region, disrupting agriculture and beer production. A dragon operating in the same area is compounding the agricultural damage, though its relationship to the Anchorites is not yet confirmed.
