## NPC Activity

**Aletra (Valphine's sister, drow)**
- Appeared in the room containing the malfunctioning planar machine, working to extract a package from the device.
- Commanded six Rift Weavers guarding the machine.
- Manipulated the party by using her wand to mark Brewbarry with a red light, causing the machine to identify him as a spatial anomaly and redirecting all Rift Weaver aggression toward him — deliberately using the party as a distraction while she worked.
- Verbally sparred with Valphine throughout the encounter, referencing shared history (a previous trap using a stuffed creature as bait) and derogatory remarks from their mother about Valphine.
- Successfully extracted a box containing vials of interdimensional poison connected to House Sartorra's illegal drug trade.
- Opened a portal and escaped despite being struck by Valphine's blinding curse; fumbled and dropped several vials as a result of the blindness.
- On escaping, referenced "the boss" being unhappy about the lost vials.
- Destroyed or intended to destroy evidence before leaving; implied she would not have left Valphine alive if circumstances required it.

**Rift Weavers (six spider-like creatures)**
- Bound to the ceiling, guarding the planar machine.
- Redirected their attacks entirely onto Brewbarry after Aletra triggered the machine's anomaly detection.
- Demonstrated ability to free themselves from magical slowing effects using cosmic abilities.
- Shown to be vulnerable to magical fire and radiant energy.
- All six were destroyed or ceased to exist; the final surviving Rift Weaver winked out of existence once the machine was shut down and removed, apparently losing its purpose.
