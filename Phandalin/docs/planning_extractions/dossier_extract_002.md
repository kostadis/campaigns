## Vukardin

Vukardin is an Aasimar bard who was performing at the First Flophouse in Heapside, Neverwinter, earning no coin. A flashback to 8/3 of Ches 1495 reveals he had been recruited by a Wintershield watchman to adventure in Phandalin, clearing monsters and keeping treasure. He pushed back morally at every step, arguing the scheme amounted to state-sanctioned banditry and money laundering, until the guard explained Lord Neverember's plan to use adventurers as a vanguard before establishing proper law. Unconvinced but broke, Vukardin quit the flophouse on 1/1 of Taraksh, recruited Brewbarry, and accepted Valphine's offer to join, declaring their goal would be honest coin.

On 8/1 of Taraksh, Vukardin led the group to the Stonehill Inn, where he negotiated meals in exchange for playing popular Waterdeep and Neverwinter songs for Toblen Stonehill's clientele. After several songs Toblen let him play his own composition and rewarded him with a free beer. Toblen warned him of a white dragon in the area, and Vukardin reacted with alarm. At the townmaster's hall he selected the Dwarven Excavation Quest for its 50 gp payout, justifying it as the most honest option despite Valphine's preference for Gnomengarde.

At the dwarven ruin on 9/1 of Taraksh, Vukardin delivered the dragon warning immediately and attempted to leave, only to be drawn back by the dwarves Dazlyn and Norbus's offer of sending stones as payment for clearing the temple of threats. He was persuaded when Valphine pointed out the stones could be used for musical effects. Inside the temple he twice attempted to parley with orcs rather than fight, was knocked unconscious both times by orc attacks, and had to be revived by Valphine both times. He also fired Starry Wisp at one orc. He played original compositions for Dazlyn and Norbus that evening.

On the return march on 10/1 of Taraksh, Vukardin attempted to speak with a whistling ogre, assuming it might be a musician or enslaved, was charged immediately, cast Starry Wisp to illuminate it, then hid while the party killed it. Back in Phandalin he collected the quest reward from Townmaster Harbin Wester but launched into a diatribe accusing adventurers of being glorified thugs, upsetting the townmaster. At the Lionshield Coster he attempted to solicit a protection contract from Linene Graywind, without success. Toblen again persuaded him to play popular music at the Stonehill Inn that evening.

On 1/2 of Taraksh, en route to Gnomengarde, Vukardin attempted to intimidate an ogre blocking the mountain path, failed, and instead accidentally convinced it the party was easy prey. Last known status: in the Sword Mountains on the road to Gnomengarde, engaged in combat with the ogre.

## Valphine Sortorra

Valphine is a drow cleric of Lathander who was drinking at the First Flophouse on 1/1 of Taraksh when she observed Vukardin and felt compelled to follow him. Her backstory reveals she fled the Underdark after witnessing Menzoberranzan's fall to rampaging demons, experienced a religious conversion upon first seeing sunlight, and became a devotee of the Morninglord. She found the clerics of Lathander's prohibition on assassination and violent scheming genuinely surprising, having initially interpreted it as a subterfuge. She joined the party by approaching Vukardin outside the inn and offering her services.

At the townmaster's hall on 8/1 of Taraksh, Valphine knocked on the door and confirmed with Harbin Wester that the quests paid as advertised. She preferred the Gnomengarde quest for its plunder potential but deferred to Vukardin's choice of the Dwarven Excavation Quest after reminding herself that Lathander's clerics disapprove of open plundering. She noted the dwarves' ruins had been thoroughly ransacked and privately categorized Dazlyn and Norbus as grave robbers.

In the temple on 9/1 of Taraksh, Valphine spotted two secret doors and contributed to combat by healing Vukardin twice after he was knocked unconscious by orcs. She also attacked orcs with divine energy and her blade, with poor results on both. She was knocked unconscious herself by Soma's miscast ice knife, and regained consciousness with an ecstatic smile that worried Soma. Her last in-combat action before being downed was a missed sword strike against an orc.

On 10/1 of Taraksh, Valphine provided context on ogres as former slaves in Menzoberranzan and suggested freeing the roadside ogre if that was "what you overbrighters do." Back in Phandalin she expressed amusement at Soma's observation that dragons don't knock on doors, rolled her eyes at Vukardin's moral lecture to the townmaster, and described her near-death experience as "glorious" to Linene Graywind. She sat quietly at the Stonehill Inn listening to Vukardin's music, finding nothing of interest among the locals.

On 1/2 of Taraksh on the road to Gnomengarde, Valphine used one of the magical bullets recovered from the previous ogre to knock the blocking ogre prone. Last known status: in the Sword Mountains on the road to Gnomengarde, in combat with an ogre.

## Soma

Soma is a tortle druid of Mieliki who encountered the party just outside Neverwinter on 1/1 of Taraksh, offering to guide them to Phandalin in exchange for company on a road she had traveled several times. She possessed knowledge of a white dragon rumor in the area that she deliberately withheld from the party. On 8/1 of Taraksh she guided the group safely along the High Road and Triboar Trail, provided a description of Phandalin and its people, produced a town map, and relayed rumors about the Lionshield Coster's disrupted supply network.

At the dwarven ruins on 9/1 of Taraksh, Soma examined the settlement and noted both its thorough ransacking and the ominous nature of the temple facade dedicated to Abbathor. She interrupted Vukardin's moral objections to encourage hearing the dwarves' offer. In combat she cast Thorn Whip to drag an orc off a twenty-foot bluff, used Poison Spray to kill another orc, and accidentally knocked out Valphine with a miscast Ice Knife. After combat she rushed to heal Valphine and recommended the party rest for the night. She noted Dazlyn and Norbus cooked a fine meal in gratitude for the orcs' deaths. She retrieved an unspecified item from the roadside ogre's corpse on the return journey, which Vukardin chose not to acknowledge.

On 10/1 of Taraksh during the ogre encounter on the road back, Soma commented "The kid is going to get us killed" as Vukardin approached the ogre, then used Poison Spray against it. She described their party dynamic to Brewbarry as "not a well-oiled machine yet," noting she had only just met them on the road.

On 1/2 of Taraksh, Soma led the expedition toward Gnomengarde through the broken hilly terrain south of Phandalin and up into the Sword Mountains. When a second ogre blocked a narrow valley path and declared it wanted to eat turtle, Soma shouted that the party had killed its brother, enraging it, then coated it in poison and withdrew. Last known status: in the Sword Mountains on the road to Gnomengarde, in combat with an ogre.

## Toblen Stonehill

Toblen Stonehill is the proprietor of the Stonehill Inn in Phandalin, described as a short, friendly male human and a native of Triboar who came to Phandalin to prospect before realizing innkeeping suited him better. On 8/1 of Taraksh he agreed to feed the party in exchange for Vukardin playing popular songs from Waterdeep and Neverwinter. After several such songs he invited Vukardin to play something of his own choosing and rewarded him with a complimentary beer. He also quietly warned Vukardin about white dragon sightings in the area. He expressed concern about orc raids from the mountains and hope that Neverwinter would send help, though he acknowledged the most recent major threat had been the barbarian hordes rather than orcs. He accepted Brewbarry's presence without hostility, remarking that the wars of the past are the past. On 1/1 of Taraskh, after the party returned from the dwarven ruins, he again persuaded Vukardin to play popular music for his clientele. Last known location: the Stonehill Inn, Phandalin.

## Harbin Wester

Harbin Wester is the Townmaster of Phandalin, operating out of a sturdy stone hall with a bell tower. He communicated through the locked door on both visits, on each occasion shouting that he was too thin and bony to make a good meal for a dragon. On 8/1 of Taraksh the party confirmed the posted quests with him without meeting him face to face. On 10/1 of Taraksh he opened the door upon recognizing Vukardin as the musician from the inn, confirmed the 50 gp reward for the Dwarven Excavation Quest, and defended the adventuring system by citing Lord Neverember's guarantee of tax-free plunder to all adventurers. He was visibly upset by Vukardin's extended moral critique of the adventuring economy. Last known location: the Townmaster's Hall, Phandalin.

## Dazlyn Grayshard

Dazlyn Grayshard is a shield dwarf prospector and business partner to Norbus Ironrune. He and Norbus discovered the ancient dwarven settlement in the canyon southwest of Phandalin while prospecting for gold and spent several months clearing rubble before finding the temple of Abbathor. He provided the party with the history of the temple, explaining that Abbathor caused the earthquake and avalanche that destroyed the settlement as punishment for inadequate sacrifices. He warned the party about possible oozes inside the temple after they agreed to enter. He offered sending stones as payment in lieu of coin for clearing the threat inside the temple. After the orcs were killed, he and Norbus cooked a celebratory meal for the party. Last known location: the dwarven ruins in the canyon southwest of Phandalin.

---

## Norbus Ironrune

Norbus Ironrune is Dazlyn Grayshard's shield dwarf business partner. He was present at the canyon ruins on 9/1 of Taraksh and joined Dazlyn in presenting the offer of sending stones as payment for clearing the temple interior. When Vukardin asked whether they intended to return found items to their rightful owners, Norbus affirmed they would, which satisfied the bard's moral objection and secured his agreement. He appeared disappointed when Vukardin initially tried to leave after delivering the dragon warning. Last known location: the dwarven ruins in the canyon southwest of Phandalin.

## Linene Graywind

Linene Graywind is the master of the Lionshield Coster outpost in Phandalin. She purchased the orc axes and hide armor the party recovered from the ruins on 10/1 of Taraksh. She confirmed to the party that the Lionshield Coster's preferred transportation network had been disrupted, attributing it to what she agreed could be called tariffs. She deflected Vukardin's offer to protect a shipment and instead passed along a rumor about strange magic pervading Neverwinter Wood that was confounding navigators and obscuring ancient ruins. Last known location: the Lionshield Coster, Phandalin.
