### Vukradin
**Class/Level:** Bard (College of Eloquence) 6 → 7 (just leveled after Axeholm) | **Species:** Variant Aasimar | **Player:** kostadis1

Vukradin is a Lawful Good aasimar bard with an uncompromising ethical code around money: he will accept only "fair-trade, conflict-free" income and insists that stolen goods be returned to rightful owners. This code is genuine and consistent, and it has earned him real political standing in Phandalin — and real friction with everyone who doesn't share it. He is the party's de facto leader, head of the Phandalin Privy Council, and an associate of the Order of the Gauntlet, but he trusts almost no institution he is affiliated with. He recently made a personal resolution to stop sabotaging his music's accessibility — to embrace performing for fans, not just for the music — and a disturbing pool vision of himself counting coins has introduced unresolved anxiety about whether his "conflict-free gold" principle reflects something purer or something more self-serving.

**Notable Relationships:**
- *Brewbarry:* Mutual loyalty; Brewbarry was his first (and for a long time, only) 10/10 paying customer. The exile story is now shared.
