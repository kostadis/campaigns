## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Tower of Storms — Moesko's Heart Destruction (area T9)
- **Session:** Chapter 08
- **Outcome:** Success
- **Details:** Vukradin destroyed the lightning rod and then struck Moesko's still-beating heart at the top of the lighthouse beacon. The heart was destroyed on the second hit, extinguishing the corrupted green light.
- **Consequences:** The lighthouse's corrupted beacon was extinguished; Vukradin cast Light as a temporary replacement. The curse of the Tower of Storms was ended.

### Tower of Storms — Daggermaw Hunter Shark Encounter
- **Session:** Chapter 08
- **Outcome:** Success
- **Details:** Soma (in giant toad form) engaged multiple hunter sharks near the shipwrecks. One shark was killed by Soma (12 damage, shark went limp). Vukradin used Sleep to put two remaining sharks under. Valphine used Fairy Fire on the sleeping sharks; coordinated attacks with Brewbarry dealt 22 points to one. Vukradin also used Vicious Mockery for 1 point of damage.
- **Consequences:** Sharks defeated/neutralized; shark meat harvested and eaten (lawfully earned per Vukradin). Giant crab expressed gratitude.

### Tower of Storms — Shipwreck Exploration (areas T10–T14)
- **Session:** Chapter 08
- **Outcome:** Success (partial recovery)
- **Details:** Soma (giant toad form) explored two wrecks — the *Star Crossed Lover* and the *Sea Urchin*. Recovered a +1 halberd from the skeletal captain of the *Star Crossed Lover*. Retrieved three locked chests (one from each wreck, one from the *Sea Urchin*). Timber from wrecks was waterlogged and unusable as immediate firewood.
- **Consequences:** Chests ferried to shore; Valphine opened all three.

### Tower of Storms — Banshee Miraal Encounter (area T1)
- **Session:** Chapter 08
- **Outcome:** Resolved — spirit laid to rest
- **Details:** Opalescent conch recovered from Moesko's chamber and presented to Miraal's ghost. Miraal accepted it and her spirit departed.
- **Consequences:** Miraal's curse lifted; she delivered a warning before vanishing (see below).
