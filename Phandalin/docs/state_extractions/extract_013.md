- **Session:** Chapter 11
- **Outcome:** Partial success / failure
- **Details:** Party encountered a corrupted Treant (former grove guardian) infected by a planar rift. The Treant was defeated in combat by Soma and an Enclave Warrior (poison and arrows). The accompanying blighted vines were partially cleared, freeing Emerald Enclave prisoners Lyra and others.

A phased, puppet-like extraplanar creature emerged from the rift, collected materials from the Treant's corpse, and returned into the planar tear — suggesting an unknown intelligence is deliberately harvesting from the corruption.

## Party Accomplishments & Acquisitions

### Lore Gained — The Naturalist/Interventionist Schism
- The Emerald Enclave is internally divided over whether planar intrusions into the Prime Material Plane are part of the natural order or alien corruptions requiring intervention.
- The schism originated during the War of the Dragons (Tiamat's attempted summoning). Delaan Winterhound, convinced by the hero Malfire, led the Enclave to join the fight. Naturalists within the Enclave condemned this as a violation of their oaths.
- Naturalists hold that the multiverse itself defines the boundary of the natural world; planar rifts and their effects are therefore natural and must not be interfered with.
- Interventionists hold that the Prime Material Plane has its own integrity; planar infections are alien corruptions and must be cleansed.
- The defeat of Tiamat is blamed by Naturalists for inspiring the Giant gods to shatter the Ordning, leading to the War of the Giants — used as evidence that intervention causes further harm.

### Soma — Staff Confirmation
- Soma's mentor-given staff (detects unnatural elements) glowed and confirmed: the Treant, the iridescent pools, and the blighted bushes are **all unnatural**. This directly contradicts Adabra's naturalist framing of such phenomena.

### Phased Puppet Creature — Observed
- An extraplanar creature, phased and marionette-like, emerged from the expanded rift after the ritual failure. It deliberately collected material from the Treant's corpse and returned into the planar tear. Identified by Brewbarry as being under external control. Implies an unknown intelligence is actively using the planar rifts for a purpose.
