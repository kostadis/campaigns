## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Dwarven Excavation Quest — warn dwarves of dragon
- **Session:** 8–10/1 of Taraskh 1495
- **Outcome:** Success
- **Details:** Party traveled to the dwarven excavation site in the Sword Mountains. Vukardin delivered the dragon warning to Dazlyn Grayshard and Norbus Ironrune. Party also cleared orcs attacking the excavation site (area E1).
- **Consequences:** Quest reward of 50 gp collected from Townmaster Harbin Wester. Dazlyn and Norbus offered sending stones as additional reward for clearing the orcs.

### Dwarven Excavation — orc attack on excavation (area E1)
- **Session:** 9/1 of Taraskh 1495
- **Outcome:** Success
- **Details:** Party engaged orcs at the excavation. Vukardin attempted to parley and was knocked unconscious twice. Valphine was knocked unconscious by friendly fire (Soma's Ice Knife). Brewbarry killed multiple orcs with his halberd. Remaining orcs defeated.
- **Consequences:** Dwarves rewarded party; Dazlyn and Norbus cooked a meal and offered the sending stones reward.

### Dazlyn Grayshard and Norbus Ironrune — sending stones reward given
- **Session:** 9–10/1 of Taraskh 1495
- **Outcome:** Confirmed
- **Details:** Dwarves offered sending stones in lieu of money as reward for clearing the orcs from the excavation site. Vukardin accepted, motivated by potential musical applications.

### Gnomengarde — gnome guard post encounter (area G9)
- **Session:** 2/1 of Taraskh 1495 (implied by Gnomengarde arrival)
- **Outcome:** Resolved peacefully through negotiation
- **Details:** Guards Pog (male) and Ulla (female) challenged the party. Vukardin argued logically; Brewbarry's claim to be a former Uthgardt barbarian resolved the standing orders ambiguity. Party granted entry without violence.

### Gnomengarde — Facktoré and autoloading crossbow encounter (area G7)
- **Session:** 2/1 of Taraskh 1495
- **Outcome:** Device disabled; Facktoré fled
- **Details:** Deranged gnome Facktoré tested his rotating four-crossbow autoloading device on the party. Party disabled the device. Facktoré escaped.

### Gnomengarde — spinning blades trap (area G10)
- **Session:** 2/1 of Taraskh 1495
- **Outcome:** Bypassed initially; subsequently disabled on second approach
- **Details:** Party initially bypassed the spinning blade trap by taking the long route at Ulla's direction. On return from the throne room, the blades were disabled to allow passage.

### Gnomengarde — King Korboz madness — first contact
- **Session:** 2/1 of Taraskh 1495
- **Outcome:** Contact made; resolution in progress
- **Details:** King Korboz is in a state of hysterical madness, convinced a shapeshifter is present. Co-King Gnerkli is sane and in contact with the party. Quest to kill the shapeshifter accepted in exchange for a Hat of Wizardry.

### Gnomengarde — mimic encounter (mystery monster)
- **Session:** 2/1 of Taraskh 1495 (ongoing at chapter end)
- **Outcome:** In progress — mimic discovered and combat initiated
- **Details:** Valphine located the mimic hiding among barrels in the cellar. Combat began: Brewbarry's halberd became stuck in the creature; Vukardin's Vicious Mockery caused the mimic to flinch. Chapter ends mid-combat.
