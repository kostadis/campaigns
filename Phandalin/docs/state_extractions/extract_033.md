## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Dragon Barrow — narrow tunnel collapse trap (area D5)
- **Session:** Chapter 31
- **Outcome:** Success
- **Details:** Vukradin triggered a hidden pressure plate in the two-foot-wide secret passageway, causing walls to collapse and burying most of the party. Soma used Mould Earth to extract all party members and excavate the entire blocked passage.
- **Consequences:** Party continued forward; no permanent casualties.

### Dragon Barrow — skeletal horse encounter (area D4)
- **Session:** Chapter 31
- **Outcome:** Success (non-combat resolution)
- **Details:** The bones of a horse in a southern niche reanimated as a skeletal horse. Soma used Speak with Animals to communicate with it. The party convinced it that Soma was Lady Alagondar reincarnated. The horse (originally named Barney, renamed Boney) accepted this and became cooperative, providing directional information about the sword's location.
- **Consequences:** Boney became a party ally/sidekick; revealed the sword was "across the way" to the north.

### Dragon Barrow — pit trap encounters (area D3)
- **Session:** Chapter 31
- **Outcome:** Triggered multiple times, party survived
- **Details:** Brewbarry fell into a non-magical pit trap (12 damage). Vukradin also fell into a pit trap later (19 damage, broke Silence concentration). Brewbarry later deliberately triggered a trap with his halberd before leaping over it.
- **Consequences:** Significant damage taken; Silence spell lost.

### Dragon Barrow — will-o'-wisp encounter (area D2)
- **Session:** Chapter 31
- **Outcome:** Success — all will-o'-wisps destroyed
- **Details:** Multiple will-o'-wisps encountered throughout the barrow. Valphine used Turn Undead to rout one; Soma's Moonbeam destroyed one caught in Vukradin's Cloud of Daggers; Brewbarry in a battle rage destroyed another in melee. A third emerged from a sarcophagus and was also eliminated.
- **Consequences:** Dungeon cleared of will-o'-wisp threat; path to the dragon slayer sword opened.

### Dragon Barrow — dragon slayer sword and invisible stalker encounter (area D8)
- **Session:** Chapter 31
- **Outcome:** Success — sword retrieved
- **Details:** Valphine navigated past remaining traps, jumped over a pit trap, entered the northern chamber containing the bones of a dragon with its skull on the floor. The Dragon Slayer Sword rested atop the dragon's skull, exactly where Boney indicated — across from Lady Alagondar's resting place. No invisible stalker encounter recorded in this session.
- **Consequences:** Dragon Slayer Sword obtained; Dragon Barrow Quest effectively completed.
