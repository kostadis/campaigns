## NPCs
- **Tiamat**: Co-resident of Avernus, duped Severin and the Cult of the Dragon to attempt bringing her into the world, but the plan was actually Zariel's
- **Zariel**: Found the loophole to bring Tiamat into the world, tired of Tiamat's presence in her kingdom, had other plans beyond Tiamat's scheme
- **Severin**: Duped by Tiamat into serving the Cult of the Dragon's plans
- **The All-Father**: God of the giants, fantasized about giant rule in the north
- **Hekaton**: Storm Giant King, created an alliance between giants and the people of the realms
- **Ilymrith**: Great Ancient Blue Dragon, almost destroyed the kingdoms of the giants, escaped
- **The sage KP**: Found a piece of an artifact he needed
- **Lolth**: Trapped in a demi-plane as part of a multi-century plan, schemer with contingency plans, orchestrated the weakening of planar bonds
- **Gromph Baenre**: Of the First House of Menozberanzan, Lolth's pawn who cast the spell twisting the Faerzess magic, thought it would give Drow control but was wrong
- **Grazz't**: Demon who instigated widespread insanity and madness in the Underdark
- **Zugtomy**: Demoness who disseminated perilous fungi
- **Orcus**: Demon who marshaled armies of undead
- **Demogorgon**: Demon who wreaked havoc on cities including Menozberranzan
- **Lord Nevermember**: Turned his eye to the hinterland of his city-state, particularly Phandalin, offers tax-free plunder to adventurers
- **Vukardin**: Avant-garde musician whose music was underappreciated, one of the adventurers hired by Lord Nevermember

## Factions
- **Cult of the Dragon**: Duped by Tiamat into attempting to bring her into the world, suffered conclusive defeat in the War of the Dragons
- **Kraken Society**: Was revealed and survived
- **Lord's Alliance**: Grew in strength, forces were recovering from the War of the Dragons
- **First House of Menozberanzan**: Gromph Baenre's house
- **Uthgartd Barbarians**: Launched devastating assault on the North, destroyed numerous smaller communities, invasion ultimately ended by combined forces
- **Northern Territories**: Recovering from the War of the Dragons
- **Silvery Marches**: Alliance was revived due to efforts of agents from Everlund

## Locations
- **Avernus**: Where Tiamat co-resides with Zariel
- **The Underdark**: Where the Faerzess grows, became chaotic when demon lords entered after planar bonds weakened
- **Menozberranzan**: Notable city wreaked havoc upon by Demogorgon
- **Triboar**: Razed by the Uthgartd Barbarians
- **Calling Horn**: Obliterated by the Uthgartd Barbarians
- **Silverymoon**: Villages on outskirts pillaged by barbarians
- **Mirabar**: Villages on outskirts pillaged by barbarians
- **Everlund**: Source of agents who helped revive the Silvery Marches alliance
- **Phandalin**: Town in Lord Nevermember's hinterland that he wants cleared of monsters, cults, and evildoers

## Events
- The great sundering completed, but gods continued to interfere through loopholes
- Tiamat's attempt to enter the world through the Cult of the Dragon was thwarted
- The All-Father's plans for giant rule in the north were thwarted
- Gromph Baenre cast a spell to twist the Faerzess magic, accidentally weakening bonds between the Abyss and Toril
- Demon lords entered the Underdark, causing chaos both above and below
- Uthgartd Barbarians launched devastating assault on the North, destroying multiple communities
- Combined forces of Giants and major cities successfully ended the barbarian invasion
- Lord Nevermember began hiring adventurers to clear the Phandalin area

## Revealed Information
- Zariel, not Tiamat, was the true architect of the plan to bring Tiamat into the world
- The Faerzess is an unknown magical substance in the Underdark that acts as naturally occurring forbiddance
- Gromph Baenre's spell backfired, weakening planar bonds instead of giving Drow control over the Faerzess
- The chaos in the Underdark may have provoked the barbarian assault on the North
- The Ordning being reinstated prevented catastrophic giant attacks on cities
- Lord Nevermember offers tax-free, no-questions-asked plunder to adventurers clearing his hinterlands
