## NPCs
- **Chief Accountant**: Luminous dwarven undead, former bookkeeper of the fortress. Face shifts geometric shapes with ink shadows leaking from eyes, voice blends grandmother's warmth with static. Attacked party with devastating wail, then panicked upon seeing unconscious Valphine, attempting to heal her while fearing "the auditor" would liquidate her account. Revealed Aletra killed her after she complained about unbalanced books. Offered override code in exchange for freedom and silence. Last seen fleeing incorporeally through closed door.

- **Aletra Satora**: Drow woman, Valphine's sister (fourth daughter of Satora family). Wears high-collared uniform, carries rapier, smells of lavender perfume and chemical bleach from family poison labs. Sharp cheekbones, cold violet eyes. Made deal with king to improve mine efficiency using the machine. Currently maintaining delicate equilibrium with malfunctioning device while extracting smuggled drow poison. Speaks condescendingly in Undercommon, threatens explosion if interference occurs. Last location: machine room beside the chaotic contraption.

- **Six out-of-phase dwarven figures**: Spectral dwarves screaming about efficiency and processes, became more agitated when offered help with efficiency. All destroyed in combat.

## Factions
- **Lionshield Coster**: Multi-dimensional merchant operations with incredible revenue that made the king jealous of their success.

## Party
- **Valphine**: Recognized sister Aletra by scent (lavender perfume and chemical bleach). Called "sister of the sun" by Chief Accountant who accused her of carrying "scent of bleach and betrayal." Knocked unconscious by Chief Accountant's wail. Observed Aletra's true purpose extracting drow poison from machine.

- **Brewbarry**: Charged forward in rage during combat, halberd cutting down spectral dwarves with brutal efficiency. Weathered Chief Accountant's supernatural wail while wounded.

- **Vukradin**: Attempted to convince agitated dwarves about efficiency improvements. Cast magic slowing enemies, summoned giant adding machine to attack Chief Accountant. Knocked unconscious by supernatural wail.

- **Soma**: Fought in bear form with claws, moved to stabilize fallen companions after Chief Accountant's attack. Weathered the supernatural assault while wounded.

- **Party acquisitions**: Discovered serviceable armor and weaponsmith tools useful for Phandalin people.

## Quests & Threads

## Locations
- **Ancient dwarven fortress first floor**: Storage room with armor and tools, barracks with cots and blocked chimneys, empty armory with only racks and hooks remaining.

- **Upper floor chambers**: More pronounced time/spatial distortions, site of combat with spectral dwarves and Chief Accountant encounter.

- **Machine room**: Contains massive brass contraption with orange organ pipes, spinning copper centrifuges, mechanical arms, venting purple steam. Device vibrates violently with magical voice booming about non-Euclidean geometry. Air thick with static electricity.
