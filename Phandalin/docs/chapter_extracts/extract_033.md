## NPCs

### Boney
Sentient bone horse, bonded with Valphine, partially alive state. Remarked that irregularity is no longer a problem for him. Watched the party debate who should carry the Dragon Slayer Sword with quiet amusement.

### Lady Alagondar (Ghost)
Materialized in the northern chamber after the party secured the Dragon Slayer Sword. Did not speak, only stared with hollow, mournful eyes toward the Ice Spire Mountains before fading away.

### Xanth
Centaur who stood bloodied in the fading light outside the chamber. Had previously offered to lead the party to orcs that worship Thalos with promise of conflict-free treasure.

### Sister Kaella
Agent of the Carver who stumbled into the clearing with torn cloak and slashed armor. Warned that the Carver is no longer a man but a hollow shell speaking in a tongue that makes ears bleed, marching on Icespire Hold to bind the white dragon. Plans to wear the dragon like armor and burn Phandalin to test his power. Was paralyzed by Vukradin and knocked unconscious by the party before completing her warning. Only person who knew the location of Icespire Hold.

### Jax
Last of the Carver's agents. Led an ambush with thugs and a Rot-Tusk Ogre. Thanked Vukradin coldly for dealing with Sister Kaella. Was paralyzed by Vukradin's Hold Person spell and killed by the party's combined assault.

### The Carver
Described by Sister Kaella as no longer human - a hollow shell speaking to his blade in a tongue that makes ears bleed. Marching on Icespire Hold to bind the white dragon, intending to wear it like armor and burn Phandalin.

## Factions

### The Carver's Forces
Lost their remaining agents - Sister Kaella was neutralized and Jax was killed along with his thugs and the Rot-Tusk Ogre. The Carver now operates alone in his march on Icespire Hold.

### Rot-Tusk Ogre Clan
One member fought alongside Jax's thugs. The party has an existing blood feud with this clan from earlier forest encounters. The ogre was paralyzed and killed.

## Party

### Valphine
Claimed the Dragon Slayer Sword from the dragon skull, was about to give it to Brewbarry when attacked by invisible stalker. Dropped the sword during combat to stop the stalker's attacks. Cast Moonbeam to reveal the stalker and Fairy Fire to outline it. Later cast Spirit Guardians during the ambush fight. Persuaded Vukradin not to kill Sister Kaella outright.

### Brewbarry
Ultimately claimed the Dragon Slayer Sword after the invisible stalker was defeated. Was persuaded by Vukradin not to pick up the sword during combat. Took significant damage from the stalker but absorbed much through Stone's Endurance. During the ambush, delivered critical hits to paralyzed enemies and pursued the fleeing thug.

### Vukradin
Cast Cloud of Daggers on the invisible stalker. Viewed Sister Kaella as a threat and moved with deadly intent against her, casting Hold Person to paralyze and silence her mid-warning. Later cast Hold Person on both Jax and the Rot-Tusk Ogre simultaneously. While restrained by a net, maintained concentration and cast Array of Cards in a cone to defeat multiple thugs.

### Soma
Cast Moonbeam against the invisible stalker, dealing devastating damage and revealing its form. Transformed into a bear during the ambush to attack the paralyzed Jax. Persuaded Vukradin not to kill Sister Kaella outright.

## Locations

### Northern Chamber
Ancient chamber containing dragon bones where the Dragon Slayer Sword was found resting on a dragon skull. Site of invisible stalker ambush after the sword was claimed.

### Icespire Hold
Location in the Ice Spire Mountains where the white dragon dwells and where the Carver is marching to complete his binding ritual. Sister Kaella was the only one who knew its exact location.

## Events

### Dragon Slayer Sword Discovery and Guardian Fight
Valphine found the Dragon Slayer Sword on a dragon skull. Invisible stalker ambushed when sword was claimed, guarding it from being taken. Party defeated stalker through combined spellcasting - Moonbeam revealed it, Cloud of Daggers damaged it consistently. Brewbarry ultimately claimed the sword.

### Lady Alagondar's Ghostly Appearance
Ghost of Lady Alagondar materialized and stared mournfully toward Ice Spire Mountains, indicating the dragon's location, before fading away.

### Sister Kaella's Warning and Neutralization
Sister Kaella arrived wounded with urgent warning about the Carver's transformation and march on Icespire Hold. Vukradin attacked and paralyzed her before warning could be completed. Party knocked her unconscious with non-lethal damage.

### Jax's Final Ambush
Jax led thugs and Rot-Tusk Ogre in immediate ambush after Sister Kaella was neutralized. Vukradin was netted but maintained concentration on Hold Person, paralyzing both Jax and the ogre. Party defeated all attackers through coordinated assault using Spirit Guardians, bear form, and focused attacks on paralyzed enemies.

## Revealed Information

The Carver has undergone a horrific transformation - no longer human but a hollow shell that speaks to his blade in a tongue that makes ears bleed. He is marching on Icespire Hold to bind the white dragon, intending to wear it like armor and burn Phandalin to test his power. Time is critical as this ritual must be stopped before completion.

The white dragon's location is confirmed to be in the Ice Spire Mountains, specifically at Icespire Hold, as indicated by Lady Alagondar's ghost.

Sister Kaella was the only person who knew the exact location of Icespire Hold, but she was knocked unconscious before revealing it, creating a significant obstacle for the party's mission.
