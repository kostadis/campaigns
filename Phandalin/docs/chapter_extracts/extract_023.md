## NPCs
**Shrimpy** - Captured Kraken Society member; delivered to Town Master Harbin Wester in Phandalin. Tried to convince Corbin the party was dead and he should be released.

**Town Master Harbin Wester** - Phandalin official; received custody of Shrimpy from the party.

**Ser Kaelen** - Order of the Gauntlet representative; expressed concern about Kraken Society and mentioned suspicions of powerful psionic creatures being involved. Revealed Candlekeep research had identified original owners of magical items found by party, promised rewards for returning items but warned process wouldn't be straightforward.

**Jenna Roscoe** - Lords' Alliance representative; dismissed Kraken Society as harmless social club for wealthy elites, though Valphine sensed she knew more than she was revealing.

**Adabra** - Became furiously enraged upon learning of party's intervention against Kraken cult. Called their actions a "temporal violation and planar distortion," claimed party should never have interfered as it triggers cascading catastrophic events. Stormed out of council meeting in righteous fury.

**Brin and Giles** - Church reconstruction workers; ecstatic about Valphine's new blindness/healing sacrament.

**Teega the Smith** - Kraken Society member; appeared disturbed and conflicted by Valphine's offer of experiencing pain and redemption.

**Don-Jon Raskin** - Verbose gold prospector/new overseer for Mountain's Toe Gold Mine, hired by Neverwinter consortium (includes Kraken Society member). Hired party as escort to mine, knows nothing about mining operations.

**Zeleen Varnaster** - Leader of Whiskered Gang (wererats); displaced from Shrine of Savras by sixteen orcs and several ogres. Proposed deal for party to clear shrine so wererats could return there. Refused to help reclaim her own home, claiming too valuable to risk. Found cheese-for-labor suggestion offensive but admitted interest in Butterskull Ranch cheese. Party sensed she was lying about intentions and would try to keep mine regardless of deals.

**Wererats (two in hybrid form)** - Confronted party at mine entrance, claimed ownership. Initially hostile toward Valphine for being drow. Invited party to meet leadership deeper in mine.

## Factions
**Cult of Talos** - Operating at Woodland Manse with shapeshifting orcs wielding lightning magic. Party reported their presence to Phandalin council.

**Order of the Gauntlet** - Spent considerable time and money researching magical items at Candlekeep to strengthen relationship with Vukradin.

**Lords' Alliance** - Representative dismissed Kraken Society as harmless social club.

**Kraken Society** - Has member(s) in Neverwinter consortium that hired Don-Jon. Teega the Smith revealed as member.

**Whiskered Gang** - Wererat group displaced from Shrine of Savras, currently occupying Mountain's Toe Gold Mine.

## Party
**Vukradin** - Advocated for tactical retreat over "glorious but pointless death," mindful of gold mine share. Called council meeting to report findings. Persuaded Don-Jon that wererats could work mine while living in part of it.

**Valphine** - Used divine powers to heal companions during retreat. Delivered powerful sermon that led congregation into religious ecstasy, unintentionally creating new sacrament where congregants blind themselves to be healed as proof of devotion. Placed severed Kraken Society cultist head on pike outside church as warning. Attempted to negotiate cheese-for-labor arrangement with wererats.

**Brewbarry** - Carried Vukradin to safety in giant form during retreat. Showed displeasure at Shrimpy's whining.

**Soma** - Carried Valphine during retreat. Expressed being "done with" Adabra after her outburst.

## Locations
**Woodland Manse** - Cult of Talos stronghold with shapeshifting orc cultists wielding lightning magic. Party retreated after being overwhelmed.

**Phandalin** - Party reported to council, delivered Shrimpy, held sermon at reconstructed church.

**Mountain's Toe Gold Mine** - Now operated by Whiskered Gang wererats under deal with party, marked by wooden plank with name.

**Shrine of Savras** - Occupied by sixteen orcs and several ogres who displaced the Whiskered Gang.
