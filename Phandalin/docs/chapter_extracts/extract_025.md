## NPCs
- **Out-of-phase dwarves**: Former inhabitants of Axelholm transformed by planar anomaly; have parchment-like skin with shifting ink, gold coins embedded in flesh, mouths sewn shut with golden wire or red tape; limbs multiply and move out of phase; mutter constantly about efficiency; can paralyze victims by "packaging" them like objects; last known location fighting the party in Axelholm's main hall

## Party
- **Soma**: Summoned rat familiar to scout; used staff to sense planar anomaly corruption; cast moonbeams and thunderwaves in combat; transformed into bear form; became paralyzed twice during combat (once healed by Valphine, second time left vulnerable at session end); caused ground to shake and knock enemies prone
- **Brewbarry**: Smashed through inner portcullis door; transformed into Big Brewbarry (enraged state); wielded halberd with devastating strikes; resisted paralysis attempt, heard voice saying "incorrect packaging"
- **Valphine**: Called upon Lathander's glory to cause creatures to flee; provided divine healing that restored Soma and broke paralysis
- **Vukradin**: Provided magical encouragement to allies; struck with rapier, causing one dwarf to blink out of existence as space folded

## Quests & Threads
- **PROGRESSED**: Opening and securing Axelholm for potential Phandalin evacuation - party successfully breached the fortress but discovered it corrupted by planar anomaly; combat ongoing with transformed inhabitants

## Locations
- **Axelholm**: Ancient dwarven fortress carved into mountain base miles south of Phandalin; features forty-foot-high main hall with stone pillars, ballistas at arrow slits; corrupted by planar anomaly causing spatial distortions, unnatural mathematically cold light, and constant ticking sounds; walls emit scratching of quills on parchment
