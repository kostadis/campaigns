## NPCs
- **Tibor**: Loggers chief who refused to transport Shrimpy to Neverwinter
- **Lyra**: Low-ranking Emerald Enclave member the party previously saved; party plans to credit her with the patrol rescue to boost her standing
- **Shrimpy**: Party's prisoner who desperately wants to be released; party decided to transport him to Phandalin for imprisonment
- **Teega the Smith**: Found by riverbank with glassy, unfocused eyes speaking in eerie monotone about metal bending, minds being cleansed, and waves seeking uniformity; drew spiral symbol in mud; eyes briefly cleared when Vukradin persuaded her before glazing over again; later pursued and subdued by Soma in bear form
- **Cult Fanatic**: Grim-looking man wielding black coral dagger who controlled the missing Emerald Enclave patrol; spoke of rising current flowing toward Neverwinter and offered party chance to submit; knocked unconscious by Brewbarry; could control minions even while unconscious; executed by Valphine after being handed over to Talosian orcs; affiliated with Kraken Society
- **Anchorite half-orc**: Cleric of Talos who identified the Cult Fanatic as an obstacle and warned the party to leave the forest
- **Missing Emerald Enclave patrol member**: One of Lyra's old crew who expressed relief that she had sent the party to rescue them

## Factions
- **Emerald Enclave**: Missing patrol was rescued by party; patrol had been forced to steal inscription about drowned gate beneath Yllyn; party plans to credit Lyra with rescue to boost her standing and gain ally
- **Kraken Society**: Cult Fanatic and controlled warriors wore spiral emblems identifying them with this faction; connected to drowned gate threat
- **Talosian orcs**: Large raiding party that ambushed party demanding the Cult Fanatic; identified him as their enemy and took his head as proof after execution

## Party
- **Vukradin**: Learned he built reputation as noble adventurer after saving town from dragon and advocating for fair wages; protests he just wants to open his own studio; persuaded Teega causing her eyes to briefly clear; raged in combat but struggled to hit Cult Fanatic
- **Brewbarry**: Consumed fermentable mushrooms entering altered state; mind controlled by Cult Fanatic compelling him to flee but broke free; delivered crushing non-lethal strike knocking Cult Fanatic unconscious; tasked with maintaining sedation during transport; insisted on returning to Falcon's Lodge for more alcohol after drinking all his ale
- **Soma**: Transformed into massive bear and charged cult leader; pursued and subdued possessed Teega in bear form
- **Valphine**: Channeled healing energy during combat; executed the Cult Fanatic after party handed him over to orcs

## Quests & Threads
- **RESOLVED**: Rescue missing Emerald Enclave patrol - party successfully rescued three mind-controlled patrol members by defeating their Cult Fanatic controller
- **PROGRESSED**: Kraken Society investigation - discovered cult leader was affiliated with Kraken Society and patrol was forced to steal inscription about drowned gate beneath Yllyn in Underdark
- **OPENED**: Drowned gate threat - rescued patrol revealed they were forced to steal inscription about drowned gate beneath Yllyn, representing significant Kraken Society threat

## Locations
- **Loggers Camp**: Where party gathered to discuss next moves and debated what to do with prisoner Shrimpy
- **Riverbank meeting point**: Eastern shore covered in unnatural dense fog; where party found possessed Teega and crossed into affected area
- **Glade**: Where party encountered Cult Fanatic and mind-controlled Emerald Enclave patrol; site of major combat
- **Phandalin**: Destination for transporting prisoners including Shrimpy
- **Falcon's Lodge**: Five miles from party's location; destination Brewbarry insists on visiting for more alcohol
