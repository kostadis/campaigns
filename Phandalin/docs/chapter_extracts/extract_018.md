## NPCs
- **Falcon the Hunter**: Lodge owner and host; confessed he is not a dragonslayer but a fraud who escaped Iymrith's wrath as the only survivor of his adventuring party; found residual gold scraps and a brass ring after others plundered Iymrith's hoard; used these as "seed capital" to buy land and build his lodge; maintains the dragonslayer lie because it's "good for business" and attracts paying clientele; accused the Cult of Talos of attempting to steal his ring artifact; gave Vukradin permission to document the ring; last known location at his lodge

## Factions
- **Cult of Talos**: Accused by Falcon of attempting to steal the brass ring artifact as an offering to their god

## Party
- **Vukradin**: Conducted solo covert reconnaissance mission; infiltrated Falcon's bedroom using Silence spell; broke into locked drawer; discovered wooden elven ears, labyrinth maze carving, and dragon head box containing brass ring; successfully deceived Falcon about his involvement in the break-in; convinced Falcon to confess the truth; secured permission to document the ring while maintaining his principled stance against taking stolen goods
- **Brewbarry**: Generated thunderous snoring that provided cover for Vukradin's infiltration
- **Soma**: Remained silent in her shell during the night; agreed that Falcon's fantasy makes guests feel safe and non-intervention was necessary
- **Valphine**: Was bemused during sleep; impressed with Falcon's lie, seeing it as "perfectly normal" and genius

## Locations
- **Main Lodge**: Falcon's private bedroom was infiltrated; contains locked drawer with significant artifacts

## Events
- Vukradin conducted nighttime infiltration of Falcon's bedroom using Silence spell
- Discovered artifacts including wooden elven ears, labyrinth maze carving, and dragon head box with brass ring
- Falcon confronted party about disturbed drawer at breakfast, blaming Cult of Talos
- Falcon confessed he was not a dragonslayer but a fraud who escaped Iymrith's attack
- Party gained permission to document the ring and gathered intelligence about its origins

## Revealed Information
- Falcon is not a dragonslayer but the sole survivor of his adventuring party's encounter with Iymrith
- The brass ring inscribed with Elvish word for "lightning" originates from blue dragon Iymrith's hoard
- Falcon found the ring and gold scraps after others had already plundered Iymrith's hoard
- The wooden labyrinth maze carving resembles the structure from "The Undercity Chorale," a forgotten Waterdeep opera about the city's deepest subterranean levels
- Falcon maintains his dragonslayer persona purely for business purposes to attract paying guests
- The miniature dragon head box is anatomically correct, as if expertly shrunk from an actual dragon's head
