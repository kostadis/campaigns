## NPCs
- **Boney**: Skeletal horse that reanimated from bones in southern niche. Originally named "Barney," served Lady Alagondar and was brought to dungeon to guard something for her. Killed by Will-o'-wisps. Believes Soma is Lady Alagondar reincarnated and accepts her as master. Pointed party north toward sword location. Missing mane and other features, being fully skeletal.

## Party
- **Soma**: Convinced skeletal horse she is Lady Alagondar reincarnated. Used Mould Earth to rescue party from tunnel collapse and clear blocked passage. Cast Speak with Animals to communicate with Boney. Used Moonbeam to destroy Will-o'-wisp, dealing 12 damage.
- **Vukradin**: Rushed ahead and triggered pressure plate causing tunnel collapse. Split from party by running west instead of north, fell into pit trap for 19 damage, lost concentration on Silence spell. Retrieved Necklace of Fireballs from sarcophagus and magical flute from dead bard's tomb. Cast Cloud of Daggers dealing only 4 damage after rolling four ones.
- **Brewbarry**: Fell into non-magical trap for 12 damage, attacked by Will-o'-wisp for 9 damage. Used Thaumaturgy to examine magical light. Deliberately triggered trap with halberd, entered rage, and destroyed Will-o'-wisp with two attacks.
- **Valphine**: Healed Brewbarry for 25 hit points. Successfully used Turn Undead to make Will-o'-wisp flee. Discovered the Dragon Slayer Sword resting atop dragon skull in northern chamber.

## Quests & Threads
- **RESOLVED**: Dragon Slayer Sword quest - party successfully located the legendary sword resting atop a dragon skull in the northern chamber, exactly where Boney indicated it would be "across the way" from Lady Alagondar's resting place.

## Locations
- **Dragonbarrow Secret Passageway**: Narrow two-foot-wide tunnel with hidden pressure plate trap that collapsed walls when triggered. Soma excavated entire blocked passage using Mould Earth.
- **Southern Niche**: Area within tunnel where skeletal horse Boney was found among bones and rotting saddle.
- **Sepulcher Chamber**: Contains multiple sarcophagi where party found Necklace of Fireballs and Flute of Illusions, encountered Will-o'-wisps.
- **Northern Chamber**: Final location containing dragon bones with Dragon Slayer Sword resting atop dragon skull.

## Events
- Vukradin triggered pressure plate, causing tunnel walls to collapse and bury party except Soma in debris
- Soma used Mould Earth repeatedly to free party members and clear blocked tunnel
- Skeletal horse bones reanimated when Soma approached, horse accepted her as Lady Alagondar reincarnated
- Brewbarry fell into trap and was attacked by invisible Will-o'-wisp for 21 total damage
- Valphine used Turn Undead to make Will-o'-wisp flee in terror
- Vukradin ran west instead of north, fell into pit trap and broke Silence spell concentration
- Multiple Will-o'-wisp combat encounters throughout chamber with party using various spells and abilities
- Valphine discovered Dragon Slayer Sword resting on dragon skull in northern chamber

## Revealed Information
- Lady Alagondar was buried in the northern chamber "across the way" from where the sword was hidden
- Will-o'-wisps killed the horse that was brought to guard Lady Alagondar's belongings
- The Dragon Slayer Sword was placed atop a dragon skull, suggesting connection between the sword and the dead dragon
- Boney was brought back specifically to serve Lady Alagondar on an important quest involving the sword
