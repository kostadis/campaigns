## NPCs
**Moesko (Orc Anchorite)**
- Identity: Orc spellcaster occupying the Tower of Storms
- Actions: Cast Lightning Bolt and Thunderwave in combat, possessed Miral's conch
- State changes: Died - killed by Soma's final strike
- Last known location: Upper level of Tower of Storms, seated in barnacle-encrusted chair
- Wore armor made from giant octopus hide, held opalescent conch artifact

**Miral the Self (Sea Elf)**
- Referenced as the undead owner of the opalescent conch artifact that Moesko possessed

**Three Harpies**
- Male harpy in grey rags: Used charming song, killed by Vukradin's Vicious Mockery
- Two female harpies (one in blue rags): Participated in combat, killed by party
- Actions: Attempted to charm party members with songs
- State changes: All killed in battle

## Party
**Vukradin**
- Cast Silence spell to counter harpy charm effects
- Used Bardic Inspiration to turn failure into success when charmed
- Cast Cloud of Daggers trapping Moesko for 9 damage
- Killed male harpy with Vicious Mockery, quipping "He is mocked to death. To death."
- Expressed intent to "repatriate" shipwreck salvage items
- Debated taking "blood money" from harpy nest

**Soma**
- Transformed into wolf spider to scale tower walls and attack harpies
- Later transformed into brown bear for combat with Moesko
- Delivered final killing blow to Moesko after reverting from bear form
- Criticized Vukradin's salvage ethics as "colonial paradise" or "mercantile" system

**Valphine**
- Was charmed by harpy song, freed by Vukradin's Silence
- Used crossbow effectively against harpies
- Recognized the opalescent conch as belonging to Miral the Sea Elf
- Found battle pain "invigorating"

**Brewbarry**
- Was charmed by harpy song initially
- Missed multiple handaxe attacks during harpy fight
- Successfully charged and hit Moesko, reducing him to single hit point

## Locations
**Tower of Storms**
- Rocky tower with multiple levels, dirty windows showing shipwrecks below
- Contains 15-foot-high empty room, stone altar with lightning bolt carvings and metal lightning rod
- Frescoes depicting stormy seas and "dark and terrible god"
- Upper level where Moesko was found in barnacle-encrusted chair
- Harpy nest on ledge containing potion of water breathing

## Events
- Three harpies attacked party at tower approach, charming Valphine and Brewbarry with songs
- Vukradin cast Silence spell, breaking charm effects and nullifying harpy songs
- Extended combat with harpies ended with all three creatures killed
- Party explored tower, finding religious imagery and harpy nest with water breathing potion
- Confronted Moesko in upper level, who possessed Miral's opalescent conch
- Moesko cast Lightning Bolt hitting most party members, then Thunderwave
- Soma in bear form and Vukradin's Cloud of Daggers dealt significant damage to Moesko
- Brewbarry's charge reduced Moesko to single hit point, Soma delivered killing blow

## Revealed Information
- The Tower of Storms contains religious imagery of a "dark and terrible god"
- Moesko possessed Miral the Sea Elf's opalescent conch artifact
- Shipwrecks visible below the tower are results of the tower's curse
- The tower serves as both harpy roosting ground and religious site with storm/lightning themes
