## NPCs
- **Dwarven King of Axeholm**: Three-headed monstrosity, twisted by interdimensional corruption; fought the party in the pillared hall; defeated and killed by the party; carried a signet ring bearing the Axeholm crest (hand clutched in dwarven gauntlet); last location was the throne room where he fell
- **Out-of-phase dwarves**: Multiple twisted creatures caught in interdimensional nightmare; systematically eliminated by the party throughout Axeholm; one fled up the fireplace after being turned by Valphine's divine energy

## Party
- **Valphine**: Used crossbow in combat, tended party's wounds with healing magic, led exploration through cramped tunnel, picked locks with thieves' tools, turned undead with divine energy, revived unconscious Soma, avoided all attacks from the King; acquired platinum amulet of Moradin and potion of superior healing from shrine
- **Soma**: Fought in bear form, was paralyzed but shook off effect, knocked unconscious by King and reverted to human form, revived by Valphine and rejoined combat
- **Brewbarry**: Delivered crushing blows in combat, flew into rage during King fight, enlarged form squeezed through doorway, absorbed King's attacks while dealing defensive strikes; examined hot spring water in kitchen
- **Vukradin**: Supported allies with inspiring words, attempted mental assaults on King using illusions of Moradin and giant bear (second attempt successful); examined mechanical construct with aesthetic offense at its unnatural design
- **Party collectively**: Leveled up after defeating the King, debated whether treasures should go to Townmaster Wester or Privy Council

## Locations
- **Axeholm mustering hall**: Site of initial combat against out-of-phase dwarves
- **Axeholm upper level**: Ransacked room with broken furniture, blackened fireplace with chimney leading to mountain face entrance
- **Dwarven shrine**: Eastern section collapsed, contained skeletal remains, steel coffer with silt, platinum amulet of Moradin and superior healing potion discovered
- **Locked empty room**: Completely cleaned out storage room, purpose of locking it remains mysterious
- **Grand pillared hall**: Contained limestone throne flanked by granite warrior statues, site of King battle, air filled with unnatural humming and dancing dust patterns
- **Grand dining room**: Massive stone table, iron chandeliers with melted candles, alcove with 60-foot deep trash chute
- **Kitchen**: Metal dishware and utensils, functioning stone sink with hot water from natural spring
- **Smithy**: Rusty anvils, blackened hearths, location where mechanical construct was discovered
