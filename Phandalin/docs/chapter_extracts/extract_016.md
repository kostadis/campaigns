## NPCs
- **Sister Kayla**: Scarred woman with cold grey eyes, armed with rapier and dagger. Killed Locutus after confirming he killed Borg. Wants to overthrow the Carver by having Valphine kill his lieutenants first to draw him out of hiding. Claims the Carver communicates telepathically. Offered Valphine payment and partnership, believing her to be an evil drow cleric of Lolth. Disappeared after the meeting.
- **Jenna**: Lord's Alliance agent from Neverwinter, late thirties with auburn hair and calculating green eyes. Attempted to recruit Vukradin as an asset, offering fame and noble connections in exchange for compromising his moral stance on fair-trade treasure. Showed contempt for Brewbarry's Uthgartian heritage. Armed with rapier, daggers, and hand crossbow. Failed to convince Vukradin to abandon his principles.
- **Harbin**: Townmaster, accused Vukradin but backed down under pressure. Agreed to form a privy council including Vukradin after the dragon incident, citing recent events demonstrated need for more community engagement. Defended his authority as appointed by Lord Nevermember.
- **Ser Kaelen**: Order of the Gauntlet member who defended Vukradin's innocence and pushed for immediate trial. Advocated for town meeting but warned against full revolution, explaining Lord Nevermember would send troops. Suggested compromise of privy council with puppet Townmaster.

## Factions
- **Lord's Alliance**: Actively recruiting Vukradin through agent Jenna, seeking to leverage his influence. Offered connections to Waterdeep and Neverwinter nobility in exchange for moral compromise on treasure sources.
- **Order of the Gauntlet**: Ser Kaelen cleared Vukradin's name and criticized Harbin's leadership. Jenna attempted to drive wedge between Vukradin and the Order by revealing House Margaster's "blood money" funding.
- **The Carver's organization**: Sister Kayla plotting internal takeover, eliminating lieutenants to force leader out of hiding. Borg's failure to kill Big Al disrupted plan to frame party for merchant theft.

## Party
- **Vukradin**: Cleared of accusations, appointed to new privy council. Drove white dragon away with phantasmal force illusion. Rejected Jenna's recruitment offer, refusing to compromise moral stance on fair-trade treasure despite promises of fame and noble connections. Maintained position of wanting to work with all non-evil groups.
- **Valphine**: Disguised as drow to meet Sister Kayla, who assumed she was evil Lolth cleric. Received payment and partnership offer to kill Carver's lieutenants. Witnessed Locutus's execution after confirming he killed Borg.
- **Brewbarry**: Protected Vukradin from dragon, disarmed Jenna before introduction. Recognized Jenna as Lord's Alliance through "stench" and insignia. Confronted her prejudice against Uthgartians, maintained suspicious stance throughout negotiation.

## Quests & Threads
- **OPENED**: Sister Kayla's plot - eliminate Carver's remaining lieutenants to force him out of telepathic hiding, then kill the Carver. Payment offered to Valphine as down payment.
- **PROGRESSED**: Phandalin governance - Vukradin appointed to new privy council (himself, Harbin, Tolbin, Elmer, Halia, Linene, Adabra) as compromise between revolution and status quo.
- **PROGRESSED**: Lord's Alliance recruitment - Jenna's attempt to turn Vukradin failed due to his moral stance, but she expressed continued interest despite setback.

## Locations
- **Phandalin town green**: Site of interrupted town meeting, white dragon attack, and Vukradin's successful dragon deterrent. Harbin announced new privy council formation here.
- **Abandoned building at town's edge**: Sister Kayla's meeting location where Locutus was executed and partnership proposed to Valphine.
- **Tavern**: Jenna's recruitment attempt of Vukradin took place here, with Brewbarry providing security and crowd calling for Valphine's performance.

## Events
- White dragon attacked town green during Vukradin's political rally. Vukradin used phantasmal force to create illusion of captured peasant, causing dragon to fly away believing it had prey.
- Sister Kayla executed Locutus with rapier and dagger after confirming Borg's death, then offered Valphine partnership in overthrowing the Carver.
- Harbin announced formation of seven-member privy council including Vukradin as compromise between democratic revolution and maintaining Lord Nevermember's authority.
- Jenna's recruitment failed when Vukradin refused to compromise on fair-trade treasure principles despite offers of fame and noble connections.

## Revealed Information
- The Carver communicates telepathically with subordinates and remains in hiding, making direct assassination difficult.
- House Margaster provides funding to Order of the Gauntlet, which Lord's Alliance considers "blood money."
- Lord's Alliance actively monitors and attempts to recruit influential individuals like Vukradin.
- Sister Kayla's plot against Big Al failed when Borg didn't eliminate witnesses, ruining frame-up attempt against the party.
