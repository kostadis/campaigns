## NPCs
**Xanth (Xanthopoulos)** - Centaur living in the forest near Icespire Mountains
- Identity: Centaur, former friend of Meryl (previous owner of Soma's staff)
- Actions: Healed by Soma, agreed to carry party members partway to Icespire Hold
- Last known location: Forest near Icespire Mountains
- Motivations: Helping Soma due to connection with Meryl and the staff; frustrated with Emerald Enclave infighting

**Sister Kaella** - Former Carver lieutenant, captured spy
- Identity: Drow cleric of Lathander, former Carver operative
- Actions: Interrogated by party, revealed Carver's plans and location, requested transfer to Axholm prison
- State changes: Captured, cooperating with party under arrangement with Valphine
- Last known location: Left in Corbin's custody at party's camp
- Motivations: Self-preservation after Carver accused her of intelligence failures; had secret arrangement with Valphine

**Corbin** - Former colleague of Kaella
- Actions: Assigned to guard Kaella, asked permission to beat her "until she has one hit point"
- Revealed information: Has history with Kaella, learning "sacraments" from Valphine

**The Carver (Jax)** - Corrupted former leader
- State changes: Completely possessed/soulless, walking without rest/breath/soul toward Icespire Hold
- Last known location: En route to Icespire Hold to possess the white dragon
- Revealed information: Carries obsidian sword (level-draining), leaves psychic residue that warps reality

**Stone Giants** - Multiple giants in mountain pass
- Actions: Observed Carver pass through, gave party two potions (greater healing, hill giant strength)
- Motivations: Following Hekaton's philosophy of only dealing with "giant things," refusing involvement in "little one" problems

**Mother Drake** - White dragon in mountain pass
- State changes: Lost her young to the Carver's blade
- Actions: Circled grief-stricken over her dead child, allowed party to pass after Vukradin and Brewbarry played music
- Last known location: Mountain pass lair with her dead young
