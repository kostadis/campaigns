## NPCs
- **Adabra**: Druid/naturalist rescued by party. Advocates for Vukradin to Harbin, describing him as an idealist who deserves fair wages. Believes in natural balance and questions what constitutes a "monster." Ignores Valphine entirely, possibly due to her being drow. Located at Phandalin.
- **Harbin Wester (Townmaster)**: Short on coins due to bandit attacks. Offers free room and board at Stonehill Inn when pressured by Adabra. Provides information about the lighthouse rumors but admits he's not an adventurer and doesn't know how to remove curses.
- **Toblen "Spiderman" Brandybrook**: Stonehill Inn keeper. Childhood nickname from being covered in baby spiders without fear. Serves drinks to patrons.
- **Daran "Silverleaf" Edermath**: Half-elf former adventurer, weathered appearance with silver-streaked hair and green eyes. Tells story about cursed lighthouse on the coast that lures ships to crash. Apologizes when Vukradin objects to treasure-hunting from shipwrecks.
- **Ser Kaelen Thorn**: Order of the Gauntlet recruiter, early thirties with chain mail and gauntlet-grasping-sword emblem. Claims his order helped in the War of the Dragon and that Milo Goodbarrel led the strike force. Recruits Vukradin as associate member, offers to help Valphine join but dismisses Soma for being a naturalist. Acknowledges brutal war against Brewbarry's tribe. Located at Barthen's Provisions, preparing to leave town.

## Factions
- **Order of the Gauntlet**: Recruited Vukradin as associate member. Claims to fund operations through conflict-free donations. Fought against barbarian tribes in brutal war. Seeks to spread their message through art and storytelling. Offers backing to reach tens of thousands with their cause.

## Party
- **Vukradin**: Joined Order of the Gauntlet as associate member, received recruitment pin. Collaborated musically with Valphine, initially resistant but then appreciative. Obsessed with "conflict-free gold" and opposes treasure-hunting from the dead. Wants to investigate/destroy the cursed lighthouse.
- **Valphine**: Developed "Choralum Lux Maleficarum" musical style fusing Lathander and Lolth influences. Successfully collaborated with Vukradin in tavern performance. Deliberately unsettling in conversation ("I find the sun deliciously painful," "Pleasure and Pain").
- **Soma**: Contemplates her children traveling the world. Concerned about Vukradin's impulsiveness. Tries to counsel patience and proper understanding of lighthouse function.
- **Brewbarry**: Reveals Order of the Gauntlet fought against his tribe in brutal war, killed many of his people. Drinks with Ser Kaelen despite past conflicts.

## Quests & Threads
- **OPENED**: Cursed lighthouse investigation - Ser Kaelen suggests three possibilities: requires cleric intervention, monster haunting, or false rumors. Believes it's likely a monster based on evidence.

## Locations
- **Phandalin**: Townmaster's office and Stonehill Inn. Under siege by bandits, causing financial strain.
- **Cursed Lighthouse**: Located west of Phandalin on the coast. Reportedly lures ships to crash on rocks, creating shipwrecks.
- **Barthen's Provisions**: Where Ser Kaelen was preparing to leave town.
- **Neverwinter Wood**: Mentioned as having strange occurrences, reminds Soma of home.
