## NPCs
- **The Shimmering Stag**: Territorial fey-like creature in Whispering Wood whose antlers constantly change shape and can teleport; explained that "other nice people" visited previously claiming they would "fix" the planar distortion but haven't returned; directed party toward forest center as source of changes

## Party
- Soma cast Speak with Animals to communicate with the stag, later transformed into bear form to subdue panicking Brewbarry and attack bramble thicket
- Valphine channeled Sacred Flame against bramble thicket but attack was ineffective; avoided looking directly into the planar pool, seemingly afraid of seeing herself happy
- Brewbarry skinned dire wolves for pelts, entered berserker rage during bramble fight, asked the pool "What side of the bread should I butter?" and saw himself buttering both sides simultaneously, became terrified and ran screaming "both sides" before being subdued
- Vukradin jumped into the planar pool seeking answers, experienced overwhelming visions and heard a distorted creature say "He saw beyond, but only what he was meant to see. The numbers. Always the numbers," fell unconscious and was revived by slap

## Locations
- **Whispering Wood**: Forest affected by planar distortion with no visible blight but unnatural magical effects
- **Bramble Thicket**: Dense growth with leaves pulsing unnatural colors, strikes anyone within 10 feet with thorny tendrils dealing piercing and necrotic damage, regenerates after damage
- **Planar Pool**: Located in forest clearing, shows distorted reflections and visions when viewed or entered, described as "planar scar" and "window into fractured realities"

## Events
- Party encountered teleporting stag whose antlers constantly change shape due to planar distortion
- Stag revealed disturbances began "in the center of the forest" and that other people had promised to fix the problem
- Party fought through bramble thicket with thorny tendrils that attacked and regenerated
- Encountered dire wolves with unnatural glowing eyes and magical fear-inducing howls, defeated them and took pelts
- Vukradin entered planar pool and experienced overwhelming visions before falling unconscious
- Brewbarry looked into pool, saw disturbing vision of himself, became terrified and ran before being subdued

## Arc Score Events
- **Planar Distortion**: Party directly encountered multiple manifestations including teleporting stag, color-shifting brambles, magically-enhanced wolves, and planar pool showing fractured realities

## Revealed Information
- Planar distortion originated from the center of Whispering Wood
- Other individuals previously attempted to fix the distortion but failed to return or complete the task
- The pool provides visions but at severe mental cost - Vukradin heard cryptic reference to "numbers" and "blood gold"
- Brewbarry possesses hidden knowledge about Uthgardt legends regarding planar pools as "shadows to other places"
