## NPCs
**Lyra** - Emerald Enclave leader, interventionist faction. Trapped by blighted vines, freed during combat. Led failed ritual attempt to seal planar rift. Explained the philosophical split between naturalists and interventionists within the Emerald Enclave, tracing it back to debates during the War of the Dragons. Revealed that Delaan Winterhound was convinced by hero Malfire to join the war against Tiamat. Last known location: fleeing the corrupted Whispering Grove after ritual failure.

**Delaan Winterhound** - Emerald Enclave leader (referenced). Described as "the wisest of us" who was convinced by Malfire to join the War of the Dragons against Tiamat.

**Malfire** - Hero of the War of the Dragons (referenced). Convinced Delaan Winterhound and the Emerald Enclave to intervene against Tiamat.

**Corrupted Treant** - Former Grove Guardian of Whispering Grove. Transformed by planar infection, feeding on planar pools instead of sunlight. Spoke with overlapping whispers, claimed ownership of grove, declared happiness with its corrupted power. Killed in combat by party.

**Inquisitive Creature** - Phased puppet-like being that emerged from planar rift after ritual failure. Collected ingredients from Treant's corpse under apparent external control, then disappeared back into the planar well.

**Unnamed Emerald Enclave Warriors** (2) - Trapped with Lyra, freed during combat. One nearly died but was healed by Vukradin.

## Factions
**Emerald Enclave** - Split into two philosophical factions over planar intervention policy. Naturalists believe planar intrusions are part of the natural multiverse and should not be interfered with. Interventionists view planar corruption as alien blight requiring active resistance. Split originated from debates during War of the Dragons over whether to oppose Tiamat. Naturalists blame Tiamat's defeat for inspiring the Giant gods to shatter the Ordning and cause the War of the Giants.

## Party
**Soma** - Fought in bear form initially, later shifted to human form. Used her mentor's staff to detect that the Treant, pools, and bushes were all unnatural. Experienced philosophical crisis questioning whether she aligns with naturalists or interventionists. Participated in ritual attempts (succeeded twice, failed twice). Decided to seek answers from Adabra about the apparent contradiction between naturalist philosophy and obvious corruption.

**Valphine** - Cast Guiding Bolt and Fairy Fire during combat. Used insight to read Lyra's deception alongside Vukradin. Made comment that "sunlight is not natural" that angered others.

**Vukradin** - Attempted Command spell on vines (failed). Cast Starry Whip at Treant. Performed healing on dying Enclave warrior. Participated in ritual (succeeded once, helped Soma succeed once). Questioned Lyra's planning and expressed frustration with the situation.

**Brewbarry** - Grew to large size for extended reach during combat. Resisted the Treant's madness aura. Successfully damaged both vines and Treant. Participated in ritual (succeeded once). Identified the inquisitive creature as "a puppet that's being controlled."
