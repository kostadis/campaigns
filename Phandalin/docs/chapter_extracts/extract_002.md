## NPCs
- **Dazlyn Grayshard**: Shield dwarf prospector, business partner with Norbus. Operating archaeological dig at ancient dwarven ruins. Offered party sending stones in exchange for clearing temple. Claimed they would return finds to rightful owners. Cooked meal for party after orc elimination.
- **Norbus Ironrune**: Shield dwarf prospector, business partner with Dazlyn. Working the dig site. Confirmed they would return artifacts to rightful owners when questioned by Vukardin.
- **Toblen Stonehill**: Proprietor of Stonehill Inn, native of Triboar. Former prospector turned innkeeper. Pays Vukardin with meals and drinks for musical performances. Warned party about white dragon rumors.
- **Townmaster Harbin Wester**: Tremulous townmaster who hides in his hall, fears dragons. Posts quest notices. Paid party 50 gp for dwarf warning quest. Became upset when Vukardin criticized adventuring as glorified theft.
- **Linene Graywind**: Master of Lionshield Coster outpost. Confirmed supply chain disruptions affecting their interdimensional network. Mentioned strange magic in Neverwinter Wood confounding navigators.

## Party
- **Vukardin**: Aasimar bard troubled by the ethics of adventuring and "treasure claiming." Acquired sending stones from dwarves. Nearly killed twice in combat, once by orcs and once by ogre. Played music for inn patrons. Received 50 gp reward from townmaster.
- **Valphine Sortorra**: Drow cleric of Lathander, former follower of Lolth who fled the fall of Menzoberranzan. Healed party members multiple times. Knocked unconscious by friendly fire from Soma's ice knife. Sold orc equipment at Lionshield Coster.
- **Brewbarry**: Uthgardt barbarian who follows Vukardin for his music. Collected orc axes after combat. Effective with halberd in battle, entering rage when allies fall.
- **Soma**: Tortle druid of Mielikki, serving as guide to the region. Provided map of Phandalin. Used poison spray and thorn whip in combat. Led party toward Gnomengarde.

## Locations
- **Phandalin**: Mining town of 40-50 log buildings amid stone ruins. No walls or garrison. Party based operations here, visiting Stonehill Inn, Townmaster's Hall, and Lionshield Coster.
- **Ancient Dwarven Ruins**: Canyon settlement destroyed by avalanche, now archaeological dig site. Contains temple of Abbathor with secret passages. Party cleared orcs from the area.
- **Gnomengarde**: Rock gnome settlement in Sword Mountains that party is traveling toward through narrow mountain valley.
