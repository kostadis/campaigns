## NPCs

**Cryovain (White Dragon)**
- Ancient white dragon, enslaved by the Carver through obsidian sword ritual
- Fought the party while mind-controlled, breathing ice and attempting aerial attacks
- Killed by Valphine's sacred flame after being weakened by the group
- Was a victim of the Carver's domination, fighting the mental control
- Located at Icespire Hold's rooftop battlements within a summoning circle

**The Carver**
- Alien entity/intelligence using obsidian sword as mental amplifier
- Was conducting domination ritual on Cryovain through geometric summoning circle
- Essence dissipated when Cryovain died, completely destroyed
- Needed three more rounds to complete the ritual when party interrupted

**Unnamed Veteran**
- Paralyzed soldier under the Carver's influence
- Held motionless by Vukradin's spell, described as terrified but aware
- Killed by Brewbarry despite Soma's protests about him being a victim
- Last known location: Icespire Hold rooftop

**Sylvine Wintermoon**
- Con artist claiming ownership of dragon's treasure hoard
- Performed elaborate deception about praying for Vukradin's help
- Switched to violence when rejected: "Second plan! We kill them all!"
- Led group of bandits/veterans in attack on party

**Runa**
- Sylvine's sister and accomplice in the treasure con
- Provided backup performance with "Blessed be!" responses
- Participated in subsequent bandit attack

**Ser Kaelen (Order of the Gauntlet)**
- Sent celebratory message praising "conflict-free triumph"
- Invited party to Phandalin for celebration, not Neverwinter as planned
- Mentioned "issues with the Emerald Enclave"
- Deliberately used Vukradin's vocabulary ("conflict-free triumph")

## Party

- Used giant's growth to engage dragon at aerial height

- Declared himself thirsty after victory, looking forward to celebration

**Valphine**
- Missed several attacks on Cryovain during aerial combat

**Soma**
- Provided consistent moonbeam damage throughout dragon fight

**Vukradin**
- Provided bardic inspiration during dragon fight

- Expressed frustration about principles preventing him from claiming treasure for music studio
