## NPCs
- **Adabra**: Former trusted ally, now revealed as opponent of "interventionists." Became cold and patronizing when confronted. Located at her shop next to Garaele's house. Believes the party was deceived by interventionists and that planar diversity should be preserved. Gave Vukradin 5 gold pieces for not "intervening." Still believes the treant is alive in "stable condition."
- **Lyra**: Interventionist leader who failed her ritual. Furious at Adabra, calling her "insane" and representing "evil" and "fanaticism." Left after declaring the party "fools" for not seeing Adabra's danger. Her mission was personal advancement rather than following Emerald Enclave protocol.
- **Thomas & Marian**: Emerald Enclave agents loyal to Lyra despite the failed mission. Revealed Lyra acted independently for promotion rather than following proper channels. Declined invitation to join the party due to oath obligations.
- **Linene Graywind**: 35-year-old manager at Lionshield Coster. Showed intense interest in the planar pelts, offering twice Barthen's price. Mentioned her bosses' interest in "this particular planar effect" and that their interplanar shipping device stopped working.
- **Ser Kaelen**: Approached Vukradin about music for Order of the Gauntlet. Evasive about helping locate rightful treasure owners.

## Factions
- **Emerald Enclave**: Internal schism revealed between "interventionists" (who want to cleanse planar effects) and those like Adabra who oppose intervention. Organization paralyzed by debate over whether to act on planar disturbances. Lyra's unauthorized action damaged faction standing.
- **Lionshield Coster**: Has bosses interested in planar effects and previously had interplanar shipping capabilities that stopped working.

## Party
- **Soma**: Felt betrayed and used by Adabra. Attempted to lie about stopping the ritual but failed. Gained understanding that not all Emerald Enclave members follow nature-centered philosophy.
- **Vukradin**: Adopted policy of wanting to be friends with everyone (except Zhentarim). Developed philosophy that popular music reaches hearts better than superior music. Successfully defused tensions between Adabra and Lyra through misdirection. Sold planar pelts for significant profit but maintained "conflict-free money" standards.
- **Valphine**: Revealed extensive backstory - fled Menzoberranzan due to demon incursion, practices pain-worship of Lathander, considers self-harm religious ritual. Other party members learned of her unusual religious practices.
