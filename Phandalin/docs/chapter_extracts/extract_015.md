## NPCs
- **Skippy**: Carver's disposable crew member, captured and interrogated. Revealed Carver's spy network and trap at Butterskull Ranch. Last seen guarded by Corbin at remote camp.
- **Sister Kayla**: Revealed as Carver's spymaster running spy network in Phandalin (revealed by Skippy).
- **Borg the Hammer**: Half-orc enforcer leading ambush at Butterskull Ranch. Wears chainmail and carries shield. Executed fleeing bandit, burned down ranch in rage when trap failed, split forces with three Rot-Tusk Ogres.
- **Alphonse "Big Al" Kalazorn**: Retired veteran and former Sheriff of Triboar. Was held captive as bait in ranch cellar. Rescued by party. Offered mithril chainmail in exchange for finding his cow Petunia.

## Factions
- **The Carver's Organization**: Operations revealed - uses predictable quest board system to lure adventurers into traps. Has spy network in Phandalin run by Sister Kayla. Lost four disposable crew members in failed ambush.
- **Rot-Tusk Ogre Tribe**: Three ogres working with Borg, seeking revenge for previously killed tribe members. Split from Borg after ranch destruction, sent in opposite direction.

## Party
- **Soma**: Transformed into brown bear, killed bandit instantly with claws. Used Speak with Animals to locate Petunia the cow through questioning pigs.
- **Brewbarry**: Used enlargement magic with primal fury, dispatched bandit instantly.
- **Vukradin**: Led interrogation of captured bandit, refused to loot corpses on moral grounds, negotiated mithril chainmail trade with Big Al, chose to follow Borg rather than save burning ranch.
- **Valphine**: Observed destruction with fascination, advocated following Borg for tactical intelligence.
- **Corbin**: Volunteered to guard captured bandit Skippy, wanted to take prisoner's armor but was denied by Vukradin.

## Quests & Threads
- **RESOLVED**: Rescue Big Al Kalazorn and recover his cow - Big Al rescued from ranch cellar, cow Petunia found and secured. Reward: mithril chainmail offered by Big Al.
- **PROGRESSED**: Tracking the Carver - Learned Carver uses predictable quest system for traps, has spy network run by Sister Kayla, employs Borg the Hammer as enforcer.

## Locations
- **Butterskull Ranch**: Ambush site with dead orcs and humans scattered around. Farmhouse cellar used to hold Big Al captive. Completely burned down by Borg including cornfields and gardens after failed trap.
- **Conyberry**: Site where party encountered and fought four of Carver's bandits.

## Events
- Party encountered four Carver bandits at Conyberry, killed three, captured one (Skippy).
- Interrogated Skippy, learned of Carver's spy network and planned ambush at Butterskull Ranch.
- Stealthily approached ranch from north, found dead orcs and humans.
- Vukradin rescued Big Al from ranch cellar.
- Used Speak with Animals to locate and recover Petunia the cow.
- Observed from hill as Borg executed fleeing bandit, burned down entire ranch in rage.
- Borg split forces with three Rot-Tusk Ogres, party chose to follow Borg.

- The Carver exploits the predictable quest board system in Phandalin to lure adventurers into traps.
- Sister Kayla serves as the Carver's spymaster, running intelligence network in Phandalin.

- The Rot-Tusk Ogre tribe seeks revenge for previously killed members.
