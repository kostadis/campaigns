## NPCs
- **Adabra**: Midwife and member of Emerald Enclave; disappointed party hasn't investigated Whispering Woods; stopped talking to Vukradin after he joined Order of the Gauntlet; believes interventionist factions cause imbalance through constant meddling; views the multiverse as one balanced thing without absolute good/evil; last known location in Phandalin town square
- **Linene Graywind**: Proprietor of Lionshield Coster; explained that due to events from War of the Giants, they can no longer provide the same level of services; getting scrolls now takes time with no guarantee of results
- **Harbin Winthill**: Townmaster of Phandalin; relieved about lighthouse cleansing; uninterested in immediate repairs; warned party not to visit Lord Neverwinter while town mission remains incomplete; last known location at his house
- **Ser Kaelen Thorne**: Order of the Gauntlet knight; delighted by party's victory at lighthouse; shocked to learn it was Talos worship; concerned about "hidden force" behind enhanced Talos shaman; explained Order's alliance with Emerald Enclave during War of Dragons; seeking help finding bandit called "the Carver"; dismissive of Whispering Woods threat
- **Toblen Stonehill**: Innkeeper at Stonehill Tavern; organized betting pool on party's lighthouse mission; won bet on Vukradin's music protecting the group; gave Vukradin 10 gold as winnings; delivered letter from Neverwinter
- **"A Friend to True Justice"**: Anonymous letter writer warning Vukradin about Order of the Gauntlet's funding sources; claims connections to House Margaster and questionable treasure acquisition methods

## Factions
- **Order of the Gauntlet**: Allied with Emerald Enclave since War of Dragons; seeks to strive for good against evil forces; accused of accepting funding from House Margaster and questionable sources; operates on principle that heroism is necessary to save the world
- **Emerald Enclave**: Believes all things are beautiful and heroism defies natural order; some members post-Tiamat intervention believe demons are part of natural world; advocates for balance over intervention
- **House Margaster**: Mentioned as "generous donors" to Order of the Gauntlet; associated with "unsavory businesses" in Waterdeep and rumored cult connections

## Party
- **Vukradin**: Lost Order of the Gauntlet pin; conflicted about faction loyalties after Adabra's criticism; obsessed with building his studio and earning money; performed music and earned silver; received anonymous warning letter about Order's funding; wants to return stolen goods to rightful owners
- **Valphine**: Revealed Vukradin's public proclamations about visiting Tower of Storms were why they were ambushed; suspicious of Ser Kaelen; performed music with Vukradin earning additional silver; skeptical about the delivered letter's origins
- **Soma**: Guided party to Phandalin; annoyed by Vukradin's focus on money over forest preservation; called Vukradin "pebble-counter" and "shell-sprout"; her staff from Merill became magically active as they approached Whispering Woods
- **Brewbarry**: Joined musical performance using halberd for percussion; reminded group that townspeople loved Vukradin's music

## Quests & Threads
- **OPENED**: Whispering Woods investigation - Adabra requested cleansing of magically blighted forest where trees have wrong colors; claims caused by interventionist meddling; Ser Kaelen dismissive but townspeople would be grateful
- **OPENED**: Find "the Carver" - Ser Kaelen seeks help locating elusive bandit who hides from both spells and his own henchmen; operates differently from typical bragging bandits
- **PROGRESSED**: Lighthouse aftermath - Townmaster will send note to Lord Neverwinter about cleansing; party warned not to visit while town mission incomplete; light needs repair but no immediate action planned

## Locations
- **Phandalin**: Town square where party met with Adabra; Stonehill Tavern where they performed music and held discussions; Townmaster's house; Lionshield Coster with reduced services
- **Whispering Woods/Grove**: Forest location one hour's walk from Phandalin; experiencing magical blight with trees showing wrong colors; strange stories circulating for months; described as "pleasant enough place" by Ser Kaelen

## Events
- Party arrived in Phandalin and spoke with Adabra about Whispering Woods in town square
- Visited Lionshield Coster and learned of reduced services post-War of Giants
- Met with Townmaster Harbin about lighthouse aftermath and received warning about visiting Lord Neverwinter
- Confronted Ser Kaelen about suspected betrayal; discovered entire tavern knew their plans through betting pool
- Performed music at Stonehill Tavern earning silver coins through combination of Vukradin and Valphine's talents with Brewbarry's percussion

- Soma's staff from Merill became magically active as party prepared to leave for Whispering Woods
