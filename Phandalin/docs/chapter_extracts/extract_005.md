## NPCs
- **Adabra**: Elderly human alchemist/healer living at windmill on Umbrage Hill. Emerald Enclave naturalist faction member. Initially refused to leave her windmill to return to Phandalin, insisting villagers should come to her as part of "natural order." Changed her mind when she learned Vukradin was a fellow naturalist who opposed killing monsters for treasure. Preparing healing potions and medicine for Townmaster Harbin. Has deed to the land and tends dwarven cairns. Last location: windmill on Umbrage Hill, preparing to travel to Phandalin.
- **Corbin**: Former farmer turned reluctant bandit. Lost family and farm when Uthgardt Black Lion tribe destroyed his village Greenmeadow during giant chaos. Wears scavenged armor from dead man. Assigned by party to help Adabra with camp duties. Terrified of spiders, relieved when offered proper armor. Currently traveling with party as camp helper.
- **Manticore**: Killed by party at windmill. Could speak, demanded human meat, offered to spare others in exchange for Corbin. Preferred human flesh over other humanoids.

## Factions
- **Emerald Enclave**: Split between naturalist and interventionist factions. Naturalists (like Adabra and Soma) believe meta-verse/divine interventions are part of natural order and oppose killing monsters for treasure. Interventionists support more aggressive action.

## Party
- **Vukradin**: Demanded Corbin remove stolen armor, then relented and promised proper "fair trade" armor. Refused to leave lute behind despite Corbin's concern. Attempted multiple charm spells on manticore (failed). Only accepts payment from customers who rate his music 10/10 - Brewbarry was his only paying customer. Desperately needs money for a studio. Cut off manticore's head as proof.
- **Soma**: Transformed from spider back to tortle form. Attempted diplomacy with manticore, conjured familiar as distraction. Hurt by Vukradin's loss of trust after giving bad tactical advice. Bonded with Adabra over naturalist philosophy, learned about Emerald Enclave schism. Revealed gaps in druidic training under master Meril.
- **Valphine**: Used hand crossbow with bolts, missed several shots. Offered to teach Adabra about Lathander. Observed party dynamics and analyzed motivations.
- **Brewbarry**: Threw hand axes, used halberd, dealt killing blow to manticore. Loves Vukradin's music. Uncomfortable when called "barbarian" - Adabra insisted he's a "nomad" and part of natural world.

## Quests & Threads
