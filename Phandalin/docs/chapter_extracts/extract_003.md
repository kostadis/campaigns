## Locations
- **Gnomengarde**: Underground gnome settlement reached via waterfall. Contains guard posts, inventor workshops, throne room, sleeping chambers, and cellar. Multiple security traps including rotating blade turnstiles and crossbow platforms.
- **Waterfall Pool**: Natural concavity with shallow pool containing islands of red, green, and purple mushrooms. Multiple cave entrances 20-30 feet above pool level.
- **Cellar**: Room with twelve 40-gallon barrels in alcoves. Location where mimic was hiding, disguised as a barrel.
