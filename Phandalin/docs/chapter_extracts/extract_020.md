## NPCs
**Shrimpy** (Orc Raider)
- Captured orc belonging to independent raiding party displaced from Ice Spire of Sword Mountains by white dragon
- Revealed his group's exile led to chaos in valley below, with chief going mad and factions forming
- Bound to party's cart as prisoner/forced laborer
- Last known location: traveling with party toward Logger's Camp

**Tibor Wester** (Logger's Camp Leader)
- Missing from Logger's Camp despite party's expected arrival
- Camp completely deserted with no explanation

**Unnamed Orc Scout**
- Incompetent lookout who panicked when discovered by Valphine (bear form)
- Escaped into woods after being bitten

## Factions
**Independent Orc Raiding Party**
- Displaced from Ice Spire of Sword Mountains by white dragon
- Split into factions after exile: some joined Cult of Talos after shaman's death, others became brigands, Shrimpy's group remained independent
- Chief went mad following displacement

**Cult of Talos**
- Recruited some displaced orcs after their shaman was killed

## Party
- Brewbarry caught javelin mid-air, snapped it, intimidated prisoner "Shrimpy"
- Valphine transformed into bear, climbed tree to attack scout, later used crossbow
- Soma realized ambush setup, leaped from cart in combat, called down radiant moonlight beam
- Vukradin used enchantments to turn orcs against each other, created illusions causing friendly fire
- Bound captured orc to their cart as forced labor
- Arrived at deserted Logger's Camp, unable to complete delivery due to missing inhabitants

- Party spotted orc scout attempting ambush setup in forest
- Chaotic battle erupted with orcs arguing among themselves about scout's incompetence
- Vukradin's magic caused orcs to attack each other while under confusion effects

## Locations
**Logger's Camp (South Shore)**
- Found completely deserted despite early morning arrival
- Contains tents on sandy beach, cabin with stacked logs, old stone foundations
- Expected to find Tibor Wester for delivery completion but no inhabitants present

- White dragon displaced orc tribes from Ice Spire of Sword Mountains, causing chaos in valley
- Displaced orc chief went mad, leading to factional splits among survivors
- Some orcs joined Cult of Talos after their shaman was killed
- Logger's Camp mysteriously abandoned, preventing party from completing their delivery
