## NPCs
**Elara** - Middle-aged human merchant traveling to Phandalin with groom Jarek. Attacked by unusually disciplined orcs with strange markings (swirl or eye). Memory gaps about the attack details. Offered reward to party for assistance, specifically to Valphine for Lathander. Last seen safely returned to Phandalin.

**Jarek** - Elara's groom. Reported hearing a "hum" in his head before the orc attack. Argued they should have traveled with protection.

**Ser Kaelen Thorne** - Order of the Gauntlet member still in Phandalin. Praised Vukradin for good deeds aligning with Order values, said "Bringing you into the fold was the right thing to do." Embarrassed about Order's chant, requested Vukradin's help improving it. Confirmed Order has magic users.

**Adabra** - Approached party at Barthen's Provisions. Told Soma about withering grove past Whisper Woods - trees twisting unnaturally, wrong colors, creatures with too many eyes and jerky movements. Mentioned interventionists and Emerald Enclave.

**Martisha Vinetalker** - Half-elf owner of Wayside Inn. Striking features, stern demeanor, cold and calculating. Provided directions to coastal trail.

**Backes Dunfield** - Human bartender at Wayside Inn with scarred face and crooked smile.

**Cooragh Struckt** - Cook at Wayside Inn, famous for well-spiced stews and meat pies.

## Factions
**Order of the Gauntlet** - Chant revealed: "Steel and shield, Heart and band. We stand as one. A steadfast band." Has magic users among members. Values good deeds and broader principles.

**Emerald Enclave** - Referenced by Adabra in context of interventionists regarding the withering grove.

## Party
**Vukradin** - Earned "conflict-free gold" helping Elara. Commissioned by Ser Kaelen to improve Order of the Gauntlet's chant. Noted unusual tactical coordination of orc attackers. Cast Sleep spell in ambush combat.

**Valphine** - Received reward from Elara for Lathander. Developing cult concept focused on Lathander as god of pain. Detected ambush through drow intuition. Used Fairy Fire and crossbow in combat, finding pleasure in the "painful light."

**Soma** - Mended Elara's cart, agreed to investigate withering grove for Adabra. Transformed into brown bear during orc ambush.

**Brewbarry** - Retrieved Elara's runaway horse. Spotted ambush signs and concealed enemies. Engaged in rage during combat.

## Locations
**Phandalin** - Where Elara and Jarek were safely returned. Ser Kaelen Thorne still present there.

**Wayside Inn** - Inn on High Road owned by Martisha Vinetalker, with bartender Backes and cook Cooragh. Party stayed overnight.

**Whisper Woods Grove** - Past Whisper Woods, formerly vibrant grove blessed by Chauntea, now withering with twisted trees, wrong colors, and mutated creatures.

**Coastal Trail** - Snakes through broken, hilly terrain with unnatural gullies too dry for rivers. Site of planned orc ambush in tight juncture.
