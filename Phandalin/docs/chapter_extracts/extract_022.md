**Teega the Smith**
- Identity: Blacksmith from the Wayside Inn (tavern with forge at corner of High Road and Tribor Trail)

- Revealed information: Had been experiencing blackouts, was called by "the Kraken" which promised peace after endless wars

**Falcon**
- Actions: Greeted party, accepted prisoners, shared news about Mountain Toe's Goldmine crisis

**Emerald Enclave Guards (2)**

**Wild Boar (talking)**
