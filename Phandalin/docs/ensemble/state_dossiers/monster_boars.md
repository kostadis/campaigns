### Boars

**Current status:** Alive  
**Current location:** Outside the Woodland Manse, with some present inside the manse’s interior (implied by warnings)  
**Allegiance/faction:** Communal boar society enforcing strict eating rules; no external allegiance  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:** None noted  
**Current assignment / role and key relationships:** Enforcing communal eating rules; observing the Woodland Manse grounds as secure but interior as dangerous. Last seen communicating directly with the party.  
**Defining recent actions:** Confirmed the permanent removal of rule-breaking boars who violated communal eating norms; expressed profound relief at their absence; warned the party that the interior of the Woodland Manse remains dangerous (“a hot mess”), despite the grounds being secure.  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:** The boars prioritize adherence to feeding hierarchy over theology or external threats; their primary concern is internal social order. Their ability to speak, reason, and enforce rules suggests sentience and structured society. The “unpleasant boars” were likely exiled or killed for insubordination.
