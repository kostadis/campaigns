### Whispering Grove

**Current status:** Active, corrupted location  
**Current location:** Unspecified, but accessible and actively changing (last observed by party in ch12)  
**Current state:** A forest now fully possessed by a planar rift. The iridescent pools and planar wells are growing and pulsing with violent, sickly light. The air is poisoned, and the fabric of existence is stretched thin and frayed. The wells continue to disrupt the world.  
**Notable features:** Iridescent pools, planar wells (active and expanding), remnants of a once-protective good tree (now absent or inert).  
**Control/occupation:** No known current occupants or controllers; the planar rift itself is the dominant force.  
**Defining recent action:** The planar rift has escalated from mere withering to full possession, causing visible and physical degradation of the environment.  
**Significance:** Once a protected forest, now a site of planar instability. Lyra’s ritual attempt was motivated by her affection for the grove, implying it was a place of personal or spiritual importance prior to corruption.
