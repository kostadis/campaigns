## Identity
*Also known as: obsidian sword*

### Obsidian Sword

- **Current holder/location**: Carried by the trail-maker (last confirmed in ch34; no later change noted).  
- **Current condition**: Intact, with a crystal embedded in its pommel (ch36).  
- **Notable properties**: Level-draining effect; serves as the Carver’s weapon and the instrument of the domination ritual (ch36).  
- **Significance**: Central to the domination ritual performed on a white dragon (ch36).  
- **Key relationships**: Feared by Vukradin, who refused to come near it (ch34).
