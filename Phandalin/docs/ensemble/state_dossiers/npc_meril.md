### Meril

**Current status:** Alive (implied by ongoing influence and references in ch40; no death or disappearance confirmed)  
**Current location:** Unknown (last known to have departed from Soma’s cabin; no current location specified)  
**Allegiance/faction:** Druidic tradition tied to the Emerald Enclave (as referenced by Soma’s recollection); operates independently, possibly outside formal Enclave structure  
**Current possessions / notable items:** Meril’s Staff (last known to be in Soma’s possession; blessed by Meril, capable of affecting Xanth in unexplained ways, and tied to the wild shape well)  
**Spells/feats:** Not specified beyond mastery of druidic practices, including access to and instruction on reaching the wild shape well  
**Current assignment / role:** Former druidic master to Soma; appears to act as a selector and guide of unconventional druids, operating outside standard training timelines  
**Key relationships:**  
- Former mentor to Soma (trained her for one year, late in her life)  
- Selected and blessed Soma intentionally, giving her the staff not out of sentiment but because he “picked her”  
- Possibly known to Xanth, who recognizes the staff’s significance and confirms Meril chose Soma on purpose  
- May have had contact with or awareness of Emerald Enclave agents, though his own stance was conflicted  

**Defining recent actions:**  
- Trained Soma for one year, teaching her names for instinctual knowledge and how to reach the wild shape well  
- Deliberately selected Soma as a recipient of his staff, despite her late start and unconventional nature  
- Left the staff in her cabin and departed, offering no full explanation for his choice  
- His blessing on the staff and his selection of Soma are now recognized as significant by others (e.g., Xanth)  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Torn between letting the wilds be and protecting them — a conflict that shaped his teaching  
- Believed the universe does not care about intentions  
- Recognized something in Soma she could not see in herself — possibly an ancient or wild connection (“old tortle outside the Enclave’s habits”)  
- Chose Soma deliberately, suggesting he saw potential beyond traditional druidic metrics  
- His reasons for giving the staff, and why he left after only a year, remain unspoken — intentional mystery
