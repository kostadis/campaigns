### Bandits

**Current status:** Alive (group active)  
**Current location:** Inside the fortress (ch36)  
**Allegiance/faction:** Carver’s disposable crew (ch15)  
**Current possessions / notable items:** Crossbows (ch04, ch36), unspecified weapons (implied by combat)  
**Current assignment / role:** Ambushers and treasure-seekers; tasked with seizing treasure and eliminating Vukradin’s party (ch36)  
**Defining recent actions:**  
- Attacked Vukradin and his companions inside the fortress in an attempt to seize treasure (ch36).  
- Previously, a group of four bandits (Carver’s crew) engaged the party at Conyberry; one fled and was later executed by Borg (ch15).  
- In an earlier skirmish on Umbrage Hill, multiple bandits attacked the party; one was killed by Soma’s poison, one decapitated by Brewbarry, one slain by Vukradin’s crossbow, and one fled screaming upon seeing Soma in spider form (ch04).  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Operate as expendable enforcers for Carver (ch15).  
- Capable of coordinated ambushes and retreats; morale breaks under extreme threat (e.g., fleeing at sight of Soma’s spider form) (ch04, ch15).  

## Uncertainty
- The exact number of bandits currently active in the fortress (ch36) is unspecified — only “a group” is mentioned, but it’s unclear if this is the same four from ch15 or a new group.  
- It is unclear whether any of the original four bandits from ch15 survived beyond Borg’s execution of Bandit Number 4 — the ch36 attack may involve replacements or a different group entirely.  
- Attribution of the ch36 attack: While the bandits “attempt to seize the treasure and attack Vukradin and his companions,” it is not explicitly stated whether Carver ordered this or if they acted independently.  
- The ch36 attack occurs immediately after Sylvine Wintermoon’s command — it is ambiguous whether the bandits were already present and waiting, or summoned/arrived in response.
