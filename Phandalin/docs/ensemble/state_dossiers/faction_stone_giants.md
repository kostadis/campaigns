## Identity
*Also known as: giants*

### Giants

**Current status:** Active, non-interventionist faction.  
**Current location:** Unspecified, but operating within their own domain; not engaged in external conflicts.  
**Allegiance/faction:** Loyal to Hekaton’s doctrine; no external alliances or hierarchies reported.  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:** None specified.  
**Current assignment / role and key relationships:** To attend solely to “giant things” as decreed by Hekaton. No known superiors or subordinates beyond internal adherence to Hekaton’s teachings.  
**Defining recent actions:** Abandoned all wars with dragons and “little ones” following the breaking of the sundering and the failure of their attempt to seize power. Refuse to interfere with or act against “little ones,” including the Carver, deeming them irrelevant to their purpose.  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:** The giants’ core motivation is preservation and stewardship of all that is “giant” — implying a philosophical or spiritual shift away from conquest toward isolationist guardianship. The breaking of the sundering and the temptation of Tiamat’s power are viewed as catastrophic failures that validated Hekaton’s doctrine.  

## Uncertainty

- Whether all giants uniformly follow Hekaton’s doctrine is unconfirmed — no dissent or variation is mentioned, but none is ruled out.
