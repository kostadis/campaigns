### Blighted Clearing

- **Current state**: A blighted, unnatural disruption in the world, radiating madness and corruption.
- **Location**: A sixty-foot-wide clearing surrounded by twisted trees with obsidian-gleaming bark and a fractured canopy.
- **Key features**:  
  - A massive Treant at its center, limbs bent in impossible geometries, dripping glowing, unnatural sap into a dark pool.  
  - Pools of iridescent sap shimmering on the ground, shifting colors in a hypnotic, repulsive dance.  
  - Blackened, thorny underbrush that snags at passing creatures.  
  - Faint, flickering tears in reality pulsing softly near the Treant, whispering of madness.
- **Atmosphere**: The air tastes wrong; the clearing induces deep unease, overriding instinctual defenses. The woods do not merely grow crooked—they *twist* as if warped by an external force.

None.
