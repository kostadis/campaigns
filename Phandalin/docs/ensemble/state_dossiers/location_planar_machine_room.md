### Planar Machine Room

- **Current state:** Abandoned and stable. The planar machine has collapsed and disappeared.
- **Location:** Unchanged from prior; still the same chamber where the machine was housed.
- **Current condition:** The room is covered in interplanar spider webs. No active spatial anomalies remain.

- **Recent events:** The machine shut down via entered code, folded in on itself, and vanished. Its malfunction and catastrophic failure warnings ceased with its disappearance. Spatial anomalies that once plagued the area have been fully resolved.
