### Sepulcher Chamber

- **Current state**: Active, undisturbed since recent item retrieval.
- **Location**: Subterranean, accessible via corridor previously traversed by Vukradin; adjacent to the room where Soma’s rat died.
- **Notable items**: 
  - Flute of Illusions, located in the sarcophagus in the northwest corner.
  - Necklace of Fireballs (seven beads), located in the sarcophagus in the southeast corner.
- **Structure**: Contains multiple sarcophagi; at least two are specifically documented — one in the northwest corner and one in the southeast corner.
- **Recent activity**: Vukradin recently opened and looted both the northwest and southeast sarcophagi, retrieving the Flute of Illusions and Necklace of Fireballs respectively. Will-o'-wisps were previously encountered here but their current presence is unconfirmed.

## Uncertainty
- The exact total number of sarcophagi is unknown beyond “multiple.”
- The term “south west” in the quote about the Necklace of Fireballs conflicts with “southeast” — this may be a directional error in transcription; it is unclear whether the sarcophagus is southeast or southwest.
- The current status of the Will-o'-wisps (alive, dormant, or banished) is not confirmed after the looting.
- Whether other sarcophagi contain items or threats is unreported.
