### ledger

- **Current holder/location:** In possession of Horia Stonetallow (last seen in ch45).
- **Current condition:** Intact, containing updated financial records.
- **Current contents:** Records of startup costs, professional costs, back-office costs, insurance for the workers, salaries, food supplies, and hazard pay for remoteness.
- **Significance:** Official financial ledger for the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine; no longer contains Aletra’s forgeries (overridden by ch45’s updated content).

## Uncertainty
- The physical location of the ledger beyond "in Horia Stonetallow’s possession" is unspecified.
- Whether Aletra’s earlier forgeries were physically erased, overwritten, or replaced entirely is not stated — only that the current content is the updated financial record. The prior forgery may still exist as a hidden layer or in a separate copy, but no evidence supports this.
- It is unclear if the ledger is the same physical object from ch29 or a new one; the text does not confirm continuity of the physical item.
