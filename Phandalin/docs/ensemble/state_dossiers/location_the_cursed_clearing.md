### The Cursed Clearing

- **Current state**: A corrupted, magically saturated clearing centered on a dead pine tree.  
- **Location**: Unspecified region, but detectable as unnaturally wrong from approach.  
- **Notable features**:  
  - A forty-foot dead pine tree with blackened needles, limbs burned off years ago.  
  - Ghastly dolls hanging from the tree’s bare branches.  
  - Soil saturated with magical wrongness.  
  - Persistent smell of char and something older beneath.  
  - Insects avoid the tree entirely.  
  - Nothing nests in or shelters under the tree.  
- **Atmosphere**: Oppressive and intuitively wrong — the wrongness is perceptible before entry.

None.
