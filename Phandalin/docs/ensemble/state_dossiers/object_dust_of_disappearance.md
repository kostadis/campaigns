### Dust of Disappearance

- **What it is:** A tiny leather packet containing a magical substance known as Dust of Disappearance.
- **Current holder/location:** Thrown into the blood pile.
- **Current condition:** Active and deployed — physically present in the blood pile as of the latest action.
- **Significance:** Originally hidden in a hollow within the book *Elvish Idioms*, which was taken from the Woodland Manse by Valphine. Identified by Brewbarry and later used by Valphine, who confessed to its removal and subsequent disposal.

## Uncertainty
- The exact moment of deployment (whether thrown by Valphine or someone else) is not explicitly attributed — Valphine took it, but the quote “Into the blood pile, then.” does not specify who spoke or acted.
- Whether the dust is still potent after being thrown into the blood pile is not stated — its current magical state is unknown.
- The phrase “the book had a hole cut into its heart...” implies intentional concealment, but who cut the hole is unattributed.
