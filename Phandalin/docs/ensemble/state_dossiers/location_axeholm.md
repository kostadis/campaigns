## Identity
*Also known as: Axelholm*

### Axeholm

Axeholm is an ancient dwarven fortress carved into a gray mountain, flanked by towering bulwarks of seamless stone. Its walls feature arrow slits with no signs of occupation. The fortress exhibits shifting spatial dimensions, causing objects to appear closer than they should at certain distances. Townmaster Harbin Wester confirmed Axeholm was designed as a last-resort backup plan, not a new home for the town.
