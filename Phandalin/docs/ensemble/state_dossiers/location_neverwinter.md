### Neverwinter

Neverwinter is a city where the party has multiple active quests, including returning a stolen statue to an art collector before House Markaster intervenes. The Order of the Gauntlet is to be formally credited for the statue’s recovery. The city is the intended destination for Vukradin and the party following a recent battle, as it is where the rightful owners of a frozen block of dragon treasure may be located — though a local functionary claims the treasure is technically the property of the Crown. Vukradin’s music was controversial in Neverwinter, and the city remains a key hub for performance networks, as previously noted by Jenna. A road leads northwest toward Neverwinter, identified in council discussions and current travel plans.
