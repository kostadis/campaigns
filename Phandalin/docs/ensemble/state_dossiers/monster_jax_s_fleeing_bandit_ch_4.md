## Identity
*Also known as: Bandit, the bandit*

### bandit

**Current status:** Dead (two confirmed; one unknown)  
**Current location:** Unknown (last bandit fled combat in ch15)  
**Allegiance/faction:** Affiliated with Jax and the Carver (forced participant)  
**Current possessions / notable items:** None specified  
**Current assignment / role:** Low-level enforcer; recruited after village destruction  
**Key relationships:** Worked with Jax; blames the Carver for his condition  
**Defining recent actions:**  
- In ch04: Multiple bandits engaged in combat with Soma and Brewbarry; one screamed at Soma’s spider form, fled toward Brewbarry, and was described as afraid of spiders. Another drew a crossbow and missed before fleeing. One bit a victim with poison, then died from it. One stated he was not a bad person and was driven to banditry by the Carver’s destruction of his village.  
- In ch15: One bandit was killed instantly by Soma’s claws. Another was dispatched by Brewbarry with primal fury. The last bandit fled the scene unchased.

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Was driven to banditry after the Carver pillaged his village and left him with “no money, nothing.”  
- Claims he “doesn’t want to steal things” and is not a bad person.  
- Has never met the “big boss” — only worked with Jax.
