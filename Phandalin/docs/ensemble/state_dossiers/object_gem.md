### gem

- **Current holder/location:** Embedded in the pommel of a sword, currently in Vukradin’s possession.
- **Current condition:** Intact and functional; its purpose remains restricted or unactivated.
- **Significance:** A powerful artifact looted from the corpse of an alien entity that attempted to use it to enslave a dragon. It is also the same gem that, in a separate context, was regarded by a superstitious dwarf as a critical object whose absence nearly halted mine production.

## Uncertainty
- Contradiction between two roles of the gem: one as a dragon-slavery tool looted from an alien entity, and another as a superstitious talisman for a dwarf. It is unclear whether these are the same gem or two distinct gems referred to by the same term. The dwarf’s gem caused a mine to stall — but no evidence links it to the sword or Vukradin.  
- The current location of the dwarf or the mine is unknown; the gem’s presence in the sword does not confirm whether the dwarf’s gem was relocated, replaced, or if this is a case of ambiguous referent.  
- It is unclear whether the “special rock” mentioned in ch45 is the same physical object as the gem in the sword pommel, or merely a similarly described artifact.
