### Twig Blights

**Current status:** Alive (reconstituted)  
**Current location:** Inside the manse, near Brewbarry  
**Allegiance/faction:** Unknown; acting autonomously with malice  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:** None; no equipment or magical abilities noted beyond biological traits  
**Current assignment / role and key relationships:** Actively hostile toward Brewbarry; previously targeted Soma in bear form  
**Defining recent actions:** After being obliterated and reduced to sawdust by Valphine’s sphere of psychic darkness, three remaining twig figures reconstituted into living blights, sprouting eyes and root-thin legs, then skittered toward Brewbarry. Prior to this, they swarmed Soma, clawed uselessly at her fur, and squeezed through doorways diagonally.  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:** Driven by instinct and malice; intelligence level 4; not convertible. Their reconstitution after total physical destruction implies a latent, possibly magical resilience or connection to an unseen force (e.g., the Circle of Thunder or Talos’s influence).

- Ambiguous attribution: [ch44] attributes the sawdust reduction to Valphine; [ch44] also attributes it to Brewbarry. Which is correct?  
- Unclear origin: The three reconstituted blights are described as “the other three figures” — were these distinct from the original dozen? Were they hidden or dormant?  
- Current number: Were only three reconstituted, or did more emerge? The text only confirms three.  
- Whether the reconstituted blights retain their original intelligence/malice or have evolved is unconfirmed.
