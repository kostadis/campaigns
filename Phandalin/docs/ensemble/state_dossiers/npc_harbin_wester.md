## Identity
*Also known as: Harbin, Harbin (Townmaster), Townmaster, Townmaster Harbin, Townmaster Harbin Wester*

### Harbin Wester

**Current status:** Alive  
**Current location:** Phandalin  
**Allegiance/faction:** Appointed Townmaster of Phandalin by Lord Neverember; operates independently but claims sole authority to be replaced by Lord Neverember alone  
**Current possessions / notable items:** None specified  
**Current assignment / role:** Townmaster of Phandalin; oversees local governance, posts quests on the town board, and manages civic response to threats  
**Key relationships:**  
- Appointed by Lord Neverember (sole authority to replace him)  
- Brother of Tibor Wester  
- Recently proposed a privy council including himself, Tolbin, Elmer, Halia, Linene, Adabra, and Vukradin  
- Has accused Vukradin and members of the Order of the Gauntlet of scandalous conduct  
- Previously offered free room and board at Stonehill Inn under duress from Adabra  

**Defining recent actions:**  
- Posted a quest regarding the orc attack on Butterskull Ranch  
- Opposed immediate town meetings, insisting on formal preparation, agendas, and pre-work  
- Proposed forming a privy council to manage Phandalin’s crises  
- Accused Vukradin of crimes, drawing condemnation from the Order of the Gauntlet  
- Warned adventurers against traveling to Lord Neverember while Phandalin remains unsafe  
- Acknowledged the cleansing of the lighthouse but showed no interest in immediate repairs  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Believes his authority is absolute and derives solely from Lord Neverember; resists any challenge to his governance structure  
- Prioritizes procedural formality over urgency, even amid active threats  
- Seeks to consolidate control through a handpicked privy council, including key figures like Adabra and Vukradin  

## Uncertainty
- Contradiction in name spelling: "Neverember" (ch06, ch10) vs. "Neverwinter" (ch10) — unclear if this is a typo or reflects two different entities; Harbin references both, but the intended lord is ambiguous.  
- Harbin is called "Townmaster of Phandolin" in ch16 — likely a typo for "Phandalin," but the inconsistency remains uncorrected in source.  
- The privy council proposal (ch16) was made, but it is unknown whether it was formally convened or accepted by the named members.  
- Harbin’s current relationship with Adabra is unclear: he feared her look (ch06), yet included her in his proposed council (ch16).  
- The nature of the "scandalous accusations" against Vukradin and the Order of the Gauntlet is not specified — only that they occurred and were condemned.  
- Whether Harbin still holds the Stonehill Inn under his control or has resumed payment obligations after the initial offer is unknown.
