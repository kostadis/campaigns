### Wintershield Watchman

**Current status:** Alive  
**Current location:** Wintershield headquarters (implied by context of treasure-handling duties)  
**Allegiance/faction:** Loyal to Lord Neverember; serves as an agent of the Wintershield organization  
**Current possessions / notable items:** None specified  
**Current assignment / role:** Oversees treasure intake and holding protocol for adventurers; enforces Lord Neverember’s policy of using adventurers to pacify the hinterland  
**Key relationships:** Reports to or operates under the authority of Lord Neverember; interacts directly with adventurers (e.g., Vukardin)  
**Defining recent actions:**  
- Informed adventurers that Lord Neverember is using them to eliminate opposition in the hinterland in lieu of deploying a formal army.  
- Clarified that unclaimed treasure turned in to Wintershield headquarters is held for 60 days, after which a portion is diverted to the Wintershield retirement fund and the remainder released to the finder.  
- Explicitly permitted adventurers to keep treasure found while clearing monsters and evildoers from the hinterland.  

## Uncertainty
- It is unclear whether the Wintershield watchman is a single individual or a title held by multiple guards — the facts refer to “the Wintershield watchman” in singular form but do not confirm uniqueness.  
- The exact chain of command beyond “Lord Neverember” is unspecified.  
- It is ambiguous whether the 60-day holding policy applies universally or only to treasure turned in voluntarily (vs. treasure kept by adventurers — which is permitted).  
- The phrase “we will take a portion for our retirement fund” implies collective benefit, but no details are given on how the portion is calculated or who qualifies for the fund.
