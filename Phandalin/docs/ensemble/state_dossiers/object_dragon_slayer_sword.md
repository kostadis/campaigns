## Identity
*Also known as: Dragon Slayer*

### Dragon Slayer Sword

- **Type:** Magical weapon (longsword)  
- **Current holder:** Brewbarry  
- **Current location:** In Brewbarry’s possession, actively carried into battle against Cryovain  
- **Current condition:** Functional, glowing when wielded; confirmed to be in hand during the attack on Cryovain  
- **Properties:** +1 sword  
- **Notable features:** Glows when held; its presence summoned an Invisible Stalker to guard its resting place  
- **Current assignment / role:** Primary weapon used by Brewbarry to confront and attack Cryovain  
- **Key relationships:** Central to the party’s quest; explicitly chosen by the party for Brewbarry to wield; previously guarded by an Invisible Stalker at its resting place atop a dragon’s skull in the Northern Chamber  
- **Defining recent action:** Used by Brewbarry to attack Cryovain; still in his possession immediately after the strike  

## Uncertainty
- Conflict between [ch33] stating “Valphine claimed the Dragon Slayer Sword” and “Brewbarry picks up the Dragon Slayer Sword” — both cannot be simultaneously true as the final act of possession. Later facts confirm Brewbarry wielded it in combat and still had it, overriding Valphine’s claim.  
- [ch32] says the sword “was buried across from Lady Alagondar,” but later facts place it atop a dragon’s skull in the Northern Chamber — this may reflect prior location before being moved or reburied, but no clear chronology confirms how or when the relocation occurred.  
- The phrase “Valphine finds the Dragon Slayer Sword” does not specify if she took it — later facts confirm Brewbarry took possession, so Valphine’s action is likely observational.
