### Bathrobe

- **Current holder:** Prutha  
- **Current location:** Unknown (last known to be taken from the Woodland Manse by Prutha)  
- **Current condition:** Intact (no damage noted)  
- **Significance:** One of two identical bathrobes taken from the Woodland Manse; Brewbarry took one, Prutha took another. Brewbarry previously owned a bathrobe from Falcon’s Lodge but returned it.  

## Uncertainty
- It is unclear which bathrobe (the one Brewbarry took from the manse or the one Prutha stole) is the same physical object now in Prutha’s possession — the facts state both took a bathrobe from the manse, but do not clarify if they are the same item or two separate ones.  
- The current location of the bathrobe Prutha stole is not specified.  
- It is unknown whether Brewbarry’s claimed possession of the manse bathrobe was temporary or if he still holds it; his confession and Prutha’s theft are presented as concurrent events with no temporal sequence given.  
- The fate of the Falcon’s Lodge bathrobe is confirmed as returned, but its current whereabouts are not stated.
