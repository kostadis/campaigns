### Icespire Hold

Icespire Hold is a mountain fortress composed of stone and ice, situated on a high peak where the landscape is defined by extreme altitude and enduring cold. It consists of two ancient structures joined by a stone bridge, and its appearance is described as indifferent, as if the mountain itself surrendered to the cold. The fortress features a gatehouse and is currently occupied by a sleeping dragon on its roof. It is visible as gray teeth rising from the Overbright, indicating its current prominence in the landscape. The location is fifty-five miles from Phandalin as the crow flies.
