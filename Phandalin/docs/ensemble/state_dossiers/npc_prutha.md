### Prutha

**Current Status:** Alive  
**Current Location:** Woodland Manse (last seen in ch45, accompanying the party)  
**Allegiance/Faction:** Zealot of Lathander; devoted follower of Valphine, the true prophet of the Church of the Searing Light  
**Current Possessions / Notable Items:**  
- Small bone whistle (carried and blown at key moments, including after conversion)  
- Great axe (used in combat at Falcon’s Hunting Lodge and Woodland Manse)  
- Javelin (used to strike anchorites)  
- Stolen candelabra (taken from the dining table at Woodland Manse)  
- Stolen bathrobe (taken from the manse)  

**Current Assignment / Role:**  
- Primary enforcer and loyal bodyguard to Valphine; declares, *“I make things safe for Cleric of Lathander.”*  
- Leading a small group of converted orcs (five additional orcs joined him after the vision in ch45)  
- Acts as theological zealot and symbol-destroyer of Talosian relics  
- Serves as a bridge between orcish tradition and Lathanderite faith  

**Key Relationships:**  
- **Valphine:** Devoted follower; calls her the “true prophet”; stands beside her in ritual chambers; follows her without question  
- **Great-uncle (deceased):** Former clan shaman; his saying, *“Dawn comes for everyone, even those born in shadow,”* guides Prutha’s worldview  
- **Eastern Heart Clan:** Recognizes their Sun and Stone banner at Falcon’s Hunting Lodge; identifies them as family; emotional connection remains  
- **Former Talosian allies:** Now enemies; killed or rejected; one orc he recognized in combat was met with violent Orcish curses  

**Defining Recent Actions:**  
- Converted from Talos worshipper to Lathanderite zealot, publicly renouncing Talos and destroying holy symbols  
- Led the destruction of Talosian anchorites, kicking their bodies and declaring theological transition  
- Joined the party in combat at Falcon’s Hunting Lodge and Woodland Manse, using great axe and javelin  
- Broke free of grasping roots during the manse incursion  
- Roared *“Behold the power of the sun!”* after Valphine’s radiance killed stirges  
- Took stolen items (candelabra, bathrobe) from the manse, signaling personal appropriation of the new faith’s spoils  
- Maintained silent, watchful presence beside Valphine during ritual chamber incursion, visibly uneasy but loyal  

**Revealed Motivations / Secrets:**  
- Conversion is total, embodied, and uncompromising — he takes faith “with his whole body, chest-thumping, a born-again Lathandrite to the marrow”  
- His loyalty to Valphine is absolute: *“I follow the true prophet! ... ALWAYS!”*  
- Deep trauma from his great-uncle’s murder by Talosians; his conversion is both vengeance and redemption  
- Despite his zeal, he retains emotional ties to his orcish heritage — recognizes clan banners, speaks Orcish with anatomical vividness, and is internally torn between tribe and faith  
- He watches others’ eyes during revelations — suggesting he seeks validation or connection in his transformation
