### Talisman

- **Current location:** In the possession of the party, recovered from Moesko’s concealed compartment.
- **Current condition:** Intact, emitting a faint rhythmic thrumming like a distant heartbeat.
- **Energy signature:** Emits a faint divine resonance and a dominant, unsettling psionic energy from its core.
- **Appearance:** Dark, iridescent object.
- **Engravings:**
  - Obverse: Crudely etched with the stylized, jagged lightning bolt of Talos, God of Storms.
  - Reverse: An inverted eye from which delicate, coiling lines—reminiscent of tentacles—unfurl.
- **Significance:** Symbols suggest worship of a giant octopus, conflicting with the iconography of Talos.

## Uncertainty
- Contradiction between the Talos lightning bolt (a known divine symbol) and the inverted eye/tentacle motif suggesting octopus worship — no resolution provided on whether this is a heretical fusion, a deception, or a misattribution.
- The claim that “he kind of worships a giant octopus” (attributed to Brewbarry) is hearsay; it is unclear whether this refers to Moesko, the talisman’s true origin, or is a misinterpretation.
- No definitive owner or creator of the talisman is established; its origin remains unconfirmed despite the dual iconography.
