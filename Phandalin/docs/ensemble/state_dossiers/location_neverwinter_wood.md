### Neverwinter Wood

- **Current state:** Overrun by orcs allied with half-orc spellcasters (Anchorites of Talos).
- **Location:** A forested region centered on a cave-ridden hill crowned with a circle of standing stones.
- **Current control:** Orcs and Anchorites of Talos.
- **Notable features:** A circle of standing stones atop a cave-ridden hill, used for dark rites.
- **Defining recent action:** Anchorites of Talos perform dark rites at the standing stone circle; a battle occurred on the hill.
- **Key relationships:** Occupied by orcs and half-orc spellcasters (Anchorites of Talos); previously a place of nostalgic resonance for Soma and Valphine.

## Uncertainty
- The exact boundaries or ecological condition of the forest beyond the hill and stone circle are unspecified.
- Whether the "strange goings-on" referenced in ch06 are the same as the current orc/Anchorite occupation is implied but not confirmed.
- The status of the cave system beneath the hill (e.g., whether it is actively used, sealed, or inhabited beyond the rites) is not detailed.
