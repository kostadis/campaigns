### The Stone Giant's Pass

- **Current state:** A narrow, unstable mountain pass where reality is fraying.  
- **Notable features:**  
  - Rocks along the trail shimmer and phase in and out of existence.  
  - Rocks are displaced and sit at wrong angles, as if reality failed to reseat them properly.  
  - The pass narrows sharply, constricting like a living thing attempting to expel travelers.
