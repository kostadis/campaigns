### Gauntlets of Ogre Power

- **Current holder:** Brewbarry (confirmed by ch34)
- **Current location:** On Brewbarry’s hands during the ascent of the pass (ch34)
- **Current condition:** Worn and actively in use
- **Notable attribution:** Previously belonged to the deceased chief accountant (ch29); now wielded by Brewbarry (ch34)
- **Significance:** Enhances strength; worn in conjunction with Goliath heritage during a perilous climb

## Uncertainty
- Contradiction between "Brewbarry wears the Gauntlets of Ogre Power" and "The narrator wears the Gauntlets of Ogre Power" in ch34 — if Brewbarry is the narrator, this is consistent; if not, the attribution is ambiguous. The text does not clarify whether Brewbarry *is* the narrator.
