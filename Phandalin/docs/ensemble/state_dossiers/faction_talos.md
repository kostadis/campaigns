### Talos

**Current status:** Active faction, worshipped as the God of Storms.  
**Current allegiance:** Followed by shapeshifted anchorites; adherents use a mark described as “simple as a scar.”  
**Current possessions / notable symbols:** The lightning bolt symbol (crudely etched) and the scar-like mark (used on wax seals) are both recognized as emblems of Talos.  
**Current assignment / role:** The faction’s known agents include shapeshifted anchorites — followers who have undergone transformation to serve Talos directly.  
**Defining recent actions:** Talos’s mark has been used to seal a satchel in chapter 44, indicating active ritual or administrative use by followers. The presence of a shapeshifted anchorite confirms ongoing operational presence.
