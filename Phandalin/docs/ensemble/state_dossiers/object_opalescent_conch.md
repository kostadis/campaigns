### Opalescent Conch

- **Current holder/location:** Held by the party, recovered from near Moesko’s lifeless form after his death.  
- **Current condition:** Intact, with subtle shimmer still present.  
- **Significance:** An artifact originally belonging to the undead creature Miral the Sea Elf, sought by Miral.  
- **Notable attribution:** Recognized by both Valphine and the narrator in Chapter 9 as Miral’s artifact; confirmed as the same object previously held by Moesko.

- The exact moment of transfer from Moesko to the party is implied but not explicitly described (e.g., was it picked up immediately after death or later?).
