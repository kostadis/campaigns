### stirges

**Current status:** Partially active; some still alive and hidden.  
**Current location:** Rafters and upper reaches of the chamber where Soma and Vukradin were engaged.  
**Allegiance/faction:** None (wild monsters).  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:** None.  
**Current assignment / role and key relationships:** Feeding on Soma and ambushing targets from above. Last seen targeting Soma and Vukradin.  
**Defining recent actions:**  
- Three stirges fed on Soma through gaps in her shell, bloated and actively draining her blood.  
- Three more dropped onto Vukradin as he was being pulled up.  
- Two stirges were killed by Brewbarry’s cleave and one by his hand in rapid succession.  
- One stirge died mid-air after Vukradin’s mockery bite.  
- One stirge was crushed when Soma bit it.  
- Two stirges remain hidden and undisturbed in the rafters, tucked where light doesn’t reach, patient and nearly invisible.  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:** Driven by hunger; ambush predators that target living creatures from above, especially when blood is accessible. Show no fear of larger foes; attack repeatedly despite losses.

## Uncertainty
- Contradiction between “three stirges are feeding on Soma” and “three more dropped onto Vukradin” — it is unclear whether these are the same three or separate groups; later fact confirms three are still on Soma, so the three on Vukradin are distinct.  
- “A couple still tucked up where nobody could touch them” and “two stirges remained hidden against the beams” — both describe two survivors, but it is unclear if these are the same two or if more than two remain.  
- Attribution of the stirge that “dropped dead in the air” — was it killed by Vukradin’s mockery bite, or did it die from some other effect? The text implies causation but does not confirm mechanical outcome.  
- It is unknown whether the two hidden stirges are still alive or merely dormant — no confirmation of their current vitality.
