### Invisible Stalker

- **Current status:** Destroyed  
- **Current location:** Northern chamber, atop the dragon skull (where the Dragon Slayer Sword was guarded)  
- **Allegiance/faction:** Summoned elemental, bound to guard the Dragon Slayer Sword  
- **Current possessions / notable items:** None (destroyed)  
- **Current assignment / role:** Formerly assigned to guard the Dragon Slayer Sword atop the dragon skull; no longer active  
- **Defining recent actions:**  
  - Launched surprise attacks on Valphine and Brewbarry, dealing significant damage.  
  - Hovered directly over the Dragon Slayer Sword after it was dropped, adopting a defensive, provocative posture.  
  - Was revealed by *Moonbeam* and destroyed by sustained damage from *Moonbeam* and *Cloud of Daggers*.  
  - After its defeat, Brewbarry picked up the Dragon Slayer Sword.  
- **Revealed motivations or secrets:** It was a summoned elemental, typically used by wizards to guard precious objects or eliminate thieves — its sole purpose was to protect the Dragon Slayer Sword at all costs.
