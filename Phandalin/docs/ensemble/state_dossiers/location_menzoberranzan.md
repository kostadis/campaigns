### Menzoberranzan

- **Current state:** In ruins, shattered by demonic invasion.  
- **Current location:** Underdark, former seat of the drow city-state.  
- **Current control/occupation:** No ruling house or authority remains intact; overrun by invading demons.  
- **Notable recent event:** Invaded by demons that erupted from the Underdark into the Overworld, causing catastrophic collapse of the city’s structure and social order.  
- **Significance:** Once the dominant drow city-state, now a broken ruin after the demons’ assault — a fall even the great houses failed to anticipate.

## Uncertainty
- The exact extent of demonic presence within the city now is unspecified — are they still actively rampaging, or has the violence subsided into occupation?  
- Whether any drow houses or survivors remain within the ruins is not stated.  
- The phrase “came up to the Overworld” is ambiguous: did the demons breach to the surface world beyond Menzoberranzan, or is this a misstatement referring to their uprising within the Underdark?
