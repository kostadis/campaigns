### Shell-Sprout

The term "shell-sprout" is not an entity but a derogatory epithet used by Adabra, Ser Kaelen Thorne, and Soma to describe **Vukradin**. It conveys criticism of his perceived naivety, detachment from the natural world, and lack of rootedness — likening him to a fragile, ungrounded sprout still wet from its shell, unfit for the harshness of the world.

No entity named "Shell-Sprout" exists independently. The term is exclusively applied to Vukradin by these three speakers.
