## Identity
*Also known as: Coneyberry*

### Conyberry

Conyberry is a ruined, abandoned town. Its homes are skeletal remains with collapsed roofs, rotted and blackened wooden frames, and crumbled wattle-and-daub walls, all signs of fire and prolonged decay. The streets are overgrown with thorny vines and tenacious weeds. The town was destroyed and utterly looted years ago, and remains a silent, ransacked wreck.
