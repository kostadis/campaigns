### Lolth

**Current status:** Alive  
**Current location:** Not bound to a physical place; her presence is a condition, not a location  
**Allegiance/faction:** Patron deity of the drow and their Underdark servitors  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:** None (as a deity, not defined by physical items)  
**Current assignment / role and key relationships:**  
- Scheming deity whose influence persists through proxies and long-term plans  
- Source of power and doctrine for drow servants, particularly those imprisoned or enduring centuries of service in the Underdark  
- Revered as the incarnate will by Valphine, who acts in her name  
- Teaches through hierarchy, appetite, fear, and the deliberate sacrifice of lesser lives  

**Defining recent actions:**  
- Maintained influence across centuries despite being thought finished after being trapped in a demi-plane  
- Continues to operate through agents (e.g., Valphine) who embody her will and perform rituals in her name  
- Her doctrine remains active in the Underdark, where comfort is rejected and suffering is institutionalized  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Her entrapment in a demi-plane was part of a multi-century contingency plan — she anticipated and engineered her own apparent defeat  
- Her presence is not localized; she is a pervasive, inescapable condition, like Talos — worshippers serve her wherever they are, not where she “resides”  

## Uncertainty
- It is unclear whether Lolth remains physically trapped in the demi-plane or has escaped/evolved beyond it — ch01 states she was trapped “as part of an elaborate multi-century plan,” but ch39 implies her influence is now omnipresent, suggesting the trap was never absolute or was part of the plan.  
- Valphine’s belief that she is the “incarnate will of Lolth” is subjective — it is not confirmed whether Lolth actively endorses or communicates with Valphine, or if this is merely Valphine’s interpretation.  
- The nature of Lolth’s “contingency plans” beyond her apparent entrapment is unspecified — no details exist on what those plans entailed or whether they have been fully activated.
