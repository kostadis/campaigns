### Manticore

- **Current status:** Dead  
- **Current location:** Killed atop the windmill, no indication of movement after death  
- **Allegiance/faction:** None; acted independently as a predatory creature  
- **Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:** None; corpse is the only remnant. Wounds inflicted by Brewbarry’s halberd and axes led to fatal injury.  
- **Current assignment / role and key relationships:** None; last seen engaging the party with intent to consume Corbin. No allegiance to any faction or individual.  
- **Defining recent actions:** Attacked Brewbarry and Valphine; flew to the second floor in pain after being struck; spoke intelligently, expressing preference for human meat and offering to spare the party if handed Corbin; roared, perked its head at the party, and was unaffected by Vukradin’s spell; ultimately slain by Brewbarry.  
- **Revealed motivations or secrets:** Believed itself to be merely seeking a “tasty meal”; viewed human flesh as superior to other humanoids; responded to provocation (including a “tasty morsel”) with rage, not hunger.  

## Uncertainty
- The exact timing of death relative to flight to the second floor is unclear — was the wound from the halberd fatal before or after the flight? (Both are described, but sequence of lethal damage is not specified.)  
- Whether the manticore’s corpse remains atop the windmill or was removed is unconfirmed — though no later chapter describes movement, its body’s disposition is not stated after death.  
- Adabra’s mention in ch06 is retrospective; it does not confirm the manticore is still present or active, only that it is cited as an example — consistent with death in ch05.
