### Sword Mountains

A rugged mountain range containing the subterranean dwarven halls of Gnomengard, located deep within its peaks. The expedition reaches Gnomengard by ascending through the mountains after traversing broken hilly terrain south of Phandalin. Ancient dwarven ruins lie near the foothills of the range.

None.
