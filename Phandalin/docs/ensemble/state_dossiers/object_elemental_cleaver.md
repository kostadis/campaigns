### Elemental Cleaver

- **Type:** Magical weapon (halberd)  
- **Current holder/location:** Wielded by the narrator (as of ch43)  
- **Origin:** Taken from a dead man (ch43)  
- **Current condition:** Emits acid upon striking (ch43)  
- **Notable property:** The blade bites acid when it wants to (ch43)  
- **Significance:** Named by the narrator to make it “more real” (ch41); its acid property is active and intentional, not merely a defect  

## Uncertainty
- Contradiction between ch41 (where the Elemental Cleaver is referred to as a handaxe) and ch43 (where it is definitively a halberd). Later chapter (ch43) overrides earlier, so current state is halberd — but the origin of the discrepancy is unexplained.  
- Unclear if the narrator and Brewbarry are the same person: Brewbarry is named as wielding it in ch43, but the narrator also says “I’d taken it from a dead man” — no explicit identification is made between Brewbarry and the narrator.  
- Vukradin’s awareness of the weapon’s origin is unknown — the narrator implies Vukradin doesn’t know, but whether Vukradin *currently* knows or has since learned is not stated.
