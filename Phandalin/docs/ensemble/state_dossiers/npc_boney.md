### Boney

**Current status:** Alive (sentient skeletal horse)  
**Current location:** Inside the palisade, near the stable house (last known position in ch42)  
**Allegiance/faction:** Faithful servant to Soma (perceived as Lady Alagondar reincarnated); honorary member of an orc tribe (ch44)  
**Current possessions / notable items:**  
- Carries and wields a two-handed longsword (ch40)  
- Wears a rotting saddle (originally found with bones, ch32)  

**Current assignment / role and key relationships:**  
- Serves as Soma’s faithful mount and combat companion (ch39)  
- Bonded with Valphine (ch33)  
- Ridden by Vukradin during evacuation plans (ch42)  
- Praised by Rimardo as a “committed horse” who maintains work ethic despite being dead (ch44)  

**Defining recent actions:**  
- Attacked a prone anchorite with a two-handed longsword strike (ch40)  
- Took a lightning bolt from an anchorite (ch43)  
- Moved into the palisade near the stable house during evacuation (ch42)  
- Accepted being enchanted without resistance, simply going where directed (ch43)  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Believes he was brought back to serve Lady Alagondar on an important quest (ch32)  
- Originally guarded her sword, which he believes is buried “across the way” to the north (ch32)  
- Was killed by Will-o’-wisps after being brought to the dungeon (ch32)  
- Has no memory of his life before death, only confusion over missing physical features (mane, etc.) (ch32)  
- Does not tire, startle, complain about weather, or attempt to eat packs (ch39)  
- Accepts supernatural interference (e.g., enchantments, reanimation) passively — “simply goes where directed” (ch43)  

## Uncertainty

- Whether he still believes Soma is Lady Alagondar reincarnated — never contradicted, but not reaffirmed since ch32.
