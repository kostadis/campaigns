### Twig Figures

- **Current location:** On the mantelpiece above the cold fireplace in the scholar's house.  
- **Current condition:** Small, bound, and bent dried twig figures, arranged in a neat row.  
- **Current representation:** Each figure corresponds to one member of the party: one tall and broad, one hunched with a staff, one slender — matching the party members’ physical forms.  
- **Notable properties:** Resemble cursed effigies previously encountered in the forest; appear to be magically bound or enchanted representations.
