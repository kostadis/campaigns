### Second Cave

- **Current state**: Occupied and intact.  
- **Location**: Unspecified, but accessible via an entrance with a carved lintel.  
- **Notable features**:  
  - Several living boars penned inside, calm and unsurprised by intruders.  
  - A hidden compartment in the floor, identified by a seam with a square cut too clean to be natural.  
  - A lintel above the entrance carved with a boar pursuing a stick figure.
