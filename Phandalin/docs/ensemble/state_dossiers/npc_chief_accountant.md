### Chief Accountant

**Current status:** Alive  
**Current location:** Within the accounting nexus (post-spatial distortions)  
**Allegiance/faction:** Unknown; appears bound to an automated accounting system under threat from “the auditor” and subject to Aletra’s authority  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:**  
- Capable of emitting blue-white static energy for stabilization  
- No physical items noted, but possesses override code to shut down the accounting machine  
- Exhibits glitching, flickering luminous dwarven form with shifting geometric facial mask and ink-like shadows leaking from eyes  
- Voice: blend of grandmotherly warmth and screeching static  

**Current assignment / role and key relationships:**  
- Chief Accountant of a sentient or automated ledger system  
- Reports to or fears “the auditor,” who may liquidate her for closing Valphine’s account  
- Previously reported to Aletra, who laughed at her complaints about unbalanced books  
- Last seen interacting with Valphine, whom she called “sister of the sun” and attempted to stabilize  

**Defining recent actions:**  
- After spatial distortions ceased, became calm and relaxed  
- Previously screamed for ten minutes over an interplanetary bill of 42,000 gold pieces, calling it outrageous and incorrect  
- Earlier attempted to stabilize Valphine with static energy while accusing her of “bleach and betrayal”  
- Offered the override code to shut down the machine in exchange for being allowed to leave and not being reported to Aletra  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Deeply afraid of being liquidated by “the auditor” for failing to properly close Valphine’s account  
- Seeks to escape accountability and conceal her actions from Aletra  
- Haunted by past dismissal from Aletra and systemic failures (“ledgers that wouldn’t close,” “regulators dividing by zero”)  

## Uncertainty  
- Contradiction: Was Valphine’s “sister of the sun” title a genuine affection, a manipulative tactic, or a glitched reference? The intent is unclear.  
- Ambiguity: It is unknown whether the Chief Accountant still possesses the override code — she offered it, but it is not confirmed she handed it over.  
- Unclear attribution: Who caused the spatial distortions? The Chief Accountant’s calmness follows their disappearance, but no actor is named.  
- Unclear chronology: Did she scream about the 42,000 gold bill before or after attempting to stabilize Valphine? The facts don’t establish sequence.  
- Ambiguity: Is “the auditor” a person, a system, or a metaphor? Not defined.
