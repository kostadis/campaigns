### Stonehill Inn

- **Type:** Location (roadhouse/tavern)  
- **Current status:** Operational and occupied  
- **Current location:** Center of Phandalin  
- **Current controller/owner:** Toblen (tavern keeper, nicknamed “Spiderman”)  
- **Current condition:** Serving as the primary lodging for the party; actively used as a dining and resting place  
- **Current possessions / notable features:** None explicitly listed beyond its structure and staff; functions as a standard two-story roadhouse with tavern service  
- **Current assignment / role:** Official lodging for the party, provided free of charge by Townmaster Harbin  
- **Key relationships:**  
  - Operated by Toblen (“Spiderman”)  
  - Primary residence for the party (Vukradin, narrator, and others)  
  - Under the patronage of Townmaster Harbin, who provided free room and board  
- **Defining recent actions:**  
  - Served as the group’s base after Harbin’s offer of free lodging  
  - Site of a meal immediately prior to Vukradin’s departure  
  - Repeatedly referenced as the group’s current location in Chapter 6
