### Bone Whistle

- **Current holder/location:** Held by Prutha, carried inside his armor.
- **Current condition:** Intact, functional; recently used to blow the dawn prayer at Falcon’s Hunting Lodge.
- **Significance:** A clan tradition from Prutha’s great-uncle; used daily in a ritual to greet the sunrise, rooted in an eastern orcish custom.
- **Notable property:** Emotionally resonant — described as having moved an observer more than expected upon first seeing it.

None.
