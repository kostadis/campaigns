### Tresendar Manor

Tresendar Manor has been restored and now houses the Temple of Lathander’s Searing Pain of Justice. The structure, once dilapidated, is no longer in ruin and serves as the official home of the temple, as confirmed by its current high priest’s reference to it as “the restored bones of Tresendar Manor.”
