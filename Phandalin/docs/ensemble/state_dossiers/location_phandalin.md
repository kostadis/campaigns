### Phandalin

- **Current status:** Thriving mining settlement, recently saved from destruction by the defeat of the Carver and the dragon.  
- **Current location:** Nestled in the snow-capped Sword Mountains, northwest of the current party position and the grove; accessible via the Triboar Trail.  
- **Allegiance/faction:** De facto under the authority of the Townmaster; indirectly aligned with Ser Kaelen Thorn and the Emerald Enclave following recent events.  
- **Current possessions / notable items:**  
  - Stonehill Inn (central lodging and meals)  
  - Townmaster’s Hall (with job board)  
  - Market square  
  - Privy Council steps  
  - Crumbling stone ruins surrounding the town (remnants of older settlement)  
- **Current assignment / role and key relationships:**  
  - Hub for regional recovery and celebration after the Carver’s defeat.  
  - Destination for Ser Kaelen Thorn, who awaits the party to celebrate and address issues with the Emerald Enclave.  
  - Planned site for Vukradin’s new studio.  
  - Destination for The Falcon to settle debts and plan next moves after losing his lodge.  
  - Site of imprisonment for Shrimpy (transported there after capture).  
- **Defining recent actions:**  
  - Successfully defended against the Carver’s threat to burn the town.  
  - Celebrated by townspeople after the party returned with evidence of the dragon’s defeat (scales, claw).  
  - Hosted a council meeting to report on recent events.  
  - Now a focal point for regional stability and rebuilding.  
- **Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
  - Once a neglected settlement needing clearance of monsters and cults (per Lord Nevermember’s original directive), now a symbol of resilience and renewal.  
  - Icespire Hold is visible from Phandalin on clear days when eastern light is low and golden — a key geographical landmark.
