### Brewbarry

**Current Status:** Alive  
**Current Location:** Phandalin (implied by recent activities at the lodge and mine; last seen in the bathhouse after defeating stirges)  
**Allegiance/Faction:** Former Uthgardt barbarian; now independent nomad. No longer affiliated with any tribe or order.  
**Current Possessions / Notable Items:**  
- **Dragon Slayer Sword** (taken after defeating the invisible stalker; currently carried)  
- **+1 Halberd** (claimed after the battle with Moesko; still in use)  
- **Pair of Gleaming Handaxes** (pulled from a hidden pouch; one named “Elemental Cleaver”)  
- **Dread Helm** (worn at all times; causes eyes to glow an ominous red)  
- **Dust of Disappearance** (identified and in possession)  
- **Mine Shares** (owned; primary financial stake)  
- **Breath Pouch from Cryovain** (contains a contained, waiting sound)  
- **Dragon Scales** (collected from Cryovain’s corpse; one held longer than necessary)  
- **Bathrobe** (formerly owned from Falcon’s Lodge; now returned, but still desired)  

**Current Assignment / Role and Key Relationships:**  
- **Role:** Primary physical enforcer and heavy combatant for the party; de facto protector of Vukradin.  
- **Key Relationships:**  
  - **Vukradin:** Deeply loyal companion; considers him a mentor and musical inspiration.  
  - **The Party:** Respected as a reliable, if unpredictable, ally.  
  - **Zeleen Varnaster:** Tense working relationship; Brewbarry views Zeleen as failing his duty to the mine.  
  - **Wererats of Phandalin:** Recently negotiated with; offered a tip and recommended the Falcon’s Lodge bathrobe.  
- **Current Motivation:** Secure a steady, legal revenue stream beyond the mine to fund his music studio.  
- **Recent Actions:**  
  - Killed the last stirge in the bathhouse, grinning under his helm.  
  - Declared “Cannonballed” and entered the warm bath, triggering a rage that transformed him into his giant form.  
  - Agreed to help rebuild the lodge and brewery after a failed Wisdom save, motivated by the promise of future beer.  
  - Drank the last surviving bottle of wine from the manse before combat.  
  - Identified and named magical items with precision (“Dust of Disappearance,” “Elemental Cleaver”).  

**Defining Recent Traits / Secrets:**  
- **Physical State:** Can grow to twice his size (15–30 feet) during rage, doubling his reach and enhancing strength.  
- **Mental State:** Haunted by exile from the Uthgardts — not for drunkenness, but for sobriety. He was blamed for the loss of a sacred artifact while sitting quietly, healed by a goodberry, and interpreted as negligent.  
- **Philosophy:** Rage is not mindless — “The rage is very clear. The rage is math.”  
- **Secret:** He believes he is not a barbarian, but a nomad — a distinction he insists on despite others’ labels.  
- **Quirks:**  
  - Obsessed with alcohol (beer, wine) — grief over lost brew is visceral and profound.  
  - Enjoys soft things (bathrobe) and is deeply moved by music.  
  - Has three names: Brewbarry Root Smasher Ogalakadu (family, nickname, clan).  
  - Refuses to touch cursed artifacts due to teachings from his village’s wise women.

- **Current location**: Last confirmed action is in the bathhouse in Phandalin, but no explicit statement confirms he hasn’t left since.  
- **Status of the music studio**: He wants to start one, and the mine shares are meant to fund it — but it is not yet built or owned. Is it a current project or a dream?  
- **Who holds the Dragon Slayer Sword now?** He took it in ch33, and there’s no later indication it was transferred — assumed current, but no explicit confirmation.  
- **Exact nature of his “giant form”**: Is it a racial trait, a rage expansion, or magical? Not fully defined — only described phenomenologically.  
- **The “Wednesday look”**: Is this a recurring expression or a one-time remark? No context given.  
- **Bathrobe ownership**: He returned it — does he still have access to it? Is it truly gone?  
- **The “voice in his head” during paralysis resistance** (“incorrect packaging”) — origin unknown. Is it the artifact’s influence, his trauma, or something else?  
- **Whether he still considers himself a “killer of ogres”**: That title was declared in ch03; later actions focus on dragons, anchorites, and bandits — is it a badge he still claims?  
- **The “something warm” in his chest** after the druid’s comment (ch38) — was it emotion, magic, or a reaction to Vukradin? Ambiguous.
