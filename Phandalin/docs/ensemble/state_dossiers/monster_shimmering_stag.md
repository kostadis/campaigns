### Shimmering Stag

- **Current status:** Alive  
- **Current location:** Center of the forest (the origin of its transformation)  
- **Allegiance/faction:** None; solitary entity, claims the surrounding area as its territory  
- **Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:**  
  - Capable of teleportation ("blink") up to 15 feet  
  - Antlers that constantly shift shape and are different each time observed  
- **Current assignment / role and key relationships:**  
  - Acts as territorial guardian of its forest area, demanding intruders leave  
  - Last interacted with the party; has spoken to other "nice people" who promised to "fix it" but have not returned  
- **Defining recent actions:**  
  - Materialized before the party and teleported 15 feet away  
  - Demanded the party exit its territory  
  - Asked if the party has anything to eat  
  - Described its transformation from a normal stag to one with unstable teleportation and ever-changing antlers  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- The unnatural changes began in the center of the forest and have spread, warping the entire woodland  
- The stag is distressed by its condition — its antlers hinder movement, and it longs for its former simple life of walking, prancing, and eating vegetables  
- It believes others (unnamed) once offered to restore it but failed to follow through  

## Uncertainty
- Who are the "other nice people" who promised to fix it? (No names or identifiers given)  
- Did the stag’s teleportation ability manifest suddenly, or has it been growing? (Facts confirm it now can "blink," but not how or when it gained the ability)  
- Is the stag’s current location definitively the center of the forest, or is it merely where the changes began? (It says the changes started there, but does not confirm it is still there)  
- Are the antlers’ changes random, magical, or caused by an external force? (Cause is implied to be the forest’s weirdness, but not confirmed)
