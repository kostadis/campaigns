## Identity
*Also known as: Xanth*

### Xanth

**Current status:** Alive  
**Current location:** Bend of the Triboar Trail toward Phandalin  
**Allegiance/faction:** Independent centaur; formerly associated with the Emerald Enclave (though critical of its internal divisions); no allegiance to Talos or any cult  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:**  
- Wields a longbow (used in ch40 to fire two high shots into the trees)  
- Carries Meryl’s staff (recognized it as such, noted its diminished leaves, and acknowledged its blessing by Meryl)  
- Healed of prior wounds sustained in ch33 (confirmed by ch34: “You have healed me!”)  
- No indication of spells or feats beyond natural centaur abilities and forest awareness  

**Current assignment / role and key relationships:**  
- Acting as guide and ally to the party, specifically tasked with leading them to the lair of the Talosian half-orc spellcasters  
- Reports to no one; acts on personal conviction and loyalty to Meryl’s memory  
- Key relationships:  
  - Meryl (deceased, but deeply revered; Xanth calls her an old friend and believes she entrusted Soma with the staff)  
  - Soma (trusted ally; praised him as “superb,” expressed emotional relief at his presence, and touched his shell in a gesture of blessing)  
  - Vukradin (recognized as Vucravinios; finds his refusal to claim the dragon’s hoard bewildering)  
  - The Carver’s lieutenants (Borg, Sister Kaella, Zax, Vukradin — named as such; implies prior knowledge of their roles, but no current alliance)  

**Defining recent actions:**  
- Responded to the party’s *Sending* spell with compressed words, directing them to the Triboar Trail bend toward Phandalin  
- Met the party at the designated location and confirmed the lair is “another day’s hike away”  
- Recognized Meryl’s staff and affirmed its blessing by Meryl, declaring to Soma: “This one, this one was blessed by Meril, and I know that she will do great things. Peace, Beauty.”  
- Fired two longbow shots into the trees during a tense moment (ch40), likely signaling or distracting unseen threats  
- Reaffirmed his stance against Talos as a “lunatic god” and described the cult as a self-destructive disease  
- Expressed deep awareness of the forest’s suffering, noting it “felt watched” and “was still hurting” after the white dragon disrupted its balance  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Believes the forest is a living, sentient entity that remembers trauma — its current unease stems from the white dragon’s incursion and the Talosian orcs’ subsequent radicalization  
- Holds Meryl in profound reverence; believes she understood the staff’s significance and entrusted it to Soma for a purpose  
- Rejects the Emerald Enclave’s ideological divisions about orcs, seeing their transformation under Talos as a clear corruption, not a naturalist or interventionist debate  
- Knows the Chosen of Talos is not in the forest — not because he lacks knowledge, but because he would recognize the Chosen if he were there  
- Secretly understands the staff’s history beyond Soma’s comprehension — his recognition of it is not merely academic, but emotional and ancestral
