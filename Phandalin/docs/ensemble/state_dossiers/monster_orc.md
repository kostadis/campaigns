### orc

**Current status:** Alive (some), Dead (many)  
**Current location:** Unknown; last observed in combat on the opposite ridge of a gully, near the Anchorite’s site.  
**Allegiance/faction:** Independent orc raiding party (per ch19); some may be neutral or uncommitted (ch40).  
**Current possessions / notable items:**  
- Necklace of bones (one orc, ch19)  
- Greataxe (one orc, ch40)  
- Javelins (commonly used; multiple orcs carried and threw them)  
**Notable spells, feats, wounds:**  
- One orc was charmed by Vukradin’s magic (ch19)  
- One orc died from Vukradin’s magic and Valphine’s strike (ch19)  
- Multiple orcs consumed by celestial fire when approaching Valphine (ch30)  
- Multiple orcs slain by spectral guardians (ch29) and Brewbarry (ch30)  
**Current assignment / role and key relationships:**  
- Independent raiders, not serving any master (ch19)  
- One orc claimed the scout as his brother’s nephew (ch19)  
- Three orcs paused and listened to Vukradin’s speech, indicating potential non-hostility or internal division (ch40)  
- One orc killed an Anchorite and shouted in orcish about the gods and the sun (ch43)  
**Defining recent actions:**  
- Engaged in coordinated ambushes and frontal assaults (ch07, ch30)  
- Used javelins extensively, often from range (ch02, ch29, ch40)  
- One orc fled upon recognizing Valphine as Lolth’s incarnate (ch19)  
- Multiple orcs were slain by divine fire when charging Valphine (ch30)  
- One orc, possibly neutral, attacked Soma’s elk form (ch40)  
- One orc killed an Anchorite and invoked divine/solar themes (ch43)  
**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- View themselves as independent, not bound to larger factions (ch19)  
- Hold familial ties among members (ch19)  
- May hold religious or spiritual beliefs tied to gods and the sun (ch43)  
- Some exhibit hesitation or responsiveness to speech, suggesting capacity for reason beyond brute aggression (ch40)
