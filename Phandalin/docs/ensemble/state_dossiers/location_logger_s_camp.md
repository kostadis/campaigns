### Logger’s Camp

- **Current status:** Abandoned but recently occupied (last known use: party’s gathering)  
- **Current location:** Along the south shore of the river  
- **Current condition:** Eerily silent and completely deserted; no living occupants observed  
- **Notable features:**  
  - A dozen tents arranged on a sandy beach  
  - A cabin with logs stacked under its awning  
  - Stone chimneys and foundations of older, torn-down cabins  
- **Current assignment / role:** None — no active operations; last known use was as a logistical staging point for logging, now unused  
- **Defining recent actions:** The party arrived at the camp and found it deserted; later, they gathered there to discuss their next move
