### King Gnercli

**Current status:** Alive  
**Current location:** Unknown (last seen in his royal chamber, issuing commands to Vukradin)  
**Allegiance/faction:** Co-King of a dual monarchy  
**Current possessions / notable items:** None explicitly stated beyond having granted a magical hat to Vukradin — possession of the hat is no longer his.  
**Current assignment / role:** Co-ruler of the kingdom; responsible for coordinating defense against the shapeshifter threat and delegating missions to adventurers.  
**Key relationships:** Rules jointly with another king; last seen interacting with Vukradin, whom he directly commissioned and equipped with a magical hat.  
**Defining recent actions:**  
- Declared himself “the king” in opposition to the dual-monarchy structure, contradicting prior statements.  
- Delivered a magical hat to Vukradin and ordered him to leave.  
- Reaffirmed the kingdom’s foundational principle: dual kingship to prevent autocratic vulnerability to shapeshifters.  

**Revealed motivations / secrets:**  
- The dual monarchy exists specifically to prevent a single ruler from being subverted by a shapeshifter.  
- He rejects autocratic rule, even at the cost of bureaucratic friction.  
- His recent declaration “I am the king” suggests internal conflict or a possible shift in stance — though he simultaneously affirms the dual system.
