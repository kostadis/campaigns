### Petunia's Cheese

- **Current holder/location**: In the possession of Zeleen, who recently offered a piece to Vukradin as a gesture of goodwill after negotiation.  
- **Current condition**: Intact, fresh, and ready for consumption.  
- **Notable properties**:  
  - Described as the best cheese in the entire area.  
  - Unique and delicious.  
  - Texture almost like Gouda but better.  
  - Organic and fair trade, sourced from the happiest cows.  
- **Significance**: A local delicacy known for its exceptional quality; used as a diplomatic offering to build trust.

None.
