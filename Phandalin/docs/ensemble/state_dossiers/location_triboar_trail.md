### Triboar Trail

A forested road that bends toward Phandalin, currently serving as the designated meeting point for the party and Xanth the Centaur. The trail is less secure than the High Road but can be traversed safely by projecting an appearance of toughness or desperation. It is notably devoid of travelers and corpses, a condition observed during recent passage. The party crossed this trail while en route to Dragonbarrow and encountered The Falcon, owner of Falcon’s Hunting Lodge, at that location.

## Uncertainty
- The exact geographic position of “the bend toward Phandalin” is not described beyond its directional relation to Phandalin and its association with the meeting with Xanth — no landmarks or distances are provided.
- Whether the absence of travelers or corpses is still ongoing (as of ch39) is implied but not explicitly confirmed; ch39 does not restate this observation, so its current status is inferred from ch07.
- The Falcon’s presence on the trail is recorded in ch31, but it is unclear whether he still operates from or patrols that stretch of the trail in ch39.
