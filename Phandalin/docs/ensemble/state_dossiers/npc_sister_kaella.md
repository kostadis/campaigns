## Identity
*Also known as: Kaella, Sister Kayla, Sister Kella*

### Sister Kaella

**Current status:** Alive  
**Current location:** Unknown (said to have “gone to the wind”)  
**Allegiance/faction:** Former lieutenant of the Carver; no longer loyal. Now operates independently, possibly as a defector or informant.  
**Current possessions / notable items:** None specified.  
**Current assignment / role:** Unknown. Last known action: warning the party of the Carver’s plan to bind the white worm at Icespire Hold and burn Phandalin. Now believed to be in hiding or on the move.  
**Key relationships:**  
- Formerly reported to the Carver as one of his lieutenants (alongside Borg and Zax).  
- Was captured and interrogated by Valphine.  
- Expresses gratitude and loyalty toward Valphine, calling her “the only one who showed up.”  
- Acknowledges that her survival now depends on the Carver’s death.  
- Vukradin references her as a living failsafe — “as long as Sister Kaella’s still breathing, you’re good” — implying her survival is tied to the party’s mission or a magical safeguard.  

**Defining recent actions:**  
- Warned the party of the Carver’s plan to possess the white worm at Icespire Hold and wear the dragon as armor to burn Phandalin.  
- After being knocked unconscious by Soma and Brewbarry and paralyzed by Vukradin, she later awoke and revealed her defection: she was never strictly loyal to the Carver, and her life expectancy became contingent on his death after being accused of intelligence failures.  
- Expressed genuine surprise and gratitude toward Valphine, and had already mourned the outcome where Jax won.  
- Has since vanished — “gone to the wind.”

## Uncertainty
- Contradiction between [ch44] (“Sister Kaella herself is said to have ‘gone to the wind’”) and [ch45] (Vukradin says “as long as Sister Kaella’s still breathing, you’re good”) — implies she is alive but unlocated; however, “gone to the wind” suggests disappearance or death. Is she in hiding, in exile, or presumed dead by some?  
- Unclear if Vukradin’s reference to her as a “failsafe” means she is physically present with the party, or if her survival is magically or magically monitored from afar.  
- No confirmation of whether she retains any equipment, spells, or items from her time as a lieutenant.  
- The exact nature of her defection — whether she actively sabotaged the Carver or merely withheld information — is implied but not detailed.  
- Whether she is still in contact with Valphine or the party is unknown.
