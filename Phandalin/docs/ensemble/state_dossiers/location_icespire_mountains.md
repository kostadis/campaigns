### Icespire Mountains

The Icespire Mountains are currently occupied by a white dragon that has displaced the orcs. The dragon descended into the mountains in Chapter 34 and now holds territory there. Lady Alagondar’s ghost, observed in Chapter 33, stared toward the mountains, indirectly hinting at the dragon’s presence, but no further connection between the ghost and the dragon is established.

## Uncertainty
- The ghost’s connection to the dragon is implied but not confirmed — is the ghost tied to the dragon, the orcs, or the mountains themselves?  
- The exact location of the dragon within the mountains is unspecified.  
- The current status of the displaced orcs (alive, dead, fled, subjugated) is not stated.
