### Rift Weavers

**Current status:** Extinct  
**Current location:** None (all destroyed or vanished)  
**Allegiance/faction:** None (bound to the malfunctioning planar device)  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:** None  
**Current assignment / role and key relationships:** Formerly guarded the malfunctioning planar device; no longer active.  
**Defining recent actions:** All six Rift Weavers were present guarding the planar device. Upon its destruction, the last remaining one winked out of existence. The others were destroyed by combat, dissolving into crackling sparkles. Prior to their destruction, they recoiled violently from radiant energy and were deeply disturbed by magical fire. In their final moments, they were confused, torn between attacking Brewbarry and the device itself.
