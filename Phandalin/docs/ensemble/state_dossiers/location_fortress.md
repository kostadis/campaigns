### Fortress

- **Current state:** Operational, occupied by bandits.
- **Location:** Unspecified, but associated with the site where the Carver performed a ritual (Ch35) and currently contains a corridor where bandits are actively patrolling (Ch36).
- **Current occupants:** Bandits (actively moving through corridors, engaging in skirmishes).
- **Notable features:** Contains a corridor used for ambushes and movement; site of a recent skirmish with Vukradin’s party.
- **Significance:** Previously the site of a ritual performed by the Carver, during which Cryovain was held and hollowed out. The ritual’s aftermath may still linger, though no current magical activity is confirmed.

## Uncertainty
- The exact geographic location of the fortress is unknown.
- It is unclear whether the Carver or Cryovain are still present within the fortress — only that the ritual occurred there in the past.
- No confirmation whether the fortress is under the control of the bandits permanently or if they are merely transient intruders.
- The structural condition of the fortress beyond the corridor is unreported.
