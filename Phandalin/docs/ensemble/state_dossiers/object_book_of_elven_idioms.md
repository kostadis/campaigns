- The exact moment the Dust of Disappearance was placed inside the book is not specified — it may have been there before Valphine took it or inserted by her.

- The phrase “the book had a hole cut into its heart, and now so did my secret…” implies a metaphorical parallel, but the physical hole’s purpose or creation is not explicitly attributed to Valphine or another actor.
