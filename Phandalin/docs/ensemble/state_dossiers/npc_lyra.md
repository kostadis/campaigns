### Lyra

**Current status:** Alive  
**Current location:** Stonehill tavern, Phandalin  
**Allegiance/faction:** Emerald Enclave (low-ranking member)  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:** None explicitly stated. Previously used four crude stones in a ritual; no current inventory noted.  
**Current assignment / role and key relationships:** Acting as a liaison and advisor following the collapse of the intervention at the Whispering Grove. Reunited with two rescued Emerald Enclave members. Reports to the Enclave council. Recently formed a working rapport with Vukradin, whom she credits for defusing Adabra’s attack. Offers assistance to the party in contacting Xanth the Centaur.  
**Defining recent actions:**  
- Publicly credited Vukradin for brilliant oratory that neutralized Adabra’s accusations before the Enclave council.  
- Entered the Stonehill glowing with renewed energy, dissolving the room’s tension.  
- Offered to help the party contact Xanth the Centaur.  
- Ordered two beers for Brewbarry with clear, emphatic intent.  
- Declared the planar corruption a spreading sickness and confirmed the death of the Whispering Grove’s Treant during a failed ritual.  
- Expressed no shame or defiance about the failure, stating only: *“this is what happened.”*  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Once an interventionist who believed planar corruption must be actively fought, but now accepts the failure of her mission with quiet realism.  
- Had a deep personal affection for the Whispering Grove, now lost.  
- No longer leads or commands; operates as a peer and advisor, having internalized her past failures.  
- Still retains a pragmatic, grounded perspective — skeptical of overuse of magic (e.g., horrified by frequent Sending spells), yet willing to act decisively to support allies.
