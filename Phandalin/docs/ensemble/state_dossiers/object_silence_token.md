### Silence Token

- **Type**: Magical object (temporary effect marker)  
- **Current location**: On the map, marking the area of effect for Valphine’s hush enchantment  
- **Current condition**: Active and visible as a magical marker  
- **Perceived appearance**:  
  - Soma perceives it as a purple circle  
  - Vukradin perceives it as a yellow dot  
  - Others (unspecified) do not see it at all  
- **Function**: Indicates the planned and active area of effect for Valphine’s hush enchantment  

## Uncertainty
- Contradictory perception descriptions: One quote says Soma saw a “yellow circle,” another says “purple circle.” The latter (“purple circle”) appears later in the same chapter and is likely the correct current perception, but the source text is inconsistent.  
- Unclear if the token is physically placed or purely illusory — described as “placed on the map” but perceived differently by different observers, suggesting it may be a magical overlay rather than a physical object.  
- Unknown who else, if anyone, can perceive the token beyond Soma and Vukradin.
