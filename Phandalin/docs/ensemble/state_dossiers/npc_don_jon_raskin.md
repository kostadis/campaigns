### Don-Jon Raskin

**Current status:** Dead  
**Last known location:** Mountain’s Toe Gold Mine  
**Allegiance/faction:** Formerly hired by a Neverwinter consortium (with Kraken Society ties); later negotiated directly with local dwarves  
**Current possessions / notable items:** None specified  
**Spells, feats, wounds:** None specified

**Key relationships:**

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**

- Motivated by cost-cutting and exploitation — sought to replace skilled dwarves with cheap, expendable wererat labor  
- Fabricated stories to manipulate both employers and workers
