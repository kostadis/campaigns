### Vial

- **Current location:** In the inner pocket of Vukradin’s pack.  
- **Current condition:** Sealed with an unopened stopper; dense, polished steel-colored liquid inside.  
- **Current magical property:** Contains deep, defensive magic that settles around its carrier like a second skin.  
- **Current holder:** Vukradin (secretly placed in his pack).  
- **Notable recent action:** Placed into Vukradin’s pack by an unnamed individual (likely the narrator of ch41) after being discovered in a hidden compartment in the third cave.  

## Uncertainty
- The identity of the person who placed the vial into Vukradin’s pack is not named — only “I” is referenced, but no confirmation of who “I” is.  
- The vial’s origin or creator is unknown — Adabra’s earlier vial (ch05) had scent and taste and was a healing potion; this vial is steel-colored, unopened, and has defensive magic — no evidence links them as the same object.  
- Whether the vial was ever used or opened prior to being found in the cave is unclear, but the stopper is confirmed unopened at time of discovery.  
- The vial’s exact name, purpose beyond “defensive magic,” or whether it is one-of-a-kind is not specified.
