### Waterfall Pool

- **Current state:** A shallow, mist-shrouded pool formed by the erosive plunge of a waterfall into a natural rock concavity.
- **Location:** At the end of a river, enclosed by a surrounding mountain that blocks all sunlight.
- **Notable features:** 
  - Two small islands rising from the pool, covered in two-foot-tall red, green, and purple mushrooms.
  - Rocky ledges twenty to thirty feet above the pool, featuring multiple cave openings that overlook the water.
- **Environmental condition:** Permanently shadowed; receives no direct or indirect sunlight due to the enclosing mountain.

## Uncertainty
None.
