### Shield

- **Type:** Object  
- **Current Location:** In possession of Valphine (last handled by her in ch42)  
- **Current Condition:**  
  - Made of dark oak with iron bands; surface features a druidic carving of stones in rings beneath a hammered iron disc nailed over the center.  
  - The iron disc bears Talos’s bolt — now loose and popped free (retrieved by Valphine).  
  - Original druidic carving underneath the iron disc remains un-sanded, still perceptible by touch.  
  - Inside the rim: a half-orc tally with four notches and a slash.  
  - Leather straps inside are newer than the wood by at least a generation; smell of pine tar and wet dog.  
- **Notable Features:**  
  - The shield bears a half-orc tally (five somethings total: four notches + one slash, possibly representing kills or storms weathered).  
  - The iron disc and bolt were added later, covering but not erasing the original druidic work.  
  - Straps replaced in the last generation, suggesting recent reuse or repair.  

## Uncertainty
- The exact meaning of the half-orc tally (kills vs. storms weathered) is ambiguous.  
- It is unclear whether the shield is currently held by Valphine, carried by someone else, or left behind after she removed the bolt — no later chapter confirms its current holder.  
- The origin of the leather straps (who replaced them and why) is unexplained.  
- The shield was unassigned in ch41; it is not stated whether it was claimed, assigned, or taken after ch42.
