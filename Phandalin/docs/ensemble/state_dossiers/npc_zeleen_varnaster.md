## Identity
*Also known as: Zeleen*

### Zeleen Varnaster

**Current status:** Alive  
**Current location:** Outside the Mountain's Toe Mine  
**Allegiance/faction:** Leader of the Whiskered Gang (wererats)  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:** None specified  
**Current assignment / role and key relationships:**  
- Officially hired as protector of the Mountain’s Toe Mine by Valphine (referred to as “Mr. Boss” or “Boss”)  
- Reports directly to Valphine; acts as her enforcer and mine overseer  
- Leads a gang of wererats who now view the mine as their home and family  
- Previously displaced from the Shrine of Savras; now firmly entrenched at the mine  
- Recently secured protection contract with Valphine for free after arguing the mine is their new home  

**Defining recent actions:**  
- Successfully negotiated free protection of the mine by framing it as defense of their new home  
- Deceived Valphine into believing the dwarves are still actively mining (“They’ve been working really hard”) despite the mine being silent  
- Avoided direct admission of involvement in Don-jon Raskin’s death, calling it “conveniently lured to his death”  
- Provided intelligence on the dragon, orcs, and ogres in the region, and suggested the Shrine of Savras as a source of dragon lore  
- Refused to undertake the “fetch quest” for the Earthstone Gem, citing their role as protectors — but relented under pressure, acknowledging the plan could “make that happen”  
- Maintained a performative, optimistic demeanor around Valphine, masking underlying tension and failure  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Values survival and personal safety over loyalty to past homes (refused to reclaim the Shrine of Savras)  
- Uses intimidation and manipulation to maintain control over his gang and negotiate with outsiders  
- Secretly responsible for Don-jon Raskin’s death, though he avoids direct culpability  
- Has a strong aversion to being treated as a stereotype (offended by cheese-for-labor proposition)  
- Possesses keen perception — saw through Vukradin’s deception about dragons polymorphing with a natural 20
