### out-of-phase dwarves

**Current status:** Alive (active, hostile entities)  
**Current location:** Axelholm (main hall and surrounding areas, materializing from unnatural light)  
**Allegiance/faction:** None (non-sentient, transformed remnants of Axelholm’s dwarven inhabitants)  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:**  
- Gold coins embedded in their flesh  
- Mouths sewn shut with golden wire or red tape  
- Parchment-like skin covered in shifting ink  
**Current assignment / role and key relationships:**  
- Act as hostile, phase-shifting defenders of Axelholm; advance relentlessly toward intruders  
- No known allegiance or command structure; behavior driven by fractured, obsessive fixation on efficiency  
**Defining recent actions:**  
- Emerged from multiple doors in Axelholm’s main hall  
- Materialized from unnatural light during combat to reinforce their numbers  
- Advanced on the party while phasing in and out of reality  
- Exhibited limbs multiplying to appear as six hands and six legs  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Minds are broken by an unknown transformation; constant muttering centers on “efficiency”  
- Physical anomalies (phasing, limb multiplication, ink skin, sewn mouths) suggest a magical or dimensional corruption tied to Axelholm’s unnatural light
