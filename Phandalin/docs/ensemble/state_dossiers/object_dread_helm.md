### Dread Helm

- **Current condition:** Warm to the touch; emits a red glow from the wearer’s eyes during combat readiness.  
- **Notable properties:** Causes the wearer’s eyes to glow red when combat-ready; previously belonged to the deceased chief accountant.  
- **Current possession:** Actively worn and functional; not stored or inert.
