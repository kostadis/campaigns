### Aletra

**Current status:** Alive  
**Current location:** Unknown (escaped after the collapse of the interdimensional device)  
**Allegiance/faction:** House Sartorra (drow poison cartel); answers to “the boss”  
**Current possessions / notable items:**  
- Box containing vials of interdimensional poison (“Midnight Tears”)  
- High-collared drow uniform  
- Rapier  
- Glowing keypad interface (likely part of or linked to the collapsed machine)  
- Small portal-activation device  
- Wand (used to inflict red-glow curse on Brewbarry)  
- High-quality silk bedroll  
- Folding desk made of black Zurkhwood  
- Personal notes in the Operator’s Guide to the Model 1489 Spatial Rectification Calliope: *“use only if KP isn't watching.”*  
- Forged shipping documents: crossed out “iron for 500 pounds”; annotated “Midnight Tears - crate 4 - do not drop.”  
- Scent signature: faint, sickly sweet mixture of lavender perfume and chemical bleach  

**Current assignment / role:** Smuggling interdimensional poison via planar machinery; extracting and securing illicit cargo from unstable devices.  
**Key relationships:**  
- Sister to Valphine Satora (hostile, with history of manipulation and betrayal)  
- Reports to an unnamed superior referred to as “the boss”  
- Previously worked with (and possibly manipulated) Valphine in past traps (e.g., stuffed creature bait)  

**Defining recent actions:**  
- Successfully extracted a box of interdimensional poison (“Midnight Tears”) from the broken planar machine.  
- Warned the party that failure to retrieve the package meant leaving the machine active and catastrophic.  
- Pointed wand at Brewbarry, inducing an ominous red glow (likely a tracking or targeting curse).  
- Pressed a device to open a shimmering portal, presumably for escape.  
- Forged official documents to conceal the true nature of her cargo.  
- Left personal operational notes indicating awareness of surveillance (“KP isn't watching”).  
- Escaped immediately after the machine collapsed into the void.  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Motivated by profit, not honor; has a history of exposing traitors for personal gain.  
- Enjoys the danger of combat (“enjoyed being the target of pointy tools”).  
- Views Valphine with condescension and a twisted sense of familiarity, referencing past encounters where Valphine was incapacitated or emotionally broken.  
- Believes herself superior to others (“handling the situation while others are unfit to understand”).  
- Operates with cold pragmatism: admits she “couldn’t leave any evidence behind.”  
- Has access to high-end drow equipment and clandestine logistics (Zurkhwood desk, silk bedroll, forged documents).
