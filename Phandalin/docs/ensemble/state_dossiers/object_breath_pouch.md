### breath pouch

- **Current holder/location:** In Soma’s possession (extracted from Cryovain’s throat in chapter 37).  
- **Current condition:** Swollen, heavy for its size, actively emitting an ancient, non-thermal cold.  
- **Significance:** Crafted from the same cold that was originally intended to kill the speaker; repurposed as a tool.
