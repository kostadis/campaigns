### Gorthok

**Current status:** Alive  
**Current location:** Outside the former position of the Moonbeam spell, at the heart of the lodge courtyard  
**Allegiance/faction:** Independent; not charmed, not under any known control  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:** None explicitly carried or possessed. Notable abilities:  
- Reflexive lightning generation (not a spell; immune to silence)  
- Immunity to hypnotic enchantments  
- Immunity to Moonbeam radiance (sheds it like a stone wall absorbs a thrown cup)  
- Massive physical strength (cracked lodge gate like kindling)  
- Tusks: pale blue-white, like the interior of a storm cloud  
- Flanks sheened in static electricity  
- Emits smell of ozone, wet fur, and something older than either  

**Current assignment / role and key relationships:**  
- Acts as a major tactical threat among the six uncharmed orcs  
- Was summoned ahead of schedule from the Woodland Manse ritual  
- Last seen advancing and stopping just outside where the Moonbeam had been  
- No known allegiance to any leader or faction; operates autonomously  

**Defining recent actions:**  
- Attacked and killed Soma with twin tusk strikes  
- Struck the narrator twice with hooves  
- Survived direct exposure to the Moonbeam spell without effect  
- Broke the lodge gate open with brute force  
- Refused to be charmed or controlled by any known means  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Appears to have no interest in submission, servitude, or alliance (“absolutely no interest in becoming anyone's favorite”)  
- Its origin is tied to the Woodland Manse ritual, though it emerged prematurely and independently
