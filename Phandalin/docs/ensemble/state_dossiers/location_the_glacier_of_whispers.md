### The Glacier of Whispers

**Current status:** Active, tangible location  
**Current location:** Unspecified, but perceptible within the region where the party encountered it (ch34)  
**Current state:** A massive formation of violet ice, internally containing dozens of faces pressed against its inner surface — mouths open, eyes wide, screaming in absolute silence.  
**Notable properties:** Emits a psychic frequency perceived as pressure behind the eyes; this frequency is unnatural and not attributable to cold alone. The whispering resonance is anomalous, defying known physical laws of glacial environments.  
**Control/occupation:** No known occupants or controllers; appears to be a self-contained, sentient-like phenomenon.  
**Defining recent action:** Manifested its presence to approaching observers through auditory and psychic distortion prior to visual confirmation, inducing immediate sensory unease.

None.
