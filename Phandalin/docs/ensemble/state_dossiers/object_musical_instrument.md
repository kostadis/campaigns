### Musical Instrument

- **Current holder/location**: Unspecified; last known to be in the northwest sarcophagus of Dragonbarrow tomb (ch31), but now implied to be a potential gift from Falcon to Vukradin (ch38).  
- **Current condition**: Unknown; described as capable of being “of real value” (ch38), suggesting it is not merely a generic artifact but one of notable worth.  
- **Significance**: Recognized as an item worthy of appeasing a bard (Vukradin) due to his vanity and skill; implies it is a functional, high-quality instrument, likely a lute, harp, or similar bardic tool.

## Uncertainty
- The instrument from the sarcophagus (ch31) has not been confirmed to have been removed or transferred; it may still be in the tomb, or may have been taken and is now the “musical instrument of real value” referenced in ch38 — but this is not stated.  
- It is unclear whether the instrument Valphine suggests Falcon give Vukradin is the same one from the sarcophagus or a different one.  
- No specific type, name, or physical description of the instrument is provided.
