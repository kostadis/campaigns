### Sending Stones

- **Current holder/location:** Vukradin  
- **Current condition:** Capable of only a single short message  
- **Significance:** Magical communication devices of notable monetary value; limited to one-use transmission  

## Uncertainty
- The sending stones offered by Dazlyn in chapter 2 are never confirmed to have been transferred to Vukradin; it is unclear if these are the same stones or different ones.  
- It is unknown whether the stones Vukradin possesses are the same ones Dazlyn offered — no direct link is established.
