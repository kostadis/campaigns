### The Mountain Pass

- **Current state:** Active, sentient-appearing terrain, hostile to intruders.  
- **Location:** Along The Carver’s path, a narrow and treacherous passage through the mountains.  
- **Notable features:**  
  - Rocks along The Carver’s path phase in and out of existence unpredictably.  
  - The pass narrows progressively, as if actively attempting to expel travelers.  
- **Behavior / Sentience:** Exhibits apparent hostility — it "wanted fewer people in it, ideally none."  
- **Control / Occupation:** Unclaimed; no known occupants or rulers. Functions as an autonomous, antagonistic landscape feature.  

## Uncertainty
None.
