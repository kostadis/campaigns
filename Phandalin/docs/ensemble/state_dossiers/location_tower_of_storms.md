### Tower of Storms

- **Current state:** Active, cursed, and occupied by the party after Moesko’s defeat.  
- **Location:** Perched on a rocky ledge, a grim sentinel overlooking the sea.  
- **Control/Occupation:** The party currently occupies the tower, having descended deeper after defeating Moesko.  
- **Current possessions / notable items:**  
  - A stone altar with lightning bolts carved into it.  
  - A metal lightning rod, integral to the curse tied to the god Talos.  
  - A beacon powered by a still-beating human heart — Moesko’s heart, now embedded in the beacon and pulsing with light.  
- **Notable features:**  
  - A harpy nest on a ledge outside.  
  - Visible shipwrecks below, observable through a dirty window.  
  - A barnacle-encrusted chair in the upper chamber (now abandoned).  
- **Current assignment / role:** Functions as a cursed nexus of divine malevolence — the lightning rod and heart-beacon sustain a dark ritual that draws storms and calamity.  
- **Defining recent actions:** After Moesko’s defeat, the party descended deeper into the tower, leaving the upper levels behind; the beacon continues to pulse with Moesko’s heart, and the curse remains active.
