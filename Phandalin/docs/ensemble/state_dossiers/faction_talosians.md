### Talosians

**Current status:** Active and advancing.  
**Current location:** Converging through a breached gate in the battlefield (per ch42).  
**Allegiance/faction:** Devotees of Talos; a theocratic militant faction.  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:** Not specified.  
**Current assignment / role and key relationships:** Part of an attacking force alongside Anchorites; actively recruiting orcs through terror and coercion. The anchorites are fanatics within the faction who revere cruelty and destruction.  
**Defining recent actions:**  
- Killed Prutha’s great-uncle to break clan resistance and enable orc recruitment.  
- Offered Prutha’s clan the ultimatum: “fight, or be what his great-uncle was.”  
- Converging on a breached gate as part of a coordinated assault.  
- Still unknown to Brewbarry, who anticipates imminent contact.  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Theology holds Lathander’s light as an affront.  
- Use targeted assassination and psychological terror as core recruitment tools.  
- Seek to transform or subjugate resistant groups into extensions of their own destructive ethos.
