### Barrel Room

The barrel room contains twelve forty-gallon barrels set into wide alcoves, each secured by a wooden brace. The south alcove’s barrels have been tapped with wooden spigots; two of these tapped barrels are half full, and two are nearly empty.

## Uncertainty
- Contradiction between “Two of those barrels are half full, and two are nearly empty.” and “Two of the tapped barrels in the barrel room are nearly empty.” — the latter does not specify whether the two nearly empty barrels are among the half-full pair or separate, creating ambiguity about total count of nearly empty tapped barrels. The former implies four tapped barrels total (two half-full, two nearly empty); the latter only confirms two nearly empty, without confirming if those are included in the four. Cannot resolve whether “two nearly empty” refers to a subset or the full set.
