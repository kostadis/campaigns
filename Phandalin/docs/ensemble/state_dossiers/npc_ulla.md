### Ulla

**Current status:** Alive  
**Current location:** Guard post within the mountain passageway (near the inventor workshop access route)  
**Allegiance/faction:** Gnome guard contingent of the mountain stronghold  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:** None specified  
**Current assignment / role and key relationships:**  
- Serves as a guard responsible for identifying and vetting travelers.  
- Reports to an unnamed command structure that previously issued a standing order to attack Uthgardt Barbarians — now in flux.  
- Last seen interacting with Pog (fellow gnome guard) and Vukradin.  
- Recognizes Vukradin’s need for aid against a dragon and directs him to the inventor workshop.  

**Defining recent actions:**

- Provided detailed directions to the inventor workshop to Vukradin, bypassing blade turnstiles.  
- Rejected the possibility that Vukradin is a shapechanger by citing the classic trope: “That’s what a shapechanger would say.”  

**Revealed motivations or secrets:**  
- Holds a strong cultural preference for Gnomish music.  
- Believes in the reliability of behavioral logic (“a shapechanger would deny it”) as a detection method.  
- Is part of a guard unit operating under unclear, possibly outdated orders — suggesting institutional uncertainty or bureaucratic decay.
