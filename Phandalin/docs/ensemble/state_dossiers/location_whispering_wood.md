### Whispering Wood

- **Current status**: Corrupted sacred grove, visibly ruined.
- **Current location**: One hour’s walk from Phandalin.
- **Current state**: The central sacred grove is destroyed; a dead treant lies with collapsed roots and a blackened crown. The surrounding forest shows no overt rot or blight, but carries a persistent magical disharmony — a discordant, unnatural energy that offends sensitive perceptions.
- **Notable features**: The trees still twist unnaturally, their leaves unnaturally bright or sickly pale. The magical blight is no longer manifest as visible decay but as an invisible, resonant dissonance in the natural order.
- **Key recent event**: The death of the treant, which caused the grove’s ruin and intensified the underlying corruption.

## Uncertainty
- The exact extent of the corruption beyond the sacred grove is unconfirmed — is the entire wood affected, or just the grove?
- It is unclear whether the “magical blight” from ch10 is the same phenomenon as the “disharmony” described in ch11, or if the blight has evolved into the current discordant energy.
- Attribution of the treant’s death is unknown — no cause or actor is named.
