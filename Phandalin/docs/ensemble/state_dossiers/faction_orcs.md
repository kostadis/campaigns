### Orcs

**Current status:** Active faction, fragmented.  
**Current location:** Primarily at Falcon’s Hunting Lodge (approx. 30 orcs), with at least three remaining there under Valphine’s authority.  
**Allegiance/faction:** Formerly tied to the Eastern Homeland Reclaiming effort; now divided.  
**Current possessions / notable items, spells, feats, wounds:** Unknown.  
**Current assignment / role and key relationships:**  
- Majority of orcs at Falcon’s Hunting Lodge are divided into charmed and uncharmed groups following an unknown magical influence (likely tied to the “it” referenced in ch43).  
- Three orcs have formally agreed to stay, work, and be useful under Valphine’s leadership.  
**Defining recent actions:**  
- Survived expulsion from Icespire by Cryovain.  
- Consolidated at Falcon’s Hunting Lodge with approximately 30 members.  
- Experienced internal division during a battle, with some orcs charmed and others not.  
- Three individuals accepted a new allegiance to Valphine, suggesting a shift from their prior Reclaiming goal.  

## Uncertainty
- The nature and source of the charm affecting some orcs is unspecified — “it” is not identified.  
- It is unclear whether the “Eastern Homeland Reclaiming” effort is still active or abandoned by the majority.  
- The current whereabouts of the non-charmed orcs and the 27 orcs not under Valphine’s authority are unknown.  
- It is ambiguous whether the three orcs who stayed with Valphine are charmed or uncharmed — no linkage is provided.  
- The total number of orcs is stated as ~30 at Falcon’s Hunting Lodge, but it is unknown if this includes all surviving orcs or only a faction.
