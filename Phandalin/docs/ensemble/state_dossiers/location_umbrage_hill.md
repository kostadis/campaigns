### Umbrage Hill

Umbrage Hill is a hill located a few miles south of Phandalin, atop which stands Adabra Gwynn’s stone windmill. The windmill is her residence, and the hill’s winds have visibly weathered her face. The group recently traveled to Umbrage Hill with the intent to rescue Adabra.
