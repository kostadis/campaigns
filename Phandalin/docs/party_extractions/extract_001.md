## Relationships & Decisions

**Vukardin**
- Formed working partnership with Dazlyn Grayshard and Norbus Ironrune; accepted sending stones as non-monetary payment to satisfy his ethical objection to plunder.
- Established rapport with Toblen Stonehill at the Stonehill Inn; plays popular music in exchange for meals and lodging negotiation.
- Openly antagonized Townmaster Harbin Wester by calling the adventurer system "glorified banditry"; relationship with the Townmaster is strained.
- Negotiated access to gnome caves through music; built goodwill with Pog, Ulla, Fibblestib, and Dabbledob.
- Accepted contract from King Gnercli to eliminate the shapeshifter in exchange for a Hat of Wizardry (and the 50gp Townmaster reward for any gnomish device).

**Valphine Sortorra**
- Actively observing Vukardin as a possible instrument of Lathander's will; her faith is being tested and shaped by his ethical behavior.
- Suspicious of all gnomes as potential shapechangers; successfully destabilized Pog and Ulla's trust in each other through accusation.
- Internally conflicted between Drow survival instincts (torture, assassination) and Lathander's doctrine; has not yet resolved this tension.

**Soma**
- Joined the party opportunistically on the road; relationship with party is functional but she privately questions Vukardin's judgment ("The kid is going to get us killed").
- Guilty of friendly-fire against Valphine; no indication this was addressed or reconciled within the party.

**Brewbarry**
- Personal loyalty to Vukardin rooted in appreciation of his music; this loyalty overrides tribal identity.
- Identified by gnome guards as a former Uthgardt barbarian; this distinction resolved a bureaucratic standoff in the party's favor.
