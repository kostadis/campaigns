**Soma**
- Formed a meaningful connection with Xanth, a centaur elder with ties to Meril. Xanth revealed Meril's Staff is powerful and not a simple walking stick; it previously had more leaves.

**Brewbarry**
- Shared the story of his exile with Vukradin: he was sober when the artifact was stolen, was falsely accused, and only began drinking after his banishment. Interpreted his own history as: *he became what the tribe accused him of being.*
