## Relationships & Decisions

**Vukradin**
- *Oath/Membership:* Accepted associate membership in the Order of the Gauntlet. This comes with an obligation: when the Order calls, he must respond as a brother.
- *Relationship formed:* Ser Kaelen Thorn — patron/recruiter; Vukradin views him with cautious optimism but scrutinized funding sources before agreeing.
- *Relationship deepened:* Adabra — she acted as his public champion; he clearly admires and is emotionally invested in her approval ("I love this woman").
- *Decision:* Chose to investigate and remove the curse on the lighthouse rather than destroy it, after the Townmaster reframed the problem.

**Valphine**
- *Relationship strained:* Adabra — Adabra visibly ignores Valphine, possibly due to her Drow heritage; Valphine is aware and suppresses her reaction ("If I were a cleric of Lolth… she would be dead").
- *Relationship forming:* Ser Kaelen Thorn — offered Order membership; Valphine declined without explanation, noting internally they would not work well together.
- *Decision:* Chose not to join the Order of the Gauntlet.

**Brewbarry**
- *Relationship hostile/unresolved:* Ser Kaelen Thorn — holds the Order of the Gauntlet responsible for killing members of his tribe; accepted a drink but did not reconcile.

**Soma**
- *Relationship noted:* Ser Kaelen Thorn explicitly excluded Soma from the Order of the Gauntlet invitation, citing philosophical incompatibility with her naturalist worldview.
