Kazneporium Ketternopappux, also known as KP,  - a short research note by Kostadinious the Sage a biographer

Although many scholars have assumed that the events of Barovia occurred before the events in the Drow conspiracy, this is wrong.KP began as a clever librarian in the Kron Hills. He ended up hooking up with Bayard, Rimardo, and others, which led him into a planar-spanning war with the Drow Empire.After the war and its successful conclusion, he went on the lecture circuit. 
After some time he learned of the Dark Realms and decided to explore Barovia where he was trapped. 

Kazneporium Ketternopappux, also known as KP, and his  post Barovia experience - a short research note by Kostadinious the Sage a biographer
After the incident in Barovia, KP decided to shift his research focus from exploring strange planar phenomena to studying trade routes. 
Unfortunately, his numerical methods required data, and there was none of the quality he needed to make progress in his analytical methods. 

The trading house of Valentine and Bonk, makers of bags of holding, has promised KP exclusive access to the data from their bags of holding - if he can solve the problem of entities being dumped into the astral plane.

Valentine and Bonk’s control of the artifact that allows them to create bags of holding has proven to be very lucrative until one of their traders saw an opportunity to sell bags of holding as a weapon.

Although it was very profitable, it also unleashed a large amount of MPE into the astral plane.,

The Mindflayers, using their ability to dominate, took advantage of that unexpected harvest of power to launch a surprise attack on a githyanki stronghold.

Vlaakith sent her legions to Sigil to demand the heads of Valentine and Bonk.

Fortunately for Valentine and Bonk, the mind flayers showed up for a different reason. They saw an opportunity to harvest MPE from planets and use those entities to wage war on the githyanki. They also saw it as a means to capture a new civilization.

Valentine and Bonk had met KP earlier on a consulting gig where he noted that the data on what was being traded in bags of holding would be invaluable to his research.

When the githyanki and the minelayers showed up at Sigil, KP was invited to the talks.

After much discussion, it was agreed that if KP could find a solution, Valentine and Bonk could retain their artifact.

KP offered the 1489 Export Regulation on Interplanar Storage Devices as a possible treaty. There was an agreement that such a treaty would be signed only if and when the Valentin and Bonk artifact was modified to produce ISDs that could only be used to carry materials per the export license. 
While KP is looking for the magical items that are required to modify Valentine and Bonk’s artifacts, the Mind Flayers and Githyanki are looking for Valentine and Bonk’s artifact and trying to find the same things that KP is looking for. 
On Faerun, a client of Valentine and Bonk, is the trading house of Lionel and Coster.

Orsik’s unauthorized use of bags of holding and the emergence of Bob has created a situation and an opportunity.

KP has discovered a magical device he needs, but he is uncertain how to get it from the hoard of a particular dragon.
Certain Mind Flayers also discovered the item in Iymrith’s lair. KP, using the magical artifact of Valentin and Bonk, trapped them on Toril. 

KP was very impressed with Unla Kee's capture of the artifact from Iymrith’s lair and decided it was worthy of being a legendary magical item. He gave Unla Kee another one, not the staff of power that he used to end Lolth's reign.
It was his way of saying 'bravo.'While KP was orchestrating the seizure of the artifact, KP researched the business of Valentin and Bonk and was startled to learn how they provided interplanar gates for Lionshield and Coster to transport their goods through Toril. 

KP, seeing how that could improve the efficiency of trade through Toril and the North, decided to leverage his arcane knowledge to engage in long-term experiments and "cosmic engineering" to "streamline interplanar routes" and "perfect interplanar efficiency.” 

Unfortunately, his experiments coincided with the work by Gromph Baenre, which twisted the boundaries of the planes and had the side effect of freeing Lolth from her prison. This has created a variety of disturbances on Toril. Normally, he would deal with those problems directly, but his work on Valentin and Bonk requires his attention to be focused elsewhere. 
He has sent a Drow named Aletra Sartorra to deal with the problem.
