# Dragon of Icespire Peak — module proper-noun inventory

Complete inventory of NPCs, locations, items, factions, and concepts
extracted from the D&D Essentials Kit: Dragon of Icespire Peak source markdown.

This is the **source-material list**, not any homebrew adaptation.
For campaign-specific NPC adjustments see relevant notes files.

## Deities

- **Abbathor** — Evil dwarven god of greed; punished his temple's priests with an earthquake and avalanche for insufficient sacrifices
- **Chauntea** — Goddess of agriculture; Adabra Gwynn is her acolyte at Umbrage Hill
- **Corellon Larethian** — Elven god who half-blinded Gruumsh with an arrow
- **Gruumsh** — Mightiest god of the orc pantheon, half-blinded by Corellon Larethian
- **Moradin** — Dwarven god of creation; his holy symbol (hammer and anvil) is found as a platinum amulet in the former shrine
- **Savras** — God of divination and fate; has an abandoned shrine south of Conyberry
- **Talos** — Deity of storms and destruction; the anchorites of Talos serve him and resent loggers encroaching on Neverwinter Wood
- **Tiamat** — Queen of Evil Dragons, trapped in the Nine Hells; antagonist of Hoard of the Dragon Queen / Rise of Tiamat
- **Tymora** — Goddess of luck and good fortune; the Shrine of Luck in Phandalin is dedicated to her
