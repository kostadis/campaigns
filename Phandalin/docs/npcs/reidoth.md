# Reidoth

## Identity
- Nominal head of the local Emerald Enclave chapter
- Has not appeared in person; known only through discussion and indirect contact

## Personality & Motivations
- Regarded as a stabilizing presence within the Enclave — someone whose mere attendance at council would alter the dynamics of what others could accomplish.
- His core drives are not yet directly demonstrated, but he is clearly positioned as a counterweight to Adabra's faction.

## History with the Party
- **Council session (absent):** Adabra deliberately called a council meeting during Reidoth's absence, calculating that he was the person most likely to complicate her majority. Thomas noted Reidoth could not have procedurally blocked Adabra from speaking, but his physical presence would have changed the texture of what she could do.
- **Post-council contact:** After leaving the council, Vukradin used a *Sending* spell to contact Reidoth, informing him of the schism within the Enclave, Adabra's faction, Falcon's confession, and the council's turn against the party's interests. The party had discussed reaching out to Reidoth earlier and kept it as a reserve option before committing.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Unknown; away from the Enclave at the time of the council.
- **Active plans:** None known. He has now received Vukradin's *Sending* and is aware of the schism, Adabra's maneuvering, and Falcon's confession.
- **Party knowledge vs. hidden:** The party knows Reidoth is the chapter head and a potential ally against Adabra's faction. They do not yet know his reaction to the *Sending*, his current location, or when he might return.

## Relationships
- **Adabra:** Adversarial or at minimum a check on her power — she specifically engineered his absence to advance her agenda.
- **Vukradin:** Direct contact via *Sending*; Vukradin chose to trust him with sensitive intelligence about the internal Enclave schism.
- **Falcon:** Reidoth has been informed of Falcon's confession, though the nature of their prior relationship is unclear.
- **The Party:** Viewed as a potential ally and counterbalance; kept "in reserve" as an option.

## Arc Score Events
- No direct arc score changes noted, but the *Sending* from Vukradin — disclosing the schism, Adabra's faction, and Falcon's confession — is a significant information event likely to prompt future developments.
