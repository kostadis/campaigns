# Townmaster Harbin Wester

## Identity
- **Role:** Civil authority (Townmaster) of Phandalin
- **Faction:** Aligned with Lord Neverember's administration
- **First Appearance:** Phandalin; met the party when they arrived in town, likely to report on or receive their assignment

## Personality & Motivations
- Harbin is cautious, bureaucratic, and visibly out of his depth — his instinct is to defer, deflect, and manage expectations rather than lead. He is cash-poor and politically dependent on Lord Neverember's goodwill.
- His primary motivation is keeping Phandalin stable and himself in Neverember's good graces. He is not corrupt, but he is self-protective and quick to point out rules and standing arrangements that spare him from having to spend money.

## History with the Party
- **Commissioned Vukradin** to rescue Adabra Ghast for 25 gold. When Vukradin attempted to negotiate higher pay, Harbin deflected by noting that Manticore treasure was available under Lord Neverember's standing adventurer arrangement.
- **Adabra's return** shifted the dynamic: her forceful advocacy on Vukradin's behalf pressured Harbin into offering free room and board at the Stonehill Inn as additional compensation in lieu of coin.
- **Confirmed the backstory** of the party's assignment: he had sent a note to Lord Neverember requesting aid and had been promised it, which originated the party's commission.
- **Disclosed the town's situation:** Phandalin is under siege from bandits, which he cited as the reason for his limited coin.
- **On the Tower of Storms:** When Vukradin proposed destroying the lighthouse, Harbin firmly stated Lord Neverember would be appalled — Neverember wants the curse removed, not the tower demolished. He directed Vukradin to **Ser Kaelen Thorn at Barthen's Provisions** for further information.
- Harbin is currently receiving **medicine prepared by Adabra Ghast**, referenced in a note she gave the party.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Phandalin, presumably the townmaster's office
- **Active Concerns:** Managing a bandit siege with limited resources; awaiting further support from Lord Neverember
- **What the party knows:** He's cash-strapped, politically cautious, and loyal to Neverember's preferences regarding the Tower of Storms
- **What remains hidden:** The nature of his illness or condition requiring Adabra's medicine; the full extent of the bandit threat and whether Harbin knows more than he's shared

## Relationships
- **Lord Neverember:** Superior and political patron; Harbin defers to Neverember's wishes in all significant decisions
- **Adabra Ghast:** She is preparing medicine for him; she also successfully pressured him on Vukradin's behalf, suggesting she holds some social leverage over him
- **Vukradin:** Transactional relationship; Harbin is wary of Vukradin's ambitions (notably his instinct to plead not to be targeted by dragons) and has offered compensation reluctantly under pressure
- **Ser Kaelen Thorn:** Directed the party to Thorn for information — implies a working relationship or at least knowledge of Thorn's expertise
