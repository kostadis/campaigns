# New notes for Prutha (from dossier_extract_044.md)

Prutha is an orc who was established earlier as Vukradin's first named convert. He fought in the allied orc line during the battle at Falcon's Lodge.

During the melee, Prutha charged an enemy orc in the cultist line and addressed him in Orcish — a torrent described as guttural, specific, and clearly anatomical in its imagery. Valphine translated this for Vukradin as "greeting each other in traditional orchest form," which Vukradin accepted as covering the fact that they were simultaneously swinging swords at each other.

He put two spear strikes into a prone Anchorite, both landing. Valphine noted this as "pretty effective."

In the final phase, Prutha charged an Anchorite while screaming, first in Orcish, then in the common tongue: "Die, you scum of scums!" He then screamed: "For uncle, for my uncle, and the sun god, and Lathander" — and something about Lathander burning all of them under his fiery bright light. Valphine's response was a dry "Alright." Vukradin watched Prutha drive into the Anchorite and reflected on the night he had acknowledged him as his first convert — noting the difference between a name you give someone and the weight they choose to carry it at, and observing that Prutha was carrying his at a run, screaming theology.

After the battle, Prutha used his axe on at least one of the fleeing or defeated orcs, prompting Soma's "Guys, calm down." He also shouted something in Orcish about the gods and the sun.

The reference to "uncle" during his battle cry has not been previously established in available session notes and may represent new character information.

Last known status: present at Falcon's Lodge at dusk, alive, having fought hard for Lathander and his uncle.
