# Locutus

## Identity
- Role/affiliation unknown; acted as intermediary between Sister Kayla and Valphine
- First appeared leading Valphine to an abandoned building on the edge of Phandalin for a clandestine meeting

## Personality & Motivations
- Primary motivation: securing passage to the Underdark, which he believed Valphine would grant as reward for his services.
- Functioned as a fixer or go-between, arranging the meeting between Sister Kayla and Valphine.

## History with the Party
- Escorted Valphine to an abandoned building at the edge of Phandalin to meet with Sister Kayla.
- Confirmed to Sister Kayla that he had killed Borg.
- Was immediately killed by Sister Kayla following this confirmation — she missed twice with her rapier before cutting him down with her offhand dagger.

## Current Status
- **Deceased.** Killed by Sister Kayla at the meeting site.
- His promised reward from Valphine (passage to the Underdark) was never delivered.

## Relationships
- **Sister Kayla:** Arranged a meeting with her; was killed by her moments after confirming Borg's death.
- **Valphine:** Served him in exchange for a promised reward; whether Valphine intended to honor that promise is unknown.
- **Borg:** Locutus killed him, apparently on behalf of one or both of the above parties.

- N/A — Locutus did not survive long enough to accrue arc score changes.
