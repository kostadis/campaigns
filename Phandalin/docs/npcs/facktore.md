# Facktore

## Identity
- Deranged gnome inventor; no formal faction affiliation noted
- First appeared in a large chamber north of the cave entrance to Gnomengarde, where he ambushed Vukradin and the party using his invention

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven by an obsessive, reckless desire to test his inventions — willingly used the party as live test subjects without provocation. Demonstrates instability and poor judgment; fled rather than face consequences when his device was disabled.

## History with the Party
- Ambushed Vukradin's party in his chamber using a rotating platform mounted with four automatically reloading heavy crossbows.
- The party disabled the device.
- Facktore fled the chamber during or after the encounter.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Somewhere within Gnomengarde; fled from his chamber, exact whereabouts unknown.
- **Active plans:** Unknown. May attempt to regroup, rebuild, or retaliate.
- **What the party knows:** He built and operated the crossbow platform and fled after it was disabled.
- **What remains hidden:** His current location and whether he has additional inventions or allies.

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Hostile. Attacked them unprovoked as test subjects; no rapport established.
