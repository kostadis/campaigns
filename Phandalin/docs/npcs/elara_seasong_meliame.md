# Elara Seasong Meliame

## Identity
- Wealthy art collector; member of House Meliame
- Not yet met in person; identified via Ser Kaelen's Candlekeep contacts as the rightful owner of a statue the party recovered from a lighthouse

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven by the acquisition and preservation of art
- No direct personality traits established yet — party has not encountered her face-to-face

## History with the Party
- Party learned of Elara through Ser Kaelen's Candlekeep contacts after recovering a statue from a lighthouse
- Party was tasked with escorting the statue to the gala and presenting it to her personally, while noting the Order of the Gauntlet's role in its recovery
- House Maragaster (known for demonic alliances) is actively attempting to intercept the statue, presumably to embarrass House Meliame

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Neverwinter, attending an extravagant gala hosted by her eccentric cousin, Lord Cassian Meliame
- **Active situation:** The statue delivery and introduction have not yet occurred; interception attempt by House Maragaster is an active threat
- **Party knows:** She is the statue's rightful owner; she is at the gala; the Order wants credit for the recovery
- **Hidden:** Her disposition toward the party, her knowledge of House Maragaster's interest, and the full significance of the statue to her

## Relationships

- **House Maragaster** — hostile faction seeking to use the statue against her family's reputation
