# Aletra of House Satora

## Identity
- **Role:** Fourth daughter of the Satora family; covert interdimensional drug smuggler
- **Faction:** House Satora / House Xorlarrin poison trade network; operates under an unnamed superior ("KP")
- **First Appearance:** Found in the machine room of Axeholm, calmly working at a glowing keypad beside a malfunctioning planar device while armed with a rapier. Identified by Valphine through the Satora family's signature scent of lavender perfume and chemical bleach.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Smuggle Midnight Tears (interdimensional drow poison) through the multiverse; maintain operational secrecy from her superior "KP"
- **Traits:** Coldly competent and self-serving, with a sardonic, condescending manner—especially toward Valphine. Entirely unconcerned with collateral damage; she laughed off complaints from the Chief Accountant before apparently killing her. Manipulative and calculating, quick to weaponize others as distractions.

## History with the Party
1. **Axeholm Machine Room:** The party discovered Aletra operating the planar device at Axeholm. She had previously convinced the Dwarven King to install the machine by promising it would boost mine profits—the machine broke instead, unleashing spatial anomalies that drove the fortress mad.
2. **Manipulation of Brewbarry:** During the Rift Weaver encounter, she pointed her wand at Brewbarry to flag him as a spatial anomaly, drawing all the spiders onto him so she could work undisturbed.
3. **Confrontation with Valphine:** Spoke to Valphine in Undercommon with characteristic condescension—mocked their mother's low opinion of Valphine, referenced a past trap using a stuffed creature as bait, and made a darkly ambiguous reference to an earlier encounter. Acknowledged, with backhanded respect, that Valphine had at least tried to stop her.
4. **Escape:** After the Rift Weavers fell, Aletra extracted a box of Midnight Tears vials and opened a portal. Valphine struck her with a blinding curse as she stepped through, causing her to fumble and drop several vials. Her parting words warned that her boss would not be pleased.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Through a portal she stepped into blindly after being cursed by Valphine—destination unknown.
- **Active Operations:** Interdimensional Midnight Tears smuggling operation connected to House Xorlarrin's poison trade; partially disrupted by the loss of dropped vials.
- **What the Party Knows:** She was smuggling Midnight Tears through the planar machine; she works for an unnamed superior; she escaped through a portal while blinded.
- **Hidden:** The identity of "KP" and the full scope of the smuggling network. Her personal notes contained the phrase *"use only if KP isn't watching,"* suggesting she operates under active oversight she actively circumvents.

## Relationships
- **Valphine (sister/party member):** Deeply dysfunctional sibling relationship. Aletra is condescending and dismissive but not entirely without acknowledgment of Valphine's competence. Has set traps for her before. Valphine successfully cursed her on the way out—the first time Aletra appears to have been meaningfully thwarted by her.
- **Dwarven King of Axeholm:** Deceived him with false promises of profit; her machine destroyed his fortress and its inhabitants.
- **Chief Accountant (deceased):** Killed (or caused the death of) the Chief Accountant for questioning the books.
- **House Xorlarrin:** Connected through the Midnight Tears trade.
- **"KP" (unknown superior):** Operates under this figure's oversight; actively hides certain actions from them. Worried about their reaction to the botched extraction.

## Arc Score Events
- **Valphine → Aletra (negative):** Aletra manipulated Brewbarry into danger, spoke with contempt about Valphine to her face, and escaped with the majority of the contraband—but Valphine's curse forced her through the portal blinded and short several vials, marking a small but meaningful win against her.
