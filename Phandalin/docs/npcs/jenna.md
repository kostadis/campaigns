# Jenna

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Agent of the Lord's Alliance; council representative for the Lord's Alliance in Phandalin
- **Faction:** Lord's Alliance
- **First Appearance:** Town green in Phandalin, during Vukradin's concert. She approached the party (via the tavern) following the dragon attack.
- **Physical Description:** Woman in her late thirties with auburn hair, sharp green eyes, and practical high-quality traveling clothes. Wears a dark green wool doublet, sturdy stylish boots, and simple silver rings.
- *Also known as: Jenna Roscoe, Jena Roscoe.*

*Also known as: Jenna Roscoe, Jena Roscoe.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Recruit Vukradin as an asset — booking him performances before Waterdeep and Neverwinter nobility, potentially for Lord Nevermember himself, in exchange for his cooperation and influence. More broadly, she seeks to cultivate leverage over party members and legitimize wealth through community investment.
- Pragmatic and calculating, she arrived armed with four concealed weapons and a pre-planted letter as leverage. She is comfortable working angles and framing arguments to suit her needs.
- Despite her maneuvering, she showed visible, apparently genuine admiration when Vukradin refused her on principled grounds — suggesting she respects conviction even when it frustrates her objectives.
- Holds open contempt for Uthgardt peoples; she called Brewbarry "Uthgartian" as a slur.
- Publicly dismissive and minimizing — characterized the Kraken Society as a harmless social club for wealthy elites, though her composure under questioning raised suspicion.
- Possibly concealing deeper knowledge of or ties to the Kraken Society.
- Patient and long-view in her thinking; treats Vukradin's idealism as a phase rather than a challenge. Comfortable with moral grey areas.

## History with the Party
1. **Concert & Dragon Attack:** Observed Vukradin at the town green with a smirk throughout his concert, remaining composed throughout the dragon attack. Spider-Man identified her as working for the Lord's Alliance, recognizing her insignia and noting agents of that organization have a distinct smell.
2. **Tavern Introduction:** Moved to the tavern after the concert. Intercepted by Brewbarry before reaching Vukradin. Disarmed at Brewbarry's insistence (rapier, dagger, second dagger, hand crossbow) before being introduced.
3. **Negotiation with Vukradin:** Revealed she authored the mysterious letter Vukradin had previously received, using it as a way to open negotiations. Offered to serve as his booking agent before nobility and Lord Nevermember. Used House Margaster's funding of the Order of the Gauntlet as leverage to create distance between Vukradin and Ser Kaelen.
4. **Philosophical Clash:** Attempted to reconcile Vukradin's "fair-trade gold" philosophy with dungeon looting, arguing the gold was stolen from the people collectively and that spending it constitutes redistribution. Vukradin flatly rejected this framing, insisting on returning items to rightful owners, and declined her offer entirely. Departed with expressed disappointment, though with a visible softening toward Vukradin.
5. **Wealth-Laundering Discussion:** After Vukradin stormed out and Harbin's cover story satisfied him, Jenna explained to the remaining party members how funneling adventuring money into community projects effectively launders it — turning "blood money" into legitimate community improvement. Suggested Vukradin was young and might eventually come to understand this concept with time.
6. **Council Meeting (Kraken Society):** At a Phandalin council meeting, downplayed the Kraken Society as a benign social club for wealthy elites. Valphine sensed she was withholding information, suggesting hidden knowledge or connection to the group.
7. **Pre-Departure (Mountain's Toe):** Approached Brewbarry privately and suggested he could easily fund his music studio by abandoning his principles and "selling out." Brewbarry refused, asserting he would do things his own way.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Phandalin (serving as Lord's Alliance council representative).
- **Active Plans:** Unknown; her proposal to Brewbarry may indicate she is actively recruiting or cultivating leverage over party members. May also be working to minimize scrutiny of the Kraken Society.
- **What the Party Knows:** She is a Lord's Alliance agent and council representative, she sent the letter, and she originally wanted Vukradin as an asset. Spider-Man identified her by insignia and smell. She publicly minimizes the Kraken Society.
- **What Remains Hidden:** Her deeper mandate from the Lord's Alliance, what she wanted from Vukradin beyond performances, whether the House Margaster angle is the full extent of her leverage, and what she actually knows about the Kraken Society — and why she downplays it.

## Relationships
- **Vukradin:** Attempted to recruit him; rebuffed. Shows apparent genuine respect for him despite failure. Views his principles as youthful and ultimately malleable. Relationship is unresolved — neither hostile nor allied. Understands him as a man of principle who believes possessions belong to their original owners.
- **Brewbarry:** Openly contemptuous due to anti-Uthgardt bias. Later approached him privately with an offer to compromise his values for money; also rebuffed.
- **Valphine:** Valphine detected deception or concealment from her during the council meeting regarding the Kraken Society.
- **Spider-Man:** Identified her covertly; she may or may not be aware she was made.
- **Lord's Alliance:** Active operative and council representative. Reports to the organization; likely has superiors directing her efforts.
- **Kraken Society:** Suspected hidden connection; nature unknown.
- **House Margaster / Order of the Gauntlet / Ser Kaelen:** Aware of Ser Kaelen's connection to Vukradin and attempted to use House Margaster's funding relationship as a wedge. Intent to undermine or complicate that relationship.
- **Harbin:** Present for Harbin's cover story; no direct relationship noted beyond this.
