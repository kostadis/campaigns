# Jenna *(last name unknown)*

## Identity
- **Role/Faction:** Agent of the Lord's Alliance, based out of Neverwinter
- **First Appearance:** Session in Phandalin; observed by Vukradin at the town green meeting, later approached the party at Stonehill Tavern that evening

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Maintain law, order, and societal stability against chaos; appears to be investigating or monitoring House Margaster and their connection to the Order of the Gauntlet
- **Personality:** Composed, calculated, and politically savvy — she approached Brewbarry rather than Vukradin directly, suggesting she reads people and works angles carefully. A flash of contempt slipped through when referencing Uthgartians, revealing that her polished professionalism has limits. She frames self-interested intelligence-gathering as altruism.

## History with the Party
1. **Town Green:** Observed Vukradin's meeting; notably unafraid of the dragon, smirked at him — suggesting prior knowledge or deliberate confidence.
2. **Stonehill Tavern:** Approached Brewbarry for an introduction to Vukradin. Complimented Vukradin's music. Revealed she authored the letter Vukradin received (sent on behalf of or connected to the Order of the Gauntlet). Warned that House Margaster — linked to that letter — deals exclusively in blood money, framing this as a warning in Vukradin's interest.
3. **Confrontation:** Brewbarry's fury over her contempt toward Uthgartians escalated the exchange. Brewbarry demanded she surrender her weapons. The session ended unresolved.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Stonehill Tavern, Phandalin — mid-standoff with Brewbarry
- **Active Plans:** Seeking an audience with Vukradin; likely has further intelligence or an ask prepared
- **What the Party Knows:** She's Lord's Alliance, from Neverwinter, authored the Order of the Gauntlet letter, and wants to steer Vukradin away from House Margaster
- **What Remains Hidden:** Her full agenda, what she actually wants from Vukradin, the extent of her knowledge about the party, and whether her warning about House Margaster is genuine or a manipulation

## Relationships
- **Vukradin:** Target of interest; has been observing him, sent him correspondence, and is actively seeking direct contact
- **Brewbarry:** Hostile first impression; her Uthgartian slip triggered his anger and she now faces his demand for her weapons
- **House Margaster:** Adversarial — she is warning against them and likely investigating them
- **Order of the Gauntlet:** Connected through the letter, though the exact nature of that relationship is unclear
