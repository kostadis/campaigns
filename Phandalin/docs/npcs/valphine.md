# Valphine

## Identity
- **Role/Affiliation:** Former drow house operative; current devotee of Lathander (heterodox interpretation); traveling with the party
- **First Appearance:** Present during the political maneuvering involving Kings Korboz and Gnercli; observed Vukradin's manipulation alongside the party

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Reconciling her drow past with a new devotion to Lathander—specifically the painful, destructive aspects of light and dawn; may be working toward founding her own Lathanderial cult centered on pain
- **Key Traits:** Valphine is coolly observant, politically shrewd, and carries the casual brutality of her former life just beneath a veneer of philosophical reflection. She finds manipulation and suffering aesthetically interesting rather than morally troubling. She is drawn to complexity—in music, in tactics, in theology—and disdains simplicity or single-mindedness. She is slowly, consciously trying to leave her former world behind, but the line between past and present remains thin.

## History with the Party
1. **Kings Korboz and Gnercli:** Watched Vukradin destabilize the dual monarchy with satisfaction; offered commentary on organizational collapse drawn from commune politics she had witnessed.
2. **Bandit Ambush (Road to Umbrage Hill):** Sensed something in the shadows but dismissed it (attributed to Underdark instincts misfiring). Was hit by a ranged attack. After the fight, killed a fleeing bandit with a hand crossbow bolt to the back.
3. **Manticore at Umbrage Hill:** Fired twice at the manticore, missed both times, expressed frustration with her weapon. Offered unprompted to teach Adabra about Lathander. Reflected briefly on Brewbarry's killing blow.
4. **Stonehill Inn:** Shared information about Toblen "Spiderman" Stonehill with Vukradin, noting privately he would have been a recruitment or elimination target in her former life. Performed *Choralum Lux Maleficarum*—a fusion of Lathanderial choral music and Lolth prayers—alongside Vukradin, winning the crowd. Vukradin acknowledged she should perform with him regularly.
5. **Ser Kaelen Thorn:** Deliberately unsettled him by describing Lathander as "deliciously painful." He declined to recruit her into the Order of the Gauntlet; she did not pursue it.
6. **Townmaster Harbin Advocacy:** Annoyed that Adabra ignored her throughout; reflected she would have responded violently in her former life but has moved past it.
7. **Journey to Tower of Storms:** Identified the unnatural gullies as potentially magical, drawing on Underdark/Faerzess experience. Mused on Lathander's abundance enabling suffering and war. Privately thanked Vukradin for expanding her understanding of the divine plan.
8. **Ambush Approach (Tower of Storms):** Her drow instincts clocked the ambush before it was sprung. Identified orc tactical sophistication—blocked exit, elevated positions, killing ground—and the probable presence of a shaman or cleric. Called it "enjoyably complex."

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Traveling with the party on the high road toward the Tower of Storms
- **Active Plans:** Considering founding a heterodox Lathanderial cult focused on the aspect of pain; musically collaborating with Vukradin on a semi-regular basis
- **What the Party Knows:** She is a former drow operative who has adopted Lathander worship in a deeply unorthodox form; she is capable and tactically sharp; she has a dark past she occasionally references
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full nature and extent of her former house affiliations; what specifically drove her away from that life; the degree to which her "reformed" worldview is genuine versus rationalized; what *Choralum Lux Maleficarum* truly represents theologically

## Relationships
- **Vukradin:** Closest dynamic in the party. Respects his manipulation and musicality; he has acknowledged her as a genuine collaborator. She feels something like gratitude toward him for expanding her perspective.
- **Adabra:** Friction. Adabra ignored her during the Harbin advocacy; Valphine found this irritating but contained herself, noting it as evidence of personal growth.
- **Brewbarry:** Noted his manticore kill; no strong dynamic established yet.
- **Ser Kaelen Thorn (NPC):** She deliberately unnerved him and showed no interest in his approval or his Order.
- **Toblen Stonehill (NPC):** Views him as a former-life type—someone to be used or eliminated. Shared this assessment with Vukradin rather than acting on it.
