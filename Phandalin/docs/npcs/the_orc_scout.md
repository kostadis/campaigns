# The Orc Scout

## Identity
- **Role:** Forward scout for an orc raiding party operating near Neverwinter Wood
- **Faction:** Unknown orc raiding party
- **First Appearance:** Encountered in bushes on the road into Neverwinter Wood; revealed himself when he spotted Soma in bear form

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations
- Disciplined enough to signal ambush positions and disengage tactically despite injury, but was reprimanded by his superior for being distracted by the bear.
- Fled in apparent terror upon recognizing Valphine as a Drow worshipping Lathander — suggesting either personal history or cultural superstition around that combination.

## History with the Party
- Positioned in bushes ahead of the party on the road into Neverwinter Wood when he spotted Soma in bear form.
- Climbed a tree and used hand signals to coordinate the ambush positions.
- Was berated by the bone-necklace orc for losing focus and being distracted by a bear.
- Took a bite from Soma (bleeding foot) but disengaged tactically and returned fire with an arrow into Soma from range.
- Recognized Valphine as a Drow of Lathander and fled in terror.
- Paused briefly to fire at Valphine before fleeing again into the forest.
- Pursued by Valphine but successfully escaped.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Fled into Neverwinter Wood; whereabouts unknown.
- **Active Plans:** Unknown — may be returning to report to the larger raiding party.
- **Party Knows:** He escaped and recognized Valphine specifically. **Hidden:** Why a Drow worshipping Lathander triggered such fear, and what he may report back to his group.

## Relationships
- **Bone-Necklace Orc:** Superior who reprimanded him during the ambush for being distracted by a bear.
- **Valphine:** Fled in terror upon recognizing her as a Drow worshipping Lathander; she pursued but lost him.
- **Soma:** Was bitten and wounded by Soma in bear form; fired an arrow into Soma from range.
