# The Cult Fanatic (Name Unknown)

## Identity
- **Role:** Field operative and mind-controller for the Kraken Society
- **Faction:** Kraken Society (confirmed via spiral emblems found on his person and his warriors)
- **First Appearance:** A forest glade beyond a foggy riverbank, where he confronted the party alongside three mind-controlled Emerald Enclave rangers

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Advancing what he called a "rising current" flowing toward Neverwinter — likely a Kraken Society operation of significant scale
- **Traits:** Coldly ideological and contemptuous of opposition, dismissing even his own death as a "petty squabble" irrelevant to larger events. He refused to betray his cause under any pressure, maintaining eerie composure even when briefly revived after being knocked unconscious.

## History with the Party
1. **First Encounter:** Appeared in the glade with three Emerald Enclave members under his mind control. Offered the party a chance to submit; they refused. He attempted to dominate Brewbarry's mind mid-combat and was ultimately knocked unconscious via non-lethal strike.
2. **Prisoner Transport:** The party carried him unconscious during their return journey. Even while incapacitated, he continued to exert mental control over his minions. Brief interrogation yielded only cryptic warnings about "tides rising."
3. **Handoff and Execution:** On the second day of travel, a Talosian orc raiding party ambushed the group specifically seeking him as an enemy. After negotiation, the party surrendered him. Given a final opportunity to speak, he dismissed the conflict as meaningless. The orc commander Valphine executed him on the spot. His death immediately broke the mind control over the surviving Emerald Enclave members.

## Current Status
- **Status:** **Dead.** Executed by Valphine of the Talosian orc warband. His head was taken as a trophy.
- **Active Plans:** Unknown — he referenced a major operation directed at Neverwinter but revealed no specifics before his death.
- **What the Party Knows:** He was a Kraken Society operative capable of sustained long-range mind control. He spoke of a "rising current" or "wave" approaching Neverwinter.
- **What Remains Hidden:** The nature, timeline, and leadership of the Kraken Society operation he served. Whether others in his cell know of his death or the party's involvement.

## Relationships
- **Kraken Society:** Loyal operative; refused to betray the organization even facing death
- **Emerald Enclave Patrol:** Victims of his mind control; freed upon his death
- **Talosian Orc Warband (Valphine):** Active enemies who sought him out specifically and claimed his head
- **Brewbarry:** Targeted directly with a domination attempt during combat
- **The Party:** Viewed them as minor obstacles to something far larger
