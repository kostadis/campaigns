# Lyra

## Identity
- **Role:** Leader of a local Emerald Enclave chapter; interventionist faction
- **First Appearance:** Found trapped by blighted vines at the edge of the corrupted clearing in the Whispering Wood. Shouted to the party for help and identified herself by name.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Protect the natural world from planar corruption; advance within the Emerald Enclave; cleanse the Whispering Grove and seal the planar rift
- Passionate and confrontational — she erupted at Adabra in the Phandalin square and stormed off when the party didn't share her alarm
- Capable and knowledgeable, but prone to acting unilaterally out of ambition and frustration with institutional paralysis
- Currently discouraged about her own leadership, though cautiously open to rebuilding her standing with the party's help

## History with the Party

1. **Whispering Wood (Session 1):** Found trapped in blighted vines at the corrupted clearing. Explained that the grove's Treant guardian had been corrupted by a planar infection and urged the party to fight it.
2. **The Ritual Attempt:** Freed by Soma's bear-form assault, she immediately produced ritual stones and led an attempt to seal a planar rift (6 successes needed, 3 failures = total failure). She was re-ensnared mid-ritual and cast Moonbeam against the Treant. The ritual failed catastrophically — the rift expanded rather than closed. She whispered, *"This did not work the way I had hoped."*
3. **Confrontation in Phandalin:** Arrived simultaneously with the party to confront Adabra. Exploded at Adabra ("You are insane!") and, when Vukradin repeatedly deflected her arguments, declared the party fools and departed in fury.
4. **Square Exposition:** During the same confrontation, provided detailed background on the Emerald Enclave's internal schism between interventionists and naturalists, tracing it to the War of the Dragons and the debate over Tiamat's summoning.
5. **Agents' Revelation:** Her agents Thomas and Marian, speaking privately with the party, revealed that Lyra had acted without sanction — driven by personal attachment to the Grove, ambition, and impatience with Enclave politics. Thomas: *"She didn't do what was best for the Emerald Enclave; she did what was best for her."*
6. **Reunion in Phandalin:** Reunited with two Enclave members the party had rescued. Was deeply discouraged, citing the treant failure and her men's capture as leadership failures. Vukradin encouraged her; one of the rescued men revealed the party had credited her planning for the rescue. Heartened, she agreed to let Vukradin help advocate for her at the Enclave's next meeting.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Phandalin
- **Active Plans:** Waiting for the Emerald Enclave's next meeting day; Vukradin has agreed to help vouch for her competence
- **What the Party Knows:** That she acted unilaterally out of ambition, that the ritual failed and worsened the rift, and that she is politically vulnerable within the Enclave
- **Hidden from the Party:** Whether her superiors are already aware of her unsanctioned actions and their consequences

## Relationships
- **Vukradin:** Tense but thawing. He deflected her during the Adabra confrontation, prompting her furious exit — but later encouraged her during her crisis of confidence. She has accepted his offer of support at the next Enclave meeting.
- **Valphine:** Expressed genuine surprise and wonder at meeting a drow cleric of Lathander (*"Can this be?"*). Relationship otherwise undeveloped.
- **Soma:** Freed her from the blighted vines; no further notable interaction recorded.
- **Adabra:** Openly hostile. Considers Adabra insane and dangerously fanatical.
- **Thomas & Marian (Enclave agents):** Her subordinates, loyal to the Enclave oath over personal loyalty to Lyra. Spoke candidly — and critically — about her motivations to the party.
- **Emerald Enclave (organization):** Politically exposed within the interventionist faction; acted without authorization and her operation worsened the crisis she was sent to fix.

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease** — Departed Phandalin in anger after the Adabra confrontation when the party (especially Vukradin) failed to support her position
- **Increase** — Heartened by Vukradin's encouragement and the rescued men's testimony; agreed to work with the party toward Enclave rehabilitation
