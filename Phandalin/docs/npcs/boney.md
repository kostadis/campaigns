# Boney (formerly Barney)

## Identity
- **Role:** Undead skeletal horse; loyal companion and mount
- **Faction:** Bound in service to Lady Alagondar (now transferred to the party)
- **First Appearance:** Reanimated from bones and a rotting saddle in a southern niche of the Dragonbarrow tunnel when Soma approached to investigate

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Drive:** Unwavering loyalty to who he believes to be Lady Alagondar — currently Soma, accepted as her reincarnation without question
- Gentle, trusting, and easily persuaded; accepted both the reincarnation story and his new name without hesitation
- Expresses genuine emotion about his skeletal state, notably sadness and confusion about his missing mane and physical features despite having no flesh
- Has a dry, self-aware humor about being "partially alive," remarking that irregularity is no longer a problem for him

## History with the Party
1. Reanimated in the southern niche of Dragonbarrow when Soma approached; Soma used Speak with Animals to communicate
2. Initially mistook Soma for Lady Alagondar; the party convinced him Soma was her reincarnation, which he accepted
3. Revealed he had been brought to the dungeon to guard something for Lady Alagondar but was killed by will-o'-wisps before completing his duty
4. Directed the party north, stating the sword was buried "across the way" from where Lady Alagondar rested — information that proved accurate
5. Agreed to be called Boney rather than Barney after party persuasion
6. Observed the party's debate over who should carry the Dragon Slayer Sword with quiet amusement
7. By Chapter 32, had developed a particular bond with Valphine

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Northern chamber of Dragonbarrow
- **Active Role:** Traveling companion and mount following the party
- **What the Party Knows:** He was originally tasked with guarding something for Lady Alagondar; was killed by will-o'-wisps before completing that duty; has only 13 HP and is fragile in combat
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full nature of his original mission and what exactly he was meant to guard

## Relationships
- **Soma:** Believes Soma to be Lady Alagondar reincarnated; primary object of his loyalty and devotion
- **Valphine:** Has developed a particular bond by Chapter 32 — closer than with other party members
- **Lady Alagondar (deceased):** Original master; died before Boney could complete his sworn duty, which he carries as unresolved grief
