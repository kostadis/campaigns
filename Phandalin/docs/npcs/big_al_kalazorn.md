# Big Al Kalazorn

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Retired veteran; former Sheriff of Triboar (predecessor to Darathra); owner of Butterskull Ranch
- **Faction:** None active; civilian
- **First Appearance:** Found by the party in the cellar of Butterskull Ranch, bound to a chair with a burlap sack over his head — the subject of a quest posted by Harbin

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Protection of his ranch, his livestock, and his way of life in retirement
- Stubborn and sentimental — flatly refused to evacuate without his prized cow, Petunia, even after being rescued from captivity. Practical enough to offer fair payment (mithril chainmail) in exchange for the party's help recovering her.

## History with the Party
1. **Butterskull Ranch (Session X):** Vukradin located Big Al in the ranch cellar, tied up and used as bait by the Rot-Tusk Ogres under Borg, who had themselves driven off an earlier orc raid and seized the property.
2. Big Al briefed the party on the sequence of events — orcs first, then Borg's ogres.
3. Refused to leave without Petunia; offered a suit of mithril chainmail as payment for her recovery. Confirmed the armor was legitimately purchased with his own funds.
4. The party accepted the trade and located Petunia using Soma's *Speak with Animals*.
5. Big Al was directed toward Corbin's camp with Petunia while the party continued after Borg.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** En route to or sheltering at Corbin's camp, accompanied by Petunia
- **Active Plans:** None known beyond returning to safety
- **What the Party Knows:** He is a retired veteran and former sheriff with legitimate means; he is alive and evacuated
- **What Remains Hidden:** Nothing flagged in notes

## Relationships
- **Darathra Shendrel:** Predecessor in the role of Sheriff of Triboar
- **Harbin:** Posted the quest to find Big Al; aware of his situation
- **Borg / Rot-Tusk Ogres:** Captors who kept him alive as bait
- **The Party:** Friendly — rescued him and recovered Petunia; rewarded them with mithril chainmail
- **Petunia (cow):** Deeply attached; her safety was a non-negotiable condition of his cooperation
