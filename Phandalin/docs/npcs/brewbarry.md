# Brewbarry

## Identity
- **Role:** Wandering warrior, former Uthgardt Barbarian; traveling with the party as a free agent
- **First Appearance:** Fought alongside the party against an ogre; integrated into the group from that engagement onward

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Unclear long-term, but demonstrates strong drive toward camaraderie, diplomatic resolution, and protecting companions — particularly Valphine
- Brewbarry is boisterous and direct, occasionally blundering tactically, but consistently pulls toward mercy and connection over violence when given the choice
- His Uthgardt past is a source of both pride and pain; he acknowledges the cruelty of his former people, identifies with others who have been hurt by them, and seems to be actively building a different identity
- He shows unexpected emotional depth — the orc prisoner's exile briefly moved him, and his apology to the fleeing bandit suggests genuine empathy rooted in personal experience

## History with the Party

**Ogre Fight**
Fought effectively alongside the party; shrugged off a full axe blow through barbarian rage. His resilience caused the ogre to flee, though he could not finish it before Vukradin did.

**Gnomengarde**
Announced himself as "Brewbarry, killer of Ogres" at the guard post. His clarification that he *used to be* Uthgardt inadvertently resolved a standing bureaucratic attack order, allowing the party entry. In the inventors' workshop, he shut down any implied suggestion of torture from Valphine with: *"We talk! We cure!"*

**Mimic Fight**
Charged the mimic, wounded it, but got his halberd lodged in its body and struggled throughout. Eventually produced handaxes at Vukradin's prompting.

**Bandit Ambush**
Decapitated one bandit with his halberd. Chose not to kill the fleeing spider-phobic bandit — dropped his weapon, said *"Friends,"* and offered a personal apology for Uthgardt cruelty. The gesture stopped the bandit cold.

**Stonehill Tavern (Phandalin)**
Helped defuse tension over an ambush accusation by reminding Vukradin of his popularity. Joined a musical session, using his halberd percussively in a style recalling Uthgardt tribal music — the performance was notably successful and earned the group additional coin. Participated in a betting pool on the lighthouse mission, including a side bet on whether Vukradin would be ditched or killed.

**Supply Cart Ambush**
Pulled the supply cart solo and refused to let anyone else touch it after it was filled with alcohol. Stumbled through the woods during the ambush and gave away the party's position. Picked up a javelin thrown at him and snapped it in two. Defended Valphine against the orc with the necklace (missed). Taunted the orc as a "shrimp," confusing the creature. After the fight, took charge of the prisoner — named him "Shrimpy," bound him to the cart, and made him pull it. Reassured Vukradin that Tribar Wester would be found.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Phandalin, Stonehill Tavern area; involved in the mission to locate Tribar Wester
- **Active:** Managing prisoner "Shrimpy," who is bound to the supply cart
- **What the Party Knows:** Former Uthgardt, exiled or estranged; has personal grievances against his old people
- **Hidden:** The specific circumstances of his exile remain unexplored

## Relationships
- **Valphine:** Protective instinct evident — defended her during the orc fight, cut off her implied torture suggestion. Relationship has friction but also clear loyalty
- **Vukradin:** Friendly; backed him socially at the tavern, collaborated musically, bet on his survival (and potential abandonment)
- **"Shrimpy" (orc prisoner):** Claimed him as a ward post-battle; the orc's story of clan exile quietly resonated with Brewbarry's own experience
- **The fleeing bandit:** Showed him unexpected compassion; outcome of that encounter unresolved
- **Uthgardt:** Estranged; views his former people's cruelty with shame and regret
