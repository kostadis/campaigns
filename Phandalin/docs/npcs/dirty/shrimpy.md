# Shrimpy (Orc Raider, Name Unknown)

## Identity
- Captured orc raider; prisoner of the party
- Mockingly renamed "Shrimpy" by Brewbarry
- Former member of an independent orc splinter faction, unaffiliated with any current organized group
- First appeared at the end of a roadside ambush, captured after being put to magical sleep while attempting to flee

## Personality & Motivations
- Primary drive: self-preservation and release from captivity
- Opportunistic — offered information during interrogation and angled for payment in return
- Whiny and cowardly under pressure; argued dramatically that the party must be dead during the Woodland Manse fight to leverage his release
- Earned consistent contempt from Brewbarry and Valphine through his behavior

## History with the Party
1. **Captured** at the end of the ambush; magically slept, bound, and interrogated. Revealed his group's origins and the fracturing of his orc clan after a white dragon drove them from the Ice Spire of the Sword Mountains.
2. **Bound to the party's cart** and forced to pull it to the Logger's Camp after Brewbarry dismissed his request for payment.
3. **Transported toward Phandalin** after the party debated his fate; Tibor refused to hand him off to Neverwinter, so imprisonment in Phandalin was decided.
4. **Held at a clearing** near the Woodland Manse while the party assaulted the cultists inside. Argued with his guard Corbin, insisting the party was dead and demanding release.
5. **Handed over to Town Master Harbin Wester** upon the party's return to Phandalin.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Phandalin, in the custody of Town Master Harbin Wester
- No active plans of his own; effectively out of play unless the party revisits Phandalin's jail
- The party knows his faction's backstory; nothing is noted as hidden

## Relationships
- **Brewbarry:** Captor and primary antagonist toward Shrimpy; named him, denied him payment, expressed open displeasure at his cowardice
- **Valphine:** Also visibly displeased by his behavior at the Woodland Manse
- **Corbin:** Served as his guard during the Woodland Manse assault; subject of Shrimpy's wheedling attempts
- **Harbin Wester (Town Master):** Current custodian; Shrimpy was formally handed over to him
- **Tibor:** Refused to take Shrimpy to Neverwinter, indirectly determining his fate
