# Sridar

## Identity
- **Role:** Independent scholar and artifact collector; self-described "interventionist" on planar matters
- **Faction:** Aligned in philosophy with Falcon (Falcon Hunting Lodge); contrasts herself with the Emerald Enclave's non-interference stance
- **First Appearance:** Encountered as a guest at the Falcon Hunting Lodge; introduced when Valphine mistook her for a cleric of Lathander

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Collecting artifacts connected to planar events; understanding and actively responding to planar transformations and disruptions
- Calm and businesslike — compensates fairly for information and objects of interest without hesitation
- Intellectually curious; found Brewbarry's theory about intentional incursions genuinely intriguing, suggesting she's open to revising her assumptions
- Originally believed planar incursions were a natural phenomenon (analogous to Faerzress); now considers deliberate interference a real possibility

## History with the Party
1. Met the party at the Falcon Hunting Lodge; clarified she is not a cleric of Lathander
2. Paid **30 gp** to Valphine for detailed information about a creature collecting strange items
3. Identified the **Talisman of Moesko** as having a definite planar connection and purchased it for **100 gp**, with Falcon adding three bottles of wine for Brewbarry
4. Discussed theories on the cause of planar incursions with the party; offered to help piece together clues if they bring her more information in the future

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Falcon Hunting Lodge
- **Active Plans:** Continuing to collect planar artifacts and study disruption events; positioned as a recurring resource for the party
- **What the Party Knows:** She is a drow artifact collector specializing in planar phenomena; she is an interventionist; she can identify planar items
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full extent of her knowledge, resources, or personal history beyond the Underdark blink event; any deeper agenda

## Relationships
- **Falcon (Hunting Lodge host):** Shares the interventionist stance; appears to have an established working relationship
- **Emerald Enclave:** Opposes their naturalist non-interference philosophy
- **Valphine:** Paid for her information; respectful transactional relationship
- **Brewbarry:** Rewarded with wine as part of the talisman deal; his theory about intentional incursions made an impression on her
- **Vukradin:** Approved of the transaction as legitimate fair trade; no deeper connection noted
