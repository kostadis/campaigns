# Horia Stonetallow

## Identity
- **Role:** Dwarven mine representative / clan liaison, Stonetallow clan
- **Faction:** Stonetallow clan (shield dwarves; ancestral roots trace to a disgruntled engineer from Gauntlgrym)
- **First Appearance:** Met the party at the mine during negotiations around a wererat protection arrangement

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Upholding the Stonetallow tradition of doing things *properly*; maximizing the mine's output and profitability; preserving dwarven focus on the craft of mining by offloading security concerns
- Intensely proud of her clan's heritage and eager to explain it at length — Vukradin's offhand remark about "Stonetallow" being an oxymoron prompted a full etymological lecture (it derives from "stone to allow," referencing the clan's mastery of archways)
- Pragmatic enough to accept the wererat arrangement, viewing it as freeing her people to focus on what they do best, though she maintains a skeptical eye on outside modifications to dwarven work

## History with the Party
1. **Mine Negotiations:** Horia met the party during discussions brokering a protection deal between the Stonetallow dwarves and the wererats (represented by Zeleen Varnaster). She voiced skepticism about the wererats' modifications to the mine but ultimately accepted the arrangement as pragmatically sound.
2. **Contract Finalization:** Formally concluded the agreement by shaking hands with Zeleen Varnaster and exchanging signed contracts.
3. **Financial Discussion:** Spoke with Vukradin about projected earnings, estimating approximately **100 gp per week**, with the potential for more given further investment.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The mine, overseeing the newly established arrangement
- **Active Operations:** Managing the mine under the new wererat protection deal; monitoring dwarven operations
- **Known to Party:** Agreed to the protection arrangement; estimated weekly earnings; skeptical of wererat modifications
- **Hidden from Party:** Nothing explicitly noted as concealed

## Relationships
- **Zeleen Varnaster:** Counterpart in the mine agreement; relationship is transactional and cautious — Horia shook hands and exchanged contracts but the dwarves remain skeptical of wererat involvement in their mine
- **Vukradin (party):** Engaged in financial discussion; also the one who (inadvertently) triggered Horia's clan-name lecture
- **Stonetallow Clan:** Deeply loyal; sees her role as protecting the clan's ability to focus on proper mining craft
