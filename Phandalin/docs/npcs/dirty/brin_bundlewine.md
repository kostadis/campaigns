# Brin Bundlewine

## Identity
- Role: Villager and laborer; aspiring chapel builder
- Faction: None (loosely aligned with an unconventional interpretation of Lathander worship)
- First appearance: Approached Valphine at the tavern alongside Giles Slipper-Shine

## Personality & Motivations
- Deeply drawn to the idea of a drow who worships Lathander through the purifying pain of the sun — suggests a fervent, perhaps zealous spiritual curiosity
- Eager and idealistic; offered his labor for free, asking only materials costs
- Pale-skinned and sunburned, which adds an ironic physical note to his sun-focused devotion

## History with the Party
- Approached Valphine at the local tavern after hearing rumors of a sun-worshipping drow
- Proposed renovating Tresendar Manor into the **Temple of Lathander's Searing Pain of Justice**
- Struck a deal: 25 gp for materials, labor provided free of charge

## Current Status
- Last known status: Agreement made with Valphine; presumably beginning or planning renovation work on Tresendar Manor
- Active project: Conversion of Tresendar Manor into the Temple of Lathander's Searing Pain of Justice
- **Party knows:** The deal terms and his enthusiasm for the project
- **Hidden:** His deeper motivations, any prior religious history, and whether he has the actual skills to complete the renovation
