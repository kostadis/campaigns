# The Carver

## Identity
- **Role:** Criminal overlord (apparent); true nature: a sentient obsidian sword, an alien intelligence using humanoid vessels as instruments
- **Faction:** Unknown — operated through intermediaries including a middleman named Jax and an unnamed human veteran vessel
- **First appearance:** Referenced by name only via a surrendered bandit's testimony; later described by Sister Kaella; true nature revealed during the confrontation at Icespire Hold

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Binding and dominating Cryovain the white dragon; using the dragon as a weapon to burn Phandalin as a demonstration of power
- The Carver was patient and methodical, building toward a slow ritual of consumption — but abandoned preparation instantly when interrupted, seizing domination by force rather than adapt to obstruction
- It communicated in contempt: *"Coward's magic. You mute the ritual because you lack the strength to shatter the vessel."* Proud, strategic, and reactive to perceived disrespect
- Spoke in a tongue that caused ears to bleed; was described by Sister Kaella as no longer fully human (a description that, in retrospect, reflected the hollowed vessel rather than the sword itself)

## History with the Party

**Early sessions — The name surfaces**
The party learned of "the Carver" from a surrendered bandit, who described him as the organizing force behind regional banditry and blamed him personally for the bandit's circumstances. The bandit had never met him directly, operating through a middleman named Jax. Location and nature unknown at this stage.

**Pre-confrontation — Sister Kaella's warning**
Sister Kaella reported that the Carver — still believed to be a person — was no longer fully human, marching on Icespire Hold with a ritual chant intended to bind the white dragon. His stated endgame: use the dragon as a suit of armor and burn Phandalin to the ground.

**Icespire Hold — The confrontation**
The party arrived to find the human vessel performing a binding ritual over the chained Cryovain inside a burning geometric summoning circle. Vukradin dropped Silence over the circle, choking the ritual entirely. The vessel stepped cleanly out of the silence zone without drawing attacks — precise, legendary footwork that marked it as no ordinary opponent.

Vukradin then cast Hold Person, paralyzing the vessel mid-motion. With the vessel stopped and silence dropped, the sword spoke directly into the party's minds, abandoning the ritual of slow consumption and dissolving Cryovain's chains immediately to seize direct domination — the expedient path, bypassing all prior preparation. After completing this domination, the sword went visually inert: no glow, no presence, apparently emptied by the effort. The vessel remained paralyzed on the floor.

**Resolution**
When Cryovain was killed, the Carver's essence dissipated instantly — not fled, not retreated, but unmade. Its obsidian sword remained. Vukradin identified the crystal pommel gem as belonging to a rightful owner and refused to keep it despite it being well-suited to his ambitions.

## Current Status
- **The Carver:** Destroyed — dissipated completely upon Cryovain's death
- **The obsidian sword:** Remains at Icespire Hold; visually inert, pommel gem intact
- **The human vessel:** Last seen paralyzed on the floor of the summoning circle; fate unresolved in notes
- **The pommel gem:** Recognized by Vukradin as belonging to someone else; not claimed

## Relationships
- **Jax:** Middleman who relayed the Carver's criminal operations to street-level bandits; no further detail in notes
- **The human vessel:** Unnamed veteran; served as physical host for the sword's intelligence; tactical competence reflects the sword's own capability
- **Cryovain:** Target and instrument — the Carver needed the dragon to execute its endgame; its destruction ended the Carver entirely
- **Vukradin:** Primary magical antagonist during the confrontation; the Carver addressed him directly with contempt; Vukradin's arcana knowledge revealed the sword's true nature mid-battle
- **Sister Kaella:** Provided the party their most detailed intelligence on the Carver prior to confrontation
