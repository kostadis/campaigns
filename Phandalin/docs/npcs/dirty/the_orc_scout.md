# The Orc Scout

## Identity
- **Role:** Forward scout for an orc raiding party operating near Neverwinter Wood
- **Faction:** Unknown orc raiding party
- **First Appearance:** Encountered in bushes on the road into Neverwinter Wood; revealed himself when he spotted Soma in bear form

## Personality & Motivations
- Disciplined enough to signal ambush positions and disengage tactically despite injury, but was reprimanded by his superior for being distracted by the bear. Fled in apparent terror upon recognizing Valphine as a Drow worshipping Lathander — suggesting either personal history or cultural superstition around that combination.

## History with the Party
- Spotted Soma in bear form and climbed a tree, using hand signals to coordinate the ambush.
- Was berated by the bone-necklace orc for losing focus.
- Took a bite from Soma (bleeding foot) but disengaged and returned fire with an arrow.
- Recognized Valphine as a Drow of Lathander and fled in terror.
- Paused briefly to fire at Valphine before fleeing again into the forest.
- Pursued by Valphine but successfully escaped.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Fled into Neverwinter Wood; whereabouts unknown.
- **Active Plans:** Unknown — may be returning to report to the larger raiding party.
- **Party Knows:** He escaped and recognized Valphine specifically. **Hidden:** Why a Drow worshipping Lathander triggered such fear, and what he may report back to his group.

## Relationships
- **Bone-Necklace Orc:** Superior who reprimanded him during the ambush.
- **Valphine:** Fled in terror upon recognizing her; she pursued but lost him.
- **Soma:** Was bitten and wounded by Soma in bear form.
