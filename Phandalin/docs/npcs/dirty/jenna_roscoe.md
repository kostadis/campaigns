# Jenna Roscoe

## Identity
- Council representative for the Lords' Alliance in Phandalin
- First appeared at a Phandalin council meeting; the party encountered her during discussions about the Kraken Society

## Personality & Motivations
- Appears politically savvy and pragmatic, willing to suggest compromising principles for financial gain
- Publicly dismissive and minimizing — characterized the Kraken Society as a harmless social club for wealthy elites, though her composure under questioning raised suspicion
- Possibly concealing deeper knowledge of or ties to the Kraken Society

## History with the Party
- **Council Meeting:** Downplayed the Kraken Society as a benign organization. Valphine sensed she was withholding information, suggesting hidden knowledge or connection to the group.
- **Pre-Departure (Mountain's Toe):** Approached Brewbarry privately and suggested he could fund his music studio by abandoning his principles and "selling out." Brewbarry refused, asserting he would do things his own way.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Phandalin
- **Active Plans:** Unknown; her proposal to Brewbarry may indicate she is actively recruiting or cultivating leverage over party members
- **Known vs. Hidden:** The party knows she is a Lords' Alliance representative who minimizes the Kraken Society. What she actually knows about the Kraken Society — and why — remains unknown.

## Relationships
- **Lords' Alliance:** Official faction affiliation; serves as their council seat in Phandalin
- **Kraken Society:** Suspected hidden connection; nature unknown
- **Brewbarry (party):** Made an unsolicited offer to help him compromise his values for money; rebuffed
- **Valphine (party):** Valphine detected deception or concealment from her during the council meeting
