# Giles Slipper-Shine

## Identity
- **Role:** Villager, lay religious enthusiast
- **Faction:** Informal followers of an unorthodox Lathanderite interpretation
- **First Appearance:** Approached Valphine at the tavern alongside Brin Bundlewine

## Personality & Motivations
- Shares Brin Bundlewine's fervent enthusiasm for a heterodox reading of Lathander as a god of **purifying solar pain**
- Appears earnest and community-minded, willing to contribute personal labor to a cause he believes in

## History with the Party
- Accompanied Brin Bundlewine in approaching Valphine at the tavern to discuss founding a new chapel
- Agreed to participate in the renovation of Tresendar Manor into a temple; signed on to provide **free labor**, with 25 gp from the party covering materials

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Village (tavern, at time of agreement)
- **Active Plans:** Committed to the Tresendar Manor renovation project
- **What the party knows:** He is a willing laborer and true believer in the solar pain doctrine
- **What remains hidden:** Nothing specifically noted — motivations appear straightforward

## Relationships
- **Brin Bundlewine:** Close associate; the two approached the party together and share the same religious outlook
- **Valphine:** Made the chapel agreement directly with them

None recorded.
