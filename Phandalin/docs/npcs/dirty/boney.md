# Boney (formerly Barney)

## Identity
- **Role:** Undead skeletal horse; party sidekick/mount
- **Faction:** Formerly in service to Lady Alagondar; now attached to the party
- **First Appearance:** Discovered by Soma as skeletal remains in a southern niche of the Dragonbarrow tunnel; rose as undead when approached

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Drive:** Loyalty — was brought back from the dead to serve Lady Alagondar and transferred that devotion to the party without hesitation once convinced Soma was her reincarnation
- Cheerful and good-natured despite his grim appearance; accepted the renaming from Barney to Boney happily and made self-deprecating humor about his undead state (remarked that "irregularity" is no longer a problem for him)
- Mournful about the loss of his mane and physical features, but not dwelling — he moves forward

## History with the Party
1. **Dragonbarrow (Session 1 encounter):** Soma discovered his remains; he rose and initially mistook Soma for Lady Alagondar. The party convinced him Soma was her reincarnation. He revealed he had been killed by Will-o'-wisps before completing his guard duty.
2. **Renamed:** Party persuaded him to go by "Boney" rather than Barney; he accepted happily.
3. **Will-o'-wisp Combat:** Confirmed the wisps' danger from personal experience — they had killed him previously. Party defeated the wisps.
4. **Key Intelligence:** Provided the directional clue that the sword was buried "across the way" from where Lady Alagondar rested, pointing the party north.
5. **Sword Discovery:** Present in the northern chamber when Valphine claimed the Dragon Slayer Sword from atop the dragon skull — the exact location he had predicted. Watched with quiet amusement.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Dragonbarrow, with the party following the Dragon Slayer Sword recovery
- **Status:** Active party companion; informally bonded with Valphine
- **No known hidden agenda** — appears genuinely loyal and content in his new role

## Relationships
- **Soma:** Initially mistook for Lady Alagondar; Soma used Speak with Animals to communicate and broker the relationship
- **Valphine:** Primary bond formed after the sword discovery; Boney is described as having bonded with her specifically
- **Lady Alagondar (deceased):** Former master; Boney's entire existence was oriented around serving her
- **Will-o'-wisps:** Killed Boney in his first life; described them as evil and not talkative

## Arc Score Events
- No arc score changes noted in source material
