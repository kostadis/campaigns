# King Korboz

## Identity
- **Role:** Co-ruler (senior king) of Gnomengarde, dual monarchy shared with King Gnercli
- **Faction:** Gnomengarde
- **First Appearance:** The party encountered him confined to his room in the Gnomengarde cave system, in a state of active hysteria

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Have the shapeshifter threat taken seriously; be believed and respected as senior king
- Paranoid and volatile, prone to outbursts — immediately accused Vukradin of being a shapeshifter upon hearing his name. Feels chronically dismissed by his co-ruler Gnercli and is deeply sensitive about his seniority and authority. His madness makes him difficult to deal with but his fears appear to have genuine basis.

## History with the Party
- **First contact:** Found hysterical and confined to his room; accused Vukradin of being a shapeshifter on introduction
- **Vukradin's questioning:** Vukradin probed the tension between the two kings by raising the question of seniority; Korboz erupted, asserting his senior status and demanding preferential treatment
- **Confrontation with Gnercli:** Shouted at Gnercli, *"That you didn't believe existed!"* — publicly airing the rift over the shapeshifter threat and significantly destabilizing their political relationship

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Confined to his room in Gnomengarde, hysterical
- **Active situation:** Inventors Fibblestib and Dabbledob are focused on finding a cure for his madness
- **What the party knows:** He believes a shapeshifter is terrorizing the caves and is responsible for the disappearances of gnomes Warren and Orrin
- **What remains hidden:** The true nature and extent of his madness — whether it is a symptom of genuine supernatural influence or purely psychological is unconfirmed

## Relationships
- **King Gnercli (co-ruler):** Long-standing friction; Gnercli has refused to believe his claims about the shapeshifter; relationship publicly destabilized by Vukradin's intervention
- **Fibblestib & Dabbledob:** The two inventors are devoted to curing him; his primary caretakers
- **Vukradin (party):** Immediately accused him of being a shapeshifter; Vukradin subsequently exploited his resentment toward Gnercli to widen the political rift

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease (trust/stability):** Vukradin deliberately provoked Korboz's grievances about seniority, escalating the fracture between the two kings
