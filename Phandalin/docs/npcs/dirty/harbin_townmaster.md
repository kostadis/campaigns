# Harbin Wester

## Identity
- **Role:** Townmaster of Phandalin
- **Faction:** Aligned with Lord Neverember's interests
- **First Appearance:** Approached by the party regarding payment for completed work

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Maintain his position, avoid conflict, satisfy Lord Neverember's expectations, and keep his coin purse intact.
- Cowardly and easily flustered — initially mistook Vukradin for a dragon. Capitulates under pressure but only to the minimum degree necessary. Dismissive of supernatural concerns he finds inconvenient.

## History with the Party
- **Payment Dispute:** Refused additional coin beyond the original agreement, citing Neverember's arrangement allowing adventurers to claim monster treasure. Buckled under Adabra's pressure and offered free room and board at the Stonehill Inn as a compromise, blaming bandit activity for his lack of funds.
- **Lighthouse Discussion:** Intercepted by Vukradin the following morning before reaching his office. Dismissed the evil rumors surrounding the cursed lighthouse and firmly stated that Lord Neverember would prefer the curse removed rather than the structure destroyed. Directed the party to seek out **Ser Kaelen** at Barthen's Provisions before departing town.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Left Phandalin; destination unknown.
- **Active Plans:** Referred the party to Ser Kaelen, suggesting Neverember's interests in the lighthouse situation are being managed through intermediaries.
- **What the Party Knows:** He reports to Lord Neverember and will not sanction destroying the lighthouse. He is cash-poor (or claims to be) due to bandit activity.
- **What Remains Hidden:** The true extent of his arrangement with Neverember; whether his claims of financial hardship are genuine.

## Relationships
- **Lord Neverember:** Operates under his authority; defers to his preferences on matters of local policy and property.
- **Ser Kaelen:** Directed the party to this figure, suggesting an existing connection or awareness of their role.
- **Adabra:** Pressured him successfully into providing inn accommodations; he is likely wary of her.
- **Vukradin:** Physically intimidating to Harbin; intercepted him in the street and received dismissive but compliant responses.
