# The Chief Accountant

## Identity
- **Role:** Former accountant/administrator of the ancient dwarven fortress; current status is that of a corrupted, possibly undead or spectral entity
- **Faction:** None current; formerly in service to the fortress and its operations
- **First Appearance:** Manifested at the end of the battle against six out-of-phase dwarven figures on the upper floor of the fortress

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Balance the books, avoid punishment from "the auditor," and secure her own freedom
- Presents with a grandmother's warmth undercut by screeching static and erratic, glitching behavior. Her speech blends mundane accounting obsessions (ledgers, depreciation, regulators) with genuine distress and paranoia. She is volatile but not purely hostile—her panic over harming Valphine revealed a capacity for fear and remorse beneath the madness.

## History with the Party
1. **First Contact:** Appeared after the party defeated the out-of-phase dwarven combatants. Immediately fixated on Valphine, calling her "sister of the sun" and accusing her of carrying "the scent of bleach and betrayal."
2. **Hostile Engagement:** Unleashed a supernatural wail that knocked both Valphine and Vukradin unconscious.
3. **Panic and Flight:** Upon seeing Valphine collapse, shifted abruptly into distress—frantically attempted to stabilize her using blue-white static energy, expressing terror that "the auditor" would liquidate her for closing Valphine's account. Fled incorporeally through a closed door into an adjacent room.
4. **Return and Deal:** After the party rested, she cautiously returned and disclosed the history of the fortress. Offered the machine's override code in exchange for her freedom and the party's silence. The party accepted. She provided directions to the machine room on the lower level.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The fortress; fled after providing the override code and directions to the machine room
- **Active Plans:** Presumably pursuing her freedom per the agreed terms; no further known operations
- **What the Party Knows:** She was the fortress's accountant, was killed or destroyed by Aletra after raising concerns, and has been in a corrupted state since. She fears an entity called "the auditor."
- **What Remains Hidden:** The true nature of "the auditor" and what it would mean for her to be "liquidated." The full extent of her current existence—undead, echo, data construct—is unclear.

## Relationships
- **Aletra (Satora family, fourth daughter):** Aletra struck the deal that brought the malfunctioning machine to the fortress. When the Chief Accountant raised concerns about the books, Aletra laughed at her and likely killed her. Deep grievance.
- **Valphine:** Fixated on her as "sister of the sun"; volatile mix of accusation and desperate protectiveness. The connection to Aletra appears to be the source of both hostility and fascination.
- **The Auditor (unknown entity):** A figure she fears above all else—believes this entity will punish or destroy her if Valphine's "account" is closed on her watch.
- **The King:** Mentioned as a third party Aletra exploited—his jealousy of the Lionshield Coster's multi-dimensional merchant operations made him susceptible to her promises.
- **Lionshield Coster:** Referenced as a multi-dimensional merchant operation whose revenue the king envied; no direct relationship established.
