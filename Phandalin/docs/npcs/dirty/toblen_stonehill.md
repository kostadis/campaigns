# Toblen Stonehill

## Identity
- **Role:** Proprietor of the Stonehill Inn, Phandalin
- **Faction:** None (civilian townsperson)
- **Background:** Native of Triboar; originally came to Phandalin to prospect, switched to innkeeping
- **First Appearance:** 8/1 Taraksh — party visited the Stonehill Inn; Toblen struck a deal with Vukardin for meals in exchange for entertainment

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Running a successful inn, keeping his clientele happy, seeing Phandalin remain safe and stable
- Warm and hospitable; quick to negotiate mutually beneficial arrangements rather than offer charity
- Pragmatic about the region's dangers — aware of orc raids and white dragon sightings but not panicked
- Holds no grudges over past wars; accepted Brewbarry without hesitation, remarking that "the wars of the past are the past"

## History with the Party
- **8/1 Taraksh:** Agreed to feed the party in exchange for Vukardin playing popular songs from Waterdeep and Neverwinter. After several songs, invited Vukardin to play something personal and rewarded him with a complimentary beer. Quietly warned Vukardin about white dragon sightings nearby. Expressed concern about orc raids from the mountains and hope that Neverwinter would send aid, while noting the most recent major threat had actually been barbarian hordes. Accepted Brewbarry without hostility.
- **1/1 Taraksh:** Upon the party's return from the dwarven ruins, again persuaded Vukardin to play popular music for the inn's clientele.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Stonehill Inn, Phandalin
- **Active Operations:** Running the inn day-to-day; leveraging Vukardin as informal entertainment to please customers
- **What the Party Knows:** Toblen is aware of white dragon sightings and orc raid concerns; he keeps his ear to local troubles
- **What Remains Hidden:** Nothing specific flagged in notes

## Relationships
- **Vukardin:** Recurring positive dynamic; Toblen consistently recruits him to play for the inn's guests and has shown personal appreciation for his original music
- **Brewbarry:** Accepted without hostility; no deeper relationship established
- **Phandalin / Neverwinter:** Loyal to the town; hopes for outside military support against regional threats
