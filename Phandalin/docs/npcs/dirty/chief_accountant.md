# Chief Accountant

## Identity
- Role: Chief Accountant of the fortress (name unknown)
- Faction: Formerly associated with the fortress and, implicitly, Aletra's operations
- First appearance: Encountered by the party after the spatial distortions in the fortress ceased

## Personality & Motivations
- Core drive: Maintaining accurate, settled accounts — a compulsion that apparently persisted beyond death
- Demonstrated traits: Methodical and high-strung; capable of a ten-minute screaming fit over an incorrect bill, yet able to achieve genuine peace once she considered her ledgers closed

## History with the Party
1. The party encountered her as a spirit after the spatial distortions disappeared; she had grown calm once the disturbances ended.
2. She was informed that Aletra had fled and the machine had imploded, visibly relieving her.
3. The party presented her with an interplanetary bill for 42,000 gold pieces, triggering an extended (~10-minute) outburst over its outrageous and incorrect nature.
4. Once her outburst subsided, she declared "The book is closed," and departed peacefully.

## Current Status
- **Deceased.** Her spirit has fully departed; she is gone permanently.
- Her former quarters contained a dusty bedroom with moldy mattresses and a soot-stained fireplace. A secret door hidden within the fireplace led to a small side room.
- The iron chest in that room held a **dread helm** and **gauntlets of ogre power** — her personal possessions, now available to the party.

## Relationships
- **Aletra:** Associated with her operations; the news of Aletra's flight visibly relieved the Chief Accountant, suggesting the relationship was a source of stress or obligation.
- **The Party:** Brief but memorable — she screamed at them for ten minutes before finding peace.
