# Sylvine Wintermoon

## Identity
- **Role:** Con artist / treasure hunter. Arrived with a named companion (Runa) and a force of veterans as muscle.
- **Faction:** None recorded. Operates with sister Runa and hired fighters.
- **First Appearance:** Arrived at Icespire Hold shortly after the party's battle with Cryovain, presenting herself and Runa as the rightful owners of the dragon's hoard.

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Acquire Cryovain's hoard by any means necessary — persuasion first, violence second.
- **Traits:** Sylvine is a calculated operator who prepares layered contingencies. She deploys emotional performance (wounded pride, religious invocation, theatrical injury) as tools and drops them the instant they fail — Soma noted the shift took less than a second. She is not rattled by rejection; she pivots without hesitation.
- **Methodology:** Researches her marks carefully. She had studied Vukradin's reputation as a principled bard who returns stolen treasure to its owners, and crafted her approach specifically to exploit that reputation.

## History with the Party
1. **Icespire Hold (Post-Battle):** Arrived with Runa and veteran bandits shortly after the dragon was defeated. Opened by asking whether Vukradin was the bard known for returning stolen treasure, then launched into a prepared performance: invoking the gods, calling Vukradin a saint, and framing all 3,000 gold of the dragon treasure as already spiritually returned with the handover a mere formality. She claimed the treasure belonged rightfully to her and her sister, presenting herself and Runa as the legitimate owners.
2. Soma identified the approach immediately as rehearsed and calculated. Vukradin rejected her outright, calling her a liar and a cheat and ordering her to leave.
3. Sylvine performed wounded pride and theatrical injury — "I am hurt! You are a thief!" — for less than a second before dropping the act entirely, revealing it was never genuine. The tell, in Soma's assessment, that the injury was never real.
4. She immediately pivoted to violence, announcing "Second plan — we kill them all," escalating to open combat and leading her veterans into the fight.
5. The ensuing fight went badly for her side. Her veteran muscle was killed or fled. **Sylvine's own final status was not confirmed.**

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Icespire Hold, at the conclusion of the ambush attempt.
- **Final Status: UNCONFIRMED.** The veterans were killed or routed; whether Sylvine was captured, killed, or escaped is not recorded.
- **Active Plans:** Unknown — her two-stage plan (con, then violence) was fully exhausted at Icespire Hold.

## Relationships
- **Runa:** Her sister. Traveled together and jointly claimed ownership of Cryovain's hoard. Runa's own role in the encounter is not detailed in the notes.
- **Vukradin:** Targeted him specifically by reputation after researching him as a bard known for returning stolen treasure. Now openly hostile after he rejected and exposed her.
- **Soma:** Soma read her accurately throughout the encounter; Sylvine likely knows she was seen through.
- **Veteran bandits (muscle):** Hired fighters who accompanied her to Icespire Hold. Defeated — killed or fled.
