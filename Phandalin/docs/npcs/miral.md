# Miral

## Identity
- Sea elf; original owner of the opalescent conch recovered from Moesko
- Faction affiliation unknown; connected to coastal/undersea territory near Leilon
- First appeared as a ghost when the party presented her recovered conch to her
- Also described as an undead creature prior to her manifestation
- *Also known as: Miral the Self.*

*Also known as: Miral the Self.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Sought rest and resolution — her spirit was bound until her conch was returned
- Cryptic but deliberate in her warnings; concerned with threats she perceived gathering below Leilon
- Generous disposition toward the party, having intended to retrieve a treasure (the halberd from the Star Crossed Lover) on their behalf

## History with the Party
1. Miral was identified by both Valphine and Soma as the rightful owner of the opalescent conch the party recovered from Moesko; she was referenced as an undead creature whose current whereabouts and circumstances by which Moesko came to possess the conch were unknown.
2. Upon presenting the conch, her ghost manifested and confirmed that Moesko was responsible for her death (referred to as "her undoing").
3. Revealed Moesko had not acted alone — external shadows had corrupted his devotion to Talos, transforming it into something "foreign and calculating."
4. Issued a warning: *"Beware, surface dweller... the true storm gathers not in the sky, but in the depths below Leilon"*
5. When shown the color-changing magical cloak, Miral — referred to briefly as a giant crab in Soma's account, suggesting a transformed or associated form — indicated the halberd recovered from the Star Crossed Lover was a treasure she had meant to retrieve for the party.
6. Her spirit departed after receiving the conch, implying she found rest.

## Current Status
- **Last known:** Spirit departed; presumably at rest
- **Active plans:** None — her arc appears concluded
- **Hidden information:** The nature of the "external shadows" that manipulated Moesko remains unexplained; the full threat gathering beneath Leilon is unresolved

## Relationships
- **Moesko:** Her killer; she confirmed his culpability but emphasized he was manipulated by outside forces
- **The Party:** Benevolent toward them — intended to gift them the halberd from the Star Crossed Lover
