# Brin Bundlewine

## Identity
- Role: Villager, laborer, and construction worker; aspiring chapel builder
- Faction: Informal followers of an unorthodox Lathanderite interpretation
- First appearance: Approached Valphine at the tavern alongside Giles Slipper-Shine

*Also known as: Brin.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Deeply drawn to the idea of a drow who worships Lathander through the purifying pain of the sun — fervent, perhaps zealous spiritual curiosity
- Eager and idealistic; offered his labor for free, asking only materials costs
- Enthusiastic and easily swept up in religious fervor; demonstrates an uncritical, even gleeful acceptance of extreme events — including ritual blinding, miraculous healing, and public display of a severed head
- Pale-skinned and sunburned, which adds an ironic physical note to his sun-focused devotion
- Believed in the purifying pain of the sun and was eager to establish a chapel dedicated to this interpretation of the sun god
- Practices self-flagellation and ritual burning as acts of devotion; bears scars and sunburn patterns consistent with this practice
- Displays the beatific radiance of a convert who has proven something to himself through physical suffering

## History with the Party
1. Approached Valphine at the local tavern after hearing rumors of a sun-worshipping drow, accompanied by Giles Slipper-Shine.
2. Proposed renovating Tresendar Manor into the **Temple of Lathander's Searing Pain of Justice**. Struck a deal: 25 gp for materials, labor provided free of charge.
3. Working on the church reconstruction in Phandalin; witnessed Valphine's sermon and the unintended sacrament of ritual blinding and healing — was ecstatic about it.
4. Reacted positively to the placement of a Kraken Society cultist's severed head on a pike outside the church.
5. Conducted a public religious parade through Phandalin alongside Giles as a fundraiser on behalf of Valphine's Temple of Lathander. Appeared scarred and sunburnt from self-flagellation, smiling beatifically. Valphine noted faint burn marks tracing his forearms. The townspeople watching appeared unsettled.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Phandalin, working on the church reconstruction and the Tresendar Manor renovation project
- **Active project:** Conversion of Tresendar Manor into the Temple of Lathander's Searing Pain of Justice; conducting public religious activities and fundraising on behalf of the temple
- **What the party knows:** The deal terms, his enthusiasm for the project, his zealous approval of Valphine's extreme religious events, and his practice of self-flagellation and ritual burning as acts of devotion
- **What remains hidden:** His deeper motivations, any prior religious history, whether he has the actual skills to complete the renovation, and the full extent of his devotional practices

## Relationships
- **Valphine (PC):** Contractor/patron relationship; Brin is working on her behalf. Apparent admirer and true believer in Valphine's unorthodox ministry. She regards him with the satisfaction of a priestess watching doctrine take physical form.
- **Giles Slipper-Shine:** Close associate; the two approached the party together and share the same religious outlook. Fellow construction worker and co-participant in public religious activities.
