# Marian

## Identity

Marian is an Emerald Enclave agent who met the party at the Stonehill Tavern. She was the second named Enclave contact to meet with the party, introduced alongside Thomas.

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations

- Demonstrated a measured, sympathetic worldview — framed Lyra's controversial actions charitably, emphasizing earned trust and decisive leadership rather than recklessness
- Perceptive and observant; noticed details about Valphine's behavior that the party apparently overlooked
- Characterized Lyra as a good boss and leader who saw an opportunity, acted on past experience of being right, and trusted herself when others in the Enclave were paralyzed by argument
- Goals beyond her Enclave affiliation are unknown

## History with the Party

- Met the party at the Stonehill Tavern alongside fellow Enclave agent Thomas
- Offered a more sympathetic characterization of Lyra than Thomas did, describing her as a good boss and leader who saw an opportunity and acted on past experience of being right when others in the Enclave were deadlocked by argument — noting that Lyra had earned that trust before and simply "wasn't right this time"
- Questioned Valphine about whether her apparent practice of harming herself and then using healing spells was a personal ritual, expressing visible surprise at Valphine's confusion over the question
- Declined to join the party and departed with Thomas

## Current Status

- Last known location: departing Stonehill Tavern with Thomas
- Active plans: unknown
- **Known to party:** Enclave affiliation, sympathetic stance toward Lyra, perceptive observer
- **Hidden:** Her personal motivations, what she intends to do with her observation about Valphine, and whether she'll report it to anyone

## Relationships

- **Thomas:** Fellow Enclave agent; departed together; exact dynamic between them not established
- **Lyra:** Speaks of her with evident respect and loyalty despite acknowledging her mistake; describes her as a good boss
- **Valphine (party):** Noticed and questioned behavior that Valphine herself didn't recognize as unusual — nature of that interest is unresolved
