# Jenna Roscoe

## Identity
- **Role:** Agent of the Lord's Alliance
- **First Appearance:** Approached Vukradin at a tavern, having sought him out deliberately; disarmed herself at the table as a show of good faith

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Recruiting talented or influential individuals to the Lord's Alliance's interests; advancing Lord Neverember's reach and agenda; acquiring and redistributing wealth from monster hoards through adventuring networks
- Polished and professionally guarded, but her composure cracked noticeably when Vukradin refused her offer — suggesting she is not entirely immune to genuine conviction
- Holds contempt for those she considers beneath her attention (notably Brewbarry), while reserving real warmth and respect for those who earn it
- Philosophically committed to the idea that redistributing adventuring wealth serves the common good — a position she holds firmly even when challenged

## History with the Party

**First Meeting (Tavern):** Jenna sought out Vukradin specifically, arriving armed but disarming at the table. She offered to leverage Lord's Alliance connections to elevate his musical career, including performances before Waterdeep and Neverwinter nobility and potentially Lord Neverember himself. She was caught off-guard by his loose affiliation with the Order of the Gauntlet rather than full membership, and further unsettled by his stated goal of spreading music to everyone regardless of faction. When Vukradin challenged her wealth-redistribution philosophy and refused her offer on moral grounds, she was visibly affected but admitted open admiration for him. She clashed with Brewbarry, mispronouncing his name and exchanging sharp words with him.

**Phandalin Council Meeting (Post-Kraken Society Encounter):** Jenna attended the council and dismissed the Kraken Society as a harmless social club for wealthy elites. Valphine sensed she was withholding significantly more than she revealed on the subject.

**Post-Axeholm (Phandalin):** Made a brief appearance and suggested to Brewbarry that he could easily fund his music studio by abandoning his principles. He declined.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Phandalin
- **Active:** Continuing Lord's Alliance business; interest in Vukradin appears unresolved
- **What the Party Knows:** She is a Lord's Alliance agent who wanted to recruit Vukradin; she has shown contempt for Brewbarry; she downplayed the Kraken Society
- **Hidden:** Her true knowledge of the Kraken Society; the full scope of her mission or who she reports to; what she actually wanted from Vukradin beyond the stated offer

## Relationships
- **Vukradin:** Genuine admiration, even after rejection; her professional mask softened in his presence. He remains a person of interest to her
- **Brewbarry:** Openly contemptuous; has attempted to tempt him to sell out on at least one occasion
- **Lord Neverember / Lord's Alliance:** Her patron and affiliation; acts as an agent on their behalf
- **Kraken Society:** Claims to consider them harmless; Valphine suspects otherwise
