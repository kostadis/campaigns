# Dabblebob

## Identity
- Role: Gnome inventor; partner/rival to Fiddlestib
- Faction: Gnome settlement
- First appearance: Encountered alongside Fiddlestib at the gnome settlement

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven by invention and a competitive desire to one-up Fiddlestib
- Became excited about constructing something from mimic remains
- Politically minded in an impractical way — proposed expanding the number of kings to enable majority voting, seemingly more to counter Fiddlestib's constitutional proposal than out of genuine civic conviction
- Easily absorbed by tangential debates (e.g., voting box design)

## History with the Party
- Met the party at the gnome settlement alongside Fiddlestib
- Proposed the idea of building a construct from mimic remains when the opportunity arose
- Engaged in the settlement's political debate, countering Fiddlestib's constitutional monarchy idea with a multi-king voting system; both then became sidetracked arguing over voting box design

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Gnome settlement
- **Current activity:** Arguing with Fiddlestib over voting box design
- **Hidden information:** None noted in available records

## Relationships
- **Fiddlestib:** Partner and rival inventor; the two are in active competition intellectually and politically
- **The Party:** Encountered briefly; relationship depth not yet established
