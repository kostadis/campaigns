# Dabblebob

## Identity
- Gnome inventor; partner of Fiddlestib
- First appeared alongside Fiddlestib after the party overheard (or were present for) a political argument among kings

## Personality & Motivations
- Pragmatically minded on governance — favors expanding the number of kings to enable a majority vote, in contrast to Fiddlestib's bicameral approach
- Becomes deeply absorbed in intellectual debates; once engaged in an argument, he is difficult to extract from it

## History with the Party
- Overheard a political dispute among kings and weighed in, countering Fiddlestib's bicameral proposal with his own "more kings = votable majority" solution
- Became locked in a heated debate with Fiddlestib over whether a tamper-proof voting box is theoretically possible; still absorbed in this argument at last sighting

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Wherever the political argument took place; still mid-debate with Fiddlestib
- **Active:** Consumed by the tamper-proof voting box argument — no other known plans or operations
- **Hidden from party:** Nothing notable flagged in notes

## Relationships
- **Fiddlestib** — Inventor partner and current debate opponent; relationship appears collaborative but contentious on matters of design and governance theory
- **The kings** — Indirect; responded to their political dispute without apparent personal stake
