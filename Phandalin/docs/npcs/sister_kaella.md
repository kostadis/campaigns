# Sister Kaella

## Identity
- **Role/Faction:** Former operative and intelligence agent of the Carver; apparent religious figure (referenced sacraments, invokes God)
- **First Appearance:** Stumbled into the clearing outside the Carver's chamber — armor slashed, cloak gone, barely standing — and collapsed before the Dragon Slayer Sword to deliver an urgent warning to the party

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Survival, above all else. Her loyalty to the Carver degraded entirely once he accused her of intelligence failures and her survival became contingent on his death.
- **Personality:** Composed and calculating under extreme pressure — her first conscious words after near-death were a calm actuarial assessment of what Jax would have done to her. She is pragmatic about moral ambiguity and openly acknowledges operating in gray spaces. She has a long-term orientation: as long as she is alive, she considers herself to have options.

## History with the Party

1. **The Clearing:** Appeared wounded and breathless with an urgent warning — the Carver had become a hollow, maddened shell, was marching on Icespire Hold that night, and intended to possess the white dragon to burn Phandalin. Mid-warning, Vukradin paralyzed her with Hold Person. Soma and Valphine prevented her execution; she was knocked unconscious with non-lethal force. The party immediately recognized the problem: she alone knew the location of Icespire Hold.

2. **Interrogation and Disclosure:** Valphine revived her through physical pressure rather than magical healing. Kaella confirmed the Carver's madness and provided directions to Icespire Hold without requiring further persuasion. She disclosed that she had made a private prior arrangement with Valphine, bypassing Vukradin deliberately — stating he had difficulty operating in morally gray environments and was therefore not a viable contact. Vukradin told her she would do well in prison.

3. **Transfer to Corbin:** Left in Corbin's custody when the party departed for Icespire Hold. Vukradin authorized Corbin to beat her to one hit point and no further. She began screaming the moment the party walked away, calling Corbin a lunatic. Her stated preference for imprisonment was Axholm.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Camp; in Corbin's custody
- **Active:** Screaming at Corbin as of last contact
- **What the Party Knows:** She was a Carver operative who turned when her survival depended on it; she had a private arrangement with Valphine; she provided genuine directions to Icespire Hold; she wants to be imprisoned at Axholm
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full nature of her prior arrangement with Valphine; the extent of her knowledge of the Carver's organization, plans, or other assets; what she actually intends once in custody or if freed

## Relationships
- **Valphine:** Private prior arrangement, confirmed still in force during interrogation. Valphine is her functional contact in the party and the reason she is alive.
- **Vukradin:** Open antagonism. He paralyzed her, authorized her to be beaten to within one hit point, and told her she'd do well in prison. She assessed him as incapable of operating in morally gray environments — a calculated statement, not an insult.
- **Soma:** Intervened to prevent her execution alongside Valphine.
- **Corbin:** Currently her custodian. She was screaming at him as the party left. Valphine has been teaching Corbin certain sacraments — relevance to this relationship unclear.
- **The Carver:** Former employer. Loyalty broke when he blamed her for intelligence failures after the party killed his muscle. She calculated he would have had her tortured to death had Jax won.

- **Valphine → Kaella (positive/neutral):** Revived her, confirmed their arrangement, kept her alive
- **Soma → Kaella (neutral/positive):** Intervened against her execution
