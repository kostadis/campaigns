# Sister Kaella

## Identity
- **Role/Faction:** Former agent of the Carver; now a defector whose survival depends on the Carver's death
- **First Appearance:** Chapter 32 — stumbled into the clearing outside Dragonbarrow, injured and cloakless, delivering an urgent warning to the party

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Survival; the Carver's death (now personally necessary for her own safety after being blamed for intelligence failures following the party's elimination of his muscle)
- Pragmatic and calculating — she approached Valphine individually rather than the full party, having assessed Vukradin as poorly suited to morally gray situations. She is frank about self-interest and makes reasoned cases for her own treatment.
- Demonstrates awareness of individual party members and their dynamics, suggesting she has been observing them for some time.
- Was building toward a pointed remark about drow clerics of Lathander and the significance of darkness and light, directed specifically at Valphine — content unknown, as Vukradin interrupted.

## History with the Party
1. **Pre-Chapter 32:** Referenced as a contact who was supposed to inform the party about the Carver's situation but had never made contact. Had a prior arrangement specifically with Valphine.
2. **Chapter 32 (First Encounter):** Stumbled into the clearing outside Dragonbarrow — armor slashed, cloakless, gasping — and began delivering an urgent warning. Vukradin paralyzed her mid-warning with an enchantment. Soma and Valphine persuaded him not to kill her outright. The party knocked her unconscious with non-lethal blows.
3. **Chapter 32 (Interrogation):** Bound and revived via controlled medical pressure (no healing magic). Expressed relief that the party — not Jax — had found her, noting Jax's victory would have meant death by torture. Confirmed her defection: the Carver blames her for intelligence failures and her survival now requires his death. Provided directions to Icespire Hold. Requested transfer to Axholm prison. Left in Corbin's custody at one hit point, immediately screaming and calling him a lunatic.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Outside Dragonbarrow, in Corbin's custody
- **Condition:** Alive, captive, held at one hit point per the party's instructions
- **Active Situation:** The party has departed for Icespire Hold, leaving her behind with Corbin
- **What the Party Knows:** She was a Carver agent, is now a defector, provided directions to Icespire Hold, and requested Axholm prison
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full content of her remark to Valphine about drow clerics of Lathander and darkness/light — she was cut off before finishing

## Relationships
- **The Carver:** Former handler/employer; now actively wants him dead as her own survival depends on it. He blames her for intelligence failures.
- **Valphine:** Had a prior arrangement with Valphine specifically; approached her rather than the full party. Directed a pointed, unfinished observation at her regarding drow clerics of Lathander.
- **Vukradin:** Distrusted her from the outset as a potential Carver agent — correctly, as it turns out. She identified him as someone who "struggles with morally gray environments," which angered him. He paralyzed her mid-warning and was prepared to kill her.
- **Soma:** Interceded alongside Valphine to prevent Vukradin from killing her outright.
- **Corbin:** Assigned as her keeper; she immediately identified him as a lunatic and began screaming.
- **Jax:** Referenced as someone whose victory over the party would have guaranteed her death by torture — suggesting Jax operates under the Carver and would have had motive to eliminate her.
