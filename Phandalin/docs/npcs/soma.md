# Soma

## Identity
- **Role/Faction:** Naturalist druid; member of the Emerald Enclave (naturalist faction)
- **First Appearance:** The party met Soma during the battle against the mimic; she fought alongside them and confirmed her druidic philosophy mid-combat when questioned by Vukradin.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Uphold the natural order by opposing constructs, extra-planar entities, and unnatural magical creations; investigate the withering grove near Whisper Woods; complete whatever personal mission she has left before time runs out.
- Soma is pragmatic and unsentimental — she loots gold without guilt but shows genuine ethical reflection when challenged directly. She holds strong convictions about the natural order but is not above being humbled by gaps in her own knowledge. She is aging and privately aware that her time is limited, lending her a quiet urgency beneath her composed exterior.

## History with the Party
1. **Mimic Battle:** Soma's poison attack failed against the mimic. She explained her naturalist philosophy to Vukradin, rejecting his framing of Aasimar and Tiefling as celestially or infernally touched. After the fight, she looted the room and kept quiet when Fiddlestib and Dabblebob discussed building a construct from the mimic's remains.
2. **Mission to Save Adabra:** Soma wildshaped into a spider, killed a bandit with poison, and nearly kept a looted druidic scroll before Vukradin's moral prodding led her to reconsider.
3. **Umbrage Hill / Manticore:** Soma sacrificed her conjured familiar as a distraction, enraging the manticore. She encouraged Vukradin to attempt his spell again; when it failed he blamed her, visibly hurting her. She helped calm Adabra and urged her to flee during the fight.
4. **Meeting Adabra:** Identifying as a naturalist unlocked Adabra's full trust. Adabra then exposed gaps in Soma's training (e.g., the manticore's diet), deflating her. Soma privately reflected that her year of training under Meril was incomplete and that she has little time left.
5. **Stonehill Inn:** Soma heard a rumor about the Neverwinter Wood that stirred homesickness. She listened to Corbin's backstory and privately connected his fall into banditry to her own transformative meeting with Meril.
6. **Road Back from Umbrage Hill:** Soma engaged with Adabra's philosophy on the Emerald Enclave schism, agreed with her worldview, and helped mediate tension between Adabra and Brewbarry over the word "barbarian."
7. **Private Commission from Adabra:** Adabra described a withering grove past Whisper Woods — twisted trees, wrong colors, too-many-eyed creatures with jerky movements — and linked it to interventionist activity in the Enclave. Soma agreed to investigate.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Traveling with the party along the Triboar Trail and high road toward the Tower of Storms.
- **Active Plans:** Investigating the withering grove past Whisper Woods as requested by Adabra.
- **What the Party Knows:** She is a naturalist druid, aging, trained for only one year under someone named Meril, and is following Vukradin partly at Meril's prompting.
- **Hidden:** The full nature of her personal urgency ("little time left"), the details of her history before Meril, and what exactly Meril's prompting entails.

## Relationships
- **Vukradin:** Traveling companion with a growing tab she owes him. Their dynamic is philosophically charged — he challenges her ethics, she pushes back on his poetic framing. His blame after the manticore fight genuinely wounded her.
- **Adabra:** Quickly developed mutual respect after Soma identified as a naturalist. Adabra has become an informal mentor, exposing gaps in Soma's education and entrusting her with the withering grove investigation.
- **Meril:** Off-screen mentor who trained Soma for one year. Soma's training is self-acknowledged as incomplete. Meril specifically prompted Soma to follow Vukradin.
- **Fiddlestib & Dabblebob:** No direct conflict, but Soma internally views their work as the seed of abomination-creation.
- **Corbin:** Soma sees a parallel between his story and her own past.

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease:** Vukradin blamed her for misplaced trust after the second failed spell on the manticore, and it visibly hurt her — suggests a stress or trust fracture in that relationship.
- **Potential Decrease (avoided):** Nearly kept the looted druidic scroll; reconsidered after Vukradin's challenge, demonstrating ethical self-correction.
- **Increase:** Adabra's full trust and engagement unlocked after Soma identified as a naturalist; Adabra entrusted her with the withering grove mission.
