# Gorthok

## Identity
- Role: Divine creature of Talos; sacred or bound boar
- Faction: Talosian (divine/storm-aligned)
- First appearance: Siege of Falcon's Lodge; encountered in the courtyard among the uncharmed six

## Personality & Motivations
- Not sapient in any demonstrated way — functions as a force of divine will rather than an independent actor
- Resistant to charm and mental influence; threw off Vukradin's Hypnosis working without apparent effort
- Embodied Talosian defiance: even after a killing blow, reconstituted and rose again before final death

## History with the Party
- **Falcon's Lodge (siege):** Gorthok stood at the center of the courtyard, flanks charged with static. He resisted Vukradin's Hypnosis and was counted among the uncharmed six.
- Struck Soma twice in rapid succession — the first blow folded her, the second ensured her fall.
- Soma landed meaningful hoof strikes on him; a Moonbeam dealt 29 damage (halved by lightning resistance), leaving him barely alive. A follow-up hoof strike from Soma killed him.
- Gorthok revived — described not as a natural rally but as a convulsion of stored divine will, analogous to *Revivify*. He rose after Soma's killing blow.
- The Moonbeam, still positioned over him, burned him again at the end of his turn. He did not rise a second time.
- Confirmed kill: **death by Moonbeam.** Both Soma and Vukradin named it.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Last known location: Falcon's Lodge courtyard.
- Did not rise after the Moonbeam's lingering burn. No indication of further revival.
- The party knows he revived once; whether a second revival was possible — or was simply not triggered — remains unaddressed.

## Relationships
- **Talos:** Divine connection; his revival and lightning attacks are framed as expressions of Talosian will rather than innate creature abilities
- **The party:** Downed Soma twice; killed by Soma and Moonbeam in return

- N/A — creature, not a sapient NPC
