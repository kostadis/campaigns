# Xanth (Xanthopoulos)

## Identity
- **Role:** Independent centaur ranger/scout; loosely affiliated with the Emerald Enclave (though critical of its current leadership)
- **First Appearance:** Encountered peacefully near Dragonbarrow burial mound; approached the party to share warnings and intelligence about the region

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Protecting the natural order of Neverwinter Wood and surrounding lands; opposing the Anchorites of Talos and the broader disruption caused by orcish displacement
- Warm, talkative, and generous — quick to offer help and express gratitude physically (kissed Soma's hand after healing). Carries himself with the dignity of someone who expects his full name to be respected, even if he's resigned to people using the short form.
- Deeply frustrated by the Emerald Enclave's ideological paralysis and relieved to find allies who share his urgency. Baffled by moral complexity around wealth and ownership — concepts like refusing a dragon's hoard are genuinely alien to him.

## History with the Party
1. **First contact (Dragonbarrow approach):** Approached peacefully; warned the party about will-o'-wisps haunting the burial mound at night. Shared intelligence about orcs and Anchorites of Talos performing dark rites in Neverwinter Wood, causing farmers to flee. Offered to guide the party to the standing stones after their business at Dragonbarrow. Recognized Valphine as a famous cleric of Lathander and praised her.
2. **Found bloodied outside Dragonbarrow (Ch. 32):** Discovered wounded in fading light after the party emerged, apparently attacked while waiting — likely caught in the aftermath of Jax's ambush.
3. **Healed and debrief:** Soma healed him; he introduced himself formally as Xanthopoulos and recognized Vukradin, addressing him in Centaurian as "Vucravinios." He delivered extensive regional intelligence: white dragon in the Icespire Mountains displacing orcs into the valleys; named the Carver's three lieutenants (Borg, Sister Kaella, Jax); confirmed Borg's death had reached him via the forest network. Reacted with visible emotion to Meril's Staff, stating he and Meril were old friends — and declared he would help Soma because Meril must know her.
4. **Departure:** Agreed to carry Vukradin and Valphine to the foothills near the gold mine. Vukradin asked him specifically as a centaur, not a horse — Xanth accepted the distinction with partial satisfaction. Parted ways at the foothills once his transport role was fulfilled.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The foothills near the gold mine; departed after dropping off Vukradin and Valphine
- **Active Plans:** No confirmed ongoing operations; had previously offered to guide the party to the Anchorites' standing stones — that offer may still stand
- **What the Party Knows:** His full name, his friendship with Meril, his intelligence network, his frustration with the Enclave, and his willingness to help
- **Hidden/Unknown:** His current whereabouts and whether he has resumed scouting the Anchorites' position independently

## Relationships
- **Soma:** Warm rapport; grateful for healing, kissed her hand. Trusts her in part because of her connection to Meril's Staff. Views her as a fellow pragmatist frustrated with Enclave politics.
- **Vukradin:** Knows him by name and reputation; addressed him formally in Centaurian. Puzzled by his ethics but not dismissive — appealed to Soma to help him understand Vukradin's thinking.
- **Valphine:** Recognized her as a famous cleric of Lathander; praised her courage and spoke approvingly of pain as purification.
- **Meril:** Old friends. The sight of Meril's Staff moved him visibly and was the deciding factor in his commitment to help the party.
- **The Carver / Lieutenants:** Informed about them through his regional intelligence network. Aware of Borg's death. Treats the Carver's operation as a serious threat to the natural order.
- **Emerald Enclave:** Member or associate, but deeply frustrated by internal debates over whether orcish displacement warrants intervention.
