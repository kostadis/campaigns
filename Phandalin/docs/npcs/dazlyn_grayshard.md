# Dazlyn Grayshard

## Identity
- **Role:** Shield dwarf prospector; business partner of Norbus Ironrune
- **Faction:** Independent (no known affiliation)
- **First Appearance:** The party encountered Dazlyn at ancient dwarven ruins in a canyon southwest of Phandalin, when they arrived to deliver a dragon warning.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Excavating and presumably profiting from the rediscovered dwarven ruins; clearing the site of dangers.
- Dazlyn is entrepreneurial and practical — he immediately offered the party sending stones as payment rather than fleeing when threatened by the unknown danger deeper in the temple. He comes across as warm and grateful, celebrating with Norbus by cooking a fine meal for the party after the orc threat was resolved.

## History with the Party
1. **First Contact:** Dazlyn and Norbus had spent months clearing rubble from a canyon ruin southwest of Phandalin, uncovering an old temple of Abbathor. When the party arrived with a dragon warning, Dazlyn briefed them on the temple's history — Abbathor destroyed it via earthquake and avalanche after deeming the priests' blood-and-gemstone sacrifices insufficient.
2. **The Orc Job:** Dazlyn hired the party with sending stones to deal with something frightening deeper in the temple, which turned out to be orcs. After the party cleared them out, Dazlyn and Norbus celebrated with a feast for the group.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The temple of Abbathor ruins, canyon southwest of Phandalin.
- **Active Plans:** Continuing excavation of the ruins now that the orc threat is eliminated.
- **Known to Party:** Temple history, the sending stones deal, and Dazlyn's partnership with Norbus.
- **Hidden from Party:** Unknown — no concealed information recorded in notes.

## Relationships
- **Norbus Ironrune:** Longstanding business partner; the two work and celebrate together closely.
- **The Party:** Friendly and grateful; paid them in sending stones and hosted them for a meal.
