# Corbin

## Identity
- **Role:** Former bandit; camp attendant and aide to the party
- **Faction:** Previously under Jax, who answered to the Carver; no current faction affiliation
- **First Appearance:** Fleeing bandit who triggered Brewbarry's compassion; recruited by Vukradin over dinner after questioning about criminal connections

## Personality & Motivations
- Core drive: survival and escape from circumstances he views as imposed upon him rather than chosen
- Corbin presents as frightened and deferential, but session notes suggest a latent capacity for cruelty when given authority over the vulnerable
- He blamed the Carver for his criminal path and claimed he never wanted to be a bandit, though his behavior around prisoners suggests more complicated motives
- Easily destabilized by fear; his panic response is consistent across encounters (spider-form Soma, the manticore, the Conyberry parley)

## History with the Party
1. **Recruitment:** Fled a bandit skirmish; Vukradin invited him to dinner, questioned him, and accepted him as camp attendant after he confessed his circumstances and reluctance to be a bandit
2. **Armor dispute:** Admitted his leather armor came from a corpse; Vukradin ordered him to remove it, Corbin appealed to Brewbarry, Vukradin ultimately relented and promised him fair-trade armor
3. **Soma encounter:** Panicked upon realizing the spider waving at him was Soma in wild shape
4. **Umbrage Hill:** Arrived looking worse and smelling; shared his full backstory with Soma over Adabra's meal — farmer from Greenmeadow, wife Elara, daughter Lily, village destroyed by the Uthgardt Black Lion tribe after the breaking of the Ordning; survived by being buried under a neighbor's body; drifted south into petty theft and eventually coerced into banditry
5. **Manticore encounter:** Singled out by the manticore as preferred prey; survived because the party killed it first
6. **Conyberry:** Sent by Vukradin to parley with former Carver gang members; his fear undermined the negotiation and the brigands drew steel. After combat, left to guard captured bandit Skippy while the party went to Butterskull Ranch
7. **Skippy incident:** Asked to take Skippy's armor ("conflict-free"), was denied by Vukradin; volunteered to stay and guard Skippy alone. Vukradin explicitly warned him Skippy had better be alive on return. Narrative notes Corbin appeared to want revenge; Vukradin noticed
8. **Death:** Valphine brought him to her nighttime meeting with Sister Kayla, presenting him as her assistant. Sister Kayla killed him during the meeting — first two rapier strikes missed, offhand dagger finished him — as a demonstration of intent to secure a private conversation with Valphine
9. **Post-death inconsistency:** A subsequent session references Corbin as alive, assigned to guard Sister Kaella while the party traveled to Icespire Hold. Valphine noted she had been teaching him some of her sacraments. Vukradin authorized him to use force on Kaella up to one hit point. Screaming after the party's departure confirmed he acted on this. Valphine observed in him a specific hunger — not cruelty exactly, but the disposition of someone who had been made small and was now presented with power over another

> **⚠ GM Note:** There is a continuity conflict in the source notes. Corbin is killed by Sister Kayla in one session and appears alive guarding Sister Kaella in a later session. Clarify with the table which version is canonical, or whether a narrative explanation (resurrection, mistaken identity, timeline discrepancy) applies.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Party's last camp, guarding Sister Kaella (if alive); or deceased at the site of Valphine's meeting with Sister Kayla (if dead)
- **Active situation:** If alive — acting on Vukradin's authorization to use force against Kaella; Valphine has been instructing him in unspecified sacraments
- **What the party knows:** That he has a violent history, limited loyalty to the Carver's organization, and a visible hunger for power over others when given the opportunity
- **What remains hidden:** The full nature of what Valphine has taught him; what actually happened with Skippy

## Relationships
- **Vukradin:** Recruited him; holds authority over him; has denied him armor and monitored his impulses around prisoners with visible suspicion
- **Brewbarry:** Corbin appealed to Brewbarry during the armor dispute; Brewbarry's compassion was the original reason Corbin survived his first encounter with the party
- **Soma:** Shared his backstory with her at Umbrage Hill; startled her (or she startled him) in spider-form
- **Valphine:** Has been teaching him her sacraments; used him as a prop in the Sister Kayla meeting, where he was killed — or, in the alternate continuity, trusts him with guarding a prisoner
- **Sister Kayla:** Killed him (in one continuity)
- **Sister Kaella:** Assigned as his prisoner; he acted on authorized force against her
- **The Carver / Jax:** Former chain of command; never met the Carver directly; views the Carver as responsible for his circumstances
