# Dabbledob

## Identity
- Role: Inventor and laboratory researcher, Gnomengarde
- Faction: Gnomengarde
- First appearance: Encountered in the inventors' laboratory alongside her coworker Fibblestib
- *Also known as: Dabblebob.*

*Also known as: Dabblebob.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Values evidence-based approaches over untested theories; skeptical of Fibblestib's more outlandish proposals
- Pragmatic and grounded — advocates for practical solutions (the straitjacket design) over speculative ones (the sanity ray)
- Reluctantly open-minded; will concede a point when evidence supports it, as when she acknowledged music has helped in some cases
- Becomes deeply absorbed in intellectual debates; once engaged in an argument, she is difficult to extract from it
- Dismissive of Fibblestib's grander claims (rolled her eyes at his rotating blade invention claim)
- Holds opinions on Gnomengarde governance — favors expanding the number of kings to enable a majority vote, as opposed to Fibblestib's bicameral approach

## History with the Party
1. The party encountered Dabbledob in the laboratory during a discussion about treating the king. She opposed Fibblestib's sanity ray proposal on grounds of lacking evidence, and pushed her own straitjacket design as an alternative (though Fibblestib dismissed it as merely "a shirt with no holes for the hands"). She rolled her eyes at Fibblestib's claim about the rotating blade invention. When Vukradin proposed music as a treatment, she acknowledged — reluctantly — that music has helped in some circumstances.
2. Overheard a political dispute among kings and weighed in, countering Fibblestib's bicameral proposal with her own "more kings = votable majority" solution. Became locked in a heated debate with Fibblestib over whether a tamper-proof voting box is theoretically possible.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The inventors' laboratory, Gnomengarde
- Engaged in ongoing debate with Fibblestib over treatment approaches for the king and over the design of a tamper-proof voting box
- **What the party knows:** She is the more skeptical, evidence-minded counterpart to Fibblestib; holds strong views on governance reform
- **What remains hidden:** Her deeper motivations, if any, regarding the king's condition or laboratory politics

- **The King:** Connected indirectly through the debate over his treatment
