# Giles Slipper-Shine

## Identity
- **Role:** Villager, lay religious enthusiast, and construction worker
- **Appearance:** Pale-skinned and sunburned
- **Faction:** Informal followers of an unorthodox Lathanderite interpretation
- **First Appearance:** Approached Valphine at the tavern alongside Brin Bundlewine
- *Also known as: Giles.*

*Also known as: Giles.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Shares Brin Bundlewine's fervent enthusiasm for a heterodox reading of Lathander as a god of **purifying solar pain**
- Appears earnest and community-minded, willing to contribute personal labor to a cause he believes in
- Enthusiastic about religious matters; deeply moved by the dramatic developments during Valphine's sermon — appears to be a true believer
- Ecstatic about the dramatic religious developments that occurred during Valphine's sermon

## History with the Party
1. Accompanied Brin Bundlewine in approaching Valphine at the tavern to discuss founding a new chapel based on the interpretation of Lathander as a god of purifying solar pain.
2. Agreed to participate in the renovation of Tresendar Manor into a temple; signed on to provide **free labor**, with 25 gp from the party covering materials. Shared in the arrangement alongside Brin.
3. Working as the second worker helping reconstruct the church in Phandalin alongside Brin; present at Valphine's sermon. Reacted with great excitement and was ecstatic about the religious and miraculous events that took place during the service.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Phandalin, working on the church reconstruction and committed to the Tresendar Manor renovation project
- **Active Plans:** Church reconstruction labor; Tresendar Manor renovation
- **What the party knows:** He is a willing laborer and true believer in the solar pain doctrine; an enthusiastic witness to Valphine's sermon; pale-skinned and sunburned in appearance
- **What remains hidden:** Nothing specifically noted — motivations appear straightforward

## Relationships
- **Brin Bundlewine:** Close associate; the two approached the party together and share the same religious outlook. Fellow construction worker on both the church reconstruction and Tresendar Manor renovation.
- **Valphine (PC):** Made the chapel agreement directly with her; object of his religious enthusiasm following the sermon.
