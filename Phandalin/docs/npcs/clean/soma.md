# Soma

## Identity
- Tortle druid of Mieliki; guide-for-hire; likely affiliated with the Emerald Enclave (naturalist faction)
- First appeared 1/1 Taraksh, outside Neverwinter, offering to guide the party to Phandalin in exchange for company

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Serve as a guide while pursuing her own druidic path; deepen her training before time runs out (her master Meril gave her only one year before sending her on)
- Pragmatic and unsentimental about coin and violence, but holds genuine ethical lines around unnatural magical creations and cruelty
- Dry and sardonic — quips under pressure, mocks inconsistency in others, and is not above moral hypocrisy when she notices it in herself
- Instinctively identifies as a naturalist within the Emerald Enclave, a position that shapes her discomfort with gnomish constructs and her sympathy for creatures living as nature intended

## History with the Party

**1/1 Taraksh** — Met the party outside Neverwinter; offered herself as a guide to Phandalin. Withheld knowledge of a white dragon rumor in the area.

**8/1 Taraksh** — Guided the group along the High Road and Triboar Trail; provided a description of Phandalin, a town map, and rumors about the Lionshield Coster's supply disruptions.

**9/1 Taraksh** — At the dwarven ruins, noted the thorough ransacking and the ominous Abbathor temple facade. In combat: dragged an orc off a bluff with Thorn Whip, killed another with Poison Spray, and accidentally knocked out Valphine with a miscast Ice Knife. Healed Valphine afterward. Quietly retrieved an unspecified item from the roadside ogre's corpse, which Vukardin chose not to acknowledge.

**10/1 Taraksh** — During a road ogre encounter, muttered "The kid is going to get us killed" about Vukardin; used Poison Spray. Described the group to Brewbarry as "not a well-oiled machine yet."

**1/2 Taraksh** — Led the expedition south toward Gnomengarde. Provoked a valley ogre by shouting that the party had killed its brother, then poisoned it and withdrew.

**Gnomengarde visit** — Located the river route to the hold. Mocked Valphine's missed swing; followed Vukardin through the waterfall searching for a nonexistent secret room. Retrieved a rope for Valphine at the cliff entrance. Challenged guard logic at the gatehouse. In negotiations, reminded Vukardin that the reward required a device, not a useful one, steering him toward accepting the hat of wizardry deal. In the mimic fight, landed the killing blow; expressed disgust at the creature as an unnatural abomination and held her tongue when the gnomes proposed harvesting it for a new construct.

**Post-Gnomengarde** — Took gold from the chests without apology. Argued the 25-gold Adabra reward was fair ("sometimes the reward is knowing you did the right thing"). During the bandit ambush, wild-shaped into a spider, bit and poisoned a bandit to death, and attempted to spare a fleeing spider-phobic bandit — shouting "Let him go" while still in spider form. Found a druidic scroll among the bandits' belongings; was challenged by Vukardin and made to question whether keeping it was any better than banditry.

**Umbrage Hill / Manticore fight** — Pushed back against Vukardin's suggestion to extort Phandalin; insisted on simply killing the manticore. Conjured a familiar and sent it into the manticore's mouth as a distraction, which enraged the creature. Was visibly hurt when Vukardin blamed her for the spell's failure. Identified herself as a naturalist to Adabra, which dramatically shifted Adabra's attitude toward the group. Was stung by Adabra's casual criticism of gaps in her druidic training. That evening listened to Corbin's full account of his past. The following morning used Adabra's shared naturalist values to finally convince her to travel to Phandalin.

**Orc ambush (most recent)** — Transformed into a bear to scout; identified an orc making hand signals and recognized the clearing as an ambush. When Brewbarry gave away their position, climbed a tree and attacked the scout. Took an arrow in return. Leaped from a branch to shove the scout toward the other orcs; cast Moonbeam; clamped her jaws on the orc with the necklace of bones, mortally wounding him. Brewbarry noted the contrast to her earlier reluctance to attack fleeing enemies.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** In combat or just after combat at an ambush clearing en route — presumably between Umbrage Hill and Phandalin
- **Active:** Serving as Vukardin's paid guide (tab growing); content to wait for payment
- **Hidden from party:** Deliberately withheld knowledge of a white dragon rumor near Neverwinter; nature of the item retrieved from the roadside ogre's corpse is unknown to the party

## Relationships
- **Vukardin:** Exasperated patience; calls him "the kid," challenges his moralizing, but has also been hurt by his blame-shifting. Serving as his paid guide.
- **Valphine:** Strained — accidentally knocked her out with Ice Knife; Valphine now visibly repositions away from Soma's line of fire. Soma did retrieve a rope for her rather than leave her behind.
- **Brewbarry:** Cordial; was candid with him about the group's dynamic early on. He has begun probing her ethical consistency.
- **Corbin:** She took time to listen to his full story after the manticore fight.
- **Adabra Gwynn:** Genuine connection through shared naturalist convictions; Soma used that bond to finally move Adabra.
- **Meril (master, off-screen):** Gave Soma one year near her home before sending her to follow Vukardin. Soma is aware she has limited time and incomplete training.
