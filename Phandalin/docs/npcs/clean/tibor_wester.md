# Tibor Wester

## Identity
- **Role:** Signatory authority and chief of the Logger's Camp; responsible for authorizing delivery notices and releasing payment
- **Faction:** Unknown
- **First Appearance:** Referenced by name during party discussions; has never been encountered in person
- *Also known as: Tibor, Tribar Wester.*

## Personality & Motivations
- No direct interaction recorded. Personality and motivations unknown.

## History with the Party
1. The party was directed to find Tibor at the Logger's Camp to have their delivery notice signed and claim payment.
2. Refused to transport the prisoner Shrimpy to Neverwinter, prompting the party to bring Shrimpy to Phandalin instead.
3. Upon the party's arrival at the camp, it was completely deserted — tents on the beach, a standing cabin with stacked logs, older cabins torn down to foundations and stone chimneys. A grim silence hung over the site. Tibor was nowhere to be found.
4. Had an outstanding financial arrangement with Vukradin, who was counting on finding Tibor at camp to collect payment.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Logger's Camp (expected but not found)
- **Whereabouts:** Missing and unaccounted for, along with the entire camp population
- **What the party knows:** He is the camp chief and delivery signatory they need; he refused to help with Shrimpy; the camp shows signs of partial abandonment or disruption
- **What remains hidden:** Where he is, why he left or disappeared, and what happened at the camp

## Relationships
- **Vukradin:** Had an outstanding financial arrangement; Vukradin was counting on collecting payment
- **Shrimpy:** Party prisoner whose transport Tibor refused to facilitate
- **Harbin Wester:** Tibor is Harbin's brother; Harbin posted the Logger's Camp supply delivery quest on his behalf
- **The Party:** Indirect, transactional; no face-to-face interaction on record
