# Valphine

## Identity
- **Role:** Cleric of Lathander (surface-convert, Drow origin)
- **Faction:** Traveling with the party; nominal affiliation with Lathander's clergy
- **First Appearance:** Battle against an ogre on a mountain path, morning of 02-01 Taraskh 1495

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Serving Lathander while reconciling her Underdark instincts with surface morality; accumulating power and resources on her own terms.
- Pragmatic and unsentimental — her first instinct in most situations is leverage, coercion, or controlled violence. She finds torture a reasonable tool and "fair trades" of lives an unremarkable concept.
- Demonstrates dry amusement at manipulation and political chaos, respecting Vukradin's destabilization techniques as recognizably Drow in character.
- Actively adjusting to Lathander's expectations, noting the tension between her Underdark reflexes (sacrifice, hunting, dominance) and her adopted faith. The adjustment is genuine but incomplete.

## History with the Party

**Ogre Ambush (02-01 Taraskh 1495)**
Participated in the mountain path battle. Missed a mace strike on the prone ogre (mocked by Soma), wounded it with a crossbow bolt as it fled. Looted the body afterward, recovering a whistle, coins, and a key.

**Gnomengarde**
Declined the cliff climb until Soma retrieved rope for her. Observed entry negotiations without engaging. Identified herself as a cleric to inventors Fibblestib and Dabbledob, who dismissed her. Privately concluded she could not cure King Korboz and that the party couldn't afford to pursue it regardless. Considered torture as a solution to the king's madness before Vukradin redirected the group toward music.

Manipulated guards Pog and Ulla into mutual paranoid accusation by weaponizing Ulla's own words — found it amusing. Located the mimic by provoking it with a crossbow, called out the discovery, and positioned herself clear of Soma's Ice Knife blast. Quoted a childhood rhyme when Vukradin's verbal attacks visibly hurt the creature.

**Post-Gnomengarde / Dual Monarchy Debrief**
Watched Vukradin's political destabilization of the dual monarchy with approval, comparing his technique to Drow practice. Inserted herself once to suggest a "clear approver structure," drawing on firsthand observation of commune-style cultures collapsing into betrayal.

**Umbrage Hill Approach**
Was annoyed at the 25-gold rescue rate for Adabra, having been warned beforehand. Spotted a shadow behind a boulder on approach and suppressed the instinct as an Underdark reflex. Took a crossbow bolt from bandits in the ambush; shot a fleeing bandit in the back, enabling Vukradin to finish him.

**Manticore Fight at Umbrage Hill**
Fired the opening shot against the manticore; took a full volley of tail spikes in retaliation. Missed a second shot. Internally noted the manticore's offer to take Corbin in exchange for the group was "a fair trade" she would have considered in another life. Observed Vukradin's combat uncertainty with a gambler-reader's eye. When Adabra emerged, immediately offered to teach her about Lathander. Attributed the exceptional quality of Adabra's food to Lathander's glory. Last seen preparing to return to Phandalin from Umbrage Hill.

**Orc Scout Encounter (Return Journey)**
Led the march, first to spot the concealed orc scout. Remarked that orcs make fine slaves. Fired repeatedly in battle, missing several times before landing a hit. Expressed open contempt for Soma's Moonbeam, comparing it unfavorably to Lathander's searing sunlight. The fleeing scout appeared to recognize her as a Drow worshipping Lathander and panicked. She pursued him into the forest but lost him — surface terrain neutralized her tracking advantage. Interrogated the captured orc (with Brewbarry) regarding orc cultists of Talos; asserted dominance by declaring he now worked for the party.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Returning to Phandalin from Umbrage Hill
- **Active:** Holding a captured orc she has conscripted as a party asset; aware of orc cultists of Talos operating in the region
- **Party Knows:** She is a Drow, a Lathander cleric, and has a violent, domineering background
- **Hidden:** The full depth of her Underdark moral calculus (e.g., having genuinely weighed trading Corbin's life to the manticore); the specific nature of her adjustment to Lathander's faith and how tenuous it may be

## Relationships
- **Soma:** Mild antagonism. Soma mocked her early miss; she has returned contempt, openly criticizing Soma's Moonbeam as inferior to Lathander's light.
- **Vukradin:** Genuine respect. Recognizes his manipulation techniques as familiar and effective; views him as a capable peer.
- **Brewbarry:** Ideological alignment on interrogation and coercion methods. Coordinated naturally with him during the orc prisoner interrogation.
- **Adabra:** Immediate missionary instinct upon meeting her; offered to teach her about Lathander.
- **Corbin:** Privately assessed him as an acceptable loss in the manticore exchange. No demonstrated warmth.
- **Lathander's Cleric (unnamed):** Warned her about the 25-gold Adabra rate; some prior rapport implied.
