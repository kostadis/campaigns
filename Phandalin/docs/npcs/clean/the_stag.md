# The Stag

## Identity
- A large wild stag inhabiting the Whispering Wood; no faction affiliation
- First appeared when the party entered the Whispering Wood, materializing suddenly before them

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: protection of its territory and restoration of the forest to its normal state
- Territorial and aggressive toward intruders, but capable of calm engagement once boundaries are respected. Communicates plainly and transactionally when treated with patience. Accepted a simple gift in exchange for information, suggesting pragmatism over hostility.

## History with the Party
- The stag confronted the party upon entry into the Whispering Wood, demanding they leave its territory with repeated aggression
- The group largely retreated to the territory boundary, de-escalating the encounter
- Soma cast Speak with Animals and held a direct conversation with the stag
- The stag described its condition (blinking, shifting antlers, difficulty with brambles) as beginning "some time ago," linked to broader strangeness in the forest
- It identified the center of the Whispering Wood as the origin point of the disturbances
- It noted that "other nice people" had previously spoken with it and promised to fix the problem — but never followed through
- It accepted mushrooms from Gnomengarde as payment for the information shared

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Its territory at the edge of the Whispering Wood
- **Current state:** Afflicted by planar instability — teleports short distances, leaves afterimages, fur shimmers and flickers, antlers constantly shift in shape
- **What the party knows:** Its condition stems from disturbances originating at the forest's center; others have previously investigated and failed to help
- **What remains hidden:** The identity of the "other nice people" who made promises; whether the stag has any further knowledge of the forest's center or its disturbances

## Relationships
- **The party:** Cautious tolerance after a tense first meeting; responded positively to Soma's direct, respectful communication and Gnomengarde's gift
- **Unknown prior investigators:** Referenced as "other nice people" who spoke with it before the party and promised aid — identity and fate unknown
- **The Whispering Wood:** Deeply tied to it as territory; the forest's instability is directly harming the stag
