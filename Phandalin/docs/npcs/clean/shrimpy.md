# Shrimpy

## Identity
- **Role:** Former raiding party member; captured orc; prisoner of the party, later transferred to Phandalin custody
- **Faction:** Unnamed orc tribe displaced from the Sword Mountains (Ice Spires region); independent splinter faction unaffiliated with any current organized group
- **First Appearance:** Encountered as part of an orc raiding party during a roadside ambush; captured after being mortally wounded by Soma and secured by Brewbarry and Vukradin
- Mockingly renamed "Shrimpy" by Brewbarry
- *Also known as: The Orc with the Necklace of Bones.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Orc independence and self-determination — he believes orcs should serve orc interests only, not outside factions or cults
- Proud and principled; opposed his own chief's alliance with the Talos cultists not out of fear but on ideological grounds
- Fierce enough to fight through serious wounds and call out a charmed tribesman; pragmatic enough to accept captivity over death
- Has a temper around Drow specifically — immediately targeted Valphine upon identifying her race
- Under sustained captivity, reverts to self-preservation mode: whiny and cowardly under pressure, opportunistic during interrogation, angling for payment or release
- Argued dramatically that the party must be dead during the Woodland Manse fight to leverage his freedom; earned consistent contempt from Brewbarry and Valphine through this behavior

## History with the Party
1. **Ambush:** Attacked the party during a raid — threw a javelin at Brewbarry, engaged with a scimitar, and singled out Valphine as a Drow.
2. **Tribal loyalty:** Screamed at a charmed orc for attacking "his brother's nephew," revealing deep tribal bonds.
3. **Capture:** Mortally wounded by Soma; captured by Brewbarry and Vukradin. Magically slept while attempting to flee.
4. **Interrogation:** Disclosed tribe's backstory — displaced from the Ice Spires by a white dragon, clan fractured — and his ideological opposition to the Talos cult alliance. Agreed to serve the party under threat of death from Brewbarry.
5. **Bound to the party's cart** and forced to pull it to the Logger's Camp after Brewbarry dismissed his request for payment.
6. **Transported toward Phandalin** after the party debated his fate; Tibor refused to hand him off to Neverwinter, so imprisonment in Phandalin was decided.
7. **Held at a clearing** near the Woodland Manse while the party assaulted the cultists inside. Argued with his guard Corbin, insisting the party was dead and demanding release.
8. **Handed over to Town Master Harbin Wester** upon the party's return to Phandalin.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Phandalin, in the custody of Town Master Harbin Wester
- No active independent plans; effectively out of play unless the party revisits Phandalin's jail
- **What the Party Knows:** His tribal history, his ideological opposition to the Talos cult, his general hostility toward Drow
- **What Remains Hidden:** The depth of his resentment toward captivity; whether his cooperation is purely coerced or carries any genuine pragmatic buy-in

## Relationships
- **Brewbarry:** Captor, namer, and primary antagonist; Shrimpy serves under direct threat — relationship is coercive. Brewbarry expressed open displeasure at his cowardice.
- **Valphine:** Active hostility; attacked her on sight due to her Drow identity. Also visibly displeased by his behavior at the Woodland Manse.
- **Soma:** The one who nearly killed him; likely source of wariness.
- **Vukradin:** Participated in capture; no further detail noted.
- **Corbin:** Served as his guard during the Woodland Manse assault; subject of Shrimpy's wheedling attempts.
- **Harbin Wester (Town Master):** Current custodian; Shrimpy was formally handed over to him.
- **Tibor:** Refused to take Shrimpy to Neverwinter, indirectly determining his fate.
- **His Tribe/Chief:** Ideologically opposed to the chief's alliance with Talos half-orc cultists; tribal loyalty still present (reacted strongly to the charmed tribesman).
- **Talos Cultists (Half-Orc):** Despises the alliance; views it as a betrayal of orc independence.
