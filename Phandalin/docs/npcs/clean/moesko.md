# Moesko

## Identity
- **Role:** Orc Anchorite; dark cleric stationed at (or claiming) the Tower of Storms
- **Faction:** Nominal devotee of Talos, though his allegiance was compromised by a second, alien influence
- **First Appearance:** Found seated in a barnacle-encrusted chair on the upper floor of the Tower of Storms, wearing giant octopus hide armor and holding an opalescent conch

## Personality & Motivations
- Presented as a guardian figure — whether self-appointed or placed by a patron is unclear
- His devotion to Talos was real but corrupted: Miral's ghost described shadows moving within it, twisting his faith into something "colder and more calculating" — foreign rather than the Stormlord's wrath
- His true motivations remain opaque; he may have been a partially unwilling instrument of a separate power

## History with the Party
1. **Combat at the Tower of Storms:** The party engaged Moesko on the upper floor. He retaliated with a Lightning Bolt (heavy damage to all but Brewbarry) and a Thunderwave (additional damage; pushed Soma in bear form). He survived Soma's multi-attack, Vukradin's Cloud of Daggers, and Brewbarry's charge before Brewbarry's final hit reduced him to 1 HP. Soma delivered the killing blow after reverting from bear form.
2. **Post-mortem examination:** His body revealed a hole in his chest — his heart had been removed and installed as the power source for the lighthouse beacon, where it continued beating and emitting pulsating green light. Vukradin destroyed it by striking it from the mechanism, breaking the tower's curse.
3. **Talisman discovered:** A concealed compartment in his living space contained a dark, iridescent talisman bearing the lightning bolt of Talos on one side and an inverted eye with coiling tentacle-like lines on the reverse. It emanated faint divine resonance and a dominant, alien psionic energy.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Heart destroyed by Vukradin. Tower curse broken.
- The opalescent conch (belonging to Miral the Self) was recovered from his body and is now in the party's possession.

## Relationships
- **Miral the Self (Miral the Sea Elf):** Moesko held Miral's conch — the nature of that relationship (theft, guardianship, orders from a patron) is unresolved. Miral's ghost implied Moesko's corruption was externally imposed.
- **Talos:** Nominal patron deity, though Moesko's devotion was subverted by a separate alien influence.
- **Unknown Patron/Entity:** Suggested by the psionic talisman's reverse sigil (inverted eye, coiling tentacles) — something cold, calculating, and of alien origin was working through or alongside Moesko.

## Arc Score Events
- No arc score changes recorded.

---
**GM Note:** The talisman's dual symbolism (Talos / alien tentacle-eye) and the psionic energy of "foreign origin" are unresolved threads pointing toward a larger power operating in the region.
