# Out-of-Phase Dwarves (Axelholm Inhabitants)

## Identity
- **Role:** Former dwarven inhabitants of Axelholm; primary antagonists
- **Faction:** None — transformed remnants of Axelholm's original population
- **First Appearance:** Encountered inside Axelholm during the party's incursion; emerged from unnatural light within the fortress

## Personality & Motivations
- Minds broken by their transformation; motivation appears reduced to obsessive muttering about **efficiency**
- No discernible goals beyond hostility; communicate only through their paralysis effect and constant murmuring
- They are not tactical in a traditional sense — more creatures of compulsion than strategy

## History with the Party
- Encountered en masse inside Axelholm; appeared in increasing numbers as combat progressed, materializing from unnatural light sources
- Swarmed a paralyzed Soma, nearly killing her
- Brewbarry briefly experienced the paralysis effect ("cold storage") but broke free; a disembodied voice told him he was **"incorrect packaging"**
- At session's end, one dwarf successfully paralyzed Soma again in bear form, leaving her critically vulnerable — **session cliffhanger**

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Active and hostile within Axelholm
- **Ongoing Threat:** Continuing to emerge from unnatural light; numbers escalating during combat
- **Party Knowledge:** The party has witnessed their appearance, paralysis ability, and growing numbers, but does not know the origin or nature of the planar anomaly that created them

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Purely hostile
- **Soma:** Targeted twice by paralysis; currently paralyzed and vulnerable
- **Brewbarry:** Targeted once; resisted the effect
