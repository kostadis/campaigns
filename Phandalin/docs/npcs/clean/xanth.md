# Xanth

## Identity
- **Role:** Wilderness guide, regional informant; affiliated with (though frustrated by) the Emerald Enclave
- **Species:** Centaur
- **First appearance:** Encountered peacefully near Dragonbarrow; warned the party about will-o'-wisps and offered to guide them to the Anchorites of Talos

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Protecting the natural world; resolving the orc displacement crisis that the Emerald Enclave has failed to address; honoring old friendships (notably with Meryl)
- Pragmatic and action-oriented — deeply frustrated by institutional paralysis and the Enclave's inability to reach consensus
- Warm but has clear limits: accepted carrying Vukradin and Valphine on his back with visible reluctance, framing the concession explicitly as a favor to Soma, not Vukradin
- Treats near-death and logistical inconvenience with equal equanimity; greets crises like administrative problems to be solved

## History with the Party

**Early contact (near Dragonbarrow):**
Xanth encountered the party near Dragonbarrow — startling Vukradin, who had never seen a centaur. Soma calmed the situation. Xanth warned the party that Dragonbarrow is haunted by restless spirits and that he avoids it at night. He identified the Anchorites of Talos as the same half-orc spellcasters who previously tried to kill the party, describing their dark rites at a circle of standing stones above a cave-ridden hill in Neverwinter Wood. He offered to guide the party there after their Dragonbarrow business. He recognized Valphine as a famous cleric of Lathander and praised her courage.

**Outside the tomb (brief appearance):**
Found wounded and bloodied in the fading light outside the tomb chamber as the party emerged. Did not speak. Status: injured but present.

**After being healed by Soma:**
Soma healed Xanth following a battle, after which he rose to his full, formidable size and immediately greeted the party with energy. He named Vukradin by the Centaurian equivalent "Vucravinios." He recognized **Meryl's Staff** in Soma's possession at once — identifying it as once having had far more leaves than its single surviving one — and stated that Meryl was an old friend. He asked Soma directly what she wanted, treating his assistance as an extension of that friendship. Soma's answer — to keep the world safer and ensure everyone had a place — satisfied him as correct.

He explained the regional crisis in full: a white dragon descended into the Icespire Mountains, displaced the orcs, who then pushed into inhabited valleys, creating a cascade of emergencies. He found Vukradin's reluctance to claim a dragon's hoard genuinely baffling. He confirmed Borg's death through animal communication networks and named the Carver's known lieutenants: **Borg, Sister Kaella, and Zax.**

He agreed to carry Vukradin and Valphine to the foothills near the gold mine, on the explicit condition that he not be asked to enter mountain terrain, and noted pointedly that Vukradin was asking him to be a horse. He accepted — on Soma's account.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Departing with the party toward the Icespire foothills
- **Active:** Serving as guide and transport to the foothills; has agreed not to enter mountain terrain
- **Condition:** Was wounded outside the tomb; has since been healed by Soma

**What the party knows:** His connection to Meryl, his frustration with the Emerald Enclave, his knowledge of regional threats and animal networks, the names of the Carver's lieutenants.
**What remains hidden:** The full extent of his history with Meryl; his own goals beyond the immediate crisis; the nature of his Enclave standing given his dissatisfaction with the organization.

## Relationships
- **Soma:** Primary bond. Respects her through the Meryl connection; found common ground immediately on naturalist worldview and practical problem-solving. His cooperation flows through her.
- **Meryl:** Old friend. Recognizes Meryl's Staff and trusts Soma partly because Meryl left it with her.
- **Vukradin:** Tolerates with visible amusement and some exasperation. Gave him a Centaurian name; agreed to carry him while making clear the indignity involved.
- **Valphine:** Recognized as a famous cleric of Lathander; praised her courage and commented on pain as purification.
- **Emerald Enclave:** Member or associate, but openly critical — describes the organization as paralyzed by internal faction disputes over the orc situation.
- **The Carver's network:** Aware of Borg's death through animal communication channels; knows the lieutenants by name.
