# Sylvine Wintermoon

## Identity
- **Role:** Con artist / treasure hunter. Arrived with a named companion (Runa) and a force of veterans as muscle.
- **Faction:** None recorded. Operates with sister Runa and hired fighters.
- **First Appearance:** Arrived at Icespire Hold shortly after the party's battle with Cryovain, presenting herself and Runa as the rightful owners of the dragon's hoard.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Acquire Cryovain's hoard by any means necessary — persuasion first, violence second.
- **Traits:** Sylvine is a calculated operator who prepares layered contingencies. She deploys emotional performance (wounded pride, religious invocation) as tools and drops them the instant they fail — Soma noted the shift took less than a second. She is not rattled by rejection; she pivots.

## History with the Party
1. **Icespire Hold (Post-Battle):** Arrived with Runa and veterans. Opened with flattery toward Vukradin, framing the treasure handover as a spiritual formality already blessed by the gods.
2. Soma identified the approach as rehearsed. Vukradin rejected her, calling her a liar and a cheat and ordering her to leave.
3. Sylvine dropped her wounded-pride performance almost instantly and announced "Second plan — we kill them all," escalating to open combat.
4. Her veteran muscle was killed or fled. **Sylvine's own final status was not confirmed.**

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Icespire Hold, at the conclusion of the ambush attempt.
- **Final Status: UNCONFIRMED.** The veterans were killed or routed; whether Sylvine was captured, killed, or escaped is not recorded.
- **Active Plans:** Unknown — her two-stage plan (con, then violence) was fully exhausted at Icespire Hold.

## Relationships
- **Runa:** Traveled together; Runa is described as her sister. Runa's own role in the encounter is not detailed in the notes.
- **Vukradin:** Targeted him specifically by reputation (bard known for returning stolen treasure). Now openly hostile after he rejected and exposed her.
- **Soma:** Soma read her accurately throughout the encounter; Sylvine likely knows she was seen through.
- **Veterans (muscle):** Hired fighters. Defeated — killed or fled.
