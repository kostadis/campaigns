# Barthen

## Identity
- **Role:** Proprietor of Barthen's Provisions
- **Faction:** None noted; operates as a local merchant
- **First Appearance:** Party visited his shop in town to fulfill a supply order

## Personality & Motivations
- Practical, accommodating merchant focused on fulfilling orders efficiently.
- Helpful without being intrusive — proactively offered a solution when Brewbarry noticed the order lacked wine, suggesting the Falcon Hunting Lodge and providing a letter of introduction.

## History with the Party
- Filled Harbin Wester's brother's supply order for the Logger's Camp: two heavy crates of dried meats, cheese, bread, ale, and oil, loaded onto a cart pulled by an ox named Vincent.
- When Brewbarry flagged the absence of wine, Barthen directed the party to the Falcon Hunting Lodge and supplied them with a letter of introduction granting lodge access.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Barthen's Provisions shop in town.
- **Active Plans:** None noted.
- **Known to Party:** Merchant who filled the supply order and pointed them toward the Falcon Hunting Lodge.
- **Hidden:** Nothing noted.

## Relationships
- **Harbin Wester:** Business relationship; fulfills supply orders on Wester's behalf.
- **Falcon Hunting Lodge:** Familiar enough with the lodge to recommend it and provide a letter of introduction.
- **Party:** Friendly, transactional. Notable rapport with **Brewbarry**, whose question prompted the lodge referral.
