# New notes for boney (from dossier_extract_032.md)

Boney is a skeletal horse whose bones and rotting saddle were discovered by Soma in a southern niche within the Dragonbarrow tunnel. When Soma approached to investigate, the skeletal remains writhed together and rose as an undead horse. Soma used Speak with Animals to communicate with it.

Upon rising, Boney initially believed Soma to be Lady Alagondar, the figure it had been brought back from the dead to serve. When confronted with the discrepancy, the party convinced Boney that Soma was Lady Alagondar reincarnated, which the loyal steed accepted without question. Boney lamented the loss of its mane and other physical features, being fully skeletal.

Boney revealed that it had been brought to the dungeon to guard something for Lady Alagondar but was killed by malevolent Will-o'-wisps before it could complete its duty. It described the wisps as evil and not very talkative. During the subsequent combat, Boney confirmed the danger of the Will-o'-wisps by recalling how they had ended its previous life.

Boney's original name was Barney, but the party persuaded it to embrace the name Boney, which it accepted happily. It provided the crucial directional clue that the sword was buried "across the way" from where Lady Alagondar rested, pointing the party northward toward the Dragon Slayer Sword. The party discussed retaining Boney as a sidekick, though noted its low stats of only 13 hit points. Last known status: present with the party in the Dragonbarrow after the Will-o'-wisps were defeated.
