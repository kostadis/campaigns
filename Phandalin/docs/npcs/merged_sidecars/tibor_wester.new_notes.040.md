# New notes for Tibor Wester (from dossier_extract_040.md)

Tibor Wester does not appear in this chunk but is discussed.

During the party's encounter with young druids confronting a logging crew in the forest, the druids identified Tibor Wester — described as Harbin's brother — as the operator of a wildcat logging operation. He had been hiring new crews who did not know or did not respect the permit agreements governing sanctioned cutting in the forest. The loggers themselves appeared to be workers who had taken available employment without full knowledge of or investment in Wester's scheme.

When his name was raised, Vukradin made a sound characterized as "a soft warning" rather than a word.

After Vukradin's speech moved the loggers to set down their axes and express remorse, they indicated they would report back to the city about what Tibor Wester had been running in the forest.
