# New notes for Ser Kaelen (from dossier_extract_037.md)

Ser Kaelen communicated with the party via Sending spell after the battle. His reply to Vukradin's message read: "Praise Torm! Your righteous victory brings light to the north. The gauntlet awaits your arrival in Phandalin. We shall celebrate your conflict-free triumph together." He followed up with a shorter addendum: "Well done. Come to Phandalin quick. Issues with the Emerald Enclave." Vukradin noted that Ser Kaelen had studied his vocabulary — specifically the phrase "conflict-free" — and fed it back to him in twenty-five words, leaving Vukradin uncertain whether to be moved or suspicious. Vukradin assessed him as "about as close as someone we can actually trust as anybody" before immediately correcting himself: "No, I don't trust him. He's as close as anybody." Vukradin specifically noted that Ser Kaelen directed them to Phandalin rather than endorsing their stated plan to head to Neverwinter, and suspected he had arranged something and already had a press release written. Ser Kaelen is currently in Phandalin, affiliated with the Order of the Gauntlet, awaiting the party's arrival. The mention of "issues with the Emerald Enclave" suggests a developing conflict involving Adabra's faction.
