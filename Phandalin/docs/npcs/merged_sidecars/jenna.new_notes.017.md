# New notes for Jenna (from dossier_extract_017.md)

Jena Roscoe observed Vukradin storming out of the tavern and explained to the remaining party members that he was a man of principle who believed possessions belonged to their original owners. After Harbin's cover story satisfied Vukradin, she took the opportunity to explain to the party how investing adventuring money into the community effectively launders it, transforming blood money into legitimate community improvement. She suggested that Vukradin was young and might come to understand this concept with time.
