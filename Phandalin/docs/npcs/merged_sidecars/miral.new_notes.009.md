# New notes for Miral (from dossier_extract_009.md)

Miral was identified as a sea elf whose opalescent conch had been the object of the party's quest. She manifested as a ghost when the party presented her conch. She confirmed that Moesko was her undoing but revealed he had not acted alone — external shadows had twisted his devotion to Talos into something foreign and calculating. She warned the party: "Beware, surface dweller... the true storm gathers not in the sky, but in the depths below Leilon." After delivering this warning, her spirit departed, implying it could finally rest upon recovering the conch. When shown the color-changing magical cloak, Miral — referred to briefly as a giant crab in Soma's account, suggesting a transformed or associated form — indicated the halberd recovered from the Star Crossed Lover was a treasure she had intended to retrieve for the party. Last known status: spirit departed after receiving the conch.
