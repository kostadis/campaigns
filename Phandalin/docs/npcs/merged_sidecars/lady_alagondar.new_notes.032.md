# New notes for Lady Alagondar (from dossier_extract_032.md)

Lady Alagondar is a figure from the past referenced entirely through Boney's recollections. She was the original owner of the Dragon Slayer Sword and was buried in the Dragonbarrow. Boney was brought back from the dead specifically to aid her on an important quest and believed it had been left in the dungeon to guard something on her behalf.

Boney indicated that Lady Alagondar was laid to rest in a chamber within the Dragonbarrow, and that the Dragon Slayer Sword was buried "across the way" from where she rested, pointing north. The Dragon Slayer Sword was ultimately found resting atop a dragon's skull in the northern chamber, confirming Boney's account. Lady Alagondar does not appear directly in the session; her presence is known only through Boney's loyalty and the arrangement of the dungeon's contents.
