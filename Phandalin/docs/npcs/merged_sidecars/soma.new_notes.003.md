# New notes for soma (from dossier_extract_003.md)

Soma guided the party to Gnomengarde by locating the river leading to the hold. At the waterfall entrance, she mocked Valphine's missed mace swing during the earlier ogre fight. She followed Vukradin through the waterfall searching for a nonexistent secret room, getting soaked, and eventually pressured him to leave. She climbed the cliff to the cave entrance and retrieved a rope for Valphine rather than leaving her behind.

At the guard post, Soma verbally challenged Pog and Ulla's logic by pointing out that shapechangers would not choose their particular forms, though the gnomes dismissed this reasoning. She muttered that she sensed more senseless violence approaching before Vukradin's diplomatic argument and Brewbarry's outburst resolved the standoff. She reacted with disbelief when bureaucratic procedure ultimately granted them entry.

In the inventors' workshop, Soma attempted to explain the value of clerical magic to Fibblestib and Dabbledob but was drowned out. She reminded Vukradin during negotiations with King Gnercli that their reward from Townmaster Harbin Wester depended on bringing back a device, not necessarily a useful one, effectively convincing him to accept the hat of wizardry deal. She quipped to Vukradin during the king encounter that he was "more of a tour guide" than a musician.

In the cellar fight against the mimic, Soma prepared to cast Ice Knife, with Valphine notably repositioning herself away from Soma's line of fire in anticipation of a poorly aimed spell.
