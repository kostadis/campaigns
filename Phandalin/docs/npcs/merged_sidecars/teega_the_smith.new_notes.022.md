# New notes for Teega the Smith (from dossier_extract_022.md)

Teega arrived at the lodge disoriented and suffering from significant memory gaps. She remembered working at the Wayside Inn, a tavern with an attached forge on the corner of the High Road and Tribor Trail, as her last coherent memory before her captivity. She disclosed that she had been experiencing blackouts for some time and had been drawn to the Kraken cult by promises of peace following the endless wars and chaos of the Sword Coast. The party determined through examination that her mind had been manipulated through psionic means, leaving her brain rewired and her memory compromised, though Brewbarry concluded this was not the work of Mind Flayers. Valphine attempted to intimidate her about her choices, while Vukradin offered her a position as a blacksmith for the Church of Lathander. She declined the immediate offer but expressed interest in potentially visiting the church after first returning to the Wayside Inn. Last known location: Falcon Hunting Lodge.
