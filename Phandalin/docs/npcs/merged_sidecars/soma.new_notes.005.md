# New notes for soma (from dossier_extract_005.md)

Soma spent the opening scene in spider form, experiencing the world through heightened senses before reverting to her tortle body and startling Corbin. During the approach to the windmill she pushed back against Vukradin's suggestion to extort more money from Phandalin, insisting they simply kill the manticore and save Adabra. In combat she conjured a familiar and sent it into the manticore's mouth as a distraction, which enraged rather than satisfied the creature. She encouraged Vukradin's second spell attempt and was visibly hurt when he blamed her for its failure. She tried to soothe both Adabra during the siege and Corbin after the battle. When Adabra asked her position in the Emerald Enclave schism between naturalists and interventionists, Soma instinctively identified as a naturalist, which dramatically shifted Adabra's attitude toward the group. She was stung by Adabra's casual criticism of gaps in her druidic training — her master Meril had only one year near her home before prompting her to follow Vukradin, and she is aware she has little time left. That evening she listened to Corbin's full account of his destroyed family and his descent into banditry. She used Adabra's naturalist convictions the following morning to finally convince her to come to Phandalin, framing the mission around shared values rather than obligation. Last known status: departing Umbrage Hill with the group.
