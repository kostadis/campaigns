# New notes for thomas (from dossier_extract_013.md)

Thomas was one of Lyra's two named agents from the Emerald Enclave who joined the party at the Stonehill Tavern after Vukradin invited them. He was the oldest of the group. He drank the beer Spiderman provided and served as the primary spokesperson for Lyra's situation, explaining that she had discovered the ritual herself, acted unilaterally rather than bringing it to Enclave leadership, and had been motivated by personal ambition and frustration with organizational inaction as much as by genuine concern for the Whispering Grove. He confirmed that a promotion was connected to the ritual's success. He showed some distress when recounting the near-death experience and the failure of the quest. He also questioned Valphine directly about her background, asking whether she was from the Underdark and expressing shock at her description of self-harm as a devotional practice to Lathander. When Vukradin offered to speak on Lyra's behalf to her superiors, Thomas firmly advised against it. He and Marian declined Vukradin's invitation to join the party, stating that their oath to the Emerald Enclave bound them. Last seen departing the Stonehill Tavern with Marian.
