# New notes for Delaan Winterhound (from dossier_extract_012.md)

Delaan Winterhound was referenced by Lyra as the wisest member of the Emerald Enclave at the time of the War of the Dragons. He had initially inclined the Enclave toward non-intervention, consistent with a naturalist or at minimum cautious position, but was persuaded by the hero Malfire to commit Enclave forces to the effort to stop Tiamat's summoning into the Prime Material Plane. His decision was consequential enough that it became the defining fault line in the Enclave's subsequent ideological split. His current status is unknown; he was mentioned only in historical context.
