# New notes for valphine (from dossier_extract_003.md)

Valphine participated in the battle against an ogre on a mountain path during the morning of 02-01 of Taraskh 1495. She attempted a mace strike on the prone ogre but missed badly, earning mockery from Soma. She fired a crossbow bolt at the fleeing ogre after it attempted to run, wounding it but not killing it. After the battle, she looted the ogre's body and found a whistle, coins, and a key.

At Gnomengarde, Valphine declined to climb the cliff initially, requiring Soma to bring her a rope before she ascended. She watched the entry negotiations with guards Pog and Ulla without engaging directly.

In the inventors' workshop, Valphine identified herself as a cleric to gnome inventors Fibblestib and Dabbledob, who dismissed clerical magic in favor of their own inventions. She privately concluded she could not cure King Korboz and that the party lacked the funds to pursue that avenue regardless. When hearing about the missing gnomes Warren and Orrin, she immediately considered torture as a remedy for the king's madness, finding herself aligned with Brewbarry's instincts before Vukradin redirected the group toward music.

When confronting guards Pog and Ulla near the barrel room, Valphine accused both gnomes of being the shapeshifter, deliberately using Ulla's own words against her and successfully turning the two gnomes on each other in paranoid accusation. She found the exercise amusing.

In the cellar, Valphine located the mimic by pointing a crossbow at the first barrel, which provoked it to transform and attack. She called out the discovery to the group, positioned herself clear of Soma's Ice Knife blast radius, and quoted a childhood rhyme after observing Vukradin's verbal attacks visibly hurt the creature.
