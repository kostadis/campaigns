# New notes for Alphonse "Big Al" Kalazorn (from dossier_extract_024.md)

Big Al is the owner of Butterskull Ranch and Petunia, a prized cow the party needed to secure for the wererat cheese supply. He mentioned that Qelline Alderleaf had offered to let Petunia graze on her land but that he lacked the one gold piece per week required. When the party returned with Qelline's condition that he apologize in person, they pressed him about his relationship with her, at which point he broke down in tears and confessed that he and Qelline had once been engaged. He had abandoned her the night before their wedding, admitting he had always loved her but hadn't been ready to settle down. At the party's insistence, he agreed to tell Qelline the full truth. He was last seen picking up Petunia and heading to Qelline's farm to make amends.
