# New notes for Alphonse "Big Al" Kalazorn (from dossier_extract_014.md)

Also known as "Big Al" Kalazorn, he is the owner of Butterskull Ranch and a retired sheriff. He is named in Harbin Wester's quest posting as the contact who can reward the party for their efforts if found alive. His current status is unknown — the quest implies he may be dead following an orc attack on the ranch.
