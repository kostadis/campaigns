# New notes for Xanth (from dossier_extract_041.md)

Xanth does not appear in this chunk beyond his departure scene. Before leaving, he stood before the group and looked at Soma's staff with recognition — not admiration but memory, the look of someone confirming something they already knew. He tapped Soma on the shell, told her the staff was blessed by Meril, and stated his belief that she would do great things. His parting words were "Peace, Beauty." He then looked at the staff one final time — a look described as measuring, or saying goodbye to it, or confirming something for himself — and departed into the trees.

Vukradin broke the resulting awkward silence by appreciating the phrase "hooves off" as Xanth did exactly that. Soma watched until the trees took him.

The narration frames Xanth's farewell as significant specifically because of what he knew about the staff and Meril — the certainty he carried about Soma having been chosen on purpose. This information, delivered at departure, is described as the kind of thing you receive and then carry at arm's length while figuring out what to do with it. Soma is actively doing that.

Last known whereabouts: departed the gully, direction unspecified.
