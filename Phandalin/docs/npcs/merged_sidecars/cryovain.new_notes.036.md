# New notes for cryovain (from dossier_extract_036.md)

Cryovain, a white dragon, was found on the rooftop battlements of Icespire Hold at the center of a burning summoning circle of alien geometry. His eyes were glazed and filmed over — present but not fully himself — as the Carver's obsidian weapon worked to dominate him mentally. He was fighting the influence, or had been; how much of him remained was unclear. He launched off the battlements, climbed thirty feet into the air in one wingbeat, and breathed ice over Brewbarry twice — hits severe enough to push the Goliath past his limits. Soma's moonbeam struck him repeatedly, Vukradin's bardic inspiration cracked something vital, and Valphine's sacred flame landed a final 3 points of damage that killed him. With his death, the Carver's essence dissipated entirely. Soma argued he was a victim of the Carver's influence and urged the party not to kill him; the plea went unheeded. His body remained on the rooftop. His hoarded treasure — approximately 3,000 gold fused with residium and ice into an inseparable block through planar magic — was left unspendable and unresolved.
