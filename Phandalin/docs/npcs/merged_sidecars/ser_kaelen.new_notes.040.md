# New notes for Ser Kaelen (from dossier_extract_040.md)

Ser Kaelen found the party almost immediately upon their return to the Stonehill Inn. He is described as having the quality of a man already in the room before you notice him. His eyes went to Soma's staff, and his expression shifted to something careful and calculating.

He said: "I see you were able to keep your staff," directed at Soma.

When Lyra entered behind the party in an incandescent mood, Ser Kaelen observed her and said, "Wow, you are in a good mood," before she launched into her account of the Druid Enclave outcome.

Later, Vukradin went to find Ser Kaelen specifically to tell him about Sister Kaella and about Corbin. Brewbarry's narration notes this conversation happened across the room and describes it as carrying weight, without detailing the exchange.

Last known whereabouts: Stonehill Inn, Phandalin.
