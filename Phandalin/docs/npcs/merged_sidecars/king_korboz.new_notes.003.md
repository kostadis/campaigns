# New notes for King Korboz (from dossier_extract_003.md)

King Korboz is the co-ruler of Gnomengarde, currently suffering from madness. He is in a state of hysterics, screaming, and immediately accused Vukradin of being a shapeshifter upon hearing his name. Inventors Fibblestib and Dabbledob are entirely focused on finding a cure for his condition. He believes a shape-changing creature is terrorizing the cave system and claims it caused the disappearance of gnomes Warren and Orrin. Current status: confined to his room, actively hysterical.
