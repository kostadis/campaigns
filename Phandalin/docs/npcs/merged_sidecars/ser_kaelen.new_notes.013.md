# New notes for Ser Kaelen (from dossier_extract_013.md)

Ser Kaelen appeared at the Stonehill Tavern and approached Vukradin. He inquired about Vukradin's work and plans. Vukradin did not trust him. When asked about the rightful ownership of treasure recovered from the ship, Ser Kaelen sighed and muttered something about how hard that was, which failed to reassure Vukradin. He praised Vukradin's goals, which pleased the bard given his need for universal approval. The two also discussed Vukradin's music in the context of the Order of the Gauntlet, with Vukradin asserting that such things could not be rushed and that whatever he produced would surpass anything Ser Kaelen had previously shared. His current status and organizational role were not further clarified in this session.
