# New notes for Falcon the Hunter (from dossier_extract_022.md)

Falcon greeted the returning party with surprise and pleasure, particularly relieved that his friend Vukradin had returned safely. He was impressed by the party's success in capturing prisoners and rescuing companions. Unable to offer a cash reward due to financial strain from his investment in Mountain Toe's Goldmine, he instead proposed giving the party a share of the mine if they investigated and resolved the poisoning of its waters. He revealed he is part of a Neverwinter mining consortium that acquired a discounted share in the mine in exchange for his consulting and scouting work. Falcon also mentioned an outstanding quest to clear orcs and half-orcs from the Woodland Manse, offering a pair of magical boots as reward. He provided the party with clear directions to the Woodland Manse and expressed a preference that they address the gold mine first, though the party ultimately chose the Manse. He agreed, after Vukradin's persuasion, to provide free accommodation for the entire group. Last known location: Falcon Hunting Lodge.
