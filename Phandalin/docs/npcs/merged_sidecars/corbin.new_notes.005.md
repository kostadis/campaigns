# New notes for corbin (from dossier_extract_005.md)

Corbin appeared before Vukradin as a new recruit, wearing scavenged leather armor taken from a dead man. Under interrogation he admitted to being a former bandit who took the armor out of survival necessity. He was commanded to remove it, then reprieved when Vukradin promised to find him fair-trade armor. During the manticore fight the creature identified him as its preferred target — a human — and flew toward him, forcing the party to act quickly to prevent his death. After the battle he arrived at the windmill looking worse and smelling badly, prompting another round of suspicion from Vukradin. He broke down and recounted his history to Soma: a farmer from Greenmeadow east of the High Forest, married with a young daughter named Lily, whose village and family were destroyed by the Black Lion Uthgardt barbarians during the chaos following the breaking of the Ordning. He survived by accident, drifted south, committed petty theft to survive, and eventually joined a bandit crew out of desperation. He helped Adabra prepare the evening meal at Vukradin's direction. Last known status: traveling with the party, unarmed and in scavenged armor.
