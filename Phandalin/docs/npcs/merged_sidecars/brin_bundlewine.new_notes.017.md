# New notes for Brin Bundlewine (from dossier_extract_017.md)

Brin Bundlewine is a pale-skinned, sunburned villager who approached Valphine at the tavern alongside Giles Slipper-Shine. He had heard rumors of a drow who worshiped Lathander and believed in the purifying pain of the sun, and was eager to establish a chapel dedicated to this interpretation of the sun god. He agreed to renovate Tresendar Manor into the Temple of Lathander's Searing Pain of Justice for twenty-five gold pieces to cover materials, providing his own labor for free.
