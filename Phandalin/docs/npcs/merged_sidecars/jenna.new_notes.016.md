# New notes for Jenna (from dossier_extract_016.md)

Jenna appeared at the town green during Vukradin's concert, watching him with a smirk throughout, including during the dragon attack. She is a woman in her late thirties with auburn hair, sharp green eyes, and practical high-quality traveling clothes, carrying a dark green wool doublet, sturdy stylish boots, and simple silver rings. Spider-Man identified her as working for the Lord's Alliance, recognizing her insignia and noting agents of that organization have a distinct smell. She later moved to the tavern and was approached by Brewbarry before she could reach Vukradin directly. She disarmed herself of a rapier, a dagger, a second dagger, and a hand crossbow at Brewbarry's insistence before being introduced to Vukradin. She revealed that she had sent the mysterious letter Vukradin previously received, using it as a way to open negotiations. Her purpose was to become Vukradin's agent, offering to book him performances before Waterdeep and Neverwinter nobility and potentially for Lord Nevermember himself. She raised House Margaster's funding of the Order of the Gauntlet as leverage to distance Vukradin from Ser Kaelen. She attempted to reframe Vukradin's "fair-trade gold" philosophy to accommodate taking dungeon treasure, arguing the gold was stolen from the people collectively and that spending it constitutes redistribution. Vukradin flatly rejected this argument and declined her offer, insisting on returning items to rightful owners. She expressed disappointment but there was a visible softening in her demeanor, suggesting genuine admiration for Vukradin even in refusal. She showed contempt toward Brewbarry, whom she called "Uthgartian" with evident disdain for Uthgardt peoples. No deal was reached. Her last known location is the tavern in Phandalin.
