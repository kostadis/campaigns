# New notes for Ser Kaelen (from dossier_extract_007.md)

Ser Kaelen Thorne was still present in Phandalin when the party returned escorting Elara and Jarek. Vukradin reported the incident to him, and Thorne responded positively, expressing that Vukradin's attention to good deeds on an individual level aligned with the broader values of the Order of the Gauntlet. He told Vukradin, "Bringing you into the fold was the right thing to do." When Vukradin pressed him for a cheer or battle chant of the Order, Thorne stammered with embarrassment before reluctantly producing: "Steal and shield, Heart and band. We stand as one. A steadfast band." He acknowledged the chant was embarrassing and that Vukradin's talents would improve anything the Order had. Last known status: in Phandalin.
