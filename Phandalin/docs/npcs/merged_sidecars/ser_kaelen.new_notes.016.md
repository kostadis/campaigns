# New notes for Ser Kaelen (from dossier_extract_016.md)

Ser Kaelen appeared in the aftermath of Harbin's accusations, claiming credit for pushing for a swift trial to clear Vukradin's name and framing it as a matter of the Order of the Gauntlet's integrity. His support of Vukradin was self-serving, focused on the Order's reputation rather than genuine concern. When Vukradin moved toward open revolution, Ser Kaelen pulled him aside and counseled pragmatism, warning that removing Harbin would prompt Lord Nevermember to send troops. He suggested a privy council solution as sufficient. He later told Jenna that Vukradin was a full-fledged member of the Order of the Gauntlet, a claim Vukradin disputed. His association with House Margaster funding was raised by Jenna as a point against him, suggesting his finances are tainted. He remains in Phandalin, now a member of the new privy council alongside Vukradin.
