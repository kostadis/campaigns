# New notes for Fibblestib (from dossier_extract_004.md)

One of the two gnome inventors who hired the party to deal with the mimic. Excited to receive proof of the creature's death, she immediately began discussing what construct could be made from its entrails—behavior Soma found deeply troubling. After overhearing the debate about the dual monarchy, Fiddlestib declared herself in favor of "a bicameral legislature with a unitary executive" and became absorbed in arguing with Dabblebob about the design of a tamper-proof voting box.
