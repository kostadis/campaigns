# New notes for Sylvine Wintermoon (from dossier_extract_037.md)

Sylvine Wintermoon arrived at Icespire Hold after the dragon was defeated, accompanied by her sister Runa and a group of veteran bandits. She approached Vukradin with a rehearsed performance, calling him a saint and claiming all 3,000 gold of dragon treasure belonged to her and her sister. She invoked the gods as collateral and framed the treasure as already rightfully theirs, presenting the handover as mere formality. She had clearly researched Vukradin's reputation as a principled bard who returns stolen treasure to its owners, and crafted her approach specifically to exploit that reputation. When Vukradin flatly refused and called her a liar, she briefly performed theatrical injury — "I am hurt! You are a thief!" — but dropped the act in under a second, revealing it was never genuine. She immediately pivoted to violence, declaring "Second plan! We kill them all!" The ensuing fight went badly for her side. Her current status after the skirmish is not explicitly stated, though her veterans were killed or fled.
