# New notes for vukardin (from dossier_extract_002.md)

Vukardin is an Aasimar bard who was performing at the First Flophouse in Heapside, Neverwinter, earning no coin. A flashback to 8/3 of Ches 1495 reveals he had been recruited by a Wintershield watchman to adventure in Phandalin, clearing monsters and keeping treasure. He pushed back morally at every step, arguing the scheme amounted to state-sanctioned banditry and money laundering, until the guard explained Lord Neverember's plan to use adventurers as a vanguard before establishing proper law. Unconvinced but broke, Vukardin quit the flophouse on 1/1 of Taraksh, recruited Brewbarry, and accepted Valphine's offer to join, declaring their goal would be honest coin.

On 8/1 of Taraksh, Vukardin led the group to the Stonehill Inn, where he negotiated meals in exchange for playing popular Waterdeep and Neverwinter songs for Toblen Stonehill's clientele. After several songs Toblen let him play his own composition and rewarded him with a free beer. Toblen warned him of a white dragon in the area, and Vukardin reacted with alarm. At the townmaster's hall he selected the Dwarven Excavation Quest for its 50 gp payout, justifying it as the most honest option despite Valphine's preference for Gnomengarde.

At the dwarven ruin on 9/1 of Taraksh, Vukardin delivered the dragon warning immediately and attempted to leave, only to be drawn back by the dwarves Dazlyn and Norbus's offer of sending stones as payment for clearing the temple of threats. He was persuaded when Valphine pointed out the stones could be used for musical effects. Inside the temple he twice attempted to parley with orcs rather than fight, was knocked unconscious both times by orc attacks, and had to be revived by Valphine both times. He also fired Starry Wisp at one orc. He played original compositions for Dazlyn and Norbus that evening.

On the return march on 10/1 of Taraksh, Vukardin attempted to speak with a whistling ogre, assuming it might be a musician or enslaved, was charged immediately, cast Starry Wisp to illuminate it, then hid while the party killed it. Back in Phandalin he collected the quest reward from Townmaster Harbin Wester but launched into a diatribe accusing adventurers of being glorified thugs, upsetting the townmaster. At the Lionshield Coster he attempted to solicit a protection contract from Linene Graywind, without success. Toblen again persuaded him to play popular music at the Stonehill Inn that evening.

On 1/2 of Taraksh, en route to Gnomengarde, Vukardin attempted to intimidate an ogre blocking the mountain path, failed, and instead accidentally convinced it the party was easy prey. Last known status: in the Sword Mountains on the road to Gnomengarde, engaged in combat with the ogre.
