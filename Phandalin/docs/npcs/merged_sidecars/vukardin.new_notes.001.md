# New notes for vukardin (from dossier_extract_001.md)

Vukardin is named as one of the adventurers recruited by Lord Neverember to clear the hinterlands around Neverwinter, specifically the area surrounding Phandalin. Described as an avant-garde musician whose music was underappreciated, Vukardin accepted Neverember's offer of tax-free, no-questions-asked plunder in exchange for eliminating monsters, cults, and evildoers from the region. No further details about Vukardin's current status or whereabouts are provided in this excerpt.
