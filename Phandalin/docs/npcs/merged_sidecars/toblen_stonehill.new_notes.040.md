# New notes for Toblen Stonehill (from dossier_extract_040.md)

Toblen Stonehill (referred to in narration as "Spider-Man," confirmed as his actual name after Brewbarry asked twice) appeared briefly at the Stonehill Inn. Lyra directed him to get Vukradin "at least two beers," and extended the order to include two beers for Brewbarry as well. He is operating in his role as innkeeper.

Last known whereabouts: Stonehill Inn, Phandalin.
