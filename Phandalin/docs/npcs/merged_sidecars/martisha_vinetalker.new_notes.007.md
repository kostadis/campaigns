# New notes for Martisha Vinetalker (from dossier_extract_007.md)

Martisha Vinetalker is the current owner of the Wayside Inn, encountered when the party stopped there overnight during their journey toward the Tower of Storms. She is described as a striking half-elf with pleasing features offset by a stern, cold, and calculating demeanor, though also charismatic. She directed the party to the coastal trail leading toward the Tower of Storms. Last known status: at the Wayside Inn.
