# New notes for Qelline Alderleaf (from dossier_extract_024.md)

Qelline Alderleaf is a local Phandalin farmer with deep-seated resentment toward Big Al. She had offered to let Big Al's prized cow Petunia graze on her land for one gold piece per week—a deliberately inflated "hurt price" meant to make Big Al suffer financially, stemming from her history with him. When the party approached to negotiate on Big Al's behalf, she immediately recognized that he had sent intermediaries rather than coming himself, which sharpened her hostility. She revealed that during Big Al's prosperous days at Butterskull Ranch, his competitive pricing had severely damaged her farm's income. She agreed to reduce the fee to five silver pieces per week, but only on the condition that Big Al come to apologize to her in person. Her motivation is personal injury compounded by financial grievance. Last seen at her farm, where Big Al arrived carrying Petunia to make amends; the party chose not to interfere in their private reconciliation.
