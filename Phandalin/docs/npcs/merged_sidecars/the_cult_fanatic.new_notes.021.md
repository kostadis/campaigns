# New notes for The Cult Fanatic (from dossier_extract_021.md)

The Cult Fanatic was encountered in the glade on the eastern shore, wielding a black coral dagger and accompanied by three mind-controlled Emerald Enclave patrol members moving with robotic precision. He coldly informed the party they were interfering with necessary adjustments and that his zealots served a greater purpose. He spoke of a rising current flowing toward Neverwinter and offered the party a chance to submit. During combat he attempted to compel Brewbarry's mind, forcing him to flee, and even while unconscious he exerted control over his minions. When briefly interrogated, he muttered only vague warnings about tides rising and waves coming, refusing to betray his cause. He wore a spiral emblem identifying him as a member of the Kraken Society. On the second day of travel, a Talosian orc raiding party ambushed the party demanding his death, identifying him as their enemy. Given a final chance to speak, he referred only to petty squabbles that meant nothing to what was coming. Valphine executed him, and the orcs took his head as proof of victory. His death broke the mind control over all his captives. The Kraken Society Echoes are implicated in his activities.
