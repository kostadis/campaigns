# New notes for moesko (from dossier_extract_008.md)

Moesko, the Orc Anchorite, was found seated in a barnacle-encrusted chair in the upper floor of the Tower of Storms, clad in armor made from giant octopus hide. In his lap rested an opalescent conch — an artifact belonging to Miral the Self (also described as Miral the Sea Elf, an undead creature). His presence at the tower and possession of the conch suggest he was stationed there as a guardian or had claimed the artifact for himself or on behalf of a patron.

In combat, Moesko demonstrated considerable power, retaliating against the party with a Lightning Bolt that dealt staggering damage to most of the group (all except Brewbarry), followed by a Thunderwave that dealt additional damage and pushed Soma back in her brown bear form. He survived Soma's multi-attack in bear form, Vukradin's Cloud of Daggers (which dealt 9 initial damage and would continue to damage him if he remained inside the 5-foot cube), and Brewbarry's charge — which landed a hit that reduced him to a single hit point. Soma, reverting from bear form to navigate the cramped tower corridor, delivered the killing blow.

The opalescent conch was recovered from Moesko's body and is now in the party's possession.
