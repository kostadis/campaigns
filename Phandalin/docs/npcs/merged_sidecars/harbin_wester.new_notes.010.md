# New notes for Harbin Wester (from dossier_extract_010.md)

The Townmaster of Phandalin answered his door surprised to see the party alive. When informed of the Talos infestation at the lighthouse and that the party had sanctified the altar, he was visibly relieved. He expressed that Lord Neverwinter would be pleased and promised to send him a note, but showed no urgency about repairing the lighthouse light. When Vukradin suggested delivering the note personally to Lord Neverember, Harbin warned against it, noting that Neverember was very busy and that appearing before the ruler with their mission incomplete was inadvisable, adding through narrowed eyes that it was not his place to tell them what to do — a clear cautionary signal. He acknowledged problems remained in the town and reminded the party they had signed on to address them. Last seen at his home in Phandalin.
