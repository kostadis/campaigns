# New notes for Harbin Wester (from dossier_extract_024.md)

Harbin Wester, the Lord Mayor of Phandalin, greeted the party warmly upon their return from the gold mine peace negotiations, expressing satisfaction with recent changes in the community. His mood turned serious when Brewbarry raised the topic of the town's ale supply; Harbin warned that a dragon occupying the mountains and the resulting orc incursions were preventing farmers from tending their crops, threatening the season's ale production. He presented the party with several new quests—investigating Dragon Barrow, securing Axeholm fortress, and exploring the Woodland Manse—explaining these were funded by Neverwinter's tax system and that the rewards could help Vukradin establish his music studio. When Vukradin questioned the safety of gold transport from Neverwinter, Harbin revealed the city's troubled history of decades of catastrophes, corruption, and crime, noting that Lord Neverember was attempting to remedy this through progressive reforms. Last seen in Phandalin in his role as Lord Mayor.
