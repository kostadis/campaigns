# New notes for adabra (from dossier_extract_012.md)

Adabra did not appear directly in this session but was referenced repeatedly by Soma as a philosophical mentor and guide. Soma recalled that Adabra had warned her against interventionists, characterizing them as the true blight — those who disrupt the natural order by meddling in planar events. Adabra's teachings were the framework through which Soma initially interpreted the Whispering Grove, predisposing her to view the Emerald Enclave's cleansing effort with suspicion.

Soma noted that Adabra had never spoken of a "motivated blight" — a deliberate, puppet-controlled corruption — and that this gap in her mentor's teachings created the central tension of the session. By the end, Soma resolved to seek Adabra out directly to demand an explanation of the paradox: how can the naturalist position justify accepting a calculated, purposeful desecration of the natural world? Adabra's current whereabouts are not specified, but she remains Soma's intended next destination.
