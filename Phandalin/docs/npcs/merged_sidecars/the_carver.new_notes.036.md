# New notes for The Carver (from dossier_extract_036.md)

The Carver was an alien intelligence operating through an obsidian weapon — described as a mental amplifier — to dominate Cryovain via a burning geometric summoning circle on the battlements of Icespire Hold. It needed three more rounds to complete the ritual when the party engaged. When Cryovain was killed, the Carver's essence dissipated instantly and completely — not fled, not retreated, but unmade by the same stroke. Its obsidian sword remained behind, its crystal pommel containing a gem capable of projecting music directly into the minds of an audience. Vukradin recognized the gem as belonging to a rightful owner and refused to keep it despite the instrument being perfectly suited to his ambitions. The Carver had no further presence after the dragon's death.
