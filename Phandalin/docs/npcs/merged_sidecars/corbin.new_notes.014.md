# New notes for corbin (from dossier_extract_014.md)

Corbin is traveling with the party as their aide and has a past membership in Carver's gang. When the party encountered four brigands in the ruined town of Conyberry whom Corbin identified as members of Carver's gang, Vukradin pressured him to approach and speak with them. Corbin resisted, explaining that leaving a gang carries serious consequences, but Vukradin overruled his objections and insisted he go speak to his former associates.
