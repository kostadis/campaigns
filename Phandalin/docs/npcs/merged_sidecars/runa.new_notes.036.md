# New notes for runa (from dossier_extract_036.md)

Runa accompanied her sister Sylvine Wintermoon to Icespire Hold as part of a scripted approach to claim Cryovain's hoard. She spoke only to echo Sylvine's performance — "Blessed be! Blessed be!" — on cue, with the affect of someone waiting to remember their lines. Soma characterized her as having rehearsed the approach on the road. She had no independent action recorded during the confrontation or the subsequent fight.
