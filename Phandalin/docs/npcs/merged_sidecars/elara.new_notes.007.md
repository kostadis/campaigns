# New notes for elara (from dossier_extract_007.md)

Elara is a middle-aged human merchant encountered with her groom Jarek on the road to the Tower of Storms. The party found her and Jarek huddled behind an overturned trade cart, visibly shaken after an orc attack that scattered their pack horses. She was initially terrified upon seeing the party's unusual composition, but was calmed when Valphine invoked Lathander — though she was confused by Valphine's description of the god as "painful," noting she had never heard that teaching.

Elara described her attackers as moving with unnerving discipline unusual for orcs, and mentioned they bore strange markings — possibly a swirl or an eye shape — though she seemed unable to hold the memory clearly. She accepted Soma's offer to mend the cart and asked the party to retrieve one of her horses from a nearby field. Upon Brewbarry's return with the horse, she offered a reward, which she gave directly to Valphine in the name of the Morninglord rather than to Vukradin, who had expected payment.

The party, at Vukradin's insistence, escorted Elara and Jarek safely back to Phandalin. Last known status: safely returned to Phandalin.
