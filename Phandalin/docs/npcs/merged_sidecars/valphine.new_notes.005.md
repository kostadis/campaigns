# New notes for valphine (from dossier_extract_005.md)

Valphine fired the opening shot against the manticore with her hand crossbow, hitting it but failing to do significant damage. She absorbed a volley of tail spikes when the manticore singled her out in retaliation. She attempted a second shot later in the fight and missed. When Adabra emerged from the windmill, Valphine immediately offered to teach her about Lathander. She reflected internally on the manticore's offer to take Corbin in exchange for sparing the rest of the group, noting it was "a fair trade" she would have considered in a different life. She observed Vukradin's uncertainty in combat with the eye of someone who has read gamblers before. She noted that the food Adabra prepared was exceptional and attributed its quality to Lathander's glory. Last known status: present at Umbrage Hill, preparing to return to Phandalin.
