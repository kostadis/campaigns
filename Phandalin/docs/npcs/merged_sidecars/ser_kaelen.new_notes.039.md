# New notes for Ser Kaelen (from dossier_extract_039.md)

Ser Kaelen broke the pageantry of Harbin's welcome festival by drawing Soma aside with quiet urgency to deliver bad news. He briefed the party on Adabra's political maneuvering within the Emerald Enclave — specifically that Adabra was moving to ostracize Lyra from the druidic order and was building a case against Soma as a dangerous interventionist who held Meril's Staff without the Enclave's blessing. Ser Kaelen's briefing set the stage for the party's journey to the druid council to defend Lyra.
