# New notes for Linene Graywind (from dossier_extract_013.md)

Linene Graywind, described as a 35-year-old woman managing the Lionshield Coster, received the party when they came to sell their pelts. She was notably enthusiastic and asked repeated questions about where and how the pelts were found. Vukradin answered honestly, which Valphine privately considered a waste of leverage. Linene offered more than twice Barthen's price, which she appeared to justify by referencing her bosses' interest in the planar effects associated with the goods — suggesting the Lionshield Coster has organizational ties or commercial interests connected to interplanar phenomena. Valphine recalled reading in Neverwinter that merchants had possessed an interplanar shipping device that had stopped working, and connected this to Linene's enthusiasm. Linene also attended the evening performance at the Stonehill Tavern, where she specifically praised Valphine as the one who made the ensemble shine, to Vukradin's brief annoyance. Last seen at the Stonehill Tavern.
