# Elara

## Identity
- Middle-aged human merchant; no stated faction affiliation
- First appeared in Session notes: found with her groom Jarek behind an overturned trade cart after an orc ambush on the road; the party aided her and escorted her back to Phandalin

## Personality & Motivations
- Pragmatic and grateful; accepted practical help (cart repair, horse retrieval) without hesitation
- Devout toward Lathander, or at least deferential to the faith — directed her reward to Valphine as the Morninglord's representative rather than to the party at large
- Charitable in interpretation: she read Valphine's odd remark about Lathander being "painful" as an unfamiliar but sincere expression of faith rather than disrespect

## History with the Party
1. **Road ambush:** Elara and Jarek were attacked by a disciplined orc warband bearing swirl/eye markings. The party found them sheltering behind their overturned cart with pack horses scattered.
2. **Aid rendered:** Soma mended the cart; Brewbarry retrieved a runaway horse.
3. **Reward given:** Elara presented the reward to Valphine in Lathander's name, not to Vukradin, who had apparently expected it.
4. **Returned to Phandalin** safely with the party's help.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Phandalin
- No active plans noted
- **Party knows:** She is safe and grateful
- **Hidden/unknown:** Her broader business, her destination, and whether she will appear again

## Relationships
- **Jarek (groom):** Traveling companion; present during the ambush, no further detail noted
- **Valphine:** Elara showed her notable deference, viewing her as Lathander's representative; interpreted her unconventional faith-talk charitably
- **Vukradin:** Did *not* direct the reward to him — potential minor tension depending on how Vukradin received that
- **Soma / Brewbarry:** Received practical help from both; grateful
