# Delaan Winterhound

## Identity
- Role/Title: Senior (possibly highest-ranking) member of the Emerald Enclave during the War of the Dragons era
- Faction: Emerald Enclave
- First Appearance: Mentioned in passing by Lyra; never appeared directly at the table

## Personality & Motivations
- Initially held a non-interventionist position consistent with naturalist philosophy — the Enclave's role was to protect the natural order, not wade into mortal conflicts.
- Was persuadable: the hero Malfire convinced him to commit Enclave forces against Tiamat's summoning, suggesting he was principled but not dogmatic.

## History with the Party
- **Pre-campaign (historical):** During the War of the Dragons, Delaan initially kept the Emerald Enclave neutral. After persuasion by Malfire, he reversed course and committed Enclave forces to prevent Tiamat's entry into the Prime Material Plane. This decision was significant enough to fracture the Enclave ideologically — his choice became the defining fault line of a subsequent internal split.
- **In-session:** Referenced only once, by Lyra, as the wisest Enclave member of that era. No direct interaction with the party.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Unknown
- **Current Activity:** Unknown — all information is historical
- **Party Knowledge:** Know of him only through Lyra's account; aware he was influential and that his intervention decision reshaped the Enclave
- **Hidden:** Whether he is alive, dead, or still active is entirely unknown

## Relationships
- **Malfire (hero, historical):** Was persuaded by Malfire to abandon neutrality; nature of their relationship beyond this moment is unknown
- **The Emerald Enclave:** His decision effectively created the ideological fault line that split the organization — he is a foundational and likely controversial figure within the faction
- **Lyra:** Spoke of him with apparent respect ("wisest member"); exact relationship unclear
