# Don-Jon Raskin

## Identity
- **Role:** Overseer of Mountain's Toe Gold Mine
- **Faction:** Employed by a Neverwinter consortium (at least one member confirmed affiliated with the Kraken Society)
- **First appearance:** Phandalin tavern; introduced himself to the party through boastful storytelling

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Fulfill his contract as mine overseer; survive and succeed despite knowing nothing about actual mining operations
- Verbose, boastful, and self-aggrandizing — spins elaborate tales of past adventures as prospector, miner, fur trader, privateer, and whaler
- Oblivious to social nuance (his "acceptance" into a barbarian tribe was, by the party's read, the tribe simply thinking him a fool)
- Beneath the bravado, pragmatic enough to be persuaded when presented with a workable arrangement

## History with the Party
1. **Phandalin Tavern:** Party encountered Raskin holding court with tall tales. Vukradin identified a Kraken Society connection among his employers.
2. **Negotiation:** Raskin admitted ignorance of the mine and his need for an escort. He initially objected to wererats sharing the mine as a living space on safety grounds, but Vukradin persuaded him. He accepted the arrangement negotiated with Zeleen Varnaster.
3. **Mountain's Toe Gold Mine:** Traveled to the mine with the party as his escort.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Mountain's Toe Gold Mine
- **Active role:** Settling in as mine overseer under terms of the negotiated arrangement with the wererats
- **What the party knows:** He is employed by a Neverwinter consortium with Kraken Society ties; he has little to no operational knowledge of the mine
- **What remains hidden:** The full nature and intentions of his Neverwinter employers; how deeply (if at all) he is personally connected to or aware of the Kraken Society involvement

## Relationships
- **Neverwinter Consortium:** His employers; at least one member is Kraken Society-affiliated
- **Zeleen Varnaster:** Accepted a negotiated living/working arrangement involving her and her group at the mine
- **Vukradin:** Vukradin identified the Kraken Society link and successfully persuaded Raskin during negotiations — the key party member in his interactions to date
- **The Party:** Hired as his escort to the mine; relationship is transactional but not hostile
