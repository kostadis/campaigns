# Don-Jon Raskin

## Identity
- **Role:** Newly hired overseer of the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine
- **Faction:** Employed by an unnamed Neverwinter consortium (at least one member has ties to the **Kraken Society**)
- **First Appearance:** Encountered at the tavern in Phandalin, holding court with boastful stories

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Fulfill his contract as mine overseer; survive and thrive through charm and bravado
- A habitual self-aggrandizer who spins tall tales of his past as prospector, miner, fur trader, privateer, and whaler — most likely embellished. He is socially adept but lacks practical knowledge of the job he's been hired to do. His obliviousness extends to social situations (he believed he'd been accepted into a barbarian tribe; the party recognized he'd been played for a fool). Despite this, his natural friendliness is genuine and functionally useful.

## History with the Party
- Met the party at the Phandalin tavern; was entertaining locals with tall tales
- Revealed his role as incoming mine overseer and admitted he knew nothing about the mine; was contractually required to hire an escort
- Traveled with the party to the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine
- His friendly demeanor helped de-escalate initial tensions with the wererats at the mine entrance
- Initially opposed allowing the wererats to remain in the mine on safety grounds; was persuaded by **Vukradin** to accept a compromise arrangement permitting cohabitation with mining operations

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Mountain's Toe Gold Mine (newly installed as overseer)
- **Active Plans:** Taking up his post and beginning oversight of mining operations under the compromise arrangement with the wererats
- **What the Party Knows:** He was hired by a Neverwinter consortium; he is inexperienced and largely clueless about the mine
- **Hidden from the Party:** The full nature and intentions of his Neverwinter employers; the extent of Kraken Society involvement in the consortium is known only to Vukradin (not necessarily shared with the full party)

## Relationships
- **Neverwinter Consortium:** His employers; at least one member is Kraken Society-affiliated — he may be unaware of this
- **The Wererats:** Reluctant acceptance following Vukradin's negotiation; relationship is cautious but functional
- **Vukradin:** Vukradin spotted the Kraken Society connection and brokered the wererat compromise; Raskin appears to respect his judgment
- **The Party (general):** Hired them as his escort; friendly and reliant on them
