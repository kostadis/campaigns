# King Gnercli

## Identity
- **Role:** Co-King of the gnome settlement; joint ruler alongside King Korboz
- **Faction:** Gnome settlement monarchy
- **First Appearance:** Throne room of the gnome settlement; greeted the party as their spokesperson when they arrived seeking information about a shapechanger threat

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Maintaining his standing as a legitimate co-equal ruler; resolving the shapechanger threat endangering the settlement
- Gnercli is the more composed and lucid of the two kings, serving as the practical face of their shared rule. He is proud and sensitive about his authority, bristling at any implication that Korboz outranks him. He tolerates outside questioning up to a point but becomes visibly annoyed when pressed on governance matters, particularly by Vukardin.

## History with the Party
- **First meeting:** Gnercli received the party in the throne room, confirmed the shapechanger threat, and offered a Hat of Wizardry as a reward for locating and eliminating the creature. He suggested it might be hiding in the cellar among barrels due to its non-bipedal form, and candidly admitted he had little else of value to offer.
- **Subsequent interaction:** Gnercli pushed back firmly against Vukardin's implications that one king held seniority over the other, explaining that the dual monarchy functions through collaborative argument. The debate eventually inflamed Korboz's demands for recognition of seniority. Exasperated, Gnercli handed over the promised magical hat and told the party to leave.
- Gnercli consistently characterized the party as adventurers despite Vukardin's insistence on framing the mission as diplomatic.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Gnome settlement, throne room
- **Active Situation:** In ongoing conflict with Korboz over questions of seniority, likely stirred up by Vukardin's persistent questioning
- **What the Party Knows:** He is the more stable of the two kings, is sensitive about co-equal authority, and was annoyed enough to end the audience abruptly
- **Hidden:** No additional hidden information noted in session records

## Relationships
- **King Korboz:** Co-ruler and counterpart; their relationship is cooperative but contentious — they govern by arguing until the best argument wins. The party's visit left their dynamic visibly strained.
- **Vukardin (party):** Source of notable irritation; Gnercli found Vukardin's governance questions presumptuous and his "diplomatic mission" framing dismissive. Asked pointedly where the bard had learned so much about governance.
- **Party (general):** Transactional but not hostile; fulfilled his end of the reward deal and dismissed them when the meeting became unproductive.
