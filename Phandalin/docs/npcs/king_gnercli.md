# King Gnercli

## Identity

Co-ruler of the kobold kingdom, alongside King Korboz; functional decision-maker during Korboz's illness. First encountered in the throne room, where the party sought an audience under Vukradin's cover story of a diplomatic mission.

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations

- Core goal: maintain stability and the dual monarchy structure; resolve the shapeshifter threat without revealing the kingdom's vulnerability
- Perceptive and pragmatic — saw through Vukradin's diplomatic pretense immediately
- Genuinely invested in the dual monarchy as a political philosophy, framing it as a safeguard against unfit leadership; grew furious when Vukradin systematically picked his arguments apart
- Short-tempered under pressure; ultimately shoved the hat of wizardry at the party and ordered them out rather than continue the argument

## History with the Party

1. **First meeting:** Gnercli received the party in the throne room, recognized them as adventurers despite Vukradin's claims, and hired them to eliminate a shapeshifter lurking in the kingdom. Offered a hat of wizardry as payment.
2. **Negotiation breakdown:** When Vukradin questioned the hat's usefulness against a dragon, Gnercli admitted he had little else to offer. He suggested the cellar — packed with large barrels the creature could mimic — as the most probable hiding spot.
3. **Philosophical argument:** Gnercli defended the dual monarchy's structure at length, insisting neither king holds seniority and that decisions go to whoever makes the best argument. He explained the system exists specifically to prevent "despotic autocratic decision making of a single individual who could be rendered unfit for leadership because of a shapeshifter." Vukradin dismantled his reasoning point by point. Gnercli ended the audience in visible fury, shoved the hat at the party, and told them to leave. He was last heard in renewed conflict with Korboz through the closed throne room door.

## Current Status

- **Last known location:** Throne room (heard through the door, not seen directly)
- **Active situation:** In the middle of a heated argument with Korboz following the party's dismissal
- **What the party knows:** Gnercli is the one actually running things; he wants the shapeshifter dead; he's resource-poor and temperamental
- **What remains hidden:** The full nature and severity of Korboz's illness; what the renewed conflict with Korboz is actually about; whether Gnercli has any further resources or leverage to offer

## Relationships

- **King Korboz:** Co-ruler and philosophical counterpart; currently in open conflict with him following the party's audience
- **The party (general):** Transactional relationship — hired them out of necessity; ended the meeting on poor terms
- **Vukradin specifically:** Active antagonism; Vukradin embarrassed him by dismantling his core political arguments in front of witnesses
