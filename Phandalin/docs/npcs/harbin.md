# Harbin Wester

## Identity
- **Role:** Townmaster of Phandalin
- **Faction:** Town governance; answers ultimately to Lord Neverwinter (Nevermember)
- **First Appearance:** Early interactions in Phandalin; came into conflict with the party over his treatment of Vukradin

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Preserve his title and authority as Townmaster; maintain the appearance of legitimacy and control
- Harbin is procedurally minded and bureaucratic — he stalls, deflects, and hides behind process when pressured. He invokes hierarchy and proper authority as shields rather than principles. He is capable of political flexibility when cornered, pivoting to compromise to protect his position rather than out of genuine conviction.

## History with the Party
- Made accusations against Vukradin that the party found deeply offensive, creating immediate antagonism
- When confronted simultaneously by Vukradin and Ser Kaelen, he invoked Lord Nevermember's authority as the only power that could remove him from office
- When Vukradin threatened to organize a town meeting on the green, Harbin stalled by insisting proper meetings require preparation, agendas, and pre-work
- After Vukradin repelled the white dragon attack using phantasmal force, Harbin publicly acknowledged his heroism
- Proposed a compromise: formation of a privy council (himself, Tolbin, Elmer, Halia, Linene, Adabra, and Vukradin) to share governance while preserving his Townmaster title

## Current Status
- **Location:** Phandalin
- **Status:** Remains Townmaster, but with significantly reduced unilateral authority
- **Active Situation:** Operates within — and likely chafes against — the newly formed privy council
- **What the party knows:** He backed down under pressure and accepted shared governance
- **What remains hidden:** Whether his public acknowledgment of Vukradin is sincere or a tactical retreat; his long-term intentions regarding the council

## Relationships
- **Vukradin:** Began as an accuser/adversary; now a fellow privy council member. Relationship is transactional at best
- **Ser Kaelen:** Applied pressure alongside Vukradin; Harbin deflected by citing Nevermember's authority
- **Lord Nevermember:** Harbin's ultimate political patron and the basis for his claim to office
- **Privy Council (Tolbin, Elmer, Halia, Linene, Adabra):** Now his governance partners; their individual loyalties and dynamics with Harbin are unestablished

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease:** Initial accusations against Vukradin and bureaucratic obstruction of the town meeting damaged standing with the party
- **Increase:** Public acknowledgment of Vukradin's heroism and willingness to propose power-sharing partially rehabilitated his reputation
