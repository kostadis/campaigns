# Barthen

## Identity
- **Role:** Proprietor of Barthen's Provisions
- **Faction:** None noted; operates as a local merchant
- **First Appearance:** Party visited his shop in town to fulfill a supply order

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations
- Practical, accommodating merchant focused on fulfilling orders efficiently.
- Helpful without being intrusive — proactively offered a solution when Brewbarry noticed the order lacked wine, suggesting the Falcon Hunting Lodge and providing a letter of introduction.

## History with the Party
- Filled Harbin Wester's brother's supply order for the Logger's Camp: two heavy crates of dried meats, cheese, bread, ale, and oil, loaded onto a cart pulled by an ox named Vincent.
- When Brewbarry flagged the absence of wine, Barthen directed the party to the Falcon Hunting Lodge (noting it was known for fine wine) and supplied them with a letter of introduction granting lodge access, suggesting they stop there along the way.
