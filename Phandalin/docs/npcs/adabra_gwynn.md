# Adabra Gwynn

## Identity
- **Role:** Local midwife of Phandalin; acolyte of Chauntea
- **Faction:** Church of Chauntea
- **First Appearance:** Not yet met directly. Introduced via a Townmaster notice board quest in the party's first session in Phandalin.

## Personality & Motivations
No direct interaction yet. Known only by reputation as a midwife living alone outside of town.

## History with the Party
- The party became aware of Adabra through a Townmaster quest offering **25 gp** to persuade her to return to Phandalin due to increased danger from dragon sightings in the region.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** A stone windmill on a hillside, a few miles south of Phandalin.
- Living alone; has not returned to town despite the surrounding danger.
- **Active situation:** The Townmaster considers her at risk and wants her brought back safely.

## Relationships

- **The Party:** No direct relationship established yet.
