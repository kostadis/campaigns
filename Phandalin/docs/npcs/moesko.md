# Moesko, the Orc Anchorite

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Anchorite of Talos (corrupted); apparent guardian/operator of the Tower of Storms
- **Faction:** Ostensibly Talos worship; likely also a tool of an unknown psionic/foreign power
- **First Appearance:** Found seated at the top of the Tower of Storms, barnacle-encrusted chair, wearing giant octopus hide armor

## Personality & Motivations
- Surface-level devotion to Talos, but Miral's ghost indicated this had been twisted "colder and more calculating" by an outside influence.
- Not entirely acting of his own will — shadows moved within him, suggesting he was at least partially a vessel or puppet rather than a true villain.

## History with the Party
- The party discovered Moesko at the top of the Tower of Storms, holding an opalescent conch belonging to the undead sea elf Miral.
- His chest bore a cavity where his heart had been removed and installed in the lighthouse beacon, powering a corrupted green light responsible for shipwrecks along the coast.
- A concealed compartment in his quarters held a dark talisman marked with Talos's lightning bolt on one side and an inverted eye with tentacle-like lines on the reverse, radiating both divine and psionic energy.
- Brewbarry reduced him to 1 HP; Soma delivered the killing blow.
- Vukradin subsequently destroyed Moesko's extracted heart, ending the corrupted beacon.
- Miral's ghost later provided context: a foreign power beyond Talos had been working through Moesko, co-opting his faith.

## Current Status
- **Deceased.** Killed by Soma; heart destroyed by Vukradin.
- The Tower of Storms beacon is no longer corrupted.

## What the Party Knows vs. What Remains Hidden
- **Known:** Moesko was a Talos worshiper operating the corrupted lighthouse; his heart powered the beacon; he possessed Miral's conch; he was not fully in control of his own actions.
- **Hidden:** The identity of the foreign psionic power that was working through him; the full purpose of the dual-sided talisman; why Moesko specifically was chosen as a vessel.

## Relationships
- **Miral (undead sea elf):** Moesko held her opalescent conch; Miral's ghost confirmed he was being manipulated by a power beyond Talos.
- **Unknown Psionic Entity:** The true power directing Moesko's actions; connected to the inverted eye symbol on the talisman.
- **The Party:** Defeated in combat. Soma (killing blow), Brewbarry (set up the kill), Vukradin (destroyed the heart).
