# Moesko

## Identity
- **Role:** Orc Anchorite; dark cleric stationed at (or claiming) the Tower of Storms
- **Faction:** Nominal devotee of Talos, though his allegiance was compromised by a second, alien influence
- **First Appearance:** Found seated in a barnacle-encrusted chair on the upper floor of the Tower of Storms, wearing giant octopus hide armor and holding an opalescent conch

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations
- Presented as a guardian figure — whether self-appointed or placed by a patron is unclear
- His devotion to Talos was real but corrupted: Miral's ghost described shadows moving within it, twisting his faith into something "colder and more calculating" — foreign rather than the Stormlord's wrath
- His true motivations remain opaque; he may have been a partially unwilling instrument of a separate power
- Did not act entirely of his own will — externally influenced rather than autonomous in his actions

## History with the Party
1. **Combat at the Tower of Storms:** The party engaged Moesko on the upper floor. He retaliated with a Lightning Bolt that dealt staggering damage to most of the group (all except Brewbarry), followed by a Thunderwave that dealt additional damage and pushed Soma back in her brown bear form. He survived Soma's multi-attack in bear form, Vukradin's Cloud of Daggers (which dealt 9 initial damage and would continue to damage him if he remained inside the 5-foot cube), and Brewbarry's charge — which landed a hit that reduced him to a single hit point. Soma, reverting from bear form to navigate the cramped tower corridor, delivered the killing blow.
2. **Post-mortem examination:** His defeated form was examined at the start of the following session. His body displayed a strange hole in his chest where his heart had been removed and installed as the power source for the lighthouse beacon, where it continued beating and emitting pulsating green light. He wore distinctive octopus armor. Vukradin destroyed the heart by striking it from the mechanism, breaking the tower's curse.
3. **Talisman discovered:** A concealed compartment in his crude living space contained a dark, iridescent talisman etched with the stylized lightning bolt of Talos on one side and an inverted eye with coiling tentacle-like lines on the reverse. It emanated faint divine resonance and a dominant, alien psionic energy.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Heart destroyed by Vukradin. Tower curse broken.
- The opalescent conch (belonging to Miral the Self) was recovered from his body and is now in the party's possession.

## Relationships
- **Miral the Self (Miral the Sea Elf):** Moesko held Miral's conch — the nature of that relationship (theft, guardianship, orders from a patron) is unresolved. Miral's ghost implied Moesko's corruption was externally imposed.
- **Talos:** Nominal patron deity, though Moesko's devotion was subverted by a separate alien influence.
- **Unknown Patron/Entity:** Suggested by the psionic talisman's reverse sigil (inverted eye, coiling tentacles) — something cold, calculating, and of alien origin was working through or alongside Moesko.

## Arc Score Events
- No arc score changes recorded.
