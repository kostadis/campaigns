# Elara

## Identity
- **Role:** Human merchant, middle-aged
- **Affiliation:** None known; follower of Lathander
- **First Appearance:** Road to the Tower of Storms — party found her and her groom Jarek sheltering behind an overturned trade cart following an orc attack

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Safe conduct of her trade; devotion to Lathander
- Elara presents as a frightened but practical woman who composed herself quickly once trust was established. She is devout enough to direct payment specifically to Lathander's representative rather than the party's apparent leader. She noted theological confusion at Valphine's description of the Morninglord as "painful" — suggesting she holds a more conventional understanding of the faith.
- Initially terrified upon seeing the party's unusual composition, but calmed when Valphine invoked Lathander.

## History with the Party
1. **Road to the Tower of Storms:** Party encountered Elara and Jarek on the road to the Tower of Storms. They found her and Jarek huddled behind an overturned trade cart, visibly shaken after an orc attack that scattered their pack horses. She was initially terrified upon seeing the party's unusual composition, but was calmed when Valphine invoked Lathander — though she was confused by Valphine's description of the god as "painful," noting she had never heard that teaching.
2. Elara reported her attackers moved with unnerving discipline unusual for orcs and bore strange markings (possibly a swirl or eye shape), though she struggled to hold the memory clearly.
3. Soma offered to mend the cart; the party was asked to retrieve one of her horses from a nearby field, which Brewbarry successfully did.
4. Upon Brewbarry's return with the horse, Elara offered a reward, which she paid directly to Valphine in the Morninglord's name, bypassing Vukradin, who had expected payment.
5. The party (at Vukradin's insistence) escorted Elara and Jarek safely back to Phandalin.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Phandalin — returned safely with her groom Jarek
- **Active Plans:** None known
- **Party Knows:** Her route, her devotion to Lathander, her account of the orc attack
- **Hidden / Unresolved:** Her inability to clearly recall the orcs' markings is notable — possible magical suppression or memory effect. The markings themselves (swirl or eye shape) are unidentified.

## Relationships
- **Jarek:** Her groom; traveled with her, no further detail recorded
- **Valphine:** Favored; Elara directed payment to her, but noted confusion over Valphine's characterization of Lathander
- **Vukradin:** Mild friction — he expected payment and was passed over
- **Soma / Brewbarry:** Received practical help; no notable tension
