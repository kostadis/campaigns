# King Korboz

## Identity

**Role:** Co-ruler (senior king) of Gnomengarde, dual monarchy shared with King Gnercli  
**Faction:** Gnomengarde  
**First Appearance:** The party encountered him confined to his room in the Gnomengarde cave system, in a state of active hysteria

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations

- **Core Goals:** Have the shapeshifter threat taken seriously; be believed and respected as senior king
- Paranoid and volatile, prone to outbursts — immediately accused Vukradin of being a shapeshifter upon hearing his name
- Feels chronically dismissed by his co-ruler Gnercli; originally complained that Gnercli "doesn't listen to him and believe a single word he has to say," suggesting long-standing friction
- Deeply sensitive about his seniority and authority
- His madness makes him difficult to deal with but his fears appear to have genuine basis
- Currently suffering from madness, in a state of hysterics and screaming

## History with the Party

- **First contact:** Found hysterical and confined to his room; accused Vukradin of being a shapeshifter on introduction
- **Vukradin's questioning:** Vukradin probed the tension between the two kings by raising the question of seniority; Korboz erupted, asserting his senior status and demanding preferential treatment — exactly the fracture Vukradin was probing for
- **Confrontation with Gnercli:** Shouted at Gnercli, *"That you didn't believe existed!"* in reference to the shapeshifter threat — publicly airing the rift over the shapeshifter and significantly destabilizing their political relationship

## Current Status

- **Last known location:** Confined to his room in Gnomengarde
- **Active situation:** Confined to his room, actively hysterical; inventors Fibblestib and Dabbledob are entirely focused on finding a cure for his madness
- **What the party knows:** He believes a shape-changing creature is terrorizing the cave system and claims it caused the disappearance of gnomes Warren and Orrin
- **What remains hidden:** The true nature and extent of his madness — whether it is a symptom of genuine supernatural influence or purely psychological is unconfirmed

## Relationships

- **King Gnercli (co-ruler):** Long-standing friction; Gnercli has refused to believe his claims about the shapeshifter; relationship publicly destabilized by Vukradin's intervention; last seen at odds with Gnercli with their political relationship significantly destabilized
- **Fibblestib & Dabbledob:** The two inventors are devoted to curing him; his primary caretakers; entirely focused on finding a cure for his condition
- **Vukradin (party):** Immediately accused him of being a shapeshifter; Vukradin subsequently exploited his resentment toward Gnercli to widen the political rift

## Arc Score Events

- **Decrease (trust/stability):** Vukradin deliberately provoked Korboz's grievances about seniority, escalating the fracture between the two kings
