# Meril

## Identity
Druid teacher and mentor to Soma. Has not appeared directly in sessions; known only through Soma's references. *Also known as: meryl.*

*Also known as: meryl.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goals and drives are unknown; motivations for sending Soma away prematurely remain unexplained.
- Implied to be deliberate and enigmatic — gave Soma a staff of significant power without explanation, and cut short a druidic education that typically spans decades after only one year.
- Knowledgeable about natural threats, particularly dangerous fey and magical phenomena.

## History with the Party
- **Prior to current campaign arc:** Meril educated Soma about will-o'-wisps, describing them as evil lights that emerge/manifest in natural environments/surroundings. This knowledge proved relevant when Soma recognized Xanth's description of strange lights haunting Dragonbarrow as a potential will-o'-wisp threat, allowing the party to enter the tomb with forewarning.
- **Referenced in conversation (not present):** Soma spoke about Meril as her druid teacher. Soma trained under Meril for only one year — extraordinarily brief for druidic training that normally takes decades. Meril gave Soma her staff before sending her out, but never explained why Soma was being sent away or what the staff truly was. Soma described Meril's hands as being "in the grain" of the staff, suggesting a deep personal or magical connection Meril imbued into it. The staff now has fewer leaves than it once did, a detail Soma has noticed but not yet understood.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Unknown. Soma did not mention where Meril is or whether she can be reached.
- **Active plans:** Unknown.
- **Party knowledge vs. hidden:** The party knows Meril is Soma's teacher, gave her the staff, and educated her about dangerous natural creatures like will-o'-wisps. The reasons behind Soma's abbreviated training, the true nature of the staff, Meril's current whereabouts, faction ties, and whether she is reachable all remain mysteries. Soma herself believes the staff's significance is something she needs to work out on her own.

## Relationships
- **Soma:** Mentor-student relationship. Soma holds deep reverence for Meril and treats the staff as her last physical connection to her teacher. There is an undercurrent of unresolved questions — why the short training, why the staff, why now. Meril served as a source of druidic knowledge that Soma retained and applied in the field.
