# The Anchorite Half-Orc

## Identity
- Role: Cleric of Talos; religious authority embedded within a Talosian orc raiding party
- First appearance: Encountered during an ambush in the forest; identified himself directly to the party

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven by devotion to Talos and the advancement of Talosian power
- Coldly pragmatic — when Valphine executed the Cult Fanatic, he dismissed the death as merely another obstacle crushed by Talos rather than showing grief or alarm
- Issued a warning to the party to leave the forest, suggesting either confidence in his position or a willingness to let them go for now

## History with the Party
- **The Ambush:** Encountered the party during a Talosian orc raid in the forest. He identified himself as a cleric of Talos embedded within the raiding party. After Valphine killed the Cult Fanatic present, he reacted without mourning, declaring the deceased cultist "one more obstacle crushed by Talos." He then warned the party to vacate the forest before departing.
