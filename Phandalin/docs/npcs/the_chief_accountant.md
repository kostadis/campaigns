# The Chief Accountant

## Identity
- **Role:** Former head accountant of Axeholm; appears as an undead/spectral glitching entity
- **Faction:** Once served Axeholm; now unaffiliated (deceased/haunting)
- **First Appearance:** The party encountered her in Axeholm, where she ambushed them with a devastating wail before recognizing Valphine and panicking

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** To see the ledgers of Axeholm balanced and all accounts properly settled before she could rest
- **Secondary Drive:** Fear of "the auditor" — an entity she believed would liquidate her if accounts were improperly closed
- Presents with the warmth of a grandmother layered over grinding static and bureaucratic obsession; she experiences events entirely through the lens of accounting metaphors (depreciation, liquidation, closing accounts)
- Volatile but not malicious — her attack on the party appeared instinctive, and she immediately reversed course when Valphine fell

## History with the Party
1. **First Contact:** Ambushed the party; unleashed a wail that knocked Valphine and Vukradin unconscious
2. **Immediate Reversal:** Panicked upon seeing Valphine fall, used blue-white static energy to attempt to stabilize her, and then fled incorporeally
3. **Post-Rest Parley:** Reemerged and explained Axeholm's full history — Aletra installed the machine on behalf of a jealous king who coveted the Lionshield Coster's multi-dimensional profits
4. **Deal Struck:** Offered the machine's override shutdown code in exchange for being allowed to leave and the party's silence toward Aletra; the party accepted
5. **Final Appearance:** When shown the interplanetary bill for 42,000 gp, she screamed for approximately ten minutes, then sighed, declared *"The book is closed,"* and disappeared permanently — apparently at peace

## Current Status
- **Last Known State:** Permanently departed; dissolved after declaring the accounts settled
- **Active Plans:** None — her arc is resolved
- **Her Quarters:** Contained a secret door hidden in the fireplace leading to a room with four audit drones and an iron chest holding a dread helm and gauntlets of ogre power (her possessions in life)

## Relationships
- **Aletra:** Killed by Aletra after complaining the books wouldn't balance following the machine's installation; deep grievance
- **Valphine:** Called her "sister of the sun" and detected "the scent of bleach and betrayal" on her; showed fierce protectiveness when Valphine was downed, suggesting a complicated prior or spiritual connection
- **Vukradin:** Collateral target of the initial wail; no noted individual relationship
- **"The Auditor":** An unseen entity the Chief Accountant feared; believed it would liquidate her for improperly closing accounts

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease (Party → Chief Accountant):** Initial wail that downed Valphine and Vukradin
- **Increase (Chief Accountant → Party):** Stabilized Valphine unprompted; shared Axeholm's full history; provided the machine's override code
- **Increase (Party → Chief Accountant):** Honored the deal; allowed her to depart; did not report her to Aletra
- **Resolution:** Departed at peace — arc fully closed
