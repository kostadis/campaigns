# Vorga

## Identity
- **Role:** Leader of an orc refugee group (~30 individuals, including ~12 warriors, elders, women with infants, and children)
- **Faction:** Displaced orc refugees from the southern mountains
- **First Appearance:** Session in which the party arrived at Falcon's Hunting Lodge; she descended from the walkway when Prutha's recognition signal opened the gate

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Keep her people alive — first through survival of the immediate dragon threat, then a safe return to their southern mountain home now that Cryovain is dead
- **Drive:** Pragmatic, protective leadership; she had already calculated the odds of evacuation and rejected it before the party finished offering options
- Speaks plainly and repeats herself when a point isn't landing ("Creature will catch up"). Not dismissive — she processes each option on its merits before discarding it.
- Projects authority through calm rather than aggression; wears Falcon's hunter cloak for warmth, not as a statement.

## History with the Party
1. **First contact:** Gate opened via Prutha's recognition signal; Vorga descended to speak with the party directly
2. **Evacuation debate:** Heard out every transport option offered (Brewbarry, Boney, Soma's elk) and rejected each on tactical grounds — too slow against a pursuing force, too many people who couldn't run
3. **Alliance confirmed:** Learned from the party that Cryovain was dead; accepted this as proof of their capability and agreed to a joint defense of the lodge
4. **Tactical contributions:** Suggested stationing someone visible on the wall so Falcon would not enter shooting; accepted Valphine's recommendation to place her archers at the arrow slits in the gatehouse tower
5. **Last event:** Positioned her forces for the siege and remained inside the compound

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Inside Falcon's Hunting Lodge compound, during the siege
- **Active Plans:** Defend the lodge against the incoming threat; afterward, lead her people south to reclaim their mountain home
- **What the party knows:** Vorga's full situation — numbers, history, and intentions — was openly shared. She is an ally for the siege.
- **What remains hidden:** Nothing of note has been concealed from the party

## Relationships
- **The Party (general):** Cautious but committed ally; alliance is based on the party's proven record of killing Cryovain
- **Prutha:** Holds some prior recognition or trust — Prutha's signal opened the gate before Vorga came down to talk
- **Valphine:** Accepted her tactical suggestion about the arrow slits without friction
- **Falcon:** Wary of his arrival; proactively suggested the party manage his first impression to prevent a violent misunderstanding
- **Her people:** Carries full responsibility for ~30 refugees, including non-combatants she explicitly named as the reason evacuation was not viable
