# Sister Kella

## Identity
- **Role/Faction:** Unknown; operates independently (or covertly) — opposes the Carver
- **First Appearance:** Made contact with the party prior to recorded sessions, initially through a prior arrangement with Lokudus; later relayed through Valphine during a tavern discussion

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Eliminating or opposing the Carver — her cooperation with the party appears entirely transactional and driven by this aim
- She is willing to work with the party pragmatically, regardless of alignment or trust
- Her offer of Lokudus's promised reward as a down payment suggests she is calculating and not above leveraging others' debts
- No direct personality data from in-person interaction — all known behavior is secondhand

## History with the Party
1. **Pre-session contact:** Kella approached the party (details not fully recorded), offering to ally against the Carver. She provided information on locating him and offered the reward previously promised to Lokudus as a down payment
2. **Tavern discussion (relayed by Valphine):** Her offer was debated internally. Vukradin refused to accept what he considered stolen goods or cooperate with someone he viewed as evil. Significant party tension resulted
3. **Later session reference:** Kella was noted as having promised to contact the party about the Carver situation — she never followed through

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Unknown
- **Active Plans:** Unclear; she committed to following up with the party but has gone silent
- **What the Party Knows:** She opposes the Carver, has resources, and made a concrete offer involving Lokudus's reward
- **What Remains Hidden:** Her true identity, faction, full agenda, and reasons for breaking contact

## Relationships
- **The Party (general):** Uneasy, unresolved — offer accepted by some, rejected outright by Vukradin
- **Vukradin:** Hostile; he considers her evil and refused her terms
- **Lokudus:** Had a prior arrangement with him; used his promised reward as leverage with the party
- **Valphine:** Served as intermediary, relaying Kella's offer to the party
- **The Carver:** Active enemy
