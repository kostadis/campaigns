# The Dwarven King of Axeholm

## Identity
- **Role:** Ruler of Axeholm
- **Faction:** Axeholm (corrupted)
- **First Appearance:** Revealed from behind the limestone throne in the grand pillared hall of Axeholm, flanked by granite statues of dwarven warriors. Encountered after the party defeated two corrupted dwarves in the same chamber.
- **Physical Form:** A three-headed monstrosity twisted by interdimensional corruption.

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations
No coherent personality or motivations were demonstrated — by the time of encounter, the King had been fully consumed by interdimensional corruption and acted only as a feral, aggressive combatant.

## History with the Party
- Emerged alongside one additional corrupted dwarf after the party cleared two others in the throne room.
- Demonstrated terrifying speed and ferocity in combat.
- Immediately targeted Soma, paralyzing her and then mauling her with bites from all three heads, dropping her and reverting her from bear form.
- Pressed the assault on Brewbarry despite suffering grievous wounds from the barbarian's defensive strikes, shrugging off the damage through his corrupted resilience.
- Resisted Vukradin's first mental assault (illusion of Moradin), but succumbed to a second (illusion of a giant bear), suffering psychic damage and confusion.
- Turned on Valphine while disoriented but failed to land any blows.
- Defeated and killed by the party in the throne room.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Slain in the pillared throne room of Axeholm.
- **Loot recovered:** A signet ring bearing the Axeholm crest — a hand clutched in a dwarven gauntlet. Valphine identified it as both a valuable artifact and a key of some kind.

## Relationships
- **The corrupted dwarves of Axeholm:** Shared the same interdimensional corruption afflicting the fortress's inhabitants.
- **Soma:** Primary target; rendered unconscious during the fight.
- **Brewbarry:** Secondary target; withstood the King's assault through defensive strikes.
- **Vukradin:** Resisted his first illusion; fell to his second.
- **Valphine:** Attacked while disoriented; no hits landed. She recognized the significance of his signet ring.
