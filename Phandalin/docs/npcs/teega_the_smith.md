# Teega the Smith

## Identity
- **Role:** Blacksmith; former Kraken Society member (coerced via psionic manipulation)
- **Faction:** Kraken Society (unwilling/manipulated); potential Church of Lathander affiliate
- **First Appearance:** Encountered standing rigidly on a fog-covered eastern riverbank; eyes glassy and unfocused, speaking in an eerie monotone about metal bending, minds being cleansed, and waves seeking uniformity

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Returning to her former life at the Wayside Inn; seeking peace and stability after the wars and chaos of the Sword Coast — the same desire that made her vulnerable to Kraken Society recruitment
- Teega is resilient but carries significant psychological damage from psionic manipulation, including memory gaps and lingering disorientation. She is cautious about new commitments, declining Vukradin's job offer despite genuine interest. Her brief moments of lucidity during mind control suggest a strong underlying will.
- Had been experiencing blackouts for some time prior to her full enthrallment by the Kraken cult.

## History with the Party
1. **Eastern Riverbank (fog-covered shore):** Found standing rigidly in an unnatural dense fog, eyes glassy and unfocused, speaking in eerie monotone about metal bending, minds being cleansed, and waves seeking uniformity. When questioned about the missing Emerald Enclave patrol, she cryptically stated they were trying to fix a leak with a wooden plug and that the current had found them, reforming their brittle thoughts. Drew a mysterious spiral symbol in the mud. Her eyes briefly cleared when Vukradin persuaded her, but they glazed over again and she wandered into the nearby glade.
2. **The Glade:** After the combat in the glade, she wandered further into the wilderness. Soma tracked her in bear form and subdued her nonlethally, bringing her back to the group. She remained under psionic compulsion and mind control as long as the Cult Fanatic lived. When the Cult Fanatic was executed by Valphine, she was freed from mental compulsion.
3. **En Route to Phandalin:** Status as recovering prisoner, freed from enthrallment.
4. **Falcon Hunting Lodge:** Arrived disoriented with significant memory gaps. Disclosed her last coherent memory was working at the Wayside Inn (a tavern with attached forge, corner of High Road and Tribor Trail). Admitted she had been experiencing blackouts for some time and had been drawn to the Kraken cult by promises of peace following the endless wars and chaos of the Sword Coast. Examination confirmed psionic brain manipulation — Brewbarry determined this was not Mind Flayer in origin, though her brain had been rewired and her memory compromised. Valphine attempted to intimidate her regarding her choices; Vukradin offered her a blacksmith position with the Church of Lathander. She declined immediately but expressed interest in visiting the church after returning to the Wayside Inn.
5. **Church of Lathander — Valphine's Sermon:** Observed the sermon and its aftermath at the church. Appeared disturbed and strangely conflicted by Valphine's unintended new sacrament of pain and redemption.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Church of Lathander (observed during Valphine's sermon)
- **Active Plans:** Intends to return to the Wayside Inn before considering any further ties to the church
- **What the Party Knows:** She was a Kraken Society member through manipulation, not true devotion; her mind was psionically rewired; she is recovering but memory-compromised; had been experiencing blackouts before full enthrallment
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full extent of her memory loss; who or what specifically conducted the psionic manipulation; her ultimate loyalties going forward

## Relationships
- **Vukradin:** Most positive relationship with the party; he persuaded her back to lucidity during her trance and offered her employment. She is open to him.
- **Valphine:** Tense. Valphine intimidated her at the lodge and executed the Cult Fanatic (which freed Teega from mind control), yet Teega was drawn to observe Valphine's sermon — seemingly conflicted by what she witnessed.
- **Soma:** Subdued her nonlethally during her fugue state; functional but not warm.
- **Brewbarry:** Examined her and determined the nature of her psionic manipulation (not Mind Flayer origin).
- **Kraken Society:** Former coerced member; nature of ongoing entanglement unknown.
- **Church of Lathander:** Cautiously curious; has not committed.
- **Wayside Inn:** Her anchor — last place of coherent memory and apparent emotional safety.

- **Negative:** Valphine's intimidation at the lodge *(−)*
