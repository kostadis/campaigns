# Thomas

## Identity
- **Role:** Named agent of the Emerald Enclave; one of two agents accompanying Lyra; the oldest of the group
- **First Appearance:** Stonehill Tavern, where he and Marian joined the party after Vukradin extended an invitation

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Fulfilling his oath to the Emerald Enclave; handling Lyra's situation through proper organizational channels
- Thomas is measured and dutiful, serving as the primary spokesperson for the group. He shows genuine emotional investment in the events — displaying distress when recounting the near-death experience and the ritual's failure. He is observant and direct, questioning Valphine openly about her background and visibly unsettled by her devotional practices.
- Loyal to Lyra, standing with her even in public confession; delivers difficult news with the weariness of someone who has thought extensively about the implications.
- Speaks without shame when admitting failure or difficult truths.

## History with the Party
- Met the party at the Stonehill Tavern after Vukradin invited Lyra's agents to sit with them.
- Drank the beer provided by Spiderman and explained Lyra's situation: she had discovered the ritual independently, acted without bringing it to Enclave leadership, and was motivated by personal ambition and frustration with organizational inaction alongside genuine concern for the Whispering Grove. He confirmed a promotion was tied to the ritual's success.
- Questioned Valphine about whether she was from the Underdark and expressed shock at her description of self-harm as devotion to Lathander.
- Firmly advised Vukradin against speaking on Lyra's behalf to her superiors.
- Declined Vukradin's invitation to join the party, citing his oath to the Emerald Enclave as binding. He and Marian both stated that their oath to the Emerald Enclave bound them.
- Departed the Stonehill Tavern alongside Marian.
- Found the party on the road to the druid council, materializing in the manner of one of Lyra's well-trained agents — not from nowhere, but from the specific nowhere that trained agents occupy when wanting to seem casual. He carried news from Lyra: Adabra was moving to ostracize Lyra from the Emerald Enclave.
- Noted that even if Reidoth were present, he could not procedurally stop Adabra from speaking — but acknowledged that Reidoth's physical presence would change the texture of what Adabra could do.
- At the druid council, stood and openly confessed alongside Lyra about the failed intervention and the party's killing of the metastasized treant, speaking without shame.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** At the druid council with Lyra
- **Active plans:** Supporting Lyra through the Emerald Enclave proceedings regarding her ostracization
- **What the party knows:** That Thomas is a loyal, oath-bound agent who will not advocate for Vukradin interceding with Lyra's superiors; that he remains loyal to Lyra even when publicly confessing failure
- **What remains hidden:** His personal opinion of Lyra's choices; what specific actions he believes the Enclave should take

## Relationships
- **Marian:** Fellow Emerald Enclave agent; departed together; close enough to act as a unit
- **Lyra:** Superior or colleague whose situation he was dispatched to manage; recounted her failures with evident emotional weight; stands with her at council and confesses alongside her
- **Vukradin:** Received his invitation cordially but rebuffed his offers of advocacy and recruitment
- **Valphine:** Openly questioned her origins and reacted with visible shock to her religious practices
- **Adabra:** Enclave member moving to ostracize Lyra
- **Reidoth:** Noted his procedural limitations but acknowledged his potential influence at council
