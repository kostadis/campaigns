# Alphonse "Big Al" Kalazorn

## Identity
- **Role:** Owner of Butterskull Ranch; retired veteran; former Sheriff of Triboar (predecessor to current Sheriff Darathra)
- **First Appearance:** Found by Vukradin in the ranch cellar, bound to a chair with a burlap sack over his head, surrounded by shelves of butter skulls — the party's primary rescue target under a standing contract

## Personality & Motivations
- Pragmatic and tough-minded, but his defining priority is his prized cow Petunia — upon rescue, he showed more urgency about her welfare than his own. Offered a suit of mithril chainmail as reward specifically for her recovery.
- Credible and straightforward; withstood questioning about the chainmail's origins without raising suspicion, consistent with his law enforcement background.

## History with the Party
1. **Rescued from cellar** — Vukradin located him bound and bagged in the ranch cellar. He explained that Borg the Hammer and the Rot-Tusk Ogres had displaced the original orc raiders and kept him alive deliberately as bait for adventurers.
2. **Offered the mithril chainmail** — Proposed it as reward for recovering Petunia. Vukradin questioned its origin; Big Al maintained he purchased it legitimately, which the party accepted.
3. **Petunia recovered** — Soma used *Speak with Animals* to locate the cow, identified by a brand reading "Big Al's Cow." She was secured safely.
4. **Escorted to Corbin's camp** — Big Al and Petunia were brought to a remote camp away from the ranch.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Corbin's camp, away from Butterskull Ranch, with Petunia
- **Active situation:** Ranch remains in hostile hands (Borg the Hammer, Rot-Tusk Ogres); Big Al has no known independent operation underway
- **Party knowledge:** Full — no information gap flagged in notes

## Relationships
- **Petunia (his cow):** His clearest emotional priority
- **Darathra (current Sheriff of Triboar):** His successor as Sheriff; professional/historical connection
- **Corbin:** Present at the camp where Big Al was delivered; direct contact
- **Vukradin:** Questioned him on the chainmail; relationship cordial but probing
- **Soma:** Directly instrumental in recovering Petunia via *Speak with Animals*
