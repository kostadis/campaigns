# Jax

## Identity

Jax was a field lieutenant and intermediary for the Carver, serving as direct superior to at least one bandit crew and acting as the Carver's last known active agent. He coordinated operations in the field and maintained the chain of command between the Carver and lower-level operatives.

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations

- **Core goal**: Executing the Carver's operations in the field
- **Cold and calculating** — observed the party neutralize Sister Kaella from concealment and immediately moved to exploit the moment, framing their work as a convenience to him
- **Opportunistic and cruel** — his sardonic thanks to Vukradin before springing the ambush revealed a willingness to manipulate and betray even nominal allies for tactical advantage
- **Tactically minded** — coordinated a well-timed ambush using crowd control effects to maximize vulnerability

## History with the Party

1. **Named by a surrendered bandit** as the crew's direct superior and go-between for the Carver. Current whereabouts unknown at that time.
2. **Ambush at the tree line**: Identified as the last of the Carver's agents. Emerged from the tree line with thugs and a Rot-Tusk Ogre immediately after Sister Kaella was subdued, timing his ambush to exploit that moment. Smiled coldly and thanked Vukradin for dealing with "that little bit of business," indicating he had observed the party neutralizing Sister Kaella and viewed it as advantageous to his own interests. Coordinated the ambush with precision: a net was thrown over Vukradin and Valphine was blinded by dark magic. Was paralyzed by Vukradin's Hold Person spell, rendering him vulnerable to automatic critical hits. Was attacked by Soma in bear form and subjected to Valphine's Spirit Guardians and Brewbarry's critical strikes. Defeated and killed by the combined assault of the party.

## Current Status

- **Dead.** Killed by the party during the ambush.
- With Jax eliminated, he was identified as the last of the Carver's known agents.

## Relationships

- **The Carver**: Direct superior; Jax served as the operational link between the Carver and field crews
- **Sister Kaella**: A fellow agent — Jax showed no loyalty to her, treating her defeat as a useful distraction
- **The surrendered bandit**: Reported directly to Jax
