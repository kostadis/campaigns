# Vukardin

## Identity
- **Role:** Avant-garde Aasimar bard; adventurer-for-hire
- **Faction:** Recruited under Lord Neverember's scheme to clear the Phandalin hinterlands using adventurers as a vanguard before establishing proper law; no formal affiliation
- **First Appearance:** Performing at the First Flophouse in Heapside, Neverwinter, earning nothing. Recruited by a Wintershield watchman on 8/3 of Ches 1495 to adventure in the Phandalin region.

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Earn honest coin; find an audience that appreciates his original compositions; avoid becoming what he despises — a mercenary thug operating under legal cover.
- **Personality:** Vukardin is a principled idealist who talks first in almost every situation, even when it gets him knocked unconscious. He has a persistent anti-establishment streak, openly lecturing NPCs about the ethics of adventuring and arguing that Neverember's scheme amounts to state-sanctioned banditry and money laundering, but his principles bend under financial pressure. He is drawn to anything that might have a musical angle — including ogres and sending stones. Described as an avant-garde musician whose music was underappreciated.

## History with the Party
- **8/3 of Ches 1495:** Recruited by a Wintershield watchman to adventure in Phandalin, clearing monsters and keeping treasure. Pushed back morally at every step, arguing the scheme amounted to state-sanctioned banditry and money laundering, until the guard explained Lord Neverember's plan to use adventurers as a vanguard before establishing proper law. Unconvinced but broke, Vukardin accepted out of desperation.
- **1/1 of Taraksh:** Quit the flophouse, recruited Brewbarry, and accepted Valphine's offer to join the group. Declared their goal would be honest coin.
- **8/1 of Taraksh:** Led the group to the Stonehill Inn; negotiated room-and-board by playing popular Waterdeep and Neverwinter songs for Toblen Stonehill's clientele, earning Toblen's goodwill. After several songs Toblen let him play his own composition and rewarded him with a free beer. Received a white dragon warning from Toblen and reacted with alarm. At the townmaster's hall, selected the Dwarven Excavation Quest (50 gp) over Gnomengarde, justifying it as the most honest option despite Valphine's preference for Gnomengarde.
- **9/1 of Taraksh:** At the dwarven ruin, immediately warned Dazlyn and Norbus about the white dragon and attempted to leave. Was persuaded to stay when the dwarves offered sending stones as payment for clearing the temple and Valphine noted the sending stones could serve as musical instruments. Inside the ruin, attempted to parley with orcs twice rather than fight, was knocked unconscious both times by orc attacks, and was revived by Valphine both times. Also cast Starry Wisp on an orc. Played original music for Dazlyn and Norbus that evening.
- **10/1 of Taraksh:** On the return march, attempted to communicate with a whistling ogre on the road, assuming it might be a musician or enslaved creature; was charged immediately. Cast Starry Wisp to illuminate the ogre, then hid while the party handled the fight. Back in Phandalin, collected the quest reward from Harbin Wester but delivered an unsolicited lecture accusing adventurers of being glorified thugs, upsetting the townmaster. Attempted (and failed) to solicit a protection contract from Linene Graywind at the Lionshield Coster. Toblen again persuaded him to play popular music at the Stonehill Inn that evening.
- **1/2 of Taraksh:** En route to Gnomengarde, attempted to intimidate a mountain ogre blocking the path, failed, and accidentally convinced it the party was easy prey.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Sword Mountains, on the road to Gnomengarde — mid-combat with an ogre that Vukardin inadvertently provoked.
- **Active Plans:** Completing the Gnomengarde quest; continuing to seek legitimate income and musical recognition.
- **What the Party Knows:** Vukardin is broke, idealistic, frequently unconscious, and genuinely committed to his ethical code — even when it makes things worse.
- **Hidden from the Party:** Nothing noted in source material.

## Relationships
- **Valphine:** Traveling companion and repeated savior; has revived Vukardin twice. Valphine's pragmatism occasionally overrides Vukardin's hesitation (e.g., sending stones argument, Gnomengarde quest preference).
- **Brewbarry:** Recruited by Vukardin on 1/1 of Taraksh; relationship otherwise undetailed.
- **Toblen Stonehill:** Friendly patron; tolerates Vukardin's originals as a reward for playing crowd-pleasers (popular Waterdeep and Neverwinter songs). A reliable source of lodging and local information.
- **Harbin Wester (Townmaster):** Strained — Vukardin collected his reward then lectured him about adventurers being thugs.
- **Linene Graywind:** Attempted to pitch her a protection contract; rebuffed.
- **Dazlyn & Norbus:** Positive; Vukardin played original compositions for them and they provided the sending stones as payment.
- **Lord Neverember:** Skeptical and reluctant benefactor; Vukardin accepted his scheme while openly condemning it as state-sanctioned banditry.
