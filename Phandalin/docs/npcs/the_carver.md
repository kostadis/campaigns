# The Carver

## Identity
- **Role:** Antagonist; entity seeking draconic domination
- **Faction:** Unknown — operates alone
- **True Nature:** The obsidian sword is the actual Carver. The veteran man is and always was a vessel — a hollow shell operated by the sword
- **First Appearance:** Referenced by Sister Kaella before the party encountered his trail; first physically encountered at the Icespire Hold summoning circle

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Bind and possess Cryovain the white dragon; use it to burn Phandalin
- Operates with complete indifference to anything in its path — kills without hesitation or deviation, ignores anything it considers beneath consideration
- Communicates telepathically when it chooses to; spoke directly into Vukradin's mind to characterize the Silence spell as cowardice
- Adaptive under pressure: when the ritual was disrupted, it immediately pivoted from controlled consumption to direct domination to achieve the same end

## History with the Party

**Before Contact (Trail Evidence)**
- Sister Kaella described the Carver as no longer fully human — hollow, speaking to its sword in a tongue that made ears bleed
- Footprints ran in a perfectly mechanical straight line over rocks and obstacles; left psychic residue that warped stone and caused phasing effects
- Stone giants in the mountain pass noted his passage as "the little one with the obsidian sword" and declined to intervene, considering it beneath their concern
- The Glacier of Whispers provided a psychic impression: *walks without rest, without breath, without soul*

**Approach to Icespire Hold**
- A young drake was found dead in the pass below the Hold — killed without hesitation, in a straight line, no deviation around its lair
- Snow around the corpse was black; eye sockets were hollow; the mother drake circled above in grief
- Vukradin noted the Carver moved as something that had already decided nothing in its path had standing to matter

**Summoning Circle Confrontation**
- Vukradin's Silence spell interrupted the ritual — a slower, presumably more controlled method of consuming Cryovain
- Rather than continue or flee, the sword abandoned the refined ritual and achieved domination of Cryovain through direct force instead
- The veteran's body was paralyzed by Vukradin's Hold Person; while locked, the sword completed the domination
- It spoke into Vukradin's mind: described Silence as cowardice, announced it had obtained everything it always wanted
- Post-domination, the sword went simply black and still — drained of its accumulated purple glow, spent

## Current Status
- **The veteran's body:** Paralyzed on the floor of the summoning chamber (Hold Person active)
- **The sword:** Attached to that body; drained and dark, power expended to complete domination
- **Cryovain:** Airborne, dominated, approximately three rounds from complete mental erasure at last known status
- **Active operation:** Domination of Cryovain is complete; the sword has achieved its stated objective; intended use of Cryovain is to burn Phandalin

**What the party knows:** The sword is the true entity. The ritual was disrupted but the goal was achieved anyway. The sword is currently drained.

**What remains hidden:** Whether a drained sword is truly inert or merely diminished. What the sword is beyond this confrontation — origin, history, larger purpose unknown.

## Relationships
- **The veteran (vessel):** The man's identity and history are unknown beyond his legendary footwork; he was always a means, not a person to the Carver
- **Cryovain:** Now a dominated instrument; the sword's obtained vehicle for attacking Phandalin
- **Stone giants:** Aware of its passage; chose non-intervention
- **Vukradin:** The Carver singled him out for direct telepathic communication; framed his Silence as personal cowardice
- **Brewbarry & Soma:** Identified the sword's level-draining properties before the confrontation

## Arc Score Events
- **Disruption of the ritual** (Vukradin's Silence): Forced escalation from controlled consumption to direct domination — objective achieved despite intervention *(no score benefit to party; the Carver adapted successfully)*
- **Hold Person on the vessel:** Body neutralized; sword drained post-domination *(potential vulnerability created)*
- **Domination of Cryovain completed:** Carver achieved its stated goal mid-confrontation *(major antagonist advancement)*
