# Valphine Sortorra

## Identity
- **Role:** Drow cleric of Lathander (Morninglord); no stated faction affiliation beyond the church
- **First Appearance:** 1/1 Taraksh — spotted Vukardin at the First Flophouse, felt compelled to follow him, and offered her services to the party outside the inn

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Unclear long-term; immediate drives include plunder (suppressed by new religious convictions), survival, and what appears to be a genuine spiritual calling to Lathander
- Pragmatic and opportunistic by instinct, actively working to reconcile those instincts with Lathanderian ethics she finds genuinely foreign rather than hypocritical
- Dry and sardonic — eye-rolls at Vukardin's moralizing, amusement at Soma's observations, and describes being nearly killed as "glorious"
- Privately judgmental of others' moral compromises (categorized the gnomes Dazlyn and Norbus as grave robbers) while acknowledging her own complicated history
- Initially interpreted the clerics of Lathander's prohibition on assassination and violent scheming as subterfuge, genuinely surprised to discover it was sincere doctrine

## History with the Party
- **1/1 Taraksh:** Observed Vukardin drinking at the First Flophouse, felt a compulsion to follow him, and approached him outside to join the party. Shared backstory: fled the Underdark after witnessing Menzoberranzan's fall to rampaging demons, experienced a religious conversion upon first seeing sunlight, and became a devotee of the Morninglord.
- **8/1 Taraksh:** Knocked on the door at the townmaster's hall and confirmed quest payment terms with Harbin Wester. Pushed for the Gnomengarde quest (plunder potential) but deferred to Vukardin on the Dwarven Excavation Quest, reminding herself that Lathander's clerics disapprove of open plundering. Noted the dwarves' ruins had been thoroughly ransacked.
- **9/1 Taraksh:** Spotted two secret doors in the temple. Contributed to combat by attacking orcs with divine energy and her blade (with poor results on both). Healed Vukardin twice after he was knocked unconscious. Her last action before being downed was a missed sword strike against an orc. Was herself knocked unconscious by Soma's miscast ice knife; regained consciousness with an ecstatic smile that unsettled/worried Soma.
- **10/1 Taraksh:** Provided background on ogres as former Underdark slaves in Menzoberranzan; suggested freeing the roadside ogre as "what you overbrighters do." Back in Phandalin, expressed amusement at Soma's observation that dragons don't knock on doors, rolled her eyes at Vukardin's moral lecture to the townmaster, and described her near-death experience as "glorious" to Linene Graywind. Sat quietly at Stonehill Inn listening to Vukardin's music; found nothing of interest among locals.
- **1/2 Taraksh:** Used a recovered magical bullet to knock a blocking ogre prone on the road to Gnomengarde.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Sword Mountains, road to Gnomengarde — mid-combat with an ogre
- **Active:** No known independent operations
- **Party knows:** Her conversion story, Underdark origins, and that she is actively (if imperfectly) trying to follow Lathanderian doctrine
- **Hidden:** The full nature of her compulsion toward Vukardin; her long-term intentions are undefined

## Relationships
- **Vukardin:** The draw that brought her to the party; finds his moral lectures tiresome but defers to him on ethical questions regarding her conduct
- **Soma:** Some tension — Soma's miscast spell downed Valphine; Soma was visibly unsettled/worried by Valphine's ecstatic response to near-death
- **Linene Graywind:** Brief interaction; Valphine described her near-death experience as "glorious" to her
- **Dazlyn & Norbus (gnomes):** Privately views them as grave robbers
- **Harbin Wester:** Transactional; verified quest payment, rolled eyes at Vukardin's lecture to him
