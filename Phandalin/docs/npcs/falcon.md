# Falcon

## Identity
- Scout and consultant; member of a Neverwinter mining consortium
- Operates out of the **Falcon Hunting Lodge**, where he first greeted the returning party with relief and approval

## Personality & Motivations
- Deeply loyal to friends, particularly Vukradin — his relief at Vukradin's safe return was immediate and genuine
- Pragmatic and generous within his means; unable to offer coin but willing to trade equity and lodging
- Driven by his financial investment in Mountain Toe's Goldmine and the need to see it made profitable
- Reasonable and persuadable — yielded to the party's preference for the Manse after Vukradin made the case

## History with the Party
1. **Reunion at the Lodge:** Welcomed the party back warmly after their successful mission capturing prisoners and rescuing companions. Expressed particular relief over Vukradin's safety.
2. **Mine Proposal:** Explained his financial constraints (funds tied up in the goldmine consortium) and offered the party a **share of Mountain Toe's Goldmine** in exchange for investigating and resolving the poisoning of its waters.
3. **Manse Quest Offered:** Separately proposed clearing orcs and half-orcs from the **Woodland Manse**, with a pair of **magical boots** as reward. Provided clear directions to the location.
4. **Accommodation Granted:** After Vukradin's persuasion, agreed to provide free lodging for the entire party.
5. **Party Chose the Manse First:** Despite Falcon's stated preference that the goldmine be prioritized, he accepted the party's decision to tackle the Manse first.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Falcon Hunting Lodge
- Waiting on the party to resolve one or both outstanding jobs (goldmine poisoning; Woodland Manse clearance)
- Financially strained due to consortium investment — cash-poor but asset-rich if the mine is restored
- **Party knows:** His consortium stake, the mine's water poisoning problem, the Manse quest and its reward, his financial situation
- **Hidden from party:** Details of the Neverwinter consortium's other members or interests; full scope of the mine's value or the poisoning's cause

## Relationships
- **Vukradin (party member):** Close personal friend; Falcon's loyalty to him was evident and influenced his generosity toward the whole group
- **Neverwinter Mining Consortium:** Business partners; Falcon's role was consulting and scouting in exchange for a discounted share
- **Mountain Toe's Goldmine:** Central financial concern; poisoned waters are an active threat to his investment
- **The Party (general):** Respectful and impressed by their capabilities; willing to deal fairly in lieu of cash
