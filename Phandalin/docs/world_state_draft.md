# World State Document

## NPCs

### Active Party Members
**Vukardin** - Aasimar avant-garde musician and bard, head of Phandalin Privy Council. Strongly opposes theft and banditry, committed to fair-trade ethics. Originally sought honest work, now focused on building conflict-free music studio. Was recruited as Order of the Gauntlet associate but doesn't consider himself a true member.

**Valphine Sortorra** - Drow cleric of Lathander, former Lolth follower who fled Underdark after Menzoberranzan's fall. Sister to Aletra. Created new religious sacrament involving intentional blinding and healing. Currently bonded with Boney, a sentient bone horse.

**Soma** - Tortle druid of Mielikki, pragmatic guide and forager. Can transform into bear, summon rat familiars, and communicate with creatures using magic. Lives in shell.

**Brewbarry** - Uthgardt barbarian wearing dread helm with glowing red eyes. Former tribe member who follows Vukardin for music. Can transform into "Big Brewbarry" rage state, has mystical damage resistance and Stone Endurance.

**Corbin** - Former bandit turned party member after losing family to Uthgardt raids. Currently helping as camp assistant and guard.

**Boney** - Sentient bone horse bonded with Valphine. Partially alive, can participate in combat.

### Phandalin
**Harbin Wester** - Townmaster appointed by Lord Neverember. Nervous around Brewbarry, paid party 250 gold for securing Axeholm. Fearful of dragons, posts quests.

**Toblen Stonehill** - Friendly proprietor of Stonehill Inn. Native of Triboar, allows Vukardin to pay with music.

**Linene Graywind** - Lionshield Coster manager and Privy Council member. Reports supply chain disruptions affecting interdimensional network.

**Adabra** - Emerald Enclave naturalist philosopher and Privy Council member. Opposes interventionist actions, believes planar effects are natural. Set up shop next to Garaele's house.

**Qelline Alderleaf** - Local farmer and cheese producer. Former fiancée of Big Al, reconciling after party intervention.

**Alphonse "Big Al" Kalazorn** - Retired sheriff and ranch owner rescued from Butterskull Ranch. Offered mithril chainmail as reward, reconciling with Qelline.

### Key Antagonists
**The Carver** - Powerful bandit leader marching on Icespire Hold to bind white dragon. No longer fully human, described as hollow shell with telepathic abilities. Plans to burn Phandalin after binding dragon.

**Sister Kaella** - The Carver's spymaster currently unconscious. Knows location of Icespire Hold, ran spy network in Phandalin.

**Aletra** - Valphine's sister from House Satora/Xorlarrin poison operations. Escaped through portal after extracting interdimensional poison from Axeholm machine.

### Other Notable NPCs
**Ser Kaelen Thorn** - Order of the Gauntlet knight in Phandalin. Has information about party's magical objects, wants statue returned to art collector.

**Horia Stonetallow** - Leader of Stonetallow clan at Mountain's Toe Gold Mine. Negotiated protection agreement with wererats.

**Zeleen Varnaster** - Wererat leader providing mine protection in exchange for shelter and cheese.

**Elara 'Seasong' Meliamne** - Wealthy art collector in Neverwinter, original owner of lighthouse statue.

**Jenna Roscoe** - Lord's Alliance agent from Neverwinter attempting to influence Vukardin against House Margaster.

**Xanth** - Wounded centaur guide near Dragonbarrow. Previously offered to guide party to evil half-orc spellcasters.

## Factions

**Phandalin Privy Council** - Governing body including Harbin, Tolbin, Elmer, Halia, Linene, Adabra, and Vukardin. Formed after dragon incident.

**The Carver's Organization** - Criminal network with telepathic leader marching on Icespire Hold. Key members: the Carver, Sister Kaella (spymaster), various thugs and enforcers.

**Order of the Gauntlet** - Heroic organization funded by House Margaster. Represented by Ser Kaelen, recruited Vukardin and Valphine as associates.

**Emerald Enclave** - Divided between interventionists (like Lyra) who oppose planar corruption and naturalists (like Adabra) who believe planar effects are natural phenomena.

**Lord's Alliance** - Represented by Jenna, provides connections to Waterdeep/Neverwinter nobility.

**House Margaster** - Neverwinter family with alleged demonic alliances funding Order of the Gauntlet through "blood money."

**Kraken Society** - Mind-controlling cult using psionic manipulation, seeking "drowned gate beneath Yllyn" in Underdark.

**Cult of Talos** - Half-orc clerics and displaced orcs performing dark rites at standing stones, overran Falcon's Lodge.

**House Satora/Xorlarrin** - Drow poison operation smuggling interdimensional drugs, Aletra's faction.

## Locations

### Phandalin Region
**Phandalin** - Mining town of 40-50 buildings, no walls. Central Stonehill Inn, townmaster's hall. Under dragon threat, using Axeholm as backup evacuation site.

**Axeholm** - Ancient dwarven fortress cleared by party, now secured with functioning hot water. Contains serviceable armor and tools.

**Mountain's Toe Gold Mine** - 15 miles northeast, operated by Stonetallow dwarves under wererat protection. Generates ~100 gold/week.

**Dragonbarrow** - 30-foot burial mound with dangerous will-o'-wisps. Contains Dragon Slayer Sword (claimed by party) and trapped sarcophagi.

**Butterskull Ranch** - Burned down ranch with alfalfa fields. Scene of Big Al's rescue.

**Whispering Wood** - Forest with expanding planar wells and corruption. Former site of corrupted Treant (defeated).

### Distant Locations
**Neverwinter** - Major city led by Lord Neverember, expanding into Phandalin hinterlands.

**Icespire Hold** - White dragon's lair in Ice Spire Mountains where Carver is marching.

**Neverwinter Wood** - Forest overrun by orcs allied with half-orc spellcasters performing dark rites.

**Gnomengarde** - Rock gnome community with co-Kings Korboz and Gnercli, previously had shapeshifter problem.

## Current Threats & Objectives

### Immediate Threats
- The Carver marching on Icespire Hold to bind white dragon and then burn Phandalin
- White dragon raids preventing farming and threatening regional stability
- Expanding planar corruption throughout region
- Orc forces allied with Anchorites of Talos controlling Neverwinter Wood

### Active Quests
- Stop the Carver from binding white dragon (Sister Kaella unconscious with location knowledge)
- Return lighthouse statue to art collector in Neverwinter
- Investigate captured audit drone from Axeholm machine room
- Resolve mining agreement between dwarves and wererats

### Ongoing Mysteries
- Source and purpose of planar corruption affecting multiple locations
- Identity of Aletra's "boss" and extent of interdimensional poison smuggling
- House Margaster's true demonic connections and funding activities
- Location and goals of "drowned gate beneath Yllyn" sought by Kraken Society
- Nature of Brewbarry's dread helm transformation effects
