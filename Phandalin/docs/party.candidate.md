### Brewbarry Root Smasher Ogalakadu
**Barbarian (Path of the Giant) 6 | Goliath | Player: Stephane Boudreau**

A competitive, celebratory goliath who measures life in battles won and hangovers earned. ### Valphine Sotorra
**Cleric (Peace Domain) 6 | Drow Elf | Player: Gary Young**

A former fifth daughter of Menzoberranzan who stumbled into sunlight and discovered Lathander — not as comfort, but as the most exquisite pain she had ever encountered. Her theology is genuinely her own: goodness causes her drow nature pain, and she experiences that pain as holy. She is not performing devotion; she is metabolizing it. Beneath the unorthodox doctrine lives a survivor of casual assassination attempts, trained in Drow intrigue, capable of running intelligence operations and covert arrangements without breaking composure.

### Soma
**Druid (Circle of the Moon) 6 | Tortle | Player: Wade**

An old tortle who came to druidcraft late in life and is grimly determined to make up for lost time. Patient, soft-spoken, and instinctively maternal, she calls her companions "shell-sprouts" and manages them with dry affection and occasional physical restraint. She is also in the middle of an identity crisis: she aligned herself with Adabra's naturalism without fully thinking it through, then watched the Whispering Grove ritual collapse and found herself unable to argue that everything is natural. The Druid Council vindicated her interventionism. Adabra called Corbin's death her fault. Both things are true enough to sting.

**Key Relationships:**
- *Meril (absent):* Her teacher; deliberately chose her for the staff; Xanth confirms it was intentional; Soma resists fully accepting what this means
- *Vukradin:* Protective; calls him a shell-sprout; some moral friction (the necklace dispute; his impulsive leadership; his persuasive influence creating resentment she privately observes but has not raised)
- *Valphine:* Protective dynamic; carries her to safety; mild tension over druidic vs Lathanderian magic
- *Brewbarry:* Practical respect; she has physically subdued him; he accepts this without resentment
- *Adabra:* Open opposition; relationship declared finished by Soma after the council; Adabra used Corbin's death against her; Adabra is a true believer (confirmed by staff resonance)
- *Lyra:* Restored to full standing; genuinely grateful; Soma defended her at council
- *Xanth:* Departed; gave a parting blessing; confirmed Meril's deliberate choice; his friendship with Meril is the subtext of every interaction with Soma
- *Boney:* Soma's companion; Boney believes Soma is Lady Alagondar reincarnated; Soma acquired him through a deception she has not undone
- *Prutha:* Observed his grief over the dead anchorites quietly; notes the possible connection to the "boy from the eastern clan" Talosian recruitment target but has not raised it with him
