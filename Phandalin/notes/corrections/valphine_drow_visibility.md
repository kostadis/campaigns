# Valphine — drow visibility canon

**Decision (2026-05-03, GM ruling during 20260429 consistency review):**
Lock in a two-stage NPC reaction to Valphine.

## The rule

1. **First read: "evil drow."** The Lolth orthodoxy of Menzoberranzan fell in Out of the Abyss, but a cult that ruled for centuries leaves an echo in time and space. On the surface, drow remain the thing parents warn children about. Any NPC seeing Valphine reads her as "evil drow" first.
2. **Second read: Lathanderian confusion.** They then register her holy symbol and vestments of Lathander (good god, dawn, hope) and become confused. They typically misread her motives as standard surface-Lathanderian comfort/dawn/hope.

Hostile NPCs may still act on the first read (e.g. Talosian cleric on the ridge, 20260429).

## Why the misreading sticks

Surface NPCs interpret Lathander as comfort/mercy. Valphine's actual theology — captured in her own words — is the opposite: she worships Lathander as **power that radiates pain openly, constantly, universally, and is loved for it.** Lolth taught pain through hierarchy; Lathander radiates it. For a daughter of Menzoberranzan, that is majesty, not gentleness. NPCs who hear her preach hear "dawn / warmth / hope" and assume she means what they mean.
