# Shield, +1 (Narux's cave, Circle of Thunder)

Recovered from Narux's cave at the Circle of Thunder, Ch 41. Currently unassigned.

A heater shield of dark, oil-blackened oak banded in iron. The face is carved with concentric rings of standing stones — old druidcraft, the same geometry as the henge above — but the central boss has been chiselled out and replaced with a hammered iron disc bearing Talos's jagged bolt. The original carving was never sanded smooth; the bolt sits crookedly atop the older work, the way a brand sits on skin. Faint scorch marks ray outward from the boss, as if it has been struck by lightning more than once and survived.

The leather straps inside are newer than the shield itself and smell faintly of pine tar and wet dog. A single rune is scratched into the wood beneath the forearm strap, where only the bearer would see it — a half-orc tally mark, four notches and a slash. Five kills, or five storms weathered, or five somethings.

It rings when struck. Not the dull thud of oak on a fist, but a low, sustained note, like a bell heard through deep water.
