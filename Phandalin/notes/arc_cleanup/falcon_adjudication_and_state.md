## The Adjudication — What Happened With Falcon

### At the meeting
Vukradin, confused by Lyra and Thomas refusing to be ashamed of the Treant, shifted tactics and approached Falcon. **Falcon attacked Vukradin personally** — went for the persona, the charm, the bard act. He was wielding his grief as a weapon, not opening it. The Senne beat was not reached.

Vukradin got upset and walked out. Brewbarry asked if he should kill anyone. Vukradin ignored him and was gone.

Falcon stood in Adabra's bloc and watched the bloc collapse around him — Aldric cracked, Adabra said *"nature will heal itself"* out loud, the council ruled against her frame.

### After the meeting — Valphine approached
**Valphine did not attack and did not argue.** In contrast to Vukradin:

- Took responsibility for the party's inaction on the orcs. Apologized for not moving on them sooner. Committed to doing something about it.
- Told her own exile story — Menzoberranzan, the demon incursion, leaving, seeing the sun.
- Did **not** pitch Lathander. Did **not** reach for pain-theology. Let the sun-story carry its own weight.

**Falcon realized he has been giving up.** Adabra's framework had given him permission to stop trying; Valphine named what stopping-trying actually is. He did not convert. He is **open but not convinced** — specifically open to *not giving up.*

### Why it worked
The move Adabra's framework cannot generate: **apologizing for giving up, and committing to act anyway.** Naturalism has no language for that. Valphine arrived at it without meaning to — she was paying cost, not performing priesthood. That is why it landed.

## The Heat Mechanic — Falcon's Religious Opening Decays

**The core idea:** After the adjudication, Falcon is *feeling* something religious — not Lathander doctrine, but the shape of it. Valphine's apology-and-commitment move opened a door. The door does not stay open on its own.

**Heat** = visible party commitment to the things Valphine promised on their behalf. Moving on the lodge. Moving on the mine. Acting instead of talking. Every time the party delivers, Falcon's opening deepens. Every session the party drifts — trades downtime for action, ducks the orcs, defers the lodge — the opening narrows.

If the party does nothing, **he stops feeling it.** Not because he stops grieving, not because he re-converts to Adabra. Because the thing Valphine offered him was *people who do not give up*, and if the party gives up, the evidence is gone. He goes back to open-not-convinced drifting toward quietly-not-anymore. The ring goes back in the drawer and stays there.

### State levels — Falcon's religious opening

| Level | Inner state | Visible signals |
|---|---|---|
| **0 — Closed** | Decayed back to baseline. Valphine was a moment, not a path. | Polite but distant. Ring in drawer. Lodge is a lost cause he talks about in past tense. |
| **1 — Open, not convinced** | Where he is now. The door exists. | Will join lodge reclamation if asked. Ring where it was. *"I'll be there."* |
| **2 — Warming** | Party is acting. Someone apologized and then did the thing. | Ring relocates in the drawer. He initiates the second Valphine conversation — mine, Lathander, or neither. |
| **3 — Feeling it** | Sustained party follow-through. The evidence is stacking. | Ring comes out of the drawer. He carries it, wears it, or offers it. Senne beat reopens — whoever asks the right question gets her name. |
| **4 — Committed (not converted)** | Not Lathander. His own version of not-giving-up. | Public repudiation of the dragonslayer persona. Acts without being asked. Material asset to the party. |

### What ratchets heat up
- Moving on the lodge after he asks
- Moving on the mine after Valphine promised
- Valphine following through on her apology in ways *she* initiates (not because Falcon is watching)
- Any party member doing the apology-and-commitment move in another scene — proves it was not a one-off trick

### What ratchets heat down
- Sessions pass and the lodge is not reclaimed
- The orcs in the Peaks are not addressed
- Valphine reverts to pain-theology pitch on someone else and he hears about it
- Vukradin returns to form without owning the walkout
- The party treats the adjudication as a win that's behind them

### Decay default
Without input, the state decays **one level per ~2 sessions of inaction.** DM judgment — faster if the party is visibly avoiding, slower if they're working on something adjacent that reads as commitment.

### What does NOT shift with heat
- Conversion to Lathander. Not available at any level. Open-not-convinced is the ceiling of the religious read; Level 4 is commitment in his own frame, not hers.
- Return to Adabra's bloc. Door is closed from his side. Even full decay to Level 0 does not send him back.
- Forgetting Senne. The name question works at Level 3+ and nowhere else.

### Why this is a mechanic, not a mood
Because *the party controls whether the opening survives.* The ring is the visible tell — players paying attention will notice where it is. That is the signal that Falcon is changing, without Falcon ever saying he is. If the ring goes back into the drawer and stays there, they should feel it: something was there, and they let it go.

## DM Logistics

- No roll on any Falcon crack or state shift. Either the scene earns it or it doesn't.
- Adabra finding out about Valphine-Falcon is held in reserve. Do not play it for its own sake. Play it when it can cost something.
- If the party never returns to the lodge: the ring stays in the drawer, and Falcon's state stays open-not-convinced until something else moves it. That is a valid steady state.
- Vukradin-Falcon is unresolved debt on both sides. Neither gets a free pass. When they next share a scene, neither one pretends it didn't happen.
