# Glazhael and Iymrith's Return from Avernus

## Glazhael's History Across Campaigns

### Tyranny of Dragons Campaign (Group A)
- Glazhael is a white dragon guarding the hoard at Skyreach Castle (Blagothkus's cloud fortress)
- The Tyranny of Dragons party kills her

### Between Adventures
- A Cult of the Dragon purist performs the dracolich ritual on Glazhael
- She is reanimated as a white dracolich

### Tyranny of Dragons (Endgame)
- Glazhael returns as a dracolich for a final attempt to kill the Tyranny of Dragons party
- She survives this encounter

### Storm King's Thunder / 3HPP Campaign
- Glazhael is on the cloud castle, working with the vampire **Escarlotta** (Blagothkus's spectral wife)
- **Escarlotta holds Glazhael's phylactery** — giving her leverage over the dracolich
- The 3HPP kill Escarlotta, sending her spirit back to her coffin
- **Glazhael makes a deal with the 3HPP:** she will permanently destroy Escarlotta in exchange for getting her phylactery back
- Glazhael kills Escarlotta, takes her phylactery, and **leaves the cloud castle**

### Post-3HPP: Glazhael's Current State
- **Free dracolich** in possession of her own phylactery
- Unkillable unless the phylactery is destroyed
- No allegiance to anyone — transactional relationship with the 3HPP, nothing owed
- Does NOT control the cloud castle (she left after the deal)

---

## Glazhael's Goal: Recreate the Cult of the Dragon

Glazhael's ambition is to **rebuild the Cult of the Dragon** under her own leadership. To do this, she needs the **dracolich potion formula** — the ritual that transforms living dragons into dracoliches.

**The problem:** Escarlotta had partial information about the dracolich creation process. Enough for Glazhael to understand the concept, but the formula as reconstructed is **too lethal** — it kills dragons without completing the transformation. She cannot recruit other dragons into her cult if the process just murders them.

She needs a biological researcher who understands dragon physiology at the deepest level. She needs **Iymrith**.

---

## The Deal: Iymrith and Glazhael

Iymrith, trapped in Avernus, contacts Glazhael (mechanism TBD — infernal communication, dream sending, intermediary). She offers a trade:

**What Glazhael provides:**
- Subvert the Cult of Myrkul's summoning ritual (Ularan Mortus's plan to pull a dragon spirit into a living dragon body)
- Instead of summoning Ebondeath's spirit, redirect the ritual to pull **Iymrith's soul from Avernus**
- This gives Iymrith a body (Claugiyliamatar) and a return to the material plane

**What Iymrith provides (upon return):**
- The perfected dracolich potion formula
- Iymrith is a centuries-old biological researcher who created drake hybrids and fey/dragon hybrids — fixing a lethal alchemical formula is well within her expertise
- With a working formula, Glazhael can offer true dracolich transformation to other dragons and rebuild the Cult of the Dragon as a real power

## How the Return Works (Subverting Divine Contention)

### The Official Source Material
In *Sleeping Dragon's Wake* / *Divine Contention*:
- Ularan Mortus (priest of Myrkul) sends Viantha Cruelhex to weaken Claugiyliamatar's mental defenses
- The spirit of Ebondeath (the dead black dragon Chardansearavitriol) is meant to possess Claugiyliamatar's body
- Ebondeath in Claugiyliamatar's body then attacks Leilon to steal the Ruinstone for Ularan Mortus

### The Modified Version
- Ularan Mortus's ritual proceeds as planned — Viantha weakens Claugiyliamatar
- **Glazhael subverts the summoning** — instead of Ebondeath's spirit answering the call, it's **Iymrith's soul pulled from Avernus**
- Ularan Mortus may not even know the switch happened (he expects a dragon spirit to possess Claugiyliamatar — he gets one, just not the one he planned)
- **Iymrith takes Claugiyliamatar's body** — a blue dragon's intellect in a green dragon's form

### Consequences
- The "Leilon Besieged" siege still happens — but the dragon is Iymrith, not Ebondeath
- Iymrith has her own agenda (the three artifacts) which may partially align with Ularan Mortus's goal of stealing the Ruinstone — but for completely different reasons
- Glazhael is positioned elsewhere, waiting for Iymrith to deliver the formula
- The party faces a dragon at the Divine Contention climax without knowing who is actually inside

## Open Questions

1. **Does Iymrith reveal herself during the siege of Leilon?** Or does she maintain the "Ebondeath" cover to manipulate Ularan Mortus?
2. **Is Glazhael present during the siege?** Or waiting in the wings for Iymrith to deliver?
3. **How does Iymrith contact Glazhael from Avernus?** Infernal intermediary? Zariel facilitates? Dream sending through the phylactery?
4. **What happens to Ebondeath?** Is his spirit still out there, blocked from the ritual? Does he become a separate threat?
5. **Does Zariel know about / approve of Iymrith's escape plan?** Or is Iymrith breaking out against Zariel's wishes?
6. **What does Ularan Mortus do when he realizes he got Iymrith instead of Ebondeath?** Does it matter to him, or is "a dragon spirit in a dragon body" sufficient for his purposes?
7. **When does Iymrith abandon Claugiyliamatar's body?** She presumably wants her own form back eventually — or does she? A green dragon body is still a dragon body.
8. **Does the Ruinstone heist happen during the siege?** If Iymrith is already targeting it for her own plan, she may grab it during the chaos of Leilon Besieged — making the party think they "lost" the siege when really Iymrith got exactly what she wanted.
