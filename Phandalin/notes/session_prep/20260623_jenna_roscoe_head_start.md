# Jenna Roscoe — Neverwinter Head Start (load-bearing, ch46+)

## What happened

After Vukradin's Universal Basic Treasure proclamation at the Phandalin town green (session 2026-06-23), Jenna Roscoe departed Phandalin in visible disgust and headed back to Neverwinter **ahead of the party**.

The party's journey to Neverwinter was uneventful and unhurried. Jenna had time to arrive first.

## Why this matters

Jenna is a Lord's Alliance agent. With a head start she could:

- Brief Alliance leadership on the UBT proclamation and its populist implications
- Frame the party as agitators or destabilizing forces within the Alliance's territorial jurisdiction (the broadsheet already shows Phandalin inside their dotted-line boundary)
- Coordinate with other interested parties — Order of the Gauntlet contacts, Neverwinter city officials, noble houses — before the party walks in
- Position the Alliance to intercept or complicate the party's errands in the city (Elara Meliamne, Perrin Aligonder, Counting House)

## Disposition

Jenna's disposition toward the party has worsened from **Neutral** to something colder. She has always viewed them as independent operators she wants to recruit; the UBT stunt makes them look like a political liability.

## Hooks available to the GM

- A herald or city official greets the party with pointed questions about "the treasure redistribution scheme" before they've said anything
- A Lord's Alliance letter has already arrived at the Counting House advising caution about "certain adventurers from Phandalin"
- Jenna herself is somewhere in Neverwinter — available for a charged encounter where she holds better cards than the party expects
- The broadsheet map (Phandalin inside Alliance jurisdiction) takes on new weight if someone in Neverwinter is already treating the UBT as a sovereignty question
