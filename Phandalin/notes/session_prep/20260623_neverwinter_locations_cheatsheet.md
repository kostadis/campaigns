## The Five Vukradin Payoffs (all staged as branches)
| Payoff | Where | Lands when… |
|---|---|---|
| **Golden Ledger Audit** | Counting house (Enclave) | Vukradin redeems the Letter of Credit → R&C audit |
| **Neverember + the studio** | (Neverember's hall, via Aldus Hern) | the meeting happens; accept / negotiate / decline all clean |
| **Orsik trip-wire** | Orsik plaque (Enclave) | Dave reads his own prior PC on Alliance propaganda — circlet stays locked behind Parnast |
| **Margaster's pitch** | Moonstone Mask (Enclave) | Cullen pitches Vukradin direct; bites if no one told him Elara's dead |
| **Exile Aesthetic flip** | Seven Suns stall / Common Chord | the author meets the genre made of his worst year |

## Brewbarry Payoffs
| Payoff | Where | Lands when… |
|---|---|---|
| **Fashion-icon recognition** | Seven Suns / city gate | a fan clocks him; lean-in (mobbed, signs autographs) or discomfort (read as *more* authentic) — he can't win it on its terms |
| **Sign Vukradin's plaque** | The Common Chord | Petra seats them on his fame, then casually asks him to sign the brass plaque on Vukradin's old table |
| **Thistle's-Echo instinct (latent)** | anywhere combat/uncanny | his anti-magic radar keeps firing without him noticing the pattern — feed it, don't name it |

> Weight under the comedy: the city romanticized his **unjust** exile into a lifestyle brand. He didn't choose it and didn't glamorize it. Let that ache sit; don't undercut it.

## Soma Payoffs
| Payoff | Where | Lands when… |
|---|---|---|
| **Home waters, new tools** | The Blue Lake (Bluelake) | she reaches the lake; staff reacts before she consciously clocks the silence — the Adabra axis: she'd once call this "natural," now she knows *motivated blight* |
| **Scope the door (not solve it)** | Hazel's lab | Hazel hands over her notes + three Underdark entry points; fixing the lake means going *down*, with prep, later |
| **The Lim reunion** | The Board Laid Bare (Tower) | Lim puts down the tray, sits across from her — *"You came back. I thought you'd gone somewhere northern."* Let it breathe; don't narrate the history |

> Don't dilute the lake scene with other PCs' hooks. If a 3–4 hr session runs long, this is the clean one to push to next session — it's framed as a marked door, not a resolution.

## Valphine Payoffs
| Payoff | Where | Lands when… |
|---|---|---|
| **The golden-eyes mirror** | Spire of the Morninglord (Enclave) | Brother Aldric sees the mark, cycles wonder→alarm; the convo holds until he says *mercy / warmth / hope* — then he asks what she actually believes |
| **Define the order first** | anywhere religious figures react | her militant Lathanderite order has no outside name recognition yet — she gets to define it before the Church does |
| **Horst's drow-hatred timer (threat, not gift)** | The Flickering Ember (Bluelake) | the moment Horst sees a drow, the clock starts; ambush comes *later*, off-site, no obvious motive |
| **Ashmadai watcher (plant)** | Seven Suns / Bluelake | a gold-eyed tiefling watches her a beat too long, then leaves — Mordai Vell associate; do not escalate today |

> Her eyes are a **brand, not a blessing** (her read), and she finds Aldric's misreading clarifying rather than worth correcting. Play him curious and a little frightened, never obstructionist.

## Quick triggers & "do not"
- **Letter of Credit** redeem = Scene V2. No back door; the auditors are the door.
- **Do NOT surface Bimble Nackle** this session — plant the missing-gnome hook, let the KP assumption ride.
- **Do NOT leak the Orsik circlet** — only available in Parnast, from Vardis, face-to-face.
- **Corrin is uncapturable** — a grab passes through the bit; never a combat roll.
- **Horst's timer** fires *later* (street ambush, no obvious motive), not in the shop.
- **Joke first, dread second** on every R&C beat.
