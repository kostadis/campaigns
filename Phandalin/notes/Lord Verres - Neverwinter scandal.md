## Established Facts

- **Lord Halueth Verres** is a famous Neverwinter noble.
- **Lord Magister of Neverwinter** is an office that exists and that one is *elevated to* via a confirmation hearing. Treat as a real institutional seat in Neverwinter governance.
- Verres' confirmation to Lord Magister was contested. **A war-mage from Waterdeep** travelled to Neverwinter and testified against him at the hearing.
- The substance of the testimony involved two things: an **artifact** (nature unspecified) and a **contract with apprentices** (nature unspecified). The phrasing "the whole mess" implies more than the two named pieces.
- Verres was confirmed despite the testimony — he got the seat.
- **Ten years later**, **Lady Verres** (his wife) initiated contact with the Waterdhavian war-mage via *Sending* and demanded the war-mage apologize to her husband for what she had said at the confirmation.
- The war-mage **actually replied** (content of the reply unspecified).
- The exchange became famous enough in Neverwinter social circles that Vukradin reaches for it as the canonical example of "audacity" as a tone — a wronged party demanding an apology from the one with grievance.
