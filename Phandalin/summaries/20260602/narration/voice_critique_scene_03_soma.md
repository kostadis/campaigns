# Voice Critique — Soma, scene 03: Negotiations with the Boars

**Narration:** session_doc_scene_03_negotiations_with_the_boars.scrubbed.md
**Voice spec:** voice/soma_new_pipeline.md
**Per-char examples:** examples/soma.md

## Flags

### [1] Voice spec conflict (philosophically abstract)

> every grievance I have ever heard from a thinking creature is, underneath, this same grievance. Someone took what was mine to eat and I was not awake to stop them. The boars just had the honesty to say it plainly.

**Why:** The spec says no long abstract moral arguments; she gives the verdict, not the lecture. "Every grievance I have ever heard from a thinking creature" is a universalizing generalization that works toward a conclusion already visible in the final sentence. Soma's rhythm is conclusion-first, evidence-minimal: she states the verdict and moves. Three sentences of working-through violates that rhythm.
**Suggested rewrite:** Invert and compress: "The boars just had the honesty to say it plainly. Every grievance I have ever heard from a creature who can talk is this one." Cut the middle elaboration.

### [2] Register softness (slight convergence)

> they do that, finish each other, the way my bale does.

**Why:** Comparing the boars' call-and-response to "my bale" reads as fonder than Soma typically is about her companions. The spec says her affection surfaces as restraint, competence, or private concern — not warm comparisons. The observation that the boars echo each other is accurate and dry; the "way my bale does" gloss softens it in a way that drifts slightly toward Vukradin's "things-are-like-other-things" rhetorical warmth.
**Suggested rewrite:** "they do that, finish each other" — the observation stands without the warm gloss.

## Verdict

The strongest Soma scene in this session. The staff-leaf beat ("Five leaves now, the newest one still tender from the storm"), "I bit down on the inside of my beak," the boar logic ("a boar does not exaggerate about danger"), and the final "Well then" verdict are all exactly right. Two minor flags; neither requires re-narration.
