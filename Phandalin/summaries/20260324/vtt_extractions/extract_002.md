## Events

- Sister Kaella (referred to as "Sister Kayla/Kella" interchangeably) regained partial consciousness long enough to warn the party that they must reach Icespire Hold before the Carver completes his dragon-binding ritual.
- The party deliberated travel options: going directly to Icespire Hold incurs 1 level of exhaustion; going through Phandalin first incurs 2 levels; being mounted/carried could eliminate exhaustion entirely.
- The party devised a multi-mount solution: Soma wild-shapes into a bear (carrying one rider), Boney carries one rider, and Xanth the centaur carries two lighter party members (Vukradin and Valphine), eliminating exhaustion.
- Decision made to leave Sister Kaella in Corbin's custody, bound and kept near 1 hit point. Corbin enthusiastically agreed.
- Valphine used insight (rolled 25) and observed that Corbin and Sister Kaella have a prior antagonistic history — Corbin smiled at the opportunity to have her in his power.
- The party departed for Icespire Hold and successfully navigated to the mountains without exhaustion.
- At the mountain approach, the party discovered the Carver's trail: perfectly straight, robotic footprints cutting directly over obstacles rather than around them, suggesting possession or loss of humanity.
- The party encountered a group of stone giants near a narrow mountain pass (defile), murmuring about rocks being "off."
- Soma used Meril's Staff to detect that rocks along the Carver's path were warped by psychic energy — phasing in and out of reality, appearing to roll without wind to the naked eye.
- The party showed the warped rocks to the stone giants as evidence of the Carver's corruption. The giants acknowledged the wrongness but declined to intervene, citing Hekaton's post-war doctrine of giants minding giant affairs. One giant gave the party a Potion of Greater Healing.
- The party confirmed from the stone giants that the Carver passed through alone and carried an obsidian sword.
- At the Glacier of Whispers, the party encountered ice stained with violet psychic energy, frozen faces pressing against the ice from within, silent screaming mouths, and whispers: "He walks without rest, without breath, without soul."
- Group Wisdom saves were rolled to resist psychic resonance at the Glacier of Whispers; the party passed. Valphine recalled a Lathander-specific blessing granting advantage on psychic saves in this area (noted but not needed).
- The party found frozen corpses of young white drakes with eyes burned out by necrotic energy — the Carver walked straight through their lair. Their mother drake circled overhead, enraged.
- Vukradin played music (persuasion/performance) to calm the mother drake; it succeeded. The party slipped past while she lay down grieving, avoiding combat.
- The party encountered impassable boulder terrain. Soma used Mold Earth (survival check, DC 15) to carve a path through; Vukradin granted bardic inspiration. Roll outcome pending (session ended before resolution).
- Session ended mid-skill-challenge on the mountain approach to Icespire Hold.

## NPC Interactions

- **Sister Kaella**: Gave final warning to reach Icespire Hold before ritual completes. Screamed and cursed at being left with Corbin. Party left her bound, near 1 HP, in Corbin's custody.
- **Corbin**: Eagerly accepted custody of Sister Kaella; asked if he could beat her. Vukradin authorized keeping her at 1 HP. Valphine observed Corbin's personal history with Kaella implied a deeper antagonism.
- **Xanth the Centaur**: Persuaded (Vukradin rolled 17) to carry the party partway. Agreed to take them to the mountain foothills (~35 miles), but refused to go into the mountains ("my feet are not for those things"). This eliminated the exhaustion cost of travel.
- **Stone Giants (unnamed, multiple)**: Encountered at mountain defile, muttering about displaced rocks. Confirmed the Carver passed through alone with an obsidian sword. Described him as "the little one" of no concern. Declined to intervene citing Hekaton's post-war doctrine. Gave party one Potion of Greater Healing. Referenced the War of the Giants and its lessons.
- **Mother Drake (unnamed)**: Calmed by Vukradin's music; lay down beside her dead young. Party avoided combat by slipping past.

- **Decision — Sister Kaella**: Left behind with Corbin rather than brought along; kept bound at near-1 HP.
