# Session — 2026-03-18

## Overview

With Jax and the Rot-Tusk Ogre dead and Sister Kaella barely alive in their custody, the party interrogated their prisoner and extracted the critical intelligence they had nearly lost last session: the precise location of Icespire Hold. Xanth the centaur, healed by Soma, provided valuable lore about the Carver and revealed an unexpected personal connection to Meril's Staff. The party devised a creative multi-mount solution to avoid exhaustion, left Sister Kaella in Corbin's unsettling custody, and set off directly into the Ice Spire Mountains. Their ascent traced the Carver's deeply unnatural trail — arrow-straight footprints warping stone and phasing reality — through a tense encounter with stone giants, across the psychically haunted Glacier of Whispers, and past a grieving mother drake soothed by Vukradin's music. The session ended mid-skill-challenge as Soma attempted to carve a path through impassable boulders blocking the final approach to Icespire Hold.

## Session Events

- **Post-battle assessment:** Party confirmed Jax, Rot-Tusk Ogre, and all thugs dead; Sister Kaella kept alive with non-lethal damage.
- **Brewbarry backstory clarified:** A discussion of Goodberries prompted Brewbarry to recall that the artifact theft happened while he was sober — he was exiled for apparent "drunkenness" caused by a Goodberry's effects, and only discovered actual drinking after exile. "Sobriety was wasted youth."
- **Xanth healed:** Soma cast Cure Wounds (12 HP) on Xanth the Centaur, restoring him to function.
- **Xanth debriefed:** He explained the white dragon displaced orcs from the Ice Spire Mountains, fracturing them into multiple factions (including the Talosian anchorites). Confirmed the Carver's three lieutenants: Borg (dead), Jax (dead), Sister Kaella (captured). Revealed personal friendship with Meril and recognized Meril's Staff — noting its single remaining leaf and declaring it a powerful artifact, not a simple stick. Pledged aid to the party.
- **Sister Kaella interrogated:** Valphine woke her to 1 HP via Medicine check (15), then intimidated her (roll 16). Kaella revealed the Carver has "gone completely mad," is marching on Icespire Hold to bind/possess the white dragon, and plans to burn Phandalin as a demonstration of power. She admitted she turned against the Carver after he blamed her for intelligence failures following Borg's death. She had approached only Valphine — judging Vukradin too principled for morally gray conspiracy. She provided exact directions to Icespire Hold and requested imprisonment at Axeholm rather than Phandalin, suggesting Phandalin residents be warned to evacuate there.
- **Travel planning:** Direct mountain route = 1 level exhaustion on foot; via Phandalin = 2 levels. Party devised multi-mount solution: Xanth carries Vukradin and Valphine, Soma wild-shapes into a bear to carry one rider, Boney carries one rider. Exhaustion eliminated entirely.
- **Sister Kaella's custody:** Left bound and near 1 HP with Corbin. Valphine's Insight (25) revealed Corbin and Kaella have a prior antagonistic history. Vukradin authorized keeping her at 1 HP. Kaella screamed and cursed as the party departed. Decision made to bypass Phandalin entirely.
- **Party took a long rest**, then departed directly for the Ice Spire Mountains.
- **The Carver's trail discovered:** Unnaturally straight, rhythmic footprints cutting directly over obstacles — human in shape but inhuman in pattern, consistent with possession or loss of humanity. Stones along the path warped by psychic energy, phasing in and out of reality. Soma detected this with Meril's Staff.
- **Stone giant encounter:** Party found stone giants in a narrow mountain defile muttering about rocks being "off." Brewbarry addressed them in Giant. Giants confirmed the Carver ("the little one with the obsidian sword") passed through alone. Declined to intervene citing Hekaton's post-war doctrine of giants minding giant affairs. Gifted the party a **Potion of Greater Healing** and a **Potion of Hill Giant Strength** before the party continued.
- **Glacier of Whispers:** Ice stained violet with psychic energy; frozen faces pressing against the surface in silent screams. Psychic whispers: *"He walks without rest, without breath, without soul."* Party passed group Wisdom saves; Valphine recalled a Lathander blessing granting psychic resistance (not needed). Party navigated successfully.
- **Mother drake encounter:** Found frozen corpse of a young white drake with eyes burned out by necrotic energy — the Carver walked straight through its lair. Enraged mother drake circled overhead. Vukradin played calming music; Brewbarry joined in. Drake descended, lay beside her dead young, and allowed the party to pass without combat.
- **Boulder obstruction:** Impassable terrain of massive boulders and ice blocked the final approach. Soma attempted Mold Earth (Survival DC 15) with bardic inspiration from Vukradin. **Roll outcome unresolved — session ended here.**
