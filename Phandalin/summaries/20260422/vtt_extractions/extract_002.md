## Events

- Xanthopoulos the centaur arrived at the council, recognized Meril's staff (noting more leaves had grown), and stated that Meril gave it not out of sentiment but because he chose Soma.
- Xanthopoulos delivered news that Corbin was dead, killed by Sister Kaella, who escaped from Dragon Barrow where the party had left her under inadequate guard with only a low-level fighter.
- Adabra attempted to use Corbin's death as evidence of the dangers of interventionism; Xanthopoulos immediately pushed back and stated he was not on her side.
- Soma owned up to the mistake of leaving Sister Kaella poorly guarded rather than sugarcoating it.
- A recess was called; the council then realized they had not yet heard from Lyra directly and brought her in to testify.
- Lyra testified that a planar rift was damaging the forest, a ritual to stop it failed, and killing the interplanar treant was the only remaining option.
- Soma argued that the interplanar treant was not a natural creature and its death was necessary, citing encounters with twisted dwarves at Axelholm and the Talosian blight on the land, including that Falcon's own lodge was overtaken by Talosians.
- Adabra delivered an increasingly elaborate and incoherent naturalist lecture condemning the party's interventionism before the vote.
- The council deliberated briefly and ruled that Adabra's case did not hold; they affirmed the Emerald Enclave's purpose is to protect the forest and region, not to wait passively.
- Lyra was restored to full good standing within the Emerald Enclave.
- Adabra left the meeting in a seething rage without another word.
- Soma mic dropped Meril's staff on the ground as a triumphant gesture.
- Druids expressed regret that Vukradin had left early, saying they had heard he plays very good music; one druid also stated that Adabra's personal attacks on Vukradin were absolutely disgraceful and crossed a line.
- The druids noted they would not tolerate Adabra's style of argument again; they did not know what to do about her longer-term.
- Valphine approached Falcon after the council and engaged him in conversation about his despondency.
- Valphine shared her personal journey from Menzoberranzan and the experience of first seeing the sun, which resonated with Falcon.
- Falcon admitted he no longer knew why he had supported Adabra, expressed a desire to deal with the orc threat, and said he probably owed Vukradin an apology.
- Falcon asked Valphine to help him find the words or approach to apologize to Vukradin.
- Valphine suggested Falcon give Vukradin a rare musical instrument, donate to the church, or build a performance hall and small shrine to Lathander in his rebuilt lodge.
- Falcon appeared receptive but not yet converted to Lathander's faith; Valphine made partial progress toward a longer-term conversion.
- Xanthopoulos remarked quietly to Soma as he departed that perhaps Meril had made the right choice.
- Lyra offered to buy the party drinks at the Stonehill Tavern to celebrate.
- The party decided their next destination is Neverwinter, with the orc threat to be dealt with along the way as it lies on the northern route.
