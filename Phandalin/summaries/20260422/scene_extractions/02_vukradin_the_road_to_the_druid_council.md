[Scene 2] The Road to the Druid Council
Narrator: Vukradin
Focus: Vukradin absorbs Thomas's warning and weighs the threat ahead, his instinct for reading a room already calculating what Adabra's carefully timed council means for him personally.

- On the way, the party encounters Thomas, one of Lyra's agents, who warns them that Adabra is seeking to ostracize Lyra.

- Thomas explains that Reidoth, the nominal head of the local Emerald Enclave chapter, is away and that Adabra called the meeting during his absence specifically to push her agenda.

- The party discusses whether to contact Reidoth and keeps it as a back-pocket option; Thomas notes that even if Reidoth were present, he could not stop Adabra from speaking.

- The party debates their strategy for the upcoming meeting, with Soma deciding to lean into proud interventionism rather than deny what happened.
