[Scene 6] The Emerald Enclave Council Verdict
Narrator: Brewbarry
Focus: Lyra first defends her actions to the council. Then the party points out the threats the interplanar events are causing. Adabra becomes furious. Brewbarry stands at the edge of the proceedings having already offered to kill everyone in the room — his perspective captures the absurdity and the stakes of a political victory won entirely without violence.

- Lyra explains to the council that a planar rift was damaging the forest and that the ritual to stop it failed, leading to the death of an interplanar treant.

- The party argues that the treant was a manifestation of planar horror and that intervention was necessary, citing encounters with twisted dwarves at Axelholm and the Talosian blight on the land.

- The party points out that the Falcon's own lodge was overtaken by Talosians — an example of what unchecked threats can do.

- Adabra delivers a furious lecture on naturalist philosophy, condemning the party's interventionist mindset.

- The Druid Council deliberates briefly and sides with Soma and Lyra, ruling that the purpose of the Emerald Enclave is to save the forest and that sitting idle makes no sense.
**GM as the presiding druid** — *delivering the verdict to Adabra*
> "Adabra, the case you've made just does not hold. Yes, it is unfortunate that Corbyn died, but he was a criminal. And the party made the decision — Soma made the decision to save the forest from the dragon. That was the right decision."


**GM as the presiding druid** — *dismantling Adabra's coalition one by one*
> "Aldric over here admits freely that if we were to listen to you, the orcs — what's going on — is perfectly normal. And the Falcon here depends on money that these interventionists saved him, and he needs them to save him. All you managed to do was offend the bard."

**GM as the presiding druid** — *stating the Enclave's core purpose as the final ruling*
> "The purpose of the Druids Enclave is not to save the multiverse, the purpose of the Druids Enclave is to save the forest. And to say that to just sit there and wait, and say that it's all part of some divine, cosmic plan — it makes no sense whatsoever."

- Adabra leaves the meeting in a seething rage after her case is dismissed by the other druids.

- Lyra is restored to full good standing within the Emerald Enclave.

- Soma performs a symbolic mic drop with her staff to punctuate the victory.

- The druids express disappointment that Vukradin left early, as they had heard he plays very good music.
