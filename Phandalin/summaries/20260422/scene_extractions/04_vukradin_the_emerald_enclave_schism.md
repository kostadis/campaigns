[Scene 4] The Emerald Enclave Schism
Narrator: Vukradin
Focus: Soma opens with a defense of interventionism, Vukradin ignores it, becomes the target of Falcon's public denunciation and the one who drags Falcon's hypocritical past into the open before the entire room — this scene belongs to the man who storms out of it.


- Soma rallies the council with a proud interventionist stance, arguing that the land is hurting and intervention is the only way to protect it.
**Wade (Soma)** — *making her proud interventionist stand before the council*
> "The land is hurting, and intervention is the only way out. We are here to protect the land at all costs, and that makes us interventionists. Then so be it. We're loud, we're proud."


- Falcon intervenes in the council, siding with Adabra and publicly denouncing Vukradin and the party, saying trees are dead because of men like them.
**GM as Falcon** — *publicly siding with Adabra and denouncing the party*
> "I came because someone finally sat long enough to listen. You can keep cheering your victories. I've seen what comes after them."

**GM as Falcon** — *pointing directly at Vukradin*
> "She's right! She's right about what needed to happen. You all wanted it simple. Well, this is what simple looks like. Trees are dead because of men like him."
- Vukradin confronts Falcon privately, reminding him of his own past as an adventurer and pointing out that his current comfortable life was built on the very interventionism he now condemns.

**David (Vukradin)** — *pulling Falcon aside for a private confrontation*
> "What's going on here? Like, I thought we were… Why are you pointing me out in front of this group? What did I do?"

**GM as Falcon** — *explaining his position*
> "You intervened, and in your intervention, people died. Adabra sees it clearly."

**David (Vukradin)** — *pressing Falcon on his own adventuring past*
> "Didn't you used to intervene? Like, what… why the change of heart?"

**GM as Falcon** — *bitterly*
> "And look what it got me."

**David (Vukradin)** — *challenging the logic of Falcon's self-pity*
> "As far as I can tell, it got you a beautiful hunting lodge, and lots of fame, and glory… and a whole life built on a lie. Got you a lot, that intervention. But now, now… you're gonna screw me over."

**David (Vukradin)** — *warning Falcon*
> "I could tell everybody about what you did. About how you intervened."

**GM as Falcon** — *dismissively*
> "Ever since I spoke to Adabra, I have learned the error of my ways."

**David (Vukradin)** — *pressing Falcon to confess publicly*
> "Well then, you should have no trouble repenting in front of the group and explaining the errors he made."


- Falcon publicly confesses his past failures as an adventurer to the council, claiming he has learned the error of his ways through Adabra's teachings.
**GM as Falcon** — *publicly siding with Adabra and denouncing the party*
> "I came because someone finally sat long enough to listen. You can keep cheering your victories. I've seen what comes after them."

**GM as Falcon** — *standing up before the entire council to publicly confess*
> "You are right. It is true. I was an adventurer once. And I once believed that the path to solving the universe was to intervene, and I once wanted to go fight the dragons, and I failed to fight the dragons, and I have learned that all the pain and misery that happened after that — yes, I had some baubles, and yes, I had some money — but what happened? Once again, nature tore it down. Once again, the Falcon Lodge was burnt. Once again, I asked heroes to intervene, to protect my lodge, and what did they do? They did nothing, and my lodge burned down."
- Offended by the personal insults and the hostile atmosphere, Vukradin storms out of the meeting and heads back to Phandalin.


**David (Vukradin)** — *walking out of the council entirely*
> "I'm going back to Phandalin. You all can stay. I do not like the personal insults. I'm not interested in helping Lyra anymore."

- Brewbarry asks if he should kill the dissenters, but Soma insists they must preserve the Druids to protect the land.
**GM as Brewbarry** — *to the remaining party*
> "Do we kill all these people, or do we leave?"

**Wade (Soma)** — *pulling Brewbarry back from the brink*
> "Without the druids, the land is even more screwed than with half of them."

- Vukradin sends a magical message to Reidoth (via Sending, taken through Magical Secrets) to inform him of the ideological conflict occurring at the council.**GM as Falcon** — *standing up before the entire council to publicly confess*
