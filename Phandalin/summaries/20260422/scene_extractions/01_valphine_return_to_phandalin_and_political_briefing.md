[Scene 1] Return to Phandalin and Political Briefing
Narrator: Valphine
Focus: Valphine watches Harbin Wester steal the party's glory while Lathander quietly approves of her church's direction — she alone sees both the political theater and the divine thread running beneath it.

- Harbin Wester welcomes the party back to Phandalin with a festival in their honor, praising their role in defeating the dragon threat and taking credit for forming the Privy Council that brought them to town.

- Ser Kaelen pulls Soma aside to warn her about Adabra's attempts to turn the Emerald Enclave against her, with the rest of the party following.

- The party learns that Adabra is using the party's actions at the Whispering Wood — specifically Lyra's failure — to paint Soma as a dangerous interventionist.

- Valphine discusses the charitable nature of her church, the Temple of Lathander's Searing Pain of Justice. 

- Vukradin admits that it would be okay to donate to her charity, if they couldn't find the real owners of the lost property. 

- Bryn and Giles, recent converts to Valphine's faith, appear scarred and sunburnt after performing a public display of self-flagellation as a fundraiser through the town.
