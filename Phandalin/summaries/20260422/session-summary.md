# Session — 2026-04-28

## Overview
The party returned to Phandalin as celebrated heroes following their victory over Cryovain, only to be immediately thrust into a high-stakes political trial within the Emerald Enclave. Adabra, a staunch naturalist druid, had called a council meeting during Reidoth's absence to ostracize Lyra and condemn the party for killing a corrupted treant in the Whispering Wood. After Vukradin stormed out following personal attacks from the embittered ranger Falcon, Soma carried the day with passionate arguments for interventionism, turning the room's moderates against Adabra's philosophy. The council ruled in the party's favor, restored Lyra to full standing, and Adabra departed in a rage. In the aftermath, Valphine began pulling Falcon back from nihilism and toward a potential apology to Vukradin, while the party set their sights on Neverwinter as their next destination.
