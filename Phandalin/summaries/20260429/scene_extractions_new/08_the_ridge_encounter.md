# The Ridge Encounter

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party reaches a ridge and encounters a large group of orcs led by a hostile spellcasting leader.
- The party arrives at a ridge overlooking a 60-foot-wide, 10-foot-deep gully and spots ten orcs and a humanoid leader with elongated claws cresting the opposite ridge with terrible battle cries.
- The DM notes there should be ten orcs and one figure who "definitely looks like an acolyte" — a Talosian cleric.
- Initiative is rolled. Vukradin has the top of the initiative order.
- Vukradin uses Universal Speech, targeting the leader and the three nearest orcs, and tells them about the wonders of Lathander. The DM rules this will not work on the Talosian cleric — it enrages him further — but some of the regular orcs are affected. Those orcs will still move forward but will not take the dash action, slowing their advance.
- Vukradin notes he plans to use Universal Speech repeatedly on other orcs as the combat proceeds, continuing the proselytizing approach.
- Valphine is next in the initiative order. She considers whether to cast Protection from Energy for lightning resistance but instead casts Daylight on her holy symbol, flooding the area — and the orc-held ridge — with a 60-foot radius of brilliant magical light.
- The DM describes the Talosian cleric screaming: "Kill the false cleric of Lathander! Kill the drow cleric of Lathander, the abomination! Destroy! Destroy the false god!"
- The party notes that their plan is to focus attacks on the Talosian cleric and attempt to convert the remaining orcs, rather than simply fighting everything.
- The session ends here, with the battle for the Circle of Thunder about to begin.
