# Planning the Next Quest

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party discusses their next move in Phandalin, deciding to pursue the half-orc spellcasters of the Circle of Thunder.
- The party reviews their available quests. The Falcon has not yet personally apologized to Vukradin, which tips the scales toward pursuing the Circle of Thunder via Xanth rather than Falcon's Hunting Lodge.
- The party notes they have previously had their "asses whooped" by Talosian spellcasting orcs and that the upcoming encounter may not be pleasant either.
- Valphine considers preparing Protection from Energy (concentration, up to one hour) to grant lightning resistance, given the party's repeated exposure to lightning damage from Talosian cultists. The spell is noted as a useful option but would compete with other concentration spells like Spiritual Guardians.
- Vukradin uses the Sending spell — a spell available to bards on the standard spell list — to contact Xanth the Centaur. Lyra reacts with mild horror at the prospect of Vukradin simply messaging everyone all day. Xanth suggests meeting at the corner of the Triboar Trail where it turns toward Phandalin.
- The group checks their inventory: three healing potions between them — one potion of greater healing, one potion of superior healing, and one "scented" potion of healing of undisclosed contents. Vukradin firmly declines to know what is in the superior potion, requesting that it only be used on him while he is unconscious.
- The party decides to head into the forest to meet Xanth and confront the cultists of Talos.
