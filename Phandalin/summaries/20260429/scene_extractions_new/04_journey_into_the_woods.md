# Journey into the Woods

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party travels through the wilderness with Xanth the Centaur and sets up camp for the night.
- The party meets Xanth the Centaur at the junction of the Triboar Trail and the path to Phandalin, as arranged via Sending.
- Xanth is extremely pleased with the party's recent performance, praising Soma in particular: "Soma, you were superb."
- The group hikes thirty miles into the forest — a full eight-hour day's travel — accompanied by Vukradin's horse Boney, who is tracked as a level-six human warrior sidekick. The GM asks Soma to manage Boney's actions during combat.
- The party decides not to push beyond thirty miles and risk a level of exhaustion, and instead camps in a safe location provided by Xanth.
- During the evening, Xanth shares tales of birds, flowers, heroes, and the general history and lore of the forest and the depredations of Neverwinter. Most of the party quietly tunes out the lore portions, to the GM's theatrical despair.
