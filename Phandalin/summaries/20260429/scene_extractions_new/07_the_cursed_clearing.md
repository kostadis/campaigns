# The Cursed Clearing

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party discovers a disturbing ritual site in a forest clearing and triggers a magical trap when they attempt to destroy the effigies.
- The group enters a 60-foot-wide clearing containing a 40-foot-tall charred, stripped, needle-like pine tree — burned long ago, its limbs gone.
- They find ten ghastly dolls made of twigs and black hair tied to the base of the dead tree.
- Brewbarry rolls a 19 on Religion and identifies the dolls from an old Uthgardian tale: they were left by those who wished to torment and hurt their enemies. His formal verdict: "Black magic, voodoo, no touch."
- Xanth, when asked whether this is typical of the Cult of Talos, says simply: "No." He also clarifies, with mild irritation at being assumed expert, that he is not a cultist of Talos and does not know their customs in detail.
- The party debates the dolls. Brewbarry argues that whoever hung them would not appreciate disturbance; Vukradin counters that they came to kill those people anyway. Brewbarry notes they do not know for certain the evil half-orc spellcasters hung the dolls — they could be enemies of the spellcasters. Vukradin ignores this and destroys a doll with a radiant spell (rolling 13 to hit, then dealing radiant damage).
- A still-beating pig's heart falls from the destroyed doll to the ground. Vukradin destroys the heart. The dead tree's roots animate and erupt from the earth.
- All characters in the 60-foot clearing must make a DC-12 Dexterity saving throw or take 5 bludgeoning damage and become restrained.
- Results: Vukradin passes (13), Soma fails and becomes restrained, Brewbarry uses Bardic Inspiration (spending the die) and just barely passes (6+D8, reaching 11 and then passing with the boost to 12+), Valphine passes, Xanth passes (centaur has a +2 to the roll), and Boney fails (rolled a 9 with a +1, not enough even with the bless die). Boney is trapped and takes 5 bludgeoning damage.
- To free oneself or another requires a successful DC-15 Strength check as an action.
- Soma uses her action and Wild Shape (bonus action) to transform into a Giant Elk, gaining a Strength bonus sufficient to break free — assisted by Valphine's Bless d4 and bardic inspiration (keeping her inspiration die since it was not ultimately needed at the final moment).
- Vukradin continues destroying dolls and hearts. The DM confirms the trap does not stack — any one heart erupts the roots, but additional destroyed hearts do not cause additional eruptions.
- Valphine also assists in destroying dolls.
- Boney is eventually freed as Soma (as elk) moves to help. All ten dolls and hearts are destroyed; the cursed tree is purged.
- The party notes that Soma lost a Wild Shape use and 10 hit points (since recovered via hit dice during short rest). Vukradin burns bardic inspirations to refresh them, and the party takes a short rest before continuing.
- The DM acknowledges he had not expected the party to actually trigger the trap.
