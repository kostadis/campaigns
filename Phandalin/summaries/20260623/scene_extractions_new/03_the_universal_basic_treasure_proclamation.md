# The Universal Basic Treasure Proclamation

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### Vukradin gathers the citizens of Phandalin to propose a radical new way of distributing adventuring wealth.
- Vukradin attempts to intimidate Harbin Wester into calling a town meeting but rolls only a 10. Harbin: *"your intimidation lands as flatly as your sweet tongue would have landed well."* The mayor refuses; Vukradin resolves to gather the people himself.
- The party holds a performance on the town green — Vukradin's idea to throw a "rock concert" to get the crowd happy and bouncing, with Brewbarry booming his voice to announce the gathering — drawing out residents and council members alike (Petunia, Qelline Alderleaf, Big Al Kalazorn, Linene Graywind, Ser Kaelen, Toblen, Elsa, Barthen, the woodworker, Brin and Giles).
- Vukradin addresses the town, criticizing the government's handling of treasure and proposing 'Universal Basic Treasure' (UBT), declaring the recovered gold was stolen from the people and should be returned to them.
- Linene Graywind and Jenna Roscoe debate the economic implications of recirculating monster-held gold into the local market — Linene noting the treasure "recirculates" once spent, Jenna citing "the dialectics of the financials."
- Vukradin commits, on his special abilities, to returning treasure to rightful owners where possible and distributing the rest as UBT — rolling a 17 with advantage where a flat roll would have been a 1.
- The crowd cheers enthusiastically; children ask whether they'll get UBT too, with Soma noting they'll never have to work a day in their lives "as long as adventurers come."
- Harbin Wester unexpectedly supports the plan, stuttering over Vukradin's name and claiming the Privy Council had been considering a similar initiative for days, then leading cheers of "Huzzah, huzzah, huzzah!"
- The town pauses to settle a name for the adventuring group; suggestions include "Slayers of Cryovain" and joking band names, with Soma cast as "manager" and tour guide rather than band member.
- The party officially donates a fused slag of dragon gold to the new UBT fund — only to learn it is fused inside a magical containment that must first be extracted; Vukradin tasks Ser Kaelen with handling it.
- Ser Kaelen raises a "sidebar" on eligibility — does the Order of the Gauntlet get a share, and what defines a "citizen of Phandalin"? — sparking a lengthy debate over property owners, renters, building a wall, immigrants from Neverwinter, a 50%+ residency requirement, primary residence, paying taxes in Phandalin, and attesting under a Zone of Truth spell.
- Harbin awkwardly questions whether the Tortle and the Barbarian have ties to the community; the crowd cheers as Brewbarry and Soma are declared honorary citizens of Phandalin so they qualify for the fund.
- Linene Graywind produces a Candlekeep text — "the Sage's Universal Theorem for Income Distribution" — and offers to lead a subcommittee, under Vukradin's supervision, to draft the distribution rules within a week or two.
- The party negotiates a 5% "organic fair trade" cut of their treasure — gold effectively "laundered" through the UBT fund and returned to them, distributed tax-free.
- Jenna Roscoe, appalled by the populist move, leaves town in a huff and rides off toward Neverwinter; Vukradin: *"I don't know who you are, and I don't care."*
- The party discusses the magical nature of the dragon slag — debating Dispel Magic and Remove Curse — with Soma identifying a planar anomaly (via Meril's Staff) that requires an expert in Neverwinter to resolve.
