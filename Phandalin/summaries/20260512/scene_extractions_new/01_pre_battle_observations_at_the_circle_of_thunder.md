# Pre-Battle Observations at the Circle of Thunder

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party arrives at the Circle of Thunder and observes a group of anchorites performing a mysterious summoning ritual, debating how to intervene.
- The party observes two half-orc spellcasters and twelve twig blight minions performing a ritual at the Circle of Thunder.
- Valphine rolls a 17 on her religion check and identifies the activity as a summoning ritual; Soma rolls a zero, Vukradin (rolling for Soma via proxy) also rolls poorly, and Brewbarry rolls a 10 — none of the others recognize the ceremony.
- The party debates whether to cast Silence to interrupt the ritual, but the DM confirms the anchorites are dancing rather than chanting — there is no vocal component to disrupt.
- Vukradin briefly considers whether the ritual can be terminated mechanically, consulting the module notes before determining the ritual will only be stopped by disrupting the dancers themselves.
- Brewbarry observes that the dancers resemble a "pantomime club," given the eerie silence.
- Initiative is rolled as the party prepares to engage; Vukradin wins several tie-breaker rolls against the party, going first among them.

## Verbatim moments

**[Religion Checks at the Circle of Thunder]**
- The GM asks the party to perform religion checks to identify what the anchorites are doing.
- Vukradin rolls for Soma (proxy), getting a zero (natural one revealed).
- Stéphane rolls a 10 for Brewbarry.
- Valphine rolls a 17 for Valfinha.

**[GM]** — *confirming Valfinha's recognition*
> "Now, Valfine recognized it as a summoning ritual."

**[Vukradin]** — *reacting to the identification*
> "Oh, yeah, you would recognize that in this Animate Dead."

**[GM]** — *clarifying*
> "Just in case you're wondering."

**[Vukradin]** — *considering tactical options*
> "I wonder who they're summoning."

**[GM]** — *teasing*
> "I mean, you'll find out."

**[Vukradin]** — *sceptical*
> "No, we won't."

**[GM]**
> "Oh, you may or may not. You see, you never know how these virtuals work, really."

---

**[Vukradin]** — *announcing intended action*
> "Alright, I'm gonna cast Silence."
> "And I, once again, have no, like, connection to the board, so… but I am casting silence on whoever it is is chanting."
> "And if that is unknown, then the two spellcasters."

**[GM]** — *checking the module for how the ritual works*
> "Hold on."
> "Just make sure. I think you can actually interrupt the ritual."
> "No, actually, mechanically, I don't think that's what… how it works."
> "I, like, that's, like, actually, I was just looking through the instructions."
> "I'm just trying to confirm it."

**[Vukradin]** — *probing for a vocal component*
> "Is there no vocal component to the, ritual?"

**[GM]** — *discovering why silence won't work*
> "Oh, I know why it hasn't happened."
> "That's interesting. Can the ritual be terminated? No, it doesn't appear to be. All right."
> "They're each dancing."

**[Vukradin]** — *acknowledging*
> "Awesome."

**[Vukradin]** — *pivoting tactics*
> "I mean, if there's no vocal component, there's whole person, that's also an option."

**[GM]** — *still investigating*
> "I'm just telling you what they're doing."
> "I have… I'm… give me a second, I, like, they literally do not account for that."
> "Okay, so, alright, I now know how the ritual works. Okay."
> "I now know what triggers the summoning. Okay. Alright."

**[Vukradin]** — *putting the decision to the group*
> "Okay, guys, do you want me to go with silence or hold person?"

---

**[GM]** — *catching a procedural gap before action*
> "Hold on, before you do anything, can someone tell me what the… did we roll initiatives? No, we did not roll initiatives."
> "Yeah, so why don't we roll initiatives?"

**[Stéphane]** — *observing the eerie silence of the dancers*
> "So they're like, what, a pantomime club?"

**[GM]** — *confirming there is nothing to hear*
> "No, no, you don't. No, you don't."

**[Initiative Roll-offs]**
- GM rolls an 18; Vukradin rolls 19 — Vukradin wins that tie.
- GM rolls again, gets 20 on a re-roll; wins against Brewbarry's 10.
- Stéphane re-rolls for Brewbarry: gets 10; GM rolls 11/12 — GM wins.
- Vukradin notes the GM won all the ties.

**[GM]** — *after winning multiple tie-breakers*
> "I gotta love it."

**[Vukradin]**
> "You won all the ties."

**[GM]**
> "Happens every so often. Alright."

**[Vukradin]** — *confirming initiative order before acting*
> "Alright, so I've not cast silence because we're following initiative order, alright?"

**[GM]**
> "Yes."
