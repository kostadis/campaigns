## ISSUE 1: Gorthok's Hit Points — Contradictory Figures in the Recap

**Location:** NPCs / Scenes (The Siege of Falcon's Hunting Lodge)

**Issue:** The NPCs section states Gorthok has "15 and 73 hit points (likely two stat blocks or a composite)," while the Scenes section says "The DM notes he has 74 hit points." These figures are inconsistent with each other within the same document. The "15 and 73" phrasing is especially unclear — 15 HP would make Gorthok trivially fragile for a climactic creature, and it may be a misattribution of the Anchorites' HP (listed as 15 each) to Gorthok.

**Evidence:** Scenes section: "The DM notes he has 74 hit points; the Anchorites have armor class 13 and 15 hit points each." NPCs section: "The DM notes his hit points are not what is shown on the Roll20 tracker; he has 15 and 73 hit points."

**Suggested Fix:** Clarify Gorthok's single correct HP total (likely 74, matching the Scenes section). The "15" figure almost certainly belongs to the Anchorites and leaked into the Gorthok entry. Correct the NPCs section to read: "The DM notes his hit points differ from the Roll20 tracker; he has 74 hit points."

## ISSUE 2: Aid Cast Twice — Contradictory Attribution Between Vukradin and Valphine

**Location:** Scenes (Negotiations at the Hunting Lodge) / Scenes (The Siege of Falcon's Hunting Lodge) / Spells

**Issue:** The Negotiations scene states "Vukradin casts Aid at second level, adding 5 hit points to the party's maximum for eight hours." The Siege scene then states "Valphine casts Aid at second level before combat begins, increasing the party's hit point maximums by 5 for eight hours." The Spells section lists Aid under its own entry without clarifying which cast is which. This creates an apparent contradiction: either both characters cast Aid (which would stack, but the recap doesn't note this), or one attribution is wrong.

**Evidence:** Negotiations scene: "Vukradin casts Aid at second level." Siege scene: "Valphine casts Aid at second level before combat begins." Aid is a Cleric/Paladin spell — Vukradin is a Bard (College of Eloquence). Bards do not have Aid on their spell list unless taken via Magical Secrets, which is not noted in Vukradin's character sheet.

**Suggested Fix:** Verify whether Vukradin has Aid via Magical Secrets. If not, the Negotiations scene attribution is wrong and Valphine cast Aid both times (or once before rest, once before combat). If Vukradin does have it, the recap should explicitly note that Aid was cast twice by different casters and clarify whether the effects stack. The Spells section entry should be split into two entries or clarified to reflect both casts.

## ISSUE 4: Sending Spell Slot Cost — Third Level Stated but Sending Is a Fifth-Level Spell

**Location:** Scenes (Planning the Next Move) / Spells (Sending)

**Issue:** Both the Planning scene and the Spells section describe Vukradin using Sending and note it costs "a third-level slot." Sending is a 5th-level spell in 5e. A 6th-level Bard has 5th-level spell slots and can cast Sending — but it costs a 5th-level slot, not a third-level slot. If Vukradin has Sending via Magical Secrets it is still cast at 5th level. The slot cost as recorded is wrong.

**Evidence:** Scenes: "Vukradin uses a Sending spell — his third-level slot." Spells: "Vukradin's player noted the spell costs a third-level slot." Standard 5e rules: Sending is a 5th-level spell.

**Suggested Fix:** Correct both references to reflect a 5th-level slot expenditure. Verify with the player/DM whether Vukradin has Sending at all (it would require Magical Secrets for a Bard, which is not noted on the sheet), and if confirmed, note it in his items/abilities.

## ISSUE 7: Orc Banners — "Three Banners on the Wall, I See One" Is Internally Contradictory

**Issue:** Prutha is quoted as saying "Eastern Heart! Three banners on the wall! I see one! Sun and Stone! My uncle's clan march!" This is internally contradictory: he says three banners but then says he sees one. This may be accurate session transcript, but as written it is ambiguous — does the lodge display three banners and Prutha only recognizes one of them? **Evidence:** Prutha's quote appears verbatim in both the Memorable Moments and NPCs sections without clarification.

**Suggested Fix:** Add a parenthetical clarification: either "(three banners visible; Prutha recognizes one as Sun and Stone)" or "(one banner visible; Prutha identifies it as Sun and Stone, his uncle's clan-mark)." Confirm with the DM which is correct for the world state.
