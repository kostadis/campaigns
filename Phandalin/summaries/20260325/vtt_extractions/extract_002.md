## Events
- The party ascends to the roof of Icespire Hold to confront the Carver mid-ritual over the chained dragon Cryovain.
- Vukradin (David) casts Silence centered on the summoning circle, disrupting the Carver's ritual.
- Combat begins; initiative is rolled. Brewbarry wins a tiebreaker with the Carver (16 vs 11).
- The GM reveals the Carver's stat block is upgraded beyond a standard veteran (more hit points).
- The GM reminds the party that Sister Kaella previously told them to "chop off the head and the monster will fall" — i.e., kill the Carver and everything collapses.
- The dragon, chained and in the summoning circle, does nothing on its turn (still bound).
- Soma rolls a 24 on animal handling and determines Cryovain is in seething psychotic rage; releasing it would cause it to attack everything indiscriminately.
- The Carver uses a legendary action to disengage and move 30 feet without provoking attacks of opportunity, repositioning out of Brewbarry's melee range.
- Vukradin casts Hold Person on the Carver's human vessel; the veteran fails his Wisdom save and is paralyzed.
- The Carver (revealed to be the sentient sword, not the human) casts Dominate on Cryovain despite the veteran being paralyzed.
- The party hears a telepathic voice: "Cowards, magic, you mute the ritual because you lack the strength to shatter the vessel. And now, now I shall have everything I always wanted."
- The magical chains on Cryovain dissolve; the dragon is now partially dominated but in homicidal madness.
- Soma rolls a 13 on Arcana and deduces that the true Carver is the sentient black obsidian sword; the human was merely a vessel. The sword was performing a ritual to consume the dragon.
- The GM explains the party has 3 rounds to kill the dragon before the sword fully dominates Cryovain.
- The obsidian sword loses its purple glow and now appears as a plain black blade (the purple energy transferred to the dragon).
- The paralyzed veteran fails to break free of Hold Person on his turn.
- Valphine casts Guiding Bolt on Cryovain, dealing 15 radiant damage and granting advantage on the next attack.
- Soma uses her spider form to climb into position, then expends her second Wild Shape charge to shift into bear form (using both Wild Shape charges for the day).
- Brewbarry equips the Dragon Slayer Sword and attacks Cryovain twice: first attack hits with advantage (21 damage including 3d6 dragon slayer bonus), second attack also hits (22 damage). Total ~43 damage.
- Cryovain retaliates with a 30-foot cone breath weapon targeting Brewbarry. Brewbarry fails the Dex save. 40 cold damage is dealt; rage does not reduce cold damage. Gary uses Stone's Endurance to reduce it to 30.
- Cryovain flies 30 feet into the air, hovering above the battlements.
- Vukradin takes cover behind a 6-foot stone wall with arrow slits. He casts Thorn Whip, rolls a natural 20 (critical hit), dealing 26 damage to the airborne dragon.
- Session ends mid-combat after round 1 of the 3-round domination window. The party marks the dragon as "1/3 dominated."

## NPC Interactions
- **The Carver (Sentient Sword):** Telepathically taunts the party after abandoning the ritual: "Cowards, magic, you mute the ritual because you lack the strength to shatter the vessel. And now, now I shall have everything I always wanted." Casts Dominate on Cryovain directly as a desperate gamble after the ritual is interrupted.
- **Cryovain (Young White Dragon):** Chained in summoning circle, in psychotic rage (per Soma's animal handling check). After chains dissolve, attacks Brewbarry with breath weapon and flies 30 feet up.
- **The Veteran (Carver's vessel):** Paralyzed by Vukradin's Hold Person. Fails to break free on his turn. Now holds only a plain black obsidian sword with no apparent magical glow.
- **Sister Kaella:** Referenced by the GM as having previously told the party to "chop off the head and the monster will fall" — advice to kill the Carver. The party confirms she was incapacitated (not killed) and left with Corbin at camp.
