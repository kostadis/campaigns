## Session Summary

# Session — 2026-04-10

## Overview
The party reached the summit of Icespire Peak and infiltrated Icespire Hold by climbing a 50-foot wall rather than taking the obvious gatehouse path. Soma scouted the rooftop in spider form and discovered the white dragon Cryovain chained in a summoning circle, with the Carver performing a dark ritual over it. The party confronted the Carver on the rooftop, disrupting his ritual with Silence and paralyzing the human vessel with Hold Person—only to discover that the true Carver is a sentient black obsidian sword, not the human wielding it. The sword abandoned the ritual and directly cast Dominate on Cryovain, dissolving the chains and beginning a three-round countdown to full domination. The session ended mid-combat after round one, with the dragon airborne, Brewbarry bloodied by a frost breath attack, and the party racing to kill Cryovain before the sword seizes total control.

## Session Events
- The party arrived at Icespire Hold (two structures joined by a stone bridge) after completing the mountain climb in a prior session without exhaustion.
- Rather than approach via the winding gatehouse path, the party chose to climb a 50-foot wall on the fortress's side. Soma wild-shaped into a Giant Wolf Spider and scaled the wall, dropping a rope for the others.
- All party members passed DC 7 Athletics/Acrobatics checks to ascend the rope.
- Soma (still in spider form) stealthily scouted the rooftop of the larger fortress building. With Vukradin's Bardic Inspiration, she achieved a 16 Stealth and avoided detection by the Carver.
- Soma rolled a 24 on Animal Handling and determined Cryovain is in psychotic rage—releasing it would cause indiscriminate attacks on everything. She failed an Arcana check to understand the binding's nature.
- The party explored the fortress interior: found a hall damaged by Cryovain smashing through walls, and a filthy room formerly occupied by orcs.
- Valphine rolled a 29 on Insight and discovered a secret door in the orc room. The party chose to ignore it and head directly to confront the Carver.
- The party ascended to the rooftop. Vukradin cast Silence on the summoning circle from 120 feet, disrupting the ritual. Combat began.
- The Carver used a legendary action to disengage and move 30 feet out of Brewbarry's melee range without provoking opportunity attacks.
- Vukradin dropped Silence (concentration swap) and cast Hold Person on the Carver's human vessel, which failed its Wisdom save and was paralyzed.
- Despite the vessel's paralysis, the Carver cast Dominate on Cryovain—revealing the true Carver is the sentient obsidian sword, not the human. The sword telepathically taunted: *"Cowards, magic, you mute the ritual because you lack the strength to shatter the vessel. And now, now I shall have everything I always wanted."*
- The magical chains dissolved. The purple glow transferred from the sword to the dragon. Soma rolled 13 Arcana and confirmed the sword is the true Carver; the human was always a vessel. The sword was performing a ritual to consume the dragon.
- **Three-round domination timer began.** The party must kill Cryovain before the sword fully dominates it.
- Valphine cast Guiding Bolt on Cryovain for 15 radiant damage, granting advantage on the next attack.
- Soma used her spider form to climb into position, then expended her second Wild Shape to shift into bear form (both charges now spent).
- Brewbarry equipped the Dragon Slayer Sword and hit Cryovain twice for approximately 43 total damage (including 3d6 dragon slayer bonus on the first hit).
- Cryovain retaliated with a 30-foot cone breath weapon targeting Brewbarry. He failed the Dex save; 40 cold damage reduced to 30 via Stone's Endurance (rage does not reduce cold damage).
- Cryovain flew 30 feet into the air, hovering above the battlements.
- Vukradin took cover behind a 6-foot stone wall with arrow slits and cast Thorn Whip—rolling a natural 20 for a critical hit dealing 26 damage to the airborne dragon.
- **Session ended after round 1 of 3 in the domination window.** Dragon marked as "1/3 dominated."
