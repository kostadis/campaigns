[Scene 5] Arrival At The Circle Of Thunder
Narrator: Soma
Focus: Soma arrives at the Circle and feels Meril's staff respond to whatever power saturates this place, making her the character whose inner world is most charged by finally standing at the threshold.

- Xanth informs Vukradin that their paths must now separate. He addresses Vukradin as "Vukradinious" — apparently his preferred form of the name — and notes he has fulfilled his end of the arrangement.

- The centaur acknowledges Soma specifically, tapping her and noting she has been chosen by Meril and is destined for greatness: "This one, this one was blessed by Meril, and I know that she will do great things. Peace, Beauty."

- Xanth takes a final look at a mysterious staff before galloping away. The DM describes him making everybody feel "a bit awkward" as he studies the staff one last time, then "hooves off." The table enjoys the verb.

- Prutha immediately proposes the party's next objective: "We go kill other Talosian clerics."

- The party approaches a 90-foot-tall hill as ominous storm clouds gather. The DM reads from prepared text: "Ominous storm clouds gather in the sky as you approach a 90-foot-tall hill with trees spreading across its slope. Atop the hill is a large ring of standing stones. Two ghastly figures dance within this henge, surrounded by a smaller number of capering creatures."

- The "smaller number of capering creatures" are identified as twelve twig blights — neutral evil plant creatures with 4 hit points each and an Intelligence of 4. The DM confirms they are not convertible.

- The party debates tactics for the approaching confrontation. The Necklace of Fireballs is raised immediately; Vukradin's player insists the party does have it ("We've got the beads!"), while Vukradin himself is reluctant to sanction its use ("it's not ours"). The twig blights are noted as not sufficiently clustered for it to be immediately effective in any case.

- Other options discussed: Thunderwave (Wade notes it could work well), Spirit Guardians plus Dash (Vukradin's player's preferred combination), and a cone-shaped ability held by Vukradin.

- The session ends with the party at the treeline studying the ritual, the Circle of Thunder waiting above them.
