# Recap and Battle Setup

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The DM recaps the previous session's events and the party prepares for the confrontation at the Circle of Thunder.
- The DM recaps Vukradin settling a debt with Adabra, the party reporting to Ser Kaelen about Sister Kaella's escape and Corbyn's death, and the Lord's Alliance representative Jenna Roscoe's failed recruitment pitch — her pitch was met with Brewbarry's thunderous burp.
- The party's journey into the wilderness with centaur Xanth is recapped, including Vukradin's speech to illegal loggers and the discovery of sinister twig dolls around a charred pine tree.
- The DM clarifies the mechanical effect of persuasive speeches mid-recap: orcs begin as hostile; a successful persuasion roll moves them to neutral (they may pause and not attack that round); further success can move them to receptive (no longer interested in fighting); a failed roll or poor delivery can flip them back. The DM emphasises: "You don't convert people because of a pleasant speech."
- The party arrives at a 60-foot gully with a ridge on the opposite side where ten orcs and an anchorite of Talos await.
- Vukradin attempts to sway the orcs with a speech about Lathander, causing some to hesitate. Three orcs — numbers 1, 5, and 6 — are moved from hostile to neutral.
- Valphine displays radiant holy energy, which enrages the anchorite and triggers an immediate attack. The DM confirms the orcs all speak Common, prompting mild exasperation from Vukradin's player, who had expended Universal Speech to address them in Orcish. The DM notes that Orcish was still the better choice for persuasion.
- Before the first round, Wade asks that Boney be made large for targeting purposes, resulting in the session's first memorable exchange.
