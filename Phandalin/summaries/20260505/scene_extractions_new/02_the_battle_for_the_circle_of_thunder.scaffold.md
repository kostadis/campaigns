[Scene 2] The Battle For The Circle Of Thunder
Narrator: Brewbarry
Focus: Brewbarry is the blade-edge of the assault, and his fierce physical engagement — Dragon Slayer sword in hand, loyalty to Vukradin driving every swing — makes him the most visceral witness to the chaos of the fight.

- Orc 2 charges Vukradin with a great axe (bonus action charge per orc stat block), rolling a 19 initially. Vukradin casts Silvery Barbs as a reaction (confirmed at table to be a 1st-level spell, not a cantrip), forcing a re-roll. The orc rolls a 9 and misses. Silvery Barbs also grants Brewbarry advantage on his next attack roll.

- The anchorite of Talos unleashes a Lightning Bolt striking Vukradin, Brewbarry, and Xanth simultaneously. Spell DC 12; total damage 29. Vukradin and Xanth both save for 14 points each. Xanth "says something profane in his local dialect." Brewbarry's saving throw result is not specified as pass or fail in the transcript, but the recap establishes he takes the full 29.

- Xanth retaliates by firing his longbow twice at the anchorite; both shots miss. The tracker reads 52 — the anchorite's HP total after Soma's ram attack, noted by the GM at this point in the initiative.

- Soma transforms into a massive giant elk (size: Large; AC 14 per Giant Elk stat block — the table played AC 17 in error throughout). She charges the anchorite, dealing 6 piercing damage on the ram, then rolling 2d6 for an additional 6 damage (total 12). The anchorite must make a DC 14 Strength save or fall prone — he fails and is knocked prone.

- Orc 3 charges and attacks Vukradin, missing. Orc 4 attacks the elk form of Soma, missing (Soma's AC 17 holds). Neutral orc 5 moves to a flanking position and throws a javelin at the elk, rolling a 17 — a hit — for 6 points of damage. Soma's hit points drop to 12.

- Orc 6 (neutral) tosses his javelin at the elk, rolling a 16 — ruled a miss at table (AC 17 was played); note the correct Giant Elk AC is 14, so a roll of 16 would have been a hit.

- Boney charges through the fray and strikes the prone anchorite. The attack roll of 14 hits (anchorite AC 13; advantage from prone applies, but the result holds regardless). Boney deals 13 points of damage using his longsword two-handed (the table notes he uses a longsword two-handed per sidekick stat block, as he cannot carry a shield). The anchorite is reduced to approximately 39 hit points remaining (tracker: 52 after Soma's attack − 13 from Boney = 39).

- Orc 7 charges toward the party, triggering a reaction attack of opportunity from Brewbarry. Brewbarry's first roll misses, but the advantage granted by Silvery Barbs applies to the re-roll; the second roll hits. Orc 7 takes 18 points of damage (including the Great Weapon Master +10, which the DM rules Brewbarry always uses by default given his character type) and is killed. Vukradin's player notes this is inconvenient for conversion efforts; Vukradin's player quips, "How are we going to win them over if you're slaughtering them?"

- Brewbarry rages and charges the prone anchorite, moving through two orcs who each take an attack of opportunity — both miss. Brewbarry attacks with his halberd; with Bless (Emboldening Bond d4) active, his first attack roll of 16 − 5 (Great Weapon Master) + 4 (Bless) = 15 hits. He deals 24 points of damage, reducing the anchorite to approximately 15 hit points. His second attack misses.

- Brewbarry's second attack on the anchorite: Brewbarry attacks again with advantage (anchorite is prone). He rolls an 18, which hits. The anchorite is killed. Vukradin: "Alright!"

- Orc 9 attacks Boney; Brewbarry uses his reaction to intercept, taking 9 points of damage (halved to 4 while raging). Orc 10 attacks Valphine with a javelin, missing spectacularly. Orc 11 attacks the elk with a javelin, hitting for an unspecified amount.

- Orc 2 demands money from Vukradin: "Give me money and I let you live." Vukradin declines, stating that followers of Lathander do not make such demands. The orc swings his axe and hits for 9 points of damage. The DM observes: "He's like, I don't want to kill you, man, I just want the money."

- Xanth fires his longbow twice more; both shots miss. The DM remarks: "Xanth sucks."

- Soma moves away from orcs 4 and 8, triggering attacks of opportunity from her movement (one hits for 9 points); she then drops her elk form as a bonus action (dropping wild shape does not itself provoke opportunity attacks — the OA were triggered by her movement). The temporary hit points from elk form are lost on transformation in any case. She mounts Boney and rides him away from the front lines. Orc 9 takes an attack of opportunity on Boney as he moves — a miss.
