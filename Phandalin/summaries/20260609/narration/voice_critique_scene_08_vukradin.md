> "This is like a Nigerian prince scam, I told them: there's all this money in the mine, but first you need to buy me a gem."

**Why:** "Nigerian prince scam" is a specific 21st-century internet fraud reference with no equivalent in the setting. The line is in Vukradin's narration (not locked dialogue), so it can be changed.
