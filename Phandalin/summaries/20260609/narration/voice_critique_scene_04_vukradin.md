## Flags

### [1] Register-wrong vocabulary — `shapes`

> "The little bundles of dried sticks had been bound and bent into shapes: a tall broad one, a hunched one with a staff, a slender one."

**Why:** "shapes" is geometric/abstract. Vukradin sees ensembles and individuals, not geometric categories. His natural word for recognizable human outlines is "figures" or "likenesses." The examples consistently prefer concrete, specific nouns.
**Suggested rewrite:** "The little bundles of dried sticks had been bound and bent into figures: a tall broad one, a hunched one with a staff, a slender one."

### [2] Structural — comma splice from em-dash removal

> "He was the only one of us who'd ever kept his head clear in front of these things, when the forest effigies had clawed at our minds, he alone had held, citing the wise women of his village, who taught him never to lay a hand on a cursed artifact."

**Why:** "he alone had held" is an independent clause fused by comma to the preceding dependent clause, creating a run-on. Vukradin's long sentences should build stacked subordinate clauses toward a verdict; this one accumulates independent clauses without landing. The tangling was left by em-dash removal in pass 1.
**Suggested rewrite:** "He was the only one of us who'd ever kept his head clear in front of these things. When the forest effigies had clawed at our minds, he alone had held, because of the wise women of his village: never lay a hand on a cursed artifact."

## Verdict

Scene is strong overall after first-pass fixes — "They knew we were coming," "Got twinkle, man," and "the first I'd answer for" are all well-calibrated Vukradin. One vocabulary substitution and one structural tightening needed.
