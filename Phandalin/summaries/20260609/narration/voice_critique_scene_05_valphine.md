## Flags

### [1] Register-wrong vocabulary — `dimension`

> "A new dimension of him."

**Why:** "dimension" is modern-casual and geometric; Valphine's vocabulary is elevated, aristocratic, and precise. The surrounding observations — "The rage and the satin, the red-glowing eyes over a collar of fine cloth" — are sharp and specific. "Dimension" flattens the thought.
**Suggested rewrite:** "A new facet of him, previously unlisted."

### [2] Voice spec conflict — too soft for Valphine

> "I almost told him: the things you cannot see are not hiding. They are simply kinder than the light you carry."

**Why:** "kinder than the light you carry" is a comforting, warm-register verdict. Valphine's spec explicitly forbids "warmth" language around light and does not allow her to offer consolation to Vukradin's self-doubt. Her read of him is tactical — she sees his sharpness deficit as a power-miscalibration, not a kindness gap. The spec: "Do not make Valphine sound sentimental, confessional, or soft."
**Suggested rewrite:** "I almost told him: the things you cannot see are not hiding. They are simply operating at a level those eyes cannot resolve."

## Verdict

Scene is strong after first-pass fixes — the Zone of Truth sequence, "spend his devotion cheaply," and "Two thieves in satin, each blaming the linen closet" are excellent Valphine. Two flags: one modern vocabulary word and one line that tips into consolation rather than analysis.
