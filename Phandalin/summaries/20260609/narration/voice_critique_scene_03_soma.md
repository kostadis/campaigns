## Flags

### [1] Voice spec conflict — conclusion buried at end of opening sentence

> "Vukradin stood over the conical little room with its cast-iron tub on clawed feet and the rusty pump still drawing hot spring water up from somewhere under the floor, and his voice caught before he finished asking."

**Why:** Soma's spec: "Conclusion before explanation. Short, practical sentences." This sentence runs through a full inventory of the room before landing on the emotional beat ("his voice caught"), which buries the lead. The examples open with verdicts or action: "Vukradin halts us," "My eyes scanned," "This spider's body is fun." Soma would lead with the thing that mattered.
**Suggested rewrite:** "His voice caught before he finished asking. Cast-iron tub on clawed feet, rusty pump, hot spring water from somewhere under the floor. That was the whole room."
