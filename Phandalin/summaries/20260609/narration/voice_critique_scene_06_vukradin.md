## Flags

### [1] Register-wrong vocabulary — `angle`

> "Then he turned fully to me, and his voice took on the particular angle of a man asking a favor he believes you can grant."

**Why:** "angle" is geometric vocabulary. Vukradin reads voice through music register — phrasing, cadence, tone. His spec says music is his "native organizing metaphor." A bard would name what he heard in Falcon's voice with a musical or performative word, not a spatial one.
**Suggested rewrite:** "Then he turned fully to me, and his voice took on the particular register of a man asking a favor he believes you can grant."

### [2] Anachronistic vocabulary

> "And the Falcon is offering free room and board to two retirees with cardboard signs."

**Why:** "Cardboard" is a modern industrial material that doesn't exist in this setting. Falcon's own word for what Rimardo and Corrin carried was "placards" — Vukradin just heard him say it. Using Falcon's word here (with Vukradin's edge) is more natural and setting-consistent.
**Suggested rewrite:** "And the Falcon is offering free room and board to two retirees with placards."

## Verdict

Scene is otherwise strong — the wounded professional pride and ledger-reflex are well-calibrated, and "She does that the way other people loosen a collar" is excellent Vukradin observation. Two vocabulary fixes this pass.
