# The Twig Blight Ambush

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party discovers cursed twig figures in the dining hall that animate into living blights when disturbed.
- The party enters a room whose walls are covered in crude drawings, painted in blood, of boars chasing stick-figure humanoids; a smashed dining table and six broken chairs lie heaped in the eastern half.
- They find small twig figures on the mantelpiece, each representing one of the party members — "They knew we were coming."
- Brewbarry warns the group not to touch the figures, recalling that he alone passed his Wisdom save against the similar cursed effigies the party encountered in the forest; he learned from "the wise women of his village" never to touch cursed artifacts.
- Vukradin needles Valphine for apparently missing that session: "We've learned this. Maybe you missed that session, Gary."
- Valphine, reasoning that leaving them for "some innocent to encounter" would be irresponsible, casts Sacred Flame to destroy her own twig figure from a safe distance, causing the remaining figures to animate as twig blights.
- Vukradin quickly dispatches one of the blights with Starry Wisp (radiant damage).
- Valphine releases a pulse of Word of Radiance, incinerating the remaining twig blights (4 hit points each) as they attempt to attack.
- The players acknowledge being "slightly OP for this dungeon."
