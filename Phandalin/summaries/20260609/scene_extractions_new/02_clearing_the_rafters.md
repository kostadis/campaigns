# Clearing the Rafters

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party finishes off the last of the stirges hiding in the upper rafters of the Woodland Manse.
- Valphine holds her area-of-effect spell, moves underneath the hidden stirges, and allows them to drop onto her before releasing a burst of radiant light to incinerate them.
- Prutha, the half-orc follower, celebrates the display of power as a divine sign, thumping his chest and proclaiming "behold the power of the sun" — taking his recent religious conversion very seriously.
- The party explores the upper balcony and a circular room containing a cast iron bathtub with clawed feet, fed by a rusty but functional iron water pump drawing from a hot spring beneath the house ("Cannonballed!").
- Vukradin marvels at the warm water — "How long has it been since I had a warm bath?"
