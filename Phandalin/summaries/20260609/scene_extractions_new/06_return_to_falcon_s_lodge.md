# Return to Falcon's Lodge

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party returns to Falcon's Lodge to report their success at the Woodland Manse and prepare for their next journey.
- The party arrives back the next day and finds the orcs settled into their new roles, with the Falcon having dressed three orcs in livery as "employees of the year."
- Vorga greets them — "You return!" — and the Falcon eagerly asks what they can tell him.
- Valphine reports she cleared the Woodland Manse, with Vukradin coaching her to phrase it as "the glory and power of Lathander has saved" the place from "all of the heathens."
- Falcon gushes about Rimardo and Corin: "they may have been the finest act I've seen north of Neverwinter. The timing, the little placards, if I could get them on my stage two nights a 10-day, I'd charge water-deep prices, and they'd pay them gratefully." He praises "the silent guy and the tall guy, the sarcasm."
- Vukradin denies any connection to the bards: "I'd never seen those people below it before in my life. Never came to see my act, why am I going to see theirs?"
- Falcon gives Vukradin an unsealed note addressed "to the extraordinary comedy act of Rimardo and Corrin," offering free room and board, a generous cut of proceeds, and excellent hunting in exchange for performing at his tavern. Vukradin files it in his "plot items list."
- Vorga, rolling her eyes at the Falcon, reminds the party of their offer to escort her tribe back to their former quarters at Icepire Hold.
- Wade notes the party had a "clock" to get to Neverwinter, which the DM confirms they have long since blown through — though they're still going, to "a very different setup."
