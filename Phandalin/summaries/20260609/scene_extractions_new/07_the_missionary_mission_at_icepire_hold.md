# The Missionary Mission at Icepire Hold

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### Upon reaching Icepire Hold, Valphine receives a divine revelation and sets a new religious movement in motion among the orcs.
- The two-day journey is filled with songs, eating, and blood; the orcs lead the party up a path that avoids the stone giants — though the party had earlier insisted on following the orcs' route rather than the leisurely path.
- Valphine speaks to Prutha, claiming a vision from Lathander and giving him new instructions to convert more orcs in the Ice Bear Mountains to Lathander's cause.
- Valphine experiences a divine vision from Lathander, and her eyes permanently transform into a blinding, molten golden hue of the midday sun (the DM sending her two new unlocked abilities privately via Discord).
- Valphine names her new order "Lathander's Searing Light," a divine arm meant to enforce Lathander's will — refined, at the DM's prompting, into a mandate to fight "aberrations and evil." Lathander embraces her concept of continuous power, wanting her to lead a militant holy order.
- The DM warns the new order will cause conflict with conventional Lathanderites.
- Inspired by Valphine's glowing transformation, five more orcs from the tribe join Prutha's holy mission — and immediately ask "where do we pillage?", reframed as a missionary crusade.
- Inspired by Valphine's transformation, Prutha and five orcs resolve to go on a missionary crusade; seeing their fervor, Valphine instructs them to convert the other orc tribes; Vukradin reacts with alarm at unleashing "religious maniacs" on the mountains.
- The party departs Icepire Hold to head back toward Phandalin via the mine.
