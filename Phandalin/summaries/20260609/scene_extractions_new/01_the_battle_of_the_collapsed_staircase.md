# The Battle of the Collapsed Staircase

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party deals with a collapsed staircase and an infestation of blood-sucking stirges in the Woodland Manse.
- Soma climbs through a hole to the upper level while Brewbarry and Vukradin remain at the bottom of a collapsed staircase.
- Brewbarry recovers from being knocked prone (having been "stabbed with a bunch of stakes" when the stairs collapsed — "Whatever, toothpicks") and enters a powerful rage, assuming his giant form.
- The collapsed staircase is eight feet tall; the DM rules Brewbarry could simply rage-jump it and could even lift Vukradin up, though Vukradin waves him off: "It's okay, you can leave me. Go kill some stirges."
- Three stirges descend from the rafters and latch onto Soma, beginning to drain his blood.
- Brewbarry leaps up the eight-foot height of the collapsed stairs to join the fray.
- Brewbarry attacks the stirges clinging to Soma, successfully snapping one in half and vaporizing another with a powerful cleaving blow — described by the DM as splattering "into a thousand pieces."
- More stirges descend from the rafters, latching onto Brewbarry, who shrugs off the bite: "It tickles." His rage grants resistance to the piercing damage by default, halving 5 points to 2.
- Valphine and Prutha move toward the hole in the floor to assist their companions; Prutha cries "Blessed be the light!"
- Vukradin uses magical mockery to distract a stirge, forcing a Wisdom save; when it succeeds, he burns Silvery Barbs (DC 15) to make it re-roll, and it dies.
- Soma, in bear form, bites a stirge causing it to explode "into a thousand gruesome little pieces," then uses claws (and Primal Strike) to swipe at the remaining pests on Brewbarry.
- Vukradin is ambushed by three stirges dropping from the ceiling, taking 5 points of damage as one latches on.
- Brewbarry continues his assault, systematically exterminating the remaining stirges with precise, powerful strikes, his Cleave letting him kill two at once.
- The players note the encounter is trivially easy because they killed most of the manse's defenders in the earlier pitched battle — "We're left with a very underleveled encounter." The DM: "Yes, this is what happens when you make choices… the world does change based on your choices."
