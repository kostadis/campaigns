# Looting and the Zone of Truth

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### After clearing the manse, Valphine uses magic to interrogate the party about items they may have taken from the premises.
- While Vukradin searches the kitchen, Prutha discreetly lifts a candelabra worth 25 gold pieces from the dining room and slips it to Valphine, whispering for her not to say anything (the DM rolling stealth to keep it from Vukradin).
- Vukradin, frustrated at being constantly outflanked, declares he wants feats for better perception — "like, see invisible or something."
- Valphine casts Zone of Truth, gathering everyone into a 15-foot radius to compel the party to reveal any items taken from the Woodland Manse or Falcon's Lodge.
- Prutha is forced to admit he took something and begins producing rocks from his pockets, being evasive (passing his Charisma save) before walking outside the 15-foot radius of the zone.
- Brewbarry admits to taking a soft satin bathrobe, slippers — and, Soma suggests, the "sample shampoos" — from the lodge: "Yes, I'm a simple shit. I like my hair to be soft."
- Soma is revealed to have taken the Staff of Bird Calls.
- Valphine admits to finding the Dust of Disappearance hidden within the Elvish Idioms book.
- It emerges Prutha also took a bathrobe — which he insists came from the Falcon, not the manse, the same source as Brewbarry's towels.
- The party debates the ethics of keeping the items versus returning them to the rightful owners; Vukradin escalates from returning items to returning "the entire building to its rightful owner."
