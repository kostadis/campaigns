# Secrets of the Scholar's House

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party searches the master bedroom and library, discovering hidden treasures and a secret passage.
- Vukradin discovers a secret door by comparing the floor plans of the manse — "compare the first and second floor maps." The DM notes "a lot of people are not capable of doing that," to which Soma replies they should be judged.
- The master bedroom contains a four-poster bed, a desk, an empty wardrobe, and a fireplace with an elegant mantelpiece; a handsome billowing cloak hangs on a hook and a clawed-foot wooden chest sits against the wall.
- Soma finds a Staff of Bird Calls hidden in the room and discreetly stows it deep in his bear fur without telling the others.
- The party debates whether the manse belongs to the Falcon or the original scholar; the DM reveals the scholar who built it studied elven ruins, had no next of kin, and the orcs had merely been planning to attack the Falcon — the Falcon only wanted the assault sabotaged.
- Valphine explores a library with a 10-foot wooden balcony, a spiral staircase, and tall bowed-and-broken bookcases. Among the moldy books she finds one undamaged volume titled "Elvish Idioms," which conceals a tiny leather packet of Dust of Disappearance.
