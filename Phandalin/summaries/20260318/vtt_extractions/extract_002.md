## Events
- Sister Kaella warns the party they must reach Ice Spire Hold before the Carver completes his ritual to possess the white dragon.
- The party debates travel options: going directly to Ice Spire Hold (1 level of exhaustion), going through Phandalin first (2 levels of exhaustion), or taking zero exhaustion but risking arriving late.
- Xanth the Centaur offers a third option: he will carry party members partway to the mountains (near the gold mine area), eliminating the exhaustion penalty entirely.
- Vukradin persuades Xanth to help (rolled 17 Persuasion); Xanth agrees to carry them to the foothills but refuses to enter the mountains due to his hooves being unsuited for climbing.
- The party devises a multi-mount travel strategy: Xanth carries Vukradin and Valphine, Soma wild shapes into a bear to carry another rider, and Boney the skeletal horse carries the remaining party member.
- Sister Kaella is left in Corbin's custody; Corbin expresses dark enthusiasm about guarding her, asking if he can beat her.
- Valphine rolls a 25 Insight check and recognizes that Corbin and Kaella have a personal history — Corbin intends to make her suffer. The party tacitly accepts this arrangement.
- Sister Kaella screams and curses at the party for leaving her with Corbin, calling him a lunatic and madman.
- The party takes a long rest before departing.
- Upon approaching the mountains, Soma senses a disturbance in the natural order.
- The party discovers the Carver's trail: footprints in a perfectly straight line with robotic rhythm, going over obstacles rather than around them. Footprints are confirmed human.
- The party passes a series of skill checks to navigate treacherous mountain paths: Vukradin rolls 20 on Survival, Brewbarry succeeds on Athletics, Valphine rolls 23 on Perception; Nature check fails but 3/6 successes achieved.
- At the Narrow Defile, the party encounters stone giants murmuring about rocks being "off."
- Brewbarry and Valphine understand Giant language and communicate with the stone giants.
- The stone giants describe the Carver as "the little one with the obsidian sword" and confirm he passed through alone.
- The stone giants explain their philosophy of non-intervention, referencing Hekaton's teachings and the failure of giants who sided with Tiamat during the breaking of the Ordning.
- Soma touches Meryl's staff to the displaced rocks and discovers psychic energy has warped reality — rocks are phasing in and out of existence along the Carver's path.
- The party shows the phasing rocks to the stone giants; the giants acknowledge the issue but maintain their non-interventionist stance, though they gift the party a Potion of Greater Healing and a Potion of Hill Giant Strength.
- The stone giants confirm the Carver was alone (no companions).
- The party reaches the Glacier of Whispers: ice stained with violet energy, frozen faces pressing against the ice from within with mouths moving in silent screams, and the Carver's footprints glowing with psychic energy.
- Whispers fill the party's minds: "He walks without rest, without breath, without soul."
- Valphine perceives a 22 on Perception to navigate dangers; Nature check fails (1 success, 1 failure on glacier checks).
- The party must make a group Wisdom save against psychic resonance; all pass successfully.
- Valphine recalls that Lathander's blessing can grant advantage on psychic saves in this area (not needed since they passed).
- The party finds the frozen corpse of a young white drake with eyes burned out by necrotic energy — killed by the Carver who walked straight through its lair.
- The mother drake circles above, enraged and confused.
- Vukradin plays calming music and Brewbarry joins in; the mother drake calms down and lies next to her dead young, allowing the party to slip past without combat.
- The party reaches impassable terrain of massive boulders and ice blocking the path.
- Soma uses Mold Earth to carve a passage through the terrain, using a Survival check (DC 15) justified by the structural/construction nature of the task.
- Vukradin provides Bardic Inspiration to Soma.
- Session ends on Soma's roll to carve the path.

## NPC Interactions
- **Sister Kaella**: Delivers final warning that the party must reach Ice Spire Hold before the ritual is complete. Screams and curses when she learns she'll be left with Corbin, calling him a lunatic.
- **Xanth (Xanthopoulos)**: Offers to carry party members partway to the mountains (to the area near the gold mine). Refuses to enter the mountains — his hooves are not suited for climbing. Persuaded by Vukradin's 17 Persuasion roll.
- **Corbin**: Eagerly accepts guard duty over Sister Kaella, asking "Can I beat her?" Valphine's Insight (25) reveals he and Kaella have a troubled history and he intends to make her suffer.
- **Stone Giants**: Encountered in a narrow mountain pass. Describe the Carver as "the little one with the obsidian sword." Confirm he passed through alone. Espouse non-interventionist philosophy citing Hekaton's teachings and the failure of giants during the Ordning crisis. Gift the party a Potion of Greater Healing and a Potion of Hill Giant Strength. One giant launches into a long sermon about giant history that bores the party.
- **Mother Drake**: Enraged white drake circling above her dead young. Calmed by Vukradin and Brewbarry's music. Lies down next to her dead offspring, allowing the party to pass.

## PC Actions & Decisions
- **Vukradin**: Rolls 17 Persuasion to convince Xanth to carry the party partway. Rolls a poor Insight check but is reminded of political risks of returning to Phandalin for help. Decides to leave Kaella with Corbin rather than bring her along. Rolls 20 on Survival to navigate mountain paths. Rolls 15 on Nature check about stone giants (learns they are "colossal introverts"). Plays calming music to soothe the mother drake (succeeds). Provides Bardic Inspiration to Soma for the Mold Earth check.
- **Valphine**: Rolls 25 Insight on Corbin/Kaella dynamic, recognizing their history and Corbin's intent. Rolls 4 on Nature check (fails). Rolls 23 on Perception navigating mountain paths. Rolls 22 Perception on Glacier of Whispers navigation. Recalls Lathander's blessing can protect against psychic effects in the glacier area.
- **Brewbarry**: Succeeds on Athletics check during mountain navigation. Speaks Giant language and communicates with stone giants on behalf of the party. Joins Vukradin in playing music to calm the mother drake.
- **Soma**: Senses disturbance in natural order at the mountain base. Touches Meryl's staff to displaced rocks, revealing psychic warping/phasing caused by the Carver's passage. Rolls Nature check with advantage (fails to beat other rolls). Uses Mold Earth cantrip to carve a path through impassable boulder/ice terrain, rolling Survival (DC 15) — result pending at session end.
- **Party collectively**: Decides not to return to Phandalin (to avoid exhaustion and political complications). Decides not to fight the mother drake. Takes a long rest before departing. Speculates the obsidian sword may be a level-draining weapon.
