# Session — 2026-03-26

## Overview
In the aftermath of the battle at Dragonbarrow, the party healed the wounded centaur Xanth, interrogated the captured Sister Kaella, and learned that the Carver is marching alone on Icespire Hold to perform a ritual to possess the white dragon. Armed with directions to the Hold and a multi-mount travel strategy devised with Xanth's help, the party left Kaella in Corbin's custody and set out for the Ice Spire Mountains. They tracked the Carver's unnervingly straight, reality-warping trail through a narrow defile where stone giants gifted them potions, across the psychically haunted Glacier of Whispers, and past a grieving mother drake whose young the Carver had killed in passing. The session ended with Soma attempting to carve a path through impassable boulders near the summit using Mold Earth, with Vukradin's bardic inspiration steadying her work.
