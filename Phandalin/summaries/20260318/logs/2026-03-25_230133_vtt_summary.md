## Session Summary

# Session — 2026-03-25

## Overview

With Jax and Rot-Tusk Ogre dead and Sister Kaella unconscious in their custody, the party regrouped at the clearing outside Dragonbarrow and turned their attention to the race against the Carver. After healing Xanth the centaur and extracting critical intelligence from Kaella under interrogation, the party closed the key information gap from the previous session: they now know the exact location of Icespire Hold. Rather than escorting Kaella to Phandalin — which would cost precious time — they left her bound and at 1 HP in Corbin's custody, took a long rest, and set out for the Sword Mountains via a multi-mount formation. The journey took them through increasingly hostile terrain: warped rocks phasing in and out of reality, the violet-stained Glacier of Whispers laced with psychic echoes, the necrotic-burned remains of young white drakes, and a grieving mother drake calmed only by Vukradin's music. The session ended mid-skill-check as Soma attempted to use Mold Earth to carve a path through boulder-choked terrain in the final approach to Icespire Hold.

## Voice Keeper Notes

**Xanth** — Verbose and patrician; always uses the full formal name before switching to the practical one. Praise is genuine but condescending. His ethics are utilitarian and stated without apology. He will reference the Emerald Enclave and Order of the Gauntlet as institutions the party should know better than they do. Drop "my friend" before criticisms.

**Sister Kaella** — Never stops negotiating. Her default register is reasonable adult conversation. She reads her interlocutors accurately and adapts her pitch to them — she already knows Vukradin won't be persuaded by pragmatism, so she stopped trying. Future encounters should have her working the most receptive party member, not the most powerful one. Her preference for Axeholm over Phandalin as a prison location is worth remembering — she may have a reason.

**Stone Giants** — Speak in short declarative sentences. "Little ones" is neutral, not contemptuous. Their neutrality is a philosophical position, not laziness — reference Hekaton and the War of Giants when invoking it. They do not explain themselves unless directly asked, and even then only once.

**Corbin** — Pure id in service of the party. Checks for permission before acting; genuinely cheerful when it's granted. The Lathander "sacraments" Valphine mentioned should be treated as real — he has been educated in something.

**PC emotional states for NPC dialogue:**
- Vukradin is operating under moral self-compression — he's made several decisions this session that sit uneasily with his stated code, and he's papering over them with busyness. NPCs who notice the gap between his principles and his recent actions will get under his skin.
- Soma is steady but watching. She's the conscience of the room and knows it. NPCs who appeal to long-term consequences over short-term convenience will find her receptive.
- Valphine's easy delivery of "I've been teaching him some of our sacraments" suggests she is more comfortable with morally ambiguous arrangements than her Lathander conversion might imply. NPCs from her drow past should treat her current faith as a costume until proven otherwise.

**Vows and threats to track:**
- Vukradin promised Kaella prison in Phandalin (or Axeholm). This is now on the record.
- "Until she has one hit point" — Corbin has standing permission for violence against Kaella within that limit.
- The party chose not to fight the grieving mother Drake. If she appears again, she was spared — that decision should have weight.
