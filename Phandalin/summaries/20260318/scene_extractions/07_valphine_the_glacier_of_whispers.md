**Arriving at the Glacier of Whispers**
The party reached the Glacier of Whispers, and the sight of it was unlike anything Valphine had encountered before. The ice was stained a deep, unsettling violet, and frozen faces pressed against it from within, mouths open in silent, endless screams.
The world felt wrong in a way that went beyond cold — something psychic and ancient had soaked into the ice itself.

---

**The Carver's Footprints**
The Carver's footprints crossed the glacier's surface, each one glowing with faint psychic energy, following that same inhuman straight line they had traced since the foothills — over rock, over ice, never around.
Whispers crept into Valphine's mind, unbidden and clear: *He walks without rest, without breath, without soul.*

---

**Lathander's Blessing Surfaces**
GM: "Valphine notices that she can pray for Lathander's blessing to grant saves against psychic effects for 10 minutes."
Valphine: "This is just… some new thing that I can do?"
GM: "You remember something about how your local priestly clerics had told you about this area, that Lathander particularly… You didn't pay that much attention to it, you figured it was all nonsense."
Something half-dismissed from her priestly studies returned to her now, here on a haunted glacier, and she understood for the first time that her teachers had not been speaking in metaphor.

---

**Pushing Through the Psychic Resonance**
The party made their Wisdom saves against the glacier's psychic resonance, holding their minds intact against the pressure of the whispers. Valphine held the knowledge of Lathander's blessing in reserve — a ward she could raise if the glacier broke through — but the party passed without needing it.
She emerged from the other side shaken but unbroken, the silent screaming faces receding behind her in the violet ice.
