**Soma Heals the Centaur**
Soma stepped forward and called upon divine magic, casting Cure Wounds on the wounded centaur — mending 12 HP worth of injuries and bringing him fully upright.
The creature rose to his full, impressive height, and for a moment the chaos of battle felt far behind them.
