**The Wall of Stone and Ice**
Soma studied the terrain ahead — massive boulders and ice packed tight across the path, no way through and no way around. She searched for purchase, looking for seams in the rock, loose soil, anything that could be worked. She found just enough: a thread of earth and stone beneath the ice that her magic could reach.

**Beginning the Work**
Soma explained her approach: "I'm trying to carve a structure out of it… use Mold Earth to pull things around to move the stones aside." She pressed her awareness into the frozen ground and began the slow, painstaking labor of shifting stone, coaxing the mountain to open rather than forcing it.

**Bardic Inspiration Offered**
Vukradin called out that he had given her bardic inspiration to steady the work. Soma's reply came back with easy confidence: "Well, I don't need it. I got it on its own." The die had been cast. Whether the path would hold — and what waited beyond it — the mountain had not yet answered.
