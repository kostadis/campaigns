**Reviving Sister Kaella**
Valphine crouched over the bound, unconscious form of Sister Kaella and applied her medical knowledge to bring her back to a conscious state — enough to question, not enough to be dangerous. The medicine check came in at 15, sufficient to pull Kaella up from the dark.

**The First Words**
Valphine: "Hello, Sister Kayla."
Sister Kaella: "Thank God you're alive!"
Vukradin: "You're the one who should be lucky he's alive."
Sister Kaella: "Oh, no, I… there was the other possibility. Jax swings, and I'm alive, and Jax is now going to torture me to death for several, you know, painfully long, protracted periods."
Valphine: "We can still do that. If you'd like."
Sister Kaella: "Yes, but it's an if. Whereas with Jax, it was a certainty, so now there's at least a…"
Valphine: "Or if I'd like."
The intimidation roll came in at 16 — and it landed.

**Kaella's Surprise**
Sister Kaella: "I didn't know you had it in you."
The admission came without sarcasm. She was genuinely impressed.

**Pressing for Information**
Valphine: "So now you need to take… you need to tell us more about this dragon. More about the Carver's plan."
Sister Kaella: "The Carver? Oh, man. The Carver, he's, like, gone completely mad. He's heading towards the dragon to possess it and wreak vengeance on everyone and everything."
Vukradin: "Would that be bad? Like, who is he gonna seek vengeance on?"
Soma: "Possibly us for, you know, knocking off Borg and others, right?"
Sister Kaella: "I mean… well, on a deeply personal level, me. I was kind of hoping that my plan wasn't to wait until there was the right moment to strike, but then you guys had to go get the sword."

**Kaella's Confession**
Vukradin: "So you're telling me… you were working against the Carver the whole time."
Sister Kaella: "Well… strictly speaking, after you killed the muscle, and the Carver accused me of not doing my work from an intelligence-gathering perspective, I kind of thought that if the Carver died, my life expectancy would increase."

**Why Valphine**
Vukradin: "Why didn't you… why weren't you just up front with us? You had to do this whole, like, secretive thing with Valphine…"
Sister Kaella: "Well, she was the only one who showed up. I don't know, like, you guys… she was the one who showed up, the rest of you didn't show up."
Sister Kaella: "And Vukradin… I had noticed that you had a difficulty in dealing with morally gray environments."
Sister Kaella: "And so, to be honest, I figured that a drow cleric of Lathander, who understands the importance of darkness and light, and all the…"
Vukradin cut her off before she could finish.

**The Arrangement Acknowledged**
Sister Kaella: "And Valphine, Valphine, we had an arrangement."
Valphine: "Yes, we have an arrangement."
Sister Kaella: "Right, and so I knew I could trust your arrangement and Soma's, you know, desire… lack of interest…"
Soma: "Value of life."

**Directions and the Warning**
Vukradin: "Do you know exactly where the dragon is?"
Sister Kaella: "Oh yes, he's in the Icepire Hold!"
Vukradin: "Can you point that out to us on a map?"
Sister Kaella: "Yes."
Vukradin: "And what will you be doing while we go and take care of this problem for you?"
Sister Kaella: "You want me to be in prison? I'll go be in prison."
Sister Kaella: "I mean, you want me… do you want me to come with you? I'm happy to come with you, if you want me to. If you wanted to put me in prison in Phandalin, I'm happy to be in prison in Phandalin. In fact, if you wanted to put me in prison in Axholm, that would even be better."
Sister Kaella: "But there's a small problem with all of that. The small problem is that, unfortunately, the Carver's also headed to Icepire Hold."

**Corbin Takes Custody**
When the party handed Kaella over to Corbin's keeping, Valphine watched the exchange closely — Insight check landing at 25. What she saw in Corbin's eyes told her everything: there was a history between these two, something personal and unresolved, and whatever Corbin had in mind for the duration of his watch was not gentle. Kaella's screaming and cursing as the party departed confirmed it. Valphine said nothing. Some things, she decided, were better left unasked.
