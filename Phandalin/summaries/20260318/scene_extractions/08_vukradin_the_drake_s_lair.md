**Finding the Dead Drake**
The party came upon the frozen corpse of a young white drake in the mountain pass. Its eyes had been burned out with necrotic energy — the Carver had not gone around the creature's lair. He had walked straight through it, killing the young drake without breaking stride.
The violation of it settled coldly over everything.

**The Mother Drake Circles**
Above, the mother drake circled in confused, furious grief, her cries echoing off the stone. She was enraged and disoriented, searching for the thing that had done this, unable to make sense of what she had come home to.

**Choosing Not to Fight**
Vukradin: "We don't need to fight her."
Vukradin: "Save it… save it for the real guy."
Wade (Soma): "Poor Mother Drake. It was one of your kids that got, you know…"
Vukradin: "Mothers are resilient."
Wade (Soma): "We are."
The decision was practical, but there was something else in it — a recognition that this creature had already lost enough.

**Playing for the Drake**
Vukradin raised his instrument and began to play — something slow and deliberate, a Moonlight Sonata cadence, a melody that did not try to fix the grief but simply acknowledged it. Brewbarry heard it and joined in without being asked.

Wade (Brewbarry): "Brewbarry would have joined in, right? He loves this shit."
David (Vukradin): "Classical, you know, we'll just do some, you know, Moonlight Sonata here."

Together they filled the cold mountain air with music, two unlikely figures standing over a dead young drake while the mother circled above them.

**The Mother Drake Descends**
The circling slowed. The drake descended. She lay down beside her dead young and let the party pass.
The music had not brought the young drake back, and it had not erased what the Carver had done — but it had reached her, and for now that was enough.
