# Session — 2026-04-10

## Overview
The party completed their assault on Icespire Hold, slaying Cryovain the white dragon with Valphine's Sacred Flame after Brewbarry weathered two blasts of freezing breath on 6 hit points and Vukradin converted a miss into a telling blow via Bardic Inspiration. The Carver's essence dissipated the instant the dragon died. A brief moral debate followed over the fate of the Carver's paralyzed bandit-vessel; Soma argued for mercy, Brewbarry killed him anyway. The party looted a 3,000 gp treasure slag fused into residium-infused ice by planar trickery (all identifying marks destroyed) and recovered the Carver's obsidian sword, whose pommel crystal is a mental amplifier the party immediately dubbed "the Bluetooth Sword." Before the party could rest, Sylvine Wintermoon and her sister Runa arrived claiming the slag as their own; when Vukradin refused and accused them of being thieves, they attacked with their veteran guards. The skirmish ended with two veterans killed (paralyzed by Hold Person, finished by Brewbarry), one fled citing better career prospects, and Sylvine and Runa's fate unresolved in the notes. Vukradin sent word to Ser Kaelen; Kaelen replied with an urgent summons to Phandalin, citing problems with the Emerald Enclave. The party set course for Phandalin.

## Session Events

**Dragon Fight — Cryovain and the Carver**
- Combat opened with Cryovain airborne at 30 feet, partially possessed; ~2 rounds before domination became irreversible.
- Valphine's upcast Guiding Bolt missed; Bardic Inspiration failed to salvage the roll.
- Soma placed a 3rd-level Moonbeam (40-ft cylinder) on the dragon — 8 radiant damage.
- Brewbarry activated his Large form (bonus action, once per long rest), extended reach, and struck Cryovain twice with his halberd for ~23+ damage total (Dragon Slayer Sword not usable at that elevation).
- The Carver's vessel (paralyzed Veteran) rolled a 6 on his Hold Person save — remained paralyzed.
- Cryovain used breath weapon on Brewbarry: 45 cold damage; Brewbarry succeeded on Con save, took only 14 (halved). Survived on 6 HP. Stone's Endurance considered but inapplicable to this damage type.
- Vukradin used Bardic Inspiration on his own attack roll (rolled 13), converted to a hit, dealt 12 damage — reducing Cryovain to 2 HP.
- Valphine cast Sacred Flame; dragon rolled a 3 on the Dex save, took lethal damage. **Cryovain dies.**
- The Carver's essence dissipates entirely on the dragon's death.

**The Veteran's Fate**
- Soma (bear form) struck the paralyzed Veteran with bear claws — automatic critical, 26 damage; then moved Moonbeam onto him for 17 more radiant damage (failed Con save). Veteran reduced well below zero threshold.
- Party debated: Soma argued for non-lethal/mercy; Brewbarry overruled and killed the Veteran with two halberd crits (advantage from paralysis; ~22 and 28+ damage).

**Short Rest and Loot**
- Party took a short rest. Brewbarry recovered ~13 HP (rolled 13; now ~19 HP).
- Treasure slag: ~3,000 gp value of coins, gems, and trade bars fused into residium-infused ice; all identifying marks destroyed by planar trickery. Soma's Arcana roll (16) identified planar cause; full explanation requires a planar theorist.
- Vukradin declared the slag stolen goods pending investigation.
- **Obsidian sword recovered:** pommel crystal is a mental amplifier projecting music directly into listeners' minds — perfect hall-filling sound with no physical structure required. Party named it "the Bluetooth Sword." Vukradin noted it seemed tailor-made for his studio ambitions but declared the gem must be returned to its rightful owner.
- GM confirmed the window to deliver the blue quartz mermaid statue to Elara Seasong Meliame in Neverwinter has closed. **Elara Seasong Meliame is dead, killed by House Margaster.** The party chose the dragon over the delivery deadline.
- Soma received an Insight (rolled 22 with advantage) that Harbin Wester and the Lord's Alliance will likely claim credit for the dragon's death; Soma's player declined to have her share this with the party.
- Sending Stones in party inventory confirmed as a legitimate reward (Dazlyn/Norbus payment) — not stolen goods.
- Vukradin planned to contact Ser Kaelen; executed in the following scene.

**Sylvine Wintermoon and Runa — Skirmish**
- Two women appeared in the fortress post-dragon: **Sylvine Wintermoon** (lean, red hair, brash) and her sister **Runa** (muscular, black hair, cold blue eyes), accompanied by veteran guards.
- Sylvine praised Vukradin as "a saint sent by the gods" and claimed the treasure slag was theirs.
- Vukradin refused, demanded proof, and accused them of being thieves and phonies.
- Sylvine declared "second plan — we kill them all" and the group attacked.
- Valphine cast Spirit Guardians: 10 damage to Veteran 3/4, 5 damage to Sylvine (who saved); lost concentration after taking 10 damage from a veteran's short sword (failed concentration check even with Bardic Inspiration).
- Soma (bear form) attacked Veteran 1 for 20 total damage across two hits (Bardic Inspiration used on the first).
- A veteran attempted Sleight of Hand to steal the slag from Vukradin; Vukradin won the opposed roll and retained it. Sylvine then attempted to steal it herself; also failed.
- Vukradin cast Hold Person (DC 15): Veterans 1 and 3 both failed their Wisdom saves — paralyzed.
- Brewbarry raged; critical hit on paralyzed Veteran 1 (30 damage), killed on second attack.
- Valphine used Word of Radiance; veterans made their saves — minimal effect.
- Veteran 2 used Disengage and fled.
- Soma (bear form, advantage) auto-critted paralyzed Veteran 3 for 30 damage; Veteran 3 failed his Hold Person save.
- Brewbarry finished Veteran 3: two attacks, second hit for 11 damage, killed.
- Veteran 4 fled, citing better career opportunities with the Carver dead.
- Sylvine and Runa's final fate not resolved in session notes — presumed fled or subdued.

**Sending to Ser Kaelen and Course Set**
- Vukradin cast Sending to Ser Kaelen mid-skirmish: *"The dragon is dead, so is the Carver. We're heading to Neverwinter. Spread the word."*
- Ser Kaelen replied (full version): *"Praise Torm! Your righteous victory brings light to the north. The gauntlet awaits your arrival in Phandalin. We shall celebrate your conflict-free triumph together."* Shorter operational version: *"Well done. Come to Phandalin quickly. Issues with the Emerald Enclave."*
- Party redirected from Neverwinter to **Phandalin** in response to Kaelen's summons.
- Vukradin stated intent to still visit Neverwinter eventually (to find Elara's next of kin).
