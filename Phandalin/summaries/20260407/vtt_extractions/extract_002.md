## Events

- Sylvine Wintermoon and her sister Runa appeared in the fortress after the dragon fight, claiming the treasure slag belonged to them, praising Vukradin as a saint sent by the gods.
- Vukradin refused to hand over the treasure, accusing Sylvine and Runa of being thieves and phonies and demanding proof of their claim.
- Insulted by Vukradin's refusal, Sylvine declared a change of plans and the sisters and their veteran companions attacked the party.
- Valphine cast Spirit Guardians, dealing 10 damage to Veteran 3/4 and 5 damage to Sylvine (who made her save); she subsequently lost concentration after being struck by a veteran for 10 damage (failed concentration check even with Bardic Inspiration).
- Soma used Wild Shape (bear form) and attacked Veteran 1, landing two hits for a total of 20 damage (Bardic Inspiration used on the first attack to confirm a hit).
- A veteran attempted to use Sleight of Hand to steal the treasure slag from Vukradin; Vukradin won the opposed roll and retained the slag.
- Veteran 1 (Sylvine) attempted to steal the treasure slag from Vukradin during combat; Sylvine failed the opposed roll, prompting her to shout "Second plan — we kill them all!"
- Vukradin cast Hold Person, paralyzing Veterans 1 and 3 (both failed DC 15 Wisdom saves).
- Brewbarry entered a rage and attacked the paralyzed Veteran 1, landing a critical hit for 30 damage on the first attack, then killed Veteran 1 with the second attack.
- Valphine used Word of Radiance; both targeted veterans made their saves, resulting in minimal effect.
- Veteran 2 used Disengage and fled.
- Soma (bear form) attacked the paralyzed Veteran 3 with advantage, auto-critting for 30 total damage; Veteran 3 failed his saving throw to break Hold Person.
- Vukradin cast Sending to Ser Kaelen, informing him: "The dragon is dead, so is the Carver. We're heading to Neverwinter. Spread the word."
- Brewbarry finished off the still-paralyzed Veteran 3 with two attacks (second attack hit for 11 damage, killing him).
- Veteran 4, seeing the tide turn, fled the fortress, citing better career opportunities now that the Carver was dead.
- Ser Kaelen responded to Vukradin's Sending: "Praise Torm! Your righteous victory brings light to the north. The gauntlet awaits your arrival in Phandalin. We shall celebrate your conflict-free triumph together." — then, in the agreed shorter version: "Well done. Come to Phandalin quickly. Issues with the Emerald Enclave."
- The party decided to set course for Phandalin in response to Ser Kaelen's summons.

## NPC Interactions

- **Sylvine Wintermoon:** Introduced herself and her sister Runa; claimed the treasure slag was theirs; praised Vukradin as a saint who returns stolen treasure; turned hostile and attacked when Vukradin refused to hand it over and accused her of being a thief.

- **Veteran 4 (fleeing bandit):** Disengaged and ran, remarking that with the Carver dead there were new criminal advancement opportunities elsewhere.
- **Ser Kaelen:** Received Vukradin's Sending; replied with urgent summons to Phandalin citing "issues with the Emerald Enclave"; his formal, effusive phrasing ("conflict-free triumph") was mocked by the players as AI-generated.

## PC Actions & Decisions

- **Vukradin:** Refused to surrender the treasure slag to Sylvine and Runa without proof; accused them of being thieves; succeeded on opposed Sleight of Hand to retain the slag; cast Hold Person (DC 15) paralyzing Veterans 1 and 3; cast Sending to Ser Kaelen mid-combat (used a spell slot); announced the party is heading to Phandalin per Kaelen's instruction; confirmed he expended two second-level spell slots (one Hold Person, one Silence) during the dragon fight plus the Sending cantrip (Vicious Mockery was identified as a cantrip).
- **Valphine:** Cast Spirit Guardians (10 damage to one veteran, 5 to Sylvine on a save); lost concentration after taking 10 damage from a veteran's short sword attack; failed subsequent concentration check even with Bardic Inspiration; used Word of Radiance to minimal effect (veterans made saves).
- **Soma:** Used Wild Shape (bear form, second use expended); attacked Veteran 1 for 20 damage across two hits (Bardic Inspiration used); attacked paralyzed Veteran 3 with advantage, auto-critting for 30 damage.
- **Brewbarry:** Entered rage; attacked paralyzed Veteran 1 with a critical hit (30 damage first strike, killed on second attack); attacked paralyzed Veteran 3, killing him with two attacks (20 damage then 11 damage final blow); victory quote: "Root Smasher stands, you don't. That's the story."
