## Events
- The session opens mid-combat with Cryovain flying 30 feet in the air, having broken free of the Carver's ritual circle. The Carver is in the process of possessing the dragon; approximately 2 rounds remain before domination is irreversible.
- Valphine (played by GM) attempts a high-level Guiding Bolt against the airborne dragon and misses; Bardic Inspiration used afterward still fails to save the roll.
- Soma casts Moonbeam upcasted to 3rd level (40-foot-high cylinder), successfully placing it on the dragon and dealing 8 radiant damage.
- Brewbarry activates his large form (bonus action, once per long rest), increasing size and reach to approximately 15 feet, enabling him to hit the airborne dragon with his halberd. Delivers two halberd strikes for 11 and 12 points of damage (plus rage bonus), totaling approximately 23+ damage.
- The Veteran (paralyzed bandit from the ritual) rolls a saving throw to break Hold Person and fails (GM rolls a 6); remains paralyzed.
- Cryovain uses his breath weapon on Brewbarry: 45 points of cold damage. Brewbarry rolls his Constitution saving throw, succeeds, and takes only 14 points (halved). Survives with 6 hit points remaining. Stone's Endurance considered but not applicable to this damage type.
- Vukradin (played by Dave) attacks the dragon using Bardic Inspiration to convert a miss (rolled 13) into a hit; deals 12 damage, reducing Cryovain to 2 hit points.
- Valphine casts Sacred Flame on Cryovain; dragon fails the Dexterity saving throw (rolled a 3) and takes enough damage to die. Cryovain collapses.
- The Carver's essence dissipates entirely upon the dragon's death.
- Soma strikes the paralyzed Veteran with bear claws intending non-lethal damage; automatic critical hit due to paralysis. Deals 26 points of damage (base + critical rolls). Then moves Moonbeam onto the Veteran, dealing 17 additional radiant damage after a failed Constitution save. Veteran reduced to approximately 47 HP below zero threshold.
- Party debates the Veteran's fate. Soma argues for mercy/non-lethal. Brewbarry decides to finish the Veteran off lethally with his halberd. Two attacks, both crits (advantage from paralysis). Deals approximately 22 and 28+ damage. Veteran killed.
- Party takes a short rest. Brewbarry recovers hit points (rolls 13, bringing him to approximately 19 HP).
- Party loots the dragon's lair and finds a block of roughly 3,000 GP worth of coins, gems, and trade bars fused into residium-infused ice, melted beyond all recognition.
- Soma rolls Arcana (16) and identifies that the ice fusion was caused by planar trickery; concludes a planar theorist would be needed to fully explain it. All identifying markings on the treasure have been destroyed.
- Vukradin picks up the Carver's obsidian sword and discovers the crystal in the pommel is a mental amplifier capable of projecting music directly into the minds of an audience, creating perfect hall-filling sound without a physical structure. Party immediately dubs it "the Bluetooth Sword."
- GM clarifies the crystal was likely the mechanism by which the Carver took control of the Veteran/vessel; for non-Carver entities it can only amplify music, but has unspecified side effects.
- Vukradin expresses concern about the crystal's power and states the gem must be returned to its rightful owner.
- GM confirms the party missed the window to return the blue quartz mermaid statue to the art collector in Neverwinter; the collector (Elara Seasong Meliame) has been killed by House Margaster. The party chose the dragon over the delivery deadline.
- Party discusses next destination. Vukradin/Dave expresses intent to go to Neverwinter anyway (to find next of kin). Brewbarry states no preference and wants to celebrate.
- GM offers Soma an insight (rolled 22 with advantage) that Harbin Wester and the Lord's Alliance will likely seize credit for the dragon's death if the party does not assert their claim. Soma's player declines to have Soma pass this information along, citing indifference to glory.
- Party identifies they have a set of Sending Stones in party inventory (confirmed as a legitimate reward, not stolen goods).
- Vukradin intends to use a Sending spell (not the stones) to contact Ser Kaelen. Session ends before this is executed in-game.
