[Scene 1] The Dragon's Mountain Fortress
Narrator: Brewbarry
Focus: Brewbarry alone survives two blasts of freezing breath on 6 hit points, feeling the battle through his body rather than his head — the fortress belongs to whoever can take the punishment.

- The party discovers Cryovain inside a glowing summoning circle, undergoing a ritual to be possessed by the Carver.

- Cryovain, now enraged and partially possessed, flies 30 feet into the air to avoid melee attacks.

- Valphine attempts to strike the dragon with a high-level guiding bolt but misses the target.

- Soma conjures a moonbeam cylinder that reaches up to the flying dragon, searing it with radiant light.

- Brewbarry assumes a giant form, increasing his size and reach, and strikes at the dragon with his halberd, delivering several powerful blows.
