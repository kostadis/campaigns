[Scene 3] The Fate of the Veteran
Narrator: Soma
Focus: Soma watches Brewbarry kill the paralyzed man she argued to spare, forcing her to reckon with what her interventionism actually costs when she loses the argument.

- The party debates whether to show mercy to the paralyzed bandit or end his life.
<!-- Registering her objection to killing the paralyzed veteran -->
Soma: "Don't kill, don't kill! He was just a victim!"

- Soma strikes the veteran with bear claws, intending for the damage to be non-lethal.

- Soma moves her moonbeam onto the veteran, further weakening him.

- Brewbarry, fueled by a lingering battle rage, decides to finish the veteran off with his halberd, killing him.
<!-- After killing the veteran, turning to Vukradin with pride -->
Brewbarry: "I hope this will inspire a song in your new music studio."
<!-- Pointing at the dead veteran and shouting in triumph -->
Brewbarry: "I win, you lose — tradition upheld."
<!-- On the subject of celebrating their dragon victory -->
Brewbarry: "I just want to celebrate our victory."
<!-- Asked why he drank the entire barrel of wine he had been carrying -->
Brewbarry: "I was thirsty."

<!-- On being told she has become an "interventionist" rather than a naturalist -->
Soma: "I… apparently I am."
