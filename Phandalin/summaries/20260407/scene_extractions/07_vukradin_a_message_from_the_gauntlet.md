[Scene 7] A Message from the Gauntlet
Narrator: Vukradin
Focus: Vukradin composes his Sending to Ser Kaelen mid-combat and receives back a suspiciously polished reply that tells him nothing he trusts and everything he needs to know about where they are headed next.

- The dragon, the man possessed by the Carver, the Carver itself, and some thieves were dead. 

- Vukradin successfully casts a sending spell to communicate with Ser Kaelen across a great distance.

- Ser Kaelen responds with praise for the party's victory over the dragon and the Carver, calling it a 'conflict-free triumph.'
<!-- Delivering his full, polished reply — the one that made everyone laugh -->
Ser: "Praise Torm! Your righteous victory brings light to the north. The gauntlet awaits your arrival in Phandalin. We shall celebrate your conflict-free triumph together."
<!-- Reacting to Ser Kaelen's phrasing -->
Brewbarry: "Fighting a fucking dragon — that was conflict."

- The party realizes that Ser Kaelen is instructing them to return to Phandalin immediately due to rising issues with the Emerald Enclave.
<!-- What Ser Kaelen wanted to say-->
Ser: "Well done. Come to Phandalin quick. Issues with the Emerald Enclave."
<!-- Assessing Ser Kaelen's trustworthiness -->
Vukradin: "He's about as close as someone we can actually trust as anybody."
<!-- Immediately walking it back -->
Vukradin: "No, I don't trust him. He's as close as anybody."

- The group discusses the utility of the sending spell, likening it to a magical text message.

- Brewbarry expresses enthusiasm for the promised celebration in Phandalin.
