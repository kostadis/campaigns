## Events

- The party gains a level following their victory over the young white dragon at Icespire Hold. Vukradin (Dave's character, absent this session) does not level up — stated reason is conflict gold.
- Soma rolls a double natural 20 on a Nature check to harvest the slain young white dragon, successfully extracting both the dragon breath pouch and a set of supple scales.
- The party discusses the harvested materials: the breath pouch can be used as a one-time weapon or crafted into a wondrous item (Dragon's Wrath) for ~2,000 gp; the scales can be crafted into dragon scale mail (very rare, ~20,000 gp) granting cold resistance, advantage on saves vs. dragon breath, and the ability to sense white dragons within 30 miles.
- The party decides to travel to Phandalin via the Mountain Toes Gold Mine, a roughly 2-day journey with a long rest assumed.
- On arrival at the Mountain Toes Gold Mine, the party finds the Whiskered Gang were-rats guarding the exterior but no sounds of mining coming from inside.
- Investigation reveals the dwarven miners are on strike. Foreman Dwolin the Grout refuses to mine until a specific Earth Elemental gem is found to "bless" the gold vein, believing gold is shy and must be treated with reverence.
- Valphine casts Daylight inside the mine, intimidating the were-rats.
- Valphine delivers a flat, deadpan interrogation of Zeleen Varnaster, establishing that the were-rats have not been fulfilling their actual purpose (producing gold from the mine) and have not obtained the gem.
- Valphine and Brewbarry order Zeleen to send his were-rats into the orc-controlled forest to retrieve the Earth Elemental gem.
- Horia (dwarven leader) vouches for Dwolin as the best gold-seeker she knows, then privately proposes a Plan B: spend 50 gp from the mine's own coffers to purchase a fake gem to trick Dwolin into resuming work if the were-rats fail.
- Brewbarry signs the secret contractual addendum with a thumbprint. Both Horia and Brewbarry agree this arrangement must not reach Vukradin's ears.
- The party continues to Phandalin, where they are greeted with a hero's welcome — townspeople cheering, children high-fiving.
- Harbin Wester organizes a festival on the town green, publicly praises Vukradin and the party for defeating the dragon, and takes partial credit for himself.
- The innkeeper brings out a special reserve for Brewbarry; Brewbarry reserves judgment until he tastes it.
- Ser Kaelen approaches the party during the festival and requests a private meeting, leading them to the Temple of Lathander.
- Ser Kaelen reveals that Adabra has discovered the truth about what happened at the Whispering Grove, including Vukradin's deception.
- Adabra is calling a conclave of local druids, intending to argue that the party's interventionism has caused harm to the forests.
- Ser Kaelen warns that neither the Lords Alliance nor the Order of the Gauntlet can intervene — the burden falls on the party.
- Ser Kaelen tells Soma that her staff holds deep symbolic power among local druids and may be key to swaying the conclave, but warns that some druids may attempt to claim it.
- Soma responds with quiet defiance: no order will take the staff from her.

- **Harbin Wester**: Publicly praised the party for defeating the dragon and abating the threat to Phandalin; took partial credit for himself. Organized the festival.
- **Ser Kaelen**: Pulled the party aside at the festival. Briefed them at the Temple of Lathander on Adabra's political moves, the upcoming druid conclave, the unavailability of Lords Alliance/Order of the Gauntlet support, and the significance of Soma's staff. Admired Brewbarry's halberd. Described Adabra as misguided rather than evil — she experienced the consequences of war but not the planar incursions that caused it.
- **Adabra** (discussed, not present): Has discovered the truth about the Whispering Grove and Vukradin's deception. Is rallying local druids. Her conclave argument will frame the party as the source of harm to the forests.

## PC Actions & Decisions

- **Soma**: Rolled double natural 20 on Nature check (result: 22 with +2 bonus) to harvest the young white dragon. Successfully extracted the dragon breath pouch and supple scales. Selected a powerful healing aura spell upon leveling up. Refused to entertain any possibility of losing her staff to the druid conclave.

Acknowledged she had forgotten about Emboldening Bond previously. Cast Daylight to intimidate the were-rats. Led the deadpan interrogation of Zeleen. Participated in the secret fake-gem negotiation with Horia.
- **Brewbarry**: Rolled 18 on the Nature check (assist on dragon harvest). Signed the secret contractual addendum with Horia using a thumbprint. Kept the Horia arrangement hidden from Vukradin.
- **Party collectively**: Decided to sell or hold the dragon breath pouch rather than immediately spend 2,000 gp crafting it. Noted dragon scale mail as a long-term goal but acknowledged cost (~20,000 gp) is far beyond current means (~700 gp noted). Agreed to travel to Phandalin via the mine.
