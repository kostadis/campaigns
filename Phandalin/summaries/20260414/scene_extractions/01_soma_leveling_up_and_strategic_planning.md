[Scene 1] Leveling Up and Strategic Planning
Narrator: Soma
Focus: Soma's double-natural-20 dragon harvest and her choice of a healing aura at level-up reveal her quiet competence and the weight of Meril's legacy pressing on every decision she makes.

- The party members, except for Vukradin, gain a level in experience.

- Valphine explores new clerical abilities, including a Peace Domain feature that allows for a mystical bond between allies to bolster their resolve with a bonus die.

- The group discusses the tactical advantages of a sixth-level ability that allows a protector to teleport next to an ally and intercept damage meant for them.

- Soma considers the utility of abjuration magic for creating safe zones or imprisoning extra-planar aberrations, noting its one-minute casting time makes it unsuitable for direct combat.

- The party debates the merits of various spells, comparing the utility of Spirit Guardians against a potent healing aura, and Soma ultimately selects a powerful healing aura spell.
