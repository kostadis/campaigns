# Session — 2026-04-18

## Overview
Following their defeat of the young white dragon at Icespire Hold, the party leveled up (Vukradin absent and excluded by GM ruling), harvested valuable dragon components, and traveled to Phandalin via the Mountain Toes Gold Mine. At the mine they found the dwarven workforce on strike over a superstitious foreman's demand for an Earth Elemental gem, coerced the nervous were-rat overseer Zeleen Varnaster into retrieving one, and secretly negotiated a fake-gem fallback with Horia — a scheme deliberately hidden from Vukradin. Back in Phandalin, the party received a public hero's welcome and a self-aggrandizing festival speech from Harbin Wester before Ser Kaelen drew them aside to deliver urgent news: Adabra has uncovered Vukradin's deception at the Whispering Grove and is mobilizing a druid conclave to condemn the party's interventionism, with neither the Lords Alliance nor the Order of the Gauntlet able to intervene. The session ended with the conclave encounter deferred to next session.
