## 💣 THE PROMISE-COLLISION MAP (who you're letting them lie to)

→ Let the lies stack. **Don't warn them.** The coalition civil-wars the moment the giants die — bank it for Maerimydra/after.

## RUN-IT REMINDERS
- **Scene 0 = 5 min.** Murssvayas hands over the menu + arsenal "why," then gets out of the way. Let *Akritas/players* connect the Hillsfar dot.
- **Play leaders as winners, not supplicants** — each thinks *they're* using the party. Fun = being underestimated and proving it.
- **Pacing:** ~2–3 doors fit a session. Drow + one = a viable assault. Slot **the Slaves fight into the back half** when they're hungry for combat.
- **Win = 1 faction committed + they grok the clock + a target picked (decapitate Dengor vs open war).**
