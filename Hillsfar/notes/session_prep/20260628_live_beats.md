## Session-Established Canon (Jun 28)

### The Clock (revised mechanics)
Each of the following buys **3 days**:
- Destroying/intercepting a cart *(done — Beat 1)*
- Freeing the slaves (Door 4)

Each faction recruited costs **3 days** of the clock. Drow + one ally = minimum 3 days burned on recruitment alone. **Math matters: every door = one cart reaching Maerimydra if they don't buy time first.**

### Drow Religious Politics (table-canon)

**The Blink** was caused by Alder and her adventurers imprisoning Lolth — *with Vhaeraun's secret help.* This is why:
- **Vhaeraun** is despised by Lolth's faithful (betrayer of their goddess)
- **Vhaeraun** nonetheless has leverage: he helped cage her, which means he has knowledge of how that was done

**The three-way split — same supremacist goal, different doctrine:**

| Faction | God | Philosophy | Hates |
|---|---|---|---|
| The Hidden | Vhaeraun | Work from shadows; Lolth's paranoia and endless internal conflict costs the Drow real power | Lolth (paranoia), Kiaransalee (death-obsession) |
| Resistance drow | Lolth | Conflict and domination are the path; weakness is purged | Vhaeraun (cowardice/betrayal), Kiaransalee |
| Nerevenans | Kiaransalee | Undeath and vengeance are ascension | Lolth, Vhaeraun, everyone — but hated more than they hate |

**The one point of unity:** every Drow faction — Lolth, Vhaeraun, Kiaransalee — agrees the Drow are the race destined to rule. They disagree on *how* and *under whom*. The fire giants are an insult to Drow supremacy that all three can agree to destroy.

**Kiaransalee** is hated by: Lolth's faithful, Vhaeraun's faithful, Bramgrim (Life cleric), Akritas (Enclave). She has no natural allies at this table except the party's willingness to use her.

**Slaves:** All slaves hate the Drow — regardless of faction. Drow across all factions view slaves as property and an asset, not people. This is the one thing that may poison the slave-recruitment beat if the Drow (including Quil'bryn) are present when it happens.
