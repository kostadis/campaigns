# Recruitment Tour — Stat-Light Encounter Pack

*Optional combat/puzzle for each of the five doors. Companion to `20260628_szith_morcane_recruitment_tour.md` (the beats) and `20260628_recruitment_cheatsheet.md` (the one-pager). Stat blocks **pulled from live 5etools canon** (the MCP is fixed) — sources noted; pull full blocks in 5etools if you need the fine print.*

**Party:** 4 × level 11 + Owlbear (a strong 5th body — every "intended" fight below is calibrated for the 4 PCs against 2014 thresholds, so the Owlbear makes them comfortably winnable; the **fail-state** fights are deliberately above-deadly deterrents, not intended brawls). Budgets: party-of-4 Hard ≈ 9,600 XP, Deadly ≈ 14,400 XP.

**Toolkit hooks to reward (these turn fights into puzzles):**
- **Felkur's cold cannon / ice bullets →** the Forge **salamanders are Vulnerable to cold.** Double damage. Tell the table nothing; let him discover it.
- **Bramgrim's radiant (& fire) →** shuts off the **Revenant's Regeneration** and out-damages **Wight** necrotic resistance. He's the anti-undead key in Door 3.
- **Akritas's bow →** wary of **Stone Giant Deflect Missile** (reduces + reflects a ranged hit, DC 17) and the **Dreamwalker's** reach. Behir is lightning-immune but takes arrows fine.
- **Daein (Str 23, Belt of Frost Giant) + voice mimicry + runic key →** grapples, shoves on the web, and the social/illusion solves below.
- **Consumables on the shelf:** Potion of Flying + Potion of Fire Giant Strength (the chasm/web), four *Sleep* tokens, *Wall of Force*, Pass Without Trace.

## DOOR 1 — The Hidden: the Proof-of-Worth Heist (PUZZLE, not a fight)

**Mode:** infiltration puzzle. The Hidden route the party to a giant-held cache and say *"bring back what's inside and we'll believe you."* Solving it clean also **scouts the outpost for free** (feed it into the assault).

**The 3-stage lock (skill puzzle, not a single DC):**
1. **The approach** — a patrol to slip, not fight (see below). Stealth vs. passive Perception, or *Pass Without Trace* trivializes it (let Akritas shine).
2. **The seal** — the cache is behind a **Faerzress-warded lock**: a glowing rune-lattice that re-scrambles when forced. *Puzzle, not a DC* — it opens to a sequence (match the Dethek runes / feed it the right resonance with the **Shard of Crystallized Faerzress** — Felkur's item is literally the key, reward that leap). Brute force = DC 20 thieves' tools **and** trips stage 3.
3. **The bite** — opening it wrong wakes the patrol (combat below) or drops a portcullis (DC 18 Str / Athletics, or Daein's belt).

**The patrol (only if they blow stealth — trivial, it's a gate not a brawl):**
- **2 × Azer** — *MM, CR 2.* AC 17, HP 39. Warhammer +5 (1d8+3 + 1d6 fire). Heated Body 1d10 fire on melee. Immune fire/poison.
- **1 × Hell Hound** — *XMM, CR 3.* AC 15, HP 58, speed 50. Bite +5 ×2 (1d8+3 + 1d6 fire); **Fire Breath (rech 5) DC 12 Dex, 17 (5d6) cone**; Pack Tactics. Immune fire.
- *Budget: ~3,200 XP adj = Easy.* The danger isn't the fight — it's the **noise** bringing the whole outpost. Make that the stakes.

**If betrayed / they try to rob the Hidden instead (the domination play):**
- **Hamadh** ≈ *Drow Mage (MM, CR 7)* — AC 12, HP 45, Summon Demon (shadow demon) 1/day; reflavor as a master thief (give him Cunning Action + Sneak Attack 4d6 if you want him slippery).
- **Kâras** ≈ *Drow Priestess of Lolth (MM, CR 8)* reflavored to **Vhaeraun** — AC 16, HP 71, Scourge +5 ×2 (1d6+2 + **5d6 poison**). Swap the yochlol summon for a shadow demon (Masked Lord, not Lolth).
- They won't fight to the death — the Hidden *vanish* when losing. Botch payoff: a master-thief crew loose in the outpost who owe the party nothing (and maybe Felkur's All-Purpose Tool walks).

**Win:** infiltration routes (cuts assault risk hard) + the Hidden as a strike team. **Material loss on botch:** a stolen tool, a burned reputation, thieves who know their faces.

## DOOR 2 — Lodge of Faces: Free the Giant from the Crystal (PUZZLE; the fight is the FAIL-STATE)

**Mode:** a cure puzzle. You **cannot** out-muscle the Lodge — the fight below is what happens if they bungle it. Steer to cleverness.

**The puzzle — separate maddened Decha from his perfect crystal.** Open design; reward any of:
- **Braincap delivery** — they carry treated braincaps (*greater restoration*). Problem: how do you make a giant *eat* one? Daein mimics the crystal "telling" him to; Felkur fabricates a delivery; hide it in food. (Persuasion/Deception DC 18, or an automatic win with a clever vector.)
- **The swap** — Felkur forges a duplicate crystal; a shell game on a demigod's infatuation (Sleight of Hand DC 18 + a distraction).
- **The outcast finishes the face** *(thematic winner — let a player reach it)* — the weeping galeb duhr completes the "imperfect" carving, breaking the spell of perfection at its root. Auto-success if they think of it; it also redeems the exile.
- **Smash it** — works, but a Faerzress crystal **detonates** (each creature within 15 ft: DC 15 Con, 27 (6d8) psychic + a Crystal-Cascade tell). Risks shattering Decha's *mind* instead of freeing it — a botch path.

**The galeb duhr (your guide — and a combat asset if allied):**
- *Galeb Duhr, XMM, CR 6.* AC 16, HP 123. Avalanche Slam +8 (2d6+5; +2d6 & prone if it charged 20+ ft). **Animate Boulders 1/day** (animates 1–2 boulders as more galeb duhrs). Immune charmed/frightened/petrified/poison. Recruited, it's a durable anchor.

**FAIL-STATE fight (telegraph as "you will lose" — it's a deterrent):**
- **Decha** = *Stone Giant Dreamwalker (VGM, CR 10).* AC 18, HP 161. **Dreamwalker's Charm** (enemies starting within 30 ft: DC 13 Cha or **charmed**), Greatclub +10 ×2 reach 15 (3d8+6), **Petrifying Touch** (DC 17 Con → **petrified**, only undone if Decha dies). Immune charmed/frightened.
- **+ 2 × Stone Giant** — *XMM, CR 7.* AC 17, HP 126. Stone Club +9 ×2 reach 15 (3d10+6); Boulder +9 (2d8+6, prone); **Deflect Missile (rech 5)** (reduce a ranged hit by 1d10+6, reflect DC 17 — *punishes Akritas*).
- *Budget: ~23,000 XP adj — roughly 1.6× Deadly.* The charm→petrify combo can remove a PC permanently mid-fight. **This is the wall that makes the puzzle worth it.** If they wade in anyway, give them a round to feel it and a clean exit.

**Win:** stone-giant avalanche muscle vs. the fire giants + the galeb duhr redeemed.

## DOOR 3 — The Nerevenans: the Grim Test (HORROR RP; small skirmish optional; big fight is the FAIL-STATE)

**Mode:** eerie, transactional negotiation + a distasteful test. Keep it creepy, not combative. **This is the Bramgrim-wedge room** — the friction is the content.

**The test (pick one):** swear a vengeance-oath on Kiaransalee (the oath is the trap Bramgrim sees), desecrate a giant shrine, or deliver a specific giant's skull. No dice needed — it's a *choice* with a moral price, not a check.

**Optional "prove yourselves" skirmish (if Velasta wants a demonstration — Medium):**
- **3 × Wight** — *XMM, CR 3.* AC 14, HP 82. Two attacks, Necrotic Sword/Bow +4 (1d8+2 + 1d8 necrotic); **Life Drain (DC 13 Con, max-HP reduction → zombie)**. Resist necrotic; **Sunlight Sensitivity** (irrelevant in the Underdark — note it). *~4,600 XP adj ≈ Medium.* Bramgrim's radiant ignores their necrotic resistance — let him be the answer.

**FAIL-STATE (if they insult the goddess — deterrent):**
- **Velasta T'sarran** = *Drow Priestess of Lolth (MM, CR 8)* reflavored to **Kiaransalee** — AC 16, HP 71, Scourge +5 ×2 (1d6+2 + **5d6 poison**); swap Summon Demon for *animate dead*/a wraith.
- **Asala** = *Revenant (XMM, CR 5).* AC 13, HP 127, **Regeneration 10/turn (OFF on fire/radiant — Bramgrim again)**, Vengeful Glare (DC 15 Wis → frightened, paralyzed if cursed), Slam +7 ×2 (2d6+4 necrotic). Immune charmed/frightened/paralyzed/stunned.
- **+ 4 × Wight.** *Whole thing ≈ 21,000 XP adj = deadly+.* A third hostile faction during the assault if they leave on bad terms.

**Win:** fearless undead shock troops + a Kiaransalee time-bomb planted under Maerimydra (good — that's the T'sarran/Undying-Temple slow burn). **Bramgrim is *relieved* if they walk.**

## DOOR 4 — The Slaves: Crack the Pen (THE FIGHT — slot this in the back half)

**Mode:** the liberation brawl the table will be hungry for after three talk-rooms. Daein, the Owlbear, Akritas's bow, Felkur's cannon all open up.

**The intended fight — free a slave pen (Hard, winnable, meaty):**
- **1 × Fire Giant** (pen overseer) — *XMM, CR 9.* AC 18, HP 162. Flame Sword +11 ×2 reach 10 (4d6+7 + 3d6 fire); Hammer Throw +11 (3d10+7 + 1d8 fire, push 15 + disadvantage). Immune fire.
- **+ 2 × Azer** (foremen) — *MM, CR 2.* AC 17, HP 39, Heated Body, Warhammer +5 (1d8+3 + 1d6 fire).
- *Budget: ~8,850 XP adj ≈ Hard.* With the Owlbear, a clean win that still bruises. **Dial down** to a **Salamander (XMM, CR 5; Vulnerable cold — Felkur's ice!)** overseer + 2 Azer for Medium if the table's tired; **dial up** by adding a Hell Hound for a tougher set.
- *Salamander note (if used):* AC 15, HP 90, **Fire Aura 2d6/turn**, Flame Spear +7 ×2 (2d8+4 + 2d6 fire), Constrict DC 15. **Cold vulnerability is the whole story** — Felkur halves the fight.

**Optional logistics puzzle (Felkur, if they want brains over brawl):** arm 200 unarmed people under the giants' noses — smuggle forge-tools, time the signal to the assault, a sequence of quiet skill checks rather than a fight.

**The succession knife-fight (theirs to own — no stats, pure RP):**
- **Rilira** ≈ *Drow Mage (MM, CR 7)* statline if it ever comes to blows; plays the entitled noble.
- **Korkan** = *Hobgoblin Warlord (XMM, CR 6).* AC 20, HP 112, **Aura of Authority** (he + allies get advantage on attacks/saves in 30 ft — *brutal against the party, decisive for the uprising if he's yours*), three attacks +6, Parry. The reason to keep him onside.

**Win:** the uprising-on-signal — turns the assault from a raid into a war the giants lose, and **starves the forge** (fewer hands = the manifest's ore shortage). **Botch:** uprising goes early/betrayed → a massacre they watch happen.

## DOOR 5 — Messanth: Solom's Vault + the Two-Voiced Dragon (PUZZLE + RP; the behir is the payoff)

**Mode:** a search puzzle, then a negotiation setpiece. The dragon is a *deal*, not a fight (unless betrayed).

**The search puzzle — Solom's chambers:** wards + ciphered notes + the Shadowfell key. DC 18 Arcana to unpick the wards (or Felkur's tools / Bramgrim's lore in stages); the notes drop **Blink / Crystal-Cascade** lore for the attentive. Finding the key is what surfaces Messanth at all (Murssvayas never volunteers him).

**Messanth — the negotiation (play the two voices):**
- Base: *Young Bronze Dragon (XMM, CR 8).* AC 17, HP 142, fly 80. Rend +8 ×3 (2d10+5); **Lightning Breath (rech 5) DC 15, 49 (9d10) line**; Repulsion Breath (DC 15, push 40 + prone). Immune lightning.
- **Half-shadow flavor (reskin, keep CR ~8–9):** add Resistance to necrotic; in dim light/darkness he has advantage on Stealth and can Hide as a bonus action; reflavor Lightning Breath as a **shadow/necrotic line** if you prefer (keep the 9d10, swap type). Speaks bronze-and-shadow, slightly out of sync.
- He'll **honor a deal** to fight the behir right now; long-term loyalty is a deliberately-open future thread.

**THE BEHIR — what Messanth is *for* (the assault's worst single obstacle):**
- *Behir, XMM, CR 11.* AC 17, HP 168, **climb 50** (crawls walls/ceiling in the dark). Bite +10 (2d12+6 + 2d10 lightning); **Constrict DC 18 (5d8+6, grapple+restrain)**; **Lightning Breath (rech 5) DC 16, 66 (12d10) in a 90-ft line**; **Swallow** (DC 18, 6d6 acid/turn, total cover). Immune lightning.
- *~7,200 XP solo ≈ Deadly for the party alone.* Freed Messanth (also lightning-immune) duels it and tears the gate open — **the party's reward for Door 5 is never having to fight this in the assault.** If they betray Messanth instead, they may face *both*.

**Win:** behir solved, chasm cleared, gate opened + a Gauntlet favor banked (Akritas's ask). **Botch:** a half-shadow dragon loose, owing them nothing — possibly worse than the behir.
