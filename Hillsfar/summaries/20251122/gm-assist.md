# Chapter 01 From Hillsfar's Aftermath to the Howling Pits

Date: Nov 22nd, 2025

## Summary
The party stood in the aftermath of their victory over Malkyn Grenefeld, having saved Hillsfar from weeks of chaos and thwarted the demon lord Graz'zt's carefully constructed plot. The city's entire ruling elite lay dead, and the Red Plume guards found themselves caught between fractured factions, uncertain of their place in the new order. Though Graz'zt had threatened terrible vengeance before his presence faded from the material plane, for the moment, victory belonged to the heroes. They had reached new heights of power, their abilities now approaching the realm of legendary warriors, though they knew greater challenges still awaited them.

Isthana, the head of the Thieves Guild, appeared in the blood-soaked temple where the final battle had taken place. She surveyed the shattered windows and destroyed artifacts with barely concealed delight, thanking the party for creating such extraordinary opportunities for her organization. When she offered them positions in Hillsfar's new political order, Daein declined immediately, stating he preferred to keep his head attached to his body. Isthana smiled knowingly and instead offered to help them acquire equipment from the castle's stores. The party gratefully accepted, with Akritas receiving enchanted bracers and superior leather armor, while Bramgrim and Daein were outfitted with magical plate mail that would better protect them in the battles to come.

As they prepared to leave through the castle's main gate, still spattered with blood from their recent battle, Red Plume guards stood ready to challenge them. However, the guards' resolve wavered when they recognized the heroes who had just slain the entire ruling class, and they wisely stepped aside. The party made their way to a garishly painted smithy, where they commissioned custom armor for their owlbear companion, determined to keep their beloved beast safe in future conflicts. The work would take several days, so they settled into a tavern to rest and recover.

During their respite, Charles arrived with urgent news from the Emerald Enclave. After some overly dramatic congratulations on their victory, he revealed that the Drow had brought terrifying intelligence about Graz'zt's continued machinations. The demon lord had infused fire giants with his own blood, creating an army powerful enough to threaten both the Underdark and the surface world. Charles emphasized that demons sought chaos and destruction for its own sake, and that waiting to confront this threat would only make it more dangerous. He directed the party to meet representatives at the Silver Shadow Cave near Elventree, warning them that the price for fighting evil always increased the longer one delayed.

The party traveled to the sacred cave of Selûne, arriving in the evening as soft moonlight bathed the clearing in pale radiance. The wide cave entrance descended into a broader chamber where natural phenomena caused the moonlight to filter mysteriously below ground. From the shadows emerged Murssvayas Dhuurniv, a Drow with silver hair, emerald green eyes, and skin so dark it appeared nearly purple. He greeted each party member individually, including the owlbear, who seemed to recognize him with unusual familiarity.

Murssvayas explained that the Drow outpost of Szith Morcane had been sacked by an army of slaves led by fiendish fire giants and demons from the city of Maerimydra. The outpost's leader, Solom Ned'razak, an incredibly powerful wizard, had fled to the fungal forest known as the Sporedome, where other survivors had gathered. The Drow had been working on a ritual to purge the fiendish blood from the giants, but their chief alchemist, Xaven Tanor'this, had been captured during the raid. Through a now-collapsed spy network, they had learned that Xaven had been sold to a creature named Maram il Vass, who operated an extraplanar brokerage called the Den of the Whispered Word in the twisted realm known as the Howling Pits.

When Bramgrim demanded proof of Murssvayas's authority, the Drow produced a pendant that Bramgrim recognized as belonging to Solom Ned'razak. Satisfied with this evidence, Bramgrim then insisted on knowing what reward the party would receive for such a dangerous mission. Murssvayas initially seemed offended by the mercenary question, emphasizing the greater threat to both the Underdark and the surface world. He warned that the fire giants were powerful enough to destroy a Drow city with all its defenses, and that Hillsfar would stand no chance against such an assault.

When the party continued to press for tangible compensation, Murssvayas revealed that Solom had anticipated this very question. The Drow wizard had promised that if the party successfully rescued Xaven and helped reclaim Szith Morcane, they would be granted an item from the legendary Drow horde. Though Daein questioned whether this promise could be trusted, the party ultimately agreed to undertake the perilous mission. They would venture into the Howling Pits, infiltrate the Den of the Whispered Word, and rescue the captured alchemist before the fire giants could complete their conquest of the Underdark and turn their attention to the defenseless cities of the surface world.

## Locations
### Hillsfar
A city recovering from weeks of chaos after the party defeated Malkyn Grenefeld and killed the entire ruling elite. The Red Plume guards are caught between factions, and the head of the Thieves Guild sees new opportunities in the power vacuum.

### Hillsfar Castle
The former seat of power in Hillsfar, now a bloody mess with shattered windows and destroyed artifacts after the party's recent battle. The party meets Isthana here and acquires new equipment before departing through the main gate.

### Tavern in Hillsfar
An unnamed establishment where the party rests for several days while waiting for their owlbear's armor to be crafted. Charles finds them here to deliver urgent news from the Emerald Enclave.

### Smithy in Hillsfar
A workshop painted in extraordinarily garish colors where the party commissions custom scale mail armor for their owlbear companion.

### Silver Shadow Cave
A sacred site for communing with the moon goddess Selûne, located near Elventree. Soft moonlight bathes the small clearing in pale radiance, highlighting moats of pollen floating in the air. The wide cave entrance descends into a broader opening where natural phenomena cause moonlight to filter below ground. This is where the party meets Murssvayas Dhuurniv.

### Elventree
A settlement near the Silver Shadow Cave, mentioned as a general location reference.

### Szith Morcane
A Drow outpost in the Underdark that was sacked by an army of slaves led by fiendish fire giants and demons from Maerimydra. Its leader, Solom Ned'razak, fled to the Sporedome after the attack.

### Sporedome
A fungal forest in the Underdark where Solom Ned'razak and other Drow survivors took refuge after the attack on Szith Morcane.

### Maerimydra
A city in the Underdark from which the fiendish fire giants and demons launched their attack on Szith Morcane.

### Den of the Whispered Word
An extraplanar brokerage of goods and services located in the Howling Pits, where the captured Drow alchemist Xaven Tanor'this was sold to Maram il Vass.

### Howling Pits
A twisted realm of the Underdark where Maram il Vass's refuge, the Den of the Whispered Word, is located.

## NPCs
### Isthana
The head of the Thieves Guild in Hillsfar. She is pragmatic and opportunistic, seeing the recent chaos and death of the ruling elite as creating many opportunities for her guild. She helps the party acquire new armor and magical items from the castle's stores, and offers them political power in Hillsfar, which they decline.

### Charles
A messenger from the Emerald Enclave and member of La Résistance, known for his dramatic and somewhat over-the-top demeanor. He congratulates the party on their victory and delivers urgent news about Graz'zt infusing fire giants with power. He directs the party to meet with Drow representatives at the Silver Shadow Cave.

### Murssvayas Dhuurniv
A Drow representative with silver hair, thick sideburns, emerald green eyes, and skin so dark it's nearly purple. Dressed in black shirt and pants with a sword at his side, he represents Solom Ned'razak and meets the party at the Silver Shadow Cave. He explains the Drow's plight and requests the party's aid in recovering the captured alchemist Xaven Tanor'this.

### Solom Ned'razak
The leader of the Drow outpost of Szith Morcane, an 18th level wizard who fled to the Sporedome after his outpost was sacked by fire giants and demons. He sent Murssvayas Dhuurniv to represent him and anticipated the party would ask for a reward, offering an item from the Drow horde upon successful completion of the mission.

### Xaven Tanor'this
The Drow's chief alchemist who was working on a ritual to cull fiendish blood from the Graz'zt-infused fire giants. He was captured during the raid on Szith Morcane and sold to Maram il Vass in the Den of the Whispered Word.

### Maram il Vass
A creature residing in the Den of the Whispered Word, an extraplanar brokerage in the Howling Pits, who purchased the captured Drow alchemist Xaven Tanor'this.

### Graz'zt
A demon lord whose carefully constructed plot to destroy Hillsfar and extend his power was thwarted by the party. He threatened vengeance and is now infusing fire giants with his blood, creating a new threat to both the Underdark and the surface world.

### Malkyn Grenefeld
The recently defeated antagonist whose death triggered the current chaos in Hillsfar and the wrath of Graz'zt. The party's victory over him is what set the current events in motion.

### Red Plume Guards
The city guards of Hillsfar, now caught between factions after the death of the ruling elite. They stand ready to fight the blood-spattered party at the castle gate but ultimately allow them to pass without confrontation.
