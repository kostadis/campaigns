## Completed Encounters & Quests

*Definitive list of finished content. Do NOT replay or re-offer these. Most recent last.*

- **Defeat of Malkyn Grenefeld (Ch1, Hillsfar).** Party killed Malkyn and thwarted Graz'zt's surface plot. *Consequence:* power vacuum in Hillsfar that Isthana exploited; city stable but recovering. Owlbear's custom armor commissioned here.
- **Descent to the Howling Pits (Ch2–3).** Two-day trek from the Waydown; evaded cloakers and a yellow-mold tunnel; fled a fire-giant ambush; fought Barlgura demons. *Consequence:* party reached 11th level; met escaped slaves Teegan & Drystan.
- **Rescue of Xaven Tanor'this (Ch4, Den of the Whispered Word).** Daein conned Maram with forged armor; party fought chasme demons in the Hanging Gardens and freed Xaven. *Consequence:* Xaven now at Sporedome aiding Solom; night hags took over the Gardens; Felkur entered Maram's no-betrayal contract.
- **Black Ember Outpost assault (Ch6–7).** Cleared the fortress (giants, ogres, hellhounds); Daein's auditory illusion sold a fake giant victory; Bramgrim turned offensive. *Consequence:* looted Potion of Fire Giant Strength, Potion of Flying, 400gp coral; opened the Path of Shadows; rescued and contracted Pipyap; learned of "the Blink" and Lolth's return.
- **Capture of Brandyr & the Spire dinner (Ch9, Maerimydra).** Captured fiendborne fire giant Brandyr at the Six-Fingered Courtyard (Sleep + shrink + portable hole; Daein mimicked Graz'zt to scatter cambions). Infiltrated Vraxen's dinner and ignited a three-way brawl (Vraxen/Veperyd/Veris). *Consequence:* stole the runic key; Pipyap vanished in the chaos; party deduced Maram's double-cross.
- **Lexicon of Oaths heist (Ch10–11, Spire of Inevitable Reckoning).** Bypassed prime-numbered glyph traps (True Seeing) and the scent-keyed stone golem; reached the Vault of Oaths. *Consequence:* Felkur forged the Lexicon (replaced his own name with "Veris of Mulmaster," voiding Maram's contract), pocketed the Faerzress Shard, recalibrated the teleport circle for escape.
- **Peak battle vs. Veris (Ch12).** Veris trapped Felkur (Wall of Force, dispelled by Bramgrim), summoned webs and a Mezzaloth. *Consequence:* Akritas destroyed the stone golem and **killed Veris with Hail of Thorns** before he could cast Fireball.
- **Escape from Maerimydra (Ch13).** A second Mezzaloth disintegrated the Wall of Force and teleported off; fire giants stormed the peak; party teleported out as the window closed and leveled up. *Consequence:* Brandyr handed to Solom for the ten-day purge ritual.
- **Glor's contract — kill the Rogue Thought (Ch14–16).** Accepted Glor's job; ten-day trek to the lair; freed and cured Arrizz; wiped out the Githyanki warriors; destroyed two Intellect Devourers; brain-keyed the sealed doors. *Consequence:* Rogue Thought reduced to near-death — **he severed his own tentacles and plane-shifted away, vowing revenge with Graz'zt** (escaped, not killed). Zhentarim spy Thera died in the chaos. Party returned to Sporedome and collected the reward (**Wand of the War Mage +2**). Arrizz departed.
- **Isthana's courtship dinner (Ch16, Hillsfar).** Isthana hosted the party and offered patronage/protection for any business venture. *Consequence:* party signed nothing; door left open.
