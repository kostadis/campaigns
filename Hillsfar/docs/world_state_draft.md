## CAMPAIGN SNAPSHOT — Where Things Stand Now

The party (11th level) is currently back on the surface in **Hillsfar** (end of Ch16) — their latest beat was a private dinner with criminal matriarch **Isthana**, who courted them to start a city business under her patronage; they signed nothing but left the door open. Their Underdark base of operations is **Sporedome**, the fungal refuge of the Szith Morcane drow, where they had just collected the reward for destroying/driving off the **Rogue Thought** (a mad mind flayer who plane-shifted away vowing revenge alongside Graz'zt) on contract for the illithid **Glor**. **Arrizz** has parted ways with them. **Solom Ned'razak** is mid-ritual at Sporedome: he holds the captive fiendborne fire giant **Brandyr** and is running a **ten-day blood-purge ritual** to cull the demonic taint from the region.

Two converging calls to action now point the party at **Maerimydra**:
- **Solom** has declared it is time to move against Graz'zt in Maerimydra; the party is to travel to **Szith Morcane** and link up with the drow resistance under **Murssvayas Dhuurniv**.
- The **Emerald Enclave** (via telepathy to Akritas) warns that the fire giants in Szith Morcane are nearing completion of their siege project and have turned their eyes toward **Hillsfar and the Dalelands**.

Back on the surface, **Isthana** (criminal matriarch of Hillsfar) is courting the party as business assets. They have left that door open but signed nothing.

**Immediate next beat:** travel to Szith Morcane → join Murssvayas's resistance → disable Maerimydra's arcane siege weapons / strike at Graz'zt's hold.

## PLAYER CHARACTERS & COMPANIONS

### Felkur Oldenwood — Gnome Artificer ("The Phantom Thief")
Tactical support, infiltration, and the party's "engineer." Maintains a cover as a "simple gnome artificer" while concealing his accidental legend as the **Phantom Thief / Phantom Ghost of Mulmaster** — a reputation he earned unknowingly years ago after a drunken sale of the **Watcher's Eye Brooch** left him untraceable to divination.
- **Gear/abilities:** Eldritch cannon (cold-infused + flamethrower, ice ammo); infused armor (AC 17); spell-storing item; spells incl. *Fireball*, *Firebolt*, *Wall of Force*, *Sleep*, *Catapult*, *Mage Hand*; expert thieves'/artificer's tools; **Shard of Crystallized Faerzress** (in pack, acts like a *wand of the war mage* for artificer abilities); **Wand of the War Mage +2**; flavor/scent-extraction gadget; three bottles of rare wine (gift from Isthana); four *Sleep* tokens remaining.
- **Maram il Vass contract:** Felkur voided his obligation by a loophole — he altered the **Lexicon of Oaths** to replace his own name with **Veris of Mulmaster**, so the book no longer identifies him (contract null if it cannot). Grants him a standing bonus vs. fear. *Open: whether Maram has detected the forgery (see Threads).*

### Bramgrim — Cleric of Life (dwarf; Underdark-wise)
Primary healer, AoE controller, and the party's expert on Underdark lore, fungi, and illusions. Once a strict "healing-only" purist; **Daein shamed him into offensive casting** and he now freely uses radiant/necrotic magic.
- **Gear/abilities:** Mithral plate (AC 20); carries an authenticated copy of **Solom's Pendant**; rides the Owlbear in deep water. Spells incl. *Spirit Guardians*, *Spiritual Weapon*, *Toll the Dead*, *True Seeing* (reveals fiends/illusions — exposed Maram's yugoloth form and prime-numbered glyph traps), *Dispel Magic* (shattered Veris's *Wall of Force*), *Guardian of Faith*, *Mass Healing Word*, *Freedom of Movement*. Poison resistance; advantage on concentration. Identified by the Rogue Thought as the party's "greatest magical threat."

### Daein — Frontline Fighter & Party Face
Frontline combatant and master deceiver. Declined a political post in Hillsfar's new order. Drives toward "**FU money**" (financial independence) and dreams of business ventures (soups/trade).
- **Gear/abilities:** Plate Mail +1 (AC 21); Underdark goggles; longsword, longbow (+ an axe used once); magical mandolin (casts *Invisibility*, once-per-day *Fly*); a **stolen runic key** (telekinesis). *Minor Illusion* voice mimicry (flawlessly imitated **Graz'zt** to scatter cambions and **Veris** to spark the dinner conspiracy); Action Surge, Second Wind, Protective Field, telekinetic Sap/psionic damage reduction; Deception/Stealth specialist.

### Akritas — 11th-Level Ranger (Emerald Enclave operative)
Ranged archery specialist and the party's Enclave contact; receives telepathic mission briefings and holds a rank granting access to scouts and supplies. Loyal but frustrated at being pulled into Enclave affairs.
- **Gear/abilities:** **Oathbow**, Bracers of Archery, Studded Leather +1. *Hunter's Mark*, *Pass Without Trace* (party stealth backbone), *Hail of Thorns* (killed Veris), Volley, Nature's Veil (bonus-action invisibility), Improved Archery; crits exceeding 50 damage.
- *Note: source transcripts are inconsistent on Akritas's gender (mostly she/her, occasionally he/his); treat as unresolved presentation.*

### Owlbear — Animal Companion (11th-level sidekick)
Loyal martial sidekick. Custom armor (commissioned Ch1), magical/enchanted claws (+1), Extra Attack, improved crit (19–20), Second Wind. Too large for narrow halls — fights from doorways. Serves as a mount through deep water/wells; broke Xaven's cage chain; inadvertently carried **Vraxen's scent** (later extracted by Felkur to bypass the vault golem). Recently stunned by an Intellect Devourer's mind blast and battered by Githyanki; healed by Bramgrim during a short rest. **Currently alive and with the party.**

### Pipyap — Imp guide (status: VANISHED)
Gluttonous, salt-obsessed imp; rescued from ogres (Ch7) and bound by a favorable verbal contract to guide/inform the party. Revealed Maerimydra's hierarchy and the Spire layout, and suggested pitting Vraxen against Veperyd. **Vanished into the chaos of Vraxen's dinner (Ch9); whereabouts and contract status unknown.**

## ALLIES & PATRONS

### Solom Ned'razak — Drow Archwizard, Patron-in-Chief
Deposed ruler of Szith Morcane (the **Inverted Tower** was his school), now leader of the drow refugees at **Sporedome**. Commissions the whole enterprise. Wears purple robes trimmed with silver.
- **Goal:** A ten-day ritual to **purge demonic taint** from the region and drive out the fiendborne giants — requiring the **blood of a living fiendish fire giant** (now supplied by the captive Brandyr).
- **Stance:** Views the party as "important but replaceable"; refuses to join them ("indispensable" to drow survival). Long subverts Lolth's clerics in Maerimydra. Blames Menzoberranzan's archmages/liches for the **"Blink"** event (see Threads). Designates **Murssvayas Dhuurniv** as the party's standing contact. As of Ch16: declares the time has come to move on Maerimydra.

### Xaven Tanor'this — Drow Alchemist
Former chief alchemist of Maerimydra's drow; the sole surviving refugee who knows the **secret route into Maerimydra** (via the Shattered Tower). Captured at Szith Morcane, sold to Maram il Vass, caged in the Hanging Gardens, and **rescued by the party (Ch4)**. Now at Sporedome, working alongside Solom; holds the alchemical knowledge to purge fiendish blood. Nervous, hurried.

### Murssvayas Dhuurniv — Drow Wizard, Resistance Leader
Solom's representative and the party's primary contact. Drow male: near-purple skin, silver hair, thick sideburns, emerald eyes. Carries Solom's Pendant to prove legitimacy. Provided the party's map to the Howling Pits (Ch2). **Now at Szith Morcane**, preparing the resistance and ready to receive the party.

### Isthana — Criminal Matriarch of Hillsfar
Head of the Hillsfar thieves' guild; pragmatic and opportunistic. Thanked the party for the power vacuum they created (Ch1). Now offers patronage/protection for any business they start — with a velvet threat that ventures without her backing will fail. Gifted Felkur three bottles of rare wine. Door left open for future deals.

### Emerald Enclave — Faction Patron
Active; contacts the party through Akritas by messenger (Ch14) and telepathy (Ch16). Possesses intel (originally from a drow source) on Graz'zt's machinations. Tasking the party against the fire giants of Szith Morcane/Maerimydra. *Charles* was their Ch1 messenger (current status unknown).

### Glor — Mind Flayer of the Ryxyg Colony
Cowled illithid who communicates telepathically. Claims his colony has chosen **peaceful coexistence** over conquest. Hired the party to destroy the **Rogue Thought** (a renegade illithid) and provided a map and intel on disabling Maerimydra's siege weapons "without the perpetrators being identified." Now respects the party. Operates out of Sporedome.

## ANTAGONISTS & THREATS

### Graz'zt — Demon Lord, "The Dark Prince" (off-screen prime mover)
Abyssal demon lord; the source of the region's corruption and madness. **Not addressable as a mortal** — he acts through cultists and lieutenants, his presence having faded from the Material Plane after Ch1. Infused the Maerimydra fire giants with his fiendish blood via a pact; brought in **Vraxen** to enforce his contracts. Worshipped at the **Six-Fingered Courtyard** (six-fingered-hand symbol). Has threatened vengeance on the party and now shares that vow with the Rogue Thought. The party's true objective is to stop his corruption from reaching the surface. *As the party levels, direct manifestation (avatar/Chosen/incursion) becomes plausible.*

### Rogue Thought — Mad Mind Flayer (ESCAPED)
The renegade illithid Glor hired the party to kill. Telepathic; plane-shifts; manipulates Faerzress. Claims a blessing from Graz'zt. Reduced to near-death by the party, he **severed his own tentacles** (left as proof/distraction) and **plane-shifted to another dimension, vowing he and Graz'zt would have revenge.** His Githyanki servants were wiped out in the fight.

### Hledh — Fiendborne Fire Giant Queen (not yet encountered)
Fiendish ruler of Maerimydra; the bloodline source of all fiendborne giants; dwells in **Castle Maerimydra** (twin towers, eastern plateau). Pipyap warned she is to be avoided at all costs. The looming endgame authority of the giant occupation.

### Veris — High Inquisitor/High Cloak of Mulmaster (DEAD)
Cold, calculating wizard sent from Mulmaster, secretly hunting Felkur. Identified Felkur as a master smith with a criminal past at Vraxen's dinner, then pursued the party to the Spire's peak — enraged by the forgery Felkur planted in the Lexicon framing *him*. Trapped Felkur with *Wall of Force* (dispelled by Bramgrim), used *Teleport*, *Web*, *Firebolt*, and a stone golem. **Killed by Akritas's *Hail of Thorns* (Ch12)** before he could cast *Fireball*. *His framed name now sits in the Lexicon — a loose end if any Mulmaster authority reads it.*

### Vraxen — Arcanaloth, Host of the Spire of Inevitable Reckoning
Fox-faced yugoloth; enforcer of Graz'zt's contracts in Maerimydra, manager of the giant pact, and co-runner of the Coliseum betting pool with Veperyd. Obsessed with financial accuracy. Held the **Lexicon of Oaths**; his scent keys the vault's stone golem. **His private journal details secret plans to betray his yugoloth superiors** — valuable blackmail. Tricked into a three-way dinner brawl (vs. Veperyd and Veris) that covered the party's heist. **Last seen alive at the Spire;** not confirmed killed.

### Veperyd — Fiendborne Fire Giant, Coliseum Master
Demon-blooded fire giant; skin weeps oily black smoke. Runs Maerimydra's **Bloody Coliseum** (duels and betting pool) under Vraxen. Caught skimming the pool — the lever the party used to ignite the dinner brawl. Volcanic temper; vowed to "repay a debt in blood." **Last seen alive at the Spire dinner.** A viable future capture/blood target.

### Brandyr — Fiendborne Fire Giant (CAPTIVE)
Demon-blooded fire giant who led Graz'zt worship at the Six-Fingered Courtyard. **Ambushed, *Sleep*'d, shrunk two sizes, stuffed in a portable hole, and delivered to Solom (Ch13).** Now unconscious in Solom's facility as the **blood source for the ten-day purge ritual.**

### Maram il Vass — Ultroloth, Broker of the Den of the Whispered Word
Seven-foot yugoloth from Gehenna; projects an illusion of a "rakishly handsome half-elf." Runs the **Den of the Whispered Word**, an extraplanar information/contract brokerage in the Howling Pits. Cunning, meticulous, expendable with his minions. **Engineered a double-cross:** sold Felkur's location to a devil, then contracted the party to retrieve the Lexicon of Oaths from Vraxen — so they'd steal the book *for him* while thinking they served a different debt. The party retrieved Xaven from him (Ch4) and later realized the scheme (Ch11). *Felkur has since voided his contract by forgery — whether Maram knows is unresolved (see Threads).*

### Githyanki (warriors wiped out; Knight at large)
Arrogant, warlike planar raiders; ancient enemies of mind flayers. Served the Rogue Thought and tormented Arrizz. Used innate *Misty Step*/teleport and greatswords. **All warriors in this arc are dead** (last slain by Bramgrim). The **Githyanki Knight** *Plane Shift*-ed away (Ch15), declaring the party a "permanent enemy" — an unresolved future threat.

### Lesser threats (resolved / transient)
- **Stone Golem(s):** Vault guardian keyed to Vraxen's scent; reassigned to Veris; **destroyed by Akritas (Ch12).**
- **Shield Guardian:** Maram's former sentry, reinforced Veris; behaved erratically after Veris's death; last seen damaged.
- **Mezzaloths:** One slain by Daein (Ch12–13); a second used a **wand of disintegration** to break the party's *Wall of Force* and **teleported away (status unknown).**
- **Intellect Devourer:** Burst from a slain Githyanki's skull; **destroyed by Felkur's *Firebolt* (Ch16).**
- **Chasme & Barlgura demons:** Maram's palace guards; destroyed.

## KEY LOCATIONS

**Surface — Moonsea**
- **Hillsfar (City of Trade)** — Party home base, and **where they are right now** (end of Ch16, after Isthana's dinner); stable but recovering after Malkyn Grenefeld's fall and the thwarting of Graz'zt's plot. **A confirmed prime target for the giants' siege weapons** (alongside Elventree). A detailed street-map of Hillsfar was found in the Rogue Thought's lair — intelligence for the giants' assault.
- **Elventree** — Non-human haven near Hillsfar; also a siege target.
- **Silver Shadow Cave** — Moonlit cave at Elventree, sacred to Selûne; site of the party's first meeting with Murssvayas and the expedition's starting point.
- **The Waydown** — Half-day sinkhole descent into the Underdark; guarded by the Red Plumes; a partial wall now rings the entrance. Feeds the River Tesh.

**Underdark**
- **Sporedome** — Luminous fungal forest/lake cavern; the party's **Underdark base of operations** (they are currently up in Hillsfar). Drow refugee city under Solom; site of Solom's ritual, Glor's contracts, and the party's distillery. Hallucinogenic and brain-capping mushrooms grow here (the latter ward against Graz'zt's madness).
- **The Howling Pits** — Maze of maddening, wind-howled tunnels.
  - **Den of the Whispered Word** — Maram il Vass's black-and-red marble palace/brokerage. Brass doors, fairy-fire lighting.
  - **Hanging Gardens** — Caged-prison cavern beneath the Den; guarded by a **coven of three night hags** (Elsa, Ilsa, Ulsa) who took over from chasme demons. Where Xaven was held.
- **Black Ember Outpost** — Obsidian fire-giant fortress 4 miles from Maerimydra; **assaulted and cleared by the party (Ch7).** Beneath it, a pool hides the **Path of Shadows** water-tunnel to Maerimydra.
- **Rogue Mind Flayer's Lair** — Twisting, Faerzress-thick complex; doors sealed by **brain basins** (an actual brain placed in each opens them). Holds surgical remains, brain-jars, freezing specimen rooms, and surface-city maps. Largely cleared; the Rogue Thought escaped.

**Maerimydra (fire-and-ash city, 600-ft cavern)**
- **Castle Maerimydra** — Hledh's twin-towered seat (eastern plateau).
- **Six-Fingered Courtyard** — Graz'zt worship plaza (western plateau); where Brandyr was captured.
- **Bloody Coliseum** — Gladiatorial arena run by Veperyd; holds Kurgoth Hellspawn's iron statue.
- **Spire of Inevitable Reckoning** — Vraxen's basalt tower. Peak chamber holds a **recalibrated teleportation circle** (Felkur reprogrammed it for escape) and an iron door; **Vault of Oaths** (crystal dome, prime-numbered glyph-trapped approach, scent-keyed golem) held the **Lexicon of Oaths**. The party infiltrated, looted, and escaped via the circle.
- **Shattered Tower** — Ruined arcane center (northern ruins); mouth of the Path of Shadows; Xaven's old workplace; the secret route in.

## CANON EVENTS TIMELINE (chronological reference)

1. **Ch1 — Hillsfar:** Party (now 10th lvl) defeats Malkyn Grenefeld, thwarting Graz'zt's plot; decline Isthana's political offer. Charles (Emerald Enclave) reports Graz'zt infusing fire giants. Meet Murssvayas at Silver Shadow Cave; accept the mission to rescue Xaven and aid Szith Morcane. Commission the Owlbear's armor.
2. **Ch2 — Descent:** Two-day trek from the Waydown to the Howling Pits; evade cloakers and a yellow-mold tunnel; ambushed by fire giants, flee.
3. **Ch3 — The Den approach:** Meet escaped slaves Teegan & Drystan; fight Barlgura demons; reach 11th level; gain entry to Maram's palace; Maram demands payment for Xaven. *True Seeing* reveals Maram as a yugoloth.
4. **Ch4 — Rescue Xaven:** Daein cons Maram with forged armor; Felkur negotiates a contract (no-betrayal clause). Party fights chasme demons in the Hanging Gardens and frees Xaven. Night hags take over the Gardens.
5. **Ch5 — Sporedome:** Reunite with Solom; learn the purge ritual needs living fiendish-giant blood; plan the Path of Shadows via Black Ember Outpost; gather mind-protecting mushrooms. Felkur learns he stole the Watcher's Eye Brooch.
6. **Ch6–7 — Black Ember Outpost:** Assault and clear the fortress (giants, ogres, hellhounds); Daein's auditory illusions sell a fake giant victory; Bramgrim turns offensive. Descend the well (Path of Shadows) into Maerimydra; rescue and contract Pipyap. Learn of the "Blink" and Lolth's return.
7. **Ch9 — The Spire dinner & Brandyr's capture:** Capture Brandyr at the Six-Fingered Courtyard (*Sleep* + shrink + portable hole; Daein mimics Graz'zt to scatter cambions). Infiltrate Vraxen's dinner; ignite a three-way brawl (Vraxen/Veperyd/Veris); steal the runic key; Pipyap vanishes. Party deduces Maram's double-cross.
8. **Ch10–11 — The Lexicon heist:** Ascend the Spire; bypass prime-numbered glyph traps (*True Seeing*) and the scent-keyed golem; reach the Vault of Oaths. Felkur finds Transaction 44781, **forges the Lexicon** (frames Veris, voids Maram's contract), pockets the Faerzress Shard, and begins recalibrating the teleport circle. Injured Veris and a stone golem arrive.
9. **Ch12 — Peak battle:** Veris traps Felkur (*Wall of Force*, dispelled), summons webs and a Mezzaloth (*Cloudkill*). Akritas destroys the stone golem; **Veris is killed by *Hail of Thorns*** before casting *Fireball*.
10. **Ch13 — Escape:** A second Mezzaloth disintegrates the *Wall of Force* and teleports off; fire giants storm the peak. Party teleports out as the window closes; level up. Brandyr handed to Solom for the **ten-day ritual**; party stays at Sporedome.
11. **Ch14 — Glor's contract:** Rest at Sporedome; the Emerald Enclave summons Akritas (giants targeting Hillsfar/Elventree). Glor hires the party to kill the Rogue Thought, offering siege-weapon intel. Ten-day trek to the lair; discover brain-basin locks and Arrizz imprisoned within.
12. **Ch15 — The lair:** Free and cure **Arrizz**; battle Githyanki warriors, their Knight (who *Plane Shift*s away vowing enmity), and an Intellect Devourer. Find the Zhentarim spy **Thera Grabelthum**.
13. **Ch16 — Rogue Thought & Hillsfar:** Brain-key the sealed doors; final-chamber battle destroys the Intellect Devourer and the remaining Githyanki; Thera flees invisibly and dies in the chaos. The **Rogue Thought severs his tentacles and plane-shifts away**, vowing revenge with Graz'zt. Return to Sporedome for reward (Wand of the War Mage +2). **Arrizz departs.** Solom and the Emerald Enclave call the party to **Szith Morcane / Maerimydra**. In Hillsfar, Isthana hosts a dinner courting them as business partners — they sign nothing.
