# DDEX3-04: It's All in the Blood — module proper-noun inventory

Complete inventory of NPCs, locations, items, factions, and concepts
extracted from the DDEX3-04 *It's All in the Blood* adventure module
(source ID `DDEX34IT`, via the 5etools reference server).

This is the **source-material list**, not the Hillsfar homebrew adaptation.
For campaign-specific NPC adjustments (renamed cambions, the Lexicon of Oaths
heist, the Auditor Vrax'en, High Cloak Veris, etc.) see the campaign docs and
`docs/Notebook-Summary-Adventure.md` — none of those appear in the published
module and are deliberately excluded here.

Pronunciations in parentheses are reproduced from the module's DM Appendix:
NPC Summary.

---

## NPCs

### Drow

- **Solom Ned'razak** (SOLE-um NED-rizz-ack) — male drow archwizard, deposed ruler of Szith Morcane; fled to Sporedome with the refugees; commissions the whole adventure
- **Xaven Tanor'this** (ZAY-vin tuh-NORE-this) — male drow alchemist of Szith Morcane; captured and sold to Maram il Vass; holds the knowledge to purge demon taint from blood
- **Murssvayas Dhuurniv** (mer-SVEY-yas DURR-neef) — male drow wizard; Solom's representative in Hillsfar, practical and cautious; first appeared in DDEX2-16 *Boltsmelter's Book*

### Elf

- **Nymarrath** (nye-MARR-ath) — male elf druid; aged keeper of Silver Shadow Cave; sends the enchanted messenger squirrel

### Yugoloths & fiends

- **Maram il Vass** (MAY-rim ill VAHS) — ultroloth (yugoloth) from Gehenna; collector and purveyor of secrets; runs the Den of the Whispered Word; disguises himself as a rakishly handsome half-elf via *alter self*
- **Pipyap** (PIP-YAP) — imp/devil; former familiar to a Red Wizard; gluttonous Faerûn-trotting explorer, evil but honest in dealings; first appeared in DDEX1-8 *Tales Trees Tell*
- **Grabber Forth** / **Gabber Forth** — svirfneblin mage guarding the brass doors of the Den (spelled "Grabber" in the text, "Gabber" in the rewards section)
- **Maram il Vass's servant** — unnamed, mute human commoner; a long-time captive conditioned into total devotion

### Fire giants (fiendborne)

- **Hledh** — fiendish fire giant queen; ruler of Maerimydra; the bloodline source for all fiendborne giants; dwells in Castle Maerimydra
- **Brandyr** (BRAN-deer) — female fiendborne fire giant; leads Graz'zt worship at the Six-Fingered Courtyard; a viable capture target
- **Veperyd** (veh-PUR-reed) — male fiendborne fire giant; chief of security at the Bloody Coliseum; a viable capture target

### Hags

- **Elsa**, **Ilsa**, **Ulsa** — coven of three night hags; keepers/mistresses of the Hanging Gardens

### Human prisoners & slaves

- **Drystan** and **Tegan** — human siblings; escaped slaves from the Den of the Whispered Word, encountered in the Howling Pits

### Surface / Red Plumes

- **Vuhm Yestral** — Guard Commandant of the Red Plume contingent at the Waydown sinkhole

### Historical / referenced

- **Kurgoth Hellspawn** — fire giant conqueror who sacked drow Maerimydra over a century ago and demolished the Shattered Tower; a 20-ft iron statue of him stands in the Bloody Coliseum
- **Halvin** — Pipyap's deceased last master; a human (Red) wizard in black-and-purple robes

## Deities & demon lords

- **Graz'zt** — demon lord, "the Dark Prince"; exiled to the Underdark; the source of the region's madness; worshipped at the Six-Fingered Courtyard
- **Selûne** — moon goddess; Silver Shadow Cave is a sacred site for communing with her power
- **Lolth** — drow goddess; referenced via the "Lolth-worshipping matron mothers" of old Maerimydra

## Locations

### Surface — Moonsea region

- **Hillsfar** — the City of Trade; the party's home base
- **Moonsea** — the broader region
- **Elventree** — small village near Hillsfar; a haven for non-humans against Hillsfar's prejudice
- **Silver Shadow Cave** / **Silver Cove Cave** — moonlit cave on Elventree's northern border, sacred to Selûne; site of the midnight meeting (appendix calls it "Silver Cove Cave")
- **The Waydown** — great sinkhole roughly half a day from Hillsfar; the descent into the Underdark
- **River Tesh** — the swift tributary at the bottom of the Waydown, followed into the Underdark

### Underdark

- **The Underdark** — the sunless realm below; suffused with faerzress
- **The Howling Pits** — maze of maddening, wind-howled tunnels shunned by Underdark residents
- **Den of the Whispered Word** — Maram il Vass's marble palace and extraplanar brokerage in the Howling Pits
- **Hanging Gardens** — chained-cage prison cavern below the Den, ruled by the night hag coven
- **Sporedome** — vast fungal forest with a lake; refuge of the displaced Szith Morcane drow and a myconid colony
- **Szith Morcane** — drow outpost, sacked by the fire giants; former home of Solom and Xaven (full events in DDEX3-3)
- **Inverted Tower** — Solom Ned'razak's school of arcane study within Szith Morcane
- **Black Ember Outpost** — fire giant fortress built (unknowingly) over the secret tunnel; one of several regional strongholds
- **Lake of Shadows** — underground lake feeding the pool beneath Black Ember Outpost
- **Path of Shadows** — the archmage's secret water tunnel linking Black Ember Outpost to Maerimydra

### Maerimydra (the fire giant city)

- **Maerimydra** — former drow metropolis, now a fire-and-ash city ruled by demon-blooded fire giants
- **Castle Maerimydra** — Hledh's twin-towered castle on the eastern plateau
- **Six-Fingered Courtyard** — obsidian worship plaza for Graz'zt on the western plateau, ringed by ebon pillars
- **Bloody Coliseum** — gladiatorial arena, the city's most popular venue; holds Kurgoth Hellspawn's statue
- **Shattered Tower** — ruined former center of arcane study in the abandoned northern section; mouth of the Path of Shadows; Pipyap's hideout

## Planes / cosmology

- **Gehenna** — Maram il Vass's home plane
- **The Abyss** / **Abyssal** — origin of the demonic taint and the demon lords' madness
- **The Nine Hells** — Pipyap's origin plane, which he is happy to avoid
- **The Material Plane** — where Maram built the Den as a refuge
- **The Outer Planes** — where Maram once served Graz'zt

## Items / spells / artifacts

- **Ioun Stone of Regeneration** — legendary wondrous item; recovered by Pipyap and gifted (or looted) from him
- **Gabber Forth's Spellbook** — engraved-stone pages bound in grey leather; full 1st–9th-level wizard list
- **Heartstone** — carried by each of the three night hags
- **Potion of Fire Giant Strength**, **Potion of Flying**, **Potion of Greater Healing**, **Potion of Superior Healing**, **Potion of Resistance** — assorted treasure potions
- **Spell scrolls** — *cone of cold*, *fire shield*, *globe of invulnerability*, *hold monster*, *prismatic spray*, *stoneskin*, *true seeing*
- **The Ritual / formula** — Xaven and Solom's process to purge demon taint from blood; the adventure's central macguffin (requires the blood of a living fiendborne fire giant)
- **Faerzress** — pervasive Underdark magical energy that fills areas with dim light, disrupts teleportation, and protects against divination

## Factions / titles / ranks

- **Red Plumes** — Hillsfar's mercenary military force; guard the Waydown
- **Guard Commandant** — Vuhm Yestral's rank
- **Harpers**, **Order of the Gauntlet**, **Emerald Enclave**, **Lord's Alliance**, **Zhentarim** — the five Adventurers League factions providing hooks
- **Red Wizards** (of Thay) — order Halvin belonged to
- **Matron mothers** — the Lolth-worshipping drow rulers of old Maerimydra
- **Hag coven** — the three-hag power structure governing the Hanging Gardens

---

## Events / concepts / dates

- **Rage of Demons** — the storyline/season in which the adventure is set
- **The sacking of Szith Morcane** — backstory event; depicted in DDEX3-3 *The Occupation of Szith Morcane*
- **Madness** — three-tier system (short-term, long-term, indefinite) driven by faerzress and the demon lords
- **Indefinite Madnesses of Graz'zt** — special table: **Narcissism**, **Megalomania**, **Infatuation**, **Hedonism**, **Abomination**
- **The Howling Winds** — the maddening wind effect of the Howling Pits
- **"A Deal with a Devil"** — story reward/favor for rescuing Pipyap
- **Skullies** / **"showing bones"** — Maerimydra games (polished-skull gambling; slave torture)
