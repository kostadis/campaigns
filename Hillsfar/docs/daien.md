### BARD FEATURES

**Core Bard Traits • PHB-2024 59**

**Bardic Inspiration • PHB-2024 59**
As a Bonus Action, you can inspire another creature within 60 ft. that you can see or hear you. That creature gains one of your Bardic Inspiration dice (1d6). Once within the next hour, the creature can roll the Bardic Inspiration die and add the number rolled to one ability check, attack roll, or saving throw. This use doesn't expend the die. A creature can have only one Bardic Inspiration die at a time.

You can confer your Bardic Inspiration die 2 times per Long Rest.

2 / Long Rest • 1 Bonus Action
