# Party Overview

Currently operating from Barovia, the party is engaged in confronting the dark powers that plague the land. They have collectively established themselves as capable adventurers with significant magical resources and combat experience. The group maintains a reputation for tactical effectiveness and magical versatility.

## Party Dynamics

The party consists of four experienced adventurers who complement each other well in combat and exploration. Bramgrim serves as the moral anchor and healer, while Akrita provides tactical reconnaissance. Daien brings both martial power and growing social versatility, and Felkur offers magical innovation and battlefield control.

Potential tensions exist around Felkur's criminal past and Daien's mysterious origins, though these have not manifested as active conflicts. The group appears to function effectively as a unit despite their diverse backgrounds and motivations.
