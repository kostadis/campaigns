# Party Overview

**Current location:** **Hillsfar** (City of Trade, surface) — the party returned here at the end of Chapter 16 for a private dinner with criminal matriarch Isthana (they signed nothing, door left open). Their Underdark base of operations is Sporedome, the drow refugee city.

**Active quests:**
- **March on Maerimydra (primary).** Travel to Szith Morcane, link up with Murssvayas's drow resistance, then strike at Graz'zt's hold. Mission accepted from both Solom and the Emerald Enclave; party has not yet departed.
- **Disable Maerimydra's arcane siege weapons** aimed at Hillsfar and Elventree. Glor's sabotage intel is in hand but unused.
- **Solom's ten-day blood-purge ritual** is running on the captive fire giant Brandyr; outcome pending.
- **Open threats:** Felkur's voided Maram contract (detection unknown) plus an outstanding devil deal over his location; enemies who vowed revenge (the Rogue Thought + Graz'zt, the Githyanki Knight, a loose Mezzaloth); Isthana's unsigned patronage offer.

**Collective resources:** Wand of the War Mage +2 (Glor's reward), Shard of Crystallized Faerzress, stolen runic key (Spire vaults), Vraxen's betrayal journal, the Soulcoin Ledger, blank infernal contracts, three bottles of rare wine, Potion of Fire Giant Strength, Potion of Flying, assorted potions/scrolls. Owlbear companion active.

**Group reputation:** Tactically effective and magically versatile. Felkur is now a figure of Mulmaster legend — the "Phantom Thief" — while his true identity stays hidden. The Githyanki Knight has named the party a "permanent enemy," and a Rogue Thought identified Bramgrim as the party's "greatest magical threat."

## Characters

### Akritas
**High Elf Ranger 11** — player: kostadis1

A focused hunter who prefers action over words, specializing in ranged archery with her oath-sworn Oathbow. She is a loyal but reluctant operative of the Emerald Enclave, receiving telepathic mission briefings on behalf of a faction coalition (Harpers, Order of the Gauntlet, Lords' Alliance) and openly frustrated at being pulled into their affairs. Sharp-eyed about missed opportunities — she has flagged the party's failure to mine intel (an unread ledger) and her disbelief at Felkur's reckless choices around the Shard.

**Notable relationships:** Emerald Enclave (ranked operative; access to scouts and supplies). Travels closely with Daein, Felkur, and the Owlbear. Collaborates with Felkur on alchemical distillation.

**Items of significance:** Oathbow (attuned), Bracers of Archery, +1 Studded Leather.

*(Source note: session extractions are inconsistent on Akritas's pronouns — review with the player.)*

### Bramgrim Stoutale
**Hill Dwarf Life Cleric 11** — player: kostadis1

A polite, respectful dwarf who serves as the party's primary healer, area-controller, and resident expert on Underdark lore and illusions. Once a strict "healing-only" cleric, he was shamed by Daein into embracing offensive necrotic and radiant magic, and now finishes enemies himself while still anchoring the party's survival. Deeply skeptical of surface-dwellers and Underdark entities who claim peace without proof.

**Notable relationships:** Close combat partner of Daein; protects Felkur (cast *Sanctuary* on him; co-negotiated the Pipyap contract). Works with Akritas on nature/stealth. Immediately suspicious of Glor. Cousins to the Rockseeker brothers (Gundren, Tharden, Nundo) — his strongest personal bonds. Identified by the Rogue Thought as the party's "greatest magical threat."

**Items of significance:** Mithral Plate, Javelin of Lightning, Ring of Evasion (attuned), Solom's Pendant. Rides the Owlbear when the water runs deep.

### Daein
**Human Fighter 9 / Bard 2** — player: kostadis1

A mysterious warrior-poet from "Far Far Away" whose origins are unclear even to him, blending martial power, psionic/telekinetic tricks, and emerging bardic deception. He is the party's face and master deceiver — flawless voice mimicry (Graz'zt, Veris, fire giants), staged illusory victories, and silent telekinetic theft. He declined a political post in Hillsfar's new order to avoid execution and is now chasing "FU money": financial independence to fund ventures like a soup business.

**Notable relationships:** Frequent combat partner and banter-rival of Bramgrim (whom he shamed into fighting). Collaborator with Felkur on illusion/deception. Recently intimidated Glor with a credible threat over the Ryxyg colony. History with Solom Ned'razak (doubted, then tried to recruit him) and with the drow Arrizz (recognized his voice).

**Items of significance:** +1 Plate Armor, +1 Longsword, Belt of Frost Giant Strength (Str 23), Canaith Mandolin (casts *Invisibility*; spell charges), Goggles of Night, stolen runic key.

### Felkur Oldenwood
**Rock Gnome Artillerist Artificer 11** — player: kostadis1

A guild artisan with a complicated past — stolen guild funds, his father's death, and a buried childhood trauma — who maintains a "simple gnome artificer" cover while privately owning his secret legend as Mulmaster's "Phantom Thief." He is the party's magical innovator and battlefield controller via his eldritch cannon, prone to clever legalistic schemes: he voided his Yugoloth contract with Maram il Vass by forging the *Lexicon of Oaths* to frame "Veris of Mulmaster" in his place.

**Notable relationships:** Maram il Vass (Yugoloth contract-master, now technically double-crossed — detection unknown). Daein (illusion/deception partner; cast *Invisibility* on him). Bramgrim (protector; co-crafted the Pipyap contract). Vraxen (arcanaloth enemy whose scent he extracted; threatened his library). Isthana (gifted him rare wine).

**Items of significance:** Wand of the War Mage +1 (sheet; note the party's Ch16 reward was a Wand of the War Mage +2), All-Purpose Tool +2, +1 Studded Leather (all attuned), Spell-Storing Item, eldritch cannon (cold/flamethrower infusions, ice bullets), Shard of Crystallized Faerzress.

## Party Dynamics

Four veteran adventurers who function as a tight, complementary unit despite divergent agendas. Bramgrim is the moral anchor and healer; Akritas the reconnaissance and ranged-damage core; Daein the social/deception engine and frontline; Felkur the magical-engineering and battlefield-control specialist. Daein and Bramgrim share the strongest in-combat partnership, with Daein having reshaped Bramgrim's whole approach to violence; Felkur and Daein run the party's con games; Akritas and Felkur share the lab bench.

Shared goal: the march on Maerimydra and the blow against Graz'zt. The standing tensions are not yet open conflicts but are worth watching: Felkur's criminal past and string of legalistic schemes (and the contract/devil-deal fallout he may drag onto the group), Daein's mysterious origins and money-first ambitions, Akritas's divided loyalty to the Emerald Enclave, and her voiced unease at Felkur's recklessness with the Faerzress Shard.
