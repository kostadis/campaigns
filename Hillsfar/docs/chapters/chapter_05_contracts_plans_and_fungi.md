# Chapter 05 Contracts, Plans, and Fungi

Date: Jan 19th, 2026

## Summary
1) The Dark Covenant

You have entered into a binding contract with the yugoloth Maram il Vass to retrieve the Lexicon of Oaths, a tome of great significance currently held by his rival, Vraxen.

The Ticking Clock: Maram has provided an hourglass representing the exact time remaining to secure the book before your window of opportunity closes.

The Strategy: Maram suggests creating a massive distraction by inciting a conflict between Vraxen’s demonic administration and the demon-powered giants occupying the city.

The Revelation: Felkur has finally learned the truth of his past—he did not steal a simple trinket, but the Watcher's Eye Brooch, a symbol of power that allowed him to accidentally evade the most powerful mages in Mulmaster for many years.

2) The Archmage’s Mandate

Upon returning to the drow encampment at Sporedome, you reunited with Solom Ned'razak, the ruler of Szith Morcane, who has presented a task that aligns with your own.

The Ritual: To purge the demonic taint from the region, Solom requires the blood of a living fire giant that has been infused with fiendish essence.

The Toll of War: Solom views this mission as essential for survival against the "demon-blooded pests" supported by Graz'zt.

The Reluctant Guide: Solom refuses to join the party directly, viewing himself as indispensable to the drow’s survival while considering the adventurers "important but replaceable."

3) The Infiltration Plan: The Path of Shadows

A frontal assault on Maerimydra is deemed suicidal due to the heavy guards commanded by the giantess Hledh. Instead, you will use a secret route known only to Solom and his collaborator, Xaven Tanor'this.

The Entrance: You must travel to the Black Ember Outpost, a fire giant stronghold built over a hidden tunnel four miles from the city.

The Shadows: The tunnel, known as the Pathway of the Shadows, leads directly into the Shattered Tower (Solom’s former center of study) inside Maerimydra.

The Extraction: To leave the city, you must reach The Spire of Inevitable Reckoning, a basalt tower with a green-glowing teleportation circle at its apex that Felkur intends to recalibrate for your escape.

## Locations
### Sporedome encampment
The tunnel opens up into a vast cavern, the ends of which are
lost to shadow beyond your sight. A great fungal forest spreads
out on the ground as far as you can see, with some stalks
reaching heights of 20 feet and beyond. Luminous fungus
provides soft light and you can see the dim waters of a large
lake surrounded by the fungus. Several hundred feet to the east,
tents have been erected in a hastily built camp, around which
mill several dozen drow elves.

The fungal forest is also resident to
a large colony of myconids, though the shy creatures keep
their distance from intruders. The drow and the myconid
have not yet engaged in any talks and the myconid seem
content to live and let live for the moment.

### Black Ember Outpost
 Black Ember Outpost is one of several outlying strongholds the fire giants use to maintain control over the region.

In an cave under the outpost is a lake and the entrance to the secret passageway into Maerimydra that Solom Ned'razak built.

A secret pathway that Solom Ned'razak built to leave the Shattered Tower and Maerimydra undetected from the clerics of Lolth.

Old tower of Solom Ned'razak

### The Spire of Inevitable Reckoning
A tower of sheer basalt that at the top had a glowing green room. The tower was a place of law under the Drow regime. 

The green glowing green room is a teleportation circle.
