# Chapter 06 The Bare Essentials of Black Ember

Date: Feb 1st, 2026

## Summary


The endless Underdark tunnel finally opened into a massive cavern where the polished obsidian of Black Ember Outpost was built directly into the cave wall. Through arrow slits, the party observed a Fire Giant sergeant attempting to drill a squad of miserable ogres and bored hell hounds.

Felkur had used magical manipulation to strip the ogres of their ill-fitting uniforms while Daein created the illusion of a female hellhound to distract the males, throwing the enemy into confusion. When the ambush began in earnest, Akritas delivered a devastating blow to the fire giant sergeant with her oath-sworn bow, while Bramgrim waded into battle and the Owlbear savagely finished off the remaining ogres. To maintain their element of surprise, Daein mimicked the fire giant's voice with uncanny precision, convincing anyone listening that the giants had won the battle—though in a moment of vanity, the performance included the giant remarking that the human prisoner was particularly beautiful.


The party pressed deeper into the outpost, navigating past a kennel reeking of burned hair until they reached a storeroom where more ogres hauled water from a well under the watchful eye of another fire giant. Recognizing this well as the entrance to the Path of Shadows, Bramgrim silenced the area to prevent any alarms. The ensuing fight was brutal, with the fire giant landing a bone-crushing blow on Daein that was barely mitigated, and Bramgrim losing his concentration after being struck by an ogre. After initially missing an attack and enduring mockery from Bramgrim, Daein responded with relentless fury, killing an ogre and striking the giant twice while winking at the cleric—a display that shamed Bramgrim into abandoning his healing-only approach to unleash devastating magic. Akritas delivered the final blow to the fire giant, and the room was secured.
