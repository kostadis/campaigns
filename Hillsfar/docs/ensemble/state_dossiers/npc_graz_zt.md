### Graz'zt

**Status:** Alive (Demon Lord)
**Current Location:** Not currently present on the Material Plane; presence faded after Chapter 1.
**Allegiance/Faction:** Independent Demon Lord; controls Maerimydra and Hillsfar.
**Current Possessions/Notable Items:**
*   **Symbol:** A six-fingered hand.
*   **Corruption:** An active corruption being utilized by fire giants in Szith Morcane to unleash upon the surface world.
*   **Madness:** Currently held back by "brain-capped mushrooms" (implying his full madness is suppressed but his corruption remains active).
**Current Assignment/Role:**
*   **Primary Goal:** Seeking revenge for a thwarted plot; both he and "Rogue Thought" intend to exact this revenge.
*   **Ongoing Projects:**
    *   Infusing fire giants with his blood/fiendish blood via a deal made with the giants.
    *   Supporting "demon-blooded pests" viewed as threats by Solom.
    *   Deploying agents to ensure plans are carried out (specifically brought in Vraxen for this purpose).
**Key Relationships:**
*   **Vraxen:** An agent brought in by Graz'zt to ensure his plans are executed.
*   **Fire Giants:** Allies/beneficiaries of a deal to imbue them with fiendish blood.
*   **Rogue Thought:** An associate with whom Graz'zt intends to share in seeking revenge.
*   **Daein:** A character who previously mimicked Graz'zt's voice to deceive Cambion guards.
*   **The Party:** The entity whose actions thwarted Graz'zt's initial plot.
**Defining Recent Actions:**
*   Threatened vengeance against the party for thwarting a plot.
*   Initiated a deal with fire giants to spread his corruption and blood.
*   Deployed Vraxen to oversee operations.
*   Maintains control over Maerimydra and Hillsfar.

## Uncertainty
*   **Current Physical Location:** While facts state his presence "faded from the material plane" in Chapter 1, the entity is still active (infusing giants, controlling cities, planning revenge). It is unclear if he is currently on the Material Plane in a different form, in the Abyss, or projecting power remotely.
*   **Status of Vraxen:** The facts state Graz'zt "brought in Vraxen" in Chapter 7, but do not confirm Vraxen's current status (alive, dead, still acting on orders, or if the mission is complete).
*   **Status of the Fire Giants:** The facts confirm the deal to imbue them with blood and the work in Szith Morcane, but do not specify the current extent of the infusion or if the giants are currently under full control.
*   **Status of "Rogue Thought":** This entity is mentioned as seeking revenge alongside Graz'zt, but no facts define who or what Rogue Thought is, nor their current location or relationship to Graz'zt beyond this shared goal.
*   **Chosen's Fate:** Chapter 4 mentions "rumors" of Graz'zt's Chosen being killed in Hillsfar; it is unconfirmed if these rumors are true or if any Chosen remain.
