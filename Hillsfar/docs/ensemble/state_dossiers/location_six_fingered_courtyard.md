### Six-Fingered Courtyard
**Type:** Location / Ritual Site
**Current State:** Active ritual area on a raised plateau in the western section of Maerimydra.
**Architecture:** Features a polished obsidian floor surrounded by ebony pillars (formerly described as black spires), each emblazoned with a prominent crimson six-fingered hand symbol of Graz'zt.
**Current Activity:** Site of a recent party ambush where the fire giant Brandyr was kidnapped; the party subsequently retreated from the location.
**Historical Context:** Previously served as a gathering point for demons and giants to worship a demon lord (specifically Graz'zt).

## Uncertainty
None.
