### Hillsfar

**Type:** Major Surface City
**Current Status:** Active and recovering. The city is currently stable following the defeat of Malkyn Grenefeld (Ch01) and the thwarting of Graz'zt's control (Ch05, Ch07). It is the primary location where the party recently returned to meet with Isthana regarding business ventures (Ch16).
**Location:** Surface world.
**Threat Level:** High. The city is a confirmed primary target for the fire giants' siege weapons, alongside Elventree (Ch14).
**Notable History (Context):** Previously saved from demonic possession and Graz'zt's influence by the party and Felkur. Rumors persist that Graz'zt's Chosen were killed within its borders (Ch04).
**Current Intelligence:** A highly detailed map of every street, building, and infrastructure piece within Hillsfar was discovered in a rogue's lair (Ch14).
**Key Relationships:** The party is recognized as local heroes who saved the city. They recently met with Isthana here to discuss starting a business.

## Uncertainty
- **Survival Probability:** While Murssvayas Dhuurniv warned in Ch01 that Hillsfar would not survive a fire giant attack, and Ch14 confirms the city is a target, the facts do not state whether the attack has occurred yet or if the city has been destroyed. The dossier assumes the city is currently intact based on Ch16 events, but the outcome of the impending siege is unknown.
- **Rumor Verification:** The specific details regarding the killing of "Graz'zt's Chosen" in Hillsfar are cited only as rumors (Ch04); no fact confirms this event definitively occurred or identifies the perpetrators.
