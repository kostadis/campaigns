### Maram il Vass

**Current Status & Identity**
*   **Status:** Alive and active.
*   **True Form:** A seven-foot-tall Yugoloth (demon) with demonic jaws and claws.
*   **Appearance:** Projects a permanent illusion of a "rakishly handsome, chic half-elf" to observers lacking magical sight (e.g., *True Seeing*).
*   **Location:** Resides in the **Den of the Whispered Word**, an extraplanar brokerage located in the **Howling Pits**. He maintains an opulent office with a desk of unusual purple wood and access to hanging gardens below.

**Allegiance & Role**
*   **Role:** Proprietor of an extraplanar brokerage specializing in the trade of information, power, and binding contracts.
*   **Nature:** A master of meticulous, binding agreements; cunning, manipulative, and unconcerned with the loss of subordinate minions (e.g., demon guards) if "hags" can handle the fallout.
*   **Current Objective:** Actively seeking to acquire **The Lexicon of Oaths**.

**Current Possessions & Assets**
*   **Property:** Owns the Den of the Whispered Word (palace/office complex) in the Howling Pits.
*   **Items:**
    *   Possesses a **key** to the hanging gardens (previously given to the party to retrieve Xaven).
    *   Holds an **hourglass** representing the time limit for retrieving the Lexicon (previously given to the party).
    *   Owns a contract regarding **Felkur's location** (which he is obligated to reveal to a devil unless a new deal is made).
*   **Debt:** Owes a specific, unnamed magical item to another demon (the subject of the deal proposed to Felkur in Chapter 4).

**Current Assignment & Relationships**
*   **The Party:** Currently in a **binding contract** with the party to retrieve *The Lexicon of Oaths*. The party realized in Chapter 11 that Maram "engineered a double cross" by selling Felkur's information and placing the contract on them to steal the very book they now hold.
*   **Felkur:** The target of a long-standing search due to an allegedly stolen item. Maram proposed a deal to Felkur (retrieve the magical item owed to another demon) in exchange for withholding his location from a devil.
*   **Xaven Tanor'this:** Previously held as a prisoner (acquired from fire giants) and used as leverage; the party retrieved him via the hanging gardens.
*   **Teegan & Drystan:** Previously held as captives.
*   **Bramgrim:** Previously suggested by Maram as a trade target for Xaven.
*   **Vraxen:** Maram instructed the party to steal the Lexicon from Vraxen's administration.

**Defining Recent Actions**
*   Engineered a scheme where he sold Felkur's information to a devil, then contracted the party to retrieve *The Lexicon of Oaths* from Vraxen, effectively setting the party up to steal the book for him while they believed they were fulfilling a different obligation.
*   Revealed his true Yugoloth form after Bramgrim cast *True Seeing*.
*   Negotiated the release of Xaven Tanor'this in exchange for the party's services and information.

## Uncertainty
*   **Current Location of Felkur:** While Maram has a deal to reveal Felkur's location to a devil unless a new arrangement is made, the facts do not confirm if this deadline has passed or if the location was actually revealed.
*   **Current Status of Xaven Tanor'this:** Facts confirm Maram held him and the party retrieved him via a key in Chapter 4, but there is no explicit fact stating Xaven is currently free, dead, or still in Maram's custody (though retrieval implies release).
*   **The "Specific Magical Item":** Maram owes a specific magical item to another demon, but the facts never name the item itself, only that it is the subject of a deal with Felkur.
*   **The Devil's Identity:** Maram has a deal with "a devil" regarding Felkur, but the devil's specific name or identity is not provided in the facts.
