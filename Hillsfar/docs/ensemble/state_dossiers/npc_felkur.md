### Felkur

**Current Status:** Alive. Felkur maintains his cover as a "simple gnome artificer" while privately acknowledging his secret past identity as the legendary "Phantom Thief" of Mulmaster.

**Current Location:** The party is currently engaged in combat within a chamber containing a Mind Flayer and Githyanki warriors (Chapter 16). Prior to this, he was in the Sporedome (Chapter 14) and the Tower of Vraxen (Chapter 11-12).

**Allegiance & Role:**
*   **Primary Role:** Artificer and tactical support for the adventuring party.
*   **Contractual Obligation:** Bound by a contract with the Yugoloth **Maram il Vass** to retrieve the *Lexicon of Oaths*. However, Felkur discovered a legal loophole: the contract is void if the book no longer contains information identifying him. He has successfully altered the Lexicon to replace his name with **High Inquisitor Veris of Mulmaster**, effectively nullifying his obligation to deliver the tome to Maram il Vass while avoiding the "no-betrayal" clause's cosmic curse.
*   **Reputation:** Publicly viewed by the Brotherhood of Cloaks as a potential Level 20 Wizard or a chosen of Mask, and an "Army" leader. He is now technically a figure of legend known as the "Phantom Thief," though his true identity remains hidden from the public.

**Current Possessions & Gear:**
*   **Eldritch Cannon:** A modified cannon equipped with a **cold infusion** (capable of ice damage) and flamethrower capabilities. He recently maneuvered it down a chute to threaten Vraxen's library.
*   **Specialized Ammunition:** Prepared **ice bullets** for elemental attacks.
*   **Armor:** Infused armor providing **17 AC**.
*   **Spell Storing Item:** Capable of storing spells (previously discussed *Cure Wounds*, *Enlarge/Reduce*, *Levitate*).
*   **Consumables:**
    *   **Four tokens** remaining from a specialized magical item used to cast high-potency *Sleep*.
    *   **Three bottles of rare wine**, a personal gift from **Isthana**.
    *   **Shard of Crystallized Faerzress:** A large pure crystal stolen in Chapter 11 to enhance artificer abilities (currently in his pack).
    *   **Flavor-Extraction Device:** A gadget originally built to remove unwanted food flavors, repurposed to extract scents (used to extract Vraxen's scent from an Owlbear).
*   **Tools:** Expert thieves' tools and artificer tools used for bypassing locks and recalibrating arcane mechanisms.

**Current Abilities & Conditions:**
*   **Spells:** Capable of casting *Firebolt*, *Fireball*, *Catapult*, *Mage Hand*, *Wall of Force* (maintained concentration through poison and fire), and *Sleep*.
*   **Infusions:** Can infuse armor and create temporary fake magical items.
*   **Contractual Buff:** Under the terms of his contract with Maram il Vass, he possesses a **bonus on saving throws against fear**.
*   **Identity Ward:** The *Watcher's Eye Brooch* (sold for 45 gold while intoxicated) granted him accidental imperviousness to magical location for years, creating his "Phantom Thief" legend.

**Key Relationships:**
*   **Maram il Vass:** A Yugoloth master of contracts. Felkur negotiated favorable terms and a "no-betrayal" clause, but has since technically voided the deal by altering the Lexicon.
*   **Daein:** A companion who provided bardic inspiration during negotiations and cast *Invisibility* on Felkur during the heist in the Tower.
*   **Bramgrim:** A companion who assisted in crafting the contract with Pipyap.
*   **High Inquisitor Veris:** The name Felkur forged in the *Lexicon of Oaths* to frame him for the theft of the *Watcher's Eye Brooch*.
*   **Vraxen:** An arcanaloth whose scent Felkur extracted to bypass a stone golem; Felkur also threatened Vraxen's library with his cannon.

**Defining Recent Actions:**
*   **The Lexicon Heist:** Recalibrated a teleportation circle to enter the Vault of Oaths, bypassed a Vraxen-keyed golem using extracted scent, and altered the *Lexicon of Oaths* to frame Veris, thereby voiding his contract with Maram il Vass.
*   **Tactical Sabotage:** Maneuvered his eldritch cannon down a chute to threaten Vraxen's library, forcing a tactical shift.
*   **Combat Support:** Maintained concentration on a *Wall of Force* to protect the party while under heavy assault (poison, fire) and recently unleashed a massive *Fireball* targeting a Mind Flayer and Githyanki warriors.
*   **Underdark Engineering:** Constructed a specialized distillery in the Sporedome using Hallucinogenic Mushrooms.

## Uncertainty
*   **Current Contract Status:** While the facts state Felkur *altered* the Lexicon to void the contract, it is not explicitly confirmed whether Maram il Vass has *detected* this alteration or if the "no-betrayal" clause (which threatens a cosmic curse if the Yugoloth switches sides or reveals the location) is still active or if the contract is fully dissolved. The facts say he "discovered a loophole" and "successfully changed the name," but the immediate magical consequence of this act on the binding nature of the contract in real-time is not explicitly stated as "resolved."
*   **Watcher's Eye Brooch Location:** The brooch was sold for 45 gold pieces (Ch 05, Ch 09, Ch 11). It is not currently in Felkur's possession, but the current location of the brooch (beyond being sold to Limping Oric in the past) is unknown.
*   **Faerzress Shard Status:** Felkur stole the shard in Chapter 11 and pocketed it. While he has it in his pack, there is no confirmation of whether he has used it, sold it, or lost it since Chapter 11.
*   **Brotherhood of Cloaks Awareness:** The facts state the Brotherhood *believes* he is a Level 20 Wizard or chosen of Mask, but it is unclear if they currently know his specific location or have actively engaged him since the "Phantom Thief" legend was solidified.
*   **Maram il Vass's Reaction:** It is unknown if Maram il Vass is aware that Felkur has altered the Lexicon to void the contract, or if the Yugoloth is currently pursuing him for the "betrayal" implied by the voided terms.
