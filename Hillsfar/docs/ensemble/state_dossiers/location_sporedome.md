### Sporedome

**Current State & Description**
A vibrant, luminous fungal city and encampment located deep within a vast cavern in the Underdark. The environment is characterized by a diverse fungal forest filled with hallucinogenic mushrooms.

**Control & Inhabitants**
*   **Primary Inhabitants:** Drow survivors (specifically refugees from Szith Morcane).
*   **Key Figures:** Solom Ned'razak (primary leader and researcher) and Xaven Tanor'this.
*   **Current Leadership Role:** Serves as the operational base for Solom Ned'razak's magical research.

**Current Activities & Relationships**
*   **Alliance:** Functions as the headquarters for the Mind Flayer Glor's hired party. The Mind Flayer resides or operates from here to assign tasks and distribute rewards.
*   **Recent Events:** The location served as the meeting point where the party reported their recent success to the Mind Flayer Glor and received their reward.
*   **Intelligence Status:** Subject of active intelligence gathering by Thera regarding local operations and Duergar movements.
*   **Party Usage:** Recently utilized by the party as a secure location for extended rest, during which they established a temporary distillery.

## Uncertainty
None.
