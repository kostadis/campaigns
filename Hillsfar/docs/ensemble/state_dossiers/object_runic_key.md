### Runic Key
**Current Status:** In the possession of the party.
**Current Location:** Secured by the party (previously on the obsidian dining table during the dinner chaos).
**Function:** The sole means of accessing the upper vaults of the Spire of Inevitable Reckoning, where the *Lexicon of Oaths* is kept.
**Acquisition:** Stolen from Vraxen's person using telekinesis during a dinner event; Daein specifically pulled the key into the party's possession after it was snatched.
**Significance:** Previously carried by Vraxen; now the party holds the only known key to the upper vaults.

## Uncertainty
None.
