*   **Nature:** A sacred site dedicated to the moon goddess Selûne.

    *   Contains a natural phenomenon where moonlight filters mysteriously into the subterranean chamber.
    *   Atmosphere includes floating "moats" of pollen.

**Recent Actions**

*   The party initiated their current expedition from this location (Chapter 2).
