### Lexicon of Oaths

**Current Location:** The Vault of Oaths, specifically resting on a central stone pedestal beneath a crystal dome in the upper vaults of the Spire.

**Physical Description:** A massive tome bound in devilhide. Its pages are crafted from processed souls that glow with a soft, eerie inner light.

**Current Function & Content:**
*   **Infernal Ledger:** The book automatically updates itself in real-time with every infernal contract struck across Maerimydra.
*   **Specific Records:** Contains a record of Felkur's drunken sale of a priceless artifact (an entry Felkur subsequently altered). It also likely contains proof of Felkur's identity as the "Phantom Ghost" thief of Mulmaster.
*   **Relevance:** Contains critical information required by Maram il Vass.

**Recent Interactions:** The party located the tome, discovered Felkur's specific entry, and read a contract aloud within the vault.
