### Hanging Gardens

**Current State & Description**
A vast cavern located 100 feet tall, situated directly beneath Maram il Vass's office and palace. The area is illuminated by luminescent fungus creating an eerie pale glow. An unusual buzzing sound echoes throughout the cavern.

**Current Occupation & Control**
*   **Guardians:** The facility is currently guarded by three hags who arrived to take over the duty from the Chasme Demons.
*   **Prisoners:** The cavern contains numerous cages suspended by heavy chains running from floor to ceiling. Most cages are occupied by single prisoners, including Xaven Tanor'this (who was held here by Maram il Vass).

**Recent Actions**
*   Three hags arrived to assume guard duties over the prisoners.
*   The party descended the stairs into the cavern to retrieve a prisoner at Maram il Vass's direction.
