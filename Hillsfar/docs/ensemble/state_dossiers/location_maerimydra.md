### Maerimydra

**Current Status & Control**
A former drow metropolis currently occupied and controlled by fire giants who have formed a pact with the demon lord Graz'zt. The city is demon-haunted and aligned with demonic evil forces.

**Location & Environment**
Situated in the Underdark within a titanic cavern 600 feet tall at its highest point. The environment is characterized by fire, ash, and a heavy charcoal scent generated by numerous burning braziers. A thick layer of smoke hangs above 250 feet.

**Architecture & Key Sites**
*   **Layout:** Features huge buildings and plateaus 100 feet high connected by 100-foot-wide grand stairs.
*   **Southern District:** Contains a massive coliseum in the southern residential district.
*   **Western Plateau:** Features a six-fingered symbol of Graz'zt above a courtyard.
*   **Eastern District:** Contains a castle with two towers.
*   **Spire of Inevitable Reckoning:** A high-security spire within the city currently being infiltrated by the party.

**Notable Assets & Holdings**
*   **Military:** Fire giants of Maerimydra have constructed devastating arcane siege weapons.
*   **Artifacts:** The *Lexicon of Oaths* is located within the city.

**Recent Activity**
The city served as the launch point for the fiendish fire giant and demon attack that sacked Szith Morcane. Currently, the party is executing an infiltration plan within the city's high-security spire.
