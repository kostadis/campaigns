### Szith Morcane
**Current State:** An Underdark city currently controlled by fire giants.
**Location:** The Underdark, situated near an abundance of Faerzress.
**History/Recent Events:** Originally a Drow outpost, it was sacked by an army of slaves led by fiendish fire giants and demons. Following the sack, fire giants took control of the city.
**Current Activity:** The fire giants controlling the city are nearing completion of their work.
**Significance:** Identified in an Emerald Enclave message as a location of interest due to the nearby Faerzress.

## Uncertainty
- **Specific Nature of "Work":** The facts state fire giants are "nearing completion of their work," but the specific nature or goal of this project is not detailed.
- **Current Occupants:** While fire giants are confirmed as controllers, the specific status of the original Drow population (enslaved, dead, or displaced) or the presence of the demons/slave army mentioned in the sack is not explicitly resolved in the current state facts.
