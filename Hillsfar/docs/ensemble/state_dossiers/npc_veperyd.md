### Veperyd

**Current Status:** Alive.
**Race/Physiology:** Fire giant infused with demonic blood (mutated); skin weeps oily black smoke.
**Current Location:** Present at the Spire of Inevitable Reckoning (specifically the dining hall).
**Allegiance/Faction:** Operates under Vraxen (host of the Spire), though currently in active conflict with him.
**Role & Responsibilities:** Runs the Colosseum in Maerimydra, overseeing duels and the betting pool.
**Notable Items/Condition:** Skin constantly weeping oily black smoke due to mutation.
**Recent Actions & Current Situation:**
*   Engaged in a tense three-way confrontation in the dining hall with Vraxen and High Inquisitor Veris.
*   Responded to Vraxen's accusations with "barely restrained volcanic rage."
*   Roared a promise to repay a debt in blood.
*   Previously identified as stealing money from Vraxen (a point of contention at the dinner).
*   Described by Pipyap (Ch07) as "a bit of an idiot," though this is an external character assessment rather than a current state fact.

## Uncertainty
