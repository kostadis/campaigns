### Glor

**Current Status:** Alive.
**Location:** Underdark (specifically representing the Ryxyg illithid encampment).
**Identity:** Mind flayer (illithid) who communicates telepathically and wears a cowl.
**Allegiance:** Ryxyg illithid encampment. Claims his community has chosen peaceful cohabitation over the typical illithid path of enslavement and conquest.
**Current Assignment/Role:** Has hired the party to hunt and destroy a "rogue thought" (a mind flayer disconnected from the collective).
**Notable Items/Assets:** Provided the party with a map illustrating the path of least resistance through the Underdark to the rogue mind flayer's lair.
**Key Relationships:**
*   **The Party:** Initially dismissed them, but now respects them and has formally hired them as agents.
*   **Daein:** Specifically targeted by Glor's dark humor regarding brain consumption; Glor stated Daein's brain would be the last eaten if forced to choose.
**Recent Actions:**
*   Warned the party of fire giant siege weapons aimed at surface cities.
*   Offered strategic intelligence on how to disable Maerimydra's arcane siege weapons without identifying the perpetrators.
*   Formalized the contract to eliminate the rogue mind flayer.
**Motivations:** To maintain the collective's restraint by eliminating rogue elements; to ensure the survival of his community through peaceful coexistence rather than conquest.
