### Vraxen

**Current Status:** Alive (last seen alive during the dinner confrontation in Chapter 11; Chapter 12 references him as an "archmage" to be drawn away from battle, implying he remains a living threat).
**Current Location:** Spire of Inevitable Reckoning (Maerimydra).
**Allegiance/Faction:** Yugoloth (specifically an Arcanaloth); ostensibly serves Graz'zt as an enforcer of contracts, but possesses secret plans to betray his Yugoloth superiors.
**Role & Assignment:** Host of the Spire of Inevitable Reckoning; enforcer of Graz'zt's contracts in Maerimydra; manager of Graz'zt's deal with the Fire Giants; oversees the Coliseum's betting pool (in partnership with Veperyd).
**Current Possessions & Traits:**
*   **Items:** Possesses the *Lexicon of Oaths* (the book the party seeks); carries a runic key granting access to the upper vaults of the Spire.
*   **Physical Traits:** Arcanaloth with a fox-like face.
*   **Traits:** Obsessed with financial accuracy.
*   **Wounds/Conditions:** No active wounds recorded; however, his scent was extracted from an Owlbear by Felkur (Ch11), and a stone golem guarding the vault is keyed to recognize his scent specifically (Ch10).
**Key Relationships:**
*   **Rivals:** Maram il Vass (rival for the *Lexicon of Oaths*).
*   **Associates:** Veperyd (business partner regarding betting pool; recent target of accusations regarding skimming); High Inquisitor Veris (present at the same dinner).
*   **Superiors:** Graz'zt (officially); Yugoloth superiors (secretly plotting betrayal).
**Recent Actions:**
*   Hosted a dinner at the Spire of Inevitable Reckoning attended by Veperyd and High Inquisitor Veris.
*   Accused Veperyd of skimming from the betting pool.
*   Shrieked about treason and ordered guards to flay a giant (presumably a Fire Giant involved in the deal) after being tricked into believing a conspiracy between Veris and Veperyd.
*   Was distracted by an engineered three-way confrontation between himself, Veperyd, and Veris, allowing the party to exploit the situation.
*   Maintains a personal journal detailing plans to betray his Yugoloth superiors.

## Uncertainty

The dossier assumes he is alive and a target, but his immediate tactical position during or after the Chapter 12 battle is not explicitly resolved by the provided facts.
