### Lolth

> **DEITY — synthesis note:** Lolth is a D&D 5e goddess (the Spider Queen, Demon Queen
> of Spiders) — not a mortal NPC. The facts below reflect only her campaign presence as
> perceived by characters: her clergy losing and regaining the ability to commune, and
> her escape/restoration via "the Blink" event. Full lore and stat blocks are in 5etools.
> She acts through the drow and her clerics, so treat her as an off-screen divine force
> whose **followers (Lolth-worshipping drow, matron mothers, clerics) are the party's
> interaction surface**, not an addressable mortal. As the party levels, direct
> interaction (omens, an avatar, the Demonweb Pits) becomes plausible.

**Status:** Alive (escaped imprisonment)
**Current Location:** Unspecified (reestablishing dominion over followers)
**Allegiance/Faction:** Drow Goddess
**Current Assignment / Role:** Reestablishing dominion over her followers; organizing her clerics.
**Key Relationships:** Her clerics (previously unable to commune, now being reorganized).
**Current Possessions / Powers:** Full divine powers returned (restored after "the Blink" event).
**Defining Recent Actions:** Used the "Blink" event to escape imprisonment; initiated the reorganization of her clergy.
**Revealed Motivations / Secrets:** Previously imprisoned; her powers were severed until the "Blink" event allowed her escape and restoration.
