### Solom Ned'razak

**Current Status & Location**
*   **Status:** Alive.
*   **Location:** The Sporedome (a fungal forest encampment).
*   **Role:** Archwizard and ruler of the drow outpost of Szith Morcane; leader of the drow forces in the region.
*   **Allegiance:** Loyal to the survival of the drow; actively subverts the power of Lolth's clerics in Maerimydra.

**Current Possessions & Appearance**
*   **Attire:** Wears finely crafted purple robes trimmed with silver.
*   **Notable Items:** Possesses a secret tunnel network connecting the Shattered Tower (his former residence) to bypass Lolth-worshipping matron mothers; hopes this tunnel remains unblocked.
*   **Physical State:** Previously described with a gaunt face and harried eyes (Chapter 05); no current wounds reported in later chapters.

**Current Assignment & Goals**
*   **Primary Objective:** Completing a ten-day ritual to purge demonic taint from the region and push back the "invaders" (demons and demon-blooded entities).
*   **Ritual Requirements:** Requires the blood of a living fire giant infused with fiendish essence.
*   **Strategic Outlook:** Firmly believes the invaders will be defeated; views the current battle as a victory but warns the war is far from over.
*   **Recent Directive:** Instructed the party to return to Hillsfar during the ritual's ten-day duration, though the party chose to remain at the Sporedome.

**Key Relationships**
*   **Xaven Tanor'this:** Travels and researches with Solom; Solom thanked the party for recovering Xaven from the palace of Maram il Vass.
*   **The Party:** Views them as "important but replaceable" assets; refuses to join them directly, citing his own indispensability to drow survival.
*   **Murssvayas Dhuurniv:** Designated as the contact for remaining in communication with the party during the ritual wait.
*   **Daein:** Previously explained the mechanics of a portable hole to him.

**Defining Recent Actions & Secrets**
*   **The Blink Event:** Blames powerful wizards and liches from Menzoberranzan for the "Blink" event in the faerzress; expressed frustration that he did not conceive of it himself.
*   **Lolth's Clerics:** His notes reveal that Lolth's clerics, previously silent, suddenly regained their powers after the Blink.
*   **Demon Negotiations:** Historically attempted to negotiate with demons but concluded they are solely interested in chaos and destruction.
*   **Subversion:** Long-term efforts to undermine the power of Lolth's clerics within Maerimydra.

## Uncertainty
*   **Current Physical Condition:** While Chapter 05 describes him as having a "gaunt face and harried eyes," Chapter 13 mentions him rushing out to greet the party but does not reiterate this physical state. It is unclear if he has recovered his vigor or if the "gaunt" description remains current.
*   **Status of the Secret Tunnel:** Solom *hopes* the tunnel from the Shattered Tower has not been blocked by time or giants (Chapter 05), but there is no confirmation in later chapters whether the tunnel is currently passable or blocked.
*   **Completion of Ritual:** Facts state the ritual *will* take ten days (Chapter 13), but the current chapter (16) only references Solom as an archmage "at the end of the session" without confirming if the ten-day period has elapsed or if the ritual has concluded.
