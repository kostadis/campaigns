### Black Ember Outpost

**Current Status:** Captured and cleared by the party; formerly a fire giant stronghold.
**Location:** Situated four miles from Maerimydra, built directly into the wall of a massive Underdark cavern at the end of an endless tunnel. The cavern floor is smoothed and devoid of natural formations, with stalactites hanging hundreds of feet above.
**Structure:** A great, black fortress constructed of polished obsidian. The only entrance is a set of obsidian double doors. Firelight was previously visible through wide arrow slits, and the interior was illuminated by natural fungal growth.
**Subterranean Features:** Beneath the fortress lies a cave containing a large pool; the southern-most portion of this pool houses the entrance to a secret passageway leading to Maerimydra.
**Current Control:** The party assaulted the outpost and "cleared it out," standing victorious over the carnage.
**Former Inhabitants:** Served as a base for fire giants, ogres, and hellhounds.
**Historical Context:** Built by fire giants over a hidden tunnel exit without realizing the tunnel's significance as a route to Maerimydra.

## Uncertainty
- None. (All facts regarding current ownership and status are consistent: the party assaulted and cleared the outpost in Chapter 7, overriding earlier descriptions of it being an active stronghold.)
