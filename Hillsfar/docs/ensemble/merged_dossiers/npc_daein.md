### Daein

**Current Status:** Alive and active.
**Current Location:** With the party (recently engaged in combat against Githyanki and a Mind Flayer).
**Allegiance:** Member of the adventuring party; explicitly declined a political position in Hillsfar's new order (Chapter 1) to avoid execution.
**Current Assignment / Role:** Frontline combatant and party face/deceiver. Currently pursuing the acquisition of "FU money" (financial independence) through business ventures (soups, trade) and adventuring.

**Current Possessions & Gear:**
*   **Armor:** Magical Plate Mail (+1) (acquired Chapter 1).
*   **Vision:** Enchanted goggles allowing clear vision in the Underdark (purchased Chapter 2).
*   **Weapons:** Longsword, Longbow, and an axe (used in Chapter 7).
*   **Magic Item:** A magical mandolin used to cast *Invisibility* (Chapter 11).
*   **Stolen Items:** Possesses a runic key (stolen via telekinesis in Chapter 9; confirmed in possession in Chapter 11).

**Abilities & Traits:**
*   **Spells/Castings:** *Pass Without Trace*, *Reduce*, *Minor Illusion* (used to mimic voices of High Inquisitor Veris and Demon Lord Graz'zt), *Invisibility* (via mandolin).
*   **Class Features:** Action Surge, Protective Field (reduces damage to self and allies), Second Wind (healing), Psionic ability (reduces damage taken), Sap ability (imposes disadvantage on allies' attacks).
*   **Skills:** High proficiency in Deception (rolled natural 20), Investigation, Arcana, and Stealth (boosted to +20 via *Pass Without Trace*).
*   **Notable Feats:** Can mimic voices flawlessly (Graz'zt, Veris, Fire Giants); can use telekinetic power to move objects silently.
*   **Current Wounds:** None reported; utilized Second Wind to recover to full health in Chapter 13.

**Key Relationships:**
*   **Bramgrim:** Frequent combat partner; Daein has protected him repeatedly and engaged in banter/shaming.
*   **Felkur:** Collaborator in illusions and deception; Daein cast *Invisibility* on him.
*   **Glor (Mind Flayer):** Recent target of intimidation; Daein issued a credible threat regarding the Ryxyg colony if intelligence promises were broken.
*   **Solom Ned'razak:** Daein questioned Solom's trustworthiness (Chapter 1) but later attempted to recruit him to the party (Chapter 5).
*   **Arrizz:** A Drow encountered in the past; Daein recognized his voice in Chapter 14.

**Defining Recent Actions:**
*   **Deception & Infiltration:** Successfully staged a fake battle victory using auditory illusions and voice mimicry to deceive Fire Giants (Ch 6-7); deceived the Yugoloth Maram il Vass regarding armor origins (Ch 4); stole a runic key using telekinesis (Ch 9).
*   **Combat:** Led charges against Githyanki warriors (Ch 15-16) and a Mind Flayer (Ch 16); defeated multiple demons, ogres, and a Mezzaloth; blocked a stone golem at a staircase (Ch 10-11).
*   **Motivation:** Explicitly declared a goal of acquiring "FU money" (financial independence) to fund future business ventures like soup sales (Ch 14).
