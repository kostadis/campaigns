### Order of the Gauntlet

**Current Status & Standing**
A powerful faction operating as part of a coalition alongside the Emerald Enclave, the Harpers, and the Lords' Alliance. They are currently the sole faction trusted by the rogue mind flayer to receive direct intelligence regarding Graz'zt's operations.

**Current Goals & Assignments**
*   **Coalition Objectives:** The faction is actively participating in a coalition effort to summon Akritas.
*   **Maerimydra Campaign:** They requested the party's assistance specifically regarding the situation in Maerimydra and were involved in recommending the party for the task of eliminating a rogue mind flayer.
*   **Intelligence Acquisition:** They are currently the designated recipient of vital intelligence regarding Graz'zt's operations, which the rogue mind flayer has promised to pass directly to them.

**Key Relationships**
*   **The Rogue Mind Flayer:** The only faction the mind flayer trusts enough to deal with directly; the creature has promised to share intelligence on Graz'zt with them.
*   **The Party:** The faction requested the party's aid and recommended them for the mind flayer elimination task.
*   **Allies:** The Emerald Enclave, the Harpers, and the Lords' Alliance (fellow coalition members).

**Recent Actions**
*   Requested party assistance for Maerimydra.
*   Recommended the party to eliminate the rogue mind flayer.
*   Established a direct intelligence channel with the rogue mind flayer regarding Graz'zt.
