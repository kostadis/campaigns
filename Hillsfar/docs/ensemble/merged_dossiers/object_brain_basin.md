### Brain Basin

**Type:** Magical Object / Security Mechanism

**Current State & Location:**
Multiple stone basins currently exist within the rogue mind flayer's lair. They are distributed across specific locations: the first chamber, the second chamber, and the fungal chamber.

**Description:**
Carved from stone to resemble the underside of a human brain.

**Function:**
Serves as a macabre biological key mechanism for arcanely sealed doors within the lair. To activate the mechanism and open the corresponding door, an actual brain must be placed inside the basin.

**Current Condition:**
The basins are functional security devices; the party has identified the requirement (actual brains) but the current state of the doors (open/closed) or whether any basins currently contain a brain is not explicitly stated in the provided facts.

## Uncertainty
- **Specific Count:** While facts state the party discovered "multiple" basins and list three specific locations (first chamber, second chamber, fungal chamber), the exact total number of basins in the lair is not quantified.
- **Current Door Status:** The facts confirm the mechanism requires a brain to open a door, but do not specify whether any of the corresponding doors are currently open or closed, or if any basin currently holds a brain.
