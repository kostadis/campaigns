### Eldritch Cannon

**Current Status:** Active and deployed by Felkur.
**Current Location:** In use alongside Felkur during combat operations (last seen engaging a stone golem).
**Current Holder/Controller:** Felkur.
**Capabilities:** A magical device capable of firing crackling blasts of energy (previously described as fire) at enemies. It possesses the ability to swivel to target specific areas, such as a golem's flank.
**Recent Actions:** Deployed by Felkur (while he remained invisible) to blast a stone golem, specifically targeting its flank with a crackling energy blast.
**Significance:** Serves as a ranged offensive asset for Felkur, utilized in both the portal chamber battle and subsequent encounters against constructs.

## Uncertainty
*   **Damage Type Discrepancy:** Chapter 10 facts explicitly describe the blasts as "fire," while Chapter 11 facts describe them as "energy" and "crackling." It is unclear if the cannon's output changed, if the description evolved, or if "energy" is a broader category encompassing fire.
*   **Current Physical Location:** While the cannon was last seen deployed against a stone golem in Chapter 11, the facts do not specify if it is still active, destroyed, or where exactly it is situated relative to Felkur after that specific engagement concluded.
