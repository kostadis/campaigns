### Den of the Whispered Word

**Type:** Location (Extraplanar Palace & Marketplace)
**Current Location:** The Howling Pits (an extraplanar realm); situated approximately one day's travel from the current positions of Teegan and Drystan.
**Current State:** An active, grand extraplanar brokerage and marketplace currently occupied by Maram il Vass as his personal refuge and palace.

**Physical Description:**
*   **Structure:** A grand central palace constructed of black and red marble, standing 50 feet high with domes. It is flanked by two smaller 30-foot buildings.
*   **Entrance:** Features ornate brass doors carved with images depicting a half-elf's rise to power.
*   **Atmosphere:** The exterior and interior are illuminated by eerie blue, green, and purple fairy fire.
*   **Interior Decor:** Filled with expensive incense and perfume. Adorned with tapestries depicting a half-elf's rise to power. Contains vases of flowers and glass containers holding unusual creatures.

**Function & Control:**
*   **Primary Role:** Serves as an extraplanar marketplace/brokerage for goods and services.
*   **Ownership:** Functions as the personal refuge and palace of Maram il Vass.
