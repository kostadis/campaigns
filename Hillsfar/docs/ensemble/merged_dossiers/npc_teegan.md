### Teegan

**Current Status:** Alive; escaped enslavement.
**Current Location:** Recently escaped from Maram il Vass's territory (Underdark); last seen refusing to follow the party further into the cavern/palace.
**Allegiance:** Formerly enslaved by Maram il Vass; currently unaffiliated but wary of the party (initially suspicious, later trusting after demon defeat).
**Relationships:**
*   **Drystan:** Her brother; she is protective of him and was last seen standing over him/trying to comfort him.
*   **Maram il Vass:** Former master; she suspects he is more than a half-elf.
*   **Gabberforth:** A "mean deep gnome" guard at the palace; she has warned the party about him.
*   **Xaven Tanor'this:** A Drow prisoner she confirmed was brought to Maram's palace.
**Notable Knowledge:**
*   She and Drystan were captured by orcs and sold to Maram il Vass five years ago.
*   They were held in the 'Den of the Whispered Word'.
*   She possesses detailed knowledge of the palace's defenses and the location of the Drow prisoner.
**Recent Actions:**
*   Escaped captivity with Drystan approximately one day prior to Chapter 3.
*   Initially accused the party of working with Maram il Vass.
*   Screamed in fright during an ambush, hindering the situation.
*   After the party defeated demons, she became relieved and trusting, sharing vital intelligence about Maram, the prisoner, and defenses.
*   Refused to accompany the party deeper into the palace/caverns.
**Condition:** Described as a "filthy, traumatized commoner."
