### Faerzess Shard

**Current Status & Location**
*   **Current Holder:** Felkur.
*   **Current Location:** Inside Felkur's pack (having been slid there in Chapter 11 after initially being pocketed in Chapter 10).
*   **Origin:** Stolen from a vault shelf where it was previously sitting as a large, pure crystal.

**Description & Properties**
*   **Appearance:** A large, pure crystal.
*   **Nature:** A powerful enhancer of artificer abilities; described as practically humming with arcane potential.
*   **Function:** Acts similarly to a *wand of the mage* specifically for an artificer's abilities.
*   **Mechanics:** Grants a bonus to any artificer ability used. It operates on a system of limited charges per day.

## Uncertainty
None.
