### Watcher's Eye Brooch

**Current Holder:** Limping Oric (acquired from Felkur for 45 gold).
**Origin & Ownership:** Originally belonged to the wizards of the Brotherhood of the Cloak; stolen unknowingly by Felkur while he was drunk.
**Current State & Abilities:**
*   **Divination Immunity:** Makes the wearer impervious to being located by divination magic.
*   **Detection Evasion:** Allows the possessor to remain undetected.
*   **Ward Bypass:** Attuned to the defensive wards of the Tower of the Blades in Mulmaster, allowing the wearer to bypass lethal magical defenses designed to vaporize intruders.
*   **Historical Significance:** Serves as a symbol of power and was previously identified as the "seat of government" in Mulmaster.

## Uncertainty
*   **Sale Price Discrepancy:** Chapter 5 states the brooch was sold for "forty pieces of silver," while Chapter 10 states it was sold to Limping Oric for "45 gold." The later chapter confirms the 45 gold transaction with Oric, but the earlier silver price remains unexplained (potentially a different transaction, a different currency conversion, or a contradiction in the source text).
*   **Symbolic vs. Literal Status:** Chapter 5 describes the brooch as "the seat of government in Mulmaster." This is likely metaphorical (symbol of power) rather than literal, but the text does not clarify if the brooch physically *is* the seat or merely represents it.
*   **Activation Method:** Chapter 10 notes Felkur "accidentally activated its special ability" via drunken fiddling, implying the ability might not be always-on or requires specific conditions, though the current state implies the abilities are inherent to the item.
