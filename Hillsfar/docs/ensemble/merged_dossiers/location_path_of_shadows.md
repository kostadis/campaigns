### Path of Shadows

**Type:** Location (Underground Passage)
**Current State:** An active, navigable route consisting of dark, murky, warm water approximately five feet deep.
**Location/Connections:**
*   **Origin:** Begins at "the well."
*   **Terminus:** Leads eventually to the city of Maerimydra.
*   **Key Junction:** Contains a specific five-foot-wide tunnel (approx. 40 feet in length) that branches off the main passage, leading directly to Solom Ned'razak's private office.
**Hydrology:** Fed by a stream that connects to a larger "Lake of Shadows."
**Recent Activity:** Recently traversed by the party via swimming through the main passage and the specific tunnel to Solom Ned'razak's office.

None.
