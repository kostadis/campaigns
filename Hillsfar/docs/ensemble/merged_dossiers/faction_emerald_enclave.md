### Emerald Enclave

**Current Status & Location**
Active and operational. The faction is currently communicating with the party (specifically Akritas) from an unspecified location, utilizing both physical messengers and telepathic means.

**Current Goals & Assignments**
- **Primary Objective:** Investigate and counter a threat involving fire giants infused with the power of Graz'zt who are nearing the completion of a project in the Underdark city of Szith Morcane.
- **Current Request:** Has formally requested the party's assistance regarding the situation in Maerimydra and the specific fire giant threat in Szith Morcane.
- **Intelligence:** Possesses intelligence regarding Graz'zt's machinations, originally brought to them by a Drow contact.

**Key Relationships & Contacts**
- **Charles:** Identified as a member and messenger for the faction (last seen in Chapter 1 delivering news).
- **Akritas:** The primary recipient of the faction's communications, receiving both physical messenger visits (Ch14) and telepathic messages (Ch16).
- **The Drow:** A source of prior intelligence regarding Graz'zt.

**Recent Actions**
- Dispatched a messenger to Akritas in Chapter 14 to assign an urgent mission regarding Maerimydra.
- Sent a telepathic message to Akritas in Chapter 16 warning of fire giants in Szith Morcane.
- Previously received intelligence from a Drow contact regarding Graz'zt.

- **Charles's Current Status:** Charles was a member/messenger in Chapter 1; there is no information confirming his current status (alive, dead, or still active as a messenger) in Chapters 14 or 16.
