### Xaven Tanor'this

**Current Status:** Alive; currently located at the encampment of Sporedome.
**Identity:** Drow Alchemist; former chief alchemist of the Drow in Maerimydra; sole surviving Maerimydra refugee who knows the secret route to the ruined city.
**Allegiance:** Collaborator of Solom Ned'razak; currently traveling with and reporting to Solom.
**Current Role & Assignment:** Providing critical knowledge to create an antidote for demonic-infused Fire Giant blood; guiding the party to Sporedome and the secret entrance to Maerimydra.
**Notable Knowledge:** Possesses the specific alchemical knowledge required to purge fiendish blood from Graz'zt-infused Fire Giants; knows the secret entrance to Maerimydra (previously worked with the archmage in the city's Shattered Tower).
**Current Demeanor:** Nervous (observed wringing hands); actively hurrying allies.
**Key History (Resolved):** Previously captured during the raid on Szith Morcane, acquired by Fire Giants, and sold to Maram il Vass. Held in a hanging cage in Maram's palace (Hanging Gardens) until rescued by the party in Chapter 04.
