### Spire of Inevitable Reckoning

**Current Status & Location**
A formidable tower of sheer basalt located within the fire giant stronghold of Maerimydra. The structure is currently surrounded by poisonous clouds at its peak, making flight impossible.

**Current Occupant & Control**
The tower is overseen and hosted by the Arcanaloth Vraxen (a Yugoloth), who resides there. It was historically a place of law under the Drow regime, but current control belongs to Vraxen.

**Key Features & Current State**
*   **Peak Chamber:** A 60-foot diameter chamber at the very top of the spire. It contains a single iron door leading to the interior staircase.
*   **Teleportation Circle:** A permanent, 20-foot diameter teleportation circle glowing with sickly green energy is located in the peak chamber. It is currently surrounded by poisonous clouds.
*   **Dining Hall:** Contains a magically distorted table; the party recently escaped a chaotic dinner event held here.
*   **Upper Vaults:** Accessible only via a runic key; these vaults currently house the *Lexicon of Oaths* (the book sought by Maram il Vass).

**Recent Activity**
The party recently bluffed past the front guards, entered the dining hall, climbed to the peak, and escaped the tower after a confrontation at the dinner table.

## Uncertainty
*   **Current Security Status:** While the party "escaped" the tower in Chapters 10 and 11, the facts do not explicitly state whether the tower's defenses have been breached, if Vraxen is still in control, or if the tower is currently locked down/reinforced against the party's return.
*   **Book Status:** The facts state the *Lexicon of Oaths* is located in the upper vaults (ch09), but do not confirm if the party successfully retrieved it or if it remains there.
*   **Felkur's Plan:** Chapter 05 mentions Felkur's *intention* to recalibrate the teleportation circle for escape; it is unknown if this recalibration was ever performed or if the circle functions exactly as Felkur intended in the current timeline.
