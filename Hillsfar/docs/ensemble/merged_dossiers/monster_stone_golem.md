### Stone Golem

**Current Status:** Active and allied with Veris.
**Current Location:** On the tower (Chapter 12).
**Allegiance:** Veris (Chapter 12).
**Nature:** A massive, mindless magical construct; its stony hide is magically immune/shrugs off most magic (Chapter 11).
**Current Role:** Fighting alongside Veris against the party (Chapter 12).
**Notable History (Past State):** Previously served as an unreasoning guardian stationed before a vault door (Chapters 10–11). It was programmed to recognize and allow passage only to those carrying Vraxen's scent. It previously smashed an iron door off its hinges to enter the vault and was damaged by spectral spirits within the portal chamber (Chapters 10–11).

## Uncertainty
- **Identity Consistency:** The facts describe a "Stone Golem" guarding a vault in Chapters 10–11 and "Veris's Stone Golem" fighting the party in Chapter 12. While the narrative implies this is the same entity (or a second one of the same type), the facts do not explicitly confirm if the vault guardian was destroyed, escaped, or if Veris possesses a *separate* golem. The dossier treats them as the same entity based on the singular "Stone Golem" entity tag, but the transition from "vault guardian" to "Veris's ally" lacks a bridging fact explaining the change in allegiance or ownership.
- **Current Damage State:** Chapter 11 notes the golem was "damaged" by spectral spirits. No facts in Chapter 12 mention this damage being healed or the golem being destroyed. The current physical condition (whether it is still damaged from Chapter 11) is unknown.
