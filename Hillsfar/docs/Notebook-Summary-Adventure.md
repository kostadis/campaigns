As the Chronicle Master, I have synthesized the complete saga of your campaign from the Grand Archives. This definitive timeline traces the narrative arc from the accidental origins of a legendary thief to your current high-stakes heist in the demon-infested Underdark.

### **Prologue: The "Ghost" of Mulmaster**

Long before the party met, Felkur—a shaky-handed apprentice jeweler in Mulmaster—stole a bauble to pay his bar tabs and sold it to a fence for a mere 45 gold pieces. This item was the *Watcher’s Eye Brooch*, an artifact equipped with the most sophisticated anti-divination wards in the multiverse. Its theft triggered a massive, decades-long manhunt by the Brotherhood of Cloaks for a "Phantom Master" thief. While the Cloaks executed inquisitors over their failure to catch this non-existent genius, Felkur wandered off to the Hillsfar region, completely unaware of his infamy.

------

### **Tier 1: The Hillsfar Rebellion (Levels 1–4)**

*Theme: The tyranny of the Red Plumes and the rot within the City of Trade.*

- **The Gathering:** The party formed at the Cloverwall Roadhouse. In their early days, they investigated strange mutations at Farbough Farms and rescued Constance Goodroot from the oppressive Red Plumes, establishing a profound bond she would later cherish.
- **The Arena:** Recruited by the Harper agent Olisara Lightsong to investigate missing halfling farmers, the party was betrayed by an elf named Deriel and sold into slavery at the Hillsfar Arena by Red Plume Captain Erlich and the half-orc sorcerer Breex Vandermast. Forced to fight in a reenactment of the "Fall of Northkeep," they turned the tables, defeated Breex, sparked a stadium riot, and escaped.
- **The Resistance:** The party allied with the dwarven farmer "Dumb Karl" (Sharles) to intercept armored caravans, successfully exposing a Red Plume slave ring that was selling locals to Duergar slavers.
- **The Carnival of Madness:** Investigating Dr. Jubal’s Carnival, the party discovered the carnival master had been replaced by Tak, a fiendish doppelganger running a soul-trapping Mirror Maze for the demon lord Graz'zt. They slew Tak, saved the carnies, and learned that the madness infecting the region was Abyssal in nature.
- **The Assassination (The Turning Point):** In a daring strike, the party infiltrated First Lord Torin Nomerthal’s masquerade to steal his schedule, subsequently ambushing his encampment at Yûlash. They assassinated the First Lord and his corrupt inner circle. However, this created a massive power vacuum and drove Torin’s lover, Chamberlain Malkyn Grenefeld, into a grief-fueled madness that opened Hillsfar entirely to Graz'zt's influence.

------

### **Tier 2: The Descent & The Cure (Levels 5–10)**

*Theme: The spread of Abyssal Madness and the fight for the soul of Hillsfar.*

- **The Oathbound Knight:** Venturing into the Cormanthor Forest, the party freed the spirit of the ancient elven knight Meira Faerenduil at the ruined Eventide Abbey. This allowed the ranger Akritas to claim the legendary Oathbow, *Shadowsong*.
- **The Source of the Malady:** Investigating an outbreak of madness, they tracked the corruption to the Underdark settlement of Zortagra. They discovered a cursed, Marilith-shaped Samovar crafted by Graz'zt that was infecting people with unnatural narcissism and hedonism.
- **The First Waydown Trip:** The party escorted the drow smuggler Arrizz and a wagon of Zhentarim supplies down the Waydown sinkhole. Posing as minstrels, they infiltrated the Red Plume Garrison, navigated the subterranean river, and defeated the Cambion Twins (Gattash and Rashara) to secure an alliance with the drow refugees.
- **The Quest for Sporedome:** Seeking protection against the spreading madness, the party traveled to the fungal colony of Sporedome. There, they met Solom Ned'razak, the displaced Archmage of Szith Morcane, and secured Braincap Mushrooms to protect their minds.
- **Curing the Madness in Hillsfar (Campaign Climax):** The party returned to the surface to find Hillsfar consumed by Graz'zt’s influence. They stormed the Obsidian Keep, rescued Constance Goodroot, and defeated Malkyn Grenefeld in a tragic final battle. Her death shattered the Abyssal link, banishing Graz'zt back to the Abyss as he screamed in fury. In the aftermath, Isthana Ro and the Thieves Guild stepped in to fill the power gap.

------

### **Tier 3: The City of Fire (Level 11+ - Current)**

*Theme: A high-stakes heist in the fire giant capital to prevent a demon army.*

- **The Alchemist's Rescue:** Solom Ned'razak revealed he needed the blood of a living, fiendborne fire giant to cure the giant army of demon blood. However, his chief alchemist, Xaven Tanor'this, had been captured and sold to a Yugoloth named Maram il Vass.
- **The Devil's Bargain:** The party descended into the Howling Pits and infiltrated the Den of the Whispered Word. To secure Xaven's release, Felkur signed a binding contract with Maram to steal the *Lexicon of Oaths* from Maram's rival in Maerimydra. During this meeting, Maram revealed he knew Felkur's true identity as the "Mulmaster Ghost".
- **The Infiltration:** The party assaulted the Black Ember Outpost, which guarded a secret tunnel into Maerimydra. Daein brilliantly used auditory illusions to mimic a fire giant's voice, making the dead giants sound victorious to delay discovery. They then swam down the outpost's well into the submerged Path of Shadows.
- **Maerimydra (Current Status):** Emerging in the ruins of the Shattered Tower inside the demon-infested city of Maerimydra, the party rescued Pipyap the Imp, who provided crucial intel on the city's layout.

**The Current Tactical State: "The Reverse Heist"** The party is currently hiding in the Shattered Tower ruins with Pipyap, preparing to execute a multi-phase plan before the clock runs out:

1. **The Kidnapping:** Ambush the fiendborne giantess Brandyr in the Six-Fingered Courtyard, knock her unconscious, and secure her inside a Portable Hole equipped with an air pump.
2. **The Sting:** Bluff their way into the "Business Dinner" at the Spire of Inevitable Reckoning to pit the Auditor Vrax'en against the General Veperyd.
3. **The Heist:** While the villains fight, steal the *Lexicon of Oaths* to erase Felkur's criminal history and thwart High Cloak Veris, who is attending the dinner to buy the book and execute Felkur.
4. **The Escape:** Reach the roof of the Spire and recalibrate the Teleportation Circle to flee back to Sporedome.
