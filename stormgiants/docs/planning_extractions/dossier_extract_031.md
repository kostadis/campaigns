## Tamalin Zoar

Tamalin Zoar is the owner and proprietor of the inn at Calling Horns, a retired adventurer now approximately 60 years old who founded the settlement by buying the inn, attracting settlers, and charging monthly fees for her protection. She is described as still spry and serves as the de facto law of Calling Horns.

When the 3HPP arrive, Orsik approaches her with flattery about her business acumen and expresses a desire to learn from her. Tamalin is surprised and flattered by this, clearing the main table of patrons and ordering food and drinks so she can share her experiences — indicating that the prospect of mentoring young adventurers appeals to her at this stage of her life.

During the evening conversation, she comments on the sustainability of hits-driven businesses like the play in Triboar the party mentions, noting that while such ventures are risky, a big hit is worth more than a dragon's hoard. She offers the party two free rooms for the night.

When trolls attack her stables, she witnesses the 3HPP dispatch them — though not before two of her horses are slaughtered. After the fight, she invites the party for a drink and raises a business problem. During this conversation, she publicly corrects Orsik's sales pitch timing, advising him he is being too aggressive and that some conversations should be private. When Orsik bristles, she realizes she has upset him and apologizes, which surprises Vardis.

Tamalin then presents her actual request: trolls from the Evermoors (which locals call the Trollmoors) have been descending on Calling Horns with increasing frequency, and she wants the party to travel into the moors to investigate why. She clarifies she wants information at minimum, and a solution if possible, and in return she will provide them a Letter of Mark.

She reveals she is a member of the Zoar family and that her brother is the captain of the guard in Everlund. She directs the party to speak with Dral, who runs the Zoar family's tavern Danivarr's House in Everlund, and says Dral can arrange for the captain to assist the party however they need.

Her last known location is Calling Horns, running her inn.

## Dral

Dral does not appear in this session but is referenced by Tamalin Zoar. Dral runs Danivarr's House, a tavern in Everlund owned by the Zoar family. According to Tamalin, if the party travels to Everlund and presents her letter to Dral, he will arrange for the captain of the guard — Tamalin's brother — to assist the party with whatever they need. Dral's current location is Everlund at Danivarr's House.
