## Wiri Fleagol

Wiri Fleagol is a gnome who was enslaved at Ironslag and chooses to remain behind when the other freed dwarves and gnomes flee. She introduces herself to the party and explains she has been imprisoned there for quite some time. When Vardis tauntingly asks whether she did something to deserve her captivity, she rebuffs him by pointing out that no one deserves to be chained up mining for giants.

She identifies Thistle as a fairy and offers to share intelligence about the layout of Ironslag in exchange for payment, stating she will help him specifically. She provides a detailed account of the following areas: the ore processing room with its bucket chain and enslaved dwarves watched over by a fire salamander; the foundry, which she warns is dangerously hot; the Great Wheel room powered by twenty enslaved commoners; and the prisoners' sleeping quarters sealed by iron portcullises with padlocked cages. When Thistle asks whether the prisoners can fight, she states they are too exhausted and dehydrated from the heat. She charges 20 gold for this intelligence.

She does not believe Thistle's account of the events in Silverymoon, which he tells in a way that implicates House Margaster while omitting his own involvement. She briefly offers to accompany the party before Orsik dissuades her. When asked about the Conch of Teleportation, she speculates it is likely in Duke Zalto's room on the lower level, though she states she was never taken there herself.

She expresses strong contempt for Silverymoon, citing the city's failure to aid the Silver Marches during the Orc and Dragon invasions, though she clarifies she does not seek vengeance due to concern for innocent gnomes. She plans to use her 20 gold to buy a farm and never work underground again, and says she will consider Yartar or Triboar based on Orsik's suggestion. She invites the party to look her up if they pass through either town. She asks Orsik to confirm the spelling of his name and whether mentioning it will carry weight, and he directs her to reference the 3HPP and their aid to the mayor of Triboar.

Last known status: departed Ironslag, heading down the stairs toward freedom, intending to make her way to Yartar or Triboar.

## Duke Zalto

Duke Zalto is the fire giant lord of Ironslag. He does not interact with the party during this session but is observed at a distance by an invisible Thistle during his reconnaissance of the forge complex. Zalto is seen in the assembly hall on the lower level, playing with his hellhounds by tossing a metal ball. The ball is noted to contain a body inside it.

Wiri Fleagol speculates that the Conch of Teleportation is most likely located in Zalto's personal quarters on the lower level, as that is where a giant lord would keep a valuable object. Thistle subsequently locates what he believes to be the bedroom alcove in the eastern section of the lower level but does not enter, electing to return to the party with his map and notes instead.

Last known whereabouts: in the assembly hall of Ironslag's lower level, observed playing with hellhounds.

## Brimskarda

Brimskarda is the fire giant duchess of Ironslag. She is observed by an invisible Thistle during his reconnaissance of the kitchen on the lower level. She is standing before a large fireplace, using an iron spoon to sample soup from a fat cauldron suspended over the flames. Five smoke mephits dance around the cauldron cackling while she works. Two ogres in grease-stained leather aprons and twenty goblins performing menial tasks are also present in the kitchen under her direction.

Her appearance is described in detail: she wears a gown made from the scaly hide and wings of a young black dragon, and her luxurious orange hair is wrapped around a black dragon's horn, forming a hairstyle resembling a curving pillar of flame.

At some point during Thistle's observation, she shouts an order to the goblins, who then carry food down the hallway toward the dining hall. Thistle follows them.

Last known whereabouts: the kitchen of Ironslag's lower level.
