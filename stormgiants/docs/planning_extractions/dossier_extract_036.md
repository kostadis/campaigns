## Cindrell

Cindrell is a wealthy noblewoman residing at Westpharl Ranch on the outskirts of Triboar, where she hosts lavish social gatherings including the party the 3HPP attend. She received the party in a private audience in her study — a large room with bookshelves, art, a lush carpet, and a fireplace — and revealed her political motivations and a covert job offer.

She explained that Hyuth Kolstaag, a retired adventurer who recently settled in Triboar, has been a disruptive presence: refusing her social invitations, attracting assassination attempts, causing violence that she believes will lower property values, and failing to compensate the owners of the Cart and Coin for a stolen horse or contribute to the luncheon she organized for the murdered stable hand's family. She characterized him as arrogant, classless, and bad for the town.

Cindrell revealed that she previously ran for Lord Protector of Triboar and lost to Darathra Shendrel, whom she views as a nobody and a Harper Agent. She believes Triboar should be allied to the Lord's Alliance rather than the Harpers, and that Darathra's ineffectual leadership — responding to Hyuth's disruptions only with notices and stern words — could be used against her in the next election. She tasked the 3HPP with dealing with Hyuth, strongly implying they should kill him, framing it as finishing the job the assassins started.

Negotiations over payment were tense. She offered 500 gold plus 50 if she won the election, and attempted to appeal to Orsik by offering to clear his name in Waterdeep, laughing off his request that it cover all of the North. Thistle insisted on a signed IOU, which she initially resisted but ultimately signed after Thistle deployed his fairy charm. The contract outlines the nature of the arrangement without explicit detail.

Her plan backfired entirely. Thistle, as a member of the Seelie Court and experienced in intrigue, recognized her conspiracy as clumsy and deliberately undermined it — revealing the contract to both Urgala and Darathra. Darathra expressed intent to use the contract to politically destroy Cindrell, anticipating a landslide victory against her. Urgala expressed surprise that Cindrell would be "that cold-blooded."

Cindrell's current status is that she is unaware her scheme has been exposed and that the signed contract is in the 3HPP's possession, en route to Darathra for safekeeping.

## Hyuth Kolstaag

Hyuth Kolstaag does not appear in person until the end of the session, but is extensively discussed beforehand. He is a retired adventurer, approximately 55 years old, described as having a paunch, grey hair, and crow's eyes. He settled in Triboar at some point prior to these events, and his presence has caused significant disruption.

According to Cindrell, he is arrogantly antisocial, has rejected all of her invitations to enter Triborian society, and attracted assassination attempts from old enemies. Several weeks before the session, two assassins infiltrated Triboar and attempted to kill him, resulting in peals of thunder and blasts of lightning during the fight. When the attempt was foiled, the assassins stole a horse from the Cart and Coin and murdered the stable hand. Hyuth declined to compensate the inn's owners or contribute to Cindrell's luncheon for the stable hand's family. Urgala corroborated this account, adding that after the attack Hyuth summoned gargoyles to protect himself and refused to send so much as a note to the dead stable hand's family — behavior Urgala described as "some pretty shitty behavior" that makes all retired adventurers look bad. Urgala noted that despite his difficult personality, Hyuth was reliable in a fight during his adventuring days.

The physical evidence of the assassination attempt is visible at his mansion: the roof has been obliterated and bears scorch marks, and his second-floor study is a complete disaster area — burned papers, a smashed desk, a destroyed roof, and weather damage throughout.

When the 3HPP approach his mansion posing as roofers sent by the Lord Protector, Hyuth is paranoid and hostile, screeching at them from within and declaring he trusts no one and believes everyone is out to kill him. He is persuaded to let them in when told the Lord Protector is footing the bill for roof repairs, apparently motivated by the financial offer despite his suspicion.

Darathra intends to use the Cindrell contract as leverage to compel Hyuth to become a more productive citizen, warning him that the next assassination attempt may not come to her desk in time and that behaving better would earn him the protection of the 12.

Last known status: alive, paranoid, in his damaged mansion, with the 3HPP in his second-floor study having gained entry under the pretense of roof repair.

## Urgala

Urgala is a resident of Triboar, based at the Northshield House, whom Thistle visits the morning after the meeting with Cindrell. She is a retired adventurer herself and knows Hyuth Kolstaag from that world, describing him as one of the more difficult magic users on the adventuring circuit — always self-centered but reliable in a fight.

Thistle visits her without revealing the full details of Cindrell's scheme but makes clear that Cindrell's plans for the next Lord Protector election are underhanded. When he presents the contract and states that Cindrell asked the party to kill Hyuth, Urgala is notably unsurprised, saying she expects anything from Cindrell. She independently recounts the story of the assassination attempt and its aftermath, expressing clear disapproval of Hyuth's refusal to compensate anyone or acknowledge the dead stable hand's family. She stops short of endorsing killing him, instead saying she does not consider herself in a position to judge, acknowledging her own past sins, but suggests that if someone were to make him a better neighbor, that would be a good thing.

As the 3HPP leave, she mutters that she never imagined Cindrell would be that cold-blooded.

Last known location: the Northshield House, Triboar.

## Darathra Shendrel

Darathra Shendrel is the Lord Protector of Triboar and a Harper Agent, having defeated Cindrell in a prior election for that office. Cindrell views her as a "nobody" and resents the Harper alignment she represents, preferring Triboar be allied with the Lord's Alliance instead. Cindrell criticized Darathra's response to the Hyuth situation as ineffectual — limited to notices and stern words.

When the 3HPP bring her the signed contract from Cindrell at the central tower, Darathra is pleased to see them and has already praised them for the business their play brought to town. She reacts to the revelation of the contract with evident satisfaction, described as smiling like a cat about to pounce on a rat. She states her intent to use the contract to politically bury Cindrell and also to leverage it against Hyuth — informing him that the next assassination attempt may not land on her desk, and that becoming a productive citizen would earn him the protection of the 12. She expresses confidence that once Hyuth calms down, she will win the next election in a landslide.

She delegates the conversation with Hyuth to the 3HPP, instructing them to make clear the intervention came from her as Lord Protector, and asks them to return the contract when finished so she can keep it for safekeeping.

Last known location: the central tower, Triboar.

## Thistle

Thistle is a player character and will not receive their own NPC section, but the session reveals background information relevant to named NPCs: Thistle is a minor member of the Seelie Court, which explains why Prince Thornacious recognized him and why Blasius was his friend. His court background makes intrigue natural to him, and he views Cindrell's conspiracy as amateurish. His "fairy eyes" — described as the same eyes that his lost love fell in love with — are used to charm Cindrell into signing the IOU.
