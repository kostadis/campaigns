## The Gold Dragon

The Gold Dragon was the unnamed inhabitant of a cave lair that coincided with the location marked on Orsik's treasure map. His lair also housed a pack of crag cats that scavenged the remains of his prey.

He was described as a profoundly philosophical dragon who had rejected the Cult of Bahamut after concluding it was hypocritical — specifically, that Bahamut's doctrine of restraint and minimizing enemies represented artificial limits on dragon freedom rather than genuine goodness. He did not defect to the Cult of Tiamat either, viewing that organization as simply trading one hypocrisy for another, and noting that the Cult of Tiamat was doctrinally unwelcoming to metallic dragons given the long history of conflict between metallic and primary-color dragon lines.

His stated long-term ambition, had he survived, was to find an entirely different philosophical path for all dragonkind — one outside both dominant cults. His motivations were thus selfish self-determination rather than alignment with either divine faction.

He was encountered when the party followed the treasure map into his cave and engaged him in combat. The 3HPP were not interested in philosophical debate and killed him after a fierce battle. His current status is deceased. The treasure from his lair was the objective that drew the party to his cave.
