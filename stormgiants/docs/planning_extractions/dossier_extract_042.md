## Thistle

Thistle appears throughout the Prettyview events, driven by anxiety over Laela's safety. He blames Orsik's greed and impulsiveness for nearly costing him the chance to find her, thinking "Laela is in terrible danger, and I must find her!" When Orsik produces coins from the Frost Giant corpses, Thistle privately reconciles himself to the windfall, musing that more money will help finance a first home in the fey with Laela. He is confused by Orsik's reference to "merchandising" until Vardis explains the concept. Thistle helps survey the destroyed buildings of Prettyview, noting the dead bodies and finding no treasure. He is present for the Slaad encounter and the aftermath, sleeping in Yazmeen's library overnight. Last known location: traveling eastward through the Middle Valley of the Spine of the World with the rest of the group.

## Yazmeen

Yazmeen is the gnome librarian of Prettyview, discovered hiding behind a couch in the town's large library building — the only structure of that kind still standing. He is described as average height for a gnome, with a crooked nose, scarred hands, face tattoos, and orange hair, appearing both terrified and relieved when found. He explains that Frost Giants attacked the town, destroying everything and leaving their feline companions behind, and that he survived by staying well hidden. He invites the party to sleep in the library overnight and offers tea, which turns out to be magical. He insists on remaining in Prettyview despite the cold and loneliness, citing his library patrons and his books as reasons he cannot leave.

During the night, a Slaad emerges from Yazmeen's bedroom into the library. Yazmeen is carried out of the burning building unconscious by Vardis. Upon waking, he reveals the full situation: the Slaad had been using the inhabitants of Prettyview as interplanar anchors to summon its kind into the world, but the Frost Giant attack disrupted the spell by killing everyone mid-casting. Yazmeen was being used as some kind of portal by the creature. He confirms Vardis's deduction that his magical tea warded off the Slaad while he remained awake, but wore off when he fell asleep, allowing the creature to return. Last known status: alive, unharmed, remaining in Prettyview with his ruined library.

## Harshnag the Grim

Harshnag the Grim appears at multiple points during this chapter. In Prettyview, when Orsik suggests burying the dead bodies to prevent them from becoming carrion, Harshnag smiles and tells Orsik he has grown — that he is not the same dwarf Harshnag knew in Waterdeep, referencing something Orsik apparently did repeatedly while working as a guard across the North, a past event Orsik finds embarrassing and wishes others would stop bringing up.

Later, gathered around the fire in the barn, Harshnag argues with Orsik about their route, insisting the group should go straight to the Oracle rather than follow a treasure map. During the argument, Harshnag looks up at the sky as if searching for words and mutters under his breath that "the All-Father sent a sign," suggesting he is receiving or interpreting divine guidance from the giant deity in favor of the Oracle route.

During the avalanche in the Middle Valley, Harshnag does not get buried by the snow and instead digs the rest of the party out, laughing heartily and offering a self-deprecating apology, acknowledging that he knows the party prefers not to be helped by giants. Last known location: traveling eastward through the Middle Valley of the Spine of the World with the group.

## Unla Kee

Unla Kee is present throughout the Prettyview stay and the subsequent journey. He eagerly accepts Yazmeen's magical tea. He is the one who remains alert during the night and notices the Slaad is about to cast a fireball, countering the spell before it can go off. He then uses a wand of web to trap the Slaad in Yazmeen's bedroom when it tries to flee through the window, maintaining the web despite the Slaad casting fire at him in an attempt to break his concentration. The Slaad misses, and Unla Kee holds the web until the creature is killed. After the fight, while Vardis rushes to evacuate the burning building, Unla Kee raises the question of checking on Yazmeen.

During the avalanche, Unla Kee contributes to the survival plan by casting water breathing on the group to prevent asphyxiation as they wait buried under snow. Last known location: traveling eastward through the Middle Valley of the Spine of the World with the group.

## Vardis

Vardis is present throughout the Prettyview events. He reacts with excitement when Orsik produces the looted coins, shouting about going from broke to rich. He explains the concept of merchandising to Thistle, describing the plan to sell dolls of the demon Bob Sagert as the play goes national.

During the Slaad encounter, Vardis rushes into Yazmeen's burning bedroom at Unla Kee's prompting and carries the sleeping gnome to safety. Afterward, Vardis has a key insight: he deduces that the magical tea warded off the Slaad only while Yazmeen was awake, and that falling asleep caused the tea's protection to wear off, allowing the creature to cross back into their dimension. Yazmeen confirms this explanation.

Later, gathered around the fire in the barn, Vardis listens intently to Harshnag and Orsik's argument about whether to go to the Oracle or follow the treasure map, and hears Harshnag mutter about a sign from the All-Father. Last known location: traveling eastward through the Middle Valley of the Spine of the World with the group.
