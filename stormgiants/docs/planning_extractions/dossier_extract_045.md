## Thistle Wendrod

Thistle appeared throughout this session exploring the spider warrens and the upper hallway of the temple. He harbors a strong aversion to giant spiders, specifically disturbed by the idea of giant spiders confusing fairies and sprites with flies. Despite his reluctance, he flew ahead through the dark tunnels into a large cavern filled with spider webs and three cocoons, describing it as a "larder." He spotted that the third cocoon contained a living person and flew down to give her berries. Later, during the granite ball trap, Thistle flew above the rolling ball, avoiding being crushed while the rest of the group could not fully evade it.

On the 6th day, Thistle volunteered to fly ahead up the stairs to scout for pressure plates, which Harshnag accepted. In the upper hallway he found a giant bag in one of the rooms containing a bong, a gong, and a waterskin, which amused him. Before leaving the room he discovered a long slender shard of ice roughly dagger-sized with a glowing rune symbol of "it" within it.

## Orsik

Orsik played a central role throughout the session. In the spider larder, he used magic to burn through the webs and reach the cocoons, slashing open the goat cocoon looking for treasure and finding nothing, then discovering the dead dwarf cocoon containing only 17 copper pieces, a deck of cards, and dice. He identified the living woman in the third cocoon as belonging to the same tribe as a chieftain the party had previously killed, and chose not to share that detail with her. He told her the tribe was found dead at the temple entrance, successfully deceiving her. He advised Noori to be quiet around the sleeping Harshnag before she ran off.

Back in the caves, Orsik recognized that the tunnels were not part of the original construction but were formed when the mountain shifted and cracked, identifying them as creature warrens — knowledge he attributed to being a dwarf and which surprised Vardis. In the spider chamber, he struck the giant spider twice with his green blade magic and finished the spider egg swarm. He noted the spider's natural poison was blocked by his dwarven resistance.

After returning to the main temple, Orsik woke Harshnag by poking him and enjoyed mocking the giant about being homeless and old, doubling down with Vardis on the teasing. During the granite ball trap, Orsik directed everyone to squeeze against the walls to avoid being crushed on the ball's return pass. He observed that the stone giant statue was pushing the ball and watched it walk back to its pedestal and become immobile. He was clobbered by the ball along with the rest of the group except Thistle.

## Noori

Noori was found alive in the third spider cocoon in the larder cavern. She is a female human dressed in the garb of an Uthgardt tribe — the same tribe as a chieftain the party had previously killed. She was freed by the group and given berries by Thistle. She expressed a need to find her tribe and asked to get out of the warrens. She stared at Unla Kee with intense hatred. Orsik deceived her into believing her tribe was found dead at the temple entrance rather than killed by the party, and she did not see through the deception. After being escorted to the entrance of the warren where Harshnag was sleeping, Orsik warned her to be quiet around the giant, and she smiled at him and ran off. Last known status: departed the temple on her own.

## Harshnag The Grim

Harshnag was found sleeping on a bed covered in rags at the entrance to the warren when the group returned with Noori. Orsik warned Noori to be quiet around him. He was poked awake by Orsik later and complained about not having slept in a proper giant bed in ages. When mocked by Orsik and Vardis about being homeless, he became infuriated and insisted his roof was the stars, his walls were the trees, and his bed was the ground, and that he did not like hammocks. He got up and walked north down the main hallway, peering into the empty bedrooms and muttering about the nice beds.

During the granite ball trap, all hallway entrances to the bedrooms were sealed by magical walls of stone and a 40-foot diameter granite sphere materialized and rolled south. Harshnag smashed at a stone wall but could not break through in time and was struck by the ball twice. After the trap reset — the stone giant pushing the ball back north and then returning to its pedestal — Harshnag agreed with Orsik not to repeat that experience and suggested sleeping in one of the rooms to recover. He was annoyed when Orsik and Vardis implied he was old, insisting he was not old by giant years.

On the 6th day, after an unusually deep and comfortable sleep, Harshnag leapt up enthusiastically declaring he hadn't slept so well in ages and calling for the group to go find the giant blade. He followed the group up the stairs to the upper hallway and watched with curiosity as they inspected a life-size statue of a female cloud giant wearing an angry mask with her arms positioned as if casting a spell. He accepted Thistle's offer to fly ahead and check for pressure plates before proceeding north.

## Vardis

Vardis participated in the cave exploration and the spider chamber encounter. He shouted asking about treasure when the dead dwarf cocoon was found. He was surprised by Orsik's knowledge of stonework and cave formation, reflecting that when they were friends in Waterdeep, Orsik was a gruff, bar-loving person who showed no interest in caves, attributing the change to Orsik's magic ring. He moved carefully through the caves and arrived at the spider chamber, where he watched with internal concern as Unla Kee rushed in ahead. He used his bow to kill the giant spider while it was attacking Orsik, then noticed the spider egg swarm hatching and worried Unla Kee would be eaten alive before the mage and Orsik dealt with the swarm. After the fight, Vardis discovered a frozen potion of climbing and an old axe bearing a spectacularly beautiful sapphire on the dead spider. He doubled down on mocking Harshnag about being homeless, suggesting the giant could have built a house or slept in a hammock.

## Unla Kee

Unla Kee does not receive extended focus in this chunk but is present and active. Noori stared at Unla Kee with intense hatred upon being freed from the cocoon. In the spider chamber, Unla Kee rushed in ahead of the group before Vardis could get his bearings, causing Vardis internal concern that the mage would be targeted by whatever was in the webs. Unla Kee's light revealed the chamber's layout including the central column, domed ceiling, and curtains of webbing. When the spider eggs hatched into a swarm, Unla Kee struck with a thundering clap, contributing to defeating the swarm alongside Orsik.
