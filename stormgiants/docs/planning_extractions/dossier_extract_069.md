## Vardis

Vardis participated in the combat against the monstrous crabs near the portcullis. He used bat guano and sulfur to cast a fireball, noting aloud that "magic operates very differently than fire underwater." After the crabs were weakened, he used Toll the Dead to further harm one of the remaining creatures. Orsik specifically exempted Vardis from his post-combat complaint about doubters, calling him "my friend and always has my back." In the coral garden cave, Vardis carefully measured the room before casting a fireball against the stalagmite creature (a roper), positioning the explosion to avoid hitting Thistle. He attempted a second Toll the Dead against the roper but the creature was unaffected. He was attacked by the roper's tendrils but took no damage. Vardis was among the group who entered the air-filled chambers past the killer whale's pool. His current status is with the 3HPP ascending the spiral staircase.

## Orsik

Orsik initiated the crab fight by advancing on the creatures, drawing their attention while the rest of the party attacked. He threw two hand axes at the final crab — the first bounced off harmlessly, the second killed it. He then dug through the treasure, recovering gold pieces, a goblet, a Magma Warpick, and Weaving Wasp Needles. He declared that anyone who thought the fight was a bad idea gets no treasure, exempting Vardis. He was skeptical of Thistle's assessment that the coral was worthless, asking him directly if he was certain. In the roper fight, he struck at the creature but its tough hide stopped his blows; a later sword strike killed it. He searched the roper's body and found nothing. He is currently with the 3HPP ascending the spiral staircase.

## Thistle

Thistle searched for a portcullis mechanism but concluded the storm giants operate the gates by brute strength. He signaled Thuul during the crab fight and attacked with arrows, killing or wounding crabs while they were distracted. He assessed the vortex environment and proposed continuing counterclockwise. He slipped through the portcullis into the killer whale's pool area, was spotted and nearly bitten by the shark, and used Speak with Animals to communicate with it. He learned the shark identifies itself as "Nym's Whale" and Nym's favorite pet, and that Nym arrives from the shore side. He fed the shark snacks to pacify it and lied about being sent by Nym and usually being invisible. He escaped the pool into the air above and observed a stairwell and snacks. He told the party the valuable coral was worthless to prevent a time-consuming and risky mining operation. In the roper fight, he was grappled by tendrils and bitten, though he dodged the teeth; both Vardis and Unla Kee carefully positioned fireballs to exclude him from the blast radius. He led the approach into the second shark room, fed that shark as well, and conducted the conversation with it. He noted the sparring dummy in the west passageway. After Unla Kee opened the anemone chest, Thistle proposed waiting for Mirran or going up, but Unla Kee had already moved. While ascending the spiral staircase, Thistle noticed a secret door. His current status is ascending the spiral staircase with the 3HPP.

## Unla Kee

Unla Kee observed the crab fight alongside Vardis and Thistle, watching Orsik advance. She cast Toll the Dead against one of the remaining crabs but the creature resisted the effect. In the roper fight, she was grappled by the creature's tendrils. She cast two fireballs during that encounter, using Vardis's earlier positioning as a reference for her own spell placement, both times avoiding Thistle. In the second shark room, while Thistle conversed with the shark, Unla Kee independently examined the anemone in the southeast corner, identified it as an engineered animal used to store valuables, recognized it was trapped, and successfully opened it through careful manipulation. Inside she found 400 platinum pieces from various countries. She was already moving up the spiral staircase before Thistle finished deliberating about whether to wait for Mirran. Her current status is ascending the spiral staircase.

## Thuul

Thuul participated in the crab fight at Thistle's signal, spitting acid at one of the crabs while they were distracted by Orsik and Unla Kee. No further actions or dialogue are recorded for Thuul in this session. Current status is with the party.

## Nym

Nym does not appear in person in this session. She is referenced by the first shark, who identifies itself as Nym's favorite pet and guardian, stating that Nym swims with it and arrives from the shore side rather than through the portcullis. Thistle falsely claimed to have been sent by Nym in order to pacify the shark. The shark noted it had never seen anyone other than Nym in that area. Nym appears to be the storm giant inhabitant of the underwater complex, likely the proprietor of the quarters above the spiral staircase.

## Mirran

Mirran does not appear in person in this session. She is referenced by the second shark, who identifies her as "Nym's pet" — a designation the shark uses for a creature Nym cares for and takes for rides in the pool. The shark described Mirran as needing "an awkward space with no water to survive," suggesting she is air-breathing. The shark also noted that Mirran spends significant time hitting something outside the pool, consistent with the sparring dummy Thistle observed in the west passageway. Mirran's nature, species, and relationship to Nym beyond being her "pet" are not further clarified. She is not currently present in the room.
