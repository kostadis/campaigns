## Bob Saget

Bob Saget appeared on the stage of the Great Theater in Silverymoon, dressed in a purple jacket, purple tie, and purple pants. He is revealed to be a demon who was brought to the Material Plane by the summoning spell cast by the fool Kaesen. He acknowledged awareness of the 3HPP, calling out to them by name when the alarm spell triggered, demonstrating he anticipated or suspected their presence.

Bob revealed his motivations and scheme from the stage: he finds joy and light boring and has been using his magic to twist the people of Silverymoon against the Spellguards, whom he calls "fascist oppressors." Simultaneously, he has been constructing a ritual that requires victims. His method involves luring commoners with the promise of performing in a theatrical production, forcing them to perform as terrified stand-ins for the 3HPP, killing them on stage, and then having a Drow Mage animate their corpses to join the zombie audience. He framed this as "solving a problem" for Silverymoon by targeting people the city wouldn't miss, particularly referencing members of "ungoodly races" who run afoul of the Spellguard.

During the performance witnessed by the 3HPP, Bob murdered all four commoner performers — including a gnome dressed as Unla Kee and a Tabaxi dressed as Orsik — mocking their desperation and deaths theatrically. He killed the gnome by driving his claw through their chest after the gnome's fake spellcasting failed, and killed the Tabaxi after feigning injury from their attack. He broke the fourth wall repeatedly, taunting his invisible guests and inviting them to take the victims' places.

Bob also controls a demi-plane to which he sends the real theatergoing audience after each performance, keeping them alive while the ritual plays out with substitute victims. After the performance, he left the stage laughing, and the surviving fans emerged from the theater alive and delighted.

Bob commands a retinue of drow knights and two cambions who appeared from backstage during his speech. He also works with a Drow Mage capable of animating corpses using a device.

By Eleasis 8th day, Bob Saget remains at large in Silverymoon, continuing his theatrical run as the toast of the town. The 3HPP chose not to confront him immediately, intending to deal with the giants first and then return to address him covertly, without alerting the Lord of Silverymoon.

## Delsephine

Delsephine appeared at Parnast when the Dragon's Breath floated past Blagothkus's castle. She dropped Orsik, Thistle, and Unla Kee onto the upper tower of the castle. ## Blagothkus

Blagothkus does not appear in this chunk but is discussed at length as background context when the 3HPP recognize the floating castle near Parnast. Blagothkus was the castle's owner and is identified as the figure who triggered the chaos of the Ordning. He had allied himself with Tiamat and the cult in a scheme to destroy the world of the small people, using his tower to transport treasure. A group called the First Faction defeated him and a Dracolich and secured the castle from him. How Blagothkus came to reacquire his castle after the First Faction took it is described as a mystery. Blagothkus's current status and whereabouts are not addressed in this session.
