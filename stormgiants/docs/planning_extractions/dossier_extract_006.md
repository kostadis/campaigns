## Leo

Leo is the proprietor of the local Lionel Coster store in Llorkh. He is described as immaculately dressed, with perfectly cut facial hair and smooth skin; the group suspects he uses magic to maintain his appearance, though he insists it is "100% natural, Leo." He greets the party when they visit to sell treasure and purchase equipment. Leo offers a 10% discount when buying the group's items. He reacts with offense and disgust when Unla Kee mentions "dd bay," clarifying that his store does not work with that firm due to its "grotesque surcharge." When Orsik asks about a magnifying glass sized for a specific item, Leo says he can source one in a few days for an extra 5 gold as it is not standard inventory. He acknowledges Llorkh is a small town and that his store carries a minimal inventory compared to Aurora Realms Stores, which specialize in magical and exotic items. When pressed on magic items, he offers a potion of healing, a potion of climbing, and a scroll of detect magic, noting that rarer items are likely out of the party's price range. After the party tracks dirt into his store, Leo rushes out and returns with cleaning implements, anxious about the mess and a potential inspection. He is last seen in his store in Llorkh.

## Nonnie (Aunt Nonnie)

Aunt Nonnie operates the rustic main dining hall and lodgings in Llorkh where the party is staying. The group wakes up in her establishment at the start of the session and eats breakfast in her dining hall. She is surprised to see Vardis and Unla Kee return after their excursion into the tower and asks if they plan to go back; she appears disappointed when Vardis says it was a one-time venture. When Orsik makes a remark that Nonnie takes as talking smack, she is offended and raises her prices. She relents after Orsik apologizes. She is last seen at her establishment in Llorkh.

## The Tower

The tower is presented as a sentient or aware entity that observes and comments on the party's actions. It notices that Vardis has used a blessing to help his companions deflect the permanent antipathy field surrounding it. It observes the party's attempts to grapple Orsik and Thistle and drag them toward the tower, and when Vardis and Unla Kee open the door, it remarks with satisfaction, "ah, this will do nicely." It later communicates telepathically with Vardis inside the dungeon, warning that using the corroded key will forfeit the gold the group has found, apparently attempting to entice the party to remain inside. Its motivations appear to be trapping adventurers within its interdimensional dungeon. Its current status is unchanged; it remains in Llorkh.

## Vardis

Vardis is a cleric in the party. On the 9th day, he attempts to grapple Orsik to drag him toward the tower and fails; Orsik escapes cursing him. He persuades Unla Kee to enter the tower with him, citing it as their chance without the fighters. Inside the dungeon, he casts a detect magic ritual that detects nothing, then leads negotiations with the bugbear, offering rations in exchange for the bugbear's cooperation and knowledge. He discovers the secret door hidden behind the tapestry and is struck by an ice dart trap when he opens it. He orders the bugbear to open what turns out to be a mimic, celebrates his guiding bolt critically wounding it, and finishes the creature off after Unla Kee puts it to sleep with a sleep spell. When a voice in his head warns that using the key forfeits the gold, he correctly interprets this as the tower trying to keep them inside and urges the group to leave. After being teleported back to Llorkh, he explains the tower's secret entrance to Diego. He is annoyed when Orsik's remark causes Nonnie to raise prices. He is currently with the group on the road to Anauroch.

## Unla Kee

Unla Kee is a mage in the party who accompanies Vardis into the tower on the 9th day. He is motivated by a consuming fear of stopping risk-taking. He is increasingly uneasy about exploring a dungeon without a fighter. Inside the dungeon, he leads the group south at T intersections and discovers a hexagonal room containing a corroded key in the southwest corner. He asks the bugbear whether to pick up the key. During combat with the mimic, his cantrip is ineffective, but he successfully casts sleep, rendering the mimic unconscious and turning the tide of the fight. He is confused when the voice warns about forfeiting gold by using the key but follows Vardis's lead to leave. After returning to Llorkh, he uses his ability to manipulate fates to convince Diego and the Guard to investigate the tower. He is currently with the group on the road to Anauroch.

## The Bugbear

The bugbear is an unnamed but meaningfully present NPC encountered inside the tower's interdimensional dungeon. He has been trapped inside for a long time, searching for the secret door and surviving by eating other trapped creatures, including goblins he claims "escaped" by being killed. He explains the three theories of escape held by the dungeon's inhabitants: finding the secret passage, accepting that death turns one into a zombie, or dying and being expelled by the tower's magic. He himself has chosen to tough it out and search for the secret door. He agrees to join Vardis and Unla Kee after they offer food, though he notes his mother's saying that "two walking meat bags is better than rations." He draws a map of the dungeon and explains its inhabitants: a tribe of goblins, ogre zombies, and himself. During the mimic fight, his morning star becomes stuck; he eventually smashes and helps kill the mimic after Unla Kee sleeps it. When the group prepares to leave using the staircase, the bugbear refuses to exit, instead turning back into the dungeon in pursuit of more treasure. His current status is unknown; he was last seen returning deeper into the dungeon.

## Diego

Diego is described as the para-amour of Fiora. He encounters the party on their way back to Aunt Nonnie's after they are teleported out of the tower. He is curious whether they have entered the tower. Unla Kee manipulates fates and convinces Diego that not only did they escape the tower, but that it is essential for Diego and the Guard to investigate it. Vardis then explains the secret of the tower's entrance to him. He is last seen departing to investigate the tower with the Guard.

## Fiora

Fiora does not appear directly in this session but is referenced as Diego's para-amour when Diego is introduced on the road near Aunt Nonnie's.

## Adimond Kroskas

Adimond Kroskas does not appear in this session but is meaningfully discussed during the party's encounter with surviving Cult of the Dragon cultists on the road to Anauroch. A captured cultist reveals that Adimond abandoned the Cult just before its defeat at the Well of the Worlds. He departed to work with a Giant, which surprises Thistle because Giants and Dragons are known to loathe one another. Thistle had considered Adimond a true believer in the Cult but now suspects he may have been an opportunist. Adimond's current whereabouts are unknown, though he is connected to a Giant somewhere.

## Thistle

Thistle is a party member and former associate of the Cult of the Dragon. On the 9th day in Llorkh, he declares he wants to buy better armor and purchases studded leather armor from Leo. He successfully resists Unla Kee's grapple attempt without significant reaction. On the road to Anauroch on the 2nd of Taraskh, he kills several cultists and captures one for interrogation. From the cultist, he learns that Adimond Kroskas abandoned the Cult before the defeat at the Well of the Worlds and went to work with a Giant. He learns his drake, if it still exists, is somewhere in the desert. He also receives terrible news about his beloved: enslaved people either died from mistreatment or were eaten alive by a Dragon as a sacrifice to bring Tiamat into the world. The cultist attempts to frame being eaten alive as an honor; Thistle disagrees and kills him. He is currently on the road to the Oasis of Vuerthyl in Anauroch to meet Azan the caravaneer.

## Azan

Azan does not appear in this session but is referenced as the caravaneer the party is traveling to meet at the Oasis of Vuerthyl in Anauroch. The group's journey toward this meeting is ongoing at the session's end.
