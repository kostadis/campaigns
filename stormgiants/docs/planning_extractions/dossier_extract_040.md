## Harshnag the Grim

Harshnag is introduced sitting under a tree, pondering intelligence he has gathered about the giant factions threatening the North. He has knowledge of four major giant threats: the Frost Giant Jarl seeking a magical artifact to turn the North to ice, the Fire Giant Duke attempting to repair a device to master dragons, a mad Thane wiping out non-giant races near Orlbar and Llorkh, and the secretive cloud giants hiding in their clouds. He dismisses the hill giants as not a serious threat. He spots the 3HPP approaching and extinguishes his fire.

He immediately urges the party to travel to the Eye of the All-Father, expressing urgency about the giant threats. When the party insists on stopping at the Mirabar Mines first, he is stunned and warns them that Krowen Valharrow has allied with the wrong people if they do not care about their people being destroyed. He is eventually persuaded when Vardis identifies a route through a valley that places the mines on the way to the Eye of the All-Father.

During the crag cat hunt, Harshnag joins the Ranger in trying to urge Orsik to abandon the quest.

After the party defeats frost drakes and discovers a corpse with a map marked with an X, Harshnag recognizes the location, describing it as a bit out of the way but reachable without adding too much time to the journey. He remains with the party throughout the chapter. His last known position is in the frozen valley terrain between the arms of the Spine of the World.

He references Krowen Valharrow as someone who has allied with the party, implying an existing relationship between Valharrow and the 3HPP.

## Krowen Valharrow

Does not appear in this chunk but is referenced by Harshnag. Harshnag states that Krowen Valharrow has allied with the 3HPP, invoking this relationship as a reason the party should take the giant threat seriously and proceed to the Eye of the All-Father without delay. No other details about Krowen Valharrow are provided.

## Fruf

A dwarf miner encountered by Thistle inside the Mirabar mining lodge. Fruf is among the roughly 200 workers eating in the large heated hall after returning from a mine shift. When Thistle approaches him asking for wing muffs, Fruf does not understand the request but offers Thistle a pair of warm, poofy extra socks as a substitute. His interaction with Thistle indirectly leads Orsik to agree to craft something for Thistle's wings.

## The Foreman

The Foreman of the Mirabar Mines compound is summoned by a guard after Orsik claims to be the hero of Mirabar who caught the Weevil. The Foreman is convinced that Orsik has already been paid for his services and dismisses the party. When Orsik presses by offering bounty hunting services, the Foreman sees an opportunity and invites them to dinner and a morning meeting.

Internally, the Foreman is suspicious of the 3HPP, wondering whether they are agents of the Weevil or have been told things by him. Before the morning meeting, he contacts someone referred to as "the Ax" using unique sending stones and learns that Orsik exaggerated his role — Orsik had reported Weevil's offer, but it was the Ax who actually extracted the caravan code information from Weevil.

Despite his suspicion and the verification that Orsik's story was embellished, the Foreman proceeds with offering them work, tasking the party with eliminating crag cats a day's hike out. He is privately annoyed when Harshnag and the Ranger try to talk Orsik out of the job, thinking the crag cats are genuinely troublesome. His plan succeeds when Vardis points out the value of the pelts and the party agrees to go. His last known location is the Mirabar Mines compound.
