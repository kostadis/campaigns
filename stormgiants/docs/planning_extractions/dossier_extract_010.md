## Mama Manyknuckles

Mama Manyknuckles (also called "Mama Mia") is a hag who resides in or near a sinkhole on the northern part of the island. She was aware of the party's approach and attempted to counter them strategically. She signaled her two guards to quietly approach the north ramp, whispered to her mud mephits (who did not respond, frustrating her), and hoped the party might simply leave when she overheard them considering a rest. When she realized they were attempting to flank her from the south, she ordered her half-ogres into ambush positions.

The ambush was partially successful — the party was surprised and frightened — but Mama Manyknuckles could only kill one member with her death gaze: Orsik, who failed to look away in time. She revealed herself as a hag during the confrontation. Despite killing Orsik, she was overwhelmed as the rest of the party defeated her half-ogres and her companion Belg. She attempted to flee but was cut down. Her final thoughts revealed that she had knowingly signed her gang up with Bad Fruul and had long believed this outcome was inevitable, blaming Bad Fruul for her fate.

On her body, the party found a curious stone pendant with a jade inlay of Stein, the stone rune.

## Feetor

Feetor is a mud mephit who had made a bet with Unla Kee about whether the party would kill the half-ogres. After the party's victory, Feetor proclaimed himself "New King of the Swamp" and handed Unla Kee his winnings — though the winnings turned out to be special mud rather than gold. When confronted about the missing gold, Feetor attempted to defuse the situation by hugging Unla Kee and declaring him an honorary mud mephit, presenting him with a stone that could be used to call on the mephits for help.

When Unla Kee threatened him with a duel, Feetor capitulated quickly, agreeing to retrieve gold in exchange for being named "#2." He shouted that the civil war among the mephits had ended and that all mephits were now followers of Unla Kee — though this declaration immediately sparked renewed squabbling among the mephits over who the true strongest leader was. Feetor shrugged off the resumed civil war with an indifferent "oh well."

## Belg

Belg is referenced as Mama Manyknuckles's "beloved" companion, apparently a creature fighting alongside her during the confrontation. Belg was defeated by the party during the battle at the sinkhole. No further details about Belg's nature or role are provided beyond their presence in the fight and their death during the encounter.

## Hsing

Hsing is a golden pseudodragon with milky-white eyes who serves as the emissary of the SEER. Hsing teleported to the party's location at the sinkhole the morning after the battle with Mama Manyknuckles, announcing its arrival with a whistling noise. It was mildly imperious in manner, annoyed that the party was not paying full attention to it upon arrival.

Hsing delivered a message on behalf of the SEER, informing the party that they had found the tower of the Wizard of the Crag — a site long sought by the SEER. It offered payment in exchange for the party retrieving manuscripts on ancient forms of magic rumored to be held in the tower's repository of lore.

When questioned by Thistle, Hsing revealed the following about the Wizard of the Crag: he was a master of at least the elemental powers of water and earth and worked with all elements; he was a devotee of both Mystara and Oghma. Hsing also disclosed the SEER's broader motivation — she needs help dealing with Bad Fruul, whose rise as a local power thwarted the Zhentarim.

Before departing, Hsing instructed the party to use the pendant they found on Mama Manyknuckles to enter the tower, warned that retrieving only a single spellbook would be unacceptable, and then apparently teleported away, citing being behind on naps and hearing the SEER calling.

## The SEER

The SEER does not appear directly in this session but is meaningfully discussed through Hsing's visit. The SEER is identified by Wallace as the top Spymaster of the Lord's Alliance. She has been seeking the tower of the Wizard of the Crag and is aware of the party's location and activities, having sent Hsing to find them in the swamp. Her interest in the tower is twofold: she wants the wizard's repository of lore, specifically manuscripts on ancient forms of magic, and she is seeking any kind of assistance in dealing with Bad Fruul, whose emergence as a regional power has thwarted the Zhentarim's plans. She is offering payment for the manuscripts. Wallace counseled against antagonizing her, noting that if she could find the party in the swamp, she could find them anywhere.

## Bad Fruul

Bad Fruul does not appear directly in this session but is referenced by two separate sources. Mama Manyknuckles's dying thoughts blamed Bad Fruul for her fate, revealing that her gang had signed up with him and that she had always known it would end badly. Hsing also referenced Bad Fruul when explaining the SEER's motivations, stating that Bad Fruul's emergence as a local power thwarted the Zhentarim, and that the SEER is seeking help to deal with him. Bad Fruul appears to be a significant regional threat with connections to multiple factions in the area.
