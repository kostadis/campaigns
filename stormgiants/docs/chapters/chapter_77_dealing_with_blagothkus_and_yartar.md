# Chapter 79 Dealing with Blagothkus and Yartar
**
Adventure Overview:**
The adventurers are caught between the mortal world and the machinations of powerful beings such as Dracoliches, vampires, and giants. Their current mission is to secure control of a legendary flying castle, a powerful stronghold that can change the course of their fate and the balance of power in the realm. Along the way, they must navigate treacherous alliances, uncover dark secrets, and face off against dangerous foes, all while seeking to restore the storm giant king, Hekaton, to his throne.
**Setting:**
- *Flying Castle:* A massive, enchanted fortress floating among the clouds, with various levels, dungeons, and hidden chambers. The castle's control systems are tied to Escarlotta, a ghost who can either aid or hinder the party depending on their actions.
- *Dracolich's Ice Cavern:* Deep within the castle lies a frozen lair where a Dracolich guards its phylactery. The cavern is filled with ancient magic, treasure, and hidden dangers, but it's also the key to Dracolich's power.
- *The Grand Dame (Gambling Boat):* Moored in Yartar, this luxurious floating casino is a den of vice and secrets. Here, the party must infiltrate to learn about the storm giant king’s fate, facing wealthy nobles, hired guards, and criminal elements while deciding whether to play by the rules or cause chaos.
- *Yartar and Thieves’ Guild Hideouts:* Yartar’s underworld provides crucial information and contacts, but navigating this criminal network may force the adventurers to make shady deals, face double-crosses, or earn valuable allies.
**Key NPCs:**
- **Blagothkus:** A cloud giant who once allied with the Cult of the Dragon but now stands as a pivotal figure in the castle’s control. His fate is tied to his son, who is trapped as a ghost until Blagothus is slain.
- **Escarlotta (Ghost):** Blagothkus’ spectral wife and the central controller of the flying castle’s systems. She is a complex entity—sometimes helpful, sometimes obstructive—who holds her own desires for the fate of the castle and her family.
- **The Dracolich Glazeal:** A cunning undead dragon who struck a deal with the party to retrieve its phylactery. Though powerful, it is not entirely trustworthy and may turn on the party if the situation benefits it.
- **Lord Khasperi Dryland:** The charismatic and secretive owner of the Grand Dame gambling boat. He is connected to the plot involving the storm giant king, Hekaton, but extracting information from him will not be easy.
- **Queen Serissa (Storm Giant):** Acting head of the storm giant court, Queen Serissa has tasked the party with finding her father, Hekaton. She provides guidance but is desperate, knowing that the disappearance of the storm king threatens the balance of power in the realm.
**Expanded Key Plot Points:**
- **Castle in Turmoil:** The adventurers must establish control over the flying castle. Blagothkis, driven by guilt and the desire to restore his family’s honor, may either help the party or become a hindrance. Escarlotta, controlling the castle’s systems, could side with the party if convinced they would save her son. This power struggle plays out as the adventurers decide the fate of the giant family and the flying fortress.
- **The Dracolich's Phylactery:** Hidden in a cavern of ice within the castle, the phylactery of the Dracolich Glazeal holds great power. To solidify their alliance with the Dracolich or to destroy it and free the castle from its influence, the party must battle through undead and icy dangers to reach the artifact.
- **Infiltration or Sabotage at The Grand Dame:** In Yartar, the adventurers must board Lord Dryland’s luxury gambling boat, "The Grand Dame," to uncover information about Hekaton's capture. The adventurers could take a subtle approach, using disguises, gambling, and gathering intelligence. Alternatively, they may choose direct action—disrupting the boat’s operations, defeating its guards, and potentially sinking it to the riverbed if they choose open conflict.
- **Gambling with Fate:** While on The Grand Dame, the adventurers can engage in high-stakes gambling that could result in tremendous riches or devastating losses. Through this, they may curry favor with or antagonize the boat’s elite patrons. They may unlock access to Dryland’s private quarters and darkest secrets if they win big.
- **The Hidden Network of Yartar:** Beneath the surface, Yartar is run by a secretive Thieves' Guild that holds the key to finding Hekaton’s kidnappers. The adventurers may need to negotiate with these criminals, trade favors, or make enemies of the city’s underworld to pursue the truth.
- **Storm Giants’ Desperation:** As the adventurers dig deeper into the plot surrounding Hekaton, they uncover a conspiracy involving human factions and ancient powers, all vying for control of the giants’ kingdom. Betrayals from unexpected sources, such as one of Hekaton’s closest advisors, add further twists to the tale.
