# Chapter 42 - Traveling through Middle Valley
## Thistle 

“Time to loot,” shouts Orsik. 
Thistle glares at him; thanks to his greed, impulsiveness, and insistence on fighting Giants, his opportunity to find the love of his fairy heart almost ends. “Laela is in terrible danger, and I must find her!” 
And so he stares at Orsik, who confidently walks up to the Frost Giant corpses, goes through their bags, and shouts, “HA! SEE!” 
His hands are full of gold and silver coins, with an unmistakable sound of more gold. 
“We went from being broke to rich!” shouts Vardis
And Thistle thinks, “Having more money will help finance our first home back in the fey....” 
His reverie is interrupted when Orsik turns to Vardis and says, “Imagine how much money we will make from the merchandising of the demon Bob Sagert.” 
“Merchandising? What’s that” he thinks. 
Vardis looks at Thistle and says, “Selling little dolls of the demon while the play goes national.”
“Time to check the buildings of this town. What is the name of this town?” 
He muttered "Prettyview" and gestured towards a sign that an unknown object had been significantly damaged. The sign was directed towards a sizable structure with gaping openings that boulders hurled by frost giants caused. The sign read, "Welcome to the finest Inn of Prettyview!"The name of the tavern is not visible. 
Thistle and Orsik look inside and are disappointed. The place is in shambles, and dead bodies are everywhere, crushed from the stones that fell through the sky or cleaved by giant swords. 
“No place to sleep,” shouts Orsik.
Thistle thinks, “And no treasure.” 
The next few houses are the same, demolished and empty. One of them has a man dressed in armor as if they are about to go and fight, and they might have if a boulder had not squished their body. 
Orsik 
“We need to bury the bodies. Can’t have them turn into carrion.” 
Orsik is pleased and annoyed when Harshang the Grim smiles and says, “You have grown, Orsik. You are not the dwarf I knew in Waterdeep. The guard that did that Thing everywhere he worked all across the North.” 
“Will they ever let it go? REALLY?” 
As Orsik steps out of the house, he notices that only two buildings are standing, one is a barn, and the other is a house. 
And so, he heads to the house. It’s a massive building, 100 feet long and wide. Inside is an enormous library, and behind a couch, there is a tiny, terrified gnome. 
Orsik observes that the gnome is of average height, with a crooked nose, scarred hands, face tattoos, and orange hair. The gnome appears to be both terrified and relieved. Yazmeen informs the fighter that the town was attacked by giants who destroyed everything in their path and left their feline companions behind. The gnome reveals that he managed to escape because he was well hidden.
While Orsik peruses the books, Yazmeen reveals he is the local librarian. Visitors from neighboring towns often come to exchange their books for those in his collection. "And of course," he adds, "we always offer tea. Would you care for some? I could use a cup myself."
Orsik declines, and Yazmeen continues as he sips some tea, “The barn is empty except for some cows. And it’s uncomfortable to sleep in; why not sleep here overnight?”
## Unla Kee

“I’ll have some tea!” the magic-user shouts, and he’s soon drinking the tea, which turns out to be magical. 
And then, Unla Kee observes as the new kinder, Orsik, tries to encourage Yazmeen to come with the group, but the gnome seems determined to stay here despite the cold and the loneliness. 
“I have other library patrons, and I can’t lose my books,” he insists as he sips some tea. “Some tea?” 
As it gets cold, Unla Kee is relieved that Orsik wants to spend the night in Prettyview and the nice warm room they are in. 

And he’s even happier when he doesn’t have to stand watch because he’s not very buff. 
Except in the middle of the night, a monstrous aberration that looks like a giant bi-pedal toad steps outside of Yazmeen’s bedroom into the library where the 3HPP are sleeping and hisses, “YOU RUINED MY BREEDING PROGRAM.” 
Unla Kee is awake, and a good thing, too, because while everyone else tries to decide what happened to Yazmeen, he notices that the Slaad is casting a fireball and counters it. 
The Slaad is furious and realizes he’s outmatched, so he hurls flame at the books in the library, setting everything ablaze, and tries to escape through the window in Yazmeen’s bedroom. 
Unla Kee stops him with a wand of the web, trapping him in the room. The Slaad is furious and casts more fire at the halfling mage, trying to break his concentration. But Slaad misses, and Unla Kee smiles. 
And then, a few moments later, Unla Kee is pleased to see the creature is dead! 
“Can we get out of this burning building” shouts Vardis. 
Unla Kee mutters, “Shouldn’t we check on Yazmeen?”
Vardis
“Fine,” says the cleric and rushes in, picks up the sleeping Yazmeen, and rushes out. 
Yazmeen is fine. And does he have a story to tell? 
“Thank goodness you came! The creature,” he shudders, "was from another plane of existence. The thing wanted to use the inhabitants of Prettyview as interplanar anchors to summon his kind here. The spell was almost complete. Fortunately, or unfortunately, the Frost Giants attacked the village while the spell was being cast, and disrupted the spell by killing everyone.”  
"I can't believe I'm still breathing! That monster was using me as some kind of portal, but the specifics are hazy. It threatened to end me, but sipping the tea appeared to ward it off. Maybe there's some magical property to that brew?"
Vardis has a eureka moment; he figures out what is going on, “the tea was only effective for a period. When you went to sleep, the tea would wear off. When it wore off, the creature could return to our dimension.” 
Yazmeen looks at Vardis and says, “Yes! That is it!” 
As the group gathered around the fire later that evening in the barn next to the cows, Vardis overheard Harshnag and Orsik arguing again, “We should go straight to the Oracle!” claimed the giant. And then Orsik discussed, “The treasure map?”
As he stared at the two of them, listening intently, he saw how Harshnag would look up into the sky as if he were trying to think of what to say and then mumbled under his breath, “The All-Father sent a sign.”

## Avalanche  
After the events at Prettyview, the 3HPP continued eastwards deeper into the Middle Valley of the Spine of the World. 
As they trudged, to their horror, they heard a large sound coming from the towering peaks on one side of the valley. 
“Avalanche!” screamed Harshnag. 
With only minutes before tons of snow collapsed on them all and nowhere to hide, Orsik and Unla Kee devised a clever plan. Unla Kee cast water breathing on the group, and Orsik threw fire bolts to melt ice. Using the combination, the group avoided any chance of asphyxiation. Orsik then proposed they use the firebolts to cut through the snow. As the group huddled to make that happen and waited as Orsik cut through snow, they heard a hearty laugh and saw the sunlight peer through an opening in the snow.
“I am so sorry for helping you, I know how you little people don’t want to be helped by us Giants!” bellowed Harshnag, who had managed not to get covered by the snow and dig 3HPP out.
