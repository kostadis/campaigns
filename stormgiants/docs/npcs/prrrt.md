# Prrrt

## Identity
- Role: Galeb Duhr sentinel, stationed at the great pyramid formation in the Greypeak Mountains
- Faction: Galeb Duhr collective at the pyramid (alongside Grrrk)
- First appearance: When the party approached the great pyramid and spoke with the Galeb Duhr sentinels

## Personality & Motivations
- Core drive: Unclear beyond his duties at the pyramid; appears content in his role and naturally sociable
- Prrrt is younger, more optimistic, and more openly friendly than his counterpart Grrrk, who views his sociability and complaints as undignified. He warmed quickly to the party, especially after receiving aid, and expressed genuine goodwill toward them throughout their interaction.

## History with the Party
- Suffered from rock mites on his feet; Thistle (the fairy) treated his mites, earning his goodwill
- In return, Prrrt relayed information the party needed — specifically something a dead giant had said
- Expressed optimism that the party would survive the pyramid, which proved correct
- Called out a friendly farewell as the party departed, thanking them for dealing with the beetles
- Clarified that the treasure the party found within the pyramid was not the Galeb Duhr's to claim (implicitly blessing their keeping it)

## Current Status
- Last known location: The great pyramid, Greypeak Mountains
- Resuming his sentinel duties alongside Grrrk
- No known active schemes or hidden agenda

## Relationships
- **Grrrk**: Fellow sentinel and apparent elder/superior; Grrrk finds Prrrt's complaints and sociability undignified
- **Thistle**: Treated his rock mites; Prrrt is likely favorably disposed toward her specifically
- **The Party (general)**: Friendly and grateful; considers them proven survivors and allies of a sort
