# The Gold Dragon (Unnamed)

## Identity
- Role: Independent dragon philosopher; no faction affiliation
- Faction history: Former Cult of Bahamut member; rejected both the Cult of Bahamut and Cult of Tiamat
- First appearance: Encountered in his cave lair, the location marked on Orsik's treasure map; the party entered and immediately engaged him in combat

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goal:** To forge an entirely new philosophical path for dragonkind outside both dominant cults — one rooted in genuine freedom rather than doctrinal constraint
- Profoundly philosophical; capable of articulating nuanced critiques of both major dragon factions
- Rejected the Cult of Bahamut as hypocritical, viewing its doctrine of restraint as artificial limits on dragon freedom masquerading as goodness; rejected the Cult of Tiamat as simply a mirror hypocrisy, and one structurally hostile to metallic dragons given historic inter-lineage conflict
- Motivated by selfish self-determination rather than divine alignment or factional loyalty

## History with the Party
- The party followed Orsik's treasure map to his cave lair, which was also home to a pack of crag cats living off his prey remains
- The party engaged him in combat without pursuing dialogue; he was killed after a fierce battle
- The treasure from his lair was recovered as the original objective of the expedition

## Current Status
- **Deceased** — killed by the party in his cave lair
- No active plans or operations

## Relationships
- **Cult of Bahamut:** Former member; departed on philosophical grounds
- **Cult of Tiamat:** Never affiliated; viewed the organization as equally hypocritical and structurally unwelcoming to metallic dragons
- **The Party:** Killed by them; no relationship developed
