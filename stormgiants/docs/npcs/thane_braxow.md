# Thane Braxow

## Identity
- **Role:** Stone Giant Thane; one of four giant lords assembled by Mirran
- **Faction:** Mirran's giant council (alongside Tartha, Vaal, and Hellenhild)
- **First Appearance:** Introduced as part of the assembled giant lords; described as "the quiet one"

## Personality & Motivations
- Earned the descriptor "the quiet one" among the giant lords, suggesting a reserved or taciturn nature.
- No further personality detail was established before his death.

## History with the Party
- **First and final encounter:** Braxow was singled out immediately by Orsik, who used Expeditious Retreat to close distance and struck him repeatedly with a giant slayer sword. Braxow initially resisted the sword's magic but was eventually wounded, knocked prone, and killed. He was the first giant casualty of the encounter.
- **Post-death:** Orsik extracted 15 gemstones embedded in his flesh. His body was dragged to a storeroom.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed by Orsik in melee combat.
- Body stored in a storeroom; 15 gemstones looted from his flesh.

## Relationships
- **Mirran:** Assembled by Mirran as one of four giant lords.
- **Tartha, Vaal, Hellenhild:** Fellow giant lords; present at the same encounter.
- **Orsik:** Killed by him directly and swiftly.
