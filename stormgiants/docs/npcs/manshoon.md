# Manshoon

## Identity
- Role / Title: Leader of a faction within the Zhentarim's Black Network operating in Waterdeep
- Faction: Zhentarim (Black Network) — one of two rival internal factions
- First Appearance: Not yet appeared in person; introduced through Darz Helgar's interrogation

## Personality & Motivations
- Insufficient direct interaction to establish personality traits.
- Demonstrated foresight and ambition through his use of cloning magic as a contingency against death.

## History with the Party
- **Darz Helgar Interrogation:** Darz revealed that the Black Network in Waterdeep is fractured into two factions, one led by Manshoon. Darz claimed that although Manshoon was killed by Fzoul and all clones were reportedly destroyed, he personally believes Manshoon is still alive. Orsik (intelligence recently augmented) connected Darz's account to a story he recalled about a spellcaster who stored copies of himself as a form of immortality, recognizing it as referring to cloning and identifying Manshoon as its subject.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Unknown; whereabouts unconfirmed
- **Active Plans:** Unknown
- **What the Party Knows:** Manshoon leads a Zhentarim faction in Waterdeep; he was reportedly killed by Fzoul; official accounts claim all clones were destroyed; Darz believes he survived via a remaining clone
- **What Remains Hidden:** Whether Manshoon is truly alive, his location, and the nature or scope of his operations

## Relationships
- **Fzoul:** Killed Manshoon (or at least a version of him)
- **Darz Helgar:** Subordinate within Manshoon's Zhentarim faction; believes Manshoon still lives
- **Rival Zhentarim Faction:** Manshoon's faction is in internal conflict with a second Black Network faction in Waterdeep
- **The Party:** No direct relationship established; known to the party only through secondhand information
