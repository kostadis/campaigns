# Fritz

## Identity
- **Role:** Goblin merchant, independent trader
- **Faction:** His own relocating clan
- **First Appearance:** Encountered on the Evermoor Road heading east; the party met him while traveling the same road

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Relocating his clan eastward to escape giant aggression and Uthgardt raider attacks; maintaining profit margins even under duress
- **Personality:** Pragmatic and optimistic, with a merchant's pride — he refuses to frame his forced relocation as a "going-out-of-business sale" on principle, citing his mother's advice to never sell for less than a reasonable profit. He assigns value through association and logic (duke's salt must be quality; a giant wouldn't carry a worthless horn), making him an eager if easily swayed buyer.

## History with the Party
- Met the party on the Evermoor Road traveling east while his clan fled westward instability
- Purchased several items from the party: a bag of salt formerly belonging to Duke Zalto, an empty chest, a drinking horn, and fairy leather armor
- Was not informed — thanks to Orsik intervening to stop Thistle — that the "fairy leather armor" is armor made *from* a fairy rather than armor designed for one

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Evermoor Road, heading east with his clan
- **Active Plans:** Continuing eastward relocation
- **What the Party Knows:** Fritz is a displaced merchant moving east due to giant and Uthgardt aggression
- **Hidden from the Party:** Nothing notable — Fritz appears to be exactly what he presents himself as
- **Hidden from Fritz:** The true nature of the fairy leather armor he purchased

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Friendly commercial interaction; Orsik and Thistle had a minor moment of tension over the armor disclosure
- **Duke Zalto:** Unaware of the connection — simply pleased to own "duke-quality" salt
- **His Clan:** Traveling with them; their welfare appears to be his primary concern
