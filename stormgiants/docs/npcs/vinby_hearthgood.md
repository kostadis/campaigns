# Vinby Hearthgood

## Identity
- Role/Title: Halfling resident of Silverymoon; no faction affiliation noted
- First appearance: Session in which Orsik and Unla Kee fled into his drawing room to escape the Cambians

## Personality & Motivations
- Goals and drives unclear beyond apparent self-interest and self-preservation
- Presents as a man of wealth and refinement — sharply dressed, fine wine, well-appointed furnishings
- Despite witnessing the death of his unnamed companion, his primary concern at court was the uninvited entry into his home, suggesting either a deeply guarded emotional interior or cold pragmatism

## History with the Party
1. **First encounter:** Orsik and Unla Kee burst through Vinby's unlocked front door while fleeing the Cambians. During the ensuing confrontation, a second Cambian killed Vinby's unnamed halfling companion. Vinby survived.
2. **Star Court testimony:** Vinby appeared as a witness, seated as far from Lady Margaster as possible. When called to testify, he stated only that Orsik was the person who smashed into his house — making no mention of the Cambians, the death of his companion, or any larger context. Unla Kee was visibly outraged at his framing.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Star Court, Silverymoon
- **Active plans:** Unknown
- **What the party knows:** He survived the Cambian encounter, he testified against them on the breaking-and-entering matter, and he was deliberately distancing himself from Lady Margaster in the courtroom
- **What remains hidden:** His relationship to Lady Margaster (if any), why he minimized his testimony, and what — if anything — he intends to do next

## Relationships
- **Unnamed halfling companion:** Killed during the Cambian confrontation; relationship unknown
- **Lady Margaster:** Notably avoided proximity to her in the Star Court — nature of tension unknown
- **Orsik & Unla Kee:** Views them primarily as trespassers; Unla Kee holds open contempt for him
