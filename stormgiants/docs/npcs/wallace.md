# Wallace

## Identity
- **Role:** Young man from Parnast; informal member of Orsik's group; son of a connected figure in Parnast with Lord's Alliance ties
- **Faction:** Lord's Alliance (by proxy through his father, and by personal inclination)
- **First Appearance:** Accompanied Orsik's group into the Marsh of Chelimber; origins tied to a quest either directed by the Lord's Alliance or engineered by his father to claim Alliance credit

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Survival, proving his worth, advancing Lord's Alliance interests, and securing personal opportunity — including, early on, finding Orsik's Zhentarim contact independently to offer treasure locations himself
- **Traits:** Wallace is observant, strategically minded beyond his years, and quietly resentful when dismissed. He keeps his own counsel when he feels unheard, filing away vindication rather than voicing it. He is institutionally loyal — his arguments tend toward pragmatism and organizational interest rather than personal conviction. He is also capable of sentiment, as shown in his eventual decision to settle down with Elspeth.

## History with the Party

**Marsh of Chelimber / Ambush Gap**
Wallace knew Ambush Gap was a favored ambush site for gangs preying on caravans avoiding Zhentarim tolls. Orsik dismissed him as a boy who knows nothing. When the Bugbear ambush occurred exactly where Wallace predicted, he said nothing. He privately noted the Bugbears had little money despite clearly working for someone, and resolved to locate Orsik's Zhentarim contact on his own to offer the treasure's location — then stayed quiet and watched.

**The Tower**
Wallace cleaned the gargoyle statue on Orsik's direction and discovered its arm rotates. He ran ahead into the tower, excited at his "first tower." He walked through a door in the gargoyle room's southeast wall (wear marks visible; not truly secret). In the Shrine of Oghma, he wrote *"My name is Wallace"* in the offered tome. He found and whispered the key inscription about allied and enemy elemental gods to Orsik. He also discovered gems and coins totaling 200 GP in the laboratory. Orsik was notably protective of him during this delve.

**Parnast — The Golden Tankard & the Seer**
Orsik told Wallace outside the Golden Tankard that "someday, you'll be feeding people to the Wyvern." Wallace stared at him funny. He stayed with Orsik and Vardis when the group hesitated at the Seer's door.

During post-Seer planning, Wallace raised the practical concern of village defense against Orsik's talk of assassination. He introduced the group to Old Parnast — a former Zhentarim fortress to the northeast, more defensible, but believed cursed by locals (tied to a legend that towers Ragnar built using logs from the old town burned down). Orsik found this thinking beyond his comfort zone and chose to defend current Parnast instead. Wallace then traveled with the group toward Prince Thornacious' camp.

**Reconnaissance & Combat**
On the 5th day, Wallace used his local knowledge to confirm the northern invasion route, conferring with Thistle and Vardis and participating in inspection of the town's fortifications. On the 6th day, he was among those who killed the orog and acolyte that knocked Unla Kee unconscious in the cave battle.

**The Headband & Retirement**
Wallace attempted to persuade Orsik to surrender the headband to the SEER, arguing on institutional grounds: the SEER is an essential Lord's Alliance member and the town needs Alliance support. Orsik rejected this, pointing out the SEER's plans nearly destroyed the town. When Orsik asked why Wallace trusted the SEER, Wallace was dismissed. Later the same session, Wallace approached Orsik to announce his retirement — he and Elspeth had decided to be together, and the town needed a new guard captain. Orsik grunted approval without sentiment.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Parnast
- **Status:** Retired from adventuring; taking up a role as guard captain; in a relationship with Elspeth
- **Active Plans:** None known beyond settling into his new life in Parnast
- **What the Party Knows:** That he has retired and is with Elspeth
- **What Remains Hidden:** His early scheme to independently contact Orsik's Zhentarim contact and offer the treasure location; the full extent of his father's Lord's Alliance maneuvering and whether Wallace was knowingly part of it

## Relationships
- **Orsik:** A complicated dynamic. Orsik repeatedly dismissed Wallace as a boy, was protective of him in the tower, and received his counsel with mixed results. Wallace respected Orsik enough to stay loyal but privately resented being underestimated. Their final exchange at retirement was affectionate only by Orsik's standards.
- **Elspeth:** Romantic partner; her relationship with Wallace was the direct cause of his retirement.
- **Thistle & Vardis:** Collaborated with them during reconnaissance.
- **The SEER:** Wallace is institutionally deferential to the SEER as a Lord's Alliance figure; this put him at odds with Orsik late in the campaign.
- **Lord's Alliance (via his father):** Wallace's loyalties and framing of decisions consistently track Alliance interests; his father appears to have positioned him in this group deliberately.
