# Iymrith

*Also known as: Ilymrith, Adimond Kroskas, the Doom of the Desert, the Bane of the Desert.*

---

## Identity
- **Role:** Ancient Blue Dragon; infiltrator; hybrid-creature experimentalist; self-described artiste and creator
- **Faction affiliations:** Former Cult of the Dragon leadership; architect of the Kraken Society (as a Cult rebranding); has investor relationships with chromatic dragon clan representatives, Red Wizards (via Szass Tam), Unseelie and Seelie Court delegates, and various giant lords
- **First appearance:** Maelstrom throne room, disguised as the storm giant Adimond Kroskas, arguing with Queen Serissa when the party emerged through the Vortex pool

---

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Complete and publicly validate her fairy/dragon hybridization experiments; secure ongoing investor confidence; accumulate political leverage across giant, dragon, and fey power structures; avoid spending her own hoard under any circumstances
- **Secondary goal:** Position herself against the SEER, whom she identifies as the true long-term threat to creatures like herself
- Iymrith presents herself as a frustrated genius whose reputation rests on results she considers accidental (drakes), and she craves recognition as a legitimate artiste and scientist. She is simultaneously one of the most powerful beings the party has encountered and visibly under pressure from her investors — a combination that makes her manipulable through flattery and theatrical framing. She is calculating, patient across centuries-long timescales, and capable of volcanic fury at perceived slights to her hoard or pride.

---

## History with the Party

**Maelstrom (first encounter):** The party emerged from the Vortex pool mid-argument between Iymrith (disguised as Adimond Kroskas) and Queen Serissa. Orsik immediately attacked, landing two deep sword blows. Iymrith dropped her storm giant disguise, revealed her dragon form, used wing buffets and dragon fear to incapacitate most of the party, and was struck by a spell from Vardis. She shoved Thistle aside, dove into the pool, and fled through the Vortex with the parting words: *"This is not the end of this."*

**Thistle's conspiracy reconstruction (between sessions):** Thistle assembled a comprehensive account of Iymrith's operations — Tiamat funding, Oracle of Amman manipulation, engineering the Ordning's collapse, Nerissa's murder, Hekaton's kidnapping, the Kraken Society rebranding — and presented it to the party. Unla Kee ultimately conceded the theory sounded correct. The party resolved to sail for the northern isles.

**Hekaton's ship:** The party identified Iymrith to Hekaton as "the blue dragon who tried to steal your kingdom." Hekaton recognized her as the Doom of the Desert and offered to join a final battle against her after the party deals with the giant faction leaders. Unla Kee immediately bolted toward a teleportation chamber, apparently intending to attack her independently.

**Desert approach to her lair:** The party traveled toward Iymrith's lair near Ascore at the edge of Anauroch. Two blue dragon–purple worm hybrids emerged from the sand near the party — later confirmed as her creations.

**Desert courtyard (negotiation):** Iymrith arrived through a portal as the party was nearly wiped out by the two hybrid sentinels, with cultists on her back who healed Orsik, Unla Kee, and Vardis. Rather than attacking, she applauded. She revealed the hybrids are mindless guardians and that her true ambition is Laela — a fairy/dragon hybrid capable of physically swapping between forms without magic. She needs the party to fight Laela at a coliseum demonstration before her investors to prove the experiment works, because Laela refuses to shift out of fey form. She negotiated a deal: the party would participate in the staged coliseum event; in exchange, she allowed them to arrive days early under the pretext of "rehearsal," giving them access to her lair and time with Laela. She provided Thistle a sending stone to speak with Laela (then took it back). She distanced herself from the SEER, calling the SEER the true evil and the real power behind the Lord's Alliance.

**Silverymoon and final planning:** The party used Iymrith's upcoming investor gathering as leverage to recruit allies. Thistle informed Serissa that Iymrith would be present at the gathering, which convinced Serissa to commit the palace guard. The plan involves Bob (polymorphed as a dragon) infiltrating the event to publicly humiliate and disgrace Iymrith before her patrons. King Hekaton has committed his forces; the final battle at her lair is set for five days out.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Ruined desert courtyard; traveling back toward her lair (a three-day journey from that location near Ascore)
- **Active operations:** Preparing the coliseum investor demonstration featuring Laela; maintaining investor relationships with chromatic dragon clans, Red Wizards, and Seelie/Unseelie delegates; holding Laela captive
- **What the party knows:** Her true identity as Iymrith/Doom of the Desert; her lair's general location near Ascore; the coliseum layout (she drew a detailed map); her investor list; Laela's situation; her anti-SEER position; that her lair is warded against scrying and teleportation
- **What remains hidden:** Her precise defensive preparations; the full scope of what her lair contains; the terms of her deal with Szass Tam; why Queen Titania approved the experiments (and what that means for Thistle's expulsion); whether she suspects the party's real intentions

---

## Relationships
- **Queen Serissa:** Infiltrated her court as Adimond Kroskas; secured Serissa's cooperation through Mirren and Nym, promising them the throne; Serissa was deceived but now knows Iymrith is a dragon and is committing forces against her
- **Mirren and Nym:** Direct allies within the storm giant court; helped broker Iymrith's access to Serissa
- **King Hekaton:** Had him kidnapped and hidden on a non-scryed ship after he refused her offer; Hekaton is now free, furious, and committed to her destruction
- **The Imperator:** Deceived along with the rest of the court about her true nature
- **Laela:** Captive experimental subject; Iymrith found her torching a caravan in dragon form; Laela is resisting the experiment through fey meditation ("fey-fullness")
- **The SEER:** Explicitly identified as an enemy; Iymrith calls the SEER the real power behind the Lord's Alliance and a long-term existential threat to free creatures
- **Unla Kee:** Initially sought to acquire her power; now traveling with the party toward her lair
- **Szass Tam / Red Wizards:** Has an existing deal; Red Wizards are among her investors
- **Queen Titania / Seelie Court:** Approved the fairy hybridization experiments — the implied reason for Thistle's expulsion from the Seelie Court
- **Sandesyl:** Devoted admirer with romantic obsession; describes Iymrith as "one of the greatest of dragon kind." His long-term personal goal is to convince Iymrith to become a dracolich.
- **Blagothkus & Escarlotta:** Horrified to learn that Serissa was working with her — regard Iymrith as dangerous and untrustworthy.
- **House Margaster:** Implied alliance, though Thistle's delivery of this information was unconvincing to Lord Aerasumé
- **The party:** Uneasy negotiated alliance; she needs them for the demonstration; they are using her access agreement as a pretext to infiltrate her lair
