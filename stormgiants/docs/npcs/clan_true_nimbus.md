# Clan True-Nimbus

## Identity
- **Role/Faction:** Cloud giant clan; rivals in an ongoing treasure hunt
- **First Appearance:** Not seen directly — introduced via a mocking note left inside an empty stone chest at the first cache location

## Personality & Motivations
- Openly contemptuous of hill giants, whom they consider slow, dim-witted, and beneath them
- The note's tone — including a condescending definition of the word "prevail" — suggests arrogance, theatricality, and a desire to humiliate as much as to win

## History with the Party
- The party discovered an empty stone chest at the first cache location; inside was a note signed by Clan True-Nimbus of the Clouds, claiming they had already taken the treasure and mocking any hill giants who arrived after them

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Unknown — departed the first cache site before the party arrived
- **Active Plans:** Participating in the same treasure hunt as the party; have at least a one-cache lead
- **Known to Party:** They exist, they are cloud giants, they beat the party to the first cache, and they are antagonistic in tone
- **Hidden:** Their numbers, base of operations, full goals, and whether they are aware of the party specifically

## Relationships
- **Hill Giants (general):** Openly hostile and dismissive
- **The Party:** No direct contact yet; party knows of them only through the note
