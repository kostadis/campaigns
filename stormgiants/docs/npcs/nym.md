# Nym

## Identity
- **Role:** Princess of the Storm Giants; younger daughter of King Hekaton; elder sister of Serissa
- **Faction:** Allied with Adimond Kroskas (and implicitly with the dragon Iymrith) following Serissa's ascension
- **First Appearance:** Great hall of the Maelstrom, performing music alongside Mirran — striking a crab shell with whalebone mallets

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Oppose Serissa's rule and her connection to "small folk"; advance her own position through alliance with Adimond Kroskas
- **Personality:** Cold, contemptuous, and aggressive — she holds an open belief in giant racial superiority and does not soften it. She is volatile (screaming at Mirran over loose ends, slamming her fist into the living organ) but also calculating enough to participate in coordinated deception. The Imperator describes her as cold and unloving, a trait Hekaton apparently weighed in passing her over for succession.

## History with the Party

1. **First meeting (great hall):** Nym performed alongside Mirran and participated in a deception about Serissa's availability. She made dismissive remarks about Serissa's interest in small folk, escalating to a direct assertion that giants are superior — prompting a brief argument with Orsik and Vardis. She remained in the great hall when the party moved to the combat area.

2. **Throne room confrontation (observed by Thistle):** Nym stormed away from the living organ, struck it with her fist, and declared she needed to speak to the Queen — inadvertently giving Thistle the opportunity to slip through. In the throne room, she aggressively mocked Serissa for allegedly seeking a dwarf suitor via matchmaking services ("Ok Ogre," "The Short for the Tall"), compared Serissa's interest in small folk unfavorably even to their mother's, and bluntly stated the Frost and Fire giants had eaten the party already.

3. **Overheard argument:** Orsik overheard Nym screaming at Mirran about "loose ends" after Mirran concluded the party must be dead.

4. **Underwater complex:** Nym does not appear in person, but the party's first shark identifies itself as her favorite pet. It notes she arrives from the shore side rather than the portcullis and that it had never seen anyone else in that area. Thistle falsely claimed to have been sent by Nym to pacify it. Nym appears to be the primary occupant of the quarters above the spiral staircase.

5. **Throne room, final confrontation:** Nym charged in alongside Mirran after the initial confrontation. When Serissa asked whether they had knowingly allied with a dragon, Nym shook her head and offered apologetics alongside Mirran. She was then escorted to her quarters under guard.

6. **Hekaton's return:** Nym greeted Hekaton with apparent joy ("Daddy, we're so happy to see you!"), though Unla Kee audibly doubted her sincerity. When Serissa's full account was given, Hekaton condemned Nym and Mirran for betraying him, betraying Serissa, and allying with Iymrith. Nym attempted to protest but was removed by guards on Serissa's order.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Confined to her chambers in the Maelstrom
- **Situation:** Fate pending — to be decided by Hekaton
- **Active operations:** None currently possible; her alliance with Adimond Kroskas is exposed
- **What the party knows:** Her alliance with Adimond Kroskas and Iymrith; her contempt for small folk; her role in opposing Serissa
- **What remains hidden:** The specific nature and terms of her alliance with Adimond Kroskas; her precise role in the conspiracy against Serissa and Hekaton

## Relationships
- **Mirran:** Co-conspirator and elder sister; the two act in concert. Nym has screamed at Mirran over perceived incompetence ("loose ends"), suggesting tension within their alliance
- **Serissa:** Younger sister and Queen; Nym openly mocks and undermines her, viewing her interest in small folk as shameful
- **Hekaton:** Father; feigns joy at his return. Now condemned by him for her betrayal
- **Adimond Kroskas:** Ally; nature and terms of the alliance unclear
- **Iymrith:** Allied, whether wittingly or not — Nym denied knowing about the dragon alliance, though this is unverified
- **The Party:** Contemptuous; considers them dead or beneath notice. Unaware of the full extent of what they uncovered
