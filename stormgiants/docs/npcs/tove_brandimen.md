# Tove Brandimen

## Identity
- Role/faction: Unknown civilian; revealed to be a **confederate of Kagrota Threeteeth**
- First appearance: Prior to the documented session; approached the party with a fabricated hostage story, providing a forged note supposedly from Kagrota and a severed finger as "evidence" that his mother was being held captive at a cabin

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Unclear, but acted in service of Kagrota's interests — likely for personal gain
- Demonstrated willingness to use emotional manipulation and fabricated evidence to direct others into danger
- Operated with enough cunning to construct a convincing cover story, though experienced con artists (Vardis and Orsik) immediately recognized the shape of the scam

## History with the Party
- **Earlier reference (Ragnar's intel):** Tove was mentioned by Ragnar Redtooth during a briefing at the Golden Tankard. Ragnar described him as one of three men he was bidding farewell to when the party arrived. Tove had delivered military intelligence to Ragnar: monsters are on the move, the forces are constantly moving and destroying what they find, and he was unable to locate their main camp. Described as a young half-elf wearing worn furs.
- **Prior to session:** Tove presented himself to the party as a desperate son, using a forged note and a severed finger to convince them his mother was being held hostage at a cabin by Kagrota Threeteeth
- **During session (discussed, not present):** Rescued prisoners confirm that Tove was "thick as thieves" with Kagrota, exposing him as a willing collaborator rather than a victim. The severed finger is identified as belonging to a member of a commune Kagrota raided; its reveal causes one of the surviving women to break down in tears

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Unknown — whereabouts not established
- **Active plans:** Unknown; his immediate usefulness to Kagrota's operation may have ended
- **What the party knows:** Tove deliberately deceived them to manipulate them into going to the cabin; the hostage story was entirely fabricated
- **What remains hidden:** His current location, his exact arrangement with Kagrota, and his deeper motivations

## Relationships
- **Kagrota Threeteeth:** Willing collaborator; described as "thick as thieves".
- **Ragnar Redtooth:** Handler/contact who received Tove's "scouting" intelligence reports; Ragnar spoke of him with enough familiarity to include him in a farewell.
- **The party (Vardis):** Vardis recognized the scam early, comparing it to cons she and Orsik had run themselves.
- **The party (Orsik):** Expressed the most open hostility upon learning the truth — *"I told you we should have fed him to the Wyvern"*.
- **Commune survivors:** The finger he used as a prop belonged to one of their own; at least one survivor was devastated by its revelation.

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease** — Confirmed betrayal: Tove's complicity with Kagrota exposed by survivor testimony; fabricated evidence caused direct emotional harm to commune survivors
