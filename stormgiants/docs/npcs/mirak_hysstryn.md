# Mirak Hysstryn

## Identity
- Deceased resident of Triboar (dead approximately ten years)
- No formal title or faction affiliation
- Subject of a missing persons/murder investigation brought to the party by his brothers Jacoby (also referred to as Jacob) and Persson Hysstryn

## Personality & Motivations
- Not applicable (deceased prior to campaign events)
- Known to have had an affair with Urgala Meltimer following the disappearance of Urgala's wife; described by Urgala as having been madly in love with her

## History with the Party
- Brothers Jacoby and Persson hired the party to investigate Mirak's disappearance, which his wife Tolmara had attributed to him running off with a lady friend
- A divination by Helm revealed Mirak never left home and was not buried in the ground but above it
- The party located his remains in the sealed attic of the Six Windows Inn, consistent with the divine tip from a drunk priest of Helm found passed out at the Talking Troll
- When Unla Kee touched the skeleton, it briefly animated before being destroyed in a single blow by Orsik
- No cause of death was determined from the bones alone
- The party used the remains as evidence to convince Lord Protector Darathra to imprison Tolmara pending a Speak with the Dead casting
- Delratha arranged for Speak with the Dead to be performed the following morning; results not yet recorded in notes

## Current Status
- Remains are in party/authority custody as evidence
- Cause of death officially unconfirmed pending Speak with the Dead
- **Hidden:** Whether Tolmara is truly responsible, and what exactly occurred in the attic, remain unresolved

## Relationships
- **Tolmara Hysstryn** — His wife; claimed he abandoned her for another woman; primary suspect in his murder per Urgala's implication; currently imprisoned by Lord Protector Darathra
- **Urgala Meltimer** — His lover following the disappearance of Urgala's wife; suspects Tolmara killed him upon discovering the affair
- **Jacoby & Persson Hysstryn** — His brothers; initiated the investigation with the party
- **The Party** — Retained by his brothers; discovered and handled his remains
