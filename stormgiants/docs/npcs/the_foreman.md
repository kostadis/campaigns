# The Foreman

## Identity
- **Role:** Foreman of the Mirabar Mines compound
- **Faction:** Mirabar Mines (employer/administrator)
- **First Appearance:** Summoned by a guard after Orsik claimed to be the hero who caught the Weevil; met the party at the compound gates

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Maintaining security and productivity at the Mirabar Mines compound; vetting potential threats before engaging them
- Cautious and methodical — he verifies claims independently before acting on them, using back-channel contacts rather than confronting the party directly
- Pragmatic opportunist: even when his suspicions are confirmed, he finds a way to extract value from the situation rather than simply dismissing the party
- Privately irritable when his plans are complicated, but patient enough to let others (like Vardis) make his argument for him

## History with the Party
1. **First Meeting:** A guard summoned the Foreman after Orsik invoked his reputation as the hero who caught the Weevil. The Foreman believed Orsik had already been compensated and moved to dismiss the group.
2. **Invitation to Dinner:** When Orsik offered bounty hunting services, the Foreman recognized a potential use for the party and invited them to dinner and a morning meeting.
3. **Background Verification:** Before the morning meeting, the Foreman contacted "the Ax" via unique sending stones. He learned that Orsik had exaggerated his role — it was the Ax who extracted the caravan code information from the Weevil, not Orsik. Orsik had only reported the Weevil's offer.
4. **Morning Meeting & Job Offer:** Despite the embellishment, the Foreman tasked the party with eliminating crag cats a day's hike out. He was privately annoyed when Harshnag and the Ranger tried to talk Orsik out of accepting. The job was salvaged when Vardis highlighted the value of the pelts, and the party agreed to go.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Mirabar Mines compound
- **Active Plans:** The crag cat elimination job is in progress — the party has accepted and departed
- **What the Party Knows:** He is the compound Foreman who offered them work; he was skeptical of Orsik's story
- **What Remains Hidden:** He independently verified Orsik's exaggerated account via the Ax; he suspects the party may be connected to the Weevil or have been fed information by him; he knows the true account of who extracted the caravan codes

## Relationships
- **"The Ax":** Trusted back-channel contact; reachable via unique sending stones; was the one who actually extracted caravan code information from the Weevil
- **Orsik (3HPP):** Suspicious of him — knows his heroic account was embellished; watching carefully
- **Harshnag & the Ranger (3HPP):** Noted their reluctance to take the job; likely views them as a complicating factor within the party
- **Vardis (3HPP):** Inadvertently aided the Foreman's agenda by making the case for the pelt value; likely viewed more favorably
