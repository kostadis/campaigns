# Mama Mia

## Identity
- **Role/Title/Faction:** Named subordinate commander or troop supplier within Bad Fruul's horde; commands or maintains ogres and half-ogres
- **First Appearance:** Never appeared directly; introduced through her belongings, living space, and a battlefield reference by Bad Fruul

## Personality & Motivations
- No direct personality information available from source notes.

## History with the Party
- The party discovered what appears to be Mama Mia's bedroom on the roof of a sunken wizard's tower. Her stone table (disguised as a bed) conceals a staircase into the tower below, opened via her pendant.
- During the assault on Parnast, Bad Fruul deployed "Mama Mia's ogres" as part of the second wave. Her half-ogres were killed by Thistle's archers; her ogres successfully breached the wall.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Unknown; not present at either referenced scene
- **Active Plans/Operations:** Troop contribution to Bad Fruul's ongoing siege/assault operations
- **Known vs. Hidden:** The party knows she commands ogres and half-ogres under Bad Fruul, and that she has a residence atop a sunken wizard's tower. Her current whereabouts, appearance, and deeper role are unknown.

## Relationships
- **Bad Fruul:** Subordinate relationship; Bad Fruul references her troops by name, suggesting she holds a recognized command role within the horde
- **The Party:** No direct contact; known only through her belongings and battlefield reference
