# Yazmeen

## Identity
- **Role:** Town librarian of Prettyview
- **Faction:** None known
- **First Appearance:** Found hiding behind a couch in the Prettyview library — the only building still standing in the ruined town. The party discovered him while investigating the aftermath of a Frost Giant attack.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Protect his books and serve his library patrons; remain in Prettyview regardless of hardship.
- Stubborn and deeply attached to his post — refuses to leave despite the town being destroyed, cold, and empty.
- Presents as hospitable and resourceful (offering tea, shelter), but was clearly concealing the severity of his situation, likely out of denial or desperation.

## History with the Party
1. **First Contact:** Party found Yazmeen hiding in the library. He explained the Frost Giant attack and the feline creatures left behind. He offered the party shelter for the night and served magical tea.
2. **The Slaad Incident:** Overnight, a Slaad emerged from Yazmeen's bedroom. The library was set ablaze during the encounter. Vardis carried Yazmeen out of the burning building unconscious.
3. **Revelation:** Upon waking, Yazmeen disclosed that the Slaad had been using Prettyview's inhabitants as interplanar anchors to summon its kind — a ritual disrupted mid-casting by the Frost Giant attack. Yazmeen himself had been used as a living portal. He confirmed that his magical tea had kept the Slaad at bay while he stayed awake, but that falling asleep had allowed the creature to return through him.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Prettyview — remaining in or near his now-ruined library.
- **Condition:** Alive and physically unharmed.
- **Active Situation:** The Slaad threat appears to have been dealt with during the party's visit, but Yazmeen's role as a former interplanar anchor and the full consequences of that connection are unclear.

**What the party knows:** Yazmeen was used as a Slaad portal; his magical tea was a warding agent; he insists on staying in Prettyview.
**What remains hidden:** The full nature of the Slaad's connection to him, whether that bond is truly severed, and the origin or recipe of his magical tea.

## Relationships
- **The Party (general):** Cautiously trusting; accepted their help and was honest once the crisis was undeniable.
- **Vardis (specific):** Owes Vardis his life — carried out of the burning library while unconscious. Vardis also correctly deduced the tea's warding properties.
- **Prettyview Patrons:** Deeply loyal to the town's former inhabitants; their memory is a driving reason he refuses to leave.
