# Darz Helgar

## Identity
- **Role:** Caretaker and overseer of Triboar's northern campgrounds; lives in a cottage on site
- **Faction:** Former member of the Coffin Nail thieves guild (Waterdeep); currently Zhentarim (Black Network)
- **First Appearance:** Party confronted him at the campgrounds after Orsik presented a leather harness and grappling hook as evidence

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Staying hidden from his past; getting revenge on Kaelen Sarssir; done running — expressed relief when caught and indifference to being reported
- Tired, resigned, and pragmatic — he folded immediately under Orsik's scrutiny rather than resist. Cooperated fully once he sensed the party could deliver consequences to Kaelen. Carries the weariness of a man who has been looking over his shoulder for a decade.

## History with the Party
1. **Confrontation at the Campgrounds:** When Orsik presented the harness, Darz confessed immediately. He admitted to stealing gold and bags from the Lionshield Coster but explained he was blackmailed into it — Kaelen Sarssir discovered his criminal past and threatened to inform Triboar's Lord Protector.
2. **The Deal:** Orsik negotiated an arrangement: the party keeps Darz's involvement and criminal past secret; in exchange, Darz provides his contacts with the Thieves Guild. Darz agreed, saying *"It was ten years ago, but I will."*
3. **Zhentarim Recognition:** During conversation, Darz made a Zhentarim hand signal, which Orsik reciprocated. This opened a more candid exchange.
4. **Intelligence Provided:** Darz revealed he was formerly a Coffin Nail guild member before his arrest ten years ago. He is uncertain whether the Coffin Nail still exists. He directed the party to **Skullport** as the best place to find traces of the guild. He also shared that the Black Network in Waterdeep is **split into two factions**, one led by **Manshoon** — whom Darz claims is alive and well, contrary to stories that his clones were destroyed.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Triboar northern campgrounds (cottage on site)
- **Active Situation:** Holding to his agreement with the party; presumably continuing his caretaker role quietly
- **What the Party Knows:** His full criminal history, his Zhentarim affiliation, the blackmail by Kaelen, and the intelligence he provided
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full extent of his remaining Zhentarim connections; whether his Coffin Nail contacts are still viable after a decade of separation

## Relationships
- **Kaelen Sarssir (the Talking Troll / Troll Tavern):** Antagonist. Kaelen blackmailed Darz into committing the theft. Darz wants him to face consequences and cooperated with the party largely out of desire for revenge.
- **Orsik:** Primary contact within the party. Struck the deal directly with Darz. Shared Zhentarim recognition signals — a significant moment of mutual trust.
- **The Coffin Nail (Waterdeep):** Former guild. Darz lost contact after his arrest ten years ago and does not know if it still operates.
- **Manshoon:** Mentioned as leader of one Zhentarim faction in Waterdeep; Darz vouches he is alive. No direct relationship indicated.
