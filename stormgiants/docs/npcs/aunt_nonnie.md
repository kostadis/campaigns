# Aunt Nonnie

## Identity
- **Role:** Innkeeper, cook, and proprietor of a rustic dining hall and lodgings in Llorkh; serves vegetarian-only meals
- **Faction:** Independent; serves visitors who do not stay with the Iron Circle
- **First Appearance:** Party entered her inn in Llorkh; she welcomed them in and served them dinner. The party returned (Day 8, 1st Tenday of Taraksh) after their journey from the summit.

*Also known as: Nonnie.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core drives:** Running her establishment with warmth and dignity; feeding and sheltering people regardless of circumstance.
- Insists everyone call her "Aunt Nonnie" and takes visible pride in her exclusively vegetarian menu.
- Warm, welcoming, and reliably hospitable — the party considers her a trusted haven.
- Carries quiet authority, particularly over Orsik, and is not above using it to keep order.
- Runs a tight ship and expects basic courtesy from her guests — she's quick to raise prices when she feels disrespected, and equally quick to relent when an apology is offered.
- Curious about (and perhaps invested in) adventuring activity around the local tower, showing visible disappointment when told the excursion was a one-time venture.
- Privately sized up the party as "a nice group of people"; treats travelers, refugees, and strangers alike as human beings deserving of basic respect.

## History with the Party
1. **First meeting:** Party arrived at her inn; she welcomed them warmly and took their order.
2. **Dinner service:** Returned twenty minutes later with four cheese pies — described as perfectly cooked, tasty beyond belief, and filling.
3. **End of evening:** Offered the party rooms for the night at cost, noting they were not fancy.
4. **Day 8, 1st Tenday of Taraksh — return visit:** Party arrived back at her inn following their journey from the summit. She greeted them warmly, provided rooms and vegetarian meals, and relayed local news about frequent kobold sightings and the town guard's refusal to act. When Orsik mumbled that he would do the same as the guards, she silenced him with a pointed reference to "that THING you did," suggesting she has knowledge of his past.
5. **Following morning:** Served the party breakfast. Expressed surprise that Vardis and Unla Kee returned safely from the tower, and asked if they planned to go back; seemed genuinely disappointed by Vardis's "one-time venture" answer. Orsik made a remark she took as disrespectful and she raised her prices on the spot; after Orsik apologized, she relented and restored normal rates.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Her establishment in Llorkh
- **Active plans:** None known beyond running her business; sheltering at least one refugee woman who fled Greenest
- **Party knows:** She is Fiora Bendaega's base of operations and a safe place to return to for future work; prickly but fair; has some interest in what's happening at the tower; has some hold over Orsik via past knowledge
- **Hidden from party:** The nature of her prior history with Orsik; reasons for her curiosity about the tower; full background unknown

## Relationships
- **Orsik:** Holds some form of authority or leverage over him due to knowledge of an unspecified past event; he defers to her. Brief friction over a careless remark resolved after an apology.
- **Vardis & Unla Kee:** Familiar enough to note their return; curious about their tower excursion.
- **Vardis:** Jokingly (or not) suspects her friendliness signals evil cult affiliation — no evidence to support this.
- **Fiora Bendaega:** Uses Nonnie's inn as her base of operations; directed the party to find her there.
- **Refugee from Greenest:** Currently sheltering at the inn; Nonnie is treating her with care and dignity.
- **The Party (general):** Trusted, familiar figure — treated as a reliable rest stop and safe haven. Functional landlord/guest relationship, slightly warmer if treated with respect.
