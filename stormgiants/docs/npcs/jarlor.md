# Jarlor

## Identity
- **Role:** Resident of Parnast; former town council member; reluctant spy for Orc Chief Kurglow
- **Faction:** None active; previously coerced into service by the Kurglow orc clan
- **First Appearance:** Not present in person — first revealed through the testimony of his family, freed from the orc cave. Later appears briefly in Parnast during early battle dialogue.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Protecting his family; no apparent ideological loyalty to Kurglow or the orcs
- Jarlor is passive under pressure, deferring to authority figures even when he privately doubts them. He is capable of dry sarcasm, muttering skeptically about the quality of Orsik's threats. He required his wife's challenge to push him into action, suggesting he is reluctant but not cowardly — simply conflict-averse.

## History with the Party
1. **Before party contact:** Jarlor was coerced into spying for Chief Kurglow, who held his family hostage in the orc cave as leverage. His child later testified he spied against his will.
2. **Spy channel message:** Jarlor was persuaded (partly by his wife's challenge) to transmit a covert message to Kurglow via the spy channel on the party's behalf. The message, composed by Orsik, was a comically non-threatening warning about denying the orc whelp dessert and a bedtime story. Jarlor internally questioned whether to correct the dwarf but deferred to him.
3. **Early battle dialogue:** Jarlor appeared briefly in Parnast, muttering sarcastically about the ineffectiveness of Orsik's threats.
- **Orsik** recognizes Jarlor from his time on the town council.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Parnast
- **Active Situation:** No ongoing operations recorded; spy role appears concluded with the freeing of his family
- **What the Party Knows:** That he was a coerced spy, that his family is now free, and that he is in Parnast
- **What Remains Hidden:** His true feelings about the party; whether he holds resentment or gratitude; any remaining ties to Kurglow or the orc clan

## Relationships
- **Chief Kurglow:** Coercer — Jarlor spied under duress due to Kurglow's hostage-taking; relationship appears dissolved with the family's rescue
- **Jarlor's Wife:** A motivating force; she challenged him to act when he hesitated
- **Jarlor's Child:** Defended Jarlor's character to the freed prisoners and the party
- **Orsik (party):** Recognized from the town council; Jarlor defers to him as an authority figure despite privately doubting his competence; relationship is mildly sardonic on Jarlor's end
