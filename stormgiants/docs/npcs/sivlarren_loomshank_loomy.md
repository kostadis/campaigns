# Sivlarren Loomshank ("Loomy")

## Identity
- **Role:** Disgraced itinerant cleric of Helm
- **Faction:** Formerly affiliated with the clerical hierarchy in Neverwinter; exiled to the Allfaiths Shrine in Red Larch
- **First Appearance:** Found drinking at the Talking Troll tavern in Triboar; identified as the cleric hired by the brothers to divine Mirak's location

## Personality & Motivations
- A self-pitying drunk who deflects blame — insists his banishment from Neverwinter's clerical hierarchy was not his fault.
- Withheld critical information from the brothers out of self-preservation, fearing their anger, but opened up once the party cleared his debts.
- Motivated primarily by avoiding conflict and staying solvent; unlikely to act heroically without direct personal incentive.

## History with the Party
1. **Triboar, Talking Troll:** Party located Loomy nursing drinks, trapped by tavern debts he couldn't pay.
2. **Party paid off his tab**, unlocking his full cooperation.
3. **Revealed the complete divine reading:** Helm had shown him that Mirak was still at home and had never left — *and* that she was not buried in the ground but above it. Loomy had deliberately suppressed the second detail when reporting to the brothers.
4. His full account directed the party to search the attic of the Six Windows Inn.
5. **Once his debt was cleared, Loomy departed.** Current whereabouts unknown.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Triboar (the Talking Troll); departed after the party settled his tab.
- **Active plans:** None known — likely drifting, possibly heading back toward Red Larch and the Allfaiths Shrine.
- **What the party knows:** His full divine reading; his troubled history with the Neverwinter clerical hierarchy; his exile.
- **What remains hidden:** The specific nature of his trouble in Neverwinter and why he was truly banished.

## Relationships
- **The Brothers (clients):** Hired Loomy to find Mirak; he deceived them by omission, fearing their reaction to the full reading.
- **The Party:** Brief but transactional — they freed him from debt, he gave them everything he knew. No ongoing bond established.
- **Neverwinter Clerical Hierarchy of Helm:** Estranged; he was formally banished.
- **Allfaiths Shrine, Red Larch:** His assigned post, which he appears to have abandoned or fled.
