# Tiamat

## Identity
- Role/Title: Devil or devil-adjacent entity; instigator of the War of the Dragons
- Faction: None confirmed; acted as patron to Iymrith during the War of the Dragons
- First appearance: Referenced in discussion (does not appear in person in recorded sessions)

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: To enter the world (material plane or equivalent)
- Method: Manipulation — engineered the War of the Dragons and subverted the original Dracolich vision by manipulating Severin into creating a summoning ritual on her behalf
- Willing to use proxies and prolonged conflict as tools toward her ends

## History with the Party
- **Referenced in session discussion:** Bressik argued that Tiamat manipulated Severin into building a summoning ritual for her, using this to subvert the original Dracolich vision and deliberately prolong conflict among dragons.
- **Referenced in Thistle's conspiracy theory:** Thistle identified Tiamat as the instigator of the War of the Dragons. The war devastated the North and, as a side effect, provided Iymrith with the financial resources to begin capturing fairies. Tiamat served as Iymrith's patron during this period.
- **Defeated:** The First Faction defeated Tiamat at some point. Following this defeat, Iymrith lost Tiamat's sponsorship and was forced to seek a new patron.

## Current Status
- Defeated by the First Faction; no longer an active patron to Iymrith
- Current whereabouts and condition unknown beyond the fact of her defeat
- Whether she remains a latent threat is not addressed in the notes

## Relationships
- **Severin:** Manipulated him into creating a summoning ritual for her
- **Iymrith:** Former patron; funded Iymrith's fairy-capturing operation through resources generated by the War of the Dragons. Relationship ended with Tiamat's defeat.
- **The First Faction:** Enemies; defeated Tiamat
- **The Party:** No direct interaction recorded
