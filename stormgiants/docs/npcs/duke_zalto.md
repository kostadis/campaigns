# Duke Zalto

## Identity
- **Role:** Duke (lord) of Ironslag; commanding officer of Fire Giant search operations across the North
- **Faction:** Fire Giants; antagonist among the giant lords during the current upheaval of the Ordning
- **Note:** All Fire Giant leaders hold the title of duke — this is a rank within Fire Giant society, not unique to Zalto
- **First appearance:** Never met directly; first referenced through a Speak with Dead cast on a dead Fire Giant in Triboar, who declared Zalto would "soon rule over all giants"

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goal:** Domination over all giant kinds; assembling the Vonindod, an artifact associated with killing dragons, appears central to this ambition
- Other giant factions — particularly storm giants — regard his bid for supremacy as laughable; Blagothkus and Escarlotta mocked him openly when his ambitions were described
- Observed at leisure playing with hellhounds by tossing a metal ball containing a body, suggesting casual cruelty
- Commands significant loyalty from Fire Giants in the field, who refuse to return to him until their assigned missions are complete

## History with the Party
1. **Triboar (early sessions):** Fire Giants searching Triboar for a piece of the Vonindod were acting on Zalto's orders, though he was not present. A Speak with Dead ritual on a slain giant revealed his name and ambitions. The party later sold a bag of salt described as "fit for a duke" — apparently looted from Fire Giant stores — to the goblin merchant Fritz.
2. **Evermoors:** A Fire Giant encountered in the Evermoors explicitly stated she could not return to Zalto until she recovered a piece of the Vonindod, confirming he is directing search missions across the region.
3. **Mirabar / Harshnag:** Harshnag cited Zalto's coordinated operations as one of several alarming signs that giant aggression was escalating across multiple factions simultaneously.
4. **Oracle consultation:** The oracle revealed that Zalto is holding Laela. A question about a conch in his possession could not be asked before the oracle's lights were exhausted. He was identified as one of the evil giant lords the party would need to confront.
5. **Second oracle consultation:** The oracle named his stronghold as **Ironslag** — a forge east over mountains and snow, near a village of yakfolk — and confirmed the Conch of Teleportation is located in his personal quarters.
6. **Thistle's reconnaissance of Ironslag:** Thistle infiltrated Ironslag invisibly and observed Zalto at a distance in the assembly hall on the lower level, playing with hellhounds. Thistle located what he believed to be Zalto's bedchamber in the eastern section of the lower level but did not enter.
7. **Ironslag heist:** The party raided Zalto's bedchamber in his absence. The room was trapped with an incendiary fire blast. Contents recovered included the Conch of Teleportation, an adamantine crown, thousands of gold pieces, several magic items, a black dragon scale shield, and two fire-scorched wardrobes. Chains and manacles were noted above the bed. Thistle heard Zalto rushing up the stairs toward the wheel room after the wheel was destroyed and immediately fled.
8. **Post-Hekaton:** King Hekaton named Zalto as one of the giant lords who must kneel before him to restore the Ordning. Both Thistle and Vardis have personal stakes near Zalto's territory — Thistle believes Escarlotta is held somewhere in the region, and Vardis has a treasure map tied to his abode.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Ironslag, his forge stronghold east of the mountains near a yakfolk village
- **Active operations:** Directing Fire Giants across the North in search of Vonindod fragments; holding Laela captive
- **What the party knows:** His name, title, stronghold location (Ironslag), his ambition to rule all giants, his possession of Vonindod pieces, that Laela is his captive, and the layout of at least the lower level of his forge
- **What remains hidden:** The current state of his Vonindod assembly, his full military strength, and whether he is aware the party raided his bedchamber

## Relationships
- **Fire Giants under his command:** Absolute authority; field agents refuse to return until their missions are complete
- **Hekaton (Storm Giant King):** Hekaton demands Zalto's submission as part of restoring the Ordning; their relationship is one of open defiance
- **Blagothkus & Escarlotta:** Find his ambitions ridiculous and mocked him when described by Thistle
- **Harshnag:** Views Zalto's coordinated operations as a serious warning sign of broader giant destabilization
- **The Party:** Has never met them directly; they have raided his stronghold and stolen the conch without (known) attribution
