# Noori

## Identity
- **Role/Affiliation:** Member of an Uthgardt tribe (the same tribe as a chieftain previously killed by the party)
- **First Appearance:** Found alive, cocooned in the larder cavern of the warrens; freed by the party

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Locate and reunite with her tribe; escape the warrens
- Presents as determined and physically capable — she wasted no time fleeing once at the exit. She carries visible, intense hatred toward Unla Kee specifically, the cause of which is not yet explained.

## History with the Party
- Discovered unconscious in the third spider cocoon in the larder cavern and freed by the group
- Received goodberries from Thistle
- Expressed urgency to find her tribe and exit the warrens
- Orsik deceived her, claiming her tribe was found dead at the temple entrance rather than killed by the party — she believed the lie
- Escorted to the warren entrance where Harshnag was sleeping; Orsik warned her to stay quiet; she smiled at him and ran off alone

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Departed the temple on her own
- **Active Plans:** Seeking her tribe (who are, unknown to her, dead at the party's hands)
- **What the Party Knows:** She's gone, she believed Orsik's deception, and she has no knowledge that the party killed her chieftain
- **What Remains Hidden:** The true cause of her hatred toward Unla Kee; what she will do or discover when she investigates her tribe's fate

## Relationships
- **Orsik:** Mild trust — believed his deception, shared a brief warm moment at parting
- **Thistle:** Neutral/positive — received care from her
- **Unla Kee:** Open, intense hatred (reason unknown)
- **Her Tribe / Dead Chieftain:** Deep loyalty; unaware the party is responsible for the chieftain's death
