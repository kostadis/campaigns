# Uthor

## Identity
- **Role:** Former Imperator of the Storm Giant court — senior military and administrative authority during King Hekaton's absence; brother of King Hekaton
- **Faction:** Storm Giant court (Maelstrom)
- **First Appearance:** Observed by Thistle during the throne room scene, seated to Serissa's right, while Serissa held the throne in Hekaton's absence

*Also known as: Imperator Uthor.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** While in office, to maintain influence over Queen Serissa and manage court stability on his own terms; Thistle's read suggested he actively preferred a young, manipulable ruler he could guide from behind.
- Uthor is authoritative, condescending, and tightly controlled — he expresses frustration through barely disguised irritation rather than open outburst.
- Politically sharp enough to shut down Adminond Kroskas's power-grab with a single cutting rebuke, but he also takes physical action when he deems it necessary, grabbing Serissa's arm to force her composure.
- Presents a composed, unreadable exterior even under pressure; he did not protest or argue when stripped of power.
- His cryptic parting words — that it was "time I took care of other business" — paired with a knowing smile suggest he anticipated this outcome or has plans already in motion.
- His attempt to deceive Hekaton about his role in the search implies self-interest and willingness to manipulate those close to him.

## History with the Party

**Throne room (during Hekaton's absence):**
- During the throne room confrontation, Uthor was present when Nym attacked Serissa. He moved to physically restrain the Queen from escalating the situation.
- He weighed in dismissively on the search for Serissa's father, indicating the effort had stalled and clues had led nowhere.
- He directed Adminond Kroskas to task Mirran and Nym with confirming the events surrounding the alleged assassins.
- When Adminond floated the idea that a change in leadership might be warranted, Uthor shut him down sharply: *"Do not confuse your privilege with the right to speak about these matters."*

**Hekaton's Restoration:**
Uthor greeted the returned king with apparent joy, then attempted to take credit (or avoid blame) for the search efforts. The party directly contradicted his account, exposing his inaction to Hekaton.

**Stripped of Office:**
Hekaton dismissed Uthor from the Imperatorship on the spot and named Serissa his replacement. Uthor accepted this without resistance — calmly handed over his insignia to Serissa, declared he had "other business" to attend to, and walked out. His composure left the throne room in stunned silence.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Left the throne room at Maelstrom under unknown circumstances following his dismissal.
- **Active Plans:** Unknown, but his calm demeanor and reference to "other business" strongly imply a preexisting agenda unrelated to his role as Imperator.
- **What the Party Knows:** Thistle observed his behavior in the throne room and concluded he prefers Serissa as queen specifically because her youth and inexperience make her easier to control. He was stripped of power and left willingly. His parting words were unsettling.
- **What Remains Hidden:** The nature of his "other business," whether he anticipated his removal, the full extent of his agenda, whether he has any role in Hekaton's earlier disappearance, the nature of his relationship with other court factions, and whether he has allies or operations already in play.

## Relationships
- **Hekaton (King / Brother):** Formally deferential, but their bond appears hollow — Uthor did little to find Hekaton and attempted to deceive him on the matter.
- **Queen Serissa:** Publicly her advisor and supporter while Hekaton was missing; privately condescending and manipulative — viewed her as a tool for his own influence. Handed his insignia directly to her on his removal. The nature of any history or rivalry between them is unknown.
- **Adminond Kroskas (Iymrith in disguise):** Tolerated him at court but would not hesitate to publicly remind him of his place; actively hostile to Adminond overstepping.
- **Mirran & Nym:** Used Adminond as a conduit to direct their activities; did not engage them directly.
- **The Party:** They directly exposed his lie to Hekaton, making them responsible for his removal from power. He has reason to view them as adversaries.

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease:** Party contradicted Uthor's deception before Hekaton, leading directly to his removal as Imperator.
