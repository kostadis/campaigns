# Annam the Allfather

## Identity
- Role: Chief deity of the giant pantheon; divine patriarch of the giant races
- Faction: Giant divine pantheon (absent/silent)
- Has not appeared directly; referenced by the Imperator during his historical account of the shattering of the Ordning

## Personality & Motivations
- No direct characterization established — known only through his *absence*
- Does not answer prayers from the giant races
- His divine offspring are engaged in ongoing warfare against one another on the outer planes

## History with the Party
- **Referenced indirectly:** The Imperator cited Annam's silence and inaction as a contributing factor to King Hekaton's belief that the age of giants had passed. This spiritual abandonment helped precipitate the shattering of the Ordning.

## Current Status
- Last known status: Silent and absent; whereabouts or divine activities unknown
- No active plans or operations known
- **What the party knows:** That Annam has withdrawn divine guidance from the giant races and does not respond to prayer
- **What remains hidden:** Reasons for his silence; whether this absence is deliberate, circumstantial, or permanent

## Relationships
- **Giant races:** Their absent divine patron
- **His divine offspring:** Engaged in warfare with one another on the outer planes; fractious and without unity
- **King Hekaton:** Hekaton's despair over Annam's silence informed his fatalistic worldview
- **The Imperator:** Cited Annam's absence as historical context for giant decline
