# Pandi

## Identity
- A small, vibrantly maned dog; apparent guardian or inhabitant of a puzzle room within the Forgotten Forest
- Associated with the Forgotten Forest itself, which appears to have sent the glowing blue door as a shortcut or aid to the party
- First encountered when the party passed through a glowing blue door in the Forgotten Forest and entered Pandi's domain

## Personality & Motivations
- Core drives: connection with others, word games, and art; deeply enthusiastic about both
- Pandi is lonely and hungry for interaction, greeting the party with immediate excitement and declarations that they were expected
- Communicates telepathically — Thistle specifically hears yipping alongside Pandi's voice in his head
- Grows frustrated when players fail to engage with challenges earnestly, but pivots quickly to help rather than punish

## History with the Party
1. **Puzzle Room Entry:** Pandi greeted the party enthusiastically, declaring they were expected, and issued a word game: name what you seek at this very moment.
2. **Word Game:** Thistle guessed "a grail" then "a hallway" (earning the rebuke: *"They don't teach fairies anything these days in fairy school"*). Vardis guessed "keep walking through an exit." Pandi grew frustrated and tried to assist. Unla Kee eventually answered correctly with **"a passage,"** sending Pandi into delighted leaping.
3. **Tangram Challenge:** Pandi presented a Tangram puzzle, explaining their paws were not suited for small objects and asking the party to assemble the pieces into art for the room's walls. The party solved it; the picture attached to the wall and Pandi howled with joy, granting the party free passage.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Inside the puzzle room — a cold, fifteen-foot square space with blue marble walls, plush velvet cushion floors, and disembodied giggles and guffaws
- Appears to remain in the room as its permanent resident or guardian; no indication of movement beyond it
- **What the party knows:** Pandi guards passage, loves word games and art, communicates telepathically, and rewarded cooperation with warmth and joy
- **What remains hidden:** The true nature of Pandi's connection to the Forgotten Forest; whether Pandi can or would interact with the party again; the source of the disembodied laughter in the room

## Relationships
- **The Forgotten Forest:** Apparently connected — the Forest sent the blue door, suggesting Pandi acts on its behalf or within its design
- **Thistle:** Singled out for telepathic communication; also the recipient of Pandi's gentle rebuke about fairy education
- **Unla Kee:** Earned Pandi's greatest delight by solving the word game correctly
- **Vardis:** Attempted the word game but did not satisfy the challenge
