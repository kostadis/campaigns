# Tolmarra Hysstryn

## Identity
- **Role:** Proprietor of the Six Windows Inn; wife (likely widow) of Mirak Hysstryn
- **Faction:** None known
- **First appearance:** Discovered Thistle attempting to sneak in through a window and attacked him with a rolling pin and thrown potatoes while he hung from a rope outside the inn

## Personality & Motivations
- **Goals:** Appears primarily motivated by protecting her business and avoiding scrutiny — particularly regarding her husband's fate
- Aggressive and reactive under pressure, quick to escalate confrontations physically
- Notably softened when Thistle mentioned a giant bumblebee, for reasons unknown — suggesting a hidden or sentimental side beneath her hostility
- Unpopular among her own tenants, described as a peeping tom by at least one resident

## History with the Party
1. **Six Windows Inn (exterior):** Caught Thistle climbing in through a window; screamed accusations of trespassing and rent-evasion; attacked him with a rolling pin and potatoes. Briefly disarmed emotionally when he mentioned a giant bumblebee, after which she softened and offered him a salve.
2. **Attic discovery:** While Tolmarra was occupied outside, Orsik, Vardis, and Unla Kee entered the inn and found bones in the attic — subsequently identified as the remains of her husband, Mirak Hysstryn.
3. **Lord Protector's tower:** Escorted to Delratha's office along with the party and the brothers. When the group was dismissed, she attempted to leave with them but was held back by Delratha. She did not emerge from the tower that evening. Marketplace vendors had apparently been wagering on whether someone would spend the night in the tower.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Lord Protector Delratha's tower — detained there overnight
- **Active situation:** Retained by Delratha for unknown reasons after the rest of the party was dismissed; fate and current whereabouts after that night are unconfirmed
- **What the party knows:** Her husband's remains were found in the attic of her inn; she was held back by Delratha
- **What remains hidden:** Whether she murdered Mirak, how long the remains had been there, what Delratha wanted with her, and the significance of the bumblebee comment

## Relationships
- **Mirak Hysstryn (husband):** Deceased; his remains were found hidden in her inn's attic — implicating her in his death or disappearance
- **Lord Protector Delratha:** Detained by Delratha following the discovery; nature of their interaction is unknown
- **Thistle:** Antagonistic encounter that briefly softened; the bumblebee reference created an unexpected moment of vulnerability
- **Inn residents:** Actively unpopular; at least one tenant expressed relief at her being taken away
- **The party:** Adversarial by default; the party is directly responsible for surfacing evidence that led to her detention
