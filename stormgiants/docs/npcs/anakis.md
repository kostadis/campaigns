# Anakis

## Identity
- Role: Student at the Lady's College, Silverymoon; informal greeter/guide
- First appearance: The party encountered her in the hallways of the Lady's College, where she was wearing an "Ask Me" sign and approached the 3HPP proactively

## Personality & Motivations
- Enthusiastic about academics, particularly infernal lore — she cited Sage Vusk's lecture on Cambions as her favorite topic
- Sensitive about her tiefling heritage; briefly self-conscious when Orsik stared at her, and quick to defend the Lady's College's acceptance of non-goodly races before any criticism was offered

## History with the Party
- **First meeting:** Greeted the party in the Lady's College hallway and directed them to Sage Vusk's lecture on Cambions, noting that locals rarely attend because Silverymoon residents assume they'll never encounter infernal creatures

## Current Status
- Last known location: Hallway of the Lady's College, Silverymoon
- No known active plans beyond her student duties
- **Party knows:** She is a tiefling student, enthusiastic about infernal topics, and helpful to visitors
- **Hidden:** Nothing notable documented yet

## Relationships
- **Sage Vusk:** Attends his lectures; appears to be an admirer of his work
- **Orsik:** Brief moment of tension — his staring prompted a defensive reaction from her
