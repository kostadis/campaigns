# The Cambian (First)

## Identity
- **Role:** Fiendish enforcer / hunter; half-human, half-demon spawn acting on behalf of an unnamed master
- **Faction:** Unknown master's service
- **First Appearance:** Silverymoon — encountered while pursuing Orsik and Unla Kee through the city streets

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Retrieve their master's treasure; silence potential witnesses
- Calculating and methodical — led with charm attempts before resorting to direct force
- Willing to negotiate, but threats are not bluffs; delivered consequences immediately when Orsik stalled
- Shows no hesitation ordering lethal action against uninvolved parties as leverage

## History with the Party
1. Located Orsik and Unla Kee in Silverymoon and attempted to charm both; failed on both counts when Unla Kee's divination shifted fate and Orsik resisted
2. Pursued the pair through the city, negating Orsik's escape plan by flying
3. Struck Unla Kee with spells during the chase, wounding him severely (~1/3 HP remaining)
4. Followed Orsik into **Vinby Hearthgood's** house and issued a direct ultimatum: leave Silverymoon by tomorrow, say nothing about the master's business, and Unla Kee would be spared
5. When Orsik did not immediately agree, signaled its companion, who **killed Vinby Hearthgood**
6. Departed the scene after the killing; was not captured or destroyed

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Silverymoon — left Vinby Hearthgood's house after the execution
- **Active Plans:** Likely still monitoring whether Orsik complies with the ultimatum; master's treasure has not been recovered
- **What the Party Knows:** It works with at least one partner; it can fly; it is an enforcer for a larger power demanding return of stolen (or claimed) treasure
- **What Remains Hidden:** Identity of the master; nature of the "treasure"; whether it will honor the deal if terms are met; its own full capabilities

## Relationships
- **Unnamed Master:** Directly serves them; acts with clear authority to negotiate and order killings on the master's behalf
- **Second Cambian:** Operates as a partner in the field; executed Vinby Hearthgood on the First's signal
- **Orsik:** Primary target of the ultimatum; the First holds Orsik's compliance as leverage
- **Unla Kee:** Secondary target; wounded during pursuit, was used as a bargaining chip
- **Vinby Hearthgood:** Collateral — killed to demonstrate the master's seriousness when Orsik hesitated
