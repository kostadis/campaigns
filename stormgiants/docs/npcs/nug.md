# Nug

## Identity
- Role: Kobold leader; led the ambush group at the barricade
- Faction: Kobold tribe (same group as Agra)
- First appearance: Referenced (not present) during the barricade encounter; described through Agra's recollections and surrender negotiations

## Personality & Motivations
- Motivated by territorial defense, choosing to confront a heavily armed party despite the odds
- Agra privately called him "that damned fool," suggesting he was hotheaded or poor at tactical judgment
- Notable for arguing with Thistle (a fairy party member) over whether she counted as a "filthy creature" — petty enough to bicker mid-confrontation

## History with the Party
- Initiated the ambush by shouting "begone foul creatures!" at the party
- Argued with Thistle during the confrontation; Agra hoped this distraction would help spring the trap
- Reported to his group that the southern room contained a glowing magical fire symbol, a treasure, and a river of lava — intelligence Agra later passed to the party during surrender negotiations
- Killed during the failed ambush at the barricade; Agra referred to him and the other fallen kobolds as "dead buffoons"

## Current Status
- **Deceased.** Killed during the kobold ambush at the barricade.
- No active plans or operations.

## Relationships
- **Agra:** Fellow kobold; Agra viewed him with private contempt for picking an unwinnable fight
- **Thistle (party member):** Argued with her directly during the confrontation over whether she was a "filthy creature"
