# Zariel

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Archdevil; ruler of Avernus, the first layer of the Nine Hells
- **Faction:** Nine Hells (Avernus)
- **First Appearance:** Not met directly; first referenced by Delsephine, later by Sandesyl Morgia

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** To rid herself of Tiamat's presence in Avernus — described as eliminating an "annoying office mate."
- Operates through layered manipulation, orchestrating schemes at a distance while keeping her own hands clean. Willing to use major cosmic players (Tiamat, Severin, the Cult of the Dragon) as disposable instruments toward her personal ends.

## History with the Party
- **Not yet encountered directly.** All information has come secondhand through NPC testimony.
- **Via Delsephine:** Zariel served as Tiamat's hostess in Avernus. She manipulated Tiamat into believing the plan to manifest in the mortal world was Tiamat's own idea — Tiamat was "played for a fool."
- **Via Sandesyl Morgia:** Zariel was the architect behind Severin's manipulation of the Cult of the Dragon. The entire Cult plot was engineered as a means to remove Tiamat from Avernus.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Avernus, the Nine Hells
- **Active Plans:** Unknown — her immediate goal (removing Tiamat from Avernus) may have been achieved or remains in motion depending on Tiamat's current status.
- **What the Party Knows:** That Zariel secretly orchestrated Tiamat's attempted manifestation and the Cult of the Dragon's activities for her own ends.
- **What Remains Hidden:** Her broader ambitions beyond removing Tiamat; whether she has further interest in the mortal world or the party.

## Relationships
- **Tiamat:** Unwilling host and target of manipulation. Zariel used Tiamat's pride against her to engineer her own removal from Avernus.
- **Severin (Cult of the Dragon):** Allegedly manipulated by Zariel as a tool in her scheme against Tiamat.
- **The Party:** No direct relationship established. The party has only learned of her through enemy NPCs.
