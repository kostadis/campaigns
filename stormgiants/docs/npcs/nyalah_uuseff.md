# Nyalah Uuseff

## Identity
- **Role:** Tavern keeper of the Dancing Goat, Silverymoon
- **Affiliation:** None confirmed; cooperative relationship with the Silverwatch (city guard)
- **First Appearance:** The party encountered her at the Dancing Goat when Orsik arrived carrying the corpse of Unla Kee

## Personality & Motivations
- Deeply invested in maintaining order and the city's reputation — her immediate reaction to Unla Kee's corpse was denial that such creatures ("demons") could exist in Silverymoon
- Pragmatic and authoritative; she dresses in formal silk and gold, projecting status and control
- Prioritizes civic procedure over the party's needs, summoning the Silverwatch and refusing to release Unla Kee's body until they arrived

## History with the Party
- **Session [unknown]:** Witnessed Orsik carry Unla Kee's body into the Dancing Goat; reacted with alarm and disbelief, insisting that demonic creatures do not belong in Silverymoon
- **Following morning:** Summoned the Silverwatch to investigate reports of "ungodly races" in the city; blocked Orsik from removing Unla Kee's corpse pending the guard's arrival

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The Dancing Goat, Silverymoon
- **Active:** Cooperating with the Silverwatch investigation; holding Unla Kee's body on-site
- **What the party knows:** She called the guard and is preventing removal of the body
- **What remains hidden:** Her motivations beyond civic order — whether she has deeper ties to the Silverwatch or personal reasons for her reaction are unknown

## Relationships
- **The Silverwatch:** Appears to have a cooperative or deferential relationship; willing to summon them and act on their behalf
- **Orsik:** Adversarial in the short term; she obstructed his actions and escalated the situation to authorities
- **Unla Kee:** No established prior relationship noted
