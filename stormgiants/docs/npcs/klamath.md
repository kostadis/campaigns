# Klamath

## Identity
- **Role:** Red Dragon; necromancer; contract holder
- **Faction:** Original Cult of the Dragon (Dracolich ideology — unified undead draconic race), distinct from Severin's Tiamat-summoning splinter
- **Agent:** Delsephine acts as his negotiating representative
- **First Appearance:** Not present in person; introduced through negotiations over Bressik's contract

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Draconic domination of the North; advancing the original Dracolich ideology of a unified undead dragon race; expanding necromantic resources (including contracted servants like Bressik)
- Views giants as a direct obstacle to draconic territorial dominance and is therefore pragmatically supportive of the party's anti-giant efforts
- Operates through intermediaries and deliberate concealment — patient and calculating rather than directly confrontational

## History with the Party
- **Negotiations over Bressik's contract:** Klamath holds Bressik's contract in his hoard. The contract covers two terms: a living term (~300 gold, based on ~30 remaining years at 10 gp/year) and an undead term (~13,000 gold over an assumed 100 years of undead service). Delsephine confirmed Klamath is willing to negotiate a payout clause. She also noted that if Bressik's body were desecrated, Klamath would have interest in the corpse and parts for necromantic constructs.
- Klamath has not met the party directly. Delsephine conveyed his support for their plans and promised he will make contact and offer reward once the giant threat is resolved.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Unknown — deliberately withheld by Delsephine
- **Active Plans:** Monitoring the giant situation; awaiting resolution before making direct contact with the party
- **What the party knows:** He is a Red Dragon necromancer; he holds Bressik's contract; he supports their anti-giant efforts; Delsephine speaks for him; he will reach out after the giants are dealt with
- **What remains hidden:** His location; the full scope of his necromantic operations; his long-term intentions toward the party

## Relationships
- **Delsephine:** His agent and negotiator; the party's sole point of contact for anything Klamath-related
- **Bressik:** Holds a binding two-term contract over him (living and undead); views him as a necromantic resource
- **Severin (absent/deceased):** Ideological opposition — Klamath represents the original Dracolich faction that Severin abandoned in favor of summoning Tiamat
- **Orsik:** Orsik expressed genuine interest in meeting Klamath personally; Delsephine deflected
