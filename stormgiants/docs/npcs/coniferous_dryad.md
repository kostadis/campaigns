# Coniferous Dryad

## Identity
- Role / title / faction affiliation: Unseelie dryad; pine/conifer aspect
- First appearance: Encountered during a combat in a grove; the party engaged him directly

## Personality & Motivations
- No stated goals or deeper motivations were revealed before his death.
- Tactically cunning in combat — used crowd control, evasion, and charm offensively rather than simply fleeing.

## History with the Party
- **Combat encounter (grove):** Opened the fight by casting Entangle, trapping Thistle, Vardis, and Unla Kee. Used tree-step teleportation to evade after taking a magic missile from Unla Kee, then reappeared beside Unla Kee and charmed him into sitting down and drinking. Teleported again to the far side of the grove. Killed by Orsik, who knocked him prone and cut him in half; his death broke the charm on Unla Kee.

## Current Status
- **Deceased.** Killed in the grove by Orsik.
- No active plans or operations.

## Relationships
- **Unla Kee:** Targeted specifically with charm magic during combat.
- **Orsik:** Delivered the killing blow.
- **Thistle, Vardis:** Caught in his opening Entangle.
