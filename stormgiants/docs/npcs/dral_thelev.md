# Dral Thelev

## Identity
- **Role:** Proprietor/manager of Danivarr's House in Everlund; Harper contact; runs Danivarr's House on behalf of the Zoar family as well
- **Faction:** Harpers (affiliated via Darathra Shendrel); also operates as a Zoar-family proxy contact in Everlund
- **First Appearance:** Referenced by both Darathra at the Talking Troll and Tamalin Zoar (as her contact in Everlund); party first met him in person at Danivarr's House in Everlund when they presented her platinum badge

*Also known as: Dral.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Facilitating Harper operations quietly; maintaining useful political and factional connections across the region
- A sardonic, self-aware half-orc who takes visible pleasure in subverting the prejudices of "goodly" societies — most notably by drinking fine Silverymoon wine specifically because it would offend Silverymoon's citizens. He is hospitable and socially adept, greasing meetings and conversations with food, drink, and wry humor. Beneath the ribbing and wit, he shows genuine investment in the people he works with, expressing quiet concern about whether the party is up to the task Krowen Valharrow set them.

## History with the Party

**First Contact (Everlund, early visits)**
The party arrived bearing Darathra's platinum badge bearing the Triboar insignia. Dral recognized it immediately and noted something must be amiss with Darathra. He shared his views on Silverymoon's cultural insularity at length, then used an enchanted bottle of Silverymoon wine to teleport the party to Moongleam Tower — a security measure to keep Harper operations inconspicuous.

**Political Briefing (Everlund)**
Dral connected the party with intelligence about the local political situation. He revealed that Tamalin Zoar's brother is High Captain Horix Zoar, commander of Everlund's army, and that Zoar broke the compact between Everlund and Silverymoon after High Marshal Methrammar Aerasumé abandoned his allies — a grievance Zoar has never forgiven. Dral suggested Horix Zoar could provide the party diplomatic cover to enter Silverymoon as Everlund representatives, shielding them from local law enforcement. He noted that Orsik has a prior connection to Tamalin Zoar and used that rapport to facilitate the intelligence exchange.

**Return Visit (After Airborne Travel)**
When the party returned to Danivarr's House after several days of travel, Dral greeted Orsik and the group with light ribbing about whether they had solved the ordning crisis. He muttered concern that Krowen Valharrow must have had a reason for choosing them. He arranged for a tavern boy to fetch Captain Zoar and later supplied food, drink, and a house specialty dish at Zoar's request, facilitating the meeting.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Danivarr's House, Everlund
- **Active Role:** Operating as a Harper contact and regional information broker; facilitating connections between the party, the Harpers, and Everlund's political leadership
- **What the Party Knows:** He is a Harper affiliate, a trusted contact of Darathra's, and has connections to Captain Horix Zoar via Tamalin Zoar; he can arrange meetings and has some form of teleportation capability tied to his establishment
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full extent of his Harper responsibilities and what other operations he may be running out of Danivarr's House

## Relationships
- **Darathra Shendrel (Harper superior/peer):** Trusted friend and professional contact; his recognition of her badge and remark that "something must be amiss" suggests genuine personal regard
- **Krowen Valharrow:** Referenced as the figure who chose the party; Dral defers to his judgment, if nervously
- **Tamalin Zoar:** Mutual acquaintance with Orsik; connected to her brother High Captain Horix Zoar; trusts him as a proxy contact (instructed the party to present her letter to him).
- **High Captain Horix Zoar:** Not a direct ally, but Dral understands the political landscape well enough to use Zoar as a resource for the party
- **Orsik (party member):** Prior acquaintance via Tamalin Zoar; Dral is warmer and more candid with him than with the group at large
