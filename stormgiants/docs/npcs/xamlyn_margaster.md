# Xamlyn Margaster

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Lady Margaster; noble of House Margaster, a respectable Waterdhavian noble house
- **Faction:** House Margaster; connected to infernal forces (the Cambions who serve her)
- **First Appearance:** Star Court in Silverymoon, flanked by two of her Cambion companions, during the party's legal proceedings

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Protecting her investments and interests in Silverymoon and Waterdeep; avoiding legal or magical scrutiny; reaching a private arrangement with the party to neutralize their threat to her
- Xamlyn is imperious and accustomed to deference, immediately attempting to reframe a criminal matter as a drunken misunderstanding and departing before magic could be used to examine testimony. She is politically shrewd, leveraging her financial importance to Silverymoon's post-war recovery as a shield. She does not negotiate from vulnerability — she sets terms and expects compliance.

## History with the Party
1. **Star Court, Silverymoon:** Xamlyn arrived flanked by her Cambion companions during the party's court appearance. She requested a private arrangement with Orsik and his companions, then refused magical examination of testimony, reframed the events as a drunken party incident, and departed before proceedings could implicate her. Orsik refused to deal with her, calling her "a literal devil."
2. **Background revealed (inter-session):** The Three Halflings and a Pony (3HPP) came to Silverymoon specifically to pursue Xamlyn, who committed what Thistle describes as "unpardonable sins" in Everlund. High Lord Aerasumé refused to extradite her, citing her noble status and guest rights. Senneth confirmed her Cambion companions are the same men who appeared at the trial. Thistle concluded that killing her is insufficient — she must be disgraced by publicly exposing her devils.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Within Silverymoon, under the protection of High Lord Aerasumé as a guest of the city
- **Active Operations:** Continuing sizeable financial investments in Silverymoon and Waterdeep; the Cambions who attacked the party were acting on behalf of her and seeking *her* treasure
- **What the Party Knows:** She is connected to the Cambions; she has committed serious offenses in Everlund; she sought a deal with the party and was rebuffed; she is politically protected
- **What Remains Hidden:** The exact nature of the "unpardonable sins" in Everlund; the full scope of her infernal connections; what treasure her Cambions were seeking

## Relationships
- **House Margaster / Waterdeep:** Affiliated noble house; her status grants her political protection in Silverymoon
- **High Lord Aerasumé:** Extended her guest protection and refused extradition, citing her noble standing and the city's financial dependence on her investments
- **The Cambions:** Her direct servants; confirmed by Senneth to be the men who accompany and act on her behalf
- **Orsik:** Targeted him specifically for a private arrangement; was flatly refused
- **Thistle (3HPP):** Thistle views her as a target requiring disgrace rather than death; the 3HPP traveled to Silverymoon expressly to pursue her
- **The Party (general):** She approached them seeking neutralization of their threat; they are now opposed to her interests
