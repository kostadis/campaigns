# Xaltivar

## Identity
- Role / title / faction affiliation: Unknown; operates as a planner/organizer of armed operations
- First appearance: Referenced indirectly via Fred and George's report; has not appeared in person

## Personality & Motivations
- Demonstrates strategic cunning — willing to mount a costly diversionary assault to conceal a precise secondary objective
- Treats open military force as a tool for misdirection rather than a direct means to an end

## History with the Party
- Organized a two-pronged attack on Xanthalar's Keep: a large frontal assault to pull the guard away, and a simultaneous bugbear infiltration over the walls targeting prisoner Worvil Forkbeard ("The Weevil"). The party, alongside the Three Halfpints and a Pint, repelled both elements and foiled the operation.

## Current Status
- Last known location: Unknown; not present at the attack
- Active plans: Unknown following the failed operation
- **Party knows:** Xaltivar organized the attack and specifically wanted The Weevil captured
- **Hidden:** Xaltivar's identity, location, faction, and reasons for wanting The Weevil remain unknown to the party

## Relationships
- **Worvil Forkbeard ("The Weevil"):** Primary target; Xaltivar wants him captured for unknown reasons
- **Bugbears:** Used as operatives in the infiltration element of the assault
- **The Party:** Indirect antagonist; plans directly thwarted by them
