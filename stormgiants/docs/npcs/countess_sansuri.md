# Countess Sansuri

## Identity
- **Role:** Cloud Giant Countess (sometimes addressed as "Queen" in conspiracy contexts); contender for dominance over the giant hierarchy (the Ordning)
- **Faction:** Cloud Giants; ruler of Lyn Armaal, her flying castle; implicated in the conspiracy around Queen Neri's assassination
- **Family:** Sister of Count Thullen
- **First Appearance:** Referenced only — has not appeared in person at the table

*Also known as: Queen Sansuri, Sansuri.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Recover magical artifacts her family held when Ostoria fell; use them to establish rulership over all giants and claim dominance in the Ordning
- Described consistently as vain, arrogant, greedy, selfish, and "untethered from reality"
- Harshnag characterizes her as dangerous and not a good person; Escarlotta views her as a "classless poseur" with no legitimate claim to giant leadership
- She is surrounded by enemies who wish to see her toppled

## History with the Party
- Has not interacted with the party directly.
- The Oracle confirmed the conch the party needs is hidden in a **secret chest in the highest spire of Lyn Armaal**.
- The Oracle also confirmed her location: Lyn Armaal, a flying castle found by traveling **southeast to the untamed moors and looking to the sky**.
- King Hekaton named Sansuri as one of the Cloud Giant leaders who must kneel before him as part of restoring the Ordning.
- **Conspiracy lead:** Referenced as the giant who provided a golden goose coin to the 3HPP during events at the Maelstrom. The coin was recovered from the body of one of Queen Neri's assassins and passed to the party as evidence of a conspiracy implicating her in the assassination plot.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Lyn Armaal (flying castle, southeastern untamed moors)
- **Active Plans:** Pursuing Ostorian magical artifacts to solidify her claim over the giants and the Ordning
- **What the party knows:** Her location, her ambitions, the fact that the conch is in her castle's highest spire
- **What remains hidden:** Her current defenses, the extent of her artifact collection, the nature of her specific plans going forward

## Relationships
- **Count Thullen** — her brother (no further detail known)
- **Harshnag** — views her negatively; calls her dangerous
- **Escarlotta** — intensely hostile; has named killing Sansuri as her price for flying her castle to the Eye of the All-Father; believes Blagothkus should have targeted Sansuri's maps rather than pursuing dragon schemes
- **King Hekaton** — sees her as a subordinate who must submit to his restored authority
- **The Party** — no direct relationship yet; she is an obstacle standing between them and the conch they need; the party holds a golden-goose coin tied to her as evidence of conspiracy
- **Queen Neri** — implicated adversarial relationship; one of Neri's assassins was carrying Sansuri's coin
