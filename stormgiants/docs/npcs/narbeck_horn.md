# Narbeck Horn

## Identity
- **Role:** Lord of Xantharl's Keep ("His Lordship"); holds authority over prisoner matters at the Keep
- **Faction:** Ax of Mirabar (references it as his enforcement authority)
- **First Appearance:** Referenced (not present) during the party's debate with the 3HPP at Xantharl's Keep about whether The Weevil's mistaken-identity claim would deceive him. The party secured an audience with Narbeck after his aide Orgazol accepted a bribe to grant them access.

*Also known as: Lord Narbeck Horn.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Maintaining order and authority within Xantharl's Keep; protecting his political position.
- Bureaucratic and dismissive in peacetime — deflects responsibility and hides behind process.
- In crisis, he becomes sharp, energized, and visibly dangerous.
- Uses threats against subordinates as a motivational tool, smiling at his lieutenant when failure became a possibility.
- Not a man who tolerates incompetence, including his own staff's.

## History with the Party

1. **Pre-meeting:** Lord Horn was discussed during a 3HPP debate about whether to flee the Keep. One 3HPP member believed Horn would be deceived by The Weevil's mistaken-identity claim. The party withheld a crucial counter-detail: Horn would likely recognize The Weevil by his distinctive beard braiding — a marker known to all shield dwarfs.

2. **First Meeting:** Narbeck was irritated that Orgazol accepted a bribe to grant the party an audience. He dismissed their report about the Weevil, redirecting them to his lieutenant and citing the Ax of Mirabar's strict standards around wrongful arrests.

3. **The Keep Attack:** When a force of goblins, hobgoblins, ogres, and a Frost Giant approached from the west, Narbeck shifted into a commanding, energized mode. He smiled threateningly at his lieutenant, implying consequences if the defense failed.

4. **Second Frost Giant:** When a second Frost Giant was reported attacking the first, Narbeck expressed genuine surprise and noted he would need to investigate further.

5. **Thistle's Offer:** Party member Thistle (fairy) volunteered assistance to Narbeck during the attack.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Xantharl's Keep, managing the aftermath of the attack.
- **Active Concerns:** Investigating the second Frost Giant and its motivations; consolidating control after the attack; overseeing/adjudicating The Weevil's capture and prisoner status.
- **What the Party Knows:** He is the Lord of the Keep, affiliated with the Ax of Mirabar, capable of identifying The Weevil by shield dwarf beard braiding; cold and politically self-protective in normal circumstances but capable in a crisis.
- **What Remains Hidden:** His full intentions toward the party; his response to Thistle's offer of help; what he will do about Orgazol; his disposition toward the party generally.

## Relationships
- **The Weevil:** Has authority over The Weevil's captivity; capable of identifying him by shield dwarf beard braiding.
- **Orgazol (Aide):** Resentful — Orgazol's acceptance of the bribe embarrassed Narbeck and created an unwanted obligation.
- **His Lieutenant:** Intimidating and threatening — uses the lieutenant as a pressure valve, implying consequences for failure.
- **Thistle (Party Member):** Personally volunteered aid during the crisis; Narbeck now has a direct, if tentative, point of contact with the party.
- **Three Halfpints and a Pint (3HPP):** At least one member believed Horn was deceivable — suggesting limited familiarity or underestimation of him.
- **The Party (General):** Dismissive and suspicious, though the attack may have shifted dynamics. Party holds information (the beard-braiding detail) that Horn does not know they possess.
