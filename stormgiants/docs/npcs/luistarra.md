# Luistarra

## Identity
- Former follower of Angharradh, elven deity of spring
- Village was located near Evereska
- Not affiliated with any current faction; deceased or otherwise absent from present events
- Has not appeared in person; discussed by the Seer and Chandra Stol

## Personality & Motivations
- Following the destruction of her village, Luistarra descended into madness.
- Despite — or because of — her breakdown, she had the presence and power to act against Narcellia Varam'myr, using hag magic to imprison her.

## History with the Party
- **Not yet encountered directly.** The party learned of her through separate conversations with the Seer and Chandra Stol.
- Her village near Evereska was destroyed by Narcellia Varam'myr.
- In the aftermath, Luistarra went mad and used hag magic to trap Narcellia inside a headband of intellect.
- A pendant belonging to Luistarra has been passed down through her family line and is currently in Chandra Stol's possession.

## Current Status
- **Last known status:** Unknown — no current location or activities established.
- Narcellia Varam'myr has active, described-as-gruesome plans connected to Luistarra's pendant and targeting Chandra Stol specifically.
- **What the party knows:** Luistarra's backstory, her role in imprisoning Narcellia, and the existence of the pendant.
- **What remains hidden:** The nature of Narcellia's plans for the pendant and Chandra; the full scope of the hag magic used; Luistarra's current fate.

## Relationships
- **Narcellia Varam'myr:** Luistarra's direct enemy. Narcellia destroyed her village; Luistarra retaliated by imprisoning her in a headband of intellect.
- **Chandra Stol:** Descendant or family connection — Chandra now carries Luistarra's pendant, making her a target of Narcellia's plans.
- **The Party:** Known only by reputation at this stage; no direct relationship established.
