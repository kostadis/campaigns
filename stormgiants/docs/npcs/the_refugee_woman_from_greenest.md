# The Refugee Woman from Greenest

## Identity
- **Role:** Refugee and survivor of the Cult of the Dragon's attack on Greenest
- **Faction:** None known
- **First Appearance:** Nonnie's inn; overheard Vardis remark about being treated like a human being and engaged the party from there

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Determine whether Greenest is safe and return to help rebuild it
- Resilient and community-minded — despite fleeing great danger, she is willing to reverse course toward it when her home may need her. She responded warmly to Nonnie's hospitality and seemed moved by basic human decency, suggesting she has experienced its absence since the attack.

## History with the Party
- **First meeting:** At Nonnie's inn, she lifted her head from the table when Vardis made a comment about being treated like a human being. She noted that Nonnie extends that treatment to everyone, refugees included. She shared that her hometown of Greenest, to the west, was destroyed by the Cult of the Dragon, and that she had fled east to escape the dragons. Upon hearing rumors of giants in the area, she decided to reverse course and head back west.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Nonnie's inn
- **Active Plans:** Preparing to travel west toward Greenest to assess its safety and assist in rebuilding
- **What the party knows:** Her origin, her reason for fleeing, and her intention to return west
- **What remains hidden:** Her name, full backstory, and any specifics about what she witnessed in Greenest

## Relationships
- **Nonnie:** Speaks of her with gratitude; Nonnie's inn has been a refuge for her
- **Vardis:** His comment prompted her to speak; no deeper relationship established
- **The Party:** Brief, cordial interaction; no ongoing bond established
