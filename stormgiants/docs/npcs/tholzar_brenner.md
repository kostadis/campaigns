# Tholzar Brenner

## Identity
- **Role:** Guard Captain, Yartar
- **Faction:** City guard (abusing position for extortion)
- **Origin:** Damaranian (from a land to the east)
- **First Appearance:** Identified by Eltio as the current holder of the sword the party is seeking; subsequently observed by the party in the market district near the Waterbarron's residence.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Goals:** Unknown beyond self-enrichment through extortion of local merchants.
- Brenner presents as brutish and swaggering — a man who uses his official position as cover for criminal behavior. Eltio's assessment that "no one would be upset if he went missing" suggests he is widely disliked and has made few allies in Yartar.

## History with the Party
1. **Identified by Eltio** as the possessor of the sword the party seeks. Eltio noted he is a Damaranian and disliked throughout the city.
2. **Observed in the market district** near the Waterbarron's residence, collecting protection money from merchants with a small group of armed guards.
3. Eltio offered to guide the party to a location suitable for an ambush.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Market district, Yartar.
- **Active Operations:** Running a protection racket under the guise of guard duties.
- **Party Knowledge:** The party knows he holds the sword, his patrol habits, and that Eltio is willing to help stage an ambush. Brenner is **unaware** of the party's interest in him.

## Relationships
- **Eltio:** Eltio dislikes him and is actively cooperating with the party against him.
- **Local Merchants/Citizens:** Broadly disliked; no apparent allies noted.
- **The Party:** Unaware of them; they are planning hostile action against him.
