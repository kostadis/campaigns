# Helia

## Identity
- **Role:** Seelie Dryad; former exile; guardian (by punishment) of the easternmost tree under the Grandfather Tree's canopy
- **Faction:** Seelie Court (exiled)
- **First Appearance:** Referenced as one of two dryads bound to the Grandfather Tree as punishment by Queen Titania; met directly when the party visited the Grandfather Tree

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Initially, to endure his exile and needle Halisa; ultimately, to be free of his punishment
- Helia is petty, sharp-tongued, and status-conscious — he immediately targeted a disguised sprite with insults hoping to provoke a reaction from apparent royalty
- Despite loudly professing hatred for Halisa, his hesitation when she left and his history of mutual bickering suggest deeply complicated feelings he refuses to acknowledge
- His pride led directly to his exile; he tried to dodge blame at Queen Titania's court and it cost him anyway

## History with the Party
1. **The Fey Ball Backstory:** Helia attended a Fey ball as Halisa's plus-one. When Halisa angered Queen Titania, Helia attempted to distance himself by publicly dumping her. Titania exiled him regardless. The two have been bound to the Grandfather Tree, bickering ever since — their conflict contributing to the cancellation of the moon festival and general misery among the other dryads.
2. **The Party's Arrival:** When Orsik-Thornacious arrived (disguised as a sprite), Helia was delighted at what appeared to be royalty and unleashed a stream of insults hoping to bait a reaction. The plan failed when Orsik-Thornacious misunderstood everything.
3. **The Deal with Thistle:** Helia offered Thistle a conditional deal — permission to search the Grandfather Tree in exchange for Thistle convincing Halisa to apologize to him.
4. **The "Royal Pardon":** Orsik-Thornacious presented Helia's freedom as a royal pardon. Helia hesitated only briefly before flying off, visibly unsettled that Halisa was also leaving.
5. **Post-Freedom:** A Sprite emissary later noted that both Helia and Halisa "fell for the trick" the party used to free them. Sleth revealed there was never an actual curse — the two dryads had imprisoned each other through their own mutual stubbornness.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Departed the Grandfather Tree after accepting the "royal pardon"
- **Active Plans:** Unknown; no information on where he went after leaving
- **What the Party Knows:** That he is free and gone
- **What Remains Hidden:** His actual destination and whether his feelings toward Halisa will drive him to seek her out

## Relationships
- **Halisa:** His fellow exile and constant antagonist. Their bickering was self-imposed — Sleth confirmed no real curse ever bound them. His reaction to her departure suggests unresolved feelings beneath the mutual hostility.
- **Queen Titania:** Exiled him despite his attempt to distance himself from Halisa's offense. His relationship with the Seelie Court is estranged.
- **Thistle:** Offered a deal; relationship transactional.
- **Orsik-Thornacious:** Attempted to provoke and embarrass him; failed when Orsik-Thornacious didn't understand the insults. Later accepted "freedom" from him as though it were a genuine royal pardon.
- **The Party (3HPP):** Freed by them through a trick he did not see through.

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease (implied):** His pride and public abandonment of Halisa caused his exile in the first place
- **Increase:** Accepted freedom and departed — a resolution of sorts, however manipulated
