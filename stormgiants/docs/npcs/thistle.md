# Thistle Wendrod

*Also known as: Thistle.*

## Identity
- **Role:** Fey Ranger, party member, scout, and former associate of the Cult of the Dragon
- **Faction:** Minor member of the Seelie Court; recognized by Prince Thornacious; noted favorably by Queen Tatiana
- **First appearance:** Met the party as a caravan guard working for Azam, traveling toward Parnast

## Personality & Motivations
- **Primary drive:** Finding and rescuing his beloved Laela, who is confirmed enslaved by Duke Zalto; everything else — coin, glory, politics — is secondary
- **Secondary drive:** Hunting down Adimond Kroskas, the former cult associate connected to Laela's fate and the Spring's First Blossom ritual
- Thistle is knowledgeable, resourceful, and tactically cautious — frequently opposing frontal assaults in favor of scouting and misdirection. He is guided heavily by the teachings of his Uncle Joon, whose stories are his primary source of lore on gods, orc tribes, cult history, and artifacts.
- He is earnest and emotionally transparent, capable of genuine kindness (rubbing herbs on Orsik for Chandra's sake) but also lethal when his values are violated (executing the cultist who called Laela's death an honor). He has a tendency to mis-step socially — offending Oblivillish, naming the group after himself — without malicious intent.

## History with the Party

**Early days (Llorkh / Road to Anauroch):**
Purchased studded leather armor in Llorkh. On the road to Anauroch, killed several Cult of the Dragon pursuers and captured one for interrogation. Learned that Adimond Kroskas abandoned the Cult and is working with a Giant. Learned his drake may survive somewhere in the desert. Learned Laela — along with other enslaved people — was either killed through mistreatment or sacrificed alive to a dragon to summon Tiamat. Executed the cultist who framed this as an honor.

**Caravan service (Azam's caravan):**
Served as night watchman; spotted and raised the alarm on a goblin raid, personally killing two goblins and the goblin boss with its mount. Azam described him as "bent on revenge." Traveled toward the Oasis of Vuerthyl to meet Azan the caravaneer.

**Swamp / Mama Manyknuckles' island:**
Identified the correct route to the island using water flow and bugbear positioning. Scouted both the southern camp (ogres, half-ogres) and the middle of the island (sunken wizard's tower occupied by someone) via aerial reconnaissance. Reconnected with his drake Thuul after losing him to a water weird.

**Wizard's tower:**
Led the group through the god-symbol puzzle. Correctly identified Mystara to open a door. Made an offering to Grumbar's altar and received resistance to earth-based harm. Led the group through the elemental wall section, initially getting zapped before deducing the correct praise-song system. Disputed ownership of the wand of webs.

**Old Parnast / Prince Thornacious:**
Identified Old Parnast as the better defensive position. Helped win Chandra's cooperation by gathering herbs to solve Orsik's smell problem. Inadvertently offended Oblivillish by calling him a "hunting master," but the pitch for Thornacious's support ultimately succeeded. Informed by Thornacious that Queen Tatiana of the Seelie Court is aware of him and pleased.

**Triboar:**
Observed the Fire Giant battle from a market tower. Revived the Lord Protector with a spell. Monitored the second Fire Giant as it excavated, observing it extract a large metal band. Used Speak with Dead to learn the band was "a piece of the Vonidod" — identified it as an artifact tied to killing dragons (source: Uncle Joon). Harvested toenails from the giant corpse for potions of giant strength.

**Triboar — The Talking Troll Tavern:**
Initiated a conversation with Urlam Stockspool, gathering intelligence on Adimond Kroskas: a free agent (not Cult of the Dragon) who specialized in hybrid magical creature creation, and whose associates are now dead. Urlam directed him to Silverymoon's magical libraries for further research. Thistle also probed Urlam about House Margaster and then awkwardly asked whether Urlam had any contraband the party could use to disgrace the house — a question that nearly ended the meeting. Orsik intervened; Thistle recovered by reframing the request as targeting House Margaster rather than a general business proposition.

**The Temple — Spider Warrens:**
Despite a strong aversion to giant spiders (particularly disturbed by their predation on small fey creatures like fairies and sprites), flew ahead through dark tunnels into a large web-filled cavern containing three cocoons, which he identified as a "larder." Spotted a living person in the third cocoon and flew down to offer her berries. During the granite ball trap, avoided being crushed by flying above it while other party members could not fully evade it.

**The Temple — Upper Hallway (Day 6):**
Volunteered to fly ahead up the stairs to scout for pressure plates; Harshnag accepted. In one of the upper rooms found a giant bag containing a bong, a gong, and a waterskin. Before leaving, discovered a long, slender shard of ice roughly dagger-sized, bearing a glowing rune symbol of "it."

**Yartar:**
Arrived by teleport into a garden meant to evoke the Seelie Court; saw through the illusion immediately and confronted Kolbaz about using fey as props. Revealed as an assassination target of the Hand of Yartar. Revealed the Cult of the Dragon targets him because he is searching for Adimond Kroskas and because the Spring's First Blossom is connected to the ritual. Named the group "Thistle Wendrod and Companions," causing friction with Orsik. Visibly moved by companions' declarations of loyalty.

**Temple of Annam / Spine of the World:**
Triggered a Storm of Vengeance by touching the Storm Giant rune (killing the entire party); was carried out by Harshnag. Later led the group through tunnels beyond the dormitory crack. In a subsequent visit, used one of the Oracle's six daily questions to confirm Laela's location: Duke Zalto has her. Sarcastically called it "confirming the Oracle's accuracy" when challenged.

**Ironslag heist:**
Served as primary planner. Destroyed the wheel mechanism prematurely, stranding the group mid-route on the bucket chain. Injured by Unla Kee's knock spell triggering a fire trap on the iron chest. Recovered the conch of teleportation. Proposed a cover story framing Bob Saget as killed by a demon to avoid magical interrogation in Silverymoon. Suggested using Bob's zombie army or the cloud castle as weapons against Ironslag's giants.

**Blagothkus's cloud castle:**
Used True Seeing to identify non-dwarf corpses as Dragonwings (high-ranking cult members), concluding the castle changed hands. Identified undead wyverns. Identified a Yondalla copper figurine. Found a note accusing Sandesyl of betrayal; shared Uncle Joon's knowledge that Sandesyl is a disciple of Sammaster. Identified Rezmir's half-dragon remains as the Black Dragon Wyrmspeaker.

**Tower battle / Glazhael:**
Spotted Glazhael the Undying sneaking toward the tower and shouted a warning (initially dismissed by Orsik). Affected by Glazhael's dragon fear along with Thuul. Observed that Glazhael's fear breath lacked conviction.

**Maelstrom:**
Suggested the right-hand rule for navigation. Fed and pacified sharks using Speak with Animals; learned one shark is "Nym's Whale" and that Nym arrives from the shore side. Discovered a secret door on the spiral staircase. Scouted the throne room alone using Pass Without Trace, observing the confrontation between Serissa, Nym, Mirran, Imperator Uthor, and Adimond Kroskas. Identified Kroskas as coordinating the sisters' arguments and signaling them — establishing him as an ally of Nym and Mirran. Overheard Kroskas reveal an ongoing experiment to create a dragon-fairy hybrid force loyal to Serissa. Deduced Kroskas's escape route is the seawater pool in the throne room.

**Ship battle / Hekaton's rescue:**
Barely survived a cone of cold. Revived Orsik with a goodberry to enable escape via the Conch. In the throne room, explained the rescue to Hekaton, then immediately pivoted to rescuing Laela. Aware that Laela is near Zalto's territory and that Sansuri's defeat matters to Escarlotta.

**Raven's Rock / Dragon's Breath:**
Questioned giant ravens about Uthgardt activity; was challenged for calling them "barbarians." Asked the ravens not to implicate the party; received "Nevermore" in reply — uncertain of its meaning. Urged haste and warned Delsephine to move the ship. In the cult debate, stated he opposes the Cult of the Dragon specifically, not all dragons.

**Netherese Mountain Range (dragon fight):**
Precipitated the fight by shouting "Pro or anti-Tiamat?" at the dragon. Opened the battle with two hits. Was partially shielded from lightning breath by his experience against dragons. Missed both shots at the critically weakened dragon at the end. Fired additional arrows into the corpse after Orsik's killing blow. Recovered 2,000 gp, fourteen gems (250 gp each), a potion of mind reading, a bag of beans, a potion of superior healing, and a peculiar star-shaped gem from the hoard. Dragon teeth harvested by Unla Kee are intended for him to craft into dragon arrows.

**Prettyview:**
Internally blamed Orsik's greed for endangering the search for Laela. Privately reconciled to the coin windfall, musing it would help fund a home in the Fey with Laela.

**Castle / theater aftermath:**
Drew bow on Unla Kee during the racist cover story proposal. Built a sustainable fire using rotting food and crushed beams. Escarlotta cited his willingness to leave his beloved for a higher cause as the moment that moved her and helped repair her relationship with Blagothkus.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Maelstrom throne room, with the party, approaching Serissa's court through underwater waterways
- **Active goals:** (1) Rescue Laela from Duke Zalto; (2) Confront Adimond Kroskas; (3) Support party objectives in the Maelstrom as necessary to achieve the above
- **What the party knows:** Laela is confirmed held by Duke Zalto. Kroskas is in the Maelstrom, allied with Nym and Mirran, and running a dragon-fairy hybrid experiment. Kroskas's likely escape route is the throne room pool.
- **What remains hidden:** Full details of Kroskas's hybrid experiment. The meaning of "Nevermore" from the ravens. The full content of what Uncle Joon knows.

## Relationships

- **Laela (beloved):** Thistle's primary motivation. Confirmed enslaved by Duke Zalto. Location near Zalto's territory. Thistle has spent years searching for her and views every resource — coin, alliances, oracle questions — through the lens of reaching her.
- **Adimond Kroskas:** Personal enemy. Former Cult of the Dragon member who abandoned the cult before the Well of the Worlds defeat, now working with giants and operating inside the Maelstrom. Connected to the Spring's First Blossom ritual and to whatever happened to Laela. Thistle restrained himself from striking when he saw Kroskas in the throne room.
- **Uncle Joon:** Mentor figure (familial or otherwise). Source of Thistle's extensive knowledge of gods, orc tribes, cult history, the Vonidod, and Faerûnian lore. Believed by Thistle to be on the side of good.
- **Thuul (drake companion):** Partnership described as "anarcho-socialist" — the two take turns being in charge. Previously lost to a water weird; conjured back. Thuul's breath weapon is contingent on Thistle being alive.
- **Orsik:** Frequent point of friction — Thistle opposes Orsik's impulsiveness and frontal assault plans, and has privately blamed him for endangering the search for Laela. They have a functional working relationship despite tension.
- **Vardis:** Generally aligned tactically; Vardis mediates some party friction.
- **Unla Kee:** Thistle drew his bow on Unla Kee during the racist cover story incident. Relationship is uneasy.
- **Prince Thornacious:** Recognized Thistle as a minor Seelie Court member; informed him Queen Tatiana is aware of and pleased with him.
- **Blasius:** Named as a friend; said farewell to Thistle directly on the 4th day before departing.
- **Escarlotta:** Was moved by Thistle's sacrifice narrative; this contributed to repairing her relationship with Blagothkus.
- **Harshnag:** Identified Thistle as one of the "non-giants" assigned by Krowen; carried the dead party out of the Temple of Annam.
- **Azam / Azam the caravaneer:** Former employer; described Thistle as "bent on revenge."
- **Hand of Yartar:** Active assassination threat against Thistle.
- **Cult of the Dragon:** Actively targeting Thistle due to his pursuit of Kroskas and connection to the Spring's First Blossom investigation.
