# Eltio

## Identity
- **Role:** Operative of the Hand of Yartar; controls access to intelligence and high-value contacts within the organization
- **Affiliation:** Hand of Yartar
- **First Appearance:** Encountered at the bar of the Wink and Kiss tavern in Yartar, dressed entirely in white — white hat, white mask, white clothes with white-on-white stitched patterns

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Serves the Hand of Yartar's interests; trades in information and favors; values competence and criminal credibility
- Sullen, distant, and dangerous on first impression — she does not suffer fools and reprimanded Maia for vouching for people unfamiliar with Tamalin Zoar
- Pragmatic and experienced with violence; she expressed mild disappointment that the party's Letter of Mark was earned through recon rather than murder or robbery
- Has a known weakness for the arts, which Vardis successfully exploited during negotiation; Orsik secured her agreement to attend their play in lieu of payment

## History with the Party

**First Meeting — Wink and Kiss (Session 1 of contact):**
The party sought information about Harthal Zymorven's whereabouts. Eltio demanded 500 gold or a letter of recommendation from Tamalin Zoar. After learning the party was unfamiliar with Tamalin, she was dismissive and reprimanded Maia for vouching for them. Vardis leveraged her interest in the arts during negotiation; ultimately Orsik convinced her to waive the fee in exchange for bringing 50 attendees to their play.

**Referenced — Intermediary Session:**
The party explained to Unla Kee that the Letter of Mark obtained from Tamalin was to be delivered to Eltio, who would then lead them to Harthal Zymorven. Eltio was not present.

**Second Meeting — Wink and Kiss (Letter of Mark delivered):**
The party presented Tamalin's Letter of Mark. Eltio questioned what they did to earn it and was mildly disappointed it involved only reconnaissance. She used the moment to speak admiringly about Tamalin's criminal history. She then left briefly and returned with intelligence: Harthos Zymorven was imprisoned for murder and scheduled for beheading on the 10th (execution method chosen specifically to prevent resurrection); the Giant Slayer sword was in the possession of Tholzar Brenner.

She escorted the party to the central market near the Waterbarron's residence to identify Brenner and offered to facilitate an ambush location. When Orsik suggested fireballing Brenner and his guards in the crowded market directly outside the Waterbarron's home, Eltio reacted with visible alarm, firmly warning the act would result in the entire party being hunted and executed before the 10th. She appeared to recognize Orsik by reputation — *"You are that guy Orsik who did that thing. I knew it."*

She also warned the party to move quickly after Brenner's death, as authorities would connect the killing to Harthal's associates and accelerate the execution.

**Final Known Action:** Eltio guided the party to a secret, unofficial passage through an abandoned temple of Bane in the sewers — intelligence gathered through her own contacts. She was candid that she was not certain of its full layout and that it was not an official backdoor.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Yartar — directed the party into the sewer passage beneath the abandoned Bane temple; her own whereabouts after that point are unspecified
- **Active Operations:** No confirmed ongoing operations beyond her role facilitating this job
- **What the Party Knows:** She is a Hand of Yartar operative with significant intelligence resources and local criminal connections; she recognizes Orsik's reputation; she is pragmatic about violence but not reckless
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full extent of her role and standing within the Hand of Yartar; whether she has further interest in the party's activities or the outcome of the Harthal situation

## Relationships
- **Tamalin Zoar:** Holds Tamalin in high regard, speaking of her with admiration as a legendary criminal figure who parlayed fear into a lucrative operation; Tamalin's Letter of Mark carries significant weight with Eltio
- **Maia:** Reprimanded her and dismissed her for vouching for people unfamiliar with Tamalin; relationship appears to carry some prior history within the Hand of Yartar
- **Orsik:** Recognizes him by reputation (*"you're Orsik, the guy who does the thing"*); her recognition suggests his notoriety has spread through criminal or adventuring circles; she was alarmed by his tactical recklessness in the market
- **Vardis:** Vardis successfully read her as dangerous and later exploited her interest in the arts during negotiation
- **3HPP (party overall):** Transactional but functional relationship; she was surprised they survived long enough to return with the Letter of Mark
