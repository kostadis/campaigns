# Daratha Shendrel (also known as "Delratha")

## Identity
- **Role:** Lord Protector of Triboar
- **Faction:** Triboar civic authority / local militia
- **Origin:** Waterdeep
- **First appearance:** Referenced secondhand via Jacoby's impression at the Talking Troll; first direct appearance when she emerges from her tower to address the commotion caused by Tolmarra's confrontation with Thistle.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Maintaining order in Triboar; practical, efficient governance.
- Described by locals as fair, clearheaded, and a good leader. Her dismissal of Mirak's disappearance as a marital embarrassment — backed by a threat of a fortnight in jail — shows she has little patience for what she perceives as time-wasting. She pivots quickly and without ego when presented with real evidence, shifting from furious dismissal to practical action the moment a murdered body is mentioned.

## History with the Party
1. **Before first meeting:** Daratha had already brushed off the brothers' concerns about Mirak's disappearance, warning them to drop the matter. Jacoby performed an impression of her at the Talking Troll, suggesting she is a recognizable local figure.
2. **Tower confrontation:** When Tolmarra's clash with Thistle drew a crowd, Daratha emerged, commanded silence, and called the party, the brothers, and Tolmarra into her office.
3. **Office meeting:** Initially furious at the adventurers for what she saw as enabling another wild goose chase. Mid-rant, Thistle informed her of the murdered body. She accepted the new information, dropped the lecture, and arranged for a cleric to cast *Speak with Dead* the following morning.
4. **After the meeting:** Dismissed the party and the brothers, but kept Tolmarra behind. Tolmarra did not emerge that evening.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Her office in the Triboar tower.
- **Active plans:** Has arranged a *Speak with Dead* casting for the next day regarding the murdered body.
- **Retained Tolmarra** in her office for an undisclosed private meeting that lasted through the evening.

## Relationships
- **The brothers:** Has a tense, dismissive history — previously threatened them with jail for pressing her about Mirak.
- **Tolmarra:** Some form of private relationship or business; kept her behind after dismissing everyone else, to the amusement of marketplace vendors.
- **The party:** Hostile first impression (enabling the brothers), softened to grudging cooperation once the murder was confirmed.
- **Mirak:** Officially attributed his disappearance to an affair; the accuracy of this assessment is unresolved.
