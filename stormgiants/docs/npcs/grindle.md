# Grindle

## Identity
- Role: Gralm's champion in contests of strength
- Faction: Gralm's encampment
- First appearance: Entered the tent at Gralm's command to compete against the party in physical contests

## Personality & Motivations
- Core drive: Winning contests; acquiring things that interest him (notably Thistle)
- Grindle is childlike in temperament — pouting, screaming, and crying foul when things don't go his way. He is easily commanded by Gralm but expresses displeasure openly. He showed immediate fixation on Thistle upon entering, treating them as a potential plaything.

## History with the Party
- Met the party when summoned by Gralm to compete in a series of strength contests.
- Upon seeing Thistle, immediately asked Gralm if they could be his "toy." Gralm shut this down; Grindle pouted.
- Lost all three physical contests to Unla Kee. Disputed the boar lifting result and accused the wall-smashing contest of being "unfair." Screamed and pouted repeatedly throughout.

## Current Status
- Last known location: Gralm's hut in the encampment
- No known active plans
- **Party knows:** Grindle is Gralm's champion, is easily defeated by Unla Kee, and has fixated on Thistle
- **Hidden:** Nothing notable concealed from the party at this time

## Relationships
- **Gralm:** Grindle obeys Gralm's commands, though reluctantly when it comes to things he wants (e.g., Thistle). Gralm overrules him without hesitation.
- **Unla Kee:** Defeated Grindle in all three contests; Grindle considers the results unfair.
- **Thistle:** Fixated on them as a desired "toy." Gralm intervened before anything developed.
