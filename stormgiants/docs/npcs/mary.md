# Mary (Human Elder, Everlund)

## Identity
- Role / Title: Head of the "Fighters of the Light," a grassroots organization based in Everlund
- Faction: Fighters of the Light (founder and leader)
- First appearance: Encountered by the party upon first arriving in Everlund, where she was shouting warnings in the street

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goals: Warning the public about the Kraken's return and rallying members to oppose it
- Characterized as an old lady, fervent enough to shout prophecies in public; her credibility with the party is ambiguous at best. She charges a 5 GP membership fee and dispatched the party on a quest she framed as retrieving an artifact — which turned out to be a trinket.

## History with the Party
- The party encountered Mary in Everlund, where she was warning passersby about the Kraken returning to destroy the city, describing its tentacles, mind control, and its allies being more powerful than anything Everlund had faced before.
- The party joined the Fighters of the Light, motivated at least in part by being too broke to pay the 5 GP membership fee and hoping membership would resolve that problem.
- Mary sent the party on a quest into the Nether Mountains to retrieve what she called an artifact but was in reality a trinket.

## Current Status
- Last known location: Everlund
- The party is now deep in the Netherese Mountain Range as a direct result of her quest
- What the party knows: She leads the Fighters of the Light, she warned about the Kraken, and her "artifact" was a trinket. What remains hidden: The extent of her knowledge about the Kraken and whether her warnings have any real basis.

## Relationships
- **The Party (Vardis):** Views the whole chain of events with disbelief and dry humor — he reflects that a 5 GP investment has led him to fight a blue dragon.
- **The Party (Thistle):** Thistle recalls her sending the group on the quest; tone of recollection suggests mild exasperation.

## Arc Score Events
- None recorded.

# Mary (Giant Elk, Flint Rock)

## Identity
- Role: Giant elk matriarch residing near Flint Rock
- Faction: None; wild creature with her mate Harry
- First appearance: 5th day of the 2nd tenday of Eleasis, at Flint Rock

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goals: To be left in peace alongside her mate Harry
- More measured, gracious, and diplomatically inclined than Harry; she frequently moderates his outbursts and apologizes on his behalf. She is the one who initiated contact with the party, and ultimately the one whose persuasion led to cooperation.

## History with the Party
- Mary noticed the party's digging activity at Flint Rock and called out to Thistle to ask whether they were permitted to be there.
- After Thistle persuaded her, she and Harry cooperated with the excavation by pushing a large stone aside.
- She apologized to Thistle for Harry's behavior during the encounter.

## Current Status
- Last known location: Flint Rock
- Last known action: Helping move the large stone to assist the party's excavation
- No active plans beyond desiring to be left in peace

## Relationships
- **Harry (mate):** Shares his desire for peace; acts as a social moderator for his more volatile behavior
- **Thistle:** Primary point of contact with the party; responded positively to Thistle's persuasion

## Arc Score Events
- None recorded.
