# Cap'n Squint

## Identity
- Captain and owner of his own barge; former crewman under Imber Chen
- First appeared (by description only) in session notes via Imber Chen's introduction; party met him in person when they boarded his barge the following morning

## Personality & Motivations
- No direct goals established yet beyond operating his vessel and pursuing personal fortune (implied by his gambling habits)
- Described as commanding and larger-than-life despite his halfling stature — barks orders at men twice his size and stomps the planks as if he were ten feet tall

## History with the Party
- Introduced secondhand by Imber Chen, who described him as an old friend they had simply drifted apart from
- Previously worked aboard Imber's boat before earning enough to acquire his own barge
- Spent the evening prior to departure gambling aboard the Grand Dame, Lord Khaspere Drylund's luxury vessel
- The party sought and secured passage with him; Thistle arrived just in time to board before departure

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Departed with the party, presumably heading north on the River Surbin toward Silverymoon
- **Active plans:** Return trip north; no other known operations
- **Party knows:** His reputation, his past with Imber, and that he was gambling on Drylund's vessel the night before departure
- **Hidden:** Unknown — motivations, business dealings, and connection (if any) to Drylund not yet established

## Relationships
- **Imber Chen:** Former crewmate and old friend; the two have drifted apart over the years
- **Lord Khaspere Drylund:** Was a guest on Drylund's vessel the night before departure; nature of the relationship unknown
- **The Party:** Newly acquainted; acting as their passage north
