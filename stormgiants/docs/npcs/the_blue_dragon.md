# The Blue Dragon (Unnamed)

## Identity
- **Role/Faction:** Follower of Tiamat; independent creature
- **First Appearance:** Encountered in a large cavern within the Netherese Mountain Range; the party confronted it during their exploration

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Devotion to Tiamat; self-preservation (demonstrated by retreat and negotiation attempts)
- **Traits:** Aggressive but tactically minded — used disengagement and repositioning rather than standing its ground. Showed an unexpected moment of civility when cornered, attempting to negotiate rather than fight to the last. Ultimately could not back down from the fight.

## History with the Party
1. **Initial Contact:** Thistle questioned the dragon; it declared "Tiamat for-ever!" which immediately triggered combat.
2. **Opening Exchange:** Took heavy damage from Thistle (two arrows), Orsik (four slashes), the guard drake (acid), and booming energy. Disengaged and flew 80 feet to the center of the cavern.
3. **Mid-Fight:** Used lightning breath on Orsik, halving his Strength. Retreated west out of sight, then relocated Thistle and attempted a breath weapon strike; Thistle avoided the worst of it.
4. **Cornered:** Forced into a steep southern cave requiring a 60-foot climb to reach. With few options, attempted to negotiate — asking if "an accommodation could be reached."
5. **Death:** Fired a final lightning bolt at Orsik (mostly dodged), then was killed by Orsik's sword strike at 6 HP remaining.

## Current Status
- **Status:** **Dead.** Killed by Orsik.
- **Body:** Harvested by Unla Kee for meat, blood, teeth, bone, and horn.
- **Hoard Recovered:**
  - 2,000 gp
  - 14 gems (250 gp each)
  - Potion of Mind Reading
  - Bag of Beans
  - Potion of Superior Healing
  - A peculiar star-shaped gem *(nature unknown)*

## Relationships
- **Tiamat:** Devoted follower; allegiance was the direct cause of the fight
- **Orsik:** Primary combatant; dealt and received the most damage; delivered the killing blow
- **Thistle:** Sparked the fight with his question; targeted by the dragon's breath weapon mid-combat
- **Unla Kee:** Harvested the corpse post-combat
