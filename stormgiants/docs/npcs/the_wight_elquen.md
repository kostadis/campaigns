# Elquen (The Wight)

## Identity
- **Role:** Self-appointed guardian of the Wizard of the Crag's library; formerly a servant or associate in the castle
- **Faction:** None current; formerly in service to the Wizard of the Crag
- **First Appearance:** Encountered in the library of the Wizard of the Crag's castle when the party sought access to its magical books

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** To protect the world from the dangers of magic — a burden he believes was literally cursed onto him by the wild magic surge that transformed him
- **Secondary Goal:** Restitution; he requested 150 GP in back pay before departing, suggesting a dry practicality beneath the tragedy
- Elquen is solemn and duty-bound, framing his undead existence as penance rather than power. He is not aggressive by nature — he can be reasoned with and responds warmly to genuine connection from his past. He carries real guilt over the fate of his companions (now skeletons), whom he inadvertently transformed while trying to help them.

## History with the Party
1. **First Contact:** The party entered the library and Elquen barred them from removing or using any magical books, citing the dangers of magic and his self-imposed protective role.
2. **Standoff:** He held firm against the party's attempts to access the library, declaring it his curse and burden to guard against magical harm.
3. **Resolution:** Unla Kee entered and manipulated the fates. Elquen recognized Unla from his past and embraced him warmly. He agreed to relinquish stewardship of the library, collected 150 GP in back pay (with a knowing remark about Unla's historical difficulty holding onto money), and departed through the castle hallways toward the laboratory.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Moving through the hallways of the Wizard of the Crag's castle, heading toward the laboratory
- **Status:** Loose in the world — his current whereabouts are unknown
- **Active Plans:** None confirmed; his stated purpose (guarding the library) has been voluntarily surrendered
- **What the Party Knows:** He was a castle servant transformed by a wild magic surge; he had a prior relationship with Unla Kee; he departed peacefully
- **What Remains Hidden:** What he intends to do now that his self-imposed purpose is gone; what he knows about the castle's laboratory or other contents; the full nature of his history with Unla Kee

## Relationships
- **Unla Kee (party member):** Known from Elquen's past life. Their reunion was warm enough to dissolve Elquen's opposition entirely. Elquen's jab about Unla's inability to hold money suggests a long, familiar relationship.
- **The Wizard of the Crag:** Former master, killed in a duel with Taskor. Elquen's transformation and guilt stem from the chaos following the wizard's death.
- **Taskor:** Killed the Wizard of the Crag in a duel; indirectly responsible for the circumstances of Elquen's transformation.
- **Two Guards/Manservants (now skeletons):** Companions Elquen tried to help during the wild magic surge; their transformation is a source of evident guilt.

## Arc Score Events
- **Increase — Unla Kee:** Elquen's recognition of and warm reunion with Unla, combined with his cooperative departure, suggests a positive relationship arc. His pointed humor about Unla's finances indicates trust and history rather than resentment.
