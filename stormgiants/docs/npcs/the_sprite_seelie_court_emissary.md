# The Sprite (Seelie Court Emissary)

## Identity
- **Role:** Emissary of the Seelie Court, acting on direct behalf of Titania
- **First Appearance:** Appeared in the sky before Thistle and the party to deliver a formal summons and task from Titania

## Personality & Motivations
- Serves as Titania's mouthpiece — precise, theatrical, and not above enjoying its own authority.
- Demonstrates a sharp awareness of deception; explicitly warned Thistle it would not be fooled as the Dryads were.
- Gleeful and somewhat imperious — opened the Feywild portal with evident relish.

## History with the Party
- Appeared before the party and announced itself as acting at the Seelie Court's request.
- Delivered Titania's acknowledgment of the party's interference with the punishment of Helia and Halisa — framing Titania's pleasure as rooted in the *trickery used* and the *selfish motivation* behind the act.
- Switched to Fey privately with Thistle to warn him directly and reveal Titania's intent to use Thornacious' involvement as grounds to bar him from the Feywild.
- Returned to Common to deliver the assigned task: deal with a Fomorian of the Unseelie Court.
- Noted that others at court had pushed for harsher punishment of the party.
- Referenced Titania's appreciation for how Thistle handled his Uncle Joon.
- Opened a portal into the Feywild and sent the party through, then vanished.
- **Orsik** briefly considered killing the Sprite undetected; Thistle talked him down.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Departed after the party entered the Feywild portal; whereabouts unknown.
- **Active Plans:** None confirmed beyond delivery of Titania's message and task.
- **What the Party Knows:** The Sprite delivered Titania's task and revealed Thornacious may be barred from the Feywild.
- **Hidden:** The full scope of what it reported back to Titania; whether it noted Orsik's impulse to kill it.

## Relationships
- **Titania / Seelie Court:** Direct emissary; speaks on Titania's behalf with apparent authority.
- **Thistle:** Spoke to him privately in Fey; distrusts him enough to issue a personal warning. Aware of the Uncle Joon situation.
- **Thornacious:** Named him specifically as a liability Titania intends to use against him.
- **Orsik:** Target of a brief (and aborted) murder impulse; the Sprite may or may not be aware.
