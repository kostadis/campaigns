# Laeral Silverhand

## Identity
- **Role:** First Lord of Waterdeep; former ruler of Silverymoon; one of the Seven Anointed (sometimes referred to in source as "Seven Anointed of Mystra/Mystara"); leader of the coalition that defeated Severin and Sandesyl
- **Faction:** Lord's Alliance (Waterdeep)
- **First Appearance:** Does not appear directly in session notes; referenced only in Unla Kee's strategic thinking and by Lord Methrammar Aersume as his mother

*Also known as: Lady Laeral Silverhand.*

> **Note for the DM:** SKT canon names Methrammar Aerasumé as the son of *Alustriel* Silverhand, not Laeral. One in-campaign reference (Methrammar himself, via Lord Methrammar Aersume) names Laeral as his mother. Treat as the campaign's recorded version unless and until corrected.

## Personality & Motivations
- No direct interaction recorded. Known by reputation as a major political and military figure in the region.
- Held a favorable view of magic and its role in society; her legacy is invoked as political authority on matters of magic in Silverymoon.

## History with the Party
- No direct interaction with the party on record.
- **Indirect reference (Methrammar):** Lord Methrammar Aersume cited his mother's pro-magic stance to legitimize his own position that magic should continue to serve the city. He used her name to deflect and shut down Orsik's line of questioning.
- **Indirect reference (Unla Kee):** Referenced as a potential "patsy" in a blame narrative — her high-profile role in defeating the Cult of the Dragon makes her a plausible scapegoat should a Dracolich attack Silverymoon and the party (or others) seek to assign culpability to the Cult.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Waterdeep (implied by title)
- **Active plans:** None recorded.
- **What the party knows:** Likely aware of her role in defeating Severin and Sandesyl; her legacy is being actively leveraged by Aersume in political discourse.
- **What remains hidden:** Unla Kee has considered using her reputation as cover for a "let it burn" strategy regarding Silverymoon — a plan Orsik has already rejected.

## Relationships
- **Lord Methrammar Aersume:** Her son (per in-campaign claim); invokes her name and legacy as political cover.
- **Orsik:** Indirectly used against him during questioning.
- **Unla Kee:** Views her instrumentally as a useful scapegoat in a Cult-of-the-Dragon blame narrative.
- **Severin & Sandesyl:** Helped lead the coalition that defeated them.
- **The Party:** No established relationship.

- None recorded.
