# Persson Hysstryn

## Identity
- Role: Civilian; brother of Mirak Hysstryn (deceased) and Jacoby Hysstryn
- Faction: None noted
- First appearance: Approached the party alongside Jacoby to convince Thistle to investigate Mirak's mysterious death

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Uncover the truth behind his brother Mirak's death
- Shares Jacoby's deep frustration at years of being dismissed or ignored by authorities. In the Lord Protector's office, both brothers erupted when the body was found — suggesting long-suppressed grief and anger. Persson appears to be the more investigative-minded of the two, having actively pursued leads including the Urgala rumor and the divination priest.

## History with the Party
- Alongside Jacoby, persuaded Thistle to dig into Mirak's disappearance/death
- Contributed key intelligence: the persistent rumor naming **Urgala Meltimer** (innkeeper) as Mirak's lover, and Urgala's consistent denial of this
- Shared that they hired a drunken priest to divine Mirak's whereabouts; **Helm's response** was that Mirak *"was still at home and never left"* — Persson found this significant; the priest withheld part of the answer
- Was present in the Lord Protector's office when Mirak's remains were discovered; reacted with an emotional outburst alongside Jacoby

## Current Status
- Last known location: The Lord Protector's office at time of the body's discovery
- Mirak's remains have been found in the attic of the **Six Windows Inn**
- Actively seeking the truth behind Mirak's death; no independent operations noted beyond working with the party

## Relationships
- **Jacoby Hysstryn** — Brother; shared mission and shared emotional state; the two act in concert
- **Mirak Hysstryn** — Deceased brother; the center of his entire motivation
- **Urgala Meltimer** — Knows her by reputation as the rumored lover; aware she has denied the connection
- **Thistle** — The party member he and Jacoby specifically persuaded to take the case
