# Mohm

- Role: Leader of a small goblin gang
- Faction: Unknown
- First appearance: Day 3 of the 3rd Tenday of Taraskh, in the Forgotten Forest. His gang surrounded the party at the moment a thunderstorm abruptly ceased.

## Personality & Motivations
- Goals and broader allegiances are unknown.
