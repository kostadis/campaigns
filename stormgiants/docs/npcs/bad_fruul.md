# Bad Fruul

## Identity
- **Role:** Hill giant warlord; regional power and primary antagonist threatening Parnast
- **Faction:** Independent; loosely opposed by the Zhentarim and Lord's Alliance
- **First Appearance:** Referenced from Session 1 onward (including a fragmentary mention as "Bad Fuul" in Unla Kee's private reflections, where she wondered if the name connected to the tower or its owner); first appears in person commanding the assault on Parnast
- **Notable Detail:** Despite giant-like appearance, Thistle recognizes him as a drow when his helm is removed; his eyes gleam with unusual intelligence

*Also known as: Bad Fuul.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Seize and destroy Parnast; acquire the statue of Angharradh; capture Chandra (for reasons tied to a family nemesis); accumulate power and rule
- Driven by a desire to be taken seriously — his internal monologue reveals acute awareness that others underestimate his intelligence
- Tactically cunning: uses expendable test waves, adapts to battlefield surprises, and maintains an informant network near Parnast
- Prone to frustrated outbursts when plans fail (banging his head when *hold person* misses, howling at Kurglow's betrayal)

## History with the Party

**Pre-Combat (Multiple Sessions):**
Mama Manyknuckles's dying thoughts blamed Bad Fruul for her gang's fate — they had signed up with him and she always knew it would end badly. The SEER identified him to Hsing as a regional power who thwarted the Zhentarim, and flagged him as a priority threat to the Lord's Alliance. A letter recovered from Chief Grik's body — written by Bad Fruul — ordered his forces to defend Ambush Gap with their lives and declared intent to seize Parnast. He also arranged for poison to be released into the pass to weaken travelers ahead of ambushes. He sent Kagrota to destroy a commune, killing several members and imprisoning the survivors. His agents, working through Kragota Threeteeth's network, learned of Parnast's new statue of Angharradh, which triggered his decision to destroy the town.

**Reconnaissance Phase:**
The party spent the 5th day before the attack confirming his route of advance (northern approach) and the composition of his horde. Orsik used disinformation to reduce goblin recruitment to his banner. Unla Kee identified his banner among horde members and used it to confirm the Spine Ripper orcs had not yet joined. The party estimated his attack for the 8th day of the 1st Tenday of Mirtul and planned an assassination.

**The Battle of Parnast:**
Bad Fruul arrived riding a mammoth, wearing bone-festooned plate armor and a beast-skull helm. He called out Chandra by name (noting her Evereska origins and her connection to someone named Luistarra), demanded the statue of Angharradh, and threatened enslavement. He sent a test wave of goblins and wolves, reacted with surprise and anger when they were slaughtered — his spies had told him militia training was going badly. He adapted, escalating to ogres, half-ogres, cave bears, and eventually an owlbear. He cast *hold person* targeting Thistle, shouting "I hate fairies that fly!" — the spell failed. His mammoth was slowed by Unla Kee's web, sprite poison, and Naz's guiding bolt. The freed wyvern attacked him directly. He was last seen engaged in combat with the wyvern outside the stables.

**Death:**
Bad Fruul was killed during the battle — described as having been tied up and killed in that state. Orsik retroactively noted he landed not a single attack. Vardis charitably called him "a bold distraction." His corpse was present on the battlefield; Orsik attempted to recover and swap the magical headband from his severed head.

## Current Status
- **Status:** **Dead.** Killed during the assault on Parnast.
- **Corpse:** Present on the battlefield; headband of intellect recovered (or swap attempted) by Orsik
- **Associated wyvern:** Confirmed dead; mourned by Chandra

## What the Party Knows vs. What Remains Hidden
- **Known:** He was a drow of unusual intelligence; he possessed the headband of intellect containing Narcellia Varam'myr; he was guided in the assault by a figure described as Chandra's family's nemesis; he commanded goblinoids, ogres, ettins, orcs, and beasts through layered intermediary chiefs
- **Hidden/Unresolved:** The identity of Chandra's family nemesis who was pressing the attack; the full nature of Bad Fruul's relationship with Narcellia Varam'myr; whether his death resolves the threat to the region or leaves a power vacuum

## Relationships
- **Narcellia Varam'myr:** Contained within the headband of intellect Bad Fruul wore; under her influence he gained wealth, arcane knowledge, and expanded ambition
- **Chandra:** Specifically targeted by name; connected to Luistarra (Chandra's family) and an unnamed nemesis driving the assault
- **Mama Manyknuckles:** Her gang signed on with Bad Fruul; she blamed him for her death
- **Chief Grik:** Subordinate; killed at Ambush Gap following Bad Fruul's written orders
- **Kragota Threeteeth:** Agent whose network fed Bad Fruul intelligence about Parnast
- **Kurglow:** Subordinate who withdrew mid-battle to retrieve his whelp; Bad Fruul howled in anger at the betrayal
- **The Zhentarim / Lord's Alliance / SEER:** All identified him as a regional threat requiring intervention
- **Gralm:** Bad Fruul was actively recruiting Gralm's goblinoid forces; the Lord's Alliance sought to prevent this
