# Oosith

## Identity
- **Role:** Aboleth spellcaster; Kraken Society operative; "campaign manager in charge of special persuasion operations" (per Lord Drylund)
- **Faction:** Kraken Society
- **First Appearance:** Referenced by Lady Atalia and Lord Drylund before being encountered; first direct (though disembodied) contact was a telepathic voice greeting the party as they entered the sewer chamber beneath Yartar

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Advance Kraken Society interests in Yartar; was the architect of a child-kidnapping voter coercion scheme tied to Lord Drylund's political campaign
- **Traits:** Presents as cordial and calculated when in control — greeting the party by name, offering drinks, feigning hospitality. Drops the pretense immediately when threatened, turning to domination and hostility. Prone to dramatic proclamations ("The Kraken will have its revenge!") but pragmatic enough to flee when the battle turns against him.

## History with the Party
1. **Pre-contact (Session 1 reference):** Lady Atalia warned the party of an evil spellcaster hiding in Yartar's sewers, protected by Kuo-Toans, evading both the Hand and the Shield of Yartar. Lord Drylund separately identified him as his coercion scheme's architect.
2. **Sewer approach:** A telepathic voice — implicitly Oosith — contacted the party cordially as they entered the sewer chamber, demonstrating knowledge of their names and personalities (notably attempting to lure Unla Kee with the mention of alcohol).
3. **Trap and hostility:** When the Kuo-Toans attempted to trap the party in his office, Oosith's demeanor shifted. He declared Drylund's death "annoying" and announced his intent to kill the party. A charm attempt on Orsik failed.
4. **Combat reveal:** Oosith revealed his true nature as an aboleth during the circular sewer chamber battle. He immediately dominated Unla Kee, commanding her to "Destroy them!" Unla Kee used the wand of wonder and turned invisible instead — prompting Oosith to shriek telepathically, *"What the hell did you just do?!"* An attack on Thistle was foiled when Vardis blinded the creature.
5. **Retreat:** With the Kuo-Toans routed, the Merrow's domination broken, and the water level falling, Oosith fled. Parting words: *"The Kraken will have its revenge!"*

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Fled the sewer chamber beneath Yartar; precise whereabouts unknown
- **Active Plans:** Survival and likely reporting the disruption of Kraken Society operations to his superiors
- **What the Party Knows:** He is an aboleth, a Kraken Society operative, the architect of the coercion scheme, and he is still alive and dangerous
- **What Remains Hidden:** His current location, the full scope of his role within the Kraken Society, and what retaliatory action he may be coordinating

## Relationships
- **Lord Drylund:** Employer/client relationship; Oosith served as Drylund's covert operations handler. Drylund's death was described by Oosith as "annoying" — suggesting Drylund was useful but not irreplaceable.
- **The Kraken Society:** Direct operative; his flight and final words confirm he answers to the Kraken and will report to it.
- **Kuo-Toans / Merrow:** Commanded them as muscle and guards in the sewer complex; these forces were destroyed or routed by the party.
- **Lady Atalia:** Adversarial. She considers his survival a serious and active threat, fearing Kraken retaliation specifically because he escaped.
- **The Party:** Attempted to charm Orsik (failed); dominated Unla Kee (broken by her own chaos); blinded by Vardis. Views the party as enemies who disrupted his operations.
