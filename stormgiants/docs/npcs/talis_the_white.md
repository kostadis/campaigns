# Talis the White

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Former White Wyrmspeaker of the Cult of the Dragon; original rider and companion of the white dragon Glazhael (later Glazhael the Undying)
- **Faction:** Cult of the Dragon (former high-ranking member; later operating independently or in opposition)
- **First Appearance:** Referenced (not present) in session; mentioned by Worug during his account of Glazhael's history, and again referenced through Glazhael's own account

*Also known as: Talis.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Appears to have been a political survivor — replaced as Wyrmspeaker by Severin's appointee yet retained enough influence and resources to continue operating independently.
- Maintained a close partnership with Glazhael, suggesting personal loyalty as a motivating factor alongside (or perhaps over) cult ideology.
- Willing to use undead assets (a Dracolich) and engage in assassination plots, indicating ruthlessness and ambition.

## History with the Party
- Not directly encountered by the party in the recorded sessions.
- Worug mentioned Talis by name when recounting Glazhael's history, establishing the dragon-rider bond between them.
- Her name surfaces through Glazhael's own account: after Glazhael was killed at the Tomb of Diderius and resurrected as a Dracolich by Jorgen, the dragon chose to return to Talis rather than serve Jorgen — suggesting her pull over Glazhael was significant.
- Talis deployed Glazhael on a northern reconnaissance mission (spying on a settlement) and was involved in a plot to murder the First Faction.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Status:** Described as "no more" following the destruction of the Cult of the Dragon and the grand alliance's victory.
- **Presumed dead** or otherwise eliminated in the aftermath of the war's conclusion.
- No active plans or operations remain on record.

## Relationships
- **Glazhael (White Dragon / Dracolich):** Former rider and close partner; Glazhael defied a new master to return to her, indicating deep loyalty between them.
- **Severin:** Antagonistic — he replaced her as White Wyrmspeaker, effectively demoting her within the cult hierarchy.
- **Jorgen:** Adversarial; Glazhael rejected Jorgen's authority specifically to return to Talis.
- **The Party:** No direct interaction recorded.
