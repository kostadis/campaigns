# Szass Tam

## Identity
- Lich; ruler of Thay; commander of the Red Wizards of Thay
- Has not appeared in person; first referenced by Iymrith when she explained her funding arrangements

## Personality & Motivations
- Goals and personality not directly observed; known only through his role as a deal-maker and investor
- Operates through proxies (the Red Wizards), suggesting preference for indirect control and leverage

## History with the Party
- **No direct interaction.** Referenced in conversation when Iymrith disclosed that Szass Tam is one of her investors, with the Red Wizards acting as his on-the-ground minions and financial backers for her hybrid creature experiments.
- Iymrith acknowledged the arrangement was a mistake but felt it was necessary at the time to secure funding.
- The contract between Szass Tam and Iymrith is binding enough that Iymrith cannot kill the Red Wizards associated with the deal — a detail surfaced when Thistle asked why she didn't simply eat them.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Thay (not confirmed in session)
- **Active operations:** Funding and supporting Iymrith's hybrid experiments via Red Wizard proxies
- **What the party knows:** Szass Tam has a financial/contractual relationship with Iymrith; the Red Wizards serve as his agents; the contract restricts Iymrith's freedom of action
- **What remains hidden:** The specific terms of the contract; what Szass Tam receives in return; whether he has other interests in the region

## Relationships
- **Iymrith:** Investor and contractual partner; Iymrith views the arrangement as a mistake she is currently bound by
- **Red Wizards of Thay:** His direct minions and field agents in this arrangement
- **The Party:** Unknown to him (as far as the party is aware)
