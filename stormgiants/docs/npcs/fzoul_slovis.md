# Fzoul Slovis

> **DM note:** A separate in-session reference attributes the killing of Manshoon to "Fzoul" (per Darz Helgar). Forgotten Realms canon attributes the famous Manshoon-killing to **Fzoul Chembryl**, High Priest of Bane and a Zhentarim leader. Per user merge confirmation, this dossier consolidates both references — treat as the same Fzoul unless and until contradicted in play.

## Identity
- **Role:** Zhentarim operative and intelligence agent
- **Faction:** Zhentarim
- **First Appearance:** Not encountered directly by the party; first surfaced when Darz Helgar mentioned in passing that Fzoul killed Manshoon prior to Manshoon's clones coming into play; later discovered through an intelligence report dated 3/1 of Taraskh, authored by Slovis regarding conditions along the Black Road

*Also known as: Fzoul.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Gather intelligence along the Black Road; advocate for systematic Zhentarim elimination of the goblin threat; recruit promising candidates into the organization
- Slovis is methodical and pragmatic — he paid a hobgoblin toll rather than risk a fight, prioritizing mission success over pride. He is a persistent voice within Zhentarim ranks, having pushed the same anti-goblin recommendation "many times" without result. His reports suggest a man who is frustrated by organizational inertia but works within proper channels to push his agenda.

## History with the Party
- The party has not met Slovis directly. They encountered his intelligence report, which revealed his recent reconnaissance mission traveling from Parnast toward Vuerthyl along the Black Road.
- His report documented: a hobgoblin toll post on the desert's edge (too well-manned to assault; 15gp per camel toll paid and expensed), and a goblin-attacked caravan near Vuerthyl that he assessed as less sophisticated than earlier raids.
- **The report identified Orsik as a recruitment target.** Slovis stated his intention to approach Orsik "at the earliest opportunity."

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Returning from his Black Road scouting run; reporting to Zhentarim leadership
- **Active Operations:**
  - Lobbying Zhentarim leadership to take systematic action against goblin threats on the Black Road
  - Flagged the hobgoblin toll post as a target for Zhentarim action
  - Planning to recruit Orsik
- **What the Party Knows:** They have read his intelligence report and know his name, role, recent movements, observations, and his intention to recruit Orsik
- **Hidden from the Party:** His current whereabouts, his full relationship with Zhentarim leadership, and the timeline or method of his planned approach to Orsik

## Relationships
- **Zhentarim Leadership:** Reporting subordinate; appears to have a frustrated dynamic — he has made the same goblin-threat recommendation repeatedly without organizational action.
- **Manshoon:** Killed him (per Darz Helgar's account), predating the activation of Manshoon's clone network.
- **Darz Helgar:** Referenced him in passing, suggesting some awareness of or connection to these events.
- **Orsik (party member):** Has identified Orsik as a recruitment target; no contact made yet.
- **Hobgoblin Toll Post Operators:** Transactional and adversarial; paid the toll but recommended the Zhentarim eliminate the post.
- **Party (general):** Unaware of the party; the party knows of him through his report.

- No direct interaction with the party has occurred. No arc score events recorded.
