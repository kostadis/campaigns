# Klauth ("Old Snarl")

## Identity
- Role: Ancient red dragon; self-styled sovereign of the North
- Faction: Independent — formerly opposed by (and possibly connected to) the Cult of the Dragon
- Has not appeared in person; all contact has been through his agent, Delsephine
- First referenced when Delsephine arrived to deliver his offer to the party

## Personality & Motivations
- Operates through proxies and calculated gifts rather than direct involvement
- Core drive appears to be self-preservation and territorial control — he does not want the Lord's Alliance investigating the Spine of the World, and the giant conflict threatens his dominion
- Orsik's read: Klauth is using the party as a convenient instrument against a rival power (giants and dragons are competing ethno-religious factions), particularly after his kind suffered losses at the Well of the Dragon
- Frames his aid generously ("a gift"), but the underlying motivation is transparently self-serving

## History with the Party
- Dispatched Delsephine and her sky vessel crew to the party as an unsolicited gift, granting them aerial passage across his territory to avoid dangers below
- His message, relayed through Delsephine: press forward, kill the giant leaders, then visit his hidden vale to claim a reward
- Subsequently sent a second message through Delsephine wishing the party luck in ending the giant war

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** His hidden vale somewhere in the North (location unknown to the party)
- **Active operations:** Has agents in the field (Delsephine and crew) supporting the party's mission
- **What the party knows:** He wants the giants dead, he's offering a reward at his vale, and his help is self-interested
- **What remains hidden:** The nature of the promised reward; the full extent of his plans for the North; his actual power and condition following losses at the Well of the Dragon; what he truly wants from the Lord's Alliance situation

## Relationships
- **Delsephine:** His devoted agent and former Cult of the Dragon member; she harbors deep personal devotion and romantic/sexual attraction to him — she left the Cult to serve him directly
- **The party (3HPP):** Transactional ally — he needs them to do what he cannot or will not do openly
- **The giants:** Rivals and current enemies; their rising power threatens dragon dominance in the region
- **The Lord's Alliance:** He actively does not want them poking around the Spine of the World — reason unstated
- **Cult of the Dragon:** Unclear relationship; Delsephine defected *from* the Cult *to* Klauth, suggesting tension or divergence between him and the Cult's agenda
