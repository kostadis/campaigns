# The Judge (Star Court)

## Identity
- **Role:** Presiding judge of the Star Court, Silverymoon
- **Faction:** Star Court (civic legal authority of Silverymoon)
- **First Appearance:** The Star Court hearing involving the party, Lady Margaster, and Vinby Hearthgood

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Maintaining civic order in Silverymoon, protecting economically valuable citizens and investors, and keeping disruptive outsiders in line.
- Openly prioritizes Lady Margaster's financial contributions to Silverymoon's post-War of the Dragons recovery over the party's legal grievances — his justice is transactional.
- Condescending and dismissive toward most of the party, but capable of performative courtesy when it suits him (his treatment of Xamlyn and Thistle).
- Quick to anger when he feels his city is being embarrassed; holds grudges against prior offenders.

## History with the Party
- Presided over the Star Court hearing in Silverymoon. Called Vinby Hearthgood to testify, then addressed Xamlyn deferentially as "a paramount citizen of Silverymoon," stating accusations against her required "the most robust evidence."
- Agreed the court magister was an excellent idea — until Xamlyn refused and departed, derailing that avenue.
- Turned on Orsik, recognizing him from a prior incident ("You're the one that did that THING!"), and accused him of creating yet another mess for the city.
- Ruled in favor of Lady Margaster's interests, explicitly citing her investment in Silverymoon's economic recovery.
- Ordered the party to be gone by sunset.
- Singled out Thistle as "a credit to your kind."

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The Star Court, Silverymoon.
- **Active Operations:** Presiding over Star Court business; no known active plans targeting the party beyond the sunset expulsion order.
- **Known to Party:** He is biased toward wealth and civic stability, dislikes Orsik personally, has a prior history with him, and will not give the party a fair hearing in his court.
- **Hidden from Party:** The nature of Orsik's prior incident and what, if any, relationship the Judge has with Lady Margaster beyond civic interest.

## Relationships
- **Lady Margaster:** Openly favorable — treats her investment in Silverymoon as grounds to dismiss grievances against her.
- **Xamlyn:** Treats her as a "paramount citizen" deserving exceptional evidentiary protection; deferential in tone.
- **Orsik:** Recognized him on sight with clear irritation; holds a grudge from a previous unnamed incident.
- **Thistle:** Singled out with backhanded praise ("a credit to your kind") — condescending but superficially positive.
- **Vinby Hearthgood:** Called him to testify; no strong relationship implied.
