# Wiri Fleagol

## Identity
- **Role:** Formerly enslaved gnome at Ironslag; now freed civilian
- **Faction:** None
- **First Appearance:** The party encountered her at Ironslag, where she had chosen to remain behind while other freed prisoners fled

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Leave Ironslag, buy a farm, and never work underground again. Eyeing Yartar or Triboar as destinations.
- Pragmatic and businesslike — she traded intelligence for coin without hesitation. Sharp enough to see through Thistle's version of the Silverymoon story. She holds a strong contempt for Silverymoon over its failure to aid the Silver Marches during the Orc and Dragon invasions, but stops short of seeking vengeance out of concern for innocent gnomes caught in the crossfire.

## History with the Party
1. **Introduction at Ironslag:** Wiri introduced herself to the party after they freed her. When Vardis tauntingly implied she may have deserved her captivity, she firmly shut him down.
2. **Intelligence Deal:** She identified Thistle as a fairy and offered to sell him detailed information about Ironslag's layout for 20 gold. She described:
   - The **ore processing room**: bucket chain system, enslaved dwarves, overseen by a fire salamander
   - The **foundry**: dangerously hot
   - The **Great Wheel room**: powered by twenty enslaved commoners
   - The **prisoners' sleeping quarters**: sealed by iron portcullises and padlocked cages
   - She noted that prisoners were too exhausted and dehydrated to fight.
   - She speculated the **Conch of Teleportation** is likely in Duke Zalto's room on the lower level, though she was never taken there herself.
3. **Silverymoon Story:** She did not believe Thistle's account of events in Silverymoon.
4. **Departure:** She briefly offered to accompany the party before Orsik dissuaded her. She asked Orsik to confirm the spelling of his name and whether it carried any weight; he directed her to reference the **3HPP** and their aid to the mayor of Triboar. She then departed down the stairs toward freedom.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Departing Ironslag via the stairs; headed toward Yartar or Triboar
- **Active Plans:** Purchase a farm; never work underground again
- **What the Party Knows:** Everything above
- **What Remains Hidden:** Nothing of note flagged in the notes

## Relationships
- **Thistle:** She identified him as a fairy and directed her business dealings primarily toward him. She doesn't fully trust his account of Silverymoon events.
- **Vardis:** Openly rebuked his implication that she deserved her imprisonment. Relationship is cool at best.
- **Orsik:** Warmer rapport; she took his suggestion of Yartar or Triboar seriously and asked to use his name as a reference. He pointed her toward the 3HPP and the mayor of Triboar.
- **The 3HPP (party faction):** She is aware of them by reputation through Orsik's reference and may invoke their name upon arriving in Triboar.
