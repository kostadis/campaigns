# Darz

## Identity
- Role/Title: Former thief; freelance operator
- Faction: None confirmed; loosely connected to merchant contacts from Mirabar
- First Appearance: Found dead near the campground entrance by Thistle during a battle; later revived by Cindell

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Self-preservation and personal enrichment; operates on a transactional worldview
- Cynical and calculating — when revived, he immediately assessed the motivations of each person claiming credit for his resurrection. He privately noted that had he known about the buried artifact, he would have dug it up and sold it himself. He shares information when it benefits him, not out of loyalty.

## History with the Party
1. **Found Dead:** Thistle discovered Darz's body near the campground entrance during the battle.
2. **Raised from the Dead:** Orsik pushed for his revival; Cindell performed *raise dead* at a discount. Darz's first words: *"I'm alive."* He noted the competing motivations of Orsik, Cindell, and Darthara with detached cynicism.
3. **Meeting at the Talking Troll:** The following day, Darz met with his benefactors. He acknowledged he had never been dead before and, as an act of gratitude, volunteered intelligence he had been holding in reserve as a personal retirement job — the location of a wanted dwarf named Weevil. He finished his drink and departed.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Departed the Talking Troll after the meeting; whereabouts unknown
- **Active Plans:** None confirmed; no longer has the Weevil information as leverage
- **What the Party Knows:** He is a former Waterdeep thief; he shared the Weevil tip freely as repayment for being raised
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full extent of his contacts and what other jobs or information he may be sitting on

## Relationships
- **Orsik:** Owes his life to Orsik's insistence on the raising; views the debt as now paid
- **Cindell:** Performed the resurrection at a discount; Darz assessed her motivations cynically
- **Darthara:** Also present and seeking credit for the revival; Darz noted this
- **Unnamed Merchant from Mirabar:** An old friend who spotted Weevil and passed the tip to Darz
- **Weevil:** No direct relationship — Darz knew of him only as a bounty worth 5,000 gold to the Axe of Mirabar

## Arc Score Events
- **Increase (gratitude/debt resolved):** Voluntarily shared a valuable bounty tip he had been holding for personal use, prompted by being raised from the dead
