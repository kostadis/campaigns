# Cindell Trueshot

## Identity
- **Role:** Wealthy noblewoman; cleric; resident of Westpharl Ranch on the outskirts of Triboar; mayoral candidate for Triboar; political rival to acting mayor Darathra Shendrel ("Darthara"); apparently well-known among Waterdhavians
- **Faction:** Politically aligned with the Lord's Alliance and the ranch-owning class by interest; not the Harpers (whom she views Darathra as serving)
- **First Appearance:** Mentioned in passing by Kaelen during a conversation with Orsik about ticket sales for a play (Orsik snarled at her name); first met in person hosting a lavish social gathering at her Westpharl Ranch estate; later approached the gathered party unbidden at the market area after the giant attack

*Also known as: Cindell, Cindrell.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Unseat Darathra Shendrel as Lord Protector of Triboar; shift Triboar's political alignment from the Harpers to the Lord's Alliance; maintain property values and social order in Triboar; cultivate allies among Triboar's propertied class.
- Frames self-interested political maneuvering in the language of civic responsibility and community standards.
- Formally bearing, accustomed to authority, and openly contemptuous of Darthara — though she wears her cynicism with a smirk rather than malice.
- Politically calculating beneath a veneer of community generosity; she is fully aware of how her own actions serve her interests and does not pretend otherwise (at least internally).
- Resistant to being defined as a cleric despite clearly being one; keeps personal distance from town and "has people" for that.
- Dismissive of those she considers beneath her class; characterized Hyuth Kolstaag as arrogant and classless, and Darathra Shendrel as a nobody.
- Her intrigue is competent in ambition but clumsy in execution — she was outmaneuvered quickly by an experienced schemer (Thistle).

## History with the Party

1. **Mention by Kaelen:** Kaelen suggested to Orsik that convincing Cindell to attend a dinner would boost ticket sales among Waterdhavians. Orsik responded with a snarl, which Kaelen read as a refusal — implying some prior personal connection or tension between Orsik and Cindell.

2. **Party at Westpharl Ranch:** Cindell hosted the 3HPP among other guests at a lavish social gathering at her estate.

3. **Private audience:** She met the party in her study and outlined her grievances against Hyuth Kolstaag — social snubs, violence, property value concerns, and failure to make financial amends for a stolen horse or contribute to a luncheon for a murdered stable hand's family. She characterized Darathra's response as toothless.

4. **Job offer:** She tasked the 3HPP with "dealing with" Hyuth, strongly implying assassination, framing it as completing what hired assassins had already started.

5. **Contract negotiations:** Payment was set at 500 gold plus 50 on election victory. She attempted to sweeten the deal for Orsik by offering to clear his name in Waterdeep, dismissing his counter-request that it cover all of the North. Thistle pressed for a signed IOU; she resisted but ultimately signed after Thistle used his fairy charm.

6. **Scheme exposed:** Thistle, recognizing the conspiracy as clumsy, deliberately sabotaged it — delivering the signed contract to both Urgala and Darathra. Darathra intends to use the contract to politically destroy Cindell and anticipates a landslide victory against her. Urgala expressed shock that Cindell would be "that cold-blooded."

7. **Post-giant-attack market scene:** Appeared at the market and immediately taunted Darthara about the destruction, threatening to use it against her in the election. Identified herself as a mayoral candidate; was called a "carpetbagger" by Darthara; Orsik extended the label to both women, which amused her.

8. **Cleric services:** Quoted prices: 90 gp for *Speak with Dead*, 1,250 gp for *Raise Dead*; declined Thistle's offer of theater tickets as payment. Performed *Speak with Dead* on the giant's skull, ruling that answering the dead giant's counter-question consumed one of the allotted questions. Performed *Raise Dead* on Darz at a significant discount, publicly framing it as community solidarity while privately motivated by political advantage and a desire to make Darthara look inadequate.

9. **Dinner at the Talking Troll:** Accepted an invitation to dinner at the Talking Troll, initially misreading Orsik's intent as flirtation; internally noted that Orsik owns the tavern and benefits from her presence there. (Earlier framed by Kaelen as a ticket-sales play; consistent with her role as a Waterdhavian draw.)

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Triboar (Westpharl Ranch / town); alive and active
- **Believes:** Her assassination scheme against Hyuth is intact and the 3HPP are acting on her behalf
- **Actual situation:** The signed contract is in Darathra's possession; her conspiracy has been fully exposed to her political rival and a prominent local figure (Urgala). Darathra is already planning to use the contract against her.
- **Active Plans:** Mayoral campaign against Darathra; building relationships with the ranch-owning class; using post-battle market visibility (Speak with Dead / Raise Dead public services) to undermine Darathra
- **What the Party Knows:** She is a powerful cleric and noblewoman, a mayoral candidate, politically sharp, and willing to deal — at a price or a discount when it suits her; her assassination plot was exposed; she has some Waterdhavian profile and prior connection to Orsik
- **Hidden from her:** The 3HPP betrayed her immediately; Darathra is already planning to use the contract against her
- **Hidden from the party:** Her full cynicism about her own motives; private calculation that the Darz revival served her interests; skepticism about Orsik's ruby valuation attempt; the nature of her prior connection to Orsik

## Relationships
- **Darathra Shendrel ("Darthara"):** Open political rival and current Lord Protector; views her as a Harper stooge and an ineffectual leader; uses every opportunity to highlight Darathra's failures publicly. Darathra now holds evidence (the assassination contract) to destroy her politically.
- **Hyuth Kolstaag:** Despises him as a disruptive, classless newcomer; wanted him killed.
- **Urgala:** Acquaintance; was surprised and disturbed to learn Cindell would go so far as to arrange a killing.
- **Orsik:** Some prior relationship implied (the ticket-sales conversation with Kaelen and Orsik's snarl); attempted to leverage his legal troubles in Waterdeep as a personal inducement; mildly amused by him at the market; aware he tried to overvalue a ruby in negotiation and that his dinner invitation benefited him as much as her; misread him initially as flirting.
- **Thistle:** Was charmed (literally) into signing the contract; Thistle then engineered her downfall. Declined Thistle's theater-ticket offer; stated she is not a fan of theater or Kaelen's work.
- **Darz:** Raised him from the dead; relationship otherwise undefined.
- **Kaelen:** Aware of her social cachet among Waterdhavians; willing to use her name as a lever for ticket sales.
- **Ranch-owning class of Triboar:** Cultivating as a political base.

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease:** Thistle exposed her contract to Darathra and Urgala, neutralizing her scheme and handing her political enemy a weapon to end her career.
