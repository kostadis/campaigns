# Slerdax

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Dark Lord of a minor Ravenloft domain
- **Faction:** Ravenloft / the Mists
- **First Appearance:** Never met directly; introduced via exposition from Unla Kee, who studied him in an advanced course on minor Ravenloft lords at his school of magic.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Drive:** Desires to be genuinely praised for his cooking and service.
- **Curse:** Compelled to believe all praise directed at him is a lie, which triggers escalating attempts to kill the complimenting guest — beginning subtly and escalating to open violence.
- His curse creates a tragic, inescapable loop: he craves the very validation he cannot accept.

## History with the Party
- No direct interaction to date. Slerdax was described at length by Unla Kee during a session in which the party received a briefing on him.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Trapped in the Mists of Ravenloft, though his domain manifests as a restaurant that periodically opens in various cities to lure in unsuspecting guests.
- **Active Operations:** The restaurant appears in different cities on an unpredictable schedule, trapping whoever enters inside his domain.
- **What the Party Knows:** His curse, his behavior pattern (subtle attempts → open violence), and both methods of escape: killing him or convincing him a compliment is genuine.
- **What Remains Hidden:** His current or next location; how to meaningfully distinguish "genuine" praise from flattery in his eyes.

## Relationships
- **Unla Kee:** Source of the party's knowledge; studied Slerdax academically. No known personal history between them.
- **The Party:** No relationship established yet.
