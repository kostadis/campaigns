# Gauche the Young

## Identity
- **Role:** Current leader of the Yartar Harper headquarters
- **Faction:** The Harpers
- **First Appearance:** Introduced at the Yartar Harper HQ when the party arrived via the teleportation circle; he had recently assumed command following the death of his predecessor, Kolbaz.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Conduct the investigation into Kolbaz's death on his own terms; maintain Harper institutional independence from outside contractors.
- **Drives:** Professional pride, institutional loyalty, and skepticism of the "Ezra model" of hiring outside adventurers.
- Gauche is stubborn and territorial — cooperative only because Ezra's program produced results he cannot ignore, not because he believes in it. He has an unselfconscious streak of bluntness, openly admitting he had been looking forward to the "plum assignment" of leading Yartar, even while offering condolences over Kolbaz's death.

## History with the Party
- **First meeting:** The party arrived via teleportation circle and encountered Gauche as the new Yartar Harper leader. He was openly hostile, calling the 3HPP members mercenaries rather than true Harpers and making clear his contempt for the Ezra model.
- **Kolbaz's death:** He described the killing — Kolbaz was struck by a powerful lightning bolt, and the destruction in the room near the teleportation circle suggested something massive had materialized and then dematerialized. Gauche noted the damage was inconsistent with giant activity.
- **Investigation dispute:** Thistle identified research into **Adimond Kroskas** as the likely cause of Kolbaz's death. Gauche acknowledged he would investigate the circumstances of the death but explicitly refused to pursue Kroskas, calling it a distraction. Orsik-Zymorven attempted to persuade him otherwise and failed. Gauche committed only to investigating wizards who masquerade as demons.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Yartar Harper headquarters, taking measurements of the destruction in the room where Kolbaz was killed.
- **Active Investigation:** Pursuing a theory that the killer was demonic in nature or a wizard disguised as a demon. Deliberately excluding Adimond Kroskas from his investigation.
- **What the party knows:** His investigative scope, his hostility toward them, and his demonic/wizard theory.
- **What remains hidden:** Whether his refusal to investigate Kroskas is mere stubbornness, political, or something more concerning.

## Relationships
- **Kolbaz (predecessor):** Killed before Gauche assumed command; Gauche is investigating his death.
- **Krowen Valharrow:** Disagrees with Krowen's "Ezra model" but is forced to work within its results.
- **Ezra:** Respects the outcome of Ezra's work enough to cooperate minimally; resents the precedent it set.
- **The Party (3HPP):** Openly hostile; views them as mercenaries, not Harpers. Tolerates their involvement but will not collaborate on Kroskas.
- **Orsik-Zymorven:** Failed to persuade Gauche to broaden his investigation; relationship is likely strained.
- **Thistle:** Provided the Kroskas lead, which Gauche rejected.
