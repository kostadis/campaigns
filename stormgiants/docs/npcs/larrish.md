# Larrish

## Identity
- Role: Town council member (former or current); coerced spy for Chief Kurglow
- Faction: Nominally civilian/town council; unwillingly entangled with Kurglow's orc faction
- First appearance: Revealed through testimony of his family, who were among the human prisoners freed from the orc cave; Larrish himself did not appear in person

## Personality & Motivations
- Core motivation: Protection of his family — he spied for Kurglow solely under threat of their deaths
- No direct personality traits have been demonstrated through play; his character is known only secondhand through his family's account

## History with the Party
- The party learned of Larrish when his family was freed from the orc cave alongside Jarlor's family. His family revealed that, like Jarlor, he had been coerced into spying for Chief Kurglow under threat of their lives.
- Orsik recognized Larrish by name from his time on the town council.
- Thistle later identified Larrish and Jarlor as potential conduits for sending a false or controlled message to Kurglow through the spy channel.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Unknown — whereabouts unconfirmed
- **Active situation:** Presumably still operating as a coerced spy within Kurglow's network, or at minimum known to that network
- **Party knows:** That he is a council member, that he was coerced, and that his family is now safe
- **Hidden:** Whether Larrish knows his family has been freed; his current location; his state of mind or willingness to cooperate

## Relationships
- **Chief Kurglow:** Coerced into serving as a spy under threat to his family
- **Jarlor:** Parallel figure — both are council-connected men turned spies by the same threat; Thistle groups them together as assets
- **Orsik:** Recognizes Larrish from the town council; some prior acquaintance implied
- **The Party:** No direct interaction; known only through reputation and family testimony
