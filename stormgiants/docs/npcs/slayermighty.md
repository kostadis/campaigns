# Slayermighty

## Identity
- Role: Self-proclaimed future ruler of all Giant-kind; lone occupant of a deep cavern
- Faction: None confirmed; claims ambitions over Giant-kind
- First appearance: Found in a deeper cavern within the cursed cave, guarding a stone chest alongside his bear, Blooddrinker

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: To rule all of Giant-kind
- Grandiose and threatening in demeanor, issuing immediate death threats upon the party's intrusion. His ambitions vastly outpaced his capability — he was unable to land a single blow before being killed.

## History with the Party
- When Thistle entered the cavern, Slayermighty was alerted and threatened to tear the party apart and feed them to Blooddrinker
- Despite his aggression, he failed to strike any party member and was killed before he could swing his club
- Orsik and Vardis found his diminutive stature difficult to take seriously throughout the encounter

## Current Status
- **Deceased**
- His stone chest and bear, Blooddrinker, were present at the time of his death
- **Loot recovered:** Two vials of alchemist's fire, a potion of healing, a necklace of twenty copper charms

## Relationships
- **Blooddrinker (bear):** His animal companion, present during the encounter
- **The Party:** Immediate hostility; killed on first meeting
