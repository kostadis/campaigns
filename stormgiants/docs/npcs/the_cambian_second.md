# The Cambian (Second)

## Identity
- **Role/Faction:** Unnamed cambion enforcer; operates as a pair with the First Cambian
- **First Appearance:** Encountered during the aerial pursuit of Orsik and Unla Kee

## Personality & Motivations
- Operates as the active muscle of the pair, carrying out killings while the First Cambian directs
- Demonstrates cold efficiency and obedience to its partner's cues — executing Vinby's companion on a simple nod without hesitation
- Goals and broader allegiances are unknown

## History with the Party
1. **The Chase:** Flew in pursuit of Orsik and Unla Kee alongside the First Cambian
2. **Kills Unla Kee:** Delivered the killing blow to Unla Kee during the chase
3. **Recovers the Package:** Located whatever Orsik and Unla Kee were carrying, announcing *"Found it"*
4. **Kills Vinby's Companion:** Inside a house, executed Vinby Hearthgood's halfling companion after Orsik failed to immediately comply with an ultimatum — acting on a nod from the First Cambian
5. **Departs:** Left the scene after the killing; was not captured or destroyed

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Departed the scene; whereabouts unknown
- **Active Operations:** Presumably still working with the First Cambian; in possession of (or having handed off) the recovered item
- **Known to Party:** The party witnessed its killings and its recovery of the item; its identity, master, and purpose remain unknown

## Relationships
- **The First Cambian:** Operates in direct tandem; defers to the First's signals for when to act
- **Unla Kee:** Killed him during the chase
- **Vinby Hearthgood:** Murdered Vinby's halfling companion in front of him as coercion
- **Orsik:** Used as leverage — the companion's death was a consequence of Orsik's non-compliance
