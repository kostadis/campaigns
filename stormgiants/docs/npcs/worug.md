# Worug

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Ogre survivor; one of the last living inhabitants of Blagothkus's cloud castle
- **Faction:** Nominally part of Blagothkus's castle population; currently self-isolated with companion Tak
- **First Appearance:** Referenced as potential vampire prey; properly encountered when the party used Orsik's *knock* spell to open his barricaded tower door

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Survival — staying hidden from Sandesyl until escape from the castle becomes possible
- Frightened but communicative once trust is established; responded well to Thistle's goodberries and calmed quickly
- Demonstrates warmth and gratitude openly (attempted to hug Thistle), and shares a quietly affectionate, unspoken bond with Tak
- Pragmatic enough to trade valuable intelligence for a concrete promise of safety

## History with the Party
1. **Pre-contact:** Worug and Tak survived Sandesyl's massacre of the castle's ogre population by barricading themselves in a side tower and refusing to invite the vampire inside
2. **First encounter:** Party breached the tower via *knock* spell; both ogres panicked; Thistle calmed them with goodberries
3. **Intelligence exchange:** In return for a promise of evacuation, Worug briefed the party on:
   - Sandesyl's arrival and the slaughter of the castle's population
   - Blagothkus's emotional breakdown relating to his son
   - The ogres' daily survival routine (sneaking out for food while vampires are inactive)
   - The secret of **Glazhael**, a dracolich residing in the glacier beneath the castle — formerly Talis the White's steed, killed by the First Faction, now returned as a dracolich; it attempted to seize the castle but was lured away on a false artifact quest before Blagothkus retook control
4. **Arrangement made:** Worug and Tak declared their tower a safe refuge for the party and offered it as a rest location

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Barricaded in their side tower on the cloud castle; stated they will not leave it
- **Active Plans:** Lying low, avoiding Sandesyl, awaiting evacuation the party promised
- **What the Party Knows:** Full account of recent castle events and the Glazhael secret as Worug understands it
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full nature or current whereabouts of Glazhael beyond what Worug described; whether Blagothkus knows Worug shared castle secrets with strangers

## Relationships
- **Tak:** Companion and fellow survivor; share the barricaded tower. Tak over-eagerly denied romantic feelings in the party's presence; Worug responded with warm confusion — an unspoken attachment is evident
- **Blagothkus:** Nominal lord of the castle; Worug has been surviving around him rather than with him during the crisis
- **Sandesyl:** Mortal threat; Worug survived only by denying her entry and staying hidden
- **Thistle:** Primary point of trust — Thistle's goodberries broke the panic; Worug attempted (and failed) to hug Thistle in gratitude
- **The Party (general):** Cautiously allied; relationship contingent on the party honoring their evacuation promise
