# Jacoby Hysstryn

## Identity
- Role: Civilian; one of two brothers searching for their missing/deceased sibling Mirak Hysstryn
- First appearance: Approached Thistle alongside his brother Persson Hysstryn at the Talking Troll tavern to commission an investigation into Mirak's disappearance/death

*Also known as: Jacob Hysstryn.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goal:** Find definitive proof of what happened to his missing/deceased brother Mirak — not a convenient dismissal or ambiguous remains; seeking justice or answers around the mysterious death.
- Outspoken and theatrical; willing to publicly mock authority figures (performed an impression of the Lord Protector for the tavern crowd).
- Deeply frustrated by what he perceives as class-based neglect — believes his family's lack of wealth caused their case to be ignored for a decade.
- Pragmatic about leverage: offered a magical bow to incentivize the party rather than relying on goodwill alone.
- Motivated and proactive — took initiative to find and convince Thistle to take the case.

## History with the Party

1. **First meeting (Talking Troll):** Jacoby and Persson approached Thistle while visibly upset and persuaded her to investigate the death of their brother Mirak, whose body was discovered in the attic of the Six Windows Inn. Jacoby engaged Thistle directly, offering a magical bow in exchange for help locating Mirak. He specifically requested hard evidence — not remains that could be dismissed or falsified.

2. **Lord Protector's office:** When the party presented their findings to Delratha, Jacoby and Persson erupted in anger, confronting the Lord Protector directly about the decade of inaction and accusing the institution of treating common families as lesser than noble ones.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The Lord Protector's office, following the confrontation with Delratha
- **Active concerns:** Whether the party's findings constitute the proof he demanded; the ongoing question of Mirak's fate
- **What the party knows:** He offered a magical bow as payment; he wants proof, not ashes; he and Persson are Mirak's surviving brothers and the ones who brought this case to the party
- **What remains hidden:** His current whereabouts after the confrontation; whether he considers the party's findings satisfactory; his deeper motivations and his relationship with Mirak; whether he knows more than he has disclosed

## Relationships
- **Persson Hysstryn (brother):** Travels and advocates alongside him; both share the same grievances and goals.
- **Mirak Hysstryn (brother):** The missing/deceased sibling driving all of Jacoby's actions.
- **Delratha (Lord Protector):** Openly hostile; holds her institution responsible for ignoring the family's case.
- **Thistle (party / PC):** Primary point of contact; Jacoby singled Thistle out for negotiation and made the bow offer directly to them; client relationship.
