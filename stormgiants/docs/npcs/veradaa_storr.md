# Veradaa Storr

## Identity
- Role: Independent treasure hunter / cartographer; held knowledge of Cult of the Dragon cache locations and abandoned dwarven outpost / Greypeak treasure trove locations
- Faction: None confirmed
- First appearance: Found dead by the party in a clearing along the Black Road near Parnast (her notes had already been used by the party on the 5th day of the 1st tenday of Taraskh to navigate an abandoned dwarven outpost)

*Also known as: Veradda Stoor, Veradda Store.*

## Personality & Motivations
- No direct interaction recorded; personality and motivations are inferred from circumstances
- She had independently mapped five Cult of the Dragon treasure caches in the Greypeak Mountains, suggesting ambition, resourcefulness, and access to significant intelligence

## History with the Party
- **Earlier note use (5th day, 1st tenday of Taraskh):** The party used her notes to locate a hallway leading into an abandoned dwarven outpost. She did not appear in person; the notes served as a navigational resource only.
- **Greypeak treasure trove notes:** Other notes she authored directed the party to a treasure trove location in the northern Greypeaks, describing a narrow path halfway up a mountain leading to secret doors, a tunnel complex, and an ice-covered chamber containing frost giants and a cursed diamond.
- **First and only direct encounter:** The party discovered Storr's body in a roadside clearing near Parnast. Orsik, Vardis, Thistle, and Unla Key had each independently sought her out, intending to offer their services as guards or assistants in exchange for a cut of the treasure. She was dead before any of them arrived.
- Thistle recovered her notes from among her belongings. The notes detail five Cult of the Dragon treasure caches hidden in the Greypeaks, and have become the party's primary operational document.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Dead; body found near the Black Road outside Parnast
- **Cause of death:** Unknown
- **Active plans:** None — her work now lives on through her recovered notes

## What the Party Knows vs. What Remains Hidden
- **Known:** She had mapped five cult treasure caches; her notes are in the party's possession
- **Unknown:** Who killed her, why, and whether anyone else knows the notes exist or that she is dead

## Relationships
- No confirmed relationships with other NPCs on record
- All four party members (Orsik, Vardis, Thistle, Unla Key) had independently sought her out — suggesting her reputation had spread, though the source of that reputation is unknown
