# Hsing

## Identity
- **Role:** Golden pseudodragon; messenger, agent, and emissary of the SEER. Also functions as patron-adjacent figure through his association with Azam.
- **Faction:** The SEER / Lord's Alliance (books delivered as official Lord's Alliance business)
- **First Appearance:** Draped across Azam's shoulders at Vuerthyl, initially appearing asleep. Stirred to approve the party's involvement via a nod to Azam.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Advance the SEER's interests — acquiring magical knowledge, monitoring regional threats (Bad Fruul, Gralm), and managing the party as assets.
- Hsing projects an air of sleepy imperious authority, mildly annoyed when not given immediate attention, and coldly dismissive when his decisions are questioned (e.g., rebuffing Orsik's protest over payment).
- Despite his affect, he intervenes decisively when needed — freezing Azam mid-outburst and delivering a measured reprimand — revealing real authority beneath the drowsy facade.
- He is not above petty deflection (teleporting away citing missed naps) and has repeatedly provoked Orsik's hostility through condescension and low payment.

## History with the Party
1. **First meeting (Vuerthyl):** Met the party via Azam. Approved their involvement with a nod. Delivered the cryptic warning: *"When there is a seed, it must be let to grow before it is killed."* Gave Thistle the only known lead on the Drake Thule: *"Where there is the yellow sky, there is the drake you are looking for."*
2. **Pre-selected the party:** Hsing had researched party members beforehand, telling Azam that Orsik "has done things that have made him unemployable" and that Vardis' clerics won't allow him in their temples — and that these were precisely the people the SEER wanted.
3. **Sinkhole morning (Wizard of the Crag mission):** Teleported to the party's location with a whistle. Briefed them on the Wizard of the Crag and the SEER's need for ancient magic manuscripts. Disclosed the SEER's conflict with Bad Fruul. Instructed use of Mama Manyknuckles' pendant to enter the tower. Departed citing naps.
4. **Parnast, Golden Tankard (payment):** Appeared when Orsik arrived with the books, initially alarmed the Wizard of the Crag had become a lich. Arranged a covered wagon with Lord's Alliance agents to collect the books. Paid 150 gp — coldly dismissed Orsik's objection. Orsik swung his axe at empty air after Hsing teleported away.
5. **Parnast, Golden Tankard (back room meeting):** Returned with Azam. Offered information on Thistle's missing uncle as mission reward. Froze Azam when he lost his temper at Thistle; reprimanded him firmly. Relayed SEER intelligence on Gralm's forces and Bad Fruul's recruitment. Deflected questions about the SEER's identity and gender. Teleported away; Azam stated Hsing had other missions.
6. **Golden Tankard (ornate door):** Beckoned Orsik through an ornate door that appeared in the wall. When Orsik refused, conjured mental images of death in Lord's Alliance cities as a threat. Orsik refused anyway and noted Hsing might have had better luck being nicer.
7. **Headband confrontation:** Arrived demanding Orsik hand over the headband. Townspeople and party allies sided with Hsing. Hsing inadvertently undermined his own credibility by conceding that one probably shouldn't trust a spymaster since they lie — Orsik cited this as validation of his distrust and named Hsing explicitly when explaining his reasons for leaving.
8. **SEER's campsite:** Present when the SEER appeared at the party's camp. No independent actions recorded.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The SEER's campsite; also noted as traveling on other missions per Azam.
- **Active Operations:** Continues to act as the SEER's field emissary, relaying intelligence and managing party assignments.
- **What the party knows:** Hsing is the SEER's agent and voice; he has significant power (freezing Azam, teleportation, mental imagery); he researched the party before recruiting them; he controls payment and mission terms.
- **What remains hidden:** The full scope of the SEER's network and goals; Hsing's own capabilities and limits; why the SEER selected this particular party beyond surface-level explanations.

## Relationships
- **The SEER:** Direct servant and emissary; speaks on her behalf and enforces her decisions. Refuses to disclose her gender or details about her.
- **Azam:** Azam serves under Hsing's patronage. Hsing publicly corrected and physically restrained Azam when he lost his temper — Hsing holds clear authority over him. (Note: Vardis corrected that *Azam* belongs to *Hsing*, not the reverse.)
- **Orsik:** Deeply antagonistic. Orsik has never trusted Hsing, named him explicitly as a reason for leaving, and has twice attempted physical retaliation (axe swing, near-attack). Hsing is dismissive and cold toward Orsik.
- **Thistle:** Thistle has directly challenged Hsing on mission value and rewards. Hsing engaged more substantively with Thistle than with others — answering questions about Thule, and offering Thistle's missing uncle as a reward hook.
- **Lord's Alliance:** Books from the Wizard of the Crag were collected as official Lord's Alliance business, suggesting Hsing or the SEER operates with or through the Alliance.

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease (Orsik):** Hsing's low payment, cold dismissal, use of fear-based coercion (mental death imagery), and the headband confrontation all eroded Orsik's trust to the breaking point. Orsik named Hsing's untrustworthiness explicitly when departing.
- **Contextual (Party general):** Hsing's inadvertent admission that spymasters lie, made during the headband confrontation, broadly damaged his credibility with those present.
