# Glazhael the Undying

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Dracolich; formerly Glazhael the Cloudchaser, a living white dragon and partner/steed to Talis the White
- **Faction:** Formerly Cult of the Dragon (ejected by Severin); now effectively independent. Phylactery formerly held by Sandesyl.
- **First Appearance:** First revealed through Worug's account during a debrief session; later encountered clinging to the ceiling of the glacier vault beneath Blagothkus' castle, above a massive treasure hoard

*Also known as: Glazhael, Glazhael the Cloudchaser, Glazeal.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Recover and protect her phylactery; freedom from subjugation (particularly from Sandesyl's hold over her phylactery); restoration of her honor after a series of defeats and humiliations; survival on her own terms; maintain her undead existence and influence
- Proud and dismissive — ends conversations on her schedule ("Now go."), laughs off questions she finds beneath her.
- Cunning, self-serving, and transactional — willing to deal with the party only as long as it serves her interests; **explicitly untrustworthy** and willing to turn on the party if circumstances favor doing so.
- Operates through calculated self-interest; her cooperation with the party has always been transactional, not loyal.
- Demonstrates a pattern of reluctant, minimal commitment: her fear attack on the party appeared half-hearted, suggesting she honors deals only to the letter, not the spirit.
- Behavior at the castle suggests strategic patience: Thistle observed her withdrawing in a way that appeared deliberate, as if she was manipulating the party's movements rather than retreating.

## History with the Party

1. **First Contact (War of the Dragon era):** As Glazhael the Cloudchaser, she made a non-aggression pact with the Heroes of the War of the Dragon (the "First Faction").

2. **Killed at the Tomb of Diderius:** Called back to cult service after the White Wyrmspeaker's capture; killed by the dwarf Stroud using a bow.

3. **Resurrected as a Dracolich:** Brought back by Jorgen in Xonathal's tower using the Blue Dragon mask.

4. **Rejected by the Cult:** Returned to Talis but was declared unfit to serve by Severin, who ruled dracoliches could not be cult members. Sent north to spy.

5. **Deceived at Mkoz Glacier:** Encountered the First Faction again; was fed false information about the Blue Dragon mask's location.

6. **Second Defeat:** To reclaim her honor, joined a plot to kill the First Faction. Was defeated again.

7. **Bound to Sandesyl:** Her phylactery came into Sandesyl's possession, subjugating her. She was furious about this.

8. **Revealed via Worug (party debrief):** The party learned she was originally Talis the White's dragon mount, was killed by the First Faction, returned as a dracolich, and attempted to claim the cloud castle. Blagothkus redirected her with a false quest for an unspecified artifact. After the return of Tiamat was thwarted, Blagothkus retook the castle and she returned, taking up residence in the glacier beneath it.

9. **The Darkness Incident:** During a period of darkness at the castle, Blagothkus told the party she had left the area — framing it as a chance to flee. Thistle noted her withdrawal appeared staged, raising suspicion she was herding the party toward Sandesyl's tower.

10. **The Failed Initial Alliance:** The party had apparently struck an early deal with her: she would intervene against Sandesyl if the party got Sandesyl seriously wounded. The plan collapsed when Thistle pointed out she had no way to physically enter Sandesyl's lair, making the arrangement unworkable. Orsik was openly frustrated by the outcome.

11. **Deal struck with the party (glacier vault):** Agreed to join the fight against Sandesyl — but only at the moment Sandesyl was nearly defeated. Refused to share how she planned to kill the vampire. Dismissed the party with "Now go." (A separate framing of this same arrangement notes the conditional truce involves the party retrieving her phylactery in exchange for cooperation; the deal is fragile and explicitly transactional.)

12. **Tower battle:** Spotted by Thistle sneaking toward the tower. Used dragon fear on the party from a safe distance (affecting Thistle, Thuul, and Unla Kee). Vardis noted she appeared to be shadowboxing — not fully committed. Advanced, breathed ice encasing the tower, screamed "THEY ARE ALL DEAD!" and flew away.

13. **Return to the glacier (post-Sandesyl):** Was surprised to find the party alive. Upon learning Sandesyl was effectively dead, honored her agreement: shattered the ice wall with her tail to reveal Sandesyl's coffin location and confirmed her phylactery was inside alongside the corpse. When asked how to kill a vampire, laughed and claimed ignorance. Departed once Unla Kee handed over the locked box containing her phylactery.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Departed the glacier after recovering her phylactery; current whereabouts unknown
- **Active Plans:** None confirmed; now free of Sandesyl's control. Her deliberate withdrawal patterns and apparent maneuvering of the party suggest she has her own ongoing agenda.
- **What the Party Knows:** She honored the deal; her phylactery has been returned; she is a dracolich free of her former captor; she may be manipulating them; she knows (or knew) something about killing Sandesyl but claimed ignorance when asked directly after the fact.
- **What Remains Hidden:** Whether she truly had a secret method to kill Sandesyl, or whether that claim was leverage. Her long-term intentions now that she is free; whether her conditional "alliance" was ever genuine.

> **GM Note:** Earlier party planning included whether to **return the phylactery** (solidifying a dangerous alliance) or **destroy it** (freeing the castle but almost certainly triggering open conflict). The party chose the former path.

## Relationships
- **Talis the White:** Former partner/rider; relationship severed by her death and transformation; she returned to Talis after resurrection but was ultimately separated from the cult.
- **Severin:** Rejected her from the cult; source of her exile and dishonor.
- **Sandesyl:** Former captor who held her phylactery; now effectively dead. She was furious at this subjugation.
- **Jorgen:** Resurrected her as a dracolich using the Blue Dragon mask.
- **Blagothkus:** Complicated history — he lured her away with a false quest and retook the castle; coexistence was uneasy.
- **The Party (3HPP):** Transactional alliance of narrow mutual interest. She honored her agreement minimally and is now square with them. No warmth; no ongoing loyalty implied. Treated as pawns or tools.
- **Thistle:** Noted her fear ability lacked conviction — "if she had a heart, it wouldn't have been in it." Suspects her of manipulating rather than cooperating with the party.
- **Vardis:** Observed her apparent half-commitment during the tower attack.
- **Unla Kee:** Dismissed her firebolt attempt; received the phylactery box from her upon departure.
- **Orsik:** Was laughed at when he asked how to kill a vampire. Frustrated by her non-appearance against Sandesyl; views her as a dangerous threat ("A vampire, a dracolich, and an army of undead — how badly do we want this castle?").
- **The Flying Castle:** Her influence was tied to the castle; destroying her phylactery would have severed this connection.
