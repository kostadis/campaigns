# Diego

## Identity
- **Role:** Personal guard and para-amour of Fiora Bendaega
- **Affiliation:** The Guard (city guard or similar organization)
- **First Appearance:** Recruitment meeting with Fiora Bendaega; stepped forward to intervene when Orsik became threatening

## Personality & Motivations
- Deeply personally loyal to Fiora, beyond what professional duty would require — described as "so fond of" her.
- Protective and quick to act on perceived threats, but responsive to Fiora's cues and not prone to unnecessary escalation.
- Curious and apparently conscientious about his duties; actively sought information about the party's involvement with the tower.

## History with the Party
1. **Recruitment Meeting:** Appeared at Fiora's side when Orsik became threatening, advancing on the group and demanding to know if they were causing problems. Stood down when Fiora signaled him with a wink after Orsik greeted him.
2. **Outside the Tower:** Encountered the party on their way back to Aunt Nonnie's after they were teleported out of the tower. He questioned them about whether they had entered the tower. Unla Kee manipulated fate to convince him not only that the party had escaped the tower, but that it was essential for Diego and the Guard to investigate it themselves. Vardis then disclosed the secret of the tower's entrance to him.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Departed to investigate the tower with the Guard.
- **Active Operations:** Leading or participating in a Guard investigation of the tower.
- **Party Knows:** Diego is Fiora's para-amour and loyal guard; he is now en route to the tower due to Unla Kee's manipulation and Vardis's disclosure.
- **Hidden:** Whether Diego will report back to Fiora about the party's involvement; what he will find in the tower; whether he suspects the party's account was manipulated.

## Relationships
- **Fiora Bendaega:** Para-amour and employer; loyalty appears personal as much as professional. Takes direction from her signals.
- **Unla Kee (party):** Was successfully manipulated by Unla Kee's fate manipulation — may not know this.
- **Vardis (party):** Received the secret of the tower's entrance from Vardis directly.
- **Orsik (party):** Initially confronted him at the recruitment meeting; tension defused without incident.
