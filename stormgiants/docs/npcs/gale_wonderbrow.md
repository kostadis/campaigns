# Gale Wonderbrow

## Identity
- Merchant based in Mirabar
- First appeared when the party sought information about Laela; Thistle made contact with him directly

## Personality & Motivations
- Pragmatic dealmaker — he had information but required a tangible favor before sharing it
- Physically imposing: athletic build, short hair, a jaw that looks like it's been tested before
- No evident larger agenda; appears to be a local businessman with useful connections or firsthand knowledge

## History with the Party
- Agreed to share information about Laela in exchange for help organizing an elaborate wedding
- Thistle, drawing on her Seelie Court abilities, delivered a spectacular ceremony
- Satisfied with the result, Gale disclosed what he knew: a fairy matching Laela's description was witnessed single-handedly slaughtering brigands who had surrounded a caravan, leaving only Laela herself and one terrified young man alive

## Current Status
- Last known location: Mirabar
- No known active plans or operations
- **Party knows:** His eyewitness account of Laela's violent encounter with the brigands
- **Hidden from party:** Unknown — no additional information present in notes

## Relationships
- **Thistle:** Transactional but satisfied; completed a fair exchange
- **Laela:** Witnessed (or heard account of) her in a violent incident; nature of any ongoing connection unknown
