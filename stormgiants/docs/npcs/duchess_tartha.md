# Duchess Tartha

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Duchess; one of four giant lords assembled by Mirran to eliminate the Three-Headed Party Problem (3HPP)
- **Faction:** Aligned with Queen Mirran; giant nobility
- **First Appearance:** Encountered in the cavernous room with the pool and teleportation circle when Mirran springs her ambush trap

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Fulfill her duty to the queen by killing the party before they can reach or harm her
- Carries herself with the pride of giant nobility — formal enough to introduce the lords by name and title at the start of combat
- Capable of grudging respect toward enemies, acknowledging Orsik's lethality after he kills Thane Braxow, while immediately pivoting to a death threat
- Prone to inter-giant mockery; taunted Jarl Hellenhild for missing Thistle with a boulder

## History with the Party
1. **The Ambush:** Tartha was present alongside Count Vaal, Jarl Hellenhild, and Thane Braxow when Mirran sprung the trap. She acted as spokesperson, declaring the party's intent against the queen and the lords' duty to stop them.
2. **Early Combat:** Walked through Vardis's wall of fire unaffected (fire immunity) and struck Thistle with her giant sword on the other side. Passed by Orsik, who landed an opportunity attack.
3. **Mid-Combat:** Struck by Thistle's magically charged arrows. Unla Kee's fireball-like explosion invigorated rather than harmed her.
4. **Knocked Prone:** Orsik's second strike knocked her prone. Orsik then moved away. She has not yet risen or been confirmed dead.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Combat area — the cavernous room with the pool and teleportation circle
- **Condition:** Knocked prone; status (alive/dead) unconfirmed at end of recorded events
- **Active:** Combat with the party is ongoing

**What the party knows:** She is a fire giant lord loyal to Mirran, is immune to fire, hits hard, and is currently prone on the battlefield.
**What remains hidden:** Her current hit point total; whether she has abilities or tactics not yet used.

## Relationships
- **Mirran (Queen):** Serves her directly; assembled on Mirran's orders; frames her actions in terms of duty to the queen
- **Thane Braxow:** Fellow giant lord; killed early in the same combat by Orsik
- **Jarl Hellenhild:** Fellow giant lord; Tartha openly mocked her for missing Thistle with a boulder — relationship is functional but not warm
- **Count Vaal:** Fellow giant lord; no notable interaction recorded
- **Orsik (PC):** She singled him out after he killed Braxow, offering backhanded respect before threatening his life; he later knocked her prone
- **Thistle (PC):** Tartha crossed through Vardis's wall of fire specifically to strike Thistle; Thistle has landed charged arrow hits on her
- **Vardis (PC):** Rendered her wall of fire useless against Tartha due to fire immunity — no direct personal interaction noted
- **Unla Kee (PC):** Unla Kee's fire-based explosion actively benefited Tartha rather than harming her
