# Sleth

## Identity
- **Role:** Dryad caretaker, assigned to the westernmost tree beneath the Grandfather Tree's canopy
- **Faction:** Serves the Grandfather Tree; appears aligned with fey/druidic interests
- **First Appearance:** Met Thistle at the Grandfather Tree during the Helia/Halisa situation

## Personality & Motivations
- Deeply devoted to the Grandfather Tree, which she considers "the greatest tree in the Universe" — this is clearly a point of pride and identity for her
- Conscientious and observant; notices negligence others cannot perceive (e.g., the misgrowing branch)
- Emotionally warm and expressive — she openly felt relief when the feuding dryads finally departed
- Appears trusting and somewhat naïve toward charming figures; she was taken in by whoever posed as "Prince Thornacious"

## History with the Party
- Spoke with **Thistle** about the neglected trees, pointing out a branch growing wrongly that will cause imbalance in thirty years — a detail Thistle could not verify herself
- Revealed the true nature of Helia and Halisa's "curse": Titania never imprisoned them. The two dryads trapped themselves through mutual hatred and refusal to sacrifice for one another
- Was present when the son of **Great Chief Boorvald Orcbane** arrived, furious about the theft of his tribe's artifact (a torc). As the party slipped away, Sleth commented that "Prince Thornacious was so gracious and helpful" — indicating she was deceived by whoever the party used to manage the confrontation, and does not know the party's role in the theft

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The Grandfather Tree
- **Active concerns:** The misgrowing branch that will cause imbalance in thirty years; the aftermath of Helia and Halisa's departure leaving two trees without caretakers
- **What the party knows:** She is friendly, knowledgeable about fey politics, and was successfully deceived (or at least kept in the dark) during the torc situation
- **What remains hidden:** Whether she will eventually learn the party was involved in the torc theft; her reaction if she discovers she vouched for their deception

## Relationships
- **Helia & Halisa:** Frustrated by their long neglect; relieved at their departure
- **Grandfather Tree:** Reverent caretaker — this relationship is central to her identity
- **Son of Boorvald Orcbane:** Encountered him directly; appears to have interacted warmly with "Prince Thornacious" (likely a disguise or alias used during the party's escape)
- **Thistle:** Spoke candidly with her; appears to regard the party (or at least Thistle) without suspicion
