# Krowen Valharrow

## Identity
- **Role:** Senior Harper Agent; later promoted to upper echelons of Harper leadership
- **Faction:** The Harpers
- **First Appearance:** Moongleam Tower, Everlund — greeted the party after Dral Thelev teleported them there; identified as a Harper by Vardis

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Investigate and resolve the sundered Ordning of the Giants; protect the North from the giant threat using deniable, contractor-based operations
- **Philosophy:** A follower of the "school of Ezra" — using outside adventurers and Harper funds to derisk core agents and provide plausible deniability, a strategy he adopted despite personal reservations about it
- Self-described old-timer who believes in the old ways but acknowledges desperate times demand pragmatism. He plays a convincing confused old mage act when cornered, but is sharp underneath. His documented success with the contractor model earned him promotion, though older agents like Kolbaz view him with contempt as someone who has degraded Harper standards. Described by Gauche as a big-picture thinker who misses operational details.

## History with the Party
1. **Initial Recruitment (Moongleam Tower, Everlund):** Krowen explained the indirect teleportation ruse — using alcohol and a roundabout route to obscure the party's connection to the Harpers. Vardis saw through the ruse; Krowen covered with his confused old mage act. He nearly directed the party out the front door before being called out on the contradiction. Offered the Harpers' teleportation network across the North as payment in lieu of gold, presenting a coffer of spell scrolls on Darathra Shendrel's behalf. He was privately uncertain about Orsik due to reported Zhentarim ties.
2. **Mission Assignment:** Directed the party to travel to Yartar to find a sword; teleported them to his agent there. Warned the party strongly against pursuing Cloud Giant assets, expressing genuine alarm at the suggestion.
3. **Ongoing Pressure via Proxy:** Krowen sent a Harper agent to pressure the 3HPP about their inaction following the Oracle's information. He offered treasure maps as further incentive and threatened to revoke access to the magical pathway network if the party did not act immediately.
4. **Contract Severed:** Following Orsik's refusal, the party terminated their relationship with Krowen and the Harpers entirely.
5. **Condemnation:** Krowen formally condemned the 3HPP — as recounted by Darathra — for betraying the Harper circles, causing the deaths of several agents, and destroying powerful and expensive magical assets. His tone was described as one of cold accountability.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Position:** Promoted to the upper echelons of Harper leadership; no specific location given
- **Active Operations:** No longer directly running the 3HPP; the contract is severed
- **What the Party Knows:** That Krowen formally condemned them and holds them responsible for Harper casualties and destroyed assets. That he sponsored their original mission and used them as deniable contractors.
- **What Remains Hidden:** His current whereabouts and whether he has taken any formal action against the party beyond condemnation. His private assessment of whether the party can still be useful or are now considered enemies.

## Relationships
- **The 3HPP (Party):** Former contractors; relationship severed and turned hostile following the party's inaction and Orsik's refusal. Has formally condemned them.
- **Harshnag:** Coordinated with Harshnag directly, telling him the party had agreed to take on the giant quest; Harshnag invoked this arrangement repeatedly to motivate the party.
- **Darathra Shendrel:** Connected — the spell scroll coffer was presented on her behalf; she later relayed his condemnation of the party.
- **Dral Thelev:** Works with Krowen's network; expressed residual faith that Krowen "chose them for a reason."
- **Kolbaz (Harper Agent, Yartar):** Krowen's agent in Yartar; holds Krowen in contempt, calling him "pompous, two-bit, self-aggrandizing" and criticizing his use of non-sworn contractors.
- **Gauche the Young & Nespril Menk:** Aware of Krowen's model; Gauche noted his big-picture thinking and detail blindness, Nespril characterized his contractors as mercenaries rather than true believers.
- **Orsik (specifically):** Krowen held private reservations about Orsik from the start due to reported Zhentarim proximity; Orsik briefly considered approaching Krowen by invoking family name redemption but dismissed it, believing Krowen would not accept that framing.

## Arc Score Events

- **Decrease:** Party severed the Harper contract following Krowen's pressure via proxy agent
- **Decrease (significant):** Krowen formally condemned the 3HPP for Harper casualties and destroyed magical assets
