# Aeglossil

## Identity
- **Role:** Vampire; former mistress of the derro/duergar inhabitants of the caves
- **Faction:** Unknown; commands loyalty of the remaining derro in the caves
- **First Appearance:** Session in which the party discovered a secret door southeast of the throne room, leading to a hidden tomb

## Personality & Motivations
- Goals and deeper motivations are largely unknown — she awoke disoriented and immediately acted on instinct, feeding on Orsik before being expelled.
- Demonstrated predatory and survival-driven behavior; showed fear when confronted with the symbol of Corellon, suggesting that divine elven power holds particular weight against her.

## History with the Party
1. **Discovery:** The party found her tomb southeast of the throne room, where two duergar were tending to her resting place reverentially. She lay in a dormant state, appearing as a white stone statue of a sleeping elven woman.
2. **Awakening:** Thistle — acting under her charm — kissed the statue, triggering her revival. She emerged disoriented and immediately grappled Orsik, biting and feeding from him.
3. **Confrontation:** Vardis presented the symbol of Corellon, causing her to snarl in fear. Unla Kee then cast Banishment, expelling her to another realm.
4. **Aftermath:** The following day, Thistle confirmed he had been charmed by her prior to the encounter. The remaining derro in the caves are waiting for her return.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Banished to another realm via Unla Kee's Banishment spell.
- **Active:** The derro remaining in the caves are loyal to her and await her return.
- **What the party knows:** She is a vampire, she can charm from a dormant state, she fears the symbol of Corellon, and she has been banished.
- **What remains hidden:** Her history, her original identity, how long she has been dormant, how she came to control the derro, and whether/when Banishment will expire or be reversed.

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Hostile. Bit Orsik; charmed Thistle. Expelled before any communication occurred.
- **Orsik:** Fed on him during her awakening.
- **Thistle:** Charmed him remotely while still dormant; used him to trigger her own revival.
- **Vardis:** Her fear response to his symbol of Corellon suggests a meaningful vulnerability to elven divine power specifically.
- **Unla Kee:** Banished by her.
- **The Derro/Duergar of the Caves:** Holds their fierce loyalty; they tend her tomb and await her return.
