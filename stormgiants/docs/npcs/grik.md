# Grik

## Identity
- **Role:** Goblin chief; field commander of Bad Fruul's forces at Ambush Gap
- **Faction:** Bad Fruul's warband
- **First Appearance:** The party encountered Grik when they arrived at Ambush Gap, where he commanded his gang from a raised tile surface overlooking the trail.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Enforce Bad Fruul's control over the pass; rob and neutralize travelers on Bad Fruul's orders.
- Grik operated through intimidation, invoking Bad Fruul's name as his primary source of authority. In combat he proved ruthless and pragmatic, using a goblin subordinate as a living shield without hesitation.

## History with the Party
- **Ambush Gap:** Grik confronted the party from the raised tile, demanding they surrender all equipment and treasure or be killed and stripped. He claimed Bad Fruul had arranged for poison to be deployed ahead of the ambush to weaken travelers before they reached the gap.
- During the ensuing fight, Grik used a goblin subordinate to block Thistle's arrow and the drake's acid damage, and evaded Unla Kee's binding ice. He was ultimately killed by Orsik on top of the tile.
- **Loot:** His body yielded a gold stylus and a letter from Bad Fruul ordering the defense of the pass and announcing Bad Fruul's intention to seize Parnast.

## Current Status
- **Deceased.** Killed by Orsik at Ambush Gap.
- The letter and stylus recovered from his body are now in the party's possession.

## Relationships
- **Bad Fruul:** Direct superior; Grik commanded the ambush under Bad Fruul's explicit written orders.
- **The Party:** Died as an enemy combatant. His letter provided the party their first concrete intelligence on Bad Fruul's plan to take Parnast.
