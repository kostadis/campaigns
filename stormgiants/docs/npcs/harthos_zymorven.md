# Harthos Zymorven

## Identity
- **Role:** Wealthy knight; son and heir of Lord Zymorven of Zymorven Hall
- **Faction:** Noble family (Zymorven); no other affiliation confirmed
- **First Appearance:** Never appeared in person at first reference (mentioned by Urgala Meltimer at the Talking Troll); physically appeared when brought before the party by a zombie servant in Yartar, where he was imprisoned and awaiting execution

*Also known as: Harthal Zymorven.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Initially sought adventure at Leila's urging; after his ordeal, he wants nothing more than to return home and become the next Lord Zymorven.
- Emotionally volatile and somewhat entitled — he screamed at Unla Kee during a battle he himself refused to participate in, hiding behind Vardis instead of fighting.
- Prone to misplaced blame; he directed his resentment toward Leila rather than reflecting on his own choices, though he did eventually express genuine remorse.
- Easily reassured and quick to accept a flattering explanation, as shown when he accepted Orsik's claim that he had been "scouting."
- Known to be a former adventuring companion of Urgala's, suggesting some history of combat experience and adventuring ties.

## History with the Party

1. **Pre-campaign / Session reference (Urgala):** Urgala mentioned Harthos to the party as someone who possesses a giant-slaying longsword. She indicated he would part with it if the party invoked her name and explained their mission to fight giants and save the realms.

2. **Eltio's revelation:** Eltio later revealed that Harthos is currently imprisoned in Yartar on charges of murder. He is scheduled to be executed by decapitation on the **10th of the month** — a method deliberately chosen to prevent magical resurrection. The party debated multiple approaches to his situation, including intimidation, a fake arrest and escape offer, and claiming his father is ill.

3. **Referenced as quest objective:** Orsik informed the party that returning Harthos to Zymorven Hall would earn a 1,000 gp reward, facilitated by Eltio.

4. **Rescue in Yartar:** Imprisoned and awaiting execution, Harthos was brought before the party by the vampire's zombie servant at Thistle's request. He immediately mistook the disguised Orsik for his father Lord Zymorven and called out to him emotionally. When he tried to run to "his father," the vampire violently restrained him. He was released in exchange for the guards' corpses.

5. **At the Harper hangout:** Recognized Thistle Wendrod and vented frustrated anger, blaming Thistle's girlfriend Leila for his predicament. He explained that Leila had persuaded him to steal the Giant Slayer sword and pursue adventure, promising experiences like his father's. After Harthos killed someone he described as deserving it, Leila abandoned him, citing a need to find "the absolute love of her life." She had come to Yartar intending to join the Hand of Yartar to help locate Thistle, found them too locally focused, and moved on. Harthos felt used and deeply resented the entire episode.

6. **Departure from Everlund:** Traveled with the party; Orsik reverted to his true form at Moongleam Tower before Harthos could witness the transformation, preserving the deception.

7. **Journey home on the Rauvin Road:** Traveled with the party as escort. During a crag cat ambush, he hid behind Vardis and did not fight. He screamed at Unla Kee to act when she appeared distracted. Accepted Orsik's "scouting" excuse without question.

8. **Arrival at Zymorven Hall:** Reunited with his father. Declared the entire ordeal "a great bit of adventuring."

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Zymorven Hall, reunited with his father Lord Zymorven
- **Active Plans:** None — he intends to settle down and eventually succeed his father as lord
- **What the party knows:** He is safely home; the 1,000 gp reward is presumably claimable.
- **What remains hidden:** Harthos never learned that "his father" during the rescue was actually Orsik in disguise; the full circumstances of the alleged murder; whether he is guilty.

## Relationships
- **Lord Zymorven (father):** Deep emotional attachment; his father's approval and image drove much of his distress and remorse during captivity. Now reunited.
- **Urgala Meltimer:** Former adventuring companion; she speaks of him warmly and believes he will help the party. "We go way back."
- **Leila:** The rogue who manipulated him into stealing the Giant Slayer sword and pursuing adventure, then abandoned him after a killing. He blames her for everything and feels used.
- **Thistle Wendrod:** Knows Thistle and associates him with Leila; expressed anger toward Thistle by proxy when venting about his situation.
- **Orsik:** Briefly and emotionally believed Orsik (in disguise) to be his father; the deception was never revealed to him.
- **Unla Kee:** Screamed at her during the crag cat ambush.
- **Vardis:** Used as a shield during combat.
- **Eltio:** Source of intelligence on Harthos's imprisonment.
