# Brenner

## Identity
- **Role:** Captain of the City Guard, Yartar
- **Faction:** Yartar city guard (no known affiliation with the vampire or the Waterbarron's experimental program)
- **First Appearance:** Encountered by the party during evening rounds in the central market, where he ran a protection racket among small stalls and alley shops.

## Personality & Motivations
- Motivated by personal enrichment and the abuse of institutional authority.
- Described as thug-like, smug, and prone to leveraging his rank over those beneath him. His own guards appeared bored and dissatisfied with his leadership.

## History with the Party
- The party identified Brenner as he moved through the market's interconnected alleys collecting cuts from shop owners and stall vendors.
- He carried the **Giant Slayer sword** openly on his belt.
- The party ambushed and killed him during this encounter, acquiring the sword.

## Current Status
- **Status: DECEASED** — killed by the party in the Yartar central market.
- His death carries significant consequences: Eltio warned that authorities would likely attribute the killing to **Harthal's allies**, creating pressure to execute Harthal's associate (the boy) before the party could mount a rescue.

## Relationships
- **City Guard:** Commanded a unit of guards who showed little loyalty or enthusiasm.
- **Market vendors:** Victims of his protection racket; no known willing allies.
- **The Party:** Killed by them. His death has set a ticking clock on the prison rescue.
- No known ties to the vampire or the Waterbarron's program.

## Arc Score Events
- No arc score relationship with the party established prior to death.
