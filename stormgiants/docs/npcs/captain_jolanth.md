# Captain Jolanth

## Identity
- **Role:** Captain of the Guards of the Iron Circle, stationed at Llorkh's town entrance
- **Faction:** Iron Circle (Llorkh garrison)
- **First Appearance:** Party's arrival at Llorkh; stopped them at the entrance road to conduct an official warning

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Maintaining (and projecting) his authority over arrivals to Llorkh; preserving the town's peace on his own terms
- Officious and accustomed to intimidating weaker-seeming visitors with his position. Fond of ceremony and proclamation. However, he is easily flustered when his authority is reframed or undermined, and struggles to recover in the moment.

## History with the Party
- **First meeting:** Jolanth stopped the party at Llorkh's entrance, announced his role as protector of the town's peace, and warned that troublemakers would be sent to the keep. Orsik reframed Jolanth's proclamation as a binding promise of free protection for the party. Jolanth was unable to counter this and the party walked past him while he was still flustered.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Entrance road to Llorkh
- **Active Plans:** None noted beyond routine gate duty
- **What the Party Knows:** He is a garrison captain with authority to detain and send people to the keep. He is easily outmaneuvered socially.
- **Hidden from the Party:** Nothing noted; he has not been further investigated.

## Relationships
- **Iron Circle:** Loyal member; long-serving enough to have outlasted the Cult of the Dragon period and the decline of Zhentarim traffic through Llorkh
- **The Party (Orsik specifically):** Was publicly wrong-footed by Orsik at their first meeting — likely harbors embarrassment or low-grade resentment
