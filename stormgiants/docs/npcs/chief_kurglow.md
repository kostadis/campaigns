# Chief Kurglow

## Identity
- **Role:** Chief of the Spine Ripper tribe
- **Faction:** Spine Ripper orc tribe
- **First Appearance:** Session in which the party interrogated the families of orc prisoners; Kurglow did not appear in person but was named as the coercive force behind the prisoners' actions.

## Personality & Motivations
- Willing to use death threats against families to ensure compliance, suggesting a ruthless and controlling leadership style.
- Core motivation appears to be maintaining tight control over tribe members, including suppressing dissent or disloyalty through fear.

## History with the Party
- **No direct interaction.** Kurglow was named by the families of captured orcs Jarlor and Larrish, who revealed that Kurglow threatened to kill their families unless the two men spied on him — suggesting Kurglow feared surveillance or betrayal from within his own ranks.
- A child clarified that their father was coerced and did not act willingly.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Unknown. Whereabouts not established.
- **Active Plans:** Unclear. His use of coerced informants suggests internal political tension or paranoia within the tribe.
- **What the Party Knows:** Kurglow is the Spine Ripper chief who coerced Jarlor and Larrish under threat of death to their families.
- **What Remains Hidden:** His current location, the full scope of his operations, who he fears is watching him, and the broader political situation within the tribe.

## Relationships
- **Jarlor & Larrish:** Coerced them into acting as spies through death threats against their families.
- **Spine Ripper Tribe:** Their chief; exercises control through intimidation.
- **The Party:** No direct relationship established.
