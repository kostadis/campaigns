# The Captain of the Silverwatch

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Captain of the Silverwatch (city guard), Silverymoon
- **Faction:** Silverwatch (city law enforcement)
- **First Appearance:** Arrived at the Dancing Goat to escort the party to city courts following a report filed by Nyalah Uuseff

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Upholding city law and ensuring due process through Silverymoon's courts
- Demonstrates professional composure but is capable of genuine surprise — visibly stunned by the revelation of demonic murder. Shows pragmatic flexibility, agreeing to an unscheduled temple detour when the situation warranted it. Appears to take his duty seriously without being obstinate.

## History with the Party
1. **Initial Contact:** Arrived at the Dancing Goat to bring the party before city courts on the basis of Nyalah Uuseff's report
2. **Temple Detour:** Agreed to Orsik's request to visit a temple in order to raise Unla Kee as a witness, pausing to ask which temple Unla Kee would have preferred
3. **Corroboration:** Accompanied the party to the high priest of Mielikki, whose account supported the party's claims about demonic involvement
4. **Star Court:** Escorted the group to the Star Court and formally announced their arrival

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Silverymoon
- **Last Known Activity:** Escorting the party into the Star Court
- **Active Plans:** None confirmed beyond fulfilling his escort duties
- **Party Knowledge:** The party knows him only by his title; he has not been named. His stance toward the party appears professionally neutral to cautiously cooperative.
- **Hidden:** Full name, rank details, and any personal stake in the matter are unknown

## Relationships
- **Nyalah Uuseff:** Acted on her report; relationship otherwise undefined
- **High Priest of Mielikki:** Accompanied the party to his testimony; appeared to credit the corroboration
- **The Party:** Professional and dutiful; not adversarial, but not an ally — a neutral authority figure who responded reasonably to an unusual situation
