# Narcellia Varam'myr

## Identity
- **Role:** Priestess of Lolth; centuries-old schemer currently bound within a magical item
- **Faction:** Lolth's clergy (drow)
- **First Appearance:** Never appeared in person; introduced through discussion between the Seer and Chandra Stol

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven by a centuries-long vendetta against the bloodline of Luistarra — the follower of Angharradh who imprisoned her — with Chandra Stol as the current target
- Patient, manipulative, and strategically ruthless: even while trapped inside a headband for hundreds of years, she has been actively shaping events to serve her ends
- Has "particularly gruesome plans" for Chandra specifically, and is aware Chandra carries Luistarra's pendant
- Harbors a notable weakness: a deep fear of fey, particularly sprites

## History with the Party
- **Backstory:** Destroyed a village near Evereska belonging to Luistarra, who responded by using hag magic to bind Narcellia into a headband of intellect. She has been trapped there for several hundred years.
- **30 years ago:** Killed Chandra Stol's mother, driving Chandra into hiding.
- **Recent events:** While bound in the headband — currently owned by the hill giant Bad Fruul — she has been manipulating him: encouraging him to amass wealth and girth, teaching him rudimentary arcane magic, and exploiting the collapse of the Ordning to push him toward raising a monstrous humanoid army.
- **Direct provocation:** Had Bad Fruul's agents (Kragota Threeteeth's farmers) expose the existence of the new statue of Angharradh in Parnast, making the village a target for destruction — a continuation of her vendetta against Angharradh's followers.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Unknown; presumed to be with or influencing Bad Fruul somewhere to the south, still bound within the headband
- **Active operations:** Directing Bad Fruul's expansion and military buildup; Parnast marked as a destruction target
- **What the party knows:** Her history, her imprisonment, her vendetta against Chandra and Luistarra's line, her fear of sprites/fey, and that Prince Thornacious may be a useful weapon against her
- **What remains hidden:** Her precise location; the full extent of her plans beyond Bad Fruul

## Relationships
- **Bad Fruul:** Current "host" — she is bound in his headband and actively manipulating him as a proxy
- **Chandra Stol:** Target of a centuries-long vendetta; killed Chandra's mother; aware Chandra carries Luistarra's pendant and has specific plans for her
- **Luistarra (deceased ancestor of Chandra):** The one who imprisoned her; the origin of her vendetta against this bloodline
- **The Seer:** The Seer's organization spent thirty years tracking Narcellia down; located her through a ritual derived from tomes from the Marsh of Chelimber
- **Prince Thornacious:** Not yet in contact, but identified as a potential threat due to her fear of fey/sprites
- **Kragota Threeteeth:** Used Kragota's farmers as agents to expose Parnast's statue
