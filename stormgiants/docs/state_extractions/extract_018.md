## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Attack on the Brandimen Cabin
- **Session:** 2nd day of the 1st Tenday of Mirtul
- **Outcome:** Success
- **Details:** Party assaulted a cabin outside Parnast where prisoners from a commune were being held by Kagrota (Mother of Bones), an ogre shaman of Bane, and her goblin/orc warband. Kagrota was killed by Thistle. All hostile forces were defeated.
- **Consequences:** Prisoners rescued; Kagrota's forces destroyed; Kagrota's Bane shrine discovered beneath the cabin; loot recovered (50 gold, six sapphires worth 100 gold each).

---

## Resolved Plot Threads

### Tove Brandimen's Mother Ruse — EXPOSED
- The rescued prisoners revealed that a half-elf matching Tove Brandimen's description of his mother was "thick as thieves with Kagrota," indicating Tove's story about his captive mother was fabricated. The severed finger used as evidence belonged to a commune member, not his mother. The party was deceived into the mission.

### Kagrota's Commune Raid — Context Established
- Kagrota had been sent by **Bad Fruul** to destroy a local commune. Several commune members died in the raid; survivors were caged at the cabin. The prisoners had no knowledge of Bad Fruul by name.

## NPC State Changes

### Kagrota (Mother of Bones) — DEAD
- Ogre shaman and Bane worshipper. Killed during the cabin assault. Operated under orders from Bad Fruul.

### Tove Brandimen — REVEALED AS DECEIVER
- His story about his captive mother was a fabrication. He had an established relationship with Kagrota. Party noted they should have "fed him to the Wyvern."

### Orc and Goblin Warband (Kagrota's forces) — DESTROYED
- Including the notable Green Jacket Goblin, killed by Unla Kee's scorcher spell.
