### Third Treasure Trove (Greypeaks mountain cache)
- **Session:** 3rd day of the 1st tenday of Taraskh
- **Outcome:** Success
- **Details:** Party navigated a two-day hike to a hidden cave in the northern Greypeaks (location provided by Veradda Store). Bypassed two pit traps. Discovered two dead frost giants clutching axes, killed by large bladed weapons prior to party arrival. Fought three translucent green energy creatures connected to a magical diamond on a pedestal. Diamond was destroyed to neutralize the creatures. A green-tinted spirit emerged from the diamond dust and attempted to sow distrust ("Your companions want to kill you and take your possessions!"), which the party ignored.
- **Consequences:** Party collected treasure left behind by the frost giants and collected the diamond dust.
