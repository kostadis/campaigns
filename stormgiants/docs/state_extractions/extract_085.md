## Resolved Plot Threads

### Iymrith's Identity and Goals — Partially Clarified
- Iymrith is confirmed alive and operating post-Maelstrom events (contradicts or extends prior campaign resolution of her death — note possible continuity divergence from base module).
- She is revealed to be a draconic scientist/artiste conducting hybrid experiments, specifically combining fey (fairy) and dragon biology.
- Her goal: create a creature that can swap between fey and dragon form intrinsically, undetectable by magical means, usable across planes.
- She is not dead in this campaign's continuity — she is active and powerful.

### Iymrith's Conspiracy Network — Revealed
- She has made deals with multiple dangerous factions as "investors" for her experiments:
  - **Giant lords** (who believed she was a giant, now know she is a dragon, thanks to the party)
  - **Red Wizards of Thay** (specifically, a deal with **Szass Tam**)
  - **Unseelie Court** representative
  - **Seelie Court** (Queen Titania's court approved the experiments on her subjects)
- She is in breach risk with all investors if the demonstration fails.

### Laela's Situation — Clarified
- Laela is confirmed alive, held in a cage in Iymrith's lair.
- She is a fey-dragon hybrid (fairy + dragon), Iymrith's creation/capture — she torched a caravan in dragon form and was subsequently captured.
- She has been practicing "fey-fullness" (fey meditation) and **refuses to shift into dragon form**, which is Iymrith's core problem.
- She was not working for Iymrith — she was independently searching for Thistle.
- Proof of life established via sending stone communication.
