## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Puzzle Room of Pandi
- **Session:** Chapter 15
- **Outcome:** Success
- **Description:** Party passed through a magical door in the Forgotten Forest into a pocket dimension guarded by Pandi, a dog-like fey creature. Solved a word puzzle (answer: "passage") and completed a tangram puzzle to earn passage through.
- **Consequences:** Party bypassed significant travel time through the Forgotten Forest and emerged near Gralm's encampment.

### Gralm's Contests of Strength and Drinking
- **Session:** Chapter 15
- **Outcome:** Success
- **Description:** To earn Gralm's consideration for an alliance, the party competed against Grindle the ogre in drinking rounds (Pixie Sangria, Everyday Ettin, Ettin Grinder, Harpy's Dream) and physical contests (boar deadlift, brick wall demolition, boar dog eating contest). Unla Kee won all three physical contests through creative use of magic (Reduce on the boar, Thunder Wave on the wall) and sheer eating capacity (24 boar dogs).
- **Consequences:** Gralm agreed to consider an alliance against Bad Fruul contingent on the party resolving the fey music problem — either delivering four fey heads or convincing the fey to stop playing music near his camp.

### Oblivillish Captured / Subdued
- **Session:** Chapter 15
- **Outcome:** Success (non-lethal)
- **Description:** When the party located Blasius, Oblivillish attacked him in anger over his past betrayal. Unla Kee knocked Oblivillish unconscious before he could kill Blasius.
- **Consequences:** Oblivillish was subdued; Blasius survived and confessed his crimes.
