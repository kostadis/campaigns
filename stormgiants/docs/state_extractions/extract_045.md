## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Noori — Rescue from Ice Spider Larder at Eye of the All-Father
- **Session:** 5th day of the 1st Tenday of Eleasis (Chapter 45)
- **Outcome:** Success
- **Details:** Noori, an Uthgardt tribeswoman, was found alive in a giant spider cocoon in the larder caverns beneath the Eye of the All-Father. The party cut her free, fed her, and escorted her to the main temple. She departed to find her tribe.
- **Consequences:** Orsik deceived Noori about the fate of her tribespeople (implied the party found them already dead rather than killed them). Noori left without hostility.

### Spider Larder Caverns — Full Exploration
- **Session:** Chapter 45
- **Outcome:** Complete — all caverns explored
- **Details:** Party explored the full extent of the natural cave system using the right-hand rule. Found a dead goat cocoon (nothing of value), a dead dwarf cocoon (17 cp, cards, dice), Noori's cocoon (rescued), a tunnel exit 400+ feet above the valley floor (deemed impassable), and a secondary chamber containing a giant spider and hatchling swarm (defeated). Orsik confirmed the caves are not original temple construction but natural cracks formed by mountain movement.
- **Loot Acquired:** Frozen potion of climbing, old axe with a spectacular sapphire.

### Hallway Granite Ball Trap — Eye of the All-Father
- **Session:** 6th day of the 1st Tenday of Eleasis, Chapter 45
- **Outcome:** Survived — trap fully cycled and reset
- **Details:** When Harshnag walked the northern main hallway, all bedroom entrances sealed with magical walls of stone and a 40-foot granite sphere materialized and rolled south. The party avoided being crushed by pressing to the walls. The sphere was being pushed by an animated stone giant statue from the south entrance. After the ball returned north and disappeared into the northern wall, the stone giant walked back to its pedestal and became immobile. All magical stone walls dissolved.
- **Consequences:** Harshnag was struck by the ball twice and took significant damage. Party learned not to trigger the hallway again.
