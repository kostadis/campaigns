## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Flint Rock — Uthgardt Spirit Mound
- **Session:** 5th day of the 2nd tenday of Eleasis 1492
- **Outcome:** Success
- **Details:** The 3HPP retrieved a giant relic from beneath a large stone at Flint Rock. Two giant elk (Harry and Mary) assisted by pushing the stone aside. The relic was a mithral spear tip (75 lbs).
- **Consequences:** Party now possesses the mithral spear tip as a giant artifact. Party departed aboard Delsephine's ship, the Dragon's Breath, bound for Morgur's Mound.

### Morgur's Mound — Uthgardt Spirit Mound (Excavation)
- **Session:** 6th–10th day of the 2nd tenday of Eleasis 1492
- **Outcome:** Success (after five days of digging)
- **Details:** The 3HPP excavated Morgur's Mound, spirit mound of the lost Thunderbeast tribe. No Thunderbeast tribe members were present — the mound had been abandoned. Previous dig sites from other treasure hunters were already present. Orsik and Vardis did all physical digging; Thistle and Unla Kee stood watch. Found two non-artifact magic items (Abjurer's Bangle, Helm of Comprehend Languages) before finally locating the giant relic on the 5th day of digging.
- **Consequences:** Party possesses a gold-plated fire giant's tooth — an ancient giant relic of giantkind, nonmagical, human-hand-sized. Party now holds at least two confirmed giant relics (mithral spear tip + giant tooth).

### Animated Thunderbeast Skeletons — Combat at Morgur's Mound
- **Session:** 10th day of the 2nd tenday of Eleasis 1492
- **Outcome:** Success — all four skeletons destroyed
- **Details:** Four animated ankylosaurus skeletons erupted from the mound when Orsik retrieved the giant tooth. Vardis's fireball destroyed the most-damaged skeleton and wounded two others. Unla Kee used his artifact (wand-of-wonder-like rock) to produce a gust of wind, trapping skeletons in the excavation pit. One skeleton was forced into the pit and destroyed by Unla Kee's fireball. Vardis struck down another. All four skeletons defeated.
- **Consequences:** No party deaths. Unla Kee was bitten during combat. Orsik is covered in five days of accumulated dirt.
