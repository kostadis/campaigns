## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Fomorian's Throne Room — Maergun's Lair
- **Session:** 1st day of the 2nd tenday of Eleasis
- **Outcome:** Success
- **Details:** The 3HPP, accompanied by goblin allies led by Tlega and Mirk, fought and killed the Fomorian Maergun, his Cyclops, and his Displacer Beast ("Haerbega") in the throne room
- **Consequences:** Drauchanar freed from captivity; treasure vault accessed via iron key found on Fomorian's body; Feywild mission completed; Queen Titania pleased

### Aeglossil the Vampire — Tomb Encounter
- **Session:** 1st day of the 2nd tenday of Eleasis
- **Outcome:** Success (banishment)
- **Details:** Thistle was charmed and kissed a statue, awakening an ancient elven vampire named Aeglossil; she bit Orsik before Unla Kee banished her to another plane; Thistle fled afterward
- **Consequences:** Vampire banished (not destroyed); Derro in caves remain, waiting for their mistress to return; party escaped back through portal to the Feywild exterior

### Beorunna's Well — Uthgardt Spirit Mound
- **Session:** 2nd day of the 2nd tenday of Eleasis
- **Outcome:** Success (relic retrieved)
- **Details:** The 3HPP descended into the cavern, fought Black Lion and Red Tiger tribe barbarians, their Shaman, and a Manticore; smashed the altar to retrieve the giant relic buried beneath it
- **Consequences:** All barbarian defenders killed except the Shaman, who feigned death; Manticore killed; the 3HPP staged the battlefield to make it appear the Red Lion Tribe attacked the Black Tiger Tribe, potentially triggering a civil war; Shaman survived and is aware of the deception, intending to pursue the thieves; giant relic retrieved

### One Stone — Sky Pony Spirit Mound
- **Session:** 3rd day of the 2nd tenday of Eleasis
- **Outcome:** Success (relic unlocked, retained in place but controlled)
- **Details:** The 3HPP approached the boulder altar; were attacked by two Cult of the Black Earth riders mounted on Bullettes; killed both riders and both Bullettes; deceived the Sky Pony tribe into believing Unla Kee is their Sky Pony Avatar; sent the tribe on a seven-day vision quest to clear the area; Unla Kee traced the relic's activation lines and unlocked its power
- **Consequences:** Relic's power accessed (shrink/enlarge three times daily; Wand of Wonder-like effect); Unla Kee suffered oozing blisters cured by the Blessed Horn; stone left in place with a stone-shaped likeness of Unla Kee and a magical water pool left as gifts; Sky Pony tribe now worships Unla Kee as their avatar; seven days before tribe returns
