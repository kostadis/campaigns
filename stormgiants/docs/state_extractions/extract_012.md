## Completed Encounters & Quests

**Wizard of the Crag's Castle — Magmin encounter**
- Session: Orsik 3rd day of the 3rd Tenday of Tarskh
- Outcome: Success — party defeated the magmin guardians in the library area
- Consequence: Party gained access to the secret treasure room and library

**Wizard of the Crag's Castle — Secret treasure room discovered**
- Outcome: Success — party found and opened the hidden chamber
- Contents: A ledger revealing the Wizard of the Crag's failed real estate investments, construction overruns, and a debt-related duel arrangement with Taskor
- Consequence: Party learned the Wizard of the Crag died in a duel with Taskor and that financial ruin preceded the castle's abandonment

**Elquen the Wight — Liberation**
- Outcome: Success (unconventional) — Unla Kee used fate manipulation to convince Elquen the Wizard of the Crag was alive and owed him back pay; Elquen accepted 150 GP and departed willingly
- Consequence: Elquen released from his self-imposed burden of guarding the library; wight now free and walking in the world

**Wizard of the Crag's Library — Book retrieval**
- Outcome: Success — party collected all books from the library shelves; transported them on a bookshelf used as a sled to the island's surface

**Water Weird and Mud Mephits — Return engagement**
- Outcome: Success — Water Weird rematerialized with Mud Mephit allies on the island surface; party defeated them by leveraging their existing mephit allies to trigger internecine fighting among the Mud Mephits
- Consequence: Party cleared the final obstacle; all headed back to Parnast
