## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Defense of Triboar — Fire Giant Attack
- **Session:** Chapter 27
- **Outcome:** Partial success / pyrrhic defense
- **Details:** Two fire giants attacked Triboar. The party engaged one giant; after a brutal fight (Orsik nearly killed, Darz killed, Lord Protector temporarily killed), the party downed one fire giant. The second giant was digging in the campground and successfully extracted a large metal band (a fragment of the Vonindod) before departing. The party chose not to engage the second giant.
- **Consequences:** One fire giant killed. One fire giant escaped with a Vonindod fragment. Urgala's inn (Northshield House), the Apothecary, and several cottages and farms were destroyed. The campground has a large excavation hole.

- Via Speak with Dead on the fire giant corpse: Duke Zalto seeks to rule all giants; the fire giant attack on Triboar was to retrieve a Vonindod fragment buried in the campground. The Vonindod is identified as an artifact associated with killing dragons.

- **Bag of salt** found on the fire giant's body.
- **Fire giant toenails** collected by Thistle (intended for potions of giant strength; Apothecary is destroyed, complicating production).

### Urgala's Quest — Giant Slayer Longsword Lead
- Urgala Meltimer directed the party to **Zymorven Hall**, northwest of Silverymoon on the Rauvin Road, where her former adventuring companion **Harthos Zymorven** resides. He possesses a **giant slayer longsword** and will part with it if approached in Urgala's name with the stated purpose of defeating giants.

### Othovir's Intelligence — Margaster Family Stash (Silverymoon)
- Othovir (harness maker) revealed the location of a Margaster family magic item stash: a three-story tower in Silverymoon with a detached carriage house (30-ft square, two floors). Access details: arcane lock on door, alarm spell on the carriage, two human guards upstairs, magic items hidden in a compartment under the passenger seat of the carriage.

### Darathra's Quest — Harper Network Contact Confirmed
- Darathra Shendrel (Lord Protector, Harper) directed the party to **Danivarr's House in Everlund** (east along the Evermoor Way). They are to present a **platinum badge with the Triboar insignia** to **Dral Thelev**, the one-eyed half-orc proprietor, to access the Harper teleportation network.

### Darz Helgar's Quest — Worvil "Weevil" Forkbeard Lead
- Darz revealed: a merchant from Mirabar spotted a dwarf matching Weevil's description cleaning stables at **Xantharl's Keep** (a fortified village on the Long Road). Weevil is wanted by the **Axe of Mirabar** for **5,000 gold**.
