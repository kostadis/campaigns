### Halisa and Helia — Exiled Dryad Standoff at Grandfather Tree
- **Outcome:** Resolved. Both dryads were freed from their self-imposed imprisonment. The party (using Orsik disguised as Prince Thornacious) convinced each dryad separately that they could leave without the other dying. In truth, Queen Titania had never placed a curse — the dryads had imprisoned themselves through mutual stubbornness and hatred. Halisa departed intending to taunt Helia from a distance every 100 years; Helia departed on his own. The elderly dryad and Sleth were relieved, and the moon festival can proceed.
