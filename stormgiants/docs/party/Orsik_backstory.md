Orsik has spent a significant portion of his life as a professional combatant. The document notes that he and his companion, Vardis, have "run out of garrisons, armies, city guards, and factions" that were willing to hire them. This implies a long history of moving from one military contract to another.

Professional Displacement

His current situation is a direct result of the recent peace. After the defeat of Tiamat at the Well of Dragons, the various factions (the Harpers, Zhentarim, Lord’s Alliance, etc.) no longer had a common enemy to fight. As these groups began to fracture and the need for large standing armies evaporated, Orsik found himself without a steady source of income.

Current Motivation: Desperation and Rumors
Orsik's backstory has reached a turning point where he can no longer find legitimate military work. This has forced him toward the more dangerous and less reputable path of adventuring. He is currently motivated by rumors of hidden Cult of the Dragon treasure—"Plan B" hoards left behind after the war—which leads him to investigate potentially dangerous locations like the magic-trap-filled caves mentioned in the chapter title.
