The Dropout of the Arcane Schools
The story suggests that Unla Kee’s training was formally rooted in the School of Divination, but his temperament was fundamentally at odds with academic life.

Institutional Rebellion: He likely found the rigid structures of wizardly colleges too slow for his high-stakes personality. His backstory implies a wizard who learned the mechanics of the "weave" only to better understand the "odds" of the universe.

The Gambler's Schooling: He specifically applies his training in the school of Divination to his life as a gambler. Rather than using magic to seek wisdom, he uses it to predict the "final turn" of a hand or a cosmic event.

The "Key" to the Universal End
His specialized training in Divination has led him to a dangerous, reckless conclusion: he believes he has decoded the ultimate end of the universe.

Cosmic Arrogance: Because of his training, he believes he is the "Key" mentioned in his own prophecies. This creates a backstory where he has likely spent years being dismissed by more conservative wizards who viewed his "The world is ending and I'm the protagonist" theories as a dangerous delusion or a side effect of too much time in casinos.

Reckless Execution: His training allows him to see glimpses of the future, which fuels his belief that he is effectively "immortal" until the scripted end occurs. This is why he is willing to walk into "magic-trap-filled caves" that would terrify a more cautious wizard—he "knows" it isn't his time yet.

Relationship to the Weave
Unlike a traditional wizard who respects the flow of magic, Unla Kee’s background suggests he treats the Schools of Magic as a deck of cards. He is particularly adept at:

Identifying "The Hand": Using his training to see through the schemes of others (like the conspiracy Thistle decodes in Chapter 84).

Predicting the Outcome: His belief that he knows how everything ends makes him a "reckless" navigator through the War of the Giants. He doesn't just study history; he feels he is the one turning the pages.

Summary of the Backstory
Unla Kee is a high-functioning arcane savant who likely left his formal order because they were too afraid to "bet it all." He wanders the North with Orsik and Vardis not for gold, but because his Divination training told him they were the specific variables needed to reach the "Final Chapter" where he plays his role as the universe's ultimate key.
