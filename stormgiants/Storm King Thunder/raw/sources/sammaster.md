# Sammaster

## Identity
- Role: Founder and original leader of the Cult of the Dragon; Chosen of Mystara
- Faction: Cult of the Dragon (founding figure)
- Has not appeared in person; referenced in dialogue and depicted in a statue within an ice cave cavern

## Personality & Motivations
- Believed dragons to be the perfect form of undead; devoted himself to devising the process for creating Dracoliches
- His tradition extended beyond the Dracolich formula — some disciples pursued other undead magical processes, suggesting broad ambition in the field of undeath research

## History with the Party
- **Referenced by Bressik:** Bressik cited Sammaster as the author of a key text on Dracoliches, claiming that Severin misread or misinterpreted Sammaster's work, which led to errors in the Tiamat summoning ritual.
- **Referenced by Thistle:** Thistle relayed background from his Uncle Joon establishing Sammaster as the originator of the Dracolich creation process and the ideological root of Sandesyl's research lineage.
- **Ice Cave Statue:** The party discovered a statue of Sammaster in an ice cave cavern. Unla Kee identified it as depicting Sammaster holding the *Tome of the Dragon* — described as the definitive text on Dracoliches — in a tableau that appears to show him learning the secrets of undeath from the dracolich Inferno. Unla Kee flagged this as odd and significant.

## Current Status
- **Last known:** No information on current status or whereabouts has been provided.
- **Active plans:** Unknown.
- **What the party knows:** Sammaster founded the Cult of the Dragon, created the Dracolich process, and is memorialized in at least one significant ritual site. The statue tableau's meaning remains uninvestigated.
- **What remains hidden:** Sammaster's current fate, his relationship to the ice cave site, and the full significance of the statue scene.

## Relationships
- **Sandesyl:** Appears to be a disciple operating within Sammaster's tradition, continuing undead research beyond the standard Dracolich formula.
- **Severin:** Indirect — allegedly misinterpreted Sammaster's work, with significant consequences (per Bressik).
- **Bressik:** Familiar with Sammaster's text; treats it as authoritative.
- **Inferno (Dracolich):** Depicted in the statue as Sammaster's teacher in the secrets of undeath — relationship and current status of Inferno unknown.
- **Cult of the Dragon:** Its founding ideological and organizational leader.
