# Inferno

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** The Hellbound Dracolich; reputed to be the first of the Dracoliches
- **Faction:** Unknown — made a forbidden pact with a demon lord
- **First Appearance:** Session unknown — encountered only as a large statue in an ice cave cavern; Inferno did not appear in person

## Personality & Motivations
- Sought immortality above all else, pursuing it through a forbidden demonic pact rather than conventional means
- No direct personality demonstrated at the table; known only through reputation and iconography

## History with the Party
- The party discovered a statue of Inferno in an ice cave cavern. Vaardis identified the figure as a once-powerful red dragon who bargained with a demon lord for immortality, becoming the first Dracolich.
- The statue depicted Inferno teaching Sammaster the secrets of undeath — identified by Unla Kee as the scene being represented.
- Unla Kee removed gem eyes set into the statue, triggering the activation of animated statues in the cavern.

## Current Status
- **Last Known:** Exists only as a statue in the ice cave; current whereabouts and whether Inferno remains active are unknown
- **Hidden from Party:** Whether Inferno is still alive, destroyed, or active in the world remains entirely unknown

## Relationships
- **Sammaster:** Inferno is depicted as Sammaster's teacher in the secrets of undeath — a foundational relationship in Dracolich history
- **Demon Lord (unnamed):** Bound by pact; the source of Inferno's immortality and the origin of the "Hellbound" title
