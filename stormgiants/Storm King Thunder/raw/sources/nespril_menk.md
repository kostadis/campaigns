# Nespril Menk

## Identity
- **Role:** Apprentice to Krowen Valharrow; stationed at Moongleam Tower, Everlund
- **Faction:** Harpers (by association/apprenticeship)
- **First Appearance:** Greeted the party by name upon their arrival through the Moongleam Tower teleportation circle; immediately took charge of their discreet exit

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Drive:** Genuine ideological commitment to the Harpers — believes membership should mean true belief, not mercenary arrangement
- **Philosophical Position:** Opposes Krowen's "Ezra model" of outsourcing Harper security work to independent contractors; shares this view with Gauche but expresses it more openly and emotionally
- Competent and detail-oriented — he noticed and corrected Krowen's failure to brief the party on the sewer exit protocol before they blundered into Zhentarim surveillance
- Speaks with polish and sophistication; his emotional outburst at Orsik's probing stood out as a moment of unguarded conviction

## History with the Party
1. **First meeting (Moongleam Tower arrival):** Greeted the 3HPP by name, managed their exit through the secret sewer passage, and flagged that Krowen had neglected to brief them on it
2. **Confrontation with Orsik:** When Orsik pressed him about the tower's randomized security system and whether it was documented in a book, Nespril warned that Krowen would be furious if that book went missing or if Orsik were implicated. When Orsik pushed further — framing it as a pitch for independent security contractors — Nespril erupted, rejecting the mercenary model outright

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Moongleam Tower, Everlund
- **Active Role:** Day-to-day operations and security management for the tower under Krowen's supervision
- **What the Party Knows:** He knows the tower's randomized security system; he is aware of and protective of the book documenting it; he is ideologically opposed to the contractor model the party represents
- **Hidden from the Party:** Full extent of his responsibilities; depth of his relationship with Krowen; whether his opposition to the Ezra model could ever become a point of leverage or alliance

## Relationships
- **Krowen Valharrow:** Master/apprentice — loyal but not uncritical; will protect Krowen's interests (and the book) even when Krowen is disorganized
- **Gauche:** Shares philosophical alignment against the outsourcing model; Nespril's position is more emotionally charged
- **Orsik (3HPP):** Tense; Nespril read Orsik's questions as a threat to tower security and reacted with open hostility to the contractor pitch
- **3HPP broadly:** Professionally managed their arrival but views them with ideological wariness
