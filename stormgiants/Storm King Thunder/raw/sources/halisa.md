# Halisa

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Seelie Dryad; former courtier of the Fey Court; exiled guardian of the Grandfather Tree
- **Faction:** Formerly Seelie Court under Queen Titania; bound under "Tree Protection Schedule D"
- **First Appearance:** Referenced before being encountered directly; met in person by Thistle when he approached the Grandfather Tree

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Initially, to fulfill the terms of her punishment and avoid further consequences from Titania or Prince Thornacious. After her release, her stated goal is to return every 100 years to taunt Helia.
- Halisa is bitter, emotionally wounded, and prone to fixating on personal grievance — her first act of freedom was not joy but a parting shot at her ex. She is rule-conscious under duress (citing the exact terms of Schedule D to rebuff the party) but clearly chafes under her exile. Her honesty, the very trait that caused her exile, is still very much intact.

## History with the Party
1. **Pre-encounter reference:** Brazzik warned the party that killing either Halisa or Helia carries powerful and dangerous consequences.
2. **First meeting (Thistle):** Halisa introduced herself to Thistle at the southernmost tree under the Grandfather Tree's canopy. She explained her exile — she had offered an honest opinion about Queen Titania's dress at a ball — and cited "Tree Protection Schedule D" to refuse his request to search for the artifact. She became visibly terrified when she believed Prince Thornacious had arrived, demanding the party leave immediately.
3. **Release:** Orsik, posing as Thornacious, returned and declared her free to leave. Rather than relief, she expressed bitterness toward Helia — he had publicly broken up with her at the exact moment Titania was about to exile her, robbing them of what she believed could have been a happy life together. She declared she would return every 100 years to taunt him, then departed without reconciliation.
4. **Aftermath:** Her departure (alongside Helia's) broke the apparent stalemate at the Grandfather Tree. Sleth later revealed there was never a true curse — the two dryads had effectively cursed each other through their mutual refusal to sacrifice themselves.
5. **Sprite emissary reference:** The Seelie Sprite emissary later noted that both Halisa and Helia had been freed through the party's trickery.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Departed the Grandfather Tree after being "released" by Orsik-as-Thornacious; whereabouts otherwise unknown.
- **Active Plans:** Has declared intent to return every 100 years to taunt Helia. No other known operations.
- **What the Party Knows:** That she is free, bitter, and gone. That the stalemate at the tree was resolved by her leaving.
- **What Remains Hidden:** Her current location; whether she is aware the "Prince Thornacious" who freed her was an imposter; how the Seelie Court views her now that she has departed her post.

## Relationships
- **Helia:** Former romantic partner and fellow exiled guardian. Their relationship is defined by mutual resentment — Helia broke up with her publicly at her lowest moment. Halisa left without reconciliation and intends to antagonize him periodically for the next several centuries.
- **Queen Titania:** Exiled Halisa for speaking honestly about her dress. Relationship is one of punishment and enforced servitude.
- **Prince Thornacious:** Halisa was visibly terrified of him. His declared word was apparently sufficient to free her from her binding.
- **The Party (Thistle):** Politely but firmly refused to help him, citing the terms of her punishment.
- **The Party (Orsik):** Accepted his impersonation of Thornacious at face value; was freed as a result. Does not appear to know she was deceived.
- **Seelie Court (Sprite Emissary):** The Court is aware she was freed through the party's trickery, framing her release as something done *to* her rather than *for* her.

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease (implied):** Departed without gratitude or reconciliation; her freedom did not produce goodwill toward the party — she left consumed by her grievance with Helia.
