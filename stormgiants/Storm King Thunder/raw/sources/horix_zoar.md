# Horix Zoar

## Identity
- **Role:** High Captain of Everlund — supreme commander of Everlund's army
- **Faction:** Everlund city authority / military leadership (formerly Lord's Alliance-aligned; now departed from that body)
- **Family:** Brother of Tamalin Zoar (mayor of Calling Horns)
- **First Appearance:** First referenced via Dral Thelev's account; first met in person at Danivarr's House after being summoned by Dral Thelev — initially dismissive of the party as "smelly adventurers"

*Also known as: Hirax Zoar, Captain Zoar, High Captain Zoar.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Maintaining Everlund's prestige and independence; advancing his own standing; seeing Silverymoon embarrassed or diminished.
- Proud, politically shrewd, and holds a deep, bitter grudge against High Marshal Methrammar Aerasumé of Silverymoon over the abandonment of allied forces (notably Sundbar) during the orc horde attacks — a betrayal that led him to personally dissolve the Everlund-Silverymoon compact.
- Privately wants to raze Silverymoon, but publicly maintains a "let bygones be bygones" posture, suggesting he is politically disciplined and capable of suppressing personal vendettas when necessary.
- Warms quickly when respect is demonstrated or mutual benefit is clear; generous and enthusiastic once onside.
- Publicly loyal to Speaker Vatrice Stormwright, though his remarks about her re-election being "years away with much that could happen" suggest he is watching the political horizon.

## History with the Party

1. **Pre-meeting briefing:** Dral Thelev briefed the party on Horix's background and political position, establishing him as a potential resource — he holds the authority to credential the party (3HPP) as official Everlund diplomats, which would grant them protection from arrest by Silverymoon's local authorities.

2. **Summoned to Danivarr's House:** Arrived with three guards, initially cold toward the party.

3. **Tamalin's letter:** Thistle presented a letter of introduction from his sister **Tamalin Zoar** (mayor of Calling Horns) — his demeanor shifted immediately to warmth and pride.

4. **Demon-trafficking briefing:** The party described a criminal organization trafficking demons through Silverymoon; Zoar responded with enthusiasm and a bitter political monologue, framing the news as confirmation of Silverymoon's moral corruption.

5. **Letter of mark:** Issued the party a letter of mark naming them as his agents, granting them assistance and grace from Silverymoon authorities — kept the party name **3HPP** deliberately, as associating Everlund with the popular theatrical production embarrasses Silverymoon.

6. **Bob Saget production:** Learned the party are silent backers of the Bob Saget theatrical production; delighted, plans to take his children to see it.

7. **Political probing:** Thistle floated the idea of Zoar seeking political office; he deflected, expressing satisfaction as High Captain and support for Stormwright — while noting the giant threat victory would boost his prestige.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Returned to his duties in Everlund
- **Condition at Departure:** Fed, slightly inebriated, and in high spirits
- **Active Interests:** Watching the party's progress against the giant threat; anticipates a prestige boost if they succeed publicly
- **What the Party Knows:** He is politically ambitious in a cautious way, despises Silverymoon, holds the authority to credential them as Everlund diplomats, and is now a genuine ally and cultivated political patron.
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full extent of his political ambitions and his relationship with Speaker Stormwright beyond surface loyalty; his current military priorities; the full circumstances of the alleged Silverymoon betrayal from his perspective.

## Relationships
- **Tamalin Zoar (sister):** Mayor of Calling Horns; described with deep pride as having transformed from "cutthroat and brigand to person of character and substance" — his affection for her was the key that unlocked his cooperation.
- **Speaker Vatrice Stormwright:** Publicly his superior and loyally supported; privately, Zoar appears to be positioning himself.
- **High Marshal Methrammar Aerasumé (Silverymoon):** Bitter enemy; holds him personally responsible for a betrayal that broke the alliance.
- **Dral Thelev:** Summoned Zoar on the party's behalf; existing connection sufficient to get him to the table.
- **Unla Kee:** Referenced him as a cultivated ally, suggesting party-wide awareness of the relationship.
- **The Party (3HPP):** Now formal allies under letter of mark; Zoar is invested in their success for his own prestige.
