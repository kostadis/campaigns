# Uzorel "The Viper" Hornblade

## Identity
- **Role:** Local Zhentarim agent, Silverymoon
- **Faction:** Zhentarim
- **Notable:** Distant relative of Taern Hornblade, former ruler of Silverymoon
- **First Appearance:** Silverymoon; initially reluctant to meet Orsik, agreed after hearing of his reputation from Parnast and Triboar

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Advancing Zhentarim interests in Silverymoon; gathering actionable intelligence
- Diminutive and physically unimposing — compensates with tact, charm, and careful social maneuvering
- Deeply paranoid and suspicious; visibly tense when Orsik offered information without requesting payment, and reached for his sword at the word "friends" before being calmed
- Responds well to clearly stated motivations — Orsik's stated desire for revenge against a Harper visibly settled his nerves

## History with the Party

**Session (Silverymoon):** Orsik sought out Uzorel and disclosed the locations of the teleportation circles in Everlund and Yartar, offering the intelligence freely with no payment requested. Uzorel accepted the information and promised to ensure Zhentarim leadership would be made aware of Orsik's gesture.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Silverymoon
- **Active Plans:** Relaying Orsik's intelligence on the teleportation circles in Everlund and Yartar up the Zhentarim chain of command
- **What the Party Knows:** Uzorel accepted the betrayal and made a promise of recognition with Zhentarim leadership
- **Hidden:** Whether Uzorel personally trusts Orsik, how quickly the intelligence will move, and what the Zhentarim intend to do with it

## Relationships
- **Orsik (Party):** Cautious, transactional regard; views Orsik as a potential asset motivated by anti-Harper revenge; no personal trust established yet
- **Taern Hornblade:** Distant familial connection — significance to Uzorel's position or loyalties unknown
- **Zhentarim Leadership:** Acts as a local intermediary; defers intelligence upward rather than acting independently
