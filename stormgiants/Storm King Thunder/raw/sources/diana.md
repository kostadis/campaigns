# Diana

## Identity
- Commanding officer of a Lord's Alliance strike team
- Faction: Lords' Alliance
- First appearance: aboard Zephyros's flying castle, when she removed her helm to identify herself after Clarion relayed the party's claim of working for the SEER

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goal:** Destroy threats to the Lords' Alliance — in this instance, Zephyros's orb
- Practical and mission-focused; reassesses quickly when new intelligence is presented rather than pressing a failed objective
- Responds well to verifiable information (lack of rock-dropping, absence of threatening speeches) and shows dry humor when the party withheld useful intel about the Cult of the Dragon

## History with the Party
1. **Strike team arrival:** Diana's team moved on Zephyros's castle with orders to destroy his orb. Clarion relayed the party's claim of working for the SEER, prompting Diana to remove her helm and engage directly.
2. **De-escalation:** Diana accepted Orsik's testimony that Zephyros had not dropped rocks on settlements or made speeches about cloud giant dominion, using these as verification that Zephyros may be benign. She acknowledged the team had interfered with the wrong target.
3. **Cult of the Dragon intel:** When the party disclosed that cultists had visited the castle seeking Zephyros as an ally, Diana laughed and said they should have led with that. She confirmed her team had previously fought the Cult of the Dragon to a standstill.
4. **Departure:** Concluded the mission a failure and withdrew with her team.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Departed Zephyros's flying castle; whereabouts unknown
- **Active operations:** None confirmed — strike mission officially called off
- **What the party knows:** Diana is a Lords' Alliance officer, competent and pragmatic, willing to abort a mission on credible testimony
- **What remains hidden:** Her broader orders, base of operations, and what she will report back to the Lords' Alliance about Zephyros and the Cult of the Dragon

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Tense first contact that ended in mutual de-escalation; no established rapport, but no hostility
- **Clarion:** Served as the initial communication intermediary between Diana and the party
- **Orsik:** His direct testimony was the deciding factor in Diana standing down
- **Thistle:** Asked Diana about Adimond Kroskas by name
- **Adimond Kroskas:** Diana has no personal knowledge of this individual, though her team has clashed with the Cult of the Dragon
- **Zephyros:** Target of her mission; departed without harming him after reassessment
- **Cult of the Dragon:** Her team has fought them to a standstill at some prior point
