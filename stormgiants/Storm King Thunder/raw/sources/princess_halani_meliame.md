# Princess Halani Meliame

## Identity
- **Role:** Elven princess of the Moonwood
- **Faction:** Moonwood (elven court); possible undisclosed affiliation
- **First Appearance:** Found captive in a Yakfolk tent alongside companions Jevin and Vandar; introduced herself after the party defeated the Yakfolk and offered a reward for safe return to the Moonwood

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Return safely to the Moonwood with her companions; advance the interests of her people and whatever organization she may be affiliated with
- Politically sharp and deeply resentful of Silverymoon, which she holds responsible for abandoning the Silver Marches
- Guarded — she caught herself making a revealing statement about a "new organization" rising to protect people and immediately walked it back, suggesting discipline and awareness of what she reveals
- Appears genuinely angry rather than coldly calculating when discussing Silverymoon, though the two are not mutually exclusive

## History with the Party
1. **Rescue:** Found imprisoned in a Yakfolk tent with Jevin and Vandar. Once the Yakfolk were defeated, she identified herself and offered a reward for escort to the Moonwood.
2. **Interrogation by Thistle:** Explained that Yakfolk used possession rituals to subvert her guards and capture her. Was escorted up to the flying castle with instructions to remain in sunlit areas.
3. **Intelligence Provided:** Voluntarily shared that a doorway to the north leads to an elevator descending into Ironslag. Also disclosed that Yakfolk-possessed dwarves infiltrated Citadel Adbar and assassinated the dwarven king.
4. **Political Conversation:** When pressed by Thistle on Silverymoon, expressed bitter condemnation of the city's abandonment of the Silver Marches. Made a partial disclosure about a "new organization" rising to protect the world, then retracted it, attributing her words to emotional distress from imprisonment. Speculated — angrily, not definitively — that Silverymoon agents may have been complicit in the Yakfolk's plots.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Aboard the 3HPP's flying castle, instructed to stay in brightly lit areas
- **Active Situation:** Awaiting transport back to the Moonwood with companions Jevin and Vandar
- **What the Party Knows:** She is a Moonwood princess, knowledgeable about Yakfolk operations, and deeply hostile toward Silverymoon
- **What Remains Hidden:** The nature of the "new organization" she referenced and her potential role in it; the full extent of her political agenda; whether her speculation about Silverymoon complicity is informed intelligence or emotional conjecture

## Relationships
- **Jevin & Vandar:** Fellow captives and companions; traveling with her
- **Silverymoon:** Openly hostile; views the city as a betrayer of the Silver Marches
- **Citadel Adbar:** Aware of its compromised leadership via Yakfolk possession; her level of ongoing contact is unknown
- **Thistle (party):** Has spoken with her directly; revealed the most to Thistle of any party member
- **The 3HPP (party):** Currently reliant on them for safe passage; has provided intelligence as a gesture of goodwill and to establish trust

## Arc Score Events
- **Positive:** Rescued from Yakfolk captivity by the party; cooperative and forthcoming with intelligence
- **Tension Point:** Partial disclosure of a secretive political organization, then retraction — raises questions about her full honesty with the party
