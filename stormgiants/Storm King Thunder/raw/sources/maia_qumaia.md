# Maia (Qumaia)

## Identity
- **Role:** Barhop at the Wink and Kiss tavern, Yartar
- **Faction:** None noted; loosely aligned with Eltio's information network by association
- **First Appearance:** The party encountered her at the Wink and Kiss; she recognized them from their reputation after defeating the giant in Triboar

## Personality & Motivations
- **Goals:** Earning income, making useful connections, keeping herself in good standing with the people she vouches for
- Forthright, businesslike, and entrepreneurial — she spotted an opportunity in the party's reputation and moved quickly to capitalize on it
- Savvy enough to broker introductions for coin, but underestimated the expectations of those she vouches to

## History with the Party
1. **First meeting:** Recognized the party as the group that downed the Triboar giant; had been advertising them to other patrons as "the three half-pints and a pint"
2. **Work referral:** Noticing the party was broke, she pointed them toward a bounty hunting opportunity
3. **Information broker:** For five gold, directed the party to Eltio at the bar as the person to speak with about Harthal
4. **Reprimand:** Eltio dressed her down for the introduction after the party didn't know who Tamlin Zoar was; Maia attempted to defend herself before being dismissed with a warning

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The Wink and Kiss tavern, Yartar
- **Situation:** Still employed at the tavern; her standing with Eltio has taken a hit following the failed introduction
- **What the party knows:** She is a social connector willing to trade information and introductions for coin
- **What remains hidden:** The extent of her relationship with Eltio and whether she has other useful connections

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Friendly and commercially minded; views them as a useful commodity due to their reputation, though her vouching for them has caused her trouble
- **Eltio:** Works within his orbit at the Wink and Kiss; was reprimanded by him and is currently in his bad graces
