# Fiora Bendaega

## Identity
- **Role:** Middleperson / adventure broker — connects merchants with suitable adventurers for hire
- **Faction:** Independent; works with merchant clients on a fee basis
- **First Appearance:** Recruited the party at a public meeting place (likely a tavern) for a black road escort/protection job involving honest tradespeople

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Profit and professional reputation; places adventurers efficiently and collects her fee with minimal friction
- Calculating and composed — she reads a room well and knew the party would accept before they agreed
- Attempts to build rapport through empathy but pivots smoothly when it backfires (see: Thistle)
- Uses her guard Diego as a social tool as much as a physical one, deploying him to de-escalate while reasserting her authority

## History with the Party
1. **First Meeting:** Approached the party with a letter of introduction for a merchant protection job on the black road. Explained her business model — merchants pay her to distribute letters to suitable candidates.
2. **Tension with Thistle:** Tried to bond over the Cult of the Dragon being "terrible for business." Thistle's revenge-driven response did not land well; the empathy play failed.
3. **Confrontation with Orsik:** When Orsik approached threateningly and asked how to take her job, she summoned Diego to the table — framed warmly as an introduction, functionally as a warning.
4. **Post-Meeting:** Informed the party they were no longer welcome at her current location and redirected them to **Nonna's place** for any future contact.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Operating out of **Nonna's place** (current location for future meetings)
- **Active:** Job placed successfully; merchant arrives at destination in ~three weeks, journey takes two weeks — party has roughly one month
- **What the Party Knows:** She is a broker, not a stakeholder. She has at least one guard (Diego). She anticipated their acceptance.
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full extent of her network, her other clients, and the nature of her relationship with Nonna's place

## Relationships
- **Diego (Guard / paramour):** Loyal and affectionate relationship; she describes him as "so fond of me." Uses him both for protection and social leverage. Diego is described elsewhere as her paramour.
- **Chandra Stol:** Knows of her; identified Fiora's role in the idol retrieval scheme (the mercenary recruitment job to retrieve an idol from Azam in Vuerthyl).
- **Azam (Vuerthyl):** Connected through the mercenary/idol operation, nature of relationship unclear.
- **The Party:** Professional and transactional. Redirected them away from her primary location after the meeting — suggests she keeps clients at arm's length
- **Thistle (specifically):** Failed rapport attempt; Thistle's desire for revenge did not align with Fiora's "it's just business" framing — mild tension
- **Orsik (specifically):** Perceived as a physical threat; responded with controlled escalation via Diego
