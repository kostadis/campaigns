# Clarion

## Identity
- Silver dragon; field asset and transport for a Lord's Alliance strike team under Commander Diana's command
- First appeared landing on the platform of Zephyros's cloud castle, carrying shield dwarves via harness; the party encountered the team mid-mission

## Personality & Motivations
- Follows orders from Diana without question, but is quick to correct anyone who assumes the dragon holds command authority — calls it a "common misconception"
- Initially aggressive and territorial, threatening to fight and eat the party on sight; shifts pragmatically once the situation is clarified
- Businesslike and mission-focused; contributes when useful but stays in a support role

## History with the Party
1. Landed on Zephyros's cloud castle platform with Diana's strike team; immediately threatened the party as presumed enemies before Thistle clarified their status as passengers
2. Informed the party to stand aside while the team destroyed the flight orb, citing intelligence about a cloud giant dropping rocks on northern settlements
3. Deferred to Diana when the party invoked the SEER, redirecting command decisions upward appropriately
4. Examined Orsik's map and offered geographic information — initially said northwest, then self-corrected to northeast
5. Noted no personal familiarity with Adimond Kroskas, citing limited involvement in efforts against the cult
6. Departed with Diana's team after Diana determined they had targeted the wrong castle

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Departed Zephyros's cloud castle with Diana and the strike team
- **Active plans:** Unknown; presumably continuing Lord's Alliance operations under Diana's direction
- **What the party knows:** Clarion is a Lord's Alliance asset, subordinate to Diana, with some geographic knowledge relevant to the party's map
- **What remains hidden:** The full scope of Clarion's assignments or what intelligence the strike team is acting on going forward

## Relationships
- **Diana:** Direct commanding officer; Clarion defers to her on all decisions without hesitation
- **The Party:** Neutral to cautious — initial hostility gave way to professional cooperation; no lasting bond formed
- **Adimond Kroskas / the cult:** Little to no personal involvement; unfamiliar with Kroskas specifically
