# Leila

## Identity
- Role: Rogue
- Faction: Briefly attempted to join the Hand of Yartar; departed before the party's arrival
- First appearance: Has not appeared in person; introduced through Harthal Zymorven's account during party conversation

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Locating Thistle, whom she considers the absolute love of her life
- Driven enough to manipulate others to pursue her objectives — she convinced Harthal to steal the Giant Slayer sword and go adventuring, setting the chain of events that led to his imprisonment
- Abandoned Harthal after he killed someone during their travels, suggesting she has moral limits, or at minimum found the situation incompatible with her priorities

## History with the Party
- Never met directly; all information comes from Harthal Zymorven's account
- Harthal revealed she persuaded him to steal a sword and go adventuring, which resulted in his imprisonment; he feels used by her
- After Harthal committed a killing, she left him, stating she needed to find Thistle
- She traveled to Yartar and attempted to join the Hand of Yartar to aid her search, but found the organization too locally focused and departed to seek other means
- Thistle learned she considers him "the absolute love of her life" and responded with hope

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Yartar — but she left before the party arrived; current whereabouts unknown
- **Active plans:** Actively searching for Thistle through unknown means
- **Hidden from the party:** Where she went after leaving Yartar and what her current method of searching looks like

## Relationships
- **Thistle:** Considers him the absolute love of her life; he does not yet know her full feelings firsthand but now knows of them and reacted with hope
- **Harthal Zymorven:** Former traveling companion; she convinced him into criminal activity and abandoned him after he crossed a moral line — he feels used by her
- **Hand of Yartar:** Brief, failed attempt at affiliation; found them too parochial for her needs
