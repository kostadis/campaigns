# Thuul

## Identity
- **Role:** Guard drake companion and scouting partner to Thistle; member of the 3HPP party group
- **Faction:** Travels with the party; previously captured alongside Thistle by a cult before escaping when the cult disintegrated
- **First Appearance:** Present during the battle with the dragon, acting under Thistle's command

## Personality & Motivations
- Thuul does not speak independently in recorded sessions, but his relationship with Thistle is characterized as a genuine partnership — described by Thistle as "anarcho-socialist," with the two taking turns being in charge.
- Bressik regards the Thistle–Thuul bond as something meaningfully different from a conventional ranger-animal dynamic.
- When Thistle falls unconscious, Thuul is observed trying to figure out what to do on his own, suggesting some degree of independent agency.

## History with the Party

**Early Encounters**
- Fought alongside Thistle during the opening dragon battle, advancing on the dragon and spitting acid on Thistle's command.
- Observed by Vardis during one battle breathing "death and destruction," astonishing him. Vardis noted that Thuul requires Thistle to be conscious to act effectively; after Thistle fell unconscious, Thuul was directionless until Vardis pointed him toward Thistle.

**Raven's Rock**
- Present with Thistle as part of a scouting pair. Bressik spoke with Thistle about their bond during this period.

**Crab Fight**
- Spat acid at a crab while it was distracted by Orsik and Unla Kee, acting at Thistle's signal.

**Glazhael's Tower**
- Affected by Glazhael's dragon fear alongside Thistle. No further actions recorded.

**Cavern Battle**
- Engaged a guard drake separately from the main party in the deep caverns. Was bitten but shrugged off the damage and killed the drake with a bite of his own. Survived the engagement.

**Battle vs. Bob (the Demon)**
- Played a critical role in Bob's defeat: rushed forward and stuffed the second bag of holding into the first bag (tossed by Unla Kee near Bob), triggering an extradimensional explosion. Was caught in the resulting blast along with Bob and the Red Wizard. **Fate after the explosion not confirmed.**

**Yartar Sewer Combat & Drylund Concealment (rendered as "Thule"):**
- Rushed through a door being held shut by a Kuo-Toa, creating an opening in the fight. The Kuo-Toa attempted to strike him as he passed but missed; Thule retaliated with his breath weapon.
- At Thistle's direction, Thule picked up Lord Drylund's corpse in his jaws, moved it to a side room, and allowed the door to be locked to hide the body.

**Ruined Courtyard Battle**
- Killed by the stinger of a blue dragon–purple worm hybrid. Subsequently revived by Iymrith's cultists when they arrived and healed the fallen party members.

**Captivity Reference**
- Thistle revealed in a later session that he and Thuul were captured together by the cult and escaped together when the cult fell apart.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** With the party following the ruined courtyard battle
- **Condition:** Revived after being killed by the hybrid creature's stinger
- **Active Plans:** None independently recorded; operates in support of Thistle
- **Hidden Information:** The full nature of Thuul's bond to Thistle ("the fairy's creature") is not fully explained — Vardis's phrasing implies something beyond a standard animal companion relationship, but details are unconfirmed

## Relationships
- **Thistle:** Primary bond; described as a partnership of equals. Thuul is functionally dependent on Thistle's consciousness to act in battle, but the relationship is framed as mutual rather than hierarchical.
- **Vardis:** Observed Thuul with fascination and awe during battle; attempted to direct Thuul after Thistle fell. Refers to Thuul as "the fairy's creature."
- **Bressik:** Found the Thistle–Thuul dynamic fascinating; spoke with Thistle about it at Raven's Rock.
- **The Party (3HPP):** Considered a party member; fought alongside them across multiple engagements.
