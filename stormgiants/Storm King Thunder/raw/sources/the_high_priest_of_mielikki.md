# High Priest of Mielikki (Name Unknown)

## Identity
- **Role:** High Priest of Mielikki's grove, Silverymoon
- **Faction:** Church of Mielikki
- **First Appearance:** The party brought the body of Unla Kee to her at Mielikki's grove in Silverymoon.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Goals:** Serving her faith and protecting the innocent; appears motivated to see justice done regarding demonic threats.
- Reacted with genuine shock to news of a demon killing Unla Kee, suggesting she does not take such threats lightly. She acted decisively — performing a resurrection and then immediately seeking to involve civic authority.

## History with the Party
- Received Unla Kee's body at the grove after his death at the hands of a demon.
- Took the body into the inner sanctum and successfully revived Unla Kee, accepting one of his magical rings as payment.
- Corroborated the party's account of the demon attack to the Captain of the Silverwatch and urged him to investigate.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Mielikki's grove, Silverymoon.
- **Active:** No known ongoing operations beyond her duties at the grove.
- **Party Knows:** Her role, her ability to perform resurrection, and that she will lend credibility to their testimony with city authorities.
- **Hidden:** Her name, her full capabilities, and any deeper agenda remain unknown.

## Relationships
- **Unla Kee:** Revived him; transactional but apparently compassionate.
- **Captain of the Silverwatch:** Enough standing to be taken seriously when vouching for the party's demon account.
- **The Party:** Cooperative and credible ally, though no personal bond has been established.
