# Tak

## Identity
- **Role:** Ogre survivor; one of two remaining inhabitants of the cloud castle
- **Faction:** None formal; allied with Worug
- **First Appearance:** The party found Tak and Worug barricaded in their tower on the cloud castle. Tak is identifiable by a bandage on his right hand.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Drive:** Survival — he and Worug stayed alive by refusing to invite the vampire Sandesyl into their tower.
- Slightly more reticent than Worug, but willing to negotiate when necessary.
- Harbors unspoken feelings for Worug, inadvertently confirmed during a throwaway remark by Vardis that flustered him into asking Worug why he "never said anything." The moment was left unresolved.

## History with the Party
1. **First Contact:** Party discovered Tak and Worug weakened and hungry in their barricaded tower. Thistle provided goodberries, which Tak consumed.
2. **Negotiation:** Tak and Worug negotiated with the party, with Tak formally promising evacuation from the castle in exchange for information about Glazhael.
3. **Vardis's Comment:** Vardis jokingly suggested the ogres' secret was romantic in nature. Tak overcorrected, coughed suspiciously, then turned and asked Worug why he never said anything — inadvertently confirming the implication.
4. **Departure:** After Worug delivered the Glazhael information, Tak put his arm around Worug as they left the party's company.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Their tower on the cloud castle, alongside Worug.
- **Active Plans:** Committed to evacuating the party from the castle per their deal.
- **What the Party Knows:** Tak and Worug survived by refusing entry to Sandesyl; Tak has made a binding promise of evacuation.
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full nature of Tak's feelings for Worug; whether those feelings are mutual or will be addressed.

## Relationships
- **Worug:** Closest companion and fellow survivor. Tak's feelings for him were partially exposed in front of the party; the two left together with Tak's arm around him.
- **The Party:** Cautious but cooperative; bound by a formal promise to evacuate them.
- **Vardis:** Tak was visibly flustered by Vardis's joke, making their dynamic potentially awkward in future interactions.
- **Sandesyl (vampire):** Antagonist by proxy — Tak's survival depended on refusing her entry.
