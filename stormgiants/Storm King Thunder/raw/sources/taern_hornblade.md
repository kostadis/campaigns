# Taern Hornblade

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** High Mage; hero of the War of the Dragons; current advisor to Lord Aerasumé
- **Faction:** Silverymoon (advisory capacity)
- **First Appearance:** Met during a formal meeting with Lord Aerasumé; Thistle recognized him from stories tied to the destruction of Tiamat's temple

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Protecting Silverymoon's interests; maintaining caution and control over unknown actors
- Skeptical, cautious, and politically sharp — he advocates for measured responses and dislikes being outmaneuvered. Reputation as a "pompous blowhard" tracks with his confident, pointed delivery (quoting wizard proverbs in pointed contexts). Beneath the bluster, he appears to respect competence when he sees it.

## History with the Party
1. **First Meeting (Lord Aerasumé's council):** Taern was openly skeptical of the party's account and motives. He pushed for cordoning the theater district, volunteered himself as an observer, and recommended four battle mages instead of the five Aerasumé offered (he was overruled). He also made an unprompted call for an investigation into House Margaster.
2. **Post-Prisoner Release:** After the operation succeeded, Taern quietly told Thistle *"Well played fairy"* — a private, grudging acknowledgment of the party's competence.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Silverymoon, in Lord Aerasumé's advisory circle
- **Active Plans:** Advocating for an investigation into House Margaster; maintaining a watchful eye on the party's activities
- **What the Party Knows:** He distrusted them from the start but showed at least private respect after the prisoner release. He dislikes House Margaster.
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full extent of his influence over Aerasumé's decisions; his personal history from the War of the Dragons beyond his presence at the final battle

## Relationships
- **Lord Aerasumé:** Close advisor; willing to push back on Aerasumé's decisions (e.g., the battle mage count) but ultimately defers
- **House Margaster:** Open hostility — called for an investigation without prompting
- **Thistle (party):** Tense but evolving; Thistle knows his reputation as a blowhard, and he singled her out for his post-mission comment
- **The Party (3HPP generally):** Started at skepticism/suspicion; moved slightly toward grudging respect after the prisoner release

## Arc Score Events
- **Increase (minor):** Whispered acknowledgment to Thistle after the successful prisoner release — indicates a shift from open skepticism toward cautious respect
