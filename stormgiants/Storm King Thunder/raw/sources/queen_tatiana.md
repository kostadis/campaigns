# Queen Tatiana

## Identity
- Role: Queen of the Seelie Court
- First appearance: Referenced in session notes but has not appeared in person; first mentioned by Sir Oblivillish

## Personality & Motivations
- Insufficient information to determine core goals or personality traits beyond her authority over the Seelie Court.

## History with the Party
- **Early reference:** Sir Oblivillish identified Queen Tatiana as the authority who sent Prince Thornacious into exile and required his court to follow.
- **Later reference:** Prince Thornacious reported that Queen Tatiana has been informed of Thistle and is pleased by what she has heard.

## Current Status
- Location unknown; has not appeared in person.
- Appears to be monitoring events from a distance, at least regarding Prince Thornacious and his interactions with the party.
- **What the party knows:** She rules the Seelie Court, she exiled Prince Thornacious and his court, and she is favorably disposed toward Thistle.
- **What remains hidden:** Her motivations, her reasons for the exile, and her broader plans.

## Relationships
- **Prince Thornacious:** She exiled him and his court; he reports to her or at minimum keeps her informed.
- **Thistle (party member):** Has heard of Thistle and is reportedly pleased.
- **Sir Oblivillish:** Serves under her authority.
