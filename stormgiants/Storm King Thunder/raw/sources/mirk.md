# Mirk

## Identity
- **Role:** Goblin boss; one of the goblin leaders alongside Tlega
- **Faction:** Goblin tribe (previously resistant to 3HPP alliance)
- **First Appearance:** Diplomatic meeting where Tlega announced the alliance with the 3HPP

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Upholding traditional goblin social structure — the Bully/Bullied hierarchy is sacred to him, and he resists anything he sees as ideologically compromising it.
- Mirk is ideologically stubborn and capable of deflating group morale with his arguments. However, he is also susceptible to distraction and can be won over — at least temporarily — by the right stimulus.
- In combat, he leads through performative bravado rather than sustained engagement, heaping praise on others until his own authority is challenged, then acting decisively and dramatically to reassert himself.

## History with the Party

**Session 1 — The Alliance Debate**
Mirk openly opposed Tlega's decision to ally with the 3HPP, arguing that Maegurn's transgression was merely crossing an imaginary line, not a violation of goblin principle. His objections visibly deflated Tlega and the other goblins. Orsik considered intimidating him, but Unla Kee intervened by deploying Jim's Glowing Coin, distracting both Mirk and Tlega long enough to reach a sincere — if short-lived — agreement that eliminating Maegurn was the right move.

**Session 2 — Maergun's Throne Room**
Mirk accompanied the 3HPP into Maergun the Fomorian's throne room as one of the goblin leaders. During the battle he stood back, lavishing praise on the goblin archers rather than fighting. This bred dissent among the ranks. When the grumbling threatened to become disruptive, Mirk threw a tantrum, seized his javelin, hurled it at Maergun, and scored a hit — shouting "Satisfied!" to the crowd's cheers. After Maergun fell, Mirk cried "Well done!" as the goblins launched into their victory chant.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Maergun's throne room, immediately after the Fomorian's death
- **Current Activity:** Celebrating the goblin victory alongside Tlega and the tribe
- **Active Plans:** Unknown; the ideological tension that prompted his original objection has not been fully resolved — the mission merely gave him something to rally around
- **Hidden:** Whether Mirk's buy-in to the 3HPP alliance is genuine or purely situational remains unclear

## Relationships
- **Tlega:** Fellow goblin boss; ideological rival within the tribe's leadership. Mirk's arguments carry enough weight to visibly shake Tlega's confidence.
- **The 3HPP (Party):** Wary coexistence. He was not won over by argument — he was distracted into cooperation. His loyalty to the alliance is transactional at best.
- **Unla Kee:** Unla Kee outmaneuvered him socially using Jim's Glowing Coin; Mirk may not be aware he was manipulated.
- **Orsik:** Orsik considered intimidating Mirk, but held off. No direct confrontation yet.
- **Maergun:** Enemy. Mirk did personally land a javelin hit on the Fomorian.
