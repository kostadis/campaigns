# Blasius

## Identity
- **Role:** Satyr (self-described in one account as "the bravest of all Seelies"; rendered as fairy/satyr depending on source); formerly de facto Chief of Staff to Prince Thornacious (Seelie Court); reinstated as the Prince's Satyr (personal companion/herald) after the Unseelie affair; assigned to accompany the party against Bad Fruul
- **Faction:** Seelie Court / Prince Thornacious' court
- **First Appearance:** Encountered near a small pond in the Forgotten Forest, drinking from a hip flask, accompanied by a giant eagle with an ornamental leaf necklace. He recognized both Thistle and Oblivillish upon hearing the approaching group.

*Also known as: Blassios.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Return to a carefree life of drinking, storytelling, and revelry — free from administrative responsibility. Deeply motivated by guilt over Oblivillish's exile and by self-preservation.
- Warm, enthusiastic, and physically affectionate, particularly toward Unla Kee. Celebrates victories loudly and often drunkenly, and is not shy about disappearing when danger is imminent.
- Presents himself outwardly as gregarious and brave, though his internal monologue reveals significant ambivalence about his involvement — privately framing his current circumstances as a kind of punishment connected to Oblivillish.
- Dismissive of court formality and protocol, yet sharp enough to leverage his Chief of Staff status strategically (e.g., using it to open a Fey portal bypassing trickster fey).
- Dislikes close-quarters combat and is uncomfortable with his sword, preferring to fight from cover with a bow.
- His private motto during his miserable tenure as Chief of Staff: *"Better dead than a chief of staff."*

## History with the Party

1. **Mentioned in briefing:** Thistle mentions Blasius as a good friend currently embedded in Prince Thornacious' court; his presence there heightens Thistle's anxiety about the Zhentarim spy situation.
2. **First Meeting (Forgotten Forest):** Blasius was drinking alone near a pond when the party found him. He confessed unprompted that he was the cause of Oblivillish's exile — while drunk, he told Prince Thornacious that Oblivillish had killed the stag. When Oblivillish attacked him, the party intervened and Unla Kee put Oblivillish to sleep.
3. **The Proposal:** Once the crisis passed, Blasius proposed a deal: if the party eliminated the Unseelie plaguing the region, he would blame the dead stag on them, claim his original accusation was drunken confusion, and get Oblivillish restored — which would also free Blasius from his hated Chief of Staff duties.
4. **Leading the Party to the Unseelie:** Blasius led the group to a clearing where Unseelie Dryads and seedy halflings were playing cards surrounded by rotted stuffed animals and a broken harp. He identified the Dryads as Unseelie and insisted they be dealt with.
5. **After the Battle:** Blasius emerged from hiding once the fighting was over, offered drinks, and celebrated enthusiastically. He announced he was no longer Chief of Staff and gifted Unla Kee a **ring of jumping**, calling him his "true honored drinking friend," then fell asleep.
6. **Before Prince Thornacious:** Using his Chief of Staff status, Blasius opened a Fey portal to bring the group before the Prince. He formally recanted his accusations against Oblivillish — claiming he had been drunk and possibly joking — and attributed the stag's death to a bear and lion. He credited Oblivillish with leading the heroes against the Unseelie. Following the Prince's decree, Blasius was reinstated as the Prince's Satyr.
7. **The Vision Quest:** Blasius organized a vision quest involving drinking and fey communion, through which the heroes learned to neutralize poison once per quest. He noted the ability "only works once per vision quest."
8. **Thistle's Pitch / Assigned to the Mission:** Present at Prince Thornacious' camp during Thistle's pitch. When the Prince agreed to help against Bad Fruul, Blasius formally announced the decision and declared he would personally accompany the group along with two sprites.
9. **Day 4 Departure:** After Orsik successfully convinced Chandra, Blasius told Thistle "Guell my boy! I am off," and departed. His destination was unspecified, and he did not appear in subsequent events that session.
10. **Battle of Parnast:** Blasius arrives with two sprites, greets the party warmly, and joins the fight. He takes a javelin hit from an orc (which he vocally protests as rude), retaliates from cover with an arrow, and directs his sprites to attack the mammoth. The sprites successfully poison it but fail to put it to sleep.
11. **Post-Battle Celebration:** Emerged from a hideout after another battle, offered fine wine to Unla Kee, and enthusiastically celebrated Orsik as "my brother, my friend, my hero." Was still drunk and singing with Unla Kee the following morning when the SEER arrived to perform a ritual.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** With or near the party, following the mission against Bad Fruul; last seen celebrating and drinking after battle, still drunk the next morning.
- **Active Plans:** Accompanying the group as Prince Thornacious' designated representative, alongside two sprites.
- **What the Party Knows:** His guilt over Oblivillish's exile, his hatred of administrative work, his plan to clear Oblivillish's name (executed successfully), and his assignment to the party.
- **What Remains Hidden:** His specific whereabouts after his "I am off" departure on Day 4; the deeper layer of his guilt/ambivalence around Oblivillish (which one source frames as Blasius privately believing himself the target of Oblivillish's revenge); no stated agenda beyond drinking and companionship.

## Relationships
- **Oblivillish:** Directly responsible for Oblivillish's exile. Guilt-ridden about it. Successfully cleared Oblivillish's name before Prince Thornacious. Privately frames his own miserable circumstances as a kind of payback from Oblivillish for an unspecified past indiscretion.
- **Unla Kee:** Closest bond in the party. Offers him alcohol, hugs him enthusiastically, and gifted him a ring of jumping. They drink and sing together; singled out warmly upon every arrival.
- **Thistle:** Recognized as a friend, connected through shared ties to the Seelie Court. Blasius is fond but dismissive of Thistle's adherence to court protocol; Thistle was noticeably pleased by his arrival at Parnast.
- **Orsik:** Celebrated him effusively as "my brother, my friend, my hero" after a battle.
- **Prince Thornacious:** Serves him — reluctantly as Chief of Staff, now happily as reinstated Satyr companion. Uses the relationship instrumentally when it benefits him.
- **Two Sprites:** Under his direction in the field at Parnast and on the road.
- **Giant Eagle:** Accompanied Blasius at first meeting; no further mention.
