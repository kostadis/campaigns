# Vardis

## Identity
- **Role:** Cleric of Corellon (light aspect); member of the 3HPP (Three-Handed Poker Partners or equivalent party designation)
- **Faction:** Formerly affiliated with Temples of Corellon in Waterdeep; currently persona non grata at all temples of his god due to heretical views on clerical responsibilities (specifically debates around Flamestrike and Ao's post-sundering reductions of divine power)
- **First Appearance:** Traveling with Azam's caravan toward Parnast; described by Azam as one of a "curious pair" alongside the dwarf Orsik

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Survival with profit; locating the treasure marked on his map (an isle that "will appear but has not yet"); maintaining the party's cohesion while pursuing his own interests
- Vardis is sardonic and quick-witted, prone to dry humor in tense moments — joking about fire giant gourmet habits and singing "Ding dong, the Duchess is dead" after victories. He is also genuinely cautious and analytical, often the one identifying traps, deducing enemy plans, or urging the group to slow down and think.
- Despite his exile, he retains strong clerical ethics: he refuses to loot shrines, insists on respecting the dead, and demonstrates real theological knowledge. He resolves the tension between principle and pragmatism by lying comfortably when it suits the party's interests.
- He has a long, close friendship with Orsik predating the campaign, rooted in their time together in Waterdeep, and watches Orsik's psychological changes with quiet concern.

## History with the Party

**Early Campaign (Caravan to Parnast)**
Met the party traveling with Azam's caravan. Corrected Wallace's misunderstanding about the pseudo-dragon Hsing. Objected to pursuing fleeing goblins after the goblin raid; his position prevailed. Examined the found map with Orsik after a bugbear ambush.

**Elemental Dungeon / Tower of the Wizard of the Crag**
Deduced the elemental alliance system (fire/air allies; earth/water allies; fire/water enemies; earth/air enemies), enabling safe navigation of elemental walls. Refused to loot Azuth's shrine or other places of worship. Received a scroll of lesser restoration from Oghma's shrine. Failed to recognize the symbol of Mystara, contributing to a boulder trap. Discovered the Wizard's ledger detailing failed investments and a duel with a figure named Taskor. Remarked drily that the party had "released a wight into the world" after freeing Elquen.

**Llorkh Area / Tower Heist**
Attempted to grapple Orsik to drag him toward the tower (failed). Persuaded Unla Kee to enter with him, framing it as their chance without the fighters. Led negotiations with a bugbear inside, offering rations for cooperation. Discovered a secret door behind a tapestry; was struck by an ice dart trap. Ordered the bugbear to open what turned out to be a mimic; celebrated his critical guiding bolt and finished it off. Correctly interpreted a psychic warning about the key as the tower trying to trap them, and urged retreat. Back in Llorkh, explained the tower's secret entrance to Diego.

**Parnast Siege Preparation**
Conducted reconnaissance confirming the northern invasion route. Inspected town fortifications. Identified a cave room as a War Hearth with capacity for many orcs and shrines to orc gods. Contributed to the debate on Old Parnast, noting it was sturdier but considered cursed by locals.

**Fire Giant Attack / Triboar**
Was on the battlefield when a fire giant attacked; fired an arrow that missed; retreated safely. Witnessed Orsik being crushed. Ran out post-battle and dragged Orsik's body to safety; healed him and sarcastically told him to go back out and fight. Found a bag of salt on the dead fire giant. Attempted and failed to remove the fire giant's heart for use as a living lamp. Internally assessed Cindell as "one of those clerics who doesn't want to be a cleric."

**Xantharl's Keep**
During the attack, spotted bugbears approaching from the east, identified the giant assault as a diversion, and deduced the bugbears were heading for the Falling Orc to claim the reward on the Weevil. Immediately ran to intercept, with Orsik following. Fired fireballs alongside Unla Kee during the bugbear battle, wounding the Weevil.

**Prettyview / Slaad Encounter**
Rushed into Yazmeen's burning bedroom at Unla Kee's prompting and carried the sleeping gnome to safety. Deduced that Yazmeen's magical tea warded off the Slaad only while she was awake, and that sleep caused the protection to lapse — confirmed by Yazmeen. Explained merchandising to Thistle (selling demon Bob Sagert dolls as the play goes national).

**Eye of the All-Father (Oracle Temple)**
Challenged Harshnag's claim about the temple's age; received the Giant gesture for "loon." Picked up an amulet from a dead Uthgardtian chieftain and wore it under his armor. Contributed to the breakthrough deduction that the issue was *who* touched the rune, not which rune — and specifically suggested trying the Frost Giant weapon. Last seen entering the spider-filled tunnel system.

**Spine of the World Caves**
Noted Orsik's unexpected knowledge of stonework, attributing it to the magic ring (contrasting with the bar-loving Waterdeep Orsik he knew). Used his bow to kill a giant spider attacking Orsik. Discovered a frozen potion of climbing and an axe with a spectacular sapphire on the dead spider. Mocked Harshnag about being homeless.

**Remorhaz Fight / Oracle Chamber**
Praised Thistle for using a practical torch. Cast Sacred Flame and summoned a spiritual weapon shaped like the longsword Sahandrian; delivered the killing blow to the remorhaz. Explained "ret-conning" to Orsik, referencing his earlier "temporal phase." Used his oracle question to ask where the conch was; received the answer that it was in the clutches of a giant lord. Called it "the most direct question."

**Uthgardt Mound Excavations (Flint Rock / Morgur's Mound / Raven's Rock / Great Worm Cavern)**
Used detect magic to locate buried objects. Became increasingly frustrated with Thistle and Unla Kee refusing to dig. Destroyed skeletons with a fireball during the ambush at Morgur's Mound. Directed the ship to Raven's Rock after receiving Delsephine's update. Helped hoist the thigh bone artifact at Raven's Rock. At Great Worm Cavern, jointly launched fireballs with Unla Kee to incinerate the tribe's defenders with smug satisfaction.

**Cloud Giant Castle Heist (Sansuri)**
Expressed discomfort with heights. Tripped and was sprawled on the ground after a chest explosion. Commented "No kink shaming" on the Duke's bedchamber decor. Received a ring of jumping (suggested by Orsik given Vardis's self-described clumsiness). During the post-heist debate, argued consistently for disengagement over attacking Silverymoon, noting no guarantee the party would be blamed and that their scheming track record was weaker than Orsik believed.

**Bob / Triboar Aftermath**
Found Bob's defeat amusing. Pushed back on the cover-up plan. Mediated between Orsik and Darathra. Aboard the airship, questioned Escarlotta about castle ownership and pressed to understand how the conch worked before using it. In the Maelstrom, correctly identified the pool as a passageway and prevented the party from being scalded by a geyser.

**Maelstrom — Guestroom Battle**
Maintained a wall of fire separating him from Orsik during the giant lord fight. Used Fireball (chosen deliberately based on his theological history with Flamestrike) to incinerate a Frost Giant and injure a Cloud Giant. Used Healing Word to revive Thistle. Cast Flame Strike against Count Vaal. Reflected internally that Orsik, since gaining intelligence, seemed to resent his fighter career and possibly have a death wish. Urged the party to move giant bodies out of sight; stared at Orsik with suppressed laughter during the suitor-matchmaker cover story. Is currently underwater in the sinkhole area approaching the throne room.

**Maelstrom — Underwater / Crab Fights**
Cast a fireball underwater, noting aloud that "magic operates very differently than fire underwater." Used Toll the Dead on a weakened crab. Precisely measured the coral garden cave before casting a fireball to avoid hitting Thistle. Was attacked by a roper's tendrils but took no damage. Currently ascending the spiral staircase with the party.

**Airship / Undead Encounters**
Expressed concern about rights of the dead when skeletal remains were tossed overboard; accepted the logic of Dwarven cremation tradition. Coordinated with Orsik in the wyvern fight — Turn Undead to separate them, followed by Sacred Flame. Cast detect magic on a found cork and humorously declared it might save the realms. Used a warning flare against a specter.

**Bob Summoning / Dracolich Planning**
Identified the strategic problem with Planar Ally: Bob returns to the Abyss with an emissary whose phylactery is invulnerable. Noted that giving a demon a dracolich "did not seem heroic." Corrected Orsik that betraying Bob would be a "stab" not a backstab.

**Tower Battle (Glazhael)**
Fired on Ogre Zombies and used Toll the Dead on an injured target (noting it's the best cantrip against wounded enemies). Criticized Unla Kee's Stinking Cloud choice. Observed that Glazhael was "shadowboxing" — advancing without committing. Unaffected by dragon fear. Deliberately left a weakened zombie for Unla Kee.

**Sewer / Aboleth Combat**
Used a mace of terror to affect three of four Kuo-Toans, driving them into the water. Blinded the aboleth Oosith with light cleric powers, causing a critical attack on Thistle to fail. Opened a drainage valve to lower the water level. Initially said he was "a light cleric, not a necromancer" when asked about raising Drylund; then remembered he could cast raise dead, but declared it impossible because Drylund's brain was missing.

**Casino Planning / Northern Isles**
Supported the flying casino concept; compared it to a cruise ship. Advocated lying to Lady Atalia about the aboleth's fate. Quietly reminded Thistle that the play script is a demon summoning ritual when Pow expressed enthusiasm about using it.

**Ship Battle / Hekaton's Rescue**
Wounded by a ballista shot before entering the sleet storm. Cast a spiritual weapon against Hekaton's chains; weapon ultimately broke two chains, and his magical mace destroyed the third. Survived two cone of cold attacks, barely alive by escape. Shouted at Thistle to stop strategizing and simply heal Orsik. Healed Orsik in the grotto afterward. Is quietly less willing than Orsik to dismiss Hekaton's offer, motivated by the treasure map near Duke Zalto's stronghold.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Maelstrom throne room, with the party following Hekaton's rescue
- **Active Concerns:** The treasure map pointing to a location near Duke Zalto's stronghold; this creates quiet tension with Orsik's confident refusal of Hekaton's offer
- **What the Party Knows:** That Vardis is a fallen cleric of Corellon, banned from every temple of his god for heretical views; that he and Orsik have a long pre-campaign friendship from Waterdeep
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full extent of his internal concern about Orsik's possible death wish since gaining intelligence; the specific nature of his heretical clerical debates beyond the Flamestrike/Ao issue

## Relationships

**Orsik (Dwarf Fighter — close companion)**
Long friendship predating the campaign, rooted in Waterdeep. Vardis watches Orsik with affectionate exasperation — covering for him, backing him up, occasionally staring at him in disbelief. He is privately worried about Orsik's changed demeanor and possible death wish since gaining intelligence. Orsik has publicly called Vardis "my friend and always has my back."

**Unla Kee (Mage — ally)**
Regularly coordinates spells with Unla Kee (joint fireballs, tactical combinations). Entered the Llorkh tower with her as co-conspirators. Expressed genuine confusion at her desire to become a vessel for an evil soul.

**Thistle (Party member)**
Occasionally at odds — argued against pursuing fleeing goblins (his position won), pushed back on Thistle's casino concerns. Saved Yazmeen at Unla Kee's prompting during the Slaad encounter. Shouted at Thistle to simply heal Orsik during the ship battle.

**Harshnag (Giant ally)**
Singled out by name by Harshnag when explaining the Ordning. Earned the Giant gesture for "loon" for challenging the temple's age. Mocked Harshnag about being homeless on multiple occasions. Relationship is functional but irreverent.

**Delsephine**
Gave her a "stink eye" when she lamented finding no artifact at Morgur's Mound. Received mission updates from her.

**Corellon Temples / Clerical Order**
Persona non grata. Fellow clerics will not allow him into their temples. The exile stems from heretical positions on clerical responsibilities, specifically his views on Flamestrike's post-sundering weakening and Ao's role — which led him to follow Corellon's light aspect and favor Fireballs theologically.
