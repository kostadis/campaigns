# Sage Vusk

## Identity
- Role: Traveling scholar and lecturer
- Faction: Guest speaker at the Lady's College, Silverymoon
- First appearance: Session in which the party attended (or intersected with) his lecture on Cambions at the Lady's College

## Personality & Motivations
- Publicly presents updated, progressive scholarly views while privately holding traditionalist beliefs — specifically that Cambions lack free will due to their infernal nature.
- Dry humor; laughed at the notion of banning magic in Silverymoon, suggesting he's pragmatic about social realities.
- Appears to value academic rigor and etiquette; warned the party that casting Dispel Magic on someone socially is a dueling offense.

## History with the Party
- **First meeting:** The party attended Vusk's sparsely attended lecture on Cambion origins at the Lady's College. He shared both scholarly consensus and tactical intelligence on Cambions, including the mechanics of Fiendish Charm and countermeasures against it.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** His lecture podium, Lady's College, Silverymoon
- **Active plans:** No known ongoing operations; appeared to be fulfilling a standard lecturing engagement
- **What the party knows:** His public and private views on Cambions, tactical information he shared openly
- **What remains hidden:** His reasons for traveling to Silverymoon; whether his traditionalist views on Cambion free will are relevant to anything the party may encounter

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Informational contact only; no apparent personal investment
- **Lady's College:** Guest lecturer, no indicated deeper institutional ties
