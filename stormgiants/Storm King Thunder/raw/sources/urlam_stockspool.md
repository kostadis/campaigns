# Urlam Stockspool

## Identity
- **Role:** Local Zhentarim agent; owner of The Triboar Travelers inn
- **Appearance:** Middle-aged, Illuskan, missing one eye; wears an eye patch bearing a red Zhentarim symbol
- **First Appearance:** Met the party at the Talking Troll tavern in Triboar, at Orsik's request

## Personality & Motivations
- Outwardly warm and gregarious (greeted Orsik with a kiss on each cheek), but has a sharp temper when he feels disrespected — Thistle's question about contraband nearly derailed the meeting entirely.
- Operates within Zhentarim channels officially, but is willing to act on personal grievances outside official sanction — he wants House Margaster dead and isn't subtle about the distinction.
- Pragmatic and transactional; smoothed over with drinks and gold as readily as he's angered.

## History with the Party
- Orsik initiated contact and requested a meeting. Urlam arrived at the Talking Troll with ten Zhentarim members in tow — whose tab Orsik ended up covering.
- Provided intelligence on **Adimond Kroskas**: a free agent (not Cult of the Dragon), hired to create hybrid magical creatures; most who knew him are now dead. Directed the party to Silverymoon's magical libraries for further research.
- Revealed that **House Margaster** has been coercing him for discounts and had his guards killed when he objected. Zhentarim leadership considers the Margasters untouchable — but Urlam personally wants them eliminated.
- Implicated **Orthovir** as potentially having ties to the Margasters, suggesting Orthovir may have set the party up.
- Offered the party **200 gold** to deal with House Margaster, framed as a "friends and family discount."
- Briefly erupted in fury when Thistle asked about contraband; required Orsik's intervention and another round of drinks to de-escalate.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The Talking Troll tavern, Triboar
- Operating independently of official Zhentarim sanction in his grudge against House Margaster
- Has extended a financial offer to the party; awaiting their action against the Margasters

## What the Party Knows vs. What Remains Hidden
- **Knows:** His Zhentarim affiliation, his grievance with the Margasters, the 200gp bounty, the Kroskas lead, the Orthovir implication
- **Hidden:** The full extent of his Zhentarim operations, what contraband he may be running, the nature of his relationship with Zhentarim leadership

## Relationships
- **Orsik:** Primary point of contact; Urlam responds warmly to him and relied on Orsik to manage Thistle's misstep
- **Thistle:** Caused a diplomatic incident; Urlam's trust here is thin
- **House Margaster:** Personal enemy — coerced him, had his guards killed; wants them dead (unofficial position)
- **Zhentarim Leadership:** Operates within their hierarchy but is acting outside official policy regarding the Margasters
- **Orthovir:** Implied connection to House Margaster; Urlam suggested Orthovir may have betrayed the party
- **Adimond Kroskas:** No direct relationship; provided secondhand intelligence only
