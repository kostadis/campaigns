# Eden "Gleeful" Gleeson

## Identity
- Role: Traveling nobleman (implied by presence of manservant); prospective groom
- Faction: None apparent
- First appearance: Encountered on the road west from the Greypeak Mountains toward Llorkh; hailed the party in a panic seeking a wedding gift

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Reach his wedding and present his bride with a meaningful gift
- Sentimental, impulsive, and genuinely devoted to his bride — willing to spend 150 GP on a repurposed nose ring on a roadside impulse. Cheerful under pressure, calling Vardis's hard bargain a fair one with good humor. Somewhat oblivious to practical consequences, leaving financial management to his manservant Cisco.

## History with the Party
- **First (and only) meeting:** Eden flagged down the party on the western road, desperate for a purchasable gift. After reviewing several items (copper necklace, a magnifying glass missing its glass, a polished gold piece), he purchased a giant silver nose ring for 150 GP, intending to have it crafted into a necklace. Vardis opened negotiations at 200 GP; Eden settled at 150 GP. His manservant Cisco scolded him afterward, noting the purchase consumed the entire wedding dessert budget.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Road heading west toward Llorkh
- **Active plans:** Traveling to attend his own wedding; intends to have the silver nose ring fashioned into a necklace for his bride
- **What the party knows:** His name, destination, and that he is sentimental and free with money
- **What remains hidden:** Identity of the bride, details of his household or station, whether he arrives in time

## Relationships
- **Cisco (manservant):** Loyal but exasperated caretaker; acts as Eden's financial conscience and voice of reason
- **His bride (unnamed):** The clear center of Eden's motivation; no details known
- **Vardis (party):** Conducted the haggling exchange; Eden parted on friendly terms
