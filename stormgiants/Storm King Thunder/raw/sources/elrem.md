# Elrem

## Identity
- **Role:** The Great Worm — legendary creature and object of worship for the Great Worm tribe
- **Faction:** Revered (and feared) by the Great Worm tribe; connected to the worship of Uthgar
- **First Appearance:** Referenced indirectly through Bressik's presentation on Great Worm Cavern; has not appeared as an active presence

## Personality & Motivations
- No direct personality has been established — Elrem is known only through tribal myth and ritual
- The tribe's relationship with Elrem is one of appeasement: they make sacrifices to prevent the creature from returning and devouring them, suggesting Elrem is perceived as a destructive, consuming force

## History with the Party
- The party learned of Elrem through Bressik's briefing on Great Worm Cavern
- Elrem has not interacted with the party directly
- At the end of a session, an unidentified creature burst from a snowbank — described as a fusion of worms, centipedes, and dragons, with wing-like membranes and dozens of legs; this may be Elrem, but has not been confirmed

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Unknown; possibly dormant beneath or near Great Worm Cavern
- **Active Plans:** None confirmed; the creature's nature and intentions remain entirely unknown
- **What the Party Knows:** Elrem is a legendary entity worshipped at a coiled-serpent altar atop the Great Worm tribe's triangular promontory; the tribe sacrifices to keep it from returning
- **What Remains Hidden:** Whether Elrem is truly the creature that emerged from the snowbank; whether Elrem is alive, mythic, or something else entirely

## Relationships
- **Great Worm Tribe:** Worshipped and deeply feared; the tribe's altar and sacrificial rites are dedicated to appeasing Elrem alongside Uthgar
- **The Party:** No direct relationship established
