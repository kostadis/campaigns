# Calder

## Identity
- Independent halfling shopkeeper in Parnast; no affiliation with Lionel Coster
- First appeared during the party's two-week stay in Parnast, when Unla Kee purchased a replacement sword from him

## Personality & Motivations
- Deeply resentful of the Lionel Coster operation, which he views as an unfair threat to independent traders like himself
- Vocal and bitter about his grievances; willing to air them to customers mid-transaction

## History with the Party
- Sold Unla Kee a replacement sword during the party's stay in Parnast, complaining throughout the sale that Lionel Coster's dimensional portals allow them to move goods faster and cheaper than small independents can compete with
- Referenced later by Chandra in her explanation to Thistle about the absence of a Lionel Coster market in Parnast; noted as a merchant forced to rely on Zhentarim physical transportation rather than transdimensional trade

## Current Status
- Last known location: his shop in Parnast
- Operating as best he can under adverse competitive conditions, dependent on Zhentarim overland/physical goods transport
- **Party knows:** He has no magic items to sell and is hostile to the Lionel Coster operation
- **Hidden:** Nothing notable flagged in notes

## Relationships
- **Lionel Coster (operation):** Hostile; views them as predatory competition undercutting local traders
- **Zhentarim:** Dependent on them for physical transportation of goods
- **Unla Kee:** Brief commercial interaction; sold her a sword

## Arc Score Events
None recorded.
