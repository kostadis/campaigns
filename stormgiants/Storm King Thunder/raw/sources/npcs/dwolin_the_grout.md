# Dwolin the Grout

## Identity
- Dwarven foreman at Mountain's Toe Gold Mine
- First encountered by the party at the mine during investigation of the production stoppage

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: Resume gold mining, but only under conditions that satisfy his deeply held superstitions about how gold veins behave.
- Dwolin is earnest, eccentric, and utterly convinced that gold veins are sentient — or at least temperamental. He speaks with great gravity about the need to treat the vein with "absolute respect," and insists that without an Earthstone Gem, mining simply cannot proceed. Despite the oddity of his beliefs, he is regarded as exceptionally skilled at finding gold.

## History with the Party
- The party met Dwolin at Mountain's Toe Gold Mine, where production had ground to a halt. He explained that the gold vein is "shy" and must be coaxed out properly, and that an Earthstone Gem is required for his rituals before mining can resume. His superstitions are the direct cause of the production stoppage.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Mountain's Toe Gold Mine
- **Active situation:** Mining is halted because Dwolin refuses to proceed without an Earthstone Gem for his rituals.
- **Known vs. hidden:** The party knows his beliefs are behind the stoppage. It remains unclear whether his rituals have any genuine efficacy or are pure superstition — though his colleague vouches for his results.

## Relationships
- **Horia Stonetallow:** Colleague at the mine. Describes Dwolin as "the best seeker of gold I have ever known," and speaks of his rituals with fond tolerance — "his little rituals, and his little gimmicks, and his little things."
- **The Party:** Dwolin has been straightforward with them about his requirements, treating the situation as self-evidently serious.
