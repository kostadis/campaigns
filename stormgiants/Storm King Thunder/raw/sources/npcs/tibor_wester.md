# Tibor Wester

## Identity
- **Role:** Signatory authority and chief of the Logger's Camp; responsible for authorizing delivery notices and releasing payment; operator of a wildcat logging operation
- **Faction:** Unknown
- **First Appearance:** Referenced by name during party discussions; has never been encountered in person
- *Also known as: Tibor, Tribar Wester.*

*Also known as: Tibor, Tribar Wester.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Hired new logging crews who did not know or did not respect the permit agreements governing sanctioned cutting in the forest
- Operated outside established forestry regulations and permit frameworks

## History with the Party
1. The party was directed to find Tibor at the Logger's Camp to have their delivery notice signed and claim payment.
2. Refused to transport the prisoner Shrimpy to Neverwinter, prompting the party to decide to bring Shrimpy to Phandalin instead.
3. Upon the party's arrival at the camp in the early morning, it was completely deserted despite the early hour — tents on the beach, a standing cabin with stacked logs, older cabins torn down to foundations and stone chimneys showing signs of habitation but no people. A grim silence hung over the site. Tibor was nowhere to be found.
4. Had an outstanding financial arrangement with Vukradin, who was counting on finding Tribar Wester at camp to collect payment.
5. His absence and the fate of the camp's inhabitants prompted the party to investigate before they could complete their delivery contract.
6. During the party's encounter with young druids confronting a logging crew in the forest, the druids identified Tibor Wester — described as Harbin's brother — as the operator of a wildcat logging operation. He had been hiring new crews who did not know or did not respect the permit agreements governing sanctioned cutting in the forest. The loggers themselves appeared to be workers who had taken available employment without full knowledge of or investment in Wester's scheme. When his name was raised, Vukradin made a soft warning sound rather than a word. After Vukradin's speech moved the loggers to set down their axes and express remorse, they indicated they would report back to the city about what Tibor Wester had been running in the forest.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Logger's Camp (expected but not found)
- **Whereabouts:** Missing and unaccounted for, along with the entire camp population
- **What the party knows:** He is the camp chief and delivery signatory they need; he refused to help with Shrimpy; the camp shows signs of partial abandonment or disruption despite evidence of recent habitation; he operated a wildcat logging operation hiring crews who didn't respect permit agreements; his workers have indicated they will report his illegal forestry activities to the city
- **What remains hidden:** Where he is, why he left or disappeared, and what happened at the camp

## Relationships
- **Vukradin:** Had an outstanding financial arrangement; Vukradin was counting on collecting payment; Vukradin made a soft warning sound when Tibor's name was raised by the druids
- **Shrimpy:** Party prisoner whose transport Tibor refused to facilitate
- **Harbin Wester:** Tibor is Harbin's brother; Harbin posted the Logger's Camp supply delivery quest on his behalf
- **The Party:** Indirect, transactional; no face-to-face interaction on record
- **Young druids:** Identified Tibor as violating forest permit agreements
- **Logging crews:** Hired workers who were unaware of or uninvested in his wildcat operation; they later expressed intent to report him
