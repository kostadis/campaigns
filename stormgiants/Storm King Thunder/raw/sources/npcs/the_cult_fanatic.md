# The Cult Fanatic *(name unknown)*

## Identity
- **Role:** Cult Fanatic, operative of the Kraken Society (identified by spiral emblem)
- **Affiliation:** Kraken Society; associated with Kraken Society Echoes
- **First Appearance:** Encountered in the glade on the eastern shore, commanding three mind-controlled Emerald Enclave patrol members
- **Weapon:** Black coral dagger

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Advancing a large-scale operation described as a "rising current flowing toward Neverwinter"; securing willing or compelled servants for his cause; conducting "necessary adjustments" on individuals
- Cold, dismissive, and fanatical — he treated the party's interference as a minor inconvenience rather than a threat
- Spoke in cryptic oceanic metaphors ("tides rising," "waves coming," "rising current")
- Showed no fear even when captured or unconscious
- Regarded the Talosian orcs' vendetta against him as a "petty squabble" beneath notice and irrelevant to what was coming
- Zealously devoted to his cause, refusing to betray the Kraken Society or reveal operational details even under interrogation

## History with the Party
1. **Eastern Shore Glade:** Confronted the party alongside three mind-controlled Emerald Enclave members who moved with robotic precision. Coldly informed them they were interfering with necessary adjustments and that his zealots served a greater purpose. Offered them a chance to submit to his cause; they refused. During combat he compelled Brewbarry's mind, forcing him to flee, and continued exerting control over his minions even while unconscious.
2. **Interrogation:** When questioned, he gave only vague warnings about rising tides and an approaching wave, muttering cryptically. He refused to betray the Kraken Society or reveal operational details.
3. **Talosian Orc Ambush (Day 2 of travel):** A Talosian orc raiding party ambushed the group specifically demanding his death, identifying him as their enemy. Given a final chance to speak, he referred only to petty squabbles that meant nothing to what was coming.
4. **Execution:** Valphine executed him. The orcs claimed his head as proof of victory. His death immediately broke mind control over all captives.

## Current Status
- **Status:** **Dead** — executed by Valphine; head taken by Talosian orcs as proof of victory
- His death freed the mind-controlled Emerald Enclave patrol members

**What the party knows:**
- He was a Kraken Society operative (spiral emblem)
- He was running mind-control operations and "adjusting" individuals for a larger purpose
- He wielded a black coral dagger
- Something significant is moving toward Neverwinter
- The Talosian orcs considered him an enemy
- His mind control broke upon his death

**What remains hidden:**
- His true name and rank within the Kraken Society
- The specific nature and scale of the Neverwinter operation
- Who leads the Kraken Society Echoes and what their full scope of activity is
- Why the Talosian orcs wanted him dead

## Relationships
- **Kraken Society / Kraken Society Echoes:** Operative; actively running field operations on their behalf
- **Emerald Enclave patrol:** Three members held under his mind control; freed upon his death
- **Talosian Orcs:** Enemies — the orcs hunted him specifically and celebrated his death, taking his head as proof of victory
- **Brewbarry:** Targeted with mental compulsion during combat; forced to flee
- **Valphine:** Delivered the killing blow
