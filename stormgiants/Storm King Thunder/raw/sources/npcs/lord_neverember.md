# Lord Neverember

## Identity
- Ruler of Neverwinter
- First encountered as a distant employer; Vukardin (and presumably the party) were recruited under his adventurer-clearance program for the Phandalin hinterlands

*Also known as: (none).*

## Personality & Motivations
- Pragmatic and politically minded; willing to use unconventional means (hired adventurers rather than formal military) to achieve strategic goals
- Motivated by post-war resource constraints and a desire to encourage settlement and economic growth in the hinterlands around Phandalin
- Constrained by the costs of recent wars, leading him to favor cost-effective solutions like adventurer recruitment over conventional military deployment

## History with the Party
- Following the end of the Uthgardt Barbarian invasion and the broader conflicts of the era, Neverember turned his attention to pacifying the Phandalin region
- Offered adventurers — including Vukardin — an arrangement of tax-free, no-questions-asked plunder in exchange for clearing the region around Phandalin of monsters, cults, and evildoers
- No direct face-to-face interaction with the party recorded yet

## Current Status
- Last known location: Neverwinter (ruling the city-state)
- Active operation: sponsoring adventurer clearance of the Phandalin hinterlands
- **Party knows:** The broad terms of the employment arrangement
- **Hidden from party:** His deeper political calculations, long-term ambitions for the region, and what he ultimately intends to do with a pacified hinterland

## Relationships
- **Vukardin:** Employer/contractor relationship — Neverember recruited him under the hinterlands clearance program
- **Party:** Distant patron; no personal relationship established
