# The Ghost of Annam's Oracle (Cloud Giant)

## Identity
- Role: Haunting spirit bound to the hexagonal oracle chamber within the Temple of Annam
- Faction: None; estranged from his father Blagothkus
- First appearance: Rose from the floor of the oracle chamber when the party entered; introduced himself as the son of Blagothkus

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goal:** Vengeance — he wants the party to kill his father, Blagothkus, who murdered him
- Carries resentment toward his father but frames his death within the oracle's prophecy, suggesting he had some awareness of the conflict before it erupted
- Calm and informative in manner; willing to explain the oracle's mechanics in exchange for the party's cooperation

## History with the Party
- **First and only meeting:** The ghost emerged from the oracle chamber floor and recounted his death. His father Blagothkus, devastated by the oracle's declaration that he could never earn the gods' favor, was further enraged when the oracle urged his son to step out from Blagothkus's "dark shadow." Overcome with despair and envy, Blagothkus slew his son on the spot.
- Explained the oracle's rules to the party: six questions per day, each consuming one lantern light from the six giant statues; every question yields a true answer.
- Tasked the party with killing Blagothkus, who has since retired to his castle in Parnast to mourn. He admitted uncertainty about whether Parnast is a real location.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Bound to the oracle chamber in the Temple of Annam
- **Active situation:** Waiting; his fate is tied to whether the party pursues Blagothkus
- **What the party knows:** His death, the oracle's mechanics, and the quest to find Blagothkus in Parnast
- **What remains hidden:** The nature of his binding to the chamber; whether killing Blagothkus would free or end him

## Relationships
- **Blagothkus (father):** Killed by him in a moment of grief-fueled rage; the ghost harbors deep resentment and seeks his death
- **The Party:** Cooperative; offered useful information in exchange for taking on his vengeance quest
