# Professor BigEyes

## Identity
- Role: Professor at the Nirany School of Magic; subject area: legendary creatures
- First appearance: Not yet met directly; referenced by Unla Kee in conversation with the party

## Personality & Motivations
- Presents as an academic authority on legendary and primeval creatures. Quoted remarks suggest a philosophical, somewhat fatalistic view of such entities — framing them as forces beyond civilization's reckoning rather than mere monsters.

## History with the Party
- The party has not interacted with Prof BigEyes directly. Unla Kee cited their teachings when discussing Krakens, quoting two observations:
  1. Krakens are primeval forces that obliterate civilization's greatest achievements as if they were castles in the sand.
  2. When such creatures interact with the world, societies and cults spring up to worship them.
