# Herald

## Identity
- **Role:** Sergeant of the Guard, Axe of Mirabar
- **First Appearance:** Party arrived in Mirabar to deliver their prisoner; Herald received the handover at the gate/processing point.

## Personality & Motivations
- Loyal and efficient servant of Mirabar's interests — professional, no-nonsense, and clearly experienced with prisoner intake.
- Quick-witted and observant; immediately saw through The Weevil's attempted deception, identifying him by his distinctive beard braid without hesitation.
- Motivated by Mirabar's security and intelligence interests — notably interested in The Weevil's knowledge of the caravan scheduling code beyond simple imprisonment.

## History with the Party
- Received The Weevil from the party upon their arrival in Mirabar, congratulating them warmly on their service.
- Shut down The Weevil's identity denial swiftly, drawing additional guards to secure the situation.
- Learned from Orsik that The Weevil had cracked the caravan scheduling code; immediately threatened The Weevil that Mirabar had means of extracting that information.
- Sentenced The Weevil to a life in the mines and paid the party the 5,000 gp bounty in Mirabar-marked coin.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Mirabar — processing The Weevil through the guard system.
- **Active Operations:** Overseeing The Weevil's incarceration; likely coordinating efforts to extract the caravan scheduling code from him.
- **Party Knows:** Herald is a competent, well-connected sergeant who pays his debts and takes intelligence seriously.
- **Hidden:** What methods Mirabar intends to use to extract the code from The Weevil; Herald's broader authority or rank within the Axe of Mirabar.

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Professional and grateful — views them as useful contractors who delivered a wanted criminal. No deeper bond established.
- **Orsik (Party):** Received the key intelligence tip about the caravan code directly from him.
- **The Weevil:** Captor. Shows no sympathy — sentenced him to life in the mines and flagged him as an intelligence asset to be interrogated.
