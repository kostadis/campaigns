# Brimskarda

## Identity
- **Role:** Fire giant duchess of Ironslag
- **Faction:** Ironslag (fire giant stronghold)
- **First Appearance:** Observed (unseen) by Thistle during a solo reconnaissance of the kitchen on Ironslag's lower level.

## Personality & Motivations
- Motivations are not yet established beyond her domestic authority within Ironslag.
- Demonstrates a commanding, hands-on presence — personally overseeing kitchen operations and directing ogre cooks and goblin servants. Her sharp orders suggest she expects immediate obedience and runs a tight household.

## History with the Party
- **Recon by Thistle:** While invisible, Thistle observed Brimskarda in the kitchen sampling soup from a cauldron over a large fireplace, surrounded by five cackling smoke mephits, two ogre cooks, and twenty goblins performing menial tasks. She issued an order to the goblins, who then carried food toward the dining hall. Thistle followed the goblins and moved on. She did not detect him.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Kitchen, lower level of Ironslag.
- **Active Activity:** Overseeing food preparation and directing kitchen staff.
- **What the Party Knows:** Her appearance, her role running the kitchen, and that she commands ogres, goblins, and smoke mephits.
- **What Remains Hidden:** Her broader role and authority within Ironslag's power structure, her relationship to the duke or other leaders, and any larger motivations.

## Relationships
- Commands two ogre cooks and approximately twenty goblins in the kitchen.
- Associated with five smoke mephits who attend her during cooking.
- No direct interaction with any party member to date.
