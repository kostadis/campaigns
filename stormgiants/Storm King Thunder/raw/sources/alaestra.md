# Alaestra

## Identity
- **Role:** Co-owner of the Lionshield Coster outpost ("Lion's Share") in Triboar, alongside partner Narth
- **Faction:** Lionshield Coster — a merchant company with an interplanar supply chain
- **First Appearance:** Met Orsik at the Lion's Share outpost while in a state of distress over a vault breach

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Protect the Lion's Share outpost's operations and recover/secure the interplanar supply keys on behalf of the Lionshield Coster organization
- Alaestra is eager, perhaps too eager — she let slip sensitive company secrets (the existence of the special keys) under stress, something her partner Narth tried to prevent
- She appears to distrust or feel alienated from the local Triboar population, likely due to friction the Lionshield Coster's planerized trade model has caused with local merchants
- When it comes to renegotiating terms, she holds firm — suggesting either personal discipline or fear of whoever sits above her in the company hierarchy

## History with the Party
1. **First contact:** Orsik arrived to shop and found Alaestra in distress over a stolen vault. She immediately solicited outside help, offering a 10% discount. Under negotiation pressure, she escalated to a 20% discount and 50 gold before Orsik accepted.
2. **During negotiation**, she inadvertently disclosed that the stolen goods included a special key integral to the Lionshield Coster's interplanar supply chain — a detail Narth visibly tried to suppress.
3. **Upon the key's return:** Alaestra was visibly and notably relieved. When Orsik attempted to renegotiate terms, she and Narth refused to budge. Orsik got the impression that whoever employs them is more intimidating than he is.
4. **Final interaction:** The party spent their (apparently settled at 15%) discount at Lion's Share, purchasing healing potions, new weapons, and Thistle's boots of elvenkind.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The Lion's Share outpost in Triboar
- **Active Operations:** Resuming normal business now that the key has been recovered; likely reporting the breach to Lionshield Coster leadership
- **What the Party Knows:** The Lionshield Coster uses special keys to interface with an interplanar supplier; losing one would have serious consequences with the company
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full nature of the interplanar supply chain, who or what the mysterious supplier is, and what exactly makes the Lionshield Coster's leadership so intimidating to Alaestra and Narth

## Relationships
- **Narth (co-owner):** Business partner. Operates as a counterbalance to Alaestra's stress-induced candor — more guarded and protective of company secrets
- **Lionshield Coster (parent organization):** Alaestra appears loyal but fearful; the threat of the organization's displeasure is enough to make her immovable in negotiation
- **Local Triboar merchants:** Likely strained relationship due to disruption caused by the Lionshield Coster's trade model
- **Orsik:** Conducted direct negotiation; views him as capable if pushy — respects the deal struck but won't renegotiate
- **Vardis / Thistle:** Present as customers; no significant individual dynamic noted
