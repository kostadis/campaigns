# The Fire Giant of the Evermoors

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Servant of Duke Zalto; artifact hunter
- **Faction:** Fire Giant forces under Duke Zalto
- **First Appearance:** Encountered in a clearing in the Evermoors, berating two trolls

## Personality & Motivations
- **Primary Goal:** Locate a fragment of the Vonindod and return it to Duke Zalto
- **Drives:** Duty to Zalto; immediate frustration over lack of food and the trolls' uselessness
- She is short-tempered, hungry, and irritable. Her complaints about mud pies suggest she is operating far from supply lines and is not enjoying the assignment. She appears competent enough to be trusted with a search rod but is clearly miserable in the Evermoors.

## History with the Party
- The party observed her from a distance while she argued with two trolls who could not understand her and kept offering her mud pies instead of food.
- Thistle eavesdropped using *Comprehend Languages* (provided by Unla Kee) and gathered the intelligence above.
- The party chose not to engage — Orsik called the recon complete, and Thistle determined she was not the Fire Giant who attacked Triboar and therefore not a personal target.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The Evermoors
- **Status:** Alive
- **Active Operations:** Searching for a fragment of the Vonindod using a divining rod identical to the one used by the Triboar attacker
- **What the Party Knows:** She works for Duke Zalto, is hunting a piece of the Vonindod, and is distinct from the Fire Giant that attacked Triboar
- **What Remains Hidden:** Her name, her exact search area, how close she is to finding the fragment, and her standing within Zalto's organization

## Relationships
- **Duke Zalto:** Her superior; she cannot return to him until her mission is complete
- **The Two Trolls:** Local "allies" she is clearly exasperated by — they do not understand her and are functionally useless to her mission
- **The Party:** Unaware she was observed; no relationship established
