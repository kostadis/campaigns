# Orsik

## Identity
- **Role:** De facto party leader, fighter; former town council member and city guard (Parnast); owner of the Talking Troll tavern (Triboar)
- **Faction interest:** Flagged by Fzoul Slovis as a Zhentarim recruitment target
- **First appearance:** Traveling with Azam's caravan toward Parnast; introduced alongside Vardis as part of a "curious pair"

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Redemption through community investment; acquisition of power, knowledge, and treasure; protecting those he considers his responsibility; uncovering the location tied to his family heirloom map
- Orsik is bold, loud, and frequently overconfident — he announces loots before taking them, dismisses contributions he doesn't respect, and makes unilateral decisions on behalf of the group. Since gaining the headband of intellect, he has developed a genuine hunger for history, lore, and strategic thinking, though his instinct remains to charge first.
- He has a strong personal code: he distrusts those who haven't earned it, takes betrayal hard, and will escalate confrontation rather than swallow an injustice — but he can be reached through honest engagement, as the SEER demonstrated.
- He is surprisingly capable of deception and social manipulation when motivated, and shows genuine loyalty to people he considers his community.

## History with the Party

**Early caravan period (Parnast road):**
Traveling with Azam's caravan. Described by Azam as part of a "curious pair" with Vardis. Hsing notes he has done things that made him unemployable as a guard. Fzoul Slovis identifies him as a Zhentarim recruitment target.

**Ambush Gap and wizard's tower approach:**
Led the group toward the wizard's tower in the Marsh of Chelimber at Hsing's request, negotiating no payment. During the bugbear ambush, threw his sword at the enemies — underwhelming to Wallace. Found a map on the bodies and announced it aloud before looting. Worked with Vardis to compare maps. Negotiated with the mephit Feetor for intel on Mama Manyknuckles' camp; agreed to give Feetor a gold coin as a token of power.

**Wizard's tower exploration:**
Directed Wallace through the tower. Failed repeatedly to earn Kossuth's blessing (tried rope, candle, torch, produce flame). Recognized Azuth; wrote "Dwarves are awesome" in Oghma's tome. Communicated with gargoyles via hand gestures; learned a wizard's arrogance bound them there. Smirked when Thistle was burned by the fire wall. Failed to open a locked door. Shouted "Look what I found!" when Wallace discovered the elemental alliance inscription.

**Bad Fruul campaign (Parnast):**
Persuaded Chandra to accept his protection against Bad Fruul's potential reprisals. Independently deceived a goblin group to reduce Bad Fruul's forces. Fought in the cave complex; shouted "FINALLY" at the first fireball. Helped kill the orog and acolyte that downed Unla Kee. Participated in sparing the orc children and Claw of Luthic. Recognized hostage families as belonging to Jarlor and Larrish, men he knew from the town council — reacted with visible murderous rage upon learning they'd been coerced into spying.

**The headband confrontation (Parnast, aftermath of Bad Fruul):**
Attempted to swap Bad Fruul's magical headband with a fake won in a card game, motivated by distrust of the SEER. Caught by Thistle. Withstood pressure from Chandra, Wallace, Thistle, Hsing, and a guard, experiencing it as a profound betrayal. Marched out of town; articulated his grievances to Thistle clearly. Hsing inadvertently conceded his point about not trusting a spymaster. Confronted the SEER directly; she offered to perform the ritual publicly. Satisfied, he agreed. Ended up in sole possession of the headband of intellect — his intelligence was transformed from "ox" to genius-level. Reflected on completing his redemption arc through character rather than cleverness. Sent Wallace off without sentiment when he departed to become guard captain.

**Departure and Triboar:**
Left Parnast with companions heading toward Triboar to find Uncle Joon.

**Battle against fire giants (Triboar):**
Wounded during the fight. Called for Darathra to run. Was crushed by a giant's sword and died briefly; last thoughts noted he'd warned the group not to fight. Pulled into the tower by Vardis and revived (Thistle's revival spell). Refused to re-enter the fight: "Nope, I'm out." Afterward, prioritized raising Darz. Suggested Speak with Dead on the fire giant's corpse; negotiated with cleric Cindell Trueshot for the spells, attempting to overstate the value of a found ruby. Invited Cindell to dinner at the Talking Troll (which he owns) for business reasons. Asked the dead giant about Duke Zalto; learned Zalto intends to rule all giants and is located in "his throne room."

**Temple of the All-Father:**
Declared the dome safe from collapse; urged caution when Unla Kee rushed ahead. Participated in the Uthgardtian ambush. Identified the six archway runes (hill, cloud, fire, storm, stone, frost — noted the frost as an old variant). Cut off Harshnag's theory to let the party work it out. Suggested touching the Storm rune first (hierarchy logic); Thistle did so, killing the party. Revived by Harshnag. Identified the key insight that a weapon must touch its corresponding rune. Suggested Harshnag try the Frost rune. After failure, proposed searching the temple for the missing Frost Giant weapon. Led the group into the northern tunnel system.

**Spider tunnels and temple continuation:**
Burned through webs to reach cocoons. Found a dead dwarf's cocoon containing copper, cards, and dice. Identified the surviving woman as belonging to a tribe whose chieftain the party killed — withheld this. Deceived her about the tribe's fate. Advised her to be quiet near Harshnag before she ran off. Identified the tunnels as creature warrens formed by mountain shifts (attributed to dwarven knowledge). Killed the giant spider and egg swarm. Mocked Harshnag about being homeless and old. Directed the group during the granite ball trap; was hit by the ball.

**Remorhaz fight and Oracle:**
Delivered an extended monologue on stonemasonry and objected philosophically to stone-shaping magic. Called for the party to stand and fight the remorhaz; struck it multiple times despite heat damage. Advised Harshnag to retrieve the Ax of Thrym alone. Was aghast when Unla Kee followed Harshnag. Used one oracle question to ask about his family heirloom map — learned what it depicts lies beyond the Silvery Marches near the Ice Spires in an iron tower. Suggested asking about Duke Zalto, but the oracle's lights ran out.

**Blue dragon fight (Netherese Mountains):**
Led the group through the passage, commenting on non-Dwarven workmanship. First to identify the creature as a blue dragon. Articulated the three values of the mountains: granite, dragon hoards, and Netherese artifacts. Helped sketch the battle plan before Thistle started early. Slashed the dragon four times; applied booming energy. Took lightning bolts reducing him to 8 HP. Spotted and used a flanking tunnel; hit the dragon with Firebolt (to Thistle's disbelief). Flatly refused the dragon's offer of accommodation. Climbed 60 feet, took another lightning bolt (partially dodged), and killed the dragon with his sword. Called off further exploration afterward.

**Raven's Rock and Great Worm Cavern:**
Immediately redirected the group to the Gray Wolf altar on arrival, cutting off Bressik. Carried the excavated thigh bone artifact with Vardis through snow. Poked Thistle about working with dragons. Decided immediately to retrieve the gong artifact; noted it would pair with an existing "bong" and joked about "the Gong Show." Agreed with Bressik's theory that the real artifact is beneath the altar. Negotiated with Delsephine to purchase Bressik's living contract, surprised to learn it would free him entirely. Agreed to Unla Kee's deal structure with Delsephine.

**Battle against giant lords:**
Survived with grim satisfaction. Looted systematically: Count Vaal (diamond-studded platinum earring, platinum vest, teleportation conch, potion of water breathing); Duke Tartha/Fire Giant (gilded dragon's teeth necklace, conch); Frost Giant Jarl Hellenhild (white dragon scale cloak, conch); Stone Giant Thane Braxow (15 gemstones from flesh, ~12,000 gp total). Led effort to hide the bodies before Mirran's descent. Overheard Mirran and Nin's exchange. Summarized the Maelstrom political situation clearly during planning (Serissa as queen, undermined by Mirran and Nym; their mother sought small-folk accommodation). Proposed full frontal assault; backed down when Vardis pointed out not everyone has a death wish. His original plan had been to pose as a matchmaker-sent suitor for Serissa. Swam into sinkhole tunnels after discovering crabs had stolen ship treasure.

**Crab and roper fight (sinkhole):**
Initiated the crab fight. Killed the last crab with a thrown hand axe. Recovered gold, a goblet, a Magma Warpick, and Weaving Wasp Needles. Declared no treasure for those who opposed the fight (exempted Vardis). Skeptical of Thistle's assessment of the coral. Killed the roper with a sword strike. Currently ascending the spiral staircase.

**Castle Naerytar / Skyreach Castle:**
Ordered collection and disposal of skeletal remains; emptied a bag of holding and dumped them overboard. Struck wyverns with his green blade. Dissuaded Unla Kee from using the Wand of Wonder twice. Found 120 gold and sweets in saddlebags. Correctly identified the spectral raven as the real threat. Struck it first but failed to kill it. Subsequently charmed, stunned, and prone after specters drained his life force.

**Recovery and Rezmir's room:**
Philosophized while stunned about the limitations of being a fighter with enhanced intelligence. Broke free when a fireball killed the remaining undead. Found a bottle of Blackstaff wine and explained its origins knowledgeably. Found Rezmir's protest note about internal Cult of the Dragon audits. Found nothing of value in Rezmir's room. Intervened in the Escarlotta/Blagothkus standoff with a conciliatory argument — successfully brokered Blagothkus's agreement to help defeat Sandesyl.

**Bob Saget plan and zombie battle:**
Authored a sealed letter proposing using Planar Ally to summon Bob Saget instead of freeing the dracolich, revealing he had previously arranged for Bob to be sent to the Astral Plane (not killed) and allowed Bob's drow allies to escape. Tabled the plan after valid objections but intends to revisit it. Also proposed double-crossing Bob by giving him the phylactery and sending the dracolich to retrieve it. During zombie battle: managed Unla Kee's spell choices, dumped two gallons of mayonnaise on a zombie via decanter, goaded Blagothkus about his son's ghost. Unaffected by Glazhael's dragon fear; retreated to cover. Shouted that Bob was still an option as Glazhael fled.

**Current conspiracy discussion:**
Suspicious of Thistle's claim nothing valuable was found in the Nether Mountains. Shut down Unla Kee's suggestion to consult the SEER. Clarified to Pow that Bob has taken a leave of absence, not disappeared.

**Ship battle and Maelstrom throne room:**
Threw axes at the wizard at the outset (hit once). Commanded Vardis to "light them up." Directed the party to free Hekaton and use the Conch. Realized mid-combat that only magical weapons can break the chains. Broke one chain while frozen by prismatic spray. Knocked unconscious by cone of cold. Revived by Thistle's goodberry; used the Conch to teleport the group out. In the throne room, ran after the King, attempted to look dashing for Serissa. Unilaterally declined Hekaton's alliance offer without consulting the party.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Maelstrom throne room, healed, following the rescue of King Hekaton
- **Active:** Unilaterally declined Hekaton's alliance offer on the party's behalf (prompting silent disagreement); has tabled but not abandoned the Bob Saget/dracolich plan; intends to pursue the iron tower near the Ice Spires tied to his family heirloom map
- **What the party knows:** He leads decisively, sometimes unilaterally; possesses the headband of intellect; owns the Talking Troll in Triboar; is motivated by the heirloom map
- **What remains hidden:** The full extent of his arrangement regarding Bob Saget (that he deliberately sent Bob to the Astral Plane and freed his drow allies); Fzoul Slovis's intention to recruit him to the Zhentarim; the specific acts that made him unemployable as a guard

## Relationships
- **Vardis (party):** Closest companion; they travel and think together, share discoveries, carry artifacts together, and team up to tease Harshnag. Vardis is one of the few people Orsik consistently defers to.
- **Thistle (party):** Frequently at odds — Thistle caught him with the headbands, Orsik has called his work "not your best" and been visibly dismissive of his suggestions. Functional but low-trust.
- **Unla Kee (party):** Orsik manages her spell use with visible frustration but respects her legal maneuvering; she revived him at a critical moment.
- **Wallace (party/former):** A subordinate figure who Orsik treated as overconfident but ultimately approved of; Wallace has since departed to serve as guard captain.
- **Hsing (pseudodragon):** Distrusts her explicitly. She inadvertently validated his concerns about the SEER during the headband confrontation.
- **The SEER:** Deeply distrusted; their confrontation ended in a workable détente after she agreed to perform the ritual publicly. Orsik is not reconciled, merely satisfied.
- **Chandra (guard):** A guard who pressured him during the headband confrontation but later came around to his perspective.
- **Harshnag:** Interacts with irreverent familiarity; mocks him openly, but relies on his knowledge and strength.
- **Cindell Trueshot (cleric, Triboar):** Negotiated with her for raise dead and Speak with Dead; attempted to cheat her on a ruby's value; invited her to the Talking Troll for business reasons.
- **Jarlor and Larrish:** Men he knew from the Parnast town council; discovering their families were taken as hostages provoked visible murderous rage.
- **Fzoul Slovis (Zhentarim):** Has flagged Orsik as a recruitment target; Orsik is unaware.
- **Bob Saget:** Deliberately arranged for Bob to be sent to the Astral Plane rather than killed; considers him a resource and potential asset, though the party has rejected his Bob-based plans.
- **Serissa (Giant Queen):** He is aware she will someday be Queen and has attempted to appear dashing in her presence; his original plan was to approach her posing as a suitor.
- **Bressik:** Recently recruited (or freed) through a deal with Delsephine; Orsik wanted him for their show.

## Arc Score Events
- **Increase:** Confronting the SEER publicly rather than acting unilaterally with the headband; completing his self-described redemption arc in Parnast; sparing the orc children and the Claw of Luthic; successfully brokering peace between Escarlotta and Blagothkus
- **Decrease:** Attempting to deceive the group by swapping the headband; announcing the loot before taking it (minor); unilaterally declining Hekaton's offer without consulting the party; deliberately concealing Bob Saget's survival and the escape of his drow allies from the group
