# Oblivillish

## Identity
- **Role:** Royal Pixie; Zhentarim intelligence asset; Chief of Staff to Prince Thornacious (reinstated)
- **Faction:** Seelie Court / Prince Thornacious's court; secretly working for the Zhentarim
- **First Appearance:** Introduced as the subject of a Zhentarim mission delivered to Orsik — the party was tasked with locating him and returning him to Thornacious's service. First met in person at the edge of the Forgotten Forest, where he announced himself by calling out to Thistle, recognizing him as the nephew of Uncle Joon.

*Also known as: Sir Oblivillish, Oblivilish.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Restore his standing in Prince Thornacious's court with glory; resume his Zhentarim service from a position of influence; survival and a desire for belonging after successive exiles.
- Pompous and self-important, with a strong aversion to anything beneath his perceived station — he was appalled at the mere suggestion of groveling, though he eventually attempted it awkwardly.
- Capable of sharp political insight when it serves his interests, and quick to embrace deception when it benefits him.
- Prone to dramatic emotional swings: he wanted Blasius dead moments before embracing him as a brother.
- Contemptuous of Prince Thornacious, whom he openly mocks as a pompous fool for pretending his own exile from the Royal Court was voluntary.
- Carries deep shame over his broken curse, which he nonetheless demonstrated openly to the party — suggesting a degree of disarming candor beneath his apparent humiliation.
- Frames his history with a flair for dramatic self-pity ("double exile").
- Implied (per Blassios's internal monologue) to be capable of and willing to enact revenge against those who wrong him.

## History with the Party

1. **Mission briefing (pre-contact):** The Zhentarim tasked Orsik with finding Oblivillish, who had gone missing from Thornacious's court. His status and location were unknown.

2. **Found in exile (first contact):** The party located Oblivillish in exile at the edge of the Forgotten Forest, having been banished after killing and eating a stag belonging to Prince Thornacious (he was unaware the stag was the Prince's; it later emerged Blasius had falsely accused him). Sir Oblivillish approached the party after the goblin skirmish, identifying himself to Thistle by invoking Uncle Joon's name. He explained his curse — loss of voluntary invisibility, replaced by an involuntary, broken cycling between visible and invisible states — and the circumstances behind it: killing the stag after two days without food.

3. **Identity confirmed:** Thistle recognized him as a former Thornacious courtier and Zhentarim spy. Orsik, prompted by Wallace to reread a Zhentarim notice, realized Sir Oblivillish was the asset he was assigned to protect — having nearly killed him moments before.

4. **Infiltration of Gralm's camp:** Oblivillish donned goblin attire and helped the party bluff their way into Gralm's encampment under Thistle's cover story. Inside, he quietly briefed Thistle on the political stakes — Thornacious had overextended into Gralm's territory, and the music problem needed resolving to secure Gralm's alliance against Bad Fruul.

5. **Confrontation with Blasius:** At the pond, Oblivillish recognized Blasius and reacted with fury, demanding the party attack him. Unla Kee put him to sleep. While unconscious, Blasius revealed *he* had falsely accused Oblivillish — drunk, he'd told Thornacious that Oblivillish killed the stag.

6. **Reconciliation:** Upon waking, Oblivillish pivoted immediately from murder-lust to tearful reunion, calling Blasius "dear friend" and "brother." He then pivoted again to political maneuvering, commanding Thistle to kill the Unseelie Dryads — framing it as Seelie duty and as the act that would restore his position at court.

7. **Battle and reinstatement:** During the fight, Oblivillish claimed the lion and bear had eaten the stag and that he had been framed. After the battle, Blasius credited Oblivillish with commanding the heroes against the Unseelie — confirmed by the party. Prince Thornacious reinstated him as **Chief of Staff**. He celebrated by attempting to hug every party member; all rejected him.

8. **Post-reinstatement friction:** At Thornacious's camp, when Thistle referred to him as a "hunting master," Oblivillish erupted — taking it as a renewed accusation regarding the stag. He flatly denied being a hunter to Prince Thornacious's face, effectively killing Thistle's pitch. The mission succeeded regardless through Thornacious's own judgment.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Prince Thornacious's camp, serving as Chief of Staff
- **Active operations:** Resumed position in Thornacious's court; Zhentarim asset status presumably reactivated (from the Zhentarim's perspective, mission accomplished)
- **What the party knows:** He is a Zhentarim agent embedded in Thornacious's court; his exile was Blasius's fault, not his own wrongdoing; his curse makes him involuntarily and unpredictably visible/invisible.
- **What remains hidden:** The full scope of his Zhentarim operations and what intelligence he reports; whether Thornacious knows or suspects his dual allegiance; the specifics of the "terrible misunderstanding" framing of his original expulsion.

## Relationships
- **Prince Thornacious:** Liege and employer; Oblivillish is fiercely protective of his standing at court and deeply status-conscious in this relationship — and openly contemptuous of Thornacious's pretensions.
- **Blasius / Blassios:** Old friend who falsely caused his banishment. After initial murderous fury, reconciled warmly — "dear friend," "brother." Now relying on Blasius to credit him with the Unseelie victory. Per Blassios's internal monologue, Blassios privately fears Oblivillish is engineering revenge against him for an unspecified past indiscretion.
- **The Zhentarim:** Handler/employer operating in the background; Oblivillish's true loyalties are split.
- **Thistle:** Tense relationship. Oblivillish respects Thistle's Seelie lineage (and recognized him via Uncle Joon's name) enough to issue commands to him, but bristles at any perceived slight or implication involving the stag incident.
- **Uncle Joon:** Some pre-existing connection through the family tie that Oblivillish used to identify himself to Thistle.
- **Orsik:** Tasked with finding him; nearly killed him first; attempted post-battle hug, rejected.
- **Wallace:** Indirectly saved Sir Oblivillish's life by prompting Orsik to reread the Zhentarim notice before acting. Attempted post-battle hug, rejected.
- **Vardis, Unla Kee:** Attempted post-battle hugs; all rejected. Unla Kee notably put him to sleep during the Blasius confrontation.
- **Queen Tatiana:** The Queen who originally exiled Thornacious, setting the chain of events in motion.

## Arc Score Events
- **Positive:** Party confirmed Blasius's account to Thornacious, directly enabling his reinstatement as Chief of Staff.
- **Negative:** Thistle's accidental "hunting master" remark reopened the stag wound and caused a public scene at Thornacious's camp.
