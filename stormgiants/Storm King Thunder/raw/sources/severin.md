# Severin

## Identity
- **Role:** Former leader of the Cult of the Dragon; leader of the Tiamat-summoning faction within the cult
- **Faction:** Cult of the Dragon (reformist/usurper faction)
- **First appearance:** Never appears directly; introduced through NPC testimony across multiple sessions

## Personality & Motivations
- Drove the Cult of the Dragon away from its founding purpose of creating Dracoliches and toward the summoning of Tiamat into the material world, which he believed would resolve the eternal dragon conflict.
- Characterized by multiple sources as a fool, a usurper, and a manipulated figure — though whether his intentions were sincere or corrupted remains debated at the table.
- Trusted Adimond Kroskas to provide critical magical support, which Kroskas failed to deliver. This reliance contributed to the ritual's failure.

## History with the Party
- **Never directly encountered.** All information comes through NPC accounts:
  - **Delsephine** condemns him as a fool who betrayed the cult's divine purpose and placed unwarranted trust in Kroskas.
  - **Bressik** frames Severin's path as an ideological coup against Sammaster's original Dracolich vision, enabled by manipulation from Tiamat or another devil. Bressik argues Severin's approach would have prolonged the dragon conflict rather than ended it.
  - **Sandesyl Morgia** states he perverted everything she believed in, hired Adimond Kroskas to create a fey hybrid dragon, and was part of a larger plot connected to Zariel. She made a clean break from his faction.
  - **Glazhael** was personally hurt by Severin's declaration that a Dracolich could not serve the cult, driving a wedge between Glazhael and Severin's faction.
  - Severin appointed **Talis** as the new White Wyrmspeaker after her predecessor was captured, and attempted to stabilize a fractured cult by offering the Blue Dragon mask to a traditionalist faction for investigation.
  - Severin made the deal with **Tiamat** directly — identified as the actual devil in the situation.

## Current Status
- **Presumed dead or deposed.** The implication from Delsephine's account and the failure of Tiamat's manifestation is that Severin is no longer in power. No source confirms his exact fate.
- His ritual ultimately failed.
- **What the party knows:** Severin led the Tiamat summoning effort, manipulated or corrupted by Tiamat/Zariel, trusted Kroskas, alienated traditionalist cult members, and is likely gone.
- **What remains hidden:** His exact fate, whether he acted with full knowledge of Zariel's involvement, and whether any remnant of his faction remains organized.

## Relationships
- **Adimond Kroskas** — Trusted to provide powerful magic; failed to deliver, contributing to ritual's collapse
- **Talis** — Appointed by Severin as White Wyrmspeaker
- **Glazhael** — Alienated by Severin's declaration that Dracoliches had no place in the cult
- **Sandesyl Morgia** — Broke from his faction; views him as a perverter of cult ideology
- **Delsephine** — Condemns him outright
- **Bressik** — Views him as an ideological usurper, not a true cultist
- **Tiamat / Zariel** — Made a direct deal with Tiamat; described as part of a larger plot connected to Zariel
