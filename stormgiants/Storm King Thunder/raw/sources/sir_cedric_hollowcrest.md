# Sir Cedric Hollowcrest

## Identity
- **Role:** Captain of the Silverymoon gate guard
- **Faction:** Silverymoon city guard
- **First Appearance:** Silverymoon city gate — summoned by a subordinate after Orsik intimidated the guard questioning the party's entry

## Personality & Motivations
- An enthusiastic admirer of Bob Saget's theatrical work, which he considers divinely inspired
- Deferential and eager to please when dealing with notable figures; quick to apologize for any slight
- No deeper motivations established beyond his role as gate captain and his clear fandom of the 3HPP

## History with the Party
- **Session [gate arrival]:** Summoned to the gate after Orsik's confrontation with a subordinate guard. Immediately recognized the party as the 3HPP, named each member by their theatrical epithet ("the redeemed Orsik," "the brokenhearted Thistle," "the honest Vardis"), praised Bob Saget's play as divinely inspired, and waved the party through without further delay.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Silverymoon city gate
- **Active Plans:** None established
- **Party Knowledge:** Knows who the party is, holds them in high regard, and is familiar with Bob Saget's work. No hidden information on record.

## Relationships
- **Bob Saget:** Deep admirer; considers his theatrical portrayal of the 3HPP to be a divine work
- **The Party (3HPP):** Starstruck respect for all three members; knows them by their stage epithets
- **Subordinate gate guard:** Commands him; was called in to resolve the confrontation Orsik caused
