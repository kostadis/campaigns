# Blooddrinker

## Identity
- Role: Animal companion (bear, magically reduced to cat size)
- Former owner: Slayermighty
- First appearance: Encountered alongside Slayermighty inside the cursed cave; shrunken to cat size by the cave's magical curse

## Personality & Motivations
- Despite being a bear, Blooddrinker behaves with surprising docility — after Slayermighty's death, he rolled onto his belly expecting pets rather than acting ferociously. His instinct appears to be affection-seeking rather than aggression.

## History with the Party
- Was present when Slayermighty was killed; did not retaliate or pose a threat to the party.
- When Thistle, Orsik, and Vardis opened the stone chest, Blooddrinker began convulsing — Thistle intervened quickly and saved his life.
- Bonded immediately to Thistle following the rescue.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** In Thistle's care
- **Condition:** Alive, cat-sized, bonded to Thistle
- **Hidden information:** The cause of his convulsing episode (tied to the stone chest opening) may not be fully understood by the party

## Relationships
- **Thistle:** New bond-companion; Blooddrinker attached to Thistle after being saved
- **Slayermighty:** Former companion, now deceased
- **Orsik & Vardis:** Present at the chest opening; no noted individual relationship
