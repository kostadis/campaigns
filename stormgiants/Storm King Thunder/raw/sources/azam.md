# Azam

## Identity
- **Role:** Merchant, caravan leader, agent of the Lord's Alliance, operative of the SEER
- **Faction:** Lord's Alliance
- **First Appearance:** Met the party at a tent tavern in Vuerthyl, where he was stranded and seeking guards for a statue delivery to Parnast

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Complete contracted deliveries reliably; serve the SEER and advance Lord's Alliance interests; recruit capable agents to the faction
- Pragmatic and professionally skeptical — he initially wished a faction agent had been sent instead of the party, but updates his assessments based on evidence
- Carries himself with measured authority while deferring to Hsing on key decisions, suggesting layered chains of command in his operations
- Becomes anxious when uncertain of outcomes; he spent two weeks in Parnast convinced the party had failed before being relieved by news of their return

## History with the Party

**Vuerthyl (Session 1):**
Met the party at the tavern, directed to them by a Dragonborn waiter. Hired them as guards for a statue delivery — a five-foot-tall, four-foot-wide religious statue of Angharradh, frustratingly large and heavy. Consulted Hsing before allowing the party to examine the statue. Visibly unsettled when Thistle asked about a Drake named Thule, deferring the response entirely to Hsing. Offered future work from Hsing contingent on successful delivery.

**Black Road Caravan:**
Led a four-wagon caravan carrying food, water, medicinal herbs, weapons, and the statue. Was asleep during a goblin attack but witnessed the combat from his tent. Documented the party in his personal journal as sunburned, exhausted, and raggedy — but revised his assessment upward after they successfully repelled the raid.

**Parnast, First Meeting:**
Appeared with Hsing in the back room of the Golden Tankard. No significant actions recorded beyond his presence.

**Taraskh — Session 10-02 (back room of the Golden Tankard):**
Met the party to brief and contract them on behalf of the Lord's Alliance. Was left speechless when Thistle pushed for a higher reward and lost his temper when Thistle questioned Hsing's honor — earning a freeze-in-place from Hsing and a firm order to sit down. Briefed the party on the mission to the Ettin Gralm in the Lonely Moor, explaining the two magical contracts and the stakes involving Bad Fruul and Parnast. Negotiated a private side arrangement with Unla Kee (terms unknown to the rest of the party). Admitted he had misread his notes and owed the party a potion of healing and a potion of greater healing for their conduct during the festival attack.

**Parnast, Festival of Greengrass:**
Had waited two weeks, growing increasingly convinced the party had failed. Greeted their return in the main square. Gave a magical potion and a pouch of gold to Unla Kee as contracted reward. Impressed by Unla Kee's honesty in accurately reporting a 200 GP deal rather than pocketing the difference — made a recruitment pitch for the Lord's Alliance on the spot. Described the Alliance as the only faction devoted to order, centralization, and safety in the North; dismissed the Harpers as anarchists, Zhentarim as trade monopolists, Order of the Gauntlet as paranoid, and Emerald Enclave as anti-development. Unla Kee declined with a burp.

**Parnast, Outside the Golden Tankard:**
Ran after the departing party to deliver a forgotten message: Thistle's Uncle Joon is in Triboar (~400 miles away). Joon did not join the Cult of the Dragon but has joined an unknown secretive organization — one the SEER has no information on. Sensing the party might leave Parnast to pursue Joon, Azam conveyed an urgent message from the SEER: defending Parnast from Bad Fruul is the Lord's Alliance's paramount concern, and success would earn the party the support of a powerful personage. Promised the Alliance would track Joon's movements. His intervention convinced the party to stay.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Outside the Golden Tankard, Parnast
- **Active:** Coordinating Lord's Alliance interests in Parnast; monitoring the threat of Bad Fruul; tracking Joon's movements in Triboar on behalf of the party
- **What the Party Knows:** He is a Lord's Alliance agent working under the SEER; he has intelligence networks capable of locating individuals like Joon
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full nature of his relationship with the SEER; the depth of his operational role beyond merchant cover

## Relationships
- **Hsing (pseudodragon):** Azam's superior or patron figure in practice — he defers to Hsing for decisions and seeks Hsing's approval, despite appearing to be the public face of the operation
- **Unla Kee:** Impressed by his honesty; attempted to recruit him to the Lord's Alliance (rebuffed)
- **Thistle:** Delivered sensitive personal intelligence about Uncle Joon; aware of Thistle's connection to Thule (reacted with visible alarm when the name was raised)
- **Ragnar & Elspeth:** Attended Azam during his two-week wait in Parnast
- **Bad Fruul:** Identified as a significant threat to Parnast — Azam is invested in the party stopping him

## Arc Score Events
- **Increase:** Unla Kee's honest reporting of the 200 GP deal prompted visible approval and a direct recruitment offer
- **Increase:** Party's successful defense against the goblin raid on the caravan moved Azam from skepticism to grudging respect
- **Neutral/Ongoing:** Unla Kee's refusal of the Alliance recruitment pitch — Azam did not appear to take offense, but the door may remain open
