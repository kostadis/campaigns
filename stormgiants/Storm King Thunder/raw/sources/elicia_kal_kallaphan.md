# Elicia "Kal" Kallaphan

## Identity
- **Role:** Bounty payout handler / operative
- **Faction:** The Hand of Yartar
- **First Appearance:** Met the party when they came to collect on bounties; she handled the payout directly.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals/drives:** Unclear beyond her operational role managing bounty payments for the Hand of Yartar.
- Severe, humorless, and perceptive — she sees through the party's attempts at levity without reaction. Projects a no-nonsense professionalism consistent with someone who handles sensitive financial transactions for a criminal or mercenary organization.

## History with the Party
- **First and only recorded interaction:** Paid out 200 gold total for two bounties — 150 gold to the party, 50 gold retained for herself. The exchange was transactional; the party's attempts at humor were met with flat indifference.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Yartar (implied by faction affiliation).
- **Active plans:** Unknown beyond her handler role.
- **What the party knows:** She works for the Hand of Yartar and pays out bounties. She takes a cut.
- **What remains hidden:** Her full role within the organization, her loyalties, and whether she tracks or monitors those she pays.

## Relationships

- **The Party:** Purely transactional so far. No warmth established.
