# Maergun

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Fomorian warlord; affiliate of the Unseelie Court; ruler of the dungeon beneath his throne room
- **Faction:** Independent (Fomorian); aligned with the Unseelie Court (in exile or refuge); antagonist against the party and Queen Titania's interests
- **First Appearance:** Introduced as the primary mission target via Titania's Sprite emissary; first encountered in person in his throne room, which served as the climactic battleground

*Also known as: Maegurn.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Dominion over his lair and its occupants; hoarding wealth beneath his throne.
- Has crossed a line even by Fomorian standards — moved from the culturally accepted "bully" role into genuine abuse of the goblins under his influence. This behavioral shift has alienated potential allies and united even goblin factions against him, suggesting either a loss of control or a deliberate escalation.
- Maergun displayed genuine emotional attachment to his displacer beast companion, Haerbega, reacting with outrage at its death ("You killed my puppy!"). He demonstrated cruel pride and brute aggression in combat, relying on supernatural abilities (evil eye) and physical strength (club). His brief humanizing moment around Haerbega suggests he was not entirely without sentiment.

## History with the Party

**Pre-confrontation (Titania's task):** The party was tasked by Titania (via Sprite emissary) to investigate and deal with Maergun. During exploration of the Feywild cave complex, Tlega briefed the party on Maegurn's breakdown of the traditional Fomorian/goblin social dynamic, explaining why even local goblin factions were motivated to see him removed. A Cyclops was observed guarding the cave entrance, confirming Maegurn commanded at least some loyal followers.

**Confrontation (throne room):**
- The party entered Maergun's throne room to find Drauchanar suspended from a chain.
- Maergun commanded a cyclops and the displacer beast Haerbega as guardians.
- Vardis killed Haerbega with a Fireball; Maergun responded furiously, revealing the creature's name after Unla Kee's taunting suggestions of "Fluffy" and "Spiky."
- Maergun attempted an evil eye on Vardis, who avoided the worst effects, then struck Vardis with his club.
- The party killed Maergun through combined efforts — a cantrip, a goblin arrow, and devastating strikes from Orsik using the Giant Sword of Lord Zymorven — with Vardis delivering the killing blow via cantrip.
- Orsik searched the body and recovered an iron key, which unlocked a secret staircase to the treasure vault beneath the throne.

## Current Status
- **Status:** **Dead** — killed by the party
- **Last Known Location:** His throne room
- **Hoard (recovered):** Turquoise figurines, brass crown, ivory statuette, tapestry depicting elves feasting, mass of coins, potion of greater healing, potion of water breathing
- His death triggered a reward from Queen Titania, delivered via sprite

## Relationships
- **Drauchanar:** Held captive in Maergun's throne room, suspended from a chain.
- **Haerbega (displacer beast):** Beloved pet/guardian; Maergun's grief at its death was immediate and genuine.
- **Cyclops:** Served as a guardian (cave entrance, then throne room).
- **Queen Titania:** Antagonistic — Titania considered him a problem significant enough to dispatch the party; his death pleased her enough to send a sprite reward.
- **Goblins (local factions):** Formerly in a tolerated bully/bullied dynamic; openly wanted him gone due to his abusive conduct.
- **The Party:** Lethal enemies; killed by Vardis, Orsik, and party allies.
