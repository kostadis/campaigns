# Harriet Uldinath

## Identity
- **Role:** Proprietor of Uldinath's Arms, a smithy in Triboar; runs the shop designated T17
- **Faction:** Independent merchant; rival of Forehammer's Forge
- **First Appearance:** The party visited her shop while investigating the origin of a grappling hook (recovered from the Lion's Share vault breach)

*Also known as: Harriet.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Warm and community-connected; has deep personal roots in Triboar's craftsman community.
- Her rivalry with Forehammer's Forge appears lighthearted rather than hostile — she speaks fondly of Ghelryn despite (or through) gentle mockery.
- Candid and self-aware — readily admitted the grappling hook exceeded her own craftsmanship rather than claiming credit or deflecting suspiciously. Willing to point the party toward a competitor, suggesting either honesty or a desire to redirect scrutiny.

## History with the Party
- The party showed Harriet a grappling hook for identification (recovered from the Lion's Share vault breach). She confirmed it was not her work and acknowledged it was of superior quality to anything she could produce. She identified the maker as **Ghelryn** of Forehammer's Forge, vouched for his character, and directed the party to his shop. Her comment that he is "a lousy smith" was noted as affectionate teasing.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Uldinath's Arms (T17), Triboar
- No active plans involving the party. She provided the lead they needed and sent them on their way.
- **Party knows:** She is a smith, knows Ghelryn personally since childhood, denied involvement in the vault-breach hook, and is not the maker of the hook.
- **Hidden:** Whether her denial is genuine; any deeper knowledge of the vault breach or its perpetrators (nothing else flagged).

## Relationships
- **Ghelryn Foehammer:** Childhood acquaintance and competitor; speaks of him with genuine fondness despite professional rivalry; identified him as capable of crafting the grappling hook.
- **The Party:** Brief, cooperative interaction; no ongoing relationship established.
