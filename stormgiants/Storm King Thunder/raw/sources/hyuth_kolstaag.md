# Hyuth Kolstaag

## Identity
- Retired adventurer, no current faction affiliation; resident of Triboar (lives in a large mansion)
- First appeared (end of session) when the party gained entry to his mansion under the pretense of being roofers sent by the Lord Protector; extensively discussed beforehand by Cindrell and Urgala, and held up by Unla Kee as an aspirational figure for post-retirement life

*Also known as: Hyuth.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goals: self-preservation at all costs; avoiding his old enemies; apparently motivated by money despite his paranoia.
- Arrogantly antisocial — has rejected all civic invitations and refused to compensate victims of collateral damage from an assassination attempt on his own life.
- Urgala, who knew him in his adventuring days, describes him as reliable in a fight but exhibiting "some pretty shitty behavior" in retirement.
- Deeply paranoid: he screeched at the party from inside his own home before the mention of free roof repairs persuaded him to open the door.
- As characterized by Unla Kee: self-centered, obsessed with his own importance, places his needs above everyone else's; involves himself in manic plots to destabilize his local community. Unla Kee views these traits with admiration and considers Hyuth a personal role model for post-retirement life.

## History with the Party
- **Prior to session:** Several weeks before the party's arrival, two assassins infiltrated Triboar and attempted to kill Hyuth. The fight produced thunder and lightning, destroyed his roof and study, and ended with the assassins fleeing — but not before stealing a horse from the Cart and Coin and murdering the stable hand. Hyuth declined to compensate the inn's owners or contribute to Cindrell's luncheon for the dead stable hand's family. He subsequently summoned gargoyles to guard himself and sent no word to the stable hand's relatives.
- **This session:** The party approached his mansion posing as roofers commissioned by the Lord Protector. Hyuth was initially paranoid and hostile, refusing entry. He relented when told Darathra was covering the cost of repairs — apparently the financial incentive outweighed his suspicion. The party is now inside his damaged second-floor study.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** His damaged mansion in Triboar; second-floor study, with the party present
- **Active situation:** Party gained entry under false pretenses; true purpose is to deliver Darathra's message compelling him toward better civic behavior
- **What the party knows:** He's paranoid, antisocial, has gargoyle guards, made enemies during his adventuring career, and can be moved by financial incentive
- **What remains hidden:** The identity and origin of the assassins; why they targeted him; the full extent of his adventuring history and enemies

## Relationships
- **Darathra Shendrel (Lord Protector):** Intends to use the Cindrell contract as leverage — will warn him the next attempt may not reach her desk in time, and that good behavior earns him protection of the 12
- **Cindrell:** Considers him a social nuisance; he has rejected all her invitations; she provided the party's initial briefing on him
- **Urgala:** Knows him from his adventuring days; corroborated Cindrell's account; disapproves of his current behavior but acknowledges his past reliability in combat
- **Cart and Coin owners:** Aggrieved — he refused to compensate them for the stolen horse or the dead stable hand
- **The 3HPP:** Currently inside his home under false pretenses; relationship adversarial-to-neutral so far.
- **Unla Kee:** Holds Hyuth in high regard; views his lifestyle as a model to emulate if he survives the current crisis.
