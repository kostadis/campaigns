# Fruf

## Identity
- **Role:** Dwarf miner, laborer
- **Faction:** Mirabar mining workforce
- **First Appearance:** Encountered by Thistle inside the Mirabar mining lodge, in the large heated hall during a post-shift meal among roughly 200 workers.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Goals/Drives:** Not established beyond ordinary working life.
- **Traits:** Practical and good-natured. When faced with a request he didn't understand, he defaulted to helpfulness — offering what he had rather than simply turning Thistle away.

## History with the Party
- Thistle approached Fruf in the mining lodge asking for wing muffs. Fruf did not understand the request but offered a pair of warm, poofy extra socks as a substitute. This interaction indirectly prompted Orsik to agree to craft something for Thistle's wings.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Mirabar mining lodge, eating hall.
- **Active Plans:** None known; presumably continuing normal mining work.
- **Known vs. Hidden:** The party knows him only as a helpful stranger. Nothing further is established.

## Relationships
- **Thistle:** Brief, friendly interaction; offered socks in lieu of wing muffs.
- **Orsik:** No direct interaction, but Fruf's inadvertent role in the wing muff situation nudged Orsik toward offering to craft for Thistle.

## Arc Score Events
- None recorded.
