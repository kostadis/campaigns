# Mama Manyknuckles

## Identity
- **Role:** Hag; warlord-figure leading a band of half-ogres in the marsh
- **Faction:** One of two major powers in the marsh's ongoing civil war; allied with Bad Fruul; some mud mephits backed her against the water weird's faction
- **First Appearance:** Discussed extensively by Feetor before the party located her island; encountered directly at the kidney-shaped island in the marsh

## Personality & Motivations
- **Goals:** Control of the marsh through her half-ogre gang; served Bad Fruul's broader operation, though she harbored fatalism about that alliance
- Tactically cunning — she detected the party's approach, attempted to redirect her guards quietly, and tried to manipulate her mephit allies before ordering an ambush when flanked
- Her dying thoughts suggest she never fully believed in Bad Fruul's cause and considered her violent end inevitable; she blamed Bad Fruul rather than the party for her fate
- Feetor described her as "particularly ugly" even by half-ogre standards; she inspired mockery toward him, suggesting a cruel and contemptuous social manner

## History with the Party
1. **Pre-encounter (Session 1 reference):** Feetor describes Mama Manyknuckles as a dangerous marsh power and urges the party to kill her to end the civil war and consolidate mephit support. Thistle locates her island within three hours using mephit intelligence.
2. **Ambush at the kidney-shaped island:** Mama Manyknuckles detects the party's approach and attempts a quiet counter — repositioning guards, whispering to unresponsive mud mephits, and hoping the party would leave. When they flanked south, she triggered an ambush.
3. **The confrontation:** The ambush surprised and frightened the party. She used her death gaze to kill **Orsik**, who failed to look away in time. Despite this, her half-ogres and companion **Belg** were defeated. She attempted to flee and was cut down.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Cut down while attempting to flee after her gang was destroyed.
- **Loot recovered:** A stone pendant with a jade inlay of **Stein**, the stone rune.

## Relationships
- **Bad Fruul:** Employer/patron. She had signed her gang to his cause but privately believed it was a mistake and blamed him for her end.
- **Her half-ogres / Belg:** Her fighting force; all defeated alongside her.
- **Mud mephits:** Some factions supported her in the marsh civil war, but during the ambush they ignored her whispered commands, visibly frustrating her.
- **Feetor:** Antagonistic. He and his mephits were mocked and attacked by her gang. He actively advocated for her death.
- **Orsik:** Killed by her death gaze.

## Arc Score Events
- **Death of Orsik:** Mama Manyknuckles successfully killed a party member with her death gaze — significant table impact.
- **Defeat and death:** Eliminated as a faction power; the marsh civil war presumably shifts in the water weird's (or the party's) favor.
