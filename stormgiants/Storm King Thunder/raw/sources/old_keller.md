# Old Keller

## Identity
- **Role:** Dwarf stonemason, resident of Llorkh
- **Faction:** None noted; holds strong personal dwarven cultural values
- **First Appearance:** Referenced before being encountered directly; the party had a prior negative interaction with him involving an incident at a keep — described by Keller as "a disgrace to all dwarves, committed in a dwarf's place of work."

## Personality & Motivations
- Holds dwarven honor and workplace conduct as deeply important values.
- Pragmatic and blunt — willing to invoke dwarf solidarity in principle but draws a firm line when he believes that solidarity has been violated.
- Not permanently closed off; left open a conditional path to redemption, suggesting his rejection is principled rather than personal.

## History with the Party
- **Prior to tracked sessions:** The party (specifically Orsik) was involved in an unnamed incident at a keep that Keller viewed as a disgrace to dwarven people.
- **Session (first tracked encounter):** Orsik approached Keller's stonemason hut calling him "kinsman." Keller ordered him to stop and not come closer. When Orsik asked how to make amends, Keller told him to stop doing "that thing" and return only when he was no longer likely to be goblin food — at which point Keller would explain what redemption looks like.
- **Subsequent session:** Keller is not present but is referenced by Orsik during an emotional outburst at a dwarven outpost. Orsik cites Keller's rejection as a painful example of being treated as unworthy, connecting it to his broader feelings of exclusion from Clan Licehair.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** His stonemason's hut in Llorkh.
- **Active stance:** Refusing further engagement with Orsik and the party until Orsik demonstrably stops the behavior Keller considers a disgrace and proves himself capable of surviving long enough to matter.
- **What the party knows:** Keller is angry, has a specific grievance tied to the keep incident, and has set conditions for reconciliation.
- **What remains hidden:** The exact nature of what Keller considers "redemption." He has not disclosed this yet.

## Relationships
- **Orsik:** The primary relationship. Keller has not severed ties entirely but has set a hard conditional boundary. Orsik appears to hold Keller's opinion in high regard, making the rejection emotionally significant.
- **The Party (general):** No individual relationships noted beyond the shared prior incident.

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease (Orsik → Keller):** Prior incident at the keep damaged Keller's regard for Orsik before tracked sessions began.
- **No change in tracked sessions:** Keller's position held firm; no movement toward or away from reconciliation has occurred.
