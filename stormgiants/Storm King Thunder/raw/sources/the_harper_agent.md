# The Harper Agent (Unnamed)

## Identity
- Role: Harper messenger / field agent
- Faction: The Harpers (symbol displayed openly)
- Acting on behalf of: **Krowen Valharrow**
- First appearance: Approached the party (3HPP) in the evening in Silverymoon

## Personality & Motivations
- Functions as a bureaucratic enforcer of Harper policy — direct, critical, and uncompromising.
- Demonstrated disdain for the use of mercenaries over full Harper agents, suggesting professional pride or institutional loyalty.
- Willing to make pointed personal accusations (allying with cultists) when pressing his case.

## History with the Party
- Approached the 3HPP in Silverymoon on Krowen Valharrow's behalf to deliver a formal warning about the party's inaction on the Giant crisis.
- Cited two specific incidents as evidence of the threat: the near-sacking of Bryn Shander by Frost Giants and the Hill Giant attack on Goldenfields.
- Warned that the party's magical pathway access would lapse if they did not act immediately.
- Accused the party of allying with cultists.
- When Orsik refused his terms and ended the contract, the agent reclaimed the treasure maps and issued a parting criticism regarding the use of mercenaries, referencing someone named **Ezra** as the source of that policy.
- Departed Silverymoon after being dismissed by Orsik.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Departing Silverymoon
- Presumably reporting back to Krowen Valharrow that the contract with 3HPP has been terminated.
- No active operations involving the party.

## Relationships
- **Krowen Valharrow:** Direct superior; acting as his messenger and enforcer.
- **Ezra (unknown):** Referenced as the architect of the policy allowing mercenary use instead of full Harper agents — the agent disapproves.
- **The Party (3HPP):** Contract terminated on poor terms. Views them as negligent and potentially compromised by cultist ties.
- **Orsik specifically:** The one who formally ended the contract and dismissed him.
