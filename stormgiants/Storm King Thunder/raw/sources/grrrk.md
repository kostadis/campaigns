# Grrrk

## Identity
- Elder Galeb Duhr; part of the great pyramid formation in the Greypeak Mountains
- First appeared when the party arrived at the pyramid; initially unengaged until Thistle (the fairy) flew up and tickled his nose, prompting conversation

## Personality & Motivations
- Gruff, old-fashioned, and dismissive — particularly toward younger rocks' complaints, contrasting with the more optimistic Galeb Duhr Prrrt
- His core drive appears to be guardianship of or presence at the pyramid formation; he issues orders to the other Galeb Duhr and commands their positions
- Skeptical of outsiders' chances of survival, though capable of quiet, understated approval when proven wrong

## History with the Party
- Observed the party's arrival and their battle with badgers from his position in the formation
- Spoke with Thistle when she flew up and tickled his nose; shared intelligence about giants and kobolds massing in the area
- Predicted the party would be "zapped like the giant" attempting to reach the pyramid's top; when they successfully descended inside, offered quiet approval
- Called out a farewell as the party departed, then ordered the other Galeb Duhr back to their positions — at which point the entire formation went invisible

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The great pyramid formation, Greypeak Mountains
- **Active operations:** Maintaining watch over the pyramid with the Galeb Duhr formation; all currently invisible and in position
- **What the party knows:** He is an elder authority among the local Galeb Duhr and has observed significant giant and kobold activity nearby
- **Hidden:** His deeper motivations for guarding the pyramid, and what he knows beyond what he shared with Thistle

## Relationships
- **Prrrt:** A fellow Galeb Duhr in the formation; Grrrk contrasts sharply with Prrrt's more optimistic disposition
- **Thistle:** The only party member he directly conversed with; interaction was initiated by her and tolerated/accepted by him
- **The party broadly:** Assessed them as likely to die; revised opinion quietly when they proved capable
