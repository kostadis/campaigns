# Yip Hammaver

## Identity
- **Role:** Bachelor and social scion of the mercantile House Hammaver of Mirabar
- **Notable:** 32 years old; medium height; unusually red eyes
- **First Appearance:** A House Hammaver dinner arranged to entertain Orsik, who was operating under the alias *John Husteem of House Husteem of Waterdeep*

## Personality & Motivations
- Socially engaged, eager to play the role of insider guide and connector within Mirabar's noble circles
- Friendly and enthusiastic, though his read on people can be badly wrong (catastrophically misjudged Tack Proudfoot as a harmless dandy)
- Appears motivated by social networking and matchmaking; takes genuine delight in facilitating connections between people

## History with the Party
- **The Hammaver Dinner:** Yip befriended Orsik-as-John and served as his social guide through Mirabar nobility events
- **The Proudfoot Duel:** Yip advised John-Orsik that Tack Proudfoot was a romantic fool with no real fighting ability — Tack then appeared in elite guard armor, making a mockery of this assessment
- **Post-Duel:** When Orsik resolved the standoff nonviolently via *sanctuary*, Yip was visibly delighted; he subsequently offered to introduce the apparently single "John" to eligible noble bachelors and bachelorettes

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Mirabar
- **Active Plans:** Intends to set up "John Husteem" with romantic connections among Mirabar's noble houses
- **Party Knows:** Yip is friendly, well-connected, and socially well-meaning
- **Hidden from Party:** Nothing notable flagged in notes; Yip appears straightforwardly what he seems — though his social judgment is demonstrably unreliable

## Relationships
- **Orsik (as "John Husteem"):** Considers him a new friend; is actively invested in his social and romantic prospects in Mirabar; does not know Orsik's true identity
- **Tack Proudfoot:** Badly underestimated him; the misjudgment is on record with the party
- **House Hammaver:** Member in good standing; has enough standing to host and socially sponsor guests at nobility events
