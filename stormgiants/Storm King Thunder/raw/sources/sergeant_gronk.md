# Sergeant Gronk

## Identity
- **Role:** Goblin military commander; Cult of the Dragon (implied, guarding a Cult treasure cache)
- **Faction:** The Cult; assigned to hold the southernmost Greypeak mountain cave—site of the first Cult treasure cache
- **First Appearance:** Party reached the cave at the base of the southernmost Greypeak mountain and triggered his ambush

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Hold the defensive position and protect the treasure cache despite a badly degraded force
- Pragmatic and grim — he openly lamented the disaster the mission had become rather than pretending otherwise
- Tactically competent: organized remaining troops into cover-based formations with a prepared fallback route
- His final concern was for his buried giant rather than himself, suggesting loyalty to his soldiers

## History with the Party
1. **Pre-contact:** Gronk was managing a deteriorating situation — a magical curse had shrunk some troops to toy size, and one of his two giants had died in a cave-in, partially blocking the eastern tunnel with debris and dust
2. **Ambush:** When the party approached, Gronk set a successful ambush in the cavern, killing or dropping two party members including **Orsik**
3. **Death:** **Unla Key** cast Thunderwave, killing Gronk and two of his goblins in the same blast. His last thought was wondering whether the buried giant would recover

## Current Status
- **Status: DEAD** — killed in the cavern by Thunderwave
- The dead giant remains buried in the eastern tunnel; Gronk died uncertain whether it might recover
- The southern tunnel (his planned fallback escape route) was never used

## Relationships
- **His troops:** Demonstrated genuine concern for them; mourned losses and organized survivors carefully
- **The buried giant:** Loyalty extended to wondering about the giant's fate in his final moments
- **The party:** Enemy combatant; his ambush directly downed **Orsik** and at least one other party member
- **Unla Key:** His killer
