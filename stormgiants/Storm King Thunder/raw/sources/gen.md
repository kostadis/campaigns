# Gen

## Identity
- Kobold; companion and apparent subordinate to Agra
- First appeared hiding in the stone building at the abandoned dwarven outpost during a tense standoff with the party

## Personality & Motivations
- Impulsive and prone to rash action under pressure — Agra had to physically restrain him during the standoff to prevent provocation
- Despite his impulsiveness, Gen shows pragmatic thinking when it counts: he was the one who suggested offering treasure to secure the party's goodwill
- Emotionally dependent on Agra's leadership; when things looked dire, he turned to Agra with "what will we ever do?"

## History with the Party
- **Abandoned Dwarven Outpost:** Gen and Agra were discovered hiding in a stone building during the party's encounter. Agra clamped a hand over Gen's mouth to keep him from doing something rash. After a trap failed and the situation grew desperate, Gen suggested offering treasure. The party accepted, and both kobolds were allowed to leave unharmed.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Departed the abandoned dwarven outpost alive, alongside Agra
- No known active plans
- **What the party knows:** He is Agra's companion, impulsive, and was willing to trade treasure for safety
- **What remains hidden:** His background, the origin of his burn scars, and what business he and Agra had at the outpost

## Relationships
- **Agra:** Clear subordinate relationship; defers to Agra's judgment, relies on Agra emotionally, and is physically managed by Agra when his impulses get the better of him
- **The Party:** Departed on neutral terms after the treasure exchange; no individual relationships established
