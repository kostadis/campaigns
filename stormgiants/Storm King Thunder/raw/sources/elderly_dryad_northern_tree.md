# Elderly Dryad (Northern Tree)

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Guardian dryad bound to the northern cardinal tree of the Grandfather Tree site
- **Faction:** Fey; loosely associated with the dryad circle maintaining the sacred grove
- **First Appearance:** Party encountered him at the northern tree when seeking permission to search the Grandfather Tree

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Relieved to be done with Helia and Halisa's conflict; cares about the restoration of the moon festival
- Grumpy and breathy in manner, but warm beneath the surface — responds genuinely to good humor and fey wit
- Has a sentimental streak; the dryad joke Thistle told was one he once shared with a beloved
- Pragmatic about fey politics: withholds cooperation until the source of his frustration is resolved

## History with the Party
1. **First Contact:** Thistle approached him seeking permission to search the Grandfather Tree. He recognized the name Joon (Thistle's uncle) and noted Joon owes him a good joke.
2. **The Joke:** Thistle told a fey joke about a dryad and the charm interaction with tree movement — the same joke he used to share with his beloved. He laughed heartily.
3. **The Condition:** Despite being amused, he refused to grant permission unless Thistle resolved the feud between Helia and Halisa, whose bickering had caused the cancellation of the moon festival.
4. **The Curse Explained:** Shared his understanding of Queen Titania's curse — if one dryad left, the other would be safe, but the departing one would die. He believed neither loved the other enough to sacrifice herself.
5. **Resolution:** When both Helia and Halisa finally departed, he appeared beside Sleth near the party and exclaimed with palpable relief: *"FINALLY LEFT!"*

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Near the party, at the Grandfather Tree site
- **Active Plans:** Presumably, the moon festival may now be able to resume
- **What the Party Knows:** He is bound to the northern tree, knows Joon, and was the gatekeeper for access to the Grandfather Tree
- **What Remains Hidden:** His full history with his beloved; deeper knowledge of Queen Titania's curse mechanics (he admitted uncertainty)

## Relationships
- **Joon (Thistle's Uncle):** Old acquaintance; Joon owes him a joke
- **Thistle:** Warmed to quickly after the joke; cooperative once conditions were met
- **Helia & Halisa:** Long-suffering frustration with both; visibly relieved at their departure
- **Sleth:** Appeared directly beside Sleth upon the dryads' departure (notable proximity)
