# Narth Tezerin

## Identity
- **Role:** Goods store owner in Triboar; co-owner and operator of the Lionshield Coster outpost "Lion's Share" (alongside his partner Alaestra)
- **Faction:** Triboar local (civilian); the Lion's Share is part of the Lionshield Coster's interplanar supply chain
- **First Appearance:** First met when Orsik and Vardis arrived at the Lion's Share outpost following a vault breach; later present in Triboar's market square after a battle, alongside Urgala and Othovir

*Also known as: Narth.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Pragmatic and protective of business interests; attempted to prevent Alaestra from voluntarily revealing the key's significance to Orsik, recognizing it as unnecessary leverage handed to an outsider.
- Firm in negotiations — holds the line alongside Alaestra and is not above implicit threats regarding their employers when pushed.
- Genuinely invested in the business; was visibly distressed by the vault breach and delighted when the key was returned.
- Demonstrated concern for community members and awareness of local events (noted Urgala's house had burned down). Participated in a group hug, suggesting he is warm and communal rather than coldly transactional.

## History with the Party

1. **First meeting (Lion's Share vault breach):** Narth and Alaestra were in disarray when Orsik and Vardis arrived after the vault was breached. The stolen goods included special keys integral to the Lionshield Coster's interplanar supply chain.

2. **Negotiation:** During an early interaction, Narth tried to stop Alaestra from disclosing the key's importance to Orsik, wary of giving him leverage. Ultimately both agreed to Orsik's negotiated terms.

3. **Key returned:** When the party returned the stolen key, Narth was delighted. He and Alaestra jointly resisted Orsik's attempt to renegotiate, and both conveyed a clear warning that their employers are not to be trifled with.

4. **Post-battle (Triboar market square):** Narth was present in the market square after a battle. He confronted Urgala directly, demanding to know what had happened and informing her that her house had burned to the ground. He then joined in a group hug following her response.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Triboar (Lion's Share outpost / market square); alive and unharmed after the battle
- **Active plans:** Resuming normal Lion's Share operations now that the key has been recovered; running his goods store
- **Known to party:** Co-owner of the Lion's Share outpost involved in an interplanar supply chain, backed by powerful employers; local goods-store owner who was present after the battle
- **Hidden from party:** The full nature of those employers and what the interplanar keys actually do

## Relationships
- **Alaestra:** Business partner and co-owner of the Lion's Share; acts in close coordination with her, though he is somewhat more guarded and tactically cautious in dealings.
- **Urgala:** Familiar enough to confront her directly and embrace her; appears to be a neighbor or community ally.
- **Othovir:** Was present together in the same moment in the market square; relationship depth unknown.
- **The Party (Orsik):** Transactional and wary; respects the deal struck but pushed back on renegotiation and issued an implicit warning.
