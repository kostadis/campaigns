# Miery

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Self-proclaimed leader of an unnamed order dedicated to fighting an ancient evil (a returning Kraken)
- **Faction:** Unknown — appears to be her own independent organization
- **First Appearance:** Main thoroughfare of Everlund, near the Bell Market area; the party encountered her preaching to a small crowd of young men

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Recruit members to her order and prepare Everlund against the return of a Kraken she believes will destroy the city using tentacles and mind control
- Passionate and charismatic, commanding enough to hold an audience on a busy street; she singles out individuals she deems worthy
- Pragmatic in her recruitment — willing to waive membership fees in exchange for favors, suggesting she is resourceful if not necessarily wealthy
- Takes pride in her name and its origins, correcting mispronunciation immediately; her identity is clearly important to her

## History with the Party
1. The party encountered Miery preaching on the main thoroughfare about a Kraken that once ravaged Everlund and will return with powerful allies
2. She addressed Orsik directly as he passed, inviting him to join her order; she physically tested his strength by grabbing his arm and approved of what she found
3. She requested **5 gold pieces** for membership and a certificate declaring the bearer "a fighter of the light"
4. When the party revealed they were broke, she offered an alternative: retrieve the **Star of Light**, an artifact of symbolic value stolen by kobolds from a cave a few miles outside of town, in exchange for waiving the fee
5. She clarified the Star of Light holds symbolic rather than functional importance

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Streets of Everlund, near the Bell Market area
- **Active Plans:** Recruiting members to her order; seeking the return of the Star of Light from a kobold-held cave outside the city
- **What the Party Knows:** Her name, her anti-Kraken mission, the artifact retrieval offer, and the fee structure
- **What Remains Hidden:** The true nature and legitimacy of her order; whether her Kraken threat is genuine or fabricated; what she actually intends to do with the Star of Light

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Potential recruits/contractors; she has made a standing offer to waive membership fees if they retrieve the Star of Light
- **Orsik (specifically):** She singled him out and physically assessed him — he appears to be a person of particular interest to her
- **Mielikki:** Her mother was a worshipper of Mielikki; her name is a direct tribute to that devotion, suggesting religious heritage if not active personal faith
