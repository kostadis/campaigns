# Boorvald Orcbane's Son

## Identity
- **Role:** Leader of a Tree Ghost Tribe war party; son of Great Chief Boorvald Orcbane
- **Faction:** Tree Ghost Tribe
- **First Appearance:** Arrived at the Grandfather Tree just as the party was returning to their airship; encountered briefly while the party was sneaking past

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Recover the stolen electrum torc, a sacred artifact of the Tree Ghost Tribe; seek vengeance on the thieves.
- Demonstrated as volatile and quick to fury upon learning of the torc's theft. Commands enough authority to lead a war party, suggesting he is respected within the tribe despite his youth.

## History with the Party
- **[Session of first appearance]:** The party returned to their airship from the Grandfather Tree just as Boorvald's son arrived with a group of Tree Ghost barbarians. He was in conversation with Sleth when the party slipped past undetected. Upon discovering the electrum torc had been stolen from the tree's roots, he flew into a rage and vowed vengeance. The party escaped before he could identify them as the culprits.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The Grandfather Tree.
- **Active Plans:** Hunting for whoever stole the electrum torc; has sworn vengeance.
- **What the Party Knows:** He is furious about the theft and has promised retribution. They do not know his name.
- **What Remains Hidden:** He does not know the party is responsible — yet.

## Relationships
- **Boorvald Orcbane (Great Chief):** His father; Boorvald's name and reputation lend him authority.
- **Sleth:** Was speaking with Sleth when the party arrived; nature of this relationship is unclear.
- **The Party:** Does not know they are the thieves. The party is aware of him but does not know his name.
