# Delsephine

## Identity
- Captain of the *Dragon's Breath*, a sky vessel (small ship with sleigh runners, red balloon) operated in service of Klauth (referred to in notes also as "Klamath"), an ancient red dragon necromancer
- Former member and leader-level figure within the Cult of the Dragon (Severin's faction); departed when ordered to halt Dracolich research in favor of Tiamat's summoning ritual
- Affiliated with the original Dracolich-focused Cult of the Dragon splinter, not Severin's Tiamat faction
- First appeared at the Eye of the World, delivering Klauth's instructions to the party

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goal:** Serve Klauth's interests competently and loyally; holds deep contempt for Severin's faction and their "insane" deviation from the cult's true divine purpose
- Displays barely concealed intense attraction toward Klauth, which other party members have noticed
- Pragmatic and professional — obeys direction without demanding explanations, negotiates contracts with precision, runs a tight commercial operation aboard her ship
- Possesses dry humor and occasional warmth; she finds Unla Kee's oversized pegasus wings on a three-foot halfling endlessly amusing, and she quietly kept Orsik's "aged father" secret, apparently recognizing something about second chances

## History with the Party

**Eye of the World (First Meeting)**
Arrived captaining the *Dragon's Breath* with a crew of eight (Nyzroth, Brassik, Laz, Oriskus, Perella, Tralt, Zalthia), all in black leather and masks. Delivered Klauth's mission: slay the giant leaders and come to his hidden vale for a reward. Agreed to transport the party to Everlund despite skepticism. Spoke candidly to Thistle about her Cult history, her contempt for Severin, and her view that Tiamat herself was played for a fool by Zariel in Avernus. Also expressed contempt for Adimond Kroskas, a figure who had operated at the highest cult levels under Severin and failed to deliver promised powerful magics.

**Feywild Portal Return**
Waited on the far side of the portal; only moments had passed for her. Confronted a visibly aged Orsik, accepted his cover story ("Orsic, with a c — Orsik's father"), smiled knowingly beneath her mask, and kept the secret. When the party chose to delay investigating One Stone until morning, she accommodated without argument. Flatly refused Orsik's suggestion that she join them on expeditions.

**Flint Rock → Morgur's Mound**
Transported the party. Remained aboard during excavation. Sighed in disappointment when days of digging produced only miscellaneous magic items rather than the sought artifact. After the thunderbeast skeleton battle, offered to help Orsik clean up (refused); instructed crew to give him space. Briefed Vardis that only Raven's Rock and Great Worm Cavern remained to be searched.

**Raven's Rock**
Observed Unla Kee's comical pegasus-wing flight with open amusement. When informed the ravens might not view the *Dragon's Breath* as a neutral party, agreed to reposition the ship toward Great Worm Cavern.

**Transit to Great Worm Cavern**
Interrupted Bressik's presentation to sell climbing equipment; pocketed 40 gold from Orsik for two sets of crampons and gear. Negotiated Bressik's contract buyout on behalf of Klauth — explaining the dual-life structure (300 gold for the 30 remaining years of the living clause; 13,000 gold for the 100-year undead clause, which has real value to Klauth as a necromancer). Confirmed Klauth is willing to negotiate and that she has authority to act on his behalf. Accepted Unla Kee's residuals counter-proposal: 500 gold upfront, residuals capped at 6,000 gold. She does not know the residuals are structured only against the current play and will not burden a sequel. Refused to reveal Klauth's location but promised he will speak with and reward the party after the giant threat is dealt with.

**Parnast / Blagothkus's Castle**
Floated the *Dragon's Breath* past the castle; dropped Orsik, Thistle, and Unla Kee onto the upper tower. Inquired after Brezzik's whereabouts before doing so. Subsequently maneuvered to drop Orsik and Vardis near the upper courtyard.

**Post-Escarlotta Agreement**
Approached the party and relayed a message from Klauth: he wishes them the best of luck in ending the war of the giants.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Aboard the *Dragon's Breath*, operating as party transport and Klauth's field agent
- **Active:** Continuing to serve as courier, transport, and commercial operator on Klauth's behalf; awaiting the party's completion of the giant mission so Klauth can meet and reward them
- **What the party knows:** She is Klauth's loyal agent and capable ship captain; she is a former high-ranking Cult of the Dragon member who left over ideological disagreement with Severin; she has negotiating authority on Klauth's behalf
- **What remains hidden:** Klauth's location; the full nature of her relationship with Klauth beyond professional loyalty (her attraction to him has been observed but not explained); the extent of her own prior cult leadership role

## Relationships
- **Klauth (Klamath):** Employer and object of intense personal loyalty (and apparent attraction). Executes his instructions with precision and speaks of him with reverence bordering on devotion.
- **Severin / Cult of the Dragon (Tiamat faction):** Bitter contempt. Views Severin as a fool who destroyed the cult's true purpose.
- **Adimond Kroskas:** Contempt. Knows Kroskas operated at the highest cult levels under Severin and failed to deliver promised magic.
- **Bressik:** Some prior acquaintance or expectation — asked after him by name at Parnast; also the subject of the contract negotiation she conducted.
- **Orsik:** Professionally respectful; obeys his operational direction. Quietly holds his aging secret. Was unsettled by his question about her feelings toward Klauth. He finds her attraction to Klauth disturbing.
- **Thistle:** Engaged openly with Thistle's questions about the cult and Kroskas — their conversations have been among the most candid she's had with any party member.
- **Unla Kee:** Finds her consistently amusing (pegasus wings); negotiated the Bressik residuals deal with her, to Unla Kee's advantage (unknown to Delsephine).
- **Vardis:** Received mission direction from Vardis (Raven's Rock after Morgur's Mound); relationship is functional and professional.
