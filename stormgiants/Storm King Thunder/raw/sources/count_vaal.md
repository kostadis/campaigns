# Count Vaal

## Identity
- **Role:** Cloud giant lord; one of four giant lords assembled by Mirran
- **Faction:** Giant lords serving under Mirran
- **First Appearance:** Introduced in the cavernous chamber where Mirran's trap was sprung; named during Tartha's formal declarations alongside the other lords

## Personality & Motivations
- **Goals:** Served Mirran's assembled coalition of giant lords; specific personal motivations not recorded
- Arrogant and contemptuous — when Orsik expressed surprise at cloud giants' flight ability, Vaal mocked him openly rather than letting it pass
- Composed enough in combat to deliver a devastating morningstar blow while absorbing fire damage

## History with the Party
1. **The Trap Room:** Vaal was among the four giant lords present when the party sprung Mirran's trap. He was named during formal declarations and immediately entered combat.
2. **Combat Exchange:** He took a hit from Unla Kee's explosive fire mote while airborne. He struck Orsik with his morningstar hard enough that Vardis feared Orsik dead; Orsik taunted him afterward, complimenting his style.
3. **Death:** Vardis brought Flame Strike against him; Unla Kee delivered the killing blows.
4. **Aftermath:** Orsik looted his body (see below) and dragged the corpse to a storeroom to conceal evidence of the battle.

## Current Status
- **Status:** **Dead**
- **Last Known Location:** Corpse hidden in a storeroom near the battle site
- **What the party knows:** They killed him, looted him, and hid the body
- **What remains hidden:** Whether Mirran or other allies will notice his absence or discover the concealed body

## Relationships
- **Mirran:** Assembled by Mirran as one of four giant lords; served under his coalition
- **Other Giant Lords:** Present alongside three other lords at the time of the ambush; relationships between them not detailed
- **Orsik:** Struck him with a morningstar blow; was mocked by Vaal mid-combat; Orsik looted and moved his body
- **Unla Kee:** Struck by her fire mote early in combat; killed by her final blasts
- **Vardis:** Targeted with Flame Strike; Vardis was alarmed by Unla Kee's report of Vaal's Misty Step capability

## Loot Recovered

- Teleportation conch
