# Orc Claw of Luthic

## Identity
- **Role:** Priestess/guardian of Luthic (the orcish Cave Mother); stationed as nursery protector within an orc cave complex
- **Faction:** Orc tribe (unnamed); servant of Luthic
- **First Appearance:** Encountered in the nursery of the cave complex while guarding orc children

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Protection of the orc whelps in her care
- Demonstrates fierce maternal protectiveness — her first and only action under threat is to secure the whelp and withdraw rather than fight. She does not posture or escalate beyond what is necessary to remove herself and the child from danger.

## History with the Party
- The party entered the nursery and initially considered killing the orc children before pulling back from that course of action.
- The party gained leverage over her by identifying that one of the whelps held some prior claim or ownership relevant to them (specifics unclear).
- Confronted with both a threat of violence and this leverage, she grabbed the relevant whelp and withdrew from the confrontation.
- She was not harmed. The encounter ended in a standoff rather than combat.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Withdrawing deeper into the cave complex nursery area
- **Active Plans:** Protecting the orc whelps; no further operations known
- **What the Party Knows:** That she guards the nursery and that one whelp has some connection to the party
- **Hidden:** The nature of the party's claim over the whelp has not been fully established; her broader role in the tribe is unknown

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Tense, unresolved. Neither ally nor enemy — the confrontation ended without bloodshed but under duress.
- **The Whelp:** Treats the child as her charge; responded protectively when it was used as leverage.
