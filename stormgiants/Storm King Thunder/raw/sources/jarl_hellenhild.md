# Jarl Hellenhild

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Jarl (frost giant lord), one of four giant lords assembled by Mirran
- **Faction:** Serves under Mirran; present at Duchess Tartha's declaration
- **First Appearance:** Introduced alongside the other giant lords at the start of combat during Tartha's declaration

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Drive:** Glory and recognition in battle; visibly frustrated when denied a chance to fight and claim honor
- Proud and competitive, particularly with Duchess Tartha, whom she seems to resent outshining her
- Deflects embarrassment with bravado — after missing Thistle badly, she claimed she was testing new grip powder rather than admit the failure
- Antagonistic dynamic with Count Vaal, who openly expressed disbelief at her miss

## History with the Party
1. Introduced as one of Mirran's four giant lords at the opening of combat alongside Tartha's declaration
2. Blocked from advancing by Vardis's wall of fire; unable to cross and visibly frustrated while Tartha pressed forward
3. Spotted Thistle flying above the wall and hurled a boulder at him — missed badly; mocked by Tartha and disbelieved by Count Vaal
4. Caught in Unla Kee's fire explosion; sustained significant damage and began showing visible wear

## Current Status
- **Status: DECEASED.** Killed during the battle (or its aftermath); body was discovered later by the party.
- **Last Known Location:** Body dragged to a storeroom by the party.
- **Loot recovered:** Orsik looted a **cloak of white dragon scales** and a **teleportation conch** from her corpse.
- **Hidden:** Her precise capabilities, any special abilities or equipment, and her relationship to the other giant lords and Mirran beyond her title.

## Relationships
- **Duchess Tartha:** Competitive and resentful; Tartha taunted her during combat and is claiming the glory Hellenhild wanted
- **Count Vaal:** Antagonistic; Vaal openly expressed disbelief at her failed throw
- **Mirran:** Assembled by Mirran alongside the other lords; no further detail on the nature of that allegiance
- **Thistle (party):** Targeted him with a boulder throw — missed; no prior relationship indicated
- **Unla Kee (party):** Struck by her fire explosion; no direct interaction beyond being a target
