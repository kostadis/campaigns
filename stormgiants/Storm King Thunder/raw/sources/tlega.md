# Tlega

## Identity
- **Role:** Goblin leader (co-boss, alongside rival Mirk) of the cave-dwelling goblin tribe
- **Faction:** Cave goblin tribe, tentative alliance with the 3HPP
- **First Appearance:** Emerged from behind a waterfall tunnel after a scout (Goblin #1) reported the party's arrival; initiated first contact with the 3HPP

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Remove Maegurn the Fomorian, whose abuse has crossed the line even by goblin standards — the traditional Bully/Bullied dynamic has become intolerable
- Enthusiastic and quick to ally when interests align, but easily deflated when his authority is challenged
- Speaks with a very nasal, squeaky voice; responds warmly to social gestures (was visibly touched by Thistle's correct pronunciation of his name)
- Considers "leading" to be a legitimate battlefield role — specifically, shouting orders from the rear while others do the fighting

## History with the Party

1. **First Contact:** Tlega emerged to meet the party, learned they intended to kill Maegurn, and immediately aligned with them, citing the breakdown of the goblin-Fomorian relationship. Guided them deeper into the caves with genuine enthusiasm.
2. **The Mirk Dispute:** When Orsik suggested enlisting the goblins in the attack, Tlega eagerly called an assembly — only for rival goblin boss Mirk to appear and challenge the decision. Tlega became deflated as the argument stalled. The deadlock was broken when Unla Kee used Jim's Glowing Coin, convincing both Tlega and Mirk to proceed.
3. **Battle of Maegurn's Throne Room:** Tlega accompanied the party into the throne room but stationed himself in the corridor entrance, shouting encouragement while his troops advanced. He made no move to enter combat personally. When called out by Orsik ("Don't you fight?"), Tlega replied, "We lead!" Orsik's retort: "From the rear!"

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Maegurn's cave complex; present at the throne room battle
- Maegurn has been defeated; Tlega's primary stated goal has been accomplished
- **What the party knows:** Tlega is an enthusiastic but self-preserving ally who leads exclusively through verbal direction
- **What remains hidden:** The internal power dynamic between Tlega and Mirk, and what the tribe intends to do now that Maegurn is gone

## Relationships
- **Mirk:** Rival goblin boss; the two co-lead the tribe and are capable of disagreement significant enough to stall group action
- **Goblin #1 (scout):** Reports to Tlega
- **Thistle (3HPP):** Mild positive regard — Tlega was genuinely moved by Thistle's correct name pronunciation
- **Orsik (3HPP):** Friction; Orsik has openly called out Tlega's rear-leadership style, twice
- **Unla Kee (3HPP):** Instrumental in breaking the Tlega/Mirk deadlock via Jim's Glowing Coin
