# Senneth

## Identity
- Cleric of Mielikki; based at Mielikki's Glade, Silverymoon
- Previously raised Thistle from the dead; formally met the party when they visited her at the Glade to discuss devil activity in the city

## Personality & Motivations
- Pragmatic and measured — not alarmed by the idea of a devil in a large city, but capable of genuine surprise when confronted with specific evidence. Quick to draw connections between disparate threads, suggesting an analytical mind. Cooperative with the party and willing to share information openly.

## History with the Party
- **Prior to campaign:** Raised Thistle from the dead (circumstances not detailed in notes).
- **Glade meeting:** The party visited Senneth to discuss the possibility of devil cults in Silverymoon. She downplayed the likelihood of active cults, reasoning that the post-Cult of the Dragon crackdown drove the "smart ones" out of the city. When Thistle described the Cambions from the trial, Senneth was visibly shocked and identified them as associates of Lady Margaster. She began drawing connections between the party, the Margasters, and a play involving a devil and a demon before the conversation was redirected by Vardis toward the Lady's College.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Mielikki's Glade, Silverymoon
- **Active plans:** None confirmed; no operations known to be in progress
- **Party knows:** She can identify the Cambions as Lady Margaster's men; she is willing to discuss sensitive topics openly
- **Hidden:** The full extent of her knowledge about the Margasters and the play connection remains unexplored — she was redirected before elaborating

## Relationships
- **Thistle:** Prior bond as the cleric who raised her; some degree of established trust
- **Lady Margaster:** Aware of her and her Cambion associates; reaction suggests this knowledge is significant
- **3HPP (general):** Cooperative and forthcoming; no signs of friction
