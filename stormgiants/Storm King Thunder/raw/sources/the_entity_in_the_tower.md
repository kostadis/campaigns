# The Entity in the Tower

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Unknown — an unidentified being residing in the Tower of Green Flame
- **Faction:** Unknown
- **First Appearance:** Observed (unseen) as the party approached the Tower of Green Flame near Llorkh

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals/Drives:** Unknown — its deeper motivations have not been revealed
- Composed and watchful; monitors intrusions from within the tower without immediately acting
- Prefers deterrence over direct confrontation — relied on existing wards rather than engaging the party personally
- Capable of emotional response: expressed sorrow when a trap it triggered inadvertently left the door vulnerable

## History with the Party
- Watched the party's approach in silence, choosing to rely on "the usual wards" as deterrent.
- Observed with interest as Thistle and Orsik located the door handle.
- When Thistle touched the handle and both were injured (but not killed), the entity considered this an adequate warning and was satisfied.
- Realized with distress that the trap's activation had left the door openable — an unintended and unwelcome consequence.
- Observed Vardis use a blessing to help companions deflect its permanent antipathy field, allowing the group to approach.
- Watched the party grapple Orsik and Thistle, dragging them toward the entrance.
- When Vardis and Unla Kee opened the door, it responded telepathically with audible satisfaction: *"ah, this will do nicely"*.
- Inside the dungeon, communicated telepathically with Vardis, warning that using the corroded key would forfeit the gold the group had found — an apparent attempt to keep them from escaping.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Inside the Tower of Green Flame, near Llorkh
- **Active Situation:** The door to the tower has been opened (initially via the misfired ward, then again by Vardis and Unla Kee); the structure's interdimensional dungeon remains active and trap members of the party who entered are subject to its trapping mechanism
- **What the Party Knows:** The tower is dangerous and warded; something reacted to their approach; it is sentient, communicates telepathically, has an antipathy field, and uses the party's greed against them
- **What Remains Hidden:** The entity's identity, origin, and full nature; the full extent of its interdimensional dungeon; whether escape is genuinely possible

## Relationships
- **Party (general):** Views them as intruders; responded with measured deterrence rather than direct action.
- **Vardis:** Primary target of its telepathic communication; singled out twice — once when it noticed his blessing, once with the corroded key warning.
- **Unla Kee:** Co-opened the door alongside Vardis; implicated in triggering the Tower's satisfaction.
- **Orsik & Thistle:** Were physically dragged toward the Tower against their will — their relationship to it is adversarial from the outset.
- **Local Population:** Locals avoid the tower entirely — their fear may or may not be directly connected to this entity.
