# Tapouach

- Role: Apple merchant, Yartar market
- Faction: None noted

## Personality & Motivations
- Appears deferential to those connected to power — when he noticed Unla Kee steal from him, he said nothing and smiled rather than cause a scene with a companion of Lord Drylund.

- **Session intro:** Met the party during the morning market in Yartar via Lord Drylund's introduction. Unla Kee pickpocketed his purse (23 coppers) and stole an apple. Tapouach noticed the theft but chose not to react, apparently out of deference to Drylund's association.

## Current Status
- Last known location: Yartar market
- No active plans noted
- **Party knows:** He noticed the theft and stayed silent. **Hidden:** Whether he resents it or simply chose pragmatism.
