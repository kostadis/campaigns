# Boorvald Orcbane

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Great Chief of the Tree Ghost Tribe
- **Faction:** Tree Ghost Tribe
- **First Appearance:** Referenced but not present; mentioned during the party's visit to the Grandfather Tree when a young barbarian leader arrived claiming ties to the tribe.

## Personality & Motivations
- No direct interaction recorded. Personality and motivations are unknown at this time.

## History with the Party
- **Grandfather Tree (referenced):** Boorvald did not appear in person. His son (a young barbarian leader) arrived at the Grandfather Tree during the party's time there. The 3HPP recovered the Tree Ghost Tribe's artifact — a nonmagical electrum torc bearing Giant runes, formerly the nose-ring of a mighty hill giant chief — from the roots of the Grandfather Tree.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Unknown; presumably with or leading the Tree Ghost Tribe somewhere in the region.
- **Active Plans:** Unknown.
- **What the party knows:** He is the Great Chief of the Tree Ghost Tribe and the father of the young barbarian leader who met the party. The recovered torc belongs to his tribe.
- **What remains hidden:** His whereabouts, motivations, disposition toward the party, and the significance the torc holds for his people beyond its description.

## Relationships
- **Son (unnamed young barbarian leader):** His son arrived at the Grandfather Tree, apparently acting on behalf of or connected to the tribe's interests.
- **Tree Ghost Tribe:** He leads this tribe as Great Chief.
- **The 3HPP:** No direct relationship established; the party holds his tribe's artifact.
