# Lord Khaspere Drylund

## Identity
- **Role:** Owner and proprietor of *The Grand Dame*, a luxurious floating casino moored in Yartar; political aspirant seeking to become Waterbaron of Yartar
- **Faction:** Associated with the Kraken Society (confirmed posthumously); connected to the Aboleth Oosith; tied to a network involving human factions and ancient powers around the Hekaton conspiracy
- **First appearance:** Identified early as a person of interest in the Hekaton-disappearance conspiracy; referenced by Imber Chen during his introduction of Cap'n Squint; later met in person at the Yartar city gates and aboard the Grand Dame

*Also known as: Lord Khasperi Dryland.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Become Waterbaron of Yartar; leverage the Grand Dame as a hub for political networking and social influence
- Drylund is a nouveau riche social climber — opportunistic, politically calculating, and unabashedly self-interested. He intervened to welcome the 3HPP not out of goodwill but to capture their gambling money and claim their arrival as a political endorsement. He outlined Yartar's three paths to electoral victory (persuasion, bribery, and hostage-holding) without apparent shame, and declined an offer of assassination only on pragmatic grounds.

## History with the Party

1. **Pre-contact:** Drylund refused the party entry to the Grand Dame. A wooden casino coin of his was used by Queen Serissa as a clue directing the party toward Yartar.
2. **City gates:** When the 3HPP landed their cloud castle near Yartar's docks, Drylund arrived with his retinue and intervened to prevent the guard captain from taking hostile action. He accepted a bottle of Champagne de Stomp from Orsik without recognizing its rarity and invited the party aboard the Grand Dame with comped food and drink.
3. **Private office meeting:** Drylund hosted the party in his ornately decorated office (purple velvet curtains, gaudy candelabras, pet octopus in an aquarium, silk hammock, silver wine decanter). He nearly opened the Champagne de Stomp before Orsik dissuaded him; he decided to save it for his inauguration as Waterbaron. He proposed an informal alliance — Yartar's friendship in exchange for the party's implicit political backing — and framed them publicly as protectors of Yartar against cloud giants. He ended the meeting by saying he needed them to meet some associates.
4. **Death:** Drylund was killed before the party's next encounter with him. His corpse was discovered aboard the Grand Dame. The party debated raising him; Vardis declared it impossible due to brain damage. Thistle searched his body (recovering a ring with keys, 120 gp, and 3 pp) and his room (no incriminating correspondence found; recovered an ornately decorated weapon with an octopus pommel). Thuul deposited the body in a room; Thistle locked the door. Orsik's verdict: *"Take the gold, leave the body."*

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Corpse aboard the Grand Dame, Yartar — room locked by Thistle
- **Active plans:** N/A — deceased
- **Power vacuum:** His death has left Yartar's political landscape open; the party has discussed filling the gap

**What the party knows:**
- Drylund was connected to the Kraken Society and the Aboleth Oosith
- His operations were linked to King Hekaton's disappearance
- No correspondence or organizational paperwork was found in his room, which Thistle found suspicious
- Thistle's assessment: Drylund (and Lady Atalia) lacked the resources to orchestrate Hekaton's abduction — a more powerful entity is behind the operation

**What remains hidden:**
- The full nature and depth of his role within the Kraken Society
- Who killed him and why
- Lady Atalia's warning that *"Drylund's going to come back"* — suggesting the Kraken Society has resurrection or replacement capabilities

## Relationships
- **Lady Atalia:** Kraken Society associate; warned the party that Drylund would return
- **Imber Chen:** Referenced Drylund when introducing Cap'n Squint; was aware of his political ambitions
- **The Kraken Society / Aboleth Oosith:** Confirmed affiliation
- **Orsik (3HPP):** Gifted him the Champagne de Stomp; offered (and had rejected) an assassination proposal; demanded the bottle back upon finding Drylund dead
- **Thistle (3HPP):** Searched the body and room; skeptical Drylund was a top-level conspirator
- **Current Waterbaron of Yartar:** Political rival; Drylund viewed the Lord Zymorven rescue as a blow against the incumbent
