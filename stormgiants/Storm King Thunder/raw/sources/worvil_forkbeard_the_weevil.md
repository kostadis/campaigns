# Worvil Forkbeard ("The Weevil" / "Larg")

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Fugitive dwarf; career criminal / caravan intelligence broker; subject of an active 5,000 gp bounty from the Axe of Mirabar; aliases include "The Weevil" and "Larg"
- **Faction:** None confirmed; was the true target of Xaltivar's raid on Xanthalar's Keep
- **First Appearance:** Introduced via a wanted poster and secondhand accounts; first met in person hiding at the Falling Orc tavern in Xantharl's Keep, where he was working as a stable hand

*Also known as: The Weevil, Larg, Worvil Forkbeard the Weevil Larg, Weevil.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Self-preservation; evasion of capture; leveraging information as currency for freedom.
- Calculating and opportunistic — wasted no time attempting to bribe the party the moment his gag was removed.
- Knows how to research his marks, addressing Orsik by name and reputation. Tried a last-ditch identity denial even upon arrival in Mirabar.
- Quick to spin a narrative — upon capture, immediately attempted to reframe the situation as the party "saving him from evil creatures." Unla Kee shut this down by producing the wanted poster.
- Characterized by Unla Kee as "a scumbag."

## History with the Party

1. **Intelligence (pre-capture):** Darz learned of Weevil's whereabouts from a Mirabar merchant who spotted him cleaning stables in Xantharl's Keep, having previously seen his wanted poster while traveling through Longsaddle. Darz had been holding this information as a personal retirement job before sharing it with the party out of gratitude after being raised from the dead. The party committed to pursuing the bounty, and Harshnag agreed to accompany them to Xantharl's Keep before the group proceeded to the Eye of the All-Father.

2. **Xantharl's Keep — Falling Orc Tavern:** Worvil was sheltering at the tavern when a giant attack created cover for the bugbears (also hunting the bounty) to close in on his location.

3. **Capture:** Wounded by fireballs from Unla Kee and Vardis, then subdued by Orsik and Thistle.

4. **Post-Capture:** Attempted to manipulate the party with a false gratitude narrative. Claim was dismissed by Unla Kee.

5. **Xanthalar's Keep handover:** Party received him as a bounty prisoner. He was gagged for transport north along the Long Road.

6. **On the Road:** When Orsik removed his gag, The Weevil immediately attempted to bribe him — claiming to have cracked a secret pattern governing the movement of ore caravans out of Mirabar's mines, and offering the information in exchange for freedom. He knew Orsik by name and reputation. Orsik declined and re-gagged him.

7. **Arrival in Mirabar:** The Weevil claimed to the gate sergeant that he was not who the party claimed. Guard Sergeant Herald identified him by his distinctive beard braiding and silenced him. Herald confirmed he would spend the rest of his days in the mines.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Mirabar — sentenced to life labor in the mines
- **Active Situation:** Mirabar authorities have indicated they intend to extract his knowledge of the caravan movement pattern through their own means
- **What the Party Knows:** He is imprisoned and sentenced; the bounty was collected; he uses at least two aliases (The Weevil, Larg); he is wanted by the Axe of Mirabar.
- **What Remains Hidden:** Whether his claimed knowledge of the caravan pattern is genuine; the full scope of his criminal history; the nature of his crimes; why Xaltivar specifically wanted him; why bugbears were also pursuing him.

## Relationships
- **Guard Sergeant Herald (Mirabar):** Herald identified and took custody of him; holds authority over his imprisonment.
- **Xaltivar:** Mounted a raid on Xanthalar's Keep specifically to retrieve The Weevil — nature of their connection unknown.
- **Bugbears:** Also sought the bounty on him — rivals, not allies.
- **Darz:** Source of the intelligence on Weevil's location; viewed the bounty as a retirement score.
- **Unla Kee:** Holds him in open contempt ("a scumbag").
- **Harshnag:** Was promised a 100 gp cut of the reward for his role in the capture.
- **Orsik (Party):** The Weevil knew Orsik by name and reputation and targeted him as the most likely to accept a bribe; Orsik refused.
- **Axe of Mirabar:** Issuing faction of the 5,000 gp bounty.
