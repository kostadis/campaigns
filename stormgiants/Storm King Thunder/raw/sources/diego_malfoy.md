# Diego Malfoy

## Identity
- **Role:** Guard, Iron Circle (Llorkh garrison)
- **First Appearance:** Outside the tavern in Llorkh, following an incident involving a ghostly bartender whose nature the party provoked

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals/Drives:** Maintaining order in Llorkh; enforcing Iron Circle authority
- Diego is exasperated, reactive, and speaks bluntly. He is familiar enough with the party to hold a grudge over their prior incompetence, and visibly rattled by supernatural encounters. Despite his frustration, he remained functional enough to direct the party to useful resources.

## History with the Party
1. **The Ghostly Bartender Incident:** Diego was among the guards who fled the tavern when the bartender's true nature was provoked. After Orsik exited, Diego confronted him in a panic, loudly insisting that no one ever tells the bartender he is a ghost.
2. **Recognition:** Diego identified Orsik and Vardis by name and reputation, calling them out for previously seeking employment with the Iron Circle guards and failing to show up sober on their first day.
3. **Grudging Assistance:** When asked about a scroll of dispel magic, Diego directed Vardis to the Lionel Coster outpost (local store) in Llorkh.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Outside the tavern in Llorkh
- **Active Plans:** None noted beyond routine guard duties
- **What the Party Knows:** Diego knows who they are, considers them unreliable, and has no apparent goodwill toward them
- **What Remains Hidden:** His broader role within the Iron Circle garrison; whether he intends to report the tavern incident or the party's presence to superiors

## Relationships
- **Iron Circle:** Member of the Llorkh garrison
- **Orsik:** Recognizes him by name; associates him with the failed guard employment incident
- **Vardis:** Same as above; directed him to the Lionel Coster outpost when asked for help
- **The Ghostly Bartender:** Visibly terrified; clearly aware of the ghost's existence and the unspoken rule not to acknowledge it
