# Kartha-Kaya

## Identity
- **Role:** Chief of the Yakfolk village atop Mount Hamarhaast
- **Faction:** Yakfolk
- **First Appearance:** The party was received into her yurt upon arriving at the Yakfolk village; she framed an invitation to feast as an offer they could not politely refuse.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Dominion over visitors through psychic possession; enforcing Yakfolk customs as social leverage.
- **Traits:** Imperious and manipulative — she weaponizes hospitality and cultural ritual to lower the party's guard. She is condescending toward dwarves specifically, making snide remarks at Orsik's expense. She operates with patience, moving from feast to ritual before revealing her true intent.

## History with the Party
1. **The Feast:** Invited (pressured) the party into her yurt — a massive 60-foot-diameter structure with a central fire pit and ornate bronze gong. Served a genuine, uncontaminated meal of vegetables, meats, and bread, successfully disarming the party's suspicions.
2. **The Ritual:** Insisted on a post-meal cleansing ritual over the objections of Thistle and Vardis, invoking ill omens as a threat. Dismissed their resistance. Made disparaging remarks about dwarves when Orsik refused to participate.
3. **The Possession Attempt:** Took Thistle's left hand and Vardis's right during the ritual, leading a communal hum that masked an attempted psychic possession of the party. The attempt failed.
4. **Combat:** The failed possession triggered open combat. Kartha-Kaya is currently engaged in melee with the party.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Inside her yurt on Mount Hamarhaast, mid-combat with the party.
- **Active Plans:** Psychic possession attempt has failed; now in direct combat.
- **What the Party Knows:** She attempted to possess them through the ritual. She is hostile.
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full extent of her psychic abilities, whether she has additional tricks or allies who may intervene, and her broader role/agenda within the Yakfolk hierarchy.

## Relationships
- **The Party (general):** Treated as prey — guests to be subdued through possession.
- **Orsik:** Open disdain; singled him out with anti-dwarf remarks.
- **Thistle & Vardis:** Physically held their hands during the possession attempt — they were her primary targets.

## Arc Score Events
- **Possession attempt failed → Combat initiated:** Relationship with the party moved from tense/manipulative hospitality to open hostility. No arc score recovery path currently apparent.
