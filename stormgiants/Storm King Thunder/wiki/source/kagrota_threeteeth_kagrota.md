# Kagrota Threeteeth

## Identity
- **Role:** Warlord / field commander; self-styled "Mother of Bones"
- **Faction:** Forces of Bad Fruul; apparent worshiper of Bane
- **First Appearance:** The Brandimen cabin — commanded an orc and goblin warband occupying the site, holding commune survivors prisoner in a cage in the clearing

## Personality & Motivations
- **Goals:** Carried out Bad Fruul's orders to destroy a nearby commune; held survivors as prisoners for unclear purposes
- Aggressive and domineering in combat, expressing frustration at her troops' failures ("Do I have to do everything?"). Demonstrated both martial and divine spellcasting ability, suggesting real power beneath the bluster. Her dying words revealed anxiety about broken agreements — she was not acting entirely alone.

## History with the Party
- The party arrived at the Brandimen cabin to investigate the disappearance of Tove Brandimen's mother
- Kagrota's warband was entrenched at the cabin; commune survivors were caged in the clearing, one of whom had a finger severed
- A prisoner revealed that Tove Brandimen was "thick as thieves" with Kagrota, implicating Tove as a collaborator and his story about a hostage mother as a deception
- Battle ensued; Kagrota threw a javelin (missed), summoned a spiritual weapon (a hammer), and struck Unla Kee, Wallace, and Orsik during the fight
- Thistle reduced her to 1 HP; she screamed **"Fairies! We had a deal with Prince Thornacious!"** before Wallace delivered the killing blow

## Current Status
- **Status: Deceased** — killed by Wallace at the Brandimen cabin
- A hidden underground chamber beneath the cabin contained a crude Bane altar (black sunburst symbol, two black candles, an elven woman's skull, shattered jade statuette) and several chests of treasure

## What the Party Knows vs. What Remains Hidden
| Known | Hidden / Unresolved |
|---|---|
| Served Bad Fruul; ordered to destroy the commune | Full nature of her relationship with Tove Brandimen |
| Worshiped (or was connected to) Bane | Identity and nature of **Prince Thornacious** |
| Had some agreement with "Prince Thornacious" | Purpose of the Bane altar and the elven woman's skull |
| Tove may have been her collaborator | Who severed the prisoner's finger and why |

## Relationships
- **Bad Fruul:** Direct superior; issued the order to destroy the commune
- **Prince Thornacious:** Had an existing "deal" with Kagrota — nature unknown; she invoked this agreement in a panic at near-death
- **Tove Brandimen:** Alleged close collaborator; a prisoner described them as "thick as thieves"
- **The Party:** Killed in combat
