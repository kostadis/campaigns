# Elara

Elara is a middle-aged human merchant who encountered the party on the road to the Tower of Storms. She and her groom, Jarek, were found seeking refuge behind an overturned cart following an attack by disciplined orcs.

## Personality & Motivation
Elara is portrayed as a practical but devout woman. Her faith in Lathander is traditional; she was visibly unsettled by Valphine's description of the Morninglord as "painful," indicating that her understanding of the deity is more conventional than the party's.

## Key Interactions
- **Valphine:** Elara felt a sense of kinship when Valphine invoked Lathander, though their differing theological views created a moment of friction.
- **Vukradin:** A minor point of tension occurred when Elara paid her reward directly to Valphine in the name of the Morninglord, bypassing Vukradin's expectation of payment.
- **Soma & Brewbarry:** These members provided practical assistance (mending a cart and retrieving a horse), for which Elara was grateful.

## Observations
Elara reported that the orcs who attacked her were unusually disciplined and bore markings resembling swirls or eyes. Her inability to clearly recall these details suggests a potential [[information-asymmetry-npc-encounters]] or magical suppression of memory.
