# Orsik

Orsik is a Mountain Dwarf fighter and artificer whose intelligence was radically transformed by the Headband of Intellect, once worn by Narcellia Varamyr. Once a blunt, impulsive soldier, he now possesses heightened strategic awareness — yet his emotional volatility and lingering loyalties to the Zhentarim create deep internal conflict. He is the emotional core of the party alongside Vardis, his lifelong friend from Waterdeep.

Orsik has committed himself to a heroic death against Iymrith, viewing it as moral closure for past failures — including his betrayal of the Harper teleportation circles and his role in the destruction of Llorkh’s stonemason, Old Keller. He deflects Vardis’s grief with dark humor, referencing “NRRRR” as a private mnemonic for unresolved trauma. His relationship with Escarlotta has evolved from transactional to genuine partnership; she called him “that’s what heroes are” after he acknowledged her as part of the team.

He operates under the alias “Orsic” to conceal his identity, and his loyalty to the Zhentarim — particularly his handler Uzorel “The Viper” Hornbalde — remains intact despite his growing bond with the party. His unilateral decision to decline King Hekaton’s military aid fractured trust, and his refusal to confront Thistle’s moral drift exemplifies [[complicit-silence]].

Orsik’s arc is defined by [[heroic-death-wish]]: not redemption through confession, but through sacrifice. His death is not a failure — it is his final act of integrity.

His possessions include the Headband of Intellect, Giant Slayer Longsword, Plate +2, Shield +1, Shard of the Ise Rune, Cube of Summoning, multiple conchs of teleportation, and the book *Dusk* from Sandesyl Morgia.
