# Vardis

Vardis is a Wood Elf cleric of the Light Domain, exiled from every temple of Corellon Larethian for his pragmatic, non-dogmatic approach to faith. He prays to any deity that answers — a stance that earned him divine scorn, including being covered in bird droppings by Corellon himself. He refuses the SEER’s offer of reconciliation with his former church, choosing instead to walk his own path.

He is the moral anchor of the party, advocating for caution, restraint, and ethical accountability — often in opposition to Orsik’s death wish and Unla Key’s recklessness. His grief over Orsik’s impending death is profound and unspoken, forming the emotional core of the group. He has privately considered founding a new cult of Corellon, inspired by Sandesyl Morgia’s long-term planning.

Vardis possesses an [[elemental-blessing-annam]] — resistance to air damage — a silent sign of divine favor despite Annam’s silence. He carries the Mace of Terror, Amulet of Proof against Detection and Location, Ring of Djinni Summoning, Dragon Wand, and a mysterious ring looted from Sandesyl’s treasure — which he has not disclosed to the party.

His arc is defined by [[complicit-silence]]: he sees Thistle’s moral drift and Orsik’s betrayal, but chooses not to confront them — not out of weakness, but because he believes the group’s fragile unity is more important than moral purity. He is the quiet witness to the party’s erosion, and his silence is his most powerful act of loyalty.
