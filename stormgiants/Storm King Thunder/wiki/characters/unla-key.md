# Unla Key

Unla Key is a Lightfoot Halfling wizard of the School of Divination, exiled from the Nirany School of the Arcane for his reckless, boundary-pushing experimentation. He believes he alone sustains the multiverse through his dangerous choices — a delusion he calls “cosmic necessity.” This conviction justifies his reckless behavior: triggering traps, casting stinking cloud on poison-immune undead, and withholding critical information from the party.

He is the party’s chaotic intellectual, secretly jealous of Orsik’s augmented intelligence, and has developed a genuine, playful bond with Thistle Wendrod, whom he has pledged to protect at all costs. He deceived the Sky Pony Tribe into worshipping him as a divine avatar and holds a summoning stone for Feetor, a mud mephit he nominally leads.

Unla Key rejected the SEER’s offer of safety and power, a rare act of defiance that aligns him with the party’s collective rejection of manipulation. He holds three spellbooks, the Rock of Wonder, Gem of Elemental Summoning, and the book *Prophecies of the Apocalypse* from the SEER — all concealed from the group.

His resistance to water damage is an [[elemental-blessing-annam]], suggesting Annam’s silent grace persists even in his chaotic nature. His arc is defined by [[cosmic-necessity-delusion]]: he is not mad — he is terrified of being wrong, and so he acts as if the universe depends on him.

His possessions include the Arcane Grimoire +3, Wand of the War Mage +1, Wand of Wonder, Wand of Web, Abjurer’s bangle, Ring of Jumping, Pegasus wings, and multiple spell scrolls.
