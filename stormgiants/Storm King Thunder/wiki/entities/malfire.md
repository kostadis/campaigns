# Malfire

Malfire is a highly influential, albeit enigmatic, historical figure associated with the [[war-of-the-dragons]]. Little is known regarding their true identity, race, or current status, making them a figure of both legend and intense political debate.

## Historical Impact
Malfire is primarily known for their ability to exert high-level political persuasion. During the [[war-of-the-dragons]], Malfire is credited with persuading [[delaan-winterhound]] to shift the stance of the [[emerald-enclave]], leading the faction to abandon its traditional neutrality to join the coalition opposing the summoning of Tiamat.

## The Dual Legacy
The reputation of Malfire is split between two primary ideological camps:

* **The Interventionist View:** Sees Malfire as a hero whose persuasive actions were essential in forming the coalition that saved the world from Tiamat.
* **The Naturalist View:** Sees Malfire as a catastrophic catalyst. They argue that Malfire's interference broke the neutrality of the [[emerald-enclave]], triggering a domino effect of global conflicts, including the [[war-of-the-giants]] and the [[shattering-of-the-ordning]].

## Unknowns
* **Identity:** Race, class, and true name remain unknown.
* **Status:** It is unclear if Malfire is still alive or if they are a purely historical/mythological figure.
