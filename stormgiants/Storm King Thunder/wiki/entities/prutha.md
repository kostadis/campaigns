# Prutha

[[prutha]] is a recently converted orc and a former warrior of the [[circle-of-thunder]]. He has defected to become a follower of [[valphine]]'s emerging [[lathanderian-practice-valphine]].

## Identity & Personality
Prutha is characterized by a blunt, decisive nature and a combat style noted for its focused directness by [[brewbarry]]. While he claims his conversion to Lathander is driven by [[theological-pragmatism]]—viewing it as a "power upgrade" over the [[talosian-devotion]] of his former faction—his actions suggest significant emotional depth.

He exhibits "complexity markers" such as:
- **Emotional Vulnerability:** He was observed weeping while using a bone whistle, a relic of his deceased great-uncle.
- **Performative Violence:** He uses aggressive actions, such as kicking the corpses of fallen enemies, as a way to signal his new allegiance and belonging.
- **Internal Conflict:** He has shown somber reflection regarding the death of his own kind (the [[the-anchorite-half-orc]] and others) in the name of his new faith.

## History
- **Defection:** Following the battle at the [[circle-of-thunder]], Prutha was the last standing orc and surrendered to [[valphine]], pledging himself to her twice. His conversion was a point of contention regarding naming conventions, as [[valphine]] suggested the name "Thrall," which was rejected by the party.
- **The Bone Whistle:** A tradition passed down from his great-uncle, a clan shaman killed by Talosians. The tradition involves blowing the whistle at sunrise.
- **Conflict with [[vukradin]]:** Prutha acted unilaterally to destroy Talosian holy symbols following a philosophical disagreement with [[vukradin]] regarding the disposition of religious artifacts.

## Relationships
- **[[valphine]]**: His primary attachment and the catalyst for his conversion.
- **[[vukradin]]**: Holds respect for him; received a blessing from him, though they have clashed over religious ethics.
- **[[brewbarry]]**: Recognizes Prutha's combat directness.
- **[[circle-of-thunder]]**: His former faction; he holds a hardline stance that committed Talosians should not be spared.
