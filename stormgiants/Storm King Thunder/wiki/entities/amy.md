# Amy

Amy is the lead actor in Kaelen Sarssir’s play at the Talking Troll theater in Triboar. Though outwardly cheerful and cooperative, she exhibits profound deference to authority and avoids conflict at all costs, even when it suppresses her own creative input. Her performance improved only after being directed by Orsik, who disguised himself as an acting coach named “Geoff Mamet.” Amy credits “Geoff” for her success and remains unaware of Orsik’s role in her transformation.

She has no known factional allegiance and appears to lack a personal narrative arc. Her motivations are rooted in social validation rather than self-expression. Thistle Wendrod identified her behavior as concealing a guarded past, though no evidence of trauma or specific history has been uncovered. Amy’s story is not one of empowerment, but of quiet submission—a mirror to systemic suppression of individual voice in the campaign world.

## Relationships
- **Kaelen Sarssir**: Creative authority figure; Amy defers to him despite disagreements.
- **Orsik**: Believes Orsik funded her coaching; unaware Orsik is “Geoff Mamet.”
- **“Geoff Mamet”**: The authoritative coach she obeyed and credited for her improvement.
- **Thistle Wendrod**: Observed and analyzed her psychological patterns.

## Status
- **Last Known Location**: The Talking Troll theater, Triboar
- **Current Activity**: Play concluded; no known future engagements
- **Hidden**: Nature of her guarded past; specific creative vision for her role
