# Barthen

Barthen is a retired sheriff and rancher who issued a letter of introduction to the party, enabling their access to Falcon Hunting Lodge. His role is strictly procedural: the letter functions as a credential that bypasses suspicion and grants entry to a restricted domain. Though his personal history is not detailed in this source, his authority to issue such credentials implies a position of local trust and institutional recognition. He is not a plot-driving character, but a functional node in the campaign’s system of verified access.

Barthen’s connection to alphonse-big-al-kalazorn suggests he operates within the same regional network of rural authority figures. His letter is not a magical artifact, yet it carries social weight equivalent to one — a testament to the enduring power of bureaucratic legitimacy in a world of planar chaos.
