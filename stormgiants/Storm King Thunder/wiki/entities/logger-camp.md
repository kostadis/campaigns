# Logger's Camp

The site of [[tibor-wester]]'s logging operations. 

## Status
The camp is currently abandoned and silent. Despite being found in the early morning, the site shows evidence of recent habitation, including:
- Tents on the beach.
- A standing cabin with stacked logs.
- Older cabins reduced to foundations and stone chimneys.

The sudden disappearance of the entire camp population remains a primary mystery.
