# Lord's Alliance

The **Lord's Alliance** is a large, multi-faceted organization that employs both human personnel and non-human "field assets." It operates with a structured, military-style hierarchy where roles are clearly defined and delegated.

The alliance utilizes diverse resources to achieve its goals:
- **Human Leadership:** Officers like **Commander Diana** provide strategic direction.
- **Non-Human Assets:** Entities such as the dragon **Clarion** serve as specialized tools for transport, scouting, and logistics.

This structure ensures that even when interacting with external parties (such as the party or merchant networks involving **Azam**), the organization maintains a professional distance, utilizing its assets to fulfill specific roles without compromising the core identity of the leadership.
