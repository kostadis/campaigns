# Gundalin Cartwright
Gundalin Cartwright is a central figure in the narrative universe described in the source document "Feetor". He is portrayed as a cartwright and militia member who is suspected of being involved in the sabotage campaign targeting Parnast's infrastructure and security. Initially accused by Ragnar (Town Commander / Redtooth), Gundalin is later shown to have a more nuanced view of the situation, shifting from a hostile stance to one of sympathetic questioning towards the SEER and Lord's Alliance.

## Relationships
- **Ragnar (Town Commander / Redtooth):** Open political adversary; publicly undermines Ragnar's decisions and blames him for the Cult of the Dragon situation. Yet stood with him during Bad Fruul's ultimatum.
- **Wallace Cartwright (Son):** Loyal and disbelieving — reacted with sharp skepticism when told his father was a suspect (*"Right, my father is doing his best to betray the town so that Orcs will take over? Really?"*). Cited by Gundalin as a source for his post-battle views on the SEER and Lord's Alliance.
- **Silas Carras:** Connected circumstantially by the footprint trail leading from Carras' garden to Gundalin's door.
- **Orsik:** Chased him after the battle, but expressed understanding of Orsik's distrust of outside powers.
- **Raggnar:** Close ally in the militia; follows his lead, though capable of diverging.
- **The Party:** Initially near-hostile post-battle; current disposition uncertain but less adversarial than Raggnar; have circumstantial evidence against him but have not confronted or questioned him directly.

## Arc Score Events
- **Slight positive shift** post-battle: broke from Raggnar's accusatory stance and voiced sympathy for Orsik's position, suggesting emerging distrust of the party's institutional backers rather than the party itself.
