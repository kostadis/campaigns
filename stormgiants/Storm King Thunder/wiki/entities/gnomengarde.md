# Gnomengarde

[[gnomengarde]] is a gnome settlement located within a cave system. It is governed by a [[dual-monarchy]] consisting of [[king-korboz]] and [[king-gnercli]].

## Governance
The settlement's political stability is currently compromised by internal disputes between the two kings. The primary points of contention are:
1. **Seniority Disputes:** Friction regarding the hierarchy of the co-rulers.
2. **The Shapeshifter Threat:** A disagreement over the validity of reports concerning a shape-changing entity within the caves.

## Key Figures
- [[king-korboz]]: Senior co-ruler, currently in a state of hysteria.
- [[king-gnercli]]: Co-ruler, skeptical of the shapeshifter claims.
- [[fibblestib]]: Inventor and caretaker.
- [[dabbledob]]: Inventor and caretaker.
