# SEER

The SEER is a covert intelligence network operating in the North, embedded within and likely subordinate to the Lord’s Alliance. It functions as a secular analog to the [[divine-oracle]]: it provides accurate, actionable intelligence — such as the location of Joon and knowledge of Thule — but is constrained by critical blind spots. Notably, it has no information on Joon’s organization, suggesting either extreme secrecy on the part of its target or internal fragmentation.

Unlike the divine oracle, which is bound by ritual and divine law, the SEER’s limitations are human: incomplete data, operational gaps, and possibly internal rivalries. It uses merchant caravans, disguised agents like Azam, and embedded informants to gather intelligence. It recruits based on demonstrated competence and integrity, as seen in its interest in Unla Kee.

The SEER is not omniscient — its power lies in precision, not breadth. Its existence challenges assumptions about institutional infallibility and introduces narrative vulnerability: even the most powerful networks can be blind to the most dangerous threats. It is a system of constrained knowledge without divine sanction — a cold, calculating mirror to the Oracle of the Eye of the World.
