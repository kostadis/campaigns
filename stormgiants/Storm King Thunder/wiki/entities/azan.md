# Azan

Azan is a caravaneer the party is traveling to meet at the Oasis of Vuerthyl in Anauroch. He has not yet been encountered. His appearance, motivations, faction affiliation, and purpose for the meeting are entirely unknown. 

Azan is intentionally presented as a narrative blank slate — a deliberate design choice to preserve ambiguity and allow for flexible plot development. He is not the same individual as Azam, the SEER operative. His potential connections to Joon’s organization, the Zhentarim, or other covert networks remain unexplored. 

The party’s journey to him is the only confirmed narrative action tied to this entity. His role may evolve into recruiter, broker, antagonist, or neutral party — all possibilities remain open.
