# Zhentarim

A powerful and opportunistic organization that received strategic divine guidance from the Oracle of the Eye of the World. The Oracle advised them to end the Giant War, restore trade routes, and secure preferential treatment from the Storm Giant King — a directive that aligns with their long-term goals of economic dominance and political influence.

This revelation confirms the Zhentarim’s access to divine-level intelligence, elevating them from a mere mercantile faction to a player in cosmic-scale power dynamics. Their alignment with the Oracle’s counsel suggests they may be acting as agents of a hidden divine order — or exploiting a loophole in divine non-intervention. Their role in the Maelstrom arc remains to be seen.
