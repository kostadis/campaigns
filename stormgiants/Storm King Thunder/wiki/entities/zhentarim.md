# Zhentarim

A powerful, shadowy organization specializing in espionage, economic manipulation, and political control. Once the party’s enemy, they are now its silent patron.

The Zhentarim received strategic divine guidance from the Oracle of the Eye of the World, which advised them to end the Giant War, restore trade routes, and secure preferential treatment from the Storm Giant King — directives that align with their long-term goals of economic dominance and political influence. This revelation confirms their access to divine-level intelligence, elevating them from a mere mercantile faction to a player in cosmic-scale power dynamics. Their alignment with the Oracle’s counsel suggests they may be acting as agents of a hidden divine order — or exploiting a loophole in divine non-intervention.

Orsik is a confirmed agent, having betrayed two Harper teleportation circles to his handler, Uzorel “The Viper” Hornbalde. The Zhentarim views him as a valuable asset — not for his power, but for his loyalty and willingness to sacrifice.

The party’s relationship with the Zhentarim is one of quiet complicity. They do not serve them — but they benefit from their silence. The Zhentarim does not demand obedience — it demands silence. And the party, in their [[complicit-silence]], have granted it.

This makes the Zhentarim not a villain, but a mirror: the party has become what they once fought — a group that trades moral clarity for survival. The Zhentarim is not the threat — it is the consequence.

Their power lies not in magic, but in structure: they do not need to control the party. They only need to wait.

Their role in the Maelstrom arc remains to be seen.
