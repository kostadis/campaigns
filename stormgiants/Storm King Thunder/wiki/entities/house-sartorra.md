# House Sartorra

A powerful entity, also associated with [[house-xorlarrin]], involved in sophisticated, illegal interdimensional poison operations.

## Operations
The house manages the smuggling of highly valuable contraband, specifically [[midnight-tears]], across dimensions using specialized planar machinery. Their operations are highly organized, involving forged ledgers, technical manuals for planar rectification, and oversight from high-level figures (possibly referred to as "KP").
