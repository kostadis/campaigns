# House Sartorra

House Sartorra is a drow noble house specializing in the production and interdimensional trafficking of illicit substances, most notably [[midnight-tears]]. Though less prominent than House Xorlarrin in public records, Sartorra operates as a clandestine subsidiary, splinter faction, or independent entity within the broader drow criminal ecosystem. The house leverages its ancestral expertise in poison alchemy and planar chemistry to create contraband that bypasses conventional trade routes.

A powerful entity also associated with [[house-xorlarrin-2026-06-05-181320]], House Sartorra is involved in sophisticated, illegal interdimensional poison operations. Its existence reveals that the drow underworld has evolved beyond traditional power structures into a decentralized, technologically sophisticated criminal infrastructure.

## Operations

The house manages the smuggling of highly valuable contraband, specifically [[midnight-tears]], across dimensions using specialized planar machinery. Its operations are highly organized and marked by administrative deception — forged ledgers, hidden inventories, technical manuals for planar rectification, and the elimination of internal dissent.

Key Operations:
- Production of [[midnight-tears]]
- Interdimensional smuggling via planar artifacts (e.g., repurposed dwarven devices like the [[model-1489-spatial-rectification-calliope]])
- Internal purges of whistleblowers (e.g., Chief Accountant)

## Leadership and Key Figures

Aletra Satora, a fourth daughter of the house, exemplifies its operational ethos: cold professionalism, technological adaptation, and ruthless efficiency. Her botched extraction has partially compromised the house’s operations.

Oversight of the network is attributed to unseen authorities, possibly referred to as “KP.”

## Relationship with House Xorlarrin

House Sartorra’s relationship with House Xorlarrin remains ambiguous: it may be a subordinate branch, a splinter faction, or an independent entity operating under Xorlarrin’s tacit tolerance. Regardless, the two are deeply entangled within the same criminal infrastructure.
