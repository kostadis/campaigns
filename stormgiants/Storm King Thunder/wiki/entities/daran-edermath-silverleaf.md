# Daran Edermath (Silverleaf)

Daran Edermath, known by the surname Silverleaf, is a retired half-elf adventurer who currently lives as an orchardist near [[phandalin]]. He is tall and lean with silver-streaked hair, emerald-green eyes, and a salt-and-pepper beard. He carries himself with the "quiet, weathered wisdom" of one who has seen much and seeks only peace; he is a living example of the [[quiet-wisdom-archetype]]—calm, unassuming, and potentially haunted by a past he refuses to name.

## Interactions
Daran approached the party at the [[stonehill-inn]] with a business proposal regarding a coastal lighthouse west of Phandalin that allegedly lures ships to wreck on the rocks. He framed the operation as a legitimate economic opportunity to salvage treasure from the wreckage. 

The proposal was flatly rejected by [[vukradin]], who characterized the operation as "grave-robbing." Daran’s reaction to this moral objection was one of genuine shock and sincere apology rather than defensiveness. This response reveals a profound moral blind spot: he has never considered the spiritual or ethical implications of disturbing unburied remains, suggesting his adventuring past may have left him emotionally detached from the cost of survival.

## Ambiguities
While presenting as a simple orchardist, Daran's true motivations for the salvage operation and the full extent of his history remain hidden from the party. It is unknown if he seeks profit, closure, or something more personal tied to the lighthouse or the shipwrecks it produces. His orchard may be a facade of peace rather than a path to redemption. 

Daran remains a narrative placeholder; should the party return to the site, his role may evolve from proposer to participant—or victim.
