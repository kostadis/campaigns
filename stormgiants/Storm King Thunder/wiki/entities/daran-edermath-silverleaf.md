# Daran Edermath (Silverleaf)

Daran Edermath, known by the surname Silverleaf, is a retired half-elf adventurer now living as an orchardist near Phandalin. Tall and lean, with silver-streaked hair, emerald-green eyes, and a salt-and-pepper beard, he carries himself with the quiet, weathered wisdom of one who has seen much and seeks only peace.

He approached the party at the [[stonehill-inn]] with a proposal to salvage treasure from the wreckage of ships lured to their doom by a stone lighthouse west of Phandalin. Framing the operation as a legitimate economic opportunity, he was visibly stunned when [[vukradin]] rejected it as grave-robbing — responding not with anger, but with sincere apology. This reaction reveals a profound moral blind spot: he has never considered the spiritual or ethical implications of disturbing unburied remains, suggesting his adventuring past has left him emotionally detached from the cost of survival.

His motivations remain hidden. Whether he seeks profit, closure, or something more personal tied to the lighthouse is unknown. His orchard is a facade of peace, not necessarily redemption. He is a living example of the [[quiet-wisdom-archetype]] — calm, unassuming, and potentially haunted by a past he refuses to name.

He remains a narrative placeholder, his full history and connection to the lighthouse deliberately withheld. Should the party return to the site, his role may evolve from proposer to participant — or victim.
