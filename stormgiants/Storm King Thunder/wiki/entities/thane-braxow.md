# Thane Braxow
Thane Braxow is a Stone Giant Thane, one of the four giant lords assembled by Mirran. He is described as the "quiet one" among the giant lords, indicating a reserved or taciturn nature. Braxow was the first giant casualty of the encounter, killed by Orsik during a melee combat. He was initially resistant to Orsik's attack but was eventually wounded, knocked prone, and killed. After his death, Orsik extracted 15 gemstones embedded in his flesh and dragged his body to a storeroom.

## Relationships
- **Mirran:** Assembled by Mirran as one of four giant lords.
- **Tartha, Vaal, Hellenhild:** Fellow giant lords; present at the same encounter.
- **Orsik:** Killed by him directly and swiftly.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed by Orsik in melee combat.
- Body stored in a storeroom; 15 gemstones looted from his flesh.
