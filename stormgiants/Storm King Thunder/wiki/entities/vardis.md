# Vardis

A peripheral figure present during the opening of the stone chest alongside Thistle Wendrod and Orsik. Vardis witnessed Blooddrinker’s convulsive reaction to the artifact’s activation but played no active role in the event or its aftermath. No further information is known about Vardis’s identity, affiliations, or motivations.

His presence is noted only as part of the scene that triggered Blooddrinker’s magical response, making him a contextual witness rather than a meaningful actor in the narrative arc.
