# Martisha Vinetalker

Martisha Vinetalker is the half-elf proprietor of the [[wayside-inn]]. 

## Profile
She is described as having striking, pleasing features, which stand in sharp contrast to her personality. Her demeanor is characterized as cold, stern, and calculating, though she possesses a notable charisma.

## Interactions
Her engagement with the party was strictly [[transactional-hospitality]]; she provided functional assistance and directions toward the coastal trail leading to the Tower of Storms, but did so without emotional warmth or personal connection.

## Potential Implications
While currently appearing as a utility NPC, her "calculating" nature may suggest deeper involvement in regional economic or political structures, such as the [[adventuring-economy]].
