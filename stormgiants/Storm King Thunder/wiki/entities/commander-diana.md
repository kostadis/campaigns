# Commander Diana

**Commander Diana** is the commanding officer of a **Lord's Alliance** strike team. She oversees various assets, including non-human entities like the dragon **Clarion**. Her leadership style is defined by clear authority and the utilization of specialized "field assets" to achieve military objectives.
