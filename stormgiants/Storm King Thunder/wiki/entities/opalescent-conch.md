# Opalescent Conch

A magical conch shell that served as a spiritual anchor for the sea elf [[miral]]. 

## Properties
- **Spiritual Binding:** The object acted as a vessel for Miral's spirit, preventing her from finding rest until the item was returned to her.
- **Origin:** Recovered from [[moesko]], who had possessed it following Miral's death.
