# Tower of Storms

The [[tower-of-storms]] is a lighthouse/tower that served as the station for [[moesko]].

## The Tower Curse
The tower was subject to a [[tower-curse]] maintained by the ritualistic integration of Moesko's beating heart into the lighthouse mechanism. The heart acted as a power source, emitting a pulsating green light. The curse was broken when the heart was destroyed.
