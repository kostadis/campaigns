# Agra
- **Role:** Kobold scout/operative, former Cult of the Dragon servant; most recently employed by fire giants
- **Faction:** Formerly Cult of the Dragon; no current affiliation (self-described as having "fluid loyalties")
- **First appearance:** Session in the abandoned dwarven outpost — found hiding in the central stone building alongside his companion Gen

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Survival above all else; avoidance of the fire giants he formerly worked for
- **Traits:** Cautious and calculating — he waited out the fight rather than joining it, and only surrendered when the odds were clear. Surprisingly candid once cornered, disclosing detailed intelligence as a bargaining chip. Shows some moral discomfort around his Cult past, particularly when confronted by Thistle. His gesture of swallowing dirt before departing suggests personal ritual or oath-keeping.

## History with the Party
1. **Initial encounter:** Agra and Gen hid in the outpost's central building while Nug and other kobolds triggered a trap against the party. Agra counseled Gen to stay quiet and wait.
2. **Surrender:** After the trap failed and the other kobolds were defeated, Agra stepped out waving a white flag.
3. **Confrontation by Thistle:** Thistle identified marks linking Agra to the Cult of the Dragon. Agra acknowledged his past service, appeared genuinely unsettled, claimed he "didn't mean any of it," and said he had no knowledge of Thistle's girlfriend's whereabouts.
4. **Intelligence exchange:** Agra provided substantial information about giant activity, kobold employment across factions, inter-giant tensions, the fire giant mission to the outpost, and what Nug had reported about the southern room (treasure, magical fire symbol, lava river, explosive creatures).
5. **Negotiation:** Attempted to offer the party his and Gen's services as employees. When refused for lack of funds, offered information. Gen suggested treasure; the party accepted. Dwarf Orsik granted permission to leave.
6. **Departure:** Released by the party. Before leaving, addressed Vardis's concern about him reporting to the fire giants — explained the giants expected treasure or death, making a return suicidal. Swallowed a handful of dirt as apparent punctuation of this point. Departed with Gen.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Left the abandoned dwarven outpost on foot with Gen; destination unknown
- **Active plans:** Avoiding the fire giants entirely; implied to be cutting ties with that employment
- **What the party knows:** He has no intention of returning to the fire giants; his loyalty is to his own survival
- **What remains hidden:** His actual destination, whether his account of the giant situation was complete, the full extent of his Cult of the Dragon history, and what the dirt-swallowing gesture signifies

## Relationships
- **Gen:** Traveling companion; appears to be his partner or close associate — Gen suggested the treasure offer that resolved the negotiation
- **Nug:** Fellow kobold in the fire giant's employ; Agra relayed Nug's scouting report of the southern room
- **Gun:** Another kobold in the group, noted for having heard the outpost was cursed
- **The fire giants:** Former employers; Agra considers returning to them a death sentence
- **Cult of the Dragon:** Former masters; connection appears to have ended when the Red Dragon the group served departed
- **Thistle:** Recognized Thistle's Cult torture marks; the interaction was visibly uncomfortable for Agra
- **Vardis:** Directly addressed his concern about the fire giants before departing
- **Orsik:** Orsik formally granted permission for Agra and Gen to leave
