# Agra
Agra is a kobold and the primary companion/leader of **Gen**. They were discovered together at an abandoned dwarven outpost.

### Role & Dynamics
Agra serves as the stabilizing force for Gen. During their encounter, she was responsible for physically restraining Gen to prevent him from acting impulsively during a standoff. Her role highlights [[servant-master-dynamics-2026-06-06-002521]], as she provides the leadership and restraint that Gen lacks.

**Current Status:** Last seen departing the abandoned dwarven outpost with Gen after a successful negotiation involving treasure.
