# Dral Thelev
Dral Thelev is a sardonic, self-aware half-orc who operates Danivarr's House in Everlund as a Harper contact and a proxy for the Zoar family. He is known for his hospitality, social adeptness, and genuine investment in the people he works with, despite his often biting humor and disdain for Silverymoon's cultural insularity.

## Relationships
- **Darathra Shendrel:** Trusted friend and professional contact; recognized her badge and remarked that "something must be amiss."
- **Tamalin Zoar:** Mutual acquaintance with Orsik; connected to her brother High Captain Horix Zoar; trusts him as a proxy contact.
- **High Captain Horix Zoar:** Not a direct ally, but Dral understands the political landscape well enough to use Zoar as a resource for the party.
- **Orsik:** Prior acquaintance via Tamalin Zoar; Dral is warmer and more candid with him than with the group at large.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Danivarr's House, Everlund
- **Active Role:** Operating as a Harper contact and regional information broker; facilitating connections between the party, the Harpers, and Everlund's political leadership.
