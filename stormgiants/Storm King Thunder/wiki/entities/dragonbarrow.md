# Dragonbarrow

A burial site and tomb of significant historical and magical importance, serving as the final resting place for [[lady-alagondar]]. Located within the [[ice-spire-mountains-white-dragon-connection-2026-06-06-133513]], Dragonbarrow was constructed as both a monument to her legacy and a ward against the very power she once subdued.

## Layout & Significance

The site is divided into distinct areas:

* **Resting Chamber:** The location where Lady Alagondar was laid to rest. Her remains are preserved in an enchanted sarcophagus, surrounded by runes that inhibit decay and ward off necromantic interference.
* **Northern Chamber:** A separate area containing a dragon's skull, upon which the [[dragon-slayer-sword]] was placed. The skull, believed to be that of the ancient drake she slew in her final battle, serves as a focal point for the sword’s binding magic.

The deliberate separation of Lady Alagondar’s body from her primary weapon suggests a ritualistic or protective purpose that remains unexplained. This spatial division may have been intended to prevent the sword’s residual dragon-slaying energy from corrupting her spirit, or to ensure the weapon could not be reclaimed without fulfilling a forgotten rite. 

Beyond its role as a tomb, Dragonbarrow served as a place of guardianship for boney—sentinel spirits bound to the site after Lady Alagondar’s death—to deter grave robbers and unworthy claimants of the sword. The chambers are sealed by arcane locks and guarded by lingering enchantments that activate upon intrusion.
