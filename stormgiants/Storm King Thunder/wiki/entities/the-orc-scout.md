# The Orc Scout

A disciplined forward scout for an orc raiding party operating near Neverwinter Wood. Unlike typical mindless raiders, he demonstrates tactical proficiency, using hand signals to coordinate ambush positions and managing tactical disengagements.

## Combat Behavior
During an ambush attempt, the scout demonstrated high discipline but was noted to be susceptible to distraction by significant threats (such as [[soma]] in bear form). He is capable of both close-quarters engagement and ranged combat with a bow.

## Psychological Profile
The scout exhibits an intense, irrational terror when encountering [[valphine]]. Upon recognizing her as a Drow worshipping Lathander, he abandoned his tactical position and fled into the forest. This suggests that her specific racial and religious identity carries significant weight or dread within his culture or specific raiding party.

## Relationships
- **[[bone-necklace-orc]]**: His superior, who reprimanded him for losing focus during the skirmish.
- **[[valphine]]**: The primary source of his sudden terror and flight.
- **[[soma]]**: An adversary who wounded him during the encounter.
