# Delaan Winterhound

[[delaan-winterhound]] was a senior, highly influential, and significant historical figure within the [[emerald-enclave]] during the era of the [[war-of-the-dragons]]. According to historical accounts provided by [[lyra]], he was regarded as one of the wisest members of the Enclave.

## Historical Significance
Delaan is the pivotal figure responsible for the current ideological fault line within the [[emerald-enclave]]. 

Originally, he held a strict non-interventionist position, believing the Enclave's role was solely to protect the natural order rather than participating in mortal or political conflicts. However, during the threat of [[tiamat]]'s summoning, he was persuaded by the hero [[malfire]] to abandon the Enclave's policy of neutrality in favor of joining the coalition against the dragon goddess.

His decision to commit Enclave forces to combat the forces of [[tiamat]] created a permanent rift between those who favor active [[interventionism]] and those who adhere to the [[anti-interventionist-faction]] philosophy.

## Personality
Historical records suggest Delaan was a principled and cautious leader. While he was deeply committed to naturalist philosophy, he was not dogmatic, as evidenced by his willingness to change course when presented with the gravity of the [[war-of-the-dragons]].

## Current Status
Unknown. All information regarding Delaan is historical and anecdotal.
