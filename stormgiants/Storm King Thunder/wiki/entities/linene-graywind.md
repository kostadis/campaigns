# Linene Graywind

Linene Graywind is the 35-year-old proprietor of the [[lionshield-coster]] outpost located in [[phandalin]]. She is characterized as a savvy, socially warm, and inquisitive merchant who maintains a professional distance from local mercenary services.

## Profile
- **Role:** Outpost Master / Merchant
- **Affiliation:** [[lionshield-coster]]
- **Location:** [[phandalin]]

## Key Observations
- **Commercial Interests:** She demonstrates a high level of enthusiasm for goods with suspected planar associations, often paying significant premiums (e.g., double market rate for pelts) to satisfy the interests of her "bosses."
- **Diplomatic Style:** She is adept at deflecting direct offers of service (such as those from [[vukradin]]) by redirecting conversations toward local rumors or logistical difficulties.
- **Social Behavior:** She is known to engage with the local community, notably attending performances at the [[stonehill-tavern]].

## Relationships
- **[[lionshield-coster]] Leadership:** Operates under the direction of unnamed "bosses" who dictate procurement priorities regarding planar goods.
- **[[vukradin]]:** A recurring contact who has attempted to provide protection and procurement services; Linene has consistently deflected these offers.
- **Valphine:** A recipient of Linene's public praise during a performance at the [[stonehill-tavern]].
