# Klauth ("Old Snarl")
Ancient red dragon, self-styled sovereign of the North. Operates through proxies and calculated gifts rather than direct involvement. Core drive appears to be self-preservation and territorial control. His aid to the party is self-interested, offering a reward at his hidden vale in exchange for the party's success in eliminating giant leaders.

## Relationships
- **Delsephine:** Devoted agent and former Cult of the Dragon member; she left the Cult to serve him directly.
- **The party (3HPP):** Transactional ally — he needs them to do what he cannot or will not do openly.
- **The giants:** Rivals and current enemies; their rising power threatens dragon dominance in the region.
- **The Lord's Alliance:** He actively does not want them poking around the Spine of the World — reason unstated.
- **Cult of the Dragon:** Unclear relationship; Delsephine defected from the Cult to serve him directly.
