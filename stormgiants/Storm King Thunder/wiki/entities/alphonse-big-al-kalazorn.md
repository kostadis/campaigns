# Alphonse "Big Al" Kalazorn

**Alphonse "Big Al" Kalazorn** (also known as **Big Al**) is the owner of [[butterskull-ranch]] and the retired Sheriff of Triboar. He is the predecessor to the current Sheriff, [[darathra]].

## Profile
Big Al is characterized by a pragmatic, tough-minded law enforcement persona, though he possesses a significant emotional vulnerability regarding his past. He was recently a victim of [[borg-the-hammer]], who held him captive in a cellar at [[butterskull-ranch]] to serve as bait for adventurers during an ambush.

## Key Motivations
* **Petunia:** His primary emotional and pragmatic priority is his prized cow, [[petunia-the-cow]]. He previously offered a suit of mithril chainmail as a reward for her recovery.
* **Reconciliation:** After being pressed by the party, he confessed to abandoning his fiancée, [[qelline-alderleaf]], the night before their wedding. He is currently attempting to make amends with her.

## History with the Party
* **Rescue:** He was located by [[vukradin]] in the ranch cellar. His successful rescue by the party effectively neutralized the trap set by [[borg-the-hammer]].
* **Recovery:** [[soma]] utilized magic to locate [[petunia-the-cow]].
* **Confession:** Following the recovery of his cow, he departed to face his past.

## Current Status
Last seen traveling to [[qelline-alderleaf]]'s farm to seek forgiveness and discuss grazing rights for his cow. [[butterskull-ranch]] remains under the hostile occupation of [[borg-the-hammer]] and the [[rot-tusk-ogres]].
