# Overview
This wiki covers the narrative universe described in the source document "Worvil Forkbeard ('The Weevil' / 'Larg')". The main focus is on the central figure, Worvil Forkbeard, a fugitive dwarf and career criminal who operates as a caravan intelligence broker. The wiki includes pages on key entities like Worvil Forkbeard, Xaltivar, and Adimond Kroskas, as well as concepts such as the Axe of Mirabar, bounty hunting, and the ore caravan movement pattern. Additionally, there are queries exploring Worvil's motivations and connections to other characters.

## Key Entities
- **Worvil Forkbeard ("The Weevil" / "Larg"):** Fugitive dwarf, career criminal, and caravan intelligence broker; subject of an active 5,000 gp bounty from the Axe of Mirabar; aliases include "The Weevil" and "Larg"
- **Xaltivar:** Leader of the Axe of Mirabar
- **Adimond Kroskas:** Investigated by Worvil, likely the cause of his death
- **Vardis:** Recalled Worvil's death and chamber damage, identified as a Blue Dragon event
- **Gauche:** Knew details about activity near the teleportation circle at the time of death

## Key Concepts
- **Bounty Hunting:** Pursuit of individuals by a faction for a reward
- **Identity Denial:** Efforts to mislead or deceive about one’s true identity
- **Ore Caravan Movement Pattern:** A pattern governing the movement of ore caravans out of Mirabar's mines
- **Bugbear Rivalries:** Rivalries between bugbears and Worvil

## Themes
- **Influence:** The extent of Worvil's influence over his followers and the commune
- **Allegiances:** The nature of Worvil's relationships with Xaltivar, Adimond Kroskas, and Vardis
