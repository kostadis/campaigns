# Soma

[[soma]] is a Tortle druid of Mieliki and a member of the adventuring party, serving as the party's paid guide — though her relationship with her primary employer, [[vukradin]], is strained by financial debt and moral friction.

## Affiliations and Background
* **[[emerald-enclave]]**: Soma is a member of the [[emerald-enclave]], specifically aligned with the [[naturalist-faction]] and its anti-interventionist doctrines. She holds a quiet contempt for magical constructs and "unnatural" creations.
* **Protégé and Trainee**: She was the primary protégé and trainee of [[adabra-gwynn]], who viewed Soma as her ideological heir. Soma internalized Adabra’s teachings completely, believing they shared a mutual understanding — a tacit deception that now haunts her.
* **The Chosen**: She is wrestling with the existential weight of being "chosen" by [[meril]], a sentiment reinforced by the centaur [[xanth]].

## Personality and Philosophy
Soma is characterized by a sardonic, blunt, and pragmatic personality. She frequently uses mockery to cut through philosophical or bureaucratic tangles. While she presents an unsentimental exterior regarding gold and violence, she is deeply invested in protecting life and acting with purpose.

She is currently undergoing a profound ideological crisis triggered by the **motivated blight paradox**: Adabra’s naturalist doctrine insists that corruption must be passive, emergent, and unguided — yet Soma has witnessed deliberate, puppet-controlled corruption (e.g., Narcellia Varammyr’s influence on Bad Fruul). When she used [[merils-staff]] to verify Adabra as a *true believer*, it confirmed the sincerity of Adabra’s worldview — not its completeness. This creates an unresolvable tension: if Adabra’s truth is real, how can the party’s necessary interventions be justified? And if the party’s actions are morally required, how can Adabra’s philosophy remain coherent?

Soma now views Adabra’s campaign as adversarial — “bringing her own war.” She was conspicuously omitted from Adabra’s advocacy to Harbin Wester and later became the target of Adabra’s political case at the Enclave council, accused of teaching others to interfere rather than observe. Her journey is no longer one of learning — it is one of unlearning. She has resolved to confront Adabra directly: “How can you accept a *motivated* blight?”

## Combat and Tactics
Soma is a tactically sharp combatant. She utilizes [[wild-shape]] (specifically elk, bear, and spider) not merely for damage, but for crowd control, scouting, and strategic repositioning. She is known to correct allies mid-combat to optimize tactical outcomes.

### Party Dynamics
There is noted tactical and ideological friction between Soma and [[valphine]], particularly regarding combat efficacy and spell usage.

## Key Assets and Mysteries
### Assets
* **[[merils-staff]]**: A reactive druidic staff that has recently grown a **fourth bud** following the encounter at the Circle of Thunder. It played a pivotal role in Soma’s verification of Adabra’s sincerity.
* **[[boney]]**: A skeletal horse that serves as her tactical companion and combat asset.

### Withheld Information
Soma has deliberately withheld knowledge regarding:
* A white dragon rumor.
* The nature of an unidentified item retrieved from an ogre's corpse.
