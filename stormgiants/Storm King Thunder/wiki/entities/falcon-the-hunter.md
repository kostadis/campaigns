# Falcon the Hunter

**Falcon the Hunter** (also known as **The Falcon**) is a figure associated with the druid conclave. A retired adventurer and pragmatic entrepreneur, he is the former proprietor of the [[falcon-hunting-lodge]]. He maintains a carefully constructed public persona as a legendary "dragonslayer" to drive business to his establishment.

## Identity & Personality
Falcon is a warm and gregarious host, though he is a "pragmatic fabricator" who uses his past—specifically his survival of an encounter with the blue dragon [[iymrith]]—to bolster his reputation. He is deeply loyal to friends but can become defensive and flustered when his contradictions are exposed. 

He is highly susceptible to psychological manipulation when exhausted or guilt-ridden, a vulnerability exploited by [[adabra-gwynn]] to pull him into the [[anti-interventionist-faction]].

## Key Relationships
* **[[vukradin]]**: A close friend with whom he has a highly strained relationship. Their bond was severely damaged by accusations of theft, a break-in at Falcon's private quarters, and Falcon's public attack on Vukradin at the [[druid-council]]. They are currently in a state of mediated reconciliation.
* **[[valphine]]**: A spiritual mentor and mediator who helped Falcon recognize the manipulation of [[adabra-gwynn]] and guided him back toward an [[interventionism]] stance. [[valphine]] has been actively laying diplomatic groundwork with him, including proposing a musical instrument as a gesture of good faith to mitigate [[vukradin]]'s complicated standing.
* **[[adabra-gwynn]]**: A druid who successfully used Falcon's trauma to recruit him into the anti-interventionist movement, a move he later came to regret.
* **[[iymrith]]**: The blue dragon whose hoard provided the "seed capital" for Falcon's business. He is the sole survivor of his adventuring party's encounter with her.

## Current Status & Objectives
Following the destruction of his lodge by the [[cult-of-talos]] and their orc allies, Falcon is currently focused on:
* Reclaiming the [[falcon-hunting-lodge]].
* Resolving the poisoning of mountain-toe-gold-mine.
* Clearing the [[woodland-manse]] of half-orc and orc threats.
* Repairing his relationship with [[vukradin]] through substantive gestures.
