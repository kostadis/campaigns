## Overview
This wiki covers the narrative universe described in the source document "Brimskarda". The main focus is on the fire giant duchess Brimskarda and her role within the stronghold of Ironslag. The wiki includes pages on key entities like Brimskarda, the unnamed gate guard, and the concept of starstruck, as well as themes such as fandom. Additionally, there are queries exploring the fan connection and a comparison page highlighting the interconnectedness of characters within the story.

## Key Entities
- **Brimskarda:** Fire giant duchess of Ironslag, merchant specializing in magical goods for discerning magic users, known to Unla Kee and associated with the Fishyard.
- **Unnamed Gate Guard:** Messenger, starstruck.
- **Starstruck:** Phenomenon of admiration or fandom.

## Key Concepts
- **Fire Giants:** Mythical beings associated with Ironslag, a fire giant stronghold.
- **Ironslag:** Fire giant stronghold, home to Brimskarda.
- **Smoke Mephits:** Small, fire-breathing demons that serve Brimskarda.
- **Ogres:** Large, brutish humanoid creatures.
- **Goblins:** Small, mischievous humanoid creatures.
- **Duchess:** A female ruler or leader.
