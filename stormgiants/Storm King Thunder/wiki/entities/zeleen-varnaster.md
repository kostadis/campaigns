# Zeleen Varnaster

Zeleen Varnaster is the leader of the [[whiskered-gang]], a crew of wererats currently operating out of the mountain-toe-gold-mine. 

## Personality and Demeanor
Zeleen operates with a "gangster" persona: he is transactional, prideful, and highly sensitive to perceived slights. While he presents as a pragmatic leader seeking security for his people, intelligence suggests he is fundamentally opportunistic. He responds well to [[tactical-pragmatism]] and shows respect for those who use [[implied-intimidation]] effectively.

## Key Interactions
- **Negotiations:** He initially demanded 1,000 gp for security but was coerced into a "protection-for-shelter" agreement by [[brewbarry]] using a combination of logical argument and physical threats.
- **The Cheese Incident:** An attempt by [[valphine]] to negotiate using [[petunias-cheese]] was initially viewed as an insult, though the commodity eventually became a key tool for maintaining the peace.
- **The Earthstone Crisis:** After being publicly rebuked by [[valphine]], Zeleen capitulated to the task of retrieving the [[earthstone-gem]] from orc-infested territory.

## Current Status
Zeleen is currently leading a delegation into the forest to retrieve the [[earthstone-gem]]. There is significant doubt regarding his competence to complete this task, leading the party to prepare a contingency involving a fake gem.
