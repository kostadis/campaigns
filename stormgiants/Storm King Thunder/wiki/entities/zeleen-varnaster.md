# Zeleen Varnaster

Zeleen Varnaster is the leader of the [[whiskered-gang]], a crew of wererats currently operating out of the [[mountain-s-toe-gold-mine]]. Once an enemy responsible for past attacks on dwarven miners, she now serves as a pragmatic, if uneasy, negotiating counterpart to [[horia-stonetallow]]. Her role is transactional: she secures wererat labor and security in exchange for access to the mine’s resources.

Horia’s acceptance of the arrangement was contingent on a formal apology for past violence — a demand that underscores her commitment to [[contractual-morality]] and procedural justice. Zeleen complied, demonstrating that even those with violent histories can be integrated into structured systems — if the terms are respected.

## Personality and Demeanor

Zeleen operates with a "gangster" persona: she is transactional, prideful, and highly sensitive to perceived slights. While she presents as a pragmatic leader seeking security for her people, intelligence suggests she is fundamentally opportunistic. She responds well to [[tactical-pragmatism]] and shows respect for those who use [[implied-intimidation]] effectively.

Zeleen is not evil — she is functional. She embodies the possibility of coexistence without trust. Her presence in the mine is a quiet challenge to the party’s assumptions: redemption does not require forgiveness — only accountability. Her leadership is rooted in survival, not nobility.

## Key Interactions

- **Negotiations with Horia Stonetallow:** She secured a formalized alliance with [[horia-stonetallow]] after issuing a public apology for past attacks on dwarven miners — a condition Horia demanded to uphold [[contractual-morality]].
- **Negotiations with Brewbarry:** She initially demanded 1,000 gp for security but was coerced into a "protection-for-shelter" agreement by [[brewbarry]] using a combination of logical argument and physical threats.
- **The Cheese Incident:** An attempt by [[valphine]] to negotiate using [[petunias-cheese]] was initially viewed as an insult, though the commodity eventually became a key tool for maintaining the peace.
- **The Earthstone Crisis:** After being publicly rebuked by [[valphine]], Zeleen capitulated to the task of retrieving the [[earthstone-gem]] from orc-infested territory.

## Current Status

Zeleen is currently leading a delegation into the forest to retrieve the [[earthstone-gem]]. There is significant doubt regarding her competence to complete this task, leading the party to prepare a contingency involving a fake gem.

Horia remains skeptical of wererat mining techniques, and their relationship is cautious, not cordial. They shake hands and exchange contracts, but mutual interest — not trust — holds their alliance together.
