# Brewbarry

[[brewbarry]] is a loud, physically imposing, and direct member of the adventuring party. A former [[uthgardt]] barbarian, he is characterized by a dichotomy between his violent, blunt combat style and a profound, instinctive empathy for victims of cruelty.

## Personality & Motivations
Brewbarry leads with his halberd and announces himself boldly. While he appears to be a "blunt instrument" socially and physically, he possesses a strong moral compass, particularly regarding the treatment of animals and those victimized by his former kin. He is motivated by companionship, a rejection of [[uthgardt]] cruelty, and a persistent search for beverages.

## Combat Profile
In battle, Brewbarry utilizes [[barbarian-rage]] as a tool for simplification and damage mitigation. He is a highly effective melee combatant who actively tracks the battlefield to reposition for tactical advantages.

* **Primary Weapon:** Halberd.
* **Special Ability:** [[elemental-cleaver]] — used to retrieve thrown handaxes.
* **Tactical Synergy:** Demonstrates high-level coordination with [[vukradin]], often benefiting from redirected attacks to gain combat advantage.

## Relationships
* **[[valphine]]**: Shares a pragmatic and combat-oriented alignment, particularly regarding the use of force and interrogation. Their dynamic involves instinctive protection (e.g., defending her from orc attacks) but also moral friction regarding [[non-lethal-vs-lethal-combat]].
* **[[vukradin]]**: A close, tactical companion. Their relationship involves high combat cohesion but also "affectionate exasperation" due to Brewbarry's tendency to kill targets the party intended to spare.
* **[[soma]]**: An effective melee partner during high-intensity engagements.
* **[[shrimpy]]**: Brewbarry has taken a self-appointed role as the orc's captor and namer, finding a shared connection in their mutual experiences of exile.
* **[[uthgardt]]**: His former faction; he is currently estranged/exiled and holds a deep distaste for their methods.

## History Highlights
* **Ogre Encounter**: First major combat alongside the party.
* **Gnomengarde**: His status as an *ex-Uthgardt* helped resolve a bureaucratic loophole regarding shapeshifter protocols.
* **Circle of Thunder**: Served as a primary melee combatant against anchorites and twig blights.
