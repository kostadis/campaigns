# Sister Kaella

Sister Kaella is a ruthless, methodical operative and former spymaster for [[the-carver]]'s network in [[phandalin]]. Known by the aliases [[Sister Kayla]] and [[Sister Kella]], she frequently utilizes the guise of a religious figure ("priest's cloth") to mask her criminal and intelligence activities through [[religious-cover-subversion]].

## Profile

### Role and Tactics
* **Former Role:** Spymaster and deadly field agent/assassin operating under [[the-carver]].
* **Current Status:** Independent defector and escapee.
* **Combat Profile:** A highly lethal combatant who utilizes a rapier and an offhand dagger.
* **Specialization:** A specialist in information manipulation, selective disclosure, and tactical independence. She is known to pre-profile targets and engineer social encounters to her advantage.
* **Personality:** Highly composed, calculating, and pragmatic, operating with a long-term survival orientation.

## History

### Defection and Transition
Kaella's loyalty to [[the-carver]] collapsed following intelligence failures caused by [[borg]], which left her vulnerable to the Carver's retribution. Following her neutralization by the party—an event that marked a significant loss of personnel for the organization's field presence—[[jax]] exploited her defeat to launch his own ambush.

### The Valphine Arrangement
She established a private, intelligence-based partnership with [[valphine]], whom she identified as a fellow practitioner of [[moral-grayness-pragmatism]].

### Notable Engagements
* **Assassination of [[locutus]]:** Following the confirmed death of [[borg-the-hammer]], Kaella assassinated the intermediary [[locutus]] to close a "loose end" during her meeting with Valphine. During this engagement, she missed two strikes with her rapier before successfully cutting him down with her offhand dagger.
* **Conflict with [[corbin]]:** While under the guard of [[corbin]], Kaella successfully murdered him to facilitate her escape while the party was engaged in other objectives.

### Current Status
Her current whereabouts are unknown, though the [[lord-s-alliance]] claims they can track her. While in custody, she delivered a critical warning to the party regarding [[the-carver]]'s descent into madness and his intent to bind a white dragon at [[icespire-hold]].
