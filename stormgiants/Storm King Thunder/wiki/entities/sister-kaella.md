# Sister Kaella

Sister Kaella is a ruthless, methodical operative and former spymaster for [[the-carver]]'s network in [[phandalin]]. Known by the aliases [[Sister Kayla]] and [[Sister Kella]], she frequently utilizes the guise of a religious figure ("priest's cloth") to mask her criminal and intelligence activities through [[religious-cover-subversion]]. She is a defector who now operates independently, embodying the party’s internal moral fractures — a figure who acts decisively in a world where gods are silent and ethics are uncertain.

## Profile

### Role and Tactics
* **Former Role:** Spymaster and deadly field agent/assassin operating under [[the-carver]].
* **Current Status:** Independent defector and escapee.
* **Combat Profile:** A highly lethal combatant who utilizes a rapier and an offhand dagger.
* **Specialization:** A specialist in information manipulation, selective disclosure, and tactical independence. She is known to pre-profile targets and engineer social encounters to her advantage.
* **Personality:** Highly composed, calculating, and pragmatic, operating with a long-term survival orientation. She operates under [[moral-grayness-pragmatism]], rejecting rigid moral codes in favor of strategic outcomes.
* **Moral Ambiguity:** Her actions — particularly the murder of corbin — are decisive and unambiguous, yet their ethical implications remain contested within the party. She serves as a catalyst for exploring the moral ambiguity of non-human sentience, especially in relation to boney, who witnessed but did not intervene.

## History

### Defection and Transition
Kaella's loyalty to [[the-carver]] collapsed following intelligence failures caused by [[borg]], which left her vulnerable to the Carver's retribution. Following her neutralization by the party—an event that marked a significant loss of personnel for the organization's field presence—[[jax]] exploited her defeat to launch his own ambush.

### The Valphine Arrangement
She established a private, intelligence-based partnership with [[valphine]], whom she identified as a fellow practitioner of [[moral-grayness-pragmatism]].

### Notable Engagements
* **Assassination of [[locutus]]:** Following the confirmed death of [[borg-the-hammer]], Kaella assassinated the intermediary [[locutus]] to close a "loose end" during her meeting with Valphine. During this engagement, she missed two strikes with her rapier before successfully cutting him down with her offhand dagger.
* **Conflict with corbin:** While under the guard of corbin, Kaella murdered him to facilitate her escape while the party was engaged in other objectives. This act became a pivotal moment in the party’s moral reckoning, particularly as boney observed but did not intervene — an inaction later questioned by Soma and tied to broader themes of [[moral-ambiguity-of-inaction-undead]].
* **Warning to the Party:** While in custody, she delivered a critical warning regarding [[the-carver]]'s descent into madness and his intent to bind a white dragon at [[icespire-hold]].

### Current Status
Her current whereabouts are unknown, though the [[lord-s-alliance]] claims they can track her. [[jenna-roscoe]], as a representative of the alliance, uses Kaella’s location as political leverage.
