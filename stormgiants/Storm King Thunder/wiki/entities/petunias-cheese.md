# Petunia's Cheese

A high-value commodity produced at [[butterskull-ranch]]. 

## Strategic Importance
In recent negotiations, this cheese has served as a vital diplomatic tool. While initially used in a way that offended [[zeleen-varnaster]], it eventually became a primary incentive for the [[whiskered-gang]] to maintain their security arrangement at the mountain-toe-gold-mine.
