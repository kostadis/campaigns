# Thistle

Thistle is a party member with a personal connection to Joon (uncle) and Thule (drake). His casual mention of Thule triggered an intense, uncharacteristic emotional reaction from Azam — a moment of visible distress and immediate deferral to Hsing.

Thistle’s relationship to these figures suggests he may be a living link to hidden histories or suppressed operations. His willingness to challenge authority (e.g., demanding higher payment) contrasts with Azam’s deference, making him an unwitting catalyst for revelations.

He is not an agent, but a conduit — his personal ties expose the cracks in the SEER’s intelligence network and force Azam to confront personal ghosts under the weight of institutional silence.
