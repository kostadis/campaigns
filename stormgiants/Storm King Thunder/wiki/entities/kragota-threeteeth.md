# Kragota-Threeteeth

Kragota-Threeteeth (also referred to as **Kagrota**) was a kobold leader, warlord, and field commander who operated an intelligence network within the kobold tribe. Self-styled as the "Mother of Bones," she was a prominent figure within the forces of [[Bad Fruul]] and an apparent worshiper of [[Bane]].

## Identity and Personality
Kragota was an aggressive and domineering combatant. She demonstrated both martial prowess and divine spellcasting abilities, suggesting significant power behind her bluster. In combat, she was known to express frustration at the failures of her troops. Her motivations were tied to the orders of [[Bad Fruul]], specifically the destruction of a nearby commune.

## History and Combat
Kragota first appeared at the Brandimen cabin, where she commanded an orc and goblin warband. At this site, she held commune survivors prisoner in a cage; one prisoner had been found with a severed finger. 

During the confrontation with the party:
- She attempted to use a javelin (which missed).
- She summoned a spiritual weapon in the form of a hammer.
- She struck several party members, including Unla Kee, Wallace, and Orsik.
- After being reduced to 1 HP by Thistle, she screamed, **"Fairies! We had a deal with Prince Thornacious!"** before being killed by Wallace.

### The Brandimen Cabin Site
Beneath the cabin where Kagrota fought, a hidden underground chamber was discovered. It contained:
- A crude altar to [[Bane]], featuring a black sunburst symbol, two black candles, an elven woman's skull, and a shattered jade statuette.
- Several chests of treasure.

## Relationships
- **[[Bad Fruul]]:** Her direct superior; they issued the orders for her to destroy the commune.
- **[[Prince Thornacious]]:** An entity with whom Kagrota had an existing "deal." The nature of this agreement remains unknown, though she invoked it in a panic during her final moments.
- **[[Tove Brandimen]]:** A local individual described by a prisoner as being "thick as thieves" with Kagrota, suggesting Tove may have been a collaborator.
- **[[Nug]]:** A kobold who led an ambush group at the barricade, motivated by territorial defense, under Kragota's broader influence.
- **[[Agra]]:** A fellow kobold who viewed Kragota with private contempt for picking an unwinnable fight.
- **The Party:** The combatants who ultimately killed her.

## Intelligence Network
Kragota operated an intelligence network designed to gather, analyze, and disseminate information. This network focused on internal tribal affairs and external threats. Such networks typically consist of:
- **Leadership:** Overseers like Kragota who make strategic decisions.
- **Recruiters:** Agents gathering information.
- **Analysts:** Professionals interpreting data.
- **Operators:** Personnel executing tasks.
