# The 3HPP

**The 3HPP** (the adventuring party) is a group of individuals currently engaged in a complex, transactional, and often hostile relationship with the demon lord [[bob-saget]].

## Relationship with Bob Saget
- **Initial Contact:** Initially believed to be Bob's liberators, the party's accidental improvement of a ritual script led to his manifestation in [[triboar]].
- **Conflict:** The relationship soured after Bob's ritualistic murders in [[silverymoon]] were exposed. The party successfully banished him to the Astral Plane.
- **Current Status:** The party is currently bound to Bob by two major deals:
    1. **Fulfilled:** The release of 200 prisoners from a pocket dimension for 20,000 gold.
    2. **Pending:** A high-stakes performance at [[iymrith]]'s grand reveal, for which Bob demands specific biological payments (hybrid dragons).

The party must balance the lethal threats posed by Bob against the potential utility of his theatrical and demonic capabilities.
