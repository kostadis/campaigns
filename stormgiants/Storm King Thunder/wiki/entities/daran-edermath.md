# Daran Edermath

Daran Edermath is a retired adventurer currently living as an orchardist near [[phandalin]]. He is a tall, lean half-elf characterized by silver-streaked hair, emerald-green eyes, and a salt-and-pepper beard.

## Profile
- **Role:** Orchardist and former adventurer.
- **Demeanor:** Carries himself with "quiet, weathered wisdom."
- **Current Objective:** Seeking a crew for a salvage operation at a coastal lighthouse west of Phandalin.

## Interactions
Daran approached the party at the [[stonehill-inn]] with a business proposition regarding a lighthouse that allegedly lures ships to wreck on the rocks. The proposal was flatly rejected by [[vukradin]], who characterized the operation as "grave-robbing." Daran's reaction to this moral objection was one of genuine shock and apology, rather than defensiveness.

## Ambiguities
While presenting as a simple orchardist, his true motivations for the salvage operation and the full extent of his adventuring history remain unverified and hidden from the party. It is unknown if he has a personal connection to the lighthouse or the shipwrecks it produces.
