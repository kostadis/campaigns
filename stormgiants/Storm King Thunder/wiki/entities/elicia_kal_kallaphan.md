- **Role:** Bounty payout handler / operative
- **Faction:** The Hand of Yartar
- **First Appearance:** Met the party when they came to collect on bounties; she handled the payout directly.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals/drives:** Unclear beyond her operational role managing bounty payments for the Hand of Yartar.
- Severe, humorless, and perceptive — she sees through the party's attempts at levity without reaction. Projects a no-nonsense professionalism consistent with someone who handles sensitive financial transactions for a criminal or mercenary organization.
