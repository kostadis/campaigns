# Stone chest

A mysterious, unadorned stone container encountered in a cursed cave. Its purpose, origin, and composition are unknown. When opened by Thistle Wendrod, Orsik, and Vardis, it triggered violent convulsions in Blooddrinker — a magical resonance event that nearly killed him.

The chest’s effect suggests a latent, non-intentional magical connection to Blooddrinker — possibly tied to the same curse that shrank him. It does not appear to be a storage vessel, trap, or ritual object in the conventional sense. Instead, it behaves like a receiver or amplifier for a specific magical frequency embedded in the bear.

Its properties parallel those of the [[headband-of-intellect-as-demonic-influence]] and [[king-hekaton]]’s conch — artifacts that exert influence on beings through hidden, non-obvious mechanisms. Unlike those items, however, the stone chest has no known user interface, inscription, or activation ritual. It reacts only to the presence or state of its bonded entity.

The party has not yet determined whether the chest has other bonded entities, or if it is unique to Blooddrinker. Its existence raises critical questions about the nature of magical bonds, the possibility of sentient artifacts, and the hidden history of the cursed cave.
