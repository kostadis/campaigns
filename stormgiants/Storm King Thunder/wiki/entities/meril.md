# Meril

Meril is a master druid and the mentor to soma. Though he has not appeared directly in recent events and his current whereabouts are unknown, his influence permeates the current political and spiritual landscape of the [[emerald-enclave]].

## Profile
Meril is characterized by an enigmatic and deliberate nature. While he is described as being "late in his life," he remains an active, albeit hidden, observer; [[xanthopoulos]] has indicated that Meril remains "very interested" in the party's activities.

## Legacy and Training
Meril's approach to mentorship is unconventional. He provided soma with only one year of formal druidic education, bypassing the traditional decades of study. This decision has left Soma with a sense of [[incomplete-training]]. 

During this period, Meril provided Soma with foundational knowledge regarding:
- [[the-well]]
- The nature of wild shape
- An "unnamed faith"

His decision to provide such brief training, combined with the gift of [[merils-staff]] (a specialized druidic tool that appears to respond to significant magical or narrative events), suggests a strategic rather than sentimental motivation.

## Relationships
- **soma**: Former student and protégé. The centaur [[xanthopoulos]] has declared Soma to be "blessed by Meril," suggesting a significant destiny or purpose intended by the master.
- **[[xanthopoulos]]**: An associate or contemporary within the [[emerald-enclave]] who understands the true weight of Meril's decisions and appears to monitor Meril's interests.
