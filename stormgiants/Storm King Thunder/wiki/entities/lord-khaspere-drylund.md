## Overview
This wiki covers the narrative universe described in the source document "Lord Khaspere Drylund". The main focus is on the central figure, Lord Khaspere Drylund, a political aspirant and owner of the luxury floating casino *The Grand Dame* in Yartar. The wiki includes pages on key entities like Lord Khaspere Drylund, Queen Serissa, and Cap'n Squint, as well as concepts such as political networks, ancient powers, and resurrection capabilities. Additionally, there are queries exploring Drylund's motivations and the true extent of his involvement in the disappearance of King Hekaton.

## Key Entities
- **Lord Khaspere Drylund:** Political aspirant and owner of *The Grand Dame*; sought to become the Waterbaron of Yartar
- **Queen Serissa:** Queen of Yartar; referenced in the source
- **Cap'n Squint:** NPC; mentioned in the source
- **King Hekaton:** Disappeared; referenced in the source
- **Yartar:** Location; central to the story
- **The Kraken Society / Aboleth Oosith:** Organizations; associated with Drylund’s operations
- **3HPP (Three Horsemen of the Plague):** Organization; peripheral to Drylund’s activities

## Key Concepts
- **Political Networks:** Systems of political connections and alliances; central to Drylund’s political ambitions
- **Ancient Powers:** Entities or forces with immense power and influence; linked to Drylund’s operations and the mystery surrounding King Hekaton’s disappearance
- **Resurrection Capabilities:** The ability to bring someone back from the dead; suggests a deeper conspiracy beyond Drylund’s immediate involvement
