# Sridar

Sridar is a Drow independent scholar and artifact collector. She serves as a "knowledge broker" for the party, specializing in the identification of items connected to planar disruptions.

## Identity & Philosophy
Sridar identifies as an **interventionist** regarding planar matters. Unlike the [[emerald-enclave]], which favors a naturalist approach of non-interference, Sridar believes that planar disruptions and transformations should be actively understood and responded to.

## Personality
She maintains a calm, businesslike demeanor. She is intellectually curious and demonstrates a willingness to revise her theories when presented with compelling evidence, such as [[brewbarry]]'s theories on intentional incursions.

## History & Expertise
Her dedication to studying planar phenomena stems from a traumatic historical event known as the **Underdark Blink Event**, during which she witnessed a sudden appearance of demons in the Underdark. This event serves as the catalyst for her life's mission to study "items that aren't quite right."

## Relationship with the Party
* **[[valphine]]**: Has established a respectful, transactional relationship, having paid for intelligence regarding creature-related artifacts.
* **[[brewbarry]]**: Respects his intellectual contributions regarding the nature of planar incursions.
* **[[vukradin]]**: Views her dealings as legitimate and fair trade.

## Current Status
Sridar is currently located at the [[falcon-the-hunter]]'s lodge and remains a recurring resource for the party to validate or challenge findings related to [[planar-instability]].
