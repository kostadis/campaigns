# Othovir
Master harness maker and shop owner in Triboar, operating independently despite some ambiguity about his true allegiance. Carries a deep personal grudge against the Margaster family, which clearly predates and shapes his actions. Volunteered information as repayment rather than dealing in coin, values settling debts on his own terms.

## First Appearance
Examined a harness recovered from a thief / the Lion's Share vault breach. Determined it was not his work, assessed it as high-quality Waterdeep craftsmanship, approximately ten years old, sized for a thin person of approximately 150 pounds. Directed the party to speak with Harriet regarding the grappling hook.

## Margaster Connection
Presented by Urlam Stockspool as having a relationship with House Margaster, but this appears to misrepresent his actual stance, which is enemy-of-Margaster rather than ally. Provided detailed information about the Margaster family's emergency magic stash in Silverymoon, including security details and the location of a three-story tower with a detached carriage house.

## Current Status
Alive and operating his harness shop in Triboar. Last known location: Triboar. Active plans: None known beyond what he has already shared. What the party knows: He hates the Margasters, has an undisclosed personal history with them, and voluntarily burned that intelligence to help the party. What remains hidden: The nature and full extent of his history with the Margasters; the contradiction between Urlam's read of him as Margaster-aligned and his own anti-Margaster intelligence dump; whether he has further involvement with them or with the party's broader situation.

## Relationships
- **Margaster Family:** Deep personal animosity; history unspecified but clearly significant.
- **Urgala / Narth Tezerin:** Shared presence at post-battle scene; relationship otherwise undefined.
- **Urlam Stockspool:** Urlam is aware of Othovir's Margaster ties and views him with suspicion (likely misreading his actual hostility toward the Margasters).
- **Orsik:** Visibly surprised to learn of Othovir's apparent Margaster connection, suggesting he had trusted Othovir's earlier cooperation.
- **The Party:** Cautiously allied; considers his debt repaid after sharing the Silverymoon intelligence.

## Arc Score Events
No arc score events recorded.

## Identity
- **Role:** Master harness maker and shop owner in Triboar
- **Faction:** Independent / no formal affiliation; carries a deep personal grudge against the Margaster family. Note: one source (Urlam) reads him as "possibly connected to House Margaster," which appears to misrepresent his actual stance — see History.
- **First Appearance:** The party brought him a leather harness left behind by a thief (recovered from the Lion's Share vault breach), seeking his professional assessment

*Also known as: Orthovir.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Unclear beyond personal vendetta against the Margaster family; not motivated by money.
- Quiet, professional, and matter-of-fact in his first interaction, but later revealed a nervous, guarded side — scanning the room before speaking at the Talking Troll.
- Holds a deep, unspoken grudge against the Margasters that clearly predates and shapes his actions.
- Volunteers information as repayment rather than dealing in coin; values settling debts on his own terms.

## History with the Party
1. **Initial Contact:** Examined a harness recovered from a thief / the Lion's Share vault breach. Determined it was not his work, assessed it as high-quality Waterdeep craftsmanship, approximately ten years old, sized for a thin person of approximately 150 pounds. Directed the party to speak with Harriet regarding the grappling hook.

2. **Margaster connection (per Urlam):** Urlam Stockspool informed the party that Othovir has a relationship with House Margaster. Urlam implied — in response to Orsik's surprise — that Othovir may have deliberately set the party up, guiding them toward information that would damage House Margaster while concealing his own allegiances. (This account appears to misread his actual stance, which is enemy-of-Margaster rather than ally; see his subsequent intelligence dump below.)

3. **Post-Battle, Market Square:** Was present alongside Urgala and Narth Tezerin after a battle in the market square.

4. **Meeting at the Talking Troll:** Approached the party privately, visibly nervous. Declined to offer coin but traded intelligence as a favor for their help. Revealed the location and layout of the Margaster family's emergency magic stash in Silverymoon — a three-story tower with a detached carriage house (30-ft square, two floors). Provided security details: arcane lock on the door, two human guards upstairs, alarm spell on the carriage itself, magic items hidden in a compartment under the passenger seat. Left immediately after sharing, without further conversation.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Triboar; alive and operating his harness shop
- **Active Plans:** None known beyond what he has already shared
- **What the Party Knows:** He hates the Margasters, has an undisclosed personal history with them, and voluntarily burned that intelligence to help the party; he examined the vault-breach harness and pointed analysis toward Waterdeep
- **What Remains Hidden:** The nature and full extent of his history with the Margasters; the contradiction between Urlam's read of him as Margaster-aligned and his own anti-Margaster intelligence dump; whether he has further involvement with them or with the party's broader situation

## Relationships
- **Margaster Family:** Deep personal animosity; history unspecified but clearly significant.
- **Urgala / Narth Tezerin:** Shared presence at post-battle scene; relationship otherwise undefined.
- **Urlam Stockspool:** Urlam is aware of Othovir's Margaster ties and views him with suspicion (likely misreading his actual hostility toward the Margasters).
- **Orsik:** Visibly surprised to learn of Othovir's apparent Margaster connection, suggesting he had trusted Othovir's earlier cooperation.
- **The Party:** Cautiously allied; considers his debt repaid after sharing the Silverymoon intelligence.
