# Rotating Crossbow Platform

A specialized mechanical invention created by the gnome [[facktore]]. 

## Description
The device consists of a rotating platform equipped with four automatically reloading heavy crossbows. It is designed to provide rapid, multi-directional fire, making it an effective tool for ambushes in confined spaces.

## Combat History
The platform was used by [[facktore]] to ambush [[vukradin]] and the party in a chamber north of [[gnomengarde]]. The device was eventually disabled by the party during the encounter.
