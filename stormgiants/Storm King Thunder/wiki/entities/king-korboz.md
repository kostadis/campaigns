# King Korboz

[[king-korboz]] is the senior co-ruler of the [[gnomengarde]] [[dual-monarchy]] and the philosophical counterpart to [[king-gnercli]]. Once a respected and capable sovereign, he now suffers from unexplained madness, marked by violent outbursts, paranoia, and an obsessive belief in a shapeshifter threat that has allegedly caused the disappearances of inventors [[warren]] and [[orrin]]. His condition is the hidden catalyst behind the collapse of the dual monarchy, transforming its theoretical strength — shared authority — into its fatal weakness.

## Profile

Korboz is characterized by extreme volatility, paranoia, and a deep sensitivity regarding his status and seniority. His psychological collapse has become the central focus of the royal inventors, particularly [[fibblestib]], who is developing the speculative **Sanity Ray** to cure him, and [[dabbledob]], who advocates for the **straitjacket-solution**. The nature of his madness remains ambiguous — whether it stems from planar corruption linked to the dragon threat, internal political decay, or the cumulative psychological toll of Gnomengarde’s technological overreach is unknown.

His silence and absence are more powerful than any declaration. He is the void at the center of the kingdom’s governance — a silent, unresponsive co-ruler whose presence is felt only in the chaos he generates. His condition reflects a deeper failure of Gnomengarde’s epistemic culture. The party’s inability to directly engage with him underscores the theme of [[information-asymmetry-npc-encounters]]: the most critical figure in the crisis is the one least accessible.

Korboz’s madness is not merely personal — it is political. It exposes the dual monarchy’s fatal flaw: it assumes both rulers are rational, competent, and aligned. When one is not, the system cannot adapt. It lacks mechanisms for removal, intervention, or succession. His condition transforms the dual monarchy from a philosophical ideal into a tragic irony: a system designed to prevent tyranny now prevents any meaningful response to its own collapse.

His primary motivation is to be believed — he feels chronically dismissed by Gnercli, whose refusal to acknowledge the shapeshifter has eroded his sense of legitimacy. His emotional vulnerability was weaponized by [[vukradin]], who deliberately provoked Korboz’s grievances over seniority, leading to a public confrontation that shattered the monarchy’s fragile stability. This dynamic exemplifies [[reactive-leadership]] and [[leadership-vulnerability-through-illness]], and embodies [[madness-as-political-vulnerability]].

## Political Standing

Korboz's authority is in direct conflict with his co-ruler, [[king-gnercli]]. Their relationship is defined by long-standing friction, primarily because Korboz feels his warnings regarding a [[shapeshifter-threat]] are being ignored. This tension was recently exacerbated by [[vukradin]], who used targeted questioning to widen the rift between the two kings. Their arguments echo through the throne room door, but the substance of their dispute remains unknown.

The escalating conflict between the two kings has left Gnercli as the functional decision-maker for the kingdom, though his leadership is reactive and constrained by the institutional paralysis caused by Korboz’s condition. The inventors’ competing approaches — Fibblestib’s **Sanity Ray** (technology-as-cure) and Dabbledob’s **straitjacket-solution** (physical containment) — have turned Korboz’s case into a moral and philosophical battleground. His fate may determine whether Gnomengarde evolves into a more humane society or collapses under the weight of its own inventions.

## Claims and Allegations

Korboz maintains several specific claims regarding the current state of the settlement:
- **The Shapeshifter:** He insists that a shape-changing entity is terrorizing the settlement — a claim that may be linked to the [[shapeshifter-threat-kobold-kingdom]] and functions symbolically as [[shapeshifter-threat-as-symbolic-fear]].
- **Disappearances:** He attributes the disappearance of the gnomes [[warren]] and [[orrin]] to this entity — rumors suggest these vanishings may be a symptom or even a cause of his unraveling.
- **Accusations:** Upon meeting the party, he immediately accused [[vukradin]] of being a shapeshifter.

While the shapeshifter threat is terrifying, it is secondary — it is merely the symptom of a deeper rot: the institutional fragility embodied by Korboz’s collapse.

## Current Status

Korboz is currently confined to his quarters within the [[gnomengarde]] cave system and is undergoing medical/magical observation by [[fibblestib]] and [[dabbledob]]. His condition has left [[king-gnercli]] as the functional decision-maker for the kingdom.

The nature and severity of his condition remain unconfirmed; it is unknown if his state is purely psychological, a result of supernatural influence, or a physical illness. Recent reports indicate he remains in a heated conflict with Gnercli.

He remains confined to his chamber, a symbol of a monarchy teetering on collapse — a living monument to the failure of rational institutions to hear truth when it comes wrapped in chaos.
