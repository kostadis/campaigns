# King Korboz

[[king-korboz]] is the senior co-ruler of the [[gnomengarde]] [[dual-monarchy]] and the philosophical counterpart to [[king-gnercli]].

## Profile
Korboz is characterized by extreme volatility, paranoia, and a deep sensitivity regarding his status and seniority. He is currently in a state of active hysteria and is confined to his quarters within the [[gnomengarde]] cave system.

## Political Standing
Korboz's authority is in direct conflict with his co-ruler, [[king-gnercli]]. Their relationship is defined by long-standing friction, primarily because Korboz feels his warnings regarding a [[shapeshifter-threat]] are being ignored. This tension was recently exacerbated by [[vukradin]], who used targeted questioning to widen the rift between the two kings.

## Claims and Allegations
Korboz maintains several specific claims regarding the current state of the settlement:
- **The Shapeshifter:** He insists that a shape-changing entity is terrorizing the settlement.
- **Disappearances:** He attributes the disappearance of the gnomes Warren and Orrin to this entity.
- **Accusations:** Upon meeting the party, he immediately accused [[vukradin]] of being a shapeshifter.

## Current Status
Korboz is currently suffering from an illness that has left [[king-gnercli]] as the functional decision-maker for the kingdom. He is undergoing medical/magical observation by [[fibblestib]] and [[dabbledob]] in an attempt to cure his madness. 

The nature and severity of his condition remain unknown; it is unconfirmed if his state is purely psychological, a result of supernatural influence, or a physical illness. Recent reports indicate he remains in a heated conflict with Gnercli.
