# Marian

Marian is an agent of the [[emerald-enclave]] who met the party at the Stonehill Tavern. She served as one of the early Enclave contacts for the party, providing context regarding the internal politics of the organization. She shares the same binding oaths to the [[emerald-enclave]] as the group accompanying [[lyra]].

## Personality and Traits
Marian is characterized as a perceptive and observant individual with a sympathetic worldview. She demonstrates high situational awareness, noting behavioral patterns in others that may go unnoticed by the party. She tends to frame controversial actions through a lens of experience and necessity rather than recklessness.

## Relationships
- **[[thomas]]**: A fellow Emerald Enclave agent. Marian operates as a unit with [[thomas]]; the two departed the Stonehill Tavern together.
- **[[lyra]]**: Marian is part of the group accompanying [[lyra]] and holds her in high regard, describing her as a "good boss" and a decisive leader who acts on intuition when others are paralyzed by debate.
- **[[valphine]]**: Marian observed a specific pattern in Valphine's behavior—specifically a cycle of self-harm followed by healing—and questioned whether this was a ritual.

## History
After providing context regarding the internal politics of the Enclave and questioning Valphine's rituals, Marian declined to join the party and departed with [[thomas]].
