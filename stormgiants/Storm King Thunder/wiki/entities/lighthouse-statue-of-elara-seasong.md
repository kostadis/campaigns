# Lighthouse Statue of Elara Seasong

The Lighthouse Statue of Elara Seasong is a sculpted artifact recovered from the ruins of the [[lighthouse-west-of-phandalin]]. It is the ancestral heirloom of House Meliame, representing their lineage, artistic patronage, and historical connection to coastal heritage. Though its physical form is not described, its symbolic weight is immense: its return to Elara Seasong Meliame is not a simple delivery, but a ritual act of cultural restoration.

House Margaster seeks to intercept the statue to publicly shame House Meliame, transforming the object from a relic into a political weapon. This aligns with the concept of [[symbolic-targeting-of-cultural-artifacts]], where the destruction or theft of heritage items serves ideological or social domination rather than material gain.

The statue’s journey from ruin to rightful owner embodies [[delivery-as-political-act]]: the act of transport is imbued with moral and ceremonial significance. Its true value lies not in gold or magic, but in the legitimacy it confers upon its bearer. Whether it holds latent enchantments remains unknown—but its power as a symbol is already undeniable.
