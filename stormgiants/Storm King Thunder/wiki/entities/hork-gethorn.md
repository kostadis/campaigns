# Hork Gethorn
A meat pie shop owner in Mirabar, Hork Gethorn is a central figure in the narrative. He seeks protection from an infestation beneath his shop and posts a notice seeking a hired sword to deal with the issue. Vardis, a hired sword, completes the job and is rewarded with payment and recovered treasure.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Protect his business and keep his shop operational.
- Physically described as a man whose face resembles a meat pie and whose size reflects his deep love of his own product — a memorable, jovial merchant type implied by his trade and willingness to pay for help.

## Relationships
- **Vardis:** Employer/employee relationship; transactional but concluded on good terms (paid reward + recovered treasure).
- No other NPC relationships noted.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** His meat pie shop, Mirabar.
- No active plans or operations noted beyond running his business.
- **Party Knowledge:** Vardis knows him as a completed-job employer; no hidden information noted in source material.
