# Cooragh Struckt

Cooragh Struckt is the cook at the [[wayside-inn]]. 

## Profile
She is currently known to the party only through her culinary output. No direct conversation or personal interaction has been recorded.

## Culinary Reputation
She is noted for producing exceptionally high-quality food, specifically "alarmingly well-spiced" stews and meat pies. Her cooking made a particularly strong positive impression on [[valphine]].

## Status
- **Location:** [[wayside-inn]] kitchen.
- **Known Information:** Excellent cook; uses heavy/exotic spices.
