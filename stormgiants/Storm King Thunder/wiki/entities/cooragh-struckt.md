# Cooragh Struckt

Cooragh Struckt is the cook at the [[wayside-inn]]. 

## Profile
Currently, Cooragh is known to the party only through her culinary output; no direct conversation or personal interaction has been recorded.

## Culinary Reputation
She is noted for producing exceptionally high-quality food, specifically "alarmingly well-spiced" stews and meat pies. Her cooking made a particularly strong positive impression on [[valphine]].
