# Kobold Kingdom

The sovereign territory ruled by the kobold people. The kingdom is governed by a unique political structure known as the [[dual-monarchy]].

## Governance
The kingdom is ruled by two kings, [[king-gnercli]] and [[king-korboz]], who hold equal seniority. This system is designed to prevent any single individual from exercising despotic power or being compromised by external threats like shapeshifters.

## Current Threats
The kingdom is currently facing an internal security crisis involving a [[shapeshifter-threat]] that is believed to be hiding within the kingdom's infrastructure (specifically noted in a cellar containing large barrels).
