# King Hekaton

The former ruler of the giants during the collapse of the Ordning, King Hekaton is now known as the keeper of the conch — a powerful artifact capable of transporting its bearer to the Maelstrom, and by extension, to the Eye of the World where the Oracle resides. His role in the Oracle’s existence is implied but not fully understood; he may have been the last to interact with Annam before the divine silence, or he may have preserved the conch as a relic of the old order.

The Oracle confirmed that the conch is the key to reaching the Maelstrom, making Hekaton an indirect but critical figure in the party’s pursuit of Adimond Kroskas. His personal history, motivations, and current status remain unknown, but his artifact is essential to accessing one of the most significant divine mechanisms in the campaign.

As a former leader of the frost giants, Hekaton once held authority over the giant clans before the Ordning shattered. His transition from sovereign to solitary artifact-keeper suggests a profound personal or cosmic transformation, possibly tied to the divine silence that followed Annam’s withdrawal. Though his current whereabouts are unconfirmed, the conch remains his enduring legacy — a bridge between the mortal realm and the planar chaos of the Maelstrom, and the only known key to the Eye of the World.
