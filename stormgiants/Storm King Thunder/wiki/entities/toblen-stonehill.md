# Toblen Stonehill

Toblen Stonehill is the pragmatic and hospitable proprietor of the [[stonehill-inn]] in [[phandalin]]. Originally a prospector from [[triboar]], he transitioned into innkeeping and has become a stable civilian anchor within the community.

## Identity & Personality
Toblen is a short, friendly human male known for his calm and unflappable demeanor. He is a fair businessman who prefers [[transactional-hospitality]]—negotiating mutually beneficial deals (such as food in exchange for musical entertainment) rather than offering simple charity.

He is famously known by the nickname **"Spider-Man"** (or various iterations thereof). He earned this moniker as a child after surviving a fall into a hatching spider nest; he reportedly walked out covered in baby spiders without panicking. While [[brewbarry]] has frequently questioned the validity of this name, Toblen maintains it is his actual nickname.

## Relationships
* **[[vukradin]]**: Maintains a recurring positive relationship with the Aasimar Bard, frequently recruiting him to provide entertainment for the inn's guests.
* **[[brewbarry]]**: Holds a favorable impression of the performer, having accepted him without hostility despite his skepticism regarding Toblen's nickname.
* **[[valphine]]**: Maintains a neutral to tepid relationship following a lackluster performance by the party at his establishment.
* **[[lyra]]**: Recognizes the inn as a social nexus for the party, often using it as a venue for celebrations.
* **ser-kaelen**: Hosted Kaelen during recruitment conversations held at the inn.

## Intelligence Provided
Toblen serves as a source of "low-stakes" regional intelligence, providing the party with observations on:
* White dragon sightings in the vicinity.
* Concerns regarding orc raids from the mountains.
* Historical mentions of "barbarian hordes" as a significant past threat.
