# Wayside Inn

The [[wayside-inn]] is a local establishment that serves as a place of rest and a waypoint for travelers moving along the coastal trail toward the [[tower-of-storms]]. It provides lodging and basic services, operating under a strictly businesslike atmosphere. The adventuring party has completed an overnight stay at this location.

## Location and Function
The inn functions as a vital stop for those traversing the coastal route. It offers essential amenities for travelers seeking rest before continuing their journey.
