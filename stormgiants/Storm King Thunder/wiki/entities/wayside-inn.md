# Wayside Inn

The [[wayside-inn]] is a local establishment serving as a vital waypoint for travelers traversing the coastal trail toward the [[tower-of-storms]]. It provides lodging and basic amenities, operating under a strictly businesslike atmosphere. The adventuring party completed an overnight stay here, making it a documented point of rest in their journey.

## Location and Function

Situated along the coastal route, the Wayside Inn functions as a critical stop for travelers seeking respite before continuing their journey. While it lacks the grandeur or mystique of locations like the Oasis of Vuerthyl or Butterskull Ranch, its practical role in the regional travel network is indispensable. Despite its functional design, the inn’s absence of detailed environmental or cultural documentation creates a gap in narrative coherence, as it remains one of the few key locations without full contextual elaboration.

## Notable Personnel

- [[martisha-vinetalker]] — Owner and operator of the inn, responsible for its day-to-day management and hospitality.
- [[backes-dunfield]] — Bartender and a familiar presence to regulars and travelers alike; his role extends beyond service, as he is often a source of local rumors and practical advice.

The inn’s significance lies not in its architecture or lore, but in its reliability as a neutral ground where journeys are paused, alliances are formed, and information is exchanged. Its understated presence underscores the realism of the world’s travel infrastructure.
