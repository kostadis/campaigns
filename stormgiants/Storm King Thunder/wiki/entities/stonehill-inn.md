# Stonehill Inn

The [[stonehill-inn]] is a modest, well-maintained establishment located in [[phandalin]], distinct from the [[wayside-inn]] in both geography and clientele. Managed by toblen-stonehill, it serves as a critical social nexus and safe zone for the local community, visiting adventurers, retired heroes, and local traders seeking quiet lodging away from the more chaotic taverns of town.

Unlike the [[wayside-inn]], which functions as a background NPC showcase, the Stonehill Inn is a deliberate narrative node: a place where retired lives intersect with unresolved pasts. It is not a quest location, but a threshold — where the party first encounters the ethical ambiguity of post-adventuring life.

## Function

The inn is frequently used by the party for:

* **Social Integration:** Meeting with locals and managing their reputation through public performances.
* **Intelligence Gathering:** Receiving low-level regional reports on threats like orcs or dragons.
* **Diplomacy & Recruitment:** Hosting private conversations, such as those involving ser-kaelen.
* **Celebration:** Serving as a venue for celebratory drinks following major events, such as [[lyra]]'s restoration to good standing.
* **Narrative Threshold:** Serving as the site where [[daran-edermath-silverleaf]] first approached the party with his salvage proposal for the lighthouse west of Phandalin. The subsequent encounter with [[vukradin]] — marked by moral disagreement and Daran’s unexpected apology — established the inn as a site of quiet moral reckoning, not just rest.

The Stonehill Inn thus functions as both a practical hub for daily interaction and a symbolic space where personal histories, ethical dilemmas, and community ties converge.
