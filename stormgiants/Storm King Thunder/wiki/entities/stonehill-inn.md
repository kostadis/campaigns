# Stonehill Inn

The [[stonehill-inn]] is a primary social hub and landmark located in [[phandalin]]. Managed by [[toblen-stonehill]], it serves as a critical "safe zone" and social nexus for the local community and visiting adventurers.

## Function
The inn is frequently used by the party for:
* **Social Integration:** Meeting with locals and managing their reputation through public performances.
* **Intelligence Gathering:** Receiving low-level regional reports on threats like orcs or dragons.
* **Diplomacy & Recruitment:** Hosting private conversations, such as those involving [[ser-kaelen]].
* **Celebration:** Serving as a venue for celebratory drinks following major events, such as [[lyra]]'s restoration to good standing.
