# Belg

Belg is the enigmatic, unnamed companion of Mama Manyknuckles, described in source material as her “beloved” companion. No species, sentience, or origin has been recorded. Belg fought alongside Mama Manyknuckles during the sinkhole confrontation and was killed by the party.

The term “beloved” is unusually emotional in a setting where companions are typically defined by function (e.g., subordinate, agent, mount). This suggests Belg held symbolic or affective significance beyond tactical utility — possibly representing loyalty, memory, or a bond untouched by the cosmic abandonment evident in the world.

Belg’s complete ambiguity — the absence of any classification — makes him a narrative void: a potential vessel for later revelation. Was he a spirit-bound animal? A cursed humanoid? A construct of forgotten magic? His death may carry consequences beyond the battlefield — possibly fueling unresolved vengeance, spiritual retribution, or a hidden connection to Joon’s organization or Hsing’s network.

No further details are known. His nature remains intentionally undefined.
