# Corbin

**Corbin** was a party aide and errand-hand who transitioned from a subsistence farmer to a bandit before joining the adventuring party. He is characterized by a desperate drive for [[survival-at-any-cost]]. His death serves as a significant point of tension within the party and has been used as political leverage in broader conflicts.

## Background
Originally a farmer from Greenmeadow, east of the High Forest, Corbin's life was destroyed by the [[black-lion-uthgardt]] barbarians during the chaos following the breaking of the Ordning. After losing his wife and daughter, [[lily]], he turned to petty theft and eventually joined [[carver-gang]] to survive.

## Personality and Motivations
* **Survivalism:** His core driver was [[survival-at-any-cost]], leading him to compromise his values repeatedly.
* **Volatility:** Both [[vukradin]] and [[valphine]] observed a dangerous quality in him—described as [[the-hunger-of-the-small]]—suggesting a volatile reaction to being granted power over others.
* **Loyalty:** Despite his criminal past, he demonstrated unexpected steadiness, such as resisting [[shrimpy]]'s attempts to convince him to flee during the Woodland Manse incident.

## Death and Aftermath
Corbin was killed by [[sister-kaella]] while he was guarding her. The party's decision to prioritize [[interventionism]] elsewhere left him vulnerable to the assassin. The event took place in the presence of [[boney]], and the perceived inaction of Boney during the event has led to moral questioning by [[soma]].

Following his death:
* The event was reported to the [[druid-council]] by [[xanthopoulos]].
* [[adabra-gwynn]] used his death as political ammunition to support the [[anti-interventionist-faction]].

## Relationships
* **[[vukradin]]**: Primary authority figure; maintained a relationship of suspicion and command.
* **[[soma]]**: Shared his personal history with her; she expressed significant guilt regarding his death.
* **[[valphine]]**: Instructed him in the sacraments of her religious practice.
* **[[sister-kaella]]**: The assassin who killed him.
* **[[adabra-gwynn]]**: Used his death as a tool for political leverage.
* **[[boney]]**: Witnessed his death.
