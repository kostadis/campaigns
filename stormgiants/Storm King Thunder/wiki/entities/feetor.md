# Feetor
Feetor is a mud mephit who declares himself the "New King of the Swamp." He is a self-proclaimed opportunist who aligns himself with the party after the party defeats the water weird. Feetor is known for his theatrical and calculating demeanor, often responding to indignation and flattery in equal measure. He is particularly responsive to titles and shows of strength, and he has a close relationship with Unla Kee, whom he identifies as a kindred schemer.

Feetor initially pushes back against Orsik's requests but eventually agrees to pay a gold coin to secure mephit allegiance. He then approaches Unla Kee privately, framing the request correctly, and pays him a gold coin. Unla Kee subsequently names Feetor an honorary mud mephit and "leader" of the mephits. However, Feetor's grand proclamations consistently outpace results, and his influence over other mephits remains unclear.

Feetor is currently aligned with the party but is nominally allied with the party. He is opportunistic and unreliable with currency, and he has a close relationship with Unla Kee. He is indifferent to the mephit civil war and the threat of a duel from Unla Kee.
