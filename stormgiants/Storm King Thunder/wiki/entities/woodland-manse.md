# Woodland Manse

The **Woodland Manse** (sometimes referred to as the Woodland Mants) is a ruined stone manse located approximately ten miles southwest of the [[falcon-hunting-lodge]]. 

It is currently inhabited by a dangerous group of half-orcs and orcs who coordinate with the [[cult-of-talos]]. The inhabitants have demonstrated the ability to use high-level magic, including fireballs and lightning, to repel intruders. The failure of the adventuring party to clear this location prior to the attack on Falcon's lodge is cited as a contributing factor to the loss of the lodge and the death of an individual associated with [[falcon-the-hunter]].
