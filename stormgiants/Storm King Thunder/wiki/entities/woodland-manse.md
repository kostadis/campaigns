# Woodland Manse

The **Woodland Manse** (sometimes referred to as the Woodland Mants) is a ruined stone stronghold located approximately ten miles southwest of the [[falcon-hunting-lodge]] in Neverwinter Wood. Once a militarized half-orc fortress, it now serves as a base for organized bands of half-orcs and ogres who coordinate with the [[cult-of-talos]].

The inhabitants have demonstrated the ability to wield high-level magic, particularly fireballs and lightning, which are signature attacks linked to the lingering influence of [[iymrith]]. These elemental weapons suggest either magical augmentation or a connection to Iymrith’s hoard, possibly tied to ancient planar energies or remnants of dwarven ruins beneath the site.

Falcon the Hunter identified the manse as the root cause of regional instability and repeatedly urged the adventuring party to clear it, even offering magical boots as a reward. The party’s failure to act allowed the threat to grow, emboldening the cult and their orc-ogre allies to launch a devastating attack on Falcon’s lodge. The manse’s continued existence directly contributed to the death of an individual closely associated with Falcon, though the exact nature of that connection remains unconfirmed.

Its location near the [[mountain-s-toe-gold-mine]] suggests it may have been used to sabotage mining operations or control access to vital mineral routes. The architecture and tactical use of magic imply prior occupation by a disciplined, possibly planar-aligned half-orc faction.

The manse remains unexplored by the party and is considered a high-priority regional threat. Falcon now seeks not only to reclaim his lodge but to eliminate the source of the chaos — the Woodland Manse — before it can further destabilize the region.
