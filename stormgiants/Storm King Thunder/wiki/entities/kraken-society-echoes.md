# Kraken Society Echoes

The [[kraken-society-echoes]] is an entity associated with the [[kraken-society]]. It is currently unclear whether this group represents a splinter cell, a specific regional branch, or a specialized subset of the main organization. Intelligence suggests they are involved in the same cryptic operations involving "rising currents" and "necessary adjustments" seen in the primary society.
