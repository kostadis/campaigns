# Elrem
The Great Worm — a legendary creature and object of worship for the Great Worm tribe, known to Unla Kee and associated with the Fishyard. Elrem is a mythical entity combining elements of worms, centipedes, and dragons, with wing-like membranes and multiple legs. The Great Worm tribe reveres and fears Elrem, making sacrifices to prevent it from returning and devouring them.

## Core Claims
- Elrem is a legendary creature worshipped by the Great Worm tribe, primarily for appeasement purposes.
- The tribe makes sacrifices to prevent Elrem from returning and devouring them.

## Evidence
- Bressik's presentation on Great Worm Cavern provides indirect references to Elrem.
- An unidentified creature emerged from a snowbank, possibly Elrem, but this has not been confirmed.

## Strength of Evidence
- Indirect and speculative, as no direct interaction with Elrem has occurred. The party's observations of the snowbank creature are preliminary and require further investigation.
