# Jarek

Jarek is the groom and traveling companion to [[elara-seasong-meliame]]. He is characterized as a cautious, observant, and somewhat reserved individual.

## Personality and Temperament
Jarek demonstrates a pragmatic approach to safety, notably pointing out that he had previously warned Elara about the need for travel protection. This suggests a temperament that is both observant and slightly self-vindicated when his warnings are ignored.

## Key Intelligence
During an encounter following an orc ambush, Jarek provided critical testimony regarding the nature of the attackers:
- **Unusual Discipline:** He corroborated observations that the orcs exhibited a level of coordination and discipline inconsistent with standard [[talosian-orc-raiding-party]] behavior.
- **Sensory Phenomenon:** He reported experiencing [[the-internal-hum]]—a sensation felt inside the head rather than an external sound—which abruptly vanished immediately before the ambush began.

## Relationships
- [[elara-seasong-meliame]]: Employer and traveling companion.
