# Don-Jon Raskin

[[don-jon-raskin]] is the newly hired overseer of the mountain-toe-gold-mine. He is characterized by a high degree of social charm and a tendency toward self-aggrandizement, often spinning tall tales of his past as a prospector, privateer, and whaler.

## Profile
- **Role:** Mine Overseer
- **Personality:** Friendly, boastful, and socially oblivious. While he lacks practical mining expertise, his genuine demeanor has proven useful in de-escalating tensions.
- **Employer:** A [[neverwinter-consortium]] (suspected of having ties to the [[kraken-society]]).

## History and Relationships
- **The Party:** Met the party in [[phandalin]] and hired them as a contractual escort for his journey to the mine.
- **[[vukradin]]:** Respects [[vukradin]]'s judgment, particularly after [[vukradin]] brokered the [[compromise-cohabitation]] agreement regarding the wererats.
- **The Wererats:** Initially opposed their presence on safety grounds, but has accepted a functional, if cautious, coexistence.

## Known Limitations
Raskin is largely unaware of the true nature of his employers or the potential [[kraken-society]] influence within the [[neverwinter-consortium]]. His professional incompetence in mining operations remains a significant factor in the management of the mountain-toe-gold-mine.
