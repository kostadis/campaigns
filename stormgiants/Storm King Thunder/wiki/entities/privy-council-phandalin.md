# Privy Council of Phandalin

The **Privy Council** is a governing body in Phandalin established as a compromise to transition the town from the unilateral rule of [[harbin-wester]] to a shared governance model.

## Composition
The council consists of the following members:
* **[[harbin-wester]]**: Head of the council and Townmaster.
* **[[vukradin]]**: Adventurer and council member.
* **[[adabra-gwynn]]**: Druid and council member.
* **[[linene]]**: Council member.
* **[[halia]]**: Council member.
* **[[elmer]]**: Council member.
* **[[tolbin]]**: Council member.

## Dynamics
The council represents a shift in Phandalin's political landscape. While intended to distribute power, [[harbin-wester]] continues to attempt to dominate the council's narrative, often framing the council's successes as a result of his own foresight and leadership.
