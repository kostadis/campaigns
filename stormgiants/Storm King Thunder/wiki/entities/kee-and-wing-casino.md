# Kee and Wing Casino

The Kee and Wing Casino is a former establishment known as the Grand Dame in Yartar, now repurposed and renamed by Unla Key, who holds naming rights. Operated by Pow, it serves as the party’s first major collective economic asset — a source of income, social influence, and narrative grounding.

Unlike loot or magic items, the casino is not a means to an end — it is an institution the party must protect, manage, and sustain. Its existence transforms the party from transient adventurers into reluctant governors. The casino’s success is tied to the party’s reputation as heroes of Parnast and their celebrity status in Yartar and Silverymoon.

It represents [[asset-based-loyalty]]: the shared ownership of this enterprise binds the party together not through ideology, but through mutual interest. Its continued operation requires secrecy, discretion, and the suppression of moral discomfort — mirroring the party’s broader pattern of [[complicit-silence]].

The casino also serves as a counterpoint to the Cloud Castle: one is a place of pleasure and commerce, the other a fortress of magic and restriction. Together, they symbolize the party’s new identity — neither heroes nor villains, but operators in a world where power is institutionalized.
