# Earthstone Gem

An [[earthstone-gem]] is a specific item required by [[dwolin-the-grout]] to perform rituals intended to "coax" gold from veins. It is currently the primary object of interest regarding the resumption of formal operations at the mountain-toe-gold-mine.

It remains unknown if the gem possesses genuine magical properties or if its use is merely a psychological component of [[ritualistic-mining]].

## Current Situation

The gem is currently located in orc-infested territory. [[zeleen-varnaster]] and a delegation of the [[whiskered-gang]] have been tasked with its retrieval. 

Due to low confidence in the [[whiskered-gang]]'s ability to succeed, a contingency plan involving a counterfeit gem has been discussed with [[horia-stonetallow]].
