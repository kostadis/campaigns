# Sandesyl Morgia

Sandesyl Morgia was Blagothkus’s castellan and a traitor who infiltrated and seized control of the flying castle at Parnast. Originally entrusted with the castle’s maintenance under a prior agreement, she betrayed Blagothkus by massacring his retinue and imprisoning him in the control room, using the castle’s systems to isolate and weaken him.

Her betrayal was not merely political — it was architectural. She exploited the castle’s design and Blagothkus’s own reliance on its systems to turn his domain into his prison. Her actions exemplify the vulnerability of even the most powerful figures when their authority is mediated through technology and trust.

She was ultimately defeated by the 3HPP and Blagothkus during the assault on her ice cave lair. Her role in the narrative underscores the theme of [[spectral-control-via-technology]]: control over space can be as decisive as control over people. Her fate after defeat remains unrecorded.
