# Jenna Roscoe

Jenna Roscoe (also known as Jena Roscoe) is a senior agent of the [[lords-alliance]] and a council representative in Phandalin. She operates at the intersection of civic authority and covert influence, using institutional legitimacy to recruit adventurers, launder wealth, and suppress dissent under the guise of public service.

## Identity
- **Role:** Lord’s Alliance agent and civic representative
- **First Appearance:** Town green in Phandalin, observing Vukradin’s concert after the dragon attack
- **Appearance:** Woman in her late thirties; auburn hair, sharp green eyes, practical high-quality traveling clothes, dark green wool doublet, sturdy boots, simple silver rings
- **Known Aliases:** Jena Roscoe

## Motivations
Jenna seeks to co-opt the party into serving the Lord’s Alliance’s long-term political and economic interests. Her goals are not ideological but instrumental: she aims to:
- Recruit Vukradin as a cultural asset for noble circles in Waterdeep and Neverwinter
- Legitimize adventuring wealth through community investment (wealth-laundering-as-moral-justification)
- Neutralize dissent by discrediting alternative worldviews (ideological-dismissal-as-strategy)
- Minimize scrutiny of the [[kraken-society]] while maintaining plausible deniability

She is patient, calculating, and deeply pragmatic. She views moral conviction — particularly Vukradin’s — as temporary, not absolute, and believes exposure to elite systems will eventually erode idealism.

## Tactics
- **Leverage-through-personal-history:** Used a pre-written letter to Vukradin as an emotional entry point.
- **Cultural-contempt-as-political-tool:** Repeatedly referred to Brewbarry as “Uthgardian” as a slur and framed Alliance membership as “civilizing” a barbarian.
- **Operational-secrecy-via-minimization:** Dismissed the Kraken Society as a harmless social club, despite sensing its deeper significance.
- **Wealth-laundering-as-moral-justification:** Argued that looted gold, when spent on community projects, becomes morally legitimate redistribution.
- **Targeted-recruitment-via-personal-needs:** Tailored offers: prestige and studio support for Vukradin, money for Brewbarry, and implied support for Valphine’s church (later withdrawn after ideological rejection).
- **Institutional-ambiguity-as-protection:** Operates within the Phandalin Council, using civic authority to mask covert objectives.

## Relationships
- **Vukradin:** Repeatedly attempted to recruit him. Respects his integrity despite failure. Views his principles as youthful and malleable. Offered resources, prestige, and assistance locating [[sister-kaella]].
- **Brewbarry:** Openly contemptuous. Attempted to bribe him into abandoning his principles; later framed Alliance membership as “civilization.” Rebuffed with a burp.
- **Valphine:** Dismissed her faith as a “torture cult,” revealing ideological bias. Suspected of concealing knowledge about the Kraken Society during council meetings.
- **Spider-Man:** Identified by him as a Lord’s Alliance agent; whether she is aware of his recognition is unknown.
- **Ser Kaelen:** Used his connection to Vukradin and House Margaster’s funding of the [[order-of-the-gauntlet]] as leverage to fracture Vukradin’s loyalties.
- **Lord’s Alliance:** Reports to an unknown superior; her actions reflect a broader strategy of civic infiltration.
- **Kraken Society:** Suspected of hidden ties; downplays its significance to obscure its role.
- **Harbin Wester:** Operates within his council framework; no direct conflict or alliance noted.
- **Lord Neverember:** Likely reports to him indirectly; her recruitment goals align with his regional control strategy.

## Arc Status
Jenna’s arc remains unresolved. She is not defeated, nor allied. She is a persistent, adaptive force — a system operator who returns with new offers after each rejection. Her respect for Vukradin’s integrity suggests she may be more than a mere functionary; she may be a mirror to the party’s own compromises.

Her true mandate, her connection to the Kraken Society, and whether her admiration for Vukradin is genuine or tactical remain hidden.
