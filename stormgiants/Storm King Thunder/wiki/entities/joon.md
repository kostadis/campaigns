# Joon

Joon is the uncle of Thistle and a member of an unknown secretive organization — one so hidden that the SEER, despite its reach, has no intelligence on its existence, structure, or goals. He is explicitly not affiliated with the Cult of the Dragon, but his true allegiance remains a mystery.

Azam delivered Joon’s location in Triboar with unusual urgency, suggesting personal or operational significance beyond Thistle’s familial connection. His organization’s ability to evade the SEER’s surveillance implies either extreme isolation, non-human origins, or a level of counter-intelligence capability rivaling or surpassing the SEER itself.

Joon represents the ultimate unknown: a threat or ally whose nature cannot be assessed by even the most advanced intelligence apparatus. His existence undermines the assumption of institutional omniscience and introduces a narrative wildcard that may intersect with the deeper mysteries of the setting.
