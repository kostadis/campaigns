# Chandra

An Evereska-born individual targeted by [[bad-fruul]] during the assault on Parnast. He specifically named her and referenced her connection to Luistarra, indicating a deep, personal vendetta tied to her lineage. Her identity as a target suggests she is connected to the origin of the [[headband-of-intellect-narcellia]] and possibly to [[narcellia-varammyr]].

Chandra’s emotional response to the death of the wyvern — a creature she mourned — reveals a capacity for deep attachment and loss, contrasting with her otherwise reserved demeanor. Her connection to the [[SEER]] and [[Azam]] positions her as a key node in the intelligence network, yet her personal history remains obscured.

The identity of her “family nemesis” — whether it is Narcellia, Luistarra, or another figure — is the central unresolved thread linking her to Bad Fruul’s campaign. Her survival makes her a potential key to unlocking the artifact’s origin and the true nature of the threat that endures after Bad Fruul’s death.
