# Cisco
- **Role:** Manservant to Eden Gleeson
- **Faction:** None noted; employed by the Gleeson household
- **First Appearance:** Encountered on the road west toward Llorkh, traveling with Eden Gleeson en route to a wedding

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Managing Eden Gleeson's affairs and finances; keeping his employer from making reckless decisions
- Cisco is exasperated but loyal, willing to publicly rebuke Eden when necessary. He projects quiet authority despite his servant role. Not hostile or aggressive — simply practical and protective.

## History with the Party
- Met the party on the road to Llorkh while accompanying Eden Gleeson
- Stepped forward during Eden's haggling with Vardis to reprimand Eden directly, warning that the impulsive purchase of a silver nose ring had wiped out the entire dessert budget for the wedding

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Road west toward Llorkh, traveling with Eden Gleeson
- **Active Plans:** Accompanying Eden to the wedding; presumably continuing to manage (and limit the damage of) Eden's financial decisions
- **Hidden Info:** Nothing noted beyond what the party has observed

## Relationships
- **Eden Gleeson:** Employer; Cisco is protective of him — financially and possibly personally — though not above scolding him openly
- **Vardis:** Present during their transaction; Cisco's rebuke was directed at Eden, not Vardis
