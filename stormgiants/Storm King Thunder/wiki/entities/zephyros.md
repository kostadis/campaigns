# Zephyros
- **Role:** Cloud giant; captain/inhabitant of a flying castle
- **Faction:** Independent (no confirmed faction affiliation)
- **First Appearance:** The party is being transported aboard his flying castle en route to Triboar; he communicates with them by shouting down from above

## Personality & Motivations
- **Goals/Drives:** Not explicitly stated; appears to act as a neutral or benevolent transporter. Shows genuine care for the party's wellbeing (sending food when rations ran low).
- **Personality:** Avuncular and talkative — he narrates landmarks and shares personal memories unprompted. He carries a subtle moral watchfulness, questioning the party's decisions after the cultist encounter. Sentimental, particularly about his past time with the Dessarin Hill dwarves.

## History with the Party
1. **En route to Triboar:** Transported the party aboard his flying castle, periodically calling down observations about landmarks — the High Forest, the Star Mounts, the Iceshield lands, and the Dessarin Hills.
2. **Post-cultist encounter:** Asked the party whether they still believed their choice was the right one, indicating he was paying close attention to their moral decisions.
3. **Rations crisis:** Took pity on the party when their food ran low and sent provisions down to them.
4. **Dwarven infiltration:** While the party dealt with Clarion and Diana's strike team on the castle platform, sounds of active combat emerged from Zephyros's quarters — he was apparently fighting dwarven infiltrators who had breached the interior. The standoff on the platform ended when Diana's team learned the party works for the SEER.
5. **Arrival:** His castle landed near Triboar, concluding the journey.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Near Triboar, where the castle landed
- **Active Concerns:** His condition following the combat inside his quarters is **unconfirmed** — the party did not verify his fate after the engagement
- **What the Party Knows:** He fought infiltrators in his room during the platform standoff; combat sounds were heard but the outcome was not checked
- **What Remains Hidden:** Whether he survived the interior fight; any deeper motivations for agreeing to transport the party

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Guardedly warm — generous enough to feed them, but willing to challenge their choices. Has not fought alongside them directly.
- **Dwarves of the Dessarin Hills:** Fond memories; uncertain if those individuals remain
- **Diana's Strike Team / Clarion:** Antagonists who breached his castle; their standdown came from SEER recognition, not from Zephyros's intervention
- **The SEER:** No direct relationship established, but the SEER's name carried enough weight to halt the assault on his castle

## Key Entities
- **Zephyros**: Cloud giant; captain/inhabitant of a flying castle
- **Cambions**: Infernal beings serving Xamlyn Margaster
- **High Lord Aerasumé**: City official granting Xamlyn guest protection
- **Orsik**: Member of the party initially approached by Xamlyn
- **Thistle (Three Halflings and a Pony)**: Group pursuing Xamlyn
