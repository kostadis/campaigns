# Thomas

Thomas is a measured and dutiful agent of the [[emerald-enclave]]. As the oldest member of the group accompanying [[lyra]], he serves as the primary spokesperson for their unit. While his specific views on Enclave leadership were less sympathetic than [[marian]]'s, he remains a known contact for the organization.

## Personality & Motivations
Thomas is characterized by his commitment to [[oath-bound-duty]]. He prioritizes organizational integrity and the established [[organizational-procedure-vs-individual-ambition]] over personal inclinations or party invitations. While he is a loyalist to the Enclave's rules, he maintains a deep personal loyalty to [[lyra]], standing by her even during her public confession at the druid council.

He is observant and direct, often acting as a social filter for the group. He has demonstrated visible discomfort with certain religious practices, specifically those of [[valphine]], and is not afraid to speak the truth regarding failures or difficult circumstances.

## History & Interactions
- **The Stonehill Tavern:** Met the party via [[vukradin]] alongside [[marian]]. During this meeting, he explained the circumstances of [[lyra]]'s failed ritual, noting it was driven by a mix of personal ambition and frustration with Enclave inaction. He was last seen departing the tavern in the company of [[marian]].
- **Refusal of Recruitment:** He declined [[vukradin]]'s invitation to join the adventuring party, citing his binding oath to the [[emerald-enclave]].
- **The Druid Council:** Rejoined the party on the road to the council to deliver news that [[adabra-gwynn]] was moving to ostracize [[lyra]]. He stood alongside [[lyra]] during her public confession regarding the failed intervention and the killing of the metastasized treant.

## Relationships
- **[[lyra]]**: His superior/colleague. He supports her through the political fallout of her unauthorized actions.
- **[[marian]]**: A fellow agent who operates as a unit with him.
- **[[adabra-gwynn]]**: An Enclave member actively working to undermine [[lyra]].
- **[[reidoth]]**: The nominal head of the local chapter; Thomas recognizes [[reidoth]]'s ability to provide [[procedural-influence]] during council proceedings.
- **[[valphine]]**: Subject of his scrutiny regarding her Underdark origins and devotional practices.
