# Thomas

Thomas is a measured and dutiful druid and agent of the [[emerald-enclave]], known for his commitment to [[oath-bound-duty]] and organizational integrity. As the oldest member of the group accompanying [[lyra]], he serves as the primary spokesperson for their unit and is a known contact within the organization. Though his personal views on Enclave leadership were less sympathetic than [[marian]]'s, he remains bound by loyalty to its structure — even as he stands by those he cares for.

## Personality & Motivations

Thomas is observant, direct, and unflinching in his honesty. He prioritizes [[organizational-procedure-vs-individual-ambition]] over personal inclinations or social pressures, and he is not afraid to voice discomfort with practices he deems inappropriate — notably those of [[valphine]], whose Underdark origins and devotional rituals he scrutinizes. His loyalty to [[lyra]] transcends bureaucratic allegiance; he supported her through the political fallout of her unauthorized actions, including her public confession at the druid council.

He is not a villain, but a witness. His actions — particularly his role in the failed intervention at the Whispering Grove — led to the death of a metastasized treant. His subsequent testimony before the council was not an act of guilt, but of accountability. Yet [[adabra-gwynn]] weaponized his confession as evidence of systemic failure, framing it as proof that interventionism inevitably leads to destruction. In this way, Thomas embodies the unintended consequences of well-meaning action — the human face of the cascade Adabra fears.

His internal struggle reflects [[guilt-as-internal-prison]], and his story is a case study in [[weaponization-of-personal-debt]], where personal responsibility is exploited for institutional control.

## History & Interactions

- **The Whispering Grove Intervention:** Participated alongside [[lyra]] in a botched attempt to contain a metastasized treant. The operation ended in the treant’s death, triggering a crisis within the Enclave.
- **The Druid Council:** Rejoined the party on the road to the council to deliver news that [[adabra-gwynn]] was moving to ostracize [[lyra]]. He stood beside her during her public confession regarding the failed intervention and the treant’s death. His own testimony was later used by Adabra to discredit the entire interventionist philosophy of the Enclave.
- **The Stonehill Tavern:** Met the party via [[vukradin]] alongside [[marian]]. There, he explained the circumstances of [[lyra]]'s failed ritual, noting it was driven by a mix of personal ambition and frustration with Enclave inaction. He was last seen departing the tavern in the company of [[marian]].
- **Refusal of Recruitment:** Declined [[vukradin]]'s invitation to join the adventuring party, citing his binding oath to the [[emerald-enclave]].

## Relationships

- **[[lyra]]**: His superior and colleague. He supports her through the political fallout of her unauthorized actions, even at personal cost.
- **[[marian]]**: A fellow agent who operates as a unit with him; they share a deep, unspoken trust forged through shared failure and loyalty.
- **[[adabra-gwynn]]**: An Enclave member actively working to undermine [[lyra]] and dismantle interventionist doctrine. She instrumentalized Thomas’s testimony to justify her purge of reformers.
- **[[reidoth]]**: The nominal head of the local chapter; Thomas recognizes [[reidoth]]'s ability to provide [[procedural-influence]] during council proceedings, even as he questions the direction of the Enclave.
- **[[valphine]]**: Subject of his scrutiny regarding her Underdark origins and devotional practices, which he views as incompatible with Enclave traditions — a tension that underscores his rigid adherence to orthodoxy.

Thomas remains a quiet pillar of the Enclave — not because he believes in its current leadership, but because he believes in the weight of his word, his duty, and the cost of silence.
