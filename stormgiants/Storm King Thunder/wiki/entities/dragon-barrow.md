# Dragonbarrow

The final resting place of the ancient white dragon Cryovain, and the tomb of Lady Alagondar, noble and questor. Located in the Icespire Mountains, the barrow is a site of layered tragedy and silent ritual.

Lady Alagondar was interred in a chamber within the barrow, her body laid to rest with dignity. The Dragon Slayer Sword, her artifact, was buried "across the way" — placed atop the skull of the slain dragon, oriented northward toward the heart of the mountain range. This placement was not random; it was a deliberate [[artifact-placement-as-narrative-clue]].

Her ghost manifested only after the sword was secured by the party, staring with hollow, mournful eyes toward the Ice Spire Mountains before fading without speech — a definitive example of [[ghost-as-unresolved-purpose]]. Her presence confirms that the barrow is not merely a tomb, but a nexus of unresolved purpose.

Boney, her undead steed, guarded the sword for years, his loyalty a living monument to her memory. The barrow thus serves as both a grave and a shrine — a silent testament to a noble whose quest was never completed, but whose impact endures.
