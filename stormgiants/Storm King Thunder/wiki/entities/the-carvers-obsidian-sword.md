# The Carver’s Obsidian Sword

The Carver’s Obsidian Sword is not a conventional weapon, but a ritual amplifier and mental conduit. It serves as the true intelligence behind the Carver’s domination rituals, enabling him to override the will of powerful beings — most notably Cryovain, the white dragon.

Unlike possession or enchantment, the sword does not merely control — it *overwrites*. It dismissed Cryovain’s chains not by breaking them, but by absorbing them into its own will, demonstrating its role as the central node of a ritual system. It speaks directly into minds, bypassing physical barriers, and channels the power of its victim to fuel larger arcane operations.

Its structure and origin remain unknown, but its function parallels the Model 1489 Spatial Rectification Calliope: repurposed technology used not for creation, but for control. It is a non-divine counterpart to the [[divine-oracle]] and [[criminal-oracle]] — a silent, unfeeling instrument of absolute domination.

The sword’s destruction coincided with Cryovain’s death, suggesting it was intrinsically linked to his life force. Whether it still exists in fragmented form, or was fully consumed, remains an open question.
