# Falcon Hunting Lodge

The [[falcon-hunting-lodge]] serves as a social and intellectual hub for those adhering to the interventionist philosophy. It is the site where the party first encountered [[sridar]].

The lodge is hosted by [[falcon-the-hunter]], who shares Sridar's views on the necessity of responding to planar disruptions.
