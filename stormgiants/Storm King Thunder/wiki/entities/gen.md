# Gen
Gen is a kobold who serves as a companion and subordinate to **[[agra]]**. He was first encountered at an abandoned dwarven outpost, where he was found hiding in a stone building.

## Personality & Traits
Gen's character is defined by the tension between **impulsive pragmatism** and emotional dependency. 
- **Impulsiveness:** He is prone to rash actions under pressure, often requiring physical restraint from Agra during tense standoffs.
- **Emotional Dependency:** He relies heavily on Agra for leadership and emotional stability.
- **Pragmatic Thinking:** Despite his impulsiveness, he demonstrates significant tactical awareness; it was Gen who suggested offering treasure to secure the party's goodwill when a situation became desperate.

## History & Current Status
**First Appearance:** Abandoned Dwarven Outpost
**Current Status:** Last seen departing the abandoned dwarven outpost alive, alongside Agra. He has no known plans beyond the immediate encounter.

**The Outpost Encounter:** 
During the party's encounter at the outpost, Agra was observed clamping a hand over Gen's mouth to prevent him from acting rashly. After a trap failed and the situation grew desperate, Gen suggested trading treasure for safety. The party accepted, and both kobolds were allowed to leave unharmed.

**Information Status:**
- **Known by the Party:** He is Agra's companion, impulsive, and willing to trade treasure for safety.
- **Hidden Information:** His backstory, the origin of his burn scars, and his specific motives at the outpost remain hidden.

## Relationships

- **The Party:** Departed on neutral terms after the treasure exchange; no individual relationships established.
