# Dwolin the Grout

Dwolin the Grout is a dwarven foreman stationed at the mountain-toe-gold-mine. He is the central figure in the current production stoppage at the mine.

## Personality and Beliefs
Dwolin is characterized by his earnestness and eccentricity. He holds the deeply held belief that gold veins possess a form of sentience or temperament, requiring "absolute respect" to be successfully harvested. He refuses to proceed with mining operations unless he can perform specific rituals using an [[earthstone-gem]].

## Professional Standing
Despite his superstitious methods, Dwolin is highly regarded for his efficacy. His colleague, [[horia-stonetallow]], describes him as the "best seeker of gold" known, suggesting that his [[ritualistic-mining]] practices—whether magical or psychological—yield significant practical results.
