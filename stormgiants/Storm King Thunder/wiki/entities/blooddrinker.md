# Blooddrinker

A magically shrunken bear, reduced to the size of a domestic cat by the curse of a mysterious cave. Formerly the companion of the deceased Slayermighty, Blooddrinker exhibited no aggression after his owner’s death — instead rolling onto his belly in a posture of submission and affection. His docility defies the natural instincts of a bear, suggesting not only physical alteration but psychological and possibly spiritual transformation.

## Role
- **Role:** Animal companion (bear, shrunken to cat size)
- **Current size:** Cat-sized
- **Status:** Alive, bonded to Thistle Wendrod

## History
- **First appearance:** Encountered alongside Slayermighty inside the cursed cave; shrunken to cat size by the cave's magical curse.
- **After Slayermighty’s death:** Did not retaliate or pose a threat; displayed affectionate, submissive behavior.
- **Stone chest incident:** When Thistle, Orsik, and Vardis opened the stone chest, Blooddrinker began convulsing violently. Thistle intervened quickly and saved his life.
- **Bond formation:** Formed an immediate and profound bond with Thistle Wendrod following the rescue.

## Personality & Motivations
Despite being a bear, Blooddrinker behaves with surprising docility. His instinct appears to be affection-seeking rather than aggressive. This behavioral shift, coupled with his magical shrinking, suggests a deeper transformation — possibly spiritual or psychological — beyond mere physical alteration. He is now a canonical example of [[beloved-companion-non-human]], distinguished by the unique mechanism of his transformation and his unresolved link to the stone chest.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** In Thistle’s care
- **Condition:** Alive, cat-sized, bonded to Thistle
- **Hidden information:** The cause of his convulsing episode (tied to the stone chest opening) may not be fully understood by the party. The convulsion implies a latent magical connection between Blooddrinker and the artifact — though its nature remains unknown.

## Relationships
- **Thistle Wendrod:** New bond-companion; Blooddrinker is emotionally attached to Thistle, serving as both emotional anchor and silent witness to Thistle’s transition from detached analyst to compassionate guardian.
- **Slayermighty:** Former companion, now deceased.
- **Orsik & Vardis:** Present at the chest opening; no noted individual relationship beyond shared experience.

## Significance
Blooddrinker’s existence challenges assumptions about sentience, trauma-induced behavioral change, and the boundaries between animal and companion. His transformation and lingering magical ties to the stone chest make him a unique and enigmatic figure within the party’s lore.
