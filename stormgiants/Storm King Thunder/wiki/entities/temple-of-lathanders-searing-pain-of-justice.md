# Temple of Lathander's Searing Pain of Justice

The **Temple of Lathander's Searing Pain of Justice** is a proposed religious site in [[phandalin]], serving as the central location and institutional heart of Valphine’s radical doctrine. It is the physical manifestation of the [[lathanderian-practice-valphine]], specifically focused on the "purifying pain of the sun" — a doctrine that emphasizes ritual burning and self-flagellation as paths to divine enlightenment.

## Construction and Location

The temple is being established through the renovation of [[tresendar-manor]], repurposing the historic estate into a center for religious activity. The project is being spearheaded by [[brin-bundlewine]], who has provided labor at minimal cost to facilitate the conversion, underscoring the deep personal and ideological commitment to Valphine’s vision.

## Religious Significance

As the primary hub for practitioners of Valphine’s unorthodox sun-worshipping sect, the temple embodies a radical reinterpretation of Lathanderian faith. Unlike traditional worship of the Morninglord, this sect venerates the sun’s destructive and purifying power, using pain and fire as sacred tools for spiritual cleansing. The temple is not merely a place of worship but a site of ascetic discipline, where rituals are performed to transcend earthly suffering through the searing justice of the sun.
