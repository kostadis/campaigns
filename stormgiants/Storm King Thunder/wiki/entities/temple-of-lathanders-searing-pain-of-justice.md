# Temple of Lathander's Searing Pain of Justice

The **Temple of Lathander's Searing Pain of Justice** is a proposed religious site in [[phandalin]], intended to be a dedicated chapel for the unorthodox sun-worshipping sect following [[valphine]].

## Construction and Location
The temple is being established through the renovation of [[tresendar-manor]]. The project is being spearheaded by [[brin-bundlewine]], who has provided labor at minimal cost to facilitate the conversion of the manor into a center for religious activity.

## Religious Significance
The temple serves as the physical manifestation of the [[lathanderian-practice-valphine]], specifically focusing on the "purifying pain of the sun." It is intended to be a hub for practitioners of ritual burning and self-flagellation.
