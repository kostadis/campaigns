# Jenna

**Jenna Roscoe** (also known as **Jena Roscoe**) is a pragmatic and calculating agent of the [[lords-alliance]] serving as a council representative in [[phandalin]].

## Profile
- **Role:** Agent and Recruiter for the [[lords-alliance]].
- **Appearance:** A woman in her late thirties with auburn hair and sharp green eyes, typically dressed in high-quality, practical traveling clothes.
- **Personality:** Highly manipulative, patient, and comfortable with moral gray areas. She utilizes leverage, pre-planted information, and tailored incentives to coerce individuals into service.

## Recruitment Tactics & Philosophy
Jenna employs several distinct methods to turn adventurers into political assets:
- **Wealth Laundering via Community Investment:** She argues that "blood money" (adventuring loot) can be legitimized by funneling it into community projects. This directly clashes with [[vukradin]]'s "fair-trade" philosophy.
- **Prestige and Access:** She attempted to recruit [[vukradin]] by offering him high-society bookings before the nobility of [[neverwinter]] and [[waterdeep]], potentially even involving [[lord-neverember]].
- **Information as Leverage:** She has offered to assist the party in locating [[sister-kaella]] as a bargaining chip for cooperation.
- **"Civilizing" Narratives:** She displays open contempt for Uthgardt peoples, specifically targeting [[brewbarry]] with slurs and framing [[lords-alliance]] membership as a way for "barbarians" to become "civilized."

## Suspected Deceptions
There is significant tension regarding Jenna's transparency:
- **The Kraken Society:** She publicly minimizes the [[kraken-society]] as a "harmless social club," a claim that has raised suspicions of her being a double agent or performing damage control for the organization. [[valphine]] has explicitly sensed her withholding information.
- **Religious Bias:** She characterizes [[valphine]]'s religious movement as a "torture cult," contradicting the party's observations.

## Relationships
- **[[vukradin]]**: Primary target for recruitment. Despite repeated rejections, she appears to hold a genuine, if patronizing, respect for his conviction.
- **[[brewbarry]]**: Subject of her open contempt and failed recruitment attempts.
- **[[valphine]]**: Adversarial; Jenna dismisses her faith, while Valphine suspects Jenna's ties to the [[kraken-society]].
- **[[lords-alliance]]**: Her primary employer and the faction she represents in local governance.
