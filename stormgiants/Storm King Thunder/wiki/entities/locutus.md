# Locutus

Locutus was an intermediary or "fixer" whose true affiliations remained unknown. He functioned as a clandestine link between high-stakes actors, specifically arranging a meeting between [[sister-kaella]] and [[valphine]].

## Role and Motivation
Locutus operated by arranging meetings and managing communications. His primary motivation appeared to be securing safe passage to the Underdark, a reward he believed [[valphine]] would provide in exchange for his services.

## Death and Final Actions
Locutus served as the functional link that brought [[sister-kaella]] and [[valphine]] together at an abandoned building on the edge of Phandalin. During this meeting, he confirmed to [[sister-kaella]] that he had successfully killed [[borg-the-hammer]]. 

Immediately following this confirmation, [[sister-kaella]] assassinated him. After two failed strikes with her rapier, she killed him using her offhand dagger. His promised reward of Underdark passage was never delivered.

## Status
**Deceased.**
