# The Presiding Druid

The authority figure presiding over the [[druid-council]]. 

## Role and Influence
The Presiding Druid holds the power to issue formal rulings that dictate the direction and policy of the [[emerald-enclave]]. During the recent debate regarding the [[emerald-enclave]], the Presiding Druid used the lack of actionable policy from supporters like [[aldric-stone-path]] to justify a ruling against [[adabra-gwynn]].
