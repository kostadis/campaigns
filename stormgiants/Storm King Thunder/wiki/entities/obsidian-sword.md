# Obsidian Sword

A sentient black obsidian weapon that served as the true identity of [[the-carver]]. 

## Properties
- **Mental Amplification:** The sword functions as a powerful mental amplifier, capable of projecting its intelligence, will, and contempt directly into the minds of nearby individuals.
- **The Pommel Gem:** A specialized component capable of projecting music directly into the minds of an audience. 

## Current Status
The sword remains at [[icespire-hold]]. Following the dissipation of the Carver's essence, the weapon has become visually inert—no longer glowing with purple energy, but remaining a still, black object.
