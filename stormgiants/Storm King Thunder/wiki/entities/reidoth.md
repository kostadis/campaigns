# Reidoth

Reidoth is the nominal head of the local [[emerald-enclave]] chapter. Though he has not yet appeared in person, his reputation and political influence are significant. His presence is noted to alter the "texture" of political proceedings; while he may lack direct procedural veto power, his attendance prevents unilateral maneuvers by more radical factions.

He is not a central figure in day-to-day operations, but his exclusion is emblematic: in systems of belief, power is not always held by those with the title — but by those who control the space in which decisions are made.

## Political Role

Reidoth serves as a critical [[political-counterweight]] within the Enclave. His influence lies not in formal authority, but in his capacity to temper extremism and uphold procedural fairness. This makes him the primary obstacle to [[adabra-gwynn]]'s influence.

## Strategic Exclusion

[[adabra-gwynn]] has demonstrated tactical awareness of his importance by deliberately scheduling council sessions during his absence to secure a majority for her faction. This act of strategic exclusion reveals her political acumen — and her willingness to manipulate institutional norms to achieve ideological ends. His absence from the council hearing convened by Adabra was not accidental; it was calculated.

## Recent Developments

Following a council session that turned against the adventuring party, [[vukradin]] utilized a *Sending* spell to contact Reidoth. He has been briefed on:
- The internal schism within the [[emerald-enclave]].
- [[adabra-gwynn]]'s political maneuvering.
- The confession of [[falcon]].

His reaction to this intelligence remains unknown. Whether he will challenge Adabra, remain silent, or reassert his authority is a pivotal unanswered question in the Enclave’s unfolding power struggle.
