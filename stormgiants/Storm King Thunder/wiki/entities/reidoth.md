# Reidoth

Reidoth is the nominal head of the local [[emerald-enclave]] chapter. While he has not yet appeared in person, his reputation and political influence are significant.

## Political Role
Reidoth serves as a critical [[political-counterweight]] within the Enclave. His presence is noted to alter the "texture" of political proceedings; while he may lack direct procedural veto power, his attendance prevents unilateral maneuvers by more radical factions.

## Relationship with Adabra
He is the primary obstacle to [[adabra-gwynn]]'s influence. [[adabra-gwynn]] has demonstrated tactical awareness of his importance by deliberately scheduling council sessions during his absence to secure a majority for her faction.

## Recent Developments
Following a council session that turned against the adventuring party, [[vukradin]] utilized a *Sending* spell to contact Reidoth. He has been briefed on:
- The internal schism within the [[emerald-enclave]].
- [[adabra-gwynn]]'s political maneuvering.
- The confession of [[falcon]].

His reaction to this intelligence remains unknown.
