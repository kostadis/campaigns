# Tibor Wester

**Tibor Wester** (also known as **Tribar Wester**) is the chief of the [[logger-camp]] and the primary signatory authority for delivery notices and payments. He is currently a missing person.

## Background and Activities
Tibor operated a [[wildcat-logging]] enterprise, hiring crews to harvest timber outside of established forestry regulations and permit frameworks. While he held the legal and administrative power to authorize deliveries, his actual operations were illicit.

There are unresolved questions regarding the extent of his brother's involvement in this operation and whether the enterprise constitutes a conflict of interest for the town of [[phandalin]].

## Disappearance
Tibor and the entire population of the [[logger-camp]] vanished unexpectedly. Although the camp shows signs of recent habitation—including stone chimneys, stacked logs, and tents—it was found in a state of "grim silence" and total desertion.

## Key Relationships

### Family
* **[[harbin-wester]]**: Tibor's brother. Harbin posted a supply delivery quest on Tibor's behalf.

### Associates and Contacts
* **[[vukradin]]**: Had an outstanding financial arrangement with Tibor. He reacted with a "soft warning sound" when Tibor's name was mentioned, suggesting knowledge of his illicit activities.
* **Logging Crews**: Hired workers who were largely unaware of the illegal nature of the operation but intended to report the activities to the city.
* **Shrimpy**: A prisoner whose transport to Neverwinter Tibor refused to facilitate.
