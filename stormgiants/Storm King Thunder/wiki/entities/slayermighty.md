# Slayermighty

The former owner of Blooddrinker, a bear later magically shrunken by a cursed cave. Slayermighty died in the cave, after which Blooddrinker ceased all aggressive behavior and began seeking affection. No further details about Slayermighty’s identity, role, or fate are known beyond his connection to Blooddrinker and the cursed location.

He serves as a narrative catalyst — his death marks the turning point in Blooddrinker’s behavioral transformation — but he is otherwise a background figure with no direct interaction with the party. His legacy lives only through the bond Blooddrinker formed with Thistle Wendrod.
