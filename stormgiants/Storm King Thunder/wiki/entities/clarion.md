# Clarion

**Clarion** is a silver dragon serving as a "field asset" and transport for the **Lord's Alliance**. Unlike many draconic entities that seek personal glory or alliance, Clarion operates strictly within its contract with the organization.

### Role and Behavior
- **Field Asset:** Clarion functions primarily as a logistical tool. It provides transportation and geographic information but does not hold command authority.
- **Command Hierarchy:** Clarion is highly disciplined, frequently correcting others who assume it holds leadership status, deferring all major decisions to **Commander Diana**.
- **Pragmatic Cooperation:** While initially aggressive and territorial, Clarion's demeanor shifts to businesslike cooperation once the situation is clarified.

### Knowledge Boundaries
Clarion has no personal connection to or knowledge of **Adimond Kroskas** or his associated cult, serving as a clear boundary between the military operations of the Lord's Alliance and the radical movements of the interior.
