# Presiding Druid (Enclave Council)

The official arbiter of the Emerald Enclave council that heard Adabra Gwynn’s case against the party and Vukradin. He rejected her arguments as “just does not hold” and condemned her personal attacks on Vukradin as “absolutely disgraceful” and “crossed the line.”

His ruling was not a rejection of her philosophy — it was a rejection of her methods. He upheld procedural integrity over ideological conviction.

He represents the institutional voice of the Enclave: measured, lawful, and weary of extremism. His presence reminds us that even in a world of magic and myth, governance still demands rules.

He is the quiet counterpoint to Adabra’s fire — the voice of order in a world unraveling.
