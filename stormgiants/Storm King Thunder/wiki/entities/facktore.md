# Facktore

Facktore is a deranged gnome inventor residing in a large chamber north of the cave entrance to [[gnomengarde]]. He is an unaligned, peripheral antagonist who operates without formal faction affiliation.

## Personality & Motivations
Facktore is characterized by extreme instability and a reckless, obsessive drive to test his mechanical inventions. He lacks ethical restraint, viewing others—including the adventuring party—merely as [[live-testing]] subjects for his unproven devices. He demonstrates poor combat resolve, preferring to flee and avoid accountability rather than face the consequences of his actions when his inventions fail.

## History with the Party
Facktore initiated unprovoked hostilities against [[vukradin]] and the party by ambushing them in his chamber. He utilized a [[rotating-crossbow-platform]] to facilitate the attack. Once the party successfully disabled his device, Facktore fled the scene.

## Current Status
* **Last Known Location:** Somewhere within the vicinity of [[gnomengarde]].
* **Status:** At large; exact whereabouts unknown following his flight from the ambush site.
