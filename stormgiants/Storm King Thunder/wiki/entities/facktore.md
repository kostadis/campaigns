# Facktore

Facktore is a deranged gnome inventor residing in a large chamber north of the cave entrance to [[gnomengarde]]. He has no formal faction affiliation and operates entirely outside institutional oversight, making him an unaligned, peripheral antagonist.

Facktore’s defining trait is his complete disregard for consent or consequence. He treats others—including the adventuring party—as [[live-testing]] subjects without provocation, justification, or warning. His ambush of [[vukradin]] and the party using a [[rotating-crossbow-platform]]—a device fitted with four automatically reloading heavy crossbows—was not an act of defense or domination, but an unregulated experiment designed to observe mechanical performance under real-world conditions. This act embodies the [[ambush-as-experiment]] archetype.

When his device was disabled, Facktore fled the chamber rather than face accountability, exemplifying the [[fleeing-inventor-archetype]]. He demonstrates poor combat resolve, preferring escape over confrontation, and shows no ethical restraint or remorse for the dangers he creates.

He is not a traditional villain — he is a symptom. His existence reveals Gnomengarde’s systemic failure to monitor its own outliers, exposing the fragility of institutions that prioritize innovation over responsibility. His backstory is intentionally absent; his narrative function is behavioral, not biographical.

## Current Status
* **Last Known Location:** Within Gnomengarde, fled from his chamber after the ambush.
* **Status:** Active, unaccounted for.
* **Party Perception:** Hostile, unpredictable, dangerous.

Facktore’s potential for further harm remains latent. Whether he has additional inventions, hidden allies, or plans for retaliation is unknown — but his pattern suggests he is still at large, and his next experiment may be worse.
