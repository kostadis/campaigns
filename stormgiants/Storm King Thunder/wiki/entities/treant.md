# Treant

A sacred, ancient being that once dwelled in the Whispering Grove, the treant was a living nexus of natural harmony and druidic reverence. Its death — roots collapsed, crown blackened — was the catalyst for Adabra Gwynn’s campaign against interventionism.

Adabra witnessed its death firsthand and interpreted it as the direct consequence of the party’s actions. To her, it was not a casualty of war — it was a sacrifice to the arrogance of intervention.

Its death is ambiguous: was it caused by Lyra’s failed ritual? By planar corruption? By the Kraken cult’s influence? Or by the party’s interference?

Regardless of cause, its death became a symbol — of loss, of imbalance, of the cost of action. It is the heart of Adabra’s grief, and the center of her moral universe.

It is not just a creature. It is a monument.
