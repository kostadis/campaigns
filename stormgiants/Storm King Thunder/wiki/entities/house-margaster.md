# House Margaster

House Margaster is a powerful and manipulative noble house of Neverwinter, distinguished by its ruthless pursuit of social dominance through indirect, deniable actions. Rather than open conflict, Margaster excels in psychological warfare—using cultural sabotage, public humiliation, and strategic interference to erode the prestige of rivals.

Its current target is House Meliame, whose cultural legitimacy is being undermined by the attempted interception of the lighthouse statue—a symbol of Meliame heritage. This act exemplifies [[noble-house-rivalry]] and extends [[symbolic-targeting-of-cultural-artifacts]] into the aristocratic sphere: the theft or loss of a heritage object is not mere looting, but a public declaration of inferiority.

House Margaster operates through proxies, misinformation, and plausible deniability, mirroring the [[disguised-influence]] and [[cover-identity-as-merchant]] patterns seen in other factions. Its agents are not mercenaries but social engineers, trained to exploit the emotional weight of tradition. The house’s power lies not in armies, but in the perception of status—and it will do anything to control that perception.
