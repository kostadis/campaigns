# Dashing Dallon Flek

Dashing Dallon Flek is a "hollow" bounty target captured by the party. He possesses no recorded personality, motivations, or backstory within the current narrative.

## Role and Status
Flek serves as a functional object in the story to facilitate the mechanics of bounty hunting. His capture over a period of five days provided a tangible reward for [[unla-kee]]. 

## Narrative Function
Flek is a prime example of [[narrative-economy]], where an NPC exists solely to provide a mechanical goal or confirm a character's competence without requiring extensive dialogue or development. He also exemplifies [[information-asymmetry-npc-encounters]], as the party knows only that he was a valid target; his specific crimes and identity remain entirely unknown.

## Relations
- **[[unla-kee]]**: The hunter who captured him.
- **[[hand-of-yartar]]**: The organization to whom he was turned in for payment.
