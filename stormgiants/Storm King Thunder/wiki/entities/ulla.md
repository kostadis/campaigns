# Ulla

A female gnome guard stationed at the cliff-top entrance to [[gnomengarde]]. She is responsible for enforcing security protocols and identifying potential infiltrators.

## Identity & Profile
- **Role:** Guard at the [[gnomengarde]] cliff-top entrance.
- **Species:** Gnome.
- **Faction:** [[gnomengarde]].
- **Partner:** [[pog]].

## Personality & Motivations
Ulla is characterized by [[bureaucratic-logic]] and a deep-seated [[shapechanger-paranoia]]. She is procedurally rigid, often using accusations of shapeshifting to deflect social pressure or arguments. While she presents a hardline exterior, she is susceptible to social manipulation.

## History with the Party
- **Entry Negotiation:** Challenged the party at the [[gnomengarde]] entrance. She only granted entry once a procedural precedent was established regarding [[brewbarry]]'s Uthgardt status.
- **Navigation:** Provided [[vukradin]] with directions to the inventors' workshop and disclosed the existence of an alternate "northern route" to avoid spinning blade traps.
- **Social Conflict:** After being manipulated by [[valphine]], Ulla turned an accusation of being a shapeshifter back onto her partner [[pog]], leading to significant interpersonal tension.

## Relationships
- **[[pog]]**: Partner guard. Their relationship is currently strained and in a state of mutual suspicion due to the shapeshifter accusations. While the conflict was triggered by external manipulation, the guards believe the tension arose organically.
- **[[valphine]]**: Successfully manipulated Ulla, causing Ulla to deflect the resulting social fallout onto others.
- **[[brewbarry]]**: Acknowledged his Uthgardt status as a basis for party entry.
- **[[vukradin]]**: Provided him with navigational intelligence.
