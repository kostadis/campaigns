# Lyra

Lyra is a low-ranking field leader and interventionist druid within the [[emerald-enclave]], characterized by high conviction, intense passion, and a tendency toward impulsive, unilateral action. She represents the institutional face of interventionism—disciplined, ritualized, and morally certain—advocating for active cleansing rituals to restore ecological balance, viewing planar incursions and blights as threats to be purged.

## Personality and Leadership

Lyra is a principled leader deeply committed to cleansing planar corruption. Her leadership is driven by personal affection for the natural sites she protects, which has previously led her to bypass official [[emerald-enclave]] protocols. While capable of profound self-awareness and candor regarding her failures, she often suffers from overconfidence and poor tactical planning. Her conflict with [[adabra-gwynn]] is not merely tactical, but metaphysical: one believes in healing through force, the other in healing through absence.

## Key Events

### The Whispering Grove Incident

Lyra attempted a sealing ritual to combat a planar infection in the Whispering Grove without official authorization. The ritual failed, not restoring the grove but instead accelerating its decay and causing planar wells to expand, nearly killing her team. This event became the cornerstone of [[adabra-gwynn]]’s political campaign against interventionism, which framed Lyra’s actions as “acts of violence against nature” and emblematic of the faction’s destructive arrogance.

Crucially, Lyra’s actions were reactive to a deeper, non-natural corruption—later revealed to be puppet-controlled by Narcellia Varammyr—a nuance Adabra deliberately ignored in her pursuit of ideological victory.

### Ostracization and Restoration

Following the failure at the grove, [[adabra-gwynn]] actively worked to ostracize Lyra from the Enclave during the party’s absence. However, through the political intervention of the party—notably [[vukradin]] and [[soma]]—Lyra was defended at the Druid Council and restored to full good standing within the [[emerald-enclave]].
