# Model 1489 Spatial Rectification Calliope

The Model 1489 Spatial Rectification Calliope is a dwarven-engineered planar device originally designed for stabilizing spatial anomalies in mining operations and dimensional bridge construction. Its core function is to harmonize fluctuating planar frequencies, preventing catastrophic rifts. However, it was repurposed by House Sartorra for illicit interdimensional smuggling.

The device operates via a glowing keypad interface and emits harmonic resonance fields that temporarily thin the barriers between planes. When misused, it generates unstable rifts and spawns [[spectral-directionality]] and [[spatial-equilibrium]] — semi-sentient entities that patrol and defend the machine’s operational zone. Aletra Satora demonstrated expert-level proficiency in operating the Calliope, using it to extract [[midnight-tears]] and open a self-sustaining portal for escape.

Its manual, annotated by Aletra with the note “use only if KP isn't watching,” reveals that its use is restricted and monitored. The machine’s failure during her extraction may have been accidental, or a deliberate sabotage by KP to eliminate evidence or test her loyalty.

**Status:** Destroyed after Aletra’s escape; core components collapsed into the void.  
**Significance:**  
- First documented case of a dwarven engineering artifact being weaponized for criminal logistics  
- Bridges [[planar-travel]] and organized crime  
- Symbolizes the institutionalization of planar technology in the drow underworld
