# Baash'a Kweelani

A merchant of magical goods known only through secondhand accounts. She has never been directly encountered by the party. Her existence is confirmed only by Unla Kee, who mentioned her unprompted during a survey of the Fishyard.

**Physical Description:** Bald, with skin of bright, flaming red — an unusual trait suggesting possible non-human lineage, magical alteration, or curse. No known parallels in existing records. This appearance aligns with the broader concept of [[exotic-appearance]], marking her as an outlier in regional demographics and reinforcing her enigmatic status.

**Known Facts:**
- Specializes in magical goods for discerning magic users, operating within the [[magical-goods-trade]] network
- Likely operates in or near the Fishyard
- Known to Unla Kee, implying prior professional or social contact
- No known faction affiliation

**Hidden Details:**
- True location and base of operations
- Nature of her inventory (e.g., Oracle-touched items? Soul-bound artifacts?)
- Whether she is independent, tied to SEER, Zhentarim, or another network
- Origin of her red skin — elemental, experimental, or divine?

Her introduction follows the [[pre-meeting-narrative-structure]] and reinforces the [[cover-identity-as-merchant]] trope. She is a deliberate narrative placeholder — a mystery to be resolved only when the story demands it. As a central figure in the game’s latent lore, she embodies the intersection of mystery, trade, and the uncanny, serving as both a potential ally and an unresolved thread in the world’s deeper magic economy.
