# Luistarra

A member of Chandra’s family, implied to be connected to the unnamed nemesis who drove [[Bad Fruul]]’s assault on Parnast. Bad Fruul specifically targeted Chandra by referencing Luistarra, suggesting a deep, personal history between the two families.

Luistarra’s role is currently unknown, but her name is tied to the core motivation behind the attack: the desire to capture Chandra and possibly reclaim or destroy something linked to her lineage. She may be the nemesis herself, or a key figure in the lineage targeted by [[Narcellia Varam’myr]].

Her existence confirms that Bad Fruul’s campaign was not merely territorial or ideological, but deeply personal and rooted in a hidden conflict predating the party’s involvement. Her connection to Evereska and the broader elven diaspora may hold clues to the artifact’s origin.
