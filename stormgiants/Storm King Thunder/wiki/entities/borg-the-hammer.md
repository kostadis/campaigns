# Borg the Hammer

[[borg-the-hammer]] was a half-orc enforcer and field commander operating under the authority of [[the-carver]]. He was characterized by a dangerous combination of tactical awareness and violent, unpredictable rage.

## Profile
- **Role:** Field Commander / Enforcer / Captor
- **Faction:** [[the-carver]]'s organization
- **Command:** Leads units of [[rot-tusk-ogres]]

## Personality and Tactics
Borg utilized [[tactical-pragmatism]] to achieve his objectives. He was capable of cold-blooded decision-making—such as executing informants who had outlived their usefulness—but was also prone to extreme emotional volatility. 

He frequently employed decoys to lure targets into traps. Most notably, he seized control of [[butterskull-ranch]] following an initial orc raid and utilized [[alphonse-big-al-kalazorn]] as bait, keeping him captive in the ranch cellar to lure adventurers into a confrontation.

## Notable Actions
- **The Butterskull Ranch Ambush:** Orchestrated a multi-stage trap at [[butterskull-ranch]]. After the ambush failed, he ordered the destruction of the entire ranch out of frustration.
- **Tactical Splitting:** Following the failure at the ranch, he deliberately split his forces—sending [[rot-tusk-ogres]] in one direction while he moved in another—to complicate any potential pursuit.

## Status
> [!CAUTION]
> **Deceased**

Last seen fleeing the ruins of [[butterskull-ranch]] while being shadowed by the party, intelligence confirms that [[borg-the-hammer]] was killed by [[locutus]] prior to the meeting between [[sister-kaella]] and [[valphine]].
