# Aunt Nonnie

Aunt Nonnie is the innkeeper and proprietor of a rustic vegetarian‑only inn in Llorkh. She provides a safe haven for travelers who do not align with the Iron Circle, offering warm hospitality and a strictly plant‑based menu. She holds quiet authority over staff such as Orsik, using knowledge of his past to maintain order, and she shows curiosity about the local tower, expressing disappointment when its exploration is deemed a one‑time venture.
