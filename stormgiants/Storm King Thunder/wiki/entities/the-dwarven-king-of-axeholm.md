# The Dwarven King of Axeholm

The former ruler of [[axeholm]], now a three-headed monstrosity. The King has been entirely consumed by [[interdimensional-corruption]], leaving no trace of his former personality or motivations. He functions as a feral, highly aggressive combatant.

## Combat Profile
During the encounter in the pillared throne room, the King demonstrated:
- **Extreme Speed and Ferocity:** Capable of rapid, multi-directional assaults.
- **Corrupted Resilience:** The ability to shrug off grievous wounds that would typically incapacitate a mortal.
- **Targeted Aggression:** Demonstrated a high capacity for identifying and neutralizing high-value targets (e.g., forcing soma out of bear form).
- **Mental Resistance:** Showed significant resistance to mental/illusion magic, though eventually succumbing to specific psychic stimuli.

## Status
**Deceased.** Slain by the party in the throne room of [[axeholm]].
