# Mountain's Toe Gold Mine

The mountain-toe-gold-mine is a significant mining operation and economic asset. It serves as a site of intense social and political negotiation between mining interests, local supernatural inhabitants, and external investors.

## Management and Oversight
* **Ownership & Investment:** Operations are conducted on behalf of a [[neverwinter-consortium]]. [[falcon-the-hunter]] holds a financial stake in the mine via his membership in a [[neverwinter-mining-consortium]].
* **Current Management:** The site is managed by [[horia-stonetallow]] and the [[stonetallow-clan]].
* **Supervision:** The site is currently under the oversight of [[don-jon-raskin]].
* **Strategic Importance:** The mine represents a key economic interest for Neverwinter-based organizations and potentially serves as a front or point of influence for the [[kraken-society]].
* **Historical Context:** The mine was previously managed by [[don-jon]], whose attempt to exploit cheap labor led to his death and the subsequent claim by the Stonetallow dwarves.

## Economic and Operational Status
* **Projected Earnings:** Approximately 100 gp per week.
* **Current Production Stoppage:** The mine is currently experiencing a total production stoppage. This halt is not due to mechanical failure or resource depletion, but is a direct result of the foreman [[dwolin-the-grout]] refusing to mine without the necessary components for his [[ritualistic-mining]] practices. 
* **Environmental Crisis:** The mine is facing an environmental crisis where its waters are being poisoned. This poses a direct threat to investments and regional stability.
* **Economic Impact:** Due to the production stoppage and the water crisis, the mine serves as a localized economic flashpoint.

## Inhabitants and Social Dynamics
Following a negotiation by [[vukradin]], the mine operates under a policy of [[compromise-cohabitation]] with a local population of wererats.

* **Labor Structure:** A compromise arrangement exists between the [[stonetallow-clan]] (responsible for mining) and wererat laborers (responsible for security and general labor).

## Active Quests
* **Water Poisoning Investigation:** [[falcon-the-hunter]] has offered the adventuring party a share of the mine's profits in exchange for investigating and resolving the poisoning of the mine's waters.
