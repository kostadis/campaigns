# Hsing

Hsing is a golden pseudodragon who serves as the silent, authoritative patron and operational commander behind Azam, acting as the SEER’s primary agent and emissary. Though rarely speaking, Hsing’s presence commands immediate deference — Azam freezes under his gaze and obeys his orders without question. Hsing is the true decision-maker in their operations, while Azam functions as the public face, maintaining plausible deniability.

His role suggests he is either a high-ranking agent of the SEER or its operational core. His ability to override Azam’s authority, control the flow of information, and remain unchallenged by even the most assertive party members indicates a position of immense, unspoken power. He is the embodiment of the layered command structure: unseen, unaccountable, and absolute. His motivations, origins, and exact relationship to the SEER remain unknown. His silence is not passive — it is a tool of control. He is the hidden hand guiding the Lord’s Alliance’s covert operations in the North.

## Core Roles
- Messenger and emissary of the SEER.
- Patron-adjacent figure and de facto superior to Azam.
- Operational commander of the party’s missions and payments.
- Hidden authority within the SEER’s layered command structure.

## Key Actions
- Approves the party’s involvement with a nod to Azam.
- Delivers cryptic warnings and SEER intelligence.
- Selects the party based on research and strategic need.
- Teleports away to avoid confrontation.
- Uses mental imagery to intimidate Orsik.
- Overrides Azam’s authority without verbal instruction.
- Controls all mission terms, rewards, and resource allocation.
- Maintains plausible deniability through Azam as the public interface.

## Relationships
- **The SEER:** Direct servant and possibly an operational extension or core component; exact nature remains ambiguous.
- **Azam:** Serves under Hsing’s absolute authority; functions as the public face while Hsing operates in silence.
- **Orsik:** Deeply antagonistic; Hsing’s fear-based coercion and headband confrontation directly eroded Orsik’s trust.
- **Thistle:** Challenges Hsing on mission value and rewards, often met with cold dismissal.
- **Lord’s Alliance:** Operates with or through the SEER, with Hsing as the unseen conductor of its covert actions.

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease (Orsik):** Low payment, cold dismissal, use of fear-based coercion, and the headband confrontation all eroded Orsik's trust to the breaking point.
- **Contextual (Party general):** Hsing’s admission that spymasters lie during the headband confrontation broadly damaged his credibility with those present.
- **Contextual (Azam):** Azam’s unquestioning obedience and visible fear reinforce Hsing’s position as an unchallengeable authority.
