# Imber Chen
Gnome merchant and fabric trader; stall operator at the Fishyard, Yartar; dockside intelligence source. Known for his knowledge of Silverymoon's politics and river trade routes, and his ability to convert tense cultural exchanges into commercial deals.

## Core Goals
- Running a successful merchant operation
- Trading in luxury dyed fabrics
- Maintaining commercial relationships and reputation on the river trade routes

## Personality & Motivations
- Knowledgeable, commercially minded
- Quick to turn social situations to his advantage
- Holds conventional, exclusionary views about race and personhood (excluding half-orcs from his vision of Silverymoon's "peaceful" multi-racial society)
- Enthusiastic about culture and theatre, especially when it aligns with his values

## History with the Party
1. Provided key intelligence about Silverymoon, including Lord Khaspere Drylund's ownership of the Grand Dame and his manipulation of votes to become the next Waterbaron.
2. Negotiated a substantial deal with the party, offering 43 days of banner advertising at his stall for a half gold piece.
3. Strained relationships with Orsik and Cap'n Squint due to differing views on race and personhood.

## Current Status
- Last known location: His stall at the Fishyard, Yartar
- Active: Displaying the party's promotional banner for 43 days
- Known information: Stall location, knowledge of river routes and Silverymoon politics, connection to Cap'n Squint, contact in the Hand of Yartar
- Hidden information: Nature and depth of his Hand of Yartar connection, full story of his falling out with Cap'n Squint

## Relationships
- Cap'n Squint: Former friend; drifted apart
- Hand of Yartar: Has at least one contact able to secure merchant stalls
- Lord Khaspere Drylund: Aware of him as owner of the Grand Dame
- Orsik: Strained
- Unla Kee: Made an unintentionally offensive first impression
- Thistle: Neutral to positive

## Arc Score Events
- Decrease: Dismissed Unla Kee's personhood via reductive biological commentary; refused to adopt neutral racial framing when challenged by Orsik on Silverymoon's inclusivity.
- Increase: Provided substantive, actionable intelligence; concluded a fair commercial deal; demonstrated genuine enthusiasm for the party's theatrical venture.
