# Corwin

Corwin is the chef and retainer at the Falcon Hunting Lodge, employed by [[falcon-the-hunter]]. He serves as the lodge’s sole point of contact for visitors, functioning as a gatekeeper who verifies credentials before granting access. His role is entirely procedural: he greets guests, checks [[barthen]]’s letter of introduction, admits them, and fetches [[4-npcs--17-falcon_the_hunter--qft71h]] — performing each step with dutiful precision and no personal deviation.

## Role and Function

Corwin manages hospitality and day-to-day operations at the lodge, ensuring that guests are vetted before being granted access to the master of the house. He operates through [[procedural-hospitality]], requiring verified credentials—such as a letter of introduction from [[barthen]]—before facilitating meetings with [[4-npcs--17-falcon_the_hunter--qft71h]]. His presence embodies [[gatekeeping-as-service]] and [[hospitality-as-ritual]], demonstrating that authority in this world is often mediated not by magic or power, but by mundane, bureaucratic acts—ringing a bell, reading a letter, walking to fetch a master.

He is the sole interface between outsiders and [[4-npcs--17-falcon_the_hunter--qft71h]], and his function is to enforce [[credential-based-access]] as a formalized ritual. He does not make exceptions, offer opinions, or deviate from protocol.

## Personality

Corwin is characterized as dutiful, professional, and cordial. His primary motivation is the efficient management of the lodge’s service functions and the fulfillment of his duties to his employer. He exhibits no emotional arc, hidden motives, or personal relationships beyond his employment. He is not a source of mystery, conflict, or revelation.
