# Corwin

Corwin is a professional chef and retainer employed by [[falcon]] at the [[falcon-hunting-lodge]]. 

## Role and Function
Corwin serves as a primary gatekeeper for the lodge. He manages hospitality and day-to-day operations, ensuring that guests are vetted before being granted access to the master of the house. He operates through [[procedural-hospitality]], requiring verified credentials—such as a letter of introduction from [[barthen]]—before facilitating meetings with [[falcon]].

## Personality
He is characterized as dutiful, professional, and cordial. His primary motivation is the efficient management of the lodge's service functions and the fulfillment of his duties to his employer.

## Relationships
- **[[falcon]]**: Direct employer.
- **[[barthen]]**: Recognized his written introduction as a valid credential for the party.
- **The Party**: Admitted them to the lodge following successful credential verification.
