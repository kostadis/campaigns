# Jarlor
Jarlor is a resident of Parnast, formerly a town council member. He was coerced into spying for Orc Chief Kurglow, who held his family hostage in an orc cave as leverage. Jarlor's child later testified that he spied against his will. Jarlor was persuaded (partly by his wife's challenge) to transmit a covert message to Kurglow via the spy channel on the party's behalf. The message, composed by Orsik, was a comically non-threatening warning about denying the orc whelp dessert and a bedtime story. Jarlor internally questioned whether to correct the dwarf but deferred to him. Jarlor appeared briefly in Parnast, muttering sarcastically about the ineffectiveness of Orsik's threats. Jarlor's true feelings about the party, any remaining ties to Kurglow or the orc clan, and his relationship with Orsik remain hidden.

## Relationships
- **Chief Kurglow:** Coercer — Jarlor spied under duress due to Kurglow's hostage-taking; relationship appears dissolved with the family's rescue.
- **Jarlor's Wife:** A motivating force; she challenged him to act when he hesitated.
- **Jarlor's Child:** Defended Jarlor's character to the freed prisoners and the party.
- **Orsik (party):** Recognized from the town council; Jarlor defers to him as an authority figure despite privately doubting his competence; relationship is mildly sardonic on Jarlor's end.
