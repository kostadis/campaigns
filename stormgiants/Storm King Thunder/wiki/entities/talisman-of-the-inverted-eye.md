# Talisman of the Inverted Eye

A dark, iridescent talisman discovered in a concealed compartment within [[moesko]]'s living quarters.

## Physical Description
The artifact features dual symbolism:
1. **Obverse:** The stylized lightning bolt of [[talos]].
2. **Reverse:** An inverted eye with coiling, tentacle-like lines.

## Properties
The talisman emits a combination of faint divine resonance and a dominant, [[alien-corruption]] characterized by intense psionic energy. It is considered a primary clue regarding the identity of the "Unknown Patron" influencing the region.
