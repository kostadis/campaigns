# Norbus Ironrune

A Shield dwarf merchant, explorer, and prospector who operates as a client for the adventuring party. He is a long-standing business partner to [[dazlyn-grayshard]].

## Profile

Norbus is characterized by a high degree of social intelligence and pragmatism. He navigates complex interpersonal dynamics with calculated ease, particularly in high-stakes negotiations where moral concerns arise—most notably when addressing the objections of [[vukradin]] during contract discussions.

## Activities and Role

Norbus is a co-discoverer of the ancient dwarven settlement and the [[temple-of-abbathor]], located in the canyon southwest of [[phandalin]]. He works alongside Dazlyn to clear rubble, prospect for gold, and excavate artifacts from the ruins. His dual expertise as a merchant and prospector makes him a key logistical and financial anchor for operations in the region.

### Contract Negotiation

- **Payment Terms:** Offered [[sending-stones]] as compensation for clearing the interior of the temple in the canyon ruins.
- **Ethical Pledges:** To assuage the moral reservations of party members, particularly [[vukradin]], Norbus explicitly pledged that all artifacts recovered during the mission would be returned to their rightful owners.

## Uncertainties

Despite his public commitments to ethical recovery and restitution, a persistent tension exists between Norbus’s stated principles and his primary motivation: profit. His true personal stake in the canyon ruins—whether financial, cultural, or otherwise—remains obscured. Questions linger regarding his actual intent toward recovered artifacts: are they truly destined for restitution, or is the pledge a strategic concession to secure cooperation? His long-term goals and hidden agendas in relation to the temple and its relics are still unknown.
