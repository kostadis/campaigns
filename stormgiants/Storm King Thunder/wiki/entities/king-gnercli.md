# King Gnercli

Co-ruler of the [[kobold-kingdom]], serving alongside [[king-korboz]]. Due to Korboz's current illness, Gnercli has become the functional decision-maker for the kingdom.

## Personality & Motivations
Gnercli is a perceptive and pragmatic leader who is deeply invested in the [[dual-monarchy]] political system. He views the shared rule as a vital safeguard against autocracy and specifically against the threat of [[shapeshifter-threat]] mimicking a single leader. However, he is notably short-tempered under intellectual or political pressure, as evidenced by his volatile reaction to [[vukradin]]'s critiques.

## Current Status
Following a tense audience with the party, Gnercli is reportedly in a state of renewed conflict with [[king-korboz]]. He has tasked the party with eliminating a shapeshifter suspected to be hiding in a cellar among large barrels, offering a [[hat-of-wizardry]] as payment for their services.

## Relationships
- [[king-korboz]]: Co-ruler and philosophical counterpart; currently in open conflict.
- [[vukradin]]: Intellectual antagonist; Gnercli was visibly infuriated by Vukradin's dismantling of his political arguments.
- The Party: Transactional relationship; hired to resolve the shapeshifter threat using a [[hat-of-wizardry]] as payment.
