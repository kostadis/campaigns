# Giles Slipper-Shine

Giles Slipper-Shine is a pale-skinned, sunburned villager and construction worker in [[phandalin]]. A fervent lay religious enthusiast, he is a "true believer" in an unorthodox Lathanderite doctrine.

## Identity & Personality
Giles is characterized by his earnestness and deep commitment to his faith. Despite physical signs of hardship—including sunburn and scars consistent with self-flagellation—he frequently displays what is described as "convert's radiance," an ecstatic joy and religious fervor. He is a community-minded individual, willing to provide free labor for religious causes he believes in.

## Religious Affiliation
He is a devoted follower of the [[theology-of-searing-pain]], viewing the deity Lathander through the lens of purifying solar pain rather than mere light. He was an enthusiastic witness to [[valphine]]'s sermon and participates in public religious activities and fundraising on behalf of [[valphine]]'s ministry.

## Activities
* **Construction:** Providing free labor for the reconstruction of the church in [[phandalin]] and the renovation of Tresendar Manor into the [[temple-of-lathanders-searing-pain-of-justice]].
* **Evangelism:** Participating in public religious parades and activities to promote the new Lathanderite interpretation.

## Relationships
* [[brin-bundlewine]]: A close associate, fellow laborer, and co-participant in religious parades.
* [[valphine]]: The religious leader whose sermons and authority he fervently supports.
