# Brass Ring of Lightning

The Brass Ring of Lightning is a small, ornate ring inscribed with the Elvish word for “lightning,” housed within a miniature iron dragon’s head box. It was recovered by [[falcon-the-hunter]] from the hoard of [[iymrith]] after the destruction of his adventuring party. Though not inherently magical in its current state, the ring resonates with latent lightning energy and is tied to Falcon’s deepest trauma — the moment he survived when all others perished.

The ring became the foundation of Falcon’s new identity: its value funded the construction of his lodge, and its existence became the secret he guarded obsessively. When [[vukradin]] discovered it, he documented it as evidence of Falcon’s moral compromise — the theft of dragon’s hoard wealth. Falcon, though initially defensive, granted Vukradin permission to record its provenance, preserving dignity over denial.

Its connection to Iymrith suggests it may be a fragment of her power, a lure, or a tracking device. The ring’s presence in Falcon’s possession makes him a target for those seeking to trace Iymrith’s movements or exploit her residual influence. It remains in Falcon’s possession, though its full nature and potential are still unknown.
