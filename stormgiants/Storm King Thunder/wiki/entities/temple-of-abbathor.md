# Temple of Abbathor

An ancient dwarven temple located within a settlement in the canyon southwest of [[phandalin]].

## History
According to [[dazlyn-grayshard]], the settlement and its temple were destroyed by an earthquake and avalanche caused by the deity [[abbathor]]. This catastrophe was reportedly a divine punishment for the inhabitants' inadequate sacrifices.

## Current Status
The temple has recently been the site of conflict involving orcs and oozes. Following the intervention of the adventuring party, the immediate threats have been cleared, allowing prospectors [[dazlyn-grayshard]] and [[norbus-ironrune]] to resume their work.
