# Dazlyn Grayshard

A Shield dwarf merchant and prospector who serves as a business partner to [[norbus-ironrune]]. The two appear to operate as equals in their mercantile and exploration ventures. Dazlyn is a key figure in the discovery and ongoing exploration of the ancient dwarven ruins located in the canyon southwest of [[phandalin]].

## Personality and Motivations
Dazlyn is characterized as knowledgeable, forthcoming, and highly practical. He operates on a transactional basis, often utilizing non-coin assets such as [[sending-stones]] when gold is unavailable. He demonstrates a warm and celebratory nature once professional or survival-based threats are resolved.

## History
Dazlyn and [[norbus-ironrune]] spent several months clearing rubble in a canyon southwest of [[phandalin]] before discovering an ancient dwarven settlement containing the [[temple-of-abbathor]]. 

During the discovery, Dazlyn provided historical context regarding the settlement's destruction and negotiated a contract for [[the-party]] to clear threats—specifically oozes and orcs—from the temple in exchange for magical items.

## Relationships
- **[[norbus-ironrune]]**: Long-standing business partner and co-discoverer of the ruins.
- **[[the-party]]**: Maintains a friendly, transactional relationship that transitioned into [[transactional-hospitality]] following the resolution of the temple threat.
