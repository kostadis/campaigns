# Miral

**Miral the Self** was a sea elf whose spirit was bound to an [[opalescent-conch]] following her death. She manifested as a ghost to the party upon the return of her stolen property.

## Identity & Nature
- **Race:** Sea elf.
- **Status:** Undead/Ghost (manifested via spiritual binding).
- **Aliases:** Miral the Self.
- **Discrepancies:** While primarily identified as a sea elf, soma has referred to her in the context of a "giant crab," suggesting a potential shapeshifting ability or a specific aquatic form associated with her spirit.

## History
Miral was killed by [[moesko]], though she clarified that his actions were driven by "external shadows" that corrupted his [[talosian-devotion]]. Her spirit remained tethered to the [[opalescent-conch]] until the party recovered the item from Moesko and presented it to her.

## Warnings & Legacy
Before her spirit departed to find rest, she issued a critical warning regarding a shifting threat:
> *"Beware, surface dweller... the true storm gathers not in the sky, but in the depths below Leilon."*

She also expressed a benevolent intent toward the party, having hoped to retrieve the *Halberd from the Star Crossed Lover* for them.
