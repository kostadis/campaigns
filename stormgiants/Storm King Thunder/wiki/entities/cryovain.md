# Cryovain

Cryovain was a white dragon and the apex predator of the Icespire region. By the time of his encounter with the party, he was no longer a sentient being but a mindless engine of rage, held in a state of mental domination by [[the-carver]].

## Death and Aftermath
Cryovain was killed atop the battlements of [[icespire-hold]] by [[valphine]] using *sacred flame*. His death resulted in the collapse of the ritualistic summoning circle and the dissipation of [[the-carver]]'s essence.

### Harvested Remains
Following the battle, the party harvested several valuable components from his carcass:
- **Breath Pouch:** A swollen, heavy organ from the throat that radiates a unique [[metaphysical-cold]].
- **Dragon Scales:** Pale, supple scales intended for potential use in dragon scale mail.
- **Cryovain's Hoard:** A block of fused ice, gems, and gold valued at approximately 3,000 gold. Due to [[planar-magic]], the hoard is currently an inseparable, unspendable block requiring planar expertise to resolve.

## Moral Complexity
The death of Cryovain remains a point of contention within the party. [[soma]] argued that the dragon was a victim of [[mental-domination]] and deserved mercy, while others viewed his death as a pragmatic necessity to prevent indiscriminate destruction. [[vukradin]] continues to express moral complexity regarding the event.
