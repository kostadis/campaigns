# Cult of Talos

A chaotic, destructive cult devoted to the god of storms and destruction. To most, it is a threat to be eradicated. To Adabra Gwynn, it is “the necessary element of distraction in the cosmic balance.”

She does not see it as evil — she sees it as natural. Its violence is not a corruption, but a rhythm in the symphony of existence. This view, assessed by Xanth and internally criticized by Valphine as “mistaking slaughter for ecology,” is the most extreme expression of her naturalist philosophy.

The Cult of Talos is not a plot device — it is a philosophical challenge. If Adabra is right, then the party’s crusade against it is not righteous — it is arrogant.

Its existence forces the question: is destruction always wrong? Or is it sometimes just… part of the music?
