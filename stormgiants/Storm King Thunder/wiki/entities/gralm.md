# Gralm
- **Role:** Ettin warlord; ruler of a large encampment in or near the Forgotten Forest, within the Lonely Moor
- **Faction:** Independent; commands a mixed force of Hobgoblins, Goblins, and at least one Hill Giant
- **First Appearance:** Introduced in session briefing by Azam and Hsing; first met in person at his encampment when the party arrived to negotiate

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Maintain and expand his power base; defeat Bad Fruul (viewed as a waning rival); secure quiet and food for his bald head
- Gralm has two distinct personalities across his two heads. The **blonde head** is patient, articulate, politically astute, and manages diplomacy. The **bald head** is aggressive, chronically sleep-deprived, and interested only in eating and silence — frequently interrupting negotiations with outbursts.
- Despite his brutish appearance, Gralm operates with political shrewdness and a genuine sense of ceremony, requiring proof of worth before formalizing alliances.
- He responds warmly to competence and appetite, calling Unla Kee "a civilized adventurer."

## History with the Party
1. **Mission Briefing (Pre-Contact):** The Lord's Alliance, via Azam and Hsing, identifies Gralm as a key target. Bad Fruul is pressuring him to join an army against Parnast. The party is sent to present two magical contracts, with preference for the one that allies Gralm against Bad Fruul.
2. **First Meeting — Trials of Worth:** Gralm knew the party was coming before they arrived. The blonde head hears out the alliance proposal and acknowledges Bad Fruul as a weakening enemy, agreeing the time to strike is now. Before signing, he demands feats of strength and drinking contests to prove worth.
   - **Drinking Contests:** Pixie Sangria, The Everyday Ettin, The Ettin Grinder, and the Harpy's Dream — all passed; Thistle developed hiccups after the Ettin Grinder.
   - **Physical Contests:** Boar deadlift, brick wall kick-down, boar dog eating contest — all won by Unla Kee (via Reduce, Thunderwave, and eating 24 boar dogs respectively).
3. **The Fey Music Problem:** Gralm's bald head was sleep-deprived due to Fey music from Thornacious's expanding territory. Unla Kee crafted ettin-sized earmuffs from magmin shells. The bald head immediately fell asleep; the blonde head briefly wore his before removing them to continue negotiations.
4. **Contract Signed:** Orsik's persuasion, the party's trial victories, and the earmuff solution together convinced Gralm to sign the contract allying him against Bad Fruul. He signed in precise script. Afterward, he hosted a small celebration, offering drinks from fine crystal vessels.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** His encampment in or near the Forgotten Forest, Lonely Moor
- **Active Operations:** Bound by magical contract to act against Bad Fruul; bald head is now able to sleep thanks to the magmin earmuffs
- **What the Party Knows:** Gralm has signed the alliance contract and is provisionally friendly; his encampment is enormous and well-guarded
- **What Remains Hidden:** Whether Gralm will fully follow through on the contract's terms; the extent of his military capacity; the nature of his fairy-hunting beyond food shortage

## Relationships
- **Bad Fruul:** Enemy. Gralm views Bad Fruul's power as waning relative to his own and is willing to act against him.
- **Thornacious (Fey):** Source of ongoing conflict — Fey music from Thornacious's territory was causing the bald head's sleep deprivation.
- **Grindle:** Gralm's champion, a nearly thirteen-foot ogre.
- **Unla Kee:** Held in high regard; Gralm named the gnome "a civilized adventurer" for appetite, magical cleverness, and crafting the earmuffs.
- **Orsik:** Persuasion noted as a contributing factor in securing the contract signature.
- **Lord's Alliance (Azam & Hsing):** Gralm is now contractually bound to their preferred outcome; his personal opinion of the Alliance is unrecorded.

## Arc Score Events

- **Increase:** Unla Kee solved the Fey music problem with magmin earmuffs → bald head's sleep deprivation resolved; significant goodwill gained
- **Increase:** Orsik's persuasion combined with trial victories → Gralm signed the anti-Bad Fruul contract
