# Qelline Alderleaf

**Qelline Alderleaf** is a figure from [[alphonse-big-al-kalazorn]]'s past. She was once engaged to Big Al but was abandoned by him the night before their wedding.

## Current Involvement
She has offered grazing land to Big Al, though he has struggled to meet her fee of one gold piece per week. Following the party's intervention, Big Al has traveled to her farm to apologize in person and attempt reconciliation.
