# Petunia

Petunia is a cow and the emotional anchor of Alphonse "Big Al" Kalazorn. Though non-combatant and seemingly mundane, Petunia represents Alphonse’s capacity for care, loyalty, and emotional connection — qualities he struggled to express toward humans. Her brand, reading “Big Al’s Cow,” is a quiet testament to their bond.

Petunia served as a central motivator for Big Al’s actions and was the primary objective of the recovery quest at [[butterskull-ranch]]. She was located by Soma using the spell *Speak with Animals*, which revealed her identity and condition. Following her recovery, she was escorted to a remote camp and eventually traveled with Big Al to [[qelline-alderleaf]]'s farm.

Her recovery was the pivotal moment that forced Alphonse to confront his guilt over abandoning Qelline Alderleaf. Her presence during his confession and subsequent journey to Qelline’s farm symbolizes the possibility of reconciliation through simple, steadfast devotion.

Petunia is a canonical example of [[animal-companion-bonding]], demonstrating that emotional anchors need not be magical, intelligent, or heroic — they can be ordinary creatures whose presence reveals the deepest truths of their human companions. As both a quest item and a living symbol of redemption, Petunia transcends her role as livestock to become a cornerstone of Alphonse’s personal transformation.
