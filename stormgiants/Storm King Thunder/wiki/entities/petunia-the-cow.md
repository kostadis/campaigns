# Petunia the Cow

**Petunia** is the prized cow belonging to [[alphonse-big-al-kalazorn]]. She serves as a central motivator for Big Al's actions and was a primary objective in the recovery quest at [[butterskull-ranch]].

## Recovery
Petunia was located by [[soma]] using *Speak with Animals*. She is identified by a brand reading "Big Al's Cow." Following her recovery, she was escorted to a remote camp and eventually traveled with Big Al to [[qelline-alderleaf]]'s farm.
