# Pog

A male gnome guard stationed at the cliff-top entrance to [[gnomengarde]].

## Identity
- **Role:** Guard and functional part of the security apparatus for [[gnomengarde]].
- **Species:** Gnome.
- **Faction:** [[gnomengarde]].
- **Personality:** While he follows standing orders, he demonstrates a capacity for independent thought and is susceptible to social and philosophical persuasion.

## Interactions
- **Security Protocol:** Alongside his partner [[ulla]], he challenged the adventuring party to undergo [[shapeshifter-threat]] screening.
- **Vukradin:** Was visibly rattled by [[vukradin]]'s philosophical questioning of the screening logic and responded warmly to his music.
- **Valphine:** Was a target of [[valphine]]'s social manipulation, which contributed to his current interpersonal conflicts.

## Current Status
Pog is the partner of [[ulla]]. Their professional relationship has become publicly strained following a series of mutual accusations regarding shapeshifting, a conflict exacerbated by the adventuring party's presence. He remains stationed at the [[gnomengarde]] entrance, currently embroiled in this conflict of suspicion with [[ulla]]. The party's role in this destabilization remains unknown to [[gnomengarde]] leadership.
