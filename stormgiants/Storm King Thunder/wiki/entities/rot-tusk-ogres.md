# Rot-Tusk Ogre Clan

The [[rot-tusk-ogres]] are a unit of ogres that serve as the primary muscle for [[borg-the-hammer]] and [[the-carver]]'s organization. They have established themselves as long-term enemies of the adventuring party, with hostility cemented during earlier forest hunts.

## Combat Profile and Tactics
The clan is utilized as heavy combatants in orchestrated ambushes. Their combat effectiveness is demonstrated through coordinated strikes against their targets.

## Current Status and Location
The group currently occupies [[butterskull-ranch]]. They arrived at the location following an initial orc raid and subsequently seized the property, working in conjunction with [[borg-the-hammer]].

## Recent Activity
A warrior of the clan recently participated in an ambush alongside [[jax]]. During this engagement, the ogre was neutralized by the party's magic users and ultimately slain by [[brewbarry]].
