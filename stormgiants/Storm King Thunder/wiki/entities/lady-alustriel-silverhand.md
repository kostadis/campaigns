# Overview
This wiki covers the narrative universe described in the source document "Lady Alustriel Silverhand". The main focus is on the central figure, Lady Alustriel Silverhand, a former ruler of Silverymoon who is the mother of Lord Methrammar Aerasumé. The wiki includes pages on key entities like Lady Alustriel Silverhand, Lord Methrammar Aerasumé, and Silverymoon, as well as concepts such as legitimacy and the Seelie Court. Additionally, there are queries exploring Lady Alustriel's lineage and its impact on Silverymoon's governance.

## Key Entities
- **Lady Alustriel Silverhand**: Former ruler of Silverymoon; mother of Lord Methrammar Aerasumé
- **Lord Methrammar Aerasumé**: Son of Lady Alustriel Silverhand
- **Silverymoon**: City-state

## Key Concepts
- **Legitimacy**: The concept that Lady Alustriel's lineage supports Lord Methrammar's claim to rule Silverymoon
