# Milo Goodbarrel

[[milo-goodbarrel]] is a historical figure and a member of the [[order-of-the-gauntlet]]. He is recognized as a strike force leader known for his tactical prowess.

## Historical Significance
According to accounts from [[ser-kaelen]], Goodbarrel led a successful strike force during the [[war-of-the-dragons]]. His unit is credited with breaching an inner sanctum and successfully disrupting a significant ritual, an event often cited as evidence of the [[order-of-the-gauntlet]]'s martial effectiveness.

## Current Status
As of the current records, Goodbarrel has not been encountered in person by the party and remains a name-only entity used to establish historical context.
