# The Whiskered Gang

The [[whiskered-gang]] is a crew of wererats formerly based at the [[shrine-of-savras]]. Following displacement by orc and ogre forces, they have relocated to the mountain-toe-gold-mine.

## Current Role
Under the leadership of [[zeleen-varnaster]], the gang serves as the de facto security force and provides menial labor for the mining operations. Their presence is maintained through a formal handshake agreement with [[horia-stonetallow]], contingent on regular shipments of [[petunias-cheese]] and the provision of shelter.

## Organizational Traits
The gang prefers roles involving muscle and protection over the hard labor of actual mining. Their loyalty is considered [[conditional-trust]], as their primary motivation is the comfort and safety of their own kind rather than long-term alliance with the dwarves or the adventuring party.
