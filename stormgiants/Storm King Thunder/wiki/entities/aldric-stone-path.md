# Aldric Stone Path

A weathered mountain man and druid aligned with [[adabra-gwynn]] and the [[anti-interventionist-faction]]. 

## Profile
Aldric is characterized by intense personal loyalty rather than intellectual or political rigor. He has held anti-interventionist views for many years but was historically marginalized by the broader druidic community. His devotion to [[adabra-gwynn]] stems from the fact that she was the first to provide him with personal validation and a platform.

## Role in the Emerald Enclave Schism
During the [[druid-council]], Aldric served as a representative of the anti-interventionist stance. However, his presence proved to be a political liability. When [[soma]] challenged him to provide a concrete proposal for addressing planar anomalies and [[motivated-blight]], Aldric was unable to offer a substantive response.

His silence was used by [[the-presiding-druid]] as a legal and logical precedent to rule against [[adabra-gwynn]], arguing that the anti-interventionist logic would necessitate treating active threats as "perfectly normal" occurrences.

## Relationships
- **[[adabra-gwynn]]**: Deeply loyal; he views her as his primary advocate.
- **[[soma]]**: The intellectual challenger who exposed the practical weaknesses of his position.
- **[[the-presiding-druid]]**: The authority figure who utilized Aldric's failure to undermine the anti-interventionist faction.
