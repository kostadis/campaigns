# Tree Ghost Tribe
The Tree Ghost Tribe is a faction within the larger magical community, led by Boorvald Orcbane. They specialize in the recovery and preservation of artifacts, particularly those with significant historical or magical value. The tribe is known for their secretive nature and their involvement in various magical transactions.

## Key Members
- **Boorvald Orcbane**: The Great Chief of the Tree Ghost Tribe, a merchant specializing in magical goods for discerning magic users. Known to Unla Kee and associated with the Fishyard.
- **Young Barbarian Leader**: Son of Boorvald Orcbane, who arrived at the Grandfather Tree on behalf of the tribe.

## Notable Artifacts
- **Nonmagical Electrum Torc**: Recovered from the roots of the Grandfather Tree, this artifact bears Giant runes and was the nose-ring of a mighty hill giant chief.

## Relationships
- **With the 3HPP**: The party holds the tribe's artifact, the nonmagical electrum torc, which was recovered from the Grandfather Tree.
- **With Unla Kee**: Boorvald Orcbane is known to Unla Kee and is associated with the Fishyard.

## Current Status
- **Location**: Unknown.
- **Disposition**: The tribe's movements and intentions remain unclear.
- **Significance**: The tribe plays a crucial role in the recovery and preservation of magical artifacts, often acting as intermediaries between powerful factions and the wider magical community.

# Artifact Recovery
Artifact recovery refers to the process of locating, retrieving, and preserving significant magical artifacts. The Tree Ghost Tribe, led by Boorvald Orcbane, is known for its expertise in this field. The tribe has recovered several notable artifacts, including the nonmagical electrum torc from the roots of the Grandfather Tree.

## Key Events
- **Recovery of Nonmagical Electrum Torc**: The nonmagical electrum torc, bearing Giant runes and formerly the nose-ring of a mighty hill giant chief, was recovered from the roots of the Grandfather Tree.

## Significance
- **Historical Value**: These artifacts hold significant historical and magical value, often serving as symbols of power or connection to ancient times.
- **Community Role**: The Tree Ghost Tribe acts as intermediaries, facilitating the recovery of these artifacts for various factions within the magical community.
