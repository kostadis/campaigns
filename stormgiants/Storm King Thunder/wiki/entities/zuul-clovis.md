# Zuul Clovis

Zuul Clovis is a member or associate of the [[zhentarim]] who functions as a "referral contact" and intermediary. While he has not appeared in person, his existence provides a tangible link to the organization's internal recruitment protocols.

## Role & Status
He acts as a broker within the Zhentarim's hierarchy. His primary role is providing "vouching"—a form of social capital used to navigate the organization's layers of secrecy.

## Relationships
- **Orsik**: Zuul vouched for [[clarify-orsiks-identity-and-role-2026-06-06-063453]] to a Zhentarim agent, claiming Orsik possessed the "moral flexibility" required by the organization. The nature of their prior relationship remains unknown.
- **Zhentarim**: He possesses sufficient standing within the [[zhentarim]] to provide credible referrals that trigger direct contact from their agents.

## Narrative Significance
Zuul represents the concept of **brokerage**; he is a "hidden" actor whose influence is felt through the consequences of his actions (the referral) rather than direct interaction. His existence highlights the information asymmetry between the party and the underworld.
