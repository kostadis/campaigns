# Kaelen

**Kaelen** is a central collaborator and the creative partner of the demonic entity [[bob-saget]]. 

## Role and Influence
Kaelen is credited as the author of [[the-summoning-script]], the theatrical play that functions as a demonic summoning ritual. Bob credits Kaelen with his successful escape from the Abyss. Their relationship appears to be a genuine, ongoing creative partnership rather than one of simple exploitation, suggesting Kaelen may possess significant influence or knowledge regarding Abyssal summoning mechanics.
