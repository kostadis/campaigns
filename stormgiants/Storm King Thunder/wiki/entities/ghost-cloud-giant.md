# Ghost (Cloud Giant)
The Ghost (Cloud Giant) is a haunting spirit bound to the hexagonal oracle chamber within the Temple of Annam. Known to Unla Kee and associated with the Fishyard, he specializes in magical goods for discerning magic users. The ghost recounts his death, explaining the oracle's rules, and tasks the party with finding and killing Blagothkus, his father, to fulfill his unfinished business.

## Core Goal
- **Vengeance:** The ghost seeks to have his father, Blagothkus, killed to avenge his death.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goal:** Vengeance — he wants the party to kill his father, Blagothkus, who murdered him.
- **Carries resentment:** Despite carrying resentment toward his father, the ghost frames his death within the oracle's prophecy, suggesting he had some awareness of the conflict before it erupted.
- **Willing to cooperate:** The ghost is calm and informative in manner, willing to explain the oracle's mechanics in exchange for the party's cooperation.

## History with the Party
- **First and only meeting:** The ghost emerged from the oracle chamber floor and recounted his death. His father Blagothkus, devastated by the oracle's declaration that he could never earn the gods' favor, was further enraged when the oracle urged his son to step out from Blagothkus's "dark shadow." Overcome with despair and envy, Blagothkus slew his son on the spot.
- **Explains oracle's rules:** The ghost explained the oracle's rules to the party: six questions per day, each consuming one lantern light from the six giant statues; every question yields a true answer.
- **Tasked with quest:** The ghost tasked the party with killing Blagothkus, who has since retired to his castle in Parnast to mourn. He admits uncertainty about whether Parnast is a real location.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Bound to the oracle chamber in the Temple of Annam.
- **Active situation:** Waiting; his fate is tied to whether the party pursues Blagothkus.
- **Known facts:** His death, the oracle's mechanics, and the quest to find Blagothkus in Parnast.
- **Unknowns:** The nature of his binding to the chamber; whether killing Blagothkus would free or end him.

## Relationships
- **Blagothkus (father):** Killed by him in a moment of grief-fueled rage; the ghost harbors deep resentment and seeks his death.
- **The Party:** Cooperative; offered useful information in exchange for taking on his vengeance quest.
