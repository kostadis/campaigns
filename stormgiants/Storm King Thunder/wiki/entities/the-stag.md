# The Stag

A large, wild stag inhabiting the [[whispering-wood]]. It serves as a primary informant regarding the current state of the forest and the nature of the local magical disturbances.

## Characteristics
The Stag exhibits physical symptoms of [[planar-instability]], including:
- Shimmering and flickering fur.
- Constant shifting of antler shapes.
- Spontaneous short-distance teleportation and the leaving of afterimages.

## Personality & Behavior
While initially territorial and aggressive toward intruders, the Stag is capable of calm, [[transactional-communication]]. It demonstrates a pragmatic nature, willing to exchange information for tangible goods, such as mushrooms from [[gnomengarde]].

## Key Intelligence
- **Origin of Disturbances:** The Stag identifies the center of the [[whispering-wood]] as the source of the forest's "weirdness."
- **Failed Interventions:** It references "other nice people" (unidentified prior investigators) who promised to resolve the disturbances but failed to follow through.
