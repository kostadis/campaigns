# Harper Network

A secretive order dedicated to preserving balance, justice, and freedom across Faerûn. Once an ally of the party, they are now severed and hostile.

Orsik’s betrayal — the disclosure of two teleportation circles to the Zhentarim — destroyed all trust. The Harpers now view the party as compromised, dangerous, and beyond redemption. The coffer of spell scrolls from Krowen Valharrow remains in the party’s possession, but the connection is broken.

The Harper Network represents the ideal the party once claimed to uphold — and now abandoned. Their hostility is not a threat — it is a judgment.

The party’s rejection of the Harpers is not a tactical loss — it is a moral one. They have chosen the path of the Zhentarim: not through force, but through silence.
