# Elara Seasong Meliame

A wealthy human merchant, prominent noble of [[house-meliame]], and devout follower of Lathander. She serves as a primary connection between the party and the Meliame household, as well as a key witness to recent anomalous events.

## Profile
- **Role:** Art Collector / Merchant / Noble
- **Faction:** [[house-meliame]]
- **Affiliation:** Lathanderite (Conventional)
- **Status:** Active (Last seen at a gala in [[neverwinter]]; currently safe in [[phandalin]])

## Narrative Role & Intelligence
Elara is a central figure in ongoing political and investigative maneuvers:

### Political Asset
[[vukradin]] has identified her as a high-value asset due to her expertise in [[artifact-identification-unlocking]]. She is the intended recipient of a recovered lighthouse statue. Her familial connection to [[lord-cassian-meliame]] provides the necessary context and motivation for the party to engage with the upcoming gala in [[neverwinter]].

### Key Testimony
Elara served as a witness to an anomalous orcish attack on the road to the Tower of Storms. Her observations include:
- **Tactical Anomalies:** She reported that the attackers moved with "unnerving discipline," which is highly unusual for standard orcish raiding parties.
- **Visual Markers:** She noted strange markings on the attackers—described as either a "swirl" or an "eye shape"—though she experienced significant difficulty retaining a clear memory of these details.

## Political Context
Her family, [[house-meliame]], is currently being targeted by [[house-margaster]]. The delivery of the lighthouse statue is viewed not merely as a transaction, but as a high-stakes event where [[political-sabotage]] is a significant threat. [[house-margaster]] agents are actively attempting to intercept the artifact to cause public embarrassment to her house.

## Observations & Interactions
- **Theological Friction:** Elara expressed confusion regarding [[valphine]]'s characterization of Lathander as a "painful" deity, indicating a disconnect between her conventional faith and the emerging [[theology-of-searing-pain]].
- **Social Dynamics:** During her interaction with the party, she bypassed [[vukradin]] to pay [[valphine]] directly, causing mild friction regarding leadership and compensation.
- **Assistance Received:** She received practical aid from [[soma]], who mended her trade cart, and [[brewbarry]], who retrieved her lost horse.

## Relationships
- **[[lord-cassian-meliame]]**: Eccentric cousin and host of the Neverwinter gala.
- **[[house-margaster]]**: Hostile faction seeking to undermine her family.
- **[[ser-kaelen]]**: Intermediary who designated her as the rightful owner of the lighthouse statue.
- **[[vukradin]]**: Identified her as a key asset; experienced social friction regarding her payment methods.
- **[[valphine]]**: Recipient of her direct payment; subject of her theological confusion.
- **[[soma]]**: Provided assistance with her trade cart.
- **[[brewbarry]]**: Provided assistance in retrieving her lost horse.
