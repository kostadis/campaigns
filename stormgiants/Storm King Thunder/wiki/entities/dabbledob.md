# Dabbledob

**Dabbledob** (also known as [[Dabblebob]]) is an inventor, laboratory researcher, and caretaker residing in [[gnomengarde]]. She serves as a co-inventor in the Gnomengarde laboratory and is the primary professional rival to [[fibblestib]].

## Role & Research
Dabbledob is currently focused on finding a cure for the madness and hysteria affecting [[king-korboz]]. She works alongside [[fibblestib]] to manage the king's condition, though their partnership is defined by loud, constant arguments and competing design philosophies.

### Competing Methodologies
The rivalry between Dabbledob and [[fibblestib]] is most visible in their divergent approaches to medical and mechanical problems:

| Feature | Dabbledob's Approach | Fibblestib's Approach |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Medical Treatment** | Favors practical, restrictive methods (e.g., functional straitjacket design). | Advocates for proactive, technological solutions (e.g., the "Sanity Ray"). |
| **Philosophy** | [[evidence-based-pragmatism]]; favors proven solutions over speculation. | Speculative and often outlandish inventions. |

The two are also currently engaged in a dispute regarding the design of a tamper-proof voting box for the [[dual-monarchy]].

## Personality & Methodology
Dabbledob is characterized by her commitment to [[evidence-based-pragmatism]]. She favors practical, proven solutions over speculative or outlandish inventions. 

She is known to become deeply absorbed in intellectual debates, making her difficult to redirect once an argument has commenced. While generally skeptical, she demonstrates a reluctant openness to valid evidence, such as acknowledging the therapeutic potential of music when suggested by [[vukradin]].

## Political Views
Beyond her scientific work, Dabbledob is an active participant in the political discourse of [[gnomengarde]]. She holds specific views on [[gnomengarde-governance]], specifically advocating for an expansion of the number of kings to facilitate a votable majority, as opposed to the bicameral models proposed by others.

## Relationships
- **[[fibblestib]]**: Coworker and frequent intellectual opponent. Their relationship is defined by mutual dismissal of each other's more extreme inventions and political theories.
- **[[vukradin]]**: Maintains a mild positive rapport after conceding the merit of his musical treatment suggestion.
- **[[king-korboz]]**: Indirectly connected through her ongoing research and work regarding his medical treatment.
