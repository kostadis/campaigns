# The Cult Fanatic

An unnamed, highly zealous operative of the [[kraken-society]]. He was characterized by a cold, dismissive personality and a fanatical devotion to a "greater purpose" involving the city of [[neverwinter]].

## Combat and Abilities
- **Mental Compulsion:** The Fanatic utilized powerful mind control to command [[emerald-enclave]] members and target party members like [[brewbarry]]. This compulsion was tied to his life force and only broke upon his death.
- **Weaponry:** He wielded a **Black Coral Dagger**.

## Fate
The Fanatic was executed by [[valphine]]. Following his death, his head was taken by the [[talosian-orc-raiding-party]] as proof of their victory.
