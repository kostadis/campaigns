# Aletra Satora

Aletra Satora is a cold, calculating smuggler and high-level operative affiliated with [[house-sartorra]] (House Xorlarrin). She specializes in the illegal interdimensional trade of [[midnight-tears]] poison and has been identified as a primary catalyst for chaos within the ancient dwarven fortress.

## Profile
- **Role:** Interdimensional smuggler and operative.
- **Faction:** [[house-sartorra]] / House Xorlarrin poison operations.
- **Family:** Fourth daughter of the Satora family; younger sister of [[valphine]].
- **Distinguishing Features:** Sharp cheekbones, cold violet eyes, and a distinctive scent of lavender perfume mixed with chemical bleach.

## Personality & Tactics
Aletra is characterized by extreme pragmatism and ruthlessness, prioritizing profit above all else. She is known to manipulate local rulers and use both allies and enemies as disposable tools. 

In combat, she employs psychological warfare, particularly targeting her sister [[valphine]]. Her tactical ruthlessness was demonstrated when she marked [[brewbarry]] as a spatial decoy to redirect [[rift-weavers]].

## History & Operations
Aletra has a history of exploiting political tensions to further her smuggling interests. She famously exploited the jealousy of the dwarven fortress's King regarding the [[lionshield-coster]]'s multi-dimensional operations. To facilitate her agenda, she brokered a deal to introduce a [[malfunctioning-machine]] to the fortress under the guise of increasing mining efficiency.

When [[the-chief-accountant]] raised concerns regarding the resulting accounting discrepancies caused by the machine, Aletra reportedly mocked her and is believed to have committed murder to protect her operations.

## Current Status
Following the implosion of the machine and a confrontation in the machine room, Aletra escaped through a self-opened interdimensional portal. Her exact destination and the identity of her superior (possibly "KP") remain unknown.
