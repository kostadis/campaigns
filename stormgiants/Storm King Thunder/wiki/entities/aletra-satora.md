# Aletra Satora

Aletra Satora is a coldly efficient drow smuggler and high-level operative embedded within the interdimensional poison trade network of [[house-sartorra]] (affiliated with House [[xorlarrin]]). She represents the *technocratic smuggler* archetype: an operative driven not by rage or madness, but by bureaucratic precision, intellectual superiority, and institutional loyalty. Her existence demonstrates how the drow underworld has evolved into a sophisticated system of technological, psychological, and administrative warfare, often utilizing a layered command structure to maintain plausible deniability.

## Profile
Aletra is characterized by her sharp cheekbones, cold violet eyes, and a distinctive signature scent—lavender perfume mixed with chemical bleach—which marks her as a product of the Satora poison labs. She is the fourth daughter of the Satora family and the younger sister of [[valphine]].

## Personality & Tactics
Aletra is defined by extreme pragmatism and ruthlessness, prioritizing profit and organizational goals above all else. She utilizes dark humor and condescension as tactical tools, particularly when confronting her sister, whom she mocks for perceived incompetence (referencing their mother’s dismissal of her as "not the sharpest tool in the shed"). 

She treats both allies and enemies as disposable tools to further her objectives. This was evidenced by her use of [[brewbarry]] as a spatial decoy to redirect [[spatial-equilibrium]], and the reported murder of the [[4-npcs--20-the_chief_accountant--5h6g2f]] after the latter raised concerns regarding financial discrepancies caused by Aletra's operations.

## History & Operations
Aletra specializes in the illegal interdimensional trade of [[midnight-tears]] poison and has been identified as a primary catalyst for chaos within an ancient dwarven fortress. She is known to exploit political tensions and local grievances; specifically, she exploited the jealousy of the fortress's King regarding the [[lionshield-coster]]'s multi-dimensional operations. To facilitate her agenda, she brokered a deal to introduce a [[malfunctioning-machine]] into the fortress under the guise of increasing mining efficiency.

She is highly proficient with the [[model-1489-spatial-rectification-calliope]], a dwarven-engineered planar device repurposed for smuggling. She possesses the technical knowledge to operate such machinery even under duress.

## Current Status
Following an implosion in the machine room and a subsequent confrontation, Aletra escaped through a self-generated interdimensional portal. Her exact destination remains unknown; however, her actions suggest she is not a rogue actor but a managed operative whose movements are overseen by a superior known as "KP."
