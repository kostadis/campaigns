# Aletra Satora

Aletra Satora is a coldly efficient drow smuggler and operative embedded within the interdimensional poison trade network of House Sartorra (affiliated with House Xorlarrin). She is the fourth daughter of the Satora family and the younger sister of party member Valphine. Aletra operates with institutional backing, leveraging advanced planar technology to move contraband across dimensions while maintaining plausible deniability through a layered command structure.

She is defined by her professional ruthlessness, intellectual superiority, and weaponized charm. Aletra uses dark humor and condescension as tactical tools, particularly when confronting Valphine, whom she openly mocks for perceived incompetence — referencing their mother’s dismissal of her as “not the sharpest tool in the shed.” She has no qualms about sacrificing allies, as demonstrated by her use of Brewbarry as a sacrificial decoy, nor about eliminating obstacles, as implied by the murder of the Chief Accountant.

Aletra’s signature scent — lavender perfume mixed with chemical bleach — marks her as a product of the Satora poison labs. She is highly proficient with the [[model-1489-spatial-rectification-calliope]], a dwarven-engineered planar device repurposed for smuggling, and possesses the technical knowledge to operate it under duress. Her escape through a self-generated portal, after dropping several vials of [[midnight-tears]], suggests she is not a rogue actor but a managed operative — her failure may have been intentional, or at least tolerated by her unseen superior, “KP.”

Aletra represents a new archetype in the campaign: the *technocratic smuggler*. She is not driven by rage or madness, but by bureaucratic precision and institutional loyalty. Her existence proves that the drow underworld has evolved beyond brute force into a system of technological, psychological, and administrative warfare.

**Last Known Status:** Escaped through an interdimensional portal after a botched extraction. Destination and current whereabouts unknown.
