# Lathanderian Practice (Valphine)

An emerging religious movement led by [[valphine]]. 

The practice is currently in its formative stages, attracting followers like [[prutha]] who seek the perceived power and stability of Lathander. The movement's specific rituals and theological nuances are still evolving, potentially incorporating elements from its followers' backgrounds, such as the "bone whistle" tradition mentioned by [[prutha]].
