# Cooragh Struckt

Cooragh Struckt is the cook at the [[wayside-inn]], known only for the quality of her food. She has never spoken to the party, revealed no personal history, and holds no known relationships beyond Valphine’s appreciation of her cooking. 

Her significance lies not in action or dialogue, but in sensory impact: her alarmingly well-spiced stews and meat pies provided a moment of emotional comfort to Valphine, a character defined by trauma and familial estrangement. This makes her a prototype of the [[quietly-resonant-npc]] — an entity with no plot function, yet profound emotional resonance.

She embodies [[sensory-driven-characterization]], where character is conveyed through taste, aroma, and texture rather than exposition. Her existence reinforces [[narrative-economy]]: she is minimal, yet memorable.

No hidden role, magical influence, or connection to interdimensional poisons has been confirmed.
