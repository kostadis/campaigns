# Boney

**Boney** (formerly known as **Barney**) is an undead skeletal horse serving as a loyal sidekick, companion, and mount to the adventuring party.

## Identity & Personality
Boney is characterized by a cheerful, good-natured disposition and a penchant for self-deprecating humor regarding his skeletal state. Despite his grim appearance, he is a source of emotional levity for the group. He is extremely loyal, a trait stemming from his previous life in service to [[lady-alagondar]].

There is an ongoing debate regarding his moral alignment; while he appears helpful and kind, [[soma]] has noted a "questionable" moral standing, particularly regarding his perceived passivity during violent events.

## History
* **Discovery:** Boney was discovered in a southern niche of the [[dragonbarrow]] tunnels. He was originally a living horse named Barney, serving [[lady-alagondar]], but was killed by malevolent [[will-o-wisps]] before he could complete his duties.
* **Reincarnation Misidentification:** Upon being reanimated by the party's presence, Boney initially mistook [[soma]] for the reincarnation of [[lady-alagondar]]. The party successfully convinced him of this identity, securing his immediate loyalty.
* **Intelligence & Utility:** Boney has proven to be a valuable asset, providing critical directional intelligence that led the party to the Dragon Slayer Sword.
* **The Death of Corbin:** Boney was present during the moment [[sister-kaella]] killed [[corbin]]. His lack of intervention during this event has caused interpersonal friction, specifically with [[soma]].

## Combat Status & Utility
* **Role:** Active party companion and mount. He serves as a tactical companion to [[soma]], who actively manages and protects him during combat.
* **Tactical Use:** [[soma]] utilizes Boney as a mobile asset to reposition herself and maintain tactical advantages.
* **Fragility:** Boney is highly fragile, possessing only 13 HP.
