# Laela

[[laela]] is a fey-dragon hybrid and a key figure in the personal narrative of [[thistle-wendrod]].

## Nature and Origin
Laela is the result of [[hybridization-experiments]] conducted by [[iymrith]]. According to accounts from [[sandesyl-morgia]], these experiments were intended to create a fey-hybrid dragon. The process was described as being more akin to lycanthropy than true fusion, causing the subject to undergo uncontrolled transformations between two creature forms.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Caged within [[iymrith]]'s lair.
- **Disposition:** 
    - Friendly to [[thistle-wendrod]]
    - Hostile to [[vardis]]
    - Resistant to [[iymrith]]
- **Narrative Role:** Her survival serves as a primary emotional driver for the current phase of the campaign.

## Relationships
- **[[thistle-wendrod]]:** Identified as the "beloved" and love interest of [[thistle-wendrod]].
- **[[iymrith]]:** Currently held captive in her lair.
- **[[iymrith]]:** The architect of her hybridization.
