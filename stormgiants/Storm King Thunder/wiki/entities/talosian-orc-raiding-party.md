# Talosian Orc Raiding Party

A group of orcs conducting raids within the forest, guided by the religious authority of the [[talos]].

## Composition & Leadership
The party is not merely a band of marauders but is organized around the worship of [[talos]]. The [[the-anchorite-half-orc]] serves as their embedded spiritual leader, suggesting that religious zealotry is a primary driver for their raiding activities.

## Behavior
The group operates with a level of religious purpose, utilizing the chaos of raids to further the interests of their deity.
