# Kolbaz
A proud, old-guard Harper who believes Harper membership should carry weight and meaning. He openly contemptuous of Krowen's use of non-sworn contractors. A member of the Harper faction, Kolbaz served as a regional agent and leader of the Harper headquarters in Yartar. He took personal and professional pride in his illusion work and his history of active service, including combat at the Well of Dragons against the Cult of the Dragon. His death was mysterious, struck by a bolt of lightning and collapsed, a small, unidentified figure standing over him and speaking in an unnaturally large voice. Thistle's examination of his papers revealed that his research into Adimond Kroskas was the proximate cause of his death. The papers contained a critical discovery: **Adimond Kroskas is an alias — he is not who he claims to be.** The physical damage to his chambers suggested something massive materialized in the room, destroyed everything, and dematerialized. Gauche knew that other individuals had come through the teleportation circle around the time of death but withheld this from the party.

## Relationships
- **Krowen:** His Harper superior; held a professional grievance over Krowen's policy of using non-sworn contractors.
- **Thistle:** Brief but pointed interactions — mutual friction over the Cult of the Dragon and Seelie Court culture; Thistle was the one who examined his papers after death.
- **Adimond Kroskas:** His investigation into Kroskas's true identity was almost certainly the direct cause of his death.
- **Vardis:** Vardis recalled Kolbaz's death and the chamber damage, connecting it to Adimond and identifying it as consistent with a **Blue Dragon**.
- **Gauche:** Knew details about activity near the teleportation circle at the time of death; withheld this from the party.

## Current Status
- **Deceased.**
- His significance is now primarily as a victim and a source of intelligence; his research into Adimond Kroskas appears to have gotten him killed.

## What the Party Knows vs. What Remains Hidden
- **Knows:** Kolbaz is dead; his research exposed Adimond Kroskas as using an alias; the damage is anomalous and violent.
- **Hidden from party (at time of discovery):** Gauche knew other individuals came through the teleportation circle around the time of death and deliberately withheld this.
