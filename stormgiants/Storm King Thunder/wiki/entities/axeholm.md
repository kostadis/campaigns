# Axeholm

[[axeholm]] is a dwarven stronghold and fortress characterized by its grand pillared halls and limestone thrones. Historically a seat of dwarven rule, the location has been fundamentally transformed by a planar anomaly of unknown origin and nature.

## The Planar Anomaly and Corruption
The stronghold has fallen under the influence of [[interdimensional-corruption]], which has turned the fortress into a hostile environment. This anomaly is responsible for the transformation of the original dwarven population into grotesque, feral monstrosities. These beings act as creatures of compulsion rather than traditional tactical combatants.

A notable phenomenon within the fortress is an **unnatural light** produced by the anomaly. Through this light, the [[out-of-phase-dwarves]] are able to materialize and increase their numbers during combat.

## Inhabitants
The corruption has profoundly affected the inhabitants and their leadership:
* **[[the-dwarven-king-of-axeholm]]:** The former ruler of the stronghold, who has been transformed into a feral monstrosity.
* **[[out-of-phase-dwarves]]:** The transformed population that utilizes the fortress's unnatural light to manifest during conflict.
