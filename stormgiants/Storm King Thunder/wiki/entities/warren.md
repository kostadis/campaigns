# Warren

Warren is one of two missing gnome inventors from Gnomengarde, alongside Orrin. Their disappearance is cited by Fibblestib as evidence of a shapechanger operating within the enclave, possibly linked to King Korboz’s madness. Warren was a member of the royal inventor guild and likely worked in proximity to Fibblestib and Dabbledob.

No further details about his personality, role, or fate are known. His absence is used by Fibblestib as a narrative device to justify the urgency of his Sanity Ray project and to invite the party’s involvement as external observers. His disappearance remains unexplained, and his status as alive, dead, or transformed is unknown.

Warren’s absence exemplifies [[narrative-economy]]: he is not a character with an arc, but a catalyst — a missing piece that deepens the mystery of Gnomengarde’s instability without requiring resolution.
