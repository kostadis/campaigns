# Skippy

A low-level, expendable brigand belonging to the-carver's road crew.

## Identity
- **Role:** Low-level brigand; member of the-carver's disposable road crew.
- **Faction:** the-carver's organization (expendable tier).
- **Status:** Currently in the custody of corbin at a remote camp.

## Personality & Motivations
Skippy demonstrates no ideological loyalty to the-carver or his criminal enterprise. He is motivated entirely by self-preservation and surrendered all known intelligence immediately upon being surrounded by the adventuring party during interrogation.

## History with the Party
During a road ambush near [[butterskull-ranch]], Skippy was rendered unconscious by [[vukradin]]'s magic. Following his interrogation, [[vukradin]] advocated for his life, leading to corbin volunteering to guard him. This has created a point of tension, as [[vukradin]] expressed concern that corbin might act on vengeful impulses.

## Intelligence Provided
Through interrogation, Skippy revealed:
- The ambush at [[butterskull-ranch]] was a deliberate act of [[quest-baiting]] designed to lure adventurers into a trap via [[harbin-wester]]'s bounty board.
- [[borg-the-hammer]] was the leader of the ranch ambush.
- [[sister-kaella]] serves as the Spymaster and manages the-carver's spy network in [[phandalin]].
