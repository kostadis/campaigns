# Xorlarrin

House Xorlarrin is one of the most powerful and influential drow noble houses in the Underdark, known for its mastery of arcane commerce, planar trade, and political infiltration. Traditionally associated with wealth, espionage, and magical innovation, the house has expanded its operations into the interdimensional drug trade through its affiliation with or absorption of [[house-sartorra]].

Aletra Satora’s operations — including the smuggling of [[midnight-tears]] via the [[model-1489-spatial-rectification-calliope]] — are now understood to be part of a broader Xorlarrin initiative to monopolize planar contraband. The house’s involvement is implied by the use of Xorlarrin-marked parchment in Aletra’s quarters and the institutional nature of her smuggling network.

While House Sartorra may operate as a semi-autonomous faction, its access to advanced dwarven planar technology and its integration with Xorlarrin’s administrative infrastructure suggest a hierarchical relationship. Xorlarrin does not directly manage field operatives like Aletra — it maintains control through obscured authorities like “KP,” ensuring deniability while reaping the profits.

**Role:** Parent or overseeing organization in the interdimensional poison trade

- Interdimensional smuggling networks
