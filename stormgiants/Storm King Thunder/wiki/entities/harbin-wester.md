# Harbin Wester

Harbin Wester is the **Townmaster and Lord Mayor of Phandalin**. He operates out of a stone hall with a bell tower and serves as the local administrator under the regional authority of [[lord-neverember]].

## Personality and Tactics
Harbin is characterized as a "survivalist bureaucrat." While not a traditional villain, he is motivated by self-preservation, minor personal profit, and the maintenance of his political title.

### Key Behavioral Patterns
* **Cowardice and Self-Protection:** He frequently avoids direct confrontation, famously communicating through locked doors to avoid perceived threats (such as dragons).
* **Proceduralism:** He uses bureaucracy, agendas, and "proper process" as a shield to stall political pressure and deflect accountability.
* **Low-grade Corruption:** Harbin engages in private, fee-for-paperwork deals (graft), often framing these transactions as "standard practice" or "community donations" to avoid scrutiny.
* **Narrative Control:** A highly skilled political actor, Harbin orchestrates public celebrations and speeches to link his name to the party's victories, ensuring he receives credit for their achievements before independent narratives can form.

## Political Standing
Initially ruling Phandalin unilaterally, Harbin was pressured by the adventuring party and members of the [[emerald-enclave]] (notably [[adabra-gwynn]]) into a power-sharing agreement. This led to the formation of the [[privy-council-phandalin]], where he serves as the head of a governing body alongside [[vukradin]], [[adabra-gwynn]], and others.

Currently, Harbin remains the Townmaster but operates with significantly reduced unilateral authority due to the [[privy-council-phandalin]]. He is focused on post-dragon recovery and consolidating his political position through orchestrated public events.

## Relationships
* **[[lord-neverember]]**: Harbin's superior; he frequently invokes Neverember's authority to justify his own decisions and shield himself from local demands.
* **[[vukradin]]**: An adversarial relationship. Harbin has been intimidated by [[vukradin]] and has faced moral critiques from him, though he now publicly praises [[vukradin]] as a tactical maneuver.
* **[[tibor-wester]]**: Harbin's brother. Tibor is involved in a "wildcat" logging operation at the [[logger-camp]]. Harbin has been known to post supply delivery quests on behalf of Tibor, serving as an indirect link between the party and the camp.
* **[[valphine]]**: Has analytically identified Harbin's [[narrative-control]] tactics.
* **[[adabra-gwynn]]**: A member of the [[emerald-enclave]] who helped pressure Harbin into the power-sharing agreement.
* **[[shrimpy]]**: A prisoner currently held in Harbin's custody.
