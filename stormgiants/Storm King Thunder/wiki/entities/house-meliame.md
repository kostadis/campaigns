# House Meliame

House Meliame is a noble family of [[neverwinter]] known for its deep investment in cultural heritage, art collection, and historical preservation. Unlike more militaristic or economically dominant houses, Meliame’s prestige derives from its role as a steward of symbolic artifacts and ancestral legacy. The house is based in Neverwinter and is deeply embedded in its high-society fabric, participating in extravagant events such as the gala hosted by Lord Cassian.

## Notable Members
- [[lord-cassian-meliame]]
- [[elara-seasong-meliame]]

## Overview

House Meliame’s influence is quiet but profound. Its members are not warlords or merchants, but curators—making them targets not for conquest, but for erasure. Their survival depends on the integrity of their cultural narrative, which is now under siege.

Elara Seasong Meliame, its most prominent public figure, exemplifies this ethos through her personal collection and advocacy for cultural restitution. The house’s holdings include significant ancestral artifacts, most notably the lighthouse statue—a key symbol tied to the Meliame lineage. The statue’s recovery and return are not mere transactions but ritual acts of honor, reinforcing the concept of [[delivery-as-political-act]].

The house is currently under targeted pressure from [[house-margaster]], which seeks to humiliate it by intercepting the recovered lighthouse statue. This attack is calculated, exploiting Meliame’s vulnerability to symbolic loss. The targeting of cultural artifacts like the statue reflects a broader strategy of [[symbolic-targeting-of-cultural-artifacts]], aiming to dismantle the house’s legacy rather than its power.

House Meliame provides a thematic parallel to other noble entities like House Margaster, contrasting quiet cultural stewardship with overt dominance. While specific political standing and history remain partially unrecorded, its role as a cultural patron is undeniable—and increasingly contested.
