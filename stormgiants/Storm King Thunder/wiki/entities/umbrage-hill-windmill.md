# Umbrage Hill Windmill

Adabra Gwynn’s home and apothecary workshop, located on land she holds a deed to near Phandalin. A secluded, fortified structure surrounded by dwarven cairns and wild herbs.

She barricaded herself inside during a manticore siege, refusing to leave until Soma and Vukradin convinced her they were “fellow naturalists.” It was here she first introduced her philosophy to the party, offering them food, shelter, and an enhanced healing potion.

The windmill symbolizes her isolation: a place of quiet labor, deep knowledge, and stubborn conviction. It is not a fortress against monsters — but against the world’s interference. She sees herself as the grove’s last guardian, and the windmill as her altar.

It remains her spiritual anchor, even after her defeat at the Enclave council. Whether she has returned there, or abandoned it for a new base, is unknown.
