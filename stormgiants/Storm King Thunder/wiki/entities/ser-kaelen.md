# Ser Kaelen

**Ser Kaelen** (also known as **Ser Kaelen Thorn** or **Thorne**) is a knight, recruiter, and field operative for the [[order-of-the-gauntlet]]. He is also a member of the [[phandalin-privy-council]].

## Profile
- **Appearance:** A man in his early thirties with dark hair, a clean-shaven face, and a scar above his right eyebrow. He wears chain mail armor bearing the insignia of the [[order-of-the-gauntlet]].
- **Personality:** Pragmatic, politically calculating, and composed. He is noted for his ability to read people and his tendency to use "linguistic mirroring" to build rapport or exert control. While he can show genuine conviction—such as when defending [[soma]]'s right to [[merils-staff]]—his actions are consistently framed around the benefit and reputation of his institution.

## Affiliations & Roles
- **[[order-of-the-gauntlet]]:** Acts as a recruiter and field operative. He seeks to advance the Order's influence, funding, and reputation.
- **[[phandalin-privy-council]]:** Serves as a governing member in Phandalin.
- **Suspected Ties:** There are ongoing suspicions, raised by [[jenna-roscoe]], regarding his financial connections to [[house-margaster]].

## Key Interactions
- **[[vukradin]]:** Kaelen recruited [[vukradin]] into the Order, leveraging the bard's celestial origins and musical talent. Their relationship is marked by a tension between professional cooperation and mutual suspicion, particularly regarding Kaelen's use of [[linguistic-mirroring]].
- **[[soma]]:** Although he views her naturalist worldview as incompatible with the Order, Kaelen has provided her with critical intelligence regarding [[adabra-gwynn]]'s political maneuvers. However, his "careful and calculating" reaction to her retention of [[merils-staff]] suggests he views the artifact as a significant political variable.
- **[[adabra-gwynn]]:** Kaelen has provided intelligence on her attempts to use [[reckless-interventionism]] as a political weapon within the [[emerald-enclave]].

## Historical Context
Kaelen cites [[milo-goodbarrel]] as a legendary figure within the Order who led the strike force during the [[war-of-the-dragons]]. He has noted that the Order is currently stretched thin due to successive historical conflicts.
