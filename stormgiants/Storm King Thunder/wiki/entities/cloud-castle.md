# Cloud Castle

A floating, magically sustained fortress once controlled by Escarlotta, the spectral wife of the executed cloud giant Blagothkus. Now owned and operated by the party, it serves as their primary base of operations and treasury.

The castle is under the authority of Escarlotta, who has transitioned from antagonist to reluctant partner. Most of its functions — particularly teleportation — are restricted by the Kraken Society’s Forbiddance, a magical curse that prevents escape or summoning.

The castle is not merely a location — it is a symbol of the party’s new identity. It represents power, isolation, and responsibility. Its walls hold 75,000 GP in treasure, but also the weight of their moral compromises.

It is a [[castle-as-character]]: its corridors echo with Escarlotta’s grief, its halls are haunted by Blagothkus’s guilt, and its magic is bound by ancient curses. To live in it is to be reminded that power without wisdom is a prison.

The party’s refusal to use its full potential — to avoid teleportation, to hide from the Kraken Society — reflects their [[collective-rejection-of-manipulation]]: they will not use the tools of control, even when they possess them.
