# The Bob Saget Incident

A significant narrative pivot occurring in Silverymoon during a play production. A ritual intended by [[kaelen-sarssir]] to summon a demon instead resulted in the accidental summoning of [[bob-saget]]. 

The incident led to the destruction of the Silverymoon theater to hide evidence, the trapping of ~200 citizens in a pocket dimension, and the eventual transformation of [[bob-saget]] from a comedic accident into a strategic [[planar-ally]] for the party.
