# Lionshield Coster

The [[lionshield-coster]] is a merchant organization with a significant presence in the region, including an outpost in [[phandalin]] managed by [[linene-graywind]].

## Organizational Profile

While appearing to be a standard merchant guild, intelligence suggests the organization has a specialized and potentially clandestine agenda.

### Potential Agenda: Interplanar Commerce

The organization's leadership (referred to locally as "the bosses") appears to prioritize the acquisition of goods linked to [[the-1489-treaty]]. This interest is evidenced by:

- Willingness to pay extreme premiums for items with planar provenance.
- Specific procurement directives regarding magical or extra-planar phenomena.
- Potential connections to defunct or specialized [[interplanar-shipping-technology]].

### Economic Status

The organization's logistics and supply chains are currently suffering from significant disruptions caused by "tariffs" and the ongoing [[war-of-the-giants]], making the acquisition of magical items like scrolls increasingly difficult.
