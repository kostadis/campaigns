# Lionshield Coster

Lionshield Coster is a merchant organization headquartered in Phandalin, operating as a node in a broader, hidden network of institutional interplanar commerce. Unlike criminal smuggling rings such as House Sartorra, Lionshield Coster functions under the veneer of legitimate trade, specializing in the acquisition and distribution of goods with planar associations.

The organization is structured around a layered command system with unnamed, distant leadership referred to as “bosses.” These figures set acquisition priorities — particularly for items resonant with planar phenomena — and maintain plausible deniability through operational obscurity. This structure extends the concept of [[organizational-secrecy-via-bosses]] into the commercial sphere, mirroring the SEER’s use of Azam as a public face while operating under Hsing’s unseen authority.

Lionshield Coster pays premium prices for goods tied to interplanar resonance — such as orc armor, pelts from planar-affected regions, and artifacts recovered from disrupted zones — reflecting [[interplanar-commercial-interests]]. This pricing strategy signals a high-demand, low-supply market for planar commodities, distinct from magical item trading and more aligned with the extraction of cosmic byproducts.

The group’s supply chains have been severely disrupted by the [[supply-chain-disruption-by-planar-conflict]] following the War of the Giants, which has introduced “tariffs” — likely bureaucratic, planar, or metaphysical barriers — that prevent reliable shipment of goods like scrolls. This confirms that even legitimate commercial networks are vulnerable to cosmological collapse, extending the consequences of [[shattering-of-the-ordning]] into the economic domain.

Lionshield Coster’s operations exemplify [[cover-identity-as-merchant]] not as deception, but as specialization: the merchant identity is not a disguise, but a legitimate framework for handling goods whose value derives from their origin in unstable planar zones. This distinguishes it from [[interdimensional-smuggling]], which is illicit and covert; Lionshield operates in the gray zone — legal, institutional, and quietly powerful.

The organization’s connection to the defunct interplanar shipping device referenced by Valphine remains unconfirmed, but its existence suggests Lionshield Coster may be a surviving fragment of a once-widespread commercial infrastructure for planar logistics — now reduced to scattered outposts like Linene’s in Phandalin.

Lionshield Coster does not seek to dominate or destroy. It seeks to *acquire*, *store*, and *redirect*. Its power lies not in force, but in access — and its silence is its strategy.

## Organizational Profile

The [[lionshield-coster]] is a merchant organization with a significant presence in the region, including an outpost in [[phandalin]] managed by [[linene-graywind]].

### Potential Agenda: Interplanar Commerce

The organization's leadership (referred to locally as "the bosses") appears to prioritize the acquisition of goods linked to [[the-1489-treaty]]. This interest is evidenced by:

- Willingness to pay extreme premiums for items with planar provenance.
- Specific procurement directives regarding magical or extra-planar phenomena.
- Potential connections to defunct or specialized [[interplanar-shipping-technology-2026-05-18]].

### Economic Status

The organization's logistics and supply chains are currently suffering from significant disruptions caused by "tariffs" and the ongoing [[war-of-the-giants]], making the acquisition of magical items like scrolls increasingly difficult.
