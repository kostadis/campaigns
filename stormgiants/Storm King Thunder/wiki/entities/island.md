# Iskander
Former inner circle member of the Cult of the Dragon; defector. Faction affiliation: Cult of the Dragon (former); Lord's Alliance (informant).

First appearance: Referenced in Sandesyl's account of the tome's history; has not appeared directly.

Personality & Motivations: No direct interaction recorded; personality not yet established at the table.

History with the Party: Referenced by Sandesyl as the inner circle member who betrayed Severin and the Cult of the Dragon to the Lord's Alliance. His intelligence enabled the Heroes of the War of the Dragon to raid Xonathal's tower. The raid secured the Blue Dragon mask but the tome Sandesyl seeks was not recovered.

Current Status: Last known location: Unknown; not yet established in play. The party knows only what Sandesyl disclosed — that he was a cult traitor who facilitated the Xonathal's tower raid. What remains hidden: His current whereabouts, fate, and motivations for betraying Severin are unknown.

Relationships: 
- **Severin**: Betrayed him and the Cult of the Dragon.
- **Lord's Alliance**: Served as an informant.
- **Sandesyl**: Views him as connected to the lost tome's history; tone of reference not yet specified.
- **The Party**: No direct relationship established.

Arc Score Events: No arc score events recorded.
