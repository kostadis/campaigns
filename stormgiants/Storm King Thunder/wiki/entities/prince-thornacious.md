# Prince Thornacious

Ruler of the Seelie Court, Prince Thornacious is a figure of quiet authority whose governance blends ceremonial tradition with pragmatic tolerance of dysfunction. He appointed Blasius as Chief of Staff — a role Blasius loathed — and later reinstated him as personal Satyr after Blasius cleared Oblivillish’s name.

His decision to reinstate Blasius is not an act of forgiveness, but containment: keeping a volatile, self-serving courtier close to prevent further disruption. He permits Blasius’s drunkenness, avoidance, and manipulations — not because he endorses them, but because Blasius remains useful.

Prince Thornacious wields authority through ritual and title, exemplified by his ability to command truth via formal recantation and his use of the [[fey-portal-bypass]] to summon the party. He is not a divine figure, but a sovereign whose power derives from institutional legitimacy — a parallel to the [[divine-oracle]]’s constrained truth-telling, but rooted in fey hierarchy rather than divine will.

His relationship with Blasius reveals a core theme of the Seelie Court: order is maintained not by virtue, but by strategic tolerance of chaos.
