# Urgala

A member of the adventuring party whose deepest personal grief was revealed by the Oracle of the Eye of the World: his wife fell to her death in a well that led to the Underdark. The revelation was not asked for, suggesting the Oracle may interpret intent or respond to unspoken questions — a potential exception to its six-question limit.

This disclosure has left Urgala emotionally unmoored, with no recorded reaction from him in session logs. The Oracle’s choice to reveal this secret — whether as mercy, test, or warning — remains unresolved. Urgala’s arc now hinges on how he processes this knowledge: whether he seeks closure, vengeance, or silence. His trauma is now a narrative thread tied to divine truth and the cost of revelation.
