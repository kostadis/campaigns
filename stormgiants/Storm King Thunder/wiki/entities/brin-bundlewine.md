# Brin Bundlewine

Brin Bundlewine is a villager and laborer in [[phandalin]] who has become a fervent, radicalized devotee of the unorthodox religious movement led by [[valphine]].

## Identity & Personality
Brin is an aspiring chapel builder characterized by an intense, almost ecstatic religious zeal. He is a pale-skinned individual who bears heavy sunburns and scars—physical evidence of his devotion to the "purifying pain of the sun." He demonstrates a "beatific radiance" and a gleeful, uncritical acceptance of extreme religious phenomena, including ritual blinding and the public display of severed heads.

## Religious Devotion
Brin is a central figure in the practical application of [[lathanderian-practice-valphine]]. His worship involves:
- **Self-flagellation:** Used as a method of spiritual discipline.
- **Ritual burning:** Acts of devotion that leave visible marks on his skin.
- **Theology of Searing Pain:** A belief in the purifying nature of solar heat and physical suffering.

## Current Activities
Brin is currently managing the renovation of [[tresendar-manor]] to serve as the [[temple-of-lathanders-searing-pain-of-justice]]. He has entered into a low-cost labor agreement, providing his services for free and requesting only 25 gp for materials. He frequently conducts public religious parades and fundraising activities alongside [[giles-slipper-shine]].

## Relationships
- [[valphine]]: Views her as a patron and a true believer in her ministry; works as a contractor for her religious cause.
- [[giles-slipper-shine]]: A close associate and fellow construction worker/religious enthusiast.
