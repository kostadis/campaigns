# Circle of Thunder

The [[circle-of-thunder]] is an orcish faction associated with the worship of Talos. 

The group is characterized by [[talosian-devotion]] and has been involved in violent skirmishes, including the recent battle at the [[circle-of-thunder]] location. The faction's history includes the targeted killing of clan shamans to break resistance and force adherence to Talosian tenets. prutha is a notable defector from this group.
