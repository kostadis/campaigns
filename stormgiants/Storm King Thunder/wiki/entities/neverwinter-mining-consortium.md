# Neverwinter Mining Consortium

The Neverwinter Mining Consortium is a corporate entity engaged in mineral extraction across the Sword Coast, with a significant stake in the Mountain’s Toe Gold Mine. It operates through intermediaries and external contractors, using adventurers like [[falcon-the-hunter]] as scouts, consultants, and risk-takers in exchange for discounted shares in profitable ventures.

Falcon’s role was to identify threats, secure access routes, and provide intelligence — effectively functioning as an [[employer-proxy-operation]]. He was not a miner, but a facilitator, trading his experience for financial security. The consortium’s hidden agenda is implied by the poisoning of the mine’s waters — a sabotage that threatens Falcon’s investment and may be linked to rival noble houses like [[house-meliame]] or [[house-margaster]].

The consortium’s structure mirrors the layered command systems of [[azam]]/[[hsing]] and [[aletra-satora]]/[[kp]]: Falcon is the visible face, but the true power and motives remain obscured. Its existence reveals how the adventuring economy is co-opted by institutional interests, turning heroes into expendable assets.
