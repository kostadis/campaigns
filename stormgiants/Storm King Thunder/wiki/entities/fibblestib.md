# Fibblestib

[[fibblestib]] (also known as **Fiddlestib**) is an inventor, researcher, and caretaker residing in [[gnomengarde]]. As the chief inventor operating out of the [[gnomengarde]] laboratory and a member of the [[gnomengarde-inventors-guild]], he is a central figure in the current efforts to stabilize the [[dual-monarchy]].

## Personality & Methodology
Fibblestib is highly eccentric, prideful, and intensely competitive. He views scientific experimentation as a primary means to solve both medical and political crises. His methodology is characterized by:

*   **Speculative Approach:** He has a penchant for outlandish, speculative, and often unproven technological proposals. This stands in stark contrast to the [[evidence-based-pragmatism]] practiced by [[dabbledob]].
*   **Clinical Detachment:** He shows little regard for biological ethics. His enthusiasm for harvesting mimic entrails to create mechanical constructs is a notable example of this trait.
*   **Prioritization:** He tends to dismiss external threats (such as dragons) in favor of addressing internal crises, specifically the mental state of [[king-korboz]].

## Current Projects & Research
Fibblestib's work spans medical, political, and mechanical engineering domains:

| Project | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **The Sanity Ray** | A proactive technological device intended to cure the King's madness; serves as a direct rival to [[dabbledob]]'s more traditional "straitjacket" approach. |
| **Mimic-derived Constructs** | Research into utilizing biological components (such as mimic entrails) for mechanical engineering. |
| **Tamper-proof Voting Box** | A project driven by his interest in political reform and [[gnomengarde-governance]]. |
| **Rotating Blade Inventions** | Various claims and designs regarding rotating blade technology. |

## Relationships

### Professional & Intellectual
*   **[[dabbledob]]**: Coworker, co-inventor, and primary intellectual antagonist. The two are frequently locked in loud, constant, and heated debates regarding both scientific methodology and [[gnomengarde-governance]].
*   **[[gnomengarde-inventors-guild]]**: His professional organization.

### Medical & Political
*   **[[king-korboz]]**: The primary subject of his current medical research and caretaking efforts; Fibblestib works alongside [[dabbledob]] to manage the king's madness and hysteria.
*   **[[vukradin]]**: Engaged in constructive dialogue regarding musical therapy, though Fibblestib mocked the party regarding the [[rotating-blade-trap]].

### Others
*   **[[soma]]**: Fibblestib has caused significant moral distress to [[soma]] due to his views on biological harvesting.
