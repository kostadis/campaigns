# Stonetallow Clan

The [[stonetallow-clan]] is a clan of shield dwarves with ancestral roots tracing back to a master engineer from [[gauntlgrym]]. 

## Characteristics
The clan is renowned for its mastery of engineering, specifically the construction of stable and complex archways. Their name is etymologically derived from "stone to allow," a reference to their professional legitimacy and ability to manipulate stone for structural perfection. They are characterized by a strong sense of honor and a strict adherence to [[contractual-integrity]].
