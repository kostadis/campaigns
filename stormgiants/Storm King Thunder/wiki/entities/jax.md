# Jax

Jax was a field lieutenant and intermediary for the-carver, serving as the operational link between high-level leadership and field crews. He was identified as the last known active agent of the organization.

## Personality & Tactics
Jax was characterized as cold, calculating, and opportunistic. He demonstrated a lack of loyalty to fellow agents, viewing them as tactical assets rather than allies. His combat style relied on [[tactical-exploitation]], specifically waiting for opponents to expend resources before striking.

## Combat History
During an ambush at the tree line, Jax coordinated an attack using [[rot-tusk-ogres]] and bandit crews. He attempted to exploit the party's exhaustion following their battle with [[sister-kaella]]. Despite utilizing crowd control measures, he was defeated and killed by the party's combined magical and physical assault.

## Status
**Deceased.** His death represents a significant disruption to the field operations of the-carver.
