# Deciduous Dryad
- **Role:** Unseelie combatant; one of two dryads encountered in battle
- **Faction:** Unseelie Court
- **First Appearance:** During an Unseelie combat encounter alongside the Coniferous Dryad

## Personality & Motivations
- **Tactically minded in combat, prioritizing charm and social manipulation over direct violence**
- **Appealed to Orsik specifically, framing the Unseelie as allies to pull him out of the fight**

## History with the Party
- During battle, called out to Orsik claiming the fight was not his and that the Unseelie were his friends — successfully charming him into sitting down
- Was struck by Thistle's arrow and covered in acid by Thuul, breaking her concentration and freeing Orsik
- Killed by Unla Kee's magic missiles

## Current Status
- **Deceased.** Killed during the battle by Unla Kee.
- Note: Her death did not end the charm on Unla Kee — that effect was sustained by the Coniferous Dryad.

## Relationships
- **Coniferous Dryad:** Fellow Unseelie combatant; the two operated together in battle, each maintaining separate charm effects
- **Orsik:** Successfully charmed him during combat
- **Unla Kee:** Killed by her magic missiles; Unla Kee remained charmed after her death (effect held by Coniferous Dryad)
- **Thistle / Thuul:** Their attacks broke her concentration and ultimately ended her effectiveness in the fight

- N/A — deceased; no ongoing arc
