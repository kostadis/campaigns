# Dragon Slayer Sword

An ancient and potent weapon once owned by [[lady-alagondar]], a noble whose quest remains shrouded in mystery. The sword was not buried with her body, but placed separately — atop the skull of a slain dragon in the northern chamber of the [[dragonbarrow]], pointing northward.

## History & Discovery

The sword was discovered by the party in the northern chamber of the [[dragonbarrow]], resting atop a dragon's skull. Its placement was described by boney as being "across the way" from Lady Alagondar's resting chamber, pointing north. The recovery of this blade appears to be the catalyst for the manifestation of [[lady-alagondar]]'s ghost.

Its placement was not accidental; it was ritualistic, designed to be discovered only after the correct conditions were met. The ghost of Lady Alagondar manifested only after the sword was secured by the party, confirming a deep, possibly magical, bond between the artifact and her spectral presence.

The sword’s name suggests a history of dragon-slaying, yet its connection to the White Dragon of the [[ice-spire-mountains-white-dragon-connection-2026-06-06-133513]] — the direction of Lady Alagondar’s final gaze — implies it may have been intended to prevent, not destroy, a greater threat. Its origin, maker, and full properties remain unknown.

## Narrative Significance

The Dragon Slayer Sword functions as a [[artifact-placement-as-narrative-clue]]: its location encodes a hidden truth, triggers spectral manifestation, and anchors the unresolved purpose of its former owner.
