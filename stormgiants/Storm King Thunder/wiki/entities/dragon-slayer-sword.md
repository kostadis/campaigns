# Dragon Slayer Sword

A significant magical artifact formerly owned by [[lady-alagondar]]. 

## History & Discovery
The sword was discovered by the party in the northern chamber of the [[dragonbarrow]], resting atop a dragon's skull. Its placement was described by [[boney]] as being "across the way" from Lady Alagondar's resting chamber, pointing north. The recovery of this blade appears to be the catalyst for the manifestation of [[lady-alagondar]]'s ghost.
