# Vinby Hearthgood

Vinby is a wealthy and refined halfling resident of Silverymoon. He serves as a primary example of the **Pragmatic Witness** archetype—an NPC whose role is to provide a "minimalist" truth that creates legal or social hurdles for the protagonists.

### Personality & Motivation
Vinby presents as a man of significant means, characterized by his refined appearance and well-appointed surroundings. However, his actions suggest a core of **cold pragmatism**. While he witnessed the death of an unnamed companion during a Cambian attack, his testimony in the Star Court focused exclusively on the "breaking and entering" committed by [[orsik]] and [[unla_kee]].

### Key Interactions
- **The Cambian Encounter:** Vinby's home was breached by the party while they were fleeing Cambians. During this event, one of Vinby's companions was killed. 
- **Star Court Testimony:** Vinby chose to omit all mention of the supernatural threat (the Cambians) or his own loss, opting instead for a narrative that framed the party as trespassers. This created a significant **truth-gap** in the public record.
- **Relationship with [[lady-margaster]]:** Vinby actively avoided physical and social proximity to her during court proceedings, suggesting a deep-seated fear or a desire to distance himself from her influence.
