# The Bugbear (Unnamed)
A lone survivor and dungeon delver, the Bugbear is a peripheral NPC in the narrative. He initially joined the party for food and left when treasure became more appealing. He continues searching for treasure even after finding the exit.

## Core Motivation
- **Survival:** His primary motivation is survival, driven by the allure of treasure.

## Relationships
- **Vardis & Unla Kee:** Functional alliance, but no lasting bond.
- **Dungeon Inhabitants:** Hostile or predatory relationship; he has been eating the goblins.

## Last Known Location
- **Deeper inside the tower's interdimensional dungeon**
- **Active:** Presumably continuing to search for treasure and/or the secret door
- **Fate Unknown:** Whether he survives, finds what he's looking for, or eventually escapes is unresolved.

## Actions
- **First Meeting:** Found alive inside the dungeon, having survived by eating other trapped creatures (goblins).
- **Alliance Formation:** Joined Vardis and Unla Kee after they offered food.
- **Mimic Fight:** Continued fighting and helped finish the mimic after Unla Kee put it to sleep.
- **Departure:** Refused to leave when the party located the staircase exit, turning back into the dungeon.

## Pragmatism
- **Philosophy:** Emphasizes practicality and realism over theoretical ideals.
- **Explanation:** Highlights the Bugbear’s approach to survival and decision-making.

## Loyalty Transactions
- **Nature:** Loyalty is conditional and based on mutual benefit.
- **Illustration:** His departure when treasure became more appealing than freedom.

## Open Questions
- What happens to the Bugbear after leaving the party?
- Does he survive his quest for treasure?
- Is he successful in finding the secret door?

## Notes
- **Self-reliant and Stubborn:** He has rejected both the zombie-acceptance and death-expulsion theories of escape, trusting only his own search.
- **Darkly Humorous:** He casually describes eating trapped goblins and quotes his mother's proverbs about "walking meat bags."
