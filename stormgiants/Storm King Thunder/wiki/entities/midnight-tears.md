# Midnight Tears

Midnight Tears is a rare and lethal interdimensional poison developed and trafficked by [[house-sartorra]] (and affiliated with [[xorlarrin]]). It is a viscous, obsidian-black liquid that glows faintly under moonlight and emits a scent of lavender and ozone. The poison is harvested from planar rifts and stabilized using dwarven alchemical techniques, allowing it to retain its potency across dimensional boundaries.

When ingested or introduced into the bloodstream, Midnight Tears induces a slow, irreversible unraveling of the victim’s connection to their native plane. Victims experience vivid hallucinations of alternate selves, followed by physical disintegration — not death, but *unmaking*. The body remains intact, but the soul is scattered across the Astral Sea, rendering resurrection impossible without divine intervention.

It is a primary driver of the smuggling operations conducted by operatives like [[aletra-sottora]] and [[aletra-satora]], who transport it via the [[model-1489-spatial-rectification-calliope]]. The poison is sold to elite buyers in the Underdark and on the Material Plane as a tool of assassination, political elimination, and psychological warfare. Its rarity and lethality make it one of the most valuable contraband items in the drow underworld.
