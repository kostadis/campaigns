# Out-of-Phase Dwarves

The [[out-of-phase-dwarves]] are the transformed remnants of the original population of [[axeholm]]. They are not a cohesive faction but rather broken beings driven by a singular, obsessive compulsion toward "efficiency."

## Physical Description
Their appearance is highly grotesque and non-Euclidean:
- **Skin:** Parchment-like skin covered in shifting ink.
- **Embedded Wealth:** Gold coins are embedded directly into their flesh.
- **Mutilation:** Mouths are often sewn shut with golden wire or red tape.
- **Phasing:** Their limbs appear to multiply and move out of sync with reality, often giving the illusion of having six hands and six legs.

## Combat Mechanics & Abilities
- **Paralysis Effect ("Cold Storage"):** A primary method of immobilization used against targets.
- **Materialization:** They emerge from sources of "unnatural light" within [[axeholm]].
- **Escalation:** Their numbers appear to increase dynamically as combat progresses.

## Psychological Profile
They lack traditional intelligence or strategic goals, instead muttering incessantly about "efficiency." They appear to view biological entities through a lens of utility, as evidenced by the auditory hallucination experienced by [[brewbarry]] regarding "incorrect packaging."
