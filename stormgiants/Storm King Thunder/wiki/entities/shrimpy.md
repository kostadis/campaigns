# Shrimpy

**Shrimpy** (also known as *The Orc with the Necklace of Bones*) is a captured orc prisoner and former raider. He was mockingly renamed by [[brewbarry]] during his capture during a supply cart ambush.

## Identity & Background
Shrimpy belongs to an unnamed orc tribe that was displaced from the [[ice-spires]] region of the Sword Mountains by the white dragon [[cryovain]]. This displacement led to a period of [[uthgardt-exile]] and subsequent [[tribal-fracturing]], resulting in several distinct factions:

* **Independentists:** A splinter faction seeking [[orc-independence]] and self-determination, of which Shrimpy was a member.
* **Cultists:** Those who allied with the [[cult-of-talos]].
* **Brigands:** Displaced raiders.

## Personality
Shrimpy exhibits a complex duality:
* **The Warrior:** Proud, principled, and fiercely loyal to tribal bonds (demonstrated by his reaction to a charmed tribesman). He holds a deep ideological hatred for the [[cult-of-talos]] due to their influence on his people.
* **The Prisoner:** Under the pressure of captivity, he often reverts to a "whiny and cowardly" persona, utilizing [[emotional-performance]] and opportunistic negotiation to seek release.

## Current Status
Shrimpy is currently held in Phandalin under the custody of [[harbin-wester]]. He is bound to the adventuring party's supply cart, as [[brewbarry]] has taken personal responsibility for his captivity. His presence in the region is a direct consequence of the displacement caused by [[cryovain]].

## Relationships
* **[[brewbarry]]**: Captor and primary antagonist; the relationship is defined by [[coercive-captivity]]. However, it is complicated by a shared sense of [[uthgardt-exile]], as Shrimpy's lamentations regarding his clan's exile resonate with Brewbarry's own history.
* **[[valphine]]**: Target of Shrimpy's racial animosity due to her Drow identity.
* **[[soma]]**: The individual who mortally wounded him during the initial ambush.
* **[[harbin-wester]]**: Current custodian in Phandalin.
* **[[tibor-wester]]**: Influenced his fate by refusing to transport him to Neverwinter.
