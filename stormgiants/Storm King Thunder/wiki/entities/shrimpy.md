# Shrimpy

**Shrimpy** (also known as *The Orc with the Necklace of Bones*) is a captured orc prisoner and former raider. He was mockingly renamed by [[brewbarry]] during his capture in a supply cart ambush.

## Identity & Background

Shrimpy belongs to an unnamed orc tribe displaced from the [[ice-spire-mountains-white-dragon-connection-2026-06-06-133513]] region of the Sword Mountains by the white dragon [[cryovain]]. This displacement triggered a period of [[uthgardt-exile]] and subsequent [[tribal-fracturing]], splitting his people into three distinct factions:

- **Independentists**: A splinter faction seeking [[orc-independence]] and self-determination; Shrimpy was a committed member.
- **Cultists**: Orcs who allied with the [[cult-of-talos]], whom Shrimpy despises for corrupting his people’s traditions.
- **Brigands**: Displaced raiders who turned to violence for survival.

Shrimpy’s identity as an Independentist shaped his worldview, emphasizing tribal loyalty and resistance to external domination—particularly from the [[cult-of-talos]], whose influence he views as a betrayal of orcish heritage.

## Personality

Shrimpy exhibits a complex psychological duality shaped by his circumstances:

- **The Warrior**: Proud, principled, and fiercely loyal to his tribal bonds. This side emerges when confronted with perceived betrayals—such as when a fellow orc was charmed—revealing his deep-seated honor and ideological rigidity. His hatred for the [[cult-of-talos]] is not merely political but spiritual, rooted in the erosion of ancestral ways.
  
- **The Prisoner**: Under the strain of captivity, he frequently adopts a performative persona of whining, fear, and cowardice. He employs [[emotional-performance]] and opportunistic negotiation as survival tactics, attempting to manipulate his captors into releasing him. This duality reflects his internal conflict between dignity and desperation.

## Current Status

Shrimpy is currently held in Phandalin under the custody of [[harbin-wester]]. He is physically bound to the adventuring party’s supply cart, as [[brewbarry]] has assumed personal responsibility for his captivity. His continued presence in the region is a direct consequence of the displacement caused by [[cryovain]] and the subsequent collapse of his tribe’s cohesion.

His fate was further shaped by [[tibor-wester]], who refused to transport him to Neverwinter, effectively trapping him in Phandalin’s orbit.

## Relationships

- **[[brewbarry]]**: Captor and primary antagonist; their dynamic is defined by [[coercive-captivity]]. Yet their relationship is paradoxically nuanced by a shared experience of [[uthgardt-exile]], as Shrimpy’s grief over his lost clan resonates with Brewbarry’s own history of displacement, creating an uneasy, unspoken kinship.
  
- **[[valphine]]**: Target of Shrimpy’s racial animosity due to her Drow identity; he views her as an embodiment of the corruption and betrayal he associates with outsiders who exploit the weak.

- **soma**: The individual who mortally wounded him during the initial ambush; though Shrimpy survived, the injury remains a physical and psychological scar.

- **[[harbin-wester]]**: Current custodian in Phandalin; maintains a pragmatic, detached oversight of Shrimpy’s confinement.

- **[[tibor-wester]]**: Played a pivotal role in determining Shrimpy’s fate by refusing to facilitate his transfer to Neverwinter, ensuring his continued presence in Phandalin and entangling him further in the party’s journey.
