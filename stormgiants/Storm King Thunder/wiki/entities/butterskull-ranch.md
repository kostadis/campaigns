# Butterskull Ranch

[[butterskull-ranch]] is a property owned by [[alphonse-big-al-kalazorn]].

## History and Occupation
Following an initial orc raid, the ranch was seized and placed under hostile occupation by [[borg-the-hammer]] and the [[rot-tusk-ogres]]. 

The site was utilized as a staging ground for a significant ambush orchestrated by [[borg-the-hammer]]. The ranch was used as a trap designed to lure adventurers, with [[alphonse-big-al-kalazorn]] held captive in the ranch cellar to serve as bait.

## The Ambush and Destruction
After the ambush failed and [[alphonse-big-al-kalazorn]] was rescued, [[borg-the-hammer]] burned the ranch to the ground in a fit of rage. The destruction resulted in the loss of the farmhouse, cornfields, and gardens.
