# Petunia

Petunia is a cow and the emotional anchor of Alphonse "Big Al" Kalazorn. Though non-combatant and seemingly mundane, Petunia represents Alphonse’s capacity for care, loyalty, and emotional connection — qualities he struggled to express toward humans. Her brand, reading “Big Al’s Cow,” is a quiet testament to their bond.

Petunia’s recovery, facilitated by Soma’s *Speak with Animals*, was the pivotal moment that forced Alphonse to confront his guilt over abandoning Qelline Alderleaf. Her presence during his confession and subsequent journey to Qelline’s farm symbolizes the possibility of reconciliation through simple, steadfast devotion.

Petunia is a canonical example of [[animal-companion-bonding]], demonstrating that emotional anchors need not be magical, intelligent, or heroic — they can be ordinary creatures whose presence reveals the deepest truths of their human companions.
