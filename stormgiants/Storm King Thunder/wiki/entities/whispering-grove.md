# Whispering Grove

A once-sacred grove of Chauntea, located in the Whispering Woods near Phandalin. Once a vibrant symbol of natural harmony, it was reduced to a ruined wasteland: trees twisted into impossible shapes, unnatural colors, and creatures with too many eyes moving in jerky patterns.

Adabra Gwynn personally confirmed its destruction three weeks before the Emerald Enclave council, using it as the central evidence in her case against interventionism. She attributed its decay to Lyra’s cleansing ritual and the party’s actions — framing it as a consequence of “careless, careless intervention.”

In truth, the grove’s corruption was accelerated and manipulated by Narcellia Varammyr, bound within the Headband of Intellect — making it a case of [[motivated-blight]]. Adabra’s failure to recognize this reveals the fatal flaw in her naturalist worldview.

The grove’s ruin is not just ecological — it is symbolic. It represents the collapse of the Enclave’s internal unity, the failure of both interventionist and anti-interventionist approaches, and the hidden hand of external manipulation. It remains a wound in the land — and a mirror to the party’s own moral ambiguity.
