*   **Sacrificial Decoy Tactics**: Using a party member (Brewbarry) as a "distraction" to manipulate environmental hazards/enemies.
    *   *Significance:* Highlights Aletra's lack of empathy and tactical ruthlessness.
    *   *Status:* Exists in wiki as `[[sacrificial-decoy-tactics]]`.
*   **Weaponized Charm & Dark Humor**: Using condescension and wit to maintain composure during high-stakes conflict.
    *   *Significance:* Aletra's primary personality trait for interacting with "lesser" beings.
    *   *Status:* Exists in wiki as `[[weaponized-charm-and-dark-humor]]`.
*   **Operational Oversight (KP)**: The concept of a hidden supervisor who dictates the limits of an operative's actions.
    *   *Significance:* Establishes the hierarchy within the smuggling network.
    *   *Status:* Related to `[[organizational-secrecy-via-bosses]]`.
