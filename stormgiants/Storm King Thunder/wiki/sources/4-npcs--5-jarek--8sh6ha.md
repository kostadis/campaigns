# Jarek

Intelligence report and encounter notes regarding Jarek, the groom to Elara Seasong Meliame. Contains critical eyewitness testimony regarding a sensory phenomenon preceding an orc ambush.

Groom and traveling companion to Elara, Jarek is a quiet, pragmatic figure whose most significant contribution to the campaign is his unverified but compelling testimony of an internal "hum" preceding an orc ambush. He appeared alongside Elara after the attack, offering no combat involvement but providing critical observational detail: he perceived a non-auditory, internally generated sensation — described as a hum felt within his mind — moments before the orcs struck. The sensation vanished immediately, leaving no physical trace.

Jarek’s demeanor is marked by caution and self-vindication; he explicitly reminded Elara that he had advised her to travel with protection, framing his inaction not as cowardice but as foresight unheeded. His role is peripheral to major arcs, yet his experience introduces a novel sensory phenomenon with potential implications for understanding coordinated enemy behavior.

He returned safely to Phandalin with Elara. No further interactions or developments have been recorded.
