## Key Concepts
*   **The "Hum" (Internal Hum)**
    *   **Definition:** A non-auditory, internal sensation of sound/vibration experienced by Jarek before an orc ambush.
    *   **Significance:** It serves as a potential indicator of a coordinating force or magical "warning" system behind the orc behavior. It links to the theme of **internal_hum_sensory_phenomenon**.
    *   **Wiki Status:** Exists as a concept; Jarek’s experience is a specific instance of it.
*   **Self-Vindicated Temperament**
    *   **Definition:** The psychological state of feeling justified in one's prior warnings or concerns after an event occurs.
    *   **Significance:** Highlights Jarek's personality as cautious and perhaps slightly weary of the dangers of the road.
    *   **Wiki Status:** Not explicitly a standalone concept, but fits under character archetypes.

## Main Arguments & Findings
*   **The "Hum" as Intelligence:** The most significant finding is that Jarek’s experience of the "hum" suggests that the orc ambush was not a random occurrence but part of a coordinated effort. The fact that it vanished exactly when the ambush began implies a tactical or magical trigger.
*   **Predictive Warning:** Jarek's insistence on traveling with protection, and his subsequent comment about it, establishes him as a character who recognizes danger before it manifests, contrasting with Elara’s more traditionalist/optimistic stance.
