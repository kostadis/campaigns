# Gralm

**Gralm** is an Ettin warlord and merchant specialist. As an Ettin, he possesses a dual personality manifested through his two heads: a blonde head and a bald head. This duality creates significant internal tension that frequently affects his decision-making processes.

## Biography and Role

Gralm operates as a central figure in regional politics and commerce, acting as both a military leader and a specialist in merchant affairs. He is known to engage in [[Magical Contracts]]—formal agreements that utilize magical means to ensure compliance between parties.

A significant turning point in his recent history involved the establishment of a provisional alliance with the **Lord's Alliance**. This alliance was formed specifically to oppose the Ettin warlord [[Bad Fruul]]. The signing of this contract was contingent upon several factors, including trial victories and the resolution of physiological disturbances affecting his temperament.

## Personality and Temperament

Gralm's demeanor is heavily influenced by external environmental factors, specifically those related to the Fey. He previously suffered from severe sleep deprivation caused by **Fey Music**—musical disturbances generated by Fey spirits such as [[Prince Thornacious]]. 

This sleep deprivation specifically impacted his bald head, making it prone to outbursts and less amenable to diplomatic negotiations. Following the intervention of the gnome [[Unla Kee]], who provided magmin earmuffs to mitigate the effects of the Fey music, Gralm's bald head became more stable, leading to improved cooperation and a more constructive negotiating style.

## Key Entities and Associates

### Allies and Associates
- **[[Unla Kee]]**: A gnome who played a pivotal role in Gralm's stability by resolving his sleep deprivation issues through the creation of magmin earmuffs.
- **Lord's Alliance**: A political entity with whom Gralm signed a provisional alliance contract.

### Rivals and Influences
- **[[Bad Fruul]]**: A rival Ettin warlord and the primary target of Gralm's alliance with the Lord's Alliance.
- **[[Prince Thornacious]]**: A Fey spirit whose musical disturbances contributed to Gralm's instability.
- **Grindle**: A nearly thirteen-foot ogre associated with Gralm's sphere of influence.
- **Orsik**: An individual of undisclosed type/role.

## Concepts

### Magical Contracts
Formal, magically-binding agreements used by Gralm to secure alliances and commercial interests, ensuring that all parties adhere to the terms through supernatural enforcement.

### Fey Music
Musical disturbances caused by Fey spirits. For Gralm, this phenomenon acted as a significant biological stressor, causing sleep deprivation and emotional volatility.
