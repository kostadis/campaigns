## Key Concepts

*   **Dual-Personality Dynamics:** Gralm’s two heads (Blonde/Diplomatic vs. Bald/Aggressive) represent a mechanical and narrative way to handle multi-faceted NPC interactions.
*   **Trial of Worth:** A ritualized negotiation method where physical feats and drinking contests serve as "proof" before formalizing alliances.
*   **Magmin Earmuffs:** A specific crafting solution to a magical environmental hazard (Fey music).
*   **Contractual Binding:** The use of magic-enforced contracts to ensure political cooperation against common enemies.

## Main Arguments & Findings

*   **Successful Diplomacy through Multi-Layered Engagement:** Gralm was won over not just by the "correct" choice of contract, but by addressing his specific physical needs (sleep) and satisfying his cultural requirements (trials of strength/drinking).
*   **The Power of Practical Solutions:** The creation of the earmuffs is a key narrative beat that transitioned Gralm from a frustrated obstacle to a cooperative ally.
*   **Evidence Strength:** High. The "Arc Score" notes explicitly track how specific actions (Unla Kee's crafting, Orsik's persuasion) directly led to the successful contract signing.
