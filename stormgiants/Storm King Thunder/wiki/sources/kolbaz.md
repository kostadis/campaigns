# Source: kolbaz.md

### Key Entities
- **Kolbaz**: Harper operative and regional agent; leader of the Harper headquarters in Yartar (Central, Check)
- **Krowen**: Harper superior; held a professional grievance over Krowen's policy of using non-sworn contractors (Peripheral, Check)
- **Thistle**: Member of the party; had a brief interaction with Kolbaz (Peripheral, Check)
- **Adimond Kroskas**: Investigated by Kolbaz, likely the cause of his death (Peripheral, Check)
- **Vardis**: Recalled Kolbaz's death and chamber damage, identified as a Blue Dragon event (Peripheral, Check)
- **Gauche**: Knew details about activity near the teleportation circle at the time of death (Peripheral, Check)

### Key Concepts
- **Harper:** A faction dedicated to protecting the realm from supernatural threats (Central, Check)
- **Cult of the Dragon:** An ancient cult believed to be responsible for the destruction of the Well of Dragons (Central, Check)
- **Seelie Court:** The fairies' court, known for its aesthetic and cultural practices (Central, Check)
- **Teleportation Circle:** A magical device used for teleportation, likely involved in Kolbaz's death (Central, Check)
- **Blue Dragon:** A mythical dragon capable of causing significant damage (Central, Check)

### Main Arguments & Findings
- **Core Claims:**
  - Kolbaz died due to a bolt of lightning and was subsequently struck by a small, unidentified figure.
  - His research into Adimond Kroskas was the proximate cause of his death.
  - Adimond Kroskas is an alias, and he is not who he claims to be.
  - The damage to Kolbaz's chambers suggests a massive entity materialized, destroyed everything, and dematerialized.
  - Other individuals came through the teleportation circle around the time of death, but Gauche withheld this information from the party.
  
- **Evidence Supporting Claims:**
  - Thistle's examination of Kolbaz's papers revealed the critical discovery that Adimond Kroskas is an alias.
  - Physical evidence of the chamber damage aligns with a Blue Dragon event.
  - Gauche's knowledge of activity near the teleportation circle at the time of death indicates a potential connection to the incident.
