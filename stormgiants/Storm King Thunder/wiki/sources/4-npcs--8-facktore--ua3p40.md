# Facktore

Intelligence regarding the deranged gnome inventor Facktore, his ambush of [[vukradin]] and the party, and his use of unproven mechanical devices.

This source documents Facktore as a deranged gnome inventor whose unprovoked ambush of the party using an automated crossbow system exposes critical institutional failures within Gnomengarde. His actions exemplify unregulated invention, the use of innocents as test subjects, and the fleeing-inventor archetype. His disappearance leaves his current location and potential for retaliation unknown, creating persistent narrative tension. This source extends the understanding of Gnomengarde’s internal fractures and serves as a cautionary case study in the absence of ethical oversight in scientific communities.
