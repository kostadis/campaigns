## Key Entities
*   **Facktore** (Gnome Inventor): A deranged, unstable inventor residing in Gnomengarde. He is a primary antagonist/hazard who represents "mad science" and reckless experimentation. (New Entity)
*   **Vukradin**: A party member who was specifically targeted by Facktore's ambush. (Existing Entity)

## Key Concepts
*   **Unregulated Invention**: Facktore embodies the danger of technological advancement without ethical oversight or safety protocols.
*   **Live Testing**: The use of sentient beings as "test subjects" for mechanical devices, highlighting a lack of moral restraint in his pursuit of innovation.
*   **Impulsive/Reckless Behavior**: His choice to flee rather than face consequences and his unprovoked attack on the party highlight his instability.
*   **Ambush as Experiment**: The specific use of an automated crossbow platform as a data-gathering or testing tool for his inventions.

## Main Arguments & Findings
*   **Role as a "Wild Card":** Facktore is not a mastermind like *The Carver* or a political player like *Adabra*. He is a localized, erratic threat whose primary danger is the unpredictability of his technology.
*   **Evidence of Instability:** His behavior—ambushing an unprovoked party and fleeing immediately upon failure—suggests he lacks the discipline for high-level conspiracy but possesses enough competence to build dangerous automated weaponry.
*   **Narrative Function:** He serves as a physical manifestation of the "unregulated invention" theme in Gnomengarde, providing a contrast to the more structured (though still flawed) systems of the city's leadership.
