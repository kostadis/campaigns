## Main Arguments & Findings
*   **Victimhood vs. Agency:** The core narrative arc for Cryovain is his status as a "victim." While he was a powerful predator, by the time of encounter, he was a broken vessel for the Carver's influence. This creates a moral tension for the party (specifically Soma).
*   **The Power of the Sword:** The sword is not just a weapon but an intelligence and amplifier. Its ability to "dismiss" chains rather than break them establishes it as a high-level threat.
*   **Resource Conversion:** The transition from "monster" to "resource" is immediate. The harvesting of his breath pouch and scales serves as the mechanical conclusion to his narrative role.
