# Source: the_entity_in_the_tower.md

### Key Entities
- **The Entity in the Tower**: Person operating or controlling an intelligence/informant network including agents and wards.
- **Llorkh**: Village targeted by the Tower of Green Flame.
- **Vardis**: Person who used a blessing to help the party.
- **Unla Kee**: Person who co-opened the door to the Tower.
- **Orsik & Thistle**: Individuals dragged towards the Tower.

### Key Concepts
- **Telepathy**: A method of communication between entities through mental signals.
- **Antipathy Field**: A defensive mechanism that repels or discourages intruders.
- **Interdimensional Dungeon**: A mysterious subterranean area accessible through the Tower’s doors.

### Main Arguments & Findings
- **Core Claims**: The entity in the Tower is a sentient being that monitors intruders and reacts to their actions. It uses telepathy to communicate and employs an antipathy field to deter potential threats.
- **Evidence**: 
  - Telepathic warnings and satisfaction expressed upon the party's entry.
  - Antipathy field preventing the party from entering the Tower freely.
  - Observations of the entity's reactions to specific actions taken by the party.
- **Strength of Evidence**: Strong, supported by detailed observations and interactions described in the source.

### Connections to Existing Wiki
- **[[intelligence-networks]]**: The entity operates as an intelligence network, monitoring and reacting to intruders.
- **[[teleportation-circle]]**: The Tower of Green Flame is associated with teleportation circles, which align with the entity's ability to react quickly to intruders.
- **[[interdimensional-dungeons]]**: The existence of an interdimensional dungeon within the Tower is consistent with the entity's role as an informant.

### Contradictions & Tensions
- **Entity's Antipathy Field**: While it prevents the party from entering freely, it also allows them to approach the Tower, suggesting a balance in its strategy.
- **Entity's Reaction to Vardis**: The entity's satisfaction upon Vardis using a blessing implies a complex relationship, possibly indicating a form of respect or acknowledgment.
