## Main Arguments & Findings
*   **The Fanatic as a Proxy:** The character is not a unique villain but a "cog" in a larger machine. His lack of a name or personal identity emphasizes that he is an instrument of the **Kraken Society**.
*   **Scale of Threat:** The fanatic's dismissal of the Talosian orcs suggests that while local factions (like those following the Cult of Talos) are dangerous, they are insignificant compared to the "rising current" toward Neverwinter.
*   **Evidence of Expansion:** The existence of "Kraken Society Echoes" and the specific mention of a "Neverwinter operation" provide concrete evidence of an escalating plot involving interplanar or large-scale maritime/oceanic forces.
