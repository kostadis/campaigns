# Source: inferno.md

### Key Entities
- **Inferno:** The Hellbound Dracolich; Merchant specializing in magical goods for discerning magic users, known to Unla Kee and associated with the Fishyard.
- **Sammaster:** A character in the story, depicted as Inferno's teacher in the secrets of undeath.
- **Demon Lord (unnamed):** The entity Inferno made a forbidden pact with, granting him immortality and leading to his "Hellbound" title.

### Key Concepts
- **Dracolich:** A powerful undead dragon that has made a pact with a demon lord for immortality.
- **Undead:** Refers to Inferno's state after making the pact with the demon lord, turning him into a Dracolich.
- **Forbidden Pact:** The agreement Inferno made with the demon lord, which led to his transformation into a Dracolich.

### Main Arguments & Findings
- **Core Claims:** Inferno is the first Dracolich, having made a forbidden pact with a demon lord for immortality. He was initially a powerful red dragon but became a Dracolich after the pact.
- **Evidence:** The statue found in the ice cave cavern depicts Inferno teaching Sammaster the secrets of undeath, indicating their relationship. Additionally, the removal of gem eyes triggered animated statues, suggesting Inferno's presence.
- **Strength of Evidence:** The evidence comes from the description of the statue and the reaction it caused, implying Inferno's existence and the nature of his pact.
