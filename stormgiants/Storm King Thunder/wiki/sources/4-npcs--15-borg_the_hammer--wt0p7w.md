# Source: Borg the Hammer

This source documents the operational profile of Borg the Hammer, a half-orc enforcer operating under the unseen authority of “The Carver.” His actions at Butterskull Ranch exemplify a meticulously planned psychological trap designed to lure adventurers through the emotional vulnerability of a non-combatant captive — Big Al Kalazorn. Borg’s tactical discipline, cold pragmatism, and explosive emotional volatility reveal a disciplined operative whose failure was not due to incompetence, but because the party circumvented the narrative design of the trap.

His destruction of the ranch after the ambush’s failure is not strategic, but a sign of emotional collapse — a vulnerability that contrasts with his otherwise calculated behavior. The source confirms Borg as a node in a larger, hidden hierarchy, reinforcing the campaign’s recurring theme of obscured authority. His existence is functional, not personal; his value lies in what he reveals about The Carver’s operational model.
