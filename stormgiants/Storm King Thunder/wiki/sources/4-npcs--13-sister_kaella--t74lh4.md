# Source: npcs/sister_kaella.md

## Key Entities

*   **Sister Kaella (also known as Sister Kayla/Kella)**
    *   **Type:** NPC / Antagonist-turned-Wildcard.
    *   **Role:** Central. She is the primary focus of this document, serving as a high-level intelligence operative and "killer in priest's cloth."
    *   **Status:** Exists in current narrative (as an escaped fugitive).
*   **The Carver**
    *   **Type:** Antagonist / Organization Leader.
    *   **Role:** Central. The overarching threat that Kaella served as a spymaster for; his "madness" and the impending ritual at Icespire Hold are primary plot drivers.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki (as part of the Carver's organization).
*   **Valphine**
    *   **Type:** Party Member / Ally.
    *   **Role:** Central. She is Kaella’s only "trusted" contact within the party and the person who physically revived her.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki.
*   **Vukradin**
    *   **Type:** Party Member / Ally.
    *   **Role:** Central. He serves as the moral foil to Kaella, representing "moral clarity" vs. her "gray" pragmatism.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki.
*   **Soma**
    *   **Type:** Party Member / Ally.
    *   **Role:** Peripheral/Support. Intervened to prevent Kaella's execution; provided the "killer in priest's cloth" descriptor.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki.
*   **Brewbarry**
    *   **Type:** Party Member / Ally.
    *   **Role:** Peripheral. Dismissed by Kaella as too loyal to be a viable contact.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki.
*   **Locutus**
    *   **Type:** NPC (Deceased).
    *   **Role:** Peripheral. A "loose end" whom Kaella killed immediately upon meeting Valphine.
    *   **Status:** New entity (likely needs a brief entry or mention of death).
*   **Borg the Hammer**
    *   **Type:** Antagonist / Enforcer.
    *   **Role:** Peripheral. A failed operative whose failure to eliminate witnesses triggered Kaella's fallout with The Carver.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki.
*   **Corbin**
    *   **Type:** NPC (Deceased).
    *   **Role:** Peripheral. The guard who held Kaella; murdered by her during her escape.
    *   **Status:** New entity (needs entry as a fallen guard/victim of Kaella).
*   **Ser Kaelen**
    *   **Type:** Authority Figure / Ally.
    *   **Role:** Peripheral. Informed by Vukradin regarding Kaella's status.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki (as the theater figure/leader).
*   **Jenna Roscoe / Lord's Alliance**
    *   **Type:** Organization / Representative.
    *   **Role:** Peripheral. Offering to track Kaella as a political bargaining chip for Vukradin.
    *   **Status:** New entity (Lord's Alliance is known; Jenna Roscoe is new).

## Key Concepts

*   **Killer in Priest’s Cloth:** A descriptor of Kaella’s duality—using religious iconography and attire to mask a ruthless, pragmatic intelligence career.
*   **Moral Grayness vs. Moral Clarity:** The ideological divide between Kaella (who views morality as a tool for survival) and Vukradin (who views it as an absolute).
