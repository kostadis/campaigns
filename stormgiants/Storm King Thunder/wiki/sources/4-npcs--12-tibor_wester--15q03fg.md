## Key Concepts

*   **Wildcat Logging Operation**
    *   **Definition:** Illegal, unregulated logging that bypasses environmental protections and permit systems.
    *   **Why it matters:** It establishes Tibor as a lawbreaker/outlaw and creates the "paper trail" of his illicit activities.
    *   **Status:** New concept (potential addition to local lore).
*   **Transaction as Narrative Catalyst**
    *   **Definition:** Using mundane, logistical tasks (like signing a delivery notice) to drive plot progression.
    *   **Why it matters:** Tibor’s role in the "delivery contract" is a prime example of this; his absence turns a routine task into an investigation.
    *   **Status:** Already exists in wiki.
*   **Information Asymmetry (The Missing Man)**
    *   **Definition:** The gap between the party's need for Tibor’s signature and the reality of his disappearance.
    *   **Why it matters:** It creates tension and mystery regarding the "abandoned" state of the camp.
    *   **Status:** Already exists in wiki (under related concepts).
