# Source: serissa.md

### Key Entities
- **People:**
  - **Queen Serissa:** Youngest daughter of King Hekaton and Queen Neri; current reigning queen of the storm giant court; Imperator of the Maelstrom.
  - **King Hekaton:** Father of Queen Serissa; initially absent, then returned but perceived as diminished and avoidant.
  - **Queen Neri:** Mother of Queen Serissa; deceased; her death is the catalyst for the current crisis.
  - **Mirran & Nym:** Elder sisters of Queen Serissa; initially hostile towards her, but now confined by her.
  - **Iymrith:** Ancient blue dragon; former de facto ally out of necessity, now fled.
  - **Adminond Kroskas:** Received a report on dragon-fairy hybrid experiments; dismissive of Serissa's concerns.
  - **Unla Kee:** Commissioned as primary investigator into Hekaton's disappearance; warned of consequences for failure.
  - **Blagothkus:** Views Serissa's cooperation with Iymrith as foolish.
  - **Thistle:** Member of the party; invoked his personal sacrifice as a moral example.
  - **Orsik:** Party member; noticed Serissa and acknowledged her potential future leadership.
- **Organizations:**
  - **Storm Giant Court:** Underwater citadel where Queen Serissa rules.
- **Products:**
  - **Golden Suit of Scale Mail:** Provided by Queen Serissa to Unla Kee as payment for investigating Hekaton's disappearance.
- **Datasets:**
  - **Divination Magic Results:** Used by Queen Serissa to locate Hekaton's prison ship.
- **Tools:**
  - **Gambling Chit:** Found on the bodies of small folk responsible for Queen Neri's death.
  - **Wooden Coin Bearing the Golden Goose:** Reference to intelligence from a mage.

### Key Concepts
- **Intelligence Networks:** Central to Queen Serissa’s operations, involving her sisters, Iymrith, and the party members.
- **Stability and Rebuilding:** Core goals of Queen Serissa, focusing on stabilizing her rule and rebuilding the storm giant court’s power.
- **Alliances with Small Folk:** Queen Serissa’s adherence to her mother’s philosophy of allying with small folk.
- **Diplomacy and Power Play:** Queen Serissa’s political shrewdness and willingness to be accommodating when it serves her interests.
- **Conflict and Betrayal:** Internal conflicts within the family, particularly between Serissa and her sisters, and external threats like the Kraken Society and Iymrith.
