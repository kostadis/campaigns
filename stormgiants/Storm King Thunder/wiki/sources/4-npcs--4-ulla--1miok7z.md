## Key Concepts
*   **Procedural Rigidity**
    *   **Definition:** Adherence to rules and bureaucracy as a primary mode of interaction, often used to deflect personal judgment or complex social nuances.
    *   **Significance:** Defines Ulla’s character; she is not "mean" but follows the "logic" of her role.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki (related to *bureaucratic_cowardice* and *gatekeeping-as-service*).
*   **Shapechanger Paranoia**
    *   **Definition:** A specific, deep-seated fear/prejudice regarding shapeshifters within the Gnomengard context.
    *   **Significance:** Serves as a recurring "trap" or hurdle for the party; it is Ulla's primary defensive mechanism in social interactions.
    *   **Status:** New nuance to existing *shapeshifter-threat* themes.
*   **Social Manipulation & Deflection**
    *   **Definition:** The use of interpersonal tactics to shift blame or navigate social pressure (e.g., Valphine's manipulation and Ulla’s subsequent redirection).
    *   **Significance:** Highlights the "game" of diplomacy in the setting where truth is often obscured by performance.
    *   **Status:** Related to *face-saving-maneuvers*.
