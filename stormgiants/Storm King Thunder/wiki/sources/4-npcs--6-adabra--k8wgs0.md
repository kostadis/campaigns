# Source: npcs/adabra.md

## Key Entities

### People
- **Adabra (Adabra Gwynn)** (Central)
    - *Role:* Apothecary, herbalist, and druid; a radical anti-interventionist within the Emerald Enclave. She serves as a primary ideological antagonist to the party's "interventionist" actions.
    - *Status:* Exists in wiki (as `adabra-gwynn`).
- **Soma** (Central)
    - *Role:* Druid-in-training and Adabra’s former protégé; currently experiencing a crisis of faith regarding Adabra's teachings.
    - *Status:* Exists in wiki (as `soma`).
- **Vukradin** (Central)
    - *Role:* Member of the Order of the Gauntlet; a "fellow naturalist" turned ideological adversary who successfully deceived Adabra regarding the Treant.
    - *Status:* Exists in wiki (as `vukradin`).
- **Lyra** (Peripheral/Antagonist)
    - *Role:* An interventionist druid and target of Adabra’s political maneuvering within the Enclave.
    - *Status:* Exists in wiki (as `lyra`).
- **Falcon the Hunter** (Peripheral)
    - *Role:* A "dracenslayer" who supported Adabra's cause but later expressed regret for doing so.
    - *Status:* Exists in wiki (as `falcon-the-hunter`).
- **Aldric Stone Path** (Peripheral)
    - *Role:* An ally of Adabra’s who shared her anti-interventionist views.
    - *Status:* Exists in wiki (as `aldric-stone-path`).
- **Harbin Wester** (Peripheral)
    - *Role:* A Phandalin authority figure whom Adabra successfully lobbied for the party's benefit.
    - *Status:* Exists in wiki (as `harbin-wester`).
- **Ser Kaelen** (Peripheral)
    - *Role:* An observer who views Adabra as "confused" or potentially mad.
    *Status:* Exists in wiki (as `ser-kaelen`).
- **Reidoth** (Peripheral)
    - *Role:* The nominal head of the local Emerald Enclave chapter; strategically avoided by Adabra during her hearing.
    - *Status:* Exists in wiki (as `reidoth`).

### Organizations & Locations
- **Emerald Enclave** (Central)
    - *Role:* A druidic organization split between "interventionist" and "anti-interventionist" factions.
    - *Status:* Exists in wiki (as concept/organization).
- **Order of the Gauntlet** (Peripheral)
    - *Role:* The military/religious order to which Vukradin belongs; viewed by Adabra as a primary source of "barbaric" intervention.
    - *Status:* Exists in wiki (as organization).
- **Whispering Grove / Whispering Wood** (Central)
    - *Role:* A site of significant ecological and political fallout involving the death of a Treant.
    - *Status:* Exists in wiki (as `whispering_grove`).
