# Source: deciduous_dryad.md

## Key Entities
- **Deciduous Dryad**: Merchant specializing in magical goods for discerning magic users, known to Unla Kee and associated with the Fishyard.
- **Coniferous Dryad**: Fellow Unseelie combatant; the two operated together in battle, each maintaining separate charm effects.
- **Orsik**: Successfully charmed him during combat.
- **Unla Kee**: Killed by her magic missiles; Unla Kee remained charmed after her death (effect held by Coniferous Dryad).
- **Thistle / Thuul**: Their attacks broke her concentration and ultimately ended her effectiveness in the fight.

## Key Concepts
- **Tactically minded in combat, prioritizing charm and social manipulation over direct violence**: A trait observed in the Deciduous Dryad's personality and motivations.
- **Appealed to Orsik specifically, framing the Unseelie as allies to pull him out of the fight**: An example of the Deciduous Dryad's strategic approach to combat.

## Main Arguments & Findings
- The Deciduous Dryad was a tactical combatant who used charm and social manipulation to influence Orsik, successfully pulling him out of the fight.
- She was killed by Unla Kee's magic missiles, but her charm effect on Orsik persisted due to the Coniferous Dryad's continued presence.
- The Deciduous Dryad's death marked the end of her role in the narrative, as she was no longer able to maintain her charm effect on Orsik.
