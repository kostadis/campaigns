## Main Arguments & Findings

*   **The Tragedy of the Anchor:** Yazmeen is not just a survivor; he was a victim of "instrumentalization." He was used as a tool for interplanar summoning, which explains his desperate need to stay in the library (his "post").
*   **The Role of Ritual and Ward:** The tea served as a specific counter-measure. The fact that it only worked while he was awake suggests a direct link between consciousness/will and magical warding.
*   **Evidence Strength:** High. The document clearly outlines his role, the mechanics of the Slaad's intrusion, and his current status.
