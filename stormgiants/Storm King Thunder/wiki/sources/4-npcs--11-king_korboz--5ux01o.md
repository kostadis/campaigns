## Key Entities

*   **King Korboz** (Person)
    *   **Role:** Central. He is the senior co-ruler of Gnomengarde and a primary victim/subject of the current narrative arc regarding madness and political instability.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki as a core NPC.
*   **King Gnercli** (Person)
    *   **Role:** Central. Co-ruler of Gnomengarde; his refusal to acknowledge Korboz's claims creates the primary political friction in the region.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki.
*   **Vukradin** (Person)
    *   **Role:** Central/Party Member. Acts as a catalyst who identifies and exploits the structural cracks in the dual monarchy.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki.
*   **Fibblestib & Dabbledob** (People)
    *   **Role:** Supporting. The inventors tasked with curing Korboz; they represent the "mad science" and desperate measures of Gnomengarde.
    *   **Status:** Exist in wiki.
*   **Warren & Orrin** (People)
    *   **Role:** Peripheral/Plot Devices. Missing gnome inventors whose disappearance serves as the "proof" for Korboz's claims regarding shapeshifters.
    *   **Status:** Exist in wiki.

## Key Concepts

*   **Dual Monarchy** (Political System)
    *   **Definition:** A system where two rulers share equal or tiered authority.
    *   **Significance:** The specific tension between Korboz and Gnercli highlights how shared power can lead to paralysis when one ruler refuses to acknowledge the other's concerns.
*   **Shapeshifter Threat** (Plot Device/Fear)
    *   **Definition:** A perceived or actual presence of shape-changing entities in the cave systems.
    *   **Significance:** It serves as the "truth gap" between Korboz and Gnercli; it is the catalyst for Korboz's paranoia and the source of political instability.
*   **Madness as Political Vulnerability** (Theme)
    *   **Definition:** The use of a ruler's mental instability to undermine their authority or create internal fractures.
    *   **Significance:** This is central to the "Gnomengarde" arc, where Korboz’s condition is both a personal tragedy and a political liability.
*   **Seniority vs. Authority** (Social Dynamic)
    *   **Definition:** The distinction between a title of rank and the actual power/influence wielded by that title.
    *   **Significance:** Korboz's obsession with his "senior" status highlights his insecurity and the fragility of his position.

## Main Arguments & Findings

*   **The Fragility of Shared Power:** The relationship between Korboz and Gnercli is not just a personal spat but a systemic failure. Gnercli’s refusal to acknowledge Korboz's claims creates a "truth gap" that allows the shapeshifter threat (real or imagined) to fester as a source of instability.
