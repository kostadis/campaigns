# Source: npcs/king_korboz.md

This source documents King Korboz, the senior co-ruler of Gnomengarde, whose escalating madness has become a catalyst for political collapse within the dual monarchy. His accusations of a shapeshifter threat, dismissed by co-ruler King Gnercli, reveal deep institutional fractures. Vukradin exploited Korboz’s sense of eroded authority to publicly fracture the monarchy’s cohesion. The inventors Fibblestib and Dabbledob have dedicated their efforts to developing the Sanity Ray as a technological cure, reflecting Gnomengarde’s faith in engineering over dialogue. Korboz’s hysteria is not merely personal pathology — it is a symptom of systemic neglect, and his unresolved fears may point to a real, hidden threat. The ambiguity surrounding the shapeshifter preserves narrative tension and underscores the fragility of rational institutions when confronted with emotional truth.
