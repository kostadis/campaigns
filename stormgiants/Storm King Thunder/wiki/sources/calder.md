# Source: calder.md

## Key Entities
- **Calder**: Independent halfling shopkeeper in Parnast, no affiliation with Lionel Coster; sells replacement swords to Unla Kee.
- **Lionel Coster**: Operation viewed by Calder as unfair competition, operates dimensional portals allowing faster and cheaper goods movement.
- **Zhentarim**: Dependent on Zhentarim for physical transportation of goods due to adverse competitive conditions.
- **Unla Kee**: Purchased a replacement sword from Calder during her stay in Parnast.

## Key Concepts
- **Dimensional Portals**: A method of transportation used by Lionel Coster, allowing for faster and cheaper goods movement compared to independent traders.
- **Magical Goods Trade**: The central trade network involving magical goods, where Calder operates within.

## Main Arguments & Findings
- Calder is resentful of Lionel Coster's operations, viewing them as unfair competition.
- Calder relies on Zhentarim for physical transportation of goods due to adverse competitive conditions.
- Calder has no magic items to sell and is hostile towards Lionel Coster's operation.
