# Blasius

The narrative traces his emotional arc from self-serving guilt to partial redemption, framed by his complex relationships with Oblivillish, Unla Kee, and the Seelie Court hierarchy.

## Main Arguments & Findings

- **Core Claims/Results**:
  - Blasius falsely accused Oblivillish, leading to Oblivillish’s exile.
  - Blasius later successfully cleared Oblivillish’s name before Prince Thornacious.
  - Blasius was reinstated as Prince Thornacious’s Satyr companion — not as honor, but as containment under surveillance.
  - Blasius orchestrated a vision quest to grant the party the ability of poison neutralization.
  - Blasius is assigned to accompany the party against Bad Fruul.
  - Blasius uses ritualized drinking to navigate social tension and evade emotional accountability.
  - His final declaration — “I am off” — remains unresolved, signaling ambiguity in his loyalty and future.
