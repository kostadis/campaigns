# Cryovain

Intelligence regarding the white dragon Cryovain, his death at [[icespire-hold]], and the subsequent harvesting of his remains. This source details the ritualistic domination performed by [[the-carver]] and the moral tensions within the party regarding the dragon's status as a victim versus a threat.
