# Cryovain

Cryovain is a white dragon whose death at [[icespire-hold]] became a pivotal moment of moral and narrative tension within the party. Though a formidable predator and combatant, Cryovain’s final moments were marked by ritualistic domination performed by the-carver, transforming his death from a simple combat encounter into a deeply ethically fraught act.

His remains were systematically harvested, not merely for material gain, but as part of a broader ritual that questioned the boundaries between threat and victim. The party was divided: some viewed Cryovain as a legitimate target in the context of survival and territorial conflict; others saw him as a sentient being whose agency and dignity were violated through posthumous domination.

Cryovain’s role extends beyond that of a slain monster—he is the most significant victim of domination in recent memory and a catalyst for profound moral ambiguity. His existence and death demand explicit indexing within the main Entities archive to prevent fragmentation of narrative focus and to ensure his symbolic weight is preserved in the collective record.
