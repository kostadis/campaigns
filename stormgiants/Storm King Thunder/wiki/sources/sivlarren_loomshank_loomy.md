# Source: sivlarren_loomshank_loomy.md

### Key Entities
- **Sivlarren Loomshank (Loomy)**: Disgraced itinerant cleric of Helm, exiled to the Allfaiths Shrine in Red Larch.
- **The Brothers**: Clients who hired Loomy to find Mirak, but he withheld critical information.
- **The Party**: Paid off Loomy’s debts and received his full divine reading.
- **Mirak**: The target of the divine reading sought by the brothers.
- **Helm**: The deity whose divine readings Loomy provided.
- **Six Windows Inn**: The location where Loomy’s full divine reading led the party to search.
- **Talking Troll Tavern**: The initial meeting place between Loomy and the party.
- **Red Larch**: The location of the Allfaiths Shrine where Loomy was exiled.
- **Neverwinter Clerical Hierarchy of Helm**: The organization Loomy was formerly affiliated with before being exiled.

### Key Concepts
- **Divine Reading**: The method by which Loomy interpreted the will of Helm.
- **Telepathy**: The ability to communicate directly with Helm through divination.
- **Deception**: Loomy’s withholding of crucial information from the brothers to avoid conflict.

### Main Arguments & Findings
- **Core Claims**: Loomy provided the party with a complete divine reading indicating that Mirak was still alive and not buried, leading them to search the attic of the Six Windows Inn.
- **Evidence**: Loomy’s full divine reading, corroborated by the party’s subsequent discovery of Mirak’s location.
- **Strength of Evidence**: Strong, as the party successfully found Mirak based on Loomy’s information.
