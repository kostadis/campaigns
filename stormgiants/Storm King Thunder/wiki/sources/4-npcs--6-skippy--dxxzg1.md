## Key Concepts

*   **Disposable Road Crew** (Concept)
    *   **Definition:** A tier of low-level, expendable assets used for high-risk tasks where the loss of life is expected.
    *   **Importance:** Highlights the hierarchy within The Carver's organization and the "meat-shield" nature of the lower ranks.
    *   **Wiki Status:** New concept or update to the-carver.
*   **Quest as Emotional Trap** (Concept)
    *   **Definition:** A deliberate setup where a public quest is designed to lure specific targets into a pre-planned ambush.
    *   **Importance:** Explains the logic behind the Butterskull Ranch incident; it wasn't a random bandit raid but a calculated trap for adventurers.
    *   **Wiki Status:** Exists as [[quest-as-emotional-trap]].
*   **Information Asymmetry (Intelligence Gathering)** (Concept)
    *   **Definition:** The gap between what the party knows and what the enemy's hierarchy actually entails.
    *   **Importance:** Skippy’s interrogation bridged this gap, revealing the roles of Borg and Sister Kayla.
    *   **Wiki Status:** Exists as [[information-asymmetry-npc-encounters]].

## Main Arguments & Findings

*   **The Carver's Strategy is Calculated:** The evidence from Skippy's testimony confirms that the "Butterskull Ranch" incident was not a random occurrence. It was a deliberate trap designed to exploit the predictable behavior of adventurers responding to Harbin’s board.
