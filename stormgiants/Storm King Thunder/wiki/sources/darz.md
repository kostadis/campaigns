## Key Concepts

*   **Transactional Worldview:** A philosophy where all interactions are viewed as exchanges of value rather than based on loyalty or morality. This defines Darz's character arc.
*   **Debt Resolution (Arc Score):** The mechanic of using a narrative action (sharing the Weevil tip) to "pay off" a mechanical/narrative debt (the cost of being raised).
*   **Information as Currency:** The use of secrets or intelligence as a literal form of payment for services.

## Main Arguments & Findings
*   **Character Archetype:** Darz is designed as a "transactional" NPC. He is not a friend, but a professional who recognizes the value of his life and pays it off immediately with information.
*   **Narrative Economy:** The document highlights that while Darz shared the Weevil tip, he did so because he no longer needed it as leverage once he was alive; this keeps the plot moving without creating an ongoing "debt" for the players to manage.
*   **Evidence of Cynicism:** His internal monologue regarding Cindell and Darthara's motivations shows he is highly observant but emotionally detached.
