## Main Arguments & Findings
*   **Commercial Intelligence as a Veil:** Linene is not just a merchant; she is a specialized agent for an organization with specific, high-level interests in interplanar phenomena. Her "enthusiasm" is a professional indicator of the value of certain items to her superiors.
*   **Strategic Deflection:** She successfully avoids direct engagement with the party's service offers (e.g., Vukradin’s escort) by redirecting them toward rumors, suggesting she operates within a strict protocol or is wary of being "too" involved with local adventurers.
*   **The Link to Interplanar Tech:** There is a strong narrative thread connecting her behavior to the "defunct interplanar shipping device." Her willingness to pay premium prices suggests her organization may be trying to replace, repair, or bypass that specific technology.
