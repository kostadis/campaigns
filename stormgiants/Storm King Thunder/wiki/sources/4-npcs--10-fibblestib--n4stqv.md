# Source: Fibblestib

This source documents Fibblestib, a central inventor within Gnomengarde and ideological rival to Dabbledob. His character exemplifies the tension between institutional innovation and moral detachment. He is driven by a singular obsession: curing King Korboz’s madness through his “Sanity Ray,” while simultaneously engaging in public, competitive rivalry with Dabbledob over design superiority. His interactions with the party reveal a man who redirects urgent external threats (e.g., dragon threat) toward his own priorities, claims credit for shared successes, and treats sentient creatures as raw material — most chillingly, his enthusiasm for harvesting mimic entrails unsettles Soma.

Fibblestib is not a villain, but a product of his environment: a technocratic culture that values ingenuity over empathy. He engages constructively with Vukradin on music-as-treatment, acknowledges empirical utility, and participates in institutional debates (e.g., tamper-proof voting box theory). His advocacy for a “bicameral legislature with a unitary executive” reflects his belief in structured, top-down innovation — a philosophy that may destabilize Gnomengarde’s existing governance.
