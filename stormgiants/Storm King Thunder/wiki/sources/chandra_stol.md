## Key Entities

### Organizations & Locations
- **Shrine of Axes (Temple of Axes)**: *Central*. Chandra’s place of worship in Parnast.
- **Parnast**: *Central*. The primary setting for her residence and current conflict.
- **Evereska**: *Peripheral*. Chandra's place of origin.
- **Zhentarim**: *Peripheral*. A pragmatic organization Chandra favors over the Lord's Alliance. (Already exists in wiki).
- **Lord's Alliance**: *Peripheral*. An organization Chandra views with growing skepticism. (Already exists in wiki).

## Key Concepts
- **Generationally-Linked Conflict**: The multi-generational feud between Chandra’s family and Narcellia, highlighting how personal trauma can span centuries.
- **Pragmatism vs. Idealism**: Represented by Chandra's preference for Zhentarim "practicality" over the Lord's Alliance's perceived failures.
- **Sacrificial Protection (Tactical Selection)**: The decision to cast *bless* on Thistle specifically because he was a target that would draw fire away from her.
- **Identity of the Victim**: Chandra’s rejection of "civilian" vs. "militia" labels, emphasizing the human cost of war over abstract categorization.
