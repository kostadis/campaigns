# Meril

Intelligence regarding the druidic mentor of [[soma]], his enigmatic departure, and the political implications of the staff he left in her care.

## Summary
Meril is a pivotal, though physically absent, figure in the current narrative. A master druid, he provided [[soma]] with an extraordinarily abbreviated training period of only one year—a stark departure from the decades typically required for druidic mastery. His decision to bestow [[merils-staff]] upon her is viewed by members of the [[emerald-enclave]], specifically [[xanthopoulos]], not as an act of sentiment, but as a calculated "choice" to designate a successor or champion.

## Key Findings
- **The "Choice":** [[xanthopoulos]] asserts that Meril "picked" [[soma]] for a specific, undisclosed purpose.
- **Philosophical Legacy:** Meril is suspected of being unable to resolve the tension between [[interventionism]] and the [[anti-interventionist-faction]], potentially leaving [[soma]] to resolve this conflict.
- **Active Observation:** Despite his apparent absence, intelligence suggests Meril remains "very interested" in the party's activities, implying he may still be alive or monitoring events through intermediaries.
- **Magical Instruction:** He taught [[soma]] how to access [[the-well]] and provided critical knowledge regarding natural threats like will-o'-wisps.
