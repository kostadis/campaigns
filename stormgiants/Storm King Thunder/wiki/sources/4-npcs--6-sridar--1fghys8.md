## Key Concepts

*   **Interventionism (Planar):** The belief that planar disruptions should be actively countered or investigated rather than observed passively. This is the primary ideological divide between Sridar and the Emerald Enclave.
*   **Artifact Collection as Scholarship:** Using the acquisition of "wrong" items to map out larger patterns of dimensional instability.
*   **Underdark Blink Event:** A specific traumatic/transformative event (demon appearance) that serves as Sridar's origin story for her current obsession.
*   **Planar Incursion Theories:** The distinction between natural phenomena (like the Faerzress) and deliberate, intentional interference by entities or mages.

## Main Arguments & Findings

*   **Sridar as a Narrative Mirror:** Sridar serves as the "pro-active" opposite to Adabra's "passive" druidism. While Adabra fears the consequences of intervention, Sridar views non-intervention as a failure to address active threats.
*   **Transactional Integrity:** Her interactions are strictly businesslike and fair. She doesn't offer "mystical" help but rather "expert" analysis in exchange for currency or information.
*   **Evidence of Growth:** The fact that she was impressed by Brewbarry’s theories suggests she is not a static character; she is capable of evolving her worldview based on new data.

## Connections to Existing Wiki
