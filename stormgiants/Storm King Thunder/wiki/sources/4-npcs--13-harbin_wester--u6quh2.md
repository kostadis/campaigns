# Source: npcs/harbin_wester.md

This source provides a granular, event-by-event log of Harbin Wester’s interactions with the party, revealing a fully realized archetype of the self-preserving bureaucrat. It documents his use of procedural delay, hierarchical deference, narrative control, and informal graft to maintain authority while avoiding accountability. Key events include his initial avoidance of direct contact, manipulation of the Privy Council as a shield, credit appropriation during victory celebrations, and the orchestration of public festivals to bind his name to others’ achievements. The source also confirms his familial ties to Tibor Wester, his custody of Shrimpy, and his operational relationship with Lord Neverember.
