## Key Concepts

*   **Narrative Control / Political Maneuvering**: The deliberate use of public events and rhetoric to frame personal success as institutional victory.
    *   **Significance:** This is a core theme for Harbin; he isn't just a bureaucrat, but a strategist who "binds" his name to the party's deeds before they can become independent narratives.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki (as `narrative-control` and related concepts).
*   **Credential-based Access / Gatekeeping as Service**: The use of official status and paperwork to control access or resources.
    *   **Significance:** Harbin uses the "rules" and his title as a shield against moral challenges.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki (as `credential-based-access`).
*   **Transactional Relationship**: A relationship based on mutual utility rather than trust or shared values.
    *   **Significance:** Defines Harbin's current standing with the party and the Privy Council.
    *   **Status:** Related to `transaction-as-narrative-catalyst`.
