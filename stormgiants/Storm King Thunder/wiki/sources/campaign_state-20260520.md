# Campaign State - 2026-05-20

Comprehensive historical log of completed encounters, resolved plot threads, and the current status of non-player characters (NPCs) for the campaign. This document tracks the transition from local infestations to high-stakes planar and draconic conflicts involving [[iymrith]], the [[storm-giant-court]], and the [[kraken-society]]. It has been updated to reflect the theological dimension of giant decline, as revealed in the source "annam_the_allfather.md".

Key narrative pivots include the [[bob-saget-incident]], the death of [[harshnag]], and the discovery of [[laela]]'s survival.

## Update: Giant Decline

The collapse of giant civilization is no longer viewed solely as a result of military defeat or internal strife. The silence of [[annam-the-allfather]] — his complete withdrawal from divine engagement — is now recognized as the primary catalyst. The Imperator’s account confirms that King Hekaton’s fatalism, and by extension the shattering of the Ordning, stemmed from spiritual abandonment, not mere political exhaustion.

This revision deepens the understanding of [[ordning-shattering]] and contextualizes the rise of entities like [[adimond-kroskas]], who exploited the resulting spiritual vacuum to manipulate and corrupt giant society.
