## Main Arguments & Findings
*   **The "Professional" Facade:** Daran presents himself as a simple orchardist, but his history as an adventurer and his specific interest in the lighthouse suggest he is not merely looking for profit.
*   **Ethical Friction:** The interaction between Daran and Vukradin serves as a primary vehicle for exploring the theme of "moral blindness." Daran's lack of defensiveness suggests he isn't a villain, but rather someone who has compartmentalized his past to live a simpler life.
*   **Evidence Strength:** High. The text clearly distinguishes between what is known (his role as an orchardist/retired adventurer) and what is hidden (his true motives for the salvage).
