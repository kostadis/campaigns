# Source: mirak_hysstryn.md

### Key Entities
- **Mirak Hysstryn**: Deceased resident of Triboar, subject of a missing persons/murder investigation.
- **Jacoby & Persson Hysstryn**: Brothers who hired the party to investigate Mirak's disappearance.
- **Tolmara Hysstryn**: Wife of Mirak, claimed to have run off with a lover.
- **Urgala Meltimer**: Lover of Mirak, suspected to have been involved in his murder.
- **Lord Protector Darathra**: Authority figure who imprisoned Tolmara.
- **Unla Kee**: Priest of Helm who provided a divination tip.
- **Orsik**: Character who destroyed the animated skeleton.
- **Delratha**: Character who arranged for a Speak with the Dead casting.

### Key Concepts
- **Divination**: A method used by Unla Kee to reveal that Mirak was not buried but remained above ground.
- **Speak with the Dead**: A ritual performed to communicate with the deceased, crucial for determining the cause of Mirak's death.
- **Teleportation Circle**: A magical device used to transport the skeleton to the attic, possibly linked to the divination result.

### Main Arguments & Findings
- The party discovered Mirak's remains in the sealed attic of the Six Windows Inn, consistent with the divination tip.
- The skeleton briefly animated upon contact with Unla Kee, suggesting it was alive at some point.
- Orsik destroyed the animated skeleton in one blow, indicating its supernatural nature.
- The cause of death remains unconfirmed, pending the results of the Speak with the Dead ritual.
- Tolmara is the primary suspect, though her guilt is not confirmed.
