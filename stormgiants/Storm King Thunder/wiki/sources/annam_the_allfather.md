# Annam the Allfather

A primary source detailing the theological absence at the heart of giant civilization's collapse. Extracted from the historical accounts of the Imperator, this document frames Annam’s silence as the foundational spiritual crisis that precipitated the shattering of the Ordning. It provides no explanation for his absence, only its consequences: the warring divine offspring, the despair of King Hekaton, and the abandonment of the giant races.

Key references:
- The Imperator’s testimony on giant decline
- The non-intervention of Annam despite ongoing prayers
- The interplanar warfare among Annam’s offspring

This source is critical for understanding the theological dimension of the Ordning’s collapse, transforming it from a political event into a cosmic tragedy of divine abandonment.
