### Key Entities
- **People/Organizations:**
  - **Uncle Joon**: Fairy relative and mentor of Thistle; former member of Queen Titania's Court (expelled for drunkenness). Central entity, treated as the same character as "Joon" and "Uncle Yorn".

- **Intelligence Networks**: Uncle Joon operates as an informant and intelligence network, interacting with various factions and individuals.
- **Fey Lore and the North**: Uncle Joon shares stories about the North as a former Fey playground, hinting at a lost era of freedom and portals between planes.
- **Debt and Financial Irresponsibility**: Uncle Joon is deeply in debt everywhere he goes, suggesting financial instability and poor management.
- **Psychic Possession Rituals**: Mentions psychic possession rituals, indicating involvement with dark magic or supernatural practices.
- **Ancient Dragons**: Knowledge of ancient dragons and their lairs, despite Uncle Joon's reluctance to engage with them.
