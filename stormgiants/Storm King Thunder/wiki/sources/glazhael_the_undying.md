## Main Arguments & Findings
*   **Transactional Nature:** The source emphasizes that Glazhael is **not** an ally. Her interactions are strictly "contractual." She honors the letter of a deal (breaking the ice, giving the box) but never the spirit (helping the party proactively).
*   **Manipulation via Information Gap:** There is strong evidence (noted by Thistle) that Glazhael uses "staged" retreats and "shadowboxing" to manage the party's movements. She treats the party as pawns in a larger game of positioning.
