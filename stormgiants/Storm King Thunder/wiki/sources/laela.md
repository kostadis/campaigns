- **Core Claims:**

- **Evidence:**
  - Oracle reports indicate Laela is held by Duke Zalto.
  - Thistle's reactions and speculations suggest Laela's resistance.

- **Strength of Evidence:**
  - Strong, based on Thistle's observations and interactions with Laela.
