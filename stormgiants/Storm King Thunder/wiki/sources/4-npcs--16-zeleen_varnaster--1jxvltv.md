## Key Entities

*   **Zeleen Varnaster** (NPC)
    *   **Role:** Central. Leader of the Whiskered Gang and de facto protector of the Mountain's Toe Gold Mine.
    *   **Status:** Exists in current narrative as a complex, morally ambiguous ally.
*   **The Whiskered Gang** (Organization)
    *   **Role:** Central. A wererat crew providing security and labor at the mine.
    *   **Status:** Likely exists/should be linked to [[zeleen-varnaster]].
*   **Horia Stonetallow** (NPC)
    *   **Role:** Central. Dwarven mine representative; formal partner in the mining agreement with Zeleen.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki.
*   **Brewbarry** (NPC)
    *   **Role:** Central. Party member/ally who used "blunt intimidation" to secure Zeleen's cooperation.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki.
*   **Valphine** (NPC)

*   **Vukradin** (NPC)
    *   **Role:** Central. Ally who shared a moment of mutual respect with Zeleen over a failed dragon bluff.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki.
*   **Don-Jon Raskin** (NPC)
    *   **Role:** Peripheral. A deceased mine worker whose death was "conveniently" handled by Zeleen.
    *   **Status:** New entity; potentially a minor mention or a "ghost" of a past conflict.
*   **Mountain's Toe Gold Mine** (Location)
    *   **Role:** Central. The primary setting for the current mining arc and the site of the wererat/dwarf cooperation.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki.
*   **Shrine of Savras** (Location)
    *   **Role:** Peripheral. Former home of the Whiskered Gang; currently occupied by orcs, ogres, and a dragon.
    *   **Status:** New location; potential site for future exploration.
