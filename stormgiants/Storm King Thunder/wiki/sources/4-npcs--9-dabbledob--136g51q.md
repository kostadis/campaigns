# Source: npcs/dabbledob.md

This source documents Dabbledob (also known as Dabblebob), a pragmatic inventor within Gnomengarde, and her intellectual opposition to Fibblestib. It details her evidence-based methodology, her engagement in governance reform debates, and her reluctant acknowledgment of music’s therapeutic efficacy. The source establishes her as a rationalist counterweight to speculative innovation and provides the foundational narrative for understanding Gnomengarde’s internal scientific and political dynamics. Her interaction with Vukradin marks the first recorded non-Adabra connection between a Gnomengarde figure and the party.
