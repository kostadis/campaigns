## Key Concepts

*   **Contractual Morality**
    *   **Definition:** The belief that contracts are sacred, binding moral acts and the primary vehicle for establishing trust/safety.
    *   **Significance:** This is the core of Horia's character; she views contracts as a shield against chaos. It contrasts sharply with Valphine’s view of them as tools for cheating.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki (as `contractual-morality`).
*   **Pragmatic Workarounds**
    *   **Definition:** Using specific, often minor "loopholes" or side-deals to manage internal complications (like Dwolin's superstitions) without compromising the main agreement.
    *   **Significance:** Shows Horia’s competence as a negotiator; she knows how to navigate complex systems.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki (as `pragmatic-integrity`).
*   **Shield Dwarf Engineering Heritage**
    *   **Definition:** The specific cultural identity of the Stonetallow clan, rooted in "proper" construction and mining.
    *   **Significance:** Establishes why they are the preferred miners over the wererats.
    *   **Status:** Exists in wiki (as `engineering-heritage-as-identity`).
