## Key Entities

*   **Annam (the Oracle)** (Central)
    *   A divine oracle and manifestation of Annam the All-Father. It serves as a conduit for truth but is bound by specific ritualistic constraints (six questions per day).
    *   *Status:* Exists in wiki as `[[annam-the-oracle-and-annam-the-all-father-of-giants]]`.
*   **Annam (the All-Father)** (Central)
    *   The primary deity of the giant pantheon. The Oracle speaks in his name and considers his "collected sayings" its primary text.
    *   *Status:* Exists in wiki as `[[annam-the-oracle-and-annam-the-all-father-of-giants]]`.
*   **Thistle Wendrod** (Central)
    *   The party member who interacted most directly with the Oracle, specifically attempting to "game" it with trick questions.
    *   *Status:* Exists in wiki as `[[thistle-wendrod]]`.
*   **Urgala** (Peripheral)
    *   A party member whose personal history (the death of his wife) was revealed by the Oracle's truth-telling.
    *   *Status:* Exists in wiki as `[[urgala]]`.
*   **Adimond Kroskas** (Peripheral)
    *   An entity/location identified by the Oracle as being at "the Maelstrom."
    *   *Status:* Exists in wiki as `[[adimond-kroskas]]`.
*   **King Hekaton** (Peripheral)
    *   Possessor of a conch artifact used to reach the Maelstrom.
    *   *Status:* Exists in wiki as `[[king-hekaton]]`.
*   **Duke Zalto** (Peripheral)
    *   The subject of Thistle's failed trick question regarding burial grounds.
    *   *Status:* Exists in wiki as `[[duke-zalto]]`.
*   **Zhentarim** (Peripheral)
    *   An organization that seeks specific geopolitical outcomes (ending the Giant War, trade routes).
    *   *Status:* Exists in wiki as `[[zhentarim]]`.

## Key Concepts

*   **Truth-Constraint Mechanism** (Core Concept)
    *   The Oracle is bound to provide truth but is limited by quantity (six questions/day) and ritual (extinguishing torches). This ensures it cannot be easily exploited for "free" information.
    *   *Status:* Exists in wiki as `[[divine-oracle]]`.
*   **Divine Silence / Mediated Revelation** (Core Concept)
    *   The Oracle represents a form of divine communication that is not an open line to the deity, but a filtered, constrained conduit.
    *   *Status:* Related to `[[divine-silence]]` and `[[divine-oracle]]`.
*   **Ritualized Questioning** (Core Concept)
    *   The physical act of extinguishing torches marks the end of an interaction, suggesting a structured magical or religious protocol.
    *   *Status:* Exists in wiki as `[[ritualized-questioning]]`.
