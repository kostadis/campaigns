# Cooragh Struckt

Cooragh Struckt is the cook at the [[wayside-inn]], encountered solely through the sensory impact of her cooking. She has no recorded dialogue, backstory, or direct interaction with the party. Her only narrative function is the preparation of exceptionally well-spiced stews and meat pies, which made a strong positive impression — particularly on [[valphine]]. 

Her presence exemplifies [[narrative-economy]] and [[background-npc]]: she contributes no plot, reveals no secrets, and engages in no relationships. Yet her food provides a moment of quiet emotional resonance, offering Valphine a rare, non-familial experience of comfort. This subtle impact challenges the assumption that background NPCs must be emotionally inert.

The alarmingly intense spicing of her dishes remains unexplained — possibly a stylistic choice, a personal signature, or an unintentional red herring. No connection to [[midnight-tears]] or [[aletra-satora]] has been established.

She is a canonical example of [[sensory-driven-characterization]] and the emerging archetype of the [[quietly-resonant-npc]].
