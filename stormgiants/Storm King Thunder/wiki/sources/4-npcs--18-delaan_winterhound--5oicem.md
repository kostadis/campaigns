## Key Concepts

*   **Naturalist Philosophy**
    *   **Definition:** A belief system prioritizing the protection of natural order over mortal political concerns.
    *   **Significance:** This was Delaan's original stance and serves as the "original" state of the Enclave before its split.
    *   **Wiki Status:** Exists (as *naturalism-as-moral-framework*).
*   **Ideological Fault Line**
    *   **Definition:** The specific point of contention regarding intervention vs. non-intervention.
    *   **Significance:** Delaan’s decision to act against Tiamat is the historical catalyst for the current division in the Emerald Enclave.
    *   **Wiki Status:** Related to *anti-interventionism_druidic*.

## Main Arguments & Findings

*   **Delaan as a Catalyst:** The primary finding is that Delaan Winterhound is not just a "wise" leader, but the pivotal figure whose change of heart created the current political landscape of the Emerald Enclave.
*   **The Weight of Persuasion:** His transition from non-interventionist to active participant highlights the power of individual heroes (like Malfire) to shift institutional trajectories.
*   **Historical Significance vs. Current Presence:** While he has no "Arc Score" and no direct interaction, his role as a foundational figure makes him a key piece of world-building for the Enclave's internal politics.

## Connections to Existing Wiki

*   **Emerald Enclave:** Directly relates to the faction's history and current internal conflict.
*   **Lyra:** Connects to the party's knowledge base regarding historical figures.

## Contradictions & Tensions

*   **The Wisdom Paradox:** Lyra describes him as the "wisest," yet his decision to abandon non-intervention is what caused the lasting fracture in the Enclave. This suggests that "wisdom" in this context may be subjective or tied to the specific threat of Tiamat.
*   **Status Ambiguity:** There is a tension between his importance as a historical figure and his total absence from current events (unknown if alive/dead).
