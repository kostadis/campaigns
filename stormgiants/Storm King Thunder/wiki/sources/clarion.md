# Clarion

This source details the interaction with a silver dragon named **Clarion**, who serves as a "field asset" and transport for a **Lord's Alliance** strike team under the command of **Commander Diana**. 

The text emphasizes that Clarion is not an independent actor or a personal ally, but a professional tool within a structured military hierarchy. It highlights the distinction between a "field asset" (a non-human entity used for logistics/tactics) and a narrative agent. The encounter at **Zephyros**'s cloud castle establishes the boundaries of Clarion's knowledge—specifically its lack of involvement with the cult of **Adimond Kroskas**.

The interaction is characterized by **pragmatic cooperation**, where initial hostility from the dragon was set aside once the party's status as non-hostile passengers was clarified.
