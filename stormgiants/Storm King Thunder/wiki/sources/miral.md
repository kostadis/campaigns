# Miral the Self

miral-the-self (also known as Miral the Sea Elf) was a sea elf whose spirit provides critical intelligence regarding the corruption of [[moesko]] and the growing threat beneath [[leilon]].

## Spirit and Warnings
As a ghost, Miral has provided warnings concerning the nature of Moesko's religious subversion. She noted that his original faith had been replaced by a cold, alien influence.

## Artifacts
An [[opalescent-conch]] belonging to Miral was recovered from Moesko's body and is currently held by the party.
