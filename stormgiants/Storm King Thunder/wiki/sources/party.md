# Source: party.md

## Key Entities

### People
- **Vardis**: Wood Elf Cleric (13th level), pragmatic and philosophically restless.
- **Orsik**: Mountain Dwarf Fighter (11th level) / Artificer (2nd level), reformed hero with a commitment to a heroic death.
- **Unla Key**: Lightfoot Halfling Wizard (13th level), high-functioning arcane savant with a belief in the end of the universe.
- **Thistle**: Player character, described as a trickster with a growing moral drift.
- **Escarlotta**: Countess, partner in the cloud castle and the Kee and Wing Casino.
- **King Hekaton**: Leader of the storm giant forces, initially dismissive of the party's abilities.
- **Countess Sansuri**: Owner of the cloud castle, conditions for continued use tied to the party's actions.
- **Titania**: Seelie Court figure, pleased with Thistle's trickery.
- **Orsik's Father (Uzorel "The Viper" Hornbalde)**: Zhentarim agent, betrayed Harper teleportation circles to them.
- **Old Keller (dwarf stonemason, Llorkh)**: Orsik has an open redemption debt with him.
- **Darathra Shendrel**: Former ally, severed ties following the Bob Saget theater operation.
- **Narcellia Varam'myr**: Former owner of the Headband of Intellect, Orsik's former master.

### Organizations
- **Kraken Society**: Enemies of the party, extracted Hekaton's location.
- **Harper Network**: Hostile to the party, relationship severed.
- **Zhentarim**: Positive towards Orsik, betrayed Harper circle locations to them.
- **Storm Giant Court**: High standing with Hekaton, supported Serissa.
- **Seelie Court**: Ambivalent towards Thistle, pleased with her trickery.
- **Harpers**: Hostile to the party, relationship severed.

### Datasets
- **Cloud Castle Treasury**: Contains ~75,000 GP and multiple spell scrolls.
- **Kee and Wing Casino**: Operated by Pow, formerly the Grand Dame, Yartar.
- **Harper Teleportation Circle Network**: Compromised, Orsik disclosed two circle locations to the Zhentarim.

### Tools
- **Conch of Teleportation**: Looted from slain giant lords.
- **Ring of Djinni Summoning**: Attuned to summon djinni.
- **Magma Warpick**: Magic item, recent acquisition.
- **Bag of Holding**: Holds significant loot.
- **Alchemists' Jug**: Used for alchemical purposes.
- **Book: *Dusk***: From Sandesyl Morgia.
- **Custom Shield**: Crafted by Orsik overnight at Calling Horns Inn.
- **Ring of Jumping**: From Blasius the Satyr.
- **Pegasus Wings**: 12-foot wings for flight capability.
