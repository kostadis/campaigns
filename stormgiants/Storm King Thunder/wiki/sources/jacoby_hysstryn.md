# Source: jacoby_hysstryn.md

### Key Entities
- **Jacoby Hysstryn**: Merchant; one of two brothers searching for their missing/deceased sibling Mirak Hysstryn.
- **Persson Hysstryn**: Brother; travels and advocates alongside Jacoby.
- **Mirak Hysstryn**: Missing/deceased sibling; driving Jacoby's actions.
- **Thistle**: PC; primary point of contact; Jacoby approached her to commission an investigation.
- **Delratha (Lord Protector)**: Authority figure; openly hostile towards Jacoby and Persson.
- **Lord Protector's Office**: Location where Jacoby and Persson confronted Delratha after presenting their findings.

### Key Concepts
- **Class-Based Neglect**: Theory suggesting that the party's institution treats common families less seriously than nobles.
- **Magical Bow**: Tool used as a form of payment and leverage in negotiations.

### Main Arguments & Findings
- **Core Claim**: Jacoby Hysstryn seeks definitive proof of what happened to his missing/deceased brother Mirak, not just ambiguous remains or a convenient dismissal.
- **Evidence Supporting Claims**:
  - Jacoby's direct approach to Thistle, offering a magical bow in exchange for help.
  - His public mockery of authority figures, such as performing an impression of the Lord Protector.
  - His frustration with perceived class-based neglect and his pragmatic approach to leveraging the party's services.
- **Strength of Evidence**: Strong, as evidenced by Jacoby's proactive behavior, willingness to confront authority, and specific demands for hard evidence.
