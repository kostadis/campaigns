## Main Arguments & Findings
*   **Character Archetype:** Big Al is a subversion of the "tough lawman" trope; while he presents as a hardened, pragmatic veteran, his actions regarding Petunia and his breakdown over Qelline reveal him to be emotionally fragile and driven by deep-seated regret.
*   **Narrative Function:** He serves as a vehicle for the **Quest-as-Emotional-Trap** concept. The party's role is not just to rescue him from Borg the Hammer, but to facilitate his journey toward Qelline.
*   **Evidence of Growth:** His willingness to leave the safety of the "rescue" zone to travel to Qelline’s farm indicates a shift from passive survival to active redemption.
