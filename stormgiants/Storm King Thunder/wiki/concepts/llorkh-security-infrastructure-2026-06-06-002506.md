# Llorkh Security Infrastructure

The entity [[captain-jolanth]] is a gatekeeper for the **Iron Circle** at the entrance to **Llorkh**. While the core concepts of "Performative Authority" and "Social Reframing" are well-documented, a specific page for the local security infrastructure would consolidate the role of the Iron Circle as a long-standing, stable force in the region.
