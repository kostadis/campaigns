# Proceduralism

**Proceduralism** is the tactical use of bureaucracy, agendas, and "proper process" to stall political momentum or deflect accountability.

As a primary method of defense, [[harbin-wester]] employs proceduralism to avoid making difficult decisions or facing direct confrontation. When pressured to hold town meetings or enact changes, he often cites the need for "preparation," "agendas," and "pre-work" to delay action.
