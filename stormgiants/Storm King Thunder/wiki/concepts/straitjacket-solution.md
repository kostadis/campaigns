# Straitjacket Solution

The **Straitjacket Solution** is a non-technological, physical restraint method proposed by Dabbledob as an alternative to Fibblestib’s **Sanity Ray** for managing King Korboz’s madness. Rather than attempting to “cure” the king’s condition, it seeks to contain it — using calibrated restraints to prevent self-harm and violent outbursts while preserving the king’s autonomy and dignity.

This concept embodies a radical humility in the face of psychological complexity. It rejects the assumption that madness must be fixed, instead accepting it as a condition to be lived with. The Straitjacket Solution is not punitive — it is protective, designed to create space for observation, ritual, or natural resolution.

It stands as a direct counterpoint to technological hubris, reflecting the wiki’s broader theme of [[moral-blind-spot-in-pragmatism]] — but inverted. Where Daran Edermath’s pragmatism ignores spiritual cost, Dabbledob’s pragmatism respects human limits. The Straitjacket Solution is not a failure of imagination — it is an act of profound wisdom.

Its existence challenges the assumption that innovation must be flashy, loud, or transformative. Sometimes, the most ethical invention is the one that does nothing — and simply holds.
