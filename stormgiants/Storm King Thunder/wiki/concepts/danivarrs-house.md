# Danivarr's House

Danivarr's House is a tavern and establishment located in Everlund, operated by Dral Thelev. It functions as a key hub for intelligence gathering and serves as a designated Harper contact point. The location is frequently used as a safe house and transition point by the party, facilitating meetings with various factions and operatives. Its role extends beyond mere hospitality, making it a critical node in the network of clandestine activities within the region.
