# The Kraken Society

The source text describes a "Kraken Society" as the organization that held King Hekaton prisoner on the ship *Morkoth*. This entity is distinct from standard aquatic monsters and appears to have sophisticated capabilities (e.g., using "specially undetectable ships"). A dedicated page should track their goals, hierarchy, and connection to Iymrith's plot.
