# Multi-Planar Economics
Theoretical framework aiming for optimal economic performance across multiple planes. Proposed by KP, a gnomish sage. Core argument: Achieving multi-planar economic efficiency is theoretically possible. Critique: Ignores the social disruption caused by such efficiency, which can lead to resentment among local merchants. Linked to [[social-disruption]].
