# Convert's Radiance

**Convert's Radiance** is a psychological and physical state of ecstatic joy and intense fervor displayed by new religious converts. 

In the context of the unorthodox Lathanderite movement, this phenomenon is noted in individuals like [[giles-slipper-shine]], where a state of apparent happiness and smiling fervor contrasts sharply with physical markers of suffering, such as scars from self-flagellation or severe sunburn. It serves as a visible indicator of deep, transformative religious commitment.
