# Vessel/Host Dynamics

A method of interaction used by sentient non-biological intelligences (such as the [[obsidian-sword]]) to manipulate the physical world. 

In this dynamic, a humanoid body (the [[human-vessel]]) serves as a physical instrument for the intelligence. The host provides the motor functions, tactical competence, and physical presence, while the intelligence provides the will, memory, and specialized abilities. This often results in the host appearing as a "hollow shell" or losing their humanity as the intelligence takes precedence.
