# Ethical Salvage vs. Grave-Robbing

A moral tension identified within the [[adventuring-economy]] regarding the legitimacy of recovering treasure from shipwrecked vessels.

## The Conflict
- **Salvage Operations:** Framed by pragmatists like [[daran-edermath-silverleaf]] as legitimate business opportunities to recover wealth from abandoned or wrecked vessels.
- **Grave-Robbing:** Viewed by moralists like [[vukradin]] as the desecration of remains and the exploitation of tragedy for profit.

This distinction serves as a significant character beat for the party, highlighting the friction between economic pragmatism and ethical conduct.
