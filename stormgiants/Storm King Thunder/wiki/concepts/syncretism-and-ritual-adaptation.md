# Syncretism and Ritual Adaptation

[[syncretism-and-ritual-adaptation]] is the process of evolving a religious practice by incorporating non-traditional or culturally specific elements. [[valphine]] utilizes this to reconcile her Drow heritage with her service to [[clergy-of-lathander]], such as using sunrise whistles or boar tusks in her sacraments.
