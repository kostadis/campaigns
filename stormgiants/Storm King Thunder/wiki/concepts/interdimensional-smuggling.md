# Interdimensional Smuggling

The practice of using specialized or malfunctioning planar machinery to move contraband across dimensions. This method allows organizations like [[house-sartorra]] to bypass traditional trade routes and customs, facilitating the movement of high-value items like [[midnight-tears]].
