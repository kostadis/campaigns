# Gnomengarde Governance

The political structure of [[gnomengarde]] is currently a subject of intense debate among its inhabitants, focusing on how leadership is distributed and how decisions are made.

## Key Debates
- **Bicameralism vs. Majority Rule**: There is a conflict between models of governance, such as the bicameral approach favored by [[fibblestib]] and the proposal by [[dabbledob]] to increase the number of kings to ensure a more effective votable majority.
- **Voting Integrity**: The theoretical possibility and design of a "tamper-proof voting box" remains a point of contention among the inventors.
