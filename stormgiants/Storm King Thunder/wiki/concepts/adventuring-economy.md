# The Adventuring Economy

**The Adventuring Economy** is a localized economic system used in Phandalin where adventurers are compensated with monster treasure and plunder in lieu of direct coin from the town treasury.

[[harbin-wester]] uses this system to justify the town's lack of liquid funds and to deflect demands for direct payment. He cites this arrangement as being sanctioned by [[lord-neverember]], framing it as a tax-free guarantee for those performing dangerous civic duties.
