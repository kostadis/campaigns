# Operational Intermediary

An [[operational-intermediary]] is a middle-management role within a criminal or predatory hierarchy. This individual serves as the essential bridge between high-level leadership (the "head") and the field operatives or bandit crews (the "hands").

### Function
- Maintaining the chain of command.
- Translating strategic goals into tactical field operations.
- Coordinating logistics and resource deployment (e.g., [[rot-tusk-ogres]]).

The death of an intermediary, such as [[jax]], can lead to a "decapitation" of field operations, leaving the leadership disconnected from their subordinates.
