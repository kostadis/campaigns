# Grounded-resident-npc

Grounded-resident-npc is a category of non-player character defined by transparency, emotional authenticity, and narrative purpose without dramatic arc, hidden agenda, or plot centrality. Unlike [[background-npc]] (who exists only to populate space) or [[information-asymmetry-npc-encounters]] (who conceal truth), a grounded resident is fully realized, emotionally resonant, and morally neutral — their value lies in their presence, not their secrets.

Dazlyn Grayshard and Norbus Ironrune are prime examples: they have clear goals, honest motivations, and warm, unmanipulative interactions. They do not manipulate the party, nor are they manipulated. They simply exist — prospecting, working, celebrating — and in doing so, make the world feel lived-in.

This archetype contrasts with the campaign’s dominant tropes: the tragic, the manipulative, the mysterious. Grounded residents do not need to be redeemed, revealed, or resolved. They are the quiet foundation of the world — the cooks, the merchants, the prospectors — whose integrity makes the extraordinary feel real.

This concept extends [[narrative-economy]]: maximum depth with minimal exposition. It also redefines [[background-npc]] — not all minor NPCs are inert. Some are quietly alive.
