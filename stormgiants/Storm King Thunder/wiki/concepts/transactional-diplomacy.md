# Transactional Diplomacy

A method of interaction where lethal threats and high-stakes objectives are balanced against material or social rewards. 

This concept is currently exemplified by the relationship between [[the-3hpp]] and [[bob-saget]]. Rather than direct combat, the parties engage in negotiations involving gold, biological specimens, and public performances to manage mutual risks and achieve specific goals.
