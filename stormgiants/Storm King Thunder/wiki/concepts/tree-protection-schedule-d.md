# Tree Protection Schedule D
A legal document Halisa was bound under, which she cited to refuse assistance from the party. Halisa was initially punished by the Seelie Court for speaking honestly about Queen Titania's attire, and she was released from her exile by Prince Thornacious, who impersonated the actual Prince Thornacious. Her release was framed as a punishment to her, not a reward.

## Evidence Supporting Claims
- Halisa's initial encounter with Thistle, where she cited "Tree Protection Schedule D" to refuse assistance.
- Halisa's declaration of intent to return every 100 years to taunt Helia.
- The sprite emissary's note that both Halisa and Helia were freed through the party's trickery.
