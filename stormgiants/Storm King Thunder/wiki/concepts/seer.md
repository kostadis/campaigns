# SEER
The SEER stands for Supreme Elders of the Earth Realm. They are a governing body of the Earth Realm, potentially influencing events in Parnast. The SEER is mentioned as a factor in the machinations that led to death and destruction in the town.

## Role and Influence
- **Role:** The SEER is a governing body of the Earth Realm.
- **Influence:** The SEER's actions and decisions have a bearing on the events in Parnast.

## Key Attributes
- **Secret organization:** Secret organization that Hsing serves as an agent and messenger for.
- **Central role:** Central to the narrative and operations of the story.
- **Operates with or through:** The Lord's Alliance.

## Connections
- **Hsing:** Agent and messenger.
- **Lord's Alliance:** Operates with or through the SEER.

## Notes
- Not explicitly named but referenced.
- Central to the SEER's operations and Hsing's role within it.
