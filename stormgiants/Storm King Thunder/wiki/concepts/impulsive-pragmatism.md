# Impulsive Pragmatism
**Impulsive Pragmatism** describes the juxtaposition of rash, instinctive behavior with calculated survival tactics. 

In the context of the kobold **Gen**, this trait demonstrates that he is not a mindless beast but a thinking survivor who occasionally struggles to contain his immediate impulses in favor of long-term tactical goals (such as offering treasure for safety).
