# Navigation
Navigation is a crucial technique used by Estrella aboard the cloud giant castle. It involves guiding the ship safely through the skies and alerting the party to their approach to Yartar.

## Importance
- Crucial for the ship's safe passage and the party's awareness of their surroundings.
- Estrella's role as navigator or lookout is vital for the party's success in their mission.
