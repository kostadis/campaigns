# Metaphysical Cold

A property identified in the [[breath-pouch]] harvested from [[cryovain]]. Unlike standard elemental frost, this cold is described as "metaphysical," suggesting it originates from a source beyond simple temperature reduction, potentially linked to the dragon's planar essence or the nature of its power.
