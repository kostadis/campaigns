# Metaphysical Cold

Metaphysical cold is a non-elemental, possibly planar or soul-corrupting phenomenon distinct from ordinary frost or ice magic. It is not merely a temperature effect — it is an absence, a draining of warmth from memory, spirit, or existential continuity.

A property identified in the [[breath-pouch]] harvested from [[cryovain]], this cold is described as “metaphysical,” suggesting it originates from a source beyond simple temperature reduction, potentially linked to the dragon's planar essence or the nature of its power. The breath pouch radiates this cold, described as “ancient” and heavy, suggesting it is tied to the dragon’s original essence or the planar instability of Icespire Hold.

Unlike elemental cold, which affects matter, metaphysical cold appears to affect the metaphysical — potentially eroding identity, severing spiritual bonds, or freezing the soul’s resonance. Its presence in Cryovain’s remains implies that the Carver’s domination may have drawn upon or amplified a deeper, older force — one that predates known magic systems. It may be connected to the same planar decay that enabled the Carver’s ritual, or to the silence of Annam, which has left cosmic gaps susceptible to such corruption.

Metaphysical cold is not a spell, but a residue — a haunting signature of beings whose souls have been touched by forces beyond natural order.
