# Meril's Choice

A theory regarding the deliberate selection of [[soma]] by [[meril]] as a successor or champion.

## Overview
The concept posits that Meril's decision to end [[soma]]'s training prematurely and gift her [[merils-staff]] was a calculated political or spiritual maneuver. According to [[xanthopoulos]], Meril did not act out of sentiment, but because he "picked her" for a specific, yet unrevealed, purpose.

## Implications
This "choice" has become a point of significant interest and contention within the [[emerald-enclave]], as it suggests a deviation from traditional druidic succession and implies a specific destiny or mission for [[soma]].
