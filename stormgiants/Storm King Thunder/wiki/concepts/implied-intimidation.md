# Implied Intimidation

A negotiation technique where physical cues and the display of weaponry are used to signal the potential for violence, thereby forcing compliance without initiating direct combat.

This was notably demonstrated by [[brewbarry]], who used a blood-stained axe and the rhythmic tapping of a halberd to break [[zeleen-varnaster]]'s resistance during the gold mine negotiations.
