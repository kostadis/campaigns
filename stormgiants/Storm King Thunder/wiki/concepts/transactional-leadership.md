# Transactional Leadership

A leadership style characterized by a focus on immediate mutual benefit and exchange rather than long-term loyalty or shared ideology. 

In the context of the [[whiskered-gang]], [[zeleen-varnaster]] utilizes this method to manage his crew and negotiate with external parties. Deals are struck based on tangible rewards—such as [[petunias-cheese]], shelter, or protection—and the relationship is maintained only as long as the exchange remains favorable to all parties.
