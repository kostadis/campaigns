# Stalemate at Grandfather Tree
Halisa and Helia were in a mutual stalemate at the Grandfather Tree, each refusing to sacrifice themselves to break the curse. Sleth later revealed there was never a true curse — the two dryads had effectively cursed each other through their mutual refusal to sacrifice themselves. Halisa was initially punished by the Seelie Court for speaking honestly about Queen Titania's attire, and she was released from her exile by Prince Thornacious, who impersonated the actual Prince Thornacious. Her release was framed as a punishment to her, not a reward.
