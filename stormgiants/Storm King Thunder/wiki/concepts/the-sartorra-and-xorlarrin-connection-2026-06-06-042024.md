# The "Sartorra" and "Xorlarrin" Connection

The overview describes a "sophisticated," "chilling," and "technocratic" threat posed by the relationship between [[house-sartorra]] and [[xorlarrin]]. This alliance is specifically linked to the trade and smuggling of [[midnight-tears]]. Their corporate/criminal synergy reflects the technocratic nature of Aletra's operations, though the full extent of their influence beyond these activities remains not fully detailed.
