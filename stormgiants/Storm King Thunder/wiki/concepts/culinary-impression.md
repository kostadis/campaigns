# Culinary Impression

A narrative phenomenon where a character is introduced to the party through sensory experiences—specifically food—rather than through direct dialogue or traditional social interaction. 

In the case of [[cooragh-struckt]], the character's presence and skill are established entirely through the quality and seasoning of her dishes at the [[wayside-inn]], creating a character beat without requiring immediate verbal engagement.
