# Orc Coordination Mechanisms

A growing body of evidence suggests that orc behavior in certain encounters is not driven solely by tribal instinct or brute force, but by hidden, non-biological coordination systems. These mechanisms override typical chaotic aggression, producing disciplined, synchronized attacks marked by unusual tactical precision and physical markings.

Key documented instances include:

- **Jarek’s Internal Hum**: A pre-attack internal sensation experienced only by Jarek, suggesting a non-physical signal triggering coordinated action.
- **Uniform Markings**: Orcs involved in the ambush bore identical, non-tribal symbols — possibly identifiers, ritual sigils, or resonance anchors.
- **Tactical Discipline**: The ambush was executed with timing and positioning inconsistent with spontaneous tribal raiding.

These traits align with other known phenomena in the setting: the layered command structures of Aletra Satora’s smuggling network, the silent control of the Oracle, and the psychic manipulation implied by the Headband of Intellect. The implication is that orcs may be acting as conduits or agents for an unseen authority — possibly drow, psionic entities, or planar influences tied to interdimensional operations.

This concept reframes orcs not as mindless savages, but as potentially manipulated components in a larger, hidden operational framework. Further investigation is needed to determine whether this is an isolated case or part of a systemic pattern.
