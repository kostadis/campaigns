# Sprite Emissary
The Seelie Sprite emissary later noted that both Halisa and Helia had been freed through the party's trickery. Halisa was initially punished by the Seelie Court for speaking honestly about Queen Titania's attire, and she was released from her exile by Prince Thornacious, who impersonated the actual Prince Thornacious. Her release was framed as a punishment to her, not a reward.
