# Financial Incentives
Used to gain Hyuth's trust and cooperation. Financial incentives were a key factor in Hyuth's decision to allow the party entry to his mansion. Despite his paranoia and antisocial tendencies, Hyuth accepted the financial compensation offered by Darathra Shendrel to cover the cost of repairs and allow the party to enter his home.

## Details
- **Context:** Hyuth's refusal to compensate the victims of the assassination attempts and his hostility towards the party upon their arrival suggest that financial incentives play a crucial role in his behavior.
- **Impact:** Hyuth's acceptance of financial compensation indicates that he is willing to be swayed by monetary benefits, even if it means compromising his principles.
- **Role in Narrative:** Financial incentives serve as a powerful tool to influence Hyuth's actions and decisions, highlighting his vulnerability to external pressures.
