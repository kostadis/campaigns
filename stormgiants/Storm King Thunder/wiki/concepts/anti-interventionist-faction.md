# Anti-Interventionist Faction

A major faction within the [[emerald-enclave]] led by figures such as [[adabra-gwynn]]. 

## Core Beliefs
The faction adheres to a philosophy of radical naturalism, believing that the multiverse maintains its own equilibrium. They argue that any attempt to "cleanse" evil or intervene in planar/natural processes—even for seemingly heroic reasons—causes cascading imbalances and attracts further instability. Their primary solution is total withdrawal and observation rather than active defense.
