# Demonic Portfolio of Jokes

A specialized form of demonic influence where comedy and humor serve as the medium for power, torment, and social manipulation. 

While highly effective for cultural infiltration and psychological warfare, this portfolio has caused [[bob-saget]] to be viewed as a "laughingstock" by other high-tier Abyssal entities. This creates a unique tension for the holder: the ability to manipulate mortal perception through laughter, contrasted with a loss of traditional martial or terrifying prestige among demonic peers.
