# Extravagant Gala

A high-society social event hosted by [[lord-cassian-meliame]] in [[neverwinter]].

## Narrative Function
The gala serves as the primary setting and objective for the party's current mission involving the delivery of a statue. It represents a convergence of noble interests and social maneuvering.
