# Ritual of Murderous Spectacle

A sophisticated method of ritualistic slaughter used by [[bob-saget]] to build power and a [[zombie-audience]].

The ritual relies on the intersection of theatricality and terror:
1. **Luring:** Victims are drawn in by the promise of entertainment or participation in a play.
2. **Execution:** Victims are murdered on stage in a highly theatrical manner.
3. **Animation:** A specialized caster (such as a Drow Mage) uses the corpses to create a permanent, animated audience.
4. **Diversion:** Real audiences are often diverted to a pocket dimension to ensure the ritual proceeds without interference from the uninitiated.
