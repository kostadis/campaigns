# Evidence-Based Pragmatism

A methodology that prioritizes proven results, practical applications, and empirical evidence over speculative, theoretical, or outlandish proposals.

In the context of [[gnomengarde]], this concept defines the professional divide between researchers like [[dabbledob]], who favor grounded solutions, and those like [[fibblestib]], who pursue unproven technological leaps.
