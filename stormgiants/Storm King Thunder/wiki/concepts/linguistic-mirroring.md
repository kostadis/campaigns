# Linguistic Mirroring

**Linguistic Mirroring** (or linguistic management) is a psychological and communicative technique used to build rapport or exert subtle control over an interlocutor. 

In the context of ser-kaelen, this involves absorbing the specific vocabulary, phrases, and rhetorical styles of an individual and reflecting them back during subsequent interactions. 

### Application
- **Rapport Building:** Making an individual feel understood and validated.
- **Manipulation:** Using an individual's own language to frame arguments or redirect their perceptions. For example, [[vukradin]] noted Kaelen's use of the phrase "conflict-free triumph" in a *Sending* message, which left the bard uncertain if the gesture was genuine or a calculated attempt to manage his perception of their alliance.
