# Pragmatic Fabrication

**Pragmatic Fabrication** is the practice of maintaining a false or exaggerated reputation for the sake of economic necessity or social stability. 

A primary example is seen in [[falcon-the-hunter]], who perpetuates the myth of being a legendary "dragonslayer" to ensure the profitability of his hunting lodge. This concept highlights the intersection of personal ego and the practical requirements of entrepreneurship in a dangerous frontier.
