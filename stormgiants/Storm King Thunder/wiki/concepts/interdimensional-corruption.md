# Interdimensional Corruption

[[interdimensional-corruption]] is a transformative and predatory magical phenomenon. Unlike standard [[planar-corruption]], this force appears to cause rapid, violent physical mutations, turning sentient beings into multi-headed, feral monstrosities.

The corruption is responsible for the fall of [[axeholm]] and the transformation of its ruler into [[the-dwarven-king-of-axeholm]]. It is characterized by its ability to overwrite the host's personality and grant a form of [[corrupted-resilience]].
