# Transactional Escort

A [[transactional-escort]] is a service provided under a formal contract, where a client pays for the protection or accompaniment of a party during transit.

## Narrative Function
This concept served as the primary narrative bridge that brought the adventuring party into the service of don-jon-raskin, moving them from [[phandalin]] to the mountain-toe-gold-mine.
