# Unseelie Combat Strategy

## Overview
During combat scenarios involving the Unseelie Court, specific tactical approaches are employed to influence opponents. A notable instance of this occurred during a battle involving the [[Deciduous Dryad]] and the [[Coniferous Dryad]].

## Tactics
The [[Deciduous Dryad]] utilizes a combat strategy centered on the following primary tactics:
* **Charm:** Used as a primary method to influence targets.
* **Social Manipulation:** Employed alongside charm to manipulate opponents during engagement.

In specific encounters, such as the combat involving Orsik, these tactics are used specifically to exert influence over the target.
