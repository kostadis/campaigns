# Wealth Laundering via Community Investment

A political and economic philosophy championed by [[jenna]] to justify the use of adventuring loot. 

The core argument posits that gold acquired through violent or questionable means ("blood money") can be effectively legitimized by being redistributed into local community projects and infrastructure. By turning chaotic, individualistic wealth into stable, public-facing community improvements, the source of the wealth is obscured and its social utility is maximized.

This concept stands in direct opposition to the "fair-trade" or restitution-based philosophy held by [[vukradin]], which insists that wealth must be returned to its rightful original owners rather than being repurposed by third parties.
