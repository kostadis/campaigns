# Tactical Pragmatism

In the context of [[borg-the-hammer]], [[tactical-pragmatism]] refers to a combat and leadership style that prioritizes efficiency and results over morality or tradition. 

### Characteristics
- **Use of Decoys:** Utilizing non-combatants or captives (e.g., [[alphonse-big-al-kalazorn]]) to manipulate enemy movement.
- **Ruthless Resource Management:** The immediate execution of assets (such as informants) that no longer provide value.
- **Force Splitting:** Deliberately dividing units to confuse pursuers and manage multiple tactical fronts.
