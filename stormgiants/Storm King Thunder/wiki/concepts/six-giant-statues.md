# Six Giant Statues
The Six Giant Statues are artifacts, each representing a lantern light consumed by the party's questions. They serve as a mechanism to ensure the oracle's answers remain truthful and to guide the party's actions.

## Key Features
- **Function:** Each question consumes one lantern light from the six giant statues.
- **Purpose:** To ensure the oracle's answers are always true and aligned with the party's actions.
