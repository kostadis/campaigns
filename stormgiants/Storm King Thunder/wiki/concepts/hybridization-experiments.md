# Hybridization Experiments

[[hybridization-experiments]] refers to the specialized and often cruel magical and alchemical research conducted by [[iymrith]].

## Methodology and Effects
The goal of these experiments is the magical fusion of disparate creature types, such as fey and dragon. The results are often unstable:
- **[[thistle-wendrod]]**: Received dragon eyes and the [[true-seeing]] ability.
- **[[laela]]**: Became a fey-dragon hybrid, experiencing uncontrolled transformations between forms, a state described as resembling lycanthropy rather than a stable fusion.

These experiments represent a core component of Kroskas's "mad science" theme and serve as a primary driver for the personal conflicts of the adventuring party.
