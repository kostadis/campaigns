# Theology of Searing Pain

The **Theology of Searing Pain** is an unorthodox interpretation of Lathanderite worship. It posits that the sun's heat and the resulting physical pain are purifying forces capable of cleansing the soul.

## Core Practices
Practitioners of this theology often engage in:
- **Ritual Burning:** Using solar heat or fire to mark the body.
- **Self-Flagellation:** Physical suffering as a means of spiritual ecstasy.
- **Ritual Blinding:** A practice associated with the "purifying" nature of light.

This concept is a primary driver for the activities of [[brin-bundlewine]] and the development of the [[temple-of-lathanders-searing-pain-of-justice]].
