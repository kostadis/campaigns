# Magic
Magic is a pervasive force in the world, influencing the behavior of giant lords and the outcomes of battles. Its presence adds complexity to the narrative and provides opportunities for the party to utilize their own magical abilities. Dral Thelev demonstrates his magical capabilities by using Silverymoon wine to teleport the party to Moongleam Tower.

## Description
Magic encompasses both supernatural forces and mystical abilities present in the narrative.

## Influence
- **Dral Thelev:** His magical capabilities are showcased through the use of Silverymoon wine to teleport the party to Moongleam Tower.
- **Harthos Zymorven:** His execution by decapitation is designed to prevent magical resurrection.

## Associated Characters
- **Dral Thelev:** Demonstrates magical abilities.
- **Harthos Zymorven:** Prevents magical resurrection through execution.
