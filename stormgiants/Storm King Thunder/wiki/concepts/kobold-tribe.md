# Kobold Tribe
The kobold tribe is a faction within the narrative, characterized by its intelligence networks and strategic actions. Leaders like Kragota-Threeteeth and Nug operate within this tribe, coordinating ambushes and other operations.

## Key Entities
- **Kragota-Threeteeth:** Leader of the kobold tribe's intelligence network
- **Nug:** Led the ambush group at the barricade
