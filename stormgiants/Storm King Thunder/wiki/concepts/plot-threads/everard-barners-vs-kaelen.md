# Everard Barney vs. Kaelen

**Status:** Open

Everard Barney, despite his initial anxiety and lack of experience, is making progress in directing Kaelen's play with the help of Thistle. His willingness to accept Thistle's suggestions, improvements in his staging approach, and his ability to work collaboratively with Amy demonstrate his growing competence as a director. However, his lingering discomfort with Kaelen—rooted in Kaelen’s family background and social standing—creates persistent tension between his professional ambitions and personal reservations, threatening to undermine the artistic synergy required to bring the play to life.
