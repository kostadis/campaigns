# Everard Barney vs. Other Directors

**Status:** Open  
**Created:** 2023-10-05  
**Updated:** 2026-06-11  

Everard Barney, despite his initial anxiety and lack of experience, is making progress in directing Kaelen's play with the help of Thistle. His willingness to accept Thistle's suggestions, improvements in his staging approach, and his ability to work collaboratively with Amy demonstrate his growing competence as a director. However, his discomfort with Kaelen and his family background continues to create tension, complicating his professional aspirations and challenging his ability to fully embrace the role he is striving to master.
