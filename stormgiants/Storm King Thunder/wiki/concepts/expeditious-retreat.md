# Expeditious Retreat
Expeditious Retreat is a spell that allows a creature to move up to its speed without provoking opportunity attacks. It was used by Orsik to close distance and strike Thane Braxow during the encounter.

## Usage
- Used by Orsik to close distance and strike Braxow.

## Description
- Allows a creature to move up to its speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
