# Sacrament of Pain and Redemption

An unintended theological phenomenon arising from a sermon delivered by [[valphine]] at the [[clergy-of-lathander]]. 

While not a formal doctrine, the sermon had a profound psychological impact on observers, specifically [[teega-the-smith]]. It is characterized by a complex intersection of suffering and spiritual cleansing, leaving those who witness it feeling both disturbed and spiritually conflicted.
