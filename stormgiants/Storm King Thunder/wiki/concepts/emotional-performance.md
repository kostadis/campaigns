# Emotional Performance

The use of manufactured emotional responses to manipulate a target's moral or emotional compass. In the context of high-level con artistry, this involves:

- **Theatrical Injury:** Feigning physical pain to elicit sympathy.
- **Religious Invocation:** Using divine authority or piety to establish false credibility.
- **Wounded Pride:** Performing indignation to deflect suspicion or guilt.

Effective practitioners, such as [[sylvine-wintermoon]], can drop these performances instantaneously once they are no longer useful, revealing the underlying coldness of the operation.
