# Talosian Conspiracy

The [[talosian-conspiracy]] is a plot uncovered through translated letters found in the Circle of Thunder caves. The conspiracy involves several key players and a fallback mechanism for summoning rituals.

## Key Details
- **Key Figures:** Includes [[narux]] (who maintained a "henge"), [[grannoc]] (described as reckless), and an entity known as [[the-chosen]].
- **The Fallback Mechanism:** The conspiracy relies on a hierarchy of summoning sites: if a "henge" falls, a "manse" will summon in its place. If the "manse" falls, a specific writer will summon personally.
- **The K Signature:** A fragment of a letter signed only with the letter "K" contains the warning: *"dawn cannot rise where the storm already lives."*
