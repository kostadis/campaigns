# Corrupted Resilience

A phenomenon observed in entities afflicted by [[interdimensional-corruption]]. [[corrupted-resilience]] allows a creature to ignore or shrug off grievous physical wounds, maintaining an aggressive combat tempo despite damage that would typically be fatal or incapacitating to natural organisms.
