# True Believer (verified)

A person whose ideological convictions are metaphysically authentic, confirmed not by behavior or rhetoric, but by the Resonance power of Meril’s Staff. Unlike [[true-seeing]], which reveals hidden truths or illusions, True Believer Verification validates the *sincerity* of a person’s belief system — not its accuracy, but its internal coherence and non-deceptive origin.

Adabra Gwynn is a verified True Believer. Vukradin confirmed: “Yeah, no, she’s a true believer.” This means her anti-interventionist philosophy is not the product of manipulation, delusion, or deceit — it is a metaphysical truth to her. She is not lying. She is not mad. She is *right*, in the only way that matters to her.

This concept extends [[truth-constraint-mechanism]] into the realm of ideology: truth is not absolute, but *verified*. The Oracle of the Eye of the World speaks constrained truth; a True Believer lives it. Both are bound by limits — one by divine law, the other by metaphysical authenticity.

This makes Adabra uniquely dangerous: she cannot be argued out of her position, because her position is *true*. To defeat her is not to refute her — it is to outmaneuver her politically, morally, or procedurally. Her defeat at the Enclave council was not ideological — it was procedural. Her threat remains because her truth endures.
