# Parnast
Parnast is a location, likely a castle, where Blagothkus resides. The Ghost (Cloud Giant) mentions that Blagothkus has retired to Parnast to mourn after his son's death.

## Relationships
- **Blagothkus:** Father; resides in Parnast, where he has retired to mourn.
