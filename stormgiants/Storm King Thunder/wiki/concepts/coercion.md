# Coercion
Coercion refers to the phenomenon of Jarlor being forced into spying for Chief Kurglow, who held his family hostage in an orc cave as leverage. This demonstrates the use of external pressure and manipulation to compel Jarlor to act against his will. The core claim is that Jarlor was coerced into spying, which is supported by multiple sources including Jarlor's own account and his interactions with Orsik.
