# Spectral Directionality

A phenomenon of non-verbal communication used by spectral entities. Rather than utilizing speech or telepathy, the entity uses a fixed, intense, or mournful gaze to direct the attention of the living toward a specific location, threat, or object of importance. 

This was observed when [[lady-alagondar]]'s ghost stared toward the [[ice-spire-mountains-white-dragon-connection-2026-06-06-133513]], effectively guiding [[vukradin]] to identify the presence of a White Dragon.
