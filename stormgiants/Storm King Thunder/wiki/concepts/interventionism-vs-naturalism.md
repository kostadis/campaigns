# Interventionism vs. Naturalism

This is the core ideological divide within the [[emerald-enclave]], stemming from the historical actions of [[malfire]] during the [[war-of-the-dragons]].

## Interventionism
The belief that organized groups (like the Enclave) have a moral or practical obligation to intervene in global events to prevent catastrophe. Interventionists view the coalition against Tiamat as a necessary and heroic act.

## Naturalism
The belief in maintaining a balance and adhering to a policy of non-interference. Naturalists argue that the intervention triggered by [[malfire]] caused a chain reaction of cataclysms, including the [[war-of-the-giants]].
