# Corrupted Data Manifestation

A visual phenomenon where a magical or spectral entity appears to "glitch" or flicker, resembling corrupted digital information.

This was notably observed in [[the-chief-accountant]], whose form consisted of shifting geometric shapes and luminous flickering, despite being a fantasy-based entity. This suggests a unique form of [[planar-corruption]] or technological-magical interference.
