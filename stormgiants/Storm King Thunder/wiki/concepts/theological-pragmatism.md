# Theological Pragmatism

**Theological Pragmatism** is the decision to adopt a new faith based on perceived power, utility, or survival advantages rather than deep-seated spiritual conviction or divine revelation. It serves as a functional counter-ideology to the pure zealotry found in [[talosian-devotion]].

## Methodology and Evangelism

As a method of evangelism, this approach is employed by [[vukradin]] to convert followers to the [[clergy-of-lathander]]. Rather than relying solely on dogma, the methodology emphasizes:

* **The Spectacle:** Utilizing the religious presentation to create impact.
* **Material Connection:** Connecting theological concepts (such as the light of the dawn) to the immediate material reality of the listener.

## Historical Examples

The most notable application of this concept is seen in the conversion of prutha. He justified his defection from the [[circle-of-thunder]] to the [[lathanderian-practice-valphine]] by stating that Lathander offered "more power" than Talos.
