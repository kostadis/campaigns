# Tactical Disengagement

The ability of a combatant to prioritize survival and mission integrity by retreating under pressure rather than fighting a losing skirmish. 

[[the-orc-scout]] demonstrated this capability by withdrawing from a wounded state to regroup, though his subsequent flight from [[valphine]] suggests a distinction between disciplined tactical retreat and fear-driven flight.
