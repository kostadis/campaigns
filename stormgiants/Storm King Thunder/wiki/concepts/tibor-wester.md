# Tibor Wester

Brother of Harbin Wester and a wildcat logger operating in the forests near Phandalin. His logging operation is not officially sanctioned, yet Harbin uses his position to post a civic supply delivery quest on his behalf — suggesting complicity, if not direct involvement.

Tibor’s existence is a quiet indicator of Harbin’s informal graft: the use of administrative power to benefit family. His operations likely contribute to environmental degradation and regional instability, yet remain unchallenged due to Harbin’s influence. He is never seen by the party, but his presence is felt through the supply quest and the implied exploitation of public resources.

Tibor represents the personal dimension of systemic corruption — the unspoken favor, the quiet nepotism that sustains bureaucratic power. His identity remains unresolved: is he a willing participant, a pawn, or unaware of his brother’s manipulation? His silence, like Harbin’s, speaks volumes.
