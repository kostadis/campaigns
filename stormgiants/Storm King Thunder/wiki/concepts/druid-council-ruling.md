# Druid Council Ruling

A formal deliberative process and legal mechanism used by the [[emerald-enclave]] to decide the factional direction of the order.

## Function
The council serves as the primary venue for debating ideological shifts, such as the tension between [[interventionism]] and the [[anti-interventionist-faction]]. Rulings are based on the ability of factions to present coherent, actionable responses to ecological and planar threats.

## Recent Precedent
In a recent session, the council used the inability of the anti-interventionist representatives to provide specific protocols for handling [[motivated-blight]] and planar anomalies as a basis for rejecting [[adabra-gwynn]]'s stance. This established a precedent that ideological purity without practical application is insufficient for official Enclave policy.
