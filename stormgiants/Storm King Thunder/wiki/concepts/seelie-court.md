# Seelie Court
The Seelie Court is the ruling body of the fairy realm, known for its aesthetic and cultural practices. It is the home of the Dryads, who are guardians of nature. The Seelie Court is also where Halisa served as a courtier, though she was initially punished for speaking honestly about Queen Titania's attire. She was later released from her exile by Prince Thornacious, who impersonated the actual Prince Thornacious, framing it as a punishment rather than a reward.

## Halisa's Exile and Release
Halisa was initially punished by the Seelie Court for her honesty regarding Queen Titania's attire. This act of speaking truthfully was seen as a betrayal of the court's aesthetic sensibilities. However, her release was not a reward but rather a form of punishment, orchestrated by Prince Thornacious to demonstrate the severity of her offense.
