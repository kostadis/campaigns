# Overview
This wiki covers the narrative universe described in the source document "Everard Barney". The main focus is on Everard Barney, a theater director who is working on Kaelen's play at the Talking Troll. The wiki includes pages on key entities like Everard Barney, Thistle, and Kaelen, as well as concepts such as staging, managing actors, and the River Surbin. Additionally, there are queries exploring Everard Barney's motivations and connections to Kaelen.

## Key Entities
- **Everard Barney**: Theater director and playwright
- **Thistle**: Theater advisor and coach
- **Kaelen**: Playwright and director
