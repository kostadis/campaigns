# Whispering Grove Incident

A pivotal event involving the death of a Treant and the subsequent corruption of a sacred grove in the Whispering Woods. The incident has become the primary catalyst for the current political crisis within the [[emerald-enclave]], serving as the central piece of evidence used by [[adabra-gwynn]] to argue against the party's "careless interventionism."
