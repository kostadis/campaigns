# Wormblod's True Status

The "Truth Gap" regarding whether Wormblod is actually dead or merely hidden is a critical narrative tension. If he is alive, the party faces consequences from the Coatl; if he is dead, his role as a "corruptor" remains a historical fact. A dedicated page should track this ambiguity and any evidence found during future investigations.
