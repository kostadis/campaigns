# Ethical Negotiation

A social technique where a negotiator directly addresses a potential client's moral or ethical objections to facilitate a contract. 

In practice, this involves identifying the values of the opposing party (such as the moral code of [[vukradin]]) and providing specific, verbal assurances that align the business objective with those values. This method is a hallmark of high social intelligence and pragmatic business conduct.
