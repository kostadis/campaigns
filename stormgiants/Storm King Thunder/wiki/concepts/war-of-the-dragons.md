# War of the Dragon

The war-of-the-dragon was a pivotal historical conflict centered around the summoning of Tiamat. The war serves as a significant benchmark for the effectiveness of various factions and forced major political shifts within the [[emerald-enclave]], influenced by figures such as [[malfire]] and [[delaan-winterhound]].

## Key Events and Military Actions
During the conflict, a strike force led by [[milo-goodbarrel]] successfully breached an inner sanctum to stop a ritual. This specific engagement is frequently cited in historical discussions regarding the martial capabilities of the [[order-of-the-gauntlet]].
