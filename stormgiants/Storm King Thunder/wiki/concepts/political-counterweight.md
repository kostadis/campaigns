# Political Counterweight

A [[political-counterweight]] refers to a figure whose mere presence or reputation shifts the power dynamics of a political body, even in the absence of formal procedural authority (such as a veto). 

In the context of the [[emerald-enclave]], [[reidoth]] acts as a stabilizer. His presence prevents radical elements, such as [[adabra-gwynn]]'s faction, from exercising unilateral control, as his influence changes the social and political "texture" of decision-making processes.
