# External Shadows

A mysterious and "foreign and calculating" force identified by [[miral]] as the true architect behind [[moesko]]'s actions. 

## Nature
Unlike standard religious zealotry, these "shadows" appear to manipulate existing faiths—specifically [[talosian-devotion]]—to serve a different, more calculated agenda. This suggests a layer of puppet-mastery operating behind the scenes of known religious conflicts.
