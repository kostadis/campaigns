# Interventionism

In the context of the [[emerald-enclave]], interventionism is the practice of actively fighting "evil," planar threats, or biological blights to protect the natural order. 

While proponents see this as necessary defense, critics (specifically the [[anti-interventionist-faction]]) argue that interventionism is a "war on nature" that forces evil to respond in kind, ultimately creating more chaos than it resolves.
