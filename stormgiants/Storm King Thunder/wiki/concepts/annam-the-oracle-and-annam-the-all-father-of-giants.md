# Annam the Oracle and Annam the All-Father of Giants
Annam the Oracle and Annam the All-Father of Giants are two distinct entities associated with the Ghost (Cloud Giant). While Annam the Oracle is the haunting spirit bound to the oracle chamber, Annam the All-Father of Giants is a separate entity known to Unla Kee and associated with the Fishyard. Both entities are connected to the Ghost (Cloud Giant), but their roles and relationships differ.

## Key Differences
- **Role:** Annam the Oracle is the haunting spirit bound to the oracle chamber, while Annam the All-Father of Giants is a separate entity.
- **Relationship:** Annam the Oracle is the ghost's primary connection, while Annam the All-Father of Giants is a peripheral figure.
