# The Ordning Shattering

The collapse of the cosmic hierarchy that governed giant society, once maintained by divine order under Annam the Allfather. Once a stable, divinely sanctioned structure, the Ordning disintegrated into chaos, warfare, and extinction-level decline.

## Spiritual Collapse: The Silence of Annam
Annam’s prolonged silence is now understood as the foundational cause of the Ordning’s fall. His withdrawal of divine presence led to:
- The despair of King Hekaton, who abandoned his role as guardian of order
- The fracturing of the divine patrilineage, as Annam’s offspring turned against each other
- The erosion of faith among the giant races, leading to cultural and spiritual decay

This spiritual dimension transforms the Ordning’s collapse from a mere political or military failure into a cosmic tragedy of abandonment.

## Consequences
- Interplanar warfare among giant deities
- Loss of identity and purpose among giant subraces
- Rise of rogue entities exploiting the vacuum (e.g., [[adimond-kroskas]])
- Emergence of soul-manipulation and false identities as replacements for divine order

## Historical Context
The Imperator explicitly cites Annam’s silence as the root cause of giant decline. This theological interpretation is now central to understanding the Ordning’s fall, superseding earlier accounts that emphasized only mortal conflict.
