# Tribal Fracturing

**Tribal Fracturing** refers to the phenomenon where a cohesive social group splits into competing, often antagonistic, factions following a cataclysmic event. 

In the current regional context, the displacement of orc tribes by [[cryovain]] led to significant fracturing. This resulted in the emergence of cultists, brigands, and independentist groups, fundamentally altering the social landscape of the Sword Mountains.
