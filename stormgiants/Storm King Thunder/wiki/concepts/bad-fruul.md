# Bad Fruul
- **Role:** Goblin warlord
- **Faction:** Bad Fruul's warband
- **First Appearance:** The party encountered Grik when they arrived at Ambush Gap, where he commanded his gang from a raised tile surface overlooking the trail.
- **Relationships:** Direct superior to Grik; central antagonist in the narrative.

## Key Attributes
- **Regional Threat:** Mentioned by Hsing.
- **Central Concept:** Central to the narrative and operations of the story.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Enforce his control over Ambush Gap and the surrounding area; rob and neutralize travelers on his orders.
- **Ruthless and Pragmatic:** Bad Fruul operates through intimidation, often using his subordinates to carry out his orders. He is ruthless and pragmatic, willing to use any means necessary to achieve his goals.

## History with the Party
- **Ambush Gap:** Bad Fruul orchestrated the ambush at Ambush Gap, arranging for poison to be deployed ahead of the party's arrival to weaken them before engaging.
- **Loot:** His letter found in Grik's body reveals his intention to seize Parnast.

## Current Status
- **Active.** Still controlling Ambush Gap and planning further actions.

## Relationships
- **Grik:** Direct subordinate; Bad Fruul gave him explicit written orders to command the ambush.
- **The Party:** Central antagonist; Bad Fruul's letter reveals his intention to seize Parnast.
