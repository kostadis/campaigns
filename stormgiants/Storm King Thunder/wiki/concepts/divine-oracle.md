# Truth-Constraint Mechanism

A **Truth-Constraint Mechanism** is a system that delivers truth while bound by fixed, non-negotiable limits. Whether these constraints are divine (sacred covenants) or institutional (bureaucratic limitations), they serve to prevent the abuse of information, ensure humility among seekers, and maintain control over knowledge. Under this mechanism, truth is transformed from a free gift into a transaction; it is not a revelation for all, but a privilege granted only to those who submit to its specific structure—be that through ritual, ceremony, or protocol.

## Divine Manifestation: The Oracle
The primary example of a divine Truth-Constraint Mechanism is the **Divine Oracle**. As a sentient, limited-capacity conduit of divine knowledge, the Oracle does not act; it only answers. It functions as a mechanism—perhaps a relic, construct, or echo—that preserves and dispenses wisdom without altering the natural order.

The Oracle of the Eye of the World is the only known example in the campaign. It speaks exclusively on behalf of [[annam-the-oracle-and-annam-the-all-father-of-giants]], citing his collected sayings as its sole authority. Its constraints are not flaws but "covenantal features"—divine disciplines designed to preserve the sanctity of truth:
*   **Ritualized Interaction:** It operates under strict rules, such as being limited to six questions per day and requiring specific ritual acts (e.g., the extinguishing of torches) to end an interaction.
*   **Immunity to Manipulation:** The Oracle cannot lie, but it is immune to deception. It may refuse to answer trick questions or react with mockery toward those attempting to manipulate it.
*   **Bounded Scope:** It provides factual knowledge rather than prophecy. It answers specific, bounded questions rather than offering open-ended visions of the future.

This system redefines [[divine-silence]]: the deity has not abandoned the world but has chosen to communicate through these constrained, non-intrusive channels. Truth is available, but only to those who honor its boundaries.

## Secular Parallel: The SEER
In contrast to the sacred covenant of the Oracle, institutional systems like the [[seer]] operate under "compromise" constraints. As an intelligence network with limited coverage, the SEER provides accurate information (such as the location of a target) but is hampered by human limitations:
*   **Systemic Gaps:** Its failures are caused by blind spots, incomplete data, and operational errors.
*   **Accidental Limitations:** Unlike the Oracle’s intentional constraints, the SEER's gaps are flaws born of chaos rather than design.

## Conceptual Distinctions
The Truth-Constraint Mechanism distinguishes between different modes of "knowing":
*   **Truth vs. Perception:** While [[true-seeing]] enhances sensory perception and clarity, a Truth-Constraint Mechanism provides factual knowledge regardless of the observer's senses.
*   **Fact vs. Prophecy:** Unlike prophecy, which deals with potential futures, these mechanisms provide specific, current facts within a defined scope.

Ultimately, this mechanism ensures that in a world where direct intervention is forbidden by both gods and governments, wisdom remains a mirror rather than a weapon. It establishes the fundamental tension of the setting: truth can be constrained by law (divine) or by chaos (human). One is a covenant; the other, a compromise.
