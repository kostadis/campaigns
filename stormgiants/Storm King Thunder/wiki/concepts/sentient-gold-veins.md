# Sentient Gold Veins

The concept of [[sentient-gold-veins]] refers to the belief that gold deposits possess temperament, personality, or a form of consciousness. 

According to practitioners like [[dwolin-the-grout]], these veins must be treated with "absolute respect" and "coaxed" rather than simply extracted. This belief drives specific [[ritualistic-mining]] behaviors and is the primary cause of the current stoppage at the mountain-toe-gold-mine.
