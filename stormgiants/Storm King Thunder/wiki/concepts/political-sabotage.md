# Political Sabotage

The use of strategic interference to undermine the reputation, authority, or stability of a political entity or noble house.

Current intelligence suggests that [[house-margaster]] is employing [[political-sabotage]] against [[house-meliame]]. Rather than direct military or economic conflict, the goal appears to be the interception of significant artifacts (such as the lighthouse statue) to cause public embarrassment and social degradation of the target house.
