# Intelligence Failure as Catalyst

The phenomenon where a single, localized tactical or intelligence error triggers a systemic collapse of loyalty and organizational structure. 

In the case of [[the-carver]]'s network, the failure of [[borg]] to eliminate all witnesses during a specific operation created a cascade of consequences: it compromised the safety of the spymaster [[sister-kaella]], leading to her loss of trust in the organization and her eventual defection.
