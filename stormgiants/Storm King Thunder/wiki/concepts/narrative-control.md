# Narrative Control

**Narrative Control** is a political technique used to shape public perception and historical memory of events. 

In the context of Phandalin, [[harbin-wester]] utilizes this method by orchestrating public celebrations, speeches, and festivals following major victories achieved by the adventuring party. By positioning himself at the forefront of these events, he effectively binds his name to the achievements of others, ensuring that the official record credits his leadership or management for successes he did not personally earn.
