# Social Disruption
Potential negative impact of KP's theoretical framework on social harmony. Specifically, KP's work on multi-planar economic efficiency may lead to social disruption due to the unintended consequences of achieving optimal economic performance across multiple planes. Linked to [[multi-planar-economics]].
