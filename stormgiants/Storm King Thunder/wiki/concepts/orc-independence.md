# Orc Independence

**Orc Independence** is the ideological belief that orcish tribes should pursue self-determination and serve only their own interests. This concept stands in direct opposition to the influence of external religious or political organizations, most notably the [[cult-of-talos]].

This ideology has become a primary driver for splinter factions within displaced orc populations, as seen in the motivations of [[shrimpy]].
