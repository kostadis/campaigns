# The "Dawn" Philosophy

The "Dawn" philosophy is a belief system originating from the orcish shamanic traditions of prutha's lineage. 

Its central tenet is: *"Dawn comes for everyone, even those born in shadow."*

This philosophy acts as a thematic bridge between traditional orcish heritage and the worship of Lathander. It suggests a universal inevitability of renewal and light, providing a framework for individuals to transition from destructive faiths (like [[talosian-devotion]]) toward more constructive ones.
