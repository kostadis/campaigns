# Talosian Devotion

A religious practice and ideology centered around the deity [[talos]]. 

## Characteristics
Based on observations of the [[the-anchorite-half-orc]], Talosian devotion is marked by:
- **Cold Pragmatism:** Followers view death and destruction as tools or inevitable obstacles rather than tragedies.
- **Lack of Empathy:** Devotion to the deity's will often supersedes communal or personal bonds, even among fellow cultists.
- **Destructive Purpose:** The ideology drives followers to engage in raiding and chaos to satisfy the nature of their god.
