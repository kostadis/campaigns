# Illicit Poison Trade (Interdimensional)

The interdimensional poison trade is a specialized criminal enterprise that leverages planar instability to produce, transport, and distribute toxins that defy conventional biological and magical defenses. Central to this trade is [[midnight-tears]], a poison that does not kill but *unmakes* — scattering the victim’s soul across the Astral Sea and rendering resurrection impossible.

Produced by [[house-sartorra]] using alchemical processes refined over generations, the poison is stabilized using dwarven engineering and smuggled via [[model-1489-spatial-rectification-calliope]] devices. Its value lies not only in its lethality, but in its psychological terror: victims are left alive, aware, and unraveling — a slow, inescapable fate that makes it ideal for political assassinations and elite intimidation.

This trade is not merely about profit; it is about control. The poison is used to eliminate rivals, silence whistleblowers (e.g., the Chief Accountant), and enforce loyalty within the drow hierarchy. Its distribution is tightly controlled by unseen authorities like “KP,” ensuring that only those deemed trustworthy receive access.

The trade exemplifies the fusion of biological warfare, planar technology, and bureaucratic secrecy. It represents a new tier of drow criminality — one that does not rely on open violence, but on the quiet, inevitable erosion of identity and existence.

**Key Products:**  
- Midnight Tears (primary)  
- Other unconfirmed planar toxins (rumored)  

**Key Players:**  
- [[house-sartorra]] — Producer  
- [[xorlarrin]] — Distributor or parent organization  
- [[aletra-satora]] — Field operative  
- [[kp]] — Oversight authority  

**Significance:**  
This trade transforms poison from a weapon into a philosophical tool — a means of erasing not just life, but legacy.
