# Post-War Resource Constraints

[[post-war-resource-constraints]] refers to the economic and logistical limitations faced by governing bodies following large-scale conflicts, such as the Uthgardt Barbarian invasion.

These constraints drive pragmatic political decisions, such as [[lord-neverember]]'s reliance on the [[adventurer-clearance-program]] rather than traditional military force. Such constraints necessitate the use of unconventional, low-overhead methods to maintain order and secure borders.
