# Unnamed Faith

A spiritual or philosophical framework left to [[soma]] by [[meril]].

## Description
Distinct from the established druidic philosophies of the [[emerald-enclave]], this "unnamed faith" was part of the legacy Meril left behind alongside his half-finished lessons. The specific tenets and nature of this faith remain a mystery to both [[soma]] and the party.
