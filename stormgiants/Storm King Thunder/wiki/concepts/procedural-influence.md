# Procedural Influence

The idea that a leader's presence does not necessarily change the formal rules of a proceeding, but changes the "texture" or social feasibility of certain political maneuvers. For example, while [[reidoth]] may not be able to procedurally stop a specific speaker (like [[adabra-gwynn]]) from acting, his physical presence at a council can influence the social weight and impact of those actions.
