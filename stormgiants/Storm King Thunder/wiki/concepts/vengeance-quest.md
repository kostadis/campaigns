# Vengeance Quest
A Vengeance Quest is a task given by a haunting spirit or oracle to a party, typically involving the execution of a specific action to satisfy the entity's unfinished business. In this case, the Ghost of Annam's Oracle tasks the party with killing his father, Blagothkus, to fulfill his unfinished business.

## Key Elements
- **Entity:** The haunting spirit or oracle issuing the quest.
- **Action:** The specific action required to complete the quest.
- **Unfinished Business:** The reason for the quest, often stemming from the entity's past.
