# Theology of Guilt

**Theology of Guilt** (also described as "Philosophy as a Collar") is a method of psychological and political manipulation where an individual's trauma and exhaustion are reframed as a moral or philosophical stance.

[[adabra-gwynn]] utilized this technique on [[falcon-the-hunter]], convincing him that his desire to intervene in regional conflicts was a moral failing rather than a natural instinct. By "putting a collar on his guilt and calling it philosophy," she was able to align his personal exhaustion with the political goals of the [[anti-interventionist-faction]].
