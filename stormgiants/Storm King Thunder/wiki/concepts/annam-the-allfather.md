# Annam the Allfather
Annam the Allfather is a character known to Unla Kee and associated with the Fishyard. He is a separate entity from Annam the Oracle and plays a peripheral role in the narrative.

## Relationships
- **Annam the Oracle:** Connected to Annam the Oracle, but Annam the Allfather is a separate entity.
- **Unla Kee:** Known to Unla Kee and associated with the Fishyard.
