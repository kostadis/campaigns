# Planar Instability

A magical phenomenon characterized by the blurring of boundaries between different planes of existence. 

## Manifestations
In the [[whispering-wood]], this instability manifests through:
- **Physical Flickering:** Entities appearing to shimmer or leave afterimages.
- **Spatial Anomalies:** Uncontrolled short-distance teleportation.
- **Biological Mutation:** Rapid, unpredictable changes in physical forms (e.g., the shifting antlers of [[the-stag]]).

This phenomenon is closely linked to [[planar-corruption]] and [[interdimensional-corruption]], representing a breakdown in the natural order of the material plane.
