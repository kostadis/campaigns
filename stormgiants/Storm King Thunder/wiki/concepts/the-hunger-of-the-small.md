# The Hunger of the Small

A psychological observation regarding the dangerous volatility of individuals who have experienced prolonged oppression or "smallness" and are suddenly granted authority or power over others. 

This concept was noted by [[vukradin]] and [[valphine]] in relation to corbin, suggesting that his transition from a victim of circumstance to a person with the authority to harm others (such as his assignment to guard [[sister-kaella]]) created a volatile and unpredictable internal state.
