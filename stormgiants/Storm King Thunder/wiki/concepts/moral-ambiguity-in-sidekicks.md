# Moral Ambiguity in Sidekicks

**Moral Ambiguity in Sidekicks** describes the tension between a companion's utility and loyalty versus their questionable moral alignment or passivity during violent or significant events.

This is currently observed in boney, who provides critical support and levity but has been criticized for his inaction during the death of corbin.
