# Spy Channel
The Spy Channel is a tool used for covert communication between Jarlor and Chief Kurglow. It was utilized by Jarlor to transmit a message on behalf of the party, which was composed by Orsik. The message was a comically non-threatening warning about denying the orc whelp dessert and a bedtime story. This tool highlights the coercive nature of Jarlor's situation and the reliance on external authority figures to act.
