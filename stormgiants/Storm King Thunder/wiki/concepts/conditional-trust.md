# Conditional Trust

A state of interpersonal or political relationship where cooperation is maintained only so long as specific conditions are met or as long as the cost of betrayal is perceived to be higher than the benefit of compliance.

The relationship between the adventuring party and the [[whiskered-gang]] is defined by [[conditional-trust]]. While a formal agreement exists, there is significant suspicion that [[zeleen-varnaster]] may attempt to usurp the mountain-toe-gold-mine if the opportunity arises.
