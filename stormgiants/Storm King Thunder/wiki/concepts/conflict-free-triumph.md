# Conflict-Free Triumph

**Conflict-Free Triumph** is a specific rhetorical phrase used by [[vukradin]] that was later strategically mirrored by [[ser-kaelen]].

While originally intended by the bard to describe a desired outcome, its adoption by Kaelen in a magical *Sending* message served as a prime example of [[linguistic-mirroring]]. This usage left the bard questioning whether the sentiment was a genuine expression of shared values or a calculated attempt by the [[order-of-the-gauntlet]] to manage his loyalty and perception of their alliance.
