# Vukradin

Vukradin is a pivotal NPC whose ideological shift from naturalist to Order of the Gauntlet member triggers Adabra’s personal vendetta and the central moral conflict. His motivations, internal conflict over music as motive, and post-council actions are critical but undocumented in a dedicated page.
