# Quest Baiting

**Quest Baiting** is a sophisticated tactical maneuver used by organized criminal elements to manipulate the local "adventurer economy."

## Definition
The practice of orchestrating a high-profile crime (such as a raid on a prominent location like [[butterskull-ranch]]) specifically to trigger a bounty or quest on a local authority's board (e.g., [[harbin-wester]]'s board in [[phandalin]]).

## Objective
The goal is to use the predictable nature of adventurers—who respond to lucrative quests—to lure them into a controlled environment or a pre-prepared ambush. This turns the heroes' own sense of duty and economic motivation into a weapon against them.
