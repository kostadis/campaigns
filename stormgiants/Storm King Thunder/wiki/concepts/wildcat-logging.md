# Wildcat Logging

[[wildcat-logging]] refers to the practice of operating timber harvesting activities outside of established forestry regulations, permit frameworks, and sanctioned cutting agreements. 

In the current context, this was the primary criminal enterprise run by [[tibor-wester]], involving the exploitation of workers who were often unaware of the legal violations being committed.
