# Artifact Identification and Unlocking

The process of determining the history, magical properties, and functional mechanisms of recovered artifacts. 

In the current context, this concept is tied to the expertise of [[elara-seasong-meliame]], who is sought out by the adventuring party to provide clarity on their acquired loot. This process is critical for transitioning items from mere "loot" to functional tools or political assets.
