# Compromise Cohabitation

[[compromise-cohabitation]] is a diplomatic arrangement brokered to allow two conflicting groups to occupy the same space for mutual benefit or stability.

## Application
In the context of the mountain-toe-gold-mine, this policy was implemented to allow mining operations to continue alongside a population of wererats. The arrangement was designed to balance the safety concerns of the overseer, [[don-jon-raskin]], with the social de-escalation and stability goals pursued by [[vukradin]].
