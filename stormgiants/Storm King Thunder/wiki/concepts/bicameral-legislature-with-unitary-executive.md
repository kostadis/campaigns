# Bicameral Legislature with a Unitary Executive

A specific model of political governance advocated by [[fibblestib]] as a means of reforming the [[dual-monarchy]] of [[gnomengarde]].

## Structure
The model proposes a two-chambered legislative body (bicameral) paired with a single, powerful executive branch (unitary executive). This suggests a preference for highly structured, efficient, and potentially authoritarian governance to replace or augment the existing shared rule system.
