# Two-Stage Methodology

A tactical approach used by predators like [[sylvine-wintermoon]] to acquire wealth or assets. The methodology consists of two distinct phases:

1. **Psychological Manipulation:** An initial attempt to use persuasion, social engineering, or [[emotional-performance]] to achieve a goal without conflict.
2. **Lethal Escalation:** If the initial deception fails, the practitioner immediately pivots to direct, often overwhelming, physical violence.

This method is designed to save resources by securing the target through words first, while maintaining a contingency for total combat.
