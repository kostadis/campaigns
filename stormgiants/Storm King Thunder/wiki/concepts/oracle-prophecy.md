# Oracle Prophecy
An Oracle Prophecy is a supernatural mechanism that dictates the actions of those who interact with it. It operates based on the rules set forth by the oracle itself, often requiring specific actions or questions to be answered truthfully.

## Key Features
- **Rules:** Six questions per day, each consuming one lantern light from the six giant statues; every question yields a true answer.
- **Purpose:** To guide and influence the actions of those who engage with the oracle.

## Examples
- The Ghost of Annam's Oracle uses the oracle's rules to manipulate the party into performing tasks aligned with his desires.
