# Political Influence on Regional Education Policy

The extent to which political entities within the political landscape of Everlund shape and influence educational systems, policies, and practices within the region. This influence is exercised primarily through control over funding, curriculum development, regulatory oversight, and teacher training programs. Political actors leverage these levers to align regional education with ideological priorities, economic goals, and cultural narratives, thereby determining access, content, and standards across schools and institutions.
