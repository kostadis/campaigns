# Shapeshifter Threat

An unidentified entity capable of mimicking inhabitants of the [[kobold-kingdom]]. 

## Current Intelligence
The creature is believed to be hiding in a cellar, potentially utilizing large barrels as cover for its mimicry. This threat is the primary driver for the current mission undertaken by the adventuring party, as the monarchy seeks to eliminate the entity without exposing the kingdom's vulnerability to the public.
