# Reckless Interventionism

**Reckless Interventionism** is a political accusation and ideological critique used primarily by the anti-interventionist faction of the [[emerald-enclave]].

### Core Argument
The accusation posits that active, forceful interference in natural or planar events—even when intended to combat evil—causes more long-term harm to the natural equilibrium than the threats themselves. Critics argue that such actions disrupt the delicate balance of the world and create unforeseen ecological or magical consequences.

### Political Usage
The term is used as a weapon to delegitimize individuals or groups who take decisive action against threats. [[adabra-gwynn]] has attempted to use this concept to frame the adventuring party's actions as harmful to the forests, specifically targeting soma and [[lyra]] during the druid conclave.
