# Blagothkus
Blagothkus is one of the Fire Giant lords, often mocking Zalto's ambitions. His mockery highlights the perceived lack of legitimacy in Zalto's claim to dominance among the giant lords. He is also known as a merchant specializing in magical goods for discerning magic users. Known to Unla Kee and associated with the Fishyard, he is the father of the Ghost (Cloud Giant).

## Relationships
- **Ghost (Cloud Giant):** Father; killed his son, the Ghost, in a moment of grief-fueled rage.
- **The Party:** Initially cooperative; offered useful information in exchange for taking on his vengeance quest.

## Background
Blagothkus was devastated by the oracle's declaration that he could never earn the gods' favor and was further enraged when the oracle urged his son to step out from Blagothkus's "dark shadow." Overcome with despair and envy, Blagothkus slew his son on the spot.
