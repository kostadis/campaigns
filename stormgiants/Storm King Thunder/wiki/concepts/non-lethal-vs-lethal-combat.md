# Non-Lethal vs. Lethal Combat

A recurring ethical tension within the adventuring party. It involves the conflict between combatants who prioritize neutralizing threats through non-lethal force (often for interrogation or moral reasons, as seen with [[valphine]]) and those who utilize blunt, reactive, and lethal force (as seen with [[brewbarry]]). This tension often results in "affectionate exasperation" among party members when targets are killed before non-lethal measures can be applied.
