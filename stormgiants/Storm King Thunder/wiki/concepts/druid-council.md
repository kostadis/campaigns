# Druid Council

The central governing body of the [[emerald-enclave]], responsible for debating policy, addressing ecological crises, and resolving factional disputes.

## Recent Proceedings
The most recent council session focused on the ideological divide between the [[anti-interventionist-faction]] and the interventionist elements of the Enclave. The session was marked by intense scrutiny of the anti-interventionist's ability to manage [[motivated-blight]] and planar anomalies. The failure of the anti-interventionist representatives to provide substance during this session led to a significant political setback for [[adabra-gwynn]].
