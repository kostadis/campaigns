# Memory Suppression / Magical Interference

A phenomenon where a witness's ability to recall specific, critical details of an event is compromised. 

## Contextual Application
In the case of [[elara-seasong-meliame]], she was unable to clearly recall the specific markings (swirl or eye shapes) worn by the orcish attackers. This inability to maintain a clear mental image of the attackers' identifiers suggests the presence of active [[psionic-manipulation]] or magical interference.

## Potential Origins
- **[[kraken-society]] Operations:** The "swirl" description aligns with the known spiral emblem of the [[kraken-society]], suggesting they may use cognitive obscuration to protect their identity.
- **Psionic Tools:** The suppression may be a specialized tool used by organized raiding parties to ensure their affiliation remains unconfirmed by civilian witnesses.
