# Orsik
Character; peripheral, initially resisted to Halisa's demands. Freed Halisa through impersonation, unaware of the deception. Halisa was initially punished by the Seelie Court for speaking honestly about Queen Titania's attire, and she was released from her exile by Prince Thornacious, who impersonated the actual Prince Thornacious. Her release was framed as a punishment to her, not a reward.
