# Barbarian Rage

A combat state utilized by warriors such as [[brewbarry]]. 

Contrary to common perceptions of losing control, for some practitioners, rage serves as a method of mental simplification, allowing the combatant to focus entirely on immediate threats and damage mitigation while absorbing significant physical punishment.
