# Political Destabilization

[[political-destabilization]] is the process of undermining the stability of a governing body or political structure. 

In the context of [[gnomengarde]], this was demonstrated by [[vukradin]], who utilized social engineering and psychological manipulation to exploit existing interpersonal friction between [[king-korboz]] and [[king-gnercli]]. By highlighting grievances regarding seniority and the validity of the [[shapeshifter-threat]], the stability of the [[dual-monarchy]] was significantly compromised.
