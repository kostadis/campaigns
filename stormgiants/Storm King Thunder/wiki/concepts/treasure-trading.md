# Treasure Trading
- **Core Claim:** The interaction revolves around the role of treasure in securing the party's goodwill and ensuring the kobolds' departure.
- **Evidence:** 
  - Gen suggested offering treasure to secure the party's goodwill.
  - The party accepted the offer, and both kobolds were allowed to leave unharmed.
- **Strength of Evidence:** Strong, supported by direct actions and outcomes of the encounter.
