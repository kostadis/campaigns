# Civilizing Mission

A biased socio-political framework used by jenna to frame recruitment into the [[lords-alliance]]. 

This concept is used to patronize non-aligned or "primitive" groups—specifically the Uthgardt peoples—by suggesting that alignment with the Alliance is the only path to becoming "civilized." It serves as a tool of psychological coercion, attempting to make the abandonment of traditional cultural values seem like a necessary step toward social progress and stability.
