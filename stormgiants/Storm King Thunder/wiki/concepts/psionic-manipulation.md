# Psionic Manipulation

A sophisticated method of mental control and "brain rewiring" utilized by the [[kraken-society]]. 

## Characteristics
Unlike the influence exerted by Mind Flayers, this specific form of manipulation is distinct in its application and effects. Key symptoms include:
- **Memory Gaps:** Significant loss of recent or long-term coherent memories.
- **Trance States:** "Glassy-eyed" and unfocused states where the subject may speak in a monotone or cryptic manner.
- **Blackouts:** Periods of lost time that precede full enthrallment.
- **Compulsion:** The ability to tie a subject's mental stability or autonomy to the life of a specific operative (e.g., [[the-cult-fanatic]]).

The exact origin and identity of the entity capable of this specific type of neurological rewiring remains an open question.
