# Sabotage Campaign
The Sabotage Campaign is a series of incidents targeting Parnast's infrastructure and security. It is the core concept driving Gundalin Cartwright's role and the narrative arc of the story. The campaign involves the disappearance of tools, the collapse of the watchtower, and the breaking of a cart axle, all of which are linked to Gundalin Cartwright.

## Key Incidents
- **Disappearance of Tools:** Tools belonging to Parnast's residents go missing, raising suspicions about the sabotage campaign.
- **Watchtower Collapse:** The watchtower collapses, leading to further concerns about the safety of the town.
- **Broken Cart Axle:** Gundalin Cartwright personally repairs a broken cart axle, implicating him in the sabotage campaign.

## Impact
The Sabotage Campaign has significant impacts on Parnast, causing unrest and mistrust among its inhabitants. It serves as a backdrop for Gundalin's evolving role and the complex interplay between internal and external factors affecting the town.
