# Gossip and Intelligence
Gossip plays a significant role in the kobold tribe, serving as a primary means of information dissemination. Leaders like Kragota-Threeteeth and Nug rely on gossip to gather and share intelligence, which is crucial for planning ambushes and other strategic actions.

## Key Points
- **Role in Intelligence:** Gossip is a vital tool for gathering and sharing intelligence within the kobold tribe.
