# Ecological Cascade Displacement Crisis

The **Ecological Cascade Displacement Crisis** is the systemic chain reaction currently destabilizing the region. According to intelligence from [[xanth]], the crisis was triggered by the descent of the white dragon [[cryovain]] into the Icespire Mountains.

### The Cascade Process:
1. **Dragon Arrival:** The presence of [[cryovain]] disrupts the established, stable equilibrium of the forest.
2. **Primary Displacement:** The dragon's presence forces local creatures to flee their territories.
3. **Secondary Displacement:** Orcish populations, displaced by the dragon, are pushed into inhabited valleys.
4. **Radicalization:** The stress of displacement, combined with the influence of the [[cult-of-talos]], has radicalized these displaced groups, turning a natural migration into a violent, theological threat.

This crisis is cited as a primary example of the [[emerald-enclave]]'s "institutional paralysis," as the organization struggles to decide whether to treat the event as a natural phenomenon or an interventionist necessity.
