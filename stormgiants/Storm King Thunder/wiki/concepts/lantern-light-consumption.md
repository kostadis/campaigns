# Lantern Light Consumption
Lantern Light Consumption refers to the process by which each question posed to the oracle consumes one lantern light from the six giant statues. This mechanism ensures that the oracle's answers remain truthful and that the party's actions are guided accordingly.

## Key Points
- **Consumption:** Each question consumes one lantern light from the six giant statues.
- **Truthfulness:** Ensures that the oracle's answers are always true and aligned with the party's actions.
