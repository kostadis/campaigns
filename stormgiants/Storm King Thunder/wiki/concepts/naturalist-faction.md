# Naturalist Faction

The [[naturalist-faction]] is a specific ideological wing within the [[emerald-enclave]]. 

## Core Beliefs
This faction emphasizes the maintenance of natural equilibrium. They are often in direct opposition to the [[interventionism]] wing of the Enclave, arguing against active interference in planar or monstrous shifts that might disrupt the natural order. This tension is a primary driver of the current political schism within the Enclave.
