# High Captain Horix Zoar

High Captain Horix Zoar is the supreme commander of Everlund's army and a central figure in the regional political landscape. A member of the [[Zoar family]], he is a politically shrewd leader who maintains Everlund's prestige and independence.

*Also known as: Hirax Zoar, Captain Zoar, High Captain Zoar.*

## Identity
- **Role:** High Captain of Everlund — supreme commander of Everlund's army.
- **Faction:** Everlund city authority / military leadership (formerly Lord's Alliance-aligned; now departed from that body).
- **Family:** Brother of [[Tamalin Zoar]] (mayor of Calling Horns).
- **First Appearance:** First referenced via [[Dral Thelev]]'s account; first met in person at Danivarr's House after being summoned by [[Dral Thelev]] — initially dismissive of the party as "smelly adventurers."

## Personality & Motivations
Horix is a proud and disciplined leader who balances personal vendettas with political necessity. While he publicly maintains a "let bygones be bygones" posture to preserve stability, he privately harbors deep-seated grudges.

- **Core Goals:** Maintaining Everlund's prestige and independence; advancing his own standing; seeing Silverymoon embarrassed or diminished.
- **The Silverymoon Grudge:** He holds a bitter grudge against [[High Marshal Methrammar Aerasumé]] of Silverymoon over the perceived abandonment of allied forces (notably Sundbar) during orc horde attacks. This perceived betrayal led him to personally dissolve the Everlund-Silverymoon compact. He privately desires to raze Silverymoon.
- **Temperament:** He warms quickly when respect is demonstrated or mutual benefit is clear; he is generous and enthusiastic once he becomes an ally.
- **Political Stance:** He is publicly loyal to [[Speaker Vatrice Stormwright]], though he remains observant of the political horizon, noting that her re-election is "years away with much that could happen."

## History with the Party
1. **Pre-meeting briefing:** [[Dral Thelev]] briefed the party on Horix's background, establishing him as a potential resource. As High Captain, he holds the authority to credential the party (**3HPP**) as official Everlund diplomats, granting them protection from arrest by Silverymoon's local authorities.
2. **Summoned to Danivarr's House:** Arrived with three guards, initially cold toward the party.
3. **Tamalin's letter:** The party's demeanor shifted when Thistle presented a letter of introduction from his sister, [[Tamalin Zoar]]. Horix's attitude immediately shifted to warmth and pride.
4. **Demon-trafficking briefing:** Upon hearing the party's report regarding a criminal organization trafficking demons through Silverymoon, Zoar responded with enthusiasm, framing the news as confirmation of Silverymoon's moral corruption.
5. **Letter of Mark:** He issued the party a letter of mark naming them as his agents. He deliberately kept the party name **3HPP**, as associating Everlund with the popular theatrical production embarrasses Silverymoon.
6. **Bob Saget production:** Upon learning the party are silent backers of the Bob Saget theatrical production, he was delighted and expressed plans to take his children to see it.
7. **Political probing:** When Thistle floated the idea of Zoar seeking political office, he deflected, expressing satisfaction as High Captain and support for Stormwright, while noting that a victory against the giant threat would boost his prestige.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Returned to his duties in Everlund.
- **Condition at Departure:** Fed, slightly inebriated, and in high spirits.
- **Active Interests:** Watching the party's progress against the giant threat; anticipates a prestige boost if they succeed publicly.
- **What the Party Knows:** He is a politically ambitious but cautious leader who despises Silverymoon. He is now a genuine ally and cultivated political patron who can provide diplomatic credentials.
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full extent of his political ambitions and his true relationship with [[Speaker Vatrice Stormwright]]; his specific military priorities; the full circumstances of the Silverymoon betrayal from his perspective.

## Relationships
- **[[Tamalin Zoar]] (sister):** Mayor of Calling Horns. He speaks of her with deep pride, noting her transformation from "cutthroat and brigand to person of character and substance." His affection for her was the key to his cooperation with the party.
- **[[Speaker Vatrice Stormwright]]:** Publicly his superior and loyally supported, though he appears to be positioning himself privately.
- **[[High Marshal Methrammar Aerasumé]] (Silverymoon):** Bitter enemy; held personally responsible for the betrayal that broke the alliance.
- **[[Dral Thelev]]:** Summoned Zoar on the party's behalf; their existing connection allowed the party to reach him.
- **[[Unla Kee]]:** Referenced him as a cultivated ally.
- **The Party (3HPP):** Now formal allies under a letter of mark; Zoar is invested in their success for his own prestige.
