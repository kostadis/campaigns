# Divine Knowledge as Strategic Tool

The use of divine revelation not for spiritual enlightenment, but for political, military, or economic advantage. The Oracle of the Eye of the World provides actionable intelligence — such as Adimond Kroskas’s location, the function of King Hekaton’s conch, and strategic advice to the Zhentarim — transforming divine truth into a lever of mortal power.

This elevates divine entities from cosmic abstractions to active participants in mortal power structures, even when they do not intervene directly. The Oracle does not choose sides, but its answers shape them. The Zhentarim, for example, now possess a divine mandate to end the Giant War and secure trade privileges — a goal that aligns with their ambitions and is now morally and strategically validated.

This concept challenges the notion that divine knowledge is purely moral or existential. Here, it is instrumental — and dangerous. Those who wield it must understand not only the answer, but the cost of asking.
