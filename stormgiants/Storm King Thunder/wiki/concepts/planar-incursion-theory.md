# Planar Incursion Theory

A developing theoretical framework regarding the nature of entities and energies entering the primary reality from other planes.

## Natural vs. Deliberate
Historically, scholars like [[sridar]] viewed these incursions as natural phenomena, similar to the presence of [[faerzress]]. However, recent theories—supported by observations of [[planar-instability]]—suggest that these events may not be accidental.

There is an emerging hypothesis that incursions may be the result of **deliberate interference** by powerful magic users, planar entities, or "careless meddlers." This shift in perspective moves the study of planar phenomena from passive observation to the investigation of intentional agency.
