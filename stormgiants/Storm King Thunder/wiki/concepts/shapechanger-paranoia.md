# Shapechanger Paranoia

A recurring defensive psychological state and social bias observed among the guards and population of [[gnomengarde]]. It is frequently used as a rhetorical shield to deflect arguments or suspicion, with individuals accusing others of being shapeshifters to invalidate their claims or social standing. It remains unclear if this is a response to a genuine threat or a localized cultural neurosis.
