# Racial Exclusionary Views
Imber Chen's views on race and personhood, particularly excluding half-orcs from his vision of Silverymoon's "peaceful" multi-racial society. These views contrast with the broader narrative of Silverymoon's racial inclusivity.

## Key Points
- Imber's exclusionary views on race and personhood
- Contrast with Silverymoon's broader narrative of racial inclusivity
- Influence on his relationships with Orsik and Cap'n Squint
