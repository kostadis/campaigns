# Underdark Blink Event

A specific, localized historical event witnessed by [[sridar]] in the Underdark. The event involved the sudden, unexplained appearance of demons within the region. This "blink" is considered a primary catalyst for the study of intentional planar incursions and serves as the empirical basis for Sridar's expertise in identifying anomalous artifacts.
