# Moral Grayness and Pragmatism

A philosophical driver used by certain high-functioning operatives, such as [[sister-kaella]], to navigate complex political and criminal landscapes. 

This worldview prioritizes survival, efficiency, and predictable outcomes over traditional moral or ethical frameworks. Individuals adhering to this concept select allies based on their perceived ability to operate outside conventional morality, viewing "pure" actors (such as [[vukradin]]) as liabilities or "dupes" who cannot be effectively utilized in high-stakes environments.
