# mother-satorra

The mother of Aletra and Valphine is repeatedly referenced as a source of familial judgment and emotional trauma, yet lacks a dedicated page. Her influence is central to the sisters’ dynamic and the theme of [[weaponization-of-personal-debt]]. Her identity, status, and role in the Satorra house are critical to understanding Aletra’s motivations and Valphine’s psychological wounds.
