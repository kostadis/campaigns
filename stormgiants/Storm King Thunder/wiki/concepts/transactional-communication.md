# Transactional Communication

A method of interaction where information or cooperation is exchanged for tangible goods or services rather than through social or instinctual bonds.

This was demonstrated during the encounter with [[the-stag]], where the creature moved from territorial aggression to calm engagement once a gift of mushrooms from [[gnomengarde]] was provided. This suggests that certain non-human entities may operate on a logic of pragmatic exchange.
