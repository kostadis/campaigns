# Coercive Captivity

**Coercive Captivity** describes a dynamic where a captive's cooperation is driven by survival instincts and the immediate threat of death rather than genuine alliance or shared goals. 

This is exemplified by the relationship between [[shrimpy]] and his captors, where information is traded for continued existence, and behavior is heavily influenced by the level of pressure applied by the captors.
