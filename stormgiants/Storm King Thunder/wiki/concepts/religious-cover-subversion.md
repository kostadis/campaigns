# Religious Cover and Subversion

The tactical use of religious identity, iconography, and terminology to mask secular, criminal, or purely pragmatic motivations. 

By adopting the persona of a devout practitioner (e.g., using "priest's cloth" or invoking sacraments), an operative can lower the defenses of targets, gain access to restricted social circles, and provide a layer of social legitimacy to otherwise ruthless actions. [[sister-kaella]] is a primary example of this technique.
