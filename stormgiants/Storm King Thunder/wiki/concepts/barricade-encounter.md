# Barricade Encounter
The barricade encounter is a pivotal scene where Nug leads an ambush group against a heavily armed party, motivated by territorial defense. This encounter sets the stage for Nug's actions and the broader narrative involving the kobold tribe and the party.

## Key Points
- **Motivation:** Territorial defense
- **Outcome:** Failed ambush, resulting in the death of Nug and other kobolds
