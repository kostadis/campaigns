# Out-of-Phase Existence

[[out-of-phase-existence]] is a state of being where an entity's physical form moves out of sync with standard reality. 

This phenomenon is most prominently observed in the inhabitants of [[axeholm]]. It manifests as a visual distortion where limbs appear to multiply or occupy multiple positions simultaneously (e.g., appearing to have six hands or legs). This state is closely linked to the "unnatural light" found within the fortress and the presence of the local planar anomaly.
