# Information Event

An [[information-event]] is the strategic disclosure of sensitive intelligence that serves as a catalyst for narrative or political shifts. 

A recent example is the *Sending* spell cast by [[vukradin]] to [[reidoth]], which transitioned the party from treating the Enclave leadership as a "reserve option" to making them active participants in the unfolding schism.
