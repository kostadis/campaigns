# Underdark Instincts

[[underdark-instincts]] refers to the psychological and behavioral traits common to denizens of the Underdark, such as [[menzoberranzan]]. These include a natural inclination toward dominance, hunting, sacrifice, and "rationed violence." For characters like [[valphine]], managing these instincts against surface-world religious doctrines is a central internal conflict.
