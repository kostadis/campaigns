# The 1489 Treaty

**The 1489 Treaty on Export Regulation of Interplanar Storage Devices** is a legal framework governing the movement of goods, entities, and planar artifacts between the Material Plane and other planes of existence. It serves as the foundational regulatory mechanism for interplanar-commerce, particularly concerning items with extra-planar signatures, such as [[interplanar-storage-devices]].

The treaty is strictly monitored and enforced by [[the-astral-empires]], which acts as the primary arbiter of interplanar trade compliance. Recent misuse of [[interplanar-storage-devices]] by entities on Toril has triggered a major geopolitical crisis, leading to the mobilization of Githyanki, Githzeri, and Mindflayer forces. This escalation has exposed critical vulnerabilities in the treaty’s enforcement mechanisms and placed individuals like [[bob-saget]] at heightened risk.

Economically, interplanar commerce remains highly lucrative but volatile. Organizations such as the [[lionshield-coster]] have built significant operations around the acquisition and transport of planar goods, relying on stable treaty-enforced supply chains. However, ongoing metaphysical conflicts—particularly the [[war-of-the-giants]]—have severely disrupted these networks, resulting in widespread scarcity of magical and planar items, increased logistical complexity, and a breakdown in trust among trading entities.

As a result, the 1489 Treaty is no longer merely a commercial regulation; it has become a central flashpoint in interplanar diplomacy, security, and economic stability. Its integrity now determines the fate of both trade routes and the safety of planar travelers.
