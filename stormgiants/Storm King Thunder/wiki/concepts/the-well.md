# The Well

A fundamental aspect of druidic magic taught by [[meril]] to [[soma]].

## Description
[[the-well]] is described as the metaphysical source from which all wild shape forms are drawn. Accessing the "well" is a key component of advanced druidic practice, allowing a practitioner to tap into the primal essence required for shapeshifting.
