# Shattering of the Ordning

A cataclysmic historical event linked to the chain reaction of wars following the [[war-of-the-dragons]]. Naturalists within the [[emerald-enclave]] cite this event as a direct consequence of the faction's loss of neutrality, a shift facilitated by [[malfire]].
