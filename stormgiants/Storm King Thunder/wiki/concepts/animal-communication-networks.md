# Animal Communication Networks

**Animal Communication Networks** refers to a method of gathering wide-ranging intelligence through non-human creatures and natural sensory channels. 

This concept is utilized by entities such as [[xanth]], who can bypass traditional human/civilized intelligence gathering to receive reports from the forest itself. Through these networks, observers can track movements, deaths, and disappearances (such as the death of [[borg]]) across vast distances. The failure of city-dwellers and institutional bodies like the [[emerald-enclave]] to utilize or understand these networks is often viewed as a form of "willful blindness" that contributes to their inability to respond to regional crises.
