# Family Rescue
The Family Rescue concept represents the resolution of Jarlor's coerced spy status. After Jarlor's wife challenged him, he was persuaded to transmit a covert message to Chief Kurglow. The message was a comically non-threatening warning about denying the orc whelp dessert and a bedtime story. This event marks the end of Jarlor's coerced role as a spy and the freeing of his family from the orc cave.
