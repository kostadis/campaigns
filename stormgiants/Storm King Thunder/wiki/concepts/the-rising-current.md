# The Rising Current

"The Rising Current" is a cryptic oceanic metaphor used by [[the-cult-fanatic]] to describe an imminent, large-scale operation being orchestrated by the [[kraken-society]]. 

The term is used alongside other maritime imagery—such as "tides rising" and "waves coming"—to signal a significant shift or event moving toward [[neverwinter]]. The exact nature of this "current" remains unknown, but it appears to be the primary driver behind the society's recent field activities.
