# Lord's Alliance
The Lord's Alliance is an influential group that operates as an external force affecting the political landscape and internal conflicts in Parnast. It is a rival faction to the Cult of the Dragon, often engaged in conflict. Klauth's relationship with the Lord's Alliance is complex, as he actively does not want them poking around the Spine of the World, suggesting tension or divergence between their interests.

## Members and Impact
- **Members:** The Lord's Alliance comprises various powerful entities.
- **Impact:** The Lord's Alliance shapes the political dynamics and internal strife within Parnast, acting as a significant external influence.

## External Influence
- **Klauth's Relationship:** Klauth actively avoids the Lord's Alliance, indicating potential tension or divergence in their interests.
