# Bureaucratic Logic

A method of decision-making that prioritizes established rules, status, and procedural precedents over social persuasion or emotional reasoning. In [[gnomengarde]], this is seen in the behavior of guards like [[ulla]], who require specific procedural justifications (such as tribal status) before granting access or changing a stance.
