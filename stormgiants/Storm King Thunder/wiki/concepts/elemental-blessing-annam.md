# Elemental Blessing (Annam)

A subtle, non-interventionist form of divine favor — resistance to elemental damage — granted to individuals despite the silence of Annam, the All-Father.

Vardis and Unla Key both possess this blessing: Vardis resists air damage; Unla Key resists water damage. These are not magical items or spells — they are unexplained, unasked-for gifts. They are not signs of divine approval — they are signs of divine presence.

In a world where Annam has withdrawn, these blessings suggest he has not abandoned his children — he has simply stopped speaking. His favor is not in words, but in quiet protection. It is not a reward — it is a reminder.

This concept transforms [[divine-silence]] from absence into ambiguity. Annam does not answer prayers — but he does not let his people die. His silence is not indifference — it is a different kind of care.

The blessing is not a tool. It is a question: if the gods are silent, why do we still live?
