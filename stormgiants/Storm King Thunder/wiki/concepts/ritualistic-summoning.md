# Ritualistic Summoning

A magical process involving "alien geometry" and burning summoning circles used to bind and manipulate powerful entities. At [[icespire-hold]], this ritual was used by [[the-carver]] to seize control of [[cryovain]]. The ritual requires specific geometric configurations and can be disrupted by the death of the primary subject or the interruption of the ritual's focus.
