# Planar Artifact as Smuggling Conduit

Planar artifact as smuggling conduit is the repurposing of originally benign or industrial planar technologies for illicit logistical purposes. The [[model-1489-spatial-rectification-calliope]] — a dwarven device designed for spatial stabilization in mining and bridge construction — was corrupted by House Sartorra into a smuggling engine, enabling the covert transport of [[midnight-tears]] across dimensional boundaries.

This practice represents a critical evolution in criminal infrastructure: it moves beyond ritual magic and natural rifts into the domain of engineered, repeatable, and scalable planar access. Unlike spontaneous planar travel, which is unpredictable and dangerous, this method allows for scheduled, high-volume trafficking — turning the multiverse into a supply chain.

The corruption of such artifacts signals a broader trend: the institutionalization of planar technology by organized crime. These devices are not stolen — they are *adapted*, often by insiders with technical knowledge. Their use requires not just magical skill, but administrative control — forged ledgers, hidden inventories, and oversight by unseen authorities like “KP.”

**Key Features:**  
- Original purpose: engineering, not magic  
- Repurposed for criminal logistics  
- Requires technical expertise to operate  
- Integrated with bureaucratic deception  

**Significance:**  
This concept transforms planar travel from a mystical phenomenon into a corporate asset — and proves that the greatest threats to cosmic order are not demons or dragons, but engineers who know how to turn stability into smuggling.
