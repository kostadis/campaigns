# Contractual Integrity

[[contractual-integrity]] is the philosophical and professional belief in the absolute sanctity of legal agreements and documentation. 

In the context of the [[stonetallow-clan]], this concept is a core component of their cultural identity. It serves as a primary point of ideological friction with characters like [[valphine]], who views contracts as tools for deception, and [[vukradin]], whose insistence on "no-skimming clauses" forces even principled negotiators like [[horia-stonetallow]] to seek [[pragmatic-workarounds]] to maintain operational flexibility.
