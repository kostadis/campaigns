# Uthgardt Exile

The state of being cast out from the [[uthgardt]] tribal societies. This concept serves as a significant thematic driver for characters like [[brewbarry]], fueling a deep empathy for other exiled or displaced individuals (such as shrimpy) and a rejection of the perceived cruelty of the tribes.
