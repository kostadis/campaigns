# Supply Chain Disruption

The breakdown of established trade routes and the resulting unavailability of goods.

## Current Drivers
In the current era, supply chain disruptions are being driven by:
- **Geopolitical Conflict:** The [[war-of-the-giants]] has directly impacted the movement of goods and the availability of magical supplies (e.g., scrolls).
- **Economic Barriers:** The imposition of "tariffs" has hindered the efficiency of merchant networks like the [[lionshield-coster]].
- **Planar Instability:** Strange magical phenomena (such as those reported in [[neverwinter-wood]]) have confounded navigators, further complicating logistics.
