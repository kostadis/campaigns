# Moonwood

Moonwood is a mystical forest located in the realm of the Yakfolk, serving as the cultural and geographic origin point for several key figures, including Jevin, Princess Halani Meliame, and Vandar. 

It is the birthplace and ancestral home of Jevin, providing essential context for his background and deep connection to the Yakfolk. The same forest is also the origin of Princess Halani Meliame and Vandar, anchoring their identities and histories within its enigmatic landscape.

Moonwood gained prominence during a pivotal rescue operation in which Jevin was captured by the Yakfolk alongside Princess Halani Meliame and Vandar. This event underscores the forest’s role not only as a place of origin but also as a site of conflict, alliance, and transformation.

Despite its significance, Moonwood remains poorly documented in external records, lacking a comprehensive lore entry that fully captures its mystical properties, ecological uniqueness, or spiritual importance to the Yakfolk.
