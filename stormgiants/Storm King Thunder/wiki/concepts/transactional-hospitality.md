# Transactional Hospitality

A method of social and business interaction where hospitality is provided through negotiated, mutually beneficial arrangements rather than through charity or simple service. 

An example of this is seen in the practices of [[toblen-stonehill]], who frequently trades meals and lodging for musical entertainment provided by [[vukradin]]. This approach maintains a professional equilibrium between the service provider and the guest, ensuring that both parties derive value from the interaction.
