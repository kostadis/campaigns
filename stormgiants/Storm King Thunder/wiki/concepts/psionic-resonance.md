# Psionic Resonance

[[psionic-resonance]] is a form of energy distinct from traditional divine or arcane magic. It was identified emanating from the [[talisman-of-the-inverted-eye]] and is associated with the "Unknown Patron" influencing [[moesko]]. This energy is characterized as being of foreign, non-standard origin.
