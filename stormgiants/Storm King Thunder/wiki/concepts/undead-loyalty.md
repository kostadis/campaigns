# Undead Loyalty

**Undead Loyalty** refers to the phenomenon where a creature's devotion to a former master persists or transfers to a perceived successor after death. 

This concept is exemplified by boney, who transferred his absolute devotion from the deceased [[lady-alagondar]] to soma based on the belief that she was the noble's reincarnation.
