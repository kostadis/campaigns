# Mental Domination

The process of overriding a sentient being's consciousness to turn them into a mindless tool or engine of rage. In the case of [[cryovain]], this was achieved through the use of the-carver and [[ritualistic-summoning]]. 

The effects of such domination can be observed physically, such as the "hollow, glazed, and filmed over" eyes noted in victims of this process.
