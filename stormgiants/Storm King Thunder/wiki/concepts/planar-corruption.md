# Planar Corruption

Planar corruption (or infection) refers to the intrusion of alien influences into the natural world. This phenomenon can manifest as "planar scars" or "wells" that disrupt the local ecosystem.

## Effects
- **Biological Corruption:** Natural guardians, such as the Treant of the Whispering Grove, can be corrupted by these influences, turning them into violent, non-natural entities.
- **Environmental Alteration:** Corruption can replace natural sustenance (like sunlight) with energy from planar pools, leading to the growth of blighted brambles and other unnatural growths.

## Ideological Response
The presence of planar corruption is the central driver of the schism within the [[emerald-enclave]], specifically the divide between [[interventionism-vs-naturalism]].
