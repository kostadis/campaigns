# Reincarnation Misidentification

**Reincarnation Misidentification** is a narrative driver where a character's loyalty is secured through the mistaken (or strategically presented) belief that a new individual is a reincarnated former master. 

In the current context, this mechanism was used to bind [[boney]] to the adventuring party by convincing him that [[soma]] was the reincarnation of [[lady-alagondar]].
