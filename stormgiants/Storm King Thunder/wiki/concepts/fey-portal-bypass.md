# Fey Portal Bypass

A mechanism by which individuals of recognized status within the Seelie Court can bypass magical barriers, wards, or trickster fey protections by invoking their official title or position. Blasius used his former role as Chief of Staff to open a portal directly to Prince Thornacious’s court, circumventing standard magical protocols.

This is not a spell or innate ability, but a social-ritualistic privilege: authority, when properly recognized, overrides magical constraints. The portal responds to institutional legitimacy, not personal power.

The Fey Portal Bypass exemplifies how power in the Seelie Court is institutionalized — access is granted not by force or knowledge, but by title. It is a direct extension of [[disguised-influence]] and [[organizational-secrecy-via-bosses]], where the appearance of legitimacy grants real access.

This concept parallels the [[divine-oracle]]’s truth-telling: both systems operate on rules of access, not raw power. To use the portal, one must be recognized. To speak truth, one must be authorized.

It also reveals a vulnerability: those without title — even if magically powerful — are locked out. The system is designed to preserve hierarchy, not equality.
