# Moral Flexibility

**Moral Flexibility** is a specific descriptor used by the [[zhentarim]] to define the "entry requirements" for their organization. It refers to a willingness to prioritize pragmatic, mercenary, or survival-based outcomes over rigid moral codes.

## Context in Narrative
The term was specifically used by [[zuul-clovis]] when he vouched for [[clarify-orsiks-identity-and-role-2026-06-06-063453]]. This creates a significant narrative hook:
1. It suggests that Orsik has operated in circles where such traits are necessary for advancement.
2. It highlights the contrast between the party's typical heroic actions and the "mercenary" ethics of the Zhentarim.

The concept serves as a litmus test for character alignment within the underworld, distinguishing those who follow a strict code from those willing to navigate the "gray areas" of power.
