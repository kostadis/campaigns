# Ritual of Consumption

A methodical, long-term magical strategy employed by the-carver to exert influence and slowly absorb power or control within a region. 

This approach relies on patience and preparation, building toward a crescendo of power. It stands in contrast to [[direct-domination]], which is a reactive, forceful seizure of power used when long-term plans are interrupted.
