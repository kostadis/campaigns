# Rose
Rose is an artifact, the hexagonal oracle chamber within the Temple of Annam. The Ghost (Cloud Giant) emerges from the floor of the oracle chamber when the party enters, recounting his death and explaining the oracle's rules.

## Relationships
- **Oracle Chamber:** Located within the Temple of Annam; the Ghost (Cloud Giant) emerges from the floor of the oracle chamber when the party enters.
