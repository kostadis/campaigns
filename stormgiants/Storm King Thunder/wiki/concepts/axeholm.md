# Axeholm

An emergency evacuation site designated by Harbin Wester as a last-resort refuge for Phandalin’s population in the event of escalating dragon attacks. It is not a permanent settlement, but a contingency plan — a symbol of Harbin’s prioritization of survival over resilience.

The party secured Axeholm as a mission objective and were paid 250 gp for their efforts. Harbin expressed relief at its security but clarified it was not intended as a new home — only a temporary fallback. This reveals his pragmatism: he plans for collapse, not renewal.

Axeholm is a quiet monument to institutional fear — a place where people go not to rebuild, but to wait. Its existence underscores the fragility of Phandalin’s order and the absence of visionary leadership. It is not a sanctuary — it is a pause button.
