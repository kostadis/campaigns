# Direct Domination

A forceful and immediate seizure of power or control, typically used as a reactive measure when a more subtle or methodical plan (such as a [[ritual-of-consumption]]) is obstructed. 

In the case of [[the-carver]], this involved abandoning a complex binding ritual in favor of immediate, violent control over its targets.
