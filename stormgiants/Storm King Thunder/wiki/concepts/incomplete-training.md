# Incomplete Training

A psychological theme affecting soma, stemming from the fact that her master, [[meril]], provided only one year of formal instruction before sending her on her journey. This creates an internal conflict regarding her competence and the "weight of being chosen" for a destiny she may not be fully prepared to fulfill.
