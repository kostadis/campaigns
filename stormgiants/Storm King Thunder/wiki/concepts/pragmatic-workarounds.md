# Pragmatic Workarounds

[[pragmatic-workarounds]] refers to the practice of using unofficial "Plan B" scenarios to navigate rigid contractual constraints, superstitions, or social obstacles without technically violating the letter of the law.

An example of this is the arrangement between [[horia-stonetallow]] and [[brewbarry]], where funds were diverted to purchase a fake gem to satisfy the superstitions of [[dwolin-the-grout]], thereby bypassing the formal restrictions of the mining contract.
