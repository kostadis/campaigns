# Planar and Interdimensional Travel

The movement of entities and parties between different planes of existence. In the current campaign, this has been facilitated through:
- The [[harper]] teleportation network.
- Zephyros's cloud castle.
- The "Dragon's Breath" airship.
- The accidental summoning and banishment of entities like [[bob-saget]].
