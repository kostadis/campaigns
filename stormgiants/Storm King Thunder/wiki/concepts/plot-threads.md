# Plot Threads
- **Title:** Everard Barney's Journey as a Director
- **Created:** 2023-10-05
- **Updated:** 2023-10-05
- **Tags:** [theater, playwriting]
- **Related:** []
- **Sources:** ["everard_barney.md"]
---
Everard Barney, despite his initial anxiety and lack of experience, is making progress in directing Kaelen's play with the help of Thistle. His willingness to accept Thistle's suggestions, improvements in his staging approach, and his ability to work with Amy demonstrate his growing competence. However, his discomfort with Kaelen and his family background create tension with his professional aspirations.
- **Title:** Managing the Cast
- **Created:** 2023-10-05
- **Updated:** 2023-10-05
- **Tags:** [theater, playwriting]
- **Related:** []
- **Sources:** ["everard_barney.md"]
---
Everard Barney struggles to manage the cast, particularly the lead actor Amy and the drunken actors. He initially tries to incorporate their drunkenness into the performance, but ultimately decides to manage them by screaming at them in the morning instead.
