# Cult of the Dragon
The Cult of the Dragon is a central organization in the story, centered around dragon worship and rituals. It represents an external force threatening Parnast's stability and peace. The Cult of the Dragon is an enigmatic entity causing unrest in Parnast, contributing to the overall instability and unrest in the town.
