# The True Storm

A cryptic prophecy or warning issued by [[miral]] regarding a gathering threat.

## Distinction from Talos
The "True Storm" is explicitly distinguished from the traditional, sky-based destruction associated with the followers of Talos. While Talosian influence manifests in storms and raids from above, the True Storm is described as gathering in the **depths below [[leilon]]**, suggesting an undersea or subterranean origin.
