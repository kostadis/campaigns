# Spatial Equilibrium

A tactical concept used by [[aletra-satora]] to justify the presence of [[spatial-equilibrium]] (spectral spider constructs) guarding planar machinery. It implies a state where magical entities are required to maintain the stability of a localized area during high-risk operations like interdimensional extraction.
