# Queen Titania
Seelie Queen, Halisa's punisher for speaking honestly about her attire. Halisa was initially punished by the Seelie Court for speaking honestly about Queen Titania's attire, and she was released from her exile by Prince Thornacious, who impersonated the actual Prince Thornacious. Her release was framed as a punishment to her, not a reward.
