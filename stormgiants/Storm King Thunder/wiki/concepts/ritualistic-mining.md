# Ritualistic Mining

[[ritualistic-mining]] is a specialized, superstitious approach to resource extraction. It involves the use of specific items, such as an [[earthstone-gem]], and the performance of rituals to ensure the success of the harvest.

This method is predicated on the idea of [[sentient-gold-veins]], treating the mineral deposits as entities that must be appeased or persuaded. While often viewed as "gimmicks" by outsiders, the method is associated with high success rates among certain skilled practitioners.
