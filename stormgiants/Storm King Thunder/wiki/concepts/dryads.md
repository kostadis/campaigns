# Dryads
Mythical forest spirits, Halisa and Helia are both guardians of the Grandfather Tree. Halisa was initially punished by the Seelie Court for speaking honestly about Queen Titania's attire, and she was released from her exile by Prince Thornacious, who impersonated the actual Prince Thornacious. Her release was framed as a punishment to her, not a reward.
