# Scripted Performance

A method of social manipulation involving the use of rehearsed dialogue, specific physical positioning, and assigned roles to deceive targets or claim legitimacy.

## Application
In the case of the Wintermoon sisters, this technique was used to create a veneer of religious authority to facilitate a claim on the hoard of [[cryovain]]. 

## Characteristics
- **Rehearsal:** The performance is practiced in advance (as observed by soma regarding [[runa-wintermoon]] and [[sylvine-wintermoon]]).
- **Role Assignment:** Participants are assigned specific functions, such as the "primary actor" and the "supporting prop/attendant."
- **Cue-Based Interaction:** Dialogue is delivered on cue rather than through spontaneous interaction, often resulting in a "flat affect" in secondary performers.
