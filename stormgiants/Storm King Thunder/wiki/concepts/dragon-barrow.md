# Dragon Barrow

The final lair of the white dragon Cryovain, located in the mountains east of Phandalin. Site of the party’s climactic battle and the dragon’s defeat. After the battle, Harbin Wester orchestrated a full festival reception at the town boundary to celebrate the victory — using the event to claim credit for the Privy Council’s role in bringing the heroes to Phandalin.

Dragon Barrow is not merely a battlefield — it is a narrative turning point. It is where the party’s agency culminates, and where Harbin’s manipulation begins. The victory belongs to the party; the memory, Harbin claims.

Its name evokes ancient burial mounds — a fitting metaphor for the buried truths of civic leadership. The dragon is dead, but the bureaucracy lives on.
