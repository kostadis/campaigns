Tiefling Heritage Sensitivity refers to the self‑consciousness and defensive reactions tieflings may exhibit when their lineage is highlighted or questioned. Anakis's brief tension with party member Orsik illustrates this theme, as she quickly defended the Lady's College's inclusivity upon perceiving scrutiny of her heritage.
