# Non-Human Perspectives on Fey Religion

The discovery of "atheism" among the giant elks (specifically their view of fae religion as a tool of oppression) provides a unique counterpoint to standard fantasy tropes where non-humanoids are often depicted as being in harmony with or subservient to fey magic. This should be cross-referenced with existing notes on [[fey-court-structure]] and [[divine-silence]].
