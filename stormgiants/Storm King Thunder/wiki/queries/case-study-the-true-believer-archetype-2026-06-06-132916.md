# Case Study: The "True Believer" Archetype

The character Giles Slipper-Shine serves as a distinct narrative foil to the more complex, calculating figures like Adabra. He represents "raw devotion" and the transition of theory into physical practice (scars/sunburns). This distinction between "Philosophical Radicalism" vs. "Commoner Zealotry" could be formalized as a concept to track how different social strata interpret the same theology.
