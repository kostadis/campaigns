# Shadow Negotiations in Contractual Systems

The interaction between Horia and Brewbarry regarding the "fake gem" reveals a specific tactic of "shadow negotiation"—creating off-the-books sub-agreements to bypass strict primary contracts (like those enforced by Vukradin). This could be expanded into a broader concept on how different factions manage internal friction within rigid legal frameworks.
