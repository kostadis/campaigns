# Does Feetor Have Real Influence Over Mephits?

## Question
- Does Feetor have real influence over mephits?
- Does Feetor have real influence over other mud mephits beyond his grand proclamations?

## Background
- Feetor emerges from hiding after the party defeats the water weird and aligns with the party.
- He provides intel on marsh politics and negotiates with Unla Kee.
- His influence over other mephits remains unclear.
- Kagrota Threeteeth (Kagrota) was a warlord under Bad Fruul's command, tasked with destroying a commune.
- She was a worshiper of Bane and invoked a deal with Prince Thornacious.
- Tove Brandimen was allegedly her collaborator.
- Kagrota was killed by Wallace during a battle.

## Evidence
- Feetor's grand proclamations are often met with indifference or capitulation.
- His ability to sway mephits towards his side through threats and promises showcases his manipulative nature.
- The mephit civil war immediately resumes after his grand proclamation, indicating that his influence is limited.
