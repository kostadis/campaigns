# elemental-blessing-annam

Vardis and Unla Key both possess resistance to air and water damage respectively, attributed to “elemental blessing from Annam.” Yet Annam is explicitly silent and absent. This creates a contradiction: is this divine grace, a cosmic accident, or a trick of perception? The wiki assumes it’s silent grace, but no evidence confirms Annam’s agency — it could be residual magic, fey influence, or psychological projection. This tension must be resolved.
