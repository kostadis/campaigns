# kp-nature

The identity and nature of “KP” remain deliberately ambiguous but are central to the campaign’s theme of institutional control. Investigating whether KP could be a drow AI, a bound spirit, a fragment of a dead archmage, or a codename for a Xorlarrin council member would deepen the lore and provide narrative hooks for future arcs.
