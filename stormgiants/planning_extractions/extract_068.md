Proposed a full frontal assault on the throne room, which was rejected. Later swam through the underwater portcullis toward the sinkhole and discovered treasure stolen by crabs.

Was unconscious at the start of the session; revived by Vardis's Healing Word. Cast Pass Without Trace and infiltrated the throne room alone, flying near the ceiling. Observed and eavesdropped on the full throne room assembly including Serissa, Uthor, Mirran, Nym, and Adminond Kroskas. Deduced Adimond's escape route via the pool and the Maelstrom's interconnected tunnels. Recalled Joon's intelligence about the Maelstrom's tunnel system. Reported findings to the party, leading to the decision to infiltrate via the waterways.

**Unla Kee (Wizard)**
Delivered the killing blow on Count Vaal with magic missiles after a Magic Missile volley. Swam through the underwater portcullis into the Maelstrom vortex. Observed and catalogued the sinkhole structure: 100-foot-deep, 180-foot-wide, with a 50-foot-diameter drain covered by an iron grate with barnacle crust and 10-foot-wide gaps. Followed Orsik toward a tunnel on the sinkhole floor.

Appeared in the great hall fighting Orsik. Struck Orsik with his morning star. Was incapacitated by psychic damage from Unla Kee, hit by Thistle's magical arrows, struck by Vardis's flame lances, and ultimately killed by Unla Kee's magic missiles. Seated on the obsidian floating throne in the amphitheater throne room. Physically described in detail: 20+ feet tall, silver-blue hair, stormy sea eyes, luminescent blue skin, storm-patterned gown, silver crown with glowing gems, pearl-and-sapphire necklace. Seated to Serissa's right on a throne. Physically described: steel-gray hair, piercing stormy gray eyes, deep blue and silver tunic, enchanted leather belt, crackling trident. Maneuvered diplomatically during the succession dispute, suggesting a "change in leadership" might be appropriate if Serissa cannot restrain her temper, then retreated to the position of "just an alchemist doing the Queen's bidding" when rebuked by Uthor. Identified by Thistle as the de facto director of Mirran and Nym's faction—casually signals them when to speak and when to act.

## Revealed Information

- **Adimond's escape route**: The pool in the throne room connects to the Maelstrom's interconnected tunnel system. Thistle deduced this is Adimond's exit strategy, consistent with Joon's prior intelligence about the Maelstrom's tunnels.
- **Dragon-fairy hybrid program**: Adimond is conducting active alchemical experiments to create dragon-fairy hybrids, promising Serissa these creatures will be loyal to her. The true purpose is presumably to serve Adimond's own agenda.
- **Serissa's father**: Serissa believes her father is alive and holds critical information. Multiple search parties dispatched by the Imperator have found nothing. Serissa's clues have not led anywhere productive.
- **Serissa's mother**: Deceased. Was known for seeking accommodation with small peoples ("little people") as a policy position. Referenced negatively by Nym in the context of Serissa's alleged romantic interest in a dwarf.
- **Adimond knows the 3HPP**: He recognized them immediately when asked to identify the supposed assassins and knows they are not dead. He has not disclosed this to anyone else in the room.
- **Imperator Uthor's motive**: Assessed by Thistle as preferring Serissa as a manipulable queen. His loyalty is to his own authority, not to Serissa personally.
- **Mirran and Nym are coordinated by Adimond**: Thistle observed Adimond directing them during the throne room session, indicating the sisters are not independent actors but part of Adimond's faction.
- **Treasure in the sinkhole**: The treasure from wrecked ships is no longer with the ships—it has been relocated by crabs to the sinkhole tunnel area.
- **Sinkhole structure**: 100-foot-deep, 180-foot-wide sinkhole at the Maelstrom's ocean floor, with a 50-foot-diameter iron-grated drain. Plugging the drain would dissolve the Maelstrom vortex.
