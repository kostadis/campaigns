## NPC Activity

**Harshnag the Grim**
Harshnag traveled with the party toward Xantharl's Keep over two days, passing through Longsaddle. He explained the Ordning — the giant caste hierarchy created by the giant god Amn — and its collapse following the disappearance of King Hekaton of the Storm Giants. He revealed knowledge of a hidden mountain temple called the Eye of the All-Father, built under the Spine of the World, which contains a divine oracle. He proposed leading the party there to learn how to end the giants' threat to the North. He negotiated a 100 gold piece share of the 5,000 gold piece bounty for Worvil Forkbeard. He provided information about Longsaddle, including its establishments (the Gilded Horseshoe inn, the Gambling Golem fest hall) and the Harpell family's protective presence at Ivy Mansion. He named Count Thullen, a cloud giant and younger brother of Countess Sansuri, as a potentially cooperative contact regarding flying towers/castles. He expressed contempt for the Lords' Alliance. He requested the party bring him soup from the Gilded Horseshoe. During the attack on Xantharl's Keep, a frost giant was reported leading a goblin/hobgoblin/ogre force attacking from the west — a second frost giant then attacked the first, suggesting Harshnag intervened.

**Krowen Valharrow**
Referenced but absent. Established as a Harper Agent who recruited the party as agents for hire to investigate the end of the Ordning, granting them access to teleportation circles in exchange for their cooperation. Harshnag cited Krowen's authority when pressing the party to comply.

**King Hekaton**
Referenced but absent. Identified as the Storm Giant king whose disappearance has destabilized the Ordning and enabled giant warlords to begin waging war in the North.

**Count Thullen**
Referenced but absent. Named by Harshnag as a cloud giant of good character and younger brother of Countess Sansuri. Suggested as a potential contact for acquiring a flying tower/castle. Harshnag contrasted him favorably against his sister.

**Countess Sansuri**
Referenced but absent. Named as Count Thullen's sister and described unfavorably by Harshnag.

**Narbeck Horn**
Lord of Xantharl's Keep. Received the party after his guard Orgazol accepted a bribe to grant them an audience. Dismissed operational details to his lieutenant. Responded to a horn signaling an attack from the west by goblin, hobgoblin, and ogre forces led by a frost giant. Noted the arrival of a second frost giant attacking the first. Expressed intent to investigate the party's connection to these giants. Smiled evilly at his lieutenant, implying the lieutenant bears responsibility if the bounty situation goes wrong.

**Orgazol**
Guard at Xantharl's Keep. Referenced as having accepted a bribe from the party to secure an audience with Narbeck Horn.

**Worvil Forkbeard (the Weevil / Larg)**
A dwarf criminal with a 5,000 gold piece bounty. Was hiding at a location called the Falling Orc in Xantharl's Keep. Targeted by bugbears apparently seeking to secure the reward themselves. Wounded by fireballs during the fight and then captured by the party. Attempted to claim the party had saved him from the bugbears; exposed by the wanted poster Thistle presented.

## Faction Movements

**Harpers**
Krowen Valharrow (Harper Agent) secured the party's cooperation as agents-for-hire to investigate the collapse of the Ordning, granting teleportation circle access as payment. Harshnag is operating in coordination with Krowen's directive.

**Lords' Alliance**
Longsaddle identified as a Lords' Alliance member town. Harshnag expressed hostility toward the organization, characterizing it as a federation of powerful elites seeking to tax and restrict freedom. No Alliance operational activity noted this session.

**Harpells (Longsaddle)**
Referenced as a powerful family of human wizards living at Ivy Mansion west of Longsaddle. Founded the village but do not govern it. Maintain magical wards throughout Longsaddle and the Ivy Mansion that alert them to trouble. Not directly encountered.

**Bugbear Group (unnamed)**
Attacked Xantharl's Keep from the east as a diversion coordinated with the western goblin/hobgoblin/ogre assault. Their objective was the Falling Orc — apparently attempting to capture Worvil Forkbeard and claim the bounty themselves. Were defeated by the party; Weevil was captured.

**Goblin/Hobgoblin/Ogre Force**
Attacked Xantharl's Keep from the west, led by a frost giant. Functioned as a diversion for the bugbear eastern approach. A second frost giant (presumed Harshnag) attacked the first mid-assault.

## Revealed Information

- The Eye of the All-Father is a hidden mountain temple beneath the Spine of the World, built by ancient giant ancestors, containing a divine oracle. Harshnag knows its location and has offered to lead the party there.
- The giant god is named Amn and created the Ordning as a religious caste hierarchy. Without it, giant factions are now actively warring.
- King Hekaton of the Storm Giants has disappeared; his absence is the proximate cause of the Ordning's collapse.
- Count Thullen (cloud giant) is characterized as good-natured and potentially approachable; his sister Countess Sansuri is not.
- The Harpell family maintains magical surveillance wards across all of Longsaddle and their estate, Ivy Mansion.
- The bugbear attack on the Falling Orc was a coordinated effort to seize the Weevil bounty, not a random assault.
