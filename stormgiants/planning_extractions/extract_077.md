## NPC Activity

**Blagothkus**
- Cloud giant; former ally of the Cult of the Dragon now positioned as a pivotal figure in the castle's control structure.
- His fate is narratively linked to his son, who is trapped as a ghost and cannot be freed until Blagothkus is slain.
- May act as either an aid or a hindrance to the party depending on how they approach the castle power struggle.
- Described as driven by guilt and a desire to restore his family's honor.

**Escarlotta (Ghost)**
- Spectral wife of Blagothkus; holds direct control over the flying castle's operational systems.
- Can be convinced to side with the party if they demonstrate intent to save her son.
- Characterized as complex — alternately helpful or obstructive depending on circumstances.
- Her son's fate (trapped as a ghost) is contingent on Blagothkus' death.

**Dracolich Glazeal**
- Undead dragon with a phylactery hidden in an ice cavern within the flying castle.
- Previously struck a deal with the party to retrieve its phylactery.
- Assessed as untrustworthy — may turn on the party if circumstances favor it.
- Not physically active in this session summary beyond being the subject of ongoing alliance negotiation.

**Lord Khasperi Dryland**
- Owner and operator of The Grand Dame gambling boat moored in Yartar.
- Characterized as charismatic and secretive.
- Connected to the plot surrounding Hekaton's disappearance/capture; holds information the party needs.
- Has private quarters aboard the boat that may contain sensitive intelligence.

**Queen Serissa (Storm Giant)**
- Acting head of the storm giant court in Hekaton's absence.
- Has tasked the party with locating her father, Hekaton.
- Described as desperate; understands the disappearance threatens the broader balance of power.
- Providing guidance to the party from her position at the storm giant court; not present at active scene locations.

## Faction Movements

**Cult of the Dragon**
- Referenced as a former ally of Blagothkus; no longer in direct control of the flying castle's political situation.
- Their prior alliance with Blagothkus is a background factor shaping current castle dynamics.

**Yartar Thieves' Guild**
- Operates as a secretive criminal network beneath Yartar's surface.
- Holds intelligence relevant to identifying Hekaton's kidnappers.
- Available as a negotiation target, potential ally, or antagonist depending on party approach.
- Controls or influences access to key underworld information in the region.

**Storm Giant Court**
- Operating under Queen Serissa's leadership in Hekaton's absence.
- Actively seeking Hekaton's return; has engaged the party as agents toward that end.
- Internal vulnerability flagged: one of Hekaton's closest advisors is implicated in a betrayal connected to the broader conspiracy.

**Human Factions (unnamed)**
- Referenced collectively as parties vying for control of the giants' kingdom.
- Involved in the conspiracy surrounding Hekaton's disappearance alongside "ancient powers."
- No specific faction names provided in this session summary.
