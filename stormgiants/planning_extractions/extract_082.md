**Iymrith (the Doom of the Desert / "Adminond Kroskas" / "Adimond Kroskas")**
- Not present but is the central subject of Thistle's conspiracy theory, which the group largely accepts.
- Alleged actions per Thistle's theory: conducted experiments on fairies (responsible for Thistle's dragon eyes); used Tiamat's war to fund fairy-capture operations; perverted the Oracle of Amman to announce the sundering of the Ordning and ignite the Giant War; attempted to use Eigeron as an ally before his death; approached King Hekaton with an offer of help (rebuffed); orchestrated the murder of Queen Nerissa to destabilize Hekaton; arranged the kidnapping of Hekaton aboard a scry-proof ship; allied with Hekaton's two daughters (the sisters) with a promise to deliver the throne to them; manipulated Queen Serissa into cooperating with dragon experiments in exchange for help; used the Kraken Society as a front—rebranded cultists (described as "Cult of the Dragon losers")—to carry out murders and kidnappings while obscuring dragon involvement.

**Queen Serissa**

**King Hekaton**

**Hekaton's Two Sisters (unnamed)**
