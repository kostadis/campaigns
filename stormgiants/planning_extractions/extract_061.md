## Revealed Information

- **Ironslag (Mount Hamarhaast)**: A dungeon forge carved into the base of a 500-foot-high mountainside cliff. The entrance consists of a pair of 50-foot-tall adamantine doors described as airtight valves sealed by ancient magic. The dwarves of Silvermarsh call it "hammer of ashes." A Yakfolk village sits on top of the cliff above the doors.
- **Yakfolk possession ritual**: The Yakfolk use a post-meal "cleansing ritual" involving a hand-holding circle with humming as a vector for attempted mind possession. The ritual requires physical contact to function and is undermined if a target is forewarned.
- **Chief Kartha-Kaya's household**: The chief has at least two consorts/wives (Imberu as "first hand," Nahala as second) who participate directly in the possession ritual, suggesting it is a practiced, multi-person technique.
- **The young moon elf servant**: Her visible fear suggests non-Yakfolk inhabitants of the village are not there willingly and may be slaves or captives.
