- **Klamath's contract with Bressik** contains a necromantic clause — servitude extends beyond death into undead form, with the undead portion estimated at 100 years of additional service (~10,000 gold of the ~13,000 total value).
- **Klamath is a Red Dragon and a necromancer** who keeps contracts in his hoard and views giants as rivals for northern territorial dominance.
- **Delsephine's faction of the Cult of the Dragon** explicitly did not support Severin's Tiamat summoning ritual and considers it a corruption of Sammaster's original Dracolich philosophy.

- **The gong** is described as the broken shield of a frost giant champion: 13 feet in diameter, 250 pounds, made of red dragon scales on a beaten copper frame, suspended by ropes.
- **Unla Kee's residuals deal** was structured to apply only to the current play, not a sequel — meaning Delsephine's residual claim will be legally voided when the current production ends and a new one begins.
