## NPC Activity

**Lord Drylund**
- Appeared aboard the Grand Dame and later at the Yartar central market; acted as host and guide for the 3HPP.
- When shown the golden goose coin, feigned disinterest but inadvertently confirmed the coin's origin ("that's one of ours"), revealing its dual value system (10 gold on the ship, worthless off it).
- Deflected questions about King Hekaton with visible anxiety, detected as lying by Unla Kee and Thistle.
- Introduced party to market vendors Tapouach, Tapuz, and Mitbach as cover for the meeting.
- Confirmed Oosith is his "campaign manager for special persuasion operations" — a euphemism for a child kidnapping operation used to coerce votes.
- Admitted knowledge of Oosith's sewer location when pressed; led the party into the sewers despite claiming plausible deniability.
- Under extreme duress, with Orsik's hands on him and Kuo-Toans closing in, revealed: he is a member of the Kraken Society; the Kraken Society has been holding King Hekaton prisoner aboard a ship called the **Morkoth**, a vessel that cannot be detected, currently sailing the northern islands of the Trackless Sea.
- Died immediately after this confession — eyes bulged, blood from nose — apparently killed remotely by Oosith.
- **Status: Deceased.** Died in Orsik's hands in Oosith's sewer office.

**Lady Atalia**
- Waited at the Yartar docks at dawn to intercept the 3HPP as they disembarked from the Grand Dame.
- Led the party to a nearby tavern and revealed she had been blackmailed by Lord Drylund after he identified her skill at social manipulation ("Suave").
- Deflected questions about King Hekaton, showing the same anxious avoidance pattern as Drylund.
- Redirected the party toward the kidnapping of a local noble ("Lord Artan") before being pressed back onto the Hekaton topic.
- Named **Oosith** as an evil spellcaster running the lordling kidnapping operation; described him as hiding in the sewers, protected by fish people (Kuo-Toans), evading both the Hand and the Shield of Yartar.
- Claimed personal blackmail as her reason for involvement and requested the party kill Oosith in exchange for his location, framing herself as a reluctant participant seeking redemption.
- The party did not agree to help her and walked away.
- **Status: Last seen at the tavern near Yartar docks. Alive.**

**Oosith**
- Not physically encountered in person; communicated telepathically with the entire party upon their entry into the sewer chamber.
- Demonstrated awareness of each party member by name, including knowledge of Unla Kee's drinking habits.
- Invited the party into his underground office via telepathy, promising alcohol to Unla Kee.
- Orchestrated a trap: Kuo-Toan guards slammed and attempted to lock the party inside his office.
- Remotely killed Lord Drylund the moment Drylund finished revealing Hekaton's location — method appeared to be some form of psychic or magical assassination (eyes bulging, blood from nose).
- After Drylund's death, telepathically announced his annoyance at losing his Waterbaron candidate and declared intent to kill the party.

- **Status: Present in or near the sewer complex beneath Yartar. Alive. Exact form/location within the complex unconfirmed.**

## Revealed Information

- **King Hekaton's location:** Held prisoner aboard a ship called the **Morkoth**, a vessel with some form of detection-suppression. Currently sailing the northern islands of the **Trackless Sea**.
- **The golden goose coins** are Kraken Society tokens with an internal economy: worth 10 gold aboard the Grand Dame, worthless elsewhere. The coin device found in Oosith's office causes the coins to glow and display a number — likely an anti-forgery verification system.
- **Lord Drylund's campaign strategy** was coercion via child kidnapping: he held the children of Yartar's voting class hostage to guarantee electoral outcomes.
- **Oosith** runs the kidnapping operation and serves as Drylund's "special persuasion" operative. He is a spellcaster capable of telepathy, charm, and likely remote magical assassination.
- **Lady Atalia** was blackmailed into assisting Drylund's operation after he identified her social manipulation abilities. She knows Oosith's location and sought to trade it for his death.
- **A pendant** bearing a capital R wrapped in robes (suggesting a noble house or organization sigil) was recovered from Oosith's office among his possessions.
