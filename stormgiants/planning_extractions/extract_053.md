## Revealed Information

- **Klamath's contract with Bressik** is necromantic in structure, covering both a living phase (valued at ~300 gold based on remaining lifespan) and an undead phase (valued at ~13,000 gold based on projected undead longevity). The contract is physically held in Klamath's hoard.
- **Delsephine's faction of the Cult of the Dragon** diverged from Severin's faction over the interpretation of Sammaster's texts. They reject the Tiamat summoning ritual as a corruption of the original Dracolich vision and view Severin and Tiamat as having staged a coup against the original movement.
- **Klamath views giants as rivals** to draconic territorial dominance in the North and is therefore strategically aligned with the party's mission. He will seek a meeting and offer reward after the giants are dealt with.
- **Unla Kee's residuals contract with Delsephine** was deliberately structured to apply only to the current play, not any sequel, limiting Klamath/Delsephine's long-term financial claim on the party's theatrical enterprise.
- **The Great Worm Cavern gong** is a broken frost giant champion's shield made of red dragon scales on a beaten copper frame, 13 feet in diameter, weighing 250 pounds. Bressik assessed it as likely decorative rather than an artifact, consistent with the pattern of artifacts being located beneath altars.
