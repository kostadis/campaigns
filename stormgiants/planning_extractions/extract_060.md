## NPC Activity

**Blagothkus (Cloud Giant)**
- Last known location: holed up in the control room of the cloud castle, refusing to leave.
- Has been unwilling to move since arriving at Parnast, reportedly crying and claiming he "killed his son."
- Refuses to open the control room door, having been deceived previously by Sandesyl.
- Uses a periscope with mirrors to verify visitors are not vampires before communicating.
- Revealed that Harshnag's "first time in a thousand years" framing of the Oracle is a recurring joke — Harshnag hides items in the temple to test whether visitors are worthy.
- Claims he cannot leave the castle because of his wife (not the dracolich), identity of wife not yet established.
- Has been trapped and isolated for months while Sandesyl's forces killed his retinue.

**Sandesyl (Vampire, referred to as "the purple lady" and "crazy vampire lady")**
- Not physically present during this session but her actions and presence dominate the session context.
- Killed Blagothkus' entire retinue over the past months, raising the dead as undead to control the castle during daylight hours.
- Used kobolds in the guest room area as a living blood bank, tying them to their beds and slowly draining them until death.
- Previously gained access to parts of the castle by persuading ogres to let her in (exploiting vampire entry rules).
- Unable to enter Blagothkus' control room or the tower where Worug and Tak sheltered, as neither invited her in.
- Described as believing she is "the big boss lady" of the castle.
- Inferred by the party to be the actual proprietor or at least to have claimed control of the castle, possibly using it as a base for necromantic experiments.
- Currently inactive during daytime; location within the castle at night is unspecified.

**Worug (Ogre, scarred face)**
- Survived Sandesyl's massacre by refusing to let her into their tower.
- Has been surviving by sneaking out during daylight hours to forage for food.
- Provided detailed account of how Sandesyl killed Blagothkus' retinue and raised the dead.
- Disclosed the secret of Glazhael the dracolich living inside the glacier in exchange for a promise of extraction from the castle.
- Revealed apparent romantic feelings for Tak during conversation.
- Last known location: their barred tower on the cloud castle.

**Tak (Ogre, bandaged right hand)**
- Survived alongside Worug using the same strategy of refusing vampire entry.
- Confirmed the dracolich secret in exchange for a promise of evacuation.
- Appeared to reciprocate Worug's feelings but had not previously disclosed them.
- Last known location: shared tower with Worug on the cloud castle.

**Glazhael (Dracolich, white dragon)**
- Not present but discussed in detail.
- Originally the steed of Talis the White.
- Killed by "the First Faction" (context suggests a previous adventuring group involved in the defeat of Tiamat).
- Rose as a dracolich and attempted to capture the cloud castle but was lured away on a false quest for an artifact during the events surrounding Tiamat's return.
- After Tiamat's defeat was thwarted, Glazhael returned to the glacier beneath/within the castle.
- Current status: residing in the glacier that the cloud castle is built around.

**Harshnag (Frost Giant, referenced but absent)**
- Revealed to have a long-standing practice of bringing "little people" to the Oracle and framing it as a once-in-a-millennium event.
- Hides the items needed for the Oracle puzzle in the temple himself, using the challenge as a worthiness test for visitors.
- Not present; referenced in conversation between Blagothkus and the party.

**Talis the White (referenced but absent)**
- Former owner/handler of Glazhael before the dragon's death and transformation into a dracolich.
- Not present; mentioned only in the context of Glazhael's history.

## Faction Movements

- Have effectively seized operational control of the cloud castle, killing all of Blagothkus' loyal retinue and raising them as undead.
- Kobold population aboard the castle has been entirely killed and used as a blood bank.
- Undead forces prevent daytime movement by survivors but are being whittled down by the party.
- The faction's hold on the castle is weakening as the party destroys undead soldiers (ogre zombies, frost giant skeletons).

**Blagothkus' Household (effectively destroyed)**
- Retinue killed by Sandesyl over the past months.
- Only surviving loyal members are Worug and Tak, who have now allied with the party in exchange for evacuation.
- Blagothkus himself is isolated and non-functional as a leader.

## Revealed Information

- Sandesyl is likely the true power claiming the castle, not merely a passenger — the party suspects she sought the sky castle as a base for necromantic operations.
- Glazhael the dracolich is inside the glacier the cloud castle is built around; she returned after Tiamat's defeat was thwarted.
- Vampire entry rules are in effect aboard the castle: Sandesyl cannot enter spaces where she has not been invited, which explains why Blagothkus and the two ogres remain alive.
- Blagothkus cannot or will not leave the castle — he states his wife is the reason he is trapped, not the dracolich. The identity and nature of this wife are unknown.
- Harshnag's Oracle ritual is a deliberate constructed test, not a genuine ancient rite. He plants the required items himself and frames each visit as unprecedented to motivate visitors.
- The cloud castle previously played a role in the defeat of Tiamat, at which time Frost Giants (not Cloud Giants) crewed the vessel.
