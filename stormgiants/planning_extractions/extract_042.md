## NPC Activity

**Yazmeen**
- Found hiding behind a couch in a large library/house in Prettyview, described as a gnome of average height with a crooked nose, scarred hands, face tattoos, and orange hair.
- Identified himself as the local librarian; explained that the town was a book-exchange hub where patrons from neighboring towns traded books, always accompanied by tea service.
- Reported that Frost Giants attacked and destroyed Prettyview, leaving "feline companions" behind after the assault.
- Claimed he survived by remaining well hidden during the attack.
- Offered the party lodging in the library and tea; the tea was later revealed to have magical properties.
- During the night, was replaced in his bedroom by a Slaad (i.e., the Slaad had been using him as a portal anchor); was found sleeping and unconscious, carried out of the burning building by Vardis.
- After rescue, revealed the full context of the Slaad's plan: the creature had been using Prettyview's inhabitants as interplanar anchors to summon more of its kind; the ritual was nearly complete when the Frost Giant attack disrupted it by killing the anchors.
- Confirmed Vardis's deduction that the magical tea warded off the Slaad's possession/portal use, but only while he remained awake and drinking; sleep caused the effect to lapse, allowing the Slaad to return.
- Declined Orsik's invitation to travel with the party, citing responsibility to his remaining library patrons and his books.
- Status after session: alive, uninjured, remaining in Prettyview; library destroyed by fire.

**The Slaad (unnamed, deceased)**
- A monstrous aberration described as a giant bipedal toad; emerged from Yazmeen's bedroom into the library during the night.
- Declared to the party "YOU RUINED MY BREEDING PROGRAM."
- Attempted to cast a fireball; countered by Unla Kee.
- Retaliated by setting the library books ablaze in an attempt to destroy the space and escape through the bedroom window.
- Trapped by Unla Kee's wand of the web; continued casting fire at Unla Kee in an attempt to break concentration.
- Killed in the library before escaping.
- Post-death context: Yazmeen revealed the Slaad had been using him as a dimensional portal and had been orchestrating an interplanar anchor ritual in Prettyview.

**Harshnag the Grim**
- Commented to Orsik that he had "grown" since his days as a guard in Waterdeep, referencing an unspecified incident ("that Thing") Orsik is embarrassed by.
- Argued with Orsik about the group's next destination, advocating for going directly to "the Oracle" rather than pursuing a treasure map.
- Muttered under his breath that "the All-Father sent a sign," apparently in reference to why the Oracle should be prioritized.
- During the avalanche in the Middle Valley, was not buried by the snow; dug the party out and laughed heartily while doing so, making a self-aware comment about giants helping "little people."
- Current whereabouts: traveling east with the party through the Middle Valley of the Spine of the World.

**Frost Giants (unnamed, deceased)**
- Their looted corpses were present at the start of the session; Orsik recovered significant gold and silver coin from their bags.
- Established as the force that destroyed Prettyview: killed inhabitants, demolished buildings with boulders and swords, and left "feline companions" behind.
- Their attack inadvertently disrupted the Slaad's interplanar anchor ritual by killing the inhabitants who were serving as anchors.
