## NPC Activity

**Bad Fruul (Hill Giant Warlord)**
- Referenced throughout as the primary threat. Not physically present.
- Orsik warns Chandra that Bad Fruul can send minions to kill her rather than acting personally.
- Goblins are observed carrying Bad Fruul's banner as part of his growing hoard, allowing Unla Kee to estimate hoard size.
- Orsik deceived some of Bad Fruul's goblin followers with false information, causing fewer goblins to join his banner.
- Attack route on Parnast confirmed to come from the north based on independent investigation by Thistle, Vardis, and Wallace.

**Chandra**
- Present in Parnast. Intimidated by Orsik's forceful argument about her safety and agreed to accept his protection.

**Prince Thornacious**
- Present in Parnast. Ordered a fine dinner for the heroes at the conclusion of the previous evening's events.

**Blasius**
- Present briefly in Parnast. Departed, bidding farewell to Thistle before leaving.

**Uncle Joon (referenced, absent)**
- Mentioned by Thistle as a source of knowledge about orc tribes and den locations. Thistle asserts Uncle Joon is "on the good guys' side."

**The Orog (Spine Ripper Tribe, unnamed)**
- Stationed as a guard at the cave entrance of the Spine Ripper orc den.
- Spotted the party and fled inside to raise the alarm.
- Later emerged with the Acolyte of Gruumsh and attacked, engaging and knocking Unla Kee unconscious.
- Killed by Orsik, Wallace, and Thistle.

**Eye of Gruumsh (Spine Ripper Shaman, unnamed)**
- Orc shaman found dead in the cave complex during post-battle exploration. Leader of the tribe's religious contingent.
- Had an acolyte follower.

**Acolyte of Gruumsh (unnamed)**
- Follower of the Eye of Gruumsh. Emerged with the Orog and attacked the party.
- Knocked Unla Kee unconscious before being killed.

**Orc Claw of Luthic (unnamed)**
- Guarding orc children in the nursery section of the cave.
- Threatened by the party but not killed. Grabbed her own whelp and withdrew when the companions leveraged the presence of her child as a hostage.
- Last seen moving away deeper into the nursery with her whelp.

**Chief Kurglow (Spine Ripper Tribe, referenced)**
- Not present. Revealed as the orc chief who coerced Jarlor and Larrish into spying by threatening their families.

**Jarlor (referenced, absent)**
- Not present in the cave. His family was held prisoner by the Spine Ripper orcs.
- Coerced by Chief Kurglow into spying under threat of his family's death.
- Orsik recognizes him from his time on the town council.

**Larrish (referenced, absent)**
- Not present in the cave. His family was also held prisoner.
- Similarly coerced by Chief Kurglow to spy.
- Recognized by Orsik from his time on the town council.

**Prisoner Families of Jarlor and Larrish**
- Two families held in a pit within the Spine Ripper cave complex.
- Freed by the companions. Believed the militia had come to rescue them upon seeing Orsik.
- Revealed the coercion scheme. One child defended Jarlor's actions, stating he had no choice.

## Faction Movements

**Bad Fruul's Hoard**
- Hoard size estimated by Unla Kee from the number of banners visible among hoard members.
- From that estimate, Unla Kee deduced that the Spine Ripper orc tribe had **not yet joined** the hoard, identifying them as a potential target for recruitment denial.
- Goblin recruitment to the hoard disrupted: Orsik's deception caused fewer goblins to join Bad Fruul's banner.
- Attack vector on Parnast confirmed as coming from the **north** by three independent investigations.

**The Spine Ripper Tribe**
- Located in a cave complex in the hills near Parnast's forest approaches.
- Tribal religious leadership (Eye of Gruumsh, Acolyte, Orog) killed in combat.
- Cave complex contained a War Hearth (confirmed by Vardis) with shrines to orc gods and capacity for many warriors.
- Tribe had been coercing Parnast locals (Jarlor and Larrish) into spying via family hostages.
- Remaining noncombatants (Orc Claw of Luthic, children) withdrew but were not eliminated.
- Tribe has **not yet joined** Bad Fruul's hoard — their status as an independent faction now disrupted by the raid.

**Parnast Defenders**
- Town fortifications active: roads barricaded, pits dug in front of barricades at the town center.
- Confirmed attack will come from the north; defenders have 2 days remaining before anticipated assault.

## Revealed Information

- **Bad Fruul's attack vector** is confirmed from the north, with fortifications already in place at Parnast's town center.
- **Bad Fruul uses proxy coercion**: Chief Kurglow (acting in alignment with or parallel to Bad Fruul's interests) held civilian families hostage to force Parnast locals to spy — suggesting intelligence-gathering operations against Parnast are ongoing.
- **The Spine Ripper tribe** was the unjoined orc faction Unla Kee deduced from hoard banner counts. Their den is in the hills near Parnast.
- **Jarlor and Larrish**, both known to Orsik from the town council, have been unwillingly turned as informants under duress. Their loyalty is coerced, not ideological.
- **Uncle Joon** is claimed by Thistle to have detailed knowledge of every orc tribe and den location in the region, and is asserted to be allied with the "good guys."
