## Revealed Information

- **Portal Activation Mechanism:** The archway portal in the Temple of Annam has six mithral-inlaid runes corresponding to the six giant subraces in ordning order (hill, cloud, fire, storm, stone, frost). Touching a rune triggers a dangerous magical effect. The correct method appears to involve using the weapon from the corresponding giant statue to touch the rune rather than direct touch. The Frost Giant statue's weapon is missing and must be located somewhere in the temple.
- **The Frost Giant Rune:** Triggers a magical ice storm when touched directly; Harshnag is immune to frost damage but the activation still failed without the correct weapon.
- **The Hill Giant Rune:** Triggers a confusion-like effect of howling magical voices when its club is used; also failed to activate the portal correctly, suggesting the hill rune is not the correct one or additional steps are required.
- **The Storm Rune:** Touching it without the proper weapon triggers a Storm of Vengeance — acid rain followed by six powerful lightning bolts, lethal to the entire party. Extremely dangerous.
- **Temple Architecture:** The stone blocks in the entrance hallway are a giant-designed trap; the ledges were designed for giant defenders to throw boulders. The temple was built 40,000 years ago by magic-wielding giants of the civilization of Osteria.
- **Annam's Statue:** The face is intentionally blank — the sculptors did not know what the All-Father looks like and chose not to guess.
- **Great Worm Tribe's Purpose:** Harshnag confirms they came to plunder the temple, not for any religious or political mission with a larger faction.
