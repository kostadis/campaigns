## NPC Activity

**Urlam Stockspool**
- Appeared at the Talking Troll tavern in Triboar, accompanied by a crew of approximately 10 Zhentarim members.
- Greeted Orsik warmly (with kisses on each cheek), indicating recognition of Orsik's Zhentarim standing.
- Provided intelligence on Adimond Kroskas: identified him as a free agent (not Cult of the Dragon), described him as a specialist in creating hybrid magical creatures, and stated that people who knew him personally are now dead.
- Directed the party to Silverymoon's magical libraries as the best source of further information on Kroskas.
- Revealed that House Margaster sent cultists to his establishment, who killed some of his guards when he resisted their demands for a discount.
- Stated that Zhentarim leadership has declared House Margaster untouchable at the organizational level.
- Implicated Orthovir as having a relationship with House Margaster, suggesting Orthovir may have deliberately set the party up.
- Offered 200 gold (framed as a "friends and family discount") for the party to eliminate House Margaster, while clarifying this is not an official Zhentarim position.
- Became briefly offended when Thistle raised the topic of contraband, but was mollified by Orsik's intervention and additional wine.

**Adimond Kroskas** (referenced, not present)
- Discussed as a historical figure; confirmed to be a free agent unaffiliated with the Cult of the Dragon.
- Described as a specialist hired to create hybrid magical creatures.
- His associates and those who knew him are reported to be dead.
- Any surviving knowledge of him is said to rest with senior Zhentarim hierarchy who were involved in events surrounding the fall of the Cult of the Dragon.

**Orthovir** (referenced, not present)
- Urlam revealed that Orthovir has a relationship with House Margaster.
- This prompted the party's suspicion that Orthovir deliberately set them up in a prior interaction.

**Hyuth** (referenced, not present)

**Harshnag the Grim**
- Appeared at the party's roadside campfire on the road to Xanthalar's Keep.
- Identified himself as a member of Force Grey; described as a legendary good-aligned frost giant of great age.
- Wears plate armor and a white dragon skull as a helm; carries a jagged greataxe.
- Stated he had been searching for the party specifically, directed by Krowen Valharrow, in connection with a prior agreement the party made to investigate giant activity.
- Delivered intelligence on the giant crisis: the Ordning has been broken, storm giants have lost the authority to keep lesser giants in check, and King Hekaton is missing.
- Proposed guiding the party to the Eye of the All-Father, a mountain temple beneath the Spine of the World built by giant ancestors, which allegedly houses a divine oracle capable of revealing what is happening with the giants.
- Agreed to accompany the party to Xanthalar's Keep first to collect the bounty on Weevil, requesting a 100 gold share of the reward.

**Krowen Valharrow** (referenced, not present)
- Mentioned by Harshnag as the source who told him the party had agreed to investigate the giant crisis.

## Faction Movements

**Zhentarim**
- Urlam Stockspool confirmed the modern Zhentarim's self-described shift away from apocalyptic goals (formerly aligned with Bane's dominion) toward commercial interests.
- Senior Zhentarim leadership has declared House Margaster off-limits organizationally, but individual agents such as Urlam have personal grievances against them.
- Urlam extended an unofficial, personal 200-gold contract to the party for eliminating House Margaster.
- The Zhentarim were historically involved in events surrounding the fall of the Cult of the Dragon, and senior members may hold surviving knowledge of Adimond Kroskas.

**House Margaster**
- Confirmed to have sent cultists to intimidate Urlam Stockspool, resulting in the deaths of some of his guards.
- Zhentarim leadership has placed them under organizational protection ("untouchable").
- Orthovir is revealed to have a relationship with House Margaster, suggesting possible infiltration or compromise of prior party contacts.

**Force Grey**
- Harshnag identified himself as a member; described as a historically significant adventuring company known for defending Waterdeep.
- Harshnag is currently operating independently in the North, pursuing the giant crisis investigation.
