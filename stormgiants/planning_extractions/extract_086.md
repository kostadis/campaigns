## Revealed Information

- **Bob's nature:** Bob is a demon whose portfolio was reassigned from traditional torture to punishment-by-comedy; he punishes souls (and powerful people who mistreated jesters) with bad jokes for eternity, with traditional tortures as backup. He is classified as an MPE (Massively Powerful Entity) under interplanar treaty law.
- **The 1489 Treaty:** Signed in Sigil between the Trading House/Chromax, Githyanki, Githizeri, and Mindflayers. ISDs (non-transient portable magical devices establishing cross-planar connections, e.g. bags of holding, portable holes) are regulated as weapons because nesting them ejects nearby MPEs to the Astral Plane, where they can be harvested by the empires. Toril is not a signatory and has secret societies/religious prescriptions against ISD use—Bob did not check this before operating here.
- **Bob's prisoners:** The 200 Silverymoon notables were held in a pocket dimension, not the Astral Plane.
- **Iymrith's grand reveal:** She is planning a public event at her lair to present her latest creations (the zombie blue-chromatic-purple worm hybrid dragons) to her giant patrons/backers.
- **Lord Aerasumé's private acknowledgment:** He privately told Thistle he does not fully believe the Iymrith framing but finds it politically useful and genuinely wants the dragon dead; he made a personal promise to elevate fairies to goodly race status contingent on Iymrith's death.
- **House Margaster:** Accused of cambion ties and alliance with Iymrith; Taern Hornblade independently considers them corrupt; formal investigation now ordered.
