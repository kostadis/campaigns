## NPC Activity

**Mirran (Storm Giant, eldest daughter of King Hekaton)**
- Appeared in the great hall of the underwater fortress performing music with her sister Nym
- Greeted Orsik's ruse with apparent warmth before revealing her true intentions
- Lured the party toward the pool/teleportation circle room under false pretense of taking them to Serissa
- Ordered four giant nobles to kill the party, framing them as assassins and claiming Serissa would never mate with a dwarf
- Hurled a lightning bolt at Vardis (which chain-struck Unla Kee) before departing the combat
- Made a swipe at Orsik during his retreat but missed
- Last seen departing the area after setting the ambush; current whereabouts unknown within the fortress

**Nym (Storm Giant, second daughter of King Hekaton)**
- Appeared in the great hall performing music (playing whalebone mallets on a living crab)
- Engaged in banter with Mirran about Serissa's schedule; implied Serissa would be "free in a few hours"
- Made condescending remarks about Serissa and their late mother sharing a "fascination" with small peoples
- Engaged Vardis in an exchange about whether the attitude constitutes racism or fetishism (declared it racism; asserted giant superiority)
- Did not participate in the ambush; last seen in the great hall

**Serissa (Storm Giant Queen, youngest daughter of King Hekaton)**
- Not physically present; referenced throughout
- Mirran claims Serissa is occupied with "activities of state" and was not expecting visitors
- Nym implies Serissa shares her late mother's interest in small peoples ("just like her mother")
- Mirran's ambush framing suggests Serissa either does not know about it or is being kept from the party deliberately

**Duchess Tartha (Fire Giant)**
- Named herself and her companions during combat
- Charged through Vardis's wall of fire unaffected (fire immunity confirmed)
- Struck Thistle with her giant sword
- Was struck by Orsik in passing, hit by Thistle's lightning arrow twice, and caught in Unla Kee's fireball (fire-immune; the explosion invigorated her)
- Rendered prone by Orsik's second strike; Orsik then moved away
- Taunted Jarl Hellenhild about stealing glory
- Still alive and in combat; last seen prone in the hallway area

**Count Vaal (Cloud Giant)**
- Named himself during combat introduction
- Activated flight ability, breaking free of gravity to hover above the battlefield
- Mocked Orsik for not knowing cloud giants could fly
- Was caught in Unla Kee's fireball explosion; took searing damage
- Confirmed ability to cast Misty Step (noted by Unla Kee)
- Still alive and in combat; airborne, last seen in the hallway/pool room area

**Jarl Hellenhild (Frost Giant)**
- Named herself during combat introduction
- Attempted to throw a boulder at Thistle (flying above the wall of fire) but missed badly
- Embarrassed herself in front of Count Vaal and Duchess Tartha; blamed a "new powder for a better grip"
- Caught in Unla Kee's fireball; described as "beginning to look worse" — most damaged of the surviving giants
- Still alive and in combat; last seen on the far side of Vardis's wall of fire

**Thane Braxow (Stone Giant)**
- Named himself ("the quiet one") during combat introduction
- Targeted first by Orsik; struck four times total
- Rendered prone by Orsik's attacks, then killed with a final blow while prone
- Deceased; body last seen in the pool/teleportation circle room area
- Notable: not affected by the Giant Slayer sword's magic, only the physical damage

## Faction Movements

**Storm Giant Royal Family (Mirran/Nym faction)**
- Mirran actively moved against the party: lured them under false pretense, framed them as assassins to the assembled giant nobles, and secured four noble giants as enforcers
- The framing as "assassins come to kill the Queen" was deliberately constructed to prevent the party from appealing to Serissa — killing Mirran would confirm the story
- Mirran's faction appears to be deliberately isolating Serissa from outside contact and managing access to her

**Assembled Giant Nobles (Tartha, Vaal, Hellenhild, Braxow)**
- Convened at the underwater fortress, apparently as guests or allies of the Storm Giant court
- Mobilized as a unit by Mirran's call to loyalty; framed as defending the Queen
- Lost one member (Thane Braxow, killed); remaining three engaged in active combat

## Threat Arc Events

**Mirran's Obstruction of Serissa Access**
- Mirran successfully framed the party as assassins before a gathering of giant nobles, creating a political and physical barrier between the party and Queen Serissa. This represents a deliberate move to isolate the Queen and eliminate outside interference — potentially relevant to any arc tracking the destabilization of the Storm Giant court or the suppression of King Hekaton's legitimate line.

## Revealed Information

- **Cloud giants can fly** and cast **Misty Step** — confirmed in combat by Count Vaal
- **Fire giants are immune to fire** — Duchess Tartha walked through Vardis's wall of fire unaffected; Unla Kee's fireball invigorated rather than harmed her
- **Serissa's mother shared an interest in small peoples** — both Nym and Mirran referenced this, suggesting it was known within the family; the guestrooms "for little people" were reportedly designed by the mother
- **Killing Mirran would confirm the "assassin" framing** — Thistle deduced this from court intrigue experience; the party has chosen not to kill her
- **Thistle has concealed abilities** — displayed an ice breath weapon during combat that surprised the rest of the party; Unla Kee theorizes this is deliberate concealment in case of betrayal by a party member
- **Lord Zymorven's Giant Slayer blade** was identified as the weapon Orsik is using; he credits saving Zymorven's son as the reason he has it
