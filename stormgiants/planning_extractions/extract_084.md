**The SEER**
- Appeared at the party's campsite during their overland journey, accompanied by her gold pseudodragon Hsing.
- Located the party through Divination, with Hekaton sharing their position.
- Offered individually tailored deals to each party member to redirect them from killing Iymrith toward resolving Hekaton's giant lord problem:
  - To Orsik: public rehabilitation, statues and monuments across Faerûn, and credit for an elaborate heroic ruse.
  - To Unla Kee: recovery of the cloud castle without Escarlotta, plus a tax-free casino license and permits in all Lord's Alliance cities.
  - To Vardis: assurance that Orsik would live and become a hero, plus resolution of his Lorellon temple problem and an engineered apology.
  - To Thistle: arrows of dragon slaying and a powerful magical bow, with permission to pursue Iymrith afterward.
- All offers were refused.
- Became flustered and openly frustrated; revealed that Hekaton would only accept the 3HPP specifically as agents, citing a sign from the All-father.
- Argued that killing dragons is trivial compared to saving the realms; that this was a once-in-40,000-year opportunity for Giant-kingdom peace.
- Threatened to curse Orsik's name for a thousand generations when negotiations broke down.
- Was trapped in Unla Kee's Forcecage but escaped — assessed by the party to have used Time Stop to write a note and teleport out.
- Left a written note addressed to "Orsic," withdrawing her threat to slander him and asking that if he defeats Iymrith, he consider doing the realms a favor.

**Hsing** (gold pseudodragon)

- **Hekaton's condition for peace:** He will only pursue a permanent Giant-kingdom alliance with the Lord's Alliance if the 3HPP specifically handles his giant lord problem. He has refused all other candidates, citing this as a sign from the All-father. The SEER confirmed this directly.

- **Forbiddance on the castle:** Most of the cloud castle is currently under Forbiddance, with only some sections still accessible via teleportation. The caster is unknown but presumed to be a powerful Kraken Society-aligned cleric.
- **Ascore geography and hazards:** Per Uncle Joon's prior briefing — the red pyramids in the city center are to be avoided; stone ships are half-buried in the sand to the east; the hill entrance is flanked by stone griffon statues.
- **Iymrith's lair proximity:** Iymrith's lair is close to Ascore on the edge of the Anauroch.
