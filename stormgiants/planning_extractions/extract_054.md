## Revealed Information

- **Morgur's Mound layout**: Shaped like a long-necked wingless dragon; a ridge of dragon bones juts from the head, neck, back, and tail; enormous cairns encircle the mound out to a quarter mile, containing the bones of revered Thunderbeast warriors. Multiple prior dig sites exist.
- **Thunderbeast tribe fate**: Unknown — tribal conflict, incompetence, or dragon are all cited as possibilities. No definitive answer established.
- **Mound's guardian skeletons**: Disturbing the gold-plated fire giant's tooth triggered the emergence of four animated thunderbeast ankylosaurus skeletons, suggesting the tooth or that excavation site is specifically protected.
- **Giant's tooth significance**: The gold-plated fire giant's tooth is described as an ancient relic of giantkind. Its presence within a Thunderbeast spirit mound is unexplained.
- **Remaining artifact locations**: Per Delsephine, only Raven's Rock and the Great Wyrm Cavern remain unsearched.
- **Bressik's background (self-disclosed)**: Confirms he was a member of a world-ending cult seeking to bring an evil goddess into the world through a draconic age, and that he avoided punishment by entering the service of an evil red dragon.
