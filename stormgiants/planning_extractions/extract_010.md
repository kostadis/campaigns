## NPC Activity

**Mama Manyknuckles (Mama Mia)**
- A hag who was encamped in the sinkhole area of the northern island, commanding half-ogres and mud mephits as her gang.
- Became aware of the party's approach and signaled her two guards to the north ramp; attempted to direct mud mephits but received no response from them.
- Ordered her half-ogres into ambush positions when she detected the party flanking from the south.
- Used a death gaze ability during combat: killed Orsik when he failed to look away, and attempted to use it on others.
- Attempted to flee but was killed by the party before escaping.
- Final internal monologue revealed she believed her gang's alignment with Bad Fruul made their destruction inevitable.
- Found on her corpse: a stone pendant with jade inlay of Stein, the stone rune.

**Feetor (Mud Mephit)**
- Self-declared "New King of the Swamp" following Mama Manyknuckles' death.
- Had made a bet with Unla Kee prior to this session regarding the party killing the half-ogres; paid out winnings in special healing mud rather than gold.
- Attempted to placate the party by granting Unla Kee honorary Mud Mephit status and presenting a summoning stone for calling mephit assistance.
- Under pressure from Unla Kee's duel threat, agreed to acknowledge Unla Kee as leader and declared him #1, naming himself #2.
- His claim of leadership immediately triggered renewed Mud Mephit civil war as factions disputed which party member was truly strongest.
- Last seen shrugging as the internal mephit conflict resumed.

**Hsing (Golden Pseudodragon)**
- Emissary of the SEER; arrived at dawn via teleportation at the sinkhole site.
- Described as a golden pseudodragon with milky-white eyes.
- Delivered a commission from the SEER to explore the newly discovered Wizard of the Crag's tower and retrieve manuscripts on ancient forms of magic.
- Revealed the following about the Wizard of the Crag: master of elemental water and earth, worked with all elements, was a devotee of both Mystara and Oghma, and was rumored to possess a repository of lore.
- Stated that the SEER needs intelligence or resources from the tower to help counter Bad Fruul, whose rise disrupted Zhentarim operations.
- Instructed the party to use the pendant found on Mama Manyknuckles to enter the tower; warned that returning with only a single spellbook would be unacceptable.
- Departed via apparent teleportation.

**Belg**
- Named individual (implied half-ogre or similar creature) fighting alongside Mama Manyknuckles; described as her "beloved Belg."
- Killed during the combat in which Mama Manyknuckles was defeated.

**Thuul**
- Drake companion under Thistle's command; participated in combat at the northern camp, delivered a killing blow to the second half-ogre on Thistle's order.

## Faction Movements

**Mud Mephits**
- Lost their prior leadership structure following Mama Manyknuckles' death.
- Feetor briefly claimed kingship and attempted to consolidate the mephits under Unla Kee's banner.
- Faction immediately fractured again into civil war, with splinter groups backing Orsik, Vardis, and Thistle respectively as rival claimants for strongest patron.
- Summoning stone issued to the party as a token of alliance (value uncertain given ongoing instability).

**The SEER / Lord's Alliance**
- Actively monitoring the party's movements; located them in a remote swamp and dispatched Hsing without apparent difficulty.
- Holds intelligence that the sinkhole site is the tower of the Wizard of the Crag, a location the SEER has "long sought."
- Offered payment for retrieval of the wizard's manuscripts on ancient magic.
- Motivation confirmed: needs resources or lore from the tower to address the threat posed by Bad Fruul, whose rise disrupted Zhentarim regional plans.

**Bad Fruul's Forces**
- Mama Manyknuckles' gang was operating under Bad Fruul's broader organization; her death represents a loss of a local gang and camp for Bad Fruul.
- Mama Manyknuckles' final thoughts confirm Bad Fruul recruited her gang at some prior point, suggesting ongoing consolidation of swamp creatures under his banner.
- Described by both Mama Manyknuckles and the SEER as an emergent local power whose rise disrupted Zhentarim operations.

## Revealed Information

- The sinkhole on the northern island is the roof of a wizard's tower — the tower of the Wizard of the Crag, a site the SEER has long sought.
- The Wizard of the Crag was a master of elemental water and earth, worked with all elements, and was a devotee of both Mystara and Oghma.
- The wizard is rumored to have possessed a repository of lore, including manuscripts on ancient forms of magic.
- The stone pendant with jade inlay of Stein (stone rune) found on Mama Manyknuckles is a key or tool for entering the tower.
- The SEER is positioned as the top Spymaster of the Lord's Alliance (per Wallace's identification) and has sufficient reach to locate the party in a remote swamp.
- Mama Manyknuckles' gang knowingly aligned with Bad Fruul; she believed this alliance made their destruction inevitable.
- Orsik noted that killing Hsing could earn "serious cred" with the Zhentarim, implying the Zhentarim and the Lord's Alliance/SEER are in opposition.
