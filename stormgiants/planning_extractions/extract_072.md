## NPC Activity

**Glazhael the Undying**
- Refused to share her plan for defeating Sandesyl, citing secrecy.
- Departed from direct interaction with the party as they left her lair.

**Blagothkus**
- Observed the party from the upper courtyard while they worked in the lower courtyard.
- Expressed enthusiastic gratitude when the party disposed of skeletal remains, stating he had wanted this done for almost a year.
- Informed the party that undead prefer darkness and would not be out in sunlight — implying awareness of an undead presence in his castle.

**Sandesyl** (absent, referenced)
- Revealed to be conducting broader experiments on undead to create alternatives to Dracoliches.
- Her undead creations are described as lich-like: tied to phylacteries, capable of returning unless the phylactery is destroyed.
- Had undead Wyverns stabled in the castle's stables, and specters present in the kitchen.

**Undead Wyverns** (destroyed)
- Two animate, decayed Wyverns were found in the stables.
- Confirmed as undead by Thistle's True Seeing.
- Destroyed by the party; remains burned to prevent Sandesyl from reclaiming them as animation material.

**Specters** (active, combat ongoing)
- Multiple specters emerged from shadows in the abandoned kitchen upon the party's entry.
- Two specters attacked Orsik with negative energy, draining his life force.
- One specter critically wounded Unla Kee after he was stunned.

**Spectral Raven** (active, combat ongoing)
- Identified by Orsik as a particularly dangerous undead creature based on childhood stories.
- Flew away from Orsik after being struck, repositioning to the far end of the kitchen.
- Used a charming caw that stunned and left both Orsik and Unla Kee prone.
- Currently at the far end of the kitchen.

- **The spectral raven** is flagged by Orsik as distinctly dangerous, separate from standard specters.
- **Blagothkus** was aware of the skeletal remains in his lower courtyard but had not addressed them for nearly a year, implying either inability or reluctance to deal with them himself.
