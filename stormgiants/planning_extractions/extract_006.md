## Revealed Information

- **Tower mechanics**: The tower generates a new dungeon layout each time it is entered. The dungeon is attributed to "Acerak's Construction Company" (noted on a plaque at the dungeon entrance). The tower uses an antipathy field to deter entry. A key found inside can be used to exit, but the tower's controlling intelligence will offer a bribe (forfeiting collected gold) to discourage its use.
- **Dungeon population**: At time of exploration, the dungeon contained one bugbear, a tribe of goblins (resigned to their fate, focused on survival), and several ogre zombies (slow enough to avoid if moving quickly).
- **Escape theories** (as reported by the bugbear): (1) a secret passage leads out; (2) there is no escape and prisoners eventually die and become zombies; (3) death triggers automatic ejection as a warning against tampering with the wizard's tower.
- **Adimond Kroskas**: Abandoned the Cult of the Dragon before the Well of the Worlds defeat and is now working with a Giant. His motivations are unclear — possibly opportunistic rather than ideologically loyal to the Cult.
- **Thistle's drake**: If still alive, it is somewhere in the Anauroch desert.
- **Thistle's beloved**: Confirmed dead — killed either through mistreatment or consumed in a Tiamat-summoning sacrifice.
