## NPC Activity

**Halisa**
- Dryad of the southernmost tree under the Grandfather Tree's canopy; introduced herself to Thistle and the party.
- Revealed she was exiled from the Fey for offering an honest opinion about Queen Titania's dress at a ball; her plus-one Helia was punished alongside her under the terms of her exile.
- Refused permission to touch or search the Grandfather Tree, citing the terms of her punishment ("Tree Protection Schedule D").
- Was terrified when she believed Prince Thornacious (actually Orsik in disguise) appeared, demanding the party leave immediately.
- When deceived by Orsik-as-Thornacious into believing she was pardoned, she departed—but expressed she planned to return every 100 years to taunt Helia, revealing lingering bitterness over Helia dumping her at the moment of Queen Titania's judgment.
- Last known status: departed the Grandfather Tree, apparently free of the exile.

**Helia**
- Dryad of the easternmost tree; introduced himself to the party.
- Revealed he was Halisa's plus-one at the Fey ball; attempted to disavow any connection to Halisa during Queen Titania's judgment but was punished regardless.
- Spent his exile bickering with Halisa and lamenting his situation.
- Offered to grant the party permission to search the Grandfather Tree if they could convince Halisa to apologize.
- When deceived by Orsik-as-Thornacious into believing he was pardoned, Helia departed—though he showed apparent upset that Halisa was leaving, contradicting his stated hatred of her.
- Last known status: departed the Grandfather Tree, apparently free of the exile.

**Elderly Dryad (unnamed)**
- Dryad of the northern cardinal point tree; grumpy and long-suffering from Helia and Halisa's bickering.
- Revealed that Uncle Joon (Thistle's relative) had visited this tree and owes the dryad a joke.
- Revealed the mechanism of Queen Titania's punishment: she told Helia and Halisa that if one left, the other would be safe, but the one leaving would die—trapping them through mutual hatred and unwillingness to sacrifice.
- The bickering had caused cancellation of the moon festival.
- Offered permission to search the tree if the party ended the bickering between Helia and Halisa.
- Once both dryads departed, appeared with Sleth at the base of the tree, expressing relief and revealing his own interpretation: there was no actual curse—Helia and Halisa had cursed each other through belief.
- Last known status: present at the Grandfather Tree, now at peace with the bickering ended.

**Sleth**
- Dryad of the western cardinal point tree; a skilled and contented tree-tender.
- Revealed she is not exiled or punished—she was assigned to tend the Grandfather Tree voluntarily, describing it as "the greatest tree in the Universe" and a "positively amazing assignment."
- Critical of Helia and Halisa for neglecting their trees; pointed out a misgrowing branch that would cause imbalance in 30 years.
- Appeared with the elderly dryad once Helia and Halisa departed, confirming their assessment that the "curse" was self-imposed.
- After the party departed, was seen speaking with the son of Chief Boorvald Orcbane about the stolen artifact; attributed its recovery to "Prince Thornacious."
- Last known status: present at the Grandfather Tree, speaking with Tree Ghost Tribe representatives.

**Prince Thornacious**
- Fey sprite; was used as a disguise by Orsik (Orsik-as-Thornacious) to deceive Helia and Halisa into believing they had received a royal pardon.
- Referenced but not physically present; his identity and form were appropriated for the deception.

**Uncle Joon (Thistle's uncle)**
- Referenced but absent; apparently visited the Grandfather Tree at some point and owes the elderly northern dryad a good joke.
- No current whereabouts established.

**Son of Chief Boorvald Orcbane (unnamed)**
- Young leader of a group of Tree Ghost Tribe barbarians encountered just before the party reached the airship.
- Was speaking with Sleth when the party overheard him; furious upon learning the tribe's precious artifact (the electrum torc) had been stolen.
- Promised vengeance against whoever took it.
- Last known status: present near the Grandfather Tree with a group of Tree Ghost Tribe barbarians.

**Centaurs (unnamed group)**
- A rank group of centaurs approached the Grandfather Tree; muttering about a cursed temple somewhere deeper in the forest.
- Traveling to the Grandfather Tree to be cured of a disease; spending a night at the tree functions as a Greater Restoration spell.
- Last known status: arriving at the Grandfather Tree seeking healing.

## Faction Movements

**Queen Titania / The Seelie Court**
- Revealed (via NPC accounts) to have exiled Halisa and Helia to the Grandfather Tree under a deceptive punishment structure—telling them that if one departed, the other would live but the departing one would die. Whether this was a true curse or a deliberate lie is now clarified: the elderly dryad and Sleth concluded there was no actual magical curse; the dryads imprisoned themselves through mutual hatred and belief.
- The deception was unraveled and the exile ended this session, though Queen Titania herself took no direct action.

**Tree Ghost Tribe**
- A group of barbarians led by the son of Chief Boorvald Orcbane arrived at the Grandfather Tree.
- Lost possession of their tribal artifact: a nonmagical electrum torc (formerly the nose-ring of a hill giant chief), which was taken by the party.
- The tribe's leader has sworn vengeance upon learning of the theft; they now believe "Prince Thornacious" was involved based on Sleth's account.

## Revealed Information

- **Queen Titania's punishment mechanism**: Helia and Halisa were told that if one left, the other would survive but the departing one would die. This appears to have been a lie—no actual curse existed. They remained imprisoned by their own belief and mutual antipathy.
- **The Grandfather Tree's properties**: Spending a night at the tree has the effect of a Greater Restoration spell.
- **The artifact**: The electrum torc is nonmagical, bears Giant runes, and was formerly the nose-ring of a mighty hill giant chief. It is a sacred relic of the Tree Ghost Tribe. The party recovered it from a chamber in the tree's roots.
- **Uncle Joon**: Thistle's uncle visited the Grandfather Tree at some unknown prior point and owes the elderly northern dryad a joke.
- **Cursed temple**: Centaurs passing through referenced a cursed temple somewhere deeper in the forest; no further details established.
- **Blagothkus**: Referenced by Unla Kee as someone whose location he knows (told to him by Eigeron), connected to the Cloud Giant's castle. Unla Kee set aside plans to pursue this for now.
- **Adimond Kroskas**: Referenced by Thistle as hidden in the Maelstorm; Thistle's personal priority target reached via the Oracle's Conch.
