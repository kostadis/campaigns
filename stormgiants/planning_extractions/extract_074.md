## NPC Activity

**Glazhael the Undying (Dracolich)**
- Approached the control tower stealthily while the party was engaged with Ogre Zombies; spotted by Thistle.
- Used her dragon fear ability at a safe distance, affecting Thistle and Unla Kee (and Thuul).
- Blagothkus assessed her tactical purpose: using the Ogre Zombies as a delay to give the dracolich time to kill the party.
- Breathed ice on the tower.
- Screamed "THEY ARE ALL DEAD!" before flying away.
- Described by Thistle as moving without genuine commitment ("if she had a heart... it wouldn't be in it"); Vardis characterized her movement as "shadowboxing."
- Last known status: flew away from the tower after the ice breath.

**Bob Saget (Demon, Astral Plane)**
- Not physically present; discussed extensively as a potential Planar Ally target.
- Orsik revealed he had Unla Kee send Bob to the Astral Plane rather than kill him, intentionally preserving Bob as a future asset; one of Bob's drow allies was sent with him, along with four stooges.
- Bob's other drow ally was allowed to escape safely at the time of that encounter.
- Bob is currently described as plotting revenge against the party from the Astral Plane.
- Faintly perceived his name being spoken during party discussion.
- Last known status: Astral Plane, plotting against the party.

**Blagothkus (Cloud Giant)**
- Participated in the tower defense, dropping boulders on Ogre Zombies.
- Expressed disdain for Unla Kee's magical competence ("I have a whole binder full of better magic users").
- Provided tactical analysis: identified the Ogre Zombies' role as a delay mechanism for Glazhael.
- Reacted with controlled anger when Orsik mentioned his son (a ghost who wanted to kill the party and told them Blagothkus wasn't good enough for the All-Father); practiced personal growth rather than retaliating.
- Retreated downstairs to retrieve more boulders when Glazhael activated her fear ability, citing self-preservation over standing in place to die.
- Last known status: descended into the castle to retrieve more boulders.

**Ogre Zombies**
- Functioned as a tactical screen for Glazhael, keeping the party engaged long enough for the dracolich to approach.
- Climbed the 70-foot control tower in response to being targeted.
- Several killed during the encounter by combined party action.
- One covered in mayonnaise by Orsik's Decanter of Endless Liquids; lost footing and fell but survived.
- Immune to poison damage (stinking cloud had no effect).
- Last known status: numbers significantly reduced; at least one surviving as the session ended.

**Harshnag (Giant, deceased)**
- Referenced in dialogue; died when the Oracle fell on his head.
- Mentioned as the party's first giant ally, in contrast to Blagothkus.

**Slerdax (Dark Lord of Ravenloft)**
- Referenced by Unla Kee as an example of forbidden lore regarding Ravenloft.
- A dark lord trapped in the mists of Ravenloft who runs a restaurant that periodically manifests in cities and traps patrons.
- Cursed to doubt sincerity; kills those who compliment him, believing them to be lying.
- Not physically present; discussed as academic lore.

**Vardis**
- Raised tactical objection to the Bob plan: noted that if Bob returns to the Abyss, the dracolich would remain on the Prime Material Plane as his emissary, with its phylactery safely in the Abyss making it effectively invulnerable.
