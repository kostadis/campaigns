## NPC Activity

**Bob Saget**
- Appeared on stage at the Great Theater in Silverymoon, dressed in a purple jacket, tie, and pants.
- Revealed he was summoned to this world by Kaesen's summoning spell.
- Revealed he has been casting magic to turn the people of Silverymoon against the Spellguards.
- Disclosed he is conducting a blood ritual at the Theater, requiring victims to die on stage.
- Sourced victims by recruiting commoners under false pretenses, dressing them as the 3HPP, and forcing them to perform a staged "fight" against him.
- Killed four commoners on stage: a gnome dressed as Unla Kee, a Tabaxi dressed as Orsik, and two others dressed as Vardis and Thistle.
- Mocked the absent (invisible) 3HPP throughout the performance, breaking the fourth wall to taunt them directly.
- Directed drow knights and two cambions to stand guard on stage during the performance.
- Departed backstage after the killings; the play continued its public run the following day.
- Remains the celebrated "toast of the town" in Silverymoon as of Eleasis 5th.

## Revealed Information

- Bob Saget was brought to this world specifically by a summoning spell cast by Kaesen.
- Bob Saget is deliberately targeting social outcasts and marginalized individuals in Silverymoon (specifically referencing non-human races who have run afoul of the Spellguard) as his ritual victims, calculating that their disappearances will go unnoticed or even be welcomed by the city's population.
- The Great Theater has an active alarm spell on its entrance (triggered this session) and at least one glyph of warding on the doors.
- The zombie audience members are former ritual victims; the theater's "audience" is composed entirely of reanimated corpses.
- Regular ticket-buying patrons are being redirected to a demi-plane rather than the actual theater, shielding civilians from the ritual while maintaining Bob's public reputation.
- Blagothkus's castle at Parnast is the same castle that previously passed over the 3HPP during the black road quest; it was used by Tiamat's cult for treasure transport before the First Faction secured it.
- Blagothkus's current re-possession of the castle is unexplained and considered a mystery by the party.
