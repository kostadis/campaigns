## NPC Activity

**Kurglow (Orc Chieftain, Spinripper Clan)**
Appeared leading the assault on Parnast. Fought the party directly, dealing 32 HP to Orsik. After the other chieftain (green sash) was killed, demanded the return of his whelp in exchange for withdrawal. When the whelp was returned, he departed as promised. Berated Naz publicly — accusing her of prioritizing her own child over his — and rejected her plea to take her with him, leaving her weeping at the well. His departure was an act of betrayal in Bad Fruul's eyes.

**Green-Sash Orc Chieftain (unnamed)**
Fought alongside Kurglow. Resisted Unla Kee's mind sliver initially, then succumbed on the second attempt. Killed by Orsik.

**Naz (Orc female caregiver)**
Previously held in Parnast with the whelps. Confronted Kurglow publicly after the battle, insisting she had protected his child. Kurglow rejected and humiliated her, leaving her at the well sobbing. After recovering, she stood up, declared intent to get revenge on Kurglow after dealing with Bad Fruul, and struck the mammoth with a guiding bolt.

**Bad Fruul (Hill Giant)**
Mounted his mammoth and led the final assault on Parnast from the east, accompanied by ettins and a horde of orcs. Smashed through houses of Parnast. Cast hold person targeting Thistle (failed). Referred to as being guided by "Chandra's family nemesis," who is reportedly driving the timing of the assault to ensure her chance for vengeance is not lost. Was struck by the freed Wyvern during the assault.

**Chandra's Family Nemesis (unnamed)**
Referenced but not present. Identified as the intelligence guiding Bad Fruul's tactical decisions. Her motivation is vengeance against Chandra's family. She is pressing Bad Fruul to attack now before the opportunity is lost.

**Blassios (Seelie fairy/sprite leader)**
Arrived with two sprites to assist the party. Was struck by an orc javelin. Took cover and returned fire. Commanded his sprites to poison the mammoth (succeeded in poisoning, failed to put it to sleep). Internal monologue reveals he considers himself there against his will — blaming someone named Oblivilish for sending him as punishment for an unspecified "indiscretion."

**Sprites (two, unnamed)**
Arrived with Blassios. Poisoned Bad Fruul's mammoth but failed to put it to sleep.

**Oblivilish (referenced, not present)**
Named in Blassios's internal monologue as the reason Blassios is at the battle — apparently as punishment for an indiscretion. No other details given.

**Wyvern (unnamed)**
Held chained in the former stables of Parnast, fed on dead orc corpses. Freed by Orsik, who used dead bodies as distraction. Upon being freed, immediately attacked Bad Fruul. Was subsequently struck by an ettin's mace.

**Ettins (multiple, unnamed)**
Present in Bad Fruul's assault force. One struck the Wyvern with a mace and expressed shock that the party freed it ("I thought you said they would not free the Wyvern!").

**Mammoth (Bad Fruul's)**
Charged through Parnast structures. Poisoned by Blassios's sprites. Entangled by Unla Kee's wand of web. Hit by Naz's guiding bolt.

**Raggnar**
Present in Parnast. Asked Orsik if he had a plan before the final assault. No further action noted.

**Gundalin**
Present. Vocally opposed to the wyvern plan, calling it "a desperate ploy of a madman."

**Jarlor (one-time spy)**
Present. Made a quip about Orsik's non-threatening hostage negotiation tactics. No combat action noted.

**Thuul**
Commanded by Thistle during the battle. Killed at least one orc to the south.

Lost their green-sash chieftain and several warriors. Kurglow withdrew with his whelp after the hostage exchange, effectively splitting from Bad Fruul's assault coalition mid-battle. This withdrawal enraged Bad Fruul.

Advanced on Parnast from three directions (south, north, east). Suffered the defection of the Spinripper Clan mid-assault. Mammoth entangled and poisoned. Bad Fruul struck by the freed Wyvern. Committed three operatives (Blassios + two sprites) to the defense of Parnast. Blassios's participation appears coerced or punitive in nature (see Oblivilish reference) rather than voluntary alliance.

## Revealed Information

- **Kurglow is the father of one of the orc whelps** taken from the caves. His non-response to earlier threats was because he found Orsik's threats insulting to his parenting rather than credible dangers.
- **Naz had a child among the whelps** and was accused by Kurglow of prioritizing her own child's safety over his — their relationship is antagonistic and personal.
- **The Wyvern can be controlled/directed** indirectly by positioning fresh meat (or a large target like a giant) in its line of sight upon release.
- **Bad Fruul's forces had intelligence** suggesting the party would not free the Wyvern (per the ettin's exclamation), implying a spy or informant within or near Parnast, or that Chandra's family nemesis had assessed the party's options and deemed this unlikely.
- **Blassios is present under duress**, sent by Oblivilish as apparent punishment for an unnamed indiscretion.
- **Chandra's family nemesis is driving Bad Fruul's tactical timing**, motivated by personal vengeance against Chandra's lineage.
