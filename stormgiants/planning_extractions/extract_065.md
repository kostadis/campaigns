## NPC Activity

### Bob Saget (Demon/Doppelganger)
- Has taken over the identity of the real Bob Saget (a famous actor), whom he killed and replaced.
- Operating out of the Royal Theater in Silverymoon, using it as a base of operations.
- Has created a false portal at the theater entrance that intercepts theatergoers rather than admitting them inside.
- Inside the real theater, is manufacturing zombies, apparently in collaboration with a Red Wizard and Drow allies.
- Has dressed the zombie horde in costumes replicating the 3HPP (Orsik, Thistle, and other party members).
- Does not yet know the party is planning an imminent attack — the element of surprise is intact as of this session.

### Kaelen
- A playwright who originally attempted a summoning ritual, partly thwarted, which inadvertently summoned Bob Saget instead of a more powerful entity.
- Currently sitting inside his house in Silverymoon; the house appears dusty and undisturbed, the door unopened for some time. Behavior described as deeply abnormal — sitting tapping his fingers.
- Party suspects he may be magically connected to or powering Bob in some way; killing him risks alerting the demon.
- Party resolves to leave him in place to preserve surprise.
- Backstory: his original plan involved the mass murder of the entire town of Triboar, which the 3HPP previously thwarted.

### Lord Zymorven
- Resides at Zymorven Hall; uses scrying magic to monitor all approaches to his homestead.
- Met the party in full armor, prepared to defend his home.
- Acknowledged the debt his family owes the 3HPP for saving his son from death and poor choices.
- Joined the strike team as an ally.
- Strongly objected to killing Kaelen, advocating for interrogation instead.
- Reacted with alarm upon learning of the vampire and dracolich associated with Blagothkus' castle.
- Demanded the castle be repositioned away from Silverymoon to prevent undead infestation risk.
- Offered to use his connections in Silverymoon to ensure the party receives public credit for their heroic actions — presented as a favor, received by Orsik with barely concealed irritation.
- Disclosed he has personal experience covering for a son's failures, drawing a pointed parallel to Orsic's situation.
- **Last known location:** Silverymoon area, participating in the pre-assault staging.

### Urgala Meltimer
- Encountered at an unspecified location and agreed to join the strike team.
- Referenced the party's prior work in "saving Silverymoon" as a precedent.
- Broke a prolonged argument over Kaelen's fate by reminding everyone of Kaelen's history — his original Triboar mass-murder plan — and stating he should have been killed on the spot when first encountered.
- **Last known location:** Silverymoon, participating in pre-assault staging.

### Darthara (Darathra) Shendrel, Lord Protector
- Initially reluctant and unhappy to see the party, due to fallout from Orsik's prior betrayal of the Harpers.
- Recalled Krowen Valharrow's condemnation: agents were killed, powerful magic artifacts lost, expensive and time-consuming to replace.
- Examined "Orsic" (believing him to be Orsik's father) and ultimately accepted the framing, though suspicious.
- Criticized Orsik's past conduct at length, including its damage to Vardis's reputation as a Corellon cleric.
- Made a pointed comment about Orsik's intelligence seeming "off" — something about it struck her as wrong.
- Agreed to join the mission, explicitly stating all debts between her and the 3HPP are cleared after this.
- On entering the theater: witnessed the zombie horde dressed in 3HPP costumes, the false portal, and the gore on the stage.
- **Last known location:** Inside the Royal Theater, Silverymoon, at the moment of infiltration.

### Krowen Valharrow
- Not present; referenced in Darthara's recollections.
- Condemned the 3HPP after Orsik's betrayal: held them responsible for Harper agent deaths and the loss of powerful magical artifacts.
- His stance toward the party remains hostile.
- **Last known location/status:** Unknown; referenced as a Harper authority figure.

### The Red Wizard (unnamed)
- Referenced as an active collaborator with Bob Saget inside the theater.
- Working in league with Drow allies to assist in zombie creation.
- **Last known location:** Presumed inside the Royal Theater, Silverymoon.

### Laela
- Referenced by Thistle only; not present.
- Believed to be near Ironslag, possibly a prisoner of giants.
- Thistle chose to prioritize Silverymoon over attempting a rescue.
- **Last known status:** Missing, presumed captive near Ironslag.

### Lord Aerasume

## Faction Movements

### Harpers
- Suffered significant losses from Orsik's prior betrayal: agents killed, powerful magical artifacts lost.
- Krowen Valharrow remains hostile to the 3HPP.
- Darthara Shendrel participated in the mission independently, without notifying Harper leadership — she explicitly acknowledged this as a compromise and an unwise choice she was making knowingly.
- No Harper institutional involvement in the assault on Bob Saget.

### The Cult of the Dragon (implicit)
- Unla Kee's internal strategizing identified the Cult of the Dragon as a useful scapegoat: if the dracolich were unleashed on Silverymoon, its attack could be blamed on the Cult, with Laeral Silverhand as First Lord of Waterdeep — who led the coalition that defeated Severin and Sandesyl — used as a pretext. Plan was rejected by Orsik.

### Bob Saget's Operation (unnamed faction)
- Has established a functioning zombie-production operation inside the Royal Theater in Silverymoon.
- Uses a false portal at the theater entrance to intercept civilian theatergoers, preventing them from entering and witnessing the interior.
- Has produced a zombie horde dressed in 3HPP-specific costumes (apparent theatrical mockery or trap preparation).
- Collaborating with at least one Red Wizard and Drow agents.
- Operation is ongoing and undetected by city authorities as of this session.

## Revealed Information

- **Bob's operation:** The Royal Theater in Silverymoon houses Bob Saget's full zombie-production operation. The theater's exterior portal is an illusion/magical trap intercepting patrons. The interior contains a blood-soaked stage, a zombie audience dressed as the 3HPP, and zombie staff (including a half-orc with a broken neck, a Tabaxi with a partially severed neck).
- **Bob's collaborators:** A Red Wizard (unnamed) and Drow agents are actively assisting Bob inside the theater.
- **Kaelen's connection:** The party suspects Kaelen may be magically linked to Bob Saget or powering him in some capacity. The nature of this link is unconfirmed.
- **Kaelen's history:** Kaelen's original ritual was the intended mass murder of Triboar's entire population; this ritual was only partly thwarted, producing Bob Saget instead of the intended entity.
- **Orsik's identity cover:** Darthara Shendrel believes "Orsic" is Orsik's father attempting to redeem the family name. She noted something "off" about the original Orsik's intelligence, but this has not led to a revelation in this session.
- **Darthara's past compromise:** She lent the 3HPP her credibility and provided Harper magical artifacts, which were subsequently lost. This decision was made against Harper leadership's later judgment.
