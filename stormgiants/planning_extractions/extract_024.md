Later revealed by Darz Helgar to have blackmailed Darz into stealing from the Lionshield Coaster by threatening to expose Darz's criminal past to the Lord Protector.

**Jacoby Hysstryn** (brother): Arrived at the Talking Troll seeking help finding his missing brother Mirak, who disappeared ten years ago. Imitated the Lord Protector to the crowd's amusement. Insisted Mirak never ran off and disputed his sister-in-law Tolmarra's story.

**Persson Hysstryn** (brother): Accompanied Jacoby. Claimed Mirak was having an affair with Urgala Meltimer. Led the group to Loomy as a clue source. Denied involvement in Mirak's disappearance. Implicated Tolmarra (Mirak's wife) without directly accusing her. Provided the party with a handwritten map and notes to a mine in the Dessarin Hills that descends into the Underdark—the site where her wife was lost. Described the Dessarin Hills as rich in abandoned mines repurposed by monsters and wanderers.

**Tolmarra** (innkeeper, Six Windows Inn): Mirak's wife. Caught Thistle attempting to enter her inn and assaulted him with a rolling pin and potatoes. Was barred from the attic while the party investigated. Residents expressed relief at her detention, one calling her a "peeping tom." Detained by Lord Protector Delratha at the end of the session. Mirak's skeletal remains were found in her attic, animated as a skeleton.

**Delratha Shendrel** (Lord Protector of Triboar, from Waterdeep): Described by locals as fair and clearheaded. Had previously dismissed the Hysstryn brothers' claims as unfounded. Convened a session with all parties after the attic discovery. Agreed to have a cleric cast *Speak with Dead* on the remains the following day. Detained Tolmarra at the session's end.

**Alaestra** (co-owner, Lionshield Coaster): Revealed the theft of both gold and a dimensional supply key to Orsik, offering a discount for recovery. Described the key as the mechanism by which goods are resupplied from an unnamed interdimensional trading firm.

**Harriet** (smith, Uldinath's Arms): Identified the grappling hook as the work of Gherlyn at Forehammer's Forge. Spoke fondly of Gherlyn.

**Gherlyn** (dwarf smith, Forehammer's Forge): Confirmed he crafted the grappling hook and sold it to Darz Helgar, the caretaker of the northern campgrounds. Responded positively to Orsik's account of killing the hill giant.

**Darz Helgar** (campground caretaker): Confessed to stealing the Lionshield Coaster's gold and key under blackmail from Kaelen Sarssir, who threatened to expose Darz's criminal history (ten years served in Waterdeep's dungeon). - Urgala Meltimer's wife was lost in the Underdark via a mine in the Dessarin Hills over ten years ago; Urgala possesses a map to this location.
- The Lionshield Coaster's supply network operates via a dimensional key; this key has been stolen. The Zhentarim have long sought to identify this network.
- The cloud castle orb can be replaced by Zephyros if destroyed, but the process takes several months.
- Kaelen Sarssir has been blackmailing Darz Helgar and orchestrated the Lionshield Coaster theft.
- Darz Helgar has a prior criminal record (ten years in Waterdeep's dungeon).
