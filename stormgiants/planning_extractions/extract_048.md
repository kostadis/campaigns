## NPC Activity

**High Lord Methrammar Aerasumé**
- Summoned the 3HPP to his audience chamber under the pretense of receiving their diplomatic credentials from Everlund.
- Expressed enthusiastic personal affection for the traveling play "Mordenkainen's Best Home Illusions," having seen it twice already.
- Agreed to offer the Royal Theater for free and contributed 1,000 gold pieces toward the play's production costs.
- Declined to extradite Xamlyn Margaster, citing her status as a guest and ally of the High Lord and member of a respectable Waterdhavian noble house.
- Confirmed that Margaster is free to speak with anyone or refuse to speak with anyone as a member of the community.
- Referenced High Captain Horix Zoar as having corrected a prior mistake involving the 3HPP being expelled from the city.

**Taern "Thunderspell" Hornblade (High Mage)**
- Present in the audience chamber.
- Examined the 3HPP's letters of introduction and declared them satisfactory.
- Was noted by the High Lord as not having informed him about the traveling play in advance.

**The Chamberlain**
- Present in the audience chamber.
- Directed by the High Lord to work with the 3HPP to arrange an extended run of the play at the Royal Theater.
- Observed to have a tight, very fake smile regarding the theater arrangement.

**The High Treasurer**
- Present in the audience chamber.
- Objected to the 50,000 gold piece request on grounds of budget, fiscal policy, deficit spending, and inflation, effectively capping the High Lord's offer at 1,000 gold.

**Xamlyn Margaster**
- Not present but discussed at length.
- Confirmed to be a current guest in Silverymoon and under the High Lord's protection.
- Identified as a member of the noble House Margaster, a respectable Waterdhavian noble house and ally of the High Lord.
- Her cambion attendants were recognized by Senneth as "the men who follow Lady Margaster."
- The 3HPP are plotting to disgrace rather than kill her by exposing her devil companions.

**Senneth (Cleric of Mielikki)**
- Previously raised Thistle from the dead; was visited by the 3HPP seeking intelligence on infernal activity in the city.
- Acknowledged that in a city of 40,000, the presence of at least one devil is plausible but dismissed active cult operations, citing a post-Cult of the Dragon crackdown that drove remaining cults out of the city.
- Recognized Thistle's description of the cambions as matching the men who follow Lady Margaster.
- Began drawing unwanted connections between the 3HPP's history with the Margasters and their current questions; was successfully distracted before she could pursue this line of inquiry.
- Noted an "interesting" connection between the party's play (featuring a devil and a demon) and the topic of the conversation.

**Anakis (Tiefling student, Lady's College)**
- Greeted the 3HPP as a volunteer guide at the Lady's College.
- Identified herself as a Cambion enthusiast and directed the party to Sage Vusk's lecture.
- Noted that no one in Silverymoon would ever expect to encounter an infernal creature, and preemptively defended her tiefling heritage as accepted at the Lady's College.

**Sage Vusk (Traveling Scholar, Lady's College)**
- Delivered a lecture on Cambion anthropology to the 3HPP (and an otherwise empty room).
- Explained that Cambions are the progeny of any union between a demon and a non-demon, correcting older assumptions that they were exclusively the offspring of male demons and human women.
- Noted scholarly debate over whether Cambions can possess free will; personally holds that their infernal nature precludes it.
- Described Cambions as foot soldiers of the Blood War, disposable by their masters.
- Identified the Fiendish Charm as a key tactical ability; noted it may require line of sight, suggesting magical darkness, blindness, or invisibility as potential counters.
- Confirmed that Dispel Magic can reveal a Cambion's true form but noted social and legal complications in Silverymoon, where magical disguises are common and dispelling them is considered a breach of etiquette that can provoke duels.
- Cited the destruction of the adventuring group "The Noble Daggers" as an example of a single Cambion's charm ability in action.
- Laughed at the suggestion of banning magic in Silverymoon, referencing the city's identity as founded by chosen of Mystra and inventors of magical schools.

**Krowen Valharrow (Harper, referenced but not present)**
- Sent an unnamed Harper agent to pressure the 3HPP into resuming their mission against the Giants.
- Offered treasure maps as an incentive.
- Described as still seeing value in the 3HPP despite frustration with their current activity.

**Unnamed Harper Agent (elf in purple robes)**
- Delivered Krowen Valharrow's ultimatum to the 3HPP: resume the Giant mission or lose access to Harper magical pathways.
- Warned that a Frost Giant nearly sacked Bryn Shander and Hill Giants attacked Goldenfields while the 3HPP remained in Silverymoon.
- Noted that Giants continue assembling magical devices to prove their worthiness to Amn.
- Criticized the 3HPP for allying with cultists and described the contractor model as a mistake attributed to someone named Ezra.
- Departed with the treasure maps after the 3HPP terminated the relationship.
- Last seen: leaving the 3HPP's location in Silverymoon on foot.

**Uzorel "The Viper" Hornblade (Zhentarim agent)**
- Distantly related to Taern Hornblade, former ruler of Silverymoon.
- Initially reluctant to meet with Orsik; agreed upon learning of Orsik's fame from Parnast and Triboar.
- Described physically as diminutive with lanky limbs, pale skin, and a sharp angular goatee; noted for tact and charm over physical presence.
- Received intelligence from Orsik on the locations of two teleportation circles: one in Everlund, one in Yartar.
- Expressed surprise at receiving the information without payment; accepted Orsik's framing of it as anti-Harper revenge.
- Confirmed the Black Network's higher-ups would be pleased and would know of Orsik.
- Last seen: Silverymoon.

**Rebecca Deakin (Archivist, Wyrmworks cataloging department)**
