## NPC Activity

**Agra (Kobold, red-scaled, burn-scarred)**
- Appeared in the stone building at the center of the dwarven outpost room, hiding with Gen during the party's entry.
- Restrained Gen from acting when the barricade kobolds engaged the party.
- Strategically waited for trap to activate; celebrated briefly when stones fell on the party, then despaired when the ambush failed and kobolds were killed (by sleep spell and falling dead).
- Surrendered to the party using a white object as a flag.
- Revealed he formerly served the Cult of the Dragon under orders; reacted with visible guilt and horror when he recognized Thistle bore marks of Cult torture.
- Claimed ignorance of the whereabouts of Thistle's girlfriend.
- Disclosed that surviving Cult-affiliated kobold tribes have been hired by Giants following the dragons' defeat; his group was hired by a Fire Giant.
- Revealed that other kobold tribes were hired by Hill Giants and Cloud Giants, and that there is unusual competition or conflict brewing between giant factions competing for something unknown.
- Stated that his former Red Dragon boss made a deal with a Cloud Giant, and when the cloud giant's castle arrived, Agra's group fled.
- Explained the Fire Giants sent his group to this outpost to recover Cultist treasure, claiming the space was too small for giants themselves; noted that a kobold named Gun had heard the place was cursed, which caused them to hide in the stone building rather than enter the southern room.
- Described the southern room as containing creatures that emerge and explode upon death, a glowing magical fire symbol, a dead kobold leader named Nug who reported a treasure in the corner, and a river of lava.
- Offered the party his and Gen's services as "employees" (not slaves), citing fluid loyalty.
- When refused employment due to lack of funds, offered information voluntarily in exchange for release.
- Clarified that the Fire Giants expect them to return with treasure or not at all, with fatal consequences for failure — implying they have no incentive to report the party to the giants.
- Consumed a handful of dirt (apparently a stress behavior) before the party let them go.

**Gen (Kobold, red-scaled, burn-scarred)**
