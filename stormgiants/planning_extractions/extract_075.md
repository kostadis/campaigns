## Revealed Information

- **Sandesyl's lair location**: Carved into the ice caves beneath her tower, not in the tower itself.
- **Sandesyl's survival strategy**: She relies on vampire spawn to retrieve proof of enemy deaths before leaving her lair. She does not trust her minions (including Glazhael) to report accurately.
- **Glazhael's tactical role**: The Zombie Ogres were not a true reserve force — they function as a trap to pin Sandesyl's enemies in place so Glazhael can kill them from above.
- **Brunvild's captivity**: He has been held alive as Sandesyl's blood source since the castle departed the Miklos Glacier.
- **Sandesyl's experiment**: She is testing whether a ghost can indefinitely command orc zombies as a captive labor force, with no overhead or free will. The experiment has been running for at least two years.
- **The flying Kenku**: Brunvild claims personal knowledge of a Kenku capable of flight who played a role in defeating the Cult of the Dragon. This contradicts the common belief that Kenkus cannot fly and that this figure was a prisoner legend only.
- **Bob's status**: Currently trapped in the Astral Plane, limiting his access to his full power. Unla Kee confirmed this. (Bob's nature as a near-invulnerable planar entity previously discussed by the party.)
- **Sammaster and Inferno**: The statues suggest a narrative in which Sammaster learned the secrets of dracolich creation directly from Inferno, positioning Inferno as the origin point of dracolich undeath.
- **Tome of the Dragon**: Depicted in Sammaster's hands in the statue; described as the definitive text on dracoliches.
