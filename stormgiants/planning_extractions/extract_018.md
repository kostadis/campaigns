## NPC Activity

**Kagrota Threeteeth (Mother of Bones)** – An ogre and apparent cleric or shaman of Bane. Led a raiding force of orcs and goblins at the Brandimen cabin. Commanded her troops in combat, hurling javelins (one of which she fumbled) and casting a spiritual weapon (hammer) that struck Unla Kee. Screamed orders at her forces throughout the battle. Reduced to 1 HP by Thistle before being killed by Wallace. Notably shouted "Fairies! We had a deal with Prince Thornacious!" as she was nearly slain. Maintained a hidden ritual chamber beneath the cabin containing a Bane altar, braided human-hair ropes strung with bells, an elven woman's skull, black candles, and a shattered jade statuette. Had treasure stockpiled in chests and bags in the chamber.

**Tove Brandimen** – Not present at the cabin. Revealed by prisoners to have been "thick as thieves with Kagrota," suggesting his hostage story was fabricated. His note, purportedly from Kagrota, was already suspected as a scam by Vardis and Orsik. A severed finger he had presented as evidence of his mother's captivity was identified by a prisoner as belonging to a commune member, not his mother.

**Bad Fruul** – Not present. Referenced by prisoners as having sent Kagrota to destroy their commune, resulting in multiple deaths. Prisoners had no knowledge of him by name ("Bad who?"), suggesting limited regional awareness of his identity.

**Prince Thornacious** – Not present. Referenced by Kagrota in her dying moments as a party to some prior deal involving fairies ("We had a deal with Prince Thornacious!"). No further details provided.

**Chandra Stol** – Not present at the cabin. Briefly referenced at the session's close as the person who will be displeased that the wyvern must be fed cooked meat instead of live prey.
