## NPC Activity

**Ghelryn Foehammer** (dwarf smithy) — Appeared at the Troll Tavern. Presented the party with a sealed letter of mark in recognition of their killing of a fire giant. Directed them to bring the letter to King Morinn or Queen Tithmel at Citadel Felbar in exchange for a reward.

**Kaelen** (playwright) — Referenced but not present at the tavern scene. Agreed under duress (threatened with bruising by Orsik) to have a play ready in 45 days, targeting the 5th day of the 3rd tenday of Mirtul. Remained non-committal until threatened.

**Imber Chen** — Lavender-haired gnome merchant with violet eyes and a Calishmanian accent. Operating a fabric stall in Yartar's Fishyard. Provided intelligence on Silverymoon and its political alignment with the Lord's Alliance. Introduced the party to Cap'n Squint by name. Also mentioned Lord Khaspere Drylund and the Hand of Yartar connection. Negotiated a banner advertising deal with the party for half a gold piece over 43 days. Expressed progressive social views but displayed blind spots toward non-humanoid species. Last seen at his Fishyard stall in Yartar.

**Talni Barcoba** — Red-haired, scarred self-proclaimed Wise Woman. Operating a home remedy potion stall in Yartar's Fishyard. Not directly interacted with by the party; observed only.

**Baash'a Kweelani** — Bald merchant with bright flaming red skin. Sells magical goods for magic users. Operating in Yartar's Fishyard. Noted by Unla Kee but not directly interacted with.

**Cap'n Squint** — Halfling captain, described as small, wearing a tricorne hat, commanding men twice his size. Former friend of Imber Chen; the two have drifted apart. Currently owns his own barge. Was gambling aboard the Grand Dame the night before the party's departure. Departed north on the River Surbrin with the party the following morning.

**Lord Khaspere Drylund** — Owner of the Grand Dame, described as the most famous and luxurious boat in Yartar. Politically ambitious; reportedly working to position himself as the next Waterbarron upon the current one's death. Was hosting gambling aboard the Grand Dame the night the party arrived in Yartar. Not directly encountered.

**Nestra Ruthiol** — Current Waterbarron of Yartar. Holds a life-term dictatorial position. Has aligned herself with the Lord's Alliance to maintain power. Not encountered; mentioned in city rumors.

**Hand of Yartar operative (unnamed thug)** — One of three thugs accompanying a Hand of Yartar magic user who surrounded Thistle and Unla Kee. Negotiated a side deal with Unla Kee for discounted VIP ticket resale rights in exchange for letting Unla Kee go.

**Hand of Yartar magic user (unnamed)** — Led the Hand of Yartar ambush of Thistle and Unla Kee in Yartar. Stated the contract was specifically on Thistle, not Unla Kee. Unable to locate Thistle after he fled and disappeared. Last seen in Yartar.

**Uthgardtian raiders (deceased, elk totem)** — Black-haired, blue-eyed Uthgardtian tribespeople bearing an elk totem. Attempted to ambush the party on the road to Yartar. Hostile specifically toward magic users; Unla Kee's intimidation attempt enraged rather than deterred them. Killed in combat. Examined by Orsik; found to have no valuables. Corpses left on the road between the Troll Tavern and Yartar.

**Yartar Shield Gate guard (unnamed)** — Recognized Orsik from a prior incident but accepted Orsik's denial. Accepted a playbill. Expressed opinions on the Lionshield Coster's use of pocket dimensions undermining local trade. Provided directions to the Three Rivers Festhall and the Fishyard.

## Faction Movements

**Hand of Yartar** — Women-only thieves guild operating in Yartar. Deployed a magic user and at least three thugs to intercept Thistle and Unla Kee after Unla Kee attempted to run ticket scams without paying the guild a cut. Issued a contract specifically targeting Thistle. Failed to locate Thistle after he escaped. Reached a side agreement with Unla Kee for a cut of discounted VIP ticket resales. Actively growing in membership and prominence amid Yartar's rising crime environment.

**Lord's Alliance** — Referenced as the external power propping up Waterbarron Nestra Ruthiol's authority in Yartar. Also framed by Imber Chen as the faction bringing order against Harper "anarchism" and Zhentarim exploitation. Silverymoon identified as a key member. No direct agents encountered.

**Lionshield Coster** — Mentioned as operating in Yartar as well as Triboar. The Yartar gate guard expressed resentment over their use of pocket dimensions to bypass traditional trade routes, cutting into local commerce and guard income.

**Shields of Yartar** — 150-strong coalition of mounted guards. Maintain peace in Yartar and protect against wilderness threats. Based at Shield Tower to the west of Yartar. Not directly encountered; mentioned in city background.

## Revealed Information

- **Silverymoon** explicitly excludes non-"goodly races" (Half-orcs and similar peoples are not welcome), confirmed by both Imber Chen's statements and Thistle's recollection of his uncle's accounts. The city is a Lord's Alliance member and politically aligned with the Waterbarron of Yartar.
- **Lord Khaspere Drylund** is politically maneuvering to become the next Waterbarron, actively courting voters and hosting high-society gambling events aboard the Grand Dame.
- **Waterbarron Nestra Ruthiol** relies on Lord's Alliance connections to enact meaningful governance due to constant internal sabotage and factional infighting undermining Yartar's unified political voice.
- **The Hand of Yartar** actively monitors and taxes independent criminal enterprise within the city; operating scams without their knowledge triggers enforcement action.
- **Cap'n Squint** was known to Imber Chen and is a viable river transport contact; he was gambling on the Grand Dame the night before departure.
- **Imber Chen** has a connection to the Hand of Yartar, offering an introduction to a member who could secure market stall space — suggesting he has existing relationships with the guild.
