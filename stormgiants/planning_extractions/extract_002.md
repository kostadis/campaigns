## NPC Activity

**Grrrk**
- Appeared as part of a Galeb Duhr formation (a "pyramid") in the Greypeak mountains.
- Revealed to the party that he had witnessed giants and kobolds in great numbers in the area.
- Thanked the party for dealing with beetles.
- Ordered younger Galeb Duhr back to their positions after the party departed.

**Prrrt**
- Younger Galeb Duhr, companion to Grrrk in the same formation.
- Complained about rock mites during the session; the party apparently helped treat them.
- Relayed information the party needed: conveyed what "the dead giant had said" to the party.
- Expressed optimism that the party would survive the pyramid's dangers, which proved correct.
- Thanked the party for dealing with beetles and clarified the treasure "wasn't ours."

**Eden "Gleeful" Gleeson**
- Traveling west along the road toward Llorkh to attend his own wedding.
- Realized en route that he had forgotten to purchase a wedding gift for his bride.
- Approached the party to buy something; ultimately purchased a giant silver nose ring from Vardis (price negotiated down from 200 GP, final price unspecified but less than 150 GP).
- Reacted with moral indignation when Vardis suggested selling servants, clarifying his staff are employees, not slaves.

**Cisco**
- Manservant and traveling companion to Eden Gleeson.
- Wears an eyepatch and carries a small teddy bear.
- Intervened in the purchase negotiation, warning Eden ("Eddie") that the impulsive buy had consumed the entire "desert budget" for the wedding.

**Old-One-Eye (bandit leader)**
- Led a group of four bandits who ambushed the party in the Greypeak foothills.
- Accused the party of seeking Cult of the Dragon treasure hidden in the hills, suggesting awareness of local cult activity.
- Backed down after Orsik's intimidation and allowed the party to pass, invoking his reputation for having a "soft spot" for down-on-their-luck mercenaries.

**Captain Jolanth**
- Captain of the Guards of the Iron Circle in Llorkh.
- Stationed at the town entrance; challenged the party on arrival.
- Referenced the town having survived "that nasty business with the Dragons," indicating prior Cult of the Dragon activity in Llorkh.
- Was verbally outmaneuvered by Orsik and Vardis into implicitly offering the party protection, then stood flustered as they walked past him.

**The Ghost Bartender (unnamed)**
- Operates the sole tavern in Llorkh; appears as a ghost but insists he is not one.
- Serves food and drink normally (ale, roasted turkey, puff pastry, pine nut salad) and handles money.
- When Orsik directly told him he was a ghost, transformed into a monstrous creature and used a fear/aging effect on Orsik, aging him 30 years.
- The town's Guards of the Iron Circle are aware of this danger and fled the tavern rather than intervene.

**Diego Malfoy**
- Guard of the Iron Circle in Llorkh.
- Confronted Orsik outside the tavern after the ghost incident; warned that one never tells the bartender he is a ghost.
- Recognized Orsik and Vardis by reputation, specifically recalling that they had previously sought employment with the guards but failed to show up sober on their first day.
- Directed the party to the Lionel Coster outpost as a source for a scroll of dispel magic.

**The Cloaked Man (unnamed)**
- Appeared seemingly out of nowhere near the tavern following the ghost incident.
- Described as wearing boots, breeches, a sturdy belt with a steel buckle, a shirt, and a brown hooded cloak.
- Approached the party with an offer of paid employment, citing urgency ("the clock is ticking") and a limited pool of available candidates.
- Identity, faction affiliation, and nature of the job are unknown.
