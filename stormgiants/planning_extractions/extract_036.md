## NPC Activity

**Cindrell**
- Hosted a large party at Westpharl Ranch, attended by the 3HPP and various wealthy Triboar residents.
- Granted the party a private audience in her study and revealed her political rivalry with Darathra Shendrel.
- Solicited the 3HPP to assassinate or otherwise "deal with" Hyuth Kolstaag, framing it as removing a nuisance affecting property values and her electoral prospects.
- Attempted to negotiate payment, initially resisting Thistle's demand for a written IOU; eventually signed a contract outlining the nature of the deal.
- Offered to clear Orsik's name in Waterdeep as alternative payment; declined to extend the offer to all of the North.
- Revealed she lost a previous election for Lord Protector to Darathra Shendrel, whom she identifies as a Harper Agent, and intends to win the next election.
- Last known location: Westpharl Ranch, Triboar.

**Bob Saget (demon)**
- Present at Cindrell's party, performing as an entertainer (cracking jokes).
- Last known location: Westpharl Ranch, Triboar.

**Hyuth Kolstaag**
- Referenced throughout as the target of Cindrell's assassination contract.
- Described as a retired adventurer, arrogant, wealthy, and a social recluse who has refused all of Cindrell's invitations.
- Survived a prior assassination attempt at his mansion; the attempt involved thunder and lightning and destroyed the roof of his home.
- In the aftermath of the assassination attempt, summoned gargoyles to protect himself but refused to compensate the owners of the Cart and Coin for a stolen horse or acknowledge the murder of a stable hand.
- Met the 3HPP in person when they arrived at his mansion under the pretense of roof repair; expressed paranoia and hostility ("Everyone is out to kill me").
- Led the party to his second-floor study, which remains heavily damaged from the assassination attempt.
- His study contains burned papers, a smashed desk, and weather damage.
- Last known location: His mansion, Triboar (second-floor study).

**Urgala**
- Met with Thistle at Northshield House.
- Provided independent background on Hyuth Kolstaag from her perspective as a fellow retired adventurer.
- Confirmed the story of the assassination attempt and the stable hand's murder; expressed moral disapproval of Hyuth's refusal to make amends.
- Expressed mild sympathy for Hyuth's situation while condemning his behavior as a neighbor.
- Showed surprise that Cindrell would be "that cold-blooded" upon seeing the contract.
- Did not explicitly endorse killing Hyuth, but implied that reforming his behavior would be a net good.
- Last known location: Northshield House, Triboar.

**Darathra Shendrel (Lord Protector of Triboar)**
- Met with the 3HPP at the central tower.
- Congratulated the party on the success of their play and the business they brought to Triboar.
- Upon being presented with Cindrell's signed assassination contract, immediately recognized its political value and stated her intent to "bury" Cindrell with it.
- Directed the 3HPP to deliver a warning to Hyuth on her behalf: that she intervened to stop this assassination, that she may not be able to intervene again, and that he should consider becoming a productive citizen under the protection of "the 12."
- Requested the contract be returned to her for safekeeping after the party's meeting with Hyuth.
- Identified by Cindrell as a Harper Agent.
- Last known location: Central tower, Triboar.

## Faction Movements

**Harpers**
- Darathra Shendrel, identified as a Harper Agent, has taken possession (pending return) of a signed contract implicating Cindrell in a solicitation to murder. This significantly strengthens her political position ahead of the next Lord Protector election.
- Harper influence in Triboar appears consolidated around Darathra's continued leadership.

**Lord's Alliance**
- Cindrell explicitly stated her goal is to align Triboar with the Lord's Alliance, which she believes cannot happen while Darathra holds office.
- No Alliance operative is directly present; Cindrell appears to be acting as an advocate rather than a formal agent.

**Seelie Court**
- Thistle is revealed (to the reader/GM, not to his companions) to be a minor member of the Seelie Court. His familiarity with political intrigue is rooted in court culture.
- His recognition by Prince Thornacious and friendship with Blasius are retroactively connected to his court membership.
- No formal court action taken this session; Thistle acted independently.

## Revealed Information

- **Cindrell's political motive**: She lost the previous Lord Protector election to Darathra and wants to use Hyuth's ongoing trouble (and Darathra's perceived inaction) as a campaign issue; she solicited assassination to manufacture a contrast.
- **Darathra is a Harper Agent**: Stated by Cindrell; not independently confirmed this session but presented as her belief.
- **Cindrell signed a written contract** commissioning the 3HPP to deal with Hyuth Kolstaag; this document is now in the party's hands and has been shown to both Urgala and Darathra.
- **Hyuth's prior assassination attempt**: Two assassins entered Triboar, triggered a violent confrontation (thunder and lightning), were foiled, stole a horse from the Cart and Coin, and murdered the stable hand. Hyuth summoned gargoyles for protection afterward and declined to compensate or apologize to affected parties.
- **Thistle's Seelie Court membership**: He is a minor member of the Seelie Court; this explains Prince Thornacious's recognition of him and his friendship with Blasius. His companions do not know this.
- **"The 12"**: Darathra referenced protection "by the 12" as an incentive for Hyuth to cooperate, suggesting an organizational or civic framework of protection in Triboar.
