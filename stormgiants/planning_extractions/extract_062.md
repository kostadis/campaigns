## Threat Arc Events

**Yakfolk Possession Ritual — Escalation**
- Confirmed by Princess Halani that the Yakfolk successfully used their possession ritual on dwarves from Citadel Adbar, resulting in the death of the dwarven king. This represents a high-value political assassination by a Yakfolk-controlled asset, indicating the ritual has been deployed at a strategic level beyond simple raiding.

**Silverymoon Betrayal / New World Order — Political Fracture Arc**
- Princess Halani explicitly stated that Silverymoon's dominance is over and referenced a "new organization" arising to protect the region before catching herself. This suggests an organized faction is coalescing in opposition to Silverymoon's political influence, potentially with Moon Elf noble involvement.

## Revealed Information

- **Yakfolk Possession Ritual:** Used on dwarven soldiers from Citadel Adbar; resulted in assassination of the dwarven king. Also used on elven guards from the Moonwood.
- **Ironslag Entry:** The primary external entrance is sealed — 50-foot adamantine airtight doors, magically sealed, at the base of a 500-foot cliff. The elevator shaft and the discovered staircase represent alternate interior routes.
- **Elevator Mechanics:** The platform makes 15 stops up and down a 500-foot shaft, each stop 30 seconds, completing a full cycle in 15 minutes.
- **Fire Salamanders:** Confirmed as skilled smiths (Uncle Joon's prior intelligence, recalled by Thistle); vulnerable to cold; immune to poison (Unla Kee recalled this when placing Cloudkill).
- **Moonwood Political Alignment:** The Moonwood elves are estranged from Silverymoon and may be organizing or aligning with a new regional power structure.
- **Silverymoon Suspicion:** Princess Halani speculated — based on frustration rather than confirmed intelligence — that Silverymoon agents may have directed the Yakfolk's operations.
