## Threat Arc Events

**Bad Fruul Arc**
- Written orders recovered from Chief Grik explicitly state: "Defend the pass with your lives! We will seize Parnast!" — confirms Bad Fruul has an active military objective targeting Parnast, not merely territorial extortion
- Bad Fruul deployed layered ambush forces (bugbear road gang + goblin pass garrison + wolves) with a pre-planned poison delivery mechanism — indicates organized, coordinated military planning rather than opportunistic banditry
- Bad Fruul's command structure involves at least three tiers of intermediary chiefs, suggesting a substantial force hierarchy

**Zhentarim Infiltration Arc**
- A Zhentarim agent successfully embedded (Oblivillish) in Prince Thornacious' fey court — the Zhentarim have active intelligence operations in fey noble circles
- Orsik confirmed as a Zhentarim affiliate; the SEER's correspondent has flagged this to the SEER

**Planar Distortion / Feywild Arc**
- Discovery of a deactivated fey portal near Ambush Gap: previously used by Adimond Kroskas to move troops into the Feywild; now inert
- Thistle identifies this as evidence that fey portal infrastructure in the North has been systematically disrupted or decayed

## Revealed Information

- **Bad Fruul's objective**: Written orders on Chief Grik's body confirm Bad Fruul intends to seize Parnast, not merely hold the pass for toll collection
- **Bad Fruul's command chain**: Bad Fruul does not meet directly with gang-level chiefs; operates through at least two tiers of intermediary chiefs
- **SEER's assessment of the party**: The Lord's Alliance intelligence apparatus views Orsik as a confirmed Zhentarim agent and the rest of the party as unaffiliated free agents; the SEER considers the group the most promising adventurers in the area
- **Lord's Alliance priorities in Parnast**: The SEER's operative is explicitly tasked with keeping Parnast within the Lord's Alliance sphere and preventing it from falling to either the Zhentarim or the Giants
- **Zhentarim spy in fey court**: Oblivillish is a Zhentarim agent placed inside Prince Thornacious' court; contact has been lost and the Zhentarim do not know if he was exposed or captured
- **Fey portal network**: Portals between the Feywild and the North were once plentiful; at least one near Ambush Gap is now inert; Adimond Kroskas exploited this network to move forces into the Feywild
- **Hsing's capabilities**: Hsing can teleport, can read magical auras, and operates under the SEER's direct authority with gold disbursement powers; the SEER's network is described as capable of locating individuals even in remote locations such as the Marsh of Chelimber
