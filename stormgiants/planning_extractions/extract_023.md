## NPC Activity

**Bad Fruul (Giant – deceased)**
- Body present on the battlefield following his death; Orsik approached the corpse to attempt a headband swap. The headband he wore was the artifact the SEER had directed the party to retrieve. Fruul's body served as the site of the session's central tension over the headband.

**Chandra Stol (Local Cleric)**
- Mourned the death of her pet wyvern, which contributed nothing offensively in the battle. Argued with Orsik over handing the headband to the SEER, initially siding with the SEER but then reconsidering as Orsik made his case. Challenged the trustworthiness of the Lord's Alliance, noting they were responsible for the death and destruction visited on the town.

**Raggnar**
- Confronted the party over the destruction to the town. Participated in arguments about rebuilding. Later raced after Orsik and acknowledged that Orsik made "excellent points" about not trusting the SEER.

**Gundalin**
- Initially inclined to agree with Raggnar's condemnation of the party. Later supported Orsik's skepticism of the Lord's Alliance, arguing the Alliance's machinations caused the town's death and destruction.

**Wallace**
- Attempted to convince Orsik to hand over the headband by invoking the SEER's status as a key member of the Lord's Alliance and the town's need for Alliance support. Later announced his retirement from adventuring, citing his relationship with Elspeth and the town's need for a new guard captain.

**Blasius**
- Emerged from hiding after the battle and offered alcohol to Unla Kee. Celebrated the victory effusively. Was still drinking and singing with Unla Kee into the following morning.

**Unla Kee**
- Accepted wine from Blasius despite the risk. Expressed a desire to serve as a vessel for the evil soul contained in Bad Fruul's headband, reasoning that it would grant access to drow magic and the lore of a 500-year-old wizard. Was still drinking and singing with Blasius into the following morning.

**Hsing (Gold Pseudodragon)**
- Appeared and demanded Orsik hand over the headband to the SEER. When the group began to push back, Hsing inadvertently undermined its own position by acknowledging that spymasters lie and that trusting one is questionable.

**The SEER**
- Materialized through a door that appeared on-site, apparently monitoring events. Invited the party to step into her garden; the party declined. When directly confronted by Orsik about her intentions for the evil soul, she pledged to perform the ritual in the town the following morning rather than elsewhere, as a gesture of transparency. Performed the ritual the next morning, resulting in Orsik receiving a headband of intellect.

**Prince Thornacious (Fey)**
- Appeared as an unexpected guest at the post-battle celebration. Informed Thistle that he had mentioned Thistle to Queen Tatiana of the Seelie Court and that she was pleased. Characterized being noticed by the Queen as the highest aspiration a fey could have.

**Queen Tatiana of the Seelie Court (referenced, absent)**
- Has been made aware of Thistle's actions by Prince Thornacious and expressed approval. Did not appear directly.

**Elspeth (referenced, absent)**
- Mentioned by Wallace as the reason for his retirement; he and Elspeth have decided to be together.

**Two Sprites (unnamed)**
- Claimed credit for delivering the killing blow on Bad Fruul via poisoned arrows. Celebrated enthusiastically and were affirmed by Orsik and Vardis as more valuable than the wyvern.

## Faction Movements

**The Lord's Alliance / The SEER**
- The SEER successfully recovered the headband artifact from Bad Fruul via the party, though only after significant resistance from Orsik. She performed a ritual the following morning in Parnast to neutralize the evil soul, converting the headband into a headband of intellect as the apparent outcome. Her willingness to perform the ritual publicly in the town was a concession to Orsik's demands and may represent a shift toward earned trust rather than assumed authority.
- Wallace's retirement removes one Alliance-aligned operative from the field.

**Seelie Court**
- Prince Thornacious is actively communicating Thistle's deeds to Queen Tatiana. The Queen's attention has been drawn to Thistle, suggesting growing fey interest in the party's activities.

## Revealed Information

- The SEER is acknowledged even by her own agent (Hsing) to be a spymaster, and Hsing conceded that spymasters lie, undermining blind trust in the SEER's directives.
- The evil soul in Bad Fruul's headband belonged to a 500-year-old wizard with drow magical knowledge, per Unla Kee's stated reasoning for wanting it as a vessel.
- The Lord's Alliance is viewed with deep suspicion by Parnast's leadership (Chandra, Gundalin, Raggnar), who hold the Alliance's machinations responsible for the destruction the town suffered.
- The SEER's ritual converted the artifact into a headband of intellect, now in Orsik's possession. Whether this was always the intended outcome or a concession to circumstance is unconfirmed.
- The Zhentarim helped defeat the Cult of the Dragon, according to Chandra, motivated by self-preservation rather than altruism.
