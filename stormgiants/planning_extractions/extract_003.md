## NPC Activity

**Fiora Bendaega**
- Acted as a middleperson broker connecting merchants with adventurers at an unspecified establishment (likely connected to Nonna's/the area)
- Offered the party a job on behalf of unnamed merchants working the Black Road
- Attempted to use empathy regarding the Cult of the Dragon tragedy as a persuasion tactic; it backfired with Thistle
- Signaled her guard Diego to intervene when Vardis became threatening
- Closed the deal when Orsik asked about the timeline (one month window; merchant arrives in three weeks, two-week journey)
- Informed the party she could be found at Nonna's place going forward, and indicated they would not be welcome back at her current location

**Diego**
- Fiora's personal guard; advanced on the party when signaled by Fiora
- Backed down when Fiora winked at him after Orsik greeted him
- Described by Fiora as being "fond of her"

**Leo (Lionel Coster franchise owner, Llorkh)**
- Greeted the party at his franchise store; refused credit to first-time buyers
- Sold a scroll of dispel magic at 250 gold (reduced from 300) after Thistle identified scuff marks and creasing on the container, which threatened Leo's "Lionel Coster best presentation award" nomination
- Notably immaculate in personal appearance; deeply invested in store presentation standards

**Old Keller (stonemason, Llorkh)**
- Refused to let Orsik approach his hut
- Expressed that Orsik had disgraced dwarves through "that thing in the keep"
- Conditioned reconciliation on Orsik stopping the offending behavior and surviving long enough to return; promised to explain redemption steps at that time

**Nonnie ("Aunt Nonnie")**
- Runs a small inn in Llorkh with a vegetarian-only menu
- Welcomed the party warmly; served four cheese pies
- Provided lodging for a fee
- Described as treating everyone, including refugees, with warmth

**Refugee Woman (unnamed, from Greenest)**
- Present at Nonnie's inn; described herself as a refugee from Greenest, destroyed by the Cult of the Dragon
- Fled east to escape dragons; now planning to return west after hearing rumors of giants nearby, to check on Greenest and help rebuild
- Not a faction operative but carries relevant intelligence about Greenest's status and giant rumors

**The Entity in the Wizard Tower (unnamed)**
- Observed the party approaching the Tower of Green Flame; activated wards that injured but did not kill Thistle and the dwarf who touched the door handle
- Spoke to itself throughout, expressing reluctant hospitality and concern
- Noted with sorrow after the party survived: "the door can now be opened" — implying the ward's defeat has created a vulnerability or new access condition

**Veradda Store (referenced, absent)**
- Her notes were the source of directions to the treasure trove location in the northern Greypeaks; she had previously identified at least three treasure troves

**The Green Diamond Spirit (destroyed)**
- Inhabited or was bound to a supernatural diamond atop a pedestal in the frost giant tomb chamber
- Animated three translucent green energy creatures with barb-tipped tentacles; creatures were linked to and powered by the diamond
- Upon destruction of the diamond, manifested as a green-tinted spirit and issued a paranoia-inducing whisper attempting to turn party members against each other
- The party ignored the message
