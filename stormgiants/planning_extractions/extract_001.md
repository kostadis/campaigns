## NPC Activity

**Veradaa Storr**
Found dead at a clearing along the Black Road near Parnast before the party arrived. She was a treasure hunter in possession of detailed notes describing five Cult of the Dragon treasure caches hidden throughout the Greypeak mountain range. Her belongings were searched by the party, and Thistle recovered her research notes. Her death preceded the session's events; cause unknown.

**Sergeant Gronk**
Commander of a goblin unit occupying the first cave cache site. Set up a defensive position inside the cavern using partial cover, exploiting a southern tunnel as a potential fallback. Reported that his unit had arrived with two giants, one of whom died in a cave-in in the eastern tunnel. Waited in ambush for the party upon their approach. Initially succeeded in downing two party members. Killed when a thunderwave spell disrupted his forces. His last thoughts noted concern about the surviving shrunken giant's recovery.

**Slayermighty (the tiny Giant)**
Found guarding a stone chest in the innermost cavern of the first cache site, reduced to miniature size by an unknown magical curse triggered in the cave. Wielded a club and claimed to be on the verge of ruling all of Giant-kind. Threatened the party in the name of himself and his bear, Blooddrinker. Was killed by the party before he could land a single blow. Had on his person: two vials of alchemist fire, a potion of healing, and a necklace of 20 copper charms.

**Blooddrinker**
Slayermighty's bear companion, also miniaturized by the curse (now cat-sized). Upon Slayermighty's death, rolled over expecting to be petted rather than attacking. Began convulsing shortly after—saved by Thistle's quick action. Bonded to Thistle following the rescue.

**Clan True-Nimbus of the Clouds (Cloud Giants — referenced, not present)**
Left a taunting written note inside the stone chest at the first cache site, addressed to "runt-giants of the hills." The note confirmed they had already claimed the treasure from this location. They mocked Hill Giants as slow and dim-witted, signed the note as Clan True-Nimbus of the Clouds.

## Faction Movements

**Cult of the Dragon (historical, referenced)**
The session's framing text establishes the Cult as destroyed following Tiamat's defeat at the Well of the World. However, evidence suggests some Cult leaders secreted away personal hoards independent of Tiamat's ritual hoard. Veradaa Storr had compiled intelligence on five such caches hidden throughout the Greypeaks, suggesting pre-planned contingency hoarding by individual Cultists. The third cache is specifically noted to have been hidden by a Cultist described as an expert in magical curses.

**Harpers (referenced)**
A Harper contact, known to Thistle, vouched for Veradaa Storr's map and intelligence as legitimate, leading Thistle to seek her out.

## Threat Arc Events

**Planar Distortion / Unknown Curse Effect**
A magical curse of unknown origin is active within the first cache cave complex. It shrunk at least two giants (one killed in a cave-in, one — Slayermighty — reduced to miniature size) and transformed a goblin unit into a swarm of goblin-sized mice. As goblin-mice were killed, they reverted to full-size goblin corpses, eventually making the southern tunnel area nearly impassable. The curse appears temporary rather than permanent, as the party correctly deduced. Source and full mechanics of the curse are unidentified.

## Revealed Information

**Veradaa Storr's Cache Notes (recovered in full):**
- **Cache 1:** Abandoned cave at the base of the southernmost mountain of the Greypeaks. Described as the smallest cache, reportedly untrapped and unprotected by magic. Structurally unstable tunnels. Known goblin habitation. *(Now confirmed looted by Clan True-Nimbus before the party's arrival.)*
- **Cache 2:** Buried beneath a stone pyramid on a flat volcanic stone field in the middle Greypeaks, between the two highest mountains of that section. Origin of the pyramid unknown.
- **Cache 3:** Hidden behind a secret entrance in the wall of a "green-sided mountain" — described as unique in the Greypeaks. Hidden by a Cultist who was an expert in magical curses.
- **Cache 4:** Secured in an abandoned dwarven ore-treatment outpost. Dwarves reportedly abandoned it centuries ago due to "something terrible and immortal." Regular subterranean volcanic activity reported nearby.
- **Cache 5:** Located at the top of the tallest Greypeak summit, at an abandoned shrine once revered by giants, later used as a dragon perch. A loud bell can occasionally be heard from the summit when wind conditions are right.

**Giant Activity in the Region:**
Vardis reported rumors along the road: rune-covered boulders, huge footprints, and missing livestock, suggesting giant movement south of their typical range. Clan True-Nimbus of the Clouds has been confirmed active in the Greypeaks, having reached the first cache ahead of the party and left a taunting note.

**Cache 1 Status:**
Looted by Clan True-Nimbus of the Clouds prior to the party's arrival. Stone chest found empty except for Slayermighty's personal possessions (alchemist fire, healing potion, copper charm necklace).
