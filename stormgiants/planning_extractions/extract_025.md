## NPC Activity

**Jacob and Persson Hysstryn** – Two brothers who hired Thistle to investigate the death of their brother Mirak. They revealed Mirak had an affair with Urgala Meltimer.

**Mirak Hysstryn** – Deceased. His remains were discovered hidden in the attic of the Six Windows Inn. The remains were used as evidence to convince Lord Protector Darathra to imprison Tolmara pending a Speak with Dead casting.

**Urgala Meltimer** – Owner of the Northshield House Inn and ex-adventurer. Was having an affair with the deceased Mirak Hysstryn. Spoke to the party about the death of her first wife in the Underdark. Provided intelligence on the Dessarin Hills — including locations of potential treasure, wildcat mining sites, and areas inhabited by bandits and uncivilized types. Provided the party with a map of one area of particular interest.

**Lord Protector Darathra** – Agreed to imprison Tolmara until a cleric capable of casting Speak with Dead can be found. Ordered the party to remain in town until the following day due to the commotion they caused.

**Tolmara** – Imprisoned by Lord Protector Darathra on suspicion of involvement in Mirak's death, pending magical investigation.

**Unnamed Priest of Helm** – Found drunk and passed out in the Talking Troll. Revealed to the party that Mirak's body was being kept at the Six Windows Inn.

**Alaestra** – Co-owner of the Lionshield Coster outpost, Lion's Share. Her vault was breached and special interplanar supply chain keys were stolen. Relieved to receive outside help investigating the theft.

**Narth** – Co-owner of Lion's Share alongside Alaestra. Present during the report of the vault breach.

**Orthovir** – Master harness maker. Examined the leather harness found at the vault and confirmed he did not make it. Assessed it as Waterdeep-made and sized for a thin person of approximately 150 pounds.

**Harriet Uldinath** – Craftsperson at location T17. Did not recognize the grappling hook and denied it came from her shop. Acknowledged it was superior to her own work and suggested it may be the work of her competitor Ghelryn Foehammer.

**Ghelryn Foehammer** – Dwarven craftsperson at location T18, values privacy. Confirmed he made and sold the grappling hook. Revealed it was sold to Darz Helgar.

**Darz Helgar** – Campground overseer and ex-criminal from Waterdeep, previously imprisoned in Waterdeep's dungeons over ten years ago. When confronted with the harness, immediately implicated Kaelen (proprietor of the Troll Tavern) as the one who coerced him into the vault break-in. Agreed to share information about the thieves guild in exchange for Orsik keeping his criminal past secret.

**Kaelen** – Proprietor of the Talking Troll tavern. Named by Darz Helgar as the individual who forced him to participate in the theft of the Lionshield Coster vault keys.

## Faction Movements

**Lionshield Coster** – Suffered a targeted vault breach at their Lion's Share outpost. Lost special keys used to access their interplanar supply chain. The chain's business model — leveraging interplanar trade routes — has generated local resentment among townspeople, who view the operation with caution and disgust. The theft may further destabilize their local standing and supply operations.
