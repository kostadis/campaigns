- Warned that Narcellia has specific gruesome plans for Chandra Stol due to the pendant Chandra carries from her ancestor Luistarra
- Offered the party 600gp base, +200gp if no one dies, +100gp if Chandra survives, in exchange for protecting Chandra, determining the timing of Bad Fruul's attack, defending Parnast, and delivering the headband
- Demonstrated intelligence reach by revealing personal information about Unla Kee's uncle Joon (still in Triboar), his nemesis Adirond Kroskas (disappeared into the North), and his beloved Laela (rumored alive, possibly captured by Fire Giant allies)
