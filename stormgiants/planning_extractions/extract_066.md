## NPC Activity

**Bob (the Demon)**
- Was defeated/dispersed when Thuul stuffed a second bag of holding into the first bag of holding at Bob's feet, triggering an astral planar explosion
- Was present at the theater with a zombie army dressed as members of the 3HPP
- Had trapped approximately 200+ Silverymoon theatergoers in a pocket dimension before the battle
- Was accompanied by a Red Wizard ally, who was also caught in the blast
- Is banished to the Astral Plane but not permanently destroyed; Darathra warns he can return

**The Red Wizard**
- Was present with Bob at the theater battle
- Was caught in the bag of holding explosion and sent to the Astral Plane along with Bob
- No further details revealed about identity or affiliation beyond working with Bob

**Darathra**
- Participated in the battle at the theater against Bob
- Was furious that Bob was sent to the Astral Plane rather than killed, understanding the multiverse well enough to know he can return
- Expressed outrage that the 300 innocent theatergoers trapped in the pocket dimension now have no known means of rescue, as the only being who knows their location (Bob) is lost in the Astral Plane
- Agreed to the cover story only to the extent of denying she was ever present
- Formally severed her alliance with the 3HPP: declared she was never there, never saw anything, and told the party not to call in favors or make special requests again
- Departed the theater before it was burned
- Last seen: exiting the theater in Silverymoon, establishing her own alibi

**Lord Zymorven**
- Participated in the battle, fighting alongside Daratha and Urgala against the zombie horde
- Was momentarily outraged that Silverymoon citizens were caught in the bag of holding blast
- Formally cleared his family's debt to the 3HPP after the battle
- Publicly distanced himself from Orsik's decisions, framing all choices as Orsik's alone and stating he was not consulted
- Negotiated and agreed to the cover story: Bob (a demon) used fire spells in the theater, causing a conflagration the party could not contain, though they defeated Bob and saved the town
- Quietly exited the theater when racial commentary entered the conversation
- Last seen: departing the theater in Silverymoon

**Urgala**
- Participated in the battle against the zombie horde
- Revealed she has a past romantic relationship with Lord Zymorven ("the experimental years")
- Encouraged the 3HPP to flee Silverymoon quickly rather than stay for praise, reasoning that absence would reduce pressure to ask hard questions and give time to find the missing people
- Expressed delight at seeing Zymorven and Darathra humbled, indicating long-standing tension with both over an affair
- Pledged future assistance to the 3HPP: "If you need anything from me, I'm here for you"
- Last seen: departing the theater in Silverymoon

**Vardis**
- Participated in the battle; used his power over undead to turn the zombie hordes, and used Fireball and Flamestrike to finish them off
- Found the ease of Bob's disposal amusing
- Participated in cover story negotiation
- Engaged Escarlotta in small talk about saving Silverymoon
- Last seen: aboard the 3HPP airship, transported to the Maelstrom

**Unla Kee**
- Participated in the execution of the bag of holding plan by tossing the first bag toward Bob
- Used fire magic to begin burning the theater (mote of fire to balcony, then a second explosive strike to the theater floor)
- Proposed an alternative cover story blaming "ungodly races" for the theater massacre, which was rejected; privately reflected that the idea would have caused violent race riots, which he found grimly appealing
- Blew the Giant Conch, transporting the party to the Maelstrom
- Last seen: in the Maelstrom, the underwater stronghold

**Drow Fighters**
- Were present at the theater to protect the Red Wizard
- Fled before being caught when the situation turned against Bob
- Current whereabouts unknown

**Escarlotta** (castle control system / storm giantess spirit)
- Revealed a significant emotional shift: had a long conversation with Blagothkus and resolved longstanding anger and grief over his killing of their son, coming to understand it was the result of manipulation by Amn and its gods rather than his choice
- Expressed that witnessing Thistle sacrifice his relationship for a higher cause influenced her reconciliation with Blagothkus
- Reframed her relationship with the party: wants genuine partnership, small talk, and to be treated as part of the team rather than given orders
- Urged the party to focus on finding King Hekaton and using the Giant Conch, rather than pursuing personal vendettas (including against the vampire)
- Acknowledged the vampire must be destroyed but placed stopping the giant war as the greater priority
- Expressed belief that the 3HPP are critical to the future of the realm
- Last known status: integrated with the airship castle, now in cooperative relationship with Blagothkus

**Blagothkus**
- Spoke briefly, observing that "ends justify the means" thinking was exactly what caused his and Escarlotta's emotional conflict
- Has reconciled with Escarlotta following their long conversation
- Last known status: aboard the airship castle, reconciled with Escarlotta

**The Vampire** (unnamed, referred to as "their friendly vampire and her spawn")
- Referenced but absent; the 3HPP deliberately boarded the airship in the morning to avoid encountering her
- Escarlotta confirmed she must eventually be destroyed but the giant crisis takes priority
- Current whereabouts: somewhere near or associated with the airship castle

**Queen Serissa**

## Revealed Information

- **Bob can return from the Astral Plane.** Darathra made this explicit; banishment is not permanent destruction.
- **The 200+ missing theatergoers are trapped in a pocket dimension**, the location of which is known only to Bob, who is now lost in the Astral Plane. There is currently no known method to retrieve them.
- **The Red Wizard was Bob's ally** and was present at the theater; both were lost in the same planar explosion.
- **Escarlotta and Blagothkus have reconciled.** The killing of their son was the result of manipulation by Amn and its gods, not Blagothkus's free choice. Both have accepted this and chosen to remain together.
- **The Maelstrom is located underwater**, explaining why it could not be found through conventional means. The physical entrance is a pool.
- **Queen Serissa is likely present in the Maelstrom**, based on the music heard above.
- **The Giant Conch can only be blown once per day** and does not function as an escape tool on demand.
- **Urgala and Lord Zymorven had a past romantic relationship**, which ended badly; Urgala holds lasting resentment toward both Zymorven and Darathra over the affair.
