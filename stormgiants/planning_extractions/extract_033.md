## Revealed Information

- **Adimond Kroskas** is an alias; his true identity is unknown. He created the ritual that allowed the Cult of Tiamat to first produce Drakes. He is believed responsible for killing Harper operative Kolbaz, apparently by sending or becoming something massive through the teleportation circle.
- **The vampire in Yartar's prison** operates independently of the Waterbarron's authority; she considers his guards no constraint on her. Her experimental program on condemned prisoners is described as sanctioned by "the highest authorities in Yartar," suggesting high-level complicity or a claim to autonomous power.
- **Leila** is confirmed alive. She was in Yartar, attempted to join the Hand of Yartar, was rejected as too small an operation, and has departed to find other means of locating Thistle. She repeatedly spoke of Thistle and described him as "the absolute love of her life."
- **The Harpers' Ezra model** was initiated in response to the weakness of Harper field agents during the Cult of the Dragon crisis; it was Ezra's program, and its success led to his promotion.
- **High Captain Horix Zoar** dissolved the Everlund-Silverymoon alliance over what he considers a personal betrayal by High Marshal Methrammar Aerasumé and retains enough residual anger to contemplate military action, though he restrains himself.
- **Zhentarim operatives** in Yartar are aware of Orsik by name, consider him a known independent agent, and track his activities. They believe they could have assisted him with the sword without conflict.
