## Revealed Information

- Laela is a fey-dragon hybrid engineered by Iymrith; her shapeshifting is intrinsic to her biology, not magical, making her undetectable by magical means
- Queen Titania and the Seelie Court knew of and approved Iymrith's experiments — this is likely connected to Thistle's expulsion from the Seelie Court
- Iymrith's investor network includes Szass Tam (Red Wizards), giant lords, the Unseelie Court, and the Seelie Court
- Iymrith does not work with the SEER and considers her the true power behind the Lords' Alliance, describing her as pursuing a long-term plan to curtail the freedom of creatures like Iymrith; Iymrith characterizes the SEER as evil
- Iymrith's lair and the coliseum are protected against scrying and teleportation
- Iymrith's primary current problem is that Laela refuses to transform into dragon form, threatening the success of the demonstration and her investor relationships
- Iymrith does not consider herself a slaver and intends to release Laela after a successful demonstration
- Iymrith has tools and equipment beyond typical draconic resources, noted by Thistle as unusual
