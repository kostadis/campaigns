## Revealed Information

- **Lord Drylund is manipulating votes** to become the next Waterbaron of Yartar, using the Grand Dame as his political and financial base.
- **Yartar's Waterbaron elections** are triggered by the sitting Waterbaron's death; the current Waterbaron works to keep rivals divided and friends close to avoid assassination.
- **Lady Atalia is a telepath** who uses mind-reading to cheat at Sava and surveil opponents; she is highly sensitive to any threat of exposure and wants to meet the party privately.
- **Lord Zymorven's son** was among nobles who had disappeared; he was freed by adventurers and magically returned to his father, which was "a terrible blow for the Waterbaron" — suggesting the sitting Waterbaron was involved in noble disappearances.
- **Three methods of winning Yartar elections** per Drylund: persuasion, bribery through post-election promises, and holding loved ones hostage.
- **A coin** connected to King Hekaton's disappearance may have some link to the Grand Dame or Lord Drylund — Orsik raised this question at the end of the session.
