## NPC Activity

**Elquen (Wight, formerly manservant/librarian to the Wizard of the Crag)**
- Appeared in the library of the Wizard of the Crag's castle, where he had been trapped as a wight following a wild magic surge
- Revealed backstory through reminiscence: he was present when the Wizard of the Crag died in a duel with Taskor, and attempted to control water elementals to prevent destruction of the castle; a wild magic surge transformed him into a wight and converted his trapped companions (two guards and manservants) into skeletons when he tried to help them
- Initially refused to allow any books to be removed or magic to be used, citing his curse to protect the world from magical disaster
- Was deceived by Unla Kee's manipulation of fate into believing Unla was the Wizard of the Crag, and willingly ceded control of the library
- Requested and received 150 GP in back pay before departing
- Walked out through the castle hallways toward the laboratory upon being "freed"

**The Wizard of the Crag (deceased, referenced)**
- Referenced throughout: died in a duel with Taskor, leaving debts unpaid (real estate investments, construction overruns)
- His death triggered the cascade of events that led to Elquen's transformation and the castle's current state
- His treasure room was found but contained nothing of value beyond a financial ledger

**Taskor (referenced, absent)**
- Killed the Wizard of the Crag in a duel; the duel was apparently connected to the Wizard's debts

**Unla Kee (Gnome Wizard — NPC-adjacent note)**
- Manipulated fate twice during the session: once to avoid a killing blow from a magmin, and once to convince Elquen he was the Wizard of the Crag
- Paid Elquen 150 GP from party funds

**The SEER (referenced, absent)**
- Referenced by Wallace as the patron of the pseudodragon; described as capable of teleporting the pseudodragon (or something more dangerous) within 5 feet of the party
- Member of the Lord's Alliance

**Water Weird**
- Rematerialized on the surface of the island after the party exited the castle
- Led a group of Mud Mephits in an attack on the party
- Defeated during the encounter

**Mud Mephits**
- Allied with the Water Weird in the surface encounter
- Turned against each other when the party called in their own mephit allies, effectively neutralizing themselves through internecine fighting
- Several died, producing muddy explosions the party had to avoid
