## NPC Activity

**Titania (Queen of the Seelie Court)**
- Referenced but absent. Her standing punishment on the Dryads Helia and Halisa is acknowledged as ongoing. She issued a new task to the party via a Seelie Court sprite: investigate and deal with a Fomorian of the Unseelie Court who has taken refuge in the upper lands. She was pleased by the party's use of trickery in freeing the Dryads, especially that it served a selfish goal. She also cited Thornacious' involvement as additional justification to keep him banned from the Feywild.

**Seelie Court Sprite (unnamed)**
- Appeared before Thistle and Orsik-Thornacious. Delivered Titania's judgment in both Common and Fey. Communicated the queen's mixed displeasure and approval, assigned the Fomorian task as punishment, and opened a portal to the Feywild. Disappeared after the party entered the portal.

**Drauchanar ("The Knife")**
- Elven hunter who specializes in rare and dangerous game, including Fomorians. Appeared in the Feywild shortly after the party arrived. Offered to guide the party to the Fomorian. Accepted the arrangement that Orsik-Thornacious would disguise himself as a dwarf so that Drauchanar could keep the Fomorian's head as a prize. Sang an old elvish song about Corellon and Araushnee during a rest. Was subsequently ambushed during the night and dragged off by a giant-like creature and a four-legged beast. Current status: abducted, whereabouts unknown — likely inside or near the Fomorian's cave.

**Maegurn (Fomorian)**
- Fomorian of the Unseelie Court who has taken refuge in the upper lands. His traditional bully-to-goblin relationship with the cave's goblin tribe has degraded into outright abuse. He holds Drauchanar captive (implied). Last known location: inside or near a large cave with a 20-foot-wide entrance guarded by a cyclops.

**Tlega (Goblin boss)**
- Named goblin leader encountered behind the waterfall tunnel. Speaks with a nasal, squeaky voice. Confirmed the party's goal of killing Maegurn and offered goblin assistance, expressing delight at the prospect. Engaged in open conflict with Mirk over the decision.

**Mirk (Goblin boss)**
- Second goblin leader. Opposed Tlega's decision to ally with the party, arguing that the bully/bullied dynamic is foundational to goblin society and that the boundary with Maegurn is "just an imaginary line." Was distracted and persuaded by Unla Kee's Jim's Glowing Coin spell and ultimately agreed to dispatch Maegurn.

**Goblin #1 (unnamed)**
- Minor goblin in studded armor who served as initial lookout in the tunnel behind the waterfall. Relayed the party's intentions to Tlega.

**Helia and Halisa (Dryads)**
- Referenced but absent. Titania's punishment on them was partially circumvented by the party in a prior session. Their release is acknowledged as the precipitating event for this session's Seelie Court contact.

## Faction Movements

**Seelie Court**
- Dispatched a sprite emissary to formally acknowledge the party's interference with Titania's punishment of Helia and Halisa. Rather than severe punishment, assigned the party a task: deal with a Fomorian operating in the upper lands. Used Thornacious' involvement as further grounds to maintain his ban from the Feywild. Opened a portal to deliver the party to the relevant location.

**Unseelie Court**
- A Fomorian (Maegurn) associated with the Unseelie Court has taken refuge in the upper lands — specifically in a large cave. His presence is considered a violation significant enough for the Seelie Court to take action.

**Cave Goblin Tribe (unnamed)**
- Split between two factions led by Tlega and Mirk. Tlega's faction is ready to revolt against Maegurn due to escalating abuse; Mirk's faction resists on ideological grounds. After Unla Kee's magical intervention, the tribe reached consensus to assist in eliminating Maegurn.

## Threat Arc Events

**Planar Distortion / Feywild Instability**
- The party was forcibly portaled into the Feywild by a Seelie Court emissary, marking an escalating entanglement with fey powers. Titania is now directly directing the party's actions through a formal geas-equivalent task.

**Unseelie Court Incursion**
- Maegurn, a Fomorian of the Unseelie Court, has established a presence in the upper lands and is holding at least one captive (Drauchanar). His abuse of the local goblin population and his ambush of the party's guide represent active hostile action.

## Revealed Information

- Titania was pleased that the Dryads' release served a selfish goal — trickery for personal gain aligns with Fey values and partially mitigated the party's punishment.
- The Seelie Court is aware of Thornacious' identity and uses his presence as a political tool to justify keeping him exiled from the Feywild.
- Drauchanar operates under fey cultural rules in which a royal (Thornacious) claiming involvement would strip him of the right to keep his prize (the Fomorian's head).
- Goblin society in this tribe is structured around a formal bully/bullied hierarchy; Maegurn's crossing from ritualized bullying into genuine abuse is what fractured the tribe's tolerance.
- The cave complex is guarded by at least one cyclops at the main entrance and has a secondary access point via a tunnel behind a nearby waterfall.
