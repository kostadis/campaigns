## NPC Activity

**Blagothkus**
- Found in the castle's control room, disheveled, harried, and exhausted; unable to operate the castle controls because Escarlotta has locked him out.
- Revealed his past plot: he allied with dragons to help bring Tiamat into the world, calculating that when the dragons failed, giants could rule over the weakened "little people."
- Revealed he killed his own son at the Eye of the All-Father as part of this plan, rather than allowing his son (Eigeron) to kill him — an act deemed unworthy by Annam.
- Laughed with Escarlotta at the notion of Fire Giants making a play for rulership.
- Was repeatedly berated by Escarlotta throughout the session; eventually sat down again after Vardis defended him during a hypothetical discussion of his death.
- Remained aboard the flying castle when it departed for the Eye of the All-Father. Last known location: the castle, status alive.

**Escarlotta**
- Disembodied presence controlling the castle's navigation console; she is trapped within it.
- Furious at Blagothkus for his dragon-Tiamat plot, for killing Eigeron instead of letting himself be killed (which would have potentially elevated her to a position of rulership), and for failing to seize maps from Countess Sansuri.
- Refused to fly the castle until the party agreed to kill Countess Sansuri as her condition.
- Provided intelligence on Countess Sansuri: a vain, greedy cloud giantess who lives in Lyn Armaal; her family retained magical artifacts when Ostoria fell, and she is searching for them.
- Provided intelligence on Blagothkus's failed plan and his political unworthiness in giant society.
- Agreed to fly the castle to the Eye of the All-Father once the party mentioned the Oracle could provide locations of the giant leaders.
- Last known status: imprisoned in the castle console, controlling navigation; castle last seen departing the Eye of the All-Father.

**Sandesyl Morgia**
- Vampire who has claimed the castle as her domain, having originally been tolerated by Blagothkus in exchange for a steady stream of victims.
- Had already consumed all of Blagothkus's minions during a prolonged period while the castle was parked north of Parnast.
- Observed the party through her zombie spawns' eyes from outside the tower.
- Approached under an umbrella in daylight via a spawn dressed in Cult of the Dragon purple; inquired about where the castle would stop for a meal.
- Emerged in the courtyard surrounded by zombies and spawns; engaged Thistle in dialogue about House Margastar, stating she had no interest in fighting them because Asmodeus protects them and she would not upset the archfiend's plans.
- Negotiated with Thistle over a feeding target; agreed to accept Uthgardt barbarians of Flint Rock as victims rather than the party's proposed orc village.
- Directed her vampires to abduct a group of Uthgardt nomads mourning a despoiled artifact, feed on them at the settled castle, and plant false evidence implicating House Margastar and Silverymoon — using the party's vendetta as cover and political leverage.
- Left a survivor to spread the fabricated tale.
- Screams of victims echoed through the castle over the subsequent days of travel.
- Last known location: aboard the cloud castle, status active.

**Worug and Tax**
- Two ogres aboard the castle; identified as among the last living creatures Sandesyl has not yet consumed.
- No direct action taken; mentioned as context for the danger of Sandesyl's presence.
- Last known location: the castle.

**Delsephine**
- Delivered a message to the party on behalf of Klauth, wishing them luck in ending the giant war.
- No other actions noted. Last known location/status: aboard or near the castle at time of delivery.

**Klauth**
- Referenced-but-absent; his agent Delsephine relayed his goodwill toward the party's mission.
- No direct appearance.

**Eigeron**
- Ghost encountered inside the Eye of the All-Father portal.
- Learned from the party that his father Blagothkus is not dead but that his castle is present; reacted with fury.
- Attempted to possess one of the heroes.
- Was turned by Vardis invoking Corellon.
- Left howling for revenge as the party fled the portal.
- Last known location: inside the Eye of the All-Father (or its collapsing ruins). Status: turned/repelled but not destroyed.

**Harshnag**
- Met the party at the Eye of the All-Father; was non-committal about Blagothkus's fate when asked.
- Picked up Thrymm's great axe, touched the Ise rune on the portal, and led the party inside.
- When the party refused to flee after Adimond Kroskas transformed, Harshnag deliberately desecrated the statue of Annam the All-Father — striking it twice with his greataxe — to collapse the temple and force the heroes to evacuate.
- Engaged Adimond Kroskas in combat to buy the party time to escape.
- Was last seen fighting the Ancient Blue Dragon as the temple collapsed around them both. Presumed dead or buried under the collapsed Eye of the All-Father; fate unconfirmed.

**Adimond Kroskas**
- Appeared in the main temple of the Eye of the All-Father.
- Upon seeing Harshnag, transformed into an Ancient Blue Dragon.
- Engaged Harshnag in combat as the temple collapsed; appeared uninterested in pursuing the fleeing party.
- Last known location: inside or beneath the collapsed Eye of the All-Father. Status: unknown.

## Faction Movements

**Cloud Giant Household (Blagothkus / Escarlotta)**
- The castle was successfully redirected to the Eye of the All-Father through party negotiation with Escarlotta.
- Escarlotta has formally declared her opposition to Blagothkus and her desire for his death, though she was not willing to act unilaterally.
- The household is politically isolated: Blagothkus is deemed unworthy by Annam's judgment; neither he nor Escarlotta is among the cloud giant families making a play for rulership.
- Escarlotta has committed to continued cooperation contingent on the party killing Countess Sansuri.

**Sandesyl's Vampire Coven (Cult of the Dragon-affiliated spawn)**
- Actively operating from the cloud castle; at least one spawn was noted wearing Cult of the Dragon purple, suggesting lingering Cult affiliation or resources.
- Executed a covert atrocity against Uthgardt barbarians of Flint Rock: abducted mourners, fed on them, planted false evidence blaming House Margastar and Silverymoon.
- This action advances Sandesyl's feeding needs while potentially destabilizing Silverymoon's relations with Uthgardt tribes and implicating House Margastar in a massacre.
- Sandesyl explicitly chose not to target House Margastar directly due to Asmodean protection.

**Countess Sansuri / Lyn Armaal (Cloud Giants)**
- Not present; identified as the dominant cloud giant faction making a play for rulership.
- Her family retained magical artifacts from when Ostoria fell; she is actively searching for them.
- She possesses maps that Escarlotta believes Blagothkus should have seized.
- Oracle confirmed: her conch of the giants is located in a secret chest in the highest spire of Lyn Armaal, which is a flying castle currently over the untamed moors.

**Hill Giant Chief (Grudd Haug)**
- Oracle revealed location: Grudd Haug, a den on an eastern arm of a great river to the south. The chief is female. Conch is near her.

**Stone Giant Thane (Deadstone Cleft)**
- Oracle revealed location: Deadstone Cleft, a canyon in gray peaks southeast of the great forest. The thane is female and has the conch in her possession.

**Frost Giant Jarl (Svardborg)**
- Oracle revealed location: Svardborg, a berg in the Sea of Moving Ice to the west. The jarl is male; conch rests on his icy throne.

**Fire Giant Duke Zalto (Ironslag)**
- Oracle confirmed location: Ironslag, a forge beneath a yakfolk village on a cold mountainside to the east. The conch is in his quarters.
- Blagothkus and Escarlotta both mocked Fire Giants' ambitions as absurd.

## Threat Arc Events

**Sandesyl's Vampire Coven — Escalating Predation**
- Sandesyl's vampires massacred Uthgardt barbarians at Flint Rock and planted fabricated evidence implicating House Margastar and Silverymoon. This is a proactive covert operation causing civilian deaths and political destabilization, representing a significant escalation in Sandesyl's reach and agenda beyond mere feeding.

**Eigeron — Ghost Threat**
- Eigeron attempted possession of a party member inside the Eye of the All-Father; was turned but not destroyed, and was left howling for revenge. His father's castle being present and his father being alive has enraged him. His threat potential is now active rather than dormant.

**Collapse of the Eye of the All-Father**
- Harshnag deliberately destroyed the temple of Annam to force the party's retreat. The Eye of the All-Father — the only known location of the Oracle of the All-Father — is now collapsed and presumably inaccessible. This eliminates a key giant sacred site and any future ability to consult the Oracle. This is a major irreversible arc event.

**Adimond Kroskas — Ancient Blue Dragon**
- Adimond Kroskas appeared at the Eye of the All-Father and transformed into an Ancient Blue Dragon upon seeing Harshnag, immediately triggering lethal combat. This is a first confirmed hostile appearance at a critical site.

## Revealed Information

- **Blagothkus's full plot**: He deliberately allied with dragons to facilitate Tiamat's summoning, intending to exploit the resulting weakening of humanoid civilizations to enable giant rulership. He killed his own son Eigeron at the Eye of the All-Father as part of this scheme rather than accept the honorable death Annam's judgment required of him.
- **Escarlotta is trapped in the castle console** and cannot leave it; she is dependent on external agents to act on her behalf.
- **Countess Sansuri** holds maps relevant to giant artifact recovery and is the primary cloud giant claimant for rulership; Escarlotta believes Blagothkus should have stolen these maps instead of pursuing dragon plots.
- **Sandesyl will not target House Margastar** because Asmodeus protects them, and she fears upsetting the archfiend's plans. She considers only an insane person would do so.
- **The Oracle's five giant locations** are now known in full (see Faction Movements above).
- **Adimond Kroskas** is an Ancient Blue Dragon (confirmed transformation witnessed by the party).
