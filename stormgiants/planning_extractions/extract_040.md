## NPC Activity

**Harshnag the Grim**
Harshnag was encountered sitting under a tree, having gathered intelligence on the movements of multiple Giant factions across the North. He relayed information about the Frost Giant Jarl, the Fire Giant Duke, and the mad Stone Giant Thane to the party. He expressed frustration at the party's detour to Mirabar, invoking Krowen Valharrow's name as leverage. He ultimately accepted the route through the valley as a viable path to the Eye of the All-Father. After the frost drake ambush, he identified the location marked on the recovered map and confirmed the party could reach it without significant delay.

**The Foreman (of Mirabar Mines)**
The Foreman was summoned to the guard post by his men when Orsik invoked his "hero of Mirabar" history. He was initially dismissive, confirming Orsik had already been compensated for prior services. He offered the party lodging in exchange for handling a crag cat problem a day's hike out. Privately, he suspected the party might be connected to the Weevil. He consulted the Ax via sending stone and learned Orsik had exaggerated his role in extracting the caravan code — Orsik had reported Weevil's offer, but the Ax had extracted the actual information.

**Fruf (dwarf miner)**
Briefly interacted with Thistle in the mine lodge. Had no wing muffs but offered spare socks. No other significance noted.

**Waterdeep Elf (unnamed)**
Approached Vardis in the lodge, identifying him as a cleric of Corellon. Reacted with hostility upon realizing Vardis was a "progressive cleric" — a theological faction the elf clearly despises. Made a religious hand sign that Vardis did not recognize, then dismissed Vardis's legitimacy as a cleric of their shared god and walked away.

**Corpse (unnamed, in the field)**
Found in a tent with a burning fire a day's hike from the mines. Body had not yet been looted when frost drakes attacked. Carried a map with an X marking an unknown location. Identity unknown.

## Faction Movements

**Frost Giant Jarl's Forces**
Actively raiding and destroying settlements to the North in search of a magical artifact intended to turn all northern lands to ice. No direct encounter, but intelligence gathered via Harshnag.

**Fire Giant Duke's Forces**
Operating to the east. Pursuing repair or reconstruction of a magical device intended to grant dominion over dragons. No direct encounter; information from Harshnag.

**Stone Giant Thane's Forces**
Operating to the south, near Orlbar and Llorkh. The Thane is described as mad, with a goal of exterminating all non-Giant races. Has already destroyed multiple towns. No direct encounter; information from refugees relayed by Harshnag.

**Cloud Giants**
Described as secretive and withdrawing into cloud cover. No known offensive action. Harshnag noted their unusual concealment.

**Mirabar Mines (organizational faction)**
Security is active — armed guards with checkpoint and weapons confiscation protocols in place. The Foreman maintains communication with a superior called "the Ax" via sending stones. The operation has prior history with an informant called the Weevil and a caravan code that was compromised. The mines are managing an ongoing crag cat problem in the surrounding hills.
