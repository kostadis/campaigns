## NPC Activity

**The Blue Dragon (unnamed)**
- Encountered in a vast cavern within the Netherese Mountain Range; identified as a blue dragon native to the range.
- Declared allegiance to Tiamat when questioned by Thistle, triggering combat.
- Used lightning breath attacks against Orsik (reducing his Strength by half) and Thistle (who evaded the bulk of the damage).
- Attempted to disengage and reposition multiple times, flying to different sections of the cavern to evade melee combatants.
- Retreated to a cave with a 60-foot steep climb as a last stand.
- Attempted to negotiate mid-combat, asking if "an accommodation" could be reached; refused by the party.
- Killed by Orsik at approximately 6 HP remaining.
- Corpse harvested by Unla Kee: dragon meat, dragon blood (for potions of dragon strength), dragon teeth (for dragon arrows), dragon bone (for dragon weapons), and a dragon horn (for crafting a dragon wand).

**Kobolds (unnamed, multiple)**
- Served as allies/guardians of the blue dragon within the cavern.
- Appeared to blink in and out of existence (likely using the Kobold's Pack Tactics or a similar blinking ability).
- Engaged Vardis and Unla Kee throughout the fight while the dragon battled Orsik and Thistle.
- Multiple killed by Rime's Binding Ice (Vardis), Magic Missile (Unla Kee), and bow fire (Vardis).
- At least one put to sleep by Unla Kee's Sleep spell; others critically missed their attacks.
- One guard drake (Thuul, a party companion) struck the dragon with acid early in the battle.

**Mary**
- Referenced as backstory context; an elderly woman encountered when the party first arrived in Everlund.
- Leader of an organization called the **Fighters of the Light**.
- Publicly warned that the Kraken would return to destroy Everlund using tentacle attacks and mind control, and that the Kraken would come with powerful allies.
- Charged a 5 GP membership fee to join the Fighters of the Light; the party joined due to being short on funds.

## Faction Movements

**Fighters of the Light**
- Led by Mary in Everlund.
- Actively warning the public about the Kraken and its allied threat to Everlund.
- The party's presence in the Netherese Mountains is indirectly connected to their membership in this organization.

**Tiamat Cult / Dragon Allies**
- The blue dragon was an active Tiamat loyalist operating in the Netherese Mountain Range.
- Kobold allies were present and coordinated with the dragon in combat.
- Both the dragon and its kobold forces are now eliminated.

## Threat Arc Events

**Kraken Society Echoes**
- Mary's public warning in Everlund referenced the Kraken returning with allies more powerful than anything previously seen, using mind control and tentacle attacks — reinforcing the Kraken as an active, escalating threat targeting Everlund specifically.

## Revealed Information

- The Netherese Mountain Range contains blue dragons, dragon hoards, and potentially powerful Netherese Artifacts, the latter considered among the most powerful magic items in the realms (per Orsik).
- The blue dragon in this cavern was a declared Tiamat worshipper.
- A peculiar gem in the shape of a star was found among the dragon's hoard; its nature and significance are currently unknown.
- The Kraken is described by Mary as planning to return to Everlund with allies, using mind control — suggesting coordinated multi-faction action rather than a solo attack.
