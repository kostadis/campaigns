## NPC Activity

**Lord Khaspere Drylund**
- Already dead at session start; body remains in the sewer chamber.
- Thistle loots the body: ring with keys, 120 gold pieces, three platinum pieces, and an ornately decorated weapon with an octopus pommel.
- Raise Dead ruled impossible by Vardis ("his brain is gone"); True Resurrection discussed but dismissed at 50,000 gp cost.
- Body is covered and locked in the sewer room by Thuul and Thistle.
- Thistle searches Drylund's private room on the Grand Dame; finds no incriminating paperwork or correspondence, but does recover the octopus-pommel weapon.

**Oosith (the Aboleth)**
- Revealed to be an aboleth, not a sorcerer as previously believed.
- Dominated Unla Kee and commanded him to destroy the party.
- Attempted to dominate Thistle; attack failed when Vardis blinded it.
- Domination of Unla Kee was broken when damage to the northern Merrow disrupted concentration.
- Fled the chamber when Thistle damaged the Merrow.
- Final telepathic declaration: "The Kraken will have its revenge!"
- Last known status: fled; whereabouts unknown.

**Unla Kee**
- Briefly dominated by Oosith; used Wand of Wonder while dominated, turning himself invisible instead of attacking the party.
- Domination broken; demands naming rights to the new casino venture — "Kee and Wing Casino."

**Lady Atalia (Lady Thalia)**
- Met with the 3HPP at a fashionable café.
- Furious upon learning the aboleth escaped; abruptly left the meeting.
- Revealed she had been performing a cold-reading act, using potions to simulate mind-reading: "It's amazing how easy it is for people to read if they think you can read their minds."
- Warned that Drylund would "come back" due to Kraken involvement before correcting herself.
- Declared the 3HPP relationship "done" and shut her door on them.
- Last known location: her house in Yartar, under protection of a group of female rogues (the Hands).

**The Hands (female rogue group)**
- Serve as Lady Atalia's protection detail.
- Intercepted the 3HPP outside the café.
- Stood down after the party claimed credit for removing the threat of "Oosith."
- Subsequently offered to take a 95% cut of the Grand Dame's turnover plus an annual security/operating fee.
- Last known status: present in Yartar, affiliated with Lady Atalia.

**Pow (Grand Dame COO)**
- Met with the 3HPP at the docks.
- Enthusiastically received the flying casino proposal; recognized the opportunity to avoid taxation and protection fees by moving between cities.
- Agreed to the "Kee and Wing Casino" rebrand and shook hands with Unla Kee.
- Speaks with a Shou accent.
- Last known location: aboard the Grand Dame.

**The Kuo-Toans (unnamed, four total)**
- Three were routed by Vardis's Mace of Terror and fled through a drainage hole.
- One Kuo-Toan Whip remained in combat; closed a drainage valve during the fight; killed by Orsik.
- Last known status: three fled into the sewers; one dead.

**The Merrow (two, unnamed)**
- Stationed at the northern and southern valves in the sewer chamber; opened them simultaneously to flood the room.
- Northern Merrow was struck by Thistle; the damage disrupted the Aboleth's domination of Unla Kee.
- Southern Merrow reduced to 5 HP by Orsik, who then closed the southern valve.
- Last known status: both incapacitated or fled; exact fate unconfirmed.

**The Crazy Woman from Everlund (unnamed)**
- Referenced but absent; recalled by Thistle while searching Drylund's room.
- Was a member of "Fighters of the Light," a group the 3HPP previously aided and joined.
- Previously warned the party about the Kraken.
