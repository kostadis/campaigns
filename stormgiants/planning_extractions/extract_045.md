**Stone Giant Statue**

- Produced and rolled a 40-foot diameter granite sphere south down the hallway, then pushed it back north.
- Showed no aggression toward the party directly.
- After the sphere disappeared into the northern wall, the stone walls vanished and the statue walked south, returned to its pedestal, and became immobile again.

**Unla Kee** *(referenced in action)*
- Rushed ahead into the spider chamber before the party could assess the room, drawing attention and potential attack.

## Revealed Information

- **Noori's tribal affiliation**: Orsik identified her clothing as belonging to the same Uthgardt tribe as the chieftain the party previously killed. She does not appear to know her chieftain is dead, or that the party was responsible.
- **Noori's hatred of Unla Kee**: Noori displayed strong, visible hostility toward Unla Kee specifically. The reason is not yet stated.
- **Harshnag's living situation**: Harshnag has been living without shelter, wandering the wilderness, and has not slept in a giant-appropriate bed or room "in eons" — suggesting long-term displacement or exile from giant society.
- **Temple cave structure**: Orsik determined the spider warrens were not original construction but natural cracks formed when the mountain shifted. The caves are occupied by creatures, not built by the temple's creators.
- **Upper hallway features**: A second floor exists above the main temple hall, with giant-sized rooms, a life-size statue of a female cloud giant in an angry mask posed as if casting a spell at the hallway entrance, and a collapsed northern end.
- **Ice shard rune object**: Thistle discovered a long, slender shard of ice the size of a dagger in a giant's meditation bag on the upper floor. A rune symbol glows within it. Its nature and purpose are unknown.
