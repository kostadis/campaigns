## NPC Activity

**Urgala** – Fought in the battle against fire giants, sustaining burn marks (likely from fighting magmin). Her inn, the Northshield House, burned to the ground along with the Apothecary and nearby cottages. She later met with the party at the Talking Troll, criticized their decision to fight the giants, and offered them a lead on a giant-slaying longsword owned by Harthos Zymorven at Zymorven Hall. She also revealed she had saved a map to an unexplored mine near where her wife died.

**Darz** – Former thief from Waterdeep. Found dead near the entrance of the campground during the battle. Later raised from the dead by Cindell Trueshot (paid for by Orsik). Upon recovery, he visited the party at the Talking Troll and provided intelligence on a wanted dwarf named Weevil, spotted cleaning stables in Xantharl's Keep, worth 5,000 gold to the Axe of Mirabar. He noted an old merchant friend from Mirabar had identified Weevil from a wanted poster near Longsaddle.

**Cindell Trueshot** – Elven or half-elven cleric and political rival to Darathra, running for mayor of Triboar. She arrived after the battle from her nearby vacation estate (described as a multi-room property with several acres). She performed Speak with Dead on the fire giant and raised Darz from the dead, offering a discount on the latter. She is connected to the local political divide between long-standing farming residents and Waterdavian newcomers. She expressed disdain for Darathra's leadership during the attack.

**Darathra (Lord Protector)** – Was killed during the battle and revived by Thistle's spell. She hesitated during combat before retreating into the tower. After the battle, she monitored the situation and later met with the party at the Talking Troll. She identified herself as a Harper (using the Harper hand gesture) and directed the party to contact Dral Thelev at Danivarr's House in Everlund, providing a platinum Triboar badge as credentials. She noted that fire giants reaching the Dessarin Valley undetected was a serious concern for her organization.

**Narth Tezerin** – Goods store owner. Present in the market square after the battle, visibly distressed about the destruction of the market and Urgala's inn.

**Othovir** – Harness store owner. Approached the party at the Talking Troll and, out of personal animosity toward the Margaster family, volunteered the location of a Margaster magic item stash: a carriage house on the grounds of a three-story Margaster-owned tower in Silverymoon. Provided specific details on the layout, security (arcane lock on the door, alarm spell on the carriage, two human guards upstairs), and the location of the hidden magic items (compartment under the passenger seat).

**Ghelryn** – A smithy who observed the party looting the fire giant's body. Informed Vardis that the giant's heart could be crafted into a living lamp capable of casting light in any room.

**The Dead Fire Giant (unnamed)** – Killed in combat. Body looted (ruby gem recovered; toenails harvested by Thistle for giant strength potions; heart removal attempted and failed). Subject to Speak with Dead. Revealed that Duke Zalto intends to rule over all giants, that the second fire giant was digging up a piece of the Vonidod (an artifact associated with killing dragons), and that Duke Zalto holds court in a throne room (location not revealed before the giant refused further answers). The giant also expressed hostility toward storm giants.

**The Second Fire Giant (unnamed)** – Present in the market area digging with a longsword, guided by a divining rod. Extracted a large 11-foot metal band (later identified as a piece of the Vonidod) and departed. Not engaged by the party on Darathra's orders.

**Duke Zalto** – Referenced but not present. Named by the dead fire giant as a fire giant leader seeking dominion over all giants.

**Harthos Zymorven** – Referenced but not present. Described by Urgala as a wealthy former adventuring companion, currently residing at Zymorven Hall near his ancestral keep on the Rauvin Road northwest of Silverymoon. Possesses a giant-slaying longsword and may part with it if approached with Urgala's name and a credible reason.

**Weevil** – Referenced but not present. A dwarf with an active wanted poster, worth 5,000 gold to the Axe of Mirabar. Last seen cleaning stables at Xantharl's Keep on the Long Road, spotted by an unnamed merchant from Mirabar traveling south through Longsaddle.

**Dral Thelev** – Referenced but not present. Described as a one-eyed half-orc proprietor of Danivarr's House in Everlund. Harper contact; Darathra directed the party to him.

**Kaelen** – Present at the Talking Troll serving drinks. No significant action beyond barkeep duties.

## Faction Movements

**The Harpers** – Darathra revealed her Harper affiliation during the post-battle meeting. She expressed organizational concern that fire giants penetrated the Dessarin Valley undetected, suggesting a lapse in Harper intelligence coverage. She activated a contact chain, directing the party toward Dral Thelev in Everlund as a reporting node.

**The Axe of Mirabar** – Referenced but not present. Has an active bounty (5,000 gold) on a dwarf named Weevil, with wanted posters distributed along the Long Road as far as Longsaddle.

**Duke Zalto's Fire Giants** – Executed a coordinated incursion into Triboar. One giant recovered a piece of the Vonidod from beneath the campground (guided by a divining rod). A second giant was killed. The Vonidod fragment has been successfully extracted and removed from Triboar by the surviving giant.

**The Margaster Family** – Referenced but not present. Othovir revealed they own a secured property in Silverymoon with hidden magic items. His willingness to expose the stash stems from a personal grudge against the family.

## Threat Arc Events

**Duke Zalto / Fire Giant Threat:**
- Fire giants conducted a deliberate, intelligence-guided operation in Triboar, using a divining rod to locate and successfully extract a piece of the Vonidod. This represents a completed mission objective for Zalto's forces.
- The dead giant confirmed Duke Zalto's overarching goal: dominion over all giants.
- A piece of the Vonidod (dragon-killing artifact) is now in Zalto's possession.

**The Vonidod (Artifact Threat):**
- Confirmed to exist in multiple pieces. One piece recovered from beneath Triboar's campground by fire giants.
- Artifact is associated with killing dragons. Reassembly implications are unknown but flagged as potentially catastrophic.

- **Duke Zalto** is a fire giant lord seeking to rule all giants. His forces conducted the Triboar raid specifically to recover a piece of the Vonidod.

- **The second fire giant** was digging under orders, not raiding for plunder. The 11-foot metal band it extracted is confirmed to be a Vonidod fragment.

- **Harthos Zymorven** possesses a giant-slaying longsword and is known to Urgala; he may be persuaded to part with it.
- **Weevil** is a wanted dwarf hiding in Xantharl's Keep, worth 5,000 gold to the Axe of Mirabar.
- **Darathra is a Harper**, using Triboar as a posting. She is concerned about giant movements going undetected.
- The local political landscape in Triboar is divided between long-standing farming families and Waterdavian newcomers, with both Darathra and Cindell Trueshot positioned as outsiders by different factions.
