[Scene 4] The Fire Giant Confrontation
Narrator: 
Focus: 

- The party approaches Duke Zalto and his four fire giant henchmen, presenting themselves as Iymrith's personal assistants.

- Thistle presents Zalto with items recovered from the fire giants' own private quarters during an earlier infiltration — framing them as gifts Iymrith had been "preserving" for him. Zalto is initially touched and responds warmly: "Iymrith, she always knows what I like."

- The party uses deception (rolling a 15) to imply that Iymrith has been gossiping to the other giants about Zalto's private tastes and is ashamed of him. This lands: "Has Iymrith been saying things about me?"

- Zalto grows increasingly suspicious and furious, eventually asking with wounded pride whether "this is why she gave me a small whip." His guards physically hold him back as he begins to curse.

- The party retreats quickly after successfully driving a wedge between Zalto and Iymrith.

- The party notes privately that the backfire — Zalto's initial warm response to Iymrith's "gift" — briefly threatened to make the situation worse before the deception landed.
