[Scene 2] The Rescue Plan and Rehearsal
Narrator: 
Focus: 

- Thistle uses his unnatural good perception to confirm no one is watching before explaining the plan to Laela.

- Thistle explains the plan: a body double named Bob has been disguised via Polymorph to look exactly like Laela; when the moment is right, they will swap and escape while the crowd watches the stage.

- Laela's initial reaction is somewhere between laughter and tears; Vardis, not present for the plan briefing, notes privately: "Of all the plans that have ever been, that's one of them."

- Laela lays out the full and alarming guest list: the five heads of the chromatic dragon families, fire giants, cloud giants, storm giants, the Red Wizards of Thay, and Prince Thornacious. The storm giants are confirmed to also be among the expected attendees.

- Laela warns that the guests will conduct detect magic inspections before the show; Thistle and the party plan the swap to occur after inspections are complete, as the show begins.

- Laela notices Thistle's dragon eyes and demands to know what happened to him. Thistle responds: "I'm not a freak. Iymrith did this to me." Laela is outraged on his behalf: "I want her dead." Then she calms: "There's something to be said for survival."

- Orsik coaches Iymrith on her monologue, directing her to lower her voice, speak faster, and sound more natural. After a stilted first attempt, Iymrith delivers the speech a second time with noticeably better confidence. She declares Orsik "a genius": "This is going to be awesome."

- Iymrith, after the successful rehearsal, notes: "I'm really going to be disappointed if I have to kill you. But it will have been a good monologue. Just one last monologue."

- Orsik secures Iymrith's permission for the party to meet the arriving guests, arguing — as he had apparently argued before — that artistic freedom requires it and that he cannot always explain why. Iymrith concedes: "All right. All right."

- The party discusses meeting guests as an opportunity for later persuasion; Orsik notes he wants to meet them all, while Vardis offers to cover other ground.
