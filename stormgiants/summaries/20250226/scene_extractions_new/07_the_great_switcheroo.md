# The Great Switcheroo

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party executes their plan to swap Laela for Bob the demon, leading to a chaotic and humiliating revelation for Iymrith.
- Thistle returns below to retrieve the real Laela while Bob — disguised as Laela — is brought up to the stage area. Bob's timing is coordinated with the party: Iymrith snaps her fingers and commands "Turn into a dragon!"
- Bob responds by telling terrible dad jokes, beginning with: "I just found out that I'm colorblind. The news came out in polymorph."
- The party, watching from the crowd, plays along — laughing and encouraging, calling out "That was good, Laela, that was good!"
- Iymrith grows increasingly furious: "If I don't see a dragon in five seconds—"
- Unla Key briefly considers casting Polymorph to actually turn Bob into a dragon, to buy more distraction time; the party debates but ultimately defers to Bob's plan.
- Bob drops his disguise with a full flourish, revealing himself as a demon and announcing: "THE JOKE'S ON YOU, DOOM OF THE DESERT! YOU THOUGHT YOU HAD LAELA — YOU HAD A DEMON!" He proceeds to explain, at length, how this was all part of an elaborate practical joke designed to publicly humiliate Iymrith in front of her most powerful allies.
- Iymrith is described as completely blindsided: "Iymrith at this point is like, what the—"
- The party immediately joins the crowd in booing, calling for rotten vegetables and "mean popcorn" to throw at Iymrith, working the room into a full frenzy of humiliation.
- The real Laela escapes through an alternate exit while all eyes are on the stage; Thistle gives her his cloak for added concealment.
