## Consistency Notes

The following issues from the consistency report could not be silently resolved in the text and are flagged here for GM verification.

**Issue 1 — Iymrith's threat: Thistle or Vardis?**
The Summary and NPCs section attribute Iymrith's "I will protect you from dragons myself" threat to Thistle. The Scenes section attributes the same threat to Vardis — as a direct response to his joke ("Can it be me?") and a reference to "his goals in life," which aligns with Vardis's known death-seeking theology. These two accounts directly contradict each other. Based on internal logic, the threat almost certainly belongs to Vardis. If both characters received variant versions, both should be documented separately. GM should confirm and the Summary/NPCs entry for Iymrith should be corrected accordingly.

**Issue 2 — Prince Thornacious: Unseelie/Gloaming Court affiliation omitted**
The recap describes Thornacious only as "Fae royalty" attending as a guest. The campaign state confirms he is the Unseelie/Gloaming Court's representative at the Grand Reveal and is barred from the Feywild — context that reframes his prior cooperation with the party. This detail is absent from the NPC entry and may mislead future sessions into treating him as generically neutral or Seelie-aligned. GM should confirm whether to add the Unseelie affiliation and the Feywild ban to the permanent record now.

**Issue 3 — Five Silverymoon battle mages: absent without acknowledgment**
The party's active coordination with five Silverymoon battle mages (part of the King Hekaton assault plan) is never mentioned in the session. The campaign state confirms they had not rendezvoused with the party in time and are expected in a subsequent session. Their absence is not flagged anywhere in the recap, which could be mistaken for a dropped thread. A brief note confirming they did not arrive in time for the Grand Reveal should be added to the Chaos at the Amphitheater scene or carried into the next session document.

**Issue 4 — Bob's full name: "Bob" vs. "Bob Saget"**
The demon ally is called "Bob" throughout this recap but "Bob Saget" in the campaign state (Collective Resources, Unla Key relationships). This inconsistency should be standardized. Recommend either using "Bob Saget" throughout to match the campaign state, or explicitly noting in the Bob NPC entry that "Bob" is short for "Bob Saget." Choose one form and apply it consistently going forward.

**Issue 5 — Storm giants: Hekaton's coordinated forces or independent guests?**
The recap presents the storm giants as attending Iymrith's event and being persuaded to fight by Orsik's speech. The campaign state lists coordinating an assault with King Hekaton's storm giant forces as an active party quest. It is unclear whether the storm giants present are Hekaton's coordinated allies (in which case the quest is resolved or partially resolved) or an independent faction who happened to be invited guests (in which case Hekaton's forces remain unaccounted for). GM should clarify this distinction; the Storm Giant King NPC entry and Chaos scene should be updated accordingly.

**Issue 7 — Giant guest list: frost giants and stone giants**
The Summary lists five types of giant representatives (frost, fire, stone, cloud, storm) as part of Laela's warning to the party. The Scenes section covering the same conversation lists only three (fire, cloud, storm), and the Chaos scene references only those same three as active factions during the battle. Frost giants and stone giants appear in the Summary but nowhere else. GM should confirm whether they were present and simply unaddressed in play, or whether the Summary's list is an error drawn from Laela's anticipated guest list rather than confirmed attendees. If absent, they should be removed from the Summary.

The campaign state lists "Adimond Kroskas" as a secondary name for Iymrith (likely a humanoid alias). This name does not appear anywhere in the recap or the Iymrith NPC entry.
