# The Battle of the Ancient Dragons Begins

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party and their giant allies engage the ancient blue dragon Iymrith and her minions in a massive cavern.
- The initiative order is established; Iymrith has a +16 initiative bonus — the DM notes she "didn't survive this long by being your average dragon." She rolls high and acts early.
- Iymrith opens by moving across the vast battlefield. She attempts to use her lightning breath but cannot line up a hit on Hekaton, so instead opts to rend the Balor physically — landing three hits for a combined 87 points of slashing and lightning damage (later revised upward with an additional ability for a total near 94 points). She then turns invisible.
- The DM clarifies the damage breakdown: 18 points from slashing, plus 11 from lightning, plus 25 from an additional ability.
- Serissa the Storm Giant charges toward the battle; she and her kin (Storm Giants 1 and 5) begin moving across the chamber.
- Orsik summons an Air Elemental via his Cube of Summoning (rolling a 5, selecting "dealer's choice," landing on Air Elemental after party discussion). The elemental is positioned near Iymrith.
- Gargoyles manning a trebuchet fire upon the Balor, hitting for 44 points of damage — but Bob takes only half due to his resistances, for 22 points of damage.
- Hekaton advances and destroys two gargoyles with a lightning blast, incapacitating one trebuchet, then moves 40 feet toward the fight.
- Dragon priests channel healing magic into Iymrith — one Archpriest among them heals her for 10 hit points.
- Storm giants continue to clear gargoyles from the battlefield, eliminating the remaining ones over the course of the round. The DM eulogizes: "The gargoyles are all dead. We barely knew you."
- The five red mages are added to the initiative order (rolling a 17 with a +2 bonus) after being left off the tracker initially. They begin moving up behind the party.
- Iymrith attacks the Balor with her main action: she hits Bob twice for a combined 63 points of damage.
- Orsik advances 100 feet (using Dash as a bonus action) to close the enormous distance across the cavern. He directs the Air Elemental to move 90 feet into flanking position.
- Hekaton hurls a boulder (the DM notes his stat block oddly lists a "Ballista" attack, describing it as "a weird set of attacks for him"), dealing 35 points of damage to Iymrith.
- Dragon priests channel a combined healing of 33 hit points into Iymrith.
- Giants 2 and 3 advance and throw boulders — one hits for 33 points of damage, one misses.
- Unla Key, always in Pegasus form, has a fly speed of 90 feet and maneuvers out of the central danger zone, repositioning to find a vantage point above the fray.
- Orsik's Air Elemental attacks Iymrith but misses. Iymrith takes 30 points of passive fire damage from Bob's aura.
- The DM notes that the other chromatic dragons present in the chamber are advancing to attack each other and do not consider the party important enough to target — "They don't think you're important enough to kill" — buying the party a temporary reprieve.
- Vardis books it 70 feet across the chamber, moving out of the central danger zone and toward the fight.
- Thistle moves 70 feet upward from the lower passage and into the main battle area. The DM moves the mages 60 feet forward simultaneously.
