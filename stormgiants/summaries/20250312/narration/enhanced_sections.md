## Memorable Moments

**Bob the Balor, after absorbing blow after blow from Iymrith's claws, tail, and even a trebuchet, is finally struck down by the dragon's legendary action and vanishes in a puff of smoke.**

*The party watches their chaotic demon ally fall after just a few rounds of combat, with Orsik lamenting his swift demise.*

> "All that time on the astral plane, and he's just — you know, not gonna make it, not gonna make it to a third round. It gets 12 seconds of combat. All that time, Bob."
> — Orsik

**Five mages unleash a simultaneous barrage of Fireballs at Iymrith, burning through three of her remaining legendary resistances in a single coordinated volley.**

*The party orchestrates a mass magical assault to strip the ancient dragon of her defenses, clearing the way for more powerful spells. Orsik directs the mages with a simple command: "They all cast Fireball. Every single one of them." Iymrith saves against two of the five normally; the other three burn her remaining resistances.*

**Iymrith unleashes her lightning breath in a devastating arc, instantly destroying the summoned Air Elemental and nearly killing Orsik in a single blast.**

*The dragon retaliates after being stripped of her legendary resistances, dealing 103 points of damage. Orsik needed a natural 20 on a DC 23 Dexterity save to halve it — and did not get one.*

> "Now we don't have to deal with any consequences of lying or swindling him."
> — Vardis, remarked with dark humor immediately after Bob the Balor was struck down, noting the silver lining of the demon's death.

**Thistle's beloved Laela bids him a tearful farewell before fleeing through the side exit, acknowledging that his willingness to fight above will likely be the key to her survival.**

*A bittersweet parting as Thistle chooses to charge into a dragon battle rather than escape with the woman he came to rescue. His persuasion roll to bring her along was a 3; his later insight check to read her sincerity was a 26.*

> "You've come so far to rescue me. Why don't you go one step further?"
> — Laela, persuading Thistle to fight upstairs so that his presence in the battle would serve as cover for her escape.

**Serissa the storm giant scores a critical hit on Iymrith with a massive boulder, dealing 80 points of damage in a single thunderous throw.**

*A highlight of the giants' contribution to the battle, demonstrating the raw power of the storm giant allies. The DM confirmed it was a natural 20.*

**Orsik shouts at Bob the Balor to press his attack on Iymrith, rolling a 13 on his persuasion check.**

> "KILL HER! KILL HER!"
> — Orsik, screaming at the enraged Balor to focus his fury on the dragon rather than on the party.

**Thistle attempts to sell Laela on charging directly into the dragon battle with him, rolling a 3 on his persuasion check.**

*Laela is visibly unimpressed. She gives him what the DM describes as the "I don't trust you" look before announcing her own counter-plan — one that Thistle is in no position to argue with.*

> "We're gonna go up there where the big battle is going to occur? Is that your plan?"
> — Laela, incredulous at Thistle's opening pitch.

> "One-dimensional chess."
> — Thistle, defending his strategy.

> "Chess is on a two-dimensional board. It's one-dimensional chess."
> — The DM, correcting him.

**Vardis drops Blade Barrier to learn Hero's Feast, having never once cast the spell she is replacing.**

*A quietly revealing moment of practical self-assessment. When pressed, Vardis confirms she has used Sunbeam — and keeps it — but Blade Barrier has gone untouched across the entire campaign.*

> "I have never used [Blade Barrier] and I never will."
> — Vardis

**Orsik eulogizes Bob with unexpected philosophical generosity, reframing the Balor's destruction as a commute rather than a death.**

*After Vardis jokes about the silver lining of Bob's demise, Orsik offers the most charitable possible reading of his ally's fate.*

> "He's not really dead, right? He's just at home. This was his, like, bus ride home."
> — Orsik
