## World Events

- The heroes returned to Parnast exhausted on the 4th day of the 1st Tenday of Mirtul.
- Orsik arranged for the captain of the guard to feed the Wyvern a thigh bone.
- Villagers and a militia corporal reported a horde of orcs approaching from the south; Orsik led his men in a flanking charge but found no orcs.
- Thistle assessed the witnesses as truthful — the orcs were real but had disappeared.
- Orsik and all companions simultaneously received a mental image of an ornate door covered in Shou writing appearing in the wall of the Golden Tankard.
- Orsik received a second mental image suggesting he open the door; he rejected the suggestion.
- Unla Kee opened the ornate door; a small white bird flew out, followed by the sound of hundreds of birds from inside.
- Hsing, the golden blind pseudodragon, beckoned Orsik to enter; he refused until social pressure from watching villagers and militia changed his mind.
- The party (and militia) entered the Seer's extradimensional octagonal room containing scrolls, books, birds, a large desk, and a map of the Sword Coast held down by marble statues of Shou gods.
- The party passed through double doors into a garden, where they met the Seer.
- The Seer demonstrated her intelligence network by revealing personal information about Unla Kee (Uncle Joon in Triboar; nemesis Adirond Kroskas disappeared into the North; beloved Laela rumored alive and captured by allies of Fire Giants) and Thistle.
- The Seer privately whispered something to Orsik about his past; Orsik silenced her and denied its relevance.
- The Seer revealed the 30-year history of her organization's pursuit of Narcellia Varam'myr and the full chain of manipulations that inadvertently made Parnast a target.
- The Seer revealed that the magical tomes from the Marsh of Chelimber provided a ritual that allowed her organization to scry out the headband's owner, identifying Bad Fruul.
- The Seer tasked the party with protecting Chandra Stol, determining when Bad Fruul will attack, defending Parnast, and retrieving the headband; she offered 600 gp base pay plus bonuses.
- The Seer explained the dissolution of the Ordning and its consequences for giant civilization.
- The Seer gave Unla Kee the book *Prophecies of the Apocalypse* as the party departed.
- The party exited the Seer's space; the door dematerialized behind them.
- The party debated defensive strategy; Vardis raised the option of old Parnast (former Zhentarim fortress) as more defensible; Orsik rejected it due to local superstition about a curse.
- Orsik ordered the militia to fortify the current town: dig trenches, build pickets, fortify buildings, and construct traps and pits.
- Orsik briefed Chandra Stol at the Temple of Axes, revealing everything the Seer had said.
- Chandra confirmed the history of Narcellia, revealed that Narcellia killed her mother 30 years ago, described her own period of hiding, and identified Narcellia's fear of fey and sprites as a tactical weakness.
- Chandra initially refused protection; Thistle obtained herbs from Syla's garden, applied them to Orsik, and persuaded her through earnest gesture.
- Unla Kee's declaration that refusal would mean "the end of the world" convinced Chandra to join the group.
- Sylas provided the party with a recipe for an antidote against the blue mists of Weathercote Forest; Unla Kee synthesized enough for four members; Orsik volunteered to go without.
- The party estimated Bad Fruul's most likely attack date as the 8th day of the 1st Tenday of Mirtul (4 days hence).
- Thistle led the group quickly to Prince Thornacious' camp in Weathercote Forest.
- Thistle pitched the request for fey assistance; initial response was positive from Blasius.
- Thistle addressed Oblivillish as a "hunting master," causing a diplomatic incident; Oblivillish denied being a hunter and referenced the prior stag/Unseelie controversy; Prince Thornacious asked Oblivillish directly if he was a hunter; Oblivillish denied it; Thistle was silenced.
- Prince Thornacious decided to send Blasius and two sprites to help fight Bad Fruul.
- Chandra asked to remain with the fey for protection, wounding Orsik's pride.
