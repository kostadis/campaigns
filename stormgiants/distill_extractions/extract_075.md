## Threads & Mysteries

- **Sandesyl remains alive and in her lair**: The party has not yet confronted her; the assault is imminent but she is forewarned by her defensive posture
- **Glazhael's deliberate withdrawal**: Thistle observed that Glazhael seemed to want the party to approach Sandesyl's tower; her motives for this behavior are unexplained — is she manipulating the party, clearing the field, or acting under other instruction?
- **The Flying Kenku**: Brunvild personally encountered a Kenku who flew and who defeated the Cult of the Dragon. Thistle knows Kenkus cannot fly and believed this figure to be a legend. The identity and nature of this individual are unresolved. Thistle wanted to ask more but was prevented by the guard drakes moving
- **Bob trapped in the Astral Plane**: Circumstances of his imprisonment are not explained; the party chose to leave him there; his eventual release or escape is unaddressed
- **Blagothkus's account vs. Escarlotta's account**: Blagothkus explicitly noted that Escarlotta has a different version of the events surrounding Brunvild, the castle, and his role; whose account is accurate is unresolved
- **The dwarves Brunvild asked about**: Brunvild asked if the party had seen dwarves he trusted who were associated with his group; Thistle deflected by suggesting they may have escaped; their actual fate (implied to be the cadavers thrown over the Evermoors) has not been disclosed to Brunvild
- **Sammaster learning from Inferno**: The statue tableau depicting Sammaster apparently learning the secrets of undeath from Inferno raises questions about the true origin of Dracolich lore and the Cult of the Dragon's founding knowledge
- **Unla Kee's withheld Haste spell**: Unla Kee admitted withholding Haste because "it would be too easy"; the extent of his other withheld capabilities is unknown
- **Thistle's Dragon Eyes**: Referenced as a source of power (used for scanning/detection); their full capabilities and origin (experimentation by a mad dragon) have not been fully explored in this session
- **Brunvild as a potential ongoing ally**: His current disposition after being freed/roused; whether he survives acting as a distraction; whether he will assist the party beyond the immediate distraction role
- **The ghost and its obsession**: The ghost supervising the orc zombies has kept them confined for two years; its origin, identity, and relationship to Sandesyl's experiments are not addressed
