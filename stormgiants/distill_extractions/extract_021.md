## Threads & Mysteries

- **The statue of Agharradh**: Bad Fruul demands it along with Chandra. Its nature, location, and significance are not explained in this chunk.
- **Chandra's identity and lineage**: She is identified as being from Evereska and the offspring of Luistarra (described as "horrid"). Why Bad Fruul specifically wants her is not explained.
- **Luistarra**: Chandra's mother; described negatively by Bad Fruul's herald. Her current whereabouts, nature, and role in the larger conflict are unknown.
- **Bad Fruul as a drow**: Thistle identifies him as a drow, but he appears as a giant-sized figure in plate armor. The nature of this (disguise, drow giant, transformation, illusion) is unresolved.
- **Kurglow's response to the message**: A message was sent threatening harm to Kurglow's son to persuade him to abandon Bad Fruul. Whether it will succeed is unresolved.
- **Bad Fruul's spies in Parnast**: Bad Fruul had spies who gave him false intelligence about the militia's training. The identity of these spies within Parnast is unresolved.
- **The wyvern**: Present but described as uncontrollable. Whether the group will find a way to use it or whether it will become a threat is unresolved.
- **SEER's book and the blood ritual story**: Unla Kee read a story about seven warriors and villagers who all die and whose combined blood summons an extra-planar power. He withheld this from the group. Whether this foreshadows the outcome of the battle is unresolved.
- **The miniature silver dragon skull**: Found in the Red Fang orcs' chest. Its significance, origin, and purpose are unknown.
- **The dead flayed elf and elven skin at the Yurtrus shrine**: A flayed elf was found in the orc shrine. The identity of the elf, why they were targeted, and the purpose of the brewing concoction are unresolved.
- **The Lionel Coster and KP Trade Equilibrium Theory**: Referenced as existing across multiple universes with violent resistance from local merchants. The broader relevance to the campaign is unresolved.
- **Jarlor and Larrish's spy channel**: The nature and full extent of this communication network (apparently connected to the Zhentarim) is not fully explained.
- **Ongoing battle**: The ogres have breached the wall and a third wave (more ogres with an owlbear) is incoming. The outcome of the defense of Parnast is unresolved.
