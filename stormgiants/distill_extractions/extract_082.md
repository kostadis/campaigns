## World Events

- Thistle recalls a past mission where his group visited Everlund, met Mary of the Fighters of the Light, and were sent on a quest to the Nether Mountains in lieu of paying a five-gold admission fee.
- During the Nether Mountains expedition, the group encountered legends of lost Netherese magic; Uncle Joon noted orcs in the mountains and blue dragons in the eastern peaks, driven out of the Anauroch desert by the Doom of the Desert (Iymrith).
- Thistle retroactively identifies the "Doom of the Desert" as Adimond Kroskas / Iymrith.
- Unla Kee recalls being taught at the Nirany School that Krakens communicate via telepathy at up to 100 feet range.
- Thistle articulates a full conspiracy theory: Iymrith funded fairy-capturing experiments using wealth from Tiamat's war, used the corrupted Oracle of Amman to announce the sundering of the Ordning and start a Giant war, killed Nerissa to provoke Hekaton, kidnapped Hekaton when provocation failed, and installed herself with Queen Serissa using Hekaton's two sisters as intermediaries, with the Kraken Society serving as rebranded Cult of the Dragon muscle.
- The group collectively accepts Thistle's conspiracy theory.
- The group decides to bypass Everlund and sail directly to the Northern Isles of the Trackless Sea.
- Pow hires ten Shou laborers for castle cleanup and demolition at approximately six silver pieces per laborer plus supplies, estimated total around 1,000 gold.
- Pow proposes a 500,000 gold full casino renovation or a 50,000 gold serviceable version.
- The theater in Yartar (or associated location) burned down; Bob took a leave of absence rather than disappearing as rumored.
- Thistle confirms the demon-summoning play script was saved; Pow expresses interest in using it to attract crowds.
- Thistle arranges for Escarlotta's tower to be cleaned and for gold trim and a nameplate to be added to the door.
- The ship departs for the Northern Isles of the Trackless Sea, with Escarlotta setting the course.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Iymrith's location and current activities**: The group has identified Iymrith as the mastermind but does not know where she is or what she is currently doing.
- **Hekaton's whereabouts**: King Hekaton is hidden on an untraceable ship; his rescue is presumably a goal but not yet enacted.
- **Whether the Kraken Society is affiliated with a real Kraken**: Unla Kee and Thistle both raise this question; it remains unresolved — the society may worship or be manipulated by an actual Kraken, or may only use the name.
- **The SEER**: Unla Kee suggested consulting the SEER; Vardis and Orsik shut this down without full explanation — the reason for avoiding the SEER is unexplained.
- **The demon-summoning play script**: The script is in the group's possession; Pow wants to use it for the casino; Vardis flagged it as a real summoning ritual that was "barely averted" — its danger remains unresolved.
- **Bob's leave of absence**: Bob's true whereabouts and activities during his leave are unknown.
- **Loyalty of Pow's Shou crew**: Thistle noted that all hired laborers appear to be from Shou and wondered to whom they are personally loyal — this is unresolved.
- **Hekaton's two sisters**: Their identities, current location, and role in the conspiracy (allegedly promising Iymrith the throne in exchange for delivering Serissa) are not fully established.
- **Queen Serissa's agreement with Iymrith**: The nature and scope of what Serissa agreed to do for Iymrith in exchange for support is unspecified.
- **Escarlotta's control gems**: Her control gems pulsated red; the full nature of her construction, who controls her, and what the gems indicate beyond blushing is unexplored.
- **Casino funding**: The 50,000–500,000 gold renovation plan has been discussed but not funded or fully agreed upon.
- **The fairy-dragon hybrid experiments**: Iymrith's experiments on fairies (which gave Thistle his dragon eyes) are ongoing or planned; their full scope and current status are unknown.
