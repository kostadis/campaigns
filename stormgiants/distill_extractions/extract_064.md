## World Events

- The party devised a plan to infiltrate Ironslag using the moving cart system on the upper level to reach the elevator shaft.
- Thistle prematurely broke the wheel in the wheel room before the party had cleared the bucket chain, stranding them mid-route.
- The party improvised a new route: jumping bucket to bucket across the Vodinoid chamber, dropping to the lower level, and proceeding to the elevator room.
- The party entered the dining hall past goblins setting tables by bluffing/ignoring them.
- Orsik opened a massive north door via feat of strength with Thistle manipulating the lever.
- The party entered Duke Zalto and the Duchess's bedroom.
- Unla Kee cast Knock on the large iron chest beneath the basalt table, triggering a trapped incendiary fire blast that killed Unla Kee and knocked Vardis prone.
- The party retrieved the conch, an adamantine crown with six black opals, thousands of gold pieces, a javelin of lightning, a wand of the warmage, adamantine-scale armor, and a ring of jumping from the chest.
- Thistle attempted to collect additional treasure (approximately 5,000 gp worth) from the basalt table; Orsik refused and the party departed without it.
- The party passed back through the dining hall goblins, took the elevator to the top of Ironslag, and Orsik opened the massive iron portcullis to the Yak Folk village.
- The party climbed back into the cloud castle via rope; Orsik noted the zombies are still present outside the walls.
- The party reported to Escarlotta in the control tower; Orsik confirmed the Duchess is dead.
- Escarlotta declared her next target is Sansuri.
- The party debated whether to fight Bob Saget in Silverymoon, walk away, or frame House Margaster; Thistle proposed a cover story presenting themselves as emissaries of Everlund come to deal with a demon that killed Bob Saget.
- Orsik tentatively resolved to kill Bob Saget, citing reputational concerns.
