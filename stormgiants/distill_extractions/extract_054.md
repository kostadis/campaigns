## World Events

- On the 5th day of the 2nd tenday of Eleasis 1492, the 3HPP located a hidden object beneath a weather-worn stone at Flint Rock using detect magic, which worked because the stone had eroded to just under one foot thick.
- Thistle used Speak with Animals to communicate with two giant elk (Harry and Mary) at Flint Rock, learning there was nothing dangerous in the area.
- Harry and Mary pushed the large stone aside at Flint Rock to help the party dig, motivated by wanting the party gone sooner.
- The 3HPP recovered a mithral spear tip weighing 75 pounds from beneath the stone at Flint Rock.
- The 3HPP traveled aboard the Dragon's Breath to Morgur's Mound, arriving on the 6th day of the 2nd tenday of Eleasis.
- Bressik briefed the party on Morgur's Mound using a map and notes sourced from Candlekeep.
- Orsik and Vardis dug at Morgur's Mound for multiple consecutive days (6th through 10th) while Thistle and Unla Kee stood watch.
- On the 7th day, Orsik and Vardis found an abjurer's bangle beneath Morgur's Mound; it was given to Unla Kee.
- On the 8th day, Orsik and Vardis found a Helm of Comprehend Languages at the creature's head section of Morgur's Mound; it was given to Delsephine.
- On the 9th day, nothing was found.
- On the 10th day, Orsik found a gold-plated fire giant's tooth, a nonmagical ancient giant relic small enough to fit in a human hand.
- Upon Orsik's discovery of the tooth, four animated thunderbeast ankylosaurus skeletons erupted from Morgur's Mound around the dig hole.
- Thistle shot two arrows into one skeleton; Orsik attacked another; Thuul dashed toward the skeletons.
- Three skeletons attacked: Orsik avoided damage via armor and skill; Vardis distracted his attacker with warding flare; Unla Kee was bitten.
- Vardis cast a fireball, destroying the skeleton Orsik had injured and wounding two more.
- Unla Kee used his artifact (rock) to produce a gust of wind, pushing one skeleton into the dig pit.
- Orsik struck one skeleton twice; Thistle impaled another with two arrows.
- Unla Kee maintained wind from the artifact to keep the pit skeleton from climbing out.
- Vardis struck down one skeleton.
- Unla Kee used a second gust of wind to push another skeleton away from the party; it fell into the pit.
- Unla Kee cast a fireball into the pit, destroying the trapped skeletons.
- The 3HPP reboarded the Dragon's Breath; Delsephine informed them the remaining search locations are Raven's Rock and the Great Wyrm Cavern.
- Vardis directed Delsephine to take them to Raven's Rock next.

- What it is: An outdoor location featuring a large, weather-worn stone beneath which a hidden object was buried
- What happened there: The 3HPP detected and excavated a mithral spear tip (75 pounds) from beneath the stone; Harry and Mary (giant elk) pushed the stone aside to assist; Thistle spoke with Harry and Mary via animal speech
- Current state: Stone moved; excavation complete; party has departed

**Morgur's Mound**
- What it is: The spirit mound of the lost Thunderbeast tribe, located in the Crags; shaped like a long-necked, wingless dragon (the Uthgardt impression of a thunderbeast); has a ridge of dragon bones along its head, neck, back, and tail; surrounded by enormous cairns within a quarter-mile radius, beneath which lie bones of revered Thunderbeast warriors
- Named after: Morgur, Uthgar's brother
- What happened there: The 3HPP dug for five days; recovered an abjurer's bangle, a Helm of Comprehend Languages, and a gold-plated fire giant's tooth; four animated thunderbeast ankylosaurus skeletons attacked and were destroyed; multiple dig sites from previous expeditions already present
- Current state: Actively excavated; skeletons destroyed; party has departed; artifact not found here
