## World Events

- Lord Khaspere Drylund was already dead at the start of this session; his body remained in the sewer chamber.
- The party engaged four Kuo-Toans and two Merrows in a flooded circular sewer chamber.
- A Kuo-Toan net attack on Orsik missed.
- Thule rushed past a Kuo-Toan guarding a door and used his breath weapon on it.
- Thistle cast a lightning arrow, hitting two Kuo-Toans on a ramp.
- Vardis used the mace of terror, frightening three of the four Kuo-Toans into the water.
- The two Merrows simultaneously opened valves, flooding the chamber with water from two eastern pipes.
- Unla Kee used telekinesis to open a drainage valve, partially slowing the flood.
- Orsik used his ring of swimming, jumped in, reduced the southern Merrow to 5 HP, and closed the southern valve.
- Oosith revealed himself as an aboleth and dominated Unla Kee.
- Oosith attempted to attack Thistle but was blinded by Vardis's light cleric powers.
- The three frightened Kuo-Toans fled through an open drainage hole.
- Thistle struck the northern Merrow; the damage broke the aboleth's domination of the Merrow.
- The aboleth, seeing the tide turning, chose to flee.
- Vardis opened another drainage valve, lowering the water level.
- The remaining Kuo-Toan Whip closed the drainage valve Unla Kee had previously opened.
- The aboleth commanded the dominated Unla Kee to "Destroy them!"
- Unla Kee used the wand of wonder and became invisible instead of attacking.
- The aboleth shrieked "What the hell did you just do?!" and then "The Kraken will have its revenge!" before fleeing.
- Orsik swam to and killed the last Kuo-Toan Whip.
- The party discussed raising Drylund; Vardis determined it was impossible (brain missing); True Resurrection was ruled out at 50,000 gp cost.
- Thistle searched Drylund's body, finding a ring with keys, 120 gp, and three platinum pieces.
- Thuul picked up Drylund's body and dropped it in the room; Thistle covered it and locked the door.
- The party emerged from the sewers into sunlight.
- The party discussed filling the power vacuum in Yartar and various plans for Lady Atalia, Pow, and the casino.
- The party met Lady Atalia at a fashionable cafe; she was furious to learn the aboleth had not been killed and walked out.
- A group of female rogues (the Hands) stopped the 3HPP in the street, claimed Lady Atalia's protection, and after being told about "Oosith the sorcerer," agreed to follow the party instead.
- The party approached Lady Atalia's house; she confronted them in a dock accent, revealed her fear of the Kraken's retribution, warned Drylund might "come back," and closed the door on them.
- One of the Hands offered the party a deal: 95% of Grand Dame turnover plus an annual security fee, in exchange for protection.
- The party proceeded to the docks and boarded the Grand Dame.
- Orsik explained the flying castle casino plan to Pow; Pow enthusiastically agreed.
- Unla Kee was granted naming rights; the casino was named *Kee and Wing Casino*.
- Thistle flew to Drylund's room on the Grand Dame; found no correspondence or incriminating paperwork; found an ornately decorated weapon with an octopus pommel.
- Thistle remembered the Everlund woman's warning about the Kraken and the Fighters of the Light.
