## Factions

**Zhentarim**
- Current goals: Maintaining and securing trade along the Black Road; recruiting useful agents; addressing the goblinoid threat to commerce
- Recent actions: Sent Fzoul Slovis on an intelligence-gathering journey along the Black Road from Parnast; paid a hobgoblin toll (15 GP per camel) to avoid a fight; filed internal reports on road conditions
- Relationships: Operate along the Black Road; threatened by goblinoid raiders; interested in recruiting Orsik
- Key members: Fzoul Slovis (operative/scout/reporter); Zhentarim leadership (unnamed, recipients of Fzoul's reports)
- Internal tensions: Fzoul Slovis has repeatedly urged leadership to take a firmer, systematic stance on the goblinoid threat; believes piecemeal attempts have emboldened goblins; leadership has not yet acted on these recommendations

**Goblinoid Raiders (Goblins, Hobgoblins, Bugbears)**
- Current goals: Extorting and raiding travelers on the Black Road; controlling toll points
- Recent actions: Hobgoblins established a toll point on the edge of the desert near the Greypeaks, charging 15 GP per camel; a goblin pack attacked Azam's caravan at night seeking goods and water (repelled by Thistle with casualties); separate goblin attacks reported on other caravans near Vuerthyl
- Relationships: Antagonistic to Zhentarim trade operations and independent caravans
- Key members: Unnamed hobgoblin toll operators; unnamed goblin raiding boss (deceased)
- Notes: Fzoul Slovis distinguishes between the hobgoblins' more sophisticated toll operation and the less sophisticated goblin raiding attacks; activity increasing along the Black Road and up to the Greypeaks

## World Events

- Azam organized a four-wagon caravan departing for Parnast, carrying food, water, medicinal herbs, weapons, and a statue of Angharradgh.
- Azam hired an independent adventuring party (Thistle, Orsik, Vardis, Unla Kee) as caravan guards; the party arrived in Vuethel exhausted and sunburned.
- Thistle proposed taking the night watch and sleeping during the day, which was adopted.
- During the night, a goblin raiding party attacked the caravan seeking goods and water.
- Thistle spotted the goblins before they could steal anything and killed two goblins and the goblin boss (and its mount).
- The remaining two goblins fled; Thistle argued for pursuit but Vardis objected, and pursuit did not occur.
- The rest of the night passed without further incident; the caravan continued its journey.
- Fzoul Slovis traveled the Black Road from Parnast and observed a hobgoblin toll operation at the edge of the desert; his party paid 15 GP per camel and did not engage.
- Fzoul Slovis encountered a caravan near Vuerthyl that had been attacked by goblins.
- Fzoul Slovis filed an intelligence report dated 3/1 of Taraskh recommending systematic extermination of the goblin threat and flagging Orsik for Zhentarim recruitment.

## Locations

**Black Road**
- What it is: A major desert trade road connecting at minimum Vuethel/Vuerthyl and Parnast, running toward the Greypeaks
- Current state: Increasingly dangerous; hobgoblin toll operations active at the edge of the desert; goblin raiding parties attacking caravans; travel is slow and hazardous due to both banditry and harsh desert conditions

**Parnast**
- What it is: A destination town/settlement accessible via the Black Road
- Current state: Referenced as the destination of Azam's caravan and the origin point of Fzoul Slovis's return journey; no further details given

**Vuethel / Vuerthyl**
- What it is: A settlement at or near the start of the Black Road desert section; appears to be where Azam's caravan originated and where the adventurers were recruited
- Current state: Referenced as the point where the adventurers joined the caravan; a separate caravan near here was also attacked by goblins (per Fzoul's report)
- Note: Spelled "Vuethel" in Azam's journal and "Vuerthyl" in Fzoul's report — likely the same location

**Greypeaks**
- What it is: A mountain range or geographic feature at the far end of the Black Road from the desert
- Current state: Referenced as the upper boundary of increased goblinoid activity; no further detail given

**Hobgoblin Toll Point**
- What it is: An improvised toll checkpoint manned by hobgoblins, located on the edge of the desert along the Black Road
- Current state: Active and operational; hobgoblins described as decently armed and armored and too numerous to fight; location documented by Fzoul Slovis and forwarded to Zhentarim leadership with a recommendation to deal with it

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Thistle's revenge:** Thistle is described as "bent on revenge" — the target, reason, and nature of this revenge are entirely unrevealed.
- **Orsik's past:** Orsik has done unspecified things that made him unemployable as a guard; the nature of these actions is unknown.
- **Vardis and the temples:** Vardis's fellow clerics will not allow him into their temples; the reason is unrevealed.
- **Zhentarim recruitment of Orsik:** Fzoul Slovis intends to recruit Orsik at the earliest opportunity; it is unknown whether Orsik is aware of or would accept this overture.
- **The statue of Angharradgh:** A statue of the deity Angharradgh is being transported to Parnast; no explanation is given for why, by whom it was commissioned, or what it will be used for.
- **The goblinoid toll point:** Fzoul Slovis has flagged the hobgoblin toll and recommended the Zhentarim deal with it; no action has been taken yet.
- **Systematic goblin threat:** Fzoul Slovis has repeatedly warned Zhentarim leadership about escalating goblinoid activity; leadership has not acted, and Fzoul believes piecemeal responses have emboldened the raiders. Whether leadership will respond remains open.
- **The two fleeing goblins:** Two goblins escaped the night attack on Azam's caravan and are at large; they may report back to a larger force.
- **"The heroes Hsing is looking for":** Hsing specifically sought out this adventuring party, suggesting he has a larger purpose or mission in mind beyond caravan guarding; this purpose is not yet stated.
- **Faction agents:** Azam had hoped one of "the factions" would send an agent; which factions he expected, and why, is not explained.
