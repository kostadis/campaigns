## World Events

- The 3HPP and Harshnag travel two days from the Golden Dragon's cave to the Temple of Annam causeway without interruption.
- On the 3rd day of the 1st tenday of Eleasis 1492, Thistle discovers fresh human tracks near the causeway.
- The group examines six bas-relief pillars at the dome entrance; Harshnag identifies each as depicting a different giant type.
- Unla Kee ignores Thistle's warnings and rushes into the Temple hallway, passing under but not triggering a stone block trap.
- The 3HPP and Harshnag are ambushed by Great Worm Tribe Uthgardtian barbarians and a white dragon in the hallway before the stairway.
- The battle turns in the party's favor, aided significantly by Harshnag's fury; most barbarians are killed.
- The Uthgardtian Shaman flees into the Temple.
- Vardis takes and conceals the chieftain's amulet.
- Harshnag opens the Temple doors, stating only a giant can open them.
- Harshnag delivers a 10–15 minute lecture on the giant civilization of Osteria, claiming the Temple is 40,000 years old.
- The party observes that the statue of Annam has a blank face.
- Unla Kee attempts to pass through the glowing mist of the 40-foot portal archway and collides with a wall behind it.
- Orsik identifies six runes on the portal archway (hill, cloud, fire, storm, stone, frost), inlaid with mithral.
- Thistle touches the Storm rune; a Storm of Vengeance begins (thunder, then acid rain).
- Touching the Storm rune again has no effect.
- Orsik touches the Frost rune; a magical ice storm is triggered, knocking Unla Kee unconscious.
- The Storm of Vengeance triggers six lightning bolts, killing the entire 3HPP party.
- Harshnag carries all four party members out of the Temple in his satchel.
- Harshnag finds a healing potion in the party's belongings and administers it to Vardis to begin the revival process.
- On the 4th day of the 1st tenday of Eleasis, the group is fully healed.
- Orsik and Vardis jointly deduce that the removable weapons on the giant statues must be used to touch the corresponding runes.
- Harshnag takes the Hill Giant statue's 500 lb bone club and touches the Hill rune; he is surrounded by howling mad voices and briefly acts as if confused, but recovers. The attempt fails to activate the portal.
- Harshnag touches the Frost rune without the Frost Giant weapon; an ice storm pelts him (he is unharmed due to frost immunity). The attempt fails.
- Harshnag reveals the Frost Giant statue's weapon is missing.
- The group decides to search the Temple for the missing Frost Giant weapon.
- The party proceeds through double doors north of the Temple of Annam into a large room containing giant stairs, a stone giant statue alcove, a north hallway, and additional stairs.
- The party enters the dormitory hallway containing fourteen 30-foot archways leading to giant-sized bedchambers with frost-covered stone beds and tattered furs.
- Vardis notices a crack in one dormitory room wall.
- Harshnag remains in the dormitory, lying on furs on a stone bed.
- Thistle, Orsik, Vardis, and Unla Kee enter a 10'-high, 5'-wide tunnel through the crack.
- At a tunnel intersection, the party is ambushed by spiders.

## Locations

**Temple of Annam (Temple of the All-Father)**
- What it is: An ancient stone dome carved from a mountainside, supported by six 30-foot-tall bas-relief pillars depicting each giant type; claimed by Harshnag to be built 40,000 years ago by magic-wielding giants of the civilization of Osteria
- Causeway: Leads to the dome entrance; ends before the stone dome
- Pillars (north to south): Hill Giant (lifting a rock), Frost Giant (cutting down a pine), Cloud Giant (flying with birds), Storm Giant (tossing lightning at a ship), Stone Giant (climbing down a mountain), Fire Giant (with chained dwarves)
- Antechamber/hallway: Contains two massive stone blocks suspended by a contraption above the hallway as a trap; ledges used by attackers; alcoves used for ambush cover
- Main Temple chamber: 480 feet long; contains a statue of Annam with a blank face; contains a 40-foot-wide by 40-foot-tall portal archway on the far eastern wall with six mithral-inlaid runes (hill, cloud, fire, storm, stone, frost) and a glowing mist; iron ice-covered sconces in niches beside the portal (empty); six giant statues with removable weapons corresponding to rune types (Frost Giant weapon is missing)
- Current state: Largely intact but occupied by Great Worm Tribe remnants (shaman still inside); Storm of Vengeance was triggered and dissipated; portal not yet activated

**Dormitory Hall (north of the Temple of Annam, through double doors)**
- What it is: A massive room connected to the Temple via double doors; contains giant stairs on the left, a stone giant statue alcove on the right, a north-leading hallway, and additional stairs past the alcove
- Dormitory hallway: Fourteen 30-foot archways along east and west walls leading to 60-foot-square bedchambers with frost-covered stone beds; some beds have tattered furs
- Current state: Largely undisturbed; Harshnag resting on a bed with furs

**Tunnel (behind cracked dormitory wall)**
- What it is: A 10'-high, 5'-wide dark tunnel network accessed through a crack in a dormitory room wall; contains at least one intersection
- Current state: Active; party ambushed by spiders at the intersection

**Golden Dragon's cave**
- Referenced as the party's point of departure two days prior; not described further in this session

## Threads & Mysteries

- **The missing Frost Giant weapon**: The Frost Giant statue's weapon is absent from the Temple. Its location is unknown. Recovering it appears necessary to activate the portal.
- **The portal archway**: The purpose and destination of the portal remain unknown. The activation mechanism (using each giant type's weapon on its corresponding rune) has been deduced but not yet successfully executed.
- **The Uthgardtian Shaman**: Fled into the Temple interior during the battle. Current location and intentions unknown; may be a threat or may have information.
- **Fresh human tracks**: Thistle noted fresh human tracks near the causeway. Their origin is unconfirmed but likely related to the Great Worm Tribe.
- **The chieftain's amulet**: Vardis took and concealed the Uthgardtian chieftain's amulet. Its properties, significance, and any consequences of wearing it are unknown.
- **The tunnel and spiders**: The tunnel behind the cracked dormitory wall leads somewhere unknown; the spider ambush is ongoing. The tunnel's destination and what it might contain (possibly the missing weapon) are unresolved.
- **Other rune activations**: Only the Hill rune (with weapon, causing confusion effect) and Frost and Storm runes (without weapons, causing damaging weather) have been attempted. The correct sequence or combination for all six runes is unknown.
- **The stone giant statue alcove and additional stairs**: Noted in the dormitory area but not yet explored.
- **The age of the world / giant civilization of Osteria**: Harshnag claims the Temple is 40,000 years old and references the giant civilization of Osteria; Vardis disputes the age of Toril as only 5,000 years. This discrepancy is unresolved and may have broader lore implications.
