## World Events

- On the 9th day of the 1st Tenday of Taraskh, the party visited Lionel Coster in Llorkh, sold treasure, and purchased supplies including studded leather armor for Thistle, rations, a potion of healing, a potion of climbing, and a scroll of Detect Magic; Leo agreed to special-order a magnifying glass for 5 gold.
- Vardis attempted to grapple Orsik to bring him to the tower; Orsik escaped. Unla Kee attempted to grapple Thistle; Thistle was subdued but did not enter.
- Vardis and Unla Kee opened the tower door and were transported into an interdimensional dungeon bearing a plaque reading "Acerak's Construction Company."
- Vardis and Unla Kee descended into the dungeon and navigated south to a large hexagonal room, where Unla Kee found a corroded key in the southwest corner.
- A cranky bugbear arrived; Vardis cast Detect Magic via ritual (no magic detected).
- The bugbear demanded the party's rations under threat of eating them; the party complied.
- The bugbear explained three theories of escape: finding a secret passage, no escape (eventual death and zombification), or death triggering ejection from the dungeon.
- The bugbear agreed to ally with the party in exchange for food and the prospect of escape.
- The bugbear drew a map of the dungeon and described its inhabitants: a goblin tribe, a few ogre zombies, and himself.
- Vardis led the group west to a large room containing a wooden door to the north, tapestries of vile acts on the wall, candles on the floor, and spirals of white stone.
- Vardis discovered the secret door behind the tapestries.
- Vardis opened the secret door and was hit by an ice dart trap (deflected by armor).
- The passageway beyond the secret door led to a door that was revealed to be a mimic.
- The bugbear attacked the mimic; his morning star became stuck in the mimic; the mimic struck the bugbear.
- Vardis cast Guiding Bolt and critically wounded the mimic.
- Unla Kee cast Sleep and put the mimic to sleep.
- The bugbear smashed the sleeping mimic; Vardis delivered the killing blow.
- The party found a pile of treasure and a note reading "congratulations for making it this far!" in the mimic's room.
- The group proceeded through the northern door and found a staircase.
- A telepathic voice told Vardis that using the key would forfeit the gold.
- Vardis and Unla Kee chose to use the key and exit; the bugbear refused and returned into the dungeon to seek more treasure.
- Vardis and Unla Kee were teleported back to Llorkh, next to Orsik and Thistle, in the late afternoon.
- The party encountered Diego on their return; Unla Kee manipulated fates to convince Diego and the Guard to investigate the tower; Vardis revealed the secret of the entrance to Diego.
- The party returned to Aunt Nonnie's; Orsik snickered at Aunt Nonnie, causing her to raise prices; he apologised.
- On the road to Anauroch (approximately 1/2 Taraskh), the party encountered a desperate, tattered group of Cult of the Dragon cultists.
- Thistle killed several cultists and kept one alive for questioning.
- The surviving cultist revealed that Adimond Kroskas abandoned the Cult just before the defeat at the Well of the Worlds and left to work with a Giant.
- The surviving cultist revealed that enslaved people either died from mistreatment or were eaten alive by a Dragon as a sacrifice to bring Tiamat into the world; he attempted to frame this as an honor.
- Thistle killed the surviving cultist.
- On approximately 1/3 Taraskh, a group of kobolds attempted to ambush the party and was defeated.
- The party continues travelling toward the Oasis of Vuerthyl in Anauroch to meet Azan the caravaneer.
