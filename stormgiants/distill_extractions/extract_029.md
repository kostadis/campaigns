## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Cambians and House Margaster**: The two Cambians who attacked the party were acting on behalf of an unnamed master and sought "the master's treasure." Xamlyn Margaster appeared in court as if orchestrating the situation. The connection between the Cambians, their unnamed master, and Xamlyn Margaster is not fully explicit. The Cambians remain at large in Silverymoon. No resolution reached.
- **The treasure**: The Cambians claimed the party had stolen their master's treasure. What this treasure is, whether the party possesses it, and what it represents remains unresolved.
- **Unla Kee's magical ring**: Lost as payment for his resurrection. Its properties and significance are not stated but it was described as magical.
- **Unla Kee's claim of prophecy**: He claims he foresaw his own resurrection and allowed himself to be killed. Whether this is true or a face-saving rationalisation is unresolved. His earlier claim that the world would end if he died was apparently false (or contingent on being raised).
- **The Ordning of the Giants is sundered**: The reason for its sundering is unknown. Krowen Valharrow wants the party to find out why it happened and either fix it or report back. This is the central quest offered by the Harpers.
- **Darathra Shendrel**: Her current status is unknown. Dral Thelev inferred something was wrong with her when the brooch was presented. She is absent from events and her fate is unresolved.
- **The Star of Light**: A symbolic artifact of Miery's order, stolen by kobolds in a cave outside Everlund. The party has been tasked with its recovery but has not yet acted on this.
- **The Kraken**: Miery warns it will return to destroy Everlund with powerful, unnamed allies described as worse than giants. No detail on the Kraken's current location or timeline. Open question as to the identity of its allies.
- **The Doom of Desert**: A dragon that drove blue dragons out of northern Anauroch into the eastern Nether Mountains. Identity, motives, and current location unknown; mentioned in passing as world-building context.
- **"That THING" Orsik did in Silverymoon**: The Judge referenced a prior incident involving Orsik ("you're the one that did that THING") but did not specify what it was. This prior incident is unresolved and may have ongoing consequences.
- **The younger Silver Sister as Open Lord of Waterdeep**: Dral Thelev noted she now heads the Lords' Alliance. Her identity is not stated by name in this session; the political implications for the North and for House Margaster's Waterdeep connections are unresolved.
- **Harper teleportation circle network**: The party now possesses a map of teleportation circles across the North. How they will use this and what safeguards or obligations attach to it is unresolved.
- **Silverymoon's betrayal of the Silver Marches**: Referenced by Uncle Joon (via Thistle) and confirmed independently by the dwarven gate guard at Everlund. The full political and personal consequences for the party's relationship with Silverymoon and the Lords' Alliance are ongoing.
- **Faction surveillance in the North**: Krowen stated that the Zhentarim, Harpers, and Lords' Alliance are all monitoring each other and none are attending to the giant threat. The implications of this intelligence vacuum around the giant crisis are unresolved.
