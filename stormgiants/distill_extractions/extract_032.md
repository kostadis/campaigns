## World Events

- The party camped in the Evermoors and Thistle scouted, hearing snarling and whooping.
- Thistle confirmed a Fire Giant and two trolls in an opening in the Evermoors swamp.
- Using comprehend languages (cast by Unla Kee), Thistle overheard the Fire Giant demanding real food, lamenting the trolls' incomprehension, and stating she cannot return to Duke Zalto without finding a piece of the Vonindod using her rod.
- The party debated attacking the Fire Giant and trolls at length and ultimately decided against it.
- The party departed the Evermoors and arrived at the Calling Horns Inn at sunset.
- Tamalin was briefed on the Fire Giant-troll alliance and the Vonindod; she had not heard of the Vonindod and suggested Everlund or Silverymoon for research.
- Tamalin gave the party a note granting a favour from her brother, the High Captain of Everlund.
- The party set out the next day on the Evermoor Road toward Yartar.
- The party encountered a hill giant on the Evermoor Road accompanied by two bugbears and an ogre.
- The party ambushed the hill giant; Unla Kee's "hiding" (hands over eyes in plain sight) successfully avoided the giant's attention.
- Thistle hit the hill giant once; Orsik hit three times for significantly more damage; Vardis cast guiding bolt but missed.
- Unla Kee cast lightning bolt, reducing the hill giant from full health (105 hp) to 15 hp.
- The hill giant attacked Orsik twice; both times Orsik misdirected it toward Thuul, and the second attack was also disrupted by Vardis blinding the giant.
- Thistle killed the hill giant with an arrow through the eye.
- The bugbears and ogre fled upon seeing the hill giant killed.
- The party looted the hill giant's possessions (including a bag of salt, empty chest, drinking horn, and fairy leather armor).
- The following day the party encountered Fritz's goblin caravan heading east.
- Fritz explained that giants had become increasingly aggressive and nomads were attacking anything that moves, driving his clan eastward.
- The party sold the hill giant's salvaged items to Fritz; Orsik prevented Thistle from revealing the fairy leather armor's true nature.
- On the 8th day of the 1st tenday of Flamerule, the party arrived in Yartar in the evening and went to the Wink and the Kiss.
- Maia greeted the party; Orsik presented the Letter of Mark and left Maia's high-five unanswered.
- Eltio of the Hand of Yartar received the party and commented on Tamalin's past criminal reputation.
- Eltio informed the party that Harthos Zymorven is imprisoned for murder and will be executed by decapitation on the 10th.
- Eltio identified Tholzar Brenner as the current holder of the sword; described him as a disliked Damaranian guard captain.
- Eltio offered to lead the party to Tholzar for an ambush opportunity.
- Eltio led the party to the market near the Waterbarron's residence where Tholzar was collecting protection money with armed guards.
- Orsik suggested fireballing the market crowd; Eltio firmly talked him out of it citing proximity to the Waterbarron's residence and personal risk.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Vonindod**: An artifact (or collection of artifacts, as the giant seeks "a piece" of it) apparently made of or associated with adamantium (a prior Fire Giant in Triboar used a similar rod to locate an adamantium artifact). Duke Zalto's agents are actively searching for pieces of it. Neither Tamalin nor the party knows what it is. Research suggested in Everlund or Silverymoon.
- **The Rod**: The Evermoors Fire Giant carries a rod identical to the one used by the Fire Giant who attacked Triboar. The function of these rods (locating pieces of the Vonindod) is now understood, but their origin, who made them, and how many exist remain unknown.
- **Duke Zalto**: Leader of a Fire Giant faction; the Evermoors giant cannot return to him without the Vonindod piece. His location, full plans, and the significance of assembling the Vonindod are unknown. Tamalin had not heard of him.
- **The Fire Giant who attacked Triboar**: Referenced but not present; distinct from the Evermoors giant. The prior giant also used a rod to locate an adamantium artifact. Thistle notes this is a different giant and has no vendetta against the Evermoors one.
- **Harthos Zymorven**: Imprisoned in Yartar for murder; scheduled for decapitation on the 10th (two days from the party's arrival). The party needs to extract him before execution. His sword is held by Tholzar Brenner. The party has not yet settled on an approach.
- **Tholzar Brenner**: Damaranian guard captain in possession of Harthos's sword; described as universally disliked. Eltio has offered to set up an ambush. How the party will retrieve the sword without causing a major incident in Yartar is unresolved.
- **The favour from Tamalin's brother**: The party holds a note granting a favour from the High Captain of Everlund. This has not yet been used. Whether the party will go to Everlund for Vonindod research using this contact is unresolved.
- **Giant and nomad unrest**: Fritz reports that giants are becoming increasingly aggressive and nomads are attacking anything that moves, making roads dangerous. The broader cause or coordination of this unrest is unclear.
- **Orsik's reputation**: Eltio recognises Orsik by name and by a past event ("you are that guy Orsik who did that thing"). The nature of this event is not stated in this session.
- **Tamalin's criminal past**: Eltio confirms Tamalin was formerly a feared criminal figure who used her reputation to build a legitimate enterprise. The details of her past activities are unrevealed.
- **Silverymoon Cambion incident**: Orsik references "Cambion demons" in Silverymoon from a previous experience. The details and consequences of this event are not elaborated on in this session.
- **Fairy leather armor sold to Fritz**: Orsik prevented Thistle from revealing the armor's true origin to Fritz. Fritz now believes it is valuable because a giant carried it. Whether this causes future complications is unresolved.
