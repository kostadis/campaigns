- The group collected treasure left behind by the frost giants and the diamond dust

## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Job from Fiora Bendaega**: The group has accepted a contract to reach a merchant on the black road within one month (two-week journey; merchant arrives in three weeks). The nature of the job, the identity of the merchant, and the identity of the employer who paid Fiora have not been revealed.
- **The Tower of Green Flame**: The entity within the tower noted with sorrow that the door can now be opened after Thistle and Orsik triggered the ward. The entity's identity, purpose, and what will happen now the door is accessible are unresolved.
- **Old Keller's Condition for Redemption**: Keller told Orsik to return when "less likely to be goblin food" and implied he would then explain how to redeem the disgrace of "the thing in the keep." The nature of the original incident at the keep, and what redemption requires, are unresolved.
- **"The Thing in the Keep"**: Referenced by both Vardis and Old Keller as a disgracing incident involving dwarves, but never explicitly described in this session. Unresolved.
- **The Frost Giants' Deaths**: The two frost giants in the ice chamber died from slashing wounds caused by large bladed weapons. No explanation is given for who or what killed them or why they were there. Unresolved.
- **The Diamond's Spirit and Its Warning**: Upon destruction, the diamond produced a green-tinted spirit that warned companions want to kill each other and take their possessions. Whether this is a residual enchantment, a genuine threat, or a manipulation is unresolved. The group dismissed it.
- **Veradda Store's Notes**: Her notes identified at least three treasure troves; this was the third. Her current location and status are unknown.
- **Lionel Coster Trading House's True Allegiance**: Suspected variously of being a front for the Harpers, the Zhentarim, a cult (Mammon referenced), or genuinely just a trading house. Unresolved.
- **Rumors of Giants Near Llorkh**: The refugee woman mentioned hearing rumors of giants nearby before deciding to return west. Whether these are connected to the frost giants found in the Greypeaks cache is unresolved.
- **Cult of the Dragon — Thistle's Revenge**: Thistle explicitly wants revenge against the Cult of the Dragon. This motivation has not yet been acted upon directly and remains an open thread.
- **Greenest's Recovery**: The refugee woman is returning west to assess whether Greenest is safe and to rebuild. Her fate and the town's current state are unresolved.
- **The Black Road and Its "Honest Tradespeople"**: Fiora described her clients as "honest tradespeople working the black road." The black road's nature and the clients' actual identity and purpose remain unexplained.
