## Threads & Mysteries

- **King Hekaton's Conch**: The Oracle stated the party's current path will lead them to the conch, which is the means of reaching the Maelstorm. The exact location and how the party will obtain it is unresolved.
- **Adimond Kroskas at the Maelstorm**: Kroskas is confirmed to be at the Maelstorm. The party has not yet traveled there. Kroskas' gender is uncertain even to Delsephine. Kroskas' role and current activities at the Storm Giant court are unknown.
- **The Storm Giant Court in Chaos**: King Hekaton's court is described as being in chaos. The nature, cause, and extent of this chaos are unresolved.
- **Duke Zalto and the Burial Ground Artifact**: The party seeks an artifact in a burial ground that will lead them to Duke Zalto. The Oracle refused to answer Thistle's deceptive question. The location of the burial ground and artifact remain unknown.
- **The Kobold Warren and the Netheril Artifact**: The party is entering a Kobold warren in the Nether Mountains in search of an artifact that once belonged to ancient Netheril, possibly lost by the Fighters of Light. What the artifact is, why Kobolds have it, and what it does are unresolved.
- **The "Doom of Desert"**: A dragon by this name expelled blue dragons from northern Anauroch into the Nether Mountains. Its identity, power, and relevance to the party are unknown. Orsik intends to ask Delsephine about it.
- **Everlund's Letter for Silverymoon**: The party has a letter from Captain Zoar referencing demon trafficking in Silverymoon. The criminal organization trafficking demons in Silverymoon has not been identified or confronted.
- **Ending the Giant War**: The Oracle confirmed this is what would most impress the Zhentarim and also what Klauth wants. The mechanism by which the party could end the war remains unresolved — it likely involves slaying giant leaders and dealing with the Maelstorm.
- **The Ordning Being Remade**: Evil giants are acting to be elevated when the ordning is remade. The process, timeline, and who controls the remaking of the ordning are unresolved.
- **Unla Kee's Claim**: She claims to know how the world ends and that the Oracle has nothing to teach her. The basis of this knowledge and its implications are unexplained.
- **Klauth's True Motives**: Orsik correctly identified that Klauth's gift is self-serving (protecting the Spine of the World from the Lord's Alliance, weakening giants). Whether Klauth has additional hidden agendas is an open question.
- **Delsephine and the Cult of the Dragon's Future**: Delsephine left the Cult after Dracolich research was halted. Whether the Cult continues to pursue Dracolich creation under new leadership is unresolved.
- **Captain Zoar and Political Ambition**: Zoar expressed that defeating the giant threat would increase his prestige; Thistle suggested he run for higher office. Whether this thread develops into political maneuvering is open.
- **The Play (Bob Saget)**: The play is touring and approaching Everlund; each performance ends differently. Whether the demon actor (Bob Saget) poses an ongoing risk or the different endings have a cause is unresolved.
- **"Frosty" Staying Behind**: A party member stayed at the Eye of the World. His reasons, reliability, and whether he will rejoin the party are unresolved.
- **Urgala's Wife**: The Oracle confirmed she fell into a well leading to the Underdark. Whether there are further implications (her fate in the Underdark, Urgala's reaction upon learning this) is unresolved.
- **The Well of the Dragon**: Referenced as a place where dragons were badly wounded. Its nature and significance are not explained in this session.
- **Krowen Valharrow**: Chose the party for their mission; expressed confidence in them. Has not appeared in this session; his role and expectations remain as background context.
