## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Giant Uprising**: Giants are at war with the peoples of the North, claiming the territory of ancient Ostoria. The scope is described as potentially encompassing the entire Sword Coast. What form this war will take, which giant clans or leaders are involved beyond Hekaton, and how soon it will reach populated areas are unresolved.
- **King Hekaton's Missing Wife**: Kraddock noted that Hekaton's wife has disappeared and that she advocated for tolerance and inclusive treatment of non-giants. The circumstances of her disappearance, who is responsible, and what effect it has had on Hekaton and giant politics are unknown.
- **The Owl Trainer**: Thistle identified that the four giant owls at the shrine flew in trained patterns, implying someone trained them as guardians. Who trained them, whether they are connected to the Cult of the Dragon desecration, and whether this person is still active are unresolved.
- **The Cult of the Dragon Remnants**: The cult desecrated the shrine and left dead members behind. With Tiamat's plot failed, the cult's current state, remaining membership, and future intentions are unknown.
- **Kobold Activity near Llorkh**: Nonnie reports increased kobold sightings around Llorkh. The guards are inactive. The source, leadership, and purpose of the kobolds are unknown.
- **Orsik's Past "THING"**: Nonnie cryptically referenced something Orsik did in the past that she clearly remembers and holds against him. The nature of this event is undisclosed.
- **Kraddock's Potions**: Kraddock gave Vardis potions on departure. The type, number, and properties of these potions are not specified in the session notes.
- **Thistle's History with the Cult of the Dragon**: Kraddock indicated that Thistle "suffered" because of the cult. The nature and full extent of this suffering are not detailed in this session.
- **Annam the All-Father's Decisions**: Kraddock serves Annam and disagrees with his decisions regarding the war. What specifically Annam has decreed, and whether divine intervention or guidance will play a role, is unresolved.
