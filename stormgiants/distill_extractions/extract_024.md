## Locations

- What it is: A vast, ancient forest larger than most kingdoms; encompasses mountains; known for oversized fauna including large brown bears and stags with wide antlers
- What happened: The party flew over it aboard Zephyros' castle

**Star Mounts**
- What it is: Mountains in the heart of the High Forest; so tall they are visible from any forest edge; surrounded by powerful, dangerous winds preventing approach
- What happened: Visible from the castle during transit; Zephyros pointed them out

**Iceshield Lands**
- What it is: Territory passed over during the journey to Triboar
- What happened: The castle passed over this region en route

**Dessarin Hills**
- What it is: A hilly region beyond the Iceshield lands; historically mined by dwarves; rich enough to attract wildcat miners but not wealthy enough to sustain permanent operations; dotted with abandoned mines, fortresses, and tombs; some mines connect to the Underdark
- What happened: Zephyros reminisced about dwarves who mined there; the castle passed over the region; Urgala Meltimer provided a rough map of the area including a mine that connects to the Underdark where her wife disappeared

**Dessarin River**
- What it is: A river in the region
- What happened: The castle passed over it on approach to Triboar

**Triboar**
- What it is: A bustling mercantile town at the intersection of the Long Road and the Evermoor Way; half the population lives on ranches and farmsteads outside town; governed by Lord Protector Delratha (from Waterdeep); policed by the Twelve
