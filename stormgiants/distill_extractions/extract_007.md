## World Events

- The Cult of the Dragon previously controlled Parnast and the Black Road through Anauroch, displacing the Zhentarim.
- Tiamat's entry into the North was blocked at the Battle at the Well of the Worlds.
- Following Tiamat's defeat, the Cult abandoned the Black Road and Parnast.
- The Zhentarim attempted to reassert control of the Black Road but their hold remains tenuous.
- The Cult of the Dragon used a flying castle over Parnast to transport large quantities of treasure.
- That flying castle subsequently crash-landed near Waterdeep, which convinced observers of a deeper, more sinister plot and contributed to the formation of the alliance that defeated the Cult at the Well of the Worlds (per Orsik's recollection).
- The party crossed Dawn Pass through the Greypeak Mountains on the Grayvale Run road on 1/4 of Taraskh.
- Two black bears were encountered and pacified with goodberry berries; they warned of dangers approaching from behind them.
- Two giant scorpions emerged from the dusty hills and attacked the party.
- The first scorpion was brought near death by Orsik and killed by Unla Kee's magic missile.
- The second scorpion grappled Orsik, struck and poisoned Vardis to near-death, then attempted to flee with Orsik; Orsik killed it by smashing its skull.
- Vardis survived lethal scorpion poison due to alcohol-conditioned resistance.
- The party arrived at Parnast on 1/7 of Taraskh.
- The party discovered a Wyvern left by the Cult of the Dragon chained in Parnast's stables; they chose not to engage it.
- Chandra Stol offered the party information about the Cult in exchange for retrieving the idol of Angharradh from Azam in Vuerthyl.
- Ragnar Redtooth at The Golden Tankard was unable to identify the treasure map's location more specifically than "somewhere in the North."
- The party departed Parnast for Vuerthyl on the Black Road.
- The party arrived at the oasis of Vuerthyl on 2/1 of Taraskh after a hot, quiet journey through the desert.
- The party met Azam and his patron Hsing in the tavern tent at Vuerthyl.
- Azam commissioned the party to transport the statue of Angharradh from Vuerthyl to Parnast.
- Hsing revealed to Thistle that Thule can be found "where there is the yellow sky."

## Locations

**Dawn Pass**
- What it is: The easiest route through the Greypeak Mountains; part of the Grayvale Run road
- What happened: Party crossed here; encountered and pacified two black bears; transition from cold and moist to cold and dry environment observed as they descended east

**The Black Road**
- What it is: A road running through the Anauroch desert, historically a significant trade and control route
- Current state: Contested; the Zhentarim's hold is tenuous following the Cult's abandonment
- Recent events: Party traveled this road east to Vuerthyl; journey described as peculiarly safe and eerily quiet

**Parnast**
- What it is: A village/hamlet at the western end of the Black Road; last settlement before the desert; contains a tavern, stables, a shrine, a wheelwright shop, and a few dozen houses with a small square
- Historical control: Zhentarim, then Cult of the Dragon, now abandoned by both
- Current state: Largely abandoned by outside factions; small number of distrustful locals remain; locals are neutral to conquerors and profit from trade
- Notable feature: Wyvern left by the Cult of the Dragon chained in the stables; kept fed by villagers and used as a deterrent/prison threat
- Shrine: "Shrine of Axes," identified by Vardis; currently lacks its idol (the statue of Angharradh)

**The Golden Tankard**
- What it is: The tavern in Parnast; named for the yellow tankard sign over the door; interior described as gloomy
- Current state: Operating but slow; business has been poor due to few travelers from the east
- Proprietor: Ragnar Redtooth

**Parnast Stables**
- What it is: Large stable building in Parnast with a wide-open interior
- Current state: Contains a Wyvern left by the Cult of the Dragon, chained but capable of breaking the chain; floor littered with carcasses picked clean; sealed doors

**Anauroch (the great desert)**
- What it is: A massive desert through which the Black Road runs
- Current state: Hot, miserable; massive dunes; sparse vegetation; described as having a large sky

**Vuerthyl**
- What it is: An oasis in the Anauroch desert; surrounded by a collection of sun-bleached tents; includes a tent functioning as a tavern
- Current state: Active settlement; Azam and Hsing are present here with the statue of Angharradh awaiting transport
- What happened: Party arrived and met Azam and Hsing; viewed and examined the statue; accepted the transport commission

**Greypeak Mountains**
- What it is: A mountain range; the Grayvale Run and Dawn Pass run through it
- Note: Creates a rain shadow on its eastern side, causing the dry environment east of the pass
