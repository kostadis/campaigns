## Locations

**Raven's Rock**
- What it is: Spirit mound of the Black Raven and Gray Wolf Uthgardt tribes on the Spine of the World; always covered in snow regardless of season (cause unknown); features a 100-foot-tall stone carved raven at the center of the highest plateau; surrounded by four 50-foot-tall menhirs used by shamans to track seasons and stars; giant ravens gather at dusk to eat offerings and perch until nightfall
- What happened there: The 3HPP recovered a thigh bone artifact from the Gray Wolf altar; Thistle spoke with giant ravens; the party departed quickly after raven warning
- Current state: Artifact removed; giant ravens potentially aware of who took it; possible tribal retaliation incoming

**Gray Wolf Altar (at Raven's Rock)**
- What it is: Altar mound in a wolf-shaped depression southwest of Raven's Rock; rectangular stone slab with moon phases carved on its sides; Gray Wolves gather here and are not permitted on the high plateau
- What happened there: Party dug and recovered a thigh bone artifact from beneath or at the altar
- Current state: Artifact removed

**Great Worm Cavern**
- What it is: Spirit mound of the Great Worm Uthgardt tribe; located inside a spacious cavern at the northeast end of the Valley of Khedrun in the Spine of the World; entrance flanked by thick ice; interior walls glazed with ice and lined with massive icicles; floor is a sheet of slippery ice; flanked by 70-foot-high rock ledges with ice totem poles carved as winged snakes; tribe members live in caves in the ledge walls warmed by natural steam vents; crypts of honored dead (standing frozen corpses) dug into upper cavern walls; 120-foot triangular promontory at the back bearing a frost-covered serpentine altar representing Elrem the Great Worm; a large broken gong (once a frost giant champion's shield, made of red dragon scales on beaten copper, 13 feet diameter, 250 lbs, missing a large piece) hangs in the cavern
- What happened there: Party arrived; Great Worm tribe defenders incinerated by fireballs; a large worm-like creature burst from a snowbank and rose up threatening the party

**Valley of Khedrun**

## Threads & Mysteries

- **The giant ravens said "Nevermore"**: Thistle does not know what this means and could not recall what Uncle Joon said about it. Whether this signals imminent Black Raven tribe retaliation, a curse, or something else is unresolved.
- **Incoming threat at Raven's Rock**: Thistle warned of possible incoming enemies after the raven encounter; the party fled. Whether the Black Raven tribe or another force is pursuing them is unresolved.
- **Klamath the Red Dragon's location**: Delsephine refused to reveal where Klamath is. She promised he will meet and reward the party after the giants are dealt with.
- **Bressik's contract buyout**: Unla Kee concluded a deal with Delsephine (500 gp upfront, residuals capped at 6,000 gp on the current play). It is unclear whether the full payment has been made, whether Bressik is aware, or whether Klamath will honor Delsephine's negotiation.
- **Unla Kee's sequel loophole**: Unla Kee structured the deal so residuals only apply to the current play. Delsephine does not yet know this. The "Gong Show" sequel is being planned.
- **Bob and the current play**: Orsik mentioned needing to tell Bob to wind down the current play. Bob's role and reaction are unresolved.
- **The creature at Great Worm Cavern**: A massive worm-dragon-centipede creature burst from a snowbank as the party approached the altar. Its identity, whether it is Elrem the Great Worm itself, and the outcome of the encounter are unresolved.
- **The gong at Great Worm Cavern**: Whether the gong is the artifact or merely decorative is unresolved. Bressik favors the altar theory. Orsik wants it regardless, possibly for Harshnag or for the show.
- **The cause of perpetual snow at Raven's Rock**: Described as a geographical anomaly with no known cause; Bressik's research has revealed nothing. Explicitly noted as a mystery.
- **Harshnag**: Referenced as a potential recipient of the gong/shield. His current location and status are not addressed in this session.
- **Klamath as necromancer and undead contract**: Bressik's contract covers his undead afterlife. Whether Klamath intends to eventually raise Bressik as an undead creature after his death remains an open implication.
- **Uncle Joon**: Referenced by Thistle as someone whose advice he could not recall. Uncle Joon's identity and relationship to Thistle are not elaborated in this session.
- **The overall artifact collection mission**: The party has now retrieved artifacts from multiple mounds (including Raven's Rock); the broader purpose of collecting these artifacts and what will be done with them is an ongoing thread.
