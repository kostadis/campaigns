## World Events

- Orsik looted the Frost Giant corpses found at or near Prettyview, recovering a large quantity of gold and silver coins.
- The group explored the town of Prettyview, finding most buildings demolished and filled with dead bodies.
- Orsik discovered only two structures still standing in Prettyview: a barn (containing cows) and a large house containing an extensive library.
- Orsik found Yazmeen, a gnome librarian, hiding behind a couch in the library building.
- Yazmeen revealed that Frost Giants attacked and destroyed Prettyview, killing the inhabitants and leaving behind feline companions.
- The group accepted Yazmeen's invitation to sleep overnight in the library; Unla Kee drank the magical tea.
- During the night, a Slaad emerged from Yazmeen's bedroom and confronted the sleeping group, accusing them of ruining its breeding program.
- Unla Kee counterspelled the Slaad's fireball attempt.
- The Slaad set fire to the library's books and attempted to escape through the bedroom window.
- Unla Kee trapped the Slaad in the bedroom using a wand of the web; the Slaad failed to break his concentration with fire.
- The Slaad was killed.
- Vardis carried the unconscious Yazmeen out of the burning library to safety.
- Yazmeen revealed that the Slaad had been using Prettyview's inhabitants as interplanar anchors to summon more of its kind; the Frost Giants' attack had inadvertently disrupted this ritual by killing the anchors.
- Yazmeen revealed that the Slaad had been using him as a portal and that drinking the tea had apparently warded it off while he was awake.
- Vardis deduced that the tea's magical protection wore off when Yazmeen fell asleep, allowing the Slaad to re-enter the dimension.
- The group spent the rest of the night in the barn.
- Harshnag and Orsik argued about whether to proceed directly to the Oracle or to follow the treasure map; Harshnag stated that the All-Father had sent a sign.
- The group continued eastward through the Middle Valley of the Spine of the World.
- An avalanche was triggered from the peaks above the valley, threatening to bury the group.
- Unla Kee cast water breathing on the group to prevent asphyxiation under the snow.
- Orsik used fire bolts to melt and cut through the snow.
- Harshnag, unburied by the avalanche due to his size, dug the group out of the snow.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Laela's danger**: Thistle believes Laela, described as "the love of his fairy heart," is in terrible danger. Her location, the nature of the danger, and whether she is still alive are unknown.
- **Orsik's past "Thing"**: Harshnag references an unspecified act Orsik repeatedly committed as a guard across the North and in Waterdeep. The nature of this incident has not been revealed.
- **The treasure map**: Orsik advocates following a treasure map rather than going directly to the Oracle. The map's origin, contents, and destination are unknown.
- **The Oracle**: Harshnag wants the group to travel directly to the Oracle. Its nature, location, and what information it might provide are unspecified. Harshnag also claims the All-Father sent a sign related to this goal.
- **The All-Father's sign**: Harshnag muttered that "the All-Father sent a sign" during his argument with Orsik. What the sign was and what it indicates are unexplained.
- **Yazmeen's magical tea**: The tea served by Yazmeen had magical properties that warded off the Slaad while Yazmeen was conscious and drinking it. The source, nature, and full extent of the tea's magic are unexplained.
- **The Slaad's breeding program**: The Slaad stated the group "ruined my breeding program." The Slaad is dead, but it is unclear whether the broader summoning effort is truly stopped or whether other Slaadi are involved.
- **The Frost Giants' feline companions**: Yazmeen mentioned the Frost Giants left their "feline companions" behind when they attacked Prettyview. These creatures have not been encountered or further described.
- **The play and "demon Bob Sagert" merchandising**: Vardis and Orsik reference a theatrical production going national, with plans to sell merchandise (dolls) of a demon character called Bob Sagert. The nature of this demon, the play, and its status are not elaborated upon in this session.
- **Prettyview's dead**: Orsik stated the bodies should be buried to prevent them turning to carrion. It is not confirmed whether this was carried out before the library fire and subsequent departure.
