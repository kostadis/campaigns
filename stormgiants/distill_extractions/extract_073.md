## Threads & Mysteries

- **Thistle's lost love**: The Raven's despair cry reminded Thistle of his lost love; he redoubled his resolve to find her. Identity, location, and fate unknown.
- **Unla Kee's "end game"**: Unla Kee referenced "THE END GAME" with apparent capital-letter significance and a prophecy; he classified Sandesyl as not part of it. The nature, content, and source of this prophecy are unexplained.
- **Sandesyl's ice lair**: Theorized to be carved into the glacier; not yet located or confirmed. The 3HPP plans to raid it but must first eliminate the Ogre Zombies and rest.
- **The Ogre Zombies**: Must be destroyed before the 3HPP can raid Sandesyl's lair; doing so will further anger Sandesyl.
- **Sandesyl's necromantic research**: As a disciple of Sammaster, she is pursuing undead creation beyond Dracoliches. The scope and current state of this research are unknown.
- **Rezmir's note and internal Cult politics**: The note implies Rezmir was killed due to internal Cult of the Dragon disputes rather than by outside heroes. The auditors, who accused her of betrayal, and the full nature of the internal conflict remain unexplored.
- **The nature of Rezmir's remains**: Only half of the dragon remains were present. The other half's location and significance are unexplained.
- **Countess Sansuri's play for the Ordning**: Escarlotta is furious about this. Whether Sansuri succeeded, is still attempting it, or has been stopped is unknown.
- **Blagothkus and the Oracle (the All-Father)**: Blagothkus was told he was a failure and that his son needed to kill him. His son failed and died. The implications for Blagothkus's legitimacy or role among giants are unresolved.
- **The Giant Renaissance / Blagothkus's obsession**: Blagothkus became convinced after the War of the Dragons that he was part of a giant renaissance. Whether this obsession is entirely abandoned or still drives him is unclear.
- **Uncle Joon**: Referenced as Thistle's source of Cult of the Dragon lore. Identity, location, and current state unknown.
- **Raise dead via "not good" clerics**: The group casually discussed using morally ambiguous clerics for resurrection. Whether this foreshadows a death in the party, and what such clerics might do (raise vs. animate), is an open question.
- **Gem of Elemental Summoning**: Pocketed by Unla Kee. Its intended use has not been stated.
- **Blackstaff wine**: Found by Orsik in the pantry. No mechanical or plot significance established yet; may be a curiosity or trade good.
