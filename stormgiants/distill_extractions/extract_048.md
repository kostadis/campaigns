- **King Hekaton**: The 3HPP are seeking a path to reach him; the conch is implied to be the mechanism; the Oracle's instructions are partially understood
- **The Grandfather Tree**: The 3HPP are en route; purpose of visiting is to seek information or an artifact related to a giant; the Dryads Halisa and Helia, the Uthgardt Tree Ghost Tribe, and allied forest creatures represent potential obstacles or allies
- **Halisa and Helia (Dryads)**: Their punishment is of "indeterminate" length; killing them is warned against with "powerful and dangerous consequences" unspecified; their relationship to the 3HPP (Thistle as a Fey, Orsik as a neutral evil wizard, Vardis as a wanted criminal) is uncertain

- **Vardis and "Lorien's cult"**: An elven crew member implied Vardis was kicked out of "Lorien's cult"; this has not been confirmed or elaborated on
- **Ezra and the Harper contractor policy**: The Harper agent blamed "Ezra" for the policy of using contractors; Ezra's identity and current status are unknown
