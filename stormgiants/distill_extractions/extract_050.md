## World Events

- The 3HPP approached the Grandfather Tree by airship; Unla Kee observed its crown over 350 feet high, an 800-foot diameter canopy spread, four additional trees beneath the canopy, and two large rings of raised earth (outer and inner) circling the main tree.
- Thistle approached the southernmost tree and was confronted by the exiled dryad Halisa, who forbade further progress and explained her exile and the terms of Tree Protection Schedule D.
- Orsik angered Halisa by intervening; she dismissed him for being a dwarf; he stormed off.
- Prince Thornacious appeared near the Grandfather Tree; Thistle deduced that Orsik was somehow involved and the two agreed to use this to their advantage.
- Thistle approached the eastern tree; Helia, the dryad there, insulted Orsik-as-Thornacious in Fey in an attempt to provoke him, but Orsik understood nothing and misread gestures; Helia gave up.
- Helia explained his backstory and offered to grant permission if Thistle could convince Halisa to apologize.
- Thistle approached the northern tree; the elderly dryad demanded a good joke in exchange for help; Thistle told a fey joke about a dryad and how charm interacts with tree movement, which he had formerly shared with his beloved; the elderly dryad laughed and then demanded that Thistle end the Helia/Halisa bickering as his condition.
- The elderly dryad revealed the stated terms of Queen Titania's supposed curse: if one dryad left, the other would be safe, and the one who left would die. He noted neither loved the other enough to sacrifice themselves, so both remained, bickering, and the moon festival was being canceled as a result.
- Thistle approached the western tree and met Sleth, a non-exiled dryad genuinely assigned to tend the Grandfather Tree; Sleth revealed that Helia and Halisa were not actually cursed — they had cursed each other through their own belief and stubbornness.
- Thistle devised a plan: have Orsik-as-Thornacious claim he had been cured of muteness at the tree and declare both dryads pardoned and free to leave.
- Orsik-as-Thornacious touched the tree, shouted a blessing to Queen Titania, and claimed he could now speak "only in COMMON."
- Orsik-as-Thornacious told Halisa she was free to leave while allowing her to believe Helia would remain; Halisa departed, stating she would return every 100 years to taunt Helia.
- Orsik-as-Thornacious and Thistle then told Helia he too was pardoned and free to leave; Helia departed immediately.
- The elderly dryad and Sleth appeared together beside the 3HPP once both exiles were gone; the elderly dryad expressed great relief; Sleth confirmed there had been no curse and lamented the wasted hundred years of bickering.
- With permission granted, the 3HPP quickly located and retrieved the artifact — a nonmagical electrum torc with Giant runes, formerly the nose-ring of a mighty hill giant chief — from a chamber in the Grandfather Tree's roots.
- On departing, the 3HPP observed a group of centaurs arriving at the tree, muttering about a cursed temple deeper in the forest and seeking the tree's healing powers.
- Just before reaching the airship, the 3HPP overheard the son of Chief Boorvald Orcbane leading Tree Ghost Tribe barbarians to Sleth; the leader learned the tribe's artifact had been taken and promised vengeance.
- Sleth praised Prince Thornacious to the Tree Ghost Tribe leader as the 3HPP slipped away.
