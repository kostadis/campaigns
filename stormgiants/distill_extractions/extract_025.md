## World Events

- Jacob and Persson Hysstryn recruited Thistle to investigate the mysterious death of their brother Mirak.
- The party learned from a drunk, passed-out priest of Helm at the Talking Troll that Mirak's body was located in the Six Windows Inn.
- Thistle created a distraction at the Six Windows Inn while the rest of the party sneaked inside.
- The party discovered Mirak's remains in the attic of the Six Windows Inn.
- The party used Mirak's remains as evidence to convince Lord Protector Darathra to imprison Tolmara pending a Speak with the Dead examination.
- Lord Protector Darathra ordered the party to remain in Triboar until the following day due to the commotion they caused.
- Urgala Meltimer revealed the death of her first wife in the Underdark to the party.
- Urgala Meltimer provided the party with a map of an area of extreme interest in the Dessarin Hills.
- Orsik and Vardis visited the Lionshield Coster outpost (Lion's Share) and discovered the vault had been breached and special interplanar supply chain keys stolen.
- The party examined the only clues left at the scene: a leather harness and a masterfully crafted grappling hook.
- Orthovir the harness maker confirmed the harness was not his work and assessed it was made in Waterdeep, fitting a thin person of approximately 150 pounds.
- Harriet Uldinath confirmed the grappling hook was not from her shop and surpassed her skill; she pointed the party toward Ghelryn Foehammer.
- Ghelryn Foehammer confirmed he made the grappling hook and that he sold it to Darz Helgar.
- Darz Helgar, confronted with the harness, immediately revealed that Kaelen of the Troll Tavern forced him into the theft.
- Darz Helgar agreed to provide Orsik with information about the thieves guild in exchange for silence about his criminal past.
