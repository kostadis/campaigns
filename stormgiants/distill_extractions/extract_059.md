- The 3HPP entered the castle's control room and encountered Blagothkus and the voice of Escarlotta, who is trapped in the control console

- A vampire spawn (dressed in Cult of the Dragon purple) knocked on the castle door to inquire about the castle's movement and where the coven would next feed

- Sandesyl confronted the 3HPP in the courtyard; she stated she would not move against House Margastar because Asmodeus protects them
- Thistle and Sandesyl negotiated feeding targets; they agreed on the Uthgardt barbarians of Flint Rock
- Delsephine delivered a message from Klauth wishing the 3HPP luck in ending the war of the giants
- The cloud castle arrived at Flint Rock; Sandesyl's vampires captured Uthgardt nomads who were mourning a despoiled artifact
- Sandesyl's vampires left fabricated evidence and a survivor to spread a tale blaming House Margastar and Silverymoon for the Flint Rock massacre
- During the multi-day journey to the Eye of the All-Father, the screams of Sandesyl's victims echoed through the castle
- The cloud castle arrived at the Eye of the All-Father on Eleint 3rd day of 1st tenday of 1492
- The 3HPP hurried past the domed entrance and six giant-carved pillars to the main temple of Annam, where Harshnag awaited
- Harshnag picked up Thrymm's great axe, touched the Ise rune on the portal, and entered the Eye with the party
- Inside the Eye, the ghost of Eigeron appeared and asked about his father Blagothkus; upon learning Blagothkus was alive, Eigeron went mad and attempted to possess a hero
- Vardis invoked the power of Corellon and turned Eigeron's ghost
- The 3HPP deposited artifacts in the Oracle; the Oracle revealed the locations of the five giant faction leaders and their conches (hill giant: Grudd Haug; stone giant: Deadstone Cleft; frost giant: Svardborg; fire giant: Ironslag; cloud giant: Lyn Armaal)
- The party exited the portal, leaving Eigeron howling behind them, and entered the main temple to find Adimond Kroskas present
- Adimond Kroskas saw Harshnag and transformed into an Ancient Blue Dragon
- Harshnag ordered the 3HPP to flee; they hesitated
- Harshnag struck the statue of Annam the All-Father twice with his greataxe, causing structural collapse of the ceiling and temple, to force the party to flee
- The heroes fled through the hallway toward the castle; Adimond Kroskas attacked Harshnag, ignoring the fleeing party
- The hallway and temple collapsed behind the fleeing heroes; Harshnag's fate is unknown
