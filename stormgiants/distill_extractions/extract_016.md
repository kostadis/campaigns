## World Events

- The Unseelie agents (Coniferous Dryad, Deciduous Dryad, Unseelie Halfling Mage) were encountered playing cards in a Fey grove on the 3rd day of the 3rd Tenday of Taraskh.
- The Coniferous Dryad cast entangle, trapping Thistle, Vardis, and Unla Kee; Orsik and Wallace broke free.
- The Coniferous Dryad teleported between trees repeatedly during the battle.
- Unla Kee cast magic missiles, hitting the Coniferous Dryad and killing the Deciduous Dryad.
- The Deciduous Dryad charmed Orsik; the charm was broken when the Dryad was wounded by Thistle's arrow and Thuul's acid.
- The Coniferous Dryad charmed Unla Kee, causing him to drink from his flask.
- Vardis cast faerie fire; only the Unseelie Halfling was affected (pink glow).
- Wallace struck the Unseelie Halfling deeply; Orsik hit it with a handaxe; Wallace killed it.
- A lion and a bear arrived during the battle; Oblivillish identified them as the creatures that ate the stag.
- The lion pounced on Thistle and dropped him; it became tangled in entangle branches before it could finish him.
- Vardis cast scorching ray, killing the lion and wounding the bear.
- Wallace killed the bear.
- Orsik killed the Coniferous Dryad (knocked prone, cut in half), ending the charm on Unla Kee.
- Blasius woke from his drunken stupor after the battle and declared his joy at no longer being Chief of Staff.
- Blasius gave Unla Kee a ring of jumping, naming him his "true honored drinking friend."
- The party returned to Gralm; Orsik's persuasion, the drinking game victory, and the magnim sound-canceling headphones convinced Gralm to sign a contract agreeing to deal with Bad Fruul.
- Gralm (via Grolm's hand) signed the contract; offered the party a celebratory drink.
- Unla Kee drank Gralm's fine brews without any apparent alarming magical effect.
- Vardis lured Unla Kee away from further drinking by claiming better booze was in the tent.
- On the morning after, the party met Blasius and Oblivillish in the clearing with the dead Unseelie.
- Blasius opened a Fey portal and transported the group directly before Prince Thornacious.
- Prince Thornacious initially called Oblivillish "traitorous scum."
- Blasius retracted his accusations, attributing them to drunkenness, credited Oblivillish with commanding the heroes, and declared the Unseelie incursion averted and the true killers (lion and bear) found.
- Thistle and the rest of the group confirmed Blasius's account to Prince Thornacious.
- Prince Thornacious reinstated Blasius as his Satyr and Oblivillish as Chief of Staff.
- On the 5th day of the 3rd Tenday of Taraskh, Blasius led the party on a vision quest involving drinking and Fey communion; the experience granted the heroes the ability to neutralize poison once per vision quest.
- Six days after the vision quest, the party arrived in Parnast.
- On the 1st day of the 1st Tenday of Mirtul, Azam greeted the party in Old Parnast's main square during the Festival of Greengrass.
- Azam paid Unla Kee the agreed reward: a magical potion and a pouch of 200 GP.
- Unla Kee did not betray the group by revealing the private details of his deal; Azam assessed him positively as a potential recruit.
- Azam pitched Lord's Alliance membership to Unla Kee, describing and disparaging the other major factions of the North.
- Unla Kee declined Lord's Alliance membership.
