## World Events

- Blagothkus' flying castle travels toward Silverymoon with the 3HPP aboard, passing over the Evermoors.
- Orsik (posing as his father "Orsic") decides to recruit Lord Zymorven, citing the debt owed for saving his son.
- The castle lands a few miles from Zymorven Hall on the 10th day of the 1st tenday of Eleint 1492.
- Lord Zymorven confronts the arriving party in full armor; is deceived into believing he is speaking to Orsik's father "Orsic."
- "Orsic" explains the situation: Bob Saget is a demon or doppelganger that killed and replaced a famous actor, and now plans to destroy Silverymoon with a zombie plague originating from the Royal Theater.
- Orsik warns Zymorven not to leave the tower, go out at night, or enter shadows due to a vampire problem aboard or near the castle.
- Lord Zymorven is alarmed to learn a vampire is nearby; demands the castle be repositioned away from Silverymoon.
- Lord Zymorven is further alarmed to learn a Dracolich is also present.
- The 3HPP recruits Urgala Meltimer on the 2nd day of the 2nd tenday of Eleint 1492.
- The 3HPP visits Darthara Shendrel to recruit her; she is deceived into believing she is speaking to Orsik's father.
- Darthara expresses displeasure at the 3HPP's past actions but credits them with saving Triboar from Cindrell and providing intelligence used against Cindrell.
- Darthara agrees to join the strike team; all debts between her and the 3HPP are declared cleared.
- The 3HPP and allies travel to Silverymoon.
- On the 4th day of the 2nd tenday of Eleint 1492, the strike team conducts reconnaissance on Kaelen's house; Thistle observes it is covered in dust and the door has not been opened in some time; Kaelen is inside, sitting and tapping his fingers, and appears deeply wrong.
- The team decides not to kill or disturb Kaelen to preserve the element of surprise against Bob.
- A city guard encounters Vardis during the reconnaissance; Vardis deflects him successfully using elven privilege.
- The team decides against alerting Silverymoon's leadership before the attack, to avoid panic and prevent Bob from releasing his horde prematurely.
- Lord Zymorven pledges to use his contacts in Silverymoon to ensure the 3HPP receive credit after the mission.
- Unla Kee dispels alarm wards on the Royal Theater entrance.
- The strike team enters the Royal Theater and discovers the interior: the stage is covered in blood and body parts; the apparent audience is composed entirely of zombies dressed in three repeating costumes mimicking the 3HPP; a zombie half-orc with a broken neck works the concession stand; a zombie in an Orsik costume is a Tabaxi with a partially severed neck.
- The "portal" at the theater entrance is revealed to be a false front: theatergoers enter thinking they are entering the theater, but the real portal is inside the theater; the door slams shut after they enter.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Laela's fate**: Thistle believes Laela is near Ironslag and may be a prisoner of Adminond Kroskas, Ilymrith, or another entity. Her status is completely unknown. Thistle chose Silverymoon over rescuing her.
- **Kaelen's condition**: Kaelen is in his house in a deeply wrong state — dust-covered, door unopened, sitting and tapping fingers. It is unknown whether he is magically compelled, undead, possessed, or otherwise incapacitated. Thistle and Vardis suspect he may be connected to or powering Bob; the team chose not to disturb him.
- **Bob's zombie horde**: Bob has created an audience of zombies dressed as the 3HPP inside the Royal Theater. The nature of this mimicry (deliberate mockery, magical preparation, or something else) is unexplained.
- **The false portal**: Theatergoers are being trapped by a false portal at the theater entrance. What happens to them afterward is not established in this chunk.
- **The Red Wizard and Drow alliance**: A Red Wizard and Drow are collaborating with Bob. Their identities, goals, and exact roles in the zombie creation are unresolved.
- **The vampire**: A vampire is associated with or traveling near Blagothkus' castle. Its identity, origin, and current location are not specified. Orsik warned Zymorven not to go out at night or into shadows because of it.
- **The Dracolich**: A Dracolich is also nearby. Its identity and origin are not specified. Unla Kee's private plan would have used it as a distraction.
- **Unla Kee's secret knowledge**: Unla Kee believes he alone knows how to stop the end of the world, and also that the world does not end — unless he decides otherwise. The nature of this knowledge and the "end of the world" threat are entirely unresolved.
- **Orsik's disguise**: Darthara Shendrel, Lord Zymorven, and Urgala Meltimer all believe they are working with Orsik's father, not Orsik himself. Vardis is knowingly complicit. Discovery of this deception is a pending thread.
- **Darthara's suspicions**: Darthara noted something "off" about the elder Orsik's intelligence — she does not yet realize she is speaking to Orsik himself. Whether she figures this out is unresolved.
- **Lord Zymorven's son**: Referenced as having been saved by Orsik from "a terrible set of choices and death"; the specifics of what he did and the full consequences are not detailed in this chunk.
- **Zymorven's own son's failing**: Zymorven obliquely states he and "Orsic" have in common the need to cover a son's failings. The nature of his son's failing is unresolved.
- **Unla Kee's original "unleash chaos" plan**: Unla Kee repeatedly advocates for using the Dracolich and vampire as a cover, and has a fully formed private plan involving framing the Cult of the Dragon and using Laeral Silverhand as a patsy. This plan was rejected but Unla Kee has not abandoned the idea.
- **Krowen Valharrow's hostility**: The Harper leader is actively hostile to the 3HPP. Whether this creates future complications — especially given that Darthara has now joined a 3HPP mission without Harper authorization — is unresolved.
- **Lord Aerasume's potential reaction**: Unla Kee raises the possibility that Silverymoon's lord may want to eliminate the 3HPP as inconvenient witnesses to the fact that a demon operated a zombie factory in his theater next to the palace.
- **The 3HPP's credit/recognition**: Orsik desires public recognition for the mission; Lord Zymorven has promised to handle this through his Silverymoon contacts. Whether this actually occurs is unresolved.
- **Adminond Kroskas**: Referenced as a possible captor of Laela; identity and nature unknown.
- **Ilymrith**: Referenced as a possible captor or tormentor of Laela; identity and nature unknown.
