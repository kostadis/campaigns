## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Eye of the All-Father**: The party is being led by Harshnag to this ancient giant temple beneath the Spine of the World to consult a divine oracle about ending the giants' threat to the North. They have not yet arrived.
- **King Hekaton's disappearance**: The Storm Giant king has vanished. The cause and current whereabouts are unknown. His disappearance is the trigger for giant leaders waging war.
- **The end of the Ordning**: The giant caste system created by the god Amn has collapsed. Why and how it ended has not been explained in this session.
- **Countess Sansuri**: Described by Harshnag as "not so much" a good person. Her goals and role in the broader giant crisis are unspecified but implied to be antagonistic.
- **Count Thullen**: A good-aligned Cloud Giant and younger brother of Countess Sansuri. Orsik expressed interest in him as a potential contact. No approach has been made.
- **The flying castle/tower**: Unla Kee inquired about making a castle fly; Count Thullen was mentioned as a possible (if laughable to Harshnag) Cloud Giant contact for this. The thread is open.
- **The enemy Frost Giant leading the goblinoid attack**: A Frost Giant led goblins, hobgoblins, and ogres against Xantharl's Keep. Harshnag fought this giant. The identity, allegiance, and fate of this Frost Giant are unresolved.
- **Narbeck Horn's investigation of the party**: Narbeck noted he would "look into" the party's apparent connection to Harshnag (the second Frost Giant who attacked the first). His intentions are unclear.
- **Weevil's captor**: Weevil has been captured. What happens next — delivery, reward collection, potential complications — is unresolved.
- **The bugbear group's connection to Weevil**: The bugbears appeared to be attempting to capture Weevil themselves, presumably for the reward. Who sent them or organized them is unknown.
- **Krowen Valharrow's broader plan**: Krowen arranged the party's involvement and their access to teleportation circles. The full scope of his agenda regarding the Ordning's end is not disclosed.
