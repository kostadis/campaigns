- Sandesyl recounted the history of Blagothkus' castle, including its alliance with the Cult of the Dragon, the First Faction's seizures, the crash in the north, Blagothkus' journey to the Spine of the World, and his return to find Eigeron dead
- The party departed the vampire's tower and proceeded to the stern tower (Blagothkus' chambers)
- At Blagothkus' tower, the party found the chambers in shambles and encountered two ogre zombie guards wearing distinctive plumed helmets, not under Sandesyl's control
