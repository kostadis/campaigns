- Unla Kee cast Rime's Binding Ice, killing four kobolds.
- Thistle flew toward the dragon's position and hid.
- Orsik located a previously ignored corridor and crawled through it to flank the dragon.
- More kobolds appeared; one critically missed an attack.

- The dragon spotted Thistle and attempted to reposition; Thistle fired an arrow that missed; the dragon breathed on Thistle, who avoided most of the damage.

- Orsik climbed the last 5 feet and killed the dragon with his sword.
- Thistle fired additional arrows into the dragon's corpse.
- Orsik and Thistle returned to the dragon's hoard, approximately 300 feet away, and retrieved: 2,000 gp, fourteen gems worth 250 gp each, a potion of mind reading, a bag of beans, a potion of superior healing, and a peculiar star-shaped gem.
- Unla Kee harvested the dragon's body: dragon meat, dragon blood (intended for potions of dragon strength), dragon teeth (intended for dragon arrows for Thistle), dragon bone (intended for dragon weapons), and a dragon horn (intended for crafting a dragon wand).
- Orsik declared the group would not continue deeper, citing depleted spell resources and near-death experience.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Kraken**: Mary warned that the Kraken will return to destroy Everlund using tentacles and mind control, and will bring powerful allies. The nature, location, timeline, and identity of these allies are unknown. The party's mission is implicitly connected to preparing for this threat.
- **The Star-Shaped Gem**: A peculiar gem in the shape of a star was found in the dragon's hoard. Its nature, origin, magical properties, and significance are entirely unexplained.
- **Netherese Artifacts**: Orsik explicitly stated that the Netherese Mountain Range contains Netherese Artifacts, described as the most powerful magic items in the realms. The party did not find any confirmed Netherese Artifacts this session; it is unclear whether any items retrieved (particularly the star-shaped gem) qualify.
- **Dragon's Kobold Followers**: The kobolds appeared to "blink into existence" repeatedly, suggesting teleportation or a spawning mechanism. The source of this ability is unexplained.
- **Orsik's Halved Strength**: Orsik's strength was reduced by half by the dragon's lightning breath. Whether this is a temporary or lasting condition is not addressed.
- **Harvested Dragon Parts**: Unla Kee intends to craft potions of dragon strength, dragon arrows, dragon weapons, and a dragon wand from the harvested materials. Whether a crafter or location is available to do so is unresolved.
- **Continuation of the Expedition**: Orsik called a halt to further exploration. Whether the party returns to this cavern complex or the broader mountain range, and what else may lie deeper inside, remains open.
