## World Events

- The Cloud Castle departed from the collapsing Eye of the All-Father.
- Escarlotta expressed frustration at lacking precise navigational coordinates; the party shared Oracle's information to assist.
- On the 7th day of the first tenday of Eleint 1492, Escarlotta again requested more precise directions; Vardis prayed to Correllon for divine guidance and was answered only with bird droppings.
- Thistle, standing outside the castle, spotted a village on a mountain spire and identified the location as Ironslag / Mount Hamarhaast ahead.
- Orsik overruled Thistle's suggestion of a subtle approach via the stairway and insisted on a "show of force," convincing Escarlotta to glide the castle over the village.
- The Cloud Castle's front gate opened; the party descended into the Yakfolk village and stood in the yurt nearest the iron gate.
- A young, frightened moon elf delivered Chief Kartha-Kaya's summons; Thistle noted she appeared afraid for the party.
- Despite initial reluctance, the party (minus Orsik) entered the Chief's hut and ate the food after Orsik tested it and confirmed it was not poisoned.
- Chief Kartha-Kaya insisted on a post-meal cleansing ritual; Thistle and Vardis objected but the Chief insisted.
- Orsik refused to join the ritual and stood outside; the Chief made snide remarks about dwarves.
- The Yakfolk (Kartha-Kaya, Imberu, Nahala) formed a hand-holding circle with Thistle, Vardis, and Unla Kee and attempted mental possession of the three heroes during the ritual.
- Orsik noticed something was wrong and warned the party; the warned heroes were better able to repel the possession attempt.
- The failed possession triggered an open melee between the party and the Yakfolk.
