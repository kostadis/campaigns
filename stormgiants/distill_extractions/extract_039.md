- **Laela's whereabouts**: Thistle's lost love was last seen heading east to the Lurkwood after reportedly massacring a group of brigands surrounding a caravan. The identity of the young man she left alive in the wagon is unknown. Her current location and condition are unresolved.
- **The identity of the young man in the wagon**: A young man was the only other survivor of Laela's caravan massacre. His identity and significance are unknown.
- **Orsik's past disgrace**: References are made to a time when Orsik was called a disgrace by an old dwarf and to a past version of himself that would have taken The Weevil's offer. The specifics of this disgrace are not elaborated upon in this chunk.
- **Giant uprising and the sundering of the Ordning**: Multiple giant factions (Frost, Hill, Stone, Fire) are simultaneously raiding and searching for artifacts. The cause of the Ordning's sundering and the nature of the artifacts being sought are unresolved.
- **Frost Giants' artifact at Bryn Shander**: Frost Giants reportedly destroyed Bryn Shander searching for an artifact. The artifact's identity and whether they found it are unknown.
- **Duke Zalto's artifact search**: Fire Giants under Duke Zalto are scouring lands for an artifact. Its identity and Zalto's progress are unresolved.
- **Harshnag's role in the giant war**: Harshnag is clearly monitoring the giant war and is deeply concerned. His own allegiances and intentions regarding the conflict are not yet stated explicitly.
- **Xaltivar's motives**: Xaltivar orchestrated the attack on Xanthalar's Keep to capture The Weevil. Why Xaltivar wanted The Weevil specifically is unresolved.
- **Tack Proudfoot's betrothal to Clan Battlehammer**: Orsik's disguise as John Husteem has now intersected with leading Mirabar noble clans. Whether Orsik's deception will be discovered or have future consequences is unresolved.
- **Unla Kee's gambling losses**: Unla Kee has lost approximately half his reward money. Whether this has lasting consequences for him or the group is unresolved.
