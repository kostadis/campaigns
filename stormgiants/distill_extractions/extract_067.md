## World Events

- The 3HPP ascended from the lower rooms to the great hall of the underwater stronghold.
- The party observed a social gathering of multiple giant types (cloud, fire, frost, stone) in the great hall.
- Mirran and Nym were performing music — Mirran singing, Nym playing a living hulking crab as a percussion instrument.
- Orsik interrupted the performance by entering the hall and announcing he had come via a "Dwarf and Giants that Love Each Other" matchmaking service to meet Queen Serissa.
- Mirran played along, telling Orsik that Serissa was busy with state affairs and would need time to prepare.
- Nym made contemptuous remarks about Serissa's interest in small folk and referenced their deceased/absent mother's similar interest.

- Mirran arrived at the guest rooms and lured the party toward the large room with the pool and teleportation circle.
- Mirran directed four giant lords present in the room to kill the party, framing them as assassins in Giant.
- Thistle advised the party not to kill Mirran, reasoning that her death would validate her accusation in Serissa's eyes.
- Combat began; Unla Kee's chain lightning struck the fire giant and ricocheted to three others.
- The four giant lords identified themselves: Duchess Tartha, Count Vaal, Jarl Hellenhild, and Thane Braxow.
- Orsik cast Expeditious Retreat and attacked Thane Braxow four times, knocking him prone and killing him.
- Vardis cast Wall of Fire across the hallway entrance, separating the party from the giants.
- Count Vaal flew, bypassing the Wall of Fire.
- Duchess Tartha walked through the Wall of Fire unharmed and slashed Thistle.
- Thistle revealed a previously hidden ability, breathing ice on Duchess Tartha.
- Mirran cast a lightning bolt as she departed, striking Vardis and chaining to Unla Kee.
- Jarl Hellenhild threw a boulder at flying Thistle; it missed.
- Unla Kee cast Fireball between Count Vaal and Duchess Tartha; Tartha was unaffected (absorbed the fire); Vaal and Hellenhild were seared.
- Orsik struck Duchess Tartha twice; the sword's magic was resisted on the first strike; the second knocked her prone.
- Unla Kee noted that cloud giants can cast Misty Step, alarming the party.
