## Locations

**The Wizard's Tower (submerged in the swamp)**
- What it is: An ancient wizard's tower that has sunk into a swamp; its roof is now an island
- Floors encountered (top to bottom): Roof/Mama Mia's bedroom → puzzle landing → shrine of Mystara → pillared hall with elemental altars (Grumbar, Istishia, Akadi, Kossuth) → gargoyle guest room → shrines of Azuth, Deneir, and Oghma → lower antechamber → elemental wall gauntlet rooms → mold room → laboratory
- Current state: Structurally intact but ancient; dust, mold, and decay throughout; original openings filled with earth and mud; air is stale and dry; no insects or spiders present; multiple magical traps still functional

**Shrine of Mystara**
- What it is: A 30' × 25' room with a prismatic will-o'-wisp hovering over an offering bowl atop a pillar; walls covered in murals of a woman performing magical feats; fresco of a blue seven-pointed star
- Current state: Intact; tourmaline remains in offering bowl

- What it is: A hall with dancing rainbow light, scintillating walls of light anchored by pillars; contains four elemental altars (Grumbar/earth – jasper geode bowl; Istishia/water – quartz wave bowl; Akadi/air – tornado-shaped tornado-eye bowl; Kossuth/fire – flame-shaped crystal brazier)
- Current state: Altars functional; three of four blessings successfully obtained; Kossuth's altar did not respond to party's attempts

**Gargoyle Guest Room**
- What it is: A room with wood-paneled walls, piles of rocks, scattered statues, fine gravel floor; contains gargoyles bound to protect the tower; has a false secret door (southeast) and a locked southern door

- **Identity of "Bad Fuul"**: Referenced by Unla Kee; no information provided in this session.

- **The two magmin**: Appeared after Unla Kee triggered the glyph trap; the encounter is unresolved.
- **The arcane circle of symbols in the elemental wall rooms**: Its purpose has not been determined.
- **Unla Kee's cipher ("Bad Hand Writing")**: Contents unknown to all but Unla Kee (and partially to Oghma).
