## Threads & Mysteries

- **Forbiddance on the cloud castle**: The Kraken Society arranged for a powerful cleric to cast forbiddance over most of the 3HPP's cloud castle. How the Society communicated this order so quickly across long distances is unexplained. Unla Kee privately suspects a hive-mind link among Kraken members but has not confirmed this or shared it.
- **Kraken Society hive mind theory**: Unla Kee suspects that the death of Lord Drylund was triggered externally, possibly via a hive-mind link among Kraken members. This is unconfirmed.
- **Escarlotta and the cloud castle**: Escarlotta is connected to the cloud castle in a way that is undesirable to Unla Kee. Her identity, nature, and how she came to be associated with the castle are unexplained.
- **Vardis and Lorellon**: Vardis has been denied access to Lorellon's temples and apparently requires some form of apology or reconciliation. The cause of this estrangement is not stated in this chunk.
- **The Giant lord problem**: An unresolved political-military crisis affecting both Giants and northern human kingdoms. Hekaton will only accept the 3HPP's handling of it as a condition for a peace alliance with the Lord's Alliance. The nature of "his problem" (the Giant lords) is not detailed in this chunk.
- **The SEER's final note**: The SEER asked Orsik to consider "doing the realms a favor" after defeating Iymrith. The nature of this favor is unspecified; presumably related to the Giant lord alliance.
- **Anomalous blue dragons**: Two blue dragons with purple worm lower halves have emerged from the sand in Ascore, surrounding the 3HPP. Whether these are Iymrith's agents, guardians, hybrid creatures, or something else is unknown. The nature of the hybrid form is unexplained.
- **Iymrith's lair**: The ancient blue dragon's lair is near Ascore; the 3HPP is about to attempt to fight or approach it. The outcome is unresolved.
- **Orsik's "girl"**: Orsik refers to saving "his girl" as a motivation; the identity, location, and current state of this person are not established in this chunk.
- **The unnamed Kraken Society member who escaped Yartar**: A high-ranking member of the Kraken Society survived the Yartar incident and is at large. Their current plans and location are unknown.
- **Uthgardtian Barbarian raids and Giant activity**: Merchant rumors report both are occurring in the Silver Marches region. The 3HPP expressed doubt; veracity and connection to broader events are unconfirmed.
- **The guard who recognized "Orsic"**: A caravan guard specifically asked if Orsik was "Orsic," suggesting the name is known or notorious in the region. The origin and implications of this recognition are unresolved; Orsik denied his identity.
- **Ascore's red pyramids**: Uncle Joon warned Thistle to avoid the red pyramids in Ascore. The reason for this warning is unstated.
- **Ascore's reputation for ancient evil**: The city is described as haunted by ancient evil. The nature of this evil is unspecified.
