## World Events

- Orsik, seeking to leverage his Zhentarim renown, arranged a meeting with local Zhentarim agent Urlam Stockspool, owner of The Triboar Travelers.
- Urlam agreed to meet at the Talking Troll and arrived with a crew of 10 Zhentarim members, whose drinks Orsik paid for.
- Thistle asked Urlam about Adimond Kroskas; Urlam revealed Kroskas was a free agent specialising in creating hybrid magical creatures, formerly associated with groups seeking to enable Bane's conquest of creation, and that all people who knew him are reportedly dead.
- Thistle asked about House Margaster; Urlam revealed that Margaster cultists killed some of his guards when he objected to their demand for a discount.
- Urlam disclosed that House Margaster is officially untouchable by Zhentarim high command.
- Urlam stated that Orthovir has a relationship with House Margaster and implied Orthovir set the party up.
- The party's cleric (normally taciturn) declared that Orthovir "set us up."
- Thistle awkwardly asked Urlam about contraband, angering him; the situation was defused by Orsik ordering another round.
- Urlam offered the party 200 gold (Zhentarim friends and family discount) to deal with House Margaster, with Orsik agreeing.
- While camped in a clearing off the road to Xanthalar's Keep, the party was approached by Harshnag the Grim.
- Harshnag revealed the ordning has been broken, storm giants no longer have power to keep lesser giants in check, and King Hekaton is missing.
- Harshnag stated that Krowen Valharrow had told him the party agreed to undertake the giant quest.
- Harshnag proposed guiding the party to the Eye of the All-Father, a mountain temple under the Spine of the World containing a divine oracle.
- The party negotiated with Harshnag; he agreed to accompany them to Xanthalar's Keep first to claim the Weevil bounty in exchange for a 100 gold share.
