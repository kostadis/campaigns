## Threads & Mysteries

- **King Hekaton's disappearance**: The 3HPP's central mission. A coin found at or near the site of King Hekaton's disappearance may link to Lord Drylund or the Grand Dame. Orsik began to raise this question with Drylund at the chapter's end; outcome unknown.
- **Lord Drylund's connection to King Hekaton**: The nature of any link between a minor political figure manipulating votes in Yartar and the disappearance of a Storm Giant King remains entirely unclear.
- **Lady Atalia's identity and allegiances**: She is a telepath who cheats at Sava aboard the Grand Dame. Her faction, employer, and reasons for being on the boat are unknown. She has agreed to meet the 3HPP privately after the boat returns to dock.
- **The private meeting with Lady Atalia**: Scheduled to occur when the Grand Dame returns to dock. What information she possesses and what she wants in return is unknown.
- **Lord Drylund's "associates"**: Drylund arranged for the 3HPP to meet unspecified associates in a different private location. Their identities and significance are unknown.
- **The Waterbaron election**: The current Waterbaron is alive but politically weakened. No timeline for Drylund becoming Waterbaron exists. The 3HPP has tentatively allied with Drylund, which may have future political consequences.
- **Lord Zymorven's son's rescue**: A group of adventurers previously freed Lord Zymorven's son (who had disappeared along with other nobles) and magically returned him to his father. Whether this group is the 3HPP is not stated. The incident damaged the current Waterbaron politically.
- **The disappearing nobles**: A rash of noble disappearances in Yartar is referenced. The mechanism (not being thrown from windows, per Drylund's deflection) and perpetrators are unknown.
- **The Champagne de Stomp**: Described as the last known bottle outside of Ravenloft. It was gifted to Drylund, who did not recognise its rarity, and subsequently drank it. Its significance and the reference to Ravenloft may carry future implications.
- **Weapons surrender**: The 3HPP was asked to surrender weapons upon boarding the Grand Dame. Orsik resolved this by noting he had "other options." The nature of these options is not specified.
- **The 3HPP's political enemies**: Multiple unnamed cities are described as not being on the 3HPP's side. These are not identified.
- **Vardis's expulsion from the Cult of Correlon**: Referenced as backstory but not elaborated upon; potential unresolved thread.
