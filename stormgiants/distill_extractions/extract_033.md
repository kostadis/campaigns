## World Events

- The 3HPP met Eltio at the Wink and the Kiss in Yartar and received intelligence on Harthal and the Giant Slayer sword.
- Brenner, captain of the guard, was ambushed and killed by the 3HPP in Yartar's central market; all but one of his accompanying guards were also killed; he was carrying the Giant Slayer sword.
- Eltio warned that Brenner's death would be linked to Harthal's friends, triggering his execution, forcing an immediate rescue.
- The 3HPP entered Yartar's sewers and navigated to an abandoned temple of Bane via a secret passageway.
- Unla Kee nearly entered a pool containing a water weird in the temple of Bane.
- Thistle identified a triangular trap formed by markings on the south wall and two pillars; the party circumvented it.
- The 3HPP located and passed through the secret door in the temple of Bane into the prison complex.
- The 3HPP killed the prison guards using fireball; a dying guard declared "you have no idea what you have done."
- The party searched prison storage chests: found a great sword, a doodled spell book, a note about a prisoner whose family removed his possessions, a potion of healing, coins, cheap gems; a second (magically trapped) chest yielded more silver and a potion of greater healing.
- The 3HPP entered the vampire's chamber — richly appointed, containing zombies, a skeleton on a table, and bookshelves.
- Orsik (disguised as Lord Zymorven) negotiated Harthal's release from the vampire by supplying the corpses of the slain guards.
- The vampire warned Harthal he must leave Yartar that night or she would be obligated to kill him upon any return.
- The vampire revealed she is not constrained by the Waterbarron's guards and moves freely through the city.
- Harthal was reunited with the disguised Orsik (believing him to be his father) and escorted out of the prison.
- Harthal recognized Thistle Wendrod and revealed that Leila had manipulated him into stealing the sword and going on an adventure; Harthal killed someone during the adventure; Leila then abandoned him to find Thistle.
- Leila came to Yartar to join the Hand of Yartar, found them too locally focused, and left to seek other means of finding Thistle.
- The 3HPP and Harthal arrived at the Harper headquarters in Yartar, where Orsik dropped the Lord Zymorven disguise.
- The 3HPP met Gauche the Young, the new head of the Yartar Harper cell, and learned of Kolbaz's death.
- Thistle examined the destruction near the teleportation circle — determined the cause was something massive materializing and dematerializing, not a giant.
- Thistle discovered from Kolbaz's research papers that Adimond Kroskas created the Drake-creation ritual used by the Cult of Tiamat, and that "Adimond Kroskas" is an alias.
- Thistle informed Gauche of his suspicion that Adimond Kroskas killed Kolbaz; Gauche agreed to prioritize investigating Kolbaz's death but explicitly refused to investigate Adimond Kroskas.
- The 3HPP and Harthal used the teleportation circle to travel from Yartar to the Moongleam Tower in Everlund.
- Orsik briefly forgot he was still disguised as Lord Zymorven upon arrival; Thistle covered with a verbal redirect; Orsik dropped the disguise before Harthal noticed.
- Nespril Menk received the 3HPP at Moongleam Tower and offered a secret sewer exit to avoid Zhentarim surveillance.
- Orsik probed Nespril about the security book; Nespril refused and warned him off; a verbal confrontation about the Ezra model followed.
- Thistle and Orsik delivered the Letter of Mark to Dral Thelev at Danivarr's House in Everlund.
- Dral Thelev revealed that Horix Zoar can get the 3HPP into Silverymoon as diplomats of Everlund, protecting them from local police.
- The 3HPP departed north on Rauvin Road toward Zymorven Hall.
- On the second day north of Silverymoon, the 3HPP were attacked by Crag Cats.
- (Sidebar) Zhentarim agents in Yartar discussed Orsik approvingly, noting they knew the sword's location and would have helped had he asked.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Adimond Kroskas's true identity**: "Adimond Kroskas" is confirmed to be an alias; his real identity is unknown; he is suspected of killing Kolbaz; Gauche has explicitly refused to investigate him, leaving this thread unresolved within the Harpers.
- **What killed Kolbaz**: Something massive materialized near the teleportation circle in Yartar Harper HQ, destroyed the room, and dematerialized; Thistle ruled out giant; Gauche theorizes demonic entity or wizard; possible connection to Adimond Kroskas uninvestigated by official channels.
- **Drake-creation ritual**: Adimond Kroskas created the ritual the Cult of Tiamat used to first produce Drakes; implications of his continued activity are unresolved.
- **The vampire's experiments**: She is conducting controlled experiments on condemned prisoners, sanctioned by the highest authorities in Yartar; the nature, purpose, and full extent of these experiments are unknown; what the dying guard meant by "you have no idea what you have done" is unresolved.
- **The Waterbarron of Yartar**: Implicitly complicit in or ignorant of the vampire's experiments; his relationship to the vampire and to the broader power structure of Yartar is unexplored.
- **Leila's whereabouts and mission**: Leila left Yartar after the Hand of Yartar proved too locally focused; she is searching for Thistle; her current location and method of search are unknown; Thistle's feelings for her are confirmed as significant.
- **The Giant Slayer sword**: Recovered from Brenner; its current handling or disposition is not explicitly stated after the fight; it was the original objective for Harthal's mission.
- **Payment from Lord Zymorven**: The 3HPP have not yet collected; traveling to Zymorven Hall for this purpose.
- **Crag Cat attack**: The party was attacked on the second day north of Silverymoon; outcome not described in this session.
- **Entry into Silverymoon**: The 3HPP may need to enter Silverymoon; Horix Zoar can provide diplomatic cover; whether and when this becomes necessary is unresolved.
- **Zhentarim knowledge of the sword**: The Zhentarim knew the Giant Slayer sword's location and would have helped Orsik obtain it; this implies a deeper Zhentarim interest in the 3HPP or their mission that has not been explained or acknowledged by the party.
- **Nespril's security book**: Documents the random system the Harpers use to evade Zhentarim surveillance in Everlund; Orsik probed for it and was warned off; potential future point of contention.
- **What Harthal did**: He killed someone during his adventure with Leila; the identity of the victim and any consequences are unresolved.
- **The dying guard's warning**: "You have no idea what you have done" — the meaning of this statement is unclear; possibly tied to the vampire's experiments or to a larger power structure within the prison.
