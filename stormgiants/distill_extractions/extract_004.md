## World Events

- The party hiked through mountains for several days in an unusually warm spring, arriving at an abandoned dwarven outpost on the 5th day of the 1st tenday of Taraskh.
- The party entered the outpost following Veradda Stoor's notes and discovered disturbed dust and cobwebs indicating recent heavy foot traffic.
- The party encountered four kobolds manning a barricade in an entry room; a kobold (Nug) immediately challenged them.
- Hidden kobolds attempted to ambush the party with falling stones; the ambush failed due to the kobolds making too much noise.
- The party defeated the kobolds in the barricade room, killing several with a sleep spell and subsequent combat.
- Agra and Gen emerged from the stone building and surrendered.
- Agra recognised Thistle's Cult of the Dragon torture scars and admitted past service to the Cult under orders.
- Agra disclosed that giant factions (Fire, Hill, Cloud) have been recruiting former Cult kobolds following the defeat of the dragons.
- Agra disclosed that his former Red Dragon master made a deal with Cloud Giants and that his group fled when the Cloud Giants arrived.
- Agra disclosed that the Fire Giants sent his group to recover cultist treasure from this outpost.
- Agra disclosed the existence of a fire symbol, a river of lava, and a treasure in the southern room.
- The party allowed Agra and Gen to leave in exchange for treasure and information.
- Orsik entered the southern forge room first, then sent Thistle ahead.
- Thistle entered the forge room, identified the fire hazards, and began attacking the fire symbol with arrows.
- A magmin emerged from the fire symbol in response to Thistle's attack.
- Additional magmin emerged from the lava, surrounding Thistle.
- Unla Kee used Thunderwave to push magmin away from Thistle; they exploded harmlessly.
- Orsik killed a magmin with a thrown hand-axe.
- A dying magmin exploded in Thistle's face; another left burning lava on him.
- Vardis healed Thistle.
- Thistle continued attacking the fire symbol, cast a spell, and deactivated the symbol using knowledge recalled from Uncle Yorn.
- A final magmin emerged from the lava before the symbol was deactivated.
- Orsik killed the final magmin; its explosion burned Thistle who was standing nearby.
- The smoke cleared from the forge room once the symbol became inert.
- Orsik identified the room as a former forge corrupted by an evil artifact.
- The party searched the room and collected silver and magmin shells.
