## World Events

- The 3HPP departed Glazhael's lair after she refused to reveal her plan for defeating Sandesyl.
- The 3HPP resolved to destroy potential undead allies of Sandesyl starting in the lower courtyard of Blagothkus' castle.
- The party discovered skeletal remains scattered throughout the lower courtyard.
- Orsik collected all skeletal remains in a bag of holding and transported them to the drawbridge gateway.
- The remains were dumped overboard from the castle; Unla Kee and Vardis burned them with spells as they fell.
- The burning of the corpses caused a visible fire in the midday sky over the Evermoors, noted as a significant visible event.
- Blagothkus, watching from the upper courtyard, expressed gratitude, stating he had been seeking someone to do this for nearly a year.
- The party entered the port-side barracks and found it previously looted and burned by another adventuring group; no treasure remained except scattered coins found by Thistle.
- Thistle's True Seeing revealed that some corpses in the barracks were dragonwings, not just dwarves, indicating the castle previously changed hands from the cult.
- The party entered the starboard stables and discovered two animate, decaying undead wyverns.
- Unla Kee cast Fireball (3rd level), hitting one wyvern fully and partially hitting the other.
- Vardis cast Turn Undead, causing both wyverns to flee to opposite sides of the stables.
- Thistle attacked a wyvern with arrows; lightning damage was ineffective due to immunity.
- Unla Kee used an unidentified artifact of wonder, which transported a pile of hay to the ethereal plane.
- Vardis cast a radiant lance; Orsik struck the glowing wyvern with his green blade, killing it.
- Unla Kee cast Magic Missile, dealing damage to the remaining wyvern.
- Vardis cast Sacred Flame, further damaging the second wyvern.
- The second wyvern attempted to flee toward the stern side of the stables.
- Thistle struck the fleeing wyvern with two arrows; Thuul used an acid burst.
- Unla Kee cast a spell dealing necrotic damage (described with a funerary bell sound), killing the second wyvern despite its resistance.
- The party looted the stables: Thistle found a copper figurine of Yondalla; Orsik found 120 gold pieces, wrapped sweets, and a cork in saddlebags.
- Vardis cast Detect Magic and Thistle used his magical eye to examine the cork; it was determined to be non-magical.
- Unla Kee revealed that the undead wyverns may return unless their phylacteries are destroyed, and that Sandesyl is experimenting on the undead to create alternatives to dracoliches.
- The party collected and burned the wyvern remains overboard using the same method as the skeletal remains.
- Thistle examined the remaining gatehouse rooms and found them empty with no corpses.
- The party entered an abandoned kitchen in the lower courtyard; Unla Kee's brash entrance triggered an undead encounter.
- Multiple specters detached from shadows and advanced on the party, including two that appeared notably different and one that manifested as a spectral raven.
- Orsik struck the spectral raven before the others could warn him.
- Two specters attacked Orsik, draining his life force with negative energy.
- Vardis used a warning flare, thwarting one specter with bright light.
- The spectral raven flew to the far end of the room and cawed, charming both Orsik and Unla Kee, leaving them stunned and prone.
- A specter attacked the stunned Unla Kee, critically wounding him.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Glazhael's secret plan**: Glazhael refused to reveal her strategy for defeating Sandesyl. The party is proceeding on trust alone. What is her plan, and does it conflict with the party's survival?
- **Sandesyl's phylactery experiments**: Unla Kee revealed Sandesyl is conducting broader experiments on undead to create alternatives to dracoliches, using phylactery-like mechanics. The destroyed wyverns may return. Are there more such experimental undead in the castle?
- **The spectral raven**: Orsik identified it as uniquely dangerous based on childhood stories. Its nature, origin, and connection to Sandesyl's experiments are unresolved. Two specters also appeared notably different from the others.
- **Current combat unresolved**: Orsik and Unla Kee are stunned and prone; Unla Kee is critically wounded; the spectral raven and multiple specters remain active in the kitchen.
- **Sunbeam spell**: The party is conserving a sunbeam spell for a later, presumably more significant battle—likely against Sandesyl or Sandesyl's primary undead forces.
- **The castle's history**: Dragonwing corpses suggest the Cult of the Dragon previously held the castle, and another adventuring group cleared part of it. The full history of how the castle changed hands is unresolved.
- **Artifact of wonder**: Unla Kee possesses an unidentified artifact of wonder that has produced unpredictable results (hay transported to the ethereal plane). Its full capabilities and remaining uses are unknown.
- **Copper figurine of Yondalla**: Unla Kee identified it as allowing ritual spells to be cast in half the time. Its prior owner and significance in this location are unexplained.
- **Remaining undead in the castle**: Blagothkus confirmed undead prefer darkness, implying more remain in unlit areas of the castle not yet explored.
- **The fire in the Evermoors sky**: The midday burning was visible across the Evermoors. Whether this drew any attention from outside parties is unaddressed.
