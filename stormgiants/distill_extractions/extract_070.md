## Threads & Mysteries

- **Iymrith's escape and location**: Iymrith fled through the Vortex wounded and is at large. Her lair location is unknown to all except King Hekaton, who is himself missing.
- **King Hekaton's disappearance**: The king set out to investigate Queen Neri's death and has not returned. His location is unknown.
- **Queen Neri's murder**: She was killed by small folk on a Sword Coast island. The Golden Goose token links her killers to Lord Drylund's Grand Dame gambling boat in Yartar. The full circumstances and who ordered or carried out her death remain unresolved.
- **Lord Drylund and the Grand Dame**: The token from the Grand Dame connects Lord Drylund's establishment to Queen Neri's death. Unla Kee has been paid to investigate; his actual intentions and methods are unclear.
- **Leela's fate**: Thistle's girlfriend was taken by Adimond Kroskas when he fled. Her current location and condition are unknown. Orsik speculated she may be a fairy-dragon hybrid produced by Iymrith's experiments; Thistle disputes this.
- **Thuul's current whereabouts**: Escaped with Thistle when the cult collapsed; current location not established.
- **Iymrith's hybridization experiments**: Adimond/Iymrith has been conducting fairy/dragon hybridization experiments as part of a deal with Tiamat; rumoured to have created Drakes; experiments are ongoing and captives including Leela may be involved. Serissa stated only Hekaton knows where Iymrith lives, implying the experiments continue at her lair.
- **Mirren and Nym's deal with Iymrith**: What exactly they promised or offered Iymrith in exchange for magical weapons has not been revealed.
- **The crystal ball**: Orsik secretly took a crystal ball from a giant clam. Its properties and significance are unknown.
- **The pearl**: A pearl worth 500 gold remains in a giant clam in Maelstrom; Thistle said they would retrieve it after dealing with Adimond, which remains unresolved.
- **Unla Kee's "research" and the golden armor**: Serissa knows Unla Kee may not conduct genuine research and has warned of consequences; the tension between payment, deception, and Serissa's awareness is unresolved.
- **The Ordning's shattering**: The collapse of the giant hierarchy and its wider consequences for giant behaviour across the world remain an ongoing background threat.
- **Annam the Allfather's silence**: The storm giant gods and Annam are described as distant and non-responsive; this was a driver of Hekaton's crisis of faith and the Ordning's collapse; no resolution is indicated.
- **Orsik's claimed date with Serissa**: Orsik claimed to Serissa that a date had been arranged; Serissa appeared confused but then considered it useful. Whether this was real or a gambit, and what comes of it, is unresolved.
