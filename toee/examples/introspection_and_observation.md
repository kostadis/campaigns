# Style Example: Introspection and Observation

The masked one had been watching Zephyr and their two new companions, trying to see if anything was off. He noticed nothing.

He suggests that they head to the Moathouse since all that they needed to do had been done.

The journey is uneventful. He takes the road heading east towards Nulb. After a few hours of walking, he sees the track heading to the ruins.

---

### Zinnia

This amateur-hour infiltration of the temple has got to stop. We have a cleric who can't keep his feet on the ground, and instead of using some kind of disguise, we just march around like the kind of intruders anyone who sees here will attack first and speak with the dead later.

But first, we need to kill this Ogre.

My fists pummel the creature. Zephyr and Squoia's blades cut him to size, but not before Calmer almost dies again.

With the dead carcass lying in the doorway, I enter carefully into the room beyond.

That's interesting.

This place looks lived in. And there in the corner is where the Ogre lived.

I look inside the Ogre's nest and discover a magical cloak of elvenkind.

"Now that's even more interesting. Who is the elf that lost this?"

An Ogre needs a lot of food. And so someone has been providing it with food.
And why is that door to the south so heavily barred?

### Zinnia - Mid-day, Planting 19, 579

*There must be a secret door somewhere here. There is too much evidence of a mind that is in charge. *
"We won't find it here."

And he marches to the room next to the cells full of zombies. "Torture room."

The rest of the crew looks bored, but he has an instinct. The place seems long abandoned, covered in dust and cobwebs. *As I thought, these skeletons are not that old. And these torture devices have been used very recently indeed. *Zinnia smiles.

*They torture their victims here, and when they are done torturing them, they leave them for the zombies to feast on. But I have seen so many things in my life as a spy, and this is not the worst of it. *

He looks around and notices a trail of blood, "Gotcha."

A trail of blood catches his eye, disappearing in a pillar to the south.

He taps the pillar, and a door opens, "The secret door." Opening it, he sees a shaft going down. He looks down and whispers, "It's about 30 feet deep, and rungs have been placed into the shaft." He can hear Calmer's anxiety, "Yes, they are new. They will not fall as we go down."

## Zephyr - Morning, Planting 20, 579

The tiefling breathes.

*The undead are finally dead. *

To the east of this hallway are cells from where the zombies fled. To the west, he notices a torture chamber.

*Damn, these poor souls may have been left here for 10 years and turned into zombies. I've heard of worse in the Abyss, but this is bad. *

He looks at Zinnia, but the spy betrays no emotion. No reaction.

Sequoia shouts, "Let's check out the rooms near the stairway that came from upstairs."

*That's interesting. *

"The locks are new, and the hinges oiled. Someone has been here recently."

*Can it be that we found our proof? Another notch in my victory belt? *
