# Style Example: Combat and Consequences

### Sequoia

"Zombies!"

Everyone says they are saying "Brains!" but that's not true. They aren't saying anything. But what happens is the sound that escapes from their decaying vocal cords sounds like "nsazsa," and everyone thinks they are saying Brains!"

I wonder if Calmer has any power over the undead! And yes, he does!

They are turned. We have about a minute to check out what's here.

And that's when I hear it.

"Brains!"
Remember those doors that won't keep anyone inside? Well, they aren't keeping those zombies inside the cell.

Okay, we can handle this. Two zombies shouldn't be a problem.

Except, what exactly is in the other cells?

I can worry about that later.
"Brains!"

There were more zombies in that cell. That's okay. We can handle it.

And then I hear the sound again.
"Brains!"
MORE ZOMBIES?

That's when Zephyr shouts, or is it Zinia? Honestly, I can't tell.

"The other three doors also have zombies!"

"Time to get out of here," I shout. Zephyr's like, "We can take them!"
I look at him and think, the next time, there will be 13 zombies to kill…

But he's not that foolish. And runs for the archway and the stairs that go up.

## Sequoia - Early Afternoon, Planting 19, 579

*My turn. *
Sequoia squeezes through the opening and looks into the room beyond. It's a crypt. Many of the niches are empty. The niche the secret door opened has a coffin that is splintered. *That's odd.*
Examining the coffin more carefully, he notices gnawed and split bones.

*That's not good. *
He looks a bit more carefully into the crypt's main hall.

A man is wearing what was once fine clothing. Sequoia does a double-take. Perhaps this wretched thing was once human, but hunger has replaced humanity. Its withered body seems composed of little more than bruised skin stretched over sinew and bone, but its bulbous belly juts out and sloshes from side to side as it moves. Filth and gore stain its claw-like hands and wizened face. And eyes as black as pitch weep a tarry substance, which a black tongue—slithering from its fanged mouth—licks from its cracked lips.

*There are four of them. I need a cleric. *
"Calmer, get over here; we have ghouls."
