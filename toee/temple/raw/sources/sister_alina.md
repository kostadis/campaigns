# Sister Alina

## Identity
- **Role:** Religious figure at the partially abandoned monastery dedicated to the nature goddess Beory, near Gallows Corner
- **Faction:** The monastery community (possibly cult leadership — unconfirmed)
- **First Appearance:** The party encountered her alongside Brother Eldren, who guided them through the "Path of Righteousness" — a series of moral and physical trials to assess their character and worth

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Unknown beyond her role as a tester and evaluator of the party's worth; deeper motivations remain hidden
- Presents herself as a measured, spiritually authoritative figure — she administered structured trials and offered a combat challenge, suggesting a belief in earned trust
- Whether her outward religious devotion conceals a more sinister agenda has not yet been established at the table

## History with the Party
1. **First Session:** Sister Alina and Brother Eldren led the party through the "Path of Righteousness," evaluating their moral character and cunning. She concluded the trial by pitting them against a feral, demonically-tainted gnoll to test their strength and loyalty.
2. **Second Session:** Identified by the party as a suspected cult leader of the monastery. Ziinnia and one other party member were sent to confront her directly while Brother Eldren ran interference as a distraction. Sister Alina did not appear in person; the confrontation was unresolved by session's end.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The monastery near Gallows Corner
- **Active Plans:** Unknown — possibly alerted to or unaware of the party's infiltration and intent to confront her
- **What the Party Knows:** She is a senior figure at the monastery, ran the trials, and is suspected of being the cult leader
- **What Remains Hidden:** Her true role and allegiances; whether she is genuinely a cult leader, a servant of Beory, or something else entirely; the outcome of the confrontation

## Relationships
- **Brother Eldren:** Close associate; co-administered the Path of Righteousness trials. Eldren was used as a distraction against her during the infiltration plan — nature of their relationship beyond collegial is unknown
- **The Party:** Initially an authority figure and evaluator; now a target of suspicion and direct confrontation
- **Ziinnia:** Specifically dispatched to confront her — no direct interaction recorded yet
