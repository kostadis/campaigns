# Commander Joradin

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Commander; claims authority over a restricted section of the Earth Temple
- **Faction:** Earth Temple (brought in by Dick Rentsch)
- **First Appearance:** Encountered when the party was exploring beyond their sanctioned area in the Earth Temple
- *Also known as: Joradin.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Asserts territorial authority firmly and directly — ordered the party back to their quarters without hesitation.
- Appears to operate with a clear sense of jurisdiction and rank.

## History with the Party
- Confronted the party while they were in a section of the temple outside their permitted area. Acknowledged their recruit status but made clear they were in his domain and sent them back to quarters.
- When the party reported the confrontation to Romag, Romag downplayed Joradin as a newcomer brought in by Dick Rentsch who "didn't yet understand how things worked" — suggesting friction or a power dynamic between factions within the temple.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The restricted section of the Earth Temple he claims authority over.
- **Active Plans:** Maintaining control over his designated area; no further known operations.
- **What the Party Knows:** He has authority (or claims it) over part of the temple; he was brought in by Dick Rentsch.
- **What Remains Hidden:** The nature of his role, what he's actually guarding or overseeing, and whether his authority is recognized by temple leadership beyond Rentsch.

## Relationships
- **Dick Rentsch:** Brought Joradin into the temple; Joradin's apparent patron or superior.
- **Romag:** Tension — Romag dismissed Joradin as a naive newcomer who doesn't understand temple culture, suggesting a factional or hierarchical divide.
- **The Party:** Adversarial first contact; views them as subordinate recruits who overstepped.
