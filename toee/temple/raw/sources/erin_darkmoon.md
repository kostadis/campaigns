# Erin Darkmoon

## Identity
- Role: Brigand leader, operated out of the watchtower
- Faction: Unknown; loosely affiliated with moathouse operations (adversarial)
- First appearance: Captured/interrogated by the party prior to Planting 20, 579; referenced in the midday session of that date by Zinnia

## Personality & Motivations
- Motivated enough by hatred or fear of Lareth the Beautiful to trade intelligence for a promise of his death
- Demonstrated pragmatism under pressure — cooperated with captors in exchange for a specific outcome

## History with the Party
- At some point before Planting 20, 579, the party captured and interrogated Erin at or near the watchtower
- She revealed that a man named **Lareth the Beautiful** runs the moathouse
- She provided this intelligence in exchange for the party's **promise to kill Lareth**

## Current Status

- Not involved in active events as of Planting 20, 579
- **Party knows:** She was a brigand leader, she named Lareth, and they made her a promise
- **Hidden/unknown:** Her current whereabouts, her full history with Lareth, and why she wants him dead

## Relationships
- **Lareth the Beautiful:** Antagonistic; willing to betray him to outsiders
