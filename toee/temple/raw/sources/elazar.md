# Elazar

## Identity
- Role: Druid; teacher of the Rite of the Wicker Giant at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon
- Faction: Circle of the Scarlet Moon (affiliation unconfirmed beyond this context)
- Has not appeared directly; introduced via Eryk Velkori's description

## Personality & Motivations
- Stated goal: restoring nature's balance through the Rite of the Wicker Giant
- True motivations unknown. The Circle's practices have been noted as suspiciously ecstatic and more religious in character than naturally druidic, raising questions about Elazar's actual agenda.

## History with the Party
- No direct interaction. Eryk Velkori named Elazar as the druid teaching the Rite of the Wicker Giant at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon.
- Jaroo observed the Circle of the Scarlet Moon's rites firsthand and found them unsettling in character — more cult-like than druidic tradition would suggest.

## Current Status
- Last known location: Hall of the Scarlet Moon (presumed)
- Believed to be actively conducting or overseeing the Rite of the Wicker Giant
- What the party knows: name, role, stated purpose
- What remains hidden: true identity, real goals, nature of the rite, connection to any broader threat

## Relationships
- **Eryk Velkori:** Spoke of Elazar positively as a teacher; source of the party's only information
- **Jaroo:** No direct contact, but Jaroo's suspicion of the Circle implicitly extends to Elazar as its apparent leader
