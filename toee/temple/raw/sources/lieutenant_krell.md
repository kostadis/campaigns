# Lieutenant Krell

## Identity
- **Role:** Officer, Earth Temple (covert Fire Temple agent)
- **Faction:** Outwardly Earth Temple; true allegiance to the Fire Temple under Alremm
- **First Appearance:** Chapter 17 — assigned the party a raiding mission on behalf of Romag

## Personality & Motivations
- Opportunistic and ideologically flexible; genuinely believed the Fire Temple offered greater rewards and prestige than the Earth Temple's expendable-soldier culture.
- Persuasive and calculating — attempted to flip the party mid-capture with a reasoned pitch about power and glory.
- Desperate when cornered but composed enough to negotiate rather than fight to the death.

## History with the Party
- **Chapter 17:** Delivered a mission from Romag to raid supposed Fire Temple forces. The target was actually an Earth Temple ogre, exposing his sabotage. Krell disappeared before the party returned to report.
- **Chapter 18:** Romag revealed he had suspected Krell and kept him surveilled. Krell had fled into gnoll and bugbear territories and was organizing rebellion against the Earth Temple. During the party's capture mission, Krell was heard inciting gnolls against allied guards, declaring loyalty to the Fire Temple ("The Fire Temple protects its own"). He engaged in battle using a hand-crafted pistol-like crossbow contraption, knocking Calmer unconscious with two shots. Surrounded, he proposed an alliance to destroy the Earth Temple together — the party refused. Subdued with non-lethal force per Romag's orders and delivered to Romag bloodied and unconscious. Executed at a ritual sacrifice, torn apart by four earth elementals after screaming that he had been tricked.

## Current Status
- **Deceased.** Killed by Romag via earth elemental ritual sacrifice.
- No active plans or operations.

## Relationships
- **Romag:** Nominal superior; Romag suspected him early, surveilled him, ordered his capture, and executed him.
- **Alremm / Fire Temple:** True allegiance; served as spy and saboteur on their behalf.
- **Gnolls & Bugbears:** Attempted to leverage them as rebel forces against the Earth Temple.
- **Calmer (party cleric):** Targeted specifically in combat; dealt devastating damage with his custom crossbow pistol.

- N/A — Krell is deceased and was never a recurring ally figure.
