# Elder Kenter

## Identity
- **Role:** Village Elder of Hommlet
- **Faction:** Hommlet village council (head or senior figure)
- **First Appearance:** The party was summoned to his compound after his son Hawthorne reported that they had visited four council members in a single day without presenting themselves to the council collectively.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Maintaining unified leadership in Hommlet; ensuring the bandit threat is addressed through coordinated, council-sanctioned action.
- Kenter presents as measured and politically shrewd — he was not angry at the party's actions but used the meeting to assert authority and set expectations. His blessing of Beory and the shared meal suggest he favors diplomacy and custom over confrontation. His wisdom appears genuine rather than performative.

## History with the Party
1. **First meeting at his compound:** Kenter received the party after his son Hawthorne reported their movements. He outlined the full council membership (Jaroo, Rufus, Burne, Ostler, Captain Zeb Fletcher, Mytch the Miller), characterized the bandit activity as an urgent threat, and praised Hommlet's unified leadership structure. He concluded by making clear that the party is expected to come to him first going forward. He and his wife Valsenora hosted the party with food, wine, and a toast invoking Beory.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** His compound in Hommlet — a well-defended property with an eight-foot stone wall, housing his wife Valsenora, his four sons, their families, and servants.
- **Active:** Has established himself as the party's primary point of contact within Hommlet's power structure.
- **What the party knows:** Full council membership; his expectation of being approached first; his concern about the bandits.
- **What remains hidden:** His deeper motivations, his relationship to the other council members' agendas, and the extent of his actual authority versus each council member's independence.

## Relationships
- **Valsenora** — Wife; co-hosted the party alongside him.
- **Hawthorne** — Second son; surveilled the party and reported their movements to Kenter, suggesting Kenter maintains active awareness of strangers in the village.
- **Council members (Jaroo, Rufus, Burne, Ostler, Captain Zeb Fletcher, Mytch the Miller)** — Speaks of them as a unified body; whether he leads or merely coordinates is unclear.
- **The Party** — Has positioned himself as their required first contact; tone was firm but hospitable.
