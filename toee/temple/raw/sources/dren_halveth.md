# Dren Halveth

## Identity
- **Role:** Captain of the Broken Blades, a professional mercenary company
- **Faction:** Nominally affiliated with the Earth Temple (recently recruited)
- **First Appearance:** Chapter 20, Watersteide Hostel in Nulb — questioned the party about their purpose, allegiances, and patron gods

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Securing profitable, sustainable work for his company; protecting his crew from unnecessary risk
- Pragmatic and transactional — weighs temple affiliations by material benefit and survival odds rather than ideology
- Projects controlled authority; his crew mirrors this, relaxed but ready for violence
- Skeptical of propaganda and cannon-fodder arrangements; responds to demonstrated respect and concrete offers

## History with the Party
1. **Ch. 20 — Watersteide Hostel:** Approached the party and interrogated them on their purpose in Nulb. Revealed his company's weapons had been stolen by the Crimson Reapers and tasked the party with recovery.
2. **Ch. 20 — Hostel, continued:** Party returned with intelligence that the Crimson Reapers served a unified organization behind all four elemental temples, delivering a join-or-die ultimatum by midnight. Dren weighed his options, expressing skepticism about the Earth Temple and interest in the Water Temple's money.
3. **Ch. 20 — Recruitment:** Sequioa persuaded him to side with the Earth Temple, arguing the Water Temple relied on propaganda while the Earth Temple demonstrated genuine commitment. The party sweetened the deal by offering shared territory and 100 gold pieces. Dren agreed.
4. **Ch. 20 — Departure:** Water Temple agent Wat whispered something to Dren as the party left; Dren shoved him away, indicating he held to his decision.
5. **Ch. 20 — Earth Temple:** Dren and the Broken Blades arrived successfully. Temple priest Romag assigned them the newly cleared territory under the party's nominal command.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Former gnoll and bugbear quarters, Earth Temple
- **Active:** Settling the Broken Blades into their assigned territory; operating under the party's nominal command per Romag's arrangement
- **What the party knows:** He is loyal to the deal as struck; he rejected the Water Temple's last-minute approach
- **What remains hidden:** His long-term intentions once established — whether he views the Earth Temple arrangement as permanent or merely convenient; the content of Wat's whisper

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Professional alliance; owes them for the recruitment deal and the 100 gp offer; placed under their nominal command by Romag
- **Romag (Earth Temple Priest):** Received his assignment from Romag; relationship is transactional and new
- **Wat (Water Temple Agent, Hostel Barkeep):** Attempted last-minute contact; Dren rejected him physically — suggests active distrust or a clean break
- **Crimson Reapers:** Stole his company's weapons; adversarial, though the fate of that dispute is unresolved in the notes
- **Sequioa (Party Member):** Directly persuaded him; likely holds her in higher regard than other party members
