# Sequoia

## Identity
- Role: Party member (ranger or rogue, implied by arrows and lock-picking)
- Faction: Traveling with the party
- First appearance: Present throughout these sessions; no origin encounter noted in these notes

## Personality & Motivations
- Observational and self-critical — he registers failures of attention (his own and the party's) and files them away rather than externalizing frustration.
- Methodical and reads situations carefully: environmental evidence, behavioral tells, structural patterns.
- Takes quiet satisfaction in clean, efficient work (the finishing blow, the 27 on the lock).

## History with the Party

**Scalding Corridor**
Took damage in the bugbear counterattack. Landed the killing blow on the second bugbear after the barricade fell.

**Ghoul Ambush**
Attempted to set a trap or obstruction at the door as ghouls approached — rolled a 1, triggering the mechanism early and failing to slow them. After Calmer's Turn Undead scattered the remaining ghouls, Sequoia put an arrow into the last fleeing ghoul before it escaped the corridor.

**Second Level — Kelno Observation**
Read the environmental evidence (broken bottles, shoved armor, blood trails without bodies) and concluded the temple war had fractured structurally, not locally. Observed Kelno's goblins and ghouls moving through a hidden door, hauling coin and weapons with organized efficiency. Assessed that Kelno had quietly positioned himself to profit from the conflict while other factions bled each other out. Noted internally that the party had underestimated Kelno — a failure of attention he attributed to themselves.

**Prisoner Chamber**
Picked the lead prisoner's shackles after Zinnia assessed the man as truthful. Rolled 27; opened the lock cleanly. Began preparing to free the remaining four prisoners. Observed the freed prisoner's pause before agreeing to share the Deep Temple route and read it as genuine deliberation rather than performance.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Prisoner chamber, second level of the temple
- **Active:** In the process of freeing the remaining four chained prisoners
- **Awareness:** Knows Kelno is actively moving looted assets through a hidden door and is treating him as a serious, underestimated faction player
