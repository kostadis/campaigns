# Jira

## Identity
- Teenage girl, resident of Hommlet; no formal title or faction affiliation
- First appeared in Session notes as one of two cloaked figures spotted by Mickey lurking behind the brewery at midnight

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven by her relationship with Marek and the desire to keep seeing him despite her father's prohibition
- Demonstrated resourcefulness in appealing to the party's status as heroes when threatened; quick to plead for outside intervention rather than accept the situation passively

## History with the Party
- Discovered by the party at the brewery during a late-night meeting with Marek
- Zephyr placed a blade to her throat, causing her to shriek
- When Zinia dismissed the matter, Jira pleaded with the party to intervene with their parents, invoking the party's reputation as heroes

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Hommlet; released following the confrontation at the brewery
- **Active situation:** Her secret relationship with Marek remains ongoing but precarious given her father's opposition
- **What the party knows:** Her father Jakub is a member of the old faith and forbids the relationship with Marek
- **What remains hidden:** No further hidden information present in notes
