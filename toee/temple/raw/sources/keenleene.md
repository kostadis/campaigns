# Keenleene

## Identity
- **Role:** Founder of Sheernobb (historical figure)
- **Faction:** Sheernobb (founder)
- **First Appearance:** Encountered as a commemorative statue at the base of the steps leading to Moot Hall in Sheernobb

## Personality & Motivations
Keenleene is known only through her memorial. The enchanted statue depicts her as joyful and performative — she laughs heartily, plays a lively tune on pipes, and winks at onlookers. The animation reflects the gnomish cultural love of illusion and spectacle.

## History with the Party
- The party encountered her statue in Sheernobb. When Sequoia approached, the statue animated via gnome illusionism: gem-like eyes sparkled, braided gemstone-adorned hair shimmered, and the figure played pipes and winked. Sequoia identified the display as an example of gnomish illusionary tradition and explained it to his companions.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Base of the steps leading to Moot Hall, Sheernobb
- **Status:** A commemorative stone statue with an enchanted illusory animation. No known active plans or living presence.
- **Party Knowledge:** The party knows she is the founder of Sheernobb. Nothing is hidden — she is a historical figure with no apparent secrets in play.

## Relationships
- **Sheernobb:** Revered as the town's founder; memorialized prominently at the civic center.
- **Sequoia:** Recognized and explained her statue's magic to the party.
