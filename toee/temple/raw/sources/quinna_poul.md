# Quinna Poul

## Identity
- **Role:** Cook at the Honey Haven Inn, Sheernobb
- **Faction:** None noted
- **First Appearance:** Planting 21, 579 — met at the Honey Haven Inn; approached the party to commission a hunt
- *Also known as: Quinna Pool, Quinna.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven by her craft and pride in her cooking; her requests center on sourcing exceptional ingredients and preparing remarkable meals.
- Practical and direct — she marked the boar's location herself and was quick to alert the party when her feast ingredients went missing.
- Warm enough to reward the party with a celebratory feast and a promised special dinner.

## History with the Party
- **Planting 21:** Commissioned the party to hunt a rare gnarled boar in the Gnarley Forest, promising an enchanted meal as reward.
- **Planting 22:** Marked the boar's location on Zephyr's map. The party returned successfully with the boar. Quinna announced a celebratory feast for the Harvest Festival on the 30th and promised the party a special dinner the following day.
- **Planting 23:** Rushed out to find the party — some of the feast's ingredients (broccoli and potatoes) had been magically animated and scattered through the village. The party recovered them.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Honey Haven Inn kitchen, Sheernobb
- **Active Plans:** Preparing for the Harvest Festival feast on Planting 30; a special dinner for the party is still owed (promised for Planting 24)
- **What the Party Knows:** She is the inn's cook, she can source or request rare ingredients, and she rewards well. The animated ingredients incident suggests something unusual is at work — cause not yet confirmed.
- **What Remains Hidden:** The source or cause of the ingredient animation has not been explained.

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Friendly patron relationship; has now made two requests of them and rewarded their help
- **Zephyr (party member):** Directly interacted with — marked the boar location on Zephyr's map
- **Honey Haven Inn:** Employed there; her role is central to the upcoming Harvest Festival
