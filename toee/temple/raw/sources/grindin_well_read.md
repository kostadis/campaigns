# Grindin Well-Read

## Identity
- **Role:** Owner and proprietor of Grindin Books, Sheernobb
- **Faction:** None known
- **First Appearance:** Planting 21, 579 — the party visited his shop on the northern branch of Sheernobb's main road, seeking information about local bandits

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Running his bookshop; apparently engaged in scholarly or professional circles (referenced an unspecified conference)
- Cantankerous and easily irritated by interruptions; initially stonewalled the party rather than engage with their questions. Prefers practical solutions (suggested enchanted wards for caravans) over direct involvement. Ultimately cooperative once his grievances were aired.

## History with the Party
- **Planting 21, 579:** Party visited seeking bandit intelligence. Grindin was annoyed by the interruption and complained that his invitation to an unspecified conference had been stolen. He offered little direct help on bandits, suggested enchanted protective wards as a workaround, and eventually pointed the party toward **Pasmarie Clee** as a better source of bandit information. **Zephyr** purchased a map of the local area. **Zinnia** noticed a book titled *"Map of the Elemental Nexus"* on the shelves.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Grindin Books, Sheernobb
- **Active Concerns:** Missing conference invitation (stolen — circumstances unknown)
- **What the party knows:** He has extensive stock of maps, books, and scrolls; knows of Pasmarie Clee; suggested enchanted wards exist as a caravan solution
- **Hidden from the party:** The nature of the conference the invitation was stolen from; whether the theft is connected to anything larger

## Relationships
- **Pasmarie Clee:** Aware of her and her knowledge of bandits; referred the party to her
- **The Party:** Mildly antagonistic first impression; tolerates them as customers
