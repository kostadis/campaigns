# Gremag

## Identity
- **Role:** Bodyguard and Temple of Elemental Evil operative, partnered with Rannos Davl
- **Faction:** Temple of Elemental Evil
- **First Appearance:** Encountered in Hommlet alongside Rannos Davl when negotiations with the party broke down

## Personality & Motivations
- Demonstrated no interest in diplomacy — attacked without warning the moment conflict became inevitable.
- Fought with swift, calculated brutality, suggesting professional combat training rather than impulsive aggression.

## History with the Party
- When negotiations with Rannos collapsed, Gremag immediately initiated combat, striking Zinnia twice in rapid succession — first severely wounding her, then knocking her unconscious and leaving her dying.
- He was killed by Calmer, who landed the finishing critical blow while Zinnia was still down.
- His body was dismembered by Thorne alongside Rannos's to prevent any future use of *Speak with Dead*.

## Current Status
- **Deceased.** Body dismembered; magically unrecoverable for interrogation.

## Relationships
- **Rannos Davl:** Partner and fellow Temple operative. The two worked in tandem in Hommlet.
- **Zinnia:** Nearly killed her single-handedly in the opening round of combat.
- **Calmer:** Delivered the killing blow.
- **Thorne:** Dismembered his corpse post-combat.

- N/A — Gremag was killed before any relationship arc could develop.
