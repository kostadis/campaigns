# Gurdy

## Identity
- Role: Freelance bard, self-styled local intelligence source
- Faction: None known
- First appearance: Planting 21, 579 — approached the party at the Honey Haven Inn in Sheernobb asking for coin to play a song

## Personality & Motivations
- Goals: Earn coin, find traveling companions, perform
- Gurdy is charming, opportunistic, and comfortable helping himself to hospitality he wasn't offered (uninvited seating, drinks at Zephyr's expense). He reads a room quickly and parleys small performances into larger opportunities.

## History with the Party
- **Planting 21, 579:** Approached the party at the Honey Haven Inn, received coin from Zephyr for a song, then sat uninvited and had Mari pour him a drink — also at Zephyr's expense. Shared local intelligence about bandit attacks on Sheernobb becoming unusually organized. Recommended the party visit Moot Hall. Offered to join the party as a traveling bard.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Honey Haven Inn, Sheernobb
- **Active:** Seeking to join the party as a companion
- **Known to party:** His offer to join, his tip about organized bandit activity, his recommendation of Moot Hall
- **Hidden:** Background, true motives for wanting to travel with the party, reliability of his intelligence

## Relationships
- **Zephyr:** Warmed to him; open to bringing Gurdy on partly to antagonize Calmer
- **Calmer:** Strongly opposed to adding Gurdy to the group
- **Mari (Honey Haven staff):** Comfortable enough to direct her to pour him drinks
