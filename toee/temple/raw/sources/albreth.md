# Albreth

## Identity
- **Role:** Fire priest / "circuit of the Scarlet Moon"; member of the Fire Temple
- **Faction:** Temple of Elemental Evil (Fire Temple, Hall of the Scarlet Moon)
- **First Appearance:** Camp Number Two of the Hall of the Scarlet Moon, encountered alongside fellow priest Ingulf; the encounter was orchestrated by Tolub as a trap
- *Also known as: Albrith.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Loyal to the Fire Temple and the broader elemental evil hierarchy
- Quick to hostility — dropped pretense and turned violent the moment the party probed his allegiances; aggressive and confrontational when pressed about Temple ties
- Willing to use lethal force to assert allegiance; declared loyalty with the phrase *"fire, water, earth, or air"*

## History with the Party
- **Hall of the Scarlet Moon — Camp Number Two:** Tolub directed the party to the Hall as a trap. Albreth and Ingulf were waiting at Camp Number Two, presented initially as robed figures connected to the Scarlet Moon gathering. When questioned about ties to the Temple of Elemental Evil, both men assumed threatening stances and declared elemental allegiance, then unleashed coordinated magical fire attacks — explosive bursts of flame — that severely wounded multiple party members and knocked Calmer unconscious.
- **Escape:** Zephyr released a bear as a diversion; the invisible party escaped (using magical dust) while Albreth was distracted by the rampaging animal.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Camp Number Two, near the Hall of the Scarlet Moon
- **Last Known Activity:** Dealing with a rampaging bear; the party had fled invisibly before he could regroup
- **What the Party Knows:** Robed spellcaster, "circuit of the Scarlet Moon," affiliated with elemental evil, capable of devastating fire magic; nearly killed Calmer
- **Hidden from Party:** His full role within the Scarlet Moon hierarchy, his broader objectives, and whether he pursued the party after the diversion

## Relationships
- **Ingulf:** Direct companion and fellow Fire Temple operative; the two fought as a coordinated pair
- **Tolub:** Operates under Tolub's direction; Tolub orchestrated the trap Albreth participated in
- **Calmer (party):** Nearly killed him during the ambush; likely a target of hostility or revenge
- **The Party:** Hostile. Attacked on sight once allegiance was confirmed. Does not know the party's identities beyond this encounter.
