# Captain Gnonsbim

## Identity
- **Role:** Captain of the Guard, Sheernobb
- **Faction:** Sheernobb municipal government; currently running for re-election
- **First Appearance:** Planting 21, 579 — approached the party unsolicited at the Honey Haven Tavern in Sheernobb

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Resolve the bandit problem (primarily for electoral optics), secure re-election, and impress Pasmarie Clee
- Brisk and transactional — commissioned the party before they could agree, bought a round to smooth it over, and left
- Politically savvy enough to structure the reward around his own public image; the speech credit condition reveals his priorities clearly

## History with the Party
- **Planting 21, 579:** Met the party at the Honey Haven Tavern. Offered 25gp for the bandit camp location, 10gp per dead bandit, and 25gp for the leader's death. Requested that upon their return, the party allow him to publicly claim credit for the operation — in exchange for custom-forged magical weapons. Directed them to speak with Pasmarie Clee and departed.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Sheernobb
- **Active Plans:** Awaiting the party's return with proof of bandit activity; prepared to deliver a political speech taking credit for their work
- **What the Party Knows:** His reward structure, the speech-credit condition, and his promise of magical weaponry
- **Hidden (GM Only):** Per Headgnome Glennddarc, the bandit focus is electoral posturing; Gnonsbim is believed to be romantically interested in Pasmarie Clee

## Relationships
- **Pasmarie Clee:** Directed the party to her; privately believed to be romantically interested in her (per Glennddarc)
- **Headgnome Glennddarc:** Glennddarc privately undercut Gnonsbim's framing, characterizing his bandit campaign as political theater
- **The Party:** Transactional employer; has made specific promises (magical weapons) contingent on cooperation with the speech arrangement
