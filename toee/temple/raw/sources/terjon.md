# Terjon

## Identity
- **Role:** High Priest and Prefect of the Temple of St. Cuthbert, Hommlet
- **Faction:** Church of St. Cuthbert
- **First Appearance:** Session 2 — met privately with Zinia, recognizing her as a recent convert
- *Also known as: Prefect Terjon.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven by an intense desire to control information about any Temple resurgence, framing it as a matter of planetary safety — though his true motives are unclear.
- Dismissive, condescending, and difficult to work with; prone to contempt when the party brings him evidence.
- Reacted with apparent physical pain when pressured to return the eye-and-tear stone, then covered with a dismissive reversal — suggests the stone may affect him in some way he is concealing.
- Transactional in practice: withholds healing from non-followers, but will accept valuable tithes in exchange for services.

## History with the Party
1. **Session 2:** Met privately with Zinia. Agreed to allow Calmer to join the party in exchange for exclusive intelligence sharing — all findings to go to him and him alone.
2. **Post-First Moathouse Excursion:** Dismissed the eye-and-tear stone as a "painted rock," complained the party released a prisoner he could have interrogated, and attempted to keep the stone. When Zephyr challenged him, he visibly winced and returned it, muttering it was worthless.
3. **Post-Second Moathouse Excursion:** Calmer reported alone; Zinia refused to attend, citing Terjon's insufferable demeanor.
4. **Village Council Meeting:** Terjon was absent. Calmer pushed to have the Prefect included in future meetings but was overruled by Sequoia. Burne and Rufus dismissed claims of Temple resurgence entirely.
5. **Calmer's Resurrection:** Zinia offered a magical trident as a tithe. Despite prior friction, Terjon accepted and performed the resurrection.

## Current Status
- **Location:** Temple of St. Cuthbert, Hommlet
- **Active:** Receiving intelligence from Calmer per their original agreement; excluded from village council proceedings
- **Hidden:** His possible role in the disappearance of Y'dey (former head cleric) is unknown to the party. His reaction to the eye-and-tear stone — apparent physical pain — has not been explained.

## Relationships
- **Zinia:** Initial point of contact; she has grown cold toward him and avoids meetings.
- **Calmer:** Primary informant; reports to Terjon as per the original agreement.
- **Zephyr:** Successfully called Terjon's bluff over the stone — Terjon backed down noticeably.
- **Y'dey (missing NPC):** Local gossip implicates Terjon in her disappearance; he now holds her former position.
- **Burne & Rufus:** Politically opposed or indifferent — dismissed any claim of Temple resurgence at council, excluding Terjon's perspective.
- **Village Council:** Terjon is not currently included; Calmer's attempt to change this was rejected.

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease — Zinia:** Dismissed her party's evidence, attempted to retain a suspicious artifact, condescending manner during reports.
- **Partial recovery — Zinia/Calmer:** Accepted tithe and performed Calmer's resurrection despite history of hostility.
