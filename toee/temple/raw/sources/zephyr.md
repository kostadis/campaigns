# Zephyr

## Identity
- Role / title / faction affiliation: Unknown — operates independently or in a freelance capacity alongside the party; appears to have some obligation to or arrangement with Hartsch
- First appearance: This session, in the scalding corridor engagement

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goals:** Locating the Deep Temple; investigating active necromantic operations in the dungeon; understanding the engineered war and who is directing it
- **Personality:** Efficient and tactically disciplined — he maintains distance, adjusts plans without complaint, and files information quietly. Beneath a composed exterior he is privately chafing at the constraints of the Hartsch alliance, experiencing it as a leash while performing unhurried compliance. His interrogation work revealed someone who defaults to persuasion but can shift to implied menace when needed, though he required Calmer's presence to land the harder approach.

## History with the Party
- **Scalding corridor:** Held distance, fired into steam cover before engagement, took damage from bugbear counterattack (less than Zinnia)
- **Wraith encounter:** Fired pistol, landed the only wound on the wraith (5 damage after resistance); broke for the secret passage ahead of the others
- **Ghoul ambush:** Killed the lead ghoul instantly with a single dagger strike (27 damage) before it could act; proposed pursuing whoever raised them, noting the ghouls were recently-dead Earth Temple members — evidence of active necromancy during the engineered war
- **Hall of Trophies:** Proposed interrogating a single elemental about the Deep Temple's location; plan collapsed when no party member spoke Primordial; adjusted without friction and supported the steam-cover exit
- **Prisoner chamber:** Led interrogation of a captured Air Temple cultist. First persuasion attempt failed; shifted to physical intimidation and implied threat. Calmer standing at his shoulder changed the dynamic and the second attempt succeeded. Extracted the location of the secret passage to the Deep Temple — information the prisoner had received directly from Kelno. When Hartsch's summons arrived, Zephyr accepted it outwardly without impatience and led the party upstairs, internally registering the tension between following Hartsch's leash and finally holding the Deep Temple route.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Leading the party upstairs in response to a summons from Hartsch
- **Active concerns:** Sitting on the Deep Temple route (via the secret passage, sourced from Kelno through the Air Temple prisoner) while being redirected by Hartsch; monitoring active necromantic operations in the dungeon
- **What the party knows:** He has the Deep Temple location and is aware of active necromancy; he accepted Hartsch's summons
- **What remains hidden:** The full nature of his arrangement with Hartsch; how far the "leash" actually constrains him; whether he intends to act on the Deep Temple information independently

## Relationships
- **Hartsch:** Some form of alliance or obligation — Zephyr experiences it as a constraint, performs compliance, but resents the leash internally
- **Zinnia:** She credited his scalding corridor caution as sensible — mild mutual professional regard implied
- **Calmer:** Zephyr relied on Calmer's physical presence to tip the interrogation; indicates he reads and uses the party's assets situationally
- **Kelno:** Indirect connection — the prisoner received the Deep Temple route from Kelno, making Kelno a priority intelligence target for Zephyr
