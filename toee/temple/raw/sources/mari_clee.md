# Mari Clee

## Identity
- **Role:** Co-owner of the Honey Haven Inn, Sheernobb
- **Faction:** None noted
- **First Appearance:** Planting 21, 579 — the party arrived at the Honey Haven Inn and Mari greeted them from behind the bar

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Running her inn, collecting payment from guests
- Cheerful, energetic, and unselfconscious — was humming and dancing behind the bar when the party arrived, and produced a hedgehog from inside a mug without missing a beat. Reads people quickly and exploits social dynamics to her financial advantage, deftly using Zephyr's reputation for stinginess against him to secure full payment from the party.

## History with the Party
- **Planting 21, 579:** Met the party upon arrival at the Honey Haven Inn. Welcomed them warmly, handled room and meal pricing, and maneuvered Zephyr into agreeing to cover costs for the group. Continued charging Zephyr for items throughout their stay.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Honey Haven Inn, Sheernobb
- **Active Plans:** Running the inn as normal
- **Party Knowledge:** The party knows her as the inn's co-owner. Nothing hidden has been noted.

## Relationships
- **Zephyr (party member):** Identified his stinginess and used it as leverage; has established a pattern of billing him directly
- **Co-owner:** Another owner of the Honey Haven Inn is referenced but not detailed in notes
