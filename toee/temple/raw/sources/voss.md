# Voss

## Identity
- Role: Low-ranking bandit grunt, no faction title
- First appearance: Session 1 (or first Moathouse incursion); captured alive in the bandit leader's room after the party cleared it

## Personality & Motivations
- A man brought low by circumstance: sold property, accumulated gambling debts, turned to drinking, and eventually fell in with bandits
- Demonstrated as frightened and emotionally fragile under pressure — wept during interrogation and became hysterically panicked when shown the eye-and-tear symbol

## History with the Party
- Captured during the first Moathouse incursion after the party killed the bandit leader and others in the same room
- Interrogated by the party; confirmed the Moathouse was used because it was believed abandoned following the Temple's original defeat
- When Sequoia presented the flat river rock bearing an eye-and-tear symbol, Voss reacted with extreme panic, revealing the symbol was passed down a chain: big boss → middleman boss → his group, used as a sign of belonging
- Zephyr assessed him as too low in the hierarchy to yield further useful intelligence

## Current Status
- Last known location: Moathouse ground floor
- Fate after interrogation is unrecorded — unclear whether he was released, detained, or otherwise dealt with
- **Party knows:** He is a low-level grunt with no direct knowledge of leadership above his middleman boss
- **Hidden/unknown:** His fate; whether he might resurface or warn others

## Relationships
- Subordinate to an unnamed middleman boss, who in turn answers to an unnamed "big boss"
- No recorded individual relationships with specific party members beyond the interrogation; Sequoia and Zephyr were the primary interactors

## Arc Score Events
- None recorded
