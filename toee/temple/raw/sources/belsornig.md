# Belsornig

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Canon and Prophet, head of the Water Temple within the Temple of Evil
- **Faction:** Water Temple (Temple of Elemental Evil)
- **First Appearance:** Never directly encountered; surfaced through multiple intelligence threads — captured Gnarley Forest brigand revealed to be in his employ; later identified by Urot (orc cook at the Abbey of Allitur) as Snej's resource supplier; finally identified by Hartsch as architect of the chimera scheme
- *Also known as: Belsoring.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Eliminate or weaken rival temples (specifically the Earth Temple and Fire Temple) while preserving and advancing the Water Temple's power
- A senior organizational figure within the Temple of Evil — operates with administrative authority and ambition
- Described by Hartsch as a cunning manipulator who engineers schemes with no lose condition — does not act directly when proxies and contingencies will do the work for him
- His use of indirect networks (bandits, intermediaries, supplier pipelines) suggests patience and a preference for asymmetric pressure over direct confrontation

## History with the Party
- **Gnarley Forest brigand encounter (caravan from Narwell):** Party encountered brigands during their journey from Narwell. One was revealed under interrogation to be working directly for Belsornig, linking him to organized bandit activity near Hommlet
- **Urot's confession (Abbey of Allitur):** Orc cook Urot confessed under questioning that Belsornig was supplying resources to bandit leader Snej, who was using the Abbey of Allitur as an operational base — characterizing Belsornig as a "big shot" within the Temple of Evil
- **Chimera scheme (Hartsch identification):** Belsornig arranged the chimera purchase through his allies Vesrek and Captain Varsik of the Crimson Trader, intending the beast to either kill the party (damaging the Earth Temple) or bankrupt the Fire Temple paying for it. Hartsch identified Belsornig and the Water Temple as the true long-term threat — the real prize to eliminate
- **Ledger evidence:** His name appears in the ledger recovered from the dead Fire Temple warlock's body, connecting him to a broader network of operations

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Somewhere within the Water Temple (exact position unknown)
- **Active Plans:** Ongoing operations in motion per the recovered ledger; managing both the bandit network (via Snej) and inter-temple destabilization (via the chimera scheme)
- **What the Party Knows:** Name, title, faction leadership role, indirect connection to the chimera plot, ledger appearance, role as Snej's supplier, and his network of brigands near Hommlet
- **What Remains Hidden:** Full scope of Water Temple operations, Belsornig's direct resources and capabilities, nature of his relationship with Vesrek

## Relationships
- **Vesrek:** Ally and operational intermediary used to arrange the chimera purchase
- **Captain Varsik (Crimson Trader):** Co-conspirator in the chimera delivery scheme
- **Snej (bandit leader):** Organizational subordinate / resource recipient; Snej operates under Belsornig's support from the Abbey of Allitur
- **Gnarley Forest brigands:** At least one member was working directly for him
- **Urot (orc cook):** Aware of his name and status; revealed him under questioning at the Abbey
- **Hartsch:** Rival faction (Earth Temple); views Belsornig as the primary threat to eliminate
- **The Party:** Indirect antagonist — has moved against them via proxy without ever meeting them
