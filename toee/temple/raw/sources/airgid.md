# Airgid

## Identity
- Young silver dragon
- First appeared sheltering with / traveling under the party's care; exact session of first appearance not recorded in notes

## Personality & Motivations
- Eager to return home; recognized her territory instinctively and departed without hesitation
- Grateful to the party for the escort, promising reward on her mother's behalf
- Her presence was a practical burden — straining party finances and raising concern about the horses

## History with the Party
- Traveled with the party for an unspecified period prior to Planting 17, at notable cost to their budget
- **Planting 17:** Party escorted Airgid along the Greenway Valley road through the Kron Hills toward Tulvar. Just before reaching the town, she recognized her home territory near the Lotrimil mountains, called it out, took wing westward, and departed — thanking the party and promising her mother would reward them.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Heading west toward the Lotrimil mountains; presumed returned home
- **Pending:** Her mother has been promised to reward the party; that reward has not yet materialized

## Relationships
- **Her mother (unnamed silver dragon):** Lives near the Lotrimil mountains; Airgid invoked her as the source of a future reward
- **The party:** Grateful; they bore real cost to see her home safely
