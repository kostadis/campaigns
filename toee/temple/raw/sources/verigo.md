# Verigo

## Identity
- Role: Druid; member of a group of four friendly human druids
- Faction: Unclear — associated with the rite of the wicker giant at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon
- First appearance: Camp Number One, near the Hall of the Scarlet Moon; the party encountered him and his companions while traveling in the area

## Personality & Motivations
- Motivated by anticipation of the upcoming rite of the wicker giant
- Presented as hospitable and welcoming — offered the party food, drink, and brandy, and invited them to stay the night

## History with the Party
- **First encounter:** Verigo and three other human druids welcomed the party at Camp Number One. They were in good spirits about the wicker giant rite and extended an open invitation for the party to rest and celebrate with them.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Camp Number One, Hall of the Scarlet Moon
- **Active plans:** Preparing for or awaiting the rite of the wicker giant
- **What the party knows:** He is friendly, human, a druid, and eager about the rite
- **What remains hidden:** His true allegiances, the nature of the rite, and his role within the broader faction at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon

## Relationships
- **Fellow druids:** Traveling and camped with three other human druids (names not recorded)
- **The party:** Hospitable first impression; no conflict noted
