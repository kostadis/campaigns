# Gonnathrad Kevanarial

## Identity
- **Role:** High Elf Diplomat
- **Faction:** Caer Ostverk (official representative)
- **First Appearance:** "The Cheap Forester" tavern in Ostverk — approached the party as a quest giver seeking adventurers to hunt bandits

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Fulfill his official duty of hiring adventurers to pursue bandit activity; clearly has a romanticized, story-fueled view of adventuring life
- Overenthusiastic and easily distracted — offered to write the party a five-page backstory and nearly divulged sensitive information before catching himself. His professional instincts are present but frequently overridden by his excitement. He is more interested in the *idea* of adventure than in asking hard questions.

## History with the Party
- Met the party at "The Cheap Forester" in Ostverk. He was in the process of hiring adventurers regarding bandit activity when his attention drifted toward the monastery.
- Nearly let slip information about the monastery before self-correcting. The party carefully fed his enthusiasm while withholding specifics about their own interest in the site.
- Issued the party an **official letter of permit** authorizing them to pursue a lead involving a chest near a monastery by Gallow's Corner, with a promised reward of **100 gold pieces** upon presentation to Caer Ostverk.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** "The Cheap Forester," Ostverk
- **Active:** His official letter of permit is in the party's possession; he presumably awaits their return to Caer Ostverk for payment
- **What the Party Knows:** He is an official diplomat with enough authority to issue letters of permit and authorize payment from Caer Ostverk
- **What Remains Hidden:** The nature of the information he almost divulged about the monastery — he clearly knows *something*, though how much is unclear

## Relationships
- **The Party:** Enthusiastic patron; views them through a romanticized lens; does not know the full scope of their interest in the monastery
- **Caer Ostverk:** Official representative; has authority to issue permits and authorize gold payments on the organization's behalf
