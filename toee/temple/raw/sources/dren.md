# Dren

## Identity
- Leader of the Crimson Guard faction; also referenced as commanding the Broken Blades mercenaries within the Earth Temple
- First appeared as a potential complication during the party's prisoner rescue — his crew occupied territory adjacent to the prison chamber and could have overheard the commotion

## Personality & Motivations
- Motivated by plunder, combat, and the strength of whoever he's aligned with
- Visibly impressed by displays of power; agreed to assist the party without hesitation after witnessing their victory over the Chimera
- Rallies his men with promises of blood and loot — a pragmatic mercenary leader rather than an ideological one

## History with the Party
1. **Prisoner Rescue (early):** Dren and his men were settled in territory adjacent to the prison chamber. The party identified him as a potential complication and posted Calmer at the door as a precaution. Dren took no direct action and had no interaction with the party.
2. **Romag Assassination Setup:** The party enlisted Dren to arrange a morning meeting with High Priest Romag, ensuring he would be present and off-guard. Dren agreed readily — impressed by the party's reputation — and returned with confirmation that Romag was looking forward to the meeting.
3. **Temple War:** As Hartsch's forces mobilized, the party encountered Dren preparing his men for battle. After a brief exchange, the party directed him and the Crimson Guard into the three-way temple conflict.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Deployed into the Earth Temple's three-way war as part of the assault force
- **Active:** Leading the Crimson Guard in open battle; rallied his men with promises of plunder and blood
- **What the party knows:** That he is a mercenary willing to work with them for the right incentives, and that he is currently fighting in the temple war
- **What remains hidden:** His ultimate loyalties and whether he would remain cooperative after the dust settles

## Relationships
- **Romag (Earth Temple High Priest):** Operated within Romag's sphere; was able to arrange a direct meeting with him, suggesting an established working relationship
- **Hartsch:** Mobilized against Hartsch's forces during the temple war
- **The Party:** Cooperative and transactional; responds to strength and respects demonstrated power
