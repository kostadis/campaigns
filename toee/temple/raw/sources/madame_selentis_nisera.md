# Madame Selentis

## Identity
- **Role:** De facto ruler of Nulb; criminal power broker
- **Faction:** Operates independently; controls Nulb's underworld and power structure
- **Origin:** Disgraced noble family from Verbobonc
- **First Appearance:** Referenced by Eryk Velkori; has not yet appeared in person
- *Also known as: Nisera, Selentis, Madame Nisera.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Maintaining absolute control over Nulb and its operations
- Described as spider-like — patient, web-building, indirect. Operates through others rather than personally. Ruthless toward anyone who crosses her, with public executions used as a tool of intimidation.

## History with the Party
- **Early sessions:** Eryk Velkori warned the party explicitly about Selentis, describing her as the true power in Nulb and cautioning that her attention would be catastrophic for them.
- Elfar Twell was found hanged in Gallows Square — executed on her orders for crossing her, serving as a visible demonstration of her reach.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Nulb (exact location unknown)
- **Active Plans:** Unknown
- **What the party knows:** She runs Nulb, comes from disgraced Verbobonc nobility, and is extremely dangerous. Crossing her results in death.
- **What remains hidden:** Her direct goals, her network, her current awareness of the party.

## Relationships
- **Eryk Velkori:** Knows of her; fears her enough to warn others away unprompted
- **Elfar Twell:** Had him executed for crossing her
- **The Party:** No direct contact established; party has been warned to avoid her notice
