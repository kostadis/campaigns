# Marek

## Identity
- Teenage boy; son of Tomlyn the tailor, a member of the Church of St. Cuthbert
- First appeared in Hommlet, discovered meeting secretly with Jira behind the brewery

## Personality & Motivations
- Motivated by his relationship with Jira, which he is willing to pursue despite family and religious pressure
- Emotionally vulnerable when confronted — broke down crying when the party discovered him

## History with the Party
- The party discovered Marek meeting secretly with Jira behind the brewery. The relationship is religiously unacceptable to both families due to Tomlyn's affiliation with the Church of St. Cuthbert. When confronted, Marek began crying. The party released him without further action.

## Current Status
- Last known location: Hommlet, released after the confrontation behind the brewery
- No known active plans beyond continuing the secret relationship
- **Party knows:** His identity, his father, the secret meetings with Jira, and the religious conflict involved
- **Hidden:** His and Jira's intentions going forward; whether either family is aware of the relationship

## Relationships
