# Tharok

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** High Templar / High Cultist of the Earth Cult
- **Faction:** Earth Cult (Earth Temple)
- **First Appearance:** Named (not present) after the party interrogated a captive monk who identified him as the orchestrator of the active ritual at the monastery; encountered in person in the underground ritual chamber beneath the abandoned monastery near Gallow's Corner, where the party interrupted his ritual
- *Also known as: Tharok Stevan, Tharok Stonevein, High Templar Tharok, High Templar Tharok Stevan.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Completing the Titan-summoning ritual centered on the Chest of Rao — channeling elemental earth power on a catastrophic scale
- An authoritative, zealous cult leader capable of organizing a large-scale ritual operation
- Fanatical and contemptuous of outsiders; immediately branded the party as "defilers" rather than attempting negotiation
- Fought with ruthless pragmatism, prioritizing crowd control (Hold Person) when overwhelmed and pressing offensive power against the most threatening targets

## History with the Party
1. **Identification:** A captive monk revealed Tharok's name, full identity (Tharok Stevan, aka Tharok Stonevein), title (High Templar), and role under questioning. He was identified as the orchestrator of an active Titan-summoning ritual connected to the monastery; the timeline was urgent.
2. **Discovery:** Party found Tharok mid-ritual in the underground chamber with three robed cultists surrounding the Chest of Rao
3. **Combat:** Declared the party defilers and opened combat. Used earthquake magic to knock members prone and disrupt Calmer's blessing. Struck down Jerry with elemental attacks; hurled Zephyr backward with a powerful blow; paralyzed both Zephyr and Zinnia with Hold Person when pressed
4. **Ritual disrupted:** Sequoia knocked the Chest of Rao from the altar, destabilizing the ritual and reducing Tharok to 1 HP
5. **Death:** Zinnia delivered the killing blow (14 damage) with advantage from Calmer's Guiding Bolt
6. His death triggered the collapse of the ritual cavern

## Current Status
- **Status:** **Dead**
- His death caused the ritual chamber to collapse
- The three cultists who accompanied him have an unknown fate

## Relationships
- **Earth Cult:** Held highest known authority (High Templar); commanded the monks operating within the monastery
- **The Party:** Hostile from first contact; viewed them as enemies of his faith
- **Calmer:** Tharok's earthquakes specifically disrupted Calmer's blessing, suggesting possible attunement to divine interference
- **Zinnia:** Delivered the killing blow
- **Sequoia:** Directly undermined the ritual by displacing the Chest of Rao
- **Jerry:** Struck down during combat
- **Zephyr:** Hurled back physically and later paralyzed
