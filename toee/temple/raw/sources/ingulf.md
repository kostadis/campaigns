# Ingulf

## Identity
- **Role:** Fire temple operative; circuit of the Scarlet Moon
- **Faction:** Hall of the Scarlet Moon (fire temple)
- **First Appearance:** Camp Number Two of the Hall of the Scarlet Moon, encountered alongside Albrith

## Personality & Motivations
- Devoted to elemental fire allegiance, demonstrated by his willing participation in Albrith's threatening declaration.
- Prone to fury when outmaneuvered — stood helpless and visibly enraged as the party escaped.

## History with the Party
- At Camp Two, Ingulf stood with Albrith and made a joint declaration of elemental allegiance as a warning/threat to the party.
- Participated in a magical fire assault against the party.
- Was defeated tactically when Zephyr cut a bear's leash, unleashing it as a distraction. Unable to detect the invisible party, Ingulf could not prevent their escape.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Camp Number Two, Hall of the Scarlet Moon
- **Active Plans:** Unknown beyond general fire temple operations
- **Hidden Info:** Full capabilities and rank within the temple hierarchy are unknown to the party

## Relationships
- **Albrith:** Close companion and fellow operative; acted in coordinated tandem at Camp Two
- **The Party:** Hostile; was made to look powerless during their escape
