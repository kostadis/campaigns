# Thalsor of the Stone

## Identity
- **Role:** Druid; field operative / commander of a Black Earth cult compound in the Kron Hills
- **Faction:** Black Earth cult (rival elemental faction to Lareth's Fire Temple)
- **First appearance:** Planting 23, 579 — encountered during the party's raid on his compound while recovering stolen honey and Lareth's chest
- *Also known as: Thalsor.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven by loyalty to the Black Earth cult and a need to protect its secrecy
- Aggressively interrogated the party to uncover who had sent them; aware they were operating as agents of a third party, suggesting some understanding of the broader political landscape between factions
- Psychologically brutal and methodical — willing to hold a corpse hostage overnight and threaten mutilation to break the party's resolve, rather than always pressing an immediate advantage

## History with the Party

**Planting 23, 579 — Initial Raid**
Party assaulted the compound. Thalsor's defenses — his stone golem, mud mephits, and a hailstorm of stones — repelled them with devastating effectiveness: knocked out Sequoia and Zinnia, then Calmer when Calmer attempted to heal them, and finally killed Calmer outright as Zephyr moved to retrieve the body. He seized Calmer's corpse and held it overnight, screaming threats of mutilation to pressure the party into surrendering and revealing who had sent them. He offered to return the body undamaged in exchange for the party revealing their patrons.

**Planting 23–24 (overnight):** Thalsor continued shouting threats while apparently preparing for a frontal assault the following morning.

**Planting 24, 579 — Counterattack and Death**
While Thalsor's attention was directed toward the expected frontal approach, Sequoia used a potion of gaseous form to slip past him and recover Calmer's body. Without Calmer's spells available, Zinnia and Zephyr launched a surprise attack and struck Thalsor down quickly — bypassing his golem and mud mephit to focus all fire on him. The mud mephit was destroyed; the stone golem became inert upon his death.

**Post-Death — Intelligence Recovered**
His compound contained correspondence between Romag and Thalsor, plus an unsent note from Thalsor confirming that a lost chest is located in a monastery in the mountains.

## Current Status
- **Status:** **Dead** — killed Planting 24, 579
- **Stone golem:** Rendered immobile after Thalsor's death; remains at the compound in the Kron Hills
- Compound has been raided; written correspondence recovered by the party

**What the party knows:** Thalsor was a Black Earth cult operative; he commanded a golem and mud mephit; his compound held notes implicating Romag and referencing a chest in a mountain monastery.

**What the party has concluded (inference, not confirmed):** Thalsor's Black Earth cultists were rivals to Lareth's faction, and Lareth manipulated the party into eliminating his competition.

## Relationships
- **Romag** — superior or peer within the Black Earth cult; corresponded directly with Thalsor
- **Lareth** — rival faction leader; the party believes Lareth engineered their attack on Thalsor to remove a competitor
- **The party** — killed Calmer, held the body hostage; Zinnia specifically flagged him as a threat requiring elimination to protect the party's identities and their connection to the village elders
- **Village elders** — Thalsor was actively trying to identify them as the party's patrons
