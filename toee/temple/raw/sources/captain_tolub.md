# Captain Tolub

## Identity
- **Role:** Ship captain with river pirate connections; covert agent of the Fire Temple / fire prophet
- **Faction:** Fire Temple operative
- **Base of operations:** Boatman's Tavern, Nulb; runs a pirate ship out of the Nulb docks on a known schedule
- **First appearance:** The party met him at the Boatman's Tavern in Nulb
- *Also known as: Tolub, Tolubb, Captain Tolubb.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven by loyalty to the fire prophet and likely personal gain
- Boisterous, intimidating, and socially dominant — commands a crew that laughs on cue
- Uses surface-level charm and generosity (buying drinks) to disarm marks while concealing treacherous intent
- Comfortable with deception; frames betrayal as helpful misdirection; willing to sell out allies for personal gain or self-preservation

## History with the Party
1. **Boatman's Tavern, first meeting:** Tolub was holding court at the tavern, trading bad jokes with his laughing crew. After some charm and negotiation from the party, he agreed to buy them drinks.
2. **Information on Serena:** Confirmed that Serena had been handed over to the Iron Lash brothel as payment for something, and mockingly revealed the brothel was located upstairs in the same building.
3. **Deliberate misdirection:** When the party sought contact with the Earth Temple and Romag, Tolub directed them instead to the Hall of the Scarlet Moon — a trap set by the Fire Temple.
4. **Post-departure smear:** After the party left, Tolub told others in the tavern he had "disposed of them," calling them meddlers who didn't belong.
5. **Treachery confirmed:** Following the party's survival of the Fire Temple trap, Skole confirmed that Tolub had deliberately betrayed them on behalf of the fire prophet.
6. **Ship schedule leaked:** Jorin Pike Kale provided the party with Tolub's ship schedule at the Nulb docks — actionable intelligence on his predictable movements that the party flagged as significant.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Boatman's Tavern, Nulb; running his pirate ship on a known, predictable schedule
- **Active operations:** Operating as an informant/agent for the fire prophet; monitoring or manipulating traffic through Nulb
- **What the party knows:** Tolub intentionally sent them into a Fire Temple trap; he works for the fire prophet; his dock schedule is now known (via Jorin Pike Kale)
- **What remains hidden:** The full extent of his relationship with the fire prophet, what he was paid or promised, and whether he knows the party survived or that they have his schedule

## Relationships
- **Fire Prophet (Alremm):** Covert agent; acts on the fire prophet's behalf in Nulb
- **Romag / Earth Temple:** Actively working against them by misdirecting would-be contacts
- **Skole:** Was aware of Tolub's treachery and disclosed it to the party
- **Jorin Pike Kale:** Knows Tolub's schedule well enough to share it — nature of their relationship unclear
- **Albreth & Ingulf:** Fire Temple priests Tolub directed the party toward at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon ambush
- **The Party:** Views them as threats or meddlers; believed (or claimed) he had eliminated them

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease:** Deliberately misdirected the party into a Fire Temple trap — a clear act of betrayal once Skole confirmed his role.
