# Eryk Velkori

## Identity
- **Role:** Leader of a Rhennee caravan encamped near Nulb
- **Faction:** Rhennee clan (matriarch: Vethra; sister: Serena)
- **First Appearance:** Party found him at the caravan camp — drunk, grieving, surrounded by ash and the smell of river rot, weeping over his missing sister Serena

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Protect his family, especially Serena and his elderly mother Vethra; maintain the safety and stability of his caravan
- Demonstrably emotional and expressive — wept openly, then erupted into celebration with equal intensity
- Protective and pragmatic; warned the party against drawing Madame Selentis's attention and cautioned that even friends cannot always be trusted
- Dark humor in despair: responded to Sequoia's joke about a map to hell with "I'm already living in one"

## History with the Party
1. **First Contact:** Party found Eryk grieving and intoxicated, the camp unnaturally still. He explained Serena had disappeared after a visit to the Boatman's Tavern and suspected the Zelrads, the Crimson Reapers, or Nisera's Talons had taken her through a location called the Iron Lash.
2. **Intelligence Provided:** Named Zelt the Rat as leader of the Crimson Reapers and identified Madame Selentis as the true power in Nulb. Issued a serious warning about her reach and danger.
3. **Serena Rescued:** When the party returned Serena safely, the camp transformed — music, dancing, full celebration. Eryk declared the party family and formally inducted them into the Rhennee clan.
4. **Reward:** The party was credited 500 gold pieces for the rescue.
5. **Later Intel:** Eryk described the Hall of the Scarlet Moon — a site where a druid named Elazar teaches the rite of the wicker giant. He noted many groups had gathered there in a festive atmosphere but found it strange that people seemed to be burning fire continuously throughout the day.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Rhennee caravan camp near Nulb
- **Active Situation:** Reunited with Serena; camp restored to normal activity
- **What the Party Knows:** Eryk considers them clan — they have his protection and friendship in Nulb. He is a reliable source of local criminal intelligence.
- **What Remains Hidden:** Depth of the Rhennee clan's own operations and connections; whether his warning about untrustworthy friends carries a specific subtext

## Relationships
- **Vethra (mother):** Protective of her; actively protested her attempts at divination, fearing the effort would harm or kill her
- **Serena (sister):** His entire emotional anchor at time of first meeting; her rescue transformed his demeanor completely
- **Madame Selentis:** Fears her; treats her as a near-untouchable power in Nulb
- **Zelt the Rat / Crimson Reapers:** Knowledgeable about them; named them as suspects in Serena's abduction
- **The Party:** Considers them adopted Rhennee family; has extended formal clan protection and friendship

## Arc Score Events
- **Increase:** Party rescued Serena and returned her safely → Eryk declared them family, rewarded them with 500gp credit, formally welcomed them into the Rhennee clan
