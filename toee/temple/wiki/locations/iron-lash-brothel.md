# Iron Lash Brothel
The Iron Lash brothel is a location in Nulb, run by [[Elvanna]]. It is located above the [[Boatman's Tavern]]. The brothel has also been involved in holding captives for leverage, as seen in the case of [[Serena Velkori]], who was held captive by [[Elvanna]] as leverage for a debt owed by the Rhennee clan.
