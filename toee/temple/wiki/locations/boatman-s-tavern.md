# Boatman's Tavern
The Boatman's Tavern is a location in [[Nulb]] where [[Skole]] and [[the-party]] interact. It is owned by [[Lodriss]], but [[Skole]] operates it under her ownership and [[Tolub]]'s shadow. The tavern is also the site of the [[Iron Lash brothel]].
