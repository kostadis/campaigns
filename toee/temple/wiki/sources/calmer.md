# Calmer

Source document detailing the life, actions, and death of Calmer, a cleric of St. Cuthbert assigned to the party by Terjon. The document chronicles his repeated tactical failures, social dysfunction, and eventual death at the hands of Thalsor during the Black Earth Compound battle. His intact corpse was recovered by Sequoia, raising questions about resurrection and institutional accountability. Calmer’s presence highlights the tension between institutional loyalty and emergent party pragmatism, serving as a narrative case study in expendable-asset tactics and pious-social-dysfunction.

His actions at the Moathouse, Honey Haven Tavern, and during the pool room retrieval demonstrate a pattern of well-intentioned but catastrophic interference. His death and the party’s response underscore the fragility of resurrection and the normalization of clerical expendability in Nulb’s underworld.
