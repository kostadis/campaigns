# Wat

Wat is a bartender at the [[Waterside Hostel]] in Nulb. He is suspected to be an agent of the [[Water Temple]]. 

His interactions with [[the-party]] and other factions are of significant interest, specifically his attempt to contact [[Dren Halveth]].
