# Captain Varsik (Source)

Ledger entry recovered from a deceased Fire Temple warlock identifies Captain Varsik and the *Crimson Trader* as the logistical link in the transport of the chimera. The document names Vesrek as the intermediary who arranged the transaction. No direct encounters with Varsik have occurred. The source establishes Varsik as a documentary-only target — known only through records, not personal interaction.

This ledger is the primary evidence linking the Fire Temple to maritime-based necromantic logistics and confirms the existence of a deniable supply chain operating beyond Nulb’s land-based networks. It corroborates Vesrek’s role as a broker and reinforces the Fire Temple’s pattern of using third-party contractors to obscure institutional involvement in forbidden activities.

The ledger does not reveal Varsik’s motivations, allegiance, or awareness of the cargo’s nature. Varsik’s current whereabouts and extent of involvement with the Fire Temple remain unknown.
