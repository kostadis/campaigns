# Elvanna

Source document detailing the interactions between the party and Elvanna, madam of the Iron Lash brothel in Nulb. Describes her role as a transactional operator who leverages captivity and property for economic gain, offering a non-violent but highly effective model of underworld power. Documents the ransom of Serena, the party’s payment without negotiation, and Elvanna’s sardonic assessment of their naivety. Contrasts with state-based terror (Madame Selentis) and highlights the economic logic of informal power structures in Nulb.

This source provides information about Elvanna, the madam of the Iron Lash brothel in Nulb, and her interactions with the party and other characters. Her operation exemplifies how informal power in Nulb functions through economic leverage rather than overt violence, distinguishing her from figures like Madame Selentis. The party’s encounter with her—particularly their unquestioning payment to secure Serena’s release—reveals both her shrewdness and their own inexperience in navigating the city’s underworld economy. Elvanna’s influence extends beyond the brothel, intersecting with the Rhennee and the social undercurrents of Boatman’s Tavern.
