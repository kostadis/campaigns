# Alrrem

Alrrem is a deity or powerful entity associated with the [[fire-temple]]. He is a significant figure within the temple's hierarchy and has had various interactions with the party and other characters.

## Worship and Servants
Alrrem is worshipped by the [[fire-temple-warlock]], who served as a dedicated follower. In this capacity, the warlock also acted as the handler for the temple's [[the-chimera]].
