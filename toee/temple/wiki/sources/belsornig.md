# Belsornig

Source document detailing the hidden operations and institutional influence of Belsornig, Canon and Prophet of the Water Temple within the Temple of Elemental Evil. Compiled from interrogations, confessions, ledger entries, and analysis by Hartsch of the Earth Temple. Confirms Belsornig as the central architect of the chimera scheme and bandit network near Hommlet, operating through proxies to destabilize rival temples.

Key evidence includes:
- Confession of Gnarley Forest brigand linking Belsornig to organized banditry
- Urot’s identification of Belsornig as Snej’s supplier at the Abbey of Allitur
- Ledger entry from Fire Temple warlock naming Belsornig as co-conspirator
- Hartsch’s analysis identifying Belsornig as the primary long-term threat

This source recontextualizes the chimera operation from a Fire Temple initiative to a Water Temple trap targeting both the party and rival factions.
