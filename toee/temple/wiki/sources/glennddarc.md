# Glennddarc

Headgnome of Sheernobb and the de facto bureaucratic authority of the settlement. Glennddarc prioritizes the existential threat of giants over the visible but politically exploited bandit problem. He dismisses Captain Gnonsbim’s campaign as performative electioneering and redirects the party toward the true threat with financial incentive. His habit of falling asleep mid-conversation suggests either profound indifference, strategic disengagement, or hidden knowledge. He holds critical but unshared intelligence on the giants and uses Pasmarie Clee as an unofficial node of redirected information.

His governance reflects a philosophy of [[concepts/existential-threat-prioritization]] and [[concepts/bureaucratic-indifference-as-strategy]], contrasting sharply with Gnonsbim’s theatrical law enforcement. He represents the quiet, unacknowledged layer of Sheernobb’s leadership — one that operates not through spectacle, but through calculated neglect and deferred action.
