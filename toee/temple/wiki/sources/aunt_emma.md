# Aunt Emma

Source document detailing the encounter with Aunt Emma, a Green Hag, in the Gnarley Forest. The account describes her use of illusion to pose as a benevolent elder, her predatory hospitality centered on poisoned food, and her eventual death at the hands of Zephyr. Recovered artifacts include the Hat of Wizardry and *The Hag's Guide to 101 Recipes Of Children*. This event marks the first confirmed encounter with a hag in the party’s operational zone and reveals the presence of fey predators in the Gnarley Forest.

## Key Details

**Key Figures:**  
- **Zinnia**: Recognized the hag’s disguise, enabling the party to counter her deception.  
- **Zephyr**: Executed the hag, ending the threat.

**Key Locations:**  
- **Gnarley Forest**: Primary setting of the encounter.  
- **Gingerbread House**: Site of the hag’s predatory hospitality ritual.

**Key Artifacts Recovered:**  
- **Hat of Wizardry**  
- ***The Hag's Guide to 101 Recipes Of Children***

## Significance

This encounter reinforces the dangers of fey deception and the critical role of perceptual awareness in survival. Aunt Emma exemplifies the predatory ritual of poisoned hospitality, a hallmark of hag behavior documented in fey lore. Her use of illusion to assume a trusted elder identity underscores the importance of recognizing fey disguises — a lesson now institutionalized in party training protocols.

This source provides foundational evidence for the existence of hags within the party’s operational zone and has informed subsequent investigations into fey predation patterns in the Gnarley Forest.
