# The Crimson Reaper Unnamed Spokesperson
This source introduces the Crimson Reaper Spokesperson, an unnamed individual serving as an enforcer for the Crimson Reapers. The spokesperson is central to the plot, issuing an ultimatum to Dren Halveth and interacting with Calmer from the Earth Temple.
