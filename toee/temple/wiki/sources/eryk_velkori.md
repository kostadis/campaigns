# Eryk Velkori

Source document detailing the life, relationships, and intelligence provided by Eryk Velkori, leader of a Rhennee caravan near Nulb. This account chronicles his emotional journey following the abduction of his sister Serena, his interactions with the party, and his revelations about key locations and factions in Nulb’s underworld.

Eryk’s testimony confirms the role of the Iron Lash brothel as a nexus for abductions, validates the threat posed by Zelt the Rat and the Crimson Reapers, and identifies Nisera’s Talons as a new criminal actor. His emotional transformation after Serena’s rescue — from grief-stricken outcast to clan patriarch — marks a pivotal shift in the party’s standing within Nulb’s social fabric.

He also provides the first known account of the Hall of the Scarlet Moon and the rite of the wicker giant, introducing a new ritualistic phenomenon with potential ties to elemental binding or necromancy. His warnings about Madame Selentis and his fear of spiritual exhaustion in his mother’s divination practices reveal deep cultural constraints within the Rhennee clan.

This source is critical for understanding the Rhennee as a non-criminal but deeply entangled nomadic faction and for mapping hidden networks of power in Nulb.
