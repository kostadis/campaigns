# Thalsor of the Stone

Thalsor of the Stone is a druid and the commander of a [[earth-cult]] compound. 

During the battle at the Black Earth compound, Thalsor acted as an enemy combatant and was responsible for the death of [[calmer]]. Following the kill, he threatened to mutilate [[calmer]]'s corpse.
