# Captain Gnonsbim

Source document detailing the municipal captain of Sheernobb and his politically motivated commissioning of the party to clear bandits. The account reveals Gnonsbim’s use of public credit, magical weaponry as patronage, and romantic interest in Pasmarie Clee as core motivators. Internal critique from Headgnome Glennddarc exposes the operation as performative political theater, not genuine law enforcement.

Key elements include:
- Commissioning the party before agreement, indicating urgency for narrative over solution
- Conditional reward structure tied to public acknowledgment
- Promise of custom magical weapons as deferred patronage
- Romantic interest in Pasmarie Clee as hidden driver
- Contrast between public persona and private critique

This source extends the wiki’s understanding of power structures by demonstrating how municipal authority replicates underworld transactional logic under the guise of legitimacy.
