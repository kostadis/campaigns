# Elfar Twell

Elfar Twell was a citizen of Nulb whose public execution by hanging served as a calculated act of political theater by Madame Selentis. His body was displayed at the Gallows Table as a warning to others who might cross her authority. The specific nature of his offense remains unknown, reinforcing the arbitrary and terror-based nature of Selentis’s rule. His death is not a criminal act but a ritualized assertion of power — a silent victim whose only narrative function is to illuminate the brutality of his executioner.

Twell’s case contrasts with other known disappearances in Nulb, such as Y’dey’s, by emphasizing visible, immediate punishment over mystery. He is not a missing person — he is a message.

This source details the discovery of Elfar Twell's body in Nulb and its implications on the power dynamics within the town.
