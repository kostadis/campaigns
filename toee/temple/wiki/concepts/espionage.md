# Espionage
Espionage refers to the practice of spying or using spies to obtain information about the plans and activities of a rival or enemy. This can involve gathering sensitive information through various means, including covert operations and surveillance.

## Examples of Espionage
There are several examples of espionage in the context of the temples:
* Lieutenant Krell's actions as a covert agent of the [[fire-temple]] within the [[earth-temple]] exemplify the use of espionage in the struggle between the two temples.
* Wat's actions, such as attempting to pry into [[the-party]]'s business, suggest that he may be engaged in espionage activities on behalf of the [[water-temple]].

## Purpose and Implications
The primary purpose of espionage is to gain a strategic advantage over a rival or enemy by obtaining valuable information. This can have significant implications, including influencing the outcome of conflicts and shaping the balance of power between different factions. In the context of the temples, espionage plays a crucial role in the ongoing struggle for power and resources.
