# Symbolic-Clothing-as-Narrative-Clue

A recurring narrative device in which clothing — particularly unusual, incongruous, or ritually significant garments — signals hidden identity, affiliation, or plot relevance. The most prominent example is Derrick’s purple robe, worn over filthy, bloodstained traveling clothes. Purple historically denotes nobility, clerical rank, or arcane affiliation in this setting, yet Derrick claims no faction. This dissonance suggests either a disguise, a stolen artifact, or an unacknowledged past. The robe functions as a deliberate narrative gap, inviting speculation about its origin: Was it taken from a victim? A relic from a hidden temple? A marker of a hidden mission? This concept encourages the wiki to treat anomalous attire not as background detail, but as potential key to unresolved mysteries.
