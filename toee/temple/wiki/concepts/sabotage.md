# Sabotage
Sabotage refers to the act of deliberately destroying or damaging something, often in a covert or surreptitious manner. This can include disrupting operations or damaging physical structures, such as the castle construction in [[Hommlet]]. In the context of the [[Earth Temple]] and [[Fire Temple]], sabotage has been used as a means of gaining an advantage.

Examples of sabotage include:
* Disrupting operations at the [[Earth Temple]] and [[Fire Temple]]
* Damaging the castle construction in [[Hommlet]]
Notable individuals associated with sabotage include [[Lieutenant Krell]] and [[Rufus of Hommlet]].
