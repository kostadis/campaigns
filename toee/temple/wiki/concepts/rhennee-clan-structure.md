# Rhennee clan structure

A matriarchal, kinship-based social organization that governs the Rhennee nomads. Leadership is inherited matrilineally, with the matriarch (currently Vethra) serving as spiritual and familial anchor. Authority is not enforced through hierarchy but through emotional bonds, shared trauma, and collective survival.

The clan operates on principles of absolute familial loyalty — Eryk’s entire identity is tied to protecting Serena and Vethra. Outsiders are rarely accepted, but formal induction — as granted to the party after Serena’s rescue — confers full kinship status, including protection, access to underground networks, and mutual obligation.

The clan avoids overt criminality but navigates Nulb’s underworld through proximity: they trade, transport, and inform, often acting as intermediaries between criminal factions and vulnerable populations. Their neutrality is not passive — it is strategic, rooted in survival.

A defining cultural constraint is their taboo against divination that risks the lives of elders — a belief that spiritual magic exacts a fatal toll on the frail. This suggests a deep, unspoken understanding of magical cost, possibly tied to the rituals at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon. Their society is thus one of emotional resilience and spiritual caution — a fragile balance between survival and sacrifice.
