# Criminal Organization Leadership

Criminal organization leadership in Nulb manifests in multiple, overlapping forms — from Zelt the Rat’s smuggling-based hierarchy to Madame Selentis’s terror-based governance. Understanding Zelt’s role and motivations within the Crimson Reapers is crucial for comprehending the organization’s operations, which rely on economic control, loyalty networks, and territorial dominance.

However, Selentis introduces a distinct subtype: **Terror-Based Governance**. Here, leadership is maintained not through profit or patronage, but through ritualized, public violence and the arbitrary elimination of perceived threats. Her rule is not criminal enterprise — it is authoritarian spectacle. The Gallows Table and the execution of Elfar Twell exemplify this model: control is achieved not by what is taken, but by what is destroyed.

This concept expands the understanding of power in Nulb beyond organized crime into the realm of political terror. The distinction between gangster and tyrant blurs — but the methods remain fundamentally different.
