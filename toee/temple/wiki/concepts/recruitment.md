# Recruitment
Recruitment refers to the process of attracting, enlisting, and hiring new members into an organization. 

## Importance in Organizational Growth
In the context of the Earth Temple, recruitment is a crucial aspect of their growth and expansion. Figures like [[Dick Rentsch]] play a key role in identifying and vetting potential recruits.

## Examples of Recruitment
One example of recruitment is [[Dren Halveth]]'s recruitment by the Earth Temple, which illustrates the concept of attracting and hiring new members for an organization. 

## Key Aspects
The recruitment process involves various stages, including:
- Identifying potential recruits
- Vetting and evaluating candidates
- Hiring new members
