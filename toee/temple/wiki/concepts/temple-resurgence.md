# Temple Resurgence
The Temple Resurgence refers to the re-emergence of the Temple of Elemental Evil as a threat, marking the return or re-emergence of the Temple's influence or activity in the Hommlet area. 
Burne is concerned about managing the narrative around this event to avoid panic or embarrassment.
