# Alliances and Rivalries
Alliances and rivalries involve the formation of alliances and rivalries between different temples and characters. The Earth Temple, led by Romag, is rivalled by the Water Temple, led by Belsornig, while the Fire Temple and Air Temple are considered less significant.
