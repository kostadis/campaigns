# Druidism
Druidism is a spiritual practice or profession that involves a connection with nature, and can also be considered a magical concept. It is a nature-based spiritual practice that individuals such as [[elazar]], [[faria]], and pasmarie-clee follow as druids. This practice is significant in understanding their interactions, motivations, and roles as druids, as it embodies the principles of Druidism. Druidism is relevant to understanding the roles and potential motivations of various characters, including those in the [[circle-of-the-scarlet-moon]] and [[verigo]].
