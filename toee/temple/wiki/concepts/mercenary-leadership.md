# Mercenary Leadership
Mercenary leadership refers to the act of leading or commanding and managing a group of mercenaries. A key example of this concept is Dren Halveth's leadership of the [[broken-blades]], which is crucial to understanding his actions and the dynamics of the [[earth-temple|temple war]]. 

This leadership style and the motivations behind it play a significant role in the context of [[faction-dynamics]]. Understanding mercenary leadership is essential to grasping the complexities of the relationships and conflicts within the [[dren-halveth]] narrative.
