# Information Brokerage
Information brokerage refers to the act of gathering, sharing, or withholding information for strategic gain. This can involve selling relevant information to interested parties, such as [[Lord Denelu]], who is willing to pay for the location of the lost chest. Alternatively, information brokerage can also involve providing crucial information to individuals or groups, like [[Skole]] does for [[the-party]], potentially altering their course or decisions.
