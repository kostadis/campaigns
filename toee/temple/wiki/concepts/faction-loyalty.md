# Faction Loyalty
Faction loyalty refers to the commitment or devotion to a faction's goals and interests. It involves allegiance to a particular group or organization, navigating complex social dynamics of loyalty and betrayal between factions.

## Examples and Case Studies
Examples of faction loyalty can be seen in the situations of [[skole]] and [[snej]], where individuals must balance their commitments to different groups, such as [[the-party]], [[fire-temple]], [[earth-temple]], and [[water temple]]. Additionally, the suspected loyalty of [[wat]] to the Water Temple drives his actions and motivations, further illustrating the concept of faction loyalty.

## Character Motivations
Understanding faction loyalty is crucial in grasping the motivations and actions of characters like [[tolub]] and [[lodriss]], as their allegiances and commitments to various factions influence their decisions and behaviors.
