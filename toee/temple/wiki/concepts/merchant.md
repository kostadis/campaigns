# Merchant
A merchant is a person who buys and sells goods. 

## Roles and Examples
In different contexts, merchants have various roles. For instance:
- In the story, [[florian]] and [[derrick]] are merchants who were captured and rescued by the party.
- In the context of [[magda]], her role as a merchant involves selling magical curiosities. 

Note that the specific activities and goods sold by merchants can vary greatly depending on the situation and the individual's area of expertise.
