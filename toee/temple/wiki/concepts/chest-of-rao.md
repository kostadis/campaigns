# Chest of Rao
The Chest of Rao is a powerful artifact of great good, created by high priests of [[Rao]]. It is sought after by both evil forces and the [[Archclericy of Veluna]].

## History and Use
The Chest of Rao was used in the [[Titan-summoning ritual]]. However, it was displaced by Sequoia, disrupting the ritual and leading to [[Tharok]]'s death. 

## Significance
The Chest of Rao remains a highly significant and coveted artifact, with various factions vying for control of it. Its power and potential uses make it a crucial element in the struggle between good and evil forces.
