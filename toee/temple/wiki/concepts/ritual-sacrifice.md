# Ritual Sacrifice
Ritual sacrifice refers to the act of killing a person or animal as an offering to a deity or supernatural force. Lieutenant Krell was executed by Romag via a ritual sacrifice, torn apart by four earth elementals, highlighting the brutal nature of this practice.
