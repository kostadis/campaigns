# Covert Operations
Covert operations refer to secret intelligence or military operations, often conducted by agents or organizations to achieve specific goals without being detected. In the context of Elmo and Verbobonc, covert operations involve monitoring threats, gathering intelligence, and containing evil presence in the region.
