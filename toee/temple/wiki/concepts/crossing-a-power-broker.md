# Crossing a Power Broker

Crossing a Power Broker refers to any perceived or actual act of defiance, interference, or challenge against a dominant authority figure in Nulb’s underworld — whether economic, political, or personal. The consequences are rarely proportional or explained; instead, they are swift, brutal, and public — designed to deter others.

Elfar Twell’s execution by Madame Selentis is a canonical example: his offense is unknown, yet his death is absolute. Similarly, Zelt the Rat eliminates rivals without warning, but Selentis elevates this into ritual. The ambiguity of what constitutes a “crossing” is intentional — it ensures constant fear, as no one can be certain what act will trigger retribution.

This concept underpins the lawless yet structured nature of power in Nulb. It is not about laws, but about perception — and the terror of being unable to predict or avoid punishment.
