# Jerry
Jerry was a soldier of [[Verbobonc]] who was found imprisoned in a monastery's torture chamber alongside [[Warda]]. He demonstrated loyalty to [[Verbobonc]] and its interests, as well as pragmatism in the face of a greater threat. 
## Death
Jerry died during the fight against [[Tharok]].
