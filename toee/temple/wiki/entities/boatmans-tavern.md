# Boatman’s Tavern

A local establishment located directly beneath the Iron Lash brothel in Nulb. It is the last known location where Serena was seen before her abduction.

Eryk Velkori identified it as the point of entry for the operation that led to Serena’s capture, suggesting the tavern serves as a front or staging ground for the brothel’s more illicit activities. Its proximity to the Iron Lash implies a coordinated system of lure, observation, and extraction — possibly involving patrons, staff, or both.

The tavern’s unassuming nature masks its role in Nulb’s underworld: a place where vulnerable travelers or locals are identified, isolated, and passed upward into the brothel’s network. Its connection to the Rhennee clan — through Eryk’s grief and Serena’s disappearance — positions it as a critical node in the trafficking chain.

No other sources have described the tavern in detail, making it a potential site for future investigation into the mechanics of abduction in Nulb.
