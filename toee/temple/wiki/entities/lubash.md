# Lubash

An ogre who serves as a guard and hunter for the lower level of the [[moathouse|Moathouse]], with loose affiliations to the [[the-temple|Temple]]. Lubash treats human captives as livestock, viewing unaffiliated individuals as potential food sources. He held [[derrick|Derrick]] and [[florian|Florian]] captive in the Moathouse’s pantry as a food source, killing and consuming others before them. His presence exemplifies the [[prisoner-as-commodity]] concept—not as a strategic actor, but as a primal force of consumption.

Characterized by his simplicity, aggression, and territorial nature, Lubash’s brutality mirrors the logic of criminal hierarchies in Nulb. His continued occupation of the Moathouse suggests either neglect by local authorities or tacit tolerance, underscoring the region’s descent into lawlessness. Though not part of any organized group, his role in the Moathouse’s horror makes him a key figure in understanding the moral decay of the area.

Lubash was eventually killed by [[the-party|the party]] after he attacked them, a confrontation that highlights the extreme dangers posed by his predatory instincts and unregulated violence.
