# Earth Cult

The Earth Cult (also known as the Black Earth Cult) is an organization operating within the Kron Hills. They are known rivals to [[lareth-the-beautiful]]'s Fire Temple.

## History and Activities
The cult was involved in a ritual intended to summon a Titan, an event that was ultimately disrupted by [[the-party]]. [[tharok]] was a member of this organization.
