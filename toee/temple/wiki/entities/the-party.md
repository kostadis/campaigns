# The Party

The Party is the central group of protagonists in the Nulb region’s unfolding narrative. Known for their ability to navigate complex social dynamics, form strategic partnerships, and adapt to various challenges, they function as moral anchors in a world defined by brutality and indifference. Their actions consistently prioritize human connection and restoration over transactional neutrality or exploitation, setting them apart from figures like Elvanna and Madame Selentis.

## History and Activities

### Contracts and Quests
The Party was initially hired by [[gonnathrad_kevanarial]] to hunt bandits; this interaction took place at [[the-cheap-forester]] tavern located in [[caer_ostverk]]. Since then, they have undertaken numerous notable missions:

* **Escort Duty:** They escorted [[Airgid]] to her home near the Lotrimil mountains.
* **Rescue Operations:** 
  * Paid 500gp to [[Elvanna]] to release [[Serena]] from captivity at the [[iron-lash-brothel]].
  * Rescued [[Jarran]] from the Moathouse pantry, though they declined his offer to form an alliance to investigate brigand activity.
  * Rescued [[Derrick]] and [[Florian]] from the Moathouse, where they were being held as food for the ogre [[Lubash]]. Derrick and Florian promised them a reward for safe return to Hommlet — an unfulfilled obligation that now hangs as a narrative thread.
* **Exploration:** While searching for a wild boar in the Gnarley Forest, they encountered [[Aunt Emma]] and discovered her gingerbread house.

### Alliances and Encounters
The Party has established deep and meaningful connections with various individuals and factions:

* **Druidic Connections:** They encountered [[Verigo]] and his fellow druids at [[camp-number-one]], near the [[hall-of-the-scarlet-moon]]. They also allied with [[Warda]] to defeat [[Tharok]].
* **Guides:** [[Spugnoir_balkof]] has joined the group as a guide. In exchange for his services, the Party has agreed to provide him with a share of treasure and first claim on arcane scrolls.
* **Other Encounters:** They met [[Faria]] at [[camp-number-one]], and have interacted with [[Zinia]], [[Zephyr]], [[Jerry]], and [[Zelt-the-Rat]] in the course of their travels.
* **Survivor Support:** Their continued engagement with survivors like [[Derrick]] and [[Florian]] reinforces their role as agents of restoration and compassion, contrasting sharply with the terror-based rule of [[Madame-Selentis]] and the transactional detachment of others.

## Locations and Affiliations
The Party's activities have taken them to several significant locations, including:

* **The Iron Lash Brothel:** Site of their interaction with [[Elvanna]] and the rescue of [[Serena]].
* **Camp Number One:** A location where they met both [[Faria]] and the druids near the [[hall-of-the-scarlet-moon]].
* **Caer Ostverk:** The location of their initial engagement with [[gonnathrad_kevanarial]].
* **The Moathouse:** Site of their rescue of [[Jarran]], [[Derrick]], and [[Florian]] from captivity and imminent consumption by [[Lubash]].
* **Gnarley Forest:** Where they encountered [[Aunt Emma]] and her gingerbread house.

Their presence in these locations often marks turning points in local narratives, transforming places of despair into sites of hope and reconnection.
