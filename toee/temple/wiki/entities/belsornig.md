# Belsornig

Also known as **Belsoring**, Belsornig is the Canon and Prophet who leads the Water Temple within the Temple of Elemental Evil. He has never been directly encountered by the party but is identified through multiple intelligence threads as the mastermind behind the chimera scheme and the bandit network operating near Hommlet.

Belsornig operates through a network of intermediaries — including bandit leader Snej, logistics agent Vesrek, and Captain Varsik of the Crimson Trader — to execute operations while maintaining plausible deniability. His strategy reflects [[indirect-warfare-via-proxy-networks]] and [[asymmetric-pressure-campaign]], avoiding direct confrontation in favor of economic, logistical, and psychological pressure. He is a master of indirect warfare, ensuring every action has contingency and every failure serves a secondary purpose — a “no lose condition” approach noted by his rival, Hartsch of the Earth Temple.

Hartsch identifies Belsornig as the greatest threat within the Temple of Elemental Evil, citing his ability to manipulate rival factions through proxy networks. His name appears in the ledger of a deceased Fire Temple warlock, confirming his reach into rival temples and his role in destabilizing their operations.

The party knows his title, faction, and operational footprint — but not his location, resources, or ultimate endgame. He is the archetype of the [[documentary-knowledge-only-target]]: a figure whose power derives from invisibility and whose influence is inferred through documents, agents, and unintended consequences.

**Relationships:**
- **Vesrek:** Operational intermediary in the chimera purchase; orchestrates deniable logistics and supply-line control
- **Captain Varsik:** Logistics coordinator for chimera transport; operates under Belsornig’s direction
- **Snej:** Bandit leader and subordinate; uses the Abbey of Allitur as a base for raids and intelligence gathering
- **Urot:** Orc cook who identified Belsornig; source of critical intelligence linking him to the chimera scheme
- **Hartsch:** Rival faction leader of the Earth Temple; views Belsornig as primary threat and greatest strategic adversary
- **The Party:** Indirect antagonist; targeted via proxy networks without direct engagement

Belsornig is a key player in the [[inter-temple-rivalry-elemental-evil]], leveraging his control over water-based supply lines and deniable logistics networks to weaken the Earth and Fire Temples while consolidating the Water Temple’s influence.
