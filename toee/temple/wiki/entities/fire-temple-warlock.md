# Fire Temple Warlock
The Fire Temple Warlock was a servant of Alrrem and the handler of the Fire Temple's chimera. He was a devotee of Alrrem the All-Consuming and played a significant role in the story. His goals were to carry out Alrrem's will and destroy the party.
