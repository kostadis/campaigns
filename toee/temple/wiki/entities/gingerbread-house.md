# Gingerbread House

A deceptively charming structure located deep within the Gnarley Forest, constructed entirely of enchanted gingerbread, icing, and candied fruit. It served as the lair and hunting ground of Aunt Emma, the Green Hag, who used its inviting appearance to lure victims—especially children—into a false sense of safety.

Inside, the house contained a table set with poisoned plum pie and shelves lined with jars of preserved children’s fingers and toes. The doorframe was lined with real gingerbread, confirmed by Zinnia to be freshly baked and magically preserved. The house emits a faint, sweet odor that lingers for miles, acting as a magical lure.

After Aunt Emma’s death, the house remains standing but dormant. Zinnia suspects it may reactivate if another hag claims it, or if the magical bindings are disturbed. The structure is now considered a haunted site and a potential trap for unwary travelers.

**Status**: Active trap (dormant).  
**Key artifacts recovered**: Hat of Wizardry, *The Hag's Guide to 101 Recipes Of Children*.  
**Warning**: Do not consume any food within. Do not touch the doorframe.
