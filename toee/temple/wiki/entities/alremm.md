# Alremm
Alremm is a prophet and head of the Fire Temple, a major authority figure within the elemental evil hierarchy. He values power and glory as core principles of the Fire Temple's philosophy and is willing to use dangerous methods to achieve his goals. However, according to [[Hartsch]], the Fire Temple is considered a spent force, suggesting a potential decline in its influence or power.

Despite this, Alremm remains a significant figure, leading the Fire Temple and playing a crucial role in the elemental evil hierarchy. His leadership and the temple's activities are likely to have implications for other elemental temples, including the [[Earth Temple]], [[Water Temple]], and [[Air Temple]].
