# Belsornig

Belsornig is a key figure within the [[Temple of Elemental Evil]] (also referred to as the [[Temple of Evil]]). He is associated with the [[water-temple]] located within the complex.

## Role and Connections
Belsornig is described by [[urot]] as a "big shot" within the Temple hierarchy. He serves as a provider of resources to [[snej]], a bandit leader.
