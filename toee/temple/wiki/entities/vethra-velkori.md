# Vethra Velkori

Vethra Velkori is a matriarch of the [[rhennee-clan]] and a prominent member of the [[velkori-family]]. She serves as the clan diviner, utilizing her mystical talents to guide her people and family.

## Family
Vethra is a central figure within her lineage. She is the mother of [[eryk-velkori]] and the grandmother of [[tala-velkori]]. She is known to be deeply protective of her family members.

## Role and Abilities
As a diviner, Vethra performs readings to reveal critical information and fate. She has utilized methods such as [[tarokka]] to assist [[the-party]], having extended an invitation to them for a reading through [[tala-velkori]]. Her visions have been instrumental in revealing the location and fate of [[serena-velkori]].

## Personal Cost
The practice of divination is not without its dangers; the act comes at a great personal cost to Vethra. Her readings compromise her physical health and potentially threaten her life.
