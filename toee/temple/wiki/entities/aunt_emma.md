# Aunt Emma

A Green Hag who operated independently in the Gnarley Forest. She lured victims—particularly children—through the guise of a harmless elderly woman, offering poisoned hospitality in her gingerbread house. Her true form, revealed upon exposure, featured green skin, clawed hands, and sharp teeth. She attempted to bargain for her life after being identified by Zinnia, offering knowledge of a hidden treasure in exchange for mercy. Zephyr rejected her plea and killed her.

She was the first confirmed hag encountered by the party and exemplifies the predatory, non-negotiating nature of certain fey entities. Her death yielded two significant artifacts: the Hat of Wizardry and *The Hag's Guide to 101 Recipes Of Children*.

**Relationships**:  
- [[zinia]]: Recognized her true form, triggering the confrontation.  
- [[zephyr]]: Delivered the killing blow; demonstrated consistent rejection of fey bargains.

**Status**: Deceased.  
**Last known location**: Gingerbread House, Gnarley Forest.
