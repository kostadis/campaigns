# Gremag
Gremag was a multifaceted operative of the Temple of Elemental Evil, serving as both a bodyguard and a covert agent. He demonstrated no interest in diplomacy and fought with professional combat training. In his role as a covert agent, Gremag worked as a merchant in Hommlet, where he collaborated with [[Rannos Davl]] to acquire the [[Chest of Rao]] for the Temple of Elemental Evil.
