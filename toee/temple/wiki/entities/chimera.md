# Chimera

A multi-species, necromantically engineered beast, the chimera is the signature weapon of the Fire Temple. Bred or assembled from the fused remains of fire drakes, lions, and serpents, it is bound to its handler through the [[control-chain-of-alrrem]], a ritual conduit that channels Alrrem’s will.

Its primary attack is a devastating breath of superheated flame, capable of incinerating armored foes. It is not merely a monster — it is a divine instrument, designed to embody Alrrem’s doctrine of purification through consumption. The chimera’s deployment via the [[crimson-trader]] confirms its role as a strategic asset, not a random threat.

Its control is absolute only so long as the chain remains intact. When Zephyr seized the chain, the chimera turned on its handler — revealing that its obedience is not trained, but magically enforced. This makes it both terrifying and vulnerable.

The chimera’s existence confirms the [[divine-weaponized-beast]] concept and elevates the Fire Temple from a cult to a military-industrial theocracy. Its origins may lie in the [[deep-temple]], and its creation may be tied to forbidden soul-binding rituals.
