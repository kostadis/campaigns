# Crimson Reapers

The Crimson Reapers are a complex and multifaceted organization with dual identities. They function both as an adversarial group involved in the theft of the Broken Blades' weapons and as a multifaceted entity with ties to both religious enforcement and organized crime.

## Dual Nature and Operations

The organization's activities are divided into two primary spheres:

### Temple Enforcement and Recovery
The Crimson Reapers serve a mysterious overarching entity behind the [[four-elemental-temples]]. In this capacity, they are involved in the recovery of stolen temple property and the enforcement of various ultimatums.

### Criminal Enterprise
Operating as a criminal organization, the group seeks to expand its control over the criminal underworld of Nulb. Under this facet, they run supply funneling operations and other illicit activities through the area.
