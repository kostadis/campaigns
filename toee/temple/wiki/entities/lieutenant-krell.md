# Krell

Krell was a lieutenant of the [[earth-temple]] who functioned as a covert agent for the [[fire-temple]]. His primary objective was to conduct espionage and sabotage [[earth-temple]] operations from within.

## Discovery and Death
Krell's activities were suspected by [[romag]], who kept him under surveillance. Upon his exposure, Krell attempted to form an alliance with [[the-party]] to destroy the [[earth-temple]], but the party refused his proposal. 

Following this refusal, Krell was executed by [[romag]] via ritual sacrifice. The party subsequently discovered his body; while there was initial speculation that he might have been killed by gnolls, it is now understood he was executed by [[romag]].

## Impact
Krell's death has created a significant power gap within the [[earth-temple]]. This vacancy and the circumstances surrounding his betrayal and execution may provide useful information for [[the-party]] to leverage in their future dealings with the [[fire-temple]].
