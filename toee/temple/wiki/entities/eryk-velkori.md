# Eryk Velkori

Leader of a Rhennee caravan encamped near [[nulb]] and the emotional anchor of his family. Eryk is a deeply expressive, pragmatic, and protective figure whose life revolves around the safety of his sister [[serena-velkori]] and his elderly mother [[vethra-velkori]]. A key informant for [[the-party]], he serves as a critical source of underworld intelligence and a bridge to nomadic networks.

## Role and Contributions

Eryk played a pivotal role in the party's investigation by disclosing the identity of [[zelt-the-rat]], providing their first significant lead regarding the leadership of the [[crimson-reapers]]. He also warned the party against crossing [[madame-selentis]], whom he regards as an untouchable force of terror in Nulb, and named [[nisera-s-talons]] as a possible threat.

After the party successfully rescued Serena from the [[iron-lash-brothel]], Eryk underwent a dramatic transformation: he celebrated with music and dancing, formally inducted the party into the [[rhennee-clan]], and granted them a 500gp credit as a sign of clan loyalty. He now considers the party family and offers them ongoing protection and intelligence in return.

He is the only known source to describe the [[hall-of-the-scarlet-moon]] and the rite of the wicker giant, suggesting a hidden layer of ritual activity in the region. His insights into [[elazar]] and the [[circle-of-the-scarlet-moon]] remain vital to the party’s understanding of the occult threats in Nulb.

## Personal Life

Eryk is the brother of [[serena-velkori]] and the son of [[vethra-velkori]]. He initiated the rescue of his sister from the Iron Lash brothel, demonstrating his fierce dedication to his kin. He is deeply suspicious of spiritual practices that risk the lives of the elderly, having actively opposed Vethra’s attempts at divination.

His dark humor — such as responding to a joke about hell with “I’m already living in one” — masks a profound awareness of Nulb’s brutality. Despite his warnings that “even friends cannot always be trusted,” his trust in the party appears genuine — raising questions about whether his clan induction is a gesture of gratitude or a subtle recruitment.

Eryk first encountered the party in a state of profound grief, weeping over Serena’s disappearance after she visited the [[boatmans-tavern]]. His emotional vulnerability and pragmatic caution make him a uniquely compelling figure in the underworld of Nulb.

He remains at the Rhennee caravan camp, a reliable source of intelligence and a key node in the party’s access to nomadic networks.
