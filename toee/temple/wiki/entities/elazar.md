# Elazar

A druid and teacher of the Rite of the Wicker Giant at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon. Known primarily through Eryk Velkori’s account, Elazar is a mysterious and reclusive figure whose true motivations remain undisclosed.

He is described as a master of ancient rites, possessing deep knowledge of elemental and spiritual magic. The Rite of the Wicker Giant, which he teaches, involves fire, transformation, and possibly sacrifice — practices that align with the Hall’s persistent, unnatural fire, hinting at dangerous, potentially necromantic or summoning-based rituals. 

Elazar draws multiple groups to the Hall under the guise of celebration, yet his isolation and the secrecy surrounding his teachings suggest deeper, hidden purposes. Is he a guardian of forgotten traditions, a cult leader, or a pawn of a larger force? His role may be central to understanding the Hall’s true purpose — and its possible connection to the giants threatening Sheernobb.

While he claims to be working toward restoring nature’s balance, the violent and enigmatic nature of his rites casts doubt on whether his goals are truly benevolent.
