# Captain Tolub
Captain Tolub is a ship captain with river pirate connections and a covert agent of the Fire Temple. He operates out of the Boatman's Tavern in Nulb and runs a pirate ship on a known schedule. Tolub is driven by loyalty to the fire prophet and personal gain, using charm and deception to achieve his goals.
