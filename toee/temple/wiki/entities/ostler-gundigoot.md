# Ostler Goodingoot

Ostler Goodingoot is the innkeeper of the [[inn-of-the-welcome-wench]] in [[hommlet]]. In addition to his role as an innkeeper, he is a member of the [[hommlet-council]].

## Affiliations and Politics
Goodingoot is aligned with [[jaroo]]'s faction within the village. He maintains a skeptical view of certain residents, viewing [[burne]] and [[rufus-of-hommlet]] as outside interlopers.

## Role in Events
He was involved in the cover story created by the [[party]] to explain the disappearance of [[ranos-davl]] and Gremag.
