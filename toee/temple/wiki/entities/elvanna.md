# Elvanna

Elvanna is the madam of the Iron Lash brothel, located above the Boatman’s Tavern in Nulb. She is a key node in Nulb’s underground economy, operating with sharp transactional acumen and leveraging human capital and property as collateral for financial gain. Unlike overtly violent power brokers such as Madame Selentis, Elvanna wields influence through control of access, information, and social capital — never resorting to physical force, yet extracting maximum value through psychological and economic pressure.

She first encountered the party when they sought to recover [[serena-velkori]], whom she held captive as leverage for damages caused by the [[the-rhennee|Rhennee clan]] at the Boatman’s Tavern. The party paid her full asking price of 500 gold pieces without negotiation, a move she later remarked upon with sardonic regret — noting she might have extracted more had she recognized their desperation. Her demeanor is polished and detached, marked by a posh accent and calculated indifference, reinforcing her identity as a purely profit-driven operator.

Elvanna maintains no ideological alignment; her neutrality is strategic. She views the party not as enemies, but as naive yet potentially lucrative future clients. Her establishment serves as a hub for discreet information exchange, and while no direct ties to the [[crimson-reapers]] or [[hartsch]] are confirmed, her position makes her a plausible conduit for intelligence on Nulb’s hidden networks.

She remains active at the Iron Lash, with no known current conflicts or active plots involving the party — but her assessment of them as “easy marks” suggests future interactions may be fraught with hidden costs.
