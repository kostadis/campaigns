# Kelno
Kelno is the leader of the [[Air Temple]], which is considered nearly non-existent by [[Hartsch]]. He is a faction leader who has been underestimated by [[The Party]]. Kelno is actively moving looted assets through a hidden door and is viewed with sharpened respect and wariness by [[Sequoia]].
