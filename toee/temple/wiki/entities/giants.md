# Giants

An ancient, looming existential threat to Sheernobb, as identified by Headgnome Glennddarc. Though never directly encountered by the party, giants are described as the primary danger to the settlement’s survival — a threat that supersedes bandits, criminal organizations, and political theater. Glennddarc’s entire governance strategy is oriented toward securing aid to confront them, yet he provides no maps, evidence, or historical context, raising questions about his knowledge, motives, or fatalism.

Giants are implied to be more than mere marauders — their presence may be tied to deeper, possibly magical or cyclical phenomena in the region. Their absence from direct narrative engagement makes them a [[documentary-knowledge-only-target]], amplifying their symbolic weight as an unspoken dread. Whether they are migrating, awakening, or being manipulated remains unknown.

Their existence challenges the party’s understanding of threat hierarchy in Nulb’s periphery and may connect to the [[deep-temple]] or other ancient ruins in the region.
