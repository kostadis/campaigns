# Robert Deakin
Robert Deakin is a gnome and the owner of The Cheap Forester tavern in Ostverk. He is a transactional and cautious individual who shares information in exchange for value. Deakin has a gnomish political bias and is well-connected, knowing who pays for information. Additionally, Robert Deakin has mentioned [[lord-denelu]]'s interest in the [[lost-chest]], indicating that Denelu is willing to pay for its location.
