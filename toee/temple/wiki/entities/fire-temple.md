# Fire Temple

A clandestine religious-military order devoted to Alrrem the All-Consuming, the Fire Temple operates as a structured, hierarchical faction that blends theological zeal with strategic warfare. Once perceived as a backwater cult, the Fire Temple has evolved into a coordinated entity capable of deploying engineered weapons, infiltrating logistical networks, and executing field operations through indoctrinated agents.

The Fire Temple is the enemy faction of the Water Temple, with Lareth as a figure within it. Its signature weapon is the [[chimera]], a necromantic construct bound through ritual means to its handlers. The discovery of the [[control-chain-of-alrrem]] confirms institutionalized training and standardized command protocols. The wax-sealed ledger recovered from the Fire Temple Warlock proves direct integration with the [[crimson-trader]] and its maritime supply chain, confirming the temple’s use of the [[deniable-logistics-network]] to move high-value assets without direct exposure.

The temple’s leadership appears to treat its agents as [[expendable-asset-tactics]] — zealots like the warlock are deployed for maximum symbolic impact and minimal long-term investment. Their collapse under pressure suggests doctrine overrides adaptability, making them predictable but dangerous.

The Fire Temple’s ultimate goals remain obscured, but its actions indicate a campaign of escalation: from transporting the chimera (via Captain Varsik) to deploying direct theological operatives. It is no longer a hidden cult — it is a shadow army with divine mandate.
