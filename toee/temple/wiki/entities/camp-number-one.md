# Camp Number One
Camp Number One is a location and organization near the [[Hall of the Scarlet Moon]], where several groups of druids have been encountered. 
## Description
Camp Number One serves as a base for various druidic groups, highlighting its significance in the region.
## Encounters
The party has encountered multiple groups of druids at this location, including:
- A group consisting of [[Faria]] and three other druids.
- A group consisting of [[Iniri]] and three other friendly human druids.
- A group that includes Mahoon and Verigo, among other druids.
These encounters demonstrate the diverse presence of druids at Camp Number One.
