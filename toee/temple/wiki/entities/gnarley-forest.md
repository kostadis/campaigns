# Gnarley Forest

A dense, ancient woodland located beyond the settled regions of Nulb and Sheernobb. Known for its twisted trees, unnatural silence, and frequent reports of missing travelers. The forest is a known haunt of fey predators, with the encounter with Aunt Emma confirming its status as a zone of active, predatory magic.

The Gnarley Forest is not merely a natural environment but a living trap, where illusions, hidden paths, and predatory entities like hags operate with impunity. The presence of the Gingerbread House within its borders suggests the forest may contain multiple such lure sites, possibly maintained by a network of fey predators.

**Notable features**:  
- Site of the Gingerbread House  
- Home to Aunt Emma, the Green Hag  
- High risk of fey deception and magical entrapment  

**Caution**: Travelers are advised to avoid the forest unless accompanied by someone with [[concepts/recognition-of-fey-disguise]] proficiency.
