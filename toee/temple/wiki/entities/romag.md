# Romag
Romag is the High Priest and leader of the [[Earth Temple]] within the Temple of Elemental Evil. He is seen as a passive leader by [[Hartsch]], who believes in a more aggressive approach. Additionally, Romag is ambitious but inexperienced, relying on micromanagement and grandiose displays rather than tactical cleverness. He is also a character who was originally intended to receive a [[missing-chest]] connected to [[Ranus Duval]].
