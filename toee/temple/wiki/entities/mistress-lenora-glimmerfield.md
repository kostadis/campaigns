# Mistress Lenora Glimmerfield

Mistress Lenora Glimmerfield is a textile merchant and the wife of [[aldous-glimmerfield]]. 

## History
She was freed by [[the-party]] during a staged escape from the [[earth-temple]] (also known as the [[temple-of-elemental-evil]]). 

## Rewards
Following her rescue, she and her husband promised a reward of 100 silver pieces to [[the-party]].
