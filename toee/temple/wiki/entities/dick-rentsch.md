# Dick Rentsch

Dick Rentsch is a character serving as a recruiter and field contact for the [[earth-temple]]. He is the proprietor of the [[waterside-hostel]] in Nulb. 

Rentsch possesses a complex, transactional, and gruff personality, though he becomes notably animated when discussing the goals of the Earth Temple. He acted as the contact for the Earth faction to which [[skole]] redirected the party.

## Role and Employment
As an employer, Rentsch employs [[wat]] at the [[waterside-hostel]]. While he is aware of Wat's divided loyalties, he continues to tolerate him for pragmatic reasons.

## Actions and Controversies
Rentsch has demonstrated an authoritarian and potentially antagonistic side to his character. He notably imprisoned [[jorin-pike-kale]] as punishment for making a pass at a woman named Dalia. This incident highlights the moral ambiguities surrounding his actions and his role within the operations of the Earth Temple.
