# Narwell

A small settlement located along the road to Hommlet, identified as the origin point of merchant survivor Derrick and his companion Florian. The two were captured by brigands while traveling from Narwell to Hommlet, suggesting the route is vulnerable to predation. Narwell’s lack of recorded political or economic significance in other sources implies it is a quiet, unremarkable town — yet its connection to a survivor with a mysterious purple robe raises questions about hidden ties to larger networks or forgotten histories. Its existence underscores the fragility of travel and the reach of violence beyond major centers.
