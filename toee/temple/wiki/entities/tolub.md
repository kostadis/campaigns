# Tolub
Tolub is a member of the Temple of Elemental Evil, specifically working for the Fire Temple. He is responsible for orchestrating the trap that led to the encounter between [[Albreth]], [[Ingulf]], and [[the-party]]. Additionally, Tolub has a coercive relationship with [[Skole]], positioning himself as an antagonist to Skole and potentially the party.
