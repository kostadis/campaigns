# Elmo
Elmo is a character who serves as a [[ranger]] and covert agent for [[Verbobonc]] in [[Hommlet]]. He maintains a convincing drunken buffoon persona as cover for his intelligence work. His core goals include monitoring threats to [[Hommlet]] and the region on behalf of [[Verbobonc]] and containing whatever evil presence remains at the [[Moathouse]].

## Roles and Incidents
Elmo is also known for his role as an enforcer, demonstrated by an incident where he threatened to summon [[Captain Zeb Fletcher]], leading to [[Furnok]]'s expulsion from the [[Inn of the Welcome Wench]]. Additionally, Elmo intervened in a confrontation between [[Ostler Gundigoot]] and [[Furnok]] in [[Hommlet]], further showcasing his involvement in local affairs and his willingness to take action to maintain order.
