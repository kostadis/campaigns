# Verigo
Verigo is a friendly human druid. He is located at [[Camp Number One]], where he shares circumstances with [[Mahoon]]. Verigo has also encountered [[the-party]] and is preparing for the Rite of the Wicker Giant. However, his true allegiances and the nature of the rite are unknown.
