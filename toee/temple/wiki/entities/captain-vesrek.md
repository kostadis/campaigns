# Captain Vesrek

Captain Vesrek is the captain of the *[[crimson-traitor]]* and an ally of [[canon-belsornig]]. 

## Activities and Schemes
Vesrek was the architect of the chimera scheme, a plot designed to weaken the [[fire-temple]] and the [[earth-temple]]. As part of these activities, Vesrek was involved in the sale of high-value, dangerous cargo—including the Chimera—to the [[fire-temple]].
