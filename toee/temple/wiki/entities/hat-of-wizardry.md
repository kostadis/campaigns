# Hat of Wizardry

A black felt hat of unknown origin, recovered from the body of Aunt Emma, the Green Hag, after her death in the Gingerbread House. The hat functions as a standard Hat of Wizardry, granting the wearer access to three 1st-level wizard spells per day (chosen from the school of illusion or enchantment), and advantage on saving throws against charm and fear effects.

However, Zinnia noted a faint green sheen on the inner lining and a lingering scent of rotting plums. The hat may carry residual hag magic or a curse tied to its previous owner. Attempts to identify its origin or potential corruption have yielded inconclusive results.

**Acquisition**: Looted from the corpse of Aunt Emma.  
**Current status**: Held by the party; not yet worn by any member.  
**Risks**:  
- May amplify illusion-based deception in the wearer  
- May attract the attention of other hags or fey entities  
- May be linked to the cookbook’s magic  

**Note**: This item exemplifies [[concepts/magical-item-recovery-from-deceased-fey]]—the acquisition of potent magic from slain supernatural predators, often at great moral and magical cost.
