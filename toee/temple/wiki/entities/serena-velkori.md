# Serena Velkori

Serena Velkori is a member of the [[rhennee-clan]] and the sister of Eryk Velkori. 

## Background and Captivity
Serena was held captive by [[elvanna]] as leverage for a debt owed by her family. This debt was incurred as compensation for damages caused by the Rhennee at the Boatman's Tavern. 

During her captivity, she was held at the [[iron-lash-brothel]]. Further insights into her situation were revealed through [[vethra-velkori]]'s readings, which indicated that she was being held on a crimson barge on the [[velverdyva]].

## Rescue
The party successfully rescued Serena by paying 500 gp for her release.
