# Vesrek

Vesrek is an operational intermediary employed by Belsornig, Canon and Prophet of the Water Temple, to facilitate the acquisition and transport of the chimera. He acted as the direct point of contact between Belsornig and Captain Varsik of the Crimson Trader, ensuring the beast was delivered to the intended target zone near Hommlet.

Vesrek’s role exemplifies [[deniable-logistics-network]]: he is neither a high-ranking temple official nor a known criminal, but a specialized facilitator whose function is to obscure the chain of command. His identity is known only through ledger entries and the testimony of the deceased Fire Temple warlock.

He operates with plausible deniability, likely unaware of the full strategic intent behind the chimera’s deployment — only that he was paid to move it. His connection to Belsornig confirms the Water Temple’s use of compartmentalized agents to execute high-risk operations without exposing its leadership.

No direct encounter with Vesrek has occurred. He remains a shadow in the supply chain — a ghost in the machine.
