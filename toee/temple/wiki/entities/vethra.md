# Vethra

Matriarch of the Rhennee clan and mother of Eryk Velkori. Vethra is an elderly figure whose spiritual authority is both revered and feared within the clan.

Eryk has actively opposed her attempts at divination, fearing the ritual effort may harm or kill her — a stance that reveals a deep cultural taboo around the use of magic among the elderly. This fear suggests the Rhennee possess an unspoken understanding of spiritual vulnerability, possibly tied to the dangerous rituals performed at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon.

Her position as matriarch implies she holds ceremonial and ancestral knowledge, yet her physical frailty limits her public role. Whether her divination attempts are acts of guidance, desperation, or a cover for deeper communion with the wicker giant remains unknown.

Her existence underscores the Rhennee’s internal tension: a society that navigates a world saturated with magic while simultaneously fearing its cost to its most vulnerable members.
