# Ingulf
Ingulf is a fire priest and companion of [[Albreth]], operating under the direction of [[Tolub]]. He is also a Fire temple operative who has encountered [[the-party]]. Ingulf is devoted to elemental fire allegiance and has participated in threatening declarations and magical fire assaults against [[the-party]].
