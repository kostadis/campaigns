# Urot

Urot is an orc cook stationed at the Abbey of Allitur, who unknowingly became a critical source of intelligence on Belsornig and the Water Temple’s operations. Though employed in the kitchen, he was not an active participant in the bandit network headquartered in the Abbey’s lower levels. Instead, he served as a low-level observer, aware of supply deliveries and the identity of Snej’s patron.

Under interrogation, Urot revealed that Belsornig — referred to as a “big shot” in the Temple of Evil — was the source of resources sustaining Snej’s bandits. His testimony was the first concrete link between the Abbey of Allitur and the Water Temple’s leadership. He was coerced into revealing this information, likely due to his proximity to the operation rather than any direct involvement.

Urot’s role exemplifies the [[documentary-knowledge-only-target]] dynamic: he possesses vital information but holds no power. His survival after the interrogation suggests he was deemed non-threatening — a useful witness, not a threat. He remains at the Abbey of Allitur, unaware of the full scope of the conspiracy he exposed. His knowledge is fragmentary but pivotal — a single voice that cracked open a hidden hierarchy.
