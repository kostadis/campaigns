# Skole
Skole is a complex figure with multiple roles. He is an informant who confirmed [[Captain Tolub]]'s treachery to the party, providing crucial information that revealed Tolub's betrayal. Additionally, Skole is the owner and bartender of the Boatman's Tavern in [[Nulb]]. 

## Relationships and Affiliations
Skole's relationship with the party and [[Captain Tolub]] is multifaceted. As the owner of the Boatman's Tavern, he is nominally under [[Lodriss]], but is coerced by [[Tolub]] and the [[Fire Temple]]. This coercion suggests that Skole's actions and decisions may be influenced by these external forces.

## Goals and Motivations
Skole's core goal is to be free of both [[Tolub]] and [[Lodriss]], who hold power over him and his establishment. This desire for autonomy indicates that Skole may be willing to take risks or form alliances to achieve his objective, potentially impacting the dynamics of the party and the larger conflict.
