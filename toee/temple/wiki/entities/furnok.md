# Furnok
Furnok is a cheating gambler and suspected thief. He operates through charm and misdirection, using loaded dice to cheat at games and potentially stealing from marks on the side. Furnok was encountered at the [[Inn of the Welcome Wench]] and is also known to be active in [[Hommlet]], where he was confronted by [[Ostler Gundigoot]].
