# Thalsor

Thalsor is a hostile figure who killed Calmer during the Black Earth Compound battle. He explicitly threatened to mutilate Calmer’s corpse to prevent resurrection — a direct invocation of [[corpse-integrity-as-resurrection-condition]]. His actions reveal a deep understanding of magical constraints and a willingness to exploit them for psychological and tactical advantage. Thalsor’s identity, affiliation, and motives remain unknown, but his presence signals a level of necromantic or arcane sophistication among the party’s enemies. His threat was not idle — it was a calculated act of spiritual warfare, designed to erase not just a life, but the possibility of its return.
