# Honey Haven Inn
The Honey Haven Inn, also known as the Honey Tavern Inn, is an establishment in Sheernobb, co-owned by [[glennon_clee]] and [[mari_clee]]. It provides lodging and food services to travelers.

## Events
The Honey Haven Inn is also a location where significant events have occurred, including where [[gurdy]] approached [[the-party]] and offered to share local intelligence.
