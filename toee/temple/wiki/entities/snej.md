# Snej

Snej was a deceased operative affiliated with the [[Water Temple]] and a bandit leader operating from the [[Abbey of Allitur]]. He was not an independent actor but a subordinate asset within [[Canon Belsornig]]’s broader strategy, functioning as a deniable proxy in the [[Temple of Evil]]’s institutional warfare.

Funded and supplied by Belsornig, Snej led a network of brigands active in the Gnarley Forest region, targeting caravans and travelers — including those from Narwell. His operations served dual purposes: disrupting regional stability and drawing attention away from the Water Temple’s larger schemes, such as the chimera deployment. His bandits exemplify [[expendable-asset-tactics]] — expendable, deniable, and strategically useful — forming part of a broader [[deniable-logistics-network]] orchestrated by Belsornig.

His connection to Belsornig was revealed by [[Urot]], an orc cook at the Abbey of Allitur, who identified Belsornig as Snej’s “big shot” supplier. This link confirms the Water Temple’s use of localized violence as a tool of institutional warfare.

Snej has never been encountered by the party, but his network was partially dismantled during the investigation into the Moathouse and Narwell caravan attacks. His current status is unknown, though his base at the Abbey of Allitur remains active.
