# Terjon
Terjon is the High Priest and Prefect of the Temple of St. Cuthbert in Hommlet. He is driven by a desire to control information and has a transactional approach to his role. Additionally, Terjon is Calmer's superior in the [[Church of St. Cuthbert]] and played a significant role in Calmer's actions and decisions. He is also a claimed affiliation of [[Hestort Flintshine]], although the [[The Party|party]] doubts its authenticity.

## Role and Relationships
Terjon is a cleric and was subordinate to Y'dey. Following Y'dey's disappearance, Terjon has been acting as the head cleric. Notably, Terjon has been evasive about the circumstances of Y'dey's disappearance, which has led to suspicion among the villagers.
