# Rhennee Clan

A nomadic, matriarchal society operating on kinship-based loyalty and ritualized protection. The Rhennee Clan is not a criminal organization but is deeply entangled in Nulb’s underworld through trade, information networks, and shared vulnerabilities.

Led by the matriarch Vethra, the clan operates through extended family units, with leadership passed matrilineally. Their social cohesion is anchored in the protection of kin — exemplified by Eryk Velkori’s desperate efforts to rescue his sister Serena. Clan membership confers not only safety but access to hidden routes, safe houses, and intelligence networks across Nulb and beyond.

The Rhennee Clan is the family organization to which Serena Velkori and Eryk Velkori belong. They are entangled in a debt arrangement with Elvanna.

The clan opposes certain forms of divination, fearing spiritual exhaustion can be lethal to the elderly — a taboo that suggests a deeper cultural fear of magical cost, possibly linked to the rituals at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon. They maintain a neutral stance toward most criminal factions but are wary of Madame Selentis and the Crimson Reapers.

The formal induction of outsiders into the clan — as occurred with the party after Serena’s rescue — is a rare and binding social contract. It transforms the party from outsiders to protected kin, granting them access to Rhennee networks and loyalty, but also imposing obligations of mutual defense and discretion.

The clan’s connection to the Boatman’s Tavern and Iron Lash brothel suggests they operate at the intersection of commerce and coercion, though their role remains ambiguous: are they victims, intermediaries, or silent partners in the region’s exploitation?
