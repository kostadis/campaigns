# The Gallows Table

The Gallows Table is a public execution site in Nulb, used by Madame Selentis to display the bodies of those who have crossed her. It is not merely a place of death, but a stage for political theater — a symbol of her absolute, unchallenged authority. Unlike the chaotic violence of Zelt’s enforcers, executions here are deliberate, silent, and devoid of trial or explanation.

The site functions similarly to the Deep Temple in its role as a locus of dread and ritual, though its purpose is secular: to reinforce obedience through fear. The body of Elfar Twell was hung here as a warning, and the Table remains a silent monument to the cost of defiance in Nulb. No records of other victims are known, but its continued use implies a pattern of terror.
