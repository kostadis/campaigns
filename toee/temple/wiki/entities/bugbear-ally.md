# Bugbear ally

A minor companion to the Fire Temple Warlock, this bugbear accompanied him during the assault on the party and attempted to intervene in combat. It was caught in the chimera’s breath attack alongside the warlock and perished instantly.

Its presence confirms the Fire Temple employs non-humanoid agents as expendable assets — reinforcing the faction’s use of [[expendable-asset-tactics]]. The bugbear was not a trained warrior but a disposable tool, likely coerced, magically bound, or bred for obedience.

No name, background, or prior affiliation is known. Its sole narrative function is to underscore the warlock’s isolation and the Fire Temple’s disregard for non-human life. Its death mirrors the warlock’s: both consumed by the very weapon they sought to control.
