# Pasmarie Clee
Pasmarie Clee is a person who is aware of bandit activities and was referred to the party by [[Grindin Well-Read]] as a potential source of information. She is also an associate of [[Captain Gnonsbim]], who directed her to interact with the party. It is believed that [[Captain Gnonsbim]] is romantically interested in her.
