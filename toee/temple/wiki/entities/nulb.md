# Nulb
Nulb is a town where [[constable_eldrin_malavar]] operates, featuring a condemned temple to the south and the Pilgrim's Trail, which has been declared off-limits by the constable. The town is also home to [[garik]]'s armory stall, where low-quality weapons are sold, indicating the town's poor economic conditions.
