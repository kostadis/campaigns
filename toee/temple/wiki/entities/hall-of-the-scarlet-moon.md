# Hall of the Scarlet Moon

A remote, ritualistic site where druidic ceremonies are conducted under the open sky, marked by an unnatural, persistent fire that burns continuously throughout the day and night — even during festive gatherings. Located near [[Camp Number One]], the Hall serves as a crucial gathering place for druids and key figures within the setting, and is largely unknown to Nulb’s authorities, making it a hidden nexus of occult practice.

## Description

The Hall of the Scarlet Moon is distinguished by its unquenchable fire, whose continuity defies natural explanation and may be tied to necromantic, elemental, or arcane forces. This persistent flame serves multiple potential functions: as a ward, a beacon, or a conduit — possibly linked to the giants identified by Headgnome Glennddarc. The site’s mystique draws multiple groups, yet its true purpose remains obscured.

Its proximity to [[Camp Number One]] makes it a strategically significant location, and its association with druidic traditions underscores its role as a center for forbidden or esoteric rites. The Hall’s connection to the [[Rhennee clan]] is indirect but significant: Eryk Velkori’s warnings about spiritual exhaustion and his clan’s taboo against divination may stem from traumatic encounters with the Hall’s rituals.

## Encounters and Events

The Hall has been the site of several pivotal narrative events:

* **Meeting with Faria:** The party encountered [[Faria]] at the Hall, where she was accompanied by three other druids.
* **Preparations for the Rite:** The Hall is currently a site of intense activity for [[Verigo]] and his fellow druids, who are preparing for the [[Rite of the Wicker Giant]].
* **Teaching of the Rite:** According to Eryk Velkori, the druid [[Elazar]] teaches the Rite of the Wicker Giant here — a secretive ceremony rumored to involve transformation, sacrifice, or elemental binding.

## Research Notes

The specific roles and deeper connections between the Hall, [[Mahoon]], [[Verigo]], and [[Elazar]] require further investigation to fully understand their influence on the location and the nature of the persistent fire. The Hall represents a critical unknown in the region’s magical landscape and may be an epicenter of future existential threats, particularly given its potential ties to necromancy and the giant-related phenomena observed by Headgnome Glennddarc.
