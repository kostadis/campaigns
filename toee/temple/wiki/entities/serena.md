# Serena

Sister of Eryk Velkori and a member of the Rhennee clan. Serena was held captive for ransom at the Iron Lash brothel after being lured to the Boatman’s Tavern.

Her abduction triggered Eryk’s emotional collapse and his desperate plea to the party for help. Her rescue was the catalyst for the party’s formal induction into the Rhennee clan — a transformation that granted them protection, loyalty, and a 500gp credit reward.

Serena’s presence at the Boatman’s Tavern remains unexplained: was she lured, seeking help, or acting on behalf of the clan? Her silence since her rescue suggests trauma, or perhaps a deeper knowledge she has not shared.

Her status as a Rhennee kin now binds her to the party’s fate — and vice versa. Her rescue did not merely end a captivity; it forged a new social contract between the party and one of Nulb’s most enigmatic nomadic groups.
