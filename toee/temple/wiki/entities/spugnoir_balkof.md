# Spugnoir Balkof
Spugnoir Balkof is a traveling magic user who has formed an agreement with the party to guide them in exchange for a share of treasure and first claim on arcane scrolls. He is driven by his desire to meet the Circle of Eight and accumulate arcane knowledge.
