# Pasmarie Clee

Pasmarie Clee is a gnome [[druid]] and beekeeper residing in [[Sheernobb]], where she operates a beehive farm and is actively involved in the local honey trade. Her close connection to the surrounding lands, cultivated through years of tending her hives, grants her intimate knowledge of regional activity—including bandit movements near her apiaries—a detail referenced by [[Headgnome Glennddarc]].

Beyond her role as a beekeeper, Pasmarie has emerged as a figure of personal influence in Sheernobb. She is the object of Captain Gnonsbim’s romantic interest, a dynamic that elevates her from a peripheral local to a potential node of hidden influence. Gnonsbim directed the party to seek her out, suggesting she may serve as a conduit for information, approval, or social validation. Her exact role in municipal affairs remains unclear, but her connection to Gnonsbim introduces the concept of [[romantic-interest-as-political-motivator]]—where personal desire becomes a silent engine of public action.

Simultaneously, Headgnome Glennddarc has strategically directed the party to consult Pasmarie regarding bandit activity, employing her as a node of decentralized intelligence in an act of [[concepts/information-redirection-as-control]]. This maneuver gives the appearance of cooperation while preserving Glennddarc’s own agenda. Whether she is aware of being used as a decoy remains unknown. Her beehives may serve as more than a source of honey—possibly as observation posts, communication hubs, or even magical sensors, subtly extending her influence beyond mere apiculture.

Whether she reciprocates Gnonsbim’s feelings, is complicit in his political theater, or remains an unwitting pawn in either scheme is deliberately obscured. She may represent the emotional stakes behind Gnonsbim’s otherwise transactional behavior, or she may be an unwitting instrument in Glennddarc’s quiet control. Regardless, her presence weaves together the natural rhythms of her beekeeping with the subtle currents of local power, embodying the informal networks that sustain Sheernobb’s fragile order—operating beneath the visibility of both Gnonsbim’s romantic posturing and Glennddarc’s calculated silence.
