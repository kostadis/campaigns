# Aldous Glimmerfield
Aldous Glimmerfield is a textile merchant from [[Divers]]. He is the husband of [[Mistress Lenora Glimmerfield]]. 

## Imprisonment and Rescue
Aldous was imprisoned in the [[Earth Temple]] and was later freed by [[the-party]] during a staged escape.

## Debt and Reward
After his rescue, Aldous, along with his wife [[Lenora Glimmerfield]], promised a reward of 100 silver pieces to [[the-party]] for their efforts.
