# Lareth the Beautiful

Lareth the Beautiful is a character in the elemental cults story and a moathouse operator. He serves as the commander of the Fire Temple faction operating out of the Moathouse dungeon.

## Personality and Traits
Lareth is characterized as calculating and composed. He frequently employs manipulation and deception to achieve his objectives.

## Role and Relationships
As a leader within the Fire Temple, Lareth was involved in a cover story concerning the elimination of [[snej]]. 

He is an antagonist to [[Erin Darkmoon]], who provided intelligence regarding his activities to [[the-party]] in exchange for a promise that he would be killed.
