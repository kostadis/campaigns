# Wat
Wat is a person who works as a bartender at the [[Waterside Hostel]] in Nulb. He is suspected to be an agent of the [[Water Temple]]. Wat previously mentioned [[Dala]] in connection with a warning to [[the-party]], although the nature of their relationship with [[Dala]] is unknown.
