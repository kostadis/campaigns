# Brother Eldin

Brother Eldin is a monk of the [[beory-monastery]] and a close associate of [[sister-alina]]. He is a principled and cautious individual who previously belonged to the [[earth-cult]] faction but has since defected. 

Following his defection, Eldin has dedicated himself to protecting the abbey and working to stop the activities of the [[earth-cult]]. He co-administered the "Path of Righteousness" trials to [[the-party]].
