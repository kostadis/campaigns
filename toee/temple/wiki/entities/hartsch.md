# Hartsch

Hartsch is a sub-general and speaker of the [[Earth Temple]], serving under [[Romag]]. He is a senior figure within the Earth Temple of the Temple of Elemental Evil and a vocal critic of the Water Temple’s covert operations. Hartsch privately disparages [[Romag]]'s leadership style as slow and passive, and has formed a secret alliance with [[the-party]] to replace Romag as leader of the Earth Temple, positioning himself as a co-conspirator and potential successor.

Beyond his internal power struggle, Hartsch has emerged as a critical external analyst who identified [[canon-belsornig]] as the true architect of the chimera scheme. His assessment — that Belsornig is the greatest threat within the Temple of Evil — is grounded in deep institutional knowledge and intercepted communications. He views the Water Temple’s indirect warfare as more dangerous than the Fire Temple’s overt aggression, because it is harder to detect, harder to counter, and harder to attribute.

Hartsch’s credibility is high: he has no direct stake in the party’s survival, and his analysis aligns with multiple independent sources (including Urot, brigand confessions, and ledger entries). However, his motives remain ambiguous — is he seeking to eliminate Belsornig for the greater good, or to position the Earth Temple as the dominant faction within the Temple of Elemental Evil?

His complex relationship with [[Zephyr]] is experienced by Zephyr as a constraint, suggesting entanglements beyond his role in the Earth Temple. Hartsch thus transforms from a mere obstacle into a multifaceted figure: a rival, a potential ally, and the party’s most accurate — and possibly most dangerous — informant. His actions are shaped by inter-temple rivalry, personal ambition, and a strategic vision that may align with, or ultimately undermine, the party’s goals.
