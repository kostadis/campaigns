# Dren Halveth

Dren Halveth is a pragmatic and transactional leader who serves as the captain of the [[Broken Blades]] mercenary company and the leader of the [[Crimson Guard]] faction within the [[Earth Temple]]. He is characterized by a decision-making process that weighs options based on material benefit and survival odds. His primary motivations include plunder, combat, and the strength of his current alignments.

## Role in the Earth Temple
As the commander of the Broken Blades, Dren Halveth plays a crucial role in the [[Earth Temple]]. His leadership and strategic influence within the temple are heavily driven by his focus on material gain and the preservation of his forces.
