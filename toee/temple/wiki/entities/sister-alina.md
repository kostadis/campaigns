# Sister Alina

Sister Alina is a monk of the [[Beory monastery]] and a religious figure dedicated to the nature goddess [[Beory]]. She is a member of the [[Earth Cult]] faction.

## Role and Activities
Sister Alina co-led [[the-party]] through the "Path of Righteousness," during which she administered their trials. She is currently on opposing sides with [[Brother Eldin]], and there are suspicions that she may be a cult leader.

## Associations
Sister Alina is associated with the following figures and locations:
* **Figures:** [[Brother Eldin]], [[Brother Eldren]], and [[Ziinnia]].
* **Locations:** [[Gallows Corner]] and [[The Monastery]].
