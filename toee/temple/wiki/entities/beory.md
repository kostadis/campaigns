# Beory

Beory is a deity revered as the Nature Goddess. She is the central focus of [[jaroo-ashstaff]]'s faith and motivations, and she is worshipped by the [[druidic-order-of-beory]]. 

[[the-monastery]] is dedicated to her worship.
