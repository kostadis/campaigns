# Broken Blades
The Broken Blades are a professional mercenary company led by [[Dren Halveth]]. They have been recruited by the [[Earth Temple]] and are operating under the [[The Party|party's]] nominal command. The company is involved in the temple war and has a cooperative relationship with the [[The Party|party]].

## Organization and Leadership
The Broken Blades are commanded by [[Dren Halveth]] within the [[Earth Temple]]. 

## Relationships and Alliances
The Broken Blades have a cooperative relationship with the [[The Party|party]] and are also connected to other groups such as the [[Crimson Guard]] and the [[Crimson Reapers]].
