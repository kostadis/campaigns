# Jaroo
Jaroo is a druid in Hommlet, politically aligned with [[Ostler Gundigoot]]. They have also been known to observe the activities of other groups, including the [[Circle of the Scarlet Moon]], whose rites they found to be unsettling and cult-like. As a result, Jaroo is also considered an observer and witness to various events in the region.
