# Derrick

A merchant from Narwell who was captured by brigands and held captive in the Moathouse’s pantry as food for the ogre Lubash. He was captured on the road to Hommlet from Narwell alongside fellow merchant Florian. Rescued by the [[the-party]], Derrick is visibly traumatized, having broken down while naming the companions killed by Lubash. He wears a distinctive purple robe over filthy traveling clothes — its origin and significance remain unexplained, serving as a symbolic narrative clue to his possible hidden status or past.

Derrick is driven by survival and the desire to return to safety in [[Hommlet]]. He and Florian promised the Party a reward for safe return, though it remains unfulfilled. Currently under the care of the Duke’s men at the Moathouse exterior campsite. His lack of factional affiliation contrasts with the robe’s implied status, raising questions about his true background.
