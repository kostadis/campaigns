# Alrrem the All-Consuming

A deity worshipped by the Fire Temple, Alrrem the All-Consuming is venerated as a god of purification through fire, consumption, and the dissolution of weakness. Worshipers believe that only through total annihilation — of flesh, spirit, and doubt — can true divine order emerge.

The warlock’s use of the chimera as a living weapon and the ritual control chain suggest Alrrem’s theology is not merely symbolic but operational: the deity demands tangible, violent manifestations of devotion. The eight-pointed star amulet recovered from the warlock is a known symbol of Alrrem’s clergy, likely denoting rank or ritual authority.

Alrrem’s interest in the party may extend beyond mere destruction — the chimera’s deployment, the unused potion of fire resistance, and the precision of the ledger’s placement suggest a deeper, possibly prophetic or strategic design. Whether Alrrem seeks to test, eliminate, or transform the party remains unknown.

Known symbols: Eight-pointed star, flame-wreathed skull.
Known agents: Fire Temple Warlock, unnamed high priests, chimera handlers.
