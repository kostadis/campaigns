# Rannos Davl

**Rannos Davl** (also referred to as **Ranos Davl** or **Ranus Duval**), known by the moniker "**the Duke**," is a multifaceted figure acting as a patron, employer, and covert operative. While appearing to be a standard merchant operating in [[Hommlet]], he is deeply involved in various clandestine organizations.

## Affiliations and Roles

### [[Temple of Elemental Evil]]
Rannos serves as a covert agent working on behalf of the [[Temple of Elemental Evil]]. In [[Hommlet]], he worked alongside his merchant partner, [[Gremag]], to further the Temple's goals. Notably, Rannos and Gremag collaborated to acquire the [[Chest of Rao]] for the Temple.

### Competing Organization
Beyond his ties to the Temple, Rannos is involved with a competing organization, though his true motivations and ultimate goals remain unknown. In this capacity, he is connected to a [[missing-chest]] that was originally intended for [[romag]].

## Interactions

Rannos Davl has interacted with [[the-party]] and various associates through several channels:

* **Thorne**: He utilizes Thorne as an employee to facilitate his interests.
* **Zephyr**: He maintains a connection to [[zephyr]] stemming from a previous promise.
* **Ostler Gundigoot**: The sudden disappearance of Rannos and [[Gremag]] from [[Hommlet]] serves as a primary plot hook, prompting [[Ostler Gundigoot]] to investigate their whereabouts.
