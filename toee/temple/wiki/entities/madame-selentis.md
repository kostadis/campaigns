# Madame Selentis

Madame Selentis, also known as **Nisera** or **Selentis**, is the feared and de facto ruler of [[nulb]], a tyrant whose authority is not derived from economic power, criminal networks, or legal institutions — but from ritualized, public acts of terror. She embodies **terror-governance**: a system where law is enforced not by codes or negotiation, but by the certainty of sudden, symbolic annihilation.

## Background
She hails from a disgraced noble family originally from [[verbobonc]].

## Methods and Reputation
Madame Selentis enforces her rule through decisive, lethal spectacle. Her most infamous act was ordering the public execution of [[elfar-twell]] at the [[the-gallows-table]], a display designed to instill fear through arbitrary, unexplained violence. This event exemplifies her strategy of **public-execution-as-political-theater**, where death becomes a tool of governance — not punishment, but performance.

Unlike Zelt the Rat, who operates through smuggling, extortion, and underworld coercion, Selentis governs through existential dread. Her power is not rooted in profit or organization, but in symbolism. She is not a crime lord — she is an executioner-queen, whose legitimacy derives from the spectacle of control.

Eryk Velkori treats her as an untouchable, near-mythic force. His warning to the party against drawing her attention underscores her reputation as a power that cannot be negotiated with, only avoided. His fear is not of punishment, but of erasure — a cultural indicator of her absolute dominance. To cross her is not to invite retribution; it is to vanish without trace, a lesson etched into the collective psyche of Nulb.

Her influence extends far beyond direct control. She is the shadow under which all of Nulb’s underworld operates — including the Iron Lash, the Crimson Reapers, and the Hall of the Scarlet Moon. Even the [[rhennee-clan]], who navigate the edges of lawlessness and thrive in chaos, fear her name. Her reach is so pervasive that her enforcers, rumored to be called **Nisera’s Talons**, operate with impunity, unseen but always present.

While her ties to necromancy or the [[Deep Temple]] remain unconfirmed, the ritualistic, almost ceremonial nature of her executions suggests a deeper, possibly state-like structure beneath Nulb’s chaos. She transforms **criminal-organization-leadership** into a theocratic form of tyranny — where fear is doctrine, and public death is liturgy.

She stands not merely as a rival to Zelt the Rat, but as an entirely different kind of authority: one whose power is inescapable because it is not anchored in the material world, but in the mind.
