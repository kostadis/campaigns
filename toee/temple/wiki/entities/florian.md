# Florian

Florian is a merchant who was captured on the road to Hommlet from Narwell alongside fellow merchant [[derrick]]. Both were taken by brigands and held as prisoners in the [[moathouse]], where they were kept as food for the ogre [[lubash]], reflecting their status as [[prisoner-as-commodity]]. 

Rescued by the [[the-party]], Florian’s liberation was a significant event, and he made a joint reward promise with Derrick to the Party upon rescue. Little is known of his personality beyond his companionship with Derrick and their shared trauma. 

His current status mirrors Derrick’s: under the Duke’s care, awaiting escort to Hommlet.
