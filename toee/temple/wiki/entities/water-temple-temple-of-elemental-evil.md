# Water Temple (Temple of Elemental Evil)

The Water Temple is one of the three major factions within the Temple of Elemental Evil, led by Canon and Prophet Belsornig. Unlike the overtly destructive Fire Temple or the brute-force Earth Temple, the Water Temple operates through subtlety, infiltration, and long-term destabilization.

Its primary goal is to weaken rival elemental temples — particularly the Earth and Fire Temples — by manipulating supply chains, funding proxy networks, and orchestrating asymmetric attacks. It does not seek immediate conquest but rather the erosion of institutional power through economic strangulation and psychological warfare.

The Water Temple’s influence extends through:
- Bandit networks (e.g., Snej at the Abbey of Allitur)
- Logistics intermediaries (e.g., Vesrek)
- Compromised or coerced agents in rival temples (e.g., Fire Temple warlock’s ledger)
- Control over resource flows to sustain proxies

Its leadership structure is bureaucratic and hierarchical, with Belsornig at its apex. Unlike other factions, it avoids direct confrontation, preferring to let others bear the cost of its schemes. The party’s investigation into the chimera plot revealed that the Water Temple was not merely involved — it was the architect, using the Fire Temple’s own logistics to set a trap.
