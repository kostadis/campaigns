# The Cheap Forester

The Cheap Forester is a tavern located in [[Ostverk]]. Owned and operated by [[Robert Deakin]], the establishment serves as a hub where information can be exchanged for value.

The tavern has served as a significant meeting point for several key figures and groups:
* [[Rithelm Drake]] encountered [[The Party]] here.
* [[Gonnathrad Kevanarial]] met [[The Party]] at the tavern and hired them to hunt bandits.
