# Burne
Burne is a wizard and co-leader of the Hommlet fortification project, representing the Viscounty of Verbobonc. He is in a romantic relationship with [[Rufus]] and prioritizes maintaining Verbobonc's political standing and managing the narrative around the Temple of Elemental Evil's resurgence.

## Personal Life and Operations
Burne operates jointly with his partner, [[Rufus of Hommlet]], from their tower in Hommlet. This partnership allows them to coordinate their efforts in both personal and professional capacities, reflecting their close relationship and shared interests in the region.
