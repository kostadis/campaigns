# Zelt the Rat

Zelt the Rat is the leader of the Crimson Reapers, a criminal organization operating in and around Nulb that controls smuggling routes and illicit trade throughout the region. His authority is rooted in economic dominance, intimidation, and a vast network of informants and enforcers. His primary drive is to dominate criminal activity in Nulb and control supply lines through the region.

While his methods are violent, they are largely transactional — punishment is a cost of doing business, not a ritual. Zelt is a gangster who operates through profit and material control, distinguishing him from Madame Selentis, who rules through terror and public spectacle. Their rivalry is not merely territorial — it is ideological. Zelt is a gangster; Selentis is a tyrant. Both are feared, but Selentis’s control is more absolute, more symbolic, and less dependent on material gain. The existence of Selentis challenges the assumption that Nulb’s power structures are purely criminal — they may include elements of state-like governance disguised as chaos.

Eryk Velkori identified Zelt as a suspect in the abduction of his sister Serena, corroborating prior intelligence from the [[zelt_the_rat]] source. This accusation adds emotional weight to Zelt’s reputation: he is not merely a smuggler or thief, but a predator who targets vulnerable families. His network extends into the trafficking of individuals for ransom or ritual use, with documented ties to the [[iron-lash-brothel]] and the [[boatmans-tavern]]. These connections suggest his operations may intersect with darker, possibly occult practices tied to the [[hall-of-the-scarlet-moon]].

His rivalry with [[nisera-s-talons]] may indicate a fragmentation of criminal control in Nulb — or a power struggle over access to the Hall of the Scarlet Moon’s hidden resources. Eryk’s fear of Zelt reinforces the perception of him as a figure of terror — one whose reach extends beyond mere crime into the realm of personal and familial destruction.
