# Moathouse

A ruined, infested structure on the outskirts of Hommlet, serving as a den of horror and captivity. Once a minor religious outpost, it is now overrun by monstrous entities, most notably the ogre Lubash, who uses prisoners as food. The lower level pantry holds the remains of multiple victims, including Derrick and Florian, who were held alive for consumption. The Moathouse embodies the [[prisoner-as-commodity]] concept and may be connected to deeper occult forces such as [[necromancy]] or the [[deep-temple]]. Its proximity to Hommlet and the Duke’s jurisdiction makes it a site of both moral failure and latent threat.
