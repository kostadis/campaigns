# Thorne
Thorne is a character who acts as an employee of [[ranos-davl]]. He was sent to [[the-party]] by [[ranos-davl]], fulfilling a promise made to [[zephyr]]. 

## Background and Motivations
Thorne is a mercenary with no allegiance to the [[temple-of-elemental-evil]]. He is motivated by money and has inside knowledge of [[deep-temple]] personnel and methods.
