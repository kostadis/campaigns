# Abbey of Allitur

The Abbey of Allitur is a ruined religious structure repurposed as a bandit stronghold and logistical hub under the control of Snej, a proxy of Belsornig of the Water Temple. Located in a remote region near Hommlet, it serves as both a base of operations and a distribution point for resources supplied by the Water Temple.

The abbey is notable for housing Urot, an orc cook who provided critical intelligence on Belsornig’s involvement after being interrogated. It also functions as a node in the Water Temple’s [[supply-line-control]] network, receiving food, weapons, and other supplies to sustain Snej’s bandits.

Its location allows for ambushes on travelers from Narwell and access to the Gnarley Forest, making it ideal for low-intensity harassment campaigns. The Water Temple uses the Abbey not for conquest, but for sustained pressure — a classic example of [[asymmetric-pressure-campaign]].

The party has not entered the Abbey of Allitur, but its existence and function were confirmed through Urot’s confession and the interrogation of captured brigands.
