# Tharok
Tharok was a High Templar of the [[Earth Cult]] who played a key role in the cult's activities and posed a significant threat to the [[Beory monastery]]. He attempted to complete a Titan-summoning ritual, but was killed by [[the-party]] during the disruption of the ritual, thus eliminating him as an enemy/threat. Tharok also posed a threat to Warda and was fought and defeated with Warda's assistance.
