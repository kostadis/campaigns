# Water Temple
The Water Temple is a subset of the [[Temple of Elemental Evil]], led by [[Belsornig]]. It is involved in various plots and operations to advance its interests. Additionally, the Water Temple operates as an organization with its own operatives, including [[Snej]], who was affiliated with it.

## Structure and Affiliations
The Water Temple's position within the [[Temple of Elemental Evil]] and its connections to other factions, such as the [[Fire Temple]], highlight its role in a broader network of elemental-themed organizations. 

## Known Operatives and Leaders
- [[Belsornig]]: Leader of the Water Temple
- [[Snej]]: Operative affiliated with the Water Temple

## Relations and Interactions
The Water Temple's interactions with [[The Party]] and other entities like [[Snej]] and [[Belsornig]] demonstrate its active engagement in the world, pursuing its goals through various means.
