# Nisera’s Talons

A suspected criminal organization operating in the shadows of Nulb, named by Eryk Velkori as a possible abductor of his sister Serena. Little is known about their structure, leadership, or methods, but their name suggests a predatory, precision-based operation — possibly focused on targeted kidnappings or elite trafficking.

Eryk identified them alongside Zelt the Rat and the Crimson Reapers as threats capable of infiltrating the Iron Lash brothel and extracting captives. Their emergence as a named actor indicates a fragmentation or expansion of Nulb’s underworld, possibly competing with or collaborating with the Crimson Reapers.

No other sources have confirmed their existence, making them a speculative but credible threat. Their potential ties to the Hall of the Scarlet Moon or the Fire Temple remain unexplored. The party’s investigation into Serena’s abduction may uncover their role in the broader network of human trafficking and ritual exploitation in the region.
