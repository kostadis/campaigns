# Marek
Marek is a teenage boy and the son of [[tomlyn]], a member of the [[church-of-st-cuthbert]]. He is involved in a secret romantic relationship with [[jira]], which is disapproved of by their families due to religious differences. The two were meeting in secret at the brewery.
