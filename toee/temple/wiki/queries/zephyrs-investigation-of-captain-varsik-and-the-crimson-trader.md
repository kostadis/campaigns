# Zephyr's Investigation of Captain Varsik and the Crimson Trader

**Status:** Open

Zephyr has acquired documentary evidence — a ledger recovered from a Fire Temple warlock — identifying Captain Varsik and the *Crimson Trader* as the logistical link in the transport of the chimera. Varsik has never been encountered; his current status and motivations are unknown.

**Key Questions:**
- Can the *Crimson Trader* be tracked via port logs, merchant guild records, or insurance registries?
- Are there other captains or vessels like Varsik operating in the same network?
- Did Varsik know the nature of his cargo? Was he paid, coerced, or deceived?
- Is the *Crimson Trader* owned by the Fire Temple, or is it a rented civilian vessel?
- Does the ship’s route connect to other known Fire Temple outposts or the [[deep-temple]]?

**Potential Avenues:**
- Infiltrate the Nulb Merchant Guild archives
- Bribe or interrogate dockworkers from ports where the ship last docked
- Trace Vesrek’s communications for further contractor names
- Compare the *Crimson Trader*’s hull markings with other vessels in regional registries

This lead may be the key to mapping the Fire Temple’s maritime supply network — and possibly exposing its broader necromantic infrastructure.
