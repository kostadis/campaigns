# Temple of Elemental Evil

The Temple of Elemental Evil is an organization involved in various activities, including operations in [[Hommlet]].

## Members and Affiliations
Several individuals are associated with the organization:
* [[Albreth]] and [[Ingulf]] are loyal to the Temple.
* [[Rannos Gremag]] and [[Rannos Davl]] have operated under its influence.
