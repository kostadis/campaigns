**The Stench of Decay**
The party had retreated to their own territory to wait out the chaos — the sounds of the temple war grinding on in the corridors beyond. Then the smell hit: acrid, unmistakable decay, the kind that meant something dead was moving toward them.

---

**Zephyr Spots the Ghouls**
Zephyr: "But, aren't they supposed to be, like, fighting upstairs?"
The ghouls lurched into view — four of them, their sunken faces eerily recognizable as recently dead Earth Temple members, raised by dark necrotic magic and drawn here to scavenge corpses.

---

**Sequioa's Trap Fails**
Sequioa moved quickly to set a trap at the door to slow their advance. The ghouls rolled badly — a 1 — and triggered it before it was ready, springing it prematurely and shambling forward anyway, the delay minimal and the element of surprise gone.

---

**Zephyr's Kill Strike**
Before the nearest ghoul could react, Zephyr moved — dagger in hand, striking with assassin's precision while the creature had not yet had the chance to act. The blow landed with lethal force: 27 damage, clean and immediate. The ghoul dropped where it stood.

---

**Calmer's Holy Bloodlust**
With three ghouls still lurching forward, Calmer channeled the full force of his divine wrath — Turn Undead blazing outward. Every remaining ghoul failed its save and fled the room in terror, taking holy damage as they ran.

GM narrating Calmer: "Palmer's like, yes, yes, yes, yes, yes, yes, yes, yes, yes, yes, yes, yes, yes! Die, you pieces of… And he starts cursing them. He goes, this is how the power of… he's like, you're starting to realize Calmer's going a little bit on the nutso job side."

Zephyr watched the war cleric come fully unhinged in the best possible way — the frustration of two failed attempts on the wraith finally releasing itself against something that couldn't resist him.

---

**Sequioa Finishes the Last One**
As the wounded ghouls scrambled for the corridor, Sequioa drew her bow and put an arrow into the back of the last fleeing creature, finishing it cleanly as it tried to escape.

---

**After the Fight: Zephyr on the Ghouls**
Zephyr: "Maybe we should go find that guy?"
The ghouls had been Earth Temple members — recently dead, recently raised. Someone with necromantic power was working in this dungeon, and the question of who hung in the air as the sounds of Calmer's cursing finally faded.
