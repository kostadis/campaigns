**Entering the Air Temple**
The party retreated through the Air Temple's corridors — cold and still compared to the scalding steam they'd just escaped — and the wraith was already waiting for them.

**The Wraith's Threat**
GM (as the Wraith): "Well, one of you is going to die today."
GM (narrating Calmer): "But… not me, explains Calmer, whose armor class is 20, and he dashes."
The wraith's declaration hung in the air, hollow and certain — and the party answered it with their feet.

**Calmer's Failed Turn Undead**
GM (narrating Calmer): "He's going to try one more time to kill this creature, because he hates it with such an abiding and pure passion."
GM (narrating Calmer): "Palmer's like, This is not fair!"
Zinnia watched Calmer level his divine focus at the wraith — once, and then again — and both times the creature simply refused to yield, its cold presence unmoved by the holy light.

**Zephyr Strikes and Runs**
Zephyr raised his pistol, landed a clean shot on the wraith, and immediately bolted toward the secret passage — no hesitation, no ceremony.
The wound didn't slow it. It barely seemed to notice.

**The Party Dashes**
Zinnia ran. There was no other calculation to make — the wraith couldn't be turned, couldn't be reasoned with, and a fight in this corridor was a fight they would lose.
Every step toward the stairs was a decision made in the body before the mind caught up.

**The Wraith Strikes Calmer**
As Calmer made his own furious retreat, the wraith closed the distance and struck him hard across the back — a blow that landed with the weight of something beyond muscle or bone.
GM (as the Wraith): "The Wraith, seeing Calmer run, laughs at him hysterically."
The laughter followed them up the stairs, hollow and contemptuous, long after the creature itself was out of sight.

**Scrambling to Safety**
The party reached the upper level and put stone and stairs between themselves and whatever still moved in the Air Temple below.
The wraith's laughter was the last thing Zinnia heard before the corridor went quiet.
