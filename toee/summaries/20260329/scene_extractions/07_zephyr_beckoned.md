**The Runner Arrives**
Before the Obsidian Edge could act on the intelligence the freed prisoner had just handed them — the exact location of the secret door to the Deep Temple — an Earth Temple runner found them in the corridor. Hartsch had summoned his Obsidian Edge to a war council upstairs. The descent would have to wait.

The moment pressed on Zephyr with particular weight: they had everything they needed to go deeper, and now they had to go back up.
