**Entering the Air Temple**
Calmer walks into the Air Temple corridor and immediately sees the wraith drifting in the shadows ahead — cold, malevolent, waiting. His reaction is instantaneous and visceral.
The sight of it makes him want to be anywhere else.

---

**First Attempt: Turn Undead**
Calmer channels the divine light of St. Cuthbert, thrusting it outward at the wraith. The wraith resists — makes its save — and the holy power dissipates into the cold air without effect.
The failure stings, but Calmer isn't finished yet.

---

**Second Attempt: Turn Undead**
Driven by what the GM describes as "an abiding and pure passion" of hatred for this creature, Calmer tries again.

GM (narrating Calmer): "He's going to try one more time to kill this creature, because he hates it with such an abiding and pure passion."

The wraith resists a second time.

GM (narrating Calmer): "Palmer's like, This is not fair!"

Two attempts, two failures — the wraith unmoved, untouched, contemptuous.

---

**The Wraith's Threat**
The wraith turns its hollow gaze on the scrambling party and speaks.

GM (as the Wraith): "Well, one of you is going to die today."

---

**Calmer's Deadpan Response**
Calmer does not argue. He does not stand his ground.

GM (narrating Calmer): "But… not me, explains Calmer, whose armor class is 20, and he dashes."

---

**Zephyr Wounds the Wraith**
Before dashing, Zephyr raises his pistol and puts a shot into the wraith — 5 damage after resistance — the only wound anyone lands on it. Then he breaks for the secret passage.
The wraith is hurt but not stopped.

---

**The Party Dashes**
One by one, the rest of the party takes the dash action and sprints for the passage Zephyr opened. The Air Temple corridor fills with the sound of running boots and held breath.
There is no fighting retreat here — this is pure flight.

---

**The Wraith Strikes Calmer**
As Calmer makes his own furious sprint, the wraith reaches out and catches him across the back — 16 damage in a single blow, cold and punishing. A stray fire bolt from somewhere in the chaos catches him for an additional 8 damage as the party clears the threshold.
He makes it through, but he is significantly wounded and the wraith's touch lingers like ice.

---

**The Wraith Laughs**
GM (as the Wraith): "The Wraith, seeing Calmer run, laughs at him hysterically."

The mocking laughter echoes up the corridor behind them as the party scrambles upstairs to safety.
It is the worst possible punctuation to two failed attempts at divine power — the undead creature not destroyed, not repelled, but deeply amused.
