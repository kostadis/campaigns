**[Entering the Scalding Battlefield]**
The party emerged onto the second level of the temple into a corridor choked with billowing scalding steam. Bodies from both the Fire and Earth temples littered the ground, and through the haze, Fire Temple bugbears held a barricade against a stalled Earth Temple advance. Calmer took in the chaos — two enemy factions locked in a fight that had nothing to do with the party, yet everything to do with their path forward.
The heat and steam pressed against skin and lungs alike, a battlefield already drenched in violence before the party had fired a single shot.

---

**[Zephyr and Zinnia Open the Fight]**
Zephyr struck first, loosing a precise shot from the mist into the bugbear line. Zinnia followed hard on his heels, rushing into the haze and cutting down an evil cultist at the front with a series of rapid strikes before the bugbears could even orient to the new threat.
Calmer watched his companions move with lethal efficiency, the engagement already tipping into chaos.

---

**[The Bugbears Retaliate]**
The bugbears answered with savage fury. A devastating blow landed on Zinnia, leaving her reeling, and Zephyr and Sequioa were struck as well. The barricade was holding, and the party's momentum threatened to stall against the creatures' brute ferocity.
The weight of the moment settled — the party had started something they now had to finish.

---

**[Calmer Smashes the Barricade Open]**
Calmer channeled divine light into a single thunderous blast and drove it into the barricaded double doors. The doors smashed wide open, and Earth Temple forces poured through the gap, crashing into the Fire Temple line.
In one concentrated act of faith and violence, Calmer had turned the entire structure of the fight — the barricade that had been holding the battlefield in stalemate ceased to exist.

---

**[The Party Presses the Advantage and Disengages]**
With the doors broken and Earth Temple forces flooding in, Zephyr cut down another bugbear with a flurry of precise strikes, and Sequioa delivered a crushing blow that finished off another. The remaining enemies were now caught between two warring factions.
Calmer read the battlefield clearly — the magical steam was not abating, the elemental violence was escalating, and there was nothing left here worth dying for. The party disengaged and retreated, leaving two factions to grind each other to dust.
