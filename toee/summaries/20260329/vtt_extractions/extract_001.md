## Events

- **Romag's Assassination (pre-session recap):** The party entered Romag's private chamber via secret passage; Zephyr magically slammed the doors shut; Zinnia (referred to as "Xenia" by the GM), Sequoia, and Calmer attacked and killed Romag. Calmer nearly invoked St. Cuthbert aloud mid-combat but caught himself, channeling necrotic energy instead.
- **Hartsch Seizes Power:** Immediately after Romag's death, Hartsch dipped his hands in Romag's blood, took his sacred vestments, declared himself Supreme Prophet of the Earth Temple, and announced an aggressive new era. He named the party "the Obsidian Edge" — his personal vanguard — before the assembled Earth Temple.
- **Fire Temple Provocation:** The party passed through a magically lit corridor on the dungeon's second level and slipped a note under the Fire Temple door. When Alremm responded, the party revealed that Belsornig of the Water Temple had orchestrated the Chimera incident. Alremm screamed Belsornig's name and ordered an immediate assault on the Water Temple.
- **Three-Way War Triggered:** Hartsch, informed that the Fire Temple was attacking the Water Temple, ordered a full-scale Earth Temple assault. All three factions are now at war.
- **Barricade Problem:** The Earth Temple advance stalled at the second level because Fire Temple bugbears had dragged pews and stone rubble across the stairway. Hartsch asked the Obsidian Edge to break the stalemate.
- **Air Temple Bypass (en route to second level):** The party chose to bypass the barricade by cutting through the Air Temple. The wraith drifted toward them; the party ran rather than engage it, and the wraith turned back once they left its domain. Calmer noted with dark humor that Kelno apparently believes the wraith is an air elemental.
- **Scalding Battlefield Combat:** The party emerged onto the second level into a chaotic, steam-filled battlefield. Zephyr attacked first (bow shot), Zinnia/Xenia killed a Fire Temple cultist in melee, bugbears retaliated critically wounding Zinnia and also striking Zephyr and Sequoia. Calmer used Guiding Bolt to smash open the barricaded double doors, allowing Earth Temple forces to charge through. Zephyr killed one bugbear, Sequoia killed another. Party recognized the magical steam made the battlefield dangerous and chose to disengage.
- **Wraith Encounter (Air Temple retreat):** On retreat through the Air Temple, Calmer attempted Turn Undead on the wraith — failed twice (wraith made both saves). Zephyr shot the wraith with his pistol (5 damage, resistant to piercing), then dashed to the secret passage. All party members dashed out. The wraith struck Calmer for 16 damage as he fled and laughed at him hysterically. A stray fire bolt also hit Calmer for 8 damage as the party emerged upstairs.
- **Wraith observes a door:** On the way out, Zinnia/Xenia (Investigation 24/25) noticed a door to the east opening and closing as if someone was watching — origin unresolved.
- **Party retreats to their territory:** Party chose to wait out the battle in their own quarters rather than remain in the crossfire.
- **Ghoul Ambush:** Four ghouls — appearing to be recently deceased Earth Temple members, raised by necrotic magic — arrived to scavenge dead bodies. Sequoia attempted to set a trap at the door; the ghouls triggered it prematurely. Zephyr killed one instantly with a dagger using Assassin advantage and sneak attack (27 damage). Calmer successfully channeled Turn Undead, causing all remaining ghouls to flee and dealing 4d8 holy damage. Sequoia finished off one fleeing ghoul with a melee strike. Calmer entered a state of religious bloodlust, cursing the retreating undead.
- **Party deduces all four temples now involved:** On descending to the second level again, the party observed goblins (identified as Kelno's) dragging looted weapons and coins through a secret door. Party concluded Kelno had mobilized his forces to scavenge the dead, meaning all four temples are now participating in the conflict.
- **The Great Hall of Revels explored:** Party passed through a massive ruined hall with mummified heads, torn battle standards, battered shields, a broken broadsword, and tapestries of raiding scenes. Broad black stone steps on the north wall descend to the lower level but appeared inaccessible/unappealing. A fight between fire snakes, a wounded salamander, and four water gargoyles was occurring nearby amid scalding steam.
- **Elemental creature encounter bypassed:** The party encountered fire snakes, a salamander, and water gargoyles fighting. No party member speaks Primordial; they could not communicate with the creatures. Zephyr considered capturing one to interrogate about the Deep Temple but abandoned the plan. Party moved south to find another route.

## NPC Interactions

- **Hartsch:** Did not need to be persuaded — immediately understood the coup when Zephyr slammed the doors, placed his weapons down, and stepped aside. After Romag died, Hartsch seized leadership unprompted, covered himself in Romag's blood, declared himself Supreme Prophet, and named the party the Obsidian Edge. He later ordered a full assault when told the Fire Temple was distracted.
- **Alremm (Fire Temple):** The party slipped a note under the Fire Temple door claiming knowledge of betrayal. When Alremm spoke through the door, party revealed Belsornig's role in the Chimera incident. Alremm screamed Belsornig's name and ordered his troops to attack the Water Temple immediately.
- **Romag:** Killed in his sleeping chamber by the party. Final expression was confusion turning to horror before being struck down.
- **Dren (Broken Blades):** Had arranged the morning meeting with Romag on the party's behalf (per recap). Was later found preparing his Crimson Guards for the assault. Party sent Dren and his men into the battle.
- **Kelno (Air Temple):** Not directly encountered, but deduced to be sending goblins to loot the dead during the temple war. Calmer observed with grim amusement that Kelno apparently believes the wraith haunting the Air Temple is an air elemental.
- **Wraith:** Resisted Turn Undead twice. Struck Calmer for 16 damage during his retreat. Declared "one of you will die today." Laughed hysterically as Calmer fled. Seemed satisfied once the party left its domain and did not pursue past the Air Temple.

## PC Actions & Decisions

- **Zephyr:** Used magic (unspecified subtle ability) to slam Romag's chamber doors shut, triggering the assassination. Initiated the barricade fight with a bow shot (21 to hit, 19 damage). Killed one bugbear (multi-attack: 20 damage, miss, then 9 damage = 29 total, confirmed kill). Shot the wraith with his pistol (22 to hit, 5 damage after resistance). Dashed out of the Air Temple. Killed one ghoul with a dagger (assassin advantage, sneak attack: 27 damage, instant kill). Disengage-and-hiked after the first ghoul kill. Attempted to interrogate elemental creatures but abandoned the plan when no Primordial speakers were available.
- **Zinnia/Xenia:** Killed a Fire Temple cultist with two melee strikes (hit AC 13, 3 damage, confirmed kill). Took a critical hit from a bugbear (25 damage). Took 4 damage from a second bugbear. Used disengage action to retreat from the barricade fight. Rolled Investigation 24–25 to notice the door opening and closing in the Air Temple. Rolled Insight 25 to deduce the goblins belonged to Kelno and were scavenging dead bodies.
- **Sequoia:** Shot a bugbear with his bow (21 to hit, 19 damage). Killed a second bugbear in melee (32 damage total including sneak attack). Attempted to set a trap at the door before the ghoul ambush — ghouls rolled a 1 and triggered it prematurely, making the trap ineffective. Used a readied action to finish off a fleeing ghoul in melee after Calmer's Turn Undead.
- **Calmer:** Used Guiding Bolt to smash open the barricaded double doors, opening the path for the Earth Temple advance. Attempted Turn Undead on the wraith twice — both failed (wraith made DC 15 saves). Nearly called on St. Cuthbert by name mid-combat but caught himself. Successfully used Turn Undead on the four ghouls — all failed their saves, fleeing and taking 4d8 holy damage. Entered a state of religious bloodlust, cursing the retreating undead. Took 16 damage from the wraith's attack of opportunity and 8 damage from a stray fire bolt (total significant HP loss this session). Used hit dice on a short rest (partial recovery to HP 23 noted).
- **Party collectively:** Chose to disengage from the barricade fight once the doors were open rather than continue fighting. Chose to run from the wraith rather than engage it. Chose to retreat to their own territory rather than remain in the active battle zone. Chose to bypass the elemental creature fight. Agreed that using the ongoing temple war as cover to explore the Deep Temple is the next objective. Took a short rest in their territory before descending again.
