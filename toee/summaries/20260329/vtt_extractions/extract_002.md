## Events

- The party navigates second-level corridors amid ongoing elemental chaos; a steam eruption temporarily blinds the area, which the party uses as cover to move.
- The party chooses to go northeast, passing a passageway to the east before discovering a chamber containing chained human prisoners.
- One prisoner claims knowledge of the Deep Temple and offers to share it in exchange for his freedom.
- Sequoia picks the prisoner's shackles (roll: 27), freeing him. The other four prisoners immediately demand to be freed as well.
- The freed prisoner reveals he was once a member of the Air Temple, and that Kelno knew of a secret door inside the Air Temple leading to the Lower/Greater Temple.
- Zephyr attempts to intimidate the prisoner into providing more specific information (initial roll: 4, failed); Calmer assists by appearing terrifying, granting advantage, and Zephyr rerolls for 19.
- The prisoner, conceding he would be "pushing his luck," reveals the exact location of the secret door to the Greater Temple on the party's map — marked adjacent to the Air Temple.
- Session ends. GM announces that next session will begin after a long rest, with Hartsch having located the party via an Earth Temple guard and dragged them into a war council meeting upstairs.

## NPC Interactions

- **Prisoners (group):** Pleaded not to be abandoned. One spoke up with knowledge of the Deep Temple; the others had no relevant information. After the one prisoner was freed, the remaining four demanded to be freed as well. Their fate was not resolved on-screen.
- **Former Air Temple Cultist (freed prisoner):** Bargained his knowledge for freedom. Revealed that Kelno knows of (or knew of) a secret door inside the Air Temple leading to the Lower Temple. Laughed at the idea that anyone would *want* to go to the Deep Temple. Resisted Zephyr's initial intimidation attempt but capitulated when Calmer's imposing presence was brought to bear. Pointed out the exact location of the secret door on the map.
- **Kelno (mentioned, not present):** Identified by the prisoner as the source of knowledge about the secret door to the Greater Temple, located within the Air Temple.
- **Hartsch (mentioned, not present):** GM reveals as a session-bridging note that Hartsch found the party with an Earth Temple guard and has summoned them to a war council meeting upstairs. This will open the next session.

## PC Actions & Decisions

- **Navigation:** Party chose to go northeast through the corridors rather than through the door directly ahead or southeast.
- **Zephyr:** Asked prisoners directly about the Deep Temple. Attempted intimidation on the freed prisoner (roll: 4, failed alone; rerolled with Calmer's assistance for 19, succeeded).
- **Zinnia:** Used Insight with advantage (as a spy) to assess whether the prisoner was lying about knowing the Deep Temple. Roll: 24. Confirmed the prisoner was telling the truth.
- **Sequoia:** Picked the lock on the prisoner's shackles. Roll: 27. Succeeded.
- **Calmer:** Assisted Zephyr's intimidation attempt by presenting a terrifying demeanor, granting advantage on the reroll.
- **Party decision:** Chose to interrogate and intimidate rather than kill the prisoner or escort him out. Obtained the secret door location without committing to further helping the prisoner.
