## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party discovers a surviving fire cleric attempting to flee with valuables and convinces her to serve their new regime.
- The party entered a 20-by-30 chamber strewn with rugs and tapestries, plush furniture, and a plastered ceiling "painted with scenes of demons and humans and lewd activities," catching the fire cleric grabbing as much as possible to flee.
- Zephyr opened with "Why are you fleeing?", which only enraged the cleric further, as the party had "killed everything that mattered to her."
- Rather than fight, the party executed a group grapple ("Let's all go hug him together to surprise him") — Zephyr landed the natural 20 (first grapple), Zinnia followed with a 21 (second grapple, with advantage from the first), and Sequioa joined to complete the pin.
- Zephyr made the pitch from within the grapple: "sometimes we lose things that are due to us, but... We gain a freedom that is more important. ... So, you could be our subordinate, or you could leave the temple."
- Calmer added the third option: "or you can die, let's not forget that option."
- Calmer demanded the cleric "acknowledge that the Earth Temple is now supreme, and that the Fire Temple is subordinate to Earth. You must acknowledge the power of earth over fire."
- The cleric, with a dagger at her throat, capitulated — "You know, I never really liked Alrrem" — and confirmed she was the last living fire cleric. *(Maeris's claim is unverified; Albreth and Ingulf, two fire priests, remain unaccounted for. Varek Solain's class is also unconfirmed.)*
- The party appointed Maeris Dorn as the new head cleric of the Fire Temple, instructing her to find the captain of the fire guard, organize the troops, and restore order.
