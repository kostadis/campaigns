## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party continues their intense struggle against fire salamanders within the Fire Temple's central pit, with Zephyr taking a devastating hit.
- A fire salamander hisses and constricts Zephyr; he rolled a 14 on a Strength saving throw when he needed a 15, leaving him restrained and grappled.
- Being restrained granted the salamander advantage on its next attack; it followed with a flaming-spear strike, dealing 18 from the constriction plus 21 fire damage (2d8+4+2d6) for 39 total.
- Zephyr used Uncanny Dodge to halve the largest attack (removing 5 points, since the fire damage was not itself an attack), ultimately taking 33 points — the DM and player worked through the new 5e grapple and Uncanny Dodge interaction in real time.
- The second salamander initially oriented on Sequioa but switched to attacking Calmer at range; both ranged attacks missed (a 10 against Calmer's Fire Shield AC was a miss, and the follow-up was also a miss).
- Sequioa struck with a Vex weapon first to set up advantage, dealing 9 on the first hit and 19 on the second sneak-attack-enabled strike before disengaging.
- Zinnia charged the low-hit-point salamander, dealt 11 points and killed it outright ("No, you just killed him"), then attacked the fully-healed salamander for 8 points.
- Calmer, with his Fire Shield (frost variant) active, cast Toll the Dead on the last salamander, but the creature made its Wisdom saving throw.
- The last salamander suddenly stopped, knelt, and put its weapon down, signaling a desire to parley rather than fight.
