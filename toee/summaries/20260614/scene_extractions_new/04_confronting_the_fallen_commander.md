## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party discovers the wounded former commander of the Fire Temple through a secret door and negotiates his submission.
- The party found a secret door to the north (opened by Zephyr) leading to a lavish 30-foot square chamber with a huge red-and-gilt lacquered wooden throne, where the wounded former commander sat.
- The commander recognized them: "oh, it's you. The obsidian edge, the interlopers who killed... Alrrem, destroyed my temple, massacred my forces. Have you come here to finish the job?" *(The GM could not provide his name in-session — "My name. I need a name, hold on" — so his name was not established at the table. "Varek Solain" is from GM prep notes, awaiting table confirmation.)*
- When Zephyr first answered "Yes," the commander drew his sword ("I won't go down without a fight!"), prompting Zephyr to backtrack: "No, I lied. It's okay. You can continue being a Fire Temple soldier."
- Calmer formally declared the new order: "the Obsidian Edge and the Chief Cleric is myself, [Calmer]," and asserted the commander would be subordinate to Dren Halveth, "supreme commander of the Earth Temple Forces." *(Calmer used "Chief Cleric" here; he later demanded the title "Prophet Calmer" during the same session.)*
- Zephyr demanded a tribute — "Two gold coins for sparing your life, please" — and when Calmer scoffed ("just two?"), explained "What I demand is not money, but respect."
- The commander handed over two gold pieces, confused, and the exchange of warnings closed the scene: Zephyr — "I will not forget this." / Commander — "Nor will I."
