# The Captain's Report

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### Captain Dren Halveth arrives with urgent news regarding the collapse of the other temple leaders.
- Captain Dren Halveth arrives after depositing the injured Calmer safely upstairs, complaining, "oh my god, that guy whines a lot... he won't shut up." Zephyr defends him: "He's a worrier at heart."
- Dren reports: "The captain, the captain? March is finished. Belsornig's people are at his door. He's screaming for you."
- Dren reminds the party that he takes orders, not initiative: "you didn't put me in charge of mercenaries, because I tell you what to do. You tell me."
- The party learns that Alrrem is on his pyre and Belsornig is pulling his treasury out the south route — "Low, healthy, vulnerable. Your calls."
- The party decides to leave Dren and his mercenaries behind to guard Calmer while they press into the Water Temple, the DM noting Dren is "the only person in this temple who might be loyal to you."
