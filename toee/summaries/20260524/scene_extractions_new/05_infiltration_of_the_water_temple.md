# Infiltration of the Water Temple

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party uses a clever ruse to bypass guards and then launches a devastating ambush on the Prophet of Water.
- After failing to break down the door, the guards shout a muffled name and demand, "You're not supposed to be here? Who is this?"
- The party bluffs the guards by claiming to be messengers bearing news of Alrrem's assassination, even offering a letter and coin as proof.
- The guards, believing the party to be allies, reveal their intentions while opening up: "She's dead, then with Alremm dying, and those fools... All that remains is for us to defeat those pompous passes known as the obsidian Edge."
- The DM rewards the roleplay directly: "Given how well you role-played this, yes, you have surprise."
- The cult fanatics (armor class 13) had pre-cast Spiritual Weapon in anticipation of trouble.
- Sequoia and Zinnia strike down the first cult fanatic; Zinnia finishes it with a bonus attack for 27 points of damage total.
- Zephyr, as an assassin with surprise, lands an automatic critical on the second fanatic.
- Sequoia kills the second cult fanatic for 16 points of damage, clearing the way to the inner sanctum.
- Kelno had previously handed the party a Dagger of Venom (a second one — the party already carried one from Rannos/Gremag's quarters), telling them, "I've been saving this to kill Belsornig for some time. If you wouldn't mind doing me the honors to do the deed?"
- Using exceptional stealth (Sequoia rolling 26, Zephyr 20, Zinnia 18), the party sneaks into the inner chamber where Belsornig (armor class 16) is preoccupied and unaware.
- The party launches a coordinated assault: Sequoia deals 26, Zinnia incinerates for 16 plus a 30-point bonus attack, and Zephyr piles on with sneak-attack criticals for 38 total — a "staggering amount of damage in one round."
- As Belsornig drops to 1 hit point, Zephyr briefly considers keeping him alive — "Maybe we should keep him alive? No?" — but Sequoia cuts him off: "No, let's just kill him."
- Zephyr delivers the killing blow to Belsornig using the Dagger of Venom.
- A loud gong resonates through the temple level as the Prophet falls.
- As the party is about to climb the stairs in the Air Temple to return to their quarters, a large group of bugbears wearing the black of the Greater Temple arrive and announce that the Greater Temple wishes to speak to the victors.
