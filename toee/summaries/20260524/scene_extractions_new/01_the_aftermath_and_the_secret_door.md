# The Aftermath and the Secret Door

## Scene summary (from gm-assist, verbatim)

#### The party regroups after a series of brutal battles and prepares to enter the Air Temple through a newly discovered secret door.
- The party reflects on their recent victory over a Wraith and several specters that had been haunting the temple passages.
- Calmer, severely injured from the previous battle, is taken to safety by Captain Dren Halveth and his mercenaries — not dead, but "deeply injured," taken up so "he doesn't get himself killed."
- The group prepares to enter through the secret door leading into the Air Temple, which had been previously exposed during a skirmish with gnolls.
- Zephyr takes the lead as the party prepares to descend further into the temple complex.
- The party discusses the strategic situation, noting that the Greater Temple has barricaded itself while the lesser temples engage in a civil war.
- The DM reminds the party of their fundamental objective: with the chaotic evil cultists motivated mainly by power, "all you have to do is let them keep fighting and periodically stab one in the back, and the battle ends."
- One of Dren's mercenaries comes along for backup with orders to protect the boss; the party leaves him at the doorway to guard against anyone coming from behind, since the mercenaries are "anything but stealthy."
