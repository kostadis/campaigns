## CURRENT SITUATION — The Upper Temple Under New Management

The four elemental sub-temples (Earth, Fire, Air, Water) are now the party's domain, reporting up to
the Greater Temple. State of each:

- **Earth Temple** — party's power base. Calmer rules as "Prophet"; **Dren Halveth** commands the
  forces (Broken Blades + remaining Earth troops). HQ moved to Hartsch's old chambers.
- **Fire Temple** — pacified. **Maeris Dorn** installed as head cleric; **Vorkal** (salamander) under
  contract as guardian; **Varek Solain** (former commander, has a Flametongue) submitted but resentful.
  Looted: Frostbrand, fire-resistance potions/ring.
- **Air Temple** — **Kelno** rules as the party's subordinate #2; supplies goblins and an informant
  network. The old wraith threat (Erith) is gone; passage cleared.
- **Water Temple** — leaderless since Belsornig's death. Party is **here now**, fighting animated stone
  gargoyle guardians; treasury and "trolls on contract" still to be dealt with.

**Above them all:** the **Greater Temple** (Levels 3–4, unexplored) — Barkinar, Lord Wizard Senshock,
the hiding wizard Falrinth. The Greater Temple sealed itself during the war and emerged to crown the
victors. This is now the campaign's true antagonist, and the party is embedded directly beneath it.

## NPCS — CURRENT STATE

### Superiors / Greater Temple (the new top of the board)
- **Barkinar** — leader of the Greater Temple. Cold, composed, genuinely impressed by the party's
  efficiency ("four problems gone by sundown"). Flanked by ogre bodyguards and a hill giant. Installed
  the party as Upper Temple rulers; set the ~one-week "look organized" deadline; wants the Chest of Rao.
- **Lord Wizard Senshock** — ambitious, paranoid, useful. Barkinar's warning: "Be civil, and let him
  underestimate you." Not yet met on-screen.
- **Falrinth** — a wizard hiding from the Greater Temple for his own reasons. Flagged by Barkinar as a
  matter "to be discussed later." Open hook.
- **The unidentified necromancer** — raising recently-dead Earth Temple members as ghouls during the
  war. Still unidentified; Barkinar explicitly named finding him as a task.

### Subordinates / allies (the party's new org chart)
- **Dren Halveth** — Broken Blades captain, now Supreme Commander of Earth Temple forces; reports to
  Calmer; the most genuinely loyal figure in the dungeon. Pragmatic, profane, takes orders not initiative.
- **Kelno** — ancient, cunning Air Temple survivor; the party's #2; "I am for chaos, but I'm also for
  living." Anticipated the party for two weeks (the "second chair"). Gave them the Dagger of Venom that
  killed Belsornig. Loyalty is transactional — trust accordingly.
- **Vorkal** — fire salamander; contracted Fire Temple guardian; hints he holds knowledge "about the
  Greater Temple and what really is going on," to be shared if the relationship improves.
- **Frostbrand** — sentient good-aligned magic short sword (Sequoia's). Anti-evil zealot, delighted by
  the undercover mission, recognized Calmer as "one of St. Cuthbert's." Held captive since Emridy Meadows.
  A talking liability around cover, but a knowledge source.
- **Maeris Dorn** — co-opted Fire cleric, now nominal Fire head-cleric. "I never really liked Alrrem."
  Compliance is grief-driven and shallow.
- **Varek Solain** — submitted Fire commander; paid 2 gp tribute; parted with "Nor will I [forget]."
  Resentful; carries a Flametongue; a future loose thread.
