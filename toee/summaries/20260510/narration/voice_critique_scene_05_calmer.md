## Flags

### [1] Voice spec conflict — wrong pronoun for Zinnia

> Zinnia put down the last surviving specter — missed the first strike, took it on the second, and when it still refused, still clinging to the last of itself, she flowed into another and it was simply over.

**Why:** The Zinnia voice spec uses "he/him" throughout ("He approaches every situation as an intelligence operation," "He doesn't struggle with his cover identity"). "She" appears again in scene 07 (Zephyr's section: "We needed her"), making this a two-scene inconsistency that needs to be corrected before final assembly — not a judgment call.
**Suggested rewrite:** `he flowed into another`

### [2] Cliché / on-the-nose simile

> They came apart in the radiance, all of them but one, scattered like the lie they were.

**Why:** Theologically, undead are lies — and Calmer would think this — but "scattered like the lie they were" announces its own symbolism. Calmer's strongest voice moments in this scene are blunt and declarative: "The Wraith was dead. The thing that had mocked me. *Dead.*" The simile here reaches for closure where blunt statement would hit harder.
**Suggested rewrite:** `They came apart in the radiance, all of them but one. Four nights of carrying this, and they just — came apart.`

### [3] Generic qualifier

> I felt the floor of myself drop out and knew, the way you know a true thing instantly, that I was at the very bottom of what a man can hold and still stand.

**Why:** "The way you know a true thing instantly" is a filler qualifier that could appear in any narrator's interiority. The sentence is already strong; the qualifier softens it by explaining the knowing rather than stating it. Calmer's spec shows he doesn't hedge his certainties — he states them flat, even when wrong.
**Suggested rewrite:** `I felt the floor of myself drop out and knew — instantly and completely — that I was at the very bottom of what a man can hold and still stand.`

## Verdict

The best Calmer scene in the document. "So I charged in. Boldly. I want that recorded." and "I want that recorded too, because of what I did next." are exactly right — the self-dramatizing warrior priest at the edge of himself. The pronoun error for Zinnia is the only fix with factual stakes; the other two are refinements that sharpen an already strong scene.
