## Spells — Fire Shield Damage Dice
**Location:** Spells (Fire Shield)
**Issue:** The recap claims the shield "deals 4d8 instead of 2d8" because salamanders are cold-vulnerable. RAW, Fire Shield deals 2d8 fire (warm) or **cold (chill)** damage regardless of target type; there is no "double dice" mechanic. The 22 damage cited is also inconsistent with a 4d8 cold roll being the cause — vulnerability would double the rolled total, not the dice count.
**Evidence:** Standard Fire Shield rules; recap's own internal math (22 cold from "4d8").
**Suggested fix:** Correct to: shield deals 2d8 cold, doubled by vulnerability. Remove the "4d8 instead of 2d8" claim.
