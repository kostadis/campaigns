## Flags

### [1] Generic simile — wrong register

> I had to push, the way you push a heavy door that's been swollen shut, and feel it give right at the moment your shoulder starts to doubt.

**Why:** This is a domestic physical metaphor for a persuasion moment. Zephyr's simile register runs financial ("the ledger does not forgive debts"), theatrical ("The tiefling breathes"), or sensory-Abyssal ("I've heard of worse in the Abyss"). A swollen door is a generic image that could appear in any narrator's section. The spec: he reads people, not rooms; he frames outcomes as expenditures, not resistance.

**Suggested rewrite:** It cost me something to land it clean — the way any investment costs, the principal up front, the return not guaranteed — but it held.

---

### [2] Stock phrase

> I told him there'd be rocky waters from now on.

**Why:** "Rocky waters" is a mixed stock phrase (rocky = terrain, waters = obstacle) that appears in any register. Zephyr's idiom is financially specific ("the only resource whose loyalty I'd buy without a discount," "the two most expensive words I own"). Generic nautical trouble isn't his frame.

**Suggested rewrite:** I told him the next portion of this would cost more than the last. (Or, leaning into his voice: I told him nothing ahead was cheap.) 

---

### [3] Narration-style flat spot

> I opened my mouth. "I'm the boss."

**Why:** Zephyr's narration doesn't typically frame his own speech-acts with "I opened my mouth." In the examples he either delivers the line directly, or gives us the calculation that precedes it ("I let the calculation run"). "I opened my mouth" is a generic he-said attribution that reads as any narrator's voice, not Zephyr's theatrical self-narration.

**Suggested rewrite:** "'I'm the boss,' I said." — or, if keeping the self-awareness: *My turn.* "I'm the boss."

---

## Verdict

Scene 02 is strongly Zephyr overall. "The two most expensive words I own. *For now.*" and "Sequoia, beside me, could not have sold ice in a steam corridor" and the closing "filed the dirt away with everything else I notice and never mention" are all exactly in register. The three flags are all fixable with line-level edits; no re-narration needed.
