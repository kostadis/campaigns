## Locations

**Sheernobb**
- What it is: A small gnome clan village in the Kron Hills
- Layout: Road ends at the Moot Hall on the largest hill; road splits into northern and southern branches around the Moot Hall forming a circle; most shops located on this circle; the Tavern is on the southern branch just past the road entry
- Current state: Active and inhabited; facing threats from bandits (Black Earth raiders) and giants

**Moot Hall, Sheernobb**
- What it is: The dominant building of Sheernobb; used for special gatherings and as the seat of village governance; located on the largest hill at the center of the road circle
- Features: Wide stone steps; grand stone doors carved with runes reading "Ni te pharkalu, quo lun verra lu" ("If you see it, it's not the right it"); large feasting chamber with three tiers of broad steps and benches descending to a central floor; intricately carved woodwork throughout; animated illusory statue of Keenleene outside
- Current state: Active; Glennddarc presides here

**Grindin Books**
- What it is: A bookshop in a small cottage on the northern branch of Sheernobb's road
- Features: Cluttered window display of books and maps; studded oak door; dimly lit interior; hundreds of books, maps, and scrolls on solid oak shelves; green-tinted glass candle bulbs; plush reclining chair and stool behind the counter
- Current state: Active; operated by Grindin Well-Read
- Notable inventory: Map of the local area; "Map of the Elemental Nexus"

**Pasmarie Clee's Beehive Farm**
- What it is: A beehive farm approximately a quarter mile southeast of Sheernobb
- Features: Verdant fields and wildflowers; neatly arranged hive rows; paths to each hive; small benches for observation; small building with a doorway
- Current state: Active but diminished due to stolen honey shipment

**The Gnarley Forest**
- What it is: A sprawling woodland straddling the borders of Verbobonc, Viscounty, Dyvers, the Domain of Greyhawk, the Wild Coast, Celene, and the Kron Hills; part of a contiguous forest including the Welkwood, Celene, and Suss Forest
- Features: Fertile glades and valleys; bronze wood trees (slender trunks, reddish-brown hard bark, up to 40 feet tall, 8-inch leaves on branches at 45-degree angles); ancient magic; inhabitants include woodsmen, Sylvan Elves, treants, and unicorns
- Current state: Active wilderness; site of hag's lair (now destroyed) and dryad glade

**The Hag's Gingerbread House**
- What it is: A single-room dwelling in a glade in the Gnarley Forest; used by the green hag as a lure for travellers
- Features: Walls and roof of genuine gingerbread; white frosting window trim; sugared plum roof fascia decorations; circular table; chairs; hot wood oven; smell of plum pie
- Current state: Abandoned; hag deceased; treasure location unknown

**The Dryad's Glade**
- What it is: A glade in the Gnarley Forest with tall grass and a solitary tree at its center
- Current state: Home to a romantically distressed dryad; barrel recovered here
