## World Events

- Dren and the Broken Blades settled into newly claimed territory within the Earth Temple.
- Romag directed the party to explore and clear areas to the south and east of his domain.
- The party discovered an old wine cellar with broken barrels and one intact small barrel; a party member sipped its contents, nearly was poisoned, and dry heaved for several minutes.
- The party discovered an adjacent storage chamber filled with moldering grain and rotting flour.
- Sequioa picked the lock and padlock on a secured prison chamber door.
- The party discovered seven prisoners inside the prison chamber: two females and five males, the males chained to the wall.
- The prisoners revealed that Romag used them as gnoll feed or sacrificial victims.
- The party learned that Jorin Pike Kale was imprisoned by Dick Rentsch's men for making a pass at a woman named Dalia.
- The party devised a plan to frame the Fire Temple for the prisoners' escape, using insignia and robes taken from Larith the Beautiful.
- Calmer stood guard at the door while Sequioa unlocked the prisoners' chains.
- Zephyr discovered a stairway leading up to an escape route.
- The party guided all seven prisoners to freedom via the escape stairway.
- Jorin Pike Kale shared intelligence about Captain Tolubb's pirate ship schedule at Nulb docks in gratitude.
- The Glimmerfields promised one hundred silver pieces as a reward for their rescue.
- The party discovered several empty armories containing broken weapons, ruined mail, and scattered human and gnoll skeletons.
- The party found five magical arrows in one of the armories.
- The party identified a passage descending to the second dungeon level, near the mysterious door concealing an eldritch creature, but chose not to descend.
- The party entered a refuse-filled chamber; Zephyr argued Calmer should investigate first; Calmer refused and insisted they go together.
- An Otyugh emerged from the refuse pile and attacked the party, its telepathic voice communicating "fresh meat" and "rot, good, do eat."
- The Otyugh grappled both Zinnia and Zephyr with its tentacles.
- The Otyugh bit Zinnia, injecting her with poison that reduced her strength and vitality.
- Zinnia wrenched free from the Otyugh's grip but was struck again while fleeing and fell unconscious.
- Calmer channeled healing energy into Zinnia, reviving her.
- The Otyugh stunned both Zephyr and Sequioa with tentacle slams.
- Zinnia delivered the killing blows, defeating the Otyugh.
- Calmer cut Zephyr free from the dead Otyugh's tentacles.
- The party searched the refuse and found one hundred eighty silver pieces, fifteen gold pieces, a potion of greater healing (sticky and foul-smelling), and a rusted Fire Temple breastplate.
- The party concluded that Romag fed Fire Temple rivals to the Otyugh as a disposal method.
- The party entered a new chamber; a gate dropped behind them, sealing them in.
- Harpies emerged from the shadows and began singing; Sequioa was charmed by one harpy's song.
- Zephyr determined that only one harpy's song was currently active and that two remaining harpies could still charm the rest of the party.
