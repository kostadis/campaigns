## Threads & Mysteries

- **The three-way war is ongoing and unresolved.** The Earth, Fire, and Water Temples are now at open war, but the outcome is not yet determined. The party's ultimate goal is the mutual destruction of all three factions.
- **The barricade on the stairway.** Fire Temple bugbears are holding the Earth Temple advance at bay; the party has begun an assault to break the stalemate but combat was not resolved this session.
- **Belsornig and the Water Temple.** Belsornig's full capabilities, resources, and response to the Fire Temple assault are unknown. He may become aware the party engineered his exposure.
- **Alrrem's fate.** It is unknown whether Alrrem is personally leading the assault on the Water Temple or what his tactical position is.
- **Kelno and the Air Temple.** The Air Temple has not been engaged or destroyed. Kelno's ignorance about the wraith raises questions about what else he misunderstands about his own faction's situation.
- **The wraith in the Air Temple.** Its nature, origin, and full threat level remain unexplored. It may become relevant if the party needs to pass through the Air Temple again.
- **Captain Vesrek and the Crimson Traitor.** Vesrek's location, allegiances, and ongoing role are unknown. He facilitated the Chimera purchase and may have further connections to Belsornig or other threats.
- **Nulb's river pirates.** The iron chains bear their marks, indicating they participated in transporting the Chimera. Their broader role in the regional power structure is unresolved.
- **Hartsch's loyalty and long-term intentions.** Hartsch is a self-declared megalomaniac who now controls the Earth Temple. His willingness to continue working with the party once he has secured power is not guaranteed.
- **The party's cover as the Obsidian Edge.** The party is publicly known to the entire Earth Temple army as Hartsch's vanguard. If they are seen acting against Earth Temple interests, or if any surviving Fire or Water Temple members recognise them, their cover may be compromised.
- **Calmer's near-invocation of Saint Cuthbert.** Calmer nearly called upon his holy patron mid-assassination inside an evil temple. The implications for his standing with his deity, or the reaction of temple cultists had he completed the invocation, remain unaddressed.
- **The ledger as evidence.** The wax-sealed ledger naming Belsornig and Captain Vesrek has been used to manipulate Alrrem but has not been presented to any outside authority. Its potential use as leverage or evidence beyond the dungeon is unresolved.
