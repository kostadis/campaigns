- A place of Beory worship within or near Hommlet

## Threads & Mysteries

- **Y'dey's disappearance**: The former head cleric of Hommlet has vanished. Terjon, who replaced her, is closed-mouthed about the circumstances. At least one local suspects Terjon of direct involvement. No resolution or further information provided.
- **The Black Jay's true allegiance**: The Black Jay is known locally as a bandit informant, but Zinia suspects a connection to the Temple of Elemental Evil that locals are unaware of. This connection has not been confirmed.
- **Temple of Elemental Evil resurgence**: Jaroo has identified that the pattern of outpost circles discovered by the party mirrors the Temple's original pre-battle expansion strategy. Whether the Temple is actively re-forming, who is leading it, and what its current goals are remain unknown.
- **The Moathouse**: Jaroo has directed the party to investigate the Moathouse as a likely nexus of current suspicious activity. Its current occupants, state, and any connection to the Temple are unknown.
- **Temple agents in Hommlet**: Jaroo warned that agents of the Temple of Elemental Evil may already be embedded in the village. No suspects have been identified.
- **Conflicting intelligence obligations**: The party has been separately instructed by Jaroo (Beory), Rufus (Verbobonc), and Terjon (St. Cuthbert) to share all findings exclusively with each of them. Sequoia and Zinia have privately noted these contradictions and intend to act independently. How this will be resolved remains open.
- **Verbobonc's true intentions in Hommlet**: Both Jaroo and local residents believe the castle construction is a pretext for absorbing Hommlet politically and religiously into Verbobonc's sphere. Whether this is accurate and what Burne and Rufus's full orders are remains unconfirmed.
- **Burne's experiments**: Burne was described as being pulled away from experiments when the party arrived. The nature of these experiments is unrevealed.
- **Extent of the Temple's new outpost network**: The party has uncovered "circles of outposts" but the full scope, location, and staffing of this network is unknown.
