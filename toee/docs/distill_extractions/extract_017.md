## World Events

- The party fought and killed Lareth the Beautiful in his inner sanctum in the Moathouse.
- Zephyr used Dust of Disappearance at the start of combat to become invisible and reposition.
- Lareth cast Mirror Image to confuse attackers and Inflict Wounds to severely wound Calmer.
- Lareth cast Hold Person, paralyzing both Zephyr and Zinnia.
- Sequoia, unparalyzed, struck the killing blow on Lareth (who was at one hit point).
- Sarge and all remaining guards were killed after Lareth's death.
- The party looted Lareth's body and quarters, recovering a Mace of Smiting, magical Plate Mail, a Ring of Free Action, a silver service set (2,000 GP), a fire opal necklace (3,500 GP), and gems worth 800 GP.
- All four party members gained a level following the Moathouse victory.
- The party contacted Thorne (Duke) and contracted him for Moathouse clean-up at 50 GP.
- The party explored the remaining Moathouse dungeon to make it safe for Thorne.
- The party discovered a secret mechanism dropping an iron grate blocking a passage, and found evidence that a false wall triggers a grate-related trap.
- The party deceived eight Gnolls into believing Lareth was alive and a pay raise was coming, stalling them.
- The party persuaded six Bugbears that Lareth was dead and the Moathouse was free for plunder, directing them toward the Gnolls.
- The party returned to Hommlet and convened the local council (Osler Goodingut, Burne, Rufus, Jaroo).
- The party presented proof of the Temple of Elemental Evil's resurgence and Lareth's death to the council.
- Jaroo declared the party must travel to Nulb to infiltrate Temple operations and identify true leadership.
- The council agreed to prepare the militia while the party continues covert operations.
- The session ended with the party preparing to travel north to Nulb, still holding the Chest of Rao.
