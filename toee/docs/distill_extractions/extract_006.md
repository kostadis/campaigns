## Threads & Mysteries

- **The marked stone**: Its elemental evil markings have not been fully explained or investigated. Terjon's suspicious initial desire to keep a "worthless" rock is unexplained.
- **Blueprint theft**: Someone in the worker encampments copied the keep's blueprints and is suspected of working with the bandits. The thief has not been identified.
- **Elemental Evil activity**: The confession and stone suggest the Temple of Elemental Evil may not be fully destroyed. Mickey's fears of temple spies remain unconfirmed or denied.
- **Moathouse dungeon level**: A stairway leading down has been found but not yet explored. The full extent of the moathouse's lower levels is unknown.
- **Trophy room heads**: Human, dwarf, and elf heads displayed as trophies suggest prior organized violence by temple forces. Not further investigated.
- **Airgid's mother's reward**: Airgid promised her mother would reward the party for returning her. The mother has not been contacted; the reward is outstanding.
- **Monster in the Gnarley Forest**: Zephyr mentioned a monster to recruit Elmo; the nature, location, and origin of this creature are unspecified.
- **Lost wizard laboratory under Emiry Meadows**: Spugnoir claims knowledge of this location. It has not been visited or verified.
- **Rumored secret passage under the Inn**: A patron claimed a passage beneath the Inn leads to four lost chambers of a dead wizard. Spugnoir denied it. Unverified.
- **Lysia's planned expedition to the ruined boathouse**: She needs equipment and companions; she may seek out the party or become a subplot.
- **Jira and Marek's family conflict**: Jira pleaded for the party's help in mediating between her father Jakub (Old Faith) and Marek's father Tomlyn (St. Cuthbert). Unresolved.
- **Calmer's appointment**: Calmer suggested meeting "tomorrow at the same time" before leaving Burne and Rufus's meeting; the purpose and outcome of this appointment are unclear.
- **Ivory box from the moathouse tower**: Found among the spider's hoard; contents and nature not yet examined.
- **Bandit confession and elemental evil connection**: The broader network of bandits and their relationship to a potential resurgent Temple of Elemental Evil remains largely uninvestigated.
- **Saboteur in the encampments**: Rufus believes the blueprint thief is a worker in the construction encampments connected to the bandits; this person has not been identified or confronted.
