- The party killed giants, ghouls, and ghasts (over twenty combined) and cleared an entire section of the dungeon, claiming it for the Earth Temple.

- Romag revealed that ten years ago the temple's forces marched out to conquer and were defeated at the Battle of Emory Meadows by the Archbishop of Veluna, the Kingdom of Furyondy, and the Circle of Eight (Bigby, Tenser, Mordenkainen and others).

- The party deduced that the Crimson Reapers serve a deeper organisation above the four elemental temples.
- Sequoia persuaded Dren Halveth to join the Earth Temple over the Water Temple; Dren agreed for 100 gold pieces.
- Zinnia observed Wat's unhappy reaction, confirming suspicions of his Water Temple affiliation.
- Wat approached Dren and whispered to him as the party departed; Dren shoved him away.
- The party returned the Broken Blades to the Temple of Elemental Evil; Romag assigned them to the newly captured territory under the party's command.
- Romag claimed an additional portion of the cleared territory for himself.
- Romag hosted a celebratory drinking party in the newly cleaned banquet hall (which still smelled of cadavers and dead creatures).
- Romag toasted Sequoia, Zephyr, Zinnia, and Calmer, specifically highlighting Calmer's conversion from a cleric of St. Cuthbert to a follower of the elder elemental god.

## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Orb**: Its current location is unknown. Romag wants it but does not appear to possess it. The party has not been directed to find it yet.
- **The Vault**: Romag confirmed the orb opens a vault in the temple's deep places but refused to reveal what it contains, stating that information must be earned. Contents remain unknown.
- **The Deeper Organisation Behind the Temples**: The Crimson Reapers do not serve any of the four elemental temples directly; they serve an overarching organisation above them. The nature, identity, and goals of this organisation are unrevealed.
- **The Crimson Reapers**: Their full membership, base of operations, and the scope of their authority within the temple hierarchy are unknown. Whether they will take action against the party or Dren following the midnight ultimatum outcome is unresolved.
- **Wat's Whisper to Dren**: Wat whispered something to Dren Halveth as the party departed. The content of this message is unknown. Dren shoved him away, but whether the message had any effect on Dren's loyalty is unclear.
- **Wat's Role**: Suspected Water Temple operative; his ongoing presence at the Watersteide Hostel and his relationship with Dick Rentsch remain open questions.
- **The Temple's Central Driving Power**: The Circle of Eight locked the temple and blocked access to this power. Its exact nature is unrevealed.
- **The Archbishop of Veluna**: Mentioned as a key figure in the defeat of the temple ten years ago; no further information given.
- **Calmer's Conversion**: Romag publicly celebrated Calmer's conversion from a cleric of St. Cuthbert to a follower of the elder elemental god. Whether this conversion is genuine or part of the party's espionage cover is not stated in the notes.
- **The Elder Elemental God**: Referenced by Romag as the object of Calmer's new devotion; its relationship to the Earth Temple's ideology and the broader temple hierarchy is unexplored.
- **Earning Romag's Full Trust**: The party has not yet been told the contents of the vault or all of the temple's deepest secrets; they must continue proving themselves to access this information.
