## World Events

- Party escaped a wraith in the octagonal chamber that had previously drained Zephyr's life essence.
- Party navigated a processional hallway lined with bronze doors carved with vile scenes, used for leading sacrificial captives to elemental temples.
- Party entered an alabaster room and encountered two radiant angelic figures who requested the party surrender all weapons, holy symbols, magic items, and silvered items for a blessing, then step outside and count to seventy-seven.
- Party refused to surrender all their equipment; each member offered only one minor weapon; the angelic figures expressed disappointment and vanished.
- Zinnia discovered a secret entrance adjacent to the eastern exit of the alabaster room.
- Party entered a hidden lair and found two werewolves who revealed the angelic trap had been arranged for the Fire Temple and the party should have arrived unarmed.
- Party fought both werewolves; their standard weapons were ineffective against the lycanthropes.
- Calmer used divine power to temporarily enchant Sequioa's short sword and Zinnia's spear with magical properties for the duration of the fight.
- Calmer's radiant guardians manifested and burned the werewolves with holy light.
- Zinnia dealt a decisive blow; party killed both werewolves.
- Party searched the werewolf lair and recovered: a silver service, a magical dagger, a magical short sword, silver pieces, holy symbols of Saint Cuthbert and Rao, five silver daggers, and nineteen silver-tipped arrows.
- Party returned to Romag's chamber, successfully sneaking past the wraith and skeletal warriors in the bone-covered hallway.
- Dick Rentsch was found seated beside Romag; had wagered the party would not survive.
- Dick Rentsch revealed that Alremm had hired the werewolves as a test of new recruits.
- Romag expressed approval of party's resistance to the angelic trap and awarded them Earth Temple badges.
- Party rested in Earth Temple bunk rooms with other guards.
- Calmer declared himself a former cleric of Saint Cuthbert converted to the Earth Temple, swaying other guards to view the Earth Temple favorably.
- Lieutenant Krell assigned the party a mission the following morning: raid Fire Temple territory and kill Fire Temple forces.
- Party entered what Krell identified as Fire Temple territory, found what appeared to be a recently used guest chamber, and killed the creature inside.
- Party discovered the slain creature was an Earth Temple ogre, not a Fire Temple enemy.
- Party returned to Romag; Lieutenant Krell was absent.
- Romag revealed he had known Krell was a spy and had kept him under surveillance.
- Romag ordered the party to capture Krell alive so he could be sacrificed in the Temple of Earth as a warning.
