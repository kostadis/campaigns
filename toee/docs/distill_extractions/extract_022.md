## World Events

- Romag woke the party after eight hours of rest to report that Lieutenant Krell had been identified as a traitor who had fled to gnoll and bugbear territories.
- Romag presented a plan to trap Krell using Earth Temple guards at the entrance and exit while the party approached from the rear.
- The party successfully persuaded Earth Temple human guards to mount a frontal assault on gnoll territory as a distraction, exploiting the guards' fear of being replaced by gnolls and eaten.
- The Earth Temple guards advanced into gnoll territory and were ambushed — caught in nets and slaughtered by gnolls.
- Zinnia and Zephyr crept forward and witnessed gnolls feeding on the bodies of the fallen guards.
- The party engaged the remaining gnolls in combat; Calmer summoned spectral guardians, devastating the clustered enemies.
- Krell revealed himself during the fight, encouraging the gnolls and declaring allegiance to the Fire Temple: "I told you they would try to kill you. The Fire Temple protects its own."
- Krell fired his custom hand crossbow pistol twice at Calmer, knocking the cleric unconscious.
- Sequioa revived Calmer with a healing potion.
- Krell attempted to negotiate with the party, arguing the Earth Temple used followers as cannon fodder and offering to ally against the Earth Temple.
- The party rejected Krell's offer and subdued him with non-lethal strikes; Sequioa delivered the final blow rendering Krell unconscious.
- The party searched Krell's quarters and surrounding rooms, finding platinum pieces in a brazier, gold, a three-dragon ante set, flasks of oil, and a secret door leading deeper into the complex.
- The party brought the unconscious Krell before Romag, who ordered a ritual sacrifice in the great chamber.
- Romag presided over Krell's ritual sacrifice: he chanted to the elder elemental earth god, shoved dirt into Krell's mouth, and commanded four earth elementals to rise and tear Krell's body apart before a cheering crowd.
- The party blended into the crowd, feigning enthusiasm to avoid suspicion.
- The party overheard Romag and Hartsch discussing how the defeat had weakened their plans against the Fire Temple, and Hartsch suggested using the party as weapons against the Fire or Air Temple.
- The party observed Romag disappearing through a secret door and explored further.
- The party discovered a charred library containing burned remnants of books on evil philosophy, double-dealing, self-advancement, and treachery, with references to the sacred earth triangle and warnings about trials of earthy terrors in the depths below.
- The party found another secret door in the charred library area.
- Four temple guards challenged the party; they identified themselves as new Earth Temple recruits; guards mentioned Krell's death as an opportunity for promotion.
- Commander Joradin confronted the party in his section of the temple, acknowledged their recruit status, and ordered them back to their quarters.
- The party returned to Romag and questioned Joradin's authority; Romag explained Joradin was a new recruit brought in by Dick Rentsch.
- Romag granted the party the cleared gnoll and bugbear territory as their own base, provided written permission to explore freely, and warned them not to betray his trust.
- Romag cautioned the party that holding territory made them targets, as rivals advance by killing those above them.

## Locations

**Temple of Elemental Evil**
- What it is: A large underground complex housing multiple elemental temples and associated factions
- Current state: Active; multiple factions (Earth, Fire, Water, Air Temples) operate within it

**Earth Temple**
- What it is: The domain of the Earth Temple within the larger complex; features polished brown marble corridors veined with black, inlaid with yellow triangular patterns, lit by burning bronze crescents
- Current state: Active under Romag's leadership; weakened by losses in the gnoll ambush

**Gnoll and Bugbear Territory (now Party's Base)**
- What it is: A section of the Temple of Elemental Evil formerly controlled by gnolls and bugbears, used by Krell to stage his rebellion
- Current state: Cleared; formally granted to the party by Romag as their base of operations

**Ritual Chamber (Great Chamber)**
- What it is: A vast chamber with rough-hewn sandstone walls, a floor of dark brown earth, dimly lit by phosphorescent lichen; contains a twenty-foot pyramid of hard-packed dirt topped with a stone column carved with triangles and bearing bronze knives
- Recent events: Site of Krell's ritual sacrifice; Romag chanted to the elder elemental earth god and commanded earth elementals to execute Krell before a crowd

**Krell's Quarters**
- What it is: Krell's personal rooms within the temple
- Recent events: Searched by the party after Krell's capture; yielded platinum pieces hidden in a brazier, gold, a three-dragon ante set, flasks of oil, and a secret door leading deeper into the complex

**Charred Library**
- What it is: A room filled with the burned remains of books on evil philosophy, double-dealing, self-advancement, and treachery
- Recent events: Explored by the party; remnants referenced the sacred earth triangle and warned of trials of earthy terrors in the depths below; a second secret door was found here

**Commander Joradin's Section**
- What it is: A distinct section of the Earth Temple under Joradin's authority
- Recent events: Party encountered four guards and then Joradin here; Joradin ordered them to leave

**The Deep Temple**
- What it is: Referenced as a place deeper within the complex; implied to be more prestigious than the Earth Temple's surface operations
- Notes: Romag's predecessor was promoted there
