## World Events

- The party was hired by Narwell merchants to escort a caravan into the Gnarley Forest as bait for organized brigands.
- An ogre blocked the caravan's path in the Gnarley Forest and demanded a toll; the party bypassed him using copper bribes and intimidation.
- The party encountered the gnome tinkerer Jijohn in the Gnarley Forest, who warned them of organized forces behind the bandits, referenced the fall of the Temple of Elemental Evil ten years prior, and directed them to seek Burne and Rufus in Hommlet.
- The party left the caravan and located the bandit stronghold at the ruined Abbey of Allitur.
- Zephyr, Sequoia, and Zinia conducted a stealth assault on the lower levels of the Abbey, killing bandits with sneak attacks before any alarm was raised.
- The party cornered the orc cook Urot in the kitchen; she confessed that Snej is an operative of the Temple of Evil, resourced by Canon Belsornig.
- The party discovered Airgid, a captive baby silver dragon, chained to a statue in the Abbey's desecrated chapel amid defaced frescos of the god Allitur.
- Airgid revealed her mother is in the Lortmil Mountains; the party agreed to escort her to Hommlet to plan a reunion.
- The party killed a giant spider in the collapsed guest room of the Abbey in seconds, then rested briefly.
- The party raided the bandits' hidden cache and recovered 123 gold pieces, dust of disappearance, and a bag of holding.
