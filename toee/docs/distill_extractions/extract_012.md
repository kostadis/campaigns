## Threads & Mysteries

- **The Lost Chest:** The chest Lareth sent the party to retrieve is reportedly located in an abandoned monastery near Gallow's Corner. Its contents and true purpose remain unconfirmed. Multiple factions (Lareth, Burne and Rufus, Caer Ostverk/Lord Denelu, the Viscounty) are seeking it.
- **Lareth's True Motives:** The party suspects Lareth knowingly sent them to eliminate Black Earth cultist rivals rather than mere bandits, thereby consolidating his own elemental faction's power. His affiliation and ultimate goals remain unconfirmed.
- **The Chest as a Treaty Instrument:** Robert Deakin theorized the chest was meant to secure a treaty between the Viscounty and the Kingdom of Celene, enabling the Viscounty to wage war on gnomes and seize their gem mines. Whether this theory is accurate is unresolved.
- **Romag:** Named in notes found on the Black Earth cultists (notes from Romag to Thalsor); Romag's identity, location, and current status are unknown.
- **The Golem:** Thalsor's golem was rendered immobile but not destroyed; its current status and whether it poses an ongoing threat is unresolved.
- **Lord Denelu:** Referenced as willing to pay well for information on the chest; his identity, faction, and relationship to Caer Ostverk are unestablished.
- **Gonnathrad's Information:** Gonnathrad nearly revealed information about the monastery before stopping himself, citing his official duty; what he knows and withheld is unknown.
- **The Party's Exposed Identities:** Zinnia noted that Thalsor, if allowed to live, could have connected the party to the village elders and exposed them to the cult. Whether the Black Earth cult (or other factions) can still trace the party's actions back to them is unresolved.
- **The Temple and "Big Evil Power":** Robert Deakin insists the Temple was truly destroyed at the Battle of Emiry Meadows and that talk of a powerful evil is false. Other factions (Jaroo, Burne, Rufus) appear to believe otherwise. This contradiction is unresolved.
- **Zephyr's Appearance:** Zephyr's "unique appearance" is referenced as a reason to avoid drawing attention; the nature of this appearance has not been specified.
- **The Elemental Factions' Conflict:** The party has identified that multiple elemental factions are in conflict; the scope, membership, and goals of these factions beyond the Black Earth cultists and Lareth's group are unknown.
