- The following morning, Burne and Rufus approached the party, confirmed the rangers had been sent after the chest, and urged the party to deal with the Moathouse.
- The party traveled to the Moathouse and killed two gnoll guards at the secret entrance.
- The party delivered severed gnoll heads to Lareth's guard as a dramatic flourish upon entering.
- Lareth greeted the party with suspicion, having heard rumors of deaths in the Earth Temple.

- Lareth accused the party of treachery and failure.
- Zephyr mocked Lareth, stating "The only person here that's backstabbing is you."
- Lareth called his guards to arms, initiating combat; the session ended as the fight was about to begin.
