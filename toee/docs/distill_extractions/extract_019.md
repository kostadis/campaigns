## World Events

- The party was betrayed by Captain Tolub, who directed them into a trap at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon (fire temple) instead of arranging a meeting with Romag of the Earth Temple.
- Fire priests Albreth and Ingulf attacked the party with magical flames, nearly killing Calmer.
- Zephyr used magical dust to allow the party to escape into the darkness.
- The party hid in the wilderness hills and debated their next move.
- Zinnia found a concealed cave for the party to rest in and obscured their tracks.
- During the night, patrols passed the party's hiding spot without discovering them.
- The party encountered a pit of poisonous snakes hidden beneath soft ground and avoided it.
- The party spotted gnolls in fire temple garb patrolling the forest and successfully hid from them.
- The party observed a bandit chief leading gnoll recruits through the forest; Sequioa overheard the chief complaining about recruiting gnolls from the local gnoll king for the fire temple.
- The party arrived at the Boatman's Tavern in Nulb; Skole revealed Tolub had declared the party dead and directed them to Dick Rentsch.
- The party passed Gallows Table and observed Elfar Twell hanging, executed on Madame Selentis's orders.
- The party visited Magda's Trove and Garik's armory in Nulb.
- The party arrived at the Waterside Hostel and met Dick Rentsch, who took them to his back office.
- Wat the bartender attempted to probe the party for information about their business and mentioned a person named Dala.
- Dick Rentsch returned that evening and tasked the party with ambushing gnoll recruits and their handlers destined for the fire temple.
- The party ambushed the bandit chief and his gnoll recruits in the forest; Calmer used a booming voice to freeze the gnolls in fear.
- Sequioa and Zinnia killed the bandit chief before he could react.
- Zephyr systematically eliminated the gnolls.
- Sequioa cut one gnoll in half with a single strike.
- The last surviving bandit made a futile attack and was struck down.
- The party killed all targets and completed Dick Rentsch's test, earning entry into the Earth Temple's confidence.
