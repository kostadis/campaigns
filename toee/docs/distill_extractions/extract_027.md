- **Kelno and the Air Temple**: Dismissed as irrelevant by Hartsch, but the prophet remains alive and present somewhere in the temple complex. Whether Kelno or the Air Temple will become relevant is unresolved.
- **Hartsch's true loyalty**: Hartsch has signaled desire for power and a more aggressive leadership style, but his ultimate reliability as a post-Romag ally for the party has not been tested or confirmed.
- **Battle of Emridy Meadows**: Referenced as a catastrophic defeat for the temples a decade prior that broke Alrrem's power. Details of the battle and its full consequences have not been elaborated upon.
