## World Events

- The party encountered Brother Eldin's stone guardian in an ancient library within the monastery; the guardian presented a riddle about eternal cycles of nature testing knowledge of Beory's old faith.
- Zephyr answered the riddle correctly, identifying the answer as the water cycle; the guardian declared the party worthy, became inanimate, and opened a passage deeper into the monastery.
- The party overheard cultists discussing Tharok's impending ritual in the stone corridors.
- Sequoia and Zephyr silently killed the cultist guards and recovered a key from their bodies.
- The key unlocked a prison chamber where Jerry and Warda were being held captive for torture.
- Jerry revealed knowledge of a secret passageway to the deeper temple, inherited from his grandmother who had worshipped Beory in the monastery.
- Both Jerry and Warda agreed to assist the party against the cultists.
- The party followed the secret passageway into a vast ritual chamber containing the Chest of Rao on a cracked altar, with Tharok and three kneeling cultists conducting a ritual.
- Tharok declared the party defilers and initiated combat.
- Zinnia, Sequoia, and Zephyr dispatched the three cultist guards swiftly.
- Jerry charged Tharok and was struck down and killed by Tharok's attacks.
- Tharok's earth magic caused the chamber to quake violently, knocking several party members to the ground and disrupting Calmer's blessing.
- Sequoia knocked the Chest of Rao from the altar, disrupting the ritual and causing magical energies in the chamber to begin dissipating.
- Zephyr landed a critical blow on Tharok.
- Tharok struck Warda for 29 damage and hurled Zephyr backward with a devastating blow.
- Calmer cast Guiding Bolt on Tharok, dealing 9 damage and granting Zinnia advantage.
- Zinnia landed a flurry of attacks dealing 14 damage total, killing High Cultist Tharok.
- Upon Tharok's death, the ritual cavern began violently collapsing.
- The party secured the Chest of Rao and fled through the secret passageway.
- The party returned to the main abbey, where damage was miraculously contained to the ritual cavern.
- Brother Eldin was found alive but wounded; he declared the Abbey of Beory safe.
- Brother Eldin rewarded the party with 70 gold pieces, 400 silver pieces, and 400 copper pieces each, plus a Scroll of Web and a Potion of Greater Healing.
- Brother Eldin provided lore on the Chest of Rao and urged the party to return it to the Archclericy of Veluna, warning it would attract dangerous forces.
- The party chose to use the Chest of Rao as leverage against Temple agents Rannos and Gremag in Hommlet rather than return it.
- The party sent a note to Rannos and Gremag requesting a meeting behind the carpenter's field.
- Rannos refused the meeting location but invited the party to his establishment after dark.
- The party waited and planned at the Inn of the Welcome Wench; Calmer purchased gnomish mead.
- Under cover of night, the party lured Thorne (Duke) out from the back of Rannos's shop and launched a surprise attack.
- Zephyr and Zinnia struck Thorne; Sequoia delivered the final blow, knocking Thorne unconscious.
- Interrogation of Thorne confirmed he was a hired mercenary with no deep cult ties.
- The party entered the inner office to confront Rannos and Gremag, using the unconscious Thorne.
- Rannos dismissed Thorne as useless and demanded the Chest of Rao; the party attempted to negotiate for the Temple to leave the region.
- Rannos and Gremag mocked the negotiation; Gremag immediately attacked Zinnia.
- Zinnia and Zephyr wounded Gremag severely; Gremag retaliated, severely injuring Zinnia and then knocking her unconscious.
- Sequoia dealt 25 damage to Gremag.
- Calmer landed a critical blow killing Gremag.
- Sequoia struck Rannos for 26 damage; the session ended with Rannos severely wounded but alive.
