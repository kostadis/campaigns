## World Events

- Party arrived in Nulb and was warned by local guards at the Drowned Cottage about the town's dangerous nature.
- Party observed signposts from Constable Eldrin Malavar warning about a condemned temple south of Nulb and declaring the Pilgrim's Trail off-limits.
- Party visited the Boatman's Tavern and met Skole and Ketta.
- Party traveled to the Rhennee encampment and met Eryk Velkori, who was grieving over his missing sister Serena.
- Eryk revealed that Serena had vanished after a night at the Boatman's Tavern and suspected she had been taken by the Zelrads, the Reapers, or Nisera's Talons through the Iron Lash.
- Eryk provided intelligence on Nulb's criminal underworld, naming Zelt the Rat (Crimson Reapers) and Madame Selentis / Nisera as major powers.
- Tala Velkori invited the party to meet her grandmother Vethra for a divination.
- Vethra performed a smoke reading, revealing visions of a crooked house with shifting shadows, a tavern called the Waterside with too many secrets, and a man with a red sash who dealt in whispers and iron.
- Vethra performed a bone reading using wolf knuckles and pigeon vertebrae, revealing that Serena was not broken.
- Vethra drew four Tarokka cards despite Eryk's protests: The Chain, The Betrayer, The Flooded Gate, and a fourth card depicting a crimson-painted barge sailing low on the Velverdyva, heavy with souls — confirming Serena had been taken there bound and traded.
- Vethra collapsed from exhaustion after the divination.
- Party returned to the Boatman's Tavern in the late evening and observed a gentleman with guards, a ship captain with river pirates, and a watchful old woman.
- Ketta nervously confirmed a fight involving Serena and the Rhennee but pointed fearfully at Captain Tolub rather than elaborate.
- Party approached Captain Tolub, who revealed Serena had been taken to the Iron Lash as payment for something.
- Tolub informed the party that the Iron Lash was located upstairs in the Boatman's Tavern.
- Party ascended to the Iron Lash and were directed to Elvanna's chambers by a large guard with an iron cudgel.
- Party met Elvanna, who demanded 500 gold pieces for Serena's release as compensation for damages caused by the Rhennee.
- Party paid 500 gold pieces without negotiation; Serena was released from the pits.
- Serena revealed she had been used as leverage for a debt she did not know about, and that her father Eryk was being coerced into conducting ambushes along the forest road.
- Party escorted Serena back to the Rhennee encampment; Eryk and the clan erupted in celebration.
- Eryk formally declared the party Rhennee clan members and offered protection and friendship; warned them not to trust all their friends.
- Party discovered they had been credited 500 gold pieces — the same amount paid for Serena's rescue.
- Party returned to find Captain Tolub at his boat and asked him how to contact Romag or other higher powers of the area.
- Tolub directed the party to the Hall of the Scarlet Moon, a day's hike northeast of Nulb in the Kron Hills.
- Eryk described the Hall as a place where druid Elazar taught the rite of the wicker giant; noted unusually constant burning fires there.
- Party traveled south through Hommlet and spoke with Ostler Goodingoot, who directed them to Jaroo.
- Jaroo expressed suspicion that the Circle of the Scarlet Moon were too showy and ecstatic for true druids, suggesting their practices were more religious than natural.
- Party continued past Shernobb into the Kron Hills and arrived at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon.
- Party observed a square tower ringed by ruined walls, a massive bonfire shaped like a fiery giant, and seven smaller bonfires with clustered tents.
- Party visited Camp Number One, where druids Verigo, Mahoon, Iniri, and Faria were friendly and offered hospitality; expressed excitement about the rite of the wicker giant.
- Party moved to Camp Number Two and questioned Albrith and Ingulf about their connection to the Temple of Elemental Evil.
- Albrith and Ingulf confirmed their elemental allegiance with the phrase "fire, water, earth, or air" and adopted threatening poses.
- Party realized Captain Tolub had deliberately sent them to the Fire Temple rather than to the Earth Temple and Romag.
- Albrith and Ingulf unleashed explosive fire magic on the party, severely wounding them and knocking Calmer unconscious.
- Zephyr used magical dust to render the entire party invisible.
- Sequioa grabbed the unconscious Calmer while the others scattered.
- Zephyr cut the rope of a leashed bear, releasing it to attack the fire priests as a diversion.
- The party escaped into the wilderness, invisible, while the fire clerics were distracted by the rampaging bear.
