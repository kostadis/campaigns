## CURRENT SITUATION

### The Temple of Elemental Evil — Active Operations

The party is currently inside the Temple of Elemental Evil, operating as the Obsidian Edge (Hartsch's vanguard). They have engineered a three-way war between the Earth, Fire, and Water Temples.

**Last known position:** Second dungeon level; party has been summoned upstairs to an Earth Temple war council by Hartsch.

**Immediate unresolved situation:**
- Hartsch's war council is pending; party must respond to his runner
- Four prisoners remain chained in the prisoner chamber on the second level; Sequoia was freeing them
- Hidden passage to the Deep/Greater Temple has been located and marked on the party's map (inside Air Temple territory); not yet accessed
- A wraith in the Air Temple corridor remains active; declared "one of you is going to die today" and has not been destroyed
- An active necromancer is operating somewhere in the dungeon (Earth Temple corpses raised as ghouls during the war)
- A self-opening door in the Hall of Trophies was observed watching the party; entity/purpose unknown

**Temple War Status:**
- Earth Temple (Hartsch): Advancing; Fire Temple barricade broken; Crimson Guard engaged
- Fire Temple (Alrrem): Fighting on two fronts — against Earth Temple and Water Temple simultaneously; financially ruined by Chimera purchase; Alrrem described as nearly broken
- Water Temple (Belsornig): Under Fire Temple assault; status uncertain
- Air Temple (Kelno): Exploiting the war; looting battlefield via hidden passage; goblins collecting coin, ghouls collecting weapons; effectively a fourth opportunistic faction

## FACTIONS — CURRENT STATE

### Temple of Elemental Evil (Overarching)
- The Deep Temple sits above the four elemental sub-temples; its leadership, nature, and goals are entirely unknown
- The Crimson Reapers appear to serve this deeper organisation — not any of the four elemental temples directly
- The Circle of Eight sealed the Temple's main entrance and blocked access to a central driving power (the Orb) after the Battle of Emridy Meadows
- The four elemental temples are now in open war with each other (engineered by the party)

### Earth Temple
- **Leader:** Hartsch (self-declared Supreme Prophet; Romag assassinated)
- **Territory:** Upper dungeon level + newly claimed gnoll/bugbear area; Broken Blades holding claimed territory
- **Military:** Earth elementals, massed low-level troops, Crimson Guard (Dren); lacks powerful monsters (unlike other temples)
- **Current action:** Full-scale assault on the second level; advance stalled pending Obsidian Edge war council
- **Key vulnerability:** Romag's predecessor defected to the Deep Temple; local undead stopped obeying the Earth Temple after that defection

### Fire Temple
- **Leader:** Alrrem (alive; severely weakened; fighting on two fronts)
- **Status:** Financially overextended; lost werewolves, lost Chimera; now simultaneously fighting Earth Temple and Water Temple
- **Key agent:** Captain Tolub (Nulb; river pirates); Tolub believes party is dead

### Water Temple
- **Leader:** Belsornig (cunning manipulator; alive; under attack from Fire Temple)
- **Key agent:** Vesrek (operative; arranged Chimera sale)
- **Status:** Now at war with Fire Temple (triggered by party's manipulation of Alrrem); relationship to Earth Temple openly hostile

### Air Temple
- **Leader:** Kelno (prophet/cleric; previously dismissed as irrelevant)
- **Status:** Reduced to a handful of rooms; however, now actively exploiting the war — looting battlefield via hidden passage; possesses knowledge of the Deep Temple entrance
- **Key deception:** Kelno mistakes the Air Temple's wraith for an air elemental

### Council of Hommlet
- **Members:** Elder Kenter (head), Burne, Rufus, Jaroo, Ostler Goodingoat, Captain Zeb Fletcher, Mytch the Miller
- **Status:** Alerted to Temple resurgence; militia being prepared; party is their field operation; Burne and Rufus have not escalated to the Archbishop of Verbobonc
- **Internal tension:** Each member has separately demanded the party report exclusively to them

### Verbobonc (State and Church)
- **Goals:** Extend political and religious influence over Hommlet; recover the Chest of Rao; cannot send troops to the Kron Hills area without risking conflict with gnomes
- **Key agents:** Elmo (field agent; rangers chasing false trail), Burne, Rufus, Terjon
- **Constraint:** The gnome-Verbobonc territorial dispute over gem mines prevents open military intervention

### Rhennee (Velkori Clan)
- **Status:** Allied with party; party formally adopted as clan members
- **Obligation:** Eryk being coerced into forest road ambushes over an unresolved debt — party knows but has not addressed this

### Nisera's Talons
- **Leader:** Madame Selentis (Nisera); disgraced Verbobonc noble family; true power in Nulb
- **Status:** Active; enforces authority through public execution; relationship to Temple unknown

### Crimson Reapers
- **Leader:** Zelt the Rat
- **Status:** Active in Nulb; appear to serve the Deep Temple organisation rather than any elemental sub-temple; stole a weapons shipment from the Broken Blades (claiming it was temple property)

### Gnomes of Kron Hills / Sheernobb
- **Status:** Facing bandit attacks (Black Earth Cult, now destroyed) and a giant threat (unaddressed); politically in tension with Verbobonc over gem mines
- **Council:** Glennddarc (Headgnome); Captain Gnonsbim (Guard)

### Old Faith / Beory
- **Key figures:** Jaroo (Hommlet), Brother Eldin (Abbey of Beory, Gallows Corner), Pasmarie Clee (Sheernobb)
- **Status:** Active; cautious about Verbobonc absorption of their faithful

### Church of St. Cuthbert (Hommlet)
- **Leader:** Prefect Terjon (suspicious figure; suspected in Y'dey's disappearance)
- **Key member:** Calmer (party member; affiliated)
- **Policy:** Will withhold healing from non-followers (per Jaroo's warning)
