# Jorin Pike Kale

## Identity
- Role: River sailor, former prisoner
- Faction: None
- First appearance: Chapter 21 — discovered as one of seven prisoners in a locked chamber within Earth Temple territory

## Personality & Motivations
- Motivated by survival and gratitude; shared useful intelligence unprompted as a way of repaying the party for his freedom.
- Practical and knowledgeable about river trade and dock operations.

## History with the Party
- **Chapter 21:** Found imprisoned by Dick Rentsch's men as punishment for making a pass at a woman named Dalia. The party freed him and staged his escape to look like the work of the Fire Temple. In gratitude, he volunteered intelligence about Captain Tolubb's pirate ship schedule at the docks in Nulb, noting Tolubb had already betrayed the party by directing them into a Fire Temple ambush.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Unknown — fled following the staged escape.
- **Active plans:** Presumably returning to life as a river sailor.
- **What the party knows:** He was freed and gave them Tolubb's dock schedule.
- **What remains hidden:** His current whereabouts; whether he stays quiet about being rescued by the party.

## Relationships
- **Dick Rentsch:** Imprisoned Jorin as punishment — antagonistic.
- **Dalia:** The woman whose attention led to his imprisonment; relationship nature unclear.
- **Captain Tolubb:** Aware of Tolubb's schedule and operations at Nulb docks; no direct relationship noted.
- **The Party:** Grateful; cooperative during single interaction.
