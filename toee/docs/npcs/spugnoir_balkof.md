# Spugnoir Balkof

## Identity
- **Role:** Traveling magic user; currently an attached companion to the party
- **Faction:** None confirmed; aspires to connect with the Circle of Eight
- **First Appearance:** Session 1 — encountered at the Inn of the Welcome Wench, Hommlet
- *Also known as: Spugnoir.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Accumulate arcane knowledge and magical resources; ultimately seek out and meet the Circle of Eight
- **Secondary Goal:** Be included — he has a notable pattern of expecting to join the party and being left behind
- Eager, talkative, and lore-hungry; he opens up freely on the march and tends toward excitement when arcane finds are involved
- Practical about arrangements — negotiated a clear treasure-split before agreeing to guide the party, with first claim on arcane scrolls

## History with the Party
1. **Session 1 onward:** Staying at the Inn of the Welcome Wench; present when the party formed but left behind on at least two occasions, including the mission to return the silver dragon Airgid
2. **Recruitment:** When the party asked about a rumored passage under the Inn, he dismissed it and counter-offered: a guide's role to a lost wizard's laboratory beneath Emiry Meadows, in exchange for a treasure split and first claim on scrolls. Sequoia agreed
3. **March to Emiry Meadows:** Provided extensive lore on the Circle of Eight (Mordenkainen, Bigby, Otiluke, Leomund, and others) — their appearance at the Battle of Emiry Meadows, subsequent binding work at the Temple of Elemental Evil, and their operating philosophy of balance rather than good or evil
4. **At the Laboratory:** Visibly thrilled by the haul. Claimed the spellbook and scroll per agreement; declared the new spells would make him significantly more useful to the party
5. **Planting 19:** Eager and ready to depart; told to wait during the council meeting, then joined the party afterward

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Traveling with the party out of Hommlet (as of Planting 19)
- **Active Plans:** Integrated into the party as a fourth companion; intends to continue adventuring alongside them
- **What the Party Knows:** His treasure deal terms; his Circle of Eight obsession; his new spell arsenal
- **What Remains Hidden:** Whether he has any deeper agenda beyond personal advancement and meeting the Circle of Eight; the extent of his actual combat capability before acquiring the new spellbook

## Relationships
- **Sequoia:** Made the formal agreement with her to join as fourth companion
- **The Party (general):** Eager to prove his worth; twice stung by being excluded — may be sensitive to any further being passed over
- **The Circle of Eight:** Personal fixation; views them with admiration and ambition
- **No known antagonistic NPC relationships on record**
