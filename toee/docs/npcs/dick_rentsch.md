# Dick Rentsch

## Identity
- **Role:** Proprietor of the Waterside Hostel in Nulb; recruiter and field contact for the Earth Temple
- **Faction:** Earth Temple (Temple of Elemental Evil)
- **First Appearance:** Party encountered him at the Waterside Hostel in Nulb when seeking a contact for the Earth Temple; he vetted them with suspicion before engaging

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Advance the Earth Temple's dominance over the other elemental factions; recruit capable personnel and resources for Romag
- Gruff and transactional on the surface, but becomes animated when discussing the Earth Temple's eventual supremacy — he called the Temple his "happy place" and envisions it filled with supplicants once the other factions are destroyed
- Pragmatic to a fault: maintains working relationships across factional lines (e.g., keeping Water Temple loyalist Wat on payroll) and views current inter-temple cooperation as a necessary step toward total Earth Temple control
- Has a calculating streak — he wagered the party wouldn't survive the werewolf encounter, suggesting he tests recruits ruthlessly and doesn't become attached

## History with the Party
1. **First meeting (Nulb):** Sized up the party at the Waterside Hostel. When they mentioned the Earth Temple, his manner shifted. Assigned them a test mission: ambush gnolls being recruited for the Fire Temple.
2. **Temple escort:** After the party completed the gnoll mission, led them along a weed-choked road to the Temple of Elemental Evil, guided them through a side entrance, down a pit corridor past skeleton-filled passages, and through Earth Temple guards. Asked whether he needed to confiscate their weapons. Presented them to Romag as new recruits.
3. **Map and the werewolf trap:** Provided the party a dungeon map marking gnoll locations — without disclosing that Alremm had planted werewolves in a secret chamber as a recruits' test. When the party survived and returned, he was found sitting beside Romag; Romag revealed Dick had wagered against their survival. When confronted, Dick admitted the werewolves were Alremm's deliberate test.
4. **Broken Blades recruitment (Ch. 20):** Sent word to Romag identifying the Broken Blades mercenaries in Nulb as an acquisition opportunity. Met the party in his back room, laid out three options for recruiting Captain Dren Halveth (outbid the Water Temple, beat him in a fist fight, or complete a task), and arranged the meeting.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Back room of the Waterside Hostel, Nulb
- **Active Operations:** Recruiting personnel and resources for the Earth Temple from Nulb; managing relationships with figures of ambiguous loyalty (e.g., Wat the barkeep)
- **What the Party Knows:** He is an Earth Temple operative and recruiter; he withheld information about the werewolf test; he wagered on their deaths
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full extent of his network in Nulb; whether depositing the party in Air Temple territory was deliberate sabotage or incidental remains unresolved

## Relationships
- **Romag:** Direct superior within the Earth Temple; Dick reports to him and brings recruits before him; they have a casual enough rapport to make wagers together
- **Alremm:** Coordinated with Alremm on the werewolf test for new recruits
- **Wat (barkeep):** Keeps him on payroll despite suspected Water Temple loyalty; views it as pragmatic until the Earth Temple consolidates power
- **Dren Halveth:** Brokered the party's access to the Broken Blades mercenary captain
- **The Party:** Recruited them, tested them covertly, and wagered against their survival — relationship is functional but carries an undercurrent of mistrust on both sides

## Arc Score Events
- **Decrease:** Revealed to have withheld information about the werewolf test and wagered the party would die — party learned he does not act fully in their interest
