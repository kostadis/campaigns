# Hestort Flintshine

## Identity
- **Role:** Freelance informant / map copyist; low-level operative; worked as a paid agent of the Duke
- **Faction:** No formal affiliation; claimed loose connection to Terjon (party doubted); operates opportunistically
- **First Appearance:** Planting 18, 579 — spotted by Zephyr at the workers' encampment near the fortress (moathouse); confronted and fled
- *Also known as: Tort, Hestort.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core goal:** Self-preservation above all else; motivated by coin rather than loyalty
- Skittish, frightened, and easily broken — fled immediately when Zephyr confronted him, cried under interrogation, and ultimately ran rather than cooperate further
- Cooperates under pressure but claims limited knowledge, making it difficult to gauge how much he's actually withholding
- Unlikely to seek confrontation or revenge — more likely to disappear entirely

## History with the Party
1. **Workers' Encampment (Planting 18, 579):** Zephyr identified Tort as the person who copied maps of the moathouse and passed them to the Duke. When confronted, he panicked and fled toward the river.
2. **Shot by Bandits:** His own allies shot him during the flight. The party intervened and saved his life with a healing potion.
3. **Interrogation:** Under Zephyr's intimidation (including a fear-inducing magical effect — severe enough to cause Tort to urinate himself), Tort revealed:
   - He had been hired by the Duke to obtain the moathouse plans
   - He claimed to work for Terjon, which the party doubted
   - The Duke was involved with "some shady people"
   - The mastermind was someone in the village — but he could not (or would not) name them
4. **Departure:** Broke down in tears, declared he was done and would never be seen again, and fled. Released by the party.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Near the river, outside the workers' encampment; whereabouts now unknown
- **Active Plans:** Claims to be gone for good
- **What the Party Knows:** He copied and delivered moathouse maps to the Duke for pay; the Duke has an unknown employer somewhere in the village; Tort claims connection to Terjon
- **What Remains Hidden:** Whether he truly doesn't know the identity of the village contact or is protecting that information; the truth of his Terjon claim; whether he actually disappears or resurfaces

## Relationships
- **The Duke:** Employer; paid Tort to obtain the moathouse plans. Tort treats this as a transactional relationship, not a loyal one.
- **Terjon:** Claimed affiliation — party doubts its authenticity
- **Bandits (unnamed):** Loosely affiliated; they shot him on sight during his flight, suggesting the relationship was already compromised or expendable
- **Unknown Village Contact:** Claims no knowledge of this person, who sits above the Duke in the conspiracy
- **The Party:** Owes them his life (healing potion); showed no gratitude — left as soon as he could
- **Zephyr (party):** Subjected Tort to magical intimidation; the primary interrogator — Tort fled largely as a result of this encounter
