# Elmo

## Identity
- **Role:** Ranger, member of Hommlet's town committee, covert agent for Verbobonc
- **Faction:** Verbobonc (covert); Hommlet town committee
- **First Appearance:** Seated at the Inn of the Welcome Wench during the Furnok confrontation. The party initially knew him as a local drunk.

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Monitor threats to Hommlet and the region on behalf of Verbobonc; contain whatever evil presence remains at the Moathouse.
- Elmo maintains a convincing drunken buffoon persona as cover for his intelligence work. Once exposed, he dropped the act immediately and engaged the party as competent equals. He is action-oriented and visibly shaken by serious threats — his dismay at news of the chest departing with Rannos and Gremag was unguarded and genuine.

## History with the Party
1. **First encounter:** Present at the Inn of the Welcome Wench during the Furnok confrontation. Intervened by threatening to fetch Captain Zeb Fletcher, pressuring Furnok to leave. Appeared heavily intoxicated. Sequoia identified him as a drunk.
2. **Volunteered as guide:** When Zephyr mentioned needing help finding a monster in the Gnarley Forest, Elmo enthusiastically offered his services for hire.
3. **Exposed as an agent:** Zinnia's natural 20 Insight roll confirmed suspicions that his inebriation was an act. When Zephyr confronted him, he dropped the facade and agreed to speak privately with the party.
4. **Urgent tasking:** Upon learning the chest had left with Rannos and Gremag, he rushed off to alert the rangers to pursue it. Before leaving, he tasked the party with returning to the Moathouse immediately to investigate and destroy whatever evil remained there, citing political constraints that prevent Verbobonc from sending official troops.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Departed the Inn of the Welcome Wench to contact rangers and pursue the chest taken by Rannos and Gremag.
- **Active Operations:** Coordinating ranger pursuit of Rannos, Gremag, and the chest; monitoring the broader threat to the region.
- **What the Party Knows:** He is a covert Verbobonc agent, son of Captain Zeb Fletcher, and a capable ranger beneath the drunk act. He has officially tasked them with clearing the Moathouse.
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full scope of his intelligence network, what the chest contains, and the nature of his relationship with Verbobonc authorities.

## Relationships
- **Captain Zeb Fletcher:** His father; Elmo used the threat of fetching him to defuse the Furnok situation.
- **Verbobonc:** Operates as their covert agent in Hommlet.
- **Rannos & Gremag:** Suspects of concern — their departure with the chest prompted an immediate, alarmed response.
- **The Party:** Shifted from passing acquaintance to collaborative contact once his cover was blown. Has entrusted them with a sensitive mission.
- **Gnomes of the Kron Hills:** A political constraint — open Verbobonc military action risks conflict with them, which is why the party is needed as proxies.
