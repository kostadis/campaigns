# Glennon Clee

## Identity
- **Role:** Owner / proprietor of the Honey Haven Inn, Sheernobb (also referenced as "Honey Tavern Inn"); also handles the bar
- **Faction:** None noted; likely related to Mari Clee
- **First Appearance:** Planting 21, 579 — commissioned the party to recover stolen enchanted mead barrels at the Honey Haven Inn
- *Also known as: Glannon, Glannon Clee, Glennon.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goal:** Recover his stolen enchanted mead and maintain his inn's stock
- Comes across as a straightforward businessman — offers fair, practical rewards for practical work
- Genuinely grateful when things go his way professionally; can be awkward in his phrasing but self-aware enough to correct himself quickly — not malicious, just occasionally fumbles his words

## History with the Party
- **Planting 21, 579:** Hired the party to recover barrels of enchanted mead stolen in bandit raids; offered one week of free lodging and a small keg of enchanted mead as payment
- **Planting 22, 579 (evening):** Zinnia returned with a recovered barrel. Glennon expressed surprise and relief, then delivered the reward — a keg of magical mead. He stumbled over the explanation before clarifying its effects: drinking a mug enhances Charisma, improves persuasiveness, attracts romantic interest, aids merchant negotiations, and boosts bardic performance tips.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Honey Haven Inn, Sheernobb
- **Active Plans:** None noted beyond running the inn and recovering his stock
- **Known to Party:** Reward fulfilled; mead properties disclosed; he stocks magical mead with Charisma-enhancing properties
- **Hidden from Party:** Origins of the barrel beyond the bandit raid; whether the magical mead is a regular commodity or a rarity

## Relationships
- **Mari Clee:** Presumed relation; likely co-owner of the Honey Haven Inn
- **Zinnia:** Grateful; rewarded her for returning the barrel
- **The Party:** Employer; contract fulfilled on good terms; potential future contact for lodging or mead supply
