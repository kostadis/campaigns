# Aunt Emma

## Identity
- Green hag; no known faction affiliation
- Encountered on Planting 22 in a fertile glade within the Gnarley Forest, while the party was searching for a wild boar
- *Also known as: Aunt Emma the Green Hag, The Green Hag.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Predatory and deceptive; lured victims with the guise of a harmless old woman offering sweets and hospitality
- Willing to bargain for survival when cornered, but her offer came too late to sway the party
- Implied habitual predation on children, evidenced by her cookbook and poisoned food

## History with the Party
- Approached the party in the form of an elderly woman in a brown dress, green woolen shirt, and leather belt, offering sweets and directing them to her gingerbread house
- Inside the house, she offered a plum pie that was poisoned
- Zinnia identified her as a hag before the deception fully took hold; when Zephyr drew his sword, her illusory human appearance dropped, revealing green skin, clawed hands, and sharp teeth
- She threatened to eat the party, then pleaded for her life in exchange for revealing a hidden treasure
- Zephyr refused and killed her

## Current Status
- **Dead**, killed by Zephyr in her gingerbread house in the Gnarley Forest
- Loot recovered: a Hat of Wizardry and a book titled *The Hag's Guide to 101 Recipes Of Children*
- The gingerbread on the door frame was confirmed by Zinnia to be genuine

## Relationships
- **Zinnia** — Zinnia was the first to see through her disguise, triggering the confrontation
- **Zephyr** — Delivered the killing blow; rejected her last-ditch bargain outright
