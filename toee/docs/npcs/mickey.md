# Mickey Tamen

## Identity
- Son of Virgil Tamen, the Braumeister of Hommlet
- No faction affiliation noted
- First appearance: The Inn of the Welcome Wench, where he approached the party seeking help

## Personality & Motivations
- Motivated by concern for his father's brewery and anxiety about temple spies amid village rumors
- Demonstrated as visibly nervous but relieved when the party agreed to help; cautious and worried by nature

## History with the Party
- Approached the party at the Inn of the Welcome Wench and asked them to investigate two cloaked figures seen lurking behind the brewery around midnight
- The figures turned out to be teenagers Jira and Marek, meeting secretly due to their parents' religious differences — not temple spies

## Current Status
- Last known location: The Inn of the Welcome Wench
- No active plans noted following the resolution of his request
- **Party knows:** The figures were harmless teenagers; his concern was unfounded
- **Hidden from party:** Nothing noted

## Relationships
- **Virgil Tamen (father):** The Braumeister of Hommlet; Mickey's concern centered on protecting the brewery
