# Urot

## Identity
- **Role:** Cook employed in the kitchen of the Abbey of Allitur
- **Faction:** Bandit operation headquartered in the Abbey (lower levels)
- **First Appearance:** Cornered by the party during their stealth assault on the Abbey's lower levels

## Personality & Motivations
- Motivated primarily by self-preservation — broke quickly under pressure.
- Demonstrated fear and compliance rather than loyalty to the bandit operation; no indication of ideological commitment to the Temple of Evil.

## History with the Party
- Cornered in the Abbey kitchen following the party's stealth assault on the lower levels.
- Terrified into confession by the party's efficiency; willingly gave up intelligence to survive.
- Revealed that bandit leader **Snej** is not a mere outlaw but an operative receiving resources from **Canon Belsornig**, a figure she described as a "big shot" within the **Temple of Evil**.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Abbey kitchen
- **Status:** Unclear following her confession — fate undetermined
- **Active plans:** None known

## What the Party Knows vs. What Remains Hidden
- **Knows:** Her testimony linking Snej to Canon Belsornig and the Temple of Evil
- **Hidden:** Her current fate; whether she poses any further risk or use to the party

## Relationships
- **Snej** — employer; Urot was aware of his true affiliation with the Temple of Evil
- **Canon Belsornig** — known to Urot by reputation as a powerful Temple figure; no direct relationship implied
- **The Party** — source of her terror; cooperated fully under duress
