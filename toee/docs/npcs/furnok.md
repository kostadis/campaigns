# Furnok

## Identity
- **Role:** Roguish gambler, suspected thief
- **Affiliation:** None known
- **First Appearance:** Inn of the Welcome Wench; encountered as a patron suspected by Ostler of stealing from other guests

## Personality & Motivations
- Operates through charm and misdirection — uses loaded dice to cheat at games, likely steals from marks on the side.
- Brazenly denied Ostler's accusations when confronted, showing confidence (or practiced deflection) under pressure.
- Backed down only when physically threatened by Elmo, suggesting he avoids direct confrontation when the odds turn against him.

## History with the Party
- Zephyr attempted to pickpocket Furnok directly and failed.
- Zephyr then created a distraction by tipping over Furnok's table; Zinia used the cover to recover Furnok's loaded dice.
- The dice were presented to Ostler as proof of cheating.
- When confronted, Furnok denied everything. Elmo intervened and threatened to summon the Captain, at which point Furnok was pressured into leaving the inn.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Expelled or pressured out of the Inn of the Welcome Wench.
- **Active Plans:** Unknown — likely seeking a new venue to run his cons.
- **What the Party Knows:** He cheats at dice and was suspected of theft; he was run out of the inn.
- **What Remains Hidden:** Whether he actually stole from guests, where he went after leaving, and whether he noticed the party's role in exposing him.

## Relationships
- **Ostler:** Hostile — Ostler suspected and publicly accused him.
- **Elmo:** Hostile — Elmo's threat was the final force that removed him.
- **Zephyr & Zinia:** Furnok may not know they were responsible for the dice recovery; relationship unknown from his perspective.
