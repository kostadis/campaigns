# Serena Velkori

## Identity
- **Role:** Member of the Rhennee clan; sister of Eryk Velkori
- **First Appearance:** The party encountered her situation through Eryk; they physically retrieved her from the Iron Lash brothel (located above the Boatman's Tavern), where she was being held by Elvanna as compensation for damages caused by the Rhennee
- *Also known as: Serena.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Defiant despite captivity — retorted sharply to Elvanna even while being led out of the pits
- Core drive appears to be loyalty to her people and family, though she was kept ignorant of the debt that caused her captivity
- Likely motivated to see her family free from Elvanna's coercion

## History with the Party
- Was held captive at the Iron Lash brothel, used as leverage for a Rhennee debt owed to Elvanna
- The party paid 500 gp to secure her release
- Upon return to the Rhennee encampment, she revealed that her father was being coerced into conducting ambushes along the forest road as part of the same debt arrangement — something she had not previously known about

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Rhennee encampment near Nulb
- Her father is actively being coerced into orchestrating ambushes along the forest road
- **Known to Party:** Her captivity, the debt, and her father's forced involvement in the ambushes
- **Hidden:** Full scope of the Rhennee's entanglement with Elvanna and whether the debt has been truly settled

## Relationships
- **Eryk Velkori (brother):** Her rescue was initiated through him
- **Elvanna:** Former captor; clear mutual hostility
- **Her father:** Being coerced by Elvanna; conducting ambushes under duress
- **The Party:** Owes them her freedom; potentially friendly
