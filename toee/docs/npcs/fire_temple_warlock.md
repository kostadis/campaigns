# Fire Temple Warlock *(unnamed)*

## Identity
- **Role:** Servant of Alrrem; handler/commander of the Fire Temple's chimera
- **Faction:** Fire Temple (devotee of Alrrem the All-Consuming)
- **First Appearance:** Interrupted the party's attempted long rest, smashing through their southern door while leading the chimera on a chain

## Personality & Motivations
- **Goals:** Carried out Alrrem's will directly — his mission was to deliver the chimera against the party and destroy them utterly.
- Theatrical and zealous; announced his arrival with a declaration that Alrrem had sent his champion to "break their bones and leave nothing but meat for the earth."
- Fell apart quickly under pressure — once Zephyr ripped the control chain from his hands, he devolved into screaming terror.

## History with the Party
1. Burst through the southern door with the chimera chained, declaring Alrrem's intent to destroy the party.
2. Used Eldritch Blast against Sequioa and maintained command of the chimera until Zephyr seized the control chain.
3. Lost control of the chimera; was caught alongside a bugbear ally in the creature's own breath attack, badly burning both.
4. Barely standing and burned, screamed that the party were "all mad."
5. Silenced permanently by Zinnia with a flurry of blows.

## Current Status
- **Dead.** Killed during the chimera encounter.
- **Items recovered from his body:**
  - Wax-sealed ledger bearing the crest of the **Crimson Trader**
  - Heavy gold amulet with the eight-pointed symbol of the Fire Temple (worth 100 gp)
  - Potion of fire resistance *(never used)*
  - Masterwork whip of alchemically treated red leather

## Relationships
- **Alrrem:** Direct servant; acted as handler for Alrrem's prized chimera.
- **The Chimera:** Controlled via chain — lost that control the moment Zephyr intervened.
- **The Crimson Trader:** Unknown connection; a ledger bearing their crest was on his person. Nature of the relationship is unresolved.
- **Bugbear ally:** A bugbear attempted to intervene during the fight and was burned alongside the warlock by the chimera's breath attack.
