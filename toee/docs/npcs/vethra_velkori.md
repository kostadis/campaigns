# Vethra Velkori

## Identity
- **Role:** Clan diviner and elder of the Rhennee river clan
- **Faction:** Rhennee (Eryk's clan)
- **First Appearance:** The party sought her out at the clan's wagon camp; she was Eryk's mother and the clan's primary seer

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Drive:** To speak true visions regardless of personal cost — she performed a fourth reading despite knowing it would break her health
- Speaks in pronouncements rather than conversation; her clouded eyes look *through* people rather than at them
- Treats her role as sacred duty, overriding Eryk's protective objections without hesitation
- Surrounded by ritual materials (bones, blackened cards, copper bowl of clove and blood), suggesting deep, long-practiced craft

## History with the Party
1. **First Reading (Smoke):** Burned a lock of braided black hair. Revealed visions of a crooked house with shifting shadows, a tavern called **the Waterside** full of secrets, and a man in a red sash who trades in whispers and iron.
2. **Second Reading (Bones):** Cast wolf knuckles and pigeon vertebrae. Declared that **Serena** was on her knees but not broken.
3. **Third Reading (Tarokka):** Drew **The Chain**, **The Betrayer**, and **The Flooded Gate** — over Eryk's protests that the effort would make her sick or kill her.
4. **Fourth Card (Uninvited):** Drew a final card with a hiss of breath — a crimson-painted barge sailing low on the Velverdyva, heavy with souls that no longer sang. Confirmed Serena was taken there, bound and traded. Then collapsed from exhaustion.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Her wagon in the Rhennee camp
- **Condition:** Collapsed and exhausted after the fourth drawing; her health may be compromised
- **Active Concerns:** Eryk fears the readings have seriously harmed or may kill her

**Party Knows:** Her visions pointed toward a crimson barge on the Velverdyva as Serena's location; the readings cost her greatly.
**Hidden:** Whether she knew the fourth card's cost before drawing it — and drew anyway.

## Relationships
- **Eryk (son):** She overrides his protective instincts; he clearly fears for her life and resents the toll divination takes on her
- **The Party:** Functional alliance — she read for them, not out of personal attachment but apparent duty or will
- **Serena:** No prior connection noted; read her fate as a stranger
