# Jerry

## Identity
- **Role/Title:** Soldier of Verbobonc
- **Faction:** Verbobonc military
- **First Appearance:** Found imprisoned in the monastery's torture chamber alongside Warda

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Loyal to Verbobonc and its interests; willing to set aside conflict to face a greater threat
- Jerry held strong political views on Verbobonc's expansion, clashing openly with Warda even while imprisoned. Despite this, he demonstrated pragmatism and courage when the stakes were made clear — agreeing to fight and charging Tharok without hesitation.

## History with the Party
1. **Discovered in Torture Chamber:** The party found Jerry imprisoned alongside Warda. He and Warda were actively arguing over Verbobonc's expansion policies despite their captivity.
2. **Agreed to Help:** After the party explained the threat of Tharok's ritual, Jerry set aside his situation and volunteered to fight.
3. **Provided Key Intelligence:** Shared knowledge of a secret passageway to the deeper temple, learned from his grandmother who had once worshipped Beory in the monastery.
4. **Fell in Battle:** Charged Tharok during the climactic battle and was struck down by the high cultist's elemental attacks.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Status:** **Deceased** — killed in combat by Tharok during the battle at the monastery.
- No active plans or hidden information remaining.

## Relationships
- **Warda:** Fellow prisoner; ideological rival on the subject of Verbobonc's expansion. Antagonistic but circumstantially allied.
- **Grandmother (unnamed):** Former worshipper of Beory at the monastery; the source of Jerry's knowledge of the secret passageway.
- **Tharok:** Killed by the high cultist in battle.
