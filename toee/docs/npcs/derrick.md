# Derrick

## Identity
- Role: Merchant (prisoner/survivor); no known faction affiliation
- First appearance: Found imprisoned in the pantry room of the Moathouse lower level, held captive as food for the ogre Lubash, alongside fellow survivor Florian

## Personality & Motivations
- Primary drive: Survival and return to safety in Hommlet
- Visibly traumatized by his captivity — broke down crying when recounting the names of companions taken by Lubash
- Wore a notable purple robe over filthy traveling clothes (dirt, blood, excrement), suggesting either some prior status or an item of interest

## History with the Party
- The party discovered Derrick and Florian locked in the Moathouse pantry, where they were being kept as a food source for Lubash
- Derrick recounted that he was originally traveling from Narwell to Hommlet when brigands captured him and five others on the road; he named those taken and killed by Lubash before breaking down
- He and Florian promised the party a reward in exchange for safe return to Hommlet

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Moathouse exterior campsite, in the care of the Duke's men, awaiting escort back to Hommlet
- **Active plans:** Intends to return to Hommlet and presumably collect/deliver the promised reward
- **Hidden from party:** The significance (if any) of the purple robe remains unexplained; his background in Narwell has not been elaborated upon

## Relationships
- **Florian:** Fellow survivor and companion; the two were held together and made the reward promise jointly
- **The Party:** Rescued by them; owes them a promised reward; relationship is one of grateful dependency
- **The Duke:** Currently under his care at the campsite
