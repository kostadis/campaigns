# Ostler Gundigoot

## Identity
- **Role:** Innkeeper of the Inn of the Welcome Wench, Hommlet; member of the village council
- **Faction:** Aligned with Jaroo's faction in Hommlet village politics
- **First Appearance:** Session 1 — met the party upon their arrival in Hommlet at the Inn of the Welcome Wench
- *Also known as: Ostler Goodingoat, Ostler Goodingoot, Osler Goodingoat, Osler Goodingut, Ostler.*

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Maintain order and propriety at his inn; protect his guests and business interests; preserve the influence of Jaroo's faction in local politics
- Pragmatic and businesslike — enforces inn policies firmly (minimum three-night stays, goods sold after three days)
- Politically shrewd and quietly calculating — tracks who the party speaks to and in what order, treating it as significant intelligence; rewards loyalty to the established local order and is conspiratorial in expressing approval
- Capable of directness when pushed: confronted Furnok personally once he had evidence, escalating the situation enough that Elmo had to intervene
- Inquisitive and attentive — noticed and followed up on the suspicious departure of Rannos and Gremag, suggesting he keeps close tabs on town affairs

## History with the Party
1. **Initial Contact:** Met the party at the inn upon their arrival in Hommlet. After their visit to Jaroo's grove, he had already been informed of the blessing — suggesting an information network within the village
2. **Soft pressure to consult Jaroo:** After the party's meeting with Burne and Rufus, Ostler approached them to note that Jaroo would expect to be consulted, steering them toward the druid before approaching the "interloping outsiders"
3. **Hall of the Scarlet Moon lead:** When the party was passing south, Ostler directed them to speak with Jaroo for information about the Hall of the Scarlet Moon
4. **Furnok job (covert):** Ushered the party into the kitchen privately and hired them to gather proof that Furnok was stealing from inn guests, offering three free nights as payment. Zephyr's pickpocket attempt failed, but the resulting distraction allowed Zinia to recover loaded dice. Ostler used the evidence to confront Furnok directly, escalating the situation enough that Elmo had to defuse it
5. **Rannos & Gremag inquiry:** Questioned the party regarding the sudden departure of merchants Rannos and Gremag; was successfully bluffed with the cover story that the merchants had urgently left after securing a magical chest
6. **Travel advice:** When the party decided to return Airgid, Ostler recommended the fastest route west through the Kron Hills and Greenway Valley, past gnomish mining towns
7. **Planting 19:** Informed Zinnia her room was paid through the 19th; whispered approval that the party had visited Jaroo before approaching the "interloping outsiders"
8. **Council meeting (post-Moathouse):** Present at the village council meeting where the party presented proof of the Temple of Elemental Evil's resurgence and confirmed Lareth's death; agreed with the council's consensus to quietly prepare the militia while the party continued covert operations against the Temple

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Inn of the Welcome Wench, Hommlet — attending council duties and inn operations
- **Active Concerns:** The Furnok situation appears resolved (with Elmo's intervention); aware of the Temple's resurgence following the council meeting; militia readiness vote in motion
- **What the Party Knows:** He is a council member with a quiet preference for Jaroo's faction over Burne and Rufus; willing to hire the party discreetly for sensitive inn business; views them favorably and cooperatively
- **What Remains Hidden:** The full extent of his political involvement with Jaroo's faction; his standing on the council relative to other factions; he does not know the party bluffed him about Rannos and Gremag's true fate

## Relationships
- **Jaroo (druid):** Politically aligned; approves of deference shown to Jaroo over Burne and Rufus
- **Burne & Rufus:** Views them as outside interlopers
- **Furnok:** Suspected thief; Ostler moved against him personally once evidence was in hand
- **Elmo:** Present at the Furnok confrontation and intervened to de-escalate
- **Rannos & Gremag:** Was suspicious of their sudden disappearance; bluffed by the party about their true fate
- **Hommlet Council:** Fellow member; voted in alignment with consensus on militia readiness
- **The Party:** Employer (covert job); has shown warmth and approval as the party has acted in ways consistent with his preferred village politics
