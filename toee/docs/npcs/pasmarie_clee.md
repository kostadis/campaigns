# Pasmarie Clee

## Identity
- Gnome beekeeper and druid of Sheernobb
- Operates a beehive farm a quarter mile southeast of Sheernobb
- First encountered on Planting 21, 579, at her farm; later directed to her by Captain Gnonsbim
- *Also known as: Palsmarie, Palsmarie Clee, Pasmarie.*

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: protect her livelihood and community; wants the honey trade route secured and the stolen honey recovered
- Warm and practical — weathered but kind-faced, willing to offer enchanted honey as fair payment for help
- Sharp situational awareness — perceptive enough to recognize the Black Earth cultists were not ordinary bandits based on guard testimony alone, and confident enough to directly contradict Captain Gnonsbim's assessment with specific intelligence from survivor accounts
- Occasionally low-profile — appeared at the Honey Haven Inn under unclear circumstances; Zephyr noted she may have slipped in unnoticed

## History with the Party
- **Planting 21, 579:** Party met Pasmarie at her farm. She reported a honey shipment to Verbobonc had been ambushed on the trade road north of Sheernobb by attackers chanting "Black Earth" and wielding stone weapons. Offered enchanted honey as a reward for investigation.
- **Planting 22–23, 579 — Honey Haven Inn:** Appeared at the inn (alternately referenced as "Honey Tavern Inn"). Marked the caravan attack site on Zephyr's map. Informed the party the caravan had taken a non-standard road. Relayed survivor accounts identifying the attackers as Black Earth cultists rather than ordinary bandits — directly contradicting Gnonsbim's assessment. Confirmed the surviving guards had since left Sheernobb and were unavailable for follow-up questioning.
- **Following the Black Earth skirmish:** Party returned the recovered stolen honey to Pasmarie; she paid out the agreed enchanted honey reward.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** Her beehive farm southeast of Sheernobb
- No known active plans beyond running her farm and trade operations
- **Party knows:** She is a druid, she reported the attack, she identified the attackers as Black Earth cultists, she paid her reward in full
- **Hidden:** Depth of her druidic capabilities is unestablished; her reasons for being at the inn and how she obtained survivor testimony before they left town

## Relationships
- **Captain Gnonsbim:** He directed the party to her; she contradicted his assessment of the attackers; Headgnome Glennddarc suggested Gnonsbim holds romantic feelings for her
- **Headgnome Glennddarc:** Aware of her and her situation; involved in directing party resources toward her problem
- **Surviving Guards:** Spoke to them before they left town; sole source of the "Black Earth" chanting detail — now inaccessible
- **The Party:** Friendly — a completed transaction, goodwill established
