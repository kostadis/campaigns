# The Dryad (Unnamed)

## Identity
- **Role/Faction:** Wild fey creature; solitary dryad bonded to a tree in the Gnarley Forest
- **First Appearance:** Encountered in a forest glade approximately one hour from Glennon Clee's business, when the party was tracking stolen barrels

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Drive:** Emotional connection and reciprocated love; currently suffering from unrequited feelings toward an unnamed beloved
- Naive and literal-minded — acted on secondhand information about human customs without understanding them. Genuinely remorseful once the wrongness of her actions was explained clearly. Not malicious; driven entirely by grief and desperation.

## History with the Party
- **Barrel Theft:** The dryad had taken one of Glennon Clee's stolen barrels after being told that humans drink when they are "dumped." She was found crying over the barrel, unable to open it.
- **Zephyr's Intervention:** Zephyr approached calmly, explained how barrels work, clarified that the barrel wasn't hers to take, and reasoned through why stealing was wrong. The dryad accepted this without resistance and surrendered the barrel. The party recovered it and departed.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Forest glade near her solitary bonded tree in the Gnarley Forest
- **Active Situation:** Nursing unrequited love; no known further plans or active schemes
- **What the Party Knows:** Her name, the identity of her beloved, and the specifics of her unrequited situation are all unknown. The party knows only that she stole out of heartbreak, not malice, and that she was cooperative when approached with patience.

## Relationships
- **Zephyr:** Responded positively; Zephyr was the one who reasoned with her and secured the barrel's return
- **Party (general):** Brief, non-hostile interaction; no ongoing connection established
- **Unknown Beloved:** An unnamed individual whose identity and nature are unknown to the party
