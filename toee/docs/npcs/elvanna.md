# Elvanna

## Identity
- Role: Madam of the Iron Lash brothel, located above the Boatman's Tavern in Nulb
- First appearance: Party sought her out to recover Serena, whom Elvanna was holding as compensation for damages caused by the Rhennee at the Boatman's Tavern

## Personality & Motivations
- Driven by profit and leverage; quick to recognize and exploit opportunity
- Sharp, self-assured, and unabashedly transactional — she openly regretted not asking for more when the party paid 500gp without negotiating
- Carries herself with a posh affect (notable accent, striking appearance) that reinforces her authority in her establishment
- Neither hostile nor friendly; purely businesslike with a sardonic edge

## History with the Party
- **First meeting:** Elvanna held Serena captive as collateral for damages tied to the Rhennee. She demanded 500gp for Serena's release.
- The party paid the full amount without negotiation. Elvanna noted she might have asked for more had she known how desperate they were.
- Upon release, Serena told Elvanna to go to hell. Elvanna responded with a mocking wish to see her again.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The Iron Lash brothel, above the Boatman's Tavern, Nulb
- No known active plots involving the party
- Transaction concluded; no outstanding obligations on either side

## Relationships
- **Serena:** Former captive; the two parted on hostile terms, with Elvanna getting the last (mocking) word
- **The Party:** Sees them as easy marks — paid full price without pushback; likely views them as naive but useful if they return with coin
- **The Rhennee:** Indirectly connected; their damages at the tavern were the pretext for holding Serena
