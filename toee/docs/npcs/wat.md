# Wat

## Identity
- **Role:** Bartender at the Waterside Hostel in Nulb
- **Faction:** Suspected agent of the Water Temple
- **First Appearance:** The party met Wat while waiting for Dick Rentsch at the Waterside Hostel; he attempted to pry into their business before warning them off the beer and serving gin instead.

## Personality & Motivations
- Likely serves the Water Temple's interests and monitors activity passing through the Hostel.
- Cautious and probing — tried to extract information from the party, then backed off when deflected. The mention of "Dala" when he relaxed suggests a personal or factional connection to that name.
- Willing to act against his employer's apparent wishes (whispering to Dren), but lacks the leverage to follow through — Dren shoved him away.

## History with the Party
- **Early meeting:** Attempted to learn the party's business; when they deflected, he seemed relieved, mentioned someone named Dala, and warned them not to drink the beer.
- **Mercenary recruitment:** When the party successfully recruited Dren Halveth and the Broken Blades for the Earth Temple, Zinnia observed Wat looking visibly unhappy — confirming Water Temple loyalties. As the party left with the mercenaries, Wat approached Dren and whispered something to him; Dren shoved him away.
- **Dick Rentsch's take:** When Calmer questioned Rentsch about keeping Wat on despite his divided loyalties, Rentsch explained that until they secured complete power, everyone had to work together.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The Waterside Hostel in Nulb, still employed as bartender.
- **Active concerns:** His attempt to intercept Dren failed. It is unclear whether he will make another move or report back to the Water Temple.
- **What the party knows:** Wat is suspected Water Temple. He tried to interfere with the Broken Blades recruitment and was rebuffed.
- **What remains hidden:** His exact role within the Water Temple; who Dala is and what that connection means; what he whispered to Dren.

## Relationships
- **Dick Rentsch:** Employer; aware of Wat's divided loyalties but tolerates him for now as a matter of pragmatic necessity.
- **Dren Halveth:** Attempted to communicate covertly with him during the mercenary handoff — Dren rejected the contact.
- **Dala (unknown):** Named by Wat in a moment of apparent relief; relationship unclear.
- **The Party:** Regards them with suspicion; they are aware he is not fully trustworthy.
