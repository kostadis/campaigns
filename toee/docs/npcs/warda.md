# Warda

## Identity
- **Role:** Gnome nationalist; no formal title noted
- **Faction:** Anti-imperial (Verbobonc expansion opponent)
- **First Appearance:** Found imprisoned in the monastery torture chamber, sharing a cell with Jerry

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Opposition to Verbobonc's imperial expansion; survival
- Argumentative and ideologically stubborn — she and Jerry clashed constantly over politics despite being cellmates
- Pragmatic when facing a greater threat; willing to set aside grievances to fight a common enemy
- Resilient under pressure — continued fighting after taking severe damage and after witnessing Jerry's death

## History with the Party
1. **Monastery Torture Chamber:** Discovered imprisoned alongside Jerry. She and Jerry argued over Verbobonc imperialism until the party intervened and explained Tharok's threat.
2. **Tharok Encounter:** Agreed to assist the party in combat. Opened the fight against Tharok (two misses, one hit). Took 29 damage from Tharok at some point but survived. Witnessed Jerry's death during the fight; redirected her hatred toward the cultist threat and fought through to the end.

## Current Status
- **Last Known:** Survived the fight against Tharok at the monastery
- **Active Plans:** Unknown post-combat
- **Hidden:** Long-term goals and whereabouts after the encounter are unestablished

## Relationships
- **Jerry:** Ideological rival and cellmate; shared imprisonment despite constant political arguments. Jerry died during the Tharok fight.
- **The Party:** Cooperative ally, brought in out of necessity; relationship untested beyond a single combat encounter
