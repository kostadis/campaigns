# Robert Deakin

## Identity
- **Role:** Tavern owner and operator of "The Cheap Forester" in Ostverk
- **Race:** Gnome
- **Faction:** None apparent; has family ties to the Clee family of Sheernobb
- **First Appearance:** Party visited his tavern in Ostverk seeking information; he required them to purchase food before agreeing to speak

## Personality & Motivations
- Transactional and cautious — won't share information without something in return
- A veteran of the Battle of Emiry Meadows; draws on firsthand experience when assessing threats, dismissing rumors of a "big evil power" as overblown
- Holds a clear gnomish political bias: pleased the Verbobonc–Celene alliance never formed, as he believes it was aimed at seizing gnomish gem mines
- Well-connected enough to know who pays for information and willing to pass that along

## History with the Party
- The party visited Deakin at The Cheap Forester seeking intelligence. After they purchased food, he shared the following:
  - Confirmed that a powerful chest had been lost to bandits and that multiple groups are actively searching for it
  - Dismissed rumors of a "big evil power" behind the bandits, insisting they are simply bandits based on his experience at the Battle of Emiry Meadows
  - Theorized the chest was intended to seal a treaty between Verbobonc and the Kingdom of Celene, which Verbobonc planned to use to wage war on gnomes and seize their gem mines
  - Noted that the elves at Caer Ostverk and Lord Denelu would pay well for information on the chest
  - Revealed he is related to the Clee family of Sheernobb

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** The Cheap Forester tavern, Ostverk
- **Active Plans:** None confirmed; appears to be operating his tavern and staying informed
- **What the Party Knows:** His intel, his gnomish sympathies, and his family connection to Sheernobb
- **Hidden from the Party:** The full extent of his information network and whether he is actively selling intelligence to other parties (e.g., Lord Denelu or the Caer Ostverk elves)

## Relationships
- **Clee Family of Sheernobb** — Related; nature of relationship unspecified
- **Lord Denelu** — Aware that Denelu pays for information on the chest; relationship beyond that unknown
- **Elves of Caer Ostverk** — Similarly aware they pay for chest-related intelligence; no closer tie established
- **The Party** — Single transactional interaction; neutral to cordial
