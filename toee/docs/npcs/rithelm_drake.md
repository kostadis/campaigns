# Rithelm Drake

## Identity
- Role: Human soldier, stationed in or near Ostverk
- First appearance: "The Cheap Forester" tavern in Ostverk; approached the party by demanding a drink, then softened and bought them one upon learning they were adventurers

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goals: Implied domestic stability — has settled down with a wife and three children
- Gruff but good-natured once he warmed to the party; expressed a wistful regret about never pursuing adventuring himself. Practical and helpful when engaged directly.

## History with the Party
- **First (and only known) meeting:** Rithelm initially confronted the party at The Cheap Forester, demanding a drink. Upon realizing they were adventurers, his demeanor shifted and he bought them a round instead. When the party asked about a monastery, he was momentarily confused, but ultimately recalled an abandoned monastery near Gallow's Corner — providing the party's primary lead on that location.

## Current Status
- Last known location: The Cheap Forester tavern, Ostverk
- No known active plans or operations
- **Party knows:** His name, appearance, profession, family situation, and that he provided the Gallow's Corner monastery lead
- **Hidden:** Nothing noted in the source material

## Relationships
- **Family:** Wife and three children (unnamed); his primary reason for not pursuing adventuring
- **The Party:** Cordial after initial friction; functioned as an incidental information source
