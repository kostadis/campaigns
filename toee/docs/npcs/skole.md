# Skole

## Identity
- Role/Title: Owner and bartender of the Boatman's Tavern, Nulb
- Faction: Nominally under Lodriss (actual tavern owner); coerced association with Tolub and the Fire Temple
- First Appearance: The party returned to the Boatman's Tavern after Tolub had reported them dead; Skole pulled them into the back room privately

## Personality & Motivations
- Core goal: To be free of both Tolub and Lodriss, who hold power over him and his establishment
- Demonstrated as cautious but willing to act when the moment is right — his private meeting with the party suggests he is calculating rather than impulsive. Visibly shaken by the party's survival, indicating he takes Tolub's threats seriously.

## History with the Party
- **First meeting:** When the party returned alive, Skole was visibly shocked — Tolub had publicly announced he'd disposed of them. Skole pulled them into his back room.
- **Key intelligence provided:** Skole revealed that Tolub works for the Fire Temple, not the Earth Temple, and redirected the party to **Dick Rentsch at the Waterside Hostel** as the correct contact for the Earth faction.

## Current Status
- **Last known location:** The Boatman's Tavern, Nulb
- **Active situation:** Still operating the tavern under Lodriss's ownership and Tolub's shadow
- **Party knows:** He despises Tolub and Lodriss and wants out; he is a potential ally
- **Hidden:** The extent of his involvement with either faction, his leverage (if any) over them, and what risk he took in speaking to the party

## Relationships
- **Tolub:** Antagonistic — Skole despises him and fears his influence
- **Lodriss:** Antagonistic — resents her as the tavern's true owner
- **Dick Rentsch:** Aware of him as the legitimate Earth Temple contact
- **The Party:** Cautious ally; trusted them enough to share sensitive information in private
