# The Crimson Reaper Spokesperson (Unnamed)

## Identity
- **Role:** Unnamed spokesperson/enforcer for the Crimson Reapers
- **Faction:** Crimson Reapers — implied servants of a deeper organization operating *behind* all four elemental temples, not any single one
- **First Appearance:** Answered the door at the Watersteide Hostel in Nulb when Calmer knocked

## Personality & Motivations
- **Core Goals:** Enforce the Crimson Reapers' ultimatum; recover or reclaim the stolen weapons chest; bring Dren Halveth into compliance
- Immediately hostile when questioned about the chest. Businesslike in issuing threats — calm enough to deliver a precise ultimatum rather than act on violence immediately. Not easily rattled, but not forthcoming either.

## History with the Party
- Calmer knocked on the door of the room adjacent to Dren Halveth's at the Watersteide Hostel.
- The spokesperson grew hostile when asked about the stolen weapons chest.
- Upon learning Calmer was affiliated with the Earth Temple, he clarified the chest was *temple property* stolen by Dren Halveth.
- Issued a midnight ultimatum: Dren joins the temple, or Dren and his crew die.

## Current Status
- **Last Known Location:** Room adjacent to Dren Halveth's room, Watersteide Hostel, Nulb
- **Active Plans:** Waiting on Dren Halveth's compliance before the midnight deadline; presumably prepared to carry out the threat if refused
- **What the Party Knows:** He works for the Crimson Reapers, who serve some overarching organization behind all four elemental temples
- **What Remains Hidden:** His name, his rank within the Reapers, the nature of the deeper organization, and how many Reapers are present at the hostel

## Relationships
- **Dren Halveth:** Target of the ultimatum; accused him of stealing temple property
- **Calmer:** Treated as a potential intermediary once Earth Temple affiliation was established; not an immediate target
- **The Four Elemental Temples:** Serves the organization *behind* them — his phrasing ("the temple" as singular) was a notable slip or deliberate clarification
- **The Party:** Limited contact; only dealt with Calmer directly
