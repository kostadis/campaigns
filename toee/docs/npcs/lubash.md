# Lubash

## Identity
- **Role:** Guard/hunter for the Moathouse lower level; stationed near the prisoner pantry
- **Faction:** Loosely affiliated with the Temple (used as muscle/disposal)
- **First Appearance:** Encountered in his chamber in the lower Moathouse when the party entered his territory

## Personality & Motivations
- Simple, aggressive, and territorial — identified the party as non-Temple members and immediately declared them food
- No apparent deeper goals beyond eating and guarding his domain

## History with the Party
- Announced himself by name, declared the party food, and attacked with his club
- Dealt significant damage before being killed by Zinia, Zephyr, and Sequoia
- His nest contained a **Cloak of Elvenkind**, origin unknown
- Had consumed **four of six captured merchants**: Martin, Damien, Eltograph the Elf, and one unnamed prisoner

## Current Status
- **Dead**

## Relationships
- **The Temple:** Was supplied with prisoners and food by Temple-affiliated parties — who exactly remains unknown
- **Surviving Merchants:** Two of the six captured merchants survived (identities to be confirmed); four were consumed by Lubash

## Arc Score Events
- N/A — deceased before any arc progression
