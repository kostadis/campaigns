## Completed Encounters & Quests

### Rescue Prisoners from Earth Temple Cells (area 114)
- **Session:** Chapter 21
- **Outcome:** Success
- **Details:** Party found seven prisoners — two females, five males — chained in a filthy locked chamber. Prisoners included Master Aldous Glimmerfield and Mistress Lenora Glimmerfield (textile merchants from Divers), their household staff, four mercenaries (Kalen, Brask, Horgus, Davine), and Jorin Pike Kale (river sailor imprisoned by Dick Rentsch's men).
- **Consequences:** Prisoners freed and guided to escape via a stairway leading up. Party staged the escape to look like a Fire Temple operation using previously acquired Fire Temple insignia and robes from Larith the Beautiful. Romag has not yet been informed. Glimmerfields promised 100 silver pieces reward. Jorin Pike Kale provided intelligence on Captain Tolubb's pirate ship schedule at Nulb docks.

### Otyugh Defeated
- **Session:** Chapter 21
- **Outcome:** Success (costly — Zinnia downed, multiple party members grappled and stunned)
- **Details:** Massive Otyugh emerged from a refuse-filled chamber. Party killed it after significant difficulty. Zinnia was bitten, poisoned, and knocked unconscious before being revived by Calmer.
- **Consequences:** Party looted 180 sp, 15 gp, and a potion of greater healing from the creature's refuse. Discovered a rusted Fire Temple breastplate among the garbage, indicating Romag fed rivals to this creature. Party suspects Romag will be displeased the creature is dead.
