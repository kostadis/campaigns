## NPC Activity

**Kelno (Air Temple Cleric)**
Identified as orchestrating a quiet opportunistic looting operation during the engineered temple war. His goblins were observed hauling bags of coin through a hidden door on the second level, while ghouls dragged looted weapons through the same passage. Operating with purposeful efficiency rather than panic — clearly had pre-planned to exploit the conflict. Revealed as having knowledge of the hidden passage to the Deep Temple, which he had shared with at least one Air Temple cultist prisoner. Previously filed by the party as background noise; now recognized as a significant independent actor.

**Wraith (Air Temple Corridor)**
Appeared in the Air Temple passage, resisted two consecutive Channel Divinity attempts from Calmer, announced "Well, one of you is going to die today," absorbed a five-damage pistol shot from Zephyr (halved by resistance), struck Calmer for sixteen cold damage during the party's flight, and was heard laughing hysterically as the party escaped. Remained in its corridor position during the party's short rest and was still present when they returned through the Air Temple afterward — observed the party but did not engage on the second pass.

**Bugbears (Fire Temple, Steam Corridor)**
Held an improvised barricade of pews and rubble in a steam-filled corridor, stalling an Earth Temple advance. Delivered a critical strike to Zinnia for twenty-five points of damage during the barricade defense. Both bugbears were eliminated — first by Zephyr, second by Sequoia — after Calmer's guiding radiance destroyed the barricade doors and broke the choke point.

**Ghouls (Second Level)**
Four ghouls identified as reanimated Earth Temple members encountered the party in their territory. One was killed instantly by Zephyr before it could act. The remaining three failed against Calmer's Turn Undead and fled the corridor taking ongoing damage; Sequoia killed the last one with an arrow. Separately, additional ghouls were observed on the second level working alongside Kelno's goblins, dragging looted weapons — suggesting they were under Kelno's or an unknown necromancer's direction rather than acting as independent scavengers.

**Air Temple Cultist Prisoner**
A man held in a prisoner chamber with four others, identified as an Air Temple member imprisoned by another faction for reasons of internal politics. Traded information for release. Confirmed knowledge of a hidden passage to the Deep Temple, obtained directly from Kelno. Described the Deep Temple as something everyone wants to avoid rather than reach. Released by Sequoia; displayed practical intelligence throughout the exchange.

**Hartsch (Earth Temple)**
Did not appear directly but sent a runner to summon the Obsidian Edge to an immediate war council upstairs. Summons described as carrying the texture of *now*. No other activity recorded this session.

**Salamander (Elemental Battle Chamber)**
Wounded but still active during combat with four water gargoyles in the Hall of Trophies. Current status unknown — party exited via southern corridor while the fight was still ongoing.

**Water Gargoyles (Elemental Battle Chamber)**
Four gargoyles engaged with fire snakes and a wounded salamander. Showed no sign of flagging during the party's observation. Did not interact with the party.

Advanced position significantly during the engineered war. Deployed goblins and ghouls to loot weapons and coin from the battlefield while other factions fought. Kelno appears to have pre-positioned assets to exploit the conflict rather than participate in it directly. Net gain: materiel and treasury assets. No combat losses recorded. Effectively a fourth faction now actively engaged in the temple war on their own terms.

**Earth Temple**
Advance stalled at Fire Temple barricade in steam corridor before party intervention broke the choke point. Multiple members confirmed killed and subsequently raised as ghouls — indicating active necromantic activity operating on or near their corpses. Hartsch convened a war council, suggesting command-level response to changed battlefield conditions.

**Fire Temple**
Held the steam corridor barricade with bugbear forces until barricade was destroyed. Line fractured into chaotic engagement with Earth Temple forces following the breach. Both bugbear defenders eliminated.

**Water Temple**
Four gargoyles engaged in active combat with Fire Temple elementals in the Hall of Trophies. No other activity observed.

**Unknown Necromantic Actor**
Raised recently-dead Earth Temple members as ghouls and appears to have directed at least some of them toward looting activity alongside Kelno's forces. Identity unconfirmed. Presence during the engineered war was unplanned and represents an unaccounted variable.
