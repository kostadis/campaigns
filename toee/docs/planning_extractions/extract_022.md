- **Fire Temple leader Alremm** is named as the figure offering power and glory to those who defect from other temples
- The charred library contained references to the **sacred earth triangle** and warnings about **trials of earthy terrors** awaiting those who descend into the depths below
- Promotion within the Earth Temple routinely occurs by eliminating rivals; Romag explicitly warned the party of this upon granting them territory
- **Commander Joradin** is a recent addition to the Earth Temple, brought in by Dick Rentsch, and not yet integrated into Romag's command structure
