## Revealed Information

- **Belsornig** (Water Temple prophet) secretly orchestrated the Chimera attack as a proxy operation — paying Captain Vesrek of the Crimson Traitor to sell the beast to the Fire Temple, with the goal of either eliminating the party or draining the Fire Temple's resources. The scheme was a calculated win-win designed to benefit the Water Temple regardless of outcome.
- **The Iron Collar and Chains** used to control the Chimera were stamped with the marks of **Nulb's river pirates**, indicating that the monster was acquired through or transported via Nulb's criminal network before being handed to the Fire Temple.
- **Alrrem's power has been waning** since a catastrophic defeat approximately a decade ago; the Fire Temple is significantly weakened relative to its former strength.
- **The Air Temple is functionally irrelevant** as a military faction, reduced to a remnant.
- **Kelno** (Air Temple prophet) mistakenly believes the wraith haunting his temple is an air elemental, indicating severe ignorance of his own faction's vulnerabilities.
- **Hartsch** had been actively suppressing his ambition under Romag and was waiting for an opportunity to seize power; he was willing to stand down and allow the party to assassinate Romag in exchange for being installed as Supreme Prophet.
- **In cultist hierarchy logic** (per Zephyr's reasoning), killing the leader is implicitly understood to confer succession rights — a dynamic the party exploited to legitimize Hartsch's takeover.
