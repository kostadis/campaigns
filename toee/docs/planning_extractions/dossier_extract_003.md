## Canon Belsoring

Canon Belsoring is a leader of the Temple of Water. One of the brigands encountered by the party in the Gnarley Forest during the caravan journey from Narwell was revealed to be working for him, connecting him to the bandit activity in the region near Hommlet.

## Spugnoir Balkof

Spugnoir Balkof was encountered by the party at the Inn of the Welcome Wench upon their arrival in Hommlet. No further details about his role or motivations are recorded at this time. Last known location: Inn of the Welcome Wench.

## Furnok

Furnok, described as a fabulous rogue, was encountered by the party at the Inn of the Welcome Wench upon their arrival in Hommlet. Last known location: Inn of the Welcome Wench.

## Osler Goodingoat

Osler Goodingoat is the innkeeper of the Inn of the Welcome Wench in Hommlet. He was met by the party upon their arrival. Last known location: Inn of the Welcome Wench.

## Rannos

Rannos is one of the traders operating in Hommlet. He, along with his partner Gremag, convinced the party to hire men-at-arms for protection. The local leatherworker sells all of his goods through their trading operation. Last known location: traders' establishment in Hommlet.

## Gremag

Gremag is one of the traders operating in Hommlet alongside Rannos. Together they persuaded the party to hire men-at-arms for protection, and the local leatherworker channels all his goods through their business. Last known location: traders' establishment in Hommlet.
