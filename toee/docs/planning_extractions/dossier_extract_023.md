## Krell

Krell was a Lieutenant of the Earth Temple who had been killed prior to this session. His death was the event that freed his former chambers, which the party now occupied as their base of operations within the Earth Temple complex. His quarters, though marked by blood and the remnants of battle with gnolls, featured a secret door and strategic access points. The party considered returning to Fire Temple agents in Nulb to report his death as one of their potential next moves. Krell is deceased; his chambers are now held by the party.

## Romag

Romag was referenced by the party at the conclusion of the session as the Earth Temple authority they would need to negotiate with in order to officially claim the territory they had cleared. The party recognized that holding ground alone was insufficient and that Romag held the power to formally grant them dominion over the dungeon section they had just purged of undead. Romag did not appear directly in this session but was established as a key figure whose cooperation the party must cultivate to consolidate their position within the Earth Temple. Current whereabouts are elsewhere in the temple complex.
