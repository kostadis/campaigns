## Glannon

Glannon is the proprietor or bartender at the Honey Tavern Inn. He appeared after the dinner rush on Planting 22 when Zinnia returned a barrel. Upon seeing the barrel, he expressed surprise and relief that it had been found. He presented Zinnia with a keg of magical mead as reward, initially phrasing the gift awkwardly before clarifying its properties: drinking a mug enhances charisma, improves persuasiveness, attracts romantic interest, helps in merchant negotiations, and improves bardic performance tips. He was quick to correct himself when he realized his initial wording sounded mocking.

## Quinna Poul

Quinna Poul is associated with the kitchen of the Honey Tavern Inn's first floor. She came rushing out of the kitchen on Planting 23 to meet Sequoia, visibly displaying a conflicted expression of both smiling and worry. She explained that ingredients from a recent feast had been magically animated and escaped into the village, and she asked the party to retrieve them. The animated ingredients included broccoli and potatoes. She was relying on the party to recover these items, suggesting she felt she was in their debt after the feast.

## Palsmarie Clee

Palsmarie Clee is the beekeeper druid of Sheernobb. She appeared in the Honey Tavern Inn on Planting 23 under unclear circumstances — Zephyr noted she may have already been present or slipped in unnoticed. She provided the party with a map location showing where the honey caravan had been attacked, noting the caravan had foolishly taken a non-standard road. She told the party that the survivors of the attack reported the assailants chanting "Black Earth" and carrying stone weapons, identifying them as likely cultists rather than ordinary bandits, contradicting the assessment of someone named Gnonsbim. She noted that the surviving guards had since left town and were unavailable to question. Her motivation appears to be recovering the stolen honey. Her last known location was the Honey Tavern Inn.

## Thalsor of the Stone

Thalsor of the Stone is a druid associated with the Black Earth Temple, encountered at a hidden encampment built into a rocky outcrop in the Kron Hills. He serves as leader or guardian of the cult encampment, which contained a stone temple, a stone statue, and the stolen honey barrels. When Calmer attempted to bluff the encampment guards, Thalsor stepped forward and confronted the party directly, identifying them as intruders on sacred ground dedicated to the Earth Mother. He delivered a monologue condemning the party as "witless insects" unworthy of the honey, which he declared a tribute to the Black Earth Temple. He demonstrated awareness of the party's purpose in coming for the honey, though how he knew this was unclear even to the party. In combat he summoned two mud creatures beside Calmer, which exploded upon death and hindered movement. He animated a stone golem by tapping a stone object. He attempted mid-combat negotiation, offering to trade anything except the honey, then responded to failed intimidation by conjuring a storm of sharp stones. By the end of the session he was barely alive, with the stone golem still active. His stated motivation is devotion to the Earth Mother and the power of stone.
