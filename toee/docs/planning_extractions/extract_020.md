## Revealed Information

- The surface temple is a shell; the real power structure descends multiple dungeon levels, with the first two divided among four elemental temples (Earth, Fire, Water, Air) in constant internecine conflict.
- Deeper levels exist below the four-temple tier, containing greater and unnamed secrets.
- The Earth Temple's strategy is to use outside agents to weaken or destroy rival temples rather than commit its own forces openly.
- The main bronze doors were sealed by the Circle of Eight (Mordenkainen, Bigby, and others) using powerful magic; the runes still burn with silvery radiance and are actively avoided by Dick Rentsch.
- Heavy traffic on the road to the temple has been deliberately disguised by someone, indicating significant covert movement of forces.
- Dick Rentsch regards the temple as his "happy place" and is ideologically aligned with the Earth Temple's goals of slaughter and domination, not merely a mercenary intermediary.
