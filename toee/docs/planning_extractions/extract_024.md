## Revealed Information

- **The Orb**: An artifact described as the only object capable of forcing chaotic elements to submit to earth. Functions as both a power mandate (granting authority over all elemental factions within the temple) and a physical key to the temple vault. Romag intends to use it to deploy earth elementals and gorgons to crush rival temples. Its current location was not disclosed.
- **The Temple Vault**: Exists somewhere in the deep places of the temple; its contents are unknown and Romag has not yet trusted the party enough to reveal them.
- **Battle of Emory Meadows**: Ten years ago the four temples unified and marched to conquer; they were defeated by the Archbishop of Veluna, the Kingdom of Furyondy, and the Circle of Eight (Bigby, Tenser, Mordenkainen specifically named). The Circle of Eight sealed the temple, cutting off its central power source. The temple has been stagnant since.
- **The Crimson Reapers' organizational affiliation**: They do not serve any of the four elemental temples. They represent a deeper, unnamed organizational layer behind the temple structure with independent enforcement authority.
- **Wat's allegiance**: The Watersteide Hostel barkeep works for the Water Temple while remaining on Dick Rentsch's payroll. Dick is aware and considers it an acceptable arrangement under current conditions.
- **Romag's strategic intent**: With the orb and control of the dungeon's territory, Romag plans to use earth elementals and gorgons to eliminate the other elemental temples entirely and rule the complex alone.
