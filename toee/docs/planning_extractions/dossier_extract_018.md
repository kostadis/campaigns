## Constable Eldrin Malavar

Constable Eldrin Malavar appeared only through signposts posted around Nulb, warning citizens about a condemned temple south of town and declaring the Pilgrim's Trail off-limits to all. His current status and whereabouts are unknown beyond his apparent role as a local law enforcement figure attempting to control access to dangerous areas near the settlement.

## Skole

Skole is the owner and bartender of the Boatman's Tavern in Nulb. He was present when the party first visited the tavern. No further details about his motivations or role were revealed in this session.

## Ketta

Ketta is a serving wench at the Boatman's Tavern. When the party spoke with her during their evening return to the tavern, she nervously revealed that there had been a fight involving Serena and the Rhennee. She pointed fearfully toward Captain Tolub as the relevant party but was clearly too terrified to say more, indicating she has knowledge of Serena's abduction but lives under intimidation.

## Eryk Velkori

Eryk Velkori is the leader of a Rhennee caravan encamped near Nulb. The party found him in a state of open grief, clutching a bottle of rum in an unnaturally still camp thick with ash and river rot, weeping over his missing sister Serena. He provided the party with detailed intelligence on Nulb's criminal underworld, identifying the Crimson Reapers under Zelt the Rat, and warning about Madame Selentis as the true controlling power in the town. He revealed the Iron Lash was the name of the local brothel, located upstairs in the Boatman's Tavern. When Sequioa jokingly asked about a map to hell, Eryk replied he was already living in one.

Eryk was also revealed to be under coercion, with Serena used as leverage to force him into conducting ambushes along the forest road — a debt he had apparently incurred that Serena knew nothing about.

Upon Serena's return, Eryk's grief transformed entirely — his eyes lit with joy and the camp erupted in music and dancing. He formally declared the party family and welcomed them as members of the Rhennee clan, offering protection and friendship in Nulb while warning them not to trust all their friends. He confirmed his honesty by leaving all the party's belongings intact through the night's celebration.

When the party sought to contact higher powers including someone named Romag, Eryk described the Hall of the Scarlet Moon as a place where a druid named Elazar taught the rite of the wicker giant. He noted that many people there seemed to be burning fire all day, which he found unusual. His last known location is his encampment near Nulb.

## Tala Velkori

Tala is the daughter of Eryk Velkori and granddaughter of Vethra Velkori. She approached the party on behalf of her grandmother, inviting them to meet Vethra for a divination reading. Her role in this session was limited to serving as an intermediary between the party and the elder Rhennee woman.

## Vethra Velkori

Vethra Velkori is an elderly Rhennee woman, mother of Eryk and grandmother of Tala, who serves as the clan's seer. The party found her sitting cross-legged in the shadows of her wagon, surrounded by bones, blackened cards, and a copper bowl smelling of clove and blood. Her clouded blue eyes appeared to look through rather than at her subjects.

She conducted two readings for the party. For the first, using smoke, she burned a lock of braided black hair and revealed visions of a crooked house with shifting shadows, a tavern with too many secrets called the Waterside, and a man with a red sash who dealt in whispers and iron. For the second, using bone, she tossed wolf knuckles and pigeon vertebrae into a shallow bowl, and the resulting pattern led her to declare that Serena might be on her knees but was not broken, citing Rhennee blood as the reason.

Despite Eryk's protests that the effort would make her sick or kill her, Vethra insisted on drawing from her faded Tarokka deck. She revealed The Chain, The Betrayer, and The Flooded Gate, then drew a fourth card that caused her to hiss — a vision of a crimson-painted barge sailing low on the Velverdyva, heavy with souls that no longer sang, confirming Serena had been taken there, bound and traded like meat. The effort left her collapsed and exhausted. Her last known location is the Rhennee encampment near Nulb.

## Serena Velkori

Serena is Eryk Velkori's sister and a member of the Rhennee clan. She had vanished after a night at the Boatman's Tavern and was being held by Elvanna at the Iron Lash brothel upstairs in the same building, used as compensation for damages caused by the Rhennee. The party paid five hundred gold pieces for her release.

When brought from the pits she appeared dazed but defiant. As she left, Elvanna mockingly wished to see her again, and Serena retorted with a wish for Elvanna to go to hell. Upon returning to the Rhennee camp, Serena explained she had been used as leverage for a debt she didn't know about and that her father was being coerced into conducting ambushes along the forest road. Her last known location is the Rhennee encampment near Nulb.

## Madame Selentis (Nisera)

Madame Selentis, also known as Nisera, was described by Eryk Velkori as the true power in Nulb. She is a member of a disgraced noble family from Verbobonc who now controls the town's affairs like a spider in a web. Eryk explicitly warned the party that if she ever took notice of them, things would go very badly. She did not appear in person during this session but was identified as a major threat and power behind Nulb's criminal structure. Her organization is referred to as Nisera's Talons, which Eryk listed alongside the Zelrads and the Crimson Reapers as groups that may have taken Serena.

## Zelt the Rat

Zelt the Rat was named by Eryk Velkori as the leader of the Crimson Reapers, a criminal organization seeking to dominate Nulb's criminal activities and funnel supplies through the area. He did not appear in person during this session.

## Captain Tolub

Captain Tolub is a boisterous and physically intimidating river pirate captain who was present at the Boatman's Tavern with his crew. He told an awful joke that his crew laughed at with exaggerated enthusiasm. After the party applied negotiation and charm, he agreed to buy them drinks and questioned why they would help someone like Eryk Velkori, laughing at their sentiment when they explained they still had heart despite looking like bandits.

Tolub confirmed that Serena had been taken to the Iron Lash as payment for something, and mockingly revealed the brothel was upstairs in the very same building. Later, when the party sought to contact higher powers including Romag, Tolub directed them to the Hall of the Scarlet Moon, a small encampment a day's hike northeast of Nulb in the Kron Hills. The party subsequently concluded that Tolub had deliberately sent them to the Fire Temple rather than the Earth Temple, effectively setting them up. His last known location is his boat near Nulb.

## Elvanna

Elvanna is the proprietor of the Iron Lash brothel, located on the upper floor of the Boatman's Tavern in Nulb. The party found her in her chambers, described as a gorgeous woman with orange-red hair who spoke with a posh accent. She was holding Serena as compensation for damages caused by the Rhennee at the tavern, and demanded five hundred gold pieces for Serena's release.

She was visibly surprised when the party paid without negotiation, remarking that she might have asked for more had she known they were that desperate. She mockingly wished to see Serena again as the woman was released, receiving Serena's sharp retort in return. Her last known location is the Iron Lash brothel above the Boatman's Tavern.

## Ostler Goodingoot

Ostler Goodingoot is the owner of a tavern in Hommlet and a member of the local council. When the party stopped in Hommlet during their southward journey, he directed them to speak with Jaroo, a local druid, regarding the Hall of the Scarlet Moon. His last known location is his tavern in Hommlet.

## Jaroo

Jaroo is a druid based in Hommlet whom the party consulted about the Hall of the Scarlet Moon on Ostler Goodingoot's direction. He expressed deep suspicion about the Circle of the Scarlet Moon, describing them as too showy, flashy, and ecstatic for true druids and suggesting their practices were more religious than natural. Despite his warnings, the party continued onward to the Hall. His last known location is Hommlet.

## Elazar

Elazar is a druid named by Eryk Velkori as the teacher at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon, described as instructing followers in the rite of the wicker giant to restore nature's balance. He did not appear in person during this session. His last known location is presumed to be at or near the Hall of the Scarlet Moon in the Kron Hills.

## Verigo

Verigo is one of four friendly human druids the party encountered at Camp Number One near the Hall of the Scarlet Moon. He and his companions were excited about the upcoming rite of the wicker giant and offered the party food, drink, and brandy, inviting them to stay the night. His last known location is Camp Number One at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon.

## Mahoon

Mahoon is one of four friendly human druids at Camp Number One near the Hall of the Scarlet Moon, sharing the same circumstances and behavior as Verigo. His last known location is Camp Number One.

## Iniri

Iniri is one of four friendly human druids at Camp Number One near the Hall of the Scarlet Moon, sharing the same circumstances and behavior as the others. Her last known location is Camp Number One.

## Faria

Faria is one of four friendly human druids at Camp Number One near the Hall of the Scarlet Moon, sharing the same circumstances and behavior as the others. Her last known location is Camp Number One.

## Albrith

Albrith is one of two robed men the party encountered at Camp Number Two near the Hall of the Scarlet Moon. He identified himself as a circuit of the Scarlet Moon. When the party asked whether they were connected to the Temple of Elemental Evil, Albrith and his companion adopted threatening poses and revealed their allegiance by stating "fire, water, earth, or air," confirming their connection to elemental evil and specifically to the Fire Temple. He and Ingulf then launched devastating magical fire attacks on the party, hurling explosive bursts of flame that severely wounded several members and knocked Calmer unconscious. He was last seen at Camp Number Two, distracted by a rampaging bear that Zephyr had released as a diversion while the invisible party escaped.

## Ingulf

Ingulf is the second of the two robed fire priests at Camp Number Two. He shared the same role and actions as Albrith during the confrontation, participating in the magical fire assault on the party. He was last seen at Camp Number Two, unable to locate the now-invisible party and dealing with the freed bear.
