## Revealed Information

- **Lareth deceived the party**: The Black Earth cultists were not random bandits but a rival elemental cult faction. Lareth's chest retrieval mission was a proxy operation to weaken his enemies.
- **The lost chest is at an abandoned monastery near Gallow's Corner** in the mountains; confirmed by notes from Romag to Thalsor, Thalsor's own unsent correspondence, and independently corroborated by Rithelm Drake.
- **The chest has geopolitical significance**: Per Deakin, it was intended as the instrument of a treaty between Verbobonc and Celene, with Verbobonc allegedly planning to use that alliance against gnomish populations to seize gem mines.
- **Multiple factions are actively searching for the chest**: Caer Ostverk elves, Lord Denelu, Lareth, Burne and Rufus, and likely others.
- **Deakin denies resurgent evil**: Argues the Temple of Elemental Evil was genuinely destroyed at Emiry Meadows and current threats are merely bandit activity; party has direct evidence contradicting this.
