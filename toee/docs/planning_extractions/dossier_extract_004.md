## Black Jay

A mysterious and forlorn herdsman mentioned by a tavern patron as being in league with local bandits. Black Jay serves as an informant, tipping off bandits about targets or movements. His connection to the Temple of Elemental Evil is unknown to the common folk of Hommlet, a fact Zinia noted with satisfaction. Black Jay's current whereabouts are unknown; he was not encountered directly.

## Y'dey

The head cleric of Hommlet's village, Y'dey has mysteriously disappeared. A tavern patron speculated that Terjon, who has become closed-mouthed about the situation and is now in charge, played no small role in her disappearance. Y'dey did not appear in person; her current status and whereabouts are unknown.

## Terjon

High priest of the Temple of St. Cuthbert in Hommlet, now in charge following Y'dey's mysterious disappearance. Terjon is described as closed-mouthed about Y'dey's fate, leading locals to suspect his involvement. He met with Zinia, recognizing him as a recent convert to St. Cuthbert and was willing to permit Calmer to join the adventuring group. However, Terjon made a request strikingly familiar to those the party had already received: that any information gathered must be shared with him and him alone, citing the safety of the people of Oerth. Terjon is thus established as another competing faction seeking to control the flow of information about the possible resurgence of the Temple of Elemental Evil. Last seen at the Temple of St. Cuthbert in Hommlet.

## Jaroo

An older, robed, bearded druid and keeper of a sacred grove in Hommlet, sent to observe events following the defeat of the Temple of Elemental Evil. Jaroo is a follower of Beory and harbors open disgust toward Tieflings, refusing to engage with Zephyr and speaking instead with Sequoia. He is deeply distrustful of Verbobonc's intentions, believing Burne and Rufus are instruments of the Archbishop of Verbobonc seeking to absorb Hommlet's faithful into the Church of St. Cuthbert rather than simply protect the region. Jaroo provided historical context about the Moathouse, explaining it was once the Temple of Elemental Evil's forward outpost used for raids and as a staging point for expanding the Temple's influence in ever-growing rings of outposts. He noted that the circles of outposts the party uncovered bear disturbing similarity to the Temple's original patterns and are too close to be coincidence. Jaroo directed the party to investigate the Moathouse and, like Terjon and Rufus, demanded that any findings be reported to him and him alone, warning that Temple agents may already be present in Hommlet and that Burne and Rufus would use any information to restrict local freedoms. Last seen at his grove in Hommlet.

## Rufus

Military commander of Hommlet and representative of Verbobonc, tasked with overseeing construction of a castle. Rufus is a powerfully built man of average height with blue eyes, a delicate nose, and golden blond hair, and is in a romantic relationship with Burne. He received the party's report about potential resurgence of the Temple of Elemental Evil with alarm, agreeing with Burne that such a thing seemed impossible. Rufus quickly moved to control the information, first attempting to subtly encourage Sequoia to volunteer to report findings only to him, and when that failed, formally deputized the party, making it a legal obligation to report to him first before sharing information with anyone else. He threatened arrest or worse for anyone who explored the Moathouse without accepting his deputization. Sequoia complied outwardly while privately reserving judgment on what he would actually share. Last seen at Burne's tower on the edge of Hommlet.

## Burne

A mage and co-leader of Hommlet alongside Rufus, whom he is romantically involved with. Burne has a slight build, long brown hair, green eyes, and an aquiline nose, and wears a scuffed and scratched mage's robe. He was called into the meeting by Rufus midway through Sequoia's report. Burne reacted with horror to the possibility of the Temple of Elemental Evil's resurgence, calling it impossible. He deferred to Rufus on the political and military response. Last seen at the tower in Hommlet.
