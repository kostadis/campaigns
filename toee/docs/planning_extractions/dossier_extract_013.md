## Ranus Duval

Known by the alias "the Duke," Ranus Duval was revealed during the interrogation of a captured cultist to be working for a larger competing organization. His connection to the missing chest originally intended for Romag was exposed — the chest is believed to be located at an abandoned monastery near Gallows Corner. Duval himself did not appear directly in this session; his role was uncovered secondhand through Zephyr's successful intimidation of the cultist. Current whereabouts unknown.

## Tilly Bizpebble Whistle

A merchant operating in or around the settlement of Gallows Corner. Ziinnia's eavesdropping surfaced Tilly's name in connection with suspicious activities being discussed in hushed conversations among locals. Nicholas purchased a painted rock from her. The exact nature of her suspicious involvement was not fully detailed in this session. Last known location: Gallows Corner.

## Sister Alina

A member of the religious community at the partially abandoned monastery dedicated to the nature goddess Beory near Gallows Corner. Sister Alina, along with Brother Eldren, guided the party through a series of moral and physical dilemmas called the "Path of Righteousness" to assess their character and worth. At the session's end, she offered the party a trial of combat and cunning, pitting them against a feral gnoll of demonic origin to test their strength and loyalty. Last known location: the monastery near Gallows Corner.

## Brother Eldren

A member of the Beory monastery near Gallows Corner. Brother Eldren accompanied Sister Alina in leading the party through the "Path of Righteousness," a structured series of dilemmas designed to evaluate the party's decision-making and moral fiber. He initially encountered the party after Calmer attempted to deceive an elderly monk by claiming to be lost in the woods, though it is unclear if Eldren was that monk. Last known location: the monastery near Gallows Corner.
