**Glennon Clee** (gnome, relationship to Pasmarie unclear — likely kin)

**Aunt Emma / Green Hag** (Gnarley Forest)
- Appeared in a fertile glade in the Gnarley Forest in the guise of a kindly old woman
- Attempted to lure the party into her gingerbread house with promises of sweets and pie

- Killed by Zephyr before any bargain was concluded
- Treasure recovered: a Hat of Wizardry; also found in her possession: *The Hag's Guide to 101 Recipes Of Children*

**The Dryad** (Gnarley Forest, unnamed)
- Appeared alone in a forest glade near a solitary tree, in possession of Glennon Clee's stolen barrels
- Humanoid in appearance with branches extruding from arms and leaves on head; visibly distressed
- Stole the barrels under the mistaken belief that heartbroken humans cope by drinking
- Expressed romantic despair — claimed a beloved does not reciprocate
- Did not resist; acknowledged the theft was wrong after Zephyr's explanation
- Barrels recovered without violence
