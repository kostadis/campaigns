## Revealed Information

- **Chimera Purchase**: The Fire Temple paid Captain Varsik of the Crimson Trader in full for the Chimera; the bill of sale was recovered in the warlock's ledger bearing the Crimson Trader's crest
- **Water Temple Manipulation**: Vesrek, an ally of Water Temple prophet Belsornig, arranged the chimera purchase — framing the Fire Temple for an attack intended to weaken both the Fire Temple and the party simultaneously; the Water Temple bore no cost or risk
- **Chimera Delivery Chain**: The iron collar and chains are stamped with the marks of Nulb's River Pirates, confirming someone in Nulb was paid to deliver the Chimera to the temple
- **Alrrem's Decline**: Alrrem led the temples at the Battle of Emridy Meadows a decade ago; the catastrophic defeat there began his decline; subsequent loss of werewolves and now the ruinous chimera expenditure have left him nearly broken
- **Kelno and the Air Temple**: The Air Temple under prophet Kelno is functionally irrelevant — reduced to a few rooms, no meaningful forces or territory; Hartsch dismissed them outright
- **Hartsch's Ambitions**: Hartsch believes in aggressive, destructive leadership and finds Romag's passive, erosion-style command inadequate; he has implied willingness to assume leadership if Romag were removed
