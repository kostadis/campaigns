## Tharok Stevan (Tharok Stonevein)

Tharok Stevan, also known as Tharok Stonevein, was revealed by the captive monk as the orchestrator of a catastrophic ritual to summon a Titan. He holds the title of High Templar and is the primary authority driving the ritual, which was already underway at the time of the session. He did not appear in person during this session but was identified as the central threat behind the monastery's activities. His role in the Earth Cult and his plan to awaken a Titan establish him as a major antagonist. Last known status: conducting or overseeing the summoning ritual somewhere within or connected to the monastery.

## Sister Alina

Sister Alina was identified as the supposed cult leader of the monastery. Ziinnia and the GM's character were dispatched to confront her directly as part of the party's split infiltration plan, with Brother Eldren providing a distraction to facilitate the approach. She did not appear in person during this session. Her true role, allegiances, and the outcome of the confrontation were not yet revealed by the session's end.

## Brother Eldren

Brother Eldren is a defector from the monastery who formed a tactical alliance with the party after the interrogation revealed the Earth Cult's true nature. He agreed to provide a distraction to enable the split-force infiltration, specifically to allow Ziinnia and the GM's character to confront Sister Alina. His defection suggests disillusionment with the cult's activities, particularly the Titan summoning ritual. Last known status: acting as a diversion inside the monastery as part of the party's coordinated plan.
