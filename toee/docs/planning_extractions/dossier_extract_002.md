## Jijohn

An eccentric, wizened gnome tinkerer encountered by the party during the caravan escort through the Gnarley Forest. He offered cryptic warnings about the organized forces backing the bandits plaguing the region. He recounted the crushing of the Temple of Elemental Evil ten years prior and advised the party to seek out Burne and Rufus upon reaching Hommlet. His knowledge of regional history and current criminal networks suggests he is more than a simple wanderer.

## Urot

An orc cook employed in the kitchen of the ruined Abbey of Allitur, serving as part of the bandit operation headquartered there. Cornered by the party following their stealth assault on the lower levels, she was terrified into confession by the party's ruthless efficiency. She revealed that the bandit leader Snej was no mere outlaw but an operative receiving resources from Canon Belsornig, described as a "big shot" within the Temple of Evil. Last known location is the Abbey kitchen. Current status unclear following her confession.

## Snej

The bandit leader operating out of the ruined Abbey of Allitur, referred to by the orc cook Urot as "Snige." According to Urot's confession, Snej is not an independent outlaw but an operative connected to and resourced by Canon Belsornig within the Temple of Evil. Not directly encountered by the party during the assault; current status and whereabouts within the Abbey are unconfirmed.

## Canon Belsornig

Not directly encountered, but named by the orc cook Urot during her confession in the Abbey kitchen. Described as a "big shot" within the Temple of Evil who was supplying resources to the bandit leader Snej operating out of the Abbey of Allitur. Represents the organizational link between the organized banditry plaguing the Gnarley Forest region and the broader threat of the Temple of Evil.

## Airgid

A baby silver dragon discovered chained to a statue in the desecrated chapel of the Abbey of Allitur, among defaced frescos of the god Allitur. Ravenous at the time of her rescue, she informed the party that her mother is located in the Lortmil Mountains. The party agreed to escort her to Hommlet to plan a reunion with her mother. Last known status: traveling with the party toward Hommlet.
