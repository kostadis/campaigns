## Lareth the Beautiful

Lareth the Beautiful was the Fire Temple leader operating out of the Moathouse dungeon. At the session's start he was furious, having seen through the party's earlier misdirection in Hommlet — specifically their lie that the Deep Temple had stolen the Chest of Rao. He had positioned himself and his guards to trap the adventurers in his inner sanctum.

During the ensuing fight, Lareth demonstrated considerable magical power: he summoned a Mirror Image of himself to confuse attackers, cast Inflict Wounds to severely wound Calmer, and used Hold Person to paralyze both Zephyr and Zinnia, neutralizing the party's two primary damage dealers. Despite these efforts, he was reduced to a single hit point before Sequoia struck the killing blow, ending his leadership of the Moathouse operation.

His body was looted afterward, yielding a Mace of Smiting, magical Plate Mail, a Ring of Free Action, a silver service set worth 2,000 GP, a fire opal necklace worth 3,500 GP, and gems worth 800 GP. His death left the Gnolls and Bugbears in the dungeon leaderless and easily manipulated by the party.

## Thorne (Duke)

Thorne, referred to as Duke, was contracted by the party following Lareth's death to handle the clean-up of the Moathouse. The party agreed to pay him a 50 GP fee for this work. To ensure his safety, they cleared the remaining dungeon of threats before handing the site over to him. His last known status is awaiting the cleared Moathouse to begin the clean-up job.

## Osler Goodingut

Osler Goodingut was one of the Hommlet council members gathered by the party upon their return from the Moathouse. He was present when the party presented undeniable proof of the Temple of Elemental Evil's resurgence and the death of Lareth. He agreed alongside the other council members that the militia should be prepared while the adventurers continued disruptive work in secret.

## Burne

Burne was one of the Hommlet council members assembled to hear the party's report on the Moathouse. He was present for the presentation of evidence regarding the Temple of Elemental Evil's resurgence and Lareth's death. He agreed with the council's conclusion that the militia should be readied while the party pursued covert operations in Nulb.

## Rufus

Rufus was one of the Hommlet council members gathered by the party after their return from the Moathouse. He was present when the party delivered proof of the Temple of Elemental Evil's resurgence and confirmed Lareth's death. He concurred with the council's plan to prepare the local militia while the adventurers operated covertly in Nulb.

## Jaroo

Jaroo was a Hommlet council member who took a leading role in determining the party's next course of action. After the party presented their evidence of the Temple of Elemental Evil's resurgence and Lareth's death, Jaroo specifically asserted that the critical next step was for the party to travel to Nulb, infiltrate the Temple's operations there, disrupt their machinations, and identify who was truly in charge of the organization. His directive set the party's immediate objective for the next phase of the campaign.
