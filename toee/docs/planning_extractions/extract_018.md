## NPC Activity

**Constable Eldrin Malavar**
Posted warning signposts throughout Nulb declaring a condemned temple south of town and the Pilgrim's Trail off-limits to all citizens. Did not appear in person.

**Skole**
Owner and bartender of the Boatman's Tavern. Present during both party visits. No significant information revealed beyond his role as proprietor.

**Ketta**
Serving wench at the Boatman's Tavern. When approached by the party, nervously confirmed there had been a fight involving Serena and the Rhennee. Pointed fearfully toward Captain Tolub, indicating his involvement, but was too terrified to elaborate further.

**Eryk Velkori**
Rhennee caravan leader camped outside Nulb. Found drinking rum and weeping openly over his missing sister Serena. Revealed the following to the party:
- Serena disappeared after a night at the Boatman's Tavern and he believed she was taken through a place called the Iron Lash.
- Identified the Crimson Reapers, led by Zelt the Rat, as seeking to dominate Nulb's criminal activities and funnel supplies through the area.
- Identified Madame Selentis (also known as Nisera) as the true power in Nulb, a disgraced noble from Verbobonc who controls everything covertly.
- Warned that Madame Selentis noticing the party would result in severe consequences.
- Confirmed the Iron Lash is the local brothel, located upstairs in the Boatman's Tavern.
- Described the Hall of the Scarlet Moon as a druid encampment in the Kron Hills where Elazar teaches the rite of the wicker giant, noting unusual amounts of fire burning there.
- After Serena's rescue, formally welcomed the party as members of the Rhennee clan and offered protection and friendship in Nulb, while warning not to trust all friends.
- Also revealed his camp is being coerced into conducting ambushes along the forest road as a result of the same debt that got Serena taken.

**Vethra Velkori**
Elderly Rhennee matriarch, Eryk's mother. Conducted three divination readings for the party despite her son's protests that it would harm her health:
- Smoke reading revealed visions of a crooked house with shifting shadows, a tavern called the Waterside with too many secrets, and a man with a red sash who deals in whispers and iron.
- Bone reading confirmed Serena was alive and not broken.
- Tarokka reading drew The Chain, The Betrayer, The Flooded Gate, and a spontaneous fourth card depicting a crimson-painted barge on the Velverdyva carrying bound souls — revealing Serena had been traded and transported by barge. Collapsed from exhaustion after completing the reading.

**Tala Velkori**
Eryk's daughter. Approached the party to invite them to meet her grandmother Vethra for a divination. No further information revealed.

**Captain Tolub**
Boisterous river pirate captain present at the Boatman's Tavern. Agreed to buy the party drinks after charm from the party. Revealed Serena had been taken to the Iron Lash as payment for something. When later approached at his boat, directed the party to the Hall of the Scarlet Moon in the Kron Hills, claiming it would connect them to Romag and regional power brokers. This direction was deliberately false or malicious — he sent them to the Fire Temple rather than the Earth Temple, suggesting he is either working with or protecting Fire Temple interests.

**Elvanna**
Operator of the Iron Lash brothel, located upstairs in the Boatman's Tavern. An orange-red-haired woman with a posh accent. Held Serena as compensation for damages the Rhennee allegedly caused at the Boatman's Tavern. Demanded 500 gold pieces for Serena's release and accepted payment without negotiation, remarking she might have asked for more. Released Serena from the pits after payment.

**Elazar**
Named druid who reportedly teaches the rite of the wicker giant at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon. Referenced by Eryk Velkori. Did not appear in session.

**Ostler Goodingoot**
Owner of the tavern in Hommlet and a council member. Directed the party to speak with the local druid Jaroo. No other activity.

**Jaroo**
Local druid in Hommlet. Expressed deep suspicion of the Circle of the Scarlet Moon, characterizing them as too showy, flashy, and ecstatic for true druids, and suggesting their practices were more religious than natural. Warned the party against them.

**Verigo, Mahoon, Iniri, and Faria**
Four human druids camped at Camp One at the Hall of the Scarlet Moon. Friendly and excited about the upcoming rite of the wicker giant. Offered the party food, drink, and brandy, and invited them to stay. Appear to be genuine or at least uninitiated attendees rather than Fire Temple operatives.

**Albrith and Ingulf**
Two robed men at Camp Two, identifying themselves as "circuits of the Scarlet Moon." When the party raised the Temple of Elemental Evil, they adopted threatening postures and confirmed elemental allegiance with the phrase "fire, water, earth, or air." Subsequently attacked the party with devastating fire magic, knocking Calmer unconscious before the party escaped under magical invisibility.

## Faction Movements

**Crimson Reapers**
Led by Zelt the Rat. Actively seeking to dominate Nulb's criminal activities and funnel supplies through the town. No direct operatives encountered this session.

**Nisera's Talons / Madame Selentis**
The dominant covert power in Nulb. Selentis, a disgraced Verbobonc noble now operating as Madame Nisera, controls the town's power structures from the shadows. No direct operatives identified this session beyond the warning from Eryk. Selentis herself did not appear.

**The Iron Lash (Elvanna's operation)**
Operates as a brothel upstairs in the Boatman's Tavern. Used Serena as financial leverage for a Rhennee debt, suggesting the Iron Lash is embedded in the debt and coercion networks of Nulb's criminal underworld. Received 500 gold pieces from the party this session.

**Rhennee (Velkori Clan)**
Lost Serena to the Iron Lash as debt payment. Eryk's camp is being coerced into conducting ambushes along the forest road. Following Serena's rescue, the clan formally adopted the party as family members, offering protection and intelligence access in Nulb. Camp morale fully restored after reunion.

**Fire Temple (Hall of the Scarlet Moon)**
Operating a deceptive recruitment encampment in the Kron Hills northeast of Nulb. Presenting itself publicly as a druidic gathering for the rite of the wicker giant while concealing Fire Temple allegiance. Multiple camps of attendees present, some apparently genuine druids and some confirmed Fire Temple operatives. Attacked the party on contact when the Temple of Elemental Evil was mentioned. Bear containment compromised during the party's escape.

The Hall of the Scarlet Moon is confirmed as a Fire Temple front operation disguised as a druidic gathering. Fire Temple operatives (Albrith and Ingulf) launched an unprovoked magical assault on the party upon detecting Temple of Elemental Evil inquiry. The Fire Temple is actively recruiting, concealing its operations behind a religious-naturalist facade, and has sufficient manpower and resources to maintain a multi-camp encampment in the Kron Hills.

**Kraken Society / Trafficking Network — SCORE INCREASE**
Vethra's divination revealed a crimson-painted barge sailing the Velverdyva carrying bound souls. Serena was confirmed transported by barge and traded like cargo. This suggests an active trafficking operation using river transport — potentially connecting to broader criminal networks beyond Nulb's local factions.

## Revealed Information

- The Iron Lash is not a location outside Nulb — it is the brothel on the upper floor of the Boatman's Tavern, operated by Elvanna.
- Serena's abduction was debt-related; she was taken as collateral for damages the Rhennee allegedly caused, used as leverage to coerce Eryk into conducting ambushes along the forest road.
- The Hall of the Scarlet Moon is a Fire Temple front, not a genuine druidic gathering. Captain Tolub directed the party there deliberately, suggesting he is an informant or ally of the Fire Temple.
- Madame Selentis (Nisera) is identified as the apex criminal power in Nulb, operating covertly with spider-like network control. She is from a disgraced Verbobonc noble family.
- Zelt the Rat leads the Crimson Reapers, who are subordinate to or competing with Selentis's operation.
- The Velverdyva river is being used as a trafficking route by an unidentified faction operating crimson barges.
- Vethra's smoke reading identified a location called the Waterside as a place with too many secrets and referenced a man with a red sash dealing in whispers and iron — an unidentified figure of interest.
