## Revealed Information

- **Lareth** has been manipulating the party as proxies to eliminate Earth Temple rivals and expand Fire Temple territorial control; this confirms Lareth is an active agent of intra-Temple politics, not simply a neutral power
- **The Chest of Rao** is an artifact created by high priests of **Rao**, patron of peace and harmony; it functions as a powerful magical battery capable of fueling large-scale rituals; it is an artifact of great good, not neutral or evil in origin
- Possession of the Chest will attract agents of the Temple of Elemental Evil seeking to weaponize it **and** forces of the Archclericy of Veluna seeking to reclaim it
- **Rannos and Gremag** were confirmed Temple of Elemental Evil operatives, not merely suspicious traders; Rannos had direct knowledge of and desire for the Chest of Rao
- **Thorne** was confirmed to be a mercenary with no deep cult allegiance
- The secret passageway to the ritual chamber was known through **Jerry's family history**—his grandmother had worshipped Beory at the monastery, indicating the passage predates the cult's occupation
- The various elemental temple factions operate as competing criminal territorial organizations all subordinate to the overarching Temple of Elemental Evil
