Notable People of Nulb
The village of Nulb is home to an eclectic array of
powerful and dangerous figures. Each wields influence
in their own way, contributing to the chaos and intrigue
that define the settlement. Below is a summary of the
key individuals whose actions shape the fate of Nulb.
Madame Selentis (alias Lady Nysera
Krivaltis) Click here
Once thought dead after her family’s disgrace, Nysera
reemerges in Nulb under the alias Madame Selentis.
Installed by Hedrack himself, she commands a band of
hardened Temple-backed enforcers from the Old
Mayor’s Manor, overseeing smuggling and logistics in
service of the Temple of Elemental Evil.
Role: Temple Agent and Logistics Overseer
Motivation: To rebuild her power through loyalty to
the Temple and tighten her hold on Nulb.
True Allegiance: The Scarlet Brotherhood seeded
Elvanna in Nulb’s brothel years ago.
Sir Torvel Hawkes Click here
A scarred veteran of the Battle of Emridy Meadows,
Torvel abandoned his noble path to become Selentis’s
sworn protector, serving as both her shield and sword.
Role: Loyal Knight and protector of Selentis.
Motivation: To redeem Nysera’s honor through
devotion and military strength.
Selentis’s Bandits Click here
Assigned by Smigmal Redhand, these hardened
brigands patrol Nulb’s key sites and guard Selentis’s
operations. Their allegiance is less about loyalty to her
and more about Temple backing—but their presence
marks Selentis as a force to be reckoned with.
Role: Selentis’s Enforcers
Motivation: To enforce Selentis’s will and ensure
Temple operations in Nulb.
Romag, Prophet of Earth
Operating through Dick Rentsch and the Waterside
Hostel, Romag manipulates Nulb’s factions to advance
the Earth Temple. His quiet strength is subtle but
pervasive, undermining Selentis.
Role: Earth Temple Leader and Manipulator
Motivation: Expand the Earth Temple’s power.
Belsornig, Prophet of Water
Belsornig’s alliance with Captain Tolub strengthens the
Water Temple’s grip on piracy and trade. His network
destabilizes Selentis’s authority.
Role: Water Temple Leader and Pirate Patron
Motivation: Control Nulb’s waterways and
outmaneuver rival prophets.
Captain Tolub Click here
Master of the Iron Dagger, Tolub is equal parts pirate
and politician. His partnership with Belsornig funds his
ventures, but his growing ambitions put him into
frequent conflict with Selentis and her Temple masters.
Role: Pirate Captain of the Iron Dagger
Motivation: Dominate Nulb’s river trade and amass
wealth while strengthening the Water Temple.
Grud Squinteye Click here
Tolub’s ruthless right hand, Grud relishes bloodshed
and intimidation. His cruel reputation cements the
pirates’ stranglehold on Nulb’s docks.
Role: Lieutenant of the Iron Dagger
Motivation: Prove himself as the deadliest enforcer.
Dick Rentsch Click here
Owner of the Waterside Hostel, Dick poses as a genial
innkeeper while secretly serving the Earth Temple. He
uses his position to collect intelligence and funnel
resources for Romag, always scheming to undercut
Selentis’s growing power.
Role: Hostelier and Earth Temple Agent
Motivation: Expand Earth Temple influence.
Wat Click here
The Waterside Hostel’s bartender hides a more
dangerous role—an informer for the Fire Temple and
Prefect Alrrem. By feeding intelligence across factions,
Wat stirs the pot in Nulb’s deadly rivalries.
Role: Bartender and Fire Temple Spy
Motivation: Aid the Fire Temple by sabotaging its
rivals through espionage.
Skole Click here
The Boatman’s Tavern is Skole’s domain. Outwardly
neutral, he covertly collaborates with the Water Temple,
serving as their eyes and ears in the taverns while
profiting from smuggling.
Role: Proprietor of the Boatman’s Tavern, Water
Temple Collaborator
Motivation: Gain wealth and influence by balancing
neutrality and Temple ties.Nulb’s chaos while
keeping pirates and bandits appeased.
Lodriss Click here
The hidden power behind the Boatman’s Tavern,
Lodriss cultivates ties with Captain Tolub while
carefully preserving her independence. Her sharp
instincts make her a dangerous rival—or ally—
depending on who crosses her path.
Role: Tavern Power Broker and Pirate Ally
Motivation: Leverage her ties with Tolub to remain
influential while avoiding Temple domination.
