Falrinth, Master Wizard, Keeper
of the Orb of Golden Death
A cunning and duplicitous figure, Falrinth operates
within the third level of the Temple of Elemental Evil.
Though he holds no formal allegiance to the elemental
temples or the Greater Temple, his scheming nature
makes him a key player in the Temple’s politics and
intrigue. An opportunist driven by ambition, he carefully
balances alliances and rivalries, ensuring his own
survival while keeping his true motives concealed.
Rogue Wizard
While not an official member of the Greater Temple,
Falrinth is deeply embedded in its web of deception. As
an 8th-level magic-user, he operates independently, both
a valuable ally and a potential threat to the leadership.
“They say the Elder Elemental God whispers in the dark. I’ve
heard those whispers… and one day, I will whisper back.”
Falrinth’s connection to his quasit familiar, Kriitch,
allows him to cast as a 9th-level wizard when within a
one-mile radius. Though he outwardly serves Lolth, the
Demon Queen of Spiders, his true allegiance is to
power itself. He schemes to advance his own goals,
betraying even Lolth if it means seizing greater control..
Ambition: Seeks power above all else, even if it
means betraying Lolth.
Duplicitous Nature: His unpredictability makes him
a potential ally or deadly foe.
Location and Role
Falrinth resides in Area 337 on the third level of the
Temple, where he safeguards his secrets and resources.
His chambers connect to an Escape Tunnel, a hidden
route leading to the Hunting Cottage, used by bandits
allied with Smigmal Redhand.
“The Nodes are not prisons; they are gateways. Those who
master them will rule both this world and the next.” - Falrinth
The Orb of Golden Death
Falrinth secretly possesses the Orb of Golden Death, an
artifact tied to the Elder Elemental God and central to
the Temple’s power. He keeps the Orb hidden from his
allies and superiors, viewing it as his ultimate
bargaining chip or tool for personal ascension.
Connection to the Nodes: The Orb serves as a key to
unlocking and controlling the elemental nodes.
Secrecy: Falrinth’s concealment of the Orb makes
him a target for both the Greater Temple and
adventurers.
