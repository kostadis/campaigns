Deggum, Commander of the
Temple Guards
Deggum, a half-elf with a brooding presence, is a rising
force within the Greater Temple of Elemental Evil. As
commander of the temple guards and their monstrous
allies, he wields considerable influence over the cult’s
security and military actions. His rapid rise stems from
a mix of ruthless pragmatism, unshakable loyalty to
Hedrack, and an uncanny ability to secure alliances with
the Temple’s less savory forces.
Appearance
Deggum’s gaunt frame, lank black hair, and piercing
green eyes lend him an eerie, almost spectral quality.
His face, perpetually shadowed with exhaustion, bears
deep lines of stress and calculation. A pronounced limp
does little to slow his predatory movements, making
him an unsettling figure even among the Temple’s
hardened warriors. He wears dusky-hued studded
leather armor, enchanted for both protection and
silence, and carries a morningstar, crossbow, hunting
knife—selected for efficiency in eliminating threats.
Personality
Deggum is a ruthless strategist who thrives in the
shadows, pulling strings with quiet precision rather
than overt displays of power. His unwavering loyalty to
Hedrack is born from both admiration and calculation—
he sees the Supreme Commander as a model of control
and dominance. Unlike his more flamboyant peers,
Deggum embodies cold efficiency, enforcing discipline
with a careful blend of fear and necessity
Cold-Blooded Tactician: Anticipates every variable,
ensuring no move is made without careful
calculation.
Enforcer of Order: Keeps his troops obedient
through a precise balance of intimidation and
calculated rewards.
Manipulative strategist: Masters the art of deception.
Role in the Temple of Elemental Evil
Deggum oversees the Temple’s security with a
meticulous and ruthless approach, ensuring its defenses
remain impenetrable. He coordinates patrols,
fortifications, and rapid response units to neutralize
intrusions. When threats arise, Deggum personally
leads scouting missions, eliminating dangers before
they reach the Temple’s doorstep. Ever watchful, he
remains the last line of defense against those who seek
to dismantle the cult.

Background
Born in the shadowed groves of Welkwood, Deggum
turned his back on his homeland in pursuit of forbidden
power. Lareth the Beautiful, a master manipulator, saw
potential in the young half-elf and whispered promises
of dominance and destiny. Under Lareth’s tutelage,
Deggum honed his skills in deception, strategy, and
coercion. His true ascent began when he brokered an
alliance with the Blackthorn ogres under Chief
Garghuk, proving his value to Hedrack and securing his
place within the Greater Temple’s inner circle.
Key Events in His Rise to Power
Forsaken Heritage: Cast aside his ties to the elves of
Welkwood, choosing power over tradition.
Recruitment by Lareth: Learned the tenets of the
Elder Elemental God.
Ogre Pact: Secured the allegiance of the Blackthorn
ogres, delivering Chief Garghuk’s warriors to the
Greater Temple.
“I didn’t leave Welkwood to cower in the shadows. I came to
rise above those who scorned me, and I will.” - Deggum
Motivations
Deggum is consumed by ambition, seeking to carve out
a legacy within the Temple’s hierarchy. Though fiercely
loyal to Hedrack, he secretly harbors dreams of
surpassing him. To solidify his power, Deggum focuses
on strengthening his grip over the temple guards and
ogre forces, ensuring their unwavering allegiance to him
alone.
Despite his public devotion to the Elder Elemental
God, Deggum knows the truth—Zuggtmoy, not the
Elder Elemental Eye, holds the Temple’s reins. He
carefully balances his allegiance, outwardly preaching
elemental destruction while privately safeguarding his
own future within the cult’s shifting tides of power.
Role in the Temple of Elemental Evil
Deggum commands the Temple’s security with ruthless
efficiency, ensuring its defenses remain impenetrable.
He oversees patrols, fortifications, and rapid-response
forces to neutralize intrusions before they escalate.
Beyond mere defense, he leads scouting missions to
monitor threats and identify potential weaknesses in
enemy forces. His influence extends to the Temple’s
monstrous allies, whom he recruits and indoctrinates,
bolstering the cult’s military strength under the guise of
the Elder Elemental God’s prophesied awakening.
Fortress Guardian: Orchestrates defenses, ensuring
the Temple’s inner sanctum remains unbreachable.
Tactical Expansion: Strengthens Temple forces by
recruiting monstrous allies and mercenaries.
Espionage and Scouting: Oversees reconnaissance
operations, tracking enemy movements and
preempting attacks.
Political Relationships
As commander, Deggum navigates the temple’s volatile
political landscape, balancing loyalty to Hedrack while
countering rivals within the Greater Temple. Both
Hedrack and Senshock personally mentor him,
recognizing his potential for leadership. Ever diligent,
Deggum carries his spellbook at all times, refining his
mastery of divine and arcane magic—many of his spells
mirroring Senshock’s, ensuring his versatility in both
faith and sorcery.
Allies and Relationships
Hedrack, Supreme Commander: One of Hedrack’s
most trusted subordinates, Deggum receives direct
guidance from him.
Senshock, High Wizard: Deggum respects
Senshock’s magical prowess but wary of ambitions.
Romag, Earth Temple Leader: Maintains a tenuous
alliance by supplying Romag with ogre forces.
“Let Romag and Belsornig squabble over their petty rivalries.
In the end, the one who controls the gates controls the
Temple.” - Commander Deggum
Rivals and Threats
Belsornig, Water Temple Leader: The two compete
for control over the temple’s defensive resources.
Commander Feldrin: Their differing approaches to
leadership often place them at odds.
Recruitment of the Blackthorn Tribe
Deggum’s most valuable contribution to the temple is
securing the allegiance of the Blackthorn Tribe, a brutal
force of ogres, bugbears, and hobgoblins. Through the
influence of Iuz and careful negotiation, he brokered a
pact with Chieftain Garghuk the Bloodtooth, providing
the temple with a steady stream of warriors—driven by
fear rather than loyalty..
Negotiation with Garghuk: Maintaining a delicate
balance of fear / reward to keep the chieftain in line.
Deployment of Forces: Strategically placing ogres
within key positions across the temple’s various
levels, including the Greater Temple.
Oversight of Ogre Leadership: Managing Chieftain
Gloarok in the Greater Temple and Gerard, who
assists Romag in the Earth Temple.
Intelligence Gathering: Utilizing Blackthorn scouts
to gather intelligence on rival factions, adventurers,
and local threats around Nulb and Hommlet.
“Hedrack sees potential in me, and I will not disappoint. The
ogres follow because they fear me… as they should. Loyalty
bought with fear and blood is the only kind that matters.”
