Falrinth, Master Wizard, Keeper
of the Orb of Golden Death
A cunning and duplicitous figure, Falrinth operates
within the third level of the Temple of Elemental Evil.
Though he holds no formal allegiance to the elemental
temples or the Greater Temple, his scheming nature
makes him a key player in the Temple’s politics and
intrigue. An opportunist driven by ambition, he carefully
balances alliances and rivalries, ensuring his own
survival while keeping his true motives concealed.
Rogue Wizard
While not an official member of the Greater Temple,
Falrinth is deeply embedded in its web of deception. As
an 8th-level magic-user, he operates independently, both
a valuable ally and a potential threat to the leadership.
“They say the Elder Elemental God whispers in the dark. I’ve
heard those whispers… and one day, I will whisper back.”
Falrinth’s connection to his quasit familiar, Kriitch,
allows him to cast as a 9th-level wizard when within a
one-mile radius. Though he outwardly serves Lolth, the
Demon Queen of Spiders, his true allegiance is to
power itself. He schemes to advance his own goals,
betraying even Lolth if it means seizing greater control..
Ambition: Seeks power above all else, even if it
means betraying Lolth.
Duplicitous Nature: His unpredictability makes him
a potential ally or deadly foe.
Location and Role
Falrinth resides in Area 337 on the third level of the
Temple, where he safeguards his secrets and resources.
His chambers connect to an Escape Tunnel, a hidden
route leading to the Hunting Cottage, used by bandits
allied with Smigmal Redhand.
“The Nodes are not prisons; they are gateways. Those who
master them will rule both this world and the next.” - Falrinth
The Orb of Golden Death
Falrinth secretly possesses the Orb of Golden Death, an
artifact tied to the Elder Elemental God and central to
the Temple’s power. He keeps the Orb hidden from his
allies and superiors, viewing it as his ultimate
bargaining chip or tool for personal ascension.
Connection to the Nodes: The Orb serves as a key to
unlocking and controlling the elemental nodes.
Secrecy: Falrinth’s concealment of the Orb makes
him a target for both the Greater Temple and
adventurers.

Role and Loyalties
Falrinth is loosely allied with Smigmal Redhand and
her bandit crew, including Commander Feldrin , who
operates out of the Broken Falrinth maintains a loose
alliance with Smigmal Redhand and her bandit crew,
including Commander Feldrin, who operates from the
Broken Tower. However, his true loyalty lies only with
himself. Though he cooperates with these factions,
Falrinth is ultimately an opportunist, always seeking the
greatest personal advantage.
He also collaborates with Lareth the Beautiful, both
serving as agents of Lolth, the Demon Queen of
Spiders. Their shared mission, as dictated by Lolth, is to
gain control of the elemental nodes (Nexus), though the
full scope of their plan remains hidden.
Relationships & Manipulations
Senshock: A Cautious Superior
Falrinth reports to Senshock, High Commander of the
Greater Temple, but their relationship is based on
mutual distrust. Senshock sees Falrinth as a useful but
untrustworthy resource, aware that his loyalty to Lolth
and secrecy regarding the Orb of Golden Death could
threaten the Greater Temple’s unity.
Smigmal Redhand & Feldrin: Bandit
Connections
Smigmal Redhand: An ally of convenience, Falrinth
works with her bandits, though his loyalty is tenuous
at best.
Commander Feldrin: While Feldrin reports to
Falrinth indirectly through Smigmal, this chain of
loyalty is weak and unreliable.
Lareth the Beautiful: A Dangerous
Partnership
Lolth’s Plan: Both serve Lolth, working to
manipulate the elemental nodes, possibly
undermining Zuggtmoy and the Elder Elemental
God.
Fragile Alliance: Despite their shared devotion to
Lolth, ambition makes their alliance precarious and
could lead to betrayal.
“Hedrack and his ilk think themselves untouchable, yet they
dance like puppets on strings they cannot see. Strings I can
cut.”
Falrinth’s Tactics and Abilities
A cautious strategist, Falrinth relies on magic,
deception, and his quasit familiar to outmaneuver
threats.
Kriitch the Quasit: Spies in giant centipede form,
relaying information.
Escape Tunnel: Enables quick retreats or ambushes.
Preparedness: Uses divination and illusions to
manipulate encounters.
Smigmal Redhand, Commander
A ruthless and cunning half-orc Fighter/Assassin,
Smigmal Redhand commands the outer defenses of the
Temple of Elemental Evil. Leading mercenaries and
bandits from the ruined tower, she serves as the
Temple’s first line of defense, eliminating threats with
cold efficiency. Using a Hat of Disguise, she
masquerades as an elf, blending deception with
assassination to manipulate both high society and
adventurers who stray too close.
Master of Deception and Deadly
Precision
Smigmal is an imposing six-foot figure, her lean, sinewy
frame hardened by years of battle and subterfuge. Her
sharp, angular features and piercing yellow eyes betray
her orcish blood, though few ever see her true face.
Under her magical elven guise, she appears as a refined
noblewoman, effortlessly infiltrating enemy ranks and
social circles.
Personality
Smigmal is a cold, calculating assassin with an iron will.
She believes in striking from the shadows and
maintaining absolute control over her surroundings.
Her loyalty is primarily to Falrinth, her lover and secret
ally within the temple, but she harbors ambitions of her
own. Ruthless, pragmatic, and untrusting, Smigmal
excels at manipulating others to serve her interests.
Calculating and Ruthless: Smigmal values efficiency
over morality, always seeking the most pragmatic
solution.
Devoted to Falrinth: She prioritizes her relationship
with the wizard over temple politics.
Paranoia and Mistrust: Never truly trusts her
superiors, always keeping an escape route in mind.
“Hedrack plays the king, Senshock schemes for the
throne, and Barkinar waits like a vulture. I simply watch
and sharpen my knives.” - Smigmal
