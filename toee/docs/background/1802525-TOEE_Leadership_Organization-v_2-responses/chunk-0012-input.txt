The Four Elemental Temples
The four elemental cults—Earth, Air, Fire, and Water—
operate semi-independently under the Greater Temple’s
authority. Each is led by a high priest who vies for
supremacy, seeking to prove their element’s dominance
over the others. The four lesser elemental temples—
Earth, Air, Fire, and Water—are devoted to the Elder
Elemental God, believing their power stems from its
boundless elemental chaos. Each faction believes their
element is supreme, their rituals, they will eventually
awaken (EEG) to reshape the world in their image.
The Black Earth (Earth Temple)
Romag (Earth Temple): Click here Is a calculating
and methodical leader who values stability and
secrecy above all else. He strengthens his influence
in Nulb through intermediaries and spies, but he
fears the Water Temple’s growing power.
Belief: The Elder Elemental God embodies the
foundation of existence. Only through destruction
and decay can new empires rise from the ruins of the
old.
Goal: Weaken Veluna and Furyondy by sabotaging
agriculture, inciting earthquakes, and toppling
fortifications.
Relationships: Feuds with Belsornig and Alrrem
while secretly seeking Hedrack’s favor.
The Howling Hatred (Air Temple)
Kelno (Air Temple): Click here Is arrogant and
fanatical, convinced that he alone speaks for
Elemental Evil. He plots against the Fire and Water
Temples through sabotage and subterfuge, though
his influence is waning.
Belief: The Elder Elemental God manifests as an
invisible storm, bringing whispers of chaos and the
power to sweep away civilization.
Goal: Focuses on controlling the skies, manipulating
weather, and summoning devastating winds to erode
their enemies’ morale.
Relationships: Distrusts Belsornig, fears Alrrem, and
seeks Romag’s alliance.
The Eternal Flame (Fire Temple)
Alrrem (Fire Temple): Click here Is aggressive and
ambitious, forging alliances with fire giants and
other destructive forces. He dreams of a world
consumed by flame, with the Temple at its heart.
Belief: The Elder Elemental God is fire incarnate,
consuming all in its path and purging the weak.
Goal: Spreading destruction through arson, terror
tactics, and fire-wielding warriors. They seek to burn
away the old world to make way for the new.
Relationships: Holds deep grudges against Romag
and Kelno, seeks Belsornig’s destruction.
The Crushing Wave (Water Temple)
Belsornig (Water Temple): Click here Is the most
cunning of the four high priests, ruling through an
intricate network of spies in Nulb and beyond. His
ultimate ambition is to dominate the Temple—even if
it means betraying the Elder Elemental God.
Belief: The Elder Elemental God resides deep within
the endless seas, waiting to drown the world in an
eternal deluge.
Goal: Flood the lands, sabotage trade routes, and
infiltrate coastal cities with spies and saboteurs.
Relationships: Seeks to manipulate Kelno and
eliminate Alrrem, while keeping Romag at bay.
Iuz’s Influence
Iuz, the demigod of deceit and domination, sees the
Temple of Elemental Evil as a pawn in his grand
strategy of conquest. The Temple’s clergy believe they
serve the Elder Elemental God, but in truth, their spells
come from Zuggtmoy, the Demon Queen of Fungi. Iuz
aims to subjugate Zuggtmoy while turning the Temple
into a launching point for war against Furyondy, Veluna,
and Verbobonc.
Hedrack’s Role: Hedrack, Supreme Commander of
the Greater Temple, is Iuz’s enforcer, keeping the
elemental cults in check while secretly orchestrating
Zuggtmoy’s release.
Political Struggles: Many clerics and warlords within
the Temple bristle at Iuz’s growing dominance,
clinging to their faith in the Elder Elemental God—
whether real or imagined. This brewing dissent
forces Hedrack to tread carefully, balancing fear and
diplomacy to maintain control.
Strategic Goal: Iuz’s ultimate goal is to forge a portal
linking the elemental nodes to his capital, Dorakaa,
unleashing his armies upon the heart of the
Flanaess.
Lolth’s Infiltration
As Iuz commands the Temple from above, Lolth, the
Demon Queen of Spiders, spins a web of deceit within
its ranks. The Temple stands upon an ancient site
sacred to the Elder Elemental God, and Lolth is
determined to prevent its full resurgence. Holding the
key to the Elder Elemental God’s prison, she is a crucial
force in deciding its fate. Unlike Iuz, who rules through
brute strength and fear, Lolth prefers subterfuge,
deception, and betrayal.
Lareth the Beautiful: A charismatic recruiter and
secret agent of Lolth, luring followers into her web of
intrigue.
Falrinth: A cunning wizard who serves Lolth from
the shadows while feigning loyalty to Hedrack.
Tactics: Sowing discord through misinformation,
eroding Iuz’s grip on the Temple, and fueling
internal rivalrie.
