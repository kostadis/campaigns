The Greater Temple
he Greater Temple of the Elder Elemental God stands as the true seat of power within the Temple of
Elemental Evil. While the lesser Elemental Temples—Earth, Air, Fire, and Water—battle for dominance,
the Greater Temple orchestrates their conflicts, ensuring they remain pawns in a grander scheme. The
elemental cultists believe they labor to awaken the Elder Elemental God, but only the highest-ranking
members know the truth: the Temple serves Zuggtmoy, the Demon Queen of Fungi. Yet even this secret
is clouded by an even darker force—Iuz, the Old One, who bends the Temple to his own ambitions. The
Greater Temple’s leadership is a web of shifting allegiances, deceit, and dark devotion, where the
strongest seek to control the elemental factions while advancing their own schemes
Hedrack – Supreme Commander
Hedrack, Supreme Commander of the Greater Temple,
is a ruthless and calculating high priest, personally
appointed by Iuz to oversee the Temple of Elemental
Evil. A master of political intrigue and religious zealotry,
he skillfully maintains the elemental cults’ allegiance
while suppressing Zuggtmoy’s lingering influence. He
views the Temple’s success as a stepping stone to
greater power under Iuz’s dominion.
Personality: Commanding, ruthless, and unwavering
in his devotion to Iuz.
Goals: Ensure the Temple’s resurgence, maintain
order of elemental factions, increase Iuz’s influence
Strengths: Powerful cleric, master manipulator,
feared by allies and enemies alike
Weaknesses: Underestimates rivals, views nonIuzian cultists as expendable
Rivals: Senshock covertly works to unseat him,
viewing himself as the Temple’s rightful master.
Barkinar positions himself as his successor. Lolth’s
agents seek to erode his control from the shadows.
“Power is not granted. It is taken from the bones of
the weak—and I leave nothing behind.” - Hedrack,
Mouth of Iuz

“Earth crumbles, fire fades, water dries, and air scatters. Only
chaos is eternal, and only I wield the reins of that chaos.”
Hedrack is the Temple of Elemental Evil’s most
formidable antagonist, surpassed only by Zuggtmoy
herself. As Supreme Commander and Mighty Priest of
Iuz, he wields immense magical and political power,
ruling through fear and ruthless ambition. His influence
stretches across the Temple’s factions, forcing chaos
into submission under his singular, terrifying vision.
Appearance and Presence
Draped in flowing black and crimson robes inscribed
with the sigils of Iuz, Hedrack’s commanding presence
exudes an air of menace, his cold gaze capable of
unsettling even the most battle-hardened warriors
Agent of Iuz, the Elder Elemental Eye
Hedrack carefully navigates the Temple’s fractured
loyalties, ensuring Iuz’s dominance while keeping
Zuggtmoy’s influence contained. He rewards obedience
and eliminates dissent before it becomes a threat.
Iuz’s Emissary: Sent by Iuz to oversee the Temple,
Hedrack is tasked with preparing the cult’s forces for
conquest and securing Zuggtmoy’s debt to Iuz.
Zuggtmoy’s Influence: While he outwardly maintains
loyalty to Zuggtmoy, he regards her imprisonment as
a convenient tool to keep the temple under his
control. To exploit without truly freeing her.
Hedrack’s Relationships and Allies
Hedrack rules the Temple’s leadership through
calculated terror and ruthless alliances, ever watchful
for those who dare challenge his dominion.
Barkinar Commander of the Greater Temple Troops
Hedrack promoted Barkinar to oversee the temple’s
growing military forces. He considers Barkinar loyal but
keeps him occupied with military concerns to prevent
political aspirations. Though a trusted enforcer,
Hedrack ensures Barkinar remains preoccupied with
warfare, preventing him from seeking greater power.
Deggum Commander of Temple Security
Hedrack has shaped Deggum into a devoted enforcer,
overseeing Temple security and reporting only to him.
While fiercely loyal, whispers of his ambition and
rumored ties to Lolth make him a dangerous asset—one
Hedrack keeps on a short leash
Feldrin Bandit Commander and Enforcer
Feldrin leads the Temple’s raiders, striking at
Hedrack’s enemies and securing vital supplies. Yet his
mercenary heart makes his loyalty fragile—an
expendable blade kept sharp for as long as he remains
useful.
Lucius Graeme Courier and Spy Master
Lucius acts as a courier and spy for the Greater Temple.
He weaves a web of whispers for Hedrack, ferrying
secrets between the Temple’s agents. His intelligence is
invaluable, often allowing Hedrack to silence dissent
before it grows. Yet trust is fleeting in the Temple—so
long as Lucius remains useful, he lives.
Falrinth Wizard, Keeper of the Orb of Golden Death
Though Falrinth reports indirectly through Senshock,
Falrinth’s arcane knowledge is invaluable to the
Temple, but his true loyalties are a shadowed enigma.
Hedrack suspects his ties to Lolth and watches him
with quiet paranoia, waiting for the moment to dispose
of him—without jeopardizing the Temple’s arcane
power.
Senshock Lord Wizard of the Greater Temple:
Senshock, the Temple’s master of arcane defenses, is
Hedrack’s most dangerous rival. A cunning and
paranoid wizard, he views himself as the true power
behind the cult and longs to unseat Hedrack. Though
their alliance is uneasy, Hedrack tolerates Senshock’s
expertise while constantly working to curb his influence.
Lareth the Beautiful (Moathouse Leader)
Hedrack sees Lareth as a valuable but dangerously
ambitious servant of the Elder Elemental God. While he
supports Lareth’s efforts at the Moathouse, he remains
cautious of his growing influence, unaware that Lareth
is, in truth, an agent of Lolth.
“Lareth the Beautiful is the dark hope of chaotic evil: young,
handsome, well endowed in abilities and aptitudes, thoroughly
wicked, depraved and capricious.” - Gary Gygax
Smigmal Redhand, Shadow of the Temple
Smigmal, the Temple’s elusive assassin, commands the
brigands in the outer ruins, yet her true loyalties lie with
her lover, Falrinth. While she serves Hedrack in name,
she operates in secrecy, using a hat of disguise to
infiltrate and manipulate from the shadows. Hedrack
tolerates her presence for now, but he watches closely,
ready to eliminate her should she overstep her bounds.

Senshock – Lord Wizard
Senshock, the Temple’s master of arcane defense, is a
cunning and ruthless wizard who views the Temple as a
tool for his own ascension. While he publicly serves
Hedrack, he secretly maneuvers to become the true
power behind the cult. Ensuring the Temple’s magical
fortifications remain impenetrable, Senshock works
tirelessly to eliminate any rival who threatens his
growing dominion.
Position: Lord Wizard of the Temple of Elemental
Evil
Personality: Coldly arrogant, ruthlessly ambitious,
and deeply paranoid.
Goals: Seize ultimate power through arcane
mastery, eliminate Hedrack, and reshape the Temple
to his vision.
Strengths: Unmatched in magical defenses, skilled
in manipulation and deception.
Weaknesses: Trusts no one, blinded by his own
genius, and prone to overcomplicated schemes
The Master of Arcane and Deception
A sinister figure draped in black and crimson robes,
Senshock is a vision of arcane mastery. Silver-threaded
sigils of Zuggtmoy and Iuz decorate his garments, and a
distinctive scarab pendant marked ‘TZGY’ grants him
control over the Temple’s hidden mechanisms. His
once-dark hair is now streaked with silver, his narrow
face frozen in perpetual disdain. Behind his piercing
eyes lies a mind brimming with forbidden knowledge. In
his grasp, a staff crowned with a pulsating black crystal
radiates dark power—a conduit of destruction and a
testament to his authority.
Daily Operations and Leadership
Senshock’s contributions extend beyond the battlefield;
his daily routine is filled with a variety of crucial
activities aimed at fortifying the temple.
Arcane Research: Obsessively experiments with
forbidden magics, crafting spells and artifacts to
increase his dominion..
Oversight: Manipulates the Temple’s clergy and
elemental factions, ensuring they remain divided and
reliant on his counsel.
Command Over Giants: Keeps the Temple’s
monstrous allies—giants, trolls, and demons—in
check through displays of raw magical supremacy.
