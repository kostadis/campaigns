Lucius Graeme, Temple Agent
Courier (a.k.a. Aldren)
Lucius Graeme, a Oeridian/Suel operative, commands
attention—not through stature, but through an aura of
quiet menace. His portly frame, draped in simple gray
garments, conceals a calculating and dangerous mind. A
small gray hat often shadows his scarred face, marked
by an old injury from falling debris. His golden,
asymmetrical eyes seem to uncover secrets no one
wishes to reveal, and his smooth, unsettling voice has a
way of disarming or unnerving listeners.
Personality
Once a vain and cruel nobleman, Lucius’s fall from
grace left him with a bitter hatred for order and
morality. Now, he is a manipulative schemer, wielding
intelligence and deception like a blade. Beneath his
calm exterior simmers a barely controlled rage, which
he unleashes without mercy when provoked.
Master Manipulator: Feigns weakness, allowing
enemies to underestimate him.
Chaos as Power: Believes disorder breeds
opportunity, shaping the world in his image.
Devotion to Elemental Forces: Sees the primordial
powers of the elemental planes as neither good nor
evil—only tools for the strong.
“Chaos is the truest form of control—those who embrace it will
shape the world to their will.” - Aldren
The Art of Deception
Lucius delights in playing the fool, allowing others to
underestimate him while he quietly gathers intelligence
and consolidates power. He thrives in chaos, seeing it as
the ultimate tool of control. To him, the elemental
planes are not moral forces, but raw engines of
destruction and renewal, waiting to be harnessed by
those strong enough to command them.
“Civilization is a fragile mask over the face of the
elemental chaos. It’s a shame when it breaks… or a
delight.“ - Lucius Graeme
