55. Hunting Cottage and Barn
The hunting cottage is a vital logistical node for the
operations of the Temple of Elemental Evil. Located
south of Nulb and near the Imeryd’s Run, this
unassuming log structure masks a sinister purpose: it
serves as a supply depot, a slave-handling station, and a
gateway into the Temple itself. Hidden deep within the
Gnarley Forest, it is a place of grim purpose and dark
machinations, overseen by loyal agents of the Greater
Temple and guarded by Deggum’s men.
The Cottage and Barn
Structure: A rustic log hunting Cottage and barn
with a simple but sturdy construction, sitting near
the ford of a meandering stream.
Water Well: Weed and algae crusted water well.
Surroundings: Dense woods encircle the Cottage,
though a wide, well-worn path leads directly to it,
bearing wagon ruts from regular supply deliveries.
Hidden Tunnel Operations
The tunnel connects directly to the Greater Temple’s
Broken Tower. Most of the traffic includes essential
goods, weapons, and slaves, ensuring the Temple
remains stocked without raising suspicion in Nulb.
Defenses and Security Measures
Deadly traps concealed within thick underbrush
surrounding the cottage and barn.
Carefully reset daily by Ralf and rotating patrol
bandits, ensuring constant vigilance.
Dire Hounds:
Three ferocious dire hounds patrol the immediate
cottage grounds.
Trained to respond solely to Gefreid’s commands,
attacking intruders without hesitation.
Tunnel Security:
The concealed tunnel entrance is constantly guarded
by pairs of well-armed bandits, rotating shifts every
few hours.
Rune-marked alarm stones linked directly to Temple
spellcasters alert Gefreid and Feldrin to intrusion
attempts.
Natural Cave Outpost (Bugbear & Goblin Guards):
Midway along the tunnel, a recently excavated
passage connects to a natural cave serving as a
fortified checkpoint.
A band of disciplined Bugbears and Goblins under
Temple command monitor this critical junction,
demanding passwords or tokens of authority from
passing personnel.
Guarded Water Well (Giant Rats):
The secret tunnel ultimately terminates near an old,
stone-lined water well deep below the Greater
Temple complex.
This final passage is infested with aggressive giant
rats purposefully bred and released to attack
intruders and deter unauthorized access.
