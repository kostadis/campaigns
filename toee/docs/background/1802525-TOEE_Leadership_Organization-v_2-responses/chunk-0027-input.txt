Chains of Command
Breaking the Supply Lines of the Temple and the
Crimson Reapers
Introduction
The adventurers have uncovered critical intelligence
about Commander Feldrin, the ruthless overseer of the
Temple of Elemental Evil’s supply operations, who
manages a complex logistical web feeding the Greater
Temple’s sinister ambitions. Operating alongside
Feldrin is the Crimson Reapers mercenary group, led by
the mage-bandit Rom Khavernus, whose brigands
terrorize local roads, delivering prisoners and
contraband directly into Temple hands.
Yet Feldrin’s network extends deeper into Nulb itself.
Mistress Selentis, the cunning logistics overseer
operating from the Old Mayor’s Manor, coordinates
crucial shipments between pirates, smugglers, and the
Greater Temple, manipulating local factions and
maintaining the fragile balance of power. Meanwhile,
Lucius Graeme, Hedrack’s trusted courier, ensures
orders and intelligence flow smoothly between Hedrack,
Feldrin, and Selentis.
With the Temple’s reach expanding rapidly into the
Viscounty of Verbobonc, the adventurers must quickly
disrupt this logistical web, creating internal turmoil, the
Temple’s unity, and crippling its war machine.
Primary Objectives
Infiltrate the Waterside Hostel and Identify Crimson
Reapers Brigand Network
Objective: Obtain critical intelligence from Rom
Khavernus or his agents regarding the nature and
timing of Temple shipments and identify his
connections to Mistress Selentis and Feldrin.
Disrupt Mistress Selentis’s Operations at the Old
Mayor’s Manor
Objective: Sabotage Selentis’s key logistical hub.
Destroy records, intercept slave transfers, disrupt
financial exchanges, and eliminate or capture
Selentis’s men to paralyze her role in the Temple.
Sabotage the Boatman’s Warehouse, Nulb Pier
Objective: Destroy pirate-held goods, weapons
caches, and slave holding areas. Capture or
eliminate key pirate figures to sever the river-based
supply chain into Nulb.
Disable the Wainwright Transport Hub
Objective: Destroy supply wagons and confiscate
shipment records; eliminating Maddok cripples
overland routes connecting bandit raids directly to
Feldrin’s forces.
Destroy the Hunting Cottage and Secret Tunnel Relay
Point
Objective: Sabotage the secure relay point, collapse
the hidden tunnel linking directly to the Greater
Temple, and neutralize Gefreid Patris to disrupt
Feldrin’s supply corridor.
Capture or Eliminate Commander Feldrin
Objective: Removing Commander Feldrin eliminates
a key Temple strategist and severely disrupts
logistics coordination.
Key Locations & Challenges
Waterside Hostel (Nulb)
Challenge: Dick Rentsch’s wary guards and
informants; navigating hostile bandit regulars loyal
to Rom Khavernus.
Opportunities: Valuable intelligence about Selentis’s
operations, Feldrin’s schedules, and Rom’s
connection to the Temple.
Old Mayor’s Manor (Mistress Selentis’s Hub)
Challenge: Madame Selentis’s disciplined bandit
enforcers; magical defenses and concealed
documents detailing supply chain operations.
Opportunities: Crucial records and prisoner
locations; potential to turn Selentis’s lieutenants
against her through bribery or threats.
Boatman’s Tavern and Warehouse
Challenge: Pirate crews under Vesrek and Tolub;
heavily fortified warehouses.
Opportunities: Sever river-based smuggling routes,
gain vital documents connecting pirates to the
Temple’s leadership.
Wainwright Depot (Managed by Ivar Maddok)
Challenge: Maddok’s violent bandit guards and
security measures; booby-trapped storage yards.
Opportunities: Disrupt key overland supply routes;
seize shipment manifests revealing Feldrin’s next
moves.
Hunting Cottage and Secret Tunnel (Gefreid Patris)
Challenge: Heavily guarded by Greater Temple
bandits, traps, dire hounds, bugbear/goblin guards in
the tunnel, and giant rats near the secret well
entrance.
Opportunities: Destroy the main relay point between
Feldrin’s operations and the Greater Temple’s lower
levels, permanently disrupting supply lines.
Aftermath & Consequences
Internal Chaos: Successfully disrupting supply
chains and eliminating key figures like Feldrin or
Selentis creates power vacuums and intensified
factional infighting within the Temple.
Increased Resistance: Surviving leaders, including
Smigmal and Lucius, rallying surviving forces to
retaliate against the adventurers, increasing the
danger of future engagements.
“Pirates, bandits, priests—we’re all chained
together by coin. The only freedom left in Nulb is
choosing who holds your leash.”
--- Lodriss, Proprietor of the Boatman’s Tavern
