# Chapter 01 And so it begins

Date: Planting 1 through 15, 579 

The heroes arrived in Hommlet, a small village at the edge of Verbobonc’s territory near the Gnarley Forest. They had protected a caravan from Narwell that went through the forest. The journey through the forest leads them to discover brigands. One of those Brigands worked for Canon Belsoring, a leader of the Temple of Water. 

Upon arrival, they visited the Inn of the Welcome Wench, met Spugnoir Balkof and Furnok, the fabulous rogue, and encountered the innkeeper Osler Goodingoat. The group also visited the church of St. Cuthbert, where the cleric was offered to join the group. They visited the traders Rannos and Gremag, who convinced them to hire men-at-arms for protection. They visited the leatherworker who would sell all his goods through the traders. The group is currently at the Inn of the Welcome Wench.
