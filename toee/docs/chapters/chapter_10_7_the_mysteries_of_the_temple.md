# Chapter 10.7 The mysteries of the temple

* **The Combat Trial:** The session opened with the party engaging in a grueling, non-lethal combat trial against the monastery's martial monks to test their strength and weaknesses. During the fierce exchange of longswords and short swords, Nicholas was knocked unconscious but was swiftly revived by Calmer's healing magic.  
* **The Interrogation:** Following the bout, Zephyr and Ziinnia ruthlessly interrogated a captive monk, with Zephyr successfully using intimidation to shatter the monastery's peaceful facade. The captive confessed that the monastery was not devoted to the nature goddess Beory, but to the Earth Cult.  
* **The Titan's Awakening:** The captive further revealed that a catastrophic ritual to summon a Titan was already underway, orchestrated by the High Templar Tharok Stevan (Tharok Stonevein).  
* **The Infiltration Plan:** Learning they needed to access the restricted library to stop the ritual, the party forged a tactical alliance with the defector Brother Eldren. They split their forces: Ziinnia and the GM's character would confront the supposed cult leader Sister Alina, with Eldren providing a distraction, while Zephyr and Nicholas infiltrated the library.  
* **The Labyrinthine Library:** Zephyr and Nicholas navigated a magical library filled with floating books, glowing runes, and earth magic. After struggling with initial investigation checks, they deciphered a clue regarding four stones and four passages, ultimately revealing a hidden door by knocking three times on a specific bookshelf.  
* **The Book Swarm:** Beyond the hidden door, they encountered a puzzle requiring four specific books. Searching the shelves for the missing fourth tome triggered the library's magical defenses, surrounding them in a violent swarm of animated books. Zephyr and Nicholas shredded the swarms in combat, causing the remaining books to vanish and revealing the path forward.  
* **The Elemental Riddle:** The session concluded with the party deciphering a cryptic poem about the four elements, correctly deducing that the overarching answer to the riddle was the Temple of Elemental Evil itself
