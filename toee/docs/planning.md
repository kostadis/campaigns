## DM Notes

**Convergence: The War Council as a Pressure Cooker**
The war council is the immediate scene. It will put Calmer's cover integrity under direct stress — he must perform Earth Temple loyalty in front of Hartsch with the Chest of Rao in the room, after having killed Romag and manipulated the faction's war. Zephyr will be performing compliance while sitting on the Deep Temple route and chafing at Hartsch's leash. The council is the ideal moment for a revelation, a demand, or a request that forces the party to choose between Hartsch's agenda and their own.

**Convergence: Zephyr's Wraith Debt Meets the Deep Temple Route**
The path to the primary objective runs directly through the wraith. Zephyr's reduced maximum HP is an unresolved mechanical wound. Calmer's Channel Divinity is the only reliable tool — and Calmer is the member of the party Zephyr is most alienated from. The confrontation with the wraith will be as much a character moment as a combat encounter. Consider whether the wraith's "one of you is going to die today" declaration has a specific target in mind.

**Convergence: Kelno and the Necromancer May Be the Same Person**
The ghouls were recently-dead Earth Temple members raised during the engineered war. The Air Temple passage route came from Kelno. The Deep Temple is the apex of the entire operation. If Kelno is directing necromantic operations against the Earth Temple while simultaneously holding the Deep Temple route, the party is about to walk into the domain of someone who already knows the dungeon's internal war better than Hartsch does.

**Foreshadowing Opportunity: Terjon and Y'dey**
Calmer's most important NPC bond in Hommlet is his obligation to Terjon. If Terjon is complicit in Y'dey's disappearance — or if Y'dey's disappearance is Temple-related — then the obligation is held by an adversary, and Calmer's intelligence reporting to Terjon has been feeding a bad actor. This is a slow burn that rewards a Hommlet interlude before it becomes irreversible.

**Foreshadowing Opportunity: Black Jay and the Three Escaped Bandits**
The three bandits fled south. Black Jay is embedded in Hommlet. If the bandits make contact with Temple structure and Black Jay is one of the relay points, the party's activities at the Moathouse will reach Temple leadership within days. This makes the saboteur investigation and the Black Jay thread time-sensitive in a way the party does not currently recognize.

**Party Fault Lines to Watch**
- *Zephyr vs. Calmer:* Zephyr is monitoring Calmer for identity drift. Calmer's increasingly unilateral violence and his near-invocation of St. Cuthbert are the two signals Zephyr is reading. The war council, with its requirement that Calmer publicly perform Earth Temple devotion, is the highest-stakes test of Cover Integrity yet.
- *Sequoia's Secret Redistribution:* If Zephyr or the group ever audits the chest treasure, the private redistribution surfaces. This is a background time bomb.
- *Sequoia's Voss Promise:* Voss was promised mercy. The Church of St. Cuthbert (via Terjon) expects prisoners to be preserved for questioning and heresy judgment. If Voss surfaces in Church custody, Sequoia's promise and Terjon's expectation will collide.

**NPC Paths About to Cross**
- *Hartsch + Zephyr:* The war council is the moment Hartsch's leash is either accepted, negotiated, or broken.
- *Calmer + Hartsch:* The Chest of Rao is in Hartsch's possession. Calmer gave it to Romag against explicit advice. Hartsch inheriting it means the
