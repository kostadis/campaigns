# HANDOUT — Kelno's Bribery Ledger (one page)

> **GM context:** Open on Kelno's table when the party enters. He does not hide it. He keeps meticulous records — the kind of bookkeeping a man does when he has no one left to trust.
>
> The ledger is bound in dull gray leather. The page they see is the most recent. Columns are kept in a thin, almost feminine hand. **Marks beside each name:** ▲ paid/loyal · ◯ paid/wavering · ✕ paid/defected or dead · ⌀ refused
>
> **Tone for read-aloud:** dry, exact, no emotion. A spreadsheet of corruption.

---

> **What the party should notice:**
>
> - **Garr ✕ "killed by adv."** with the margin note explicitly identifying *"halfling and tiefling"* — Kelno knows it was them. He has known for at least a day. He is not pretending otherwise.
> - **"They are the ones Belsornig is leaving alone"** — Kelno has Belsornig's intercepted dispatch on his table for a reason. He has connected the two. He thinks the party are *protected* by Belsornig's restraint.
> - **"They came in on the wraith's path. That was the wraith's path."** — Kelno notes the route. He understands what they cleared.
> - **"Set out the second chair."** — The single most damning line. He decided to receive them as guests *the day before they arrived.* This is not surprise. This is a man who has prepared for them.
> - **Patris ✕✕ "DO NOT approach. F's."** — Gefreid Patris, the Hunting Cottage relay overseer (Hedrack supply chain canon). The "F" is Feldrin. Kelno tried to recruit/bribe him once, was told off, and recorded the failure as a permanent warning to himself. *This is the party's first written reference to "Patris" and "F" — seed-planting for the supply chain quest later.*
> - **Joseph at Doomwatch / "Lucius bal."** — *Joseph Millar the saboteur* at Doomwatch Keep, on Lucius Graeme's books. The party doesn't know either name yet. This is the breadcrumb. (See `hommlet_threats.md` and `nulb_network.md` for the GM picture.)
> - **Dalla / paid through Wat** — Dala the serving wench at the Waterside (Nulb), paid through Wat. Multi-network informant (canon: she sells to Lucius and Smigmal too). Another long-game seed.
> - **Skole ⌀ "refuses coin / fears Lodriss"** — Confirms what the party already knows about Skole. Validates him as a real ally; he isn't on Kelno's payroll.

> **GM use:**
> - Hand this to the players physically and let them sit with it. The columns reward close reading.
> - The "set out the second chair" line should land like a punchline when Kelno gestures to the empty chair in his own room.
> - Several seeds are planted for sessions 28+. Don't gloss them — the players who read carefully should feel rewarded later when "Joseph" and "Patris" reappear in Lucius-related material.
